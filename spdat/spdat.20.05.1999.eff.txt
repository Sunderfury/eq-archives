20/05/1999

Just for fun (boy, I must have been bored!) I went thru the spdat.eff file,
and cleaned out everything but the spell names themselves.  Here's the full
(current) list of spells, and spell effects, like animal poisons, rabies, GM
special spells, Guide spells, etc.
This is in the exact order they appear in the file.  There is obviously
meaning here, mostly sorted by what class gets the spells, I think, but it's
somewhat obscure in places.  The end of the list is game items/animals
effects, it seems.

Have fun with it.  If you wonder what a specific spell is for/does, try a
hex editor on a COPY of spdat.eff, search for the spell name.  After it will
be the text messages associated with that spell/effect, which should give
you a clue what it's for.
