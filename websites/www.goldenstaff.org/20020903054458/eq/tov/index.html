Guide to Temple of Veeshan (Hall of Testing)

This guide will help you to find your way to and through ToV Hall of Testing. It covers general things about the HoT and some suggestions on Fighting Strategies and also some useful Links.
This guide isn't the Testament and it only contain my experience I made and suggestions out of my experience (3 weeks Eastern Faith)/what I read.

What you need for the Raid

In order to plan a successful raid of Hot (Hall of Testing), one must have a proper force to attack and should have at least 3 hours time to play.
All players should be of level 54 or higher and have at least 100 MR. and 120 FR/CR unbuffed (lower Levels need higher resists as lower Levels). How many groups and what types of classes you have available will decide how many mobs and named mobs you can kill (Repop time in HoT is 1 hour - not sure on named).
My experience shows that u can do ToV with 2 High Level groups or 4 55+.
To the normal classes you bring to the raid (Tanks, Clerics...) you should bring at least one Bard, Shaman (alternative Magician) and Enchanter with you - epics preferred and as higher as better (Bard need to have the Song Occlusion of Sounds - Level 55 and a Drum).

Getting to ToV

- Port to Cobalt Scar , ask for levitate and invis
- walk to Sirens Grotto (invis!!!) - Entrance is located at +1150, +1560
- evac through SG (need Druid or Wizard ) and zone to Western Wastes
- walk to Entrance to the Temple of Veeshann (invis if you are kos to CoV!!!) which is located at +700, +700
- Casters bind at the two Columns before entering ToV

Layout

The map below shows the layout of Temple of Veeshan. The orange dots represent mobs that are kos to everyone, the green dots show mobs that only kos if u haven't at least dubious CoV Faction.

ToV is broken up by three areas. One is HoT for kos people to CoV where the Kael Quest armor drops (West) and the other is the HoT for nonkos (=at least dubious) people where the SS Quest armor drops (East). The third is the "Northern Part of ToV" - home of the Named Dragons (Dragons 70+).

Once into ToV you can only exit ToV through Gate, Port or the exits in the West - which requires at least dubious to CoV and the exit in the North.

Hall of Testing

Arrived in ToV (as far I know drakes along the way to HoT see invis so if you are kos wait for CoH) open the door to the north and follow the hallway to the end. At the end turn right and open the door. Now you are in a big room with 4 doors - (West - Kos CoV Hot, North - North ToV with Named Dragons level 70+, East Non-kos HoT). Move to the middle of the room and head east. DON'T OPEN THE DOOR !!!!! - ask if anyone inside is fighting and if its ok if you open the door.

Some Basics on HoT

  • Never enter or open the Door (No.1 on the Map) without asking ! - why? Cause the guards form outside HoT will assist the mob you fight atm
    or incase u have CoV kos peoples with you they will jump on you.
  • Hunting in HoT wont ruin your Faction to CoV - except Boss of the HoT (Dozekar - Level 70 bad ass!)
  • Dragons cant be mezzed - so if you get adds multitank them
  • Clerics and Caster stand behind the wall (No. 3 on Map). Cleric all the time as long you can heal your target - caster until you start nuking.
    Why? so you are not hit by the AOE there. (its not an exploit!)
  • Fighting the Mob at the pot in the Corner (No.2 on Map) seems to be the best solution.
  • On pulls all STAND !!!
  • Mobs are Level 60 to 65 - named level 65 to 70.
  • Only named dropping quest items

Fighting Strategies

As my experience showed M.T.T (Main Tank Tactics) and C.H.R (Complete Heal Rotations) are the best tactics to bring high level encounter down.

M.T.T.

The idea of M.T.T is that only one Tank will need to be completely healed.
How you achieve this? Its quite easy the Main Tank (declared by Raid Leader (AC, Weapons, Level)) should taunt as only person and build agro before all other engage the mob. All other Melees attack the mob at the max. attack range. Why? Cause as far you away from the mob less agro you produce.

This idea I got of a melee board. They suggested to make a hot button like this one:

hotkey name( up to you any you like )
Line 1: /discipline defensive
Line 2: /pause 600*
Line 3: /pause 520*
Line 4: /tell xxxxxx (Main Tank 2) my def gone in 5 sec,move up taunt and open defensive plz!!
Line 5: /gu my def gone ,change your target to xxxxxx (Main Tank 2) for CH plz!!

*10 = 1 sec

C.H.R.

The idea of C.H.R is to force the healing on one target cause for one Cleric it is almost impossible to keep the main Tank alive (on High Level encounters) and she/he will produce agro and be summoned and killed. So you try to split the heals among the clerics.
How does it work? All clerics should make a hot button like this one:

Line 1: /cast (ch spell slot number - if u have the spell in first slot /cast 1 in second slot /cast 2 and so on.....)
Line 2: /shout Casting CH on %t xxxxxx be ready!
Line 3: /pause 30
Line 4: /tell xxxxxx your next on list to CH !

(Now your asking, what does this macro do ? Well, it heals your target, and includes a pause on 3 seconds where you after automatically let the next person who are up for CH know, that it is his/hers turn. )

I see this working best when all clerics in one group (communication, loose of information) and if possible with a Bard 55+ for mana song.
This wont work the first time you try (getting message to heal to late cause of battle spam) and the timing must be finded out, but as soon this problems solved and you trained it its quite easy.

Suggestions on Groups/Buffing

  • One Enchanter or Shaman should be in each tank Group for haste and one Enchanter in each Caster group for C2. This will reduce the downtime for buffing and the spam in tells/ooc/guild chat asking for buff xy.
  • One Druid and/or Shaman should be enough to heal the members of the group - cause if the M.T.Ts. are followed you only will be hitted by the AOE Spell (full dmg aprox 600 HP).
  • Clerics should group together cause of better communication and concentrate on healing the Main Tank(s).
  • Bard is adding 160 MR 80FR/CR/DR/PR with his songs (Elemental Rhythms and Guardian Rhythms) using a Selos Drum of the March - what means using his drums he wont melee. Best use of her/him is with peoples who laggin on resists or in AOE Range (Resists are at 255 maxed)

We all know form our Naggy / Vox raid buffing/froming Groups is the most consuming and boring point of a raid and is causing most downtime.

There are two ways to do the buffing. First way, and I prefer this one, is build the group as supposed except one and invite the person (Cleric, Enchanter, Druid, Shaman) to your group. At end all none buffed persons group and getting buffed.
Other way is people try to get their buffs themselves by telling the person for buffs - what is a pain for the person cause she/he gets into tell hell so be patient u will get your buff. If not retell after some min.

All in all you can help making this faster if you be patient listen to the Raid leader, do /LFG ON at start of the raid and don't go AFK or do other things.

Fighting Procedure

  • After all preparations are done (making Groups, Buffing, Hot buttons (all should have an assist button)) Monk starts pulling (first u have to kill the 2 Wurms down the stairs - level 60ties if I remind right). They are hard to split cause of the room the Monk has so please all stand back and wait till main tank shout to attack) - on pulls all stand please!!!
  • Main tank engage the mob and tank/taunt him for 10 seconds using defensive disciplines.
  • While main tank building agro Bard should debuff the mob using Occlusion of Sounds (30 resistance debuff unresistable) - as soon done msg %T is debuffed so enchanter/magician/shaman know they can start debuffing now (Shaman/Magician with Malosi Serie and Enchanter with Tashani Serie).
  • As soon the mob is dispelled enchanter or shaman try to slow the mob.
  • After main tank built agro he shout Engage %T and this is the sign for all tanks to attack - plz try to attack from the max. Melee range.
  • If disc fades or Main Tank down Main Tank 2 move forward and build agro (see M.T.T)
  • Only Main tank use TAUNT !!!!
  • at 50% Casters nuke the bad mofo to death :-)
  • If you get agro DON'T RUN!!! STAND AND HOPE MAIN TANK GET THE MOB OF YOU - if not wait for the rez window. Ping Pong will only get others killed!!!
  • If you die consent all clerics with rezsticks.
  • Loot and hope it has an armor piece
