The header column denotes the class, followed by the djinn name and the two npc’s he summons that give the actual quest. The first column is the keyword that with other text triggers the actual quest info. Second column is the items necessary to complete the quest, and the third is the item that is returned. Most of these are unknown, because all the quest says is something like makes you a better warrior, or something similarly vague. I have the full quest details at http://www.jrh.net/skyquests.doc in word form, use the outline view to get a nice easy to manage presentation. Oh yeah, the actual room that has all these djinns is of course in the sky plane. When you first zone in, that rock with the keymaster on it is actually hollow inside. To get into the room you have to jump off with levitate and use the hallway into the rock that is just under the edge. Use your external camera and you can see it before you jump off (if you are the keymaster its almost directly to his left I believe). The room is called the sunset home as I was informed by a gm. My email address is jr@jrh.net if anybody gets any quest to work i would love to hear about it.
