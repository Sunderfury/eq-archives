Ranger FA Quest

  1. BW: Telin Darkforest [Near Skyfire Zone or near Hornet Nest]:
    Say the following:
    • what are you doing in this forest?
    • what answers?
    • what evil?
    • what do you suspect?
    • perhaps it is Innoruuk?
    • what action should I take?

    receive a note

  2. GFay: Give Telin's note to Faelin Bloodbriar [+1500, +500; rare spawn] & receive ring
  3. Kith: Give Faelin's ring to Giz X`Tin & receive coin
  4. BW: Give Giz's coin to Telin Darkforest
  5. EK: Give Giz's coin to Althele & receive amulet
  6. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Sionae & get amulet back
  7. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Nuien & get amulet back
  8. EK: Give Althele's amulet to Teloa & follow to gathering
  9. EK: Talk to Althele & Dark Elf Corruptor spawns
  10. EK: Kill Dark Elf Corruptor & loot book
  11. EK: Give book to Althele & receive earth stained note
  12. Misty: Give earth stained note to Elle Foodcrafter and receive bowl
  13. Create a Hardened Mixture by combining the following foraged items in Elle's bowl:
    • Everfrost: Chilled Tundra Root
    • Greater Faydark: Ripened Heartfruit
    • Innothule Swamp: Speckled Molded Mushroom
    • Misty Thicket: Sweetened Mudroot
  14. Create the Runecrested Bowl
    1. Obtain the incomplete pattern [sub-quest]:
      1. TD: Alrik Farsight [chessboard]
        Say the following:
        • I seek an ancient type of bowl made by the elves of old/
        • I will take the artifact.

        recieve a receipt crushed pot

      2. FelB: Give Alrik's crushed pot to Farios Elianos & receive a shopping list
      3. FelA: Give Farios's shopping list to Merchant Nora & receive groceries
      4. FelB: Give groceries to Farios Elianos & receive a recipt for services rendered
      5. TD: Give recipt for services rendered to Alrik Farsight and recieve an incomplete pattern
    2. Obtain the Platinum Speckled Powder [sub-quest]:
      1. FV: Forage a Firiona Rose
      2. SG: Hail Merdan Fleetfoot, say 'Who is Niera?', and give him the Firiona Rose to recieve a wood painting.
      3. FM: Give the wood painting to a human skeleton [-2376, +1963] to recieve a silver chained locket.
      4. SG: find Niera Farbreeze and say the following:
        • What tune is that.
        • Why are they always there?
        • Where did she find the speckled, flecked dust?

        Give Niera the silver chained locket to receive Platinum Speckled Powder.

    3. Obtain the Enchanted Clay [sub-quest]:
      KAL B: Kinlo Strongarm in Everhot's Forge, bottom floor.
      Say the following:
      • what mud
      • do it for me

      Give Kinlo Strongarm a Jade Reaver [from City of Mist] & he will give you Enchanted Clay.

    Combine the Ancient Patter, Platinum Speckled Powder, and Enchanted Clay in a Pottery Wheel to get the Runecrested Bowl

  15. Misty: Give Ella Foodcrafter the Hardened Mixture and Runecrested Bowl to get the Softly Glowing Stone.
  16. FV: Talk to Foloal Stormforest.
    Say the following:
    • are you one such as myself?
    • what answers?
    • what about venril sathir?
  17. FV: Foloal Stormforest wants the Softly Glowing Stone that you have and whatever "conduit of power" of power you can find on Venril Sathir's corpse.
  18. KC: Kill Venril Sathir's Remains to get the sickly pulsating stone.
  19. FV: Give Foloal Stormforest the stone from Venril Sathir and the Softly Glowning Stone to get the Warmly Glowing Stone.
  20. BW: Give Telin Darkwood the Warmly Glowing Stone to get the Ancient Longsword.
  21. (optional)KALB: Say the following to Kinlo Strongarm:
    • what ancient
    • what few tales
    • what hammer hammers
    • what Erudite necromancer

    He tells you to look on Odus for a hammer with the soul of a blacksmith trapped inside

  22. Hole: Jaeil the Wretched needs to be at least indifferent to you. Use Sneak if he is not and con him to make sure it works.
    Give Jaeil the Tin Bowl and Jaeil the Insane spawns. Kill him and loot the hammer.
  23. (optional) KALB: Kinlo Strongarm again. Give him the hammer and he tells you to find a way to release the trapped dwarf.
  24. Erudin Palace: Mairee Silentone is in the jewlrey shop near the bank. She is at the back of the store.
    Give her the hammer and she give it back, saying to bring it back with a Swirling Globe of Color from the Plane of Air.
  25. POA: Go to Island Four of the Plane of Air. Kill the Essensce Tamer to get the "Swirling Globe."
  26. Erudin Palace: Give Mairee Silentone the hammer and the Swirling Sphere. She alters the hammer and returns it to you, telling you to give the altered hammer to Kinlo.
  27. KALB: Give Kinlo Strongarm the hammer (he shatters it), which causes Usbak the Old to spawn. Usbak throws you some mithril ore.
  28. KALB: Hail Usbak the Old and give him the ancient longsword. He balances it and gives you back a "refined ancient longsword".
  29. KALB: Give Kinlo Strongarm the mithril ore, and he gives you a "refined mithril blade".
  30. BW: Give Telin Darkforest the Refined Ancient Blade and he gives it back. Faelin Bloodbriar appears. Give her the Refined Ancient Blade. She enchants it and returns it to you. RECEIVE SWIFTWIND
  31. POH: Kill Innoruuk to get the Shattered Emerald of Corruption
  32. NK: Hail Xansuus. Give Xanuusus the mithril sword and the Shattered Emerald of Corruption. He joins them together and returns them to you. RECEIVE EARTHCALLER
