Halfling Cultural Armor

Ingredients for Halfling Cultural Armor

Halfling cultural armor requires the following special ingredients:

  • High Quality Metal Bits

    Smithing - This can only be made by Halfling Smiths, and must be made in the Rivervale forge. Combine two pieces of High Quality Ore and a Flask of Water. The High Quality Ore is occasionally found on Runney Eye goblins. It can also be found on goblins in Permafrost. This becomes trivial at a smithing skill of around 80.

  • Vale Sewing Kit

    Smithing - This can only be made by Halfling Smiths, and must be made in the Rivervale forge. Combine High Quality Metal Bits (quantity unknown), a Needle Mold, a Thimble Mold and a Flask of Water. This becomes trivial at a smithing skill of around 160.

  • Acorn Oil

    Brewing - This can be created by a member of any race, using any Brew Barrel. It is not restricted to Halflings or Rivervale only. Combine a Flask of Water and a Misty Acorn in a brewing barrel. The acorns are random ground spawn in the Misty Thisket. This becomes trivial at a brewing skill of around 103.

  • Imbued Plains Pebble

    Cleric - Plains Pebbles are foraged in the various Karana zones. They must be imbued by a cleric who worships the god Karana. Imbued Plains Pebbles add STA and WIS attributes to armor created with them.


Vale Studded Leather

Vale studded leather armor is created by combining the appropriate tailoring pattern with Acorn Oil, some quantity of Metal Studs, and a Brute Hide. You can optionally include an Imbued Plains Pebble, which will give the items you create additional stamina and wisdom enhancements. These items can only be crafted by a Halfling Tailor, and must be combined in a Vale Sewing Kit. The Brute Hides drop off Vicious Brutes in Warsliks Woods. Vale Studded Leather becomes trivial at around skill 115. If you include an Imbued Plains Pebbles, the trivial level rises to 122.

Vale Reinforced Leather

Vale reinforced leather armor is created by combining the appropriate tailoring pattern with Acorn Oil, some quantity of Steel Boning, and a High Quality Brute Hide. You can optionally include an Imbued Plains Pebble, which will give the items you create additional stamina and wisdom enhancements. These items can only be crafted by a Halfling Tailor, and must be combined in a Vale Sewing Kit. The High Quality Brute Hides drop off Vicious Brutes in Warsliks Woods and the Frontier Mountans. The trivial level is unknown.

Wood Elf Cultural Armor

Ingredients for Wood Elf Cultural Armor

Wood Elf cultural armor requires the following special ingredients:

  • Mithril Bits

    Smithing - 2 small pieces mithril ore, morning dew.

  • Mithril Boning

    Smithing - small brick mithril ore, file, morning dew. Recipe unconfirmed.

  • Mithril Studs

    Smithing - 3 mithril bits, file, morning dew. Recipe unconfirmed.

  • Fier'dal Sewing Kit

    Smithing - mithril bits, needle mold, thimble mold, flask water. Creates a ten-slot sewing kit that only works for the cultural armor. You'll still need a regular sewing kit to make conventional items.

  • Oak Bark Tanin

    Brewing - A flask of water and a piece of Oak Bark, combined in a bewing barrel. The Oak Bark can be foraged in Kithicor and Warslik's Woods, and also occurs as a ground spawn in Butcherblock. This recipe is currently bugged.

Mithril Studded Leather

Mithril studded leather armor is created by combining the appropriate tailoring pattern with oak bark tanin, some quantity of mithril studs, and a Sabertooth Tiger Hide. The items must be combined in a Fier'dal Sewing Kit, and the sabertooth tiger hides drop from Kunark sabertooth tigers. These recipes are unconfirmed.
