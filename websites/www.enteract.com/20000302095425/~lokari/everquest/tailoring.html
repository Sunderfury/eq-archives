Leather Armor

Tailors can create three kinds of leather armor – patchwork, studded and reinforced. The size of the armor made depends upon the kind of pelt used.

Silk Armor

All silk-based items require at least some of the following components:

  • Silk Swatch

    These are made by combining two spider (not spiderling) silks in a sewing kit. Swatches become trivial at skill 17; however, they never fail.

  • Heady Kiola

    Cured silk armor requires a Heady Kiola component, which is created using the brewing skill. Combine two kiola saps, one water flask, and one bottle in a brewers barrel. This item requires only a low skill at brewing, so any tailor should be able to make them as needed. The cost to make them, assuming no failures, is just under 3 gold each. Heady Kiola becomes trivial at brewing skill 46.

  • Viscous Mana

    This is an enchanter made item. It uses the 12th level enchanter spell Thicken Mana, which is available in the Brownie village in Lesser Faydark, and on an island in the Ocean of Tears. Casting the spell requires the material components of a poison vial (made by a potter) and a pearl.

Raw Silk

Raw silk armor is wearable by Monks, Mages, Necromancers, Wizards, and Enchanters, of any race. Raw silk armor is made by combining silk swatches with the appropriate pattern. All raw silk items have a weight of 0.4. Raw silk becomes trivial to create at skill 39.

The items that will be the most popular among monks are: Sleeves, Mantle, Cape, Pants, Boots and Robe.

The items that will be in least demand are: Bracers, Gloves, Headband, Belt, Gorget and Mask.

Wu's Fighting Gauntlets

These magical glowing silk gloves are Monk only, AC 4, with a weight of 0.1, and give off light as a greater lightstone. The recipe is 1 glove pattern, 1 swatch of silk, 1 greater lightstone, 1 vial of viscous mana, and 4 heady kiolas. Assuming no failures, and considering the market value of the components, these gloves cost a minimum of 17 plat to make. The suggested sale price is 50 platinum. These become trivial to make at skill 88.

Shadow Silk

The various kinds of Shadow Silk are used as parts of necromancers quests.

  • Shadow Silk
    1 Shadow Wolf Pelt, 1 Silk Swatch, & 1 Scroll of Gather Shadows.

  • Large Shadow Silk
    2 Shadow Wolf Pelts, 1 Silk Swatch, & 1 Scroll of Gather Shadows.

  • Inkie Shadow Silk:
    1 Shadow Wolf Pelt, 1 Silk Swatch, & 2 Scrolls of Gather Shadows.

Other Items

  • Backpacks

    Tailors made backpacks weigh the same as regular backpacks (3 pounds) but have 10 slots. These require a backpack pattern and a high quality bear skin. It is possible to begin making backpacks with a 20% or so success rate at skill level 35. Backpacks become trivial to create at skill 88.

  • Whips

    1 Medium Quality wolf pelt, 4 Heady Kiola, 1 pattern. It creates a "Tailor Made Whip" with damage 7 and 33 delay.
