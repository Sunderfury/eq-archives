Ingredients for Velious Recipes

Yew Leaf Tannin

Brewing - Combine a Yew Leaf and a Flask of Water in a brewing barrel. Yew Leaves are ground spawn in the Wakening Lands.

Drake Egg Oil

Brewing - Combine a Drake Egg and a Flask of Water in a brewing barrel. Drake Eggs are foraged in Skyshrine.

Cod Oil

Brewing - Recipe not known.

Velium Studs

Smithing - Combine two Pieces of Velium with a Coldain Velium Temper in a forge. This creates stackable Velium Bits. Then combine three Velium Bits, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper in a forge. This creates two Velium Studs, which are stackable.

Velium Bonings

Smithing - Combine a Small Brick of Velium, a File, and a Coldain Velium Temper in a forge. This creates one Velium Boning, which is not stackable.

Clear Mana

Enchanter - Recipe not known.

Distilled Mana

Enchanter - Recipe not known.

Purified Mana

Enchanter - Recipe not known.
