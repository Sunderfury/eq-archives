    When you left me I cried,   
   and tried to forget,   
   What we had shared together,   
   or that we had ever met.

    I built strong walls around me,    
   to protect me from the pain,   
   pretended I'd never seen or kissed you,   
   and never heard your name.

    But when I passed you,   
   in the street today.   
   it brought back memories,   
   that I had pushed away.

    My walls collapsed,   
   the pain returned,   
   now I now that,   
   I have much yet to learn.

    Shouldn't try to forget you,   
   instead just try to go on,   
   and after a while the hurt,   
   won't be quite so strong.

    Because nothing lasts forever,   
   this loss won't seem quite real,   
   just have to be patient and realize,   
   that wounds take time to heal.


          MLR 1988
