    The coin is spinning, the dice are thrown,
    The fate is unaswered, the fears are grown.
    While haunted for a day is she,
    Standing haunted for years is he.
    Called for advice is the wise woman of the clan,
    Her words simple, Place thy chip on the board young man.
    For life is a gamble, with none to know the result,
    The choice is your own, if you are to be an adult.
    The decision was weighed, a small deception against the truth,
    The plunge was made, cold waters shaking the memories of youth.
