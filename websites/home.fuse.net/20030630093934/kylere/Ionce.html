    I once stood straight slowly shedding soft silent tears of pride,
    now that emotion is frail next to dreams of you being my bride.
    I once felt bright laughter and thought it was easing my pain,
    next to the happiness and comfort of you, it was legerdemain.
    I once lay silently on my back observing the night time stars,
    that sensation of wonder is weakened by the love that is ours.
    I once experienced glory and honor fighting for my nation,
    but that bright lit glory pales next to that of your creation.
    I once spent hours lost in the natural beauty of the sea,
    that feeling is for naught next to the way you look at me.
    I once thought I was warm laying on a pure white sands,
    but I know true warmth lies only in holding your hands.
    I once gazed amused in morning misty mountains far,
    they unlike my love for you will soon weather and mar.
    I once felt friendship reaching out to sooth my life,
    but now your companionship eases all my strife.
    I once beheld a forest shining glass ice clad,
    To compare it to you I would be called mad.
    I once noticed a flower full of wild intricacy,
    was but a toy compared to your delicacy.
    I once let a snowflake melt on my hand,
    next to your skin it felt rough like sand.
    I once drank a glass of perfect wine,
    next to your kiss it merely was fine.
    I once decided I knew of true care,
    next to yours was but dark despair.
    I once called out in happiness,
    now I know it was emptiness.
    I once found morning light,
    now the sun seems slight.
    I once loved being alone.
    now I must simply atone.
    I once near perished,
    now I am cherished.
    I once...
   
    -MLR 2003
