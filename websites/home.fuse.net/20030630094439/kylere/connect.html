    Can you hear me, for I am the scream behind this screen.
    Searching for reality, lost amongst the dream.
    I seek you, in a world of others, found through lines of force,
    A normal relationship, running a virtual course.
    Routed to your perfection, switched to your soul.
    Internetworked to your fantasy, connection my only goal.
   
    Yes, I can hear you, a voice from distant past-
    Running through my memory, catching up at last.
    The seeker and the sought after overcome futility,
    Connect to send and recieve, to make the dream reality.
    Though time detained the signal, and we've paid the painful cost,
    It has met it's destination, and now never shall be lost.
   
    They who hesitate are lost, and we have been found,
    Connected by fate, like electricity seeking the ground.
    Our running is near over except for side by side,
    We skip into perfection, outrun pain stride by stride.
    We ran a marathon while thinking life was just a foot race,
    All those past aches will ease as we fold into an embrace.
