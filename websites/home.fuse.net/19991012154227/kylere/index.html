As an informational bit, I have stopped playing EQ, but will keep the page going. If you want to know why I quit, well, to be honest EQ started boring me, I made it to 33rd level with my HIE Paladin, and found that all the other warrior type classes were outdamaging me. The solution to this is Ghoulbane, a weapon that does not load that frequently, I broke my own rule and went to camp it. After 24 hours I had seen it once and a 48th level Wizard decided it was his instead, I even offered him my full armour set in exchange for it and was denied. I really feel no game is worth that frustration and as such I am looking at some things like Shattered Light and the upcoming Neverwinter Nights to provide a smaller community game, that has people that desire fun gameplay not monthly income to make their decisions for them.
