/rofl

Soandso rolls on the floor laughing

Soandso waves at (targeted player) (Actually seen in game)

/cry

Soandso cries over (targeted player) (Seen in game, used on corpses a lot)

Miscellaneous Commands

/played

How long you have played

/filter

This is fun, trying to decide what curse people were using

/follow

Great in wide open areas, you can auto follow

/roleplay

Turns name purple, turns you anon, through manual settings you can remove anon but it returns every time you zone. The company moron must have thought up and coded this

       ~

Kills the overlays, F9 cycle works just as well and I have remapped this as my auto-attack key

/random (x)

Random number generator

/shownames

Names on, names off

/split # # # #

Splits cash, 0 0 0 10 splits 10 copper with party 31 alone splits 31 plat

/time

Game time (useful for some quests)

/who [filter]

all, all "Guildname", all cleric 40, all 40 50, etc

/petition <msg>

Well you can try it but answer times if any are long, try to play through it first

Speech Commands

/reply <msg>

Sends a /tell to the last person who sent you a /tell message. Also /r

/say <msg>

Lets those in local area hear you

/ooc <msg>

Used by many, abused by a few

/shout <msg>

Shouts to all of the zone.

/group <msg>

To talk to your group.  Also /g

/auction <msg>

Auction channel

/tell [name]

Used to send a direct message to an individual
