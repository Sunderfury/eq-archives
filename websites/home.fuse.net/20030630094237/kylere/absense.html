    Flashing lights on rainy glass,
    shattering through to my heart,
    pulselike beams of strife and hurt.
    Shards of emotion,
    crystalline patterns of disillusion,
    slicing through my soul.
    The fury of sound, the racing of thought,
    tell me the relativity of a shattered heart.
    Imagination ripped away,
    leaving only the endless void of my mind.
    Apathy enveloping, nonchalance developing,
    death of dreams, bleeding screams.
    Standing at the edge of infinity,
    seeing the end of eternity.
    Solar fire engulfs the night,
    It falls not uttering a cry,
    but fading with forlorn sigh.
    The fall of skies, the end of lies,
    sound to silence, everything to emptiness.
    End of all pain, death of the sane,
    fading bits of blasted plains,
    only vacuum remains.
    Life has no cost,
    when all hope is lost.
    The end of the lonely, leaving only
    The hate that remains, always the same.
   
   MLR 1991
