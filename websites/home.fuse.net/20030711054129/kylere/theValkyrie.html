    Encased in steel I stand, guarding the walls of my soul,
    a knight in dulled armour, emptiness is my only goal.
    I wear a flaming sword, created in the forges of pain,
    enpowered by the memories, I will never face again.
   
    Standing on the tower, built of flawless steel,
    surveying my twisted realm, wondering what is real.
    Am I or am I merely a spector, like the ghastly figures of light,
    on the plains around me, locked in my eternal work of night.
   
    I feel something close to pity, for the denizens of my land,
    for each of them were created, by the sword in my hand.
    Some for their love, some for their hare,
    some for the emotion, I've been feeling of late.
   
    Doesn't matter how strong you are, loneliness, the stuff of screams
    Can take your world over, and destroy all your dreams.
    This is how I stood, living a horrid lie,
    designed by myself, waiting only to die.
   
    Then I found her, a flash in the corner of my eye,
    the first true light, in my worlds darkened sky.
    I smiled and turned to destroy, this new challenger of fate,
    Who was saying that I could love, when I could only hate.
   
    She fought like a Valkyrie, then I saw her eyes,
    seeing the depth of her hope, I began to fantasize.
    A world of laughter and light, where pain is not the king,
    sorrow is replaced by joy, and we can do anything.
   
    Distracted by her beauty, weakened by her charms,
    I let her draw first blood, then staggered into her arms.
    Shocked by the innocence and passion that I found,
    I realized that she was where, my future was bound.
