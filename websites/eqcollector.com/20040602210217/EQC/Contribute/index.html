[ Guest ] . . . .. :: .: ..: . . ..: [ Helping EQCollector Project ] : . . . . : .

You can help EQCollector in different ways.

1. Testing and discusing EQCollector features and result data.

Currently there are only few people who actively analyzing collected data. The algoritms of the data collection are not simple. Especially if you are talking about mobs and quests collection. EQCollector still have some problems while trying to collect those types of data. Also any ideas on what new kind of data can be collected and how it can be done will be very helpful.

2. Helping others in forums. Writing documentation.

I would like to thank all people who are now answering questions in the emulator forum. Whithout them forum would be dead by now. If you willing to help, you always welcome to join them. Also I have to admit that EQCollector is not well documented. Even readme.txt have to be re-written. This is a big area where people with good English and good undestanding of EQCollector can be very helpful.

3. Helping to support website.

Emulator website (as well as Hackersquest) need to be supported on more regular basis. If you willing to help, please contact ashran@ethernalquest.org

4. Donating to this project.

And the last but not the least. Support of this project takes a lot of time. Hosting of related websites are not free to us. If you willing to help us finantially, you can donate to this project via PapPal. Just click on the icon below to do so.
