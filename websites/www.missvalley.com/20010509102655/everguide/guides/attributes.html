Attributes Info

Strength (Str)
Strength determines how much a person can carry. You can carry one weight point for every strength point you have. Strength also determines your upper damage cap. It is a primary attribute for Bards, Clerics, Monks, Paladins, Rangers, Shadowknights, and Warriors.

Stamina (Sta)
Stamina determines how many hit points you start with and the amount of hit points you get for each level. It also determines how much stamina you have for your stamina bar. It is a primary attribute for Clerics, Druids, Magicians, Monks, Paladins, Rangers, Shadowknights, Shamans, Warriors, and Wizards.

Agility (Agi)
Agility affects how well you can dodge attacks. It also determines your base AC. It is a primary attribute for Monks, Rangers, Rogues, and Warriors.

Dexterity (Dex)
Dexterity determines your chances to hit. It also determines how good bards can play their instruments. It is a primary attribute for Bards, Monks, Necromancers and Rogues.

Wisdom (Wis)
Wisdom determines mana for Clerics, Shamans, Druids, Rangers, and Paladins. It also determines resistance against mind spells. It is a primary attribute for Clerics, Druids, Paladins, Rangers, and Shamans.

Intelligence (Int)
Intelligence determines the mana for Wizards, Enchanters, Magicians, Necromancers, and Shadowknights. It also determines how quick you gain skills. It is a primary attribute for Enchanters, Magicians, Necromancers, Shadowknights, and Wizards.

Charisma (Cha)
Charisma determines how much you buy and sell for to NPCs. It determines how fast your faction changes. It also plays a part in how charm spells affect you. It is a primary attribute for Bards, Enchanters, Paladins, Shadowknights, and Shamans.
