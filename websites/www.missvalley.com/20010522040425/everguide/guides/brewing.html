Brewing

So you've decided to brew, huh? Well good for you! There aren't many of us out there. I have listed here everything I know about brewing, including where to get the materials and how to make different types of Alcohol. Brewing is fairly inexpensive so practicing it a lot shouldn't be too hard.

First, you should put a few, I would say between 10 to 15, skill points into brewing. The next thing you need is a book of limericks. If you do not want to buy the book I have put all the limericks in the book on this page. There is also a big book with a bunch of formulas. To start the quest for it, talk to the bartender in the Seafarer's Roost in Freeport. I also have all the formulas I know for brewing listed here. The next thing you need is the materials for brewing, including a brew barrel. There are a couple in about every town so they shouldn't be hard to find. The only place I know of right now that sells brewing materials is in West Freeport. There is a girl near the militia house and the entrance to North Freeport, Krystin Charcoal. To brew something, click on the brew barrel and put in the materials for one of the drinks. Click combine and if it works, you get the drink, if not, you don't get the drink and you still lose the materials.

Book Limericks

Limerick #1
When Ssra goes to move barley
he meets a girl named Darley
To the cask in the vault
He adds hops and malt
and drinks till her face isn't gnarly.

Limerick #2
With hops, malt, and yeast
An ogre made mead for a feast
When his cask they did fill
Not a drop he did spill
So they cheered the ugly beast.

Limerick #3
My good friend where is my cask?
Is the water too much to ask?
Deliver barley and hops
With the rest of my crops
Or your death will be my next task.

Limerick #4
Ogre's lust for a wench named Sulock
For a date bring a cask and froglok
Malt and yeast to the barber
Get them near her harbor
And you'll hear an uproar if they dock.
