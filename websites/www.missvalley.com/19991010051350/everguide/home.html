Every Man's Guide to Clerics


 


 
 

Tip of the Week

The Wicked Unrest

One of the most hated dungeons in the game by lower level players, and one of the most loved dungeon in the game for the high 20's or early 30's player. Even on a good day Unrest can be killer. How do you survive?

Lower Levels 10-20

Ok, you've got two choices here, group or die. The trains are terrible in Unrest. You will most likely have problems with death beetles who only train you if you attack undead creatures. Fighting one skeleton is going to mean you're going to fight a couple of death beetles at the same time, which can end a low level character's career in a jiffy.

Mid Levels 20-32

Ok, you're the cream of the crop for Unrest adventurers. Some of you will be able to solo Dark Boned Skels all the way up to the harder high 20's creatures like Skeletal Monks. BUT, if you can get a group i'd sure advise it. Don't be shy, stay near the zone, and auction for a group while you pick off trains that pass by. I've spent hours looking for a group in the past, but the exp I gained while grouped was definately worth the wait.

Another rule of thumb to follow in unrest, and any other dungeon for that matter, is keep a mage/cleric/caster in your group. One caster per 2 tanks is pretty much a minimum at mid to high levels. I've gone in the basement of Unrest myself with a full group of level 30 tanks, and we were all dead within about 5 minutes. Clerics are what you need in unrest, and you're also either going to need backup for that cleric (another cleric) or you're going to need a nuker. Bottom line is, stay out of the higher level areas unless you've got 2 casters in your group, and at least one should be a cleric.

Higher Levels 32-50

Why are you wasting your time in Unrest? Get that item you want and move on, there are better places for you!

I think most people understand the mechanics of Unrest fairly well, at least those that travel there alot. First you've got to get through the cauldron, which for a low level player can be a death warrant as well. Then you've got to get IN Unrest. The zone into unrest is full of trains at least every 2-3 minutes. Be careful zoning in, you might just be dead by the time your computer finishes loading. Clerics should keep invis to undead mem'd, as you're going to need it. Druids and Shamans should keep SOW on their party, it'll save someone's life every time. Clerics, if you're the only healer, don't do dammage, save it for heals! I've seen small fights in Unrest last 5 minutes or longer because the mob just keeps getting larger with trains. Shamans, Druids, and Paladins, as well as anyone else who can heal, BACK UP YOUR CLERIC! Watch for the dreaded "low manna" or "oom" from your cleric, use your heal spells to give your cleric time to meditate! And at all costs, keep the mobs off the healer, if you don't you're going to find yourself very dead.
