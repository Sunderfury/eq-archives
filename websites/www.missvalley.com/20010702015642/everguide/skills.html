Skills Guide

The following is a brief description of each skill. If you are looking for more specific information on a trade skill you should read the trade skills section.

Abjure - One of the schools of magic. Abjuration is the grouping of defensive type spells. This school of magic enables the spell caster to use buff spells such as courage, holy armor, etc.

Alchemy - Alchemy allows you to make potions. Potions have magical effects and can enhance your abilities. They also sell for quite a bit of money. A good alchemist can make a very nice living.

Alcohol Tolerance - Dwarves are well known for this skill. This measures your ability to handle the effects of alcohol without adverse side effects. Mostly a role-playing skill (Dwarven drinking contests and such).

Alter - Another school of magic. Spells from this grouping are for altering or changing things. Some examples of this form of magic are illusion, teleport, and change form spells.

Apply Poison - This is typically a thief or assassin skill and is used for placing poisons on weapon's .

Archery - This is the ability to use a ranged or missle weapon. Some examples of missle weapons are bows, slings, and crossbows.

Backstab - This is another theif skill that is for a surprise attack. If you are able to get behind someone and attack from behind it will enhance the amount of damage that you can inflict. It is difficult to use but when you score you score big.

Baking - This ability deals with the making of foods. Specifically cakes, breads, and casseroles.

Bandage - This allows you to be able to heal someone without the need of spells. This skill comes in quite handy when you are out of mana or can't cast spells because of your class.

Bash - This is a combat based skill that deals with pummelling your opponent to death. Some classes may use this skill without a shield but most require a shield to be able to use the skill. In any event the bash attack makes a real nifty sound when used with a shield.

Begging - This is a very hard skill to succeed with. And a sometimes dangerous skill. This skill allows you to beg from npc's in the hopes that they will give you money for nothing. Most npc's won't give you anything else you are really really good in this skill, the problem is, most npc's hate beggars and if you repeatedly beg from someone you could wind up dead instead of richer.

Blacksmithing - This is a very difficult but rewarding skill. This skill enables you to make weapons, armor, and other items made from metal. A good blacksmith will be a rich blacksmith, but the supplies are very expensive and most items require many different materials to make.

Block - This allows you to effectively negate an opponents attack by blocking it. Very useful skill, especially when combined with a shield. A good block skill will effectively make up for a lack of armor.

Blunt Weapons 1-Handed - This is another combat based skill and allows you to use one handed blunt weapons. Some examples are maces and clubs.

Blunt Weapons 2-Handed - This is the same as the one handed blunt weapon skill but gives you the ability to use two handed blunt weapons. Two handed weapons deal out more damage but you can not use a shield while wielding them. So there is some give and take.

Brass Instruments - This is the ability to use brass instruments to make music. This is also the beginning skill for bards and affects the ability to cast their spell songs.

Brewing - This is the ability to make beverages, typically alcoholic beverages.

Channel - This is yet another school of magic, and enables the user to cast channeling type magic. This type of magic deals with reducing the amount of time required to cast a spell regardless of it's nature.

Conjure - This is another school of magic, and enables the caster to use conjuring spells. Conjuring spells allow you to bring items and creatures into existence from somewhere else. Some examples are summon creature spells and summon food and drink spells.

Defense - This is the ability to defend yourself. Unlike the block and dodge spells, this is a more general form of defense.

Disarm - This is the ability to disarm an opponent while in combat.

Disarm Traps - This is the ability to disarm a trap so that it will not harm you or your party.

Divinate - This is yet another form of magic. This form of magic deals with detecting things and concealing things. Some examples of this would be detect invisible and invisible.

Dodge - This is the ability to negate the effects of an opponents attack by not getting hit in the first place.

Double Attack - This is a combat based ability that allows you to take two attacks per round of combat instead of one.

Dragon Punch - This is a monk only skill that allows you to use a special type of attack called the Dragon Punch. The punch inflicts special damage.

Dual Wield - This is the ability to use two weapons at once. Very nice way to inflict lots and lots of damage. Using two weapons at once combined with the double attack skill will make you a walking death machine.

Eagle Strike - This is another monk attack.

Evoke - This is a school of magic that deals with offensive spells such as fire ball.

Feign Death - This ability allows you to appear as if you were dead. This is a very useful skill if you are very low on health and need a way out of a fight. Playing dead may make the monster go away.

Fishing - This is the ability to catch fish using a fishing pole. A nice past time or hobby and a easy way to make some decent money.

Fletching - This skill allows you to make arrows.

Flying Kick - This is yet another monk skill. It is a special form of attack.

Foraging - This is the ability to find food. This skill can difinately save you some money and some trips back to town.

Hand-To-Hand - This is another combat based skill that allows you to attack without a weapon other than your hands.

Hide - This is the ability to hide yourself from sight. This can be very useful if you find yourself in a situation where you can't fight or run.

Jewelry Making - This is a very profitable trade skill. It allows you to make magical jewelry. The magical jewelry can give you added protection or raise some skills and some other nifty effects as well.

Kick - This is another combat based skill that allows to kick an opponent for damage.

Make Poison - This ability allows you to make poisons. Poisons can give you that added damage you need against monsters.

Meditate - This enables spell casters to more quickly recover mana.

Mend - Monk only ability. Allows you to heal yourself, but only yourself.

Offense - This is your base attacking ability.

Parry - This is the ability to deflect an attack using your weapon.

Percussion - This is another bard skill that allows you to used percussion instruments for your songs.

Pick Lock - This is the ability to open locks. Very nice to have if you need to get through a door or open a chest and you don't have a key.

Piercing - This is the combat based skill for using piercing weapons.

Pottery - This is the ability to make pottery.

Research - This is the ability to make scrolls. Very very useful skill for spell casters. Also a very nice trade skill.

Riposte - This is another combat based skill that allows you to negate an opponents attackand quickly strike back. Basically a counter attack method of defense.

Round Kick - This is another monk based special attack.

Safe Fall - This is another monk skill which allows you to safely fall from great heights.

Sense Direction - This is a very useful skill. It is very easy to get lost in Norrath and knowing your heading is half the battle to not getting lost.

Singing - This is the ability to sing. This is basically the bards backup should he ever lose his instrument.

Slashing Weapons 1-Handed - This is the combat ability that allows you to use slashing weapons that are one handed.

Slashing Weapons 2-Handed - This is the combat ability that allows you to use two handed slashing weapons.

Sneak - This is the ability to move about unseen. Different from the hiding skill because with hide you must remain in place.

Steal - This is the ability to steal something. This is different from the pick pocket skill. Picking pockets is used form stealing items that are located on a PC or NPC's body while stealing is for items that are anywhere else.

Stringed Instruments - This is the bards ability to play stringed instruments.

Swimming - This is the ability to not drown when you get dropped in the drink.

Tailoring - This is the ability to make clothing and lower forms of armor. This can be a very profitable and useful skill. It requires much less sophistication than blacksmithing and a lot less money.

Throwing - This is the ability to use thrown weapons. Such as rocks, throwing daggers, etc.

Tiger Claw - This is another monk special attack (they get alot of them don't they?)

Tinkering - This is the ability to all types of items and tools. Some examples would be gnomish gadgets and compasses.

Track - This is the ability to locate npc's within the vacinity of yourself.

Wind Instruments - This is another bard based skill. It allows bards to use wind instruments to make their spell songs.
