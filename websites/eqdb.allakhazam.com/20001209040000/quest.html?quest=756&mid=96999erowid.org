    <h3 align=center id=qname>The Singing Short Sword</h3><hr size=1>
	 <table width=100% cellpadding=3 cellspacing=0 border=0>
	 <tr><td valign=top>
	 <table cellpadding=1 cellspacing=0 border=0 align=left>
	 <tr><td><span id=rt>Starts in:</span></td><td><a href='http://everquest.allakhazam.com/zones/Kunark/Dreadlands.html'>Dreadlands</a></td></tr>
	 <tr><td id=rt>Who:</td><td>Baldric Slezaf</a></td></tr>
	 <tr><td id=rt>Rating:</td><td>Good (4.33)</td></tr>
	 <tr><td id=rt>Estimated Time:</td><td>Days</td></tr>
	 <tr><td id=rt>Minimum Level:</td><td>50</td></tr><tr><td id=rt>Best Class:</td><td>Bard</td></tr><tr><td colspan=2><span id=rt>Related Creatures:</span><li>Amygdalan Tendril</li><li>Blackwing</li><li>Drolvarg Warlord</li><li>Edrik the Old</li><li><a href=http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=5034>Gorenaire</a></li><li>Kelkin Mekia</li><li>Konia Swiftfoot</li><li>Nezekezena</li><li><a href=http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=5270>Quag Maelstrom</a></li><li><a href=http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=5072>Talendor</a></li><li><a href=http://eqbeastiary.allakhazam.com/search.shtml?id=4503>Trakanon</a></li></ul></td></tr></td></tr>
	 </table> A) Baldric Slezaf asks you for 4 items <br><br>1) page 24 (actually in two halves)<br>2) page 25<br>3) Lute made by Forper Fizfla<br><br>B) To get top part page 24 go to West Karana and see Konia Swiftfoot.  He gives you a torch and starts a race.  Take the torch to Fajio Knejo in Misty Thicket (close to the wall guards).  He gives you another torch.  Then take that torch to Andad Filla in South Ro (close to innothule zone) who gives you another torch.  Then take that torch to Misty Tekchita in Lake Rathe (by arena zone).  This will get you a ring.  Finally, take the ring back to Konia Swiftfoot and you will get the top part of page 24<br><br>C) To get the bottom half of you need to give Konia Swiftfoot Mahlin's Bongos.  Talk to Braenar Swiftsong.  He will give you an invoice after you ask "what doll".  Go to Solusek A and give the gnome vendor, Marfen the invoice and you will recieve a doll. Take the doll to the Estate of Unrest and give the doll to Serra, who is next to the gnomish speulunker. Serra will give you a note.  Then take the note from Serra back to Braenar, who will give a new note for Maligar. Take the note from Braenar to Maligar.  Maligar's Enraged Doppleganger will attack at that point (bring a group). Kill the Doppleganger <br>and loot his head. Take the head back to Braenar and recieve "Mahlin's Bongo's". Take the Bongo's to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana to get the bottom of Page 24.<br><br>D) To get page 25 go to South Karana and talk to the woodelf bard Kelkin Mekia.  She wants 3 items:<br><br>1) Guts of a named chromatic drake in skyfire (Edrik the Old)<br><br>2) guts from the new named wrum in Burning Woods (Nezekezena); and<br><br>3) guts form the Huge Oynx Drake in Rathe Mountains (Blackwing)<br><br>Give these to her and you will get page 25<br><br>E) Now you need Forpar's Lute. This is rewarded as head, neck, body and strings.<br><br>First you need to get horn from Ocean of Tears (Alluring horn from Quag Maelstrom).  Take the alluring horn from Ocean of Tears to a half elf bard in Butcherblock, by the docks (Vedico).  Take the note she gives you to Forpar.  He then gives you a note and a list of items he needs:<br><br>1) Backbone of an Ancient Fishman - Phnagel's Spine from Kedge Keep <br><br>2) Amalgym Tentacle - Amygdalan Tendril from Plane of Fear<br><br>3) Petrified skull of a Lycanthrope - petrified werewolf skull from Karnor on Drolvarg Warlord <br><br>Take all 3 items and the note back give to Forpar and he gives you back the Head and Neck of the Lute and the quest for the body, which requires the following items:<br><br>1)Large red Dragon Scale (Talendor of Skyfire's scale works. Nagafen's scale may work but unconfirmed)<br><br>2)Large white Dragon Scale (Gorenaire of Dreadlands works, Vox's scale may work but unconfirmed)<br><br>3)Two Metal bits (smithed metal bits)<br><br>Finally, get the strings for the Lute - Trakanon Gut Strings.  These drop from Trakanon himself.<br><br>Once you have all four, give following to Foplar to recieve the mystical lute:<br><br>1) Head and neck<br>2) Body<br>3) Gut strings<br><br>I) You are almost there.  To get your singing short sword give following items to Baldric Slezaf:<br><br>1) Top of page 24<br>2) Bottom of page 24<br>3) Page 25<br>4) Mystical lute from Foplar<br><br>He will reward you with the singing short sword.</td></tr>
