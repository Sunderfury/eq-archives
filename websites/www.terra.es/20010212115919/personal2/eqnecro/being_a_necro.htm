Being a Necro

Well, well, well. You have chosen to be a necromancer, haven´t you? First of all, you gotta know that you have chosen right! Necromancers are indeed the most powerful class in the game, no doubt about that. But as any type of power, you must know how to use handle it.

A necro´s main way of hunting is soloing. That´s what they do best. Mages and druids are also fairly good soloers, but none like the necro. Our spells combo allows us to take easily down an even or yellow conning preys, even low red ones if we know what we are doing. Besides, we have got an amazingly wide spells range, so we´ve got many ways to use our skills in combat. In the other hand, there´s one big issue against us : we are not meant to hunt in groups. I mean, we can but we won´t be the most desireble class for the other players to group up with us. In stead, they´d rather go for a cleric or a tank-class. Why is that? many reassons : Our DD (direct damage spells) cannot be compared with mages´s or wizzards´s. We are no healer (even though we do can heal a bit). Nor us or our pets can take large amounts of damage as tanks do. And on top of that our DoTs (Damage over Time spells) are not the best weapon in full parties´s hunting because the time that a combat takes to be finished is so short that those DoTs wont do their full damage. So don´t expect loads of parties to come along and cry for you to join them up. But hey!! You are a necro, who the hell needs a group when you can kill them off by yourself !!!

Another drawback is that you´ll ve KOS (kill on sight) in most of the cities of Norrath, so guards and NPCs (non-player characters) will tend to wipe their ass with your robe. Sooner or later you´ll learn how to avoid this nuisances and how to make your way throught such cities and non-amiably NPCs.
