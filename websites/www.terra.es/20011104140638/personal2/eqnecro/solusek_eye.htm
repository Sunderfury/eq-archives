Solusek´s Eye

This zone´s entrance is in the boiling area of the Lavastorm Mountains.

Solusek´s Eye (also known as Sol A, being Sol B Nagafen´s Lair) is a very messy and twisted dungeon. Fire goblins are its inhabitants, along with a bunch of miner gnomes.

This is a tipical group-orientated zone. And not only that, but YOUR-group-orientated zone, meaning that you should bring your own group here (gather up some friends or guild mates). Why? Because this dungeon is not very popular, so there won´t be many groups out there. Second, since you can get lovely items camping some easy mobs, the groups won´t be really willing to take a new member in, cutting down their own chances to win a lotto for any item they are after. So, listen to me, bring here you´r own full group and get ready for this interesting and lucrative zone.

Ok, take a look on the map. You will see a red squared area and a few blue ones. At lvl 25, your group could not be ready yet to go to camp the nice items dropper lvl 30 guys spawning in the blue squared areas. So I´d advice you to hit first lvl 29 at the red squared room, and fast pulling fire goblings from any of your surroundings. This way, if your group does a good job, those three levels will fly away very quickly (few days).

Once you´ve gotten into lvl 29, and before leaving this zone, spend couple of days trying to get any of the items dropped in the blue squared areas (map). The critters that drop them are in their high 20´s or even 30, but with a group of lvl 29 members like yours, that shouldn´t be any problem at all. Most of the spawns take 20 minutes since last death, so you won´t be really looking for exp here but for the items. Once you get any of them, check its selling price at www.maximumeq.com. They update every item´s average price quite oftenly.
