Highpass Hold

Ok, HH (Highpass Hold) is as good and famous zone for players between lvl 16 to 20 as Oasis is for levels 12 to 16. This zone is great not only because of the exp, but also because of the great loot. Here you´ll make in the next levels hundreds of pp and therefore you´ll be able to afford some items that you were only dreaming about before.

There are only two drawbacks : You are highly KoS to the guards (so don´t mess arround the inner part of the zone) and you will have to hunt here in group. Trains of orcs and gnolls are common here, and also the zone´s heavily camped, so forget about hunting solo here. But don´t worry, it´s worthy. I would recommend you to go there with a full group of friends or guildmates, because both the orc area (red squared area on the map) and the gnoll area (blue squared area on the map) are most of the times fully camped and they won´t accept new members. And don´t worry about KS (kills stealing), this place is so messy and wild that you can´t bother on such a nuisances.

Now, have your gruop headed to either the gnolls or orcs spawn, Gather Shadows spell always up if you need to cross thru the city itself. An now... start pulling away : ). There´s no real tip I can give you here since you´ll see by yourself that everything happens very fast in HH. Just try to keep the zonning location sense always up due to escape from those dangerous trains. And that´s it !! During these 4 levels you´ll spend here try to loot as many ringmail armour pieces and bronze weapons in order to sell them for good cash at the vendors (I happen to recall the closest one is in West Commonlands, but I could be mistaken), and levels will fly upwards one after another, and so your bank account will !!

This zone is just amazing. Come with your group here, pull the gnolls/orcs fast and see how you hit lvl 20 within very few days.

Lovely zone indeed : )
