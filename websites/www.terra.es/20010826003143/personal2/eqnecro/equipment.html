Equipment

Ok, I will assume you´ve chosen an erudite as your race and top base intelligence 142. That´s the way your character should be set up.

Since Int and mana bonus items are what you should be seeking for, I´ll describe a list of fairly cheap items you could purchase in order to hit Int 200. Farther Int will hardly increase mana, so no real need of it.

These items that I´m going to list out now are not the best of the game, neither the most wanted. They are just the most economical way to reach Int 200 with a tight budget. Once you become rich, you´ll probably sell away all these items in order to buy new and more spectacular ones, but for now they´ll work just fine. Once you have hit 200 int, you might probably want mana bonus items, in order to have a larger mana pool. Also, AC and HP enhancing gear wont hurt either.

Now, lets say after many levels adventuring you have become a rich and powerful fellow (or a heavily twinked newbie) and you have some cash to burn. Hehe, lets go shopping !!  Some other casters state that once intelligence has been maxed, you should go either for mana or AC bonus items. Well, my own opinion is that at high levels a small extra chunk of mana is not that important since Lich and Demi Lich work great and therefore you won't be OOM often. Besides, you wont add more than 300 mana points to your mana pool, and thats more less what one single spell burns. So not big deal in my opinion. 

Of course, if you are often invited to raids (either with friends or with your own guild) you must have an alternative equipment set based on SV gear. Uber mobs (ie. Trakanon, Nagafen, etc) cast davastating AoE spells which will easily wipe out the whole raid if you guys don't wear the propper gear. I will list below two sets of armour : 

  • Everyday exp group hunting equipment.

  • Gear aimed exclusively to raids.
