Qeynos Sewers

Now, to get to this zone is a bit tricky because you´ve got to discover out a secret entrance in the outer walls of North Qeynos. Ok, imagine you have just step out the city thru the gate. Turn on your right and follow the wall till it turns the corner. Once you have done so, you´ll see a portion of the wall covered up with moss. That´s the fake wall, so just walk thru and dive into the well.

Once you have zoned in Qeynos sewers zone, follow the arrow I have drawn in red on the map of the zone.

Ok, so now you are in the thugs´s room. As you can see, they spawn in couples. That´s the problem, at level 7 you cannot take both of them. In stead of that, you´ll have to ask a high level guy in the zone (hunting smugglers or sharks) to brake the spawn for you, meaning to kill one of them and let the other alive for a couple or minutes more. Doing this you have achieved that they´ll spawn separately, so you can kill one, med mana up, and be ready when the second thug spawns up.

This guys will hit considerably harder than the gnolls or kobolds you were fighting last level. So, how can I take them down? Piece of cake : have your pet posted in the corridor and once the spawn is broken, pull the single thug out of the room. This is just in case the second one spawns up whilst still fighting the first one. If you were fighting inside the room, the just spawned thug would join his mate and tear you apart, but if you are in the corridor, he won´t see you and therefore he will stay still picking his nose and humming silly balads. Got it?

At level 8 go to the necromancers´s guild and purchanse the lvl 8 spells (check spells list section to find out which ones are worthy to buy), but unfortunately there are not many attack spells in this spells circle, so I´m afraid you´ll have to stick to the lifetap-mellee fighting strategy.

The thugs give very good experience, so stick to them till you reach level 9. and then head to the smuggler rooms (check map again). Now, do exactly the same that what you were doing in the thug room : call assistance to brake spawn, pull the smuggler out of the room, lifetap-mellee strategy, etc. 

The smugglers are lvl 9 to 12 so they can hit over 20´s, not to mention their boss, the Ring leader, who is lvl 14 and hits very very hard indeed. She spawns randomly from time to time, but whenever she does, do not mess with her. Just beg any higher level fellow within the zone to kill her for you. Also, Verant has recently inclued in the spawning table a level 40ish female named smugler who hits for 120s. If she spawns, just forget about it. Move to the another smugler room or call for assistance to high level guildmates.

Hunting smugglers will push you up to lvl 12 very quickly. Just buy the spells in the guild and pack up your belongings ´cause we´ll be on the road towards the eastern part of the continent. A long travel´s ahead mate : )

The only withdraw I could find worth to point out in this zone is that there are very few rooms where to hunt in, so they could be camped when you arrive there. In case they are, ask the guy whether he´s going to be arround there for long time. If he is, ask him to group up(better half exp than none at all !! ) and if he doesn´t want to, go to BlackBurrow, which is just two zones away. If you are lucky and when you arrive to the sewers, the room you are interested about is available, do no leave it at any rate. Spawns are precious goods in EverQuest : )

Tip : If for any reasson you see that things are getting too tough and you think that you are gonna kick the backet, run like a mad to the blue cross I´ve marked down on the map. There´s a small cliff there leading to another room on the lower floor. So if the thug or the smuggler is on you kicking your back, just jump off. You´ll get hit for about 5 HP, but you´ll get rid of him since he´s got to make all the way arround down the ramp in order to get to that very room. They do that because of a path finding bug, but I´d rather say they are too wimps to jump !!. Anyway, doing this you´ll have more less 10 seconds to quickly cast Gate spell and pop up safely in your binding spot.
