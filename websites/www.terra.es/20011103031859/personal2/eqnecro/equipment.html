Equipment

 

1. Low and mid levels equipment

Ok, I will assume you´ve chosen an erudite as your race and top base intelligence 142. That´s the way your character should be set up.

Since Int and mana bonus items are what you should be seeking for, I´ll describe a list of fairly cheap items you could purchase in order to hit Int 200. Farther Int will hardly increase mana, so no real need of it.

These items that I´m going to list out now are not the best of the game, neither the most wanted. They are just the most economical way to reach Int 200 with a tight budget. Once you become rich, you´ll probably sell away all these items in order to buy new and more spectacular ones, but for now they´ll work just fine. Once you have hit 200 int, you might probably want mana bonus items, in order to have a larger mana pool. Also, AC and HP enhancing gear wont hurt either. In my opinion, in your low-mid levels your goal should be to have the highest amount of mana you can get, either via INT or MANA items.

2. Post 50 equipment

Now, lets say after many levels adventuring you have become a rich and powerful fellow (or a heavily twinked newbie) and you have some cash to burn. Hehe, lets go shopping !!  Some other casters state that once intelligence has been maxed, you should go either for mana or resist bonus items. Well, my own opinion is that at high levels a small extra chunk of mana is not that important since Lich and Demi Lich work great and therefore you won't be OOM often.  

Of course, if you are often invited to raids (either with friends or with your own guild) you must have an alternative equipment set based on SV gear. Uber mobs (ie. Trakanon, Nagafen, Velious dragons, etc) cast davastating AoE spells which will easily wipe out the whole raid if you guys don't wear the propper gear. I will list below two sets of armour : 

  • Everyday exp group hunting equipment.

  • Gear aimed exclusively to raids.

 

 

2.1. Everyday armour set

As a high level necro, you know that lich is one of our best and most used spells. We hardly ever go OOM since we regen it back very fast. The main problem is that lich will eat up our health quite fast. Therefore, at high levels your number one priority will be +HP gear, being AC and mana a nice adding up too. I honestly believe that extra HP is both a lifesaver and a feasible way to offset the lich s health problems.
