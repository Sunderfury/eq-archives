Gargoyle´s Isle

Gargoyles´s isle is in the OoT (Ocean of Tears) zone. This zone is actually a deepsea zone made out of several tiny islands spread all over the zone.

Grab a friend, a mage or druid prefferable and take him with you, because the gargoyles you are just about to hunt go from lvl 17 to 20, so you won´t be able to solo them. But believe me, if you convince that friend to come along, non of you will regret it. Besides, there´s no need of any larger group. Just 2 of you should be enough.

To get here, get on the boat at the East Freeport docks. Once you have zone into the OoT, jump at the first stop (sister isle´s dock´s been marked out with a red cross on the map) but don´t get off on the dock ´cause you might be KoS to the NPC sister standing on that very Dock. Wait till the boat has departed from that dock and jump off about 100 feet away. Then swim to the beach and stay there. Now have a look to the OoT map and spot out the gargoyles´s isle (purple squared area).

Cast Gather Shadows on you (there are loads of sharks in the water) and swim to any of the two blue crosses on the map. That will be your binding point. Don´t adventure any farther inside that isle because there are some very aggro cyclops who would tear your ass apart without a second thought.

Now, with Gather Shadows again on you swim up to the gargoyles´s isle (purple squared area on the map) and look for kinda cape sticking out into the sea. I think it should be where the yellow cross lies on the map, but I´m not sure. Just circle arround the island by the seaside and you´ll see it. There´s a single tree on the cape. That would be your pulling spot since it´s the isle´s most far away point from its center, where 4 bored spectre stand still composed : ) But don´t worry, as soon as you don´t get anycloser than about 50 feet from there, they´ll let you in peace.

Ok now, there´s a place holder: the greater skelletons, easy lvl 12 skelletons. A place holder means that if you don´t kill them, the gargoyles won´t spawn ´cause there is a certain number of spawns allowed at the same time, so the more skellies, the less gargs. So from time to time you´ll have to do the skellies cleaning up.

As soon as you see a garg, pull it to the cape with darkness. You and your mate should be strong enough to kill it, but just in case cast Fear so the pet won´t die. Of course, before casting it away, check arround that no other garg is anyclose. Don´t cast all your DoTs in a quick row or the garg will aggro on you, and they hit very hard, so count few seconds between DoTs. As I said, they hit up to 42, so try to share the damage between you, your pet, your mate, and your mate´s pet if he´s got any.

The gargs drop A gargoyle´s eye which can be sold for 8 pp each at Oasis gypsy camp. On top of that, they mostly drop them by couples among some research words !! Of course, the eyes are not dropped every single kill, but maybe once every five kills. If you and your friend stick to this place for few levels, I promisse you that you´ll leave OoT with hundreds of pp in eyes, ready to be sold in Oasis, and that is good money, ain´t it?

Another good point of this isle is that you´ll NEVER find it camped up. I don´t know why, but it looks like this spot has remained kinda secret for most of the players. So take your chance !!

Before I told you about your binding point at the isle next by gargoyles´s isle. Why to have it there? Because this is a very tiny isle, and if you are in problems Feign Death (check spell list for more info) is not the solution. As soon as you stand up, the garg will fly back towards you. Alternatively, when the garg is hitting you ass hard, you´ll cast Shadow Step (almost instant spell casting) and right afterwards cast Gate. Then, once you get to the other isle, you may cast Feign Death in order to wipe you from the gargoyle´s aggro list. And now, med up, cast gather shadow, swim back safely to the cape, cast a new pet and let´s start again. Easy, isn´t it?

Gargoyles´s isle is a good hunting place from lvl 16 to 20. The only problem is that you cannot solo here (not at least untill lvl 18) without taking a big risk because gargs have got a high MR (magic resistance). So you´ll have to find someone who is up to spend few days in a deserted volocanic island with no other company but yours : ) But if you get that person, this will be a fine hunting place.

Tip 1: There´s no vendor at all in OoT, so before coming here, store loads of bone chips, food and water supplies in your inventory, or you´ll have to wait ages for the boat to come over.

Tip 2: Whenever you want to come back to Freeport, you would have to get back to sister´s isle, wait for the boat, and then to sail to Faydwer first and then all the way arround towards Freeport. This whole trip could easily take more than half an hour !! But hey, I´m here to assist you : Pinch a small boat ( there´s one at sister´s isle on the right hand of the docks) and go to this very location : neg 4637, neg 6510 and wait there for the boat to come towards you. Then, you just have to release the boat´s captain helm and walk forth to the boat. You will pop up inside the boat and few seconds later you´ll zone into Freeport. Do not leave the captain´s before the boat comes to you, because the small boat takes about 5 mins to respawn at sister´s isle and you along with it !!. So, whenever you see the boat aproaching, right click once (this way you won´t be in charge of the boat anylonger and will be free of movement) and when the boat swallows you, walk straight towards it.

Back to level 16 to 20 zones
