Where to Hunt

Ok then, this part of the web is the main reason I wanted to make this site. And of course, the most difficult one too.

Where should I hunt at my level? Ahh mate, that´s the ever-question. Every single EverQuest player has at some point of his adventure´s life asked himself where the hell should he has his ass headed?, cause he can´t really find any place where level up. Well, I have made a list of several zones explaining which one is good considering your level, what should you hunt in such zones and how to do it in order to fulfil you experience and loot needs.

I have splat the interesting zones depending on the level you´d be required to have. Those zones I personally believe are the best, I´ll remark them out as ´my personal choice would be...´. But since not everybody preffer the same way of hunting, or the same kind of zones, I have also included and described some other zones where you could also do fairly well. Take a look to all of them and then you decide which is more suitable for you.

Happy hunting : )
