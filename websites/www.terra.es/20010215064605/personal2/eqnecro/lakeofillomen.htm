Lake of Ill Omen

Welcome to the new discovered land of Kunark !! where the wind swings the branches of the trees, where the high detailed monsters will take your breath away, and where a most horrible and desperating lag will drive you crazy!!

To get here you must take the boat from the docks at Oasis, then take a new boat (a weird one I´d add) on Timorus Deep, then cross the Overthere zone, and then so do you in Frontier Mountains till eventually you get your tired ass to the wonderful area of Lake of ill Omen.

First thing about Lake of Ill Omen : This is the place to boost up your levels from 20 to 25 in no time. Second thing to say : Since mobs here will con white or higher, you better know by heart how to use the Darkness-Fear-DoT combo or you´ll die pretty quick. Third thing to say : Enjoy this zone, I love it.

Ok now, check the map of Lake of Ill Omen and go to the red cross. Bind yourself there and that very same point will be your pulling spot. The lake will be on your back and you´ll be facing the hills, got it? Ok, all along the hill slope will most likely be roaming some mobs : goblings, tigers, iskar, surnak and skellies. They go from lvl 20 to 27. You have just arrived to this zone, so keep away from the red conning ones. But the rest are your potential preys. Have your pet to guard that inicial spot and you go off searching out for a monster. When you spot it, pull it with darkness towards your pet by the lakeside, where no other mob will disturb you. And know start teaching that impertinent creature what you´r made of !! At this level they hit very hard, so don´t let them get into mellee distance at any rate, but neither is a good idea to have your pet to deal with large amounts of damage because he won´t last long. Remember, Darkness and Fear are your best life insurance.

If at some point you don´t see any monster by the slope, adverture forth up to the top of the hill. There you´ll see the haunted house (full up with nasty lvl 24 to 27 skellies) on the other part of the hill. It´s marked on the map with a black cross. But I´m sure there won´t be any need to get down there, since plenty of monsters are always at the top having a sun bathe. Always have your pet to guard the binding point by the Lakeside and pull the critters there, and you´ll do just find.

Loot in this zone is no as good as it used to be in Highpass Hold : ( but the exp really makes it worthy.

Many players group up at the Surnak fortress gates exactly where the blue cross is placed on the map. As a necro, you should need no group to hunt and level up (actually you should level up much quicker on your own), but if still you decide to hunt among some other players, get headed towards there and ask for a group. Still it can be good exp if the group is good and specially if they do non stop pulling. Otherwise, you better leave it.

Myself, I stuck to the red cross pulling spot (see map) and I leveled up 5 levels in one week of average playing hours. Which is not bad if you consider that at theese levels, the leveling speed rate starts to seriously slow down comparing to what you have been used to for the last 8 levels.
