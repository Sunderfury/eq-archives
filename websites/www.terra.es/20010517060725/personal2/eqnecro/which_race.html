Which Race

If you visit any which-race-should-I-go-for? forum around, you´ll notice that there´s no general agreement about which race is the best over the rest. Some people say erudites are the best due to their high intelligence. Some would say dark elves are the top of the rank because of their high agility and their natural ultravision and hiding skills. Some others would go for the gnome, since they´ve got a good overall starting points and they look so cute  : ) . Even humans´ve got their own supporters even though they are crap necros hehe. As I said before, no race stands on top of the rest (well, apart from humans).

But since this is my page, I´ll feel free to give you my choice: go for the erudite!! Why? Well, because they start with a maxed intelligence of 142 (if you distribute as many points as you can to raise up intelligence) and that´s exactly what you want, believe me. If you do so, you will have 5 remaining points left. You could use them to raise either agility (to avoid getting hit) or strength (in order to be able to carry more weight on you). I´d put them to agility, because at low levels you have to melee, and some dodging wont hurt.

But now, why is so important to have a high base intelligence? Well, more intelligence means more mana (till you reach 200, when any farther intelligence will hardly increase mana a bit), and more mana means that you can cast more spells in a row. Intelligence is crucial for casters since our power´s based on our spells. Now, you can increase intelligence with magical int-bonus items, which some of them are not cheap or easy to get at all. Your aim is to max intelligence up to 200, and therefore you´ll have to shop around trying to acquire plenty of these items. Some people say its easy to get the money to buy all those items, so you can have an impressive equipment fairly quickly. Lets say you choose to be a dark elf, or even a human. Even if you spend most of your starting points to intelligence, still it´ll stand bellow 120, so you´ve got to buy a bunch of these magical artefacts. In order to get an over-80-intelligence-bonus-items you may easily be looking for over 20000 platinum coins, and that is no easy task to achieve. Its true that when you are in your forties (lvl 40+), then you will be earning money much much faster, but before that your intelligence´ll be too low and won´t be able to cast many spells without having to keep medding up often. I´m telling you, get yourself an erudite, max up your intelligence, and don´t worry about having to save up all your earning cause of the damn expensive intelligence bonus items. And whenever the hell you become rich, then you´ll be able to spend your money in whatever items you fancy, not only intelligence bonus items because you will have already reached 200 !!

I have adviced you to chose and erudite because being my own race, is the one I know the best. But it is also fair to say that there is another race which is as powerful as the erudite when it comes to follow the dark path of the necromantic arts : the iksar. The iksar has got its own characteristics that makes them worlds apart from the 'old' races (erudite, human, gnome and dark elf). First of all they got natural faster health regeneration which, of course, increses level by level. The regeneration is specially important to necro in order to offset the Lich series (HP to mana transfer, check spell list if needed). This means that, while liching up, you wont lose as many HP as you would with any other race, hence having to lifetap less often, hence saving up mana, hence cutting down medding downtimes. The result is quite outstanding, I tested it myself with a high lvl necro iksar friend of mine: he would lose less than half the HP I would while having lich up !! That is a lot.

In the other hand, Iksars have got very awful starting base stats (max base int is 110 whereas erudites is 142 !!). The problem having such a low starting int is that either you waste lots of inventory slots towards int bonus items (in order to reach the 200 int cap) or else you will definitely have a shorter mana pool. This is not, in my opinion, something so horrible : we necros once we get at lvl 34 Call of Bones, and specially at 49 with Lich, we regen mana fast as hell, so probably you shouldnt be OOM oftenly. That being said, never forget that to have an extra mana bubble is always a lifesaver indeed.

Also, iksars have got high agility, so their AC is better than erudites.That might help a lot when a mob is taking you for a beat and you are trying to cast Gate spell off.

Top that off with the fact that Iksar race has got a 20% exp penalty. Yes that's right, it's like the exp bar had 6 bubbles instead of 5. Some guys say that since they regen HP faster, and since they don't burn so much mana via lifetaps, their shorter downtimes make it up for the exp penalty. My opinion is that pre 50 that might be possible, but it won't post 50, where every single God damned level is hell level (you nice twice exp to level up). Adding that up, remember that post 50 you don't solo anymore and downtimes will deppend on the whole party's need and not just your own. And, as I said in the where to hunt section -lvl 50 to 60- , your way thru the 50s will take most likely longer that from lvl 1 to 50. Believe me, you don't want an exp penalty post 50.

Ok, lets sum it up :

  • Iksars have shorter mana pool, which balances with their faster mana regen (they don't burn so much mana in lifetaps as erudtes).
  • Iksars won't lose as many HP while liching up as erudites.
  • Erudites will have more HPs and better saves (magic, fire, cold, etc) because thanks to their high base int, they can spare some inventory slots towards non-int bonus items.
  • Iksars have got less downtimes, which is balanced with their exp penalty (pre 50 only. Post 50 the exp penalty weights way heavier).
  • Iksars will probably have higher AC than erudites.
  • Erudites have got better racial gear : Flyed skin armour, mantle of souls, etc.

In my opinion, and since EQ is a long term game (read long way from 1 to 60), I would still chose an erudite on top of the iksar. Why? Mainly because, as I said, the exp penalty will indeed be a pain post 50 and the HP regen wont make it up I'm afraid. If EQ was only 1 to 50, I would go lizzard, but since post 50 levels are so tough... Also, to equip an erudite is much cheaper, more rewarding and wider in possibilities. At the end of the day, Iksars have always to keep a rather high amount of int bonus items, and that leaves them with less real chances to wear weird and exotic items with weird and exotic stats/effects.

If the necro is your second char, and you have a rich and high level main character who will generously twink the new iksar necro, then you might decide to go for the iksar. But, overall, I still believe that the erudite makes the best necromancer out there.
