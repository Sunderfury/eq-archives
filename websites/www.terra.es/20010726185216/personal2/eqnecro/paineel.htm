Paineel

I have assumed that you have taken my advice as good and have made a necro erudite. That means you will start is paineel, in the western continent of Odus. Paineel is not a very exciting area, but it´s got, in my opinion, the best newbie area all arround : It´s small, not crowded at all (Freeport´s or Qeynos´s are much busier), guards are always close in case you need to run up to them for help and it´s plenty of low level monsters like decaying skelletons, bats, rats, snakes and the toughest of the loot, fire beattles.

The first step will buy your level 1 spells (check the spells list section). Since you start with no money at all (stingy Verant´s staff), I´d feel no shame to beg any higher level guy to spare some golds. Level 1 spells cost 1 gold each, so just ask for couple of golds to each player. Don´t be greedy, since high level players get several incoming tells from newbies begging for money. Just ask few golds, and you´ll probably get them.

Once you´ve gotten the money, its time to go to the necromancers´s guild inside the fortress. Have a look to the Paineel map to find your way to the necromancers´s guild. But before that, you´ve got to tell the guard in the newbie hunting ground ´need keyz´. He´ll give you the key that is needed to operate the elevator (see map). To do so, just stand by a big rock in front of the lift, and with the key as a pointer, point the rock and press ´U´ button in your keyboard. I know its kinda weird, but that´s the way it is. Same system when you have bought your spells and want to get back upstairs.

Ok, time´s up !! Time to prove yourself and teh whole world why necromancers rock !! Get to the newbie area and again beg anybody to either give you a couple of bones chips or to kill a decaying skelleton to loot them off the corpse. Now cast your first skellie pet ever and med up mana till full. And that´s it, begin the slaughter. The solo hunting startegy you´ll use is the lifetap mellee fighting (check strategies section for more info).

Do no walk far away from the guards, because if you see that the critter is winning the battle, you always can run up to the guard and he´ll kill it for you. At level 1 kill bats (they are the easiet ones) and keep all the loot you get bacuse it can be sold for few coppers or silvers to the vendors inside the fortress. At lvl 2 and 3 kill decaying skelletons, especialy those ones carrying weapons. Give the first weapon you get to you pet (it will increase his attacking power) and sell the rest to vendors (you´ll get golds for each weapon !!, and that at this level is big money man). Also, try to store in your inventory as many bone chips stacks as you can because you need them to cast your pet up. If you see any high level necromancer offer them to him, being the average price per stack 2 platinium coins !!! At level 4 hunt fire beattles because its risky for you to try to take them down before. Also sell all the fire beattle´s eyes you get.

If you haven´t had any major problem, which you shouldn´t have had, you´ll reach level 4 within couple of hours maybe, and then you are ready to buy the next circle spells (check the spells list section) and move one to the next area !!!

The only problem you may find in this otherwise very safe newbie area is Neclo´s pet, a level 5 skelleton who wanders arround the newbie area. He spawns every 20 mins or so. If you can, when you get lvl 4 and once you have bought your brand new spells, try to kill him. The loot is a bone you are due to give to the NPC shadow knight called Neclo, who also stands in that very same newbie area. He´ll give you back a very nice bone staff which I think is quite an item for your level. Congratulations, you´ve completed your first quest !!
