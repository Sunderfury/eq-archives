Toxxulia

Just outside Paineel lies the dark, wet and spooky Toxxulia Forest, one of the most boring and atractionless areas in Norrath. But hey, you are only lvl 4, what did you expect, Disneyland? : )

Ok, here you´ll be headed to the closest knobold camp (see Toxxulia Forest map). This camp counts with 3 lvl 3 to 5 kobolds. You will have to group up with few other players (2 to 3 of you will do it) because you´ve got to break the spawn in other to brake apart the spawn. This will be your first group experience most likely. Hunting in a group is not as stressful as when soloing, but still you´ve got to do your job : send pet in, cast poison and few lifetaps, that should be enough.

Also, if you preffer to stick to yourself, there are several kobolds wandering arround pineel entrance. Pull them to the gate, dont attempt to kill them in the middle of the forest or you´ll get jumped on by another kobolds. Skelletons will make a nice prey too at theese levels, but beware, those fellows hit hard indeed.

Again, the hunting strategy will be lifetap-mellee fighting, but since you have just gotten Poison, Darkness and Fear, you could also try the Darkness-Fear combo (check out the strategies section), but always pulling the critters to the wall or the gate, never deep inside the forest.

You´ll also notice that leveling up now is much more slower that it was from lvl 1 to lvl 4. Don´t worry, it´s they way is supossed to be. Every higher level takes a bit more to be completed than the previous one. Besides, in this zone you´ll be fighting mostly white and blue conning creatures, not yellowish, so exp will take its tame to raise up.
