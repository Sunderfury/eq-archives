The Overthere

The overthere zone is very, very similar to Lake of Ill Omen. It´s an flat, large outdoors zone with plenty of creatures around. That is, a soloer´s heaven. The monsters level range goes from level 30 to 38, so at lvl 34 thisis definitily the place to be.

Have a look to the map. Bind yourself at the blue cross. See the red squared area by Frontier mountains entrance? That´ll be where you should hunt. Pulling your preys with darkness to the top of the hill so no other walking-by-mob will put you or your pet in problems.

For some reason, the surnak knights and the Stonegazer cockatrice, only 35 to 38 level monster here (apart from the very mighty Sabretooth tiger) have a high Magic Resistance, so I wouldn´t try to take any of those down. That means you´ll have to forget about juicy yellowish conning fellows and stick to the easier blue ones. That´s still not bad because with call of bones spells (check spells list) you´ll be a mana battery and medding up times will drastically get cut down, and therefore you´ll hunt more preys than you used to in Lake of Ill Omen at your low 20´s.

Unfortunatelly, loot does suck. It is unbelieveable how at your high 30´s still we gotta restrain ourselves with few crappy golds : ( Anyway, exp is good, and I´d personaly choose exp over loot anyday.

Use the Darkness-Fear combo and everything will work out just fine.

Did you like Lake of ill Omen at your early 20´s? Then you´ll love this one now !!
