Ak´Anon

Ak´Anon is the gnomes´s underground home town. It´s a place full up with plenty of mechanical and steamy devices all over.

There´s a spawn here that will be most suitable for your level. I did it myself and leveled up reasonably safe from lvl 25 to 29 in few days. Besides, the loot is excellent (up to 3 pp in cash plus ocasionally some few plats worth pieces of armour and fine steel weapons. And, rarely, some expensive jewels and even black pearls !!).

Now, your goal is the mechanic´s spawn. He is a lvl 26 to 28 gnome and takes exactly 6 minutes to spawn up. As you have noticed, this place is a dungeaon-alike zone, so not very good idea to Fear the creatures arround. Therefore, you´ll have to use the Lifetap-Mellee-combo to solo this mech. Yes, I know, don´t faint down to floor yet. I know you´r are not supossed to use this combo because the mobs at theese levels hit just too hard, but trust uncle Necro (that's me) : this mech is weaker than a pneumatic gay !! Max up his blows at 32 !! That´s nothing, critters 8 levels ago used to hit harder, so don´t tell me you can´t kick this butt. But, of course, have your shielding spells always up ( just in case, you know).

Bind yourself at the blue cross (check map). That will be also your pulling spot. The mech spawns at the red cross (map), just next by a vendor, whom you can sell your stuff to. Don´t missclick and attack the vendor, because it wouldn´t be healthy : ). So, remember? : Darkness, send pet, few secs, Heat Blood, few secs, Shock of Poison, get hit, lifetap, get hit, lifetap, you gain experience. Piece of cake, ain´t it?

The only problem is that when the mech is about to die, he jumps into the water diving towards a clockwork (spidery guards) posted 300 feet away. Don´t let the mech go!! As soon as he jumps into the water, jump too after him and kill him with Shock of Poison as quickly as you can, otherwise the guard will kill you. In orden to cast underwater, you must stand still, not moving at all or the casting will be interrupted.

Once you have killed him, you´ve got 6 minutes before it respawns. which is time enough to recover mana and HP back.

There´re few more mech spawns throughtout the zone, but the only one suitable for tall erudites like you (you cannot cast away while ducking in any of the low silling Ak´Anon passages) is inside the castle (marked on the map with the number 13), where King Ak´Anon holds his crown. Is the one in a closed tiny just-one-entrance room. But I think the one we where talking about before is better.

Eventually, and because you´ve been a naughty fellow, the guards and any other Ak´Anon citizen will glare at you threateningly, what will make Gather Shadows become your best friend. But still the vendor would buy stuff from you for a longer period.

This zone is not heavily camped at all. Actually, the average number of players is always bellow 10. Still, you may be unlucky and find the mech camped. Then you´ll have to risk it and look for the other mech spawn inside the castle (sorry it´s not detailed on the map the inner rooms of the castle). But I believe you´ll be able to camp it peacefully.
