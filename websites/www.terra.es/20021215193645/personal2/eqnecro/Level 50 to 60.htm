Well, well, well. So you finally made it, didnt you? Now you are one of the big fellas  : )  

First of all, or at least this has been my own experience, the walk throught the 50s is a pain !! And it might indeed take longer to hit 60 from 50 than it took 50 from 1. The exp bar seems   h  e  a  v  y  and rather than move, Id say it crawls. But anyway, lets not get frustrated and try to take it as easy as we can.

Now, there are several differences between a pre-50 necro and a post-50 necro. Most of you have probably soloed your way thru in order to reach level  50. I did that myself. Unfortunately, this is feasible no longer. Dont get me wrong, you do can still solo post-50, but you might find grouping far more proffitable in exp terms (and loot, of course). Actually, once you have hit 54, the very only zone in game where you can perform the Darkness-Fear combo is in SkyFire, a huge and volcanic outdoor zone with plenty of high level mobs with loads of HPs (expect long mana recovering downtimes). But eventually you will get bored, or at least I did, of being able to hunt only in this zone, and you will most likely find yourself willing to do what you have always hated : gang up in dungeons !!

That, along with the fact that you might want to update your gear, will undoubtly lead you to the Kunark high level dungeons. And once that happens, a new big problem will come up : how the hell do I play my ever-soloer necro in a dungeon group ??  Unless you had spent your 40s in Lower Guk or Lord Nagafens Lair (aka Sol B), you probably know little about teaming up in high level dungeons, do you? Well, I remember myself when I finally decided that the lone wolf times had finished, and I started to go on raids with my guildmates. Hehe, it was so funny man, I used to drive them nut !! : "No, man, dont Fear the mob, no !!!" (/shout TRAIN TO ZONE !! ). It took me few levels to fully adjust my playing style into the groupy mood. That is why I have recently prepared a brief  "How to play a necromancer in a post 50 dungeon group" guide. It is not aimed to fully described every group strategies and situations, but rather some tips and roles you might find useful while grouping. Please, mind that due to the necro s versatility, you should adjust your doing according to the group s needs. Anyway, read the above mentioned guide to have a clue what is all this about  : )

Also, I have found four zones where you still can solo, which are Burning Woods, EverFrost peaks and Timorus Deep (until level 53/54) and SkyFire (up to 60 is you feel like). I will include these four zones in case you miss some of the old boogey-boogey solo action. Anyway, you better start putting some group rudiments in that head of yours : )
