Queynos Hills

In this zone you will certanly level up from level 4 to 7 very quickly, but first you´ve got to get there.

First take the boat at Erudin´s docks. This, after crossing Erud´s crossing, will eventually take you to South Qeynos. When you get off the boat, don´t move from the docks because you are KoS in Qeynos, and either a guard or a NPC will definatelly kill you. What you´ve got to do is to shout at docks to any kind caster to bind you there (so if you die, you won´t have to make the trip all way back again) and then to cast invisibility spell on you. Once, done this, go to North Qeynos (see South Qeynos and North Qeynos map) and the head for the northern gate. Once there, walk away the main path and again ask for a bind, otherwise if you die you´ll must shout arround for invisivility again.

Now just follow the path (but from the distance, don´t walk it along due to the patrolling guards) and there you are, welcome to Qeynos Hills.

Once in Qeynos Hills, you´ll face again the same problem than in Qeynos town, the hostile patrolling guards. In the Qeynos Hills map, you´ll see a squared area by Black Burrow entrance. Well, that´s your destination. It´s a long walk thru the hills, so try to avoid any guard (use a wide angle view for better guard-spotting) or any other creatures like wolves or bears. Once you get to BB (black burrow) entrance, walk arround and you´ll see some gnolls. Those gnolls go from lvl 5 to 7, so leveling up here will be a piece of cake. Besides, some of them carry rusty weapons and a fairly good loot. Ashame that you can´t sell the weapons to any vendor since you are very KoS indeed in the whereabouts : ( . Well, keep few of them in order to have a well armed pet.

Another extra value for this area is that killing gnolls will increse you faction standing with Qeynos citizens, and eventually you might not being KoS anylonger, but don´t get to exited yet since the number of gnolls you are to kills in huge !!  Most likely you won´t kill so many during theese levels, but step by step one day you may even be warmly welcome in the Goddamn Qeynos ( I hate that city man!! )
