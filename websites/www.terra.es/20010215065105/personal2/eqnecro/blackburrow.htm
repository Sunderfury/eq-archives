Blackburrow

BB (BlackBurrow) is in the estern wall of Qeynos hills (check Qeynos Hills map to get there).

BB is a wild zone, with plenty of deadly trains of furious gnolls and it´s almost impossible to solo here. That´s why you must shop arround for a group if you´r planning to hunt in BB. Believe me, if you try to solo here, you´ll die oftenly and will have to walk all your way back from your binding point in North Qeynos. I had tried myself to solo here at level 8 for 1 whole week and died and walked that trip so many times that finally I had to quit and shout out for a group.

At level 7, and once you have gotten into a full group, hunt in the red squared area in the upper level of the Blackburrow map. This is a good idea ´cause the zone to Qeynos hills is just few steps away in case you get jumped on by a train and your group can´t keep it under control.

When you make your way to lvl 8, and after buying you new lvl 8 spells (check spells list) in the necromancers´s guild in Qeynos sewers (check map of the sewers), you can take you group, or look for a new one, to the lower level. I have drawn 2 blue squared areas in the map where you should be fine hunting gnolls, burly gnolls and patrolling gnolls. The main problem here is that if a huge gnoll-orient-express-alike-train comes up, you´ll have to run the ramp all the way up towards the upper level and then zone to Qeynos Hills while those ugly gnolls kick your butt.

At lvl 9 there is another posibility, and hey, here you´ll hunt by yourself !! It´s the snakes´pit (greenish squared area in the mid level. Check the map out). This snakes are lvl 8 to 10 and hit very hard, but they are great exp indeed and on top of that they drop snake´s fang, which can be used as piercing weapon (DMG 5, DLY 24). Theese are great pet weapons, and you can sell them to any vendor for over 1 pp !! If you decide to solo this snakes, I swear on God you´ll love the exp and the loot.

Now you´r level 10, aren´t you? Ok, get yourself back in a group and lets kick some big gnolls´s asses : Gnoll shamman, gnoll guardsman and gnoll commander (this fellow is lvl 13 to 15 and is very strong, watch out). Again, in the map you´ll see some yellow squared areas, and there´s exactly where you should go at lvl 10. Same tactics as couple of levels ago : Full party, fast pulling, send pet in and lifetap the gnoll off. Easy job.

If you have been a smart apprentice, you´ll hit level 12 in few days since you arrived to BB. Go again to the Qeynos sewers (map of the sewers)  to purchase you proudly earned new spells (spells list) and let´s move on mate !!
