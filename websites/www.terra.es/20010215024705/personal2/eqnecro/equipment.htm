Equipment

Ok, I will assume you´ve chosen an erudite as your race and top base intelligence 142. That´s the way your character should be set up.

Since Int and mana bonus items are what you should be seeking for, I´ll describe a list of fairly cheap items you could purchase in order to hit Int 200. Farther Int will hardly increase mana, so no real need of it.

These items that I´m going to list out now are not the best of the game, neither the most wanted. They are just the most economical way to reach Int 200 with a tight budget. Once you become rich, you´ll probably sell away all theese items in order to buy new and more spectacular ones, but for now they´ll work just fine. Once you have hit 200 int, you might probably want magic, fire, cold, poison and disease resistance bonus items, in order to resist caster´s spells ( either in your preys hunting as when dueling). A high magic resistance is as important as a high int !!

Now, lets say after many levels adventuring you have become a rich and powerful fellow (or a heavily twinked newbie) and you have some cash to burn. Hehe, lets go shopping !!  Some other casters state that once intelligence has been maxed, you should go either for mana or AC bonus items. Well, my own opinion is that at high levels a small extra chunk of mana is not that important since Lich and Demi Lich work great and therefore you wont be OOM oftenly. Besides, you wont add more than 300 mana points to your mana pool, and thats more less what one single spell burns. So not big deal in my opinion. What about some extra AC? well, thats nice. It helps to avoid the mob s blows and hence having your spell cast away while getting hit. But, since this equipment is supossedly only for high level characters, and since you should not be within melee reach, lets go for something else.

Of course, my opinion is to go for Magic Resistance gear. Fire and Cold wont hurt either. To have a high MR will save your butt many times while fighting a high level caster (both mob or another player). You will resist most of their stuns, roots, snares, DDs, Fears, DoTs, etc. and THAT is a lifesaver. My necro has his MR above 100 and he is a duel devil  : )
