South Karanas (lvl 12 to 16)

South Karanas zone is just next to North Karanas, crossing thru the stone bridge guarded by guards. Watch out because I´ve heard that they can see thru Gather Shadows spell, I´m not sure. Just in case they do, ask for SoW and run the hell thru the guards´s post and don´t stop till you hit the zone which is in the middle of the bridge. Once you have zone into SK (south karanas) there won´t be any patrolling guards anymore.

In SK there are two places you could go hunting, depending if you preffer to solo or to group up with some other players. Of course, my advice is to be on your own.

If you decide to solo, then your next preys would be the elephant calves (check map to find them on the red squared areas). They go from lvl 10 to 14, so you´ll get good exp till lvl 14-15. Only thing here to worry about is that the walk in crowds so you´ve got to pull one without having their much larger mum along too. Once you spot a lonely calf, pull it towards a clear area an kill it pleassently there. They drop tusks, which are useless apart from few golds at the vendor placed at the very top of the aviak village (check map). One tip here : do not fall down from the top floor of the village or you´ll become a shapeless-smashed-potatoes-alike mass on the floor out of the 500 HP damage hit !! Once you reach level 15, try to go for the calves´s parents : the elephants. Theese ones are much tougher and some of them will con red to you. Avoid those ones because they could resist your Darkness and Fear spells. Stick to yellows or lower and you´ll do just fine. Again, same problem than before : you must pull them away to a non-crowded area.

Also, keep in mind that while fighting with either any calf or elephant, a roaming lion may walk by and jump on you too. Lions are lvl 10ish, so they are not too tough. Just Fear it away and continue your dance with the elephant. By the time the Fear effects wear off, you should be already cutting the tusks off the elephant´s corpse!!

If you happen to pull two elephants, don´t try to take them both down. Just let the pet deal desperately with them while you gain some valuable seconds to run the hell away towards the zone (Lake Rathe zone should be the closest one).

But if you preffer to group up with some mates, then your place is at the base of the main ramp of the Aviak village (blue squared area on the map). Here you´ll face may kind of the commonly called ´birds´ (Aviaks) along with a wide level range groups. They go from the skinny Aviak Egret (lvl 13 to 15) to the very powerful Aviak Avocet (lvl 28 to 30). Of course, at this level you can only aim to the Egrets. Be very carefull not to pull by mistake any bigger fellow ´cause they hit very hard. Loot on the Aviaks are slightly better than on the elephants, but still nothing really speciall.

At level 15, and considering you are in a group, you should move on and hunt the Aviak Darter (lvl 15 to 18). Same stuff as the Egrets, just a bit more bulky bird : )

I don´t particulary like this Aviak village as my hunting place. Why? Because there are many kinds of Aviaks, and some of them are very very nasty indeed. And since they all spawn toghether within a 100 feet wide area, there is a real chance of you pulling the wrong class of bird along with the one you initially meant to pull, leading a supossely easy battle into a Quentin Tarantino´s savage slaughtering scene. Being frank, rather stick to the elephants or go to the far more adviceable zone of Oasis of Marr.
