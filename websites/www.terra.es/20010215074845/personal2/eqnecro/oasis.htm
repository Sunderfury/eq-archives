Oasis of Marr

Now, I can´t really think of any other zone more adviceable than Oasis of Marr for lvl 12 to 16. I´d even say that no adventurer on Norrath has made his way thru from lvl 12 up to 18 without having hunted ever in Oasis. This zone is so good that you will love it and you´ll hit lvl 16 within very few days.

Last level you were in Qeynos whereabouts, weren´t you?. Ok then, to get here to Oasis will be kinda hard, because you´ve got to cross Antonica. Of course, the best way to do so is to ask any guild mate for a TP (teleport spell) to West Commonland or to North or South dessert of Ro. Also, you can shout out for one and pay an average fee of 10 pp for it. But if you´r broke or no one in Qeynos is able to satisfy your demands, you will have to do it by foot. This is the way : Qeynos-Qeynos Hills-West Karanas-North Karanas-East Karanas-High Pass Hold-Kithicor Forrest-West Commonlands-East Commonlands-North Ro-Oasis. As you can see is a looong walk, so try to get SoW or it´ll take you ages. Also, be aware that you´ll be KoS in many of those zones, so have Gather Shadows spell (check spells list for more info) always on. In Kithicor forrest (specially at night) there are plenty of undead criatures, and therefore Gather Shadows won´t be the solution but Invisibility vs Undead spell (check spells list for more info).

Ok, so eventually you have arrived to the lovely land of Oasis. Bind yourself at docks (Check the map of Oasis, I´ve marked the docks down with a yellow cross) and get ready to rumble. Since you´ve just gotten the powerful lvl 12 spells (check spells list), you are now up to use the Darkness-Fear-DoT combo when soloing.

The place to solo in Oasis is the Crocs highway (see map) since plenty or crocs roam up and down the seaside. At lvl 12 and 13 go for caimans and crocodiles. At lvl 14 and 15 go for crocodiles and Deepwater caimans. At lvl 16 go for Deepwater caimans and the maighty Deepwater crocodiles. This DW crocodiles max their blows up to 42, so don´t even try to mellee them or you´ll be on the floor pretty quickly. The mayor bonus the Crocs highway´s got is that crocs are not aggro on you, even while fighting another croc. This means you can confidently pull one croc and be possitively sure that non of its colleagues will join up against you. So you can take them 1 by 1.

Whenever you need to med up, climb any of the 2 pillars on the beach ( marked on the map with green crosses) because from time to time some moron pull a sand giant or a spectre to the seaside, slaughtering off any absent-minded newbie in their way ( they hit for almost 100 !! ). Believe me, I have seen spectres gotten loose along the beach and slicing off several newbies in less than 1 minute !! Now, that was fun to watch.

The other mayor hunting place in Oasis is the Orc highway. This place is meant to be camped by full groups ´cause everytime you pull an orc, another one or two might easily come along too. Anyway, if you want to try to solo at the Orc highway, place your pulling spot at the blue cross I´ve marked on the map. Pull your preys up there at the top of the zone limit hill and deal with the orc safely there. Go for the An orc warriors, but keep away from the Orc Priests (due to their damn self-healing up skills) and the A orc warrior (please note that theese ones are ´A´ orc warriors and not ´An´ orc warriors. The A orc warriors are lvl 20 or higher and hit far too hard for you at this level).

If in stead of soloing orcs, you´d rather preffer to hunt them in a group, go to the spot with a purple cross on the map. There is where most of groups pull their preys. Ask them to let you in or just shout out to start a new orc group. In Oasis there are always loads of candidates, you´ll see, and it´s very easy to create new groups in few minutes.

In spite of the fact that hunting orcs in groups is much safer and you will not die half as often as you´d do soloing crocs, I would advice you to choose the crocs. Why? well, two reassons : First, if you do your job propperly and don´t die every second combat, exp will fly upwards. That I can promise you. Second, this is your first chance to practise the Darkness-Fear-DoT combo (check hunting strategies) and that´s very important since from now on that strategy will be your main fighting weapon, and it´s very important you learn it quickly. I know that crocs´s loot sucks, but still I think that they are what you should be hunting down.

At the gypsy camp you can always sell or buy stuff (including food and water), so you don´t need to go to any other zone in order to do so.

The only problem I would consider about hunting in Oasis are, as I said before, the wandering high level spectres and sand giants. But don´t worry to much, people shout it when any of those are out of control heading either the orc or the crocs highway. Also, you can avoid being caught by any of them while medding up if you do it up the pillars by the beach, as I explained above. Also watch out for the Dry Bone Skelletons, lvl 20 to 22 very nasty ideed skellie casters.

You will love this zone, and the jump from lvl 12 up to 16 will be even faster than the one from 7 to 12, I´m 100% sure about that.
