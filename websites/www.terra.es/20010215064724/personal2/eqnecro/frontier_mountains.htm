Frontier Mountains

This is another zone of the newly discovered continent of Kunark, between Lake of ill Omen, the Overthere and the Dreadlands.

In spite of the fact that there´s plenty of roaming creatures here you could solo, this is the first time I´m gonna tell you to group. Why? Because in Frontier Mountains there is probably one of the best spawns in the game : The giants fortress. Theese lvl 28-30 giants spawn so fast that there´s enough room for several groups camping the same place. Actually, I´ve seen up to 7 different groups camping the fortress and non of them argued about KS (kills stealing).

There are 2 places where you can set up your group´s base : At the front gate or inside by the eastern wall (see map of Frontier Mountains). Obviouly, pulling inside the fortress itself is much faster, but you need a whole group there because some times two giants jump on you at the same time. Besides, you don´t want to Fear the giant neither whilst inside the fortress!!

If you plan to camp outside at the fortress gates, I would not recomend you to gather up a full party. Three of you will be enough, specially if your colleagues are either mages or wizards. If you don´t have a tank, and therefore the pet will be the main damage taker, then use the Darkness-Fear combo and have the mage or wizzard to nuke him down. Your dutty will be keeping the giant out of mellee distance (they hit for 70´s !!).

What about the loot? Well, forget about cash loot, it just plainly sucks. But on the other hand they sometimes drop pieces of armour or large size weapons that can be sold at any vendor for very good money (20 to 50 pp). But the big prize is the forest loop ( earing, AC2, wis3, agi3, dex3 ). They are a very rare drop. I´d spent 4 days hunting there and only got 2 of them. But hey, I sold them for 1500pp each !! Druids love those earings and they pay huge amounts of money for them. So, if you spend few days hunting here and you are lucky enough to get one or two of them, your financial problems will dissapeard straight away, won´t they?

Even though you can solo at lvl 29 one of theese giants, don´t do it. They will burn most of your mana and medding up times will be too long. Besides they have a high MR (magic resistance) and if they resist two times in a row your Fear or Darkness spell, your pet will become nothing but dust (remember they hit very very hard indeed for their level), and once your pet´s died, guess who is the next on the list : ) I´m telling you, is far more proffitable to be in a small gruop pulling them fast than to try it on your on. And I like this just as little as you do, mate : (
