Burning Woods

In Burning Woods you prolly dinged 50 (If you followed my advice), and now you can push it a bit farther all the way till you ding 54. 

Basically, it will be the same story all over, but just with tougher mobs : Ancient and Verdant Giants, Sarnaks Zealot, Extremist and Avenger, Moldering Gorillas, Greater skellies, Ember Hornets and Wurms (not till level 51 when u get splurt and envenomed bolt -check spells list- cuz ignite blood wont work on them due to their high fire resistance). As you can see, there are still lots of blue conning mobs so you ll never lack of preys. Actually, I loved hunting in BW, there was always a creature by to pull away.

There are two places where you can park your pet and pull to : The giants hut and the northern wall.

1. Giants hut : I had leveled my way thru lvls 47 - 53 here. Loot is fairly good, with lots of gems and weapons worth 50pp at vendors. There is always a prey willing to be pulled around there  : )  Both from the hut itself (Giants) or from the forest (undeads, sarnaks, wurms and wasps). Take a look at the map to locate yourself. 

This place is excellent because it has a funny pathing, so when a mob is feared off just walks back and forth the same spot, without fleeing away. Also, if you pull to the place marked with a red cross on the map, no one mob will jump on you ganging up another.

Giants drop a nice loot, far better than the thougher guys in Sky Fire. Ancients drop spells, and the named one -Gargul- drops the treewaave which is worth 5 000 pp, not so bad uh?

Also, its worth to mention that there are always few groups hunting there, so if you DMF them, you ll get SOW and cloro, and that doesnt hurt anyone. Or even a 49 exp rez in case things turn sour.

2. Northern Wall : Basically the same stuff, but not many giants up there, meaning less loot. Also, there are more chances of trains, so I would rather go for the Giants outpost in stead.

Pre 54, the game still let us solo while getting a nice exp while doing it, and Burning Woods is probably the last soloing zone where we will still be able to get better exp than grouping (in my opinion, of course). SkyFire post 54 is feasible, but downtimes grow longer and I dont really think its worthy neither in exp nor in loot terms. So enjoy your lasts moments as a lonely wolf before the Verant s "Vision" of the game -meaning to play it in group- sucks you with no way back possible  : (

Tip : Its important for you to not to worry about overdamaging your pet so he doesnt pinch exp. Here in BW there are plenty of preys, so its more profitable to share some exp with your pet (I believe it to be less than 50%, maybe a 33%) and to be full of mana ready to pull right away. What I did was: 

Low blues : Verdants, Gorillas, Sarnaks Avengers and Extremists. Pull with bond of death, run up to pet, sick him in, land dooming darkness on mob, fear it off, and just renew fear or dooming as needed letting the pet (augmentation always up) to wipe him off and medding between casts with lich. 

High blues : Ancient Giants, Wurms, Sarnaks Zealots, Greater undeads and Wasps. Pull with Splurt, run to pet, sick him in, land dooming darkness on mob, fear it off, suck the crap outta him with bond of death, and renew dooming and fear as needed. While pet (augmentation always up) teaches him a lesson, you med up to full with lich.

So, even though your pet eats up a chunk of exp from you, then non-stop pullin will make it up, believe me.
