Lower Guk Death Side

Oh boy, here we are. Before the Kunark expansion, Lower Guk was the most popular zone for high level players. And still lots of them consider this dungeon as the best in Norrath. And now you are scratching your chin wondering why, arent you? 

Well, it is pretty simple : good exp and AWESOME loot. In fact, some of the most outstanding items in game are dropped here. Want some few examples? there you go : Crown of the froglok kings ( 20 sv fire, 20 sv cold, 12 AC), Runed cowl (5 wis, 9 int, 2 ac), Shinning matalic robe ( 9 int, 8 sv cold, 8 sv fire, 10 ac), Flowing Black silk Sash ( effect : haste), White gold necklace ( 7 magic resistance, 7 sv fire, 7 sv cold, 5 char), Executioner s axe ( attack 25, delay 25, effect: haste), etc. Have a look to the zone map to know where do these items drop.

Lower Guk is divided in two subzones : the dead and the live side. Out of those two, I honestly believe that the dead one is the one for you. One reason is that the best items are dropped there, but most important is that, as a necro, it would not be any easy for you to get into a party in the life side. Alternatively, you are indeed of good use to any group when it cames up to kick some undead asses: you can nuke them, charm them, mesmerize them, root them, hail them, etc.

To be group - dessireable is very important in Lower Guk because it is absolutely imposible to solo here. Lower Guk has been dessigned kinda maze-alike, with severals rooms where the big fellows ( who drop the most looked for items) spawn every half an hour aproximately. On top of that, there are plenty of minor froggies roaming up and down the corridors. This means that you cannot camp by yourself the room boss because he will tear you apart, and you cant solo in the corridors because you will end up being chased down by a nasty train. So I am very afraid this zone is only group based hunting  : (

The most important character in any party when hunting in Lower Guk is the enchanter. He will undoubtly safe far more lifes than the cleric himself due to his/her mass control skills. Well, I would say you are also an undead mass control master yourself !!  And thats exactly what you gotta make your group to understand. There is no real bad need of an enchanter if a necro is around. You can charm one froggie, sick her to another of her colleagues, root or mesmerize a third one (with rest the dead spell. Check spells list if needed), etc, hence cooling a highly harzardous train down into a female primary schooll music band. I cannot stress it enough mate : either you play the enchanter rol or you ll stand small chances to get into any group. Come on, lets face it : if they want a pet/nuker, they would go for the mage everyday, and since Fear is not feasible inside a dungeon, theres no real reason any group would take a mage, wizzard, druid or enchanter down for you in stead  : (

So, we have got here a dungeon where you can get good exp and where you can acquire very good (and expensive) items, right?. Well, do you want to know my personal opinion? Lower Guk fucking sucks !!!  First of all, there is no way you can solo here, and even if you are in a group you wont be able to perform your wonderful Darkness-Fear-Dot combo. Only thing you will be asked to do is either play as a mage (send pet in and nuke the hell away with your DD vs undead spells) or as an enchanter (as explained above), but never as what you actually are, a necro. Second, exp is not that awesome. I mean, its good but since you gotta share the exp with a full group, either you find a very professional non-stop-pulling party, or the exp bar will stay put for ages. And on top of that the good items take hours to drop and even if it eventually drops, you will have to win the lotto. I swear on God, there is nothing more frustrating than to camp a room for the whole fu***ng evening and when the big boss finally drops whatever item he drops, to lose the lotto !!  ACK !! you wanna kill somebody !!  I camped once myself the magi room (who drops the shinning metallic robe) for more than 20 hours on a weekend and didnt ever got the robe. Only two robes were dropped while my group was camping the room and I lost both lottos. Believe me, after that weekend I was sooo upset that I didnt play EQ for the next week at all !!

Well, give it a try anyway for few days. If you get lucky you ll leave Lower Guk with dozens of thousands plats in items (selling/trading time in Commonlands). But, if you are looking for exp, you better look somewhere else where you may solo. Just my warning : If you are planning to go to Lower Guk to get some lovely items and therefore update your inventory, supply yourself with plenty of food, water, bone chips and coffee because you are gonna spend here loooooong time. That I can promise you  : )
