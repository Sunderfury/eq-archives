Infected Paw

Infected Paw entrance is right in the middle of South Karanas zone.

This zone will remind you on your BlackBurrow times because it´s a dungeon filled up to the top with ugly gnolls. Only difference is that theese ones are lvl 30ish : )

Actually, this zone can be a real pain in the very ass if you have had some extasy and decide to venture any farther than the double door (see map). Why? Because the deeper you walk into this dungeon, the higher the level of the gnolls and the nastier the trains become. Seriously guys, at your early 30´s don´t cross the double door, unless you would love to have a pot of tea with a gang of pissed off gnolls in their high 30´s.

My advice is: Stay at the red cross on the map (after killing an initial easier couple of lesser lvl 25 gnolls at the very biginning of the corridor), pull the 30ish gnolls guarding the double door. If you´ve got a good enchanter in the group (oh yeah I forgot, this is a zone meant for groups only due to the high chances of trains), then open the double door and mesmerize (mass control enchanter spell) all the gnolls but one or two, and try to brake the spawn that way.

This zone can give you very good experience and could be reasonably safe if you play wise, not wandering arround the inner part of the dungeon (not at this level at least).

It´s true that in the rooms descrived in the map some nice items are dropped, and if you get the chance to get into a higher level group (lvl 34 to 39 that is), then you should do well to try to acquire any of those items. But I repeat, don´t attempt to do that with your equal level mates. It would be quite risky.
