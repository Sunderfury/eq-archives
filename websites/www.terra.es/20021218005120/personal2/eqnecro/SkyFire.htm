Sky Fire

Sky Fire is probably the last zone where you will yet be able to solo with the Darkness-Fear combo. Indeed, I can think of no other higher level soloing zone as the game currently stands.

There is a huge difficulty gap from Burning Woods and Sky Fire. I mean, dont expect to find this zone like a sunday walk in the park pretty much the same as BW was in your early 50s. In stead, Sky Fire mobs have easily double HPs than giants, wasps, greater undeads or any other creature you had been fighting last few levels in BW. And, of course, they hit harder.

Some of the Sky Fire inhabitants are : 

  • Wurms : you know them already from BW. Highly fire resitant.
  • Mature wurms : same story all over but few levels higher and loads of HPs.
  • Chromadracs : Huge mosquitos. Avoid them at all cost cose they cast DoTs and dispell buffs like crazy.
  • Wyverns : Only creature here that sees thru invivility.
  • Drakes : They go from lvl 39 to 51. Being the skyash drake the easiest and the skyfire drake the toughest.
  • Bottomless : They are the easiest mob here, they got low HPs. Bottomless gnawer as the easiest (40ish) and bottomless dovourer the toughest (high 40s).
  • Elders and Ancients : They all summon (meaning post 50) and Ancients are lvl 60, so keep away  : )

There are 3 spots where you can settle down as you pulling spot. Each of them, no matter which, will require you to have either J/boots or SOW in order to pull safely :

  1. At The OverThere entrance passage : Marked with a blue cross on the map. Have your pet to guard the bottom of the tunnel, just at zoning border line while you scout out trying to single pull. Run back inside the tunnel and let the Fear kitting begin. If you successfully single pull deep indide the tunnel, no other mob will join in; and in case things turn sour, zoning line is just few feet away.
  2. By the Burning Woods zone : Marked in the map with a red cross. Here is where most groups decide to hang around. If you want to look for a group in SkyFire, this is you place. If you are soloing, better look somewhere else cuz those groups will clean the area free of mobs.
  3. Following the right wall from OverThere tunnel : Marked with a yellow cross on the map. This used to be my favorite spot when I would hunt here in Sky Fire. I dont know why, but it seemed to me easier to single pull here. Since the zone is pretty far away, you better have SOW or J/boots to run it up to zone.

As you can see Sky Fire is yet again the same kind of zone you have been looking for throughout your necro career (wide and flat open outdoors zone). So, this is supossed to be a nice an suitable place for you, right? Well, not really  :(  There are two main drawbacks here in SkyFire : a) loot sucks badly. Its far worse than in Burning Woods, and  b) mobs have just too many HPs to make it worth the effort. You will prolly burn up at least 3 bubles of mana per fight, meaning that you may forget about non stop pulling and rather get used to long mana meding up downtimes. 

I strongly encourage you to avoid this zone. It was the first time in my necro life that I realized that soloing here was way less exp rewarding than dungeon grouping. Fights are tough due to the large amount of HPs they got and because they hit damn hard. And once you finally drop the creature down, you only loot few plats and gotta sit your butt down for at least 5 minutes  :(

Tip 1 : Dont bother with fire based spells, they will all get resisted.

Tip 2 : Time up the dooming darkness s longevity because you will most likely have to renew it before the mob s falling. 

Tip 3 : I wouldnt use the lvl 53 rogue pet in stead of the 49 tank one. He just aint got enough HPs, and considering a mature wurm hits for double 140s, if either darkness or fear gets resisted couple of times in a row, your pet will be toast.
