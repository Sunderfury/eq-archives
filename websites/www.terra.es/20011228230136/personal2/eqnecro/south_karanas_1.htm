South Karanas (lvl 20 to 25)

In SK (South Karanas) inhabits one of the most dessirable and hunted creature for players in their 20´s : the treants (lvl 24 to 28 tree form druid). Three of them spawn on the upper-right corner of the map, marked down by a green squared area.

This place is heavily camped, and besides they are probably too strong for you at your earlies 20´s to solo them. So I´m afraid you gotta beg for a group here. But, once you get into one, you´ll see how the exp bar will be non-stop moving forwards. It is indeed that good exp. And so is the loot !

The treant itself is a nasty fellow : casts DDs, powerful DoTs and hit hard in mellee combat. But thanks God they only have 650 HP, so if you´r in a group with few casters, the tree will get chopped down to floor within seconds.

There´s nothing much else to say. It´s an easy prey if the group is formed by 3 or 4 guys, and the spawn is reasonably fast, so as I said the exp is awesome.

Only 1 main drawback : It´s always camped, and therefore not easy to have your group coming along to hunt them. Only chance is to snake into any group, and that´s no easy task since players keep this small treasure tight for themselves !!

Oh, one last thing: The faction hits are awfully bad, but since we are necros and since we´re already KoS to most druids, who the fuck cares !! Kill them all !! Blast those tree-huggers away !! I love being mean, don´t you? : )
