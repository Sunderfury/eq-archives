East Karanas

In East Karanas you´ll do pretty much the same that what you may have done in South Karanas with the treants hunting during levels 20 to 25: Camp a heavily camped three druids spawn in a group. That´s it.

The place is where the red squared area is drawn on the map, and the blue cross will be... bingo! your binding and pulling point.

Nothing really remarkable that I haven´t already said in the treants camping zone, so just read it all again to refresh your memory ´cause everything is 100% applicable to this new zone.

Same characteristics : good exp, good loot, group only, heavily camped.

Only one tip: Don´t venture inside this zone because high level non-amiably NPCs walk arround such as guards and druids. I died myself 3 times in a row due to theese morons the first time I explored throughtout East Karanas. That very day I became a druids-serial-killer !! : )
