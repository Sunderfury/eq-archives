Timorous Deep

In the SW corner of Timorous Deep (exact loc is -9200, +3200), there is a tiny isle full up with nasty raptors. Have a look to the map to find your way there (please note that the Isle has been zoomed in. Real proportions are indeed smaller).

These raptors are low-mid 40s, and 110 hp hitters, but surprisingly they aint have more than 3000 hp. Besides, loot is absolutely zero, nothing, nada, niente  : ) 

The main problem here is the huge aggro radius. I have never seen such a wide aggro radius like these guys have. This, added to the small size of the isle, makes imposible the fear-kitting.So, what you do is to pull them with Ignite Blood (check spells list if needed) to the hidden cove marked with a white cross on the map. This cove is aside and far away enough from the raptors roaming path, so if you single pull here, no other will jump along. But thats exactly the tough thing : to single pull raptors. If you see that two of them are close to each other and that you will pull them both, do this : Pull away casting Ignite blood on one of them. Now run with DMF and J/boots (a must in here. I warn you, dont try to solo here without a shiny pair of J/boots) the hell deep inside the ocean till you are out of the casting range. Turn around, quickly dive into the water, and cast Root on the one who isnt Dotted. Now, run back to the cove with the other one on your back. By the time Root brakes free, you will be far away from his aggro range.

And what do we do once we have single pulled a raptor to the cove? well, we Root - DoT it to death. Since we dont have to much space to walk backwards leaving a safety gap between us and the raptor, I advice to recast Root every 20 secs. Its very mana cheap to cast, so dont worry if you cast it 5 or 6 times per battle. This is the plan : single pull to cove with ignite blood, root, splurt, bond of death, root, 20 secs, stand up and root, 20 secs, stand up and root, 20 secs, stand up and root, 20 secs and loot the corpse (ops, I forgot there is no loot at all hehe ). Since they have less than 3000 hps, those three DOTs should be enough to take them down, and with lich up you wont burn too much mana per fight. 

As you can see, pet is no needed here.

Bonuses of this zone :

  • Raptors s low hp.
  • Excellent exp. I believe there is an exp bonus here, I swear.
  • It is never camped.

Drawbacks : 

  • Huge aggro radius. Might be dificult to single pull.
  • Loot plainly sucks.
  • If things turn sour, you will most likely find no lvl 49+ cleric around, and the binding point will be far away ( you cant bind yourself in this zone).
  • Journeyman Boots are a must.

In my opinion, if you are 53 or lower and you want to solo, I personally find Burning Woods more suitable : better loot, safer and 49+ exp rez always within reach.
