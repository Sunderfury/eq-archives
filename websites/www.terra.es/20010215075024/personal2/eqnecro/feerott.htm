The Feerott

The Feerott is the wet, smelly and sticky swamp the ogres use as their newbie zone. And you could now reply me back : A newbie zone as a hunting place for a lvl 30+ necro? Yeppers !! Because within this zone is the entrance to the Plane of Fear. Now, don´t shit your pants yet, we are not going to any Plane of Fear, I said only the entrance.

The entrance to such a plane is a hidding fake wall where the red cross lies on the map. Inside that cave there are few spectres (lvl 33 to 37 very spooky looking undead creature). Do not pull any of those, even if you see only one standing there, because I promisse you, there are many more around the corner and you WILL pull them all along !!

So what then? aha ! now is when you realize how brilliant this necro guide is : ) Check the map once more. Within that reddish rectangular area I have marked out on it, there is a hill, and somewhere on that hill there is a tree, and sitting down by that tree there´s a... lonely spectre !! Looks awfully scary, doesn´t it? But hey, don´t be a wimp. You can easily take it down.

There are four very good thing about hunting specs:

1.Awesome exp when soloing.

2.They have a low Magic Resistance and

3.You can outrun them without SoW.

4. It´s a very fast 6 minutes re-spawn, which is just time enough to med mana to full with allure of Death.

Now, how do you hunt them? First of all, memorice panic the dead in stead of the beloved Fear. And now same stuff as usual: Darkness-Panic the dead-DoT combo. You have heard about this before, haven´t you? : ) Anyhow, do not let them get any close too you, ´cause the hit for 92 !! They cast minor lifetap spells and Fear too, but you´ll resist it most of the times.

What about the loot ? Up to 6 pp cash, rusty scythes and sometimes a scythe (worth 11 pp at vendors). Not too bad.

Actually, hunting spectres are much easier than it would initially seem due to their low magic resistance and to that crappy lvl 12 drain life spell of theirs. Psa ! nothing like a good necro !!

Only drawback is that is not easy to find this spot uncamped. Probably you´ll have to get into a waiting list if it´s not available, but at higher levels that´s every single day´s business. But I think is worthy, honestly. At these levels, specs are the best exp creature you can solo.

Also, I must warn you about a nasty NPC who roams all over the zone : Roror and his pet. He´s a high 40´s shamman lizzard and he´s very very aggro on you. Many times, while camping this otherwise safe spec spawn, Roror would sneak on my back and hit me for over 100´s, killing me and my pet within seconds. There´s no real chance to escape away if you ever run into him, ´cause he´ll hit you so hard and so fast that not ever feing death will safe you. Just pray your God to keep you out of his way and Gate the fuck out if you spot him at once. There are another dangerous NPCs like Ogre guards, but they post still in certain places, so not really big deal.
