Awards for the Lost Order of Akalabeth Members

Awards, Medals and Badges of Honor; EverQuest Faction of LOA
The LOA believes in honoring those members who time and again strive to make a strong, impressionable example to those around us. Sometimes, a member of our guild, or our community, does something so wonderful that they truly deserve recognition for their actions or deeds. This page will be a testament to those people who have made an impression upon our guild, and the game.

Writing the Citation; Who Can Nominate Members

If you wish to nominate a member for an award, please make sure that you spend adequate time writing the citation and that the recipient TRULY has earned the award in question. Awards should not be thrown around and trivialized, they are meant to be great awards that will forever be displayed in our guild history for future generations to read. Your citation must be mailed to the loa-council@lostorder.com mailing list, where one of our FOUNDERS or the faction leader will accept or deny the citation. For this reason, please keep the citation somewhat private until you know for sure that it has been accepted. Once accepted, your citation will be posted to the guild mailing list for ALL to see. The citation will also be posted on the guild news page, and then the awards page will be updated as well.

Santiago's Heart Award; Roleplay

One of the greatest and meaningful awards that we can offer. This award is granted to those members who have embraced the roleplaying aspects of EQ and the LOA. Those who motivate and inspire other members to do more than 'level' and 'hack-n-slash'. For those who capture our hearts and our imaginations, we honor them with this prestigious award.

Recipients: Santiago (8/99), Azlana (8/99), Jediit (1/00)

Medal of Guidance; Community

You do not have to be an official "LOA Guide" to receive this award. If you spend your time helping others, either members, guests, commoners, friends, or perfect strangers, you are eligible for this award. We are looking for people who make this a common practice, not just a one time show of assistance.

Recipients: Xirius (8/99), Dearg (8/99), Aanlain (8/99), Walterus (10/99)

Medal of Events; Roleplay

To any member who can dream up an epic event, or several small quests that help to increase the fun and involvement of other guild members and/or guests, this award will be granted to them. We cannot rely on Verant to provide us with several events, quests, and plots... therefor, we depend on our members to take up this challenge and make it happen.

Recipients: Santiago (8/99), Azlana (11/99)

Medal of Discipline; Power

Although we do not focus on 'powergaming', we cannot deny that attaining new levels is a fun and exciting aspect of the game. With this in mind, we want to award those members who have spent countless hours fine-tuning their characters and becoming a powerful character in our guild, as well as in Norrath. This award is granted to any member who attains the 50th Level.

Recipients: Lillith [Gildan] (11/99), Dearg (1/00)

Well, it is every members duty to keep an eye open for potential candidates who would be perfect for our community.
