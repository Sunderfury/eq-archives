Safefall Skill by Notiturit

 

To build safefall safely all that you need to do is to take a boat ride. While on the ship you run from the upper level to the lower, you will take no damage and it builds all the way to mastery. There is no need to turn around either, just use the arrow keys and keep hitting up arrow/down arrow, it builds really fast even at high levels. 1 boat ride per level and it will easily reach its maximum for your level.
