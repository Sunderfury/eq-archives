Closing Recruiting...Posted Tuesday, October 15, 2002 by Volx
Thanks for all the inquiries, but at this point we are closing recruiting for all classes except Mages and Necros (people from those two classes should contact an officer ASAP if you wish to be considered pre-PoP). We may consider reopening recruiting a few months after PoP is out. For those people currently raiding with us (ie, current recruits), we will be finalizing decisions on you all this week - some will make the cut, others will not - this is a matter of us hitting our target numbers and is in no way a comment on yourself or your skills.

-Volx
