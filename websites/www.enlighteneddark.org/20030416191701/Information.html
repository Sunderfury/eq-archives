General Membership information:

Must play hardcore, at least 5 days a week, 5-11 e.s.t. is our normal raid time. We are a no nonsense guild when we raid. There are 36 or more other people depending on you to be on time, at the correct location, and prepared to tackle whatever may come your way. Must have a good reputation on the server. If you are controversial or into drama, please look elsewhere. Must be patient and able to follow instructions. Must have a desire to defeat the hardest encounters in the game of EQ! We are a raid guild, and that is what we do, so if this play style is for you, feel free to contact one of our officers or a member and they can point you in the right direction. We are not a lovy-dovy family guild - those guilds are appropriate for some folks, but not for us.

The steps/requirements for joining ED are as follows:

1. Start by grouping with members or friends in the guild. From there ask your friend in the guild to get you invited on some small raids. Start small! Dont ask to come on a huge raid as your first introductory raid. These battles require very precise planning, strategy, and execution as well as compensating for things such as lag, etc.

2. You should be level 65 and have at the minimum 100 AAXP. We very rarely will accept people who do not have the AA and never for those under level 65. The nature of EQ at high levels requires high-level players. If you have any questions, feel free to message an officer listed on the Roster section of this web page.

3. Be patient. ED very seldom will invite someone after they have been on one or two raids. Sometimes it takes weeks, sometimes it takes months and no two candidates can expect to wait the same amount of time. It is just the way we do things.

4. If you are in another guild, we will not invite you (to ED) until you have been out of that guild for some time, a week or two at the minumum. We do not encourage guild jumping.

5. DO NOT expect loot while being a recruit or for 3 months after joining ED. Even if you happen to be the only Paladin at a raid, do not expect to receive by default the pally loot that drops. We expect people to earn their stripes, so to speak. Enlightened Dark distributes loot on a Merit basis (some exceptions are made in which case the item in question is "randomed" for, this is decided by the officers present) If you do not agree with this type of system, then do not even bother trying to join. We do not do DKP.

6. Please have proper resist gear for poison, disease, magic, fire, and cold, as you WILL be expect to have appropriate resists on raids requiring them. 100 should be an absolute minumum for each resist, 200+ is preferred (UNBUFFED).

7. MUST KNOW HOW TO LISTEN TO AND FOLLOW INSTRUCTIONS... this means if you are told something by the leader of the raid, do it. Period. A 'consent' hotkey is a must.

8. Know your way around, by level 65... you should know how to get from place to place. If you do not know, there are maps online available all over the place, print them out and use them. DO NOT expect us to lead you around on a leash. Figure out in advance the travel time it will take you to make a raid on time - nothing pisses off folks more than late guildmembers.

9. Be on frequently. Enlightened Dark is an active guild, if you join our ranks you should be someone who plays a lot. We are not saying that you cannot have a life outside EQ, but we are a guild of "EQ junkies", and if you are invited it is because we need you to be on for raids and such. If you are looking for an online chat guild, or family love-dovey type shit, well then, look elsewhere.

10. If you are invited, you play this class as your primary character. If you decide to start or pursue an alt-character, do so on your free time. At raids you are expected to be present with the class we invited, since it is a class we needed! If you decide to change characters all of the sudden because you just used this class to join ED, you will find out you are no longer in ED. Playing alts is extremely LOW priority - improving your primary toon should always come first and foremost.

11. Have a solid connection, the faster the better. We are facing more and more the problem that people on raids are going LD all the time. If you know this happens to you frequently, then sorry, but a LD character does not help us at all. Have a solid non-winmodem 45k+ connection or ISDN/DSL/Cable.

12. Have the basic equipment needed to function in the high-end game:

  • Your ssra EMP key must be complete except for the insignia and you must have all 10 of your VT shards and the container (we will assist with the powersource obviously)
  • Must have basic PoP flags including all Tier 1, 2, and 3 flags except RZ and Sol Ro flags (you should be fully flagged with everything except RZ death and the Sol Ro flags)
  • Helps if you have your appropriate Shissarbane and Serubane weapons made (if applicable to your class) - some help may be available to complete these
  • EB item, Invis item, See-invis item, and Gate item (for melee in case you get stuck somewhere), be it potions or whatnot.
  • Carry the day-to-day materials you need (food, water, bandages, reagants, etc). While officers will often help with the costs of reagants, do not expect them to procure them for you. Come prepared!

One final note, if you join this guild with Loot as your priority, you will be disappointed. We play the game for FUN and to WIN. Loot comes as a by-product of all our hard work, not as the reason for our efforts....

-Nazlug (GM, Enlightened Dark)
