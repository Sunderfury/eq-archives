The steps/requirements for joining ED are as follows:

1. Start by grouping with members or friends in the guild. From there ask your friend in the guild to get you invited on some small raids.

2. Start small! Dont ask to come on a Dain raid as your first introductory raid. These battles require very precise strategy, as well as compensating for things such as lag, etc.

3. Be patient. ED very seldom will invite someone after they have been on one or two raids. Sometimes it takes weeks. We move like a large, slow, red tape filled government at times. It is just the way we do things.

4. If you are in another guild, we will not invite you (to ED) until you have been out of that guild for some time, a week or two at the minumum. We do not encourage guild jumping.

5. DO NOT expect loot for 3 months after joining ED. Even if you are the only Paladin at a raid for example, dont expect to get by default the pally loot that drops. We expect people to earn their stripes, so to speak. Enlightened Dark distributes loot on a Merit basis(some exceptions are made in which case the item in question is "randomed" for, this is decided by the officers present) If you do not agree with this type of system, then dont bother trying to join.

6. Please have proper resist gear for poison, disease, magic, fire, and cold, as these WILL be expect of you to have on raids requireing them.

7. We rarely will accept people who are under level 58. Occasional exceptions are made, but not often. The nature of EQ at high lvls requires high lvl players. If you have any questions, feel free to msg an officer listed on the character/member section of this webpage.

8. MUST KNOW HOW TO LISTEN TO INSTRUCTIONS... this means if your told something by the leader of the raid, do it. Period.

9. Know your way around, by lv 58... you should know how to get from place to place. If you dont know there are maps online available all over the place, print them out and use them, DO NOT expect us to lead you around on a leash.

10. Be on often, Enlightened Dark is an active guild, if you join our ranks you should be someone who plays alot. Not saying that you cant have a life, but we are a guild of "EQ" junkies, and if your invited its because we need you to be on for raids and such. If you are looking for an online chat guild, or family lovedovey type shit well look elsewhere.

11. If you get invited you play this class, IF you decide to start an alt-char do so but at raids you are present with the class we invited, since its a class we needed! If you decide to change all of the sudden because you just used this class to join ED, you will find out you are no longer in ED.

12. Have a solid connection, the faster the better. We are facing more and more the problem that people on raids are going 99% LD all the time if you know this happens to you then sorry but a LD character doesnt help.

13. So in a list here are the "tangable requirements"

a. Be lv 58 or higher
b. Have resist gear for all types (perferably over 100+ for each type)
c. Have keys to Howlingstones, Sebilis, Cobalt Scar Portal, & Skyshrine... ALSO Have the quest for the key to Veeshan started and be ready to finish it within 2 weeks of joining the guild.
d. A solid non-winmodem 45k+ connection, or ISDN+ (DSL/Cable).This applies to ALL candidates if you cant stay connected then we cant use you.
e. Have some sort of EB and See Invis Item, be it potions or whatnot.



One final note, we are not actively recruting any specific class and yes some classes are more desireable, so if we say "no" to you because your a ranger or some other less highly demanded class, dont take it personally, thank verant :)
