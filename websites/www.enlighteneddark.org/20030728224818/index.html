Terris Thule down!!!Posted Sunday, July 6, 2003 by Nazlug

Yeah, yeah you gimps, I know you are saying, "Wtf we care? Terris? she is a sucka!" Well, the Terris in Plane of Time is not the same mob. :p This is a crazy fight, with adds galore, insane AoEs including a direct mana drain and a mana/hp dot. (200 per tick? hihi!) She is one tough chickadie.

Anyway.... Plane of Time is interesting, it sorta works like so... You have 5 sets of trials, that must be completed in a certain time limit, which then advance you to 3 more trials, then to 1 more BIG trial, then the gods themselves. The gods have the same names, and some simliar abilites, but they are a f*ckload harder. Tonight we took out Saryrn and Terris. The Zeks are in the lineup for tommorow. :)

Finally, the loots! Priest classes rejoice. We scored x2 Hammer of Hours. Not sure what this proc does, something like a 750pt DD. Not too shabby, but dont quote me on numbers, go look it up yourself.

-Naz
