Sushi anyone?Posted Tuesday, April 22, 2003 by Lelaa

The squid/shrimp/lobster looking thing got owned hardcore today. With 2 min and 15 sec left for despawn we engaged him and this time around he didn't have a chance. I like this encounter cause it doesn't drain me and bore me out like some of the other mobs in PoP. He is timed and you get one shot at him then he dies or poofs. Killing him didn't bring me the same joy and orgasm like Xegony fight but still felt good. I still need a smoke after doing him =p
At first I didn't like the idea of a timed event where you don't get another shot at him. But now I can say that it's a good thing cause I would hate to sit there and try him over and over. Xegony is far the best fight in PoP so far... Only got Rathe left before times but not holding my breath till they fix that encounter. So we will keep farming the gods for now and get some nice loot.
