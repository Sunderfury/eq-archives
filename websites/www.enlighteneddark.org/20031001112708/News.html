Quarm down again!Posted Monday, September 15, 2003 by Nazlug

Well, after some bad luck last time with us losing the Quarm loot, we went back after respawn, and took care of business. The fight went well, and everyone really did the job. Excellent work! The loots were very nice, with 3 items, Cord for Corgadarn, Bracer for Ashwynd, and Spool for Veeeeeela. (Thats Vila for you strangers out there.)Here are some shots of the loots.





Alrighty then, on to the Lost Dungeons of Norath. This is the latest expansion for Everquest, as we all know. We completed one of the raids, and scored a nice AC25 Augment, a 50 mana Augment, and a Vengeance 3 Augment. Not bad, I can tell you that some of these raids are hard as you know what. If you think you are uber, try the Guk raid on the hard level.....

What do I think of the expansion? If they place items to Augment Time and Elemental Items on the Vendors, then we will have a reason to play it more then a month, otherwise, most of us will have bought the few Augments that help us, and we will end up only doing raids. I have to say that I am sorely disapointed there are no real dungeons in this expansion. I guess I am just an old timer, but I liked the Kunark era of Sebilis and Chardok crawls, not the idea of spawning my own dungeon. I like playing with lots of other people around, I dont want to play a 6 person game like some of the others out there. If I want that, there are other games to play. Oh well, lots seem to like the idea of spawning a dungeon at will, with the idea to save and buy rewards. Just not for me is all.

-Naz
