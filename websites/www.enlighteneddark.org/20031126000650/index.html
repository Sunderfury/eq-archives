All in a days work.Posted Sunday, October 19, 2003 by Nazlug

Yes, updates are few and far between. We are in the farm stage of PoP and Ldon just doesnt really excite most of us, so there isnt a whole lot of revolutionairy stuff going on. Sooner or later we will get too bored and start working the Ldon raids, but right now with the current setup, they just dont provide adequte Risk vs. Reward.

Here are a few changes myself and many other people suggest to make Ldon much more fun, at least in the raid environment. First of all, they need to allow you to add people to a raid. This is just a major inconveience that once you start, you are not able to add or lose anyone. This was supposed to be the casual gamers expansion, but that aspect doesnt really cater to that side of the game. I wont get started on the size of the raids being 36, when we were all forced to bulk up our guild size to support the 72 man raids of PoP.

Second, there should be raid merchants. They tally the points different, and have items only available with raid points. This will provide incentive to do these raids much more frequently. On the FoH board there is a long thread covering the topic, and I feel the idea is a good one.

These two changes would make Ldon much more appealing, at least for our style of guild.

Ok, enough of that. We did Plane of Time Saturday, and had very solid results, clearing it from start to finish. Good work by all the people of ED. Here are the loots, and dont ask me who got what. Hard to keep track when so much loot flows in!
