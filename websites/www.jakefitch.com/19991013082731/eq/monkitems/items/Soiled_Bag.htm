Where: [Spoiler highlight to read]
Get yourself a Ranger to track the "Guano Harvester" In Solusek B. Once he spawns, head toward the bat area just to the right of the Champion room. You MUST have see invisibility to see this creature and he is a lower level Goblin. Grab him quick, or a Bat will follow. Kill him but do not loot. (lore items, only one per person)

Now. He drops 1 of 2 items that are a MUST have. The first is called a "Soiled Bag" or more affectionately called the poop bag (LOL) This is not your regular weigt reducing bag.... After extensive testing last weekend from our party, it is a 100% weigth reducing bag. Thats right. 100% but it’s for small and medium items only.
