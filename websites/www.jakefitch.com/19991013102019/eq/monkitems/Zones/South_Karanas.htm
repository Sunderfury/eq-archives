South Karanas
Approximate Level: 15-32
Description: South Karana is a vast plain that contains two villages.  The first village is the home of the Centaurs.  The second village, home of the Aviaks, is a high-rise into the sky.  Watch your step when you up in the top of it, because even with a high Safe Fall skill you will still take several hundred points of damage if you should fall.  Also be aware that if you are fighting an aviak up in the village and it happens to fall or run to the bottom, it can still hit you.

Places of Interest
Aviak Village: (-6800, 1150) A high-rise that aviaks, bird-men, live in.  Also a good place to pickup a SoW because many druids and shamans hangout near the bottom of the village.
Centaur Village: (200, -2400) Home of the Centaurs.  There is also a vendor in one of the buildings.  The Crafter Armor quest is run by a couple of centaurs.
Krak ??? - A vendor in the Aviak Village.  Once you reach the village, go all the way up the ramp, then go to the back of the "roost".  He will not deal with humans, but if you use Sneak then right-click on him you can sell to him.  He also sells bandages.  Easy way to burn silver and coppers.
Raiders: (2400, -1300)
Ruins: 
(1400, 1100) The first ruins is very close to the bridge that connects South and North Karanas.  Lord Grimlot also spawns in this camp, he is a high level Shadow Knight (I believe) that doesn't like monks.
(-2000, 2400) Zombies and skeletons populate these ruins.  It may also be a Wizard's Pryamid.
