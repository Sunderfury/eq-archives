North Qeynos
Approximate Level: 1-5
Description: Qeynos is a city built by Antonius Bayle in the name of Justice.  It is also home of the Silent Fist Monk Guild.  
NPC Items
Fire Beetles, Klicknicks Fire Beetle Eyes
??? (Newbie Quest) Torn Brown Shirt (Guild)
Phin (Quest) White Headband, Yellow Headband, Orange Headband
??? (Quest) Red Headband, Purple Headband

Places of Interest
Silent Fist (Monk Guild): West side of town.  Coming in through the gates, go past the shop, then turn to the right.  The guild house is just past the pond.  Bandages can be bought here.
Phin: He can be found either in front of the shop just inside of town, in front of the Silent Fist guild house or inside the guild house.
??? Shop: Just inside of town.  Milk and bandages can be bought here.
Entrances to Qeynos Aqueducts: One is in the pond in front of the Silent Fist guild house and another is in the wall of the town behind a secret wall to the East of the town gates.  The wall looks like it is covered by moss or vines.
