These are a rare loot item found on Drelzna in Najena. I have heard of people camping Drelzna for 10+ hours and not getting a single pair of JBoots. As for the price, they are priceless. Literally. They are a "NO DROP" item so they can’t be bought, sold, traded, or stored in the bank. Too bad that is, because these are high on my list of toys I want Santa Claus to deliver. Guess I will have to earn them somehow.

As for how tough this would be, I just saw a lvl 26 Monk on E’ci (Dragnfist) with JBoots on. I would say a group of 6 - 26th lvl PCs could camp the room effectively. More like 30+ though to be on the safe side though.
