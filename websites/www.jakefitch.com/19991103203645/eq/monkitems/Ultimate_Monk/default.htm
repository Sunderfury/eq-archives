Ultimate Monk

The purpose of this page is to show different sets of armor, based upon the difficulty in getting those items.

Super-Human -  This set is the best of the best.  Items on this list come from dragons, alternate Planes, and possibly from killing you guildmaster.

High Level - This set has the best items, that are not of super human nature.  I.E. No dragon loot items, etc.

Fortified Set - This set is optimized for high AC.  It would be considered on the same level as the High Level set.

Mid-Low Level - This is the set for monks in the low 20's.  All items on this list can either be attained from the NPC who has them by a group in the low 20's or items that are for sale often enough that monks in this range should be able to easily purchase them.  No Jewel-craft item are on this list.
