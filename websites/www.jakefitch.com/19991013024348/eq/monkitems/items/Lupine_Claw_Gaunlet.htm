Lupine Claw Gauntlets are made by Karg Icebear, who can be found in Everfrost.  You need to bring him a Werewolf claw and 75 pp.  Werewolf claws look like a typical rat paw.  Werewolves are a rare spawn in Mistmoore, West Commons, and Kithicor.  Werewolves spawn in the "piano room" in Mistmoore.  During the day Gypsies spawn there, but they actually are werewolves also.  You can get Werewolf claws from killing either the werewolves or the gypsies.
