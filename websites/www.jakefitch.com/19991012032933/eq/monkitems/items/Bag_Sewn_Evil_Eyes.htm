There seems to be 2 ways to get this bag.  The first is:
"On a named evil eye in the Gorge of King Xorbb."
And the second is:
"Enter Lower Guk by going past the spiders.  Go down tunnel and take the right fork.  You will come to water.  You swim under the water going right, and come up in what is know as the Bedroom.  From the bedroom you should take the tunnels to the right (you can go left,  but there are 4 Greater Ice Bone Skels that way) Eventually, after fighting some ghouls, bats and spiders you will come to an area where 3 Garg’s spawn. On the right is a door.  Go in door.  From here it’s all live frogs so if you are Killed on Sight by lives, forget it.  Go down ladder.   Go through first room to tunnel.  There are Greater Minos down here as well, these will not attack if your faction with live frogs is good.  In this tunnel there is a dark spot on wall, this is the location of a trap/pit in floor. Jump over that and continue on. As you come out of tunnel with trap, you enter an open area. keep to your left. A little ways down is a tunnel on the left. Go in this tunnel and you are home free. Tunnel is quite long. Eventually you will come to a room with water and planks. Straight across is a raised room with a window over the water. This is the Evil Eye spawn. Sometimes there will be a frog shaman there and you have to kill shaman to start new spawn.

Be warned.  Killing Evil Eye hurts your faction with Live Frogs. This includes the greater Mino’s down in Evil Eye Tunnels. Con discreetly before you go running down tunnel.

Evil Eye Drop is: Common Evil Eye bag, Rare Mana Stone."
