RUNE OF FLAME EMERGES

    Rune of Flame has been added to the guild section. Here is a great description of their guild written by their leader, Inyx.

-Storm

Rune of Flame is a society of scholars, educators, and adventurers. The members scower Norrath and the planes to collect information and items that use to attain a higher understanding of the world, its inhabitants and the forces of the gods that are at work within it.

The Rune of Flame is dedicated to the collection and storage of information. At times they will exchange information with others for secrets found by others and items of power that will assist them in their quest.

Through this knowledge comes power and with this power comes a responsibility to protect the less fortunate of Norrathian society. Therefore the members also make a point to protect those who are oppressed by the chaos that inhabits Norrath.

GM EVENT REPORT

Following are the details of the event as told by Felwan Silverblade of the
Solidaire Guild, Lanys T'Vyl:

"Hail,

Last night, I was strolling around west commons when I heard a shout from a
man named Jeroth asking if any warriors, paladins, or shadowknights were up
to a challenge, a test of honor. Well, feeling that if I didn't respond my
honor would be in question, I answered his request and was then told that I
was the one who would face the challenge. I met Jeroth in the corner of
commons near the shadowmen camp and he proceeded to tell me what was in
store. I found out that I was to face his 3 brothers, one at a time, in a
battle to the death. Once I killed the first brother, I was to return the
amulet wore around his neck back to Jeroth and would then receive my clue as
to the location of the next brother. Also, I was able to choose a team of
companions to aid my in my fight. Well, I choose friends that were near,
Carbo, Stratten, Taycie, Kaetlyn, and Canalon agreed to help me and we were
off to the desert of North Ro to kill the first brother. After that fight,
we returned and were told to meet the next in Kithicor, and after that
battle, my final brother to meet was in lava storm.

The first brother's name was Drewyn Wyndsteel and he was a warrior as
myself. The second brother was a monk by the name of Zev Thunderfis. The
third and final brother was an evil dark elf shadowknight by the name of
Dalesk V'Syrn. All three were difficult competitors, but all three died by
the end of my blades. Upon returning to Jeroth after the third brother, I
was told I would face my final confrontation in the Arena in West Freeport
and this battle was to be me alone. Well, we all assembled in Freeport and
after some problems with some over zealous guards, I was in the arena ready
to face my final battle. My opponent was the Spirit of Honor, and I was no
match for his obvious powers. I knew my doom was pending, so I saluted the
Spirit before he made the final blow, and was told to STOP fighting. He
congratulated me for passing all the trials and two more spirits showed up,
the Spirit of Fury and the Spirit of Heart. I was congratulated by all
three spirits, and was then awarded a blade, the Blade of Champions. I
vowed to do my best to uphold the name of this mighty blade and thanked the
Spirit who had gathered to test me.

I am honored that I was chosen to take place in this test, and I am
greateful for all those that helped. I wanted to take a moment to thank
everyone who followed me around to all the different spots and congratulated
me in the end. Thanks.


Felwan Silverblade
Emerald Warrior of Solidaire
Lanys server
