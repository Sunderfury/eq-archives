Necromancer Spells
Spell Name Mana Reagent Spell Description
Level 1
Cavorting Bones Bone Chips This is the first in the line of necromancer pet spells. This spell summons a level 1 decaying skeleton.
Cold Light This spell summons a dim light which can be used as a lightsource.
Disease Cloud
 This spell will disease your opponent and do a small amount of damage initially as well.
Invisibility To Undead This spell renders you invisible to undead mobs.
Lifetap This is the first in a line of spells which is unique to necromancers. This spell will suck hit points away from your opponent and give them to you.
Locate Corpse This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest corpse.
Minor Shielding This is the first in the line of the wizards shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.
Reclaim Energy This spell is cast on summoned pets. When cast on a pet this allows you to reclaim the mana you spent on him to a certain extent and "unsummon" him.
Sense The Dead This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.
Siphon Strength This spell will lower your targets strength and add it to yours.

Level 4
Clinging Darkness This is a duration of time spell which causes damage over time. It also slows the targets speed down as it is somewhat of a blinding spell.
Endure Cold This spell adds to the targets resistance to cold.
Fear Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. Be warned however that he will return when the spell wears off and he may bring friends.
Gate The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.
Grim Aura This spell grants the caster a damage shield. Whenever a monster hits you it will inflict some damage upon him.
Leering Corpse 2 Bone Chips This spell summons a level 3-5 skeleton.
Lifespike Lifespike is the upgrade to lifetap. This spell takes transfers some of the hit points of your opponent and adds them to your hit points.
Numb The Dead Numb the dead is an undead calming spell. By casting this spell on undead beings you will cause them not to attack players they would normally attack.
Poison Bolt This spell will poison your opponent doing damage to him over time.
Sense Magic This spell will give you information on magic items.

Level 8
Bonewalk 2 Bone Chips This spell will summon a level 7-9 skeletal pet that has the ability to Bash, Double Attack, and Parry.
Dark Empathy This spell will heal your target while damaging yourself.
Dark Pact Converts Health into Mana
Deadeye Deadeye grants the caster not only infravision but also the ability to see undead beings. This is important for races who do not have the benefit of infravision.
Gather Shadows Gather shadows is an invisibility spell that can only be cast on yourself. This is very important to evil players as it will allow you to travel places you previously were cut off from.
Impart Strength This spell will give some of your strength to your target.
Lesser Shielding This is an upgrade to the minor shielding spell. It adds armor class and hit points.
Mend Bones This spell allows you to heal your pet.
Shadow Step This spell is a short mini teleportation spell that will teleport you a short distance away from your current location. It is mainly used as an escape spell.
Vampiric Embrace This is an more powerful version of the lifetap spell. This spell will drain hit points away from your target and give it to you. This has the benefit of not only damaging your enemy but healing yourself as well.   You must hit the monster to drain.
Ward Undead This is a direct damage spell for use versus undead beings. It is the first in a line of spells.

Level 12
Bind Affinity This spell allows you to change your binding point where you will return if you gate or die.
Convoke Shadow This spell will summon a level 11-13 pet.
Endure Disease This spell will increase your resistance to disease.
Engulfing Darkness This damage over time spell is an upgrade to the clinging darkness spell.
Heat Blood This is a damage over time spell. The importance of it is that it can be stacked with the clinging->engulfing darkness line of spells which will allow you to do a lot more damage stacking the two of them.
Lifedraw Lifedraw is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells which will heal yourself by stealing a monsters hit points.
Sight Graft This somewhat useless spell will allow you to see through your pets eyes.
Spook The Dead This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with numb as you can numb one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one.
Wave Of Enfeeblement Wave of Enfeeblement is an area effect weaken spell.

Level 16
Banshee Aura This is an upgrade to the grim aura spell. It allows you to cast a damage shield around yourself which will punish your opponents for making contact with you.
Cancel Magic This spell can be used to cancel magical effects that have been cast on you or your party members. It's great to eliminate damage over time, charm or root spells.
Cure Disease Cure disease will allow you to cure yourself or another player of a disease.
Feign Death This spell allows you to play dead. If it works the monster will assume you are dead and will leave you alone. You can lay around and regain hit points and then get up and run.
Heart Flutter This is a triple threat spell. It not only weakens your opponent and lowers their armor class but it also does damage over time.
Hungry Earth This is a root spell which works only on undead.
Infectous Cloud This is an area effect disease spell.
Restless Bones Restless bones will summon a 13-16 lvl skeletal pet.
Shielding This is an upgrade to the minor shielding->lesser shielding line of spells. It adds not only armor class and hit points but magic resistance as well.
Shieldskin This spell allows you to absorb a certain amount of damage without being injured.
Spirit Armor Spirit armor is a buffing spell which can be cast on yourself and others which will add armor class.
Shadow Vortex Siphones AC from your Oponent.
Voice Graft This somewhat useless spell will allow you to speak through your pet. It is great for toying around with(especially when you are invisible) however it does not have much use.
Word of Shadows AoE DD spell centered on caster.   Like Word of Pain Cleric spell.

Level 20
Allure Of Death This spell transforms the caster into a skeleton. It is a -hp and +mana spell.
Animate Dead This spell will summon a level 20ish pet.
Dominate Undead Dominate undead is a spell that can be used to charm undead monsters and use them as pets.
Expulse Undead Expulse undead is an upgrade to the ward undead spell. It not only does more damage but is also more mana efficient.
Harm Shield Harm shield grants the caster a brief period of invulnerability.
Identify This spell identifies magical items. I really do not see much use for it in this game.
Siphon Life Siphon life is another upgrade to the lifetap line of spells which drains a monsters hit points and gives them to the caster.
Word Of Shadow This is an area effect damage spell that centers around the caster.

Level 24
Breath Of The Dead This is a water breathing spell.
Haunting Corpse Haunting corpse is the level 24 upgrade to your pet summoning spells.
Intensify Dead This spell allows your pet to dish out more damage and it also acts as a regeneration spell on your pet.
Leatherskin Leatherskin is an upgrade to the shieldskin spell. It absorbs more damage.
Major Shielding This is an upgrade to the minor->lesser->shielding line of spells.
Resist Cold This spell will add to your resistance to cold.
Rest The Dead Upgrade to the Numb Dead spell.
Shadow Sight Shadow sight grants ultravision to the caster.
Shock Of Poison This spell does a bit of direct damage and also poisons your victim.

Level 29
Blood Boil This spell is an upgrade to the heat blood spell. It can be stacked with darkness spells for maximum damage over time.
Dismiss Undead
 This is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It is a high mana high efficiency spell which is very useful versus undead foes.
Dooming Darkness Dooming darkness is an upgrade to the clinging darkness->engulfing darkness line of spells. It does damage over time.
Panic The Dead Panic The Dead is an upgrade to the spook undead spell. It has a longer duration and will work more on higher level foes.
Renew Bones Anyone who has information on this spell please mail me.
Spirit Tap This is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells.
Summon Dead Summon dead is the level 29 pet summoning spell.
Vampiric Wave This is an area effect spell similar to vampiric embrace.
Level 34
Beguile Undead This is an upgrade to the charm undead spell.
Call Of Bones Turns caster into a Skeleton.
Greater Shielding Greater shielding is an upgrade to the lesser->minor->shielding->major shielding line of spells.
Invoke Fear Invoke fear is an upgrade to the fear spell. It has a longer duration.
Invoke Shadow This is the level 34 summoning spell.
Resist Disease This is an upgrade to the endure disease spell. It adds more resistance.
Root Root will prevent an NPC from running.
Steelskin Steelskin is an upgrade to the leatherskin spell. It absorbs damage.
Surge Of Enfeeblement This is an AE weaken spell.
Venom Of The Snake This is a poison spell which will do damage over time.
Level 39
Augment Dead Anyone who has information on this spell please mail me.
Counteract Disease This is an upgrade to the cure disease spell. It is more effective with high level poisons.
Drain Spirit This is an upgrade to the Spirit Tap spell.
Expel Undead Expel undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse->dismiss undead line of spells. It does DD to undead foes.
Malignant Dead This is the level 39 summoning spell.
Nullify Magic Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.
Scourge This is a disease attack.
Word Of Souls Word of souls is an area effect spell which centers around the caster.
Level 44
Arch Shielding Arch shielding is the final upgrade to the shielding spells.
Beguile Beguile is a more powerful version of the charm spell.
Crackling Bones Crackling bones is the level 44 pet summoning spell.
Dead Man Walking This spell is an upgrade to the level 20 allure of death spell. It will turn you into skeletal form.
Diamondskin This is the final upgrade to the shieldskin->leatherskin line of spells. It absorbs damage that would otherwise hurt the caster.

Level 49
Banish Undead This is the last in the line of the ward->expulse->dismiss->expel undead line of spells.
Cajole Undead Cajole undead is a high level undead charming spell.
Cascading Darkness Cascading darkness is an upgrade to the clinging->engulfing darkness line of spells. It does damage over time.
Drain Soul Drain soul is an upgrade to the lifetap line of spells.
Expel Summoned Expel summoned will do good damage to summoned beasts such as elements.
Invoke Death This is the level 49 summoning spell.
Lifetap AEIV This is an area effect lifetap spell. Since there are no level 49 necromancers in the game we can only ponder how nice it must be.
