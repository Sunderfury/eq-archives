Enchanter Spells
Enchanter animations: While these are like pets in that they defend the caster, they cannot take /pet commands the way other caster's pets do. Charmed beasts however will respond normally to /pet commands.

Spell Description

Level 1
Illusion: Half Elf     This spell will allow you to appear as if you were a half elf to others. It will even effect your appearance if they do a /who on you. Monsters and NPC's will respond to you as if you were a half elf. The caster also receives special advantages of that particular class such as infravision.
Lull     This is the first in the line of spells which are aimed to calm your foe. This spell can be used to lower your opponents rage and(hopefully) prevent him from attacking you.
Minor Shielding     This is the first in the line of the wizards shielding spells. This spell adds armor class and hit points. It can be cast on yourself only.
Pendril's Animation     This spell will summon an illusionary pet that can is similar to the pets of a magician or necromancer only less powerful.
Sense Magic     This spell will give you information on magic items.
Shallow Breath     This spell not only damages your opponent but also lowers his strength. It does damage over time.
Strengthen     This buffing spell allows you to add to the strength of yourself or another player.
Taper Enchantment     This allows you to break out of or shorten the duration of an enchantment. Meaning that if you are charmed this spell might allow you to break free before the spell would normally expire.
Weaken     This spell can be used to weaken(reduce the strength) of your target.

Level 4
Color Flux     This is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster.
Enchantment Of Light     This spell will cause an item to glow.
Enfeeblement     This spell will lower your opponents strength.
Fear     Fear will cause your opponent to turn and run away at full speed. Be warned however that he will return when the spell wears off and he may bring friends.
Gate     The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.
Haze     This is an armor class buffing spell.
Illusion: Human     This spell will make you appear to be a human(if you are not already). It will also cause NPC's to respond to you as if you were a human.
Invisibility     Invisibility is a very important spell. This allows you to get into areas that you normally could not get into and also allows you to skip by monsters you might otherwise have to fight. Beware however that this spells duration varies and it has a way of wearing off at the wrong time. Some monsters can also see invisible creatures and will see through this spell.
Mesmerize     Mesmerize is the enchanters version of the calm spell. This spell will cause a monster to begin mesmerized and not attack people that it would normally attack.
Suffocating Sphere     Suffocating Sphere is does a bit of damage and also lowers the victims strength.
Tashan     This spell will lower your targets magic resistance.

Level 8
Alliance     This spell will raise your faction will the particular NPC for the duration of the spell.
Bind Sight     This spell will allow you to bind your sight to another being and see through their eyes. The caster will slowly lose stamina while using this spell.
Cancel Magic     This spell will cancel a magical effect which is bothering you or one of your party members.
Chaotic Feedback     Chaotic feedback is a direct damage offensive spell.
Enchant Silver     This spell is used by those who are interested in jewelcrafting. The enchant metals spells can be used at higher skill levels to create magical jewelry.
Eye Of Confusion     This spell will make your target run around aimlessly for up to thirty seconds. It is similar to fear except that they will run in more than one direction.
Illusion: Gnome, Wood Elf     These spells allow you to appear to be a gnome or a wood elf. Other races will treat you as if you are a member of the race they are part of.
Lesser Shielding     Lesser shielding is an upgrade to the minor shielding spell. It adds armor class and hit points.
Mircyl's Animation     Mircyl's animation is an upgrade to Pendril's animation. It summons a more powerful illusionary pet.
Root     This spell will root a being in one place not allowing them to escape.
See Invisible     This spell allows you to see invisible beings.
Sentinel     This spell creates a sphere around the caster and will alert you whenever another being penetrates it.
Soothe     Soothe is an upgrade to the lull spell. It makes a monster less aggressive.

Level 12
Bind Affinity     This allows you to change your bind location(the location where you will be returned if you gate or die).
Charm     This spell allows you to charm another being and use it as a pet. Be careful with charm spells as the duration is very unpredictable and your former pet will not be happy about the way you have been using him.
Choke     This is a damage over time spell which also lowers your opponents strength.
Ebbing Strength     Ebbing strength is a debuff spell which can be used to weaken your opponents.
Enchantment Of Brilliance     This spell will cause an item to glow.
Enduring Breath     Enduring breath is a water breathing spell which can be cast on yourself or others.
Illusion: Dark Elf, Erudite, Halfling     More illusions, same rules apply as with their predecessors.
Languid Pace     This spell will cause your target to attack more slowly.
Memory Blur     Memory blur can be useful for getting rid of aggro NPC's. For example if you had to run from a monster and you have escaped you could then cast this spell to keep him from attacking you next time he sees you(without having to zone).
Mist     Mist is an upgrade to the haze line of armor class buffing spells.
Serpent Sight     Serpent sight grants you infravision.
Whirl Til You Hurl     This spell will root a monster and cause it to spin in place(which makes it difficult for him to land blows).

Level 16
Chase The Moon     Chase the moon is a fear spell which will cause your opponent to tuck tail and run.
Disempower     Disempower is a great debuffing spell which will lower your targets strength, armor class and drain his stamina.
Enchant Electrum     This is an upgrade to the enchant silver spell.
Enthrall     This is an upgrade to the mesmerize spell. It acts as a calming effect on your target.
Levitate     Levitate allows you to levitate above ground. This is useful for travelling over bodies of water or lava. It can also be used to prevent you from being knocked off of ledges.
Identify     This spell identifies a magical item.
Illusion: Barbarian, Dwarf, Tree     More illusion spells.
Mesmerization     Mesmerization is an area effect mesmerize spell.
Quickness     This spell adds to a players attack speed.
Rune I     Rune spells are among the most useful enchanter spells in a group. These spells can be cast on yourself or others and allow a player to absorb a certain amount of damage without actually being hurt. It requires a component.
Sanity Warp     Sanity warp is a direct damage spell.
Shielding     This is an upgrade to the minor->lesser shielding spells. It adds armor class, hit points and magic resistance to yourself only.

Level 20
Berserker Strength     This spell adds to your targets strength, but lowers their agility.
Calm     Calm is an upgrade to the lull and soothe spells. It lowers your targets aggressiveness
Cloud     Cloud is an upgrade to the haze->mist line of armor class buffing spells.
Color Shift     Color shift is an area effect stun spell which centers around the caster.
Endure Magic     This spell adds to a players magic resistance.
Feckless Might     This spell is a strength debuff which will drain a monsters strength.
Illusion: Ogre, Troll     More illusions. These are among the most useful for non-evil races. The troll illusion is also particularly useful since it grants the casters regeneration.
Shifting Sight     This is an upgrade to the bind sight spell. It allows the caster to see through the target's eyes using infravision in this case however.
Sympathetic Aura     Sympathetic aura adds to a players charisma ability.

Level 24

Beguile     Beguile is an upgrade to the charm spell.
Chaos Flux     This is a direct damage offensive spell.
Enchant Gold     Enchant Gold is an upgrade to Enchant Electrum
Illusion: Earth Element, Skeleton     Yet more illusions. These ones are a bit different in that they allow you to appear to be a monster.
Invigor     Invigor will restore a players stamina.
Major Shielding     This is an upgrade to the shielding line of spells.
Rune II     Rune II is an upgrade to Rune I(wow really?). It absorbs more damage than Rune and requires a more expensive component.
Strip Enchantment     This spell will allow you to nullify all magic effects cast on yourself in one fell swoop. It will remove one or two spells cast on another party member.
Tepid Deeds     This is an upgrade to the languid pace spell. It will slow your opponent.

Ultravision     This spell allows you to grant ultravision.
