This is also the first level in which you are able to summon your pets. From this point on you will receive four new pet spells every circle. Pet's differ in a couple of ways. Fire elements for example are not great warriors by any means but every time a monster hits them it will damage the monster due to their fire shield. Air elements are invisible, Earth Elementals have the most hit points but are slow,etc. You may also give your pets weapons to use, just hand it to them like you would any NPC.  Once given the weapon goes away with the pet.
