Druid Spells
Spell Names Mana Reagent Spell Description

Level 1
Burst Of Flame     Burst of flame is the first direct damage spell that a magician receives. It is a low damage spell but will have to do until you make level 4.
Dance Of The Fireflies     This is a light spell which only works in outdoor zones.
Endure Fire     This spell adds to a players resistance to fire.
Flame Lick     This spell is used to lower an opponents armor class, making him more succeptible.
Lull Animal     his spell will can cause animals that are normally agressive to not attack if succesful.
Minor Healing     Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.
Panic Animal     This spell will cause an animal to run away in a panicked state. Beware that after this spell wears off(approximately 30 seconds) the animal will return.
Sense Animal     This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest animal.
Skin Like Wood     This is the first in the line of druid buffing spells. It adds to both the armor class and hit points of a player.
Snare     Snare is sort of a limited root spell. While it does not hold a monster perfectly still(and prevent it from fleeing) it does drastically lower it's movement rate which accomplishes nearly the same effect.
Level 5
Burst Of Fire     Burst of fire is a direct damage spell. Basically it is an upgrade to Burst Of Flame.
Camoflauge     This is an outdoor only invisibility spell.
Cure Disease     This spell can be used to cure disease.
Cure Poison     This spell will counteract poisons.
Gate     The gate spell will teleport you home to your bind point.
Grasping Roots     Grasping Roots is an upgrade to snare. This spell actually roots the monster in place(instead of slowing him down) and also does a small amount of damage for the duration.
Invoke Lightning     Invoke lightning is an outdoor only area effect spell.
Whirling Wind     Whirling wind is a direct damage spell. While not as mana efficient as Burst of Fire it does more damage(and uses more mana). For that extra bit of mana you gain a short stun effect.
Winged Courie     This spell allows you to send an item to a friend who is in a far away place.
Level 9
Endure Cold     This spell will increase your resistance to cold based attacks.
Enduring Breath     Enduring breath is a water breathing spell.
Firefist     This spell will add damage for your offensive blows.
Ignite     Ignite is a fire based direct damage spell.
Invisibility vs. Animals     This spell will render you invisible to animals. It is not very useful as you will usually prefer to use camo or superior camo instead.
Light Healing     Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.
Shield Of Thistles     This spell is a damage shield. It does more damage than thistecoat but has a much shorter duration.
Starshine     Starshine is an outdoor light spell.
Strength Of Earth     This spell will add to a players strength ability score.
Thistlecoat     This spell is a minor damage shield which also adds armor class.
Treeform     Treeform will allow you to take the shape of a tree. It is really a rather useless spell.
Whirling Wind     Whirling wind is a direct damage spell which does an extra amout of damage to flying beasts.
Level 14
Befriend Animals     Befriend animals is a charming spell which will work on animals only.
Bind Affinity     This spell is used to change a players bind point. This is the point where a player will return to if they die or gate.
Cascade Of Hail     This is an outdoor rain spell.
Expulse Summoned     Expulse summoned is an update to the ward summoned line of spells which does direct damage to elements.
Halo Of Light     This spell summons a magical headband which casts light as a greater lightstone.
Invigor     Invigor will return a players stamina much like a heal spell returns health.
Levitate     This spell will allow a character to levitate. It is useful for travelling over water or lava and in situations where you are afraid you might be knocked off of a ledge.
See Invisible     This spell will allow you to see invisible beings.
Skin Like Rock     Skin like rock is an update to the skin like wood spell. It adds armor class and hit points.
Spirit Of The Wolf     This is one of the most useful travel spells in the game. It allows you to run at double speed in outdoor zones which is great for escaping attack or for reducing travel time.
Stinging Swarm     This spell summons a swarm beings that will do damage over time to your target. It is an outdoor only spell.
Summon Drink     Summons some water. This spell isn't really useful since you have the forage skill.
Summon Food     Summons some food. As with summon drink it's not really useful to a druid.
Level 19
Barbcoat     This spell is an upgrade to thistlecoat. It adds armor class and a small damage shield.
Calm Animal     Calm animal will calm animals and lower their agressivenes.
Cancel Magic     This spell will cancel a magic effect cast on you or one of your party members. It is useful if someone is rooted, charmed or has a nasty DoT spell cast on them.
Careless Lightning     This is an outdoor only offensive spell.
Dizzying Wind     This outdoor only spell will do extra damage to flying beasts. It is an upgrade to the whirling wind spell.
Endure Disease     This spell adds to your resistance to disease.
Endure Poison     This spell adds to your resistance to poison.
Feral Spirit     Feral spirit is a buffing spell which can be used on your pet(a befriended animal perhaps). It will make them much more formidable.
Healing     Healing is the third in the line of healing spells. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.
Rings Of Butcher, Tox, Karana and Commons     These are the first of your personal travel spells. They will teleport you to one of the above locations
Shield Of Barbs     Shield of barbs is an upgrade to shield of thistles. It creates a damage shield.
Superior Camoflauge     This is an invisibility spell which works both indoor and outdoor.
Terrorize Animal     This spell is a fear spell that works only on animals.

Level 24
Charm Animals     This spell will charm animals and allow you to use them as pets.
Creeping Crud     This is an upgrade to the stinging swarm spell. It does damage over time.
Dismiss Summoned     Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the ward->expulse summoned line of spells.
Ensnaring Roots     This spell not only roots your opponent but also does damage while rooted.
Pogonip     This spell is a direct damage offensive spell.
Resist Fire     Adds to your resistance to fire based attacks
Skin Like Steel     Skin like steel is an upgrade to the skin like wood->rock line of spells. It adds armor class and hit points.
Spirit Of The Cheetah     This spell allows you to go into super fast sprint mode. It is much like spirit of the wolf only faster and with a shorter duration.
Sun Beam     This spell Blinds your opponent.
Tremor     Tremor is an area effect spell which has a large area of effect. In exchange for its effect radius it sacrifices mana efficiency
Wolf Form     This spell allows you to assume the form of a wolf. Some NPC's respond differently to wolves so it can be both a blessing and a curse. It is self only.

Level 29
Bramblecoat     This is an upgrade to the thistlecoat line of spells. It adds armor class and a damage shield.
Circle Of Butcher, Commons, Karana and Tox     The circle spells are group teleport spells.
Combust     DD damage spell does 174 damage.
Counteract Disease     This is an upgrade to the cure disease spell. It works on more powerful diseases.
Counteract Poison     This is an upgrade to the cure poison spell. It works on more powerful types of poison.
Ensnare     Ensnare is an upgrade to the snare line of spells which will root a mob in place for an increased amount of time.
Extinguish Fatigue     This spell will return a players lost stamina.
Greater Healing     Greater healing is the last in the line of druid healing spells. It heals roughly 270 damage for 150 mana.
Immolate     I'm not positive but I believe this is an area effect spell. Anyone with further info please mail me.
Ring Of Misty     This is a self teleport spell to misty thicket.
Shield Of Brambles     This is the level 29 damage shield.
Succor: East     Succor spells are emergency party escape spells. They cast faster than the normal teleport spells and can be used in emergency situations. The weakness is that someone might get left behind in rare instances.

Level 34
Beguile Animals     Beguile animals is an upgrade to the charm animal line of spells.
Circle Of Feerot, Lavastorm, Ro and Steamfont     The circle spells are group teleport spells.
Drones Of Doom     Drones of doom is an upgrade to the stinging swarm line of DoT spells.
Earthquake     Earthquake is an upgrade to tremor. It is a wide radius area effect spell.
Endure Magic     This spell adds to your resistance to magic.
Greater Wolf Form     This is an upgrade to wolf form it not only assumes the form of a wolf but gives special wolf like abilities. It can only be cast on yourself.
Lightning Strike     This is an outdoor only direct damage spell.
Regeneration     This spell allows a player to regenerate hit points. It is a nice spell for reducing downtime while you meditate.
Resist Cold     Resist cold adds to a players resistance to cold based attacks.
Strength Of Stone     This is an upgrade to the strength of earth line of spells.
Succor: Butcher     The butcherblock escape spell.

Level 39
Avalanche     Avalanche is an outdoor only area effect spell.
Circle Of Misty     Group teleportation to misty thicket.
Enveloping Roots     This spell not only roots a player but also does some damage in the process.
Firestrike     This is a fire based direct damage spell.
Pack Regeneration     Pack regeneration will cast regeneration on your entire party. It is useful in battles where several players take good damage.
Pack Spirit     Pack spirit is a group spirit of the wolf spell.
Share Wolf Form     Shared Wolf form.  Whole grop gets wolf form.
Shield Of Spikes     Shield of spikes is the next in the line of damage shield spells.
Skin Like Diamond     This is an upgrade to the skin like wood line of spells which adds armor class and hit points.
Spikecoat     Spikecoat is an upgrade to the thistlecoat line of spells which adds armor class and a small damage shield.
Succor: Ro     The escape spell for Ro desert.

Level 44
Allure Of The Wild     This is the last in the line of charm animal spells.
Banish Summoned     Banish summoned is the last in the line of direct damage spells to be used versus summoned foes.
Chloroplast     Chloroplast is an upgrade to the regeneration spells which regenerate at a higher rate.
Drifting Death     This is the last in the line of damage over time spells. It is an upgrade to stinging swarm and drones of death.
Form Of The Great Wolf     This allows you to cast greater wolf form on others.
Nullify Magic     Nullify magic is an upgrade to the cancel magic spell.
Resist Disease     Adds to your resistance to disease.
Resist Poison     Adds to your resistance of poison.
Savage Spirit     I'm not really certain what this spell does. I believe it is a pet buffing spell.
Storm Strength     This is the last in the line of druid strength spells.
Succor: Lavastorm     The escape spell to lavastorm.

Level 49
Engulfing Roots     This is the last in the line of ensnaring root type spells which both root and do damage
Fire     Fire based direct damage spell.
Ice     Ice based direct damage spell.
Lightning Blast     This is the last in the line of lightning based attacks. These spells are outdoor only.
Resist Magic     This adds to your resistance to magic.
Shield Of Thorns     This is the last in the line of damage shields.
Skin Like Nature     Skin like nature is an upgrade to the skin like wood line of spells. It adds to your armor class and hit points.
Starfire     I am not positive on this one but I believe it is a druids most powerful direct damage spell. Anyone with further information please mail me.
Succor: North     This is the escape spell which will teleport your group to everfrost.
Thorncoat     Thorncoat adds both armor class and a light damage shield.
