Ranger Spells
Spell Name Mana Reagent Spell Description

Level 9
Endure Fire     This spell adds to a players resistance to fire.
Flame Lick     Flame lick is a DoT spell that reduces AC by 2 and does 10 points over time.
Glimpse     This spell lets you take a brief glimpse at objects far away. Basically it gives you a 15-20 second period where your sight is zoomed in to the distance.
Lull Animal     This spell will can cause animals that are normally agressive to not attack if succesful.
Minor Healing     Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.
Skin Like Wood     This is the first in the line of buffing spells. It adds to both the armor class and hit points of a player.
Snare     Snare is sort of a limited root spell. While it does not hold a monster perfectly still(and prevent it from fleeing) it does drastically lower it's movement rate which accomplishes nearly the same effect.

Level 15
Burst Of Fire

15

   This is a direct damage spell. Does 15pts of Damage
Camoflauge     This is an outdoor only invisibility spell.   Has a random time limit
Cure Poison     This spell will counteract poisons.
Feet Like Cat

40

   This spell will raise a players agility score.
Grasping Roots     Grasping Roots is an upgrade to snare. This spell actually roots the monster in place(instead of slowing him down) and also does a small amount of damage for the duration.
Invoke Lightning     This is an area effect spell which centers around your target. Beware however it will also damage you if you are within range. This spell is outdoor only. Does 25pts of Damage.
Thistle Coat     This spell creates a mild damage shield.

Level 22
Bind Sight     This very interesting spell allows you to bind your sight to your target and view from their perspective.  Eats up Stamina while in use.
Enduring Breath     Enduring breath is a water breathing spell. This is important if you wish to go to some of the underwater locations in the game. It is also useful if you get rooted while underwater.
Harmony     Is an AoE Lull Animal spell..
Ignite     Ignite is a direct damage spell.  Does 37pts of damage.
Light Healing     This is the second in the line of healing spells. It heals roughly 25 points of damage.
Skin Like Rock

60

   This is the second in the skin series. It may not be stacked with others in the line(see skin like rock). It adds armor class and hit points
Ward Summoned     This spell is a direct damage spell for use versus summmoned monsters(elements). It has a very good damage:mana efficiency compared to your other direct damage spells.

Level 30
Barb Coat     This spell adds armor class as well as a light damage shield.
Eye of the Cat     Unknown
Cancel Magic     This spell will cancel out a magic effect on you or another party member. It can be a life saver if someone in your group get's charmed or has one of those particularly nasty Damage over Time spells cast on them.
Invigore     Invigore will restore a characters stamina.
Shield Of Thistles     Shield of thistles is a damage shield.
Stinging Swarm     Stinging swarm is a damage over time spell. This is a great spell to use during combat as it will slowly eat away at your opponents hit points.
Strength Of Earth     This spell will add to a characters strength ability for a period of time.

Level 39
Calm Animal     This spell will prevent an animal from attacking your party by lowering it's agressiveness.
Careless Lightning     Careless lightning is an upgrade to invoke lightning. It is an area effect damage spell which centers around the target.
Dismiss Summoned     Dismiss summoned is an upgrade to the level 22 ward summoned spell. This spell does a good amount of damage to summoned monsters and also features the best damage:mana ratio of any ranger spell.
Heal     This is the last heal spell that rangers will ever receive. It heals roughly 80 points of damage.
Levitate     Levitate allows you to levitate above ground. This can be useful if you are on a thin ledge and the mobs are casting spells which knock you off balance(particularly useful in  lower guk).. It can also be useful in places where there is a lot of lava or for quickly travelling over bodies of water.
Skin Like Steel     Skin like steel is the third in the skin like series of spells which add armor class and hit points.
Spirit Of The Wolf     Spirit of the wolf is one of the most desired spells in the game. This spell allows you to travel quickly in outdoor zones. It also is used in conjunction with Damage over Time spells in a cheesy tactic known as kiting where you outrun your opponent while the DoT spells slowly eat away at his hit points.

Level 49
Ensnaring Roots     This spell is an upgrade to the level 15 grasping roots spell. This spell does a decent amount of damage as well as rooting your prey.
Resist Fire     Resist fire is an upgrade to the level 9 endure fire. It grants greater resistance to fire based attacks.
Shield Of Barbs     This is an upgrade to the shield of thistles spell. It is a higher level damage shield.
Superior Camoflauge     Superior camoflauge is an upgrade to camoflauge. While they both render you invisible this one works indoors as well as outdoors.
Wolf Form     This spell allows you to take the form of a wolf also taking on some of it's characteristics. Most important being that NPC's and monsters will react to you as if you were a wolf possibly preventing you from being attacked by certain mobs.
