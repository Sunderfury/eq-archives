Paladin Spells
Spell Name Mana Reagent Spell Description

Level 9
Courage     Courage the first in a line of buffing spells for the cleric class. This line of spells adds both hit points and armor class and can be cast on yourself and on others.
Cure Poison     This spell will counteract poisons.
Flash Of Light     This spell will temporarily blind your opponent.
Minor Healing     Minor healing is the first in the line of healing spells.
Sense Magic     This spell will give you information on magic items.
Spook The Dead     This is an undead fear spell. When can upon the undead it will cause them to turn and run away. This can be a useful spell when used in conjunction with lull as you can lull one of the undead to prevent him from attacking you and spook the other one away from the area and get a chance to fight it one on one.
Yalup     This spell endows the caster a brief surge of strength which not only adds to the casters strength but also to his armor class. The duration is very short but the spell also takes very little mana.

Level 15
Cure Disease     This spell can be used to cure disease.
Hammer of Wrath     This spell summons a dmg 6 delay 32 magical warhammer that can only be used by the player casting the spell and will dissapear when you log out or die.
Holy Armor     Holy armor is the first in the line of armor spells. This spell adds additional armor class to a player.
Light Healing     Light healing is the second in the line of healing spells.
Lull     This is the first in the line of spells which are aimed to calm your foe. This spell can be used to lower your opponents rage and(hopefully) prevent him from attacking you.
Sense The Dead     This spell will point you in the direction of the nearest undead monster.
Ward Undead     Ward undead is the first in the line of direct damage undead ward spells. These spells only affect undead. Ward spells are important because at the level which you attain these spells they are the most efficient direct damage spells in the game at this level.

Level 22
Center     This is an upgrade to the courage spell. It will not stack with courage but can be stacked with the holy armor and the symbol of transal line of spells. It grants +ac and +hp.
Endure Poison     This spell will increase your resistance to poison as well as curing it.
Invigore     Invigore will restore a players stamina.
Invisibility To Undead     This spell renders you invisible to undead mobs.
Reckless Strength     Reckless strength gives a burst of strength which slowly dies out.
Root     This spell will hold your opponent in one place. It serves two different purposes. The first is to keep a monster from running so your party can finish it off. The second is that it prevents the monster from getting near you, this is very important if you plan on soloing at higher levels.

Level 30
Expulse Undead     Expulse undead is an upgrade to ward undead. It has a bit better damage to mana ratio and also does more damage
Healing     Healing is the third in the line of heal spells. It heals around 80 points of damage and is more mana efficient than it's predecessors.
Soothe     Soothe is an upgrade to lull. It will reduce a monsters aggro range and perhaps prevent it from attacking you.
Spirit Armor     Spirit armor is an upgrade to holy armor. This spell gives an armor class bonus.
Symbol Of Transal     Symbol of transal is the third line of buffing spells that clerics and paladins get. It requires a component and grants hit points only. However when considering that this line can be stacked with the holy armor and the courage line of spells it is very useful in sticky situations.

Level 39
Cancel Magic     This will allow you to cancel a magical effect on you or one of your party members. This can be a life saver if one of your party members is charmed or rooted in a sticky situation.
Daring     Daring is an upgrade to the courage->center line of spells. It grants armor class and hit points.
Endure Disease     This will increase a characters resistance to disease.
Yalup 2     This is an upgrade to the yalup spell. It grants additional strength and armor class.

Level 49
Bravery     Bravery is an upgrade to the courage->center->daring line of spells. It grants additional armor class and hit points.
Calm     Calm is an upgrade to the lull->soothe line of spells. This one is by far the most effective.
Dismiss Undead     Dismiss undead is an upgrade to the ward->expulse undead line of spells. It has a much better mana:dmg ratio and does quite a bit more damage than expulse.
Greater Heal     Greater heal is the fourth in the line of healing spells. This is the last in the line for all classes except for clerics. It heals close to 400 hit points of damage.
Holy Might     Holy might is an upgrade to the stun spell. It does a small amount of damage and momentarily stuns your opponent. This can be good against runners and casters.
