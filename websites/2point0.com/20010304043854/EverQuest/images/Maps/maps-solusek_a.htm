Solusek A

(by Maps)
 

Notes:
This dungeon has a HIGH risk of falling into the lava. If you fall into the stream of lava nearby the entrance you will die certainly. It is 48 damage (In the case of Sv Fire 25) in a second. Please, absolutely do not fall! Because it is easy to fall, you move ahead while squatting with the "D" key. If you are  SoWed, please dispell it. If you die in the lava, and have to recover your body, use the Levitate command to float above the lava safely.
1. Your corpse floats on the lava, if you use "corpse" command in the neighborhood of your corpse.
2. You can retake your equipment using the "loot" command (right-click your body).
3. But you CAN'T go east (the right of map), *even* if you use levitate spells. (Big Wall of lava is interfering)
4. So, you can escape from Lava stream By using Gate (If you are a Caster) or Group gate (Or Not caster) spells.
