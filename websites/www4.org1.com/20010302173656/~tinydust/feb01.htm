      Amber Gold Earring +3 str +3 int -3 charisma.
      Amber Platinum Ring +4 str +4 int -4 charisma
      Sapphire Gold Earring +2 str +2 int +2 dex
      Sapphire Platinum Necklace +6 str +6 int +2 dex
      Rudy Gold Ring +4 wis +2 str +3 stamina -3 agility
      Ruby Platinum Veil +7 str +7 wis +4 stamina -4 agility

      Bristlebane imbues(Peridot):
      silver: ring, AC 1, AGI 3, sv. cold 3
      electrum: bracelet, AC 2, AGI 3, sv cold 2
      gold: bracelet, AC 3, AGI 4, sv cold 3
      platinum: ring, AC 3, AGI 6, sv cold 5
