Membership Requirements...

I. Guild Nomination

A. The candidate must have achieved at least level 5

B. The candidate must be nominated by at least 2 permanent members in good standing with the Guild.

C. The candidate must fit all required criteria set forth in the Clachan Brigh Guild Charter. The candidate, once s/he has been told by 2 permanent members that they will be vouched for, should then contact an Officer.

 

II. Guild Trial Membership

A. All nominees can petition for Trial Membership to any Officer. All Officers play frequently and should be easy to contact.

B. The nominee will be asked questions at the Officer's discretion, relevant to game play and playing expectations.

C. The nominee, once vouched for by 2 members in good standing, may be approved by the Officer who he petitioned to. At this point the nominee is granted Trial Member Status, and will move to phase III.

 

III. Trial Guild Membership

A. The Trial Member will go through a 30-day trial period, during which they will be subject to Officer examination (at the discretion of any officer)

B. During the trial phase the new member will uphold all the articles of the Charter and will be subject to all the accountability of a permanent member. This includes, but not limited to, paying dues and participating in weekly Guild events.

C. After the 30 day trial period is successfully completed, the person is a full member of the family which is Clachan Brigh.....

 

Welcome To Clachan Brigh

If the candidate fails to meet or exceed the above requirements at any time during the qualification process, s/he will be dismissed from the process and no further consideration for that person will be fielded.
