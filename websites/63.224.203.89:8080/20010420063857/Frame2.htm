Welcome Friend...

 

Clachan Brigh taken from the old Gaelic tongue translated literally means Group Power. In this single meaning is the essence of why our guild exists. We fostered this belief from before we became an entity on Bristelbane, we embody our purpose daily and will continue to drive our cause forward in the future.

We are not a collection of persons whose goal is to become the largest guild on Norrath. Nor is our goal to have the most advanced characters. Our goal is to have the most fun. We recognize that it is a lot more fun to adventure with friends and to be successful than it is to go solo and die frequently. We intend to aggressively grow our characters by adventuring together so that the cause of good over evil may be successfully forwarded in Norrath.

We intend to be a small but extremely powerful group. It is our vision that every member of the Guild know every other member well. We do not desire “dead weight” members. We do not intend to solicit mass membership or to encourage every new player we meet to join our guild. It is our intention to “speak so loudly with our actions” that people scarcely hear our words. Of course, what words they do hear from us should always be fair and just. We intend that when people see a group of Clachan Brigh that they think to themselves “there goes the tightest, best organized, most together group on Bristelbane they are also fair, just and noble.”
