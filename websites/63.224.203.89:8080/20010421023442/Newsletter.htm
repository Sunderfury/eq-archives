A note on things Military by Moos Alini, Capitan of Clachan Brigh...

First, regarding the securing of unique quest items.

After a discussion with the Officers of the Guild, I can state that we are in unanimous agreement that the policy of the Guild is that Guild funds will not be used in any way to equip individual members with unique items. So, do not ask us to ante up 500pp to buy you that Beholder Staff or loan you the 50pp you need to buy a SBH. It's not going to happen.

However, the Guild does want to encourage and support members acquiring these type of items. How we will do this is as follows: a given member (we'll him Bob) wants a unique item (Axe of Whoopass). What Bob must do first is some research. Bob should find out where the Axe of Whoopass is to be located, on what critter, what other fights must be fought to win the item etc… Bob then sends me an e-mail (moosealini@hotmail.com) requesting assistance obtaining this device; this e-mail should include the reason he desires this item and all he knows of it, including what times he would be available to get the item.

Reasons that I may judge Bob's submission to be invalid include pure monetary or twinking value (eg - wants to acquire the item to turn around and sell it, or he wants it for his "second character") or that the information provided is somehow lacking (eg - it's a cut & paste from EQ'Lizer). I will reject the request and annotate my reasons for doing so.

If, on the other hand, the request is found to be valid then I will set a time and day for a hunting party to be formed. Bob must plan on being a part of this party. We will not go fetch items for people who are so low a level that they dare not even be in a party acquiring the item. In a party we will then acquire this item if it is to be had.

Second, regarding guild hunting parties.

It is my intention to have weekly organized hunting parties. The days on which these events occur will vary somewhat from week to week. This is to accommodate peoples' varied schedules in the hope that if a member or potential recruit can't make one week's event then perhaps the scheduling the next week will be more accommodating. These events will occur on either Thursday evening, Friday evening, Saturday morning or Sunday afternoon, on a week by week basis.

The location of these hunting parties will also be random. One week we may have a "Clachan Brigh takes over Crushbone" theme, the next "Clachan Brigh has a mad drunk brawl in the Arena" or whatnot.

Some of the purposes of these events: First, this provides us an opportunity to have fun together. Second, these events give us the opportunity to make a strong showing in the zone on which they occur. Hence, giving other players an opportunity to see our guild as strong and resolute, perhaps even inspiring membership inquiries. Third, all persons considering applying for guild membership would be welcome. This gives us good opportunity to take the measure of a potential candidate and hopefully insight germane to membership concerns.

It is not a requirement at this time of a guild member to participate in these events. However, it is highly encouraged and if you are unable to participate ever then it really raises the question of why are you in the guild in the first place?

Third, the guild takes seriously the responsibility of helping members recover their corpses.

This does not mean that members should overdose on stupid pills and go adventuring in the basement of Unrest at level 15 just because they wonder if the Hag Sisters really do have a torture chamber down there. (Trust me, they do.) Your corpse should be in a position that, with reasonable effort, a decently high level person or party should be able to help you retrieve it. Your corpse should not be in a place that only a party of 6 level 50s have a prayer of getting it. Having said that, if you need help with corpse recovery please say so through /guildsay or make arrangements with me through e-mail. I will always give corpse recovery missions top priority.

Many regard,

Moos
Capitan and Drinking Champion of Clachan Brigh.


Monetary Matters and our Guild by Seliris, the keeper of the coin…


The time has come for our Officers to draw up some rules and regs to make us the well oiled machine we can be. I intend to speak to you of money, and how we can all benefit. Sit a bit, rest your bones and have a mead with me.
Over time, we will be growing, both in numbers and in legend, but to do this we need some funds… a base to work off of per say. We have come up with a plan to do this, to fill our coffers, and to use this money to better us as a whole.

Here are our thoughts….

As you toil away and kill the many beasties that Norrath has poured out to stop you, you sell the trinkets, baubles, and crude weapons of your prey. This adds up.. and armour and weapons do not come cheap. We DO NOT ask a large sum of your money. All we ask is a tithe comparable to your level.

Level Range Dues Required: (weekly)
1-9 None.. Save up, you need it
10-19 1pp
20-29 2pp
30-39 3pp
40-49 4pp
50+ 5pp


As you can see, this isn't blood we are asking for, just a few coins a day.

Now for the questions and the answers:


What is this money going to be used for????

We spoke of many things when we first started this thread, many of which were fledgling ideas.. and Tunare knows we are open to new ones… but here are a few.
1. Low level tournaments and scavenger hunts. Cash prizes…
2. Corpse re equipping.. (ONLY used if TOTAL corpse loss and cannot be retrieved by GM)
3. Materials for our smiths/tailors/jewelers to create items FOR THE GUILD (ALL items MUST be pre approved. DO NOT buy 10 bars of gold and expect us to cash in on it without approval)

WE WILL NOT USE FUNDS FOR THE FOLLOWING:
1. Any item that we cannot retrieve by a quest. We are strong, and if a party cant get it, then by Tunare, you don't need it yet.
2. Equipment that you can easily save up and buy on yer own. (Yes, that LJ vest is spiffy, but save the cash and buy it, or ask for a loan from another player.)

Who do we pay this to?
Seliris is often on and will be keeping a ledger of this. Feel free to pay for any and all chars you have in the guild, for as many weeks in advance as you like… I am very mobile, and am often running across the globe at any given moment.

What if I don't pay?
Guild standing is partially dependant on this... if you do not follow the rules and be a team player, we really don't need you. This is for us as a whole... we look good... we grow... Clachan Brigh means "Group Strength".

Dues are due weekly, or monthly if you so choose. Please pay. We can all benefit from this.



Requirements for Clachan Brigh
guild membership
by Yewe, Guild Master
Listed below are the requirements for guild membership consideration


I Guild Nomination

A. The candidate must have achieved at least level 5
B. The candidate must be nominated by at least 2 permanent members in good standing with the Guild.
C. The candidate must fit all required criteria set forth in the Clachan Brigh guild charter. The candidate, once s/he has been told by 2 permanent members that they will be vouched for should then contact an Officer.


II Guild Trial Membership

A. All nominees can petition for Trial membership to any Officer (Yewe, Seliris, and Moos). The Officers all play quite a bit and should be easy to find.
B. The nominee will be asked questions at the Officer's discretion relevant to game play and playing expectations.
C. The nominee, once vouched for by 2 members in good standing, may be approved by the Officer who he petitioned to. At this point the nominee becomes a Trial Member Status, and will move to phase III.


III Trial Guild Membership

A. The Trial Member will go through a 30 day trial period during which they will be subject to Officer examination (at the discretion of any officers)
B. During the trial phase the new member will uphold all the articles of the charter and will be subject to all the accountability of a permanent member including paying dues and participating in weekly guild events.
C. After the 30 days is completed, the person is a full member of the family which is Clachan Brigh.

Welcome To Clachan Brigh

If the candidate fails to meet or exceed the above requirements at any time during the qualification process they will be dismissed from process and no further consideration for that person will be fielded
