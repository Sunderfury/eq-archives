EverQuest: General Resources

EQ Adventurer's Journal
A nice site following the life of a ranger and those he meets in his travels. Great place for RP'rs to check out.

EQ'Lizer's Beastiary
An excellent list of beasties listed by name with descriptions of level, type of attacks and where they can be found.

EQ Vault
Part of the Vault Network, the EverQuest support site is no less in quality than the other Vault sites. EQVault has the best daily news and most active message boards. Also, head honchos of Verant frequent the boards, and many consider EQVault their primary source of information.

EverEvent
An excellent place to find out when things are taking place on each server. A calendar for each is available.

Everlore
Yet another huge site devoted to EverQuest. Has an excellent map section. Other areas are being developed. Also has a decent list of quests.

EverQuest Center
Yet another general information site for EQ, with its own forums, player guides and other reference materials.

EverQuest Lexicon
A general resource site with a decent layout and helpful information, such as an armor and weapons guide and jewelry guide.

EverQuest Official Site
The official site from Sony, which commonly includes news updates and active forums.

EverQuest Stratics
EQ megasite at Stratics covers daily news, as well as a wealth of game information including spell guides, player guides, etc.

Molten Lava's Bestiary
Listed by zone (game area) are the creatures found there. Unfortunately, this site suffers from the dreaded 404 errors. However, there are a few guides in place, worth a quick stop when you can't find it elsewhere.
