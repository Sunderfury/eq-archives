Welcome to the Official site of our EverQuest™ guild.  We are the...

Wanderers of the Stoic

For those of you who are not familiar with the name, we are a very close-knit group of players on the Povar server.  We were formed from a small group of some of the most talented players on the server.  All of us were tired of finding ourselves in groups who had no idea how to play effectively, thus WoTs was formed.

Now, we all know that we can count on our guildmates to support us when we need their help.  If there is a zone that needs raiding, a quick call is all that is needed for our members to come running.  There is always something to do, as we are never sitting idle, waiting for something to happen, or someone to find the time to join their guild on the hunt.

"Be prepared to put into the guild what you expect to receive from it."

-Narya
