LOA Gathering Fever - Pennsylvania Gathering Announced!

With the Atlanta Gathering right around the corner, I figured it was a good time to spread the word about our Pennsylvania Gathering which is scheduled to take place during the last weekend of September. I will be organizing/hosting this event, so if you have questions -- please let me know (click my name below to email me).

During the PA gathering, we will be revisiting Dorney Park (Amusement Park) as well as the Celtic Classic event that is held each year. Should be interesting to see LOA members (and our friends, allies, etc.) participating in the "Timber Toss", "Axe Throwing" and other celtic-goodness events. For those of you who enjoy SPENDING, there will be TONS of good celtic-stuff to buy.

Also, we will be handing out the LOA Volume One Music CD, titled, "Music Transcending Worlds". A collection of songs that were submitted by our members from around the world. The songs are not "everyday, pop songs". Rather, they are rare or personally important to those who participated. For every member or alliance member that attends, we will be giving the CDs out, free of charge.

Hotel, directions, agenda, etc. will all be posted shortly after the ATLANTA GATHERING.

If you hope to attend this gathering, or simply want to discuss it with others, click here >>
