Note About LOA:

The Lost Order of Akalabeth, Founded in 1997 by Freejack, Brimstone, Ralineth, Ghoti, Brewman, Tasslehof, Zari, Carnifex and Avenger. Guild members are held to a very strict and high level of honor, maturity, and dedication to guild, members and other players.

We are currently active in the following games: Ultima Online, EverQuest, Asheron's Call

We only allow people to 'apply' for the games listed above. As of right now, we are temporarily 'closed' for the AC faction. We are hoping to open that faction up to the public on/about January 4th.

Other Info About LOA:

We plan to enter the following games as a unified guild: Middle Earth, Neverwinter Nights, Wheel of Time, Sovereign (Pending), Anarchy Online (Pending)

Alternate games we play: Homeworld, Unreal Tournament, Age of Empires I/II, Quake Series, Tribes, Rainbow Six (Rogue Spear), Worms Armageddon

Did you know?

- Richard Garriot, aka Lord British (uo), and Brad McQuaid of Everquest own "LOA Guild T-Shirts".  Richard Garriot once stated, "I consider the LOA shirt to be one of my top 10 favorites to wear!"

- LOA was the FIRST guild to ever offer REAL LIFE services in exchange for VIRTUAL gold in Ultima Online, and then later, in EverQuest. It all started when Brimstone designed graphics that many guilds and players seemed to like. With demand very high, Brim charged about 5 to 10k for logos, paid for on Atlantic. Business boomed, and soon, other players began to offer services (writing, graphics, cgi assistance, html) for in-game gold. The rest is history...

- In April 1999, Brimstone was awarded the Lord British Excellence in Community award (Ultima Online) for his work in promoting and assisting guilds, organizations, etc.

- Freejack, co-founder of the LOA, works for the company who created Wheel of Time (the game). Freejack has been working on the support, testing and in some cases, marketing of the game. LOA is very proud of Freejack, and we are eager to get involved in this game! - Brimstone
