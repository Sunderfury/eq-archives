walking forward looking back
by solf

I walk these roads.

My pack loaded on my back.

My staff aids my step, as I scan the horizon for signs of danger.

Strange how the mind can wander with such a mundane task as a long walk.

Voices come and go as my eyes glaze over, entering a state of daydream while I remain cognicent enough to continue my journey.

Voices from the past.. from different worlds, long ago.

A man named Brimstone, and Freejack.

Lochedahnan and Akira... A Keep.. my home.

A clearing... buildings... Santiago, Stubby, Broog, Deathwish Dragon.

Brewman, Hawkeye, Brigadier, and Kelsam.

Names and voices of the past come and go, washing over me like a cleansing rain.

Another world, another time... a more recent past.

There's a marble city... and more voices.. images.

Aanlain and Azlana.

Bagulan, Argil, and Waran...

Penaden, and Wryliak...

More voices of friends from long ago.

Too many to focus on.

With a start, I realise that I had not been paying attention to my steps, lost in the past.

I bring myself back to the present and focus on those I now call my friends.

Some have made the journeys with me from world to world.

Some are new.. I can hear their voices as well.

Enosh, Joxer, and Orion.

Tasslehof, Kileyan, and Gwenn.

I smile to myself as I approach my destination: The hamlet of Hebian-To, my home for now.

I feel very fortunate to have had such an existance, moving from world to world.

My life feels endless, and those I have called my friends have helped to shape who I am.

Some call me reclusive, or mysterious.

I have been called a lunatic, and a prophet.

I have been known as a saviour and as a doombringer.

I have had many names, some I've already forgotten.

Now they just call me the solf.

Less of a name, more of a word.

A word meaning what I have become over the years and through the worlds.

Perhaps I am Warped, a bit jaded from all I have seen and done.

I like to think I am enlightened, educated, and better prepared for the next turn in the road.

I suppose it is who you ask, my friends or my enemies, as to what is the meaning of my name.

I enter the center of town, and the locals avoid me like the plague.

They do not even know me, but they can tell that I am different.

Different for better or worse, I do not know, but I continue as I always have.

Waiting for the next fork in the road, I will do what I can in this world.

I will move forward, and I will succeed, knowing that I shall always have my brothers and sisters by my side.

I bow my head and silently thank all my friends, for helping to mold me into what I have become.

My friends, brothers, and sisters. Past, present, and future.

I look forward to see what the future holds.

It is good to be me.

-The Solf-
