A Note
by Calixy

Drachiero,

Would have joined you if I could. The warriors of my family have been watching me. *glances around nervously* Ever since the guards brought me home, battered and bloody claiming I fell, I am not allowed freedom without prying eyes upon me. Those guards beat me, knowing me for what I am. Luckily to hide their own duplicity they failed to disclose mine.

I have managed a bit of experience but not much. And I did swindle a fellow out of a whole plat! For five measly scraps of bone. Ah the gullible. Twas after that when the guards chanced upon me. But I kept my plat! Hid it right away and search as they might they could not find it.

I shall sneak out when you next wish to meet. Name the time here as I have seen conflicting notes passed about.

Seek the shadows friend, for you shall find me there.

*slips away, into the darkness*

Calixy
