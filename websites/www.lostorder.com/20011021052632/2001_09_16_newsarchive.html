mIRC RPGserv Script Available

If you are interested in joining us for Dungeons and Dragons, our kickoff date is September 30th, at 8:00 P.M. EDT. To play, as a character, you will need the dnd.txt file and a basic understanding of the Dungeons and Dragons game. If you are NOT familiar with the rules, but are curious as to what we do and how we do it, please show up, and the Dungeon Master can put you to work as a Non-Player Character (npc). Either way, it's lots of fun!

Please email me at stubby@lostorder.com and I will send you a copy of the D&D script (it's only 4k).

Thanks!

Mystical.net Chat Network Installs RPGServ
Fans of Dungeons and Dragons should consider joining the mystical.net chat network, for free. You can create a chat room and use RPGServ to "roll the dice". LOA has long participated in such things on other networks. Mystical.net is the new home for LOA and we are BEYOND happy to finally have a method on the chat servers to play D&D online.

Also worthy of note, Mystical.net offers a web-based chat client - perfect for chatting anywhere.
