Tuesday, September 10, 2002

Sign our GuestMap!

Many thanks to our allies, The Stormcrows, for bringing this wonderful GuestMap to our attention. The GuestMap allows members/friends of our community to show us, visually, where they live in the world. The GuestMap also allows those who post to share their nickname, guild name, web site URL, ICQ info and more. All in all, this is an application that we have wanted for well over 3 years -- and finally it falls into our laps. Very cool.

To sign the guest map, click here OR use the link found on the left of our site (see the menu).

Monday, September 09, 2002

Swords, Daggers and Knives!

The LOA PA Gathering is just weeks away. What would a gathering be without dangerous gifts to raffle off. That said, I found an online store that sells knives of all types, 50% to 60% cheaper than other stores (brick/mortar and online). I spent about 2 days researching various weapons - hoping to find one (or two) of the coolest I could find. I'm glad to report that I found them.

One of the "items" will be raffled at the LOA PA Gathering. People must be in attendance to 'win' and it costs $5.00 to enter, one raffle-ticket per member. The other "item" will then be available for our entire online community - members and non-members, alike. The raffle tickets will cost $5.00 (or more, if paid via paypal.com - $5.35), no limit to how many tickets are purchased. The winner will receive the "item" via mail, and the money earned will be deposited into our guild 'fund'.

If you are interested in buying a blade, head over to: www.knives-knives.com. The website could use a little work, but the prices are JUST RIGHT!
