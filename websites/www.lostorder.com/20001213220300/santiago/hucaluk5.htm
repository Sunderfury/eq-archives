The 'Death' of Hucaluk

"This what Hucaluk look like," said the troll captain, pointing to a crudely drawn picture on a kobold hide. "Hucaluk bad troll. He blow up little prisoner. You go find Hucaluk. You bash Hucaluk. Any questions?"

The trolls looked at each other in confusion.

"Um, captain? How we know who Hucaluk is? That troll look like any troll."

The captain glared. "You so stupid. This what you do. You go to swamp. You know where swamp is?"

The warrior nodded eagerly.

"Good. You go to swamp. You look for troll. You see troll, you ask if he Hucaluk. He say yes, you bash him. You know how to bash?"

The warrior nodded eagerly.

"Good. More stupid questions?"

The warrior raised his hand.

"What?" shouted the captain.

"What if he say no?" asked the warrior.

"What you name?"

"Me called Gugguk, sir."

"Gugguk, come here."

Gugguk did so, and the captain whacked him on the head with his club.

"Gugguk, go back to line."

"Yes, sir. Thank you, sir."

The captain scowled. "If troll say he not Hucaluk, he not Hucaluk. Why troll say he Hucaluk if he not Hucaluk?"

The trolls simply stared at the captain, dazzled by his logic.

"Why you all so stupid? You go, now. Bash Hucaluk. You no come back until you find him."

"Me like rat meat stew," said Hucaluk, sitting on a log by the fire. "Rat meat stew taste good."

"Hucaluk!" came a voice from behind him. Hucaluk jumped up in the air and screamed.

"Zulaan! You scare Hucaluk! What you want?"

"Me come to warn you," said Zulaan, stepping forward from the shadows. "Captain send warriors to find you. They find you, you dead meat."

"Me no do it! Little go boom! Not my fault!"

"Me know you not do it. But that not matter. Warriors still bash you."

"Why you help Hucaluk?"

"You help Zulaan once. Me return favor."

"What me do?"

"Me have idea. Me bash you on head."

"Me no like that idea."

"You no understand. Me bash you on head. But not hard. You sleep. Warriors find you. They think you dead. They go home. You wake up. You still alive. Good plan, yes?"

"No. But me no have better plan."

"Here they come," said Zulaan, raising his club. "You ready?"

"Yes. Not too hard!"

Zonk! World go dark.

"Guards!" shouted Zulaan. "Come here! Me find Hucaluk!"

The warriors ran over to look at the body on the ground.

"You sure that Hucaluk?"

"Yes! He say so before I bash him."

"Good. We go home now. We tell captain. But…what if he no believe us? Maybe we bring back Hucaluk head?"

"What captain say? He say bash Hucaluk. We bash Hucaluk. He no say bring back Hucaluk head. We not bring back Hucaluk head. Captain think you stupid. Captain hit you on head again."

"You right. We no bring back Hucaluk head. We go home."

The warriors trudged off through the marsh. Zulaan sat down by the fire and helped himself to some rat meat stew.
