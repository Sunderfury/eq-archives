Each year, LOA offers a special shirt design that features the names of each guild member, along with the year that the shirt was made. This design is available in colors other than white (i.e. sand, grey, white, yellow, blue, etc.) and usually costs a little more money due to shipping/handling costs, prep fees, etc. Because this design is more involved, we have a printing shop work on the silk-screening for us.
