The Meeting
by Hadana

*looks about her small home, thinking of this evenings meeting*

My son came to me this eve. Asked me if I was ready to meet his chosen girl. I nodded my readiness an off he went to get her. I rushed about straightening a bit here and there then sat down to compose myself.

My Hakadek, brought his Azyana to me. My impressions were favorable even though she is horribly disfigured without a beard. She looked somewhat strong and formidable and as if she is training to be a good cleric.

I hope she will join her heart with my son. Help him on his quest to clear his fathers name. I long to wear my husbands name proudly once again, and see my son in with it. Hopefully together they can unravel the mystery.

I feel that once she learns to cook and sew and builds up her strength she can bear me the grandbabes I long for. Though she did pale considerably at the thought of a mere 7 children. Good thing I did not mention that 9 would be an ideal number of children to make a family.

May they be quick and successful on their quest together. I hope to attend the wedding soon.

*puts the lovely necklace Azyana gave her away for safekeeping and retires to the bed*
