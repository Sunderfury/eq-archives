Overview of Module:

The "Akalabeth" mod is designed for many, many variations of gameplay. This involves all levels, classes and also the use of a DM, or no DM at all. We wanted to create a module that people could play 'out of the box' without worrying about having a DM available to 'lead them around'. As always, having a skilled DM or two is always the best case scenario.

Akalabeth contains dozens of areas, including a village (complete with tavern, shops, wilderness, etc.), several dungeons, a glistening glade, a housing area with many deep, dark secrets, the Plains of Adventure, the Southern Lands and of course, the Void. The adventure is non-linear, so anyone can head in any direction and find great things to do.

Inspiration for the various areas and features were provided by our guild members and friends, from past experiences, as well as current ones. Our guild has been through Ultima Online, EverQuest, Asheron's Call and now, Neverwinter Nights. Since 1996, we have shared many great times together and forged many wonderful tales of adventure. A little bit of everything found it's way into this module and we hope you can find some fun with what we created.

Lead Designer:

The lead designer on this project was "Kromeus", long-time member of the Lost Order of Akalabeth. Kromeus was the first one out of the gate when NWN hit the shelves. Within days, our guild had a working module to enjoy and expand upon. Kromeus enabled fellow members to contribute ideas, scripting, design and fully-created areas that would later be incorporated into the final mod we now have. In the end, this module is a project that was completed by several people in LOA.
