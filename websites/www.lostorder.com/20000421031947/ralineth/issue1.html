Editorial
Hail and good day to ye!

Welcome to the very first issue of the akalabethian, a journal for all members of the lost order to enjoy, detailing our lives within this glorious land of norrath. Before ye set forth I hath a couple of requests for ye. Firstly if ye feel ye hath something yer feel is fit to print then please send it via courier to the druid Ralineth Oakenheart - but a word of warning ... don't use the orc runners as they are notoriously unreliable, especially through the Butcherblock Mountains. Secondly, anything written here does not express the opinions of the editor, nor that of the Lost Order of Akalabeth.
Anyway read on...

 Influx of Druids in Kelethin
Reports from the Heartwood Master, the druidic master in Kelethin, indicate there has been a sharp increase in the number of young woodelves graduating from his basic forestry courses. At a loss to explain the sudden influx of students the elderly druid could only shrug and mutter something about jumping onto a bandwagon - perhaps he means that they are all dropouts from the bardic college.

Wanted: Rusty black boxes from rogue clockworks. Please return these to the Gem Choppers Hall in Ak'Anon

 Undead in a state of unrest
The unlucky Estate of Unrest has recently been the focus of attention for a large number of Akalabethians. In an effort to discover the lure of this place our resident wolf joined up with a bizarre combination of stumpy legs, long noses,  and a tree hugger. It appears the Estate is over run with the living dead, and is also in dire need of a good spring clean. After bashing a large number of these of creatures into dust, Woozle the long nose decided to bring a large group of ghouls to see us. Needless to say a certain wolf then spent some time recovering items from his corpse.
Ugly Ogre spotted in the Ocean of Tears
Over recent weeks there have been numerous reports by travelers across the ocean of tears of a large ogre wearing excessive amounts of bronze platemail wandering between the islands. What he is doing so far from the swamps remains unknown, and it has been reported that he is occasionally in the company of a renegade woodelf ranger. If any further information on these two miscreants becomes available we will let you all know.		 Conclave in Halas a success!
We recently caught up with Gradishar T'Alon, Lord of Assembly of the Norrathian LOA and discussed the Halas Conclave. Gradishar was pleased with the turnout, despite a no show from a number of inkies and other undesirables within the city itself. Pressed for further details Grad was rather unclear on what actually happened, explaining that the 15 bottles of dwarvern ale he consumed before the drunken goblin chase knocked him around a bit.		 Something Afoot?
We have no firm details yet, but by all reports there is something big brewing that will affect all us. My sources are unsure yet if this is another plot by that the Draconian Deity Veeshan to regain control of Norrath, or a threat from somewhere completely different. Rest assured readers that we let you know when we get more information on this.
