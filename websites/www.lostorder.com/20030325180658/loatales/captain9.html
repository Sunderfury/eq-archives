Captain's Log: Morningthaw 1, 11 P.Y.
by Enosh Flam

Captain's Log: Morningthaw 1

Another very busy and profitable two days fer me. Things were going very well fer me in Lytelthorpe. I've been making a good number of raids into the lost distillary, and it was proving to be very profitable. My skills with the bow are returning, slowly, but surely. I would have liked to stay in towne fer a few more days perhaps, but there was that little matter of Selfora. There was something that she wasn't tellin' me, though I found out the hard way. I managed to jump into the Rithwic portal just in time, and with a little help from the flat side of her husband's ax head. Lucky fer me it was the flat side or there might be two of me writin' this tale.

Still apart from me wounded pride, and sober demeaner, I decided to make the best of it. After all its been a month or so already and I was sure the cur at the Skull and Flagons has fergotten about me tab already. Well at least I would have fergotten, seems the cur behind the bar didn't. After I brushed the dirt from the road off me face and armor, I opted to head fer the Royal Firkin again. In me short time in Rithwic, Ricetha seemed more than friendly to an ole sailor anyway.

Still after gettin' shown the door twice today I wanted to go out and turn something into a pin cushion. Ricetha told be about the ole abandonded warehouse just south of town. I figured that was as good a place as any to bash some skulls in and releave some tension. She was right, the plae was right up me alley. The warehouse was full of those ilegidamate sons of an alley cat, drudges. They were everywhere. Sharpenin' their claws on all the discarded furnature and usin' young advetures as chew toys.

I ran out of arrows not one, not twice, but three times in my adventuring today. Trothyd Fletcher was right, if you run out of arrows ye run. Still I managed to go back to the Royal Firkin tonight with more than a couple scars and a pair of trade notes. T'was a great evenin' adventure.

Just as I was about to settle in with Ricetha, and enjoy a nice stout, I recieved word that Greyhawk and managed te get pulled through the portals from Norath. Well I sent the messager boy to go bring him in so I could help him get adjusted, and get him a good ale and all. We chated fer a bit, the decided to hit the warehouse together fer a peice.

About this time I suddenly felt extreamly fatigued. I'm startin' to suspect that Ricetha put something in me ale, but who knows. I decieded that I should call it a night before I got meself killed (again) in that bloody drudge hole.

I hear tell that the lost order's going to be gathering again soon. If I hear more it looks like I might be makin' another long trip 'cross the bloody island. I just hope I don't have to jump though any more portals this week, they always make me sick...
