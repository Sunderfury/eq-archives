Information about LOA
The Lost Order of Akalabeth, Founded in 1997 by Freejack, Brimstone, Ralineth, Ghoti, Brewman, Tasslehof, Zari, Carnifex and Avenger. Guild members are held to a very strict and high level of honor, maturity, and dedication to guild, members and other players. The MAIN purpose of this establishment is to promote honest, fair and mature gameplay for not only our own members, but for everyone who plays the games in the same world that we inhabit. This 'vision' has taken us far and earned us much respect in the gaming community. If you ever see one of OUR members acting anything less than mature and honest, you can contact us and we will look into it asap. [Report] | back to top?

Did you know?
- Freejack, co-founder of the LOA, works for GT Interactive, the company who created Wheel of Time (the game). Freejack has been working on the support, testing and in some cases, marketing of the game. LOA is very proud of Freejack and wishes him much success in the coming years!

- Richard Garriot, aka Lord British (uo), and Brad McQuaid of Everquest own "LOA Guild T-Shirts".  Richard Garriot once stated, "I consider the LOA shirt to be one of my top 10 favorites to wear!"
back to top?

- LOA was the FIRST guild to ever offer REAL LIFE services in exchange for VIRTUAL gold in Ultima Online, and then later, in EverQuest. It all started when Brimstone designed graphics that many guilds and players seemed to like. With demand very high, Brim charged about 5 to 10k for logos, paid for on Atlantic. Business boomed, and soon, other players began to offer services (writing, graphics, cgi assistance, html) for in-game gold. The rest is history...
