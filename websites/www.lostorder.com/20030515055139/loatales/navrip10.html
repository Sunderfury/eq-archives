A Metal Knight's Lament
by Navrip

A body of iron, bolts, and steel;
arms, legs, nerves that don't feel.
What I once possessed has left;
fled to nether realms I no longer see.
Some people say they feel pity,
some people say they grieve,
others say they hate me.
I care not for what they say.
I am inhuman and stay that way.

Cold reason and logic are my mind;
good emotion is hard to find.
Lacking a feeling heart
I do what I please with no remorse.
I may seem cruel but I am not
I just am what I was made;
a perfection of monstrosity.
People can say I dim the light
but I’ll do that to further might.

There is an empty hole
in the place where should be my soul.
I lost it long ago when I was unaware.
But give me the chance to change fate
and I’d do it the same all again.
Love, joy, happiness no longer matter.
I ask myself what does it all mean,
but I realize that I live but for one thing -
My Queen.
