Posted by Dedigan on October 18, 2000 at 13:33:51:

Fire dungeon level 2 holds quite a few rooms. Some of these rooms are death traps, such as the one with 3 liches and a lich lord. Some of these rooms just hold easy gold for anybody, such as the one holding two fire elementals. Though the hell hound spawn in the hall there can make that room rather difficult. The room just west of the stairs spawns a single efreet. A warrior with good parry or a single mage can have a bit of fun there. The next room south and east of that room spawns a pair of demons. The best thing about the demon pair is that they don't have an additional spawn zone out in the hall, so you only need to fight two demons at a time. The other best thing about them is that they don't open the door to their room.

So Dedigan was down in the demon area wondering how to get them out of the room while giving me time to provoke them before they hit me and decided that standing directly northwest of the door against the far wall and casting telekenisis on the door would do wonders. And so it did.

The demons would come out of the room, be provoked against each other before they got half way to Dedigan, and would be respawned within a couple minutes after they died... Easy money.

A tamer would only have to put a pet closer to the demons than they are before opening the door.

There are other tricks of the various trades down there, but I won't get into many of them. I'm almost shocked I haven't seen many campers down there yet... Soon I'm sure, probably whenever Khaldun loses its luster for the easy money crowd.
