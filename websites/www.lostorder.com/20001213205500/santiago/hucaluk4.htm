Hucaluk on Watch

"Where you from?" asked the troll captain.

The dwarf made no response, but glared at his jailors.

"Why you come to swamp?" asked the troll captain.

Silence.

"Bet you sorry you come to swamp. Any more littles in swamp?"

Silence.

"ANY MORE LITTLES IN SWAMP?" the troll captain yelled.

"He not say nothing," said Zulaan. "I tell you so. He stubborn. Me torture now?" He smiled with eager hope.

"Me give one last chance. Little tell us nothing, you torture little. Little still tell us nothing, little get sliced up. Bit by bit. Then we eat while he watch."

"I’ll not tell you anything!" shouted the dwarf.

This was music to the ears of Zulaan, who began to hop up and down with glee. "Me torture now?"

"You torture now."

Zulaan did a little dance. "Me go get tools!" He ran off down the cavern.

"You be sorry you say nothing," said the troll captain. "Zulaan good at torture. He like torture. You die slow." The captain spotted another troll walking by. "Hucaluk!"

Hucaluk froze. "Me no do it! Me innocent!"

"You not in trouble. Me have job for you. You guard prisoner. Me go get halfling sandwich."

"Me guard good!"

"You better. Dwarf maybe know something. You keep dwarf alive. Me come back soon." The troll captain departed.

"Captain ask Hucaluk for help!" thought Hucaluk. "Me important now!"

The dwarf began to chuckle.

"Why you laugh, little?" asked Hucaluk. "You crazy now?"

"I am laughing, stupid troll, because you and your friends will never get any information out of me. I have in my belt a self-destruct mechanism that was given to me by a gnome. In a few seconds, I will die, and my secrets will die with me. For the glory of Brell Serilis!" There was a small explosion, and Hucaluk was knocked off his feet.

When he got up, he saw little dwarf bits lying everywhere.

"This no good. This look bad for Hucaluk. Guards think me let dwarf die. Me better run back to swamp, while me still can!"
