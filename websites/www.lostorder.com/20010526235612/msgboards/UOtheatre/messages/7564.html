Talesn, first of all, thank you for your beautiful post on "another board" a few days ago -- gave me food for thought, and refreshed my motivation. (Now if I could remember my forum code to get a purple name!)

Anyhow, I agree with your thoughts on wrestling. I have had both in´recent years, and eventually decided to "want" wrestling. Although I have peacenmaking, and still consider this my most important skill that defines my character, in busy areas with many monsters it happens often that plenty of critters try to hit me (ophidian pit, is a good example). If they do hit, my ability to get spells off -- especially to heal or cure -- is greatly limited. I cannot always make peace, because of provocation delays, stubborn critters, newly spawned monsters. Therefore I need to be able to take some hits without actually taking damage. Wrestling enables me to do exactly this.

Resist on the flipside is -- for me -- only needed if I a) fail to provoke a spellcasting monster or b) cause it to cast by other means. For these cases, I have magic reflect up.

Also, the hunting place matters ... I mostly hunt in the ice dungeon (now that the "blue staff" troops have moved on to look for red bone armour) and the ophidian pit. There is only one monster type in the ice dungeon that casts (not counting the white wyrm), and the Ice Elementals do not have too high magery (meaning, I can survive a flamestrike).

So, in my case, wrestling is of greater value than magic resist. If I mainly hunted lichs, titans or bone magi, my deicison might be a different one.

Just my two pennies
- Cirander
