Hucaluk's Bad Day

Me Hucaluk. Me troll. Me strong! But me not very smart.

Me talk to bartender. Me want ale. Me beg, but he no give. Bartender mean.

Other troll comes in. Me forget name. Not important. He say, "Hail!" Me want to say, "Arg!" But me make mistake. Me attack bartender by accident. Bartender not happy. He call guards. Guards hit Hucaluk on head. Me fall down. World go dark.

Me wake up in swamp. Swamp dark, wet, foggy. Snakes and rats. Me hungry! Me beat snakes and rats with club. Mmmmm!

Other troll runs by. Alligator chase troll. Me chase alligator. Me not very smart.

Me jump into water. Me swim! Water dark and murky. Me see other troll. Other troll dead. Me see alligator. Alligator hungry. Me climb out of water. Me run like hell.

Alligator quick. Me not quick. Me fall down. World go dark.

Me wake up in swamp. Me not feel so good. Me get thrown out of town. Me get bitten by alligator. Me have bad day. But bad day no get worse, right? Wrong.

It start to rain. Me get wet. Sky flash and make loud noise. Me get scared. Me run like hell.

Me run for long time. Me run and run and run. Me run some more.

Me lost. Me so very lost. Me no see trolls no more. Only snakes and rats and alligators.

Me find path. Path go to desert. Me no like desert. Desert hot. Me tired. Me sit down. Me sleep. This bad dream. Me hope to wake up tomorrow back in Grobb.
