Posted by Talesn of Wodclf, Bard (Pacific) on July 11, 2000 at 13:32:57:

* looks around*

A big house fell out of the sky and crushed all the CBA Bards!
'Cept me?
Where b' ye all?

*tunes harp*
*notes echo from empty hall*

Hummm de dummm …
Th' harp b' me favorit' music.
Is me newby harp, ye know. Been with me all me life in UO Sosaria, my missing friends!

*quiet harp notes float through the ether*

In a land far away
In a land long ago
There b' th' CBA
Where nay many would a go!

We did play
And we did sing
And told we tales
O' many a thing!

There b' th' Master Spinner
Tales o' th' sea.
'Twas Santiag th' Pirate
Now where can e' be?

*discordant notes rise from Hythloth*
*Talesn quickly retunes harp*

There b' Amber Starfire
Teller o' scary tales o' th night.
An' Bobo (the other one)
Lamenter o' things not right.

An Damion Master Bardic Knight
Not here with us long,
But crafter of th' poem
Most rhymin' an' sing song right!

*music picks up tempo*

Are ye listen me friends,
Missin' from these halls?
Are ye listen to me song,
Or is it just these 'ere walls?

Dedigan, part-time tavern keeper, ale turner
'ave kinda fallen to th' back-burner.
An' Kirshaat M'Drahn, may b' Kelt
A lady o' words 'twer deeply fe'lt.

Where b' ye all?
This I ask o' ye today.
Where 'av ye gone?
Why b' ye not stay?

*harp notes drop low into a minor chord*

Minuette Darcrow of so long ago
A year an more I see
chanted A' End and a Beginnin'
for all o' us t' se'

There b' ol' Trinculo
Who tol' us how t' run, an' hum
"If yoyeu play death,
you 'ave t' keep th' drum"

*newby drum beats slowly in background*

O' these friend an' many more,
Why b' ye not with me
Here
On th' CBA floor?

Where b' me friends o' Pacific,
Who 'av 'ventured with me a day.
Leaves' o' Grass,
we met th' matriarch, aye?

Lute Olson,
long gone away
Shari, lost 'er 'orse on th' hill,
I guess 'cause I din'a play.

Was it just a house,
Fallin' from th' sky?
I fear there b' a'other reason,
An' o' this I'll tell ye why.

Aye! Th' Great Nothin'
May 'Av passed through th' sky.
An' served us each a slice
O' th' Bored O' Sosaria Pie.

*music rises and fades away*

*Amber Starfire enters the hall*

Hail Amber! I see yer not missin' after all!
Nope, sorry, I'm not goin',
To th' New York Renaissance Faire
Tho' I b' glad to see ye, in this CBA Hall.
