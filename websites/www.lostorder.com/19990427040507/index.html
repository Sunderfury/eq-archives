Welcome to the Lost Order of Akalabeth

We greet you and thank you for visiting our home on the internet. Our guild was founded in 1997 by several members of the Games-L mailing list, namely, Brimstone and Freejack.

Originally, we started out with "Ultima Online" as our primary objective. We set strong goals, strict rules of conduct and a policy of professionalism above all else. While other guilds increased rapidly in size, we lumbered behind them gaining only 1 or 2 members per month. After our first year, we were known as a guild of mature players who sought to increase the enjoyment of the games we played, not only for ourselves, but for those strangers we met along the way as well. Today, our small guild of 10 members has grown to an organization of 130+ members.

The Lost Order of Akalabeth [LOA] is now involved in Ultima Online, where we have a huge presence, including a village, Sage Keep, goods & services, dragon masters as well as an elite group of roleplayers. LOA in Ultima can be found on the Atlantic Shard.   In addition to Ultima Online, the LOA is now involved deeply in EverQuest. We have been granted official guild recognition and already we have noticed that those who knew us and respected us in UO, are now quite loyal and respectful of us in Norrath as well. In EverQuest, we are located on the Veeshan Server.
