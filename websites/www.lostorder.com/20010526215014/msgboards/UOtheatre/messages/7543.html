Well, I had to greet ye some way diferent, now didn' I? Seems to me that Dedigan an' Shari have given ye good advice.

It is fairly well agreed that Bard skills gain when ye move about, and ye are at least eight tiles from the last place ye practiced yer enchantment. That is the point of th' boat. Make Peace, while ye are sailin' and ye will be practicin' yer skill in different locations, almost automatic like.

Seems as though this advice means more when yer skills are high and yer gains are low.

Boats don't work well for Provocation, of course. 'Tis little out there on the Briny Deep that is good for Provoking. Moonglow may be a good place to walk about and provoke different animals. Not only do ye want to move on different tiles, but ye want to provoke different animals, don' ye see? I prefer th' City o' th' Dead, West of Delucia for me Provoking practice these days, but even there the monster spawn has been diminished. That makes fewer chances to Provoke ye see, and ye don't want to stand in the same spot while ye do it. Ah well, times change.

Why not try out all of these ideas and then come back and tell us how ye fared with it? Maybe ye can tell us a tale or two along th' way.

May th' Virtues Bless ye, Conlin !
