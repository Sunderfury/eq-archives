Full screne resolution 800 x 600
Game Play window size 800 x 600
I do not have "use full screne display" checked.

This way if I click the full screne option of the UO window (the choice to the left of the x in the upper right corner, which looks like overlapping squares) then I get the screen with a black border around it. I place my bacppack and map etc.on the black border and that way my view of UO is not covered. Most people do not want to play this way because it makes the rest of the view of UO small, but I have a large monitor. I am able to see my typing in the lower left of the screen using this configuration.

In fact you will have to experiement until you find the choices which give you the options you prefer.

Then record them somewhere. It seems there is a bit of chaos in the universe that wants to disturb all our carefuly laid plans.
