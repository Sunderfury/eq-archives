Hucaluk's Poem:  Ode to a Tode

me name iz Hucaluk
me liv in da swomp
me play in da mud
me find tingz to stomp

wun day me saw
a green liddle frog
he wuz just sittin dair
on a big ole ded log

me tryed to bash
dat frog on da hed
but he wuz too fast
me hit da log insted

da frog he just laft
an sed me wuz too slow
he hopt up an bit me
rite on da toe

“ow you dum frog
now me bash yoo into jellee
den me cook yoo an eet yoo
an yoo wind up in me bellee”

“haha dats wot yoo tink
yoo big meen green troll
yoo too klumzee to kach me
yoo too stoopid an dull”

“we see hooz da stoopid wun
yoo green liddle fop”
me cast a root spell on him
so dat frog kan no hop

“help me kant moov!”
cryed da doomd liddle tode
“its too late” me sed
me gonna eet yoo a la mode

“me no tok to yoo
me tok to dem!”
me lookd up an saw froggeez
lots of dem…more dan ten!

me pikt up dat frog
an me ran uway
me run reel fast
wen dair iz hell to pay

me run an me run
til me feets got sore
den me run an me jump
til me kant jump no more

den me see da gards
way up uhed
me smile an me no
dat doze froggeez iz ded

an dat just goze to show
wot slow klumzee trollz kan do
dat nite me hav gud feest
uv froggee stoo!

yoo wont mor?  ok den klik heer
