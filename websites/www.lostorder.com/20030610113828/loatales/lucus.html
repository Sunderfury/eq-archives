The Search Begins
by Lucus

Felucca's light poured across the grounds of the Moonglow Lycaeum, giving it an otherworldly look, the night was almost banished it's shadows as much as the day. The translucent shade hiding among the trees hissed softly. Leaving the cover of the forest, it drifted lazily towards the ancient building, passing through the walls surrounding the complex. It stopped in the court yard for a moment, listening to the scribes and scholars go about their chronicling within. Eyes blazing, the shade slowly manifested in it's full glory. Resplendent in hideous midnight armor, scrolled masterfully with horrific depiction's and daemonic symbols stood Fustiar. Surveying the area, seeing no one present, the Death Knight strode purposefully towards the main scriptorium. A glint of silver pierced the sides of his vision, whirling out of the way, barely missing the deadly blade, he turned around to face the unexpected foe.

"Who is the fool who dares challenge me?", spoke the Death Knight in a sepulchral voice, orbs flaring in its sockets.

The Paladin looked into Fustiars' orbs and shouted,"Daren SilverBlade, Paladin of the Virtues, foul scum, prepare to meet thy doom!" Eyeing the advancing paladin with disdain, the Death Knight chuckled softly.

"Very well mortal, for the glory of Entropy!" Drawing an ebony kryss, and mentally calling his shield, Fustiar effortlessly parried the paladins attack. Slashing with deft precision and speed, Fustiar left the enemies shield in pieces. Howling with desperate rage, knowing his skills could never measure up to his evil opponent, he knew there was only one thing to do, embrace death, before this horrid creature could claim his soul. A skeletal grin passed across the Death Knights face, as he brought his around slamming it into the paladins chest, crumpling his breast plate and sending him flying a dozen feet before he crashed into the wall, bone snapping, ribcage shattering, but still alive.

"Get it over with devil spawn" the paladin said, spitting blood, struggling to maintain consciousness. Coming over, Fustiar looked down at the broken knight, contemplating.

"My masssster, shall have use for your ssssoul, even if this wassss unexpected" it croaked. Withdrawing a black gem, the Death Knight placed it over the paladins chest and started chanting long lost words of power. Screaming in agony, the knight convulsed repeated, the gem pulsing with an unholy light, it drew the energy, the very life force from the paladin leaving nothing but an empty husk, which began decaying rapidly. Knowing full well, that many more mortals, who had been called by the scribes during the battle, were on their way, Fustiar once again faded, traveling through the nether realms back to the Isle of Ice. Apon arrival, it made its way to the eyesore known as Caina, hastening to return the to the Sepulchre of the Damned, where his master awaited.

"You have the information I seek, slave?", asked Lucus.

"Nay, masster, there wass some trouble, a paladin, who interrupted my attemptssss, I did however claim his soul and - ", Fustiar spoke, but before finishing, his masters clawed hand slammed into his skull sending him careening across the room, right into a stone bookshelf, which shattered on his impact.

"Stupid fool! I ask for the location of Claudia's lair and you give me a paladins soul?! Give me what I ask, or nothing at all, do not fail me again, or even you, an exalted Death Knight, shall feel terrible pain"

Nodding in servitude, Fustiar faded once again to his wraith form, to find a way to perform the task his master required.
