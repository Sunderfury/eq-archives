I like the PvP bard character. The skill tree for a PvP bard looks this way:

100 music
100 provo
100 magery
100 eval
100 meditation
100 resist
100 swords/fenceing

Peacemakeing is worth nothing in PvP quit it players are more fun to fight ;).
Only a PvM bard has any use for that.
The 100 skill points in swords/fenceing are used to wield deadly poisoned weapons.
You do not need the tactics or anatomy since the poison will do the job *smiles grimly*.
The music and provocation is used to manipulate computer controled killers to
focus on your prey. It can give you quite an edge in certain areas and even in an
ordinary "non-lethal" forest it can be very nice to have a passing grizzley bear (or whatever)
annoy your target. Or even worse things he he.

And even if you should want to hunt monsters now and then,
this combo works alright for that as well even though peacemakeing would make
you more effective in PvM.

Have fun and do not kill the innocents!
