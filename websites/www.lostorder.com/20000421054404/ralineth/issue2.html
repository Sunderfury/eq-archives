The unofficial and very irregular journal of the Lost Order of Akalabeth in Norrath
ISSUE #2


EDITORIAL
Hail and wellmet gentle readers (I write this safe in the knowledge that neither Ogres or Trolls can read). After being underwhelmed with contributions from you all I bring to you another edition of the Akalabethian filled with cynicism, sarcasm, and just the smallest hint of bitterness. As always if you wish to contribute then please email Ralineth Oakenheart.

CAZIC THULE CATASTROPHE!
A small and well-armed party of Akalabethians recently journied into the legendary temple to the evil God of Cazic Thule, accompanied by a pair of Ogres who tagged along thinking we were going there to worship. After sending a great number of lizardmen to meet their maker, it seemed that the Ogres finally twigged to the fact that we weren't there to pray to evil gods, in fact quite the opposite. This prompted one of them to produce a train of rather tough lizardmen with a penchance for healing each other. Valiantly we battled this horde of reptiles, until it was obvious that discretion would be the better part of valour. After calling for an evacuation we discovered that our mage hadn't quite grasped the concept of saving mana for emergencies. At this point everyone decided to hot-foot it through the temples maze. Strangely enough the lizards decided that Ogres make very good sacrifices and after jettisoning our over-sized friends to the pursuing mob the rest of us made it back into the jungle of Feerott alive.

 DOING THE ORC-SHOE SHUFFLE
Inndur, our high-elven cleric wannabe recently discovered that Orc Centurions are not blue fluffy rabbits. After depositing a number of corpses upon the soil of Greater Faydark, Inndur came to the realization that that if it /cons red you'll soon be dead. We are all looking forward to Inndur reaching level 5 and discovering the joys of exploring the minotaur caves...

TROLLS AND OGRES MASSING IN THE SOUTHLANDS
Reports from the Innothule swamps and jungle around Feerott indicate a massing of Trolls and Ogres in these locations, hacking at the local wildlife to hone their 'barshing' skills. Whether this means they are preparing to attack the human infested lands to the North or they are about to commence their biannual warts wars between each other we are not sure, but rest assured we will keep you informed of any further developments.

FREEPORT  ROGUE DISCOVERED BLIND DRUNK
Beur Tenlah, a two-bit pickpocket who haunts the tunnels around Freeport was recently brought to the local healers after his liver gave out on him. It seems that a large number of undesirables have been feeding him massive quantities of Dwarvern Ale. Upon hearing of this local miltia were reported to have been ecstatic, hoping that Beur had finally drunk himself into his grave.
 

 SHADOW KNIGHT SWORD EXPEDITION PLANNED
Upright paladin of the Order Zirc is planning a trip to a currently undisclosed location to retrieve a legendary sword of evil named FleshRipper. Zirc plans to obtain this sword and place it in safe-keeping to ensure it remains out of the hands of evil. He is currently assembling a party of powerful dwarves, humans, elves and wolves to accompany him on this expedition. One only hopes that the Ogre Shadow Knight Bagulan (who is rumoured to have been hanging around the Ocean of Tears, and the main culprit behind Beur Tenlah's current malaise) doesn't get wind of this and intercept the sword before it can be destroyed. As always when we get more information we will let you know.

PALADIN GOES FERAL
Syndaen, potential paladin in-training recently turned to the 'dark side' while hunting in Lesser Faydark. After his heroic actions in saving a besieged stumpy legged creature named Suezzette from a horrible death at the fangs of the spider Queen Nashjeeli. Still engulfed by his blood-lust Syndaen then raced up to an innocent pixie in the forest and proceeded to murder him in cold blood. Syndaen was reported to unremorseful of killing the harmless creature and actually proud of the bow that he stole from the still warm corpse before the pixies own family could retrieve his belongings. The Paladins High Court has been unusually quite on this subject, whereas the Wood Elf Council of Tunare is demanding blood.
