In Reply to: Hey, Talsn and Amber how about some tales form the UO fair? posted by Bobo (the other one) on November 24, 2000 at 20:31:21:

saturday got to conference center bright and early... since they didnt need me for my assigned volunteer duties i offered help at other spots and still had time to get in an extra session... attended one on player towns, thiefs, (missed some cause had to stay to finish silent auction, and one on future of UO

during lunch folks were getting their tshirts signed i was like what a cool idea since i left my tshirt at the hotel i got out my fair program and got lots of autographs (next year i can start earlier and get everyone) spotted sunsword !!! had met him in new york and politely asked for his too then the guys started laughing and i was like what.. and they all pointed to this guy.... and i was like mmm ok read his name tag and was like omg !!! it was designer dragon they said we thought youliked his too hehehe so with a big grin i asked for his too and apologized for not recognizig him but i'd never met him in person (sunsword i had met at a ny luncheon last year so i knew what he looked like)

ran around for some last minute souvenirs... a cloth map to hang on my wall, a black uo shirt (they were of extra larges in my first choice, but got another any way...next year... shop early!)

managed to squeeze in a few minutes here and there to try out computers to look at uo third dawn (females get gstring undies now, got to see new ratman and lizardman, watched some others play and kill harpies and demons.... graphics look very nice)

the town hall was the last event... we all gathered in the big area and tyrant made his last speech he is a riot he was like we know we suck hehehe but at the same time you could tell they do want uo to be the best his announcing the new hardward for all servers was met with big cheers folks got up to speak a few words at the mike in the center of the room suggestions for improvements were made for next years fair (like not having music so loud, banners up for all shards so folks could have a point to meet others from shard if they wanted to) but over all every one seemed to agree it was an awesome event

as we were leaving a friend grabbed one of the huge signs... and i said hey me too... and grabbed the one in lobby it was about five feet... and said uo fair registration w/ uo logo as i'm walking down street... it dawns on me its too big for a carry on for the plane hehehe but a friend said he would mail it to me but we had to cut off the bottom portion (all white any way)

we all decide to skip the happy hour -- to loud and to crowded and head to the area where the restaurant is where all folks from chessie shard are going to gather later

hung out talked.... then went and had dinner nice steak house, private room we needed it hehehe about 30 or 40 of us all got together - went around and made introductions and then talked, laughed, talked and laughed some more

then the bunch of us who play together all went back and hung out at the hotel and stayed up almost all night again it wasnt till our eye lids were closing on their own that we decided we better get some sleep which for most was about 4 a.m. i had to leave by 6 a.m. so i managed to get about 90 mins rest but i can sleep any time and it was just great finally meeting the goods friends i play with in britannia and i hope next year even more can come to the fair

it was a great time !!!
