Captain's Log: Wintersebb 17, 10 P.Y.
by Enosh Flam

Captain's Log: Wintersebb 17

Today started out with rain, lots of rain. Its almost as if all the bar mades that have gone on to their final rest were a cryin' fer me. Once again I shouldered my quiver of arrows and made fer the depths of the abandoned distillery. Its almost becoming a routine fer me. I guess that's why I got a wee bit cocky today and got meself in trouble.

Things were going along rather well down there. I killed a good number of gnawer shreths and some minor drudges. I was startin' to feel me oats and get a little bolder. I rounded on corner, walked down a ramp to the bottom of the distillery, met up with the biggest drudge I've see to date.

Well bein' that I'd had some ale with breakfast, (and she wasn't all the fillin' er um,. never ye mind that) I thought to meself, "all the others I've found I've easily turned to pin cushions, lets give this bloke a try" So I notched and arrow and let it fly. The bugger pulled the arrow right out of 'is chest and started stabbin' me with it.

I came to at the lifestone on the eastern outpost of Lytelthrope. Not bein' one to give up on me pyreals and ale that easily I ran back to the distillery and hunted down that bloody drudge prowler.

This I repeated three times.

I did finally get the bugger, with the assistance of a mage I met along the way. The fella didn't talk much, but then again we were both a little busy with the drudges and shreths at the time. Once that prowler was dispatched though I was able to recover me coin and beat a hasty retreat.

I ran all the way back to the Roasted Shreth in Lytelthorpe, where Selfora the barkeep 'elped me bandage a few, quite painful, wounds.

In good news, Selfora insits that I lay up a few days. After all I'm one of her best customers and she'd hate to have all that stout go to waste if I should really get put down fer good. All I have to say it thank goodness fer them blasted life stones!

Also somewhere along the line I collected near 500 pyreal! T'was most profitable, althrough I got handed me gizzards more than a few times. Also I'm finding that some of me skills in fletching and archery are returning, though I think its more new skill that returned old. Still I can kill most shreath with a handfull of arrows, which is quite an improvement.

I hear tell on the rumor mill that the forces of light are gathering again in Nanto to help clear the countryside of evil. Mayhaps I shall heed the call and head there meself, though I would need to find another of the cursed portals to take me there I fear. Ah well, I'll safe those troubles fer another day.
