Da Troll Hoo Wood Be King
by Hucaluk

Me allweez noo dat sum day me wood be King Uv All Trollz. Udders no tink so. Day call Hucaluk crazy. Day say me nebber be King. Well day wuz rong. Me bet day iz sorry now, but not az sorry az day gonna be wen me lok dem in da dunjin!

But me tel yoo wun ting. Dat throne, it iz not kumfee. It iz hard an wood an me get liddle peesez uv sharp wood in me butt.

Ranjor, he iz da kapten uv da worrierz. Ranjor woks up to me an sez, “Yoo sed yoo want to see me?”

Me reach over and bonk Ranjor on da hed. “Me told yoo! Yoo call Hucaluk King!”

Ranjor bow so low dat da horns uv hiz helmet get dirty on da ground. He sed, “Me sorry, King Hucaluk. Pleez hav mersy on por Ranjor.”

“Dats ok,” me sed. “Me let yoo liv dis time.”

“O, tank yoo, King Hucaluk!” he sed. “Yoo iz da best!”

“Me no dat allreddy. Why yoo tel Hucaluk tings me allreddy noze? Now yoo go to da swamp an bring me sum froggy stoo!”

Den gess hoo shode up? Me bet yoo nebber gess. So me tel yoo. It wuz Kaglari! Kaglari is so bootiful. Dis is wot she sed.

“Oh, Hucaluk!” Yup, dat is wot she sed. “Me iz so sorry dat me was mean to yoo! Me wish me had not thrown yoo into da fire. Can yoo ever forgiv Kaglari?”

Me sed, “Nope.”

She sed, “But me lub yoo! Me want gib yoo big kiss, ok?”

“Ok.” Me not dum. Me no good ting wen me see it!

Me see Kaglari kum up to me. Me see her bootiful green ize, her bootiful pointy eerz, da bootiful worts on her lips. Me iz about to get kiss from Kaglari, da most bootiful woomin in all da world! Me iz happy troll! Iz good to be da king!
