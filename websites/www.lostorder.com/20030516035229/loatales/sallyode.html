Ode to Jared
by Sally

Hello Love:

Jared Farstrider...I want to prove to you that my love for you is much greater than my sister Daisy's. You really hurt my feelings last night running away from me...so I wrote this little ode to express my true love for thee.

Ode to Jared
I love thee Jared...
No matter how much I declare it you still run from me like a ferret.
And even if we are appart for now...I will always follow thee like a love sick cow.
So take heed my love Jared...I will never be far.
For you see I know you are my true love and I will get thee my dove.

So love now I have made my claim to you public so this warning is to my no good sister Daisy...STAY AWAY FROM JARED HE BELONGS TO ME!

Now I don't want you other gents to get jealous...I can take very good care of all of ye.
