Hail Friends, Bards o' th' CBA an' Mates o' Steel & Magic [S&M]. Th' adventure last weekend turned out a treasure hunt

when Steel & Magic's own Sundance th' Treasure Mistress (Tara's sister in Sosaria) took th' lead. We all rushed to our bank boxes an' retrieve our treasure maps

an' Sundance proceeded to decode th'm. Some were a fun challenge level three treasure. Ogre Lords

an' such spawned an' we all did our own version o' th' dance. Hard to kill David Dfalder, DemiGod died several times on that one. It's th' spider poison I think. Oth'r maps marked level one treasures which spawned several o' th' deadly mongbats.

Yeow!

These here are said to be "the weakest monster in existence"!

I had two maps which evidently posed a challenge to th' intrepid Treasure Mistress. At first neith'r one worked, though she dug an' dug where th' X marked th' spot, so as to speak. I don't know if this was a flaw in Arcane th' Royal Knight's treasure runes or some oth'r glitch. Sundance knows better than I as she finally dug one o' me treasures up an' I was able to collect various magical items an' release a few defending monsters in th' process.

*BIG silly grin*

Must o' been a level two. Th' oth'r one is still a dud, a mystery an' a conundrum for our mystery solver Treasure Mistress.

As for those runes, I don't know. Erin Pinesong an' I an' oth'rs like Tara an' I duno who else had marked th'm for Arcane in th' past. No doubt some errors were made. Mebe we shoudn' drink too much Orc Ale before marking treasure runes. Sundance swears it is th' Oracle STRATICS which is at fault, but we should be careful with oracles. Like th' Sphinx, th'y sometimes speak in riddles that turn out to be th' truth. I am sure th're will be more to come at a later date as Sundance increases in her powers to find th' elusive treasures o' th' fourth an' fifth kind. Unfortunately Arcane will not be about to help us with this mystery. Th' RW has called him an' he leaves UO Sosaria for our tender care.

*feels sad*

Arcane's maker says th' account is for sale, but Sundance is to inherit all those treasure runes. WooHoo! All that rune marking an' monster bashing we accomplished to make th'm is not lost to Steel & Magic.

Not all th' treasure hunting was unending excitement. After all, Sundance had to take our maps an' decipher th'm. It is all a mystery to me. I pick em up an' th'y just look like a bit o' tattered an' faded parchment. Without an expert th'y just collect dust (or whatever th' virtual equivalent is) in me bank box. After taking some time she was able to reveal th'ir secrets, for th' most part. Taking some time … that was th' challenge for some. Borrrring stuff waiting around for th' Treasure Mistress to work her enchantments. Probably went to th' tavern an' is drinking Orc Ale or something while th' rest o' us stan' around th' log cabin with th' runes somewhere in th' wilderness.

HAR! Har! har!!

David Dfalder, DemiGod was stan'ing around first on one foot an' th'n th' oth'r, probably thinking about killing nobles an' oth'r such un-Virtuous stuff an' finally announced "Lets go kill Balrons!"

Heck, he didn't say this just once, he said it every time he got bored. Ye figure it out. So I think we are going Balron hunting next. Mebe Saturday, 25 November, but Dave hasn't spoke for sure on that yet. Well, If Dave doesn't post on Balrons I can take ye to th' Terathian Dungeon an' visit th' one what lives there, or we can always go for Dragons

in Destard or Titans on Titan Isle. Now whatever we do, will not be for the faint of heart. Th're is fun an games an' titles for all. If we don't end up doing th' OooooOoO dance an' all. Which we will.

HAR! Har!!

Anyone want to join me in some o' this here Orc Ale?
