Captain's Log: Wintersebb 28, 10 P.Y.
by Enosh Flam

Captain's Log: Wintersebb 28

Today has been a very long day! Though I must say it has also been a very profitable day fer me.

I awoke this mornin' to find Selfora already hard at work downstairs in the pub. This gave me the chance to grab all me adventurin' gear and escape out the balcony. While I very much enjoyed allowin' her to play nurse maid to me (hello nurse) I was itchin' to head back out and scower the lost distillary again fer those rumored vintages.

You know in me time here, which is goin' on a full month now, I don't think I've seen the sun but once in me entire stay. I truely do like this fair city, but the gods do like to rain on it I find. Still I braved the cold winter's wind and rain and headed back to the underground distillery's entrance.

Once again I found my self, dropin' drudges and spliting shreths left and right. I even made it so far as to find the confounded drudge prowler that was combing the depths. This time I got me revenge and fed him a quiver full of arrows. Low and behold, ye know what he was guarding? Well I'll tell ye, it was the distillery nectar itself! I managed to grab two bottles of the stuff and haul my sorry carcas clean away.

I figure I can give one bottle to sweet Selfora, since she's got a rather exotic taste in beverages. And the other I can keep fer meself. Perhaps tomorrow I shall head back down there and look for more of the good stuff.
