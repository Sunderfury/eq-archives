As You Commanded.....
by unknown

Most Powerful Mistress,

As you commanded I began my search for those Walayrn gnomes. I caught up to them in Steamfront. I heard that Wyzer say the stone bird and I knew exactly what he meant so I went off to where they would be going. You were right, dear mistress, they are not smart. I had to wait a full day and half the night before they arrived but they finally came.

That stone bird spoke though Wyll.

Using the powers you gave me I was able to get close and hear what they were saying. Sentimental gnomes were more concerned with the health of Wyll than figuring out what was said.

They made their way to Felwithe and are spending the night at the inn. Stupid gnomes… they do not know I watch them as they sleep.

Your Humble Servant
