Cannon Balls
by Santiago

(to the tune of “Jingle Bells”):

Sailin’ on the sea
In a great big pirate ship
O’er the waves we go
Laughin’ all the way
(Yo ho ho!)

Foes with sword wounds scream
An’ then they run in fright
What fun it is to laugh an’ launch a pirate raid tonight!

Oh! Cannon balls, cannon balls
Blastin’ all the way
Lots o’ gold in the pirate hold
An’ the booze will last all day…hey!

Cannon balls, cannon balls
Blastin’ all the way
Say yer prayers, ‘cause yer worst nightmares
Have just sailed into bay!

An hour or two ago
I sobered up at last
An’ found a buxom wench
Tied up against the mast

Me thoughts was full o’ sin
Me hands was on her thighs
An’ then I saw the enemy ship…
Too late! We got capsized!

Oh! Cannon balls, cannon balls
This just ain’t me day
Ain’t no fun when yer the one
What’s got to duck out o’ the way…hey!

Cannon balls, cannon balls
Ain’t that just the way?
Fooled around an’ then nearly drowned
But tomorrow’s another day!
