The State of the Guild...

Posted by Salkind - The Errant Guildmaster of Pacific on August 30, 2000 at 04:46:28:

Hail my comrades - tis been a long long sojurn in the lightless realms for me, I am most glad to return, although I expect few of you noticed my diasppearence.

The Conservatory seems to be in decline, on Pacific anyway. I could not find a Guildmaster to replace me before I left and our membership has fled in the absence of leadership. Now the very house which once housed the stone is gone to us and we are faced with extinction!

I implore some Compassionate soul to aid me, I have not been in the Britannia that is called Pacific for some time and I have no-one to call upon. Thus I am reduced to pleas.

I have re-located to Skara Brae as Trinsic when I last saw it was infested with the dread legions of the Enchantress. If any wish to seek me out I am living upon the kind charity of the proprieter of the Shattered Skull.

I am glad to be back at last and hope to once again disturb the mordant blather of the banks of Britannia with my songs and speeches.

Virtues be with thee all!
Salkind, once of Trinsic, of late from Skara Brae.
