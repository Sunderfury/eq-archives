To My Mistress.....
by unknown

Oh dear mistress, they are tiring running all over the place but they are amusing little folk. First they went to the wizard’s guild.

When I followed them along, they tried to show off their powers to Naddwin. Those little gnomes ganging up on that little weasel, even a giant rat could have killed that Naddwin * smirks *

These little ‘powerful’ gnomes could not even make it to Rivervale with out an escort from TWO of them big folk. I wish, dear mistess, you had given me the power to cast SoW because I would have loved to see that giant step on each one of them. They did finally make it there.

I thank you dear mistress for providing me the most amusement of the evening. That little gnome dead surrounded by all his family and those big folk tickled my heart.

Your Faithful Servant
