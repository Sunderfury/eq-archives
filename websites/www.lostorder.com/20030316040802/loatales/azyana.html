Azyana's Journal, Entry One
by Azyana

*begins to write*

I went to Qeynos to look for some spells I'm still missing but all I found were ones I have. I noticed this book at one of the merchants so bought it but found it filled with nothing but blank pages. I was so upset that I had wasted my money. Then I began to think that I could fill it with my thought so it would no longer be just blank pages. So after a lot of seaching I found a place to buy this quill and some ink.

Last eve I watched my golden one perform the ceremony for Eala and Sunmoon. Twas beautiful. As I sat there I found my mind drifting off wondering what my golden one could possibly see in me as I am so young, but am greatful that he does see something.

Gads it is soo cold here in Everfrost. Too cold to write any more.

*closes the book and puts everything away*
