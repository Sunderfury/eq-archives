He was not an Avatar of Vice. His name was (and is?) Lobar.
He lived in the world of Killorn Keep (Ultima Underworld). He embodied the eight virtues
of the Guardian, which are:

Yellow
Punctuality - clock
is a habit to be formed by all those who call the Guardian their Master. Symbolized by the
Clock, it is the commitment to being prepared on a moment's notice to carry out the
Guardian's orders.
Blue
Sobriety - empty bottle
is the state all followers of the Guardian should maintain. Symbolized by the Empty Bottle it
is one free of any chemical or magical substances that may impede the Inner Voice from
being heard.
Red
Diligence - sword
is the ability to seek out a goal and continue toward it. Symbolized by the Sword it is
especially important to the armies of the Guardian to ensure victory.
Green
Conformity - Guardian mask
is complete acceptance of all the Guardian's beliefs and values. Symbolized by the Mask of
the Guardian it is the lack of desire to suceed or surpass any person's accomplishments or
ideas.
Orange
Efficiency - Abacus
is the prevention of any waste. Symbolized by the Abacus it applies to every function of life,
from food, energy, money, even lives.
Purple
Obedience- closed fist
is something required of all the Guardian's servants. Symbolized by a Closed Fist it is
complete submission to any and all the Guardian's demands and wants.
White
Silence - closed mouth
is the need for all the Guardian's servants to not speak against the order. Symbolized by
the Closed Mouth it is essential for the stability and safety of our world.
Black
Vigilance - open eye
is the constant state of mind any servant of the Guardian should maintain. Symbolized by
the Open Eye it is the backbone of the Guardian's defenses, to watch for foreign and
domestic foes.

One could ask why he was not an Avatar of the eight vices opposing the eight Virtues of the Avatar
I think myself that it is because the Virtue system of Ultima is based on the base assumption
of platonic and late christian thinking (and probably at other times as well in among other groups).
In those systems evil lacked substance and was to be understood as leech phenomeners.
E.g. To lie was to lack truth or to hate was to lack love.
Therefore if the Avatar is the pure expression of something then this "something" has to have a
substance if someone is to embody it. And since Vice is understood to hold no substance it cannot be
embodied. Therefore there is no Anti-Avatar of Vice.
In u9 however it is suggested that the Guardian is "the evil twin" of the Avatar and one could
ask if that would not ruin my theory. Yet, I think not. For in u9 it is made clear that it is the
Avatar's fault that the Guardian came to Britannia, aye that the Guardian came into existance at all.
This is because the Guardian is a leech phenomener just like the eight vices are.

But how about Lobar then if the Guardian is a leech phenomener?
That is a very good question indeed and I do not have an answer to that.
I will however point out that Ultima Underworld I + II are odd side stories of
the main tread formed by Ultima I-IX. Therefore they are not as predictable as sources
to the Ultima Lore. But still they should not be neglected and thus they present us
with an enigma.
There are indeed aspects of the Ultima lore which are clouded and self-contradicting,
yet perhaps there is even a deeper meaning to that. Who knows. Such odd selfcontradictions
appear in even real religious texts in the Real World so indeed who knows...
