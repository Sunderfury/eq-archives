Easy and safe ways of disposing of a bard.

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Ultima Online Performing Arts Theatre ] [ FAQ ]

Posted by Dedigan on October 28, 2000 at 16:38:10:

I write this not to show ways of twinking a bard, but rather to let you know people can do these things and to help getting ideas about how to defend myself against these 'tactics.'

The first method of getting rid of a bard is to wait until the bard has a few spell casting creatures who aren't provoked yet. The offender will attack all the spell casting creatures targetted on the bard. The worst that has happened to me in this case is I was paralyzed, double flame struck, paralyzed again then meleed to death. Its an old tactic, but it still works (but its usually done out of ignorance than malice).

The second method of disposing of a bard (one I believe doesn't work on Trammel, but I'm not certain) is to wait until provoke is successful, then cast invisibility on the weaker of the provoked monsters. The one still visible will retarget. This isn't likely to kill a bard, but its so annoying, I usually scream in frustration then go home. On the bright side, I rarely see this tactic. I learned it myself due to an accident with a friend who wanted to remount her nightmare. She missed the mare and got one of two provoked monsters. The other one retargetted me and I came within half a second of seeing black and white.

How can I counter these tricks to make it more annoying for the trickster than for me?
