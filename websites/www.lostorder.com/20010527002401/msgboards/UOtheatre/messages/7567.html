Still, I'm not sure how useful having swords rather than wrestling would be. Against a melee opponent with any decent connection, that character should be toast any time you need to heal. I'm not sure poisoning your blades would do you much good either since anyone I know carries plenty of cure potions. 'Course, since I use a lot of poison, the cures are a necessity for anybody I fight against. On the other hand, picking your opponents is half the battle, and that template would work wonders against some opponents.

The bard I used to use for PvP has:
100 music
85 provoke (and climbing)
100 swords
100 tactics
100 anatomy
90 healing (and climbing)
and the rest is currently split between magery and resisting magic. If I could ever realistically GM resist, I'd probably get that.

Most say the dex monkey isn't a fun PvP character because they don't have the options the mage character does... but I say they're wrong. With the wide variety of weapons and the option of poisoning some of them and the use of potions (I love purple!), there's always something different you could do for each opponent. The only person I wouldn't have that character fight is a mace-fighting parry character. Unless there's a spawn in the area, then I could provoke nasties on the enemy and maybe even win.
