... before th' time o' Oceania made th'ir way to Pacific, an' occasionally found high adventure an' good companionship. Such is th' venerable Salkind, former Pacific CBA Guild Master. Alas, 'tis well known that one's spirit moves but slowly from so far away. Hence came into existence, after a hard birth, th' UO Sosarian lan' o' Oceania. Yet th' tyranny o' distance does not wholly preclude visits between kindred souls. Upon occasion I have visited oth'r shards, o'ten in th' o'fice o' CBA ambassador to th' Shard an' all.

So, an' getting to th' point

*o'fers elixir o' companionship*

ye are welcome to likewise visit our rath'r populous shard. Well, not to hunt th' bad humored Balron but, perhaps, for tamer adventure as th' treasure hunts an' such as ye have heard o'. In th'se modern times newbys soon have respectable abilities, an' make a fine showing on th'ir own. Besides, on our adventures th're are many an' th' o'ten needed res' is readily o'fered.

*grins*

Just a thought an' most likely an impractical one I know, considering th' aforementioned eth'real conditions. Yet on that th're is th' mystery o' time zones an' whatnot to further work their mischief. Also, along those lines, never have I visited th' fair lan' o' Oceania and so experience th' famed lag o' who knows how many hundreds o' ping. Though I confess to haveing traveled to mysterious Draconfelds an' found than not always am I forever fixed in one place.

May th' Virtues bless ye, Sindaer Roze,
Whatever paths ye may travel.
