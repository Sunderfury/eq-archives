Legasi's Birthday Song
by Orbison

I've known Legasi but a short while
A wood elf ranger of grace and style
Whose beauty, courage, and honor true
Sparkle like the morning dew

She calls to me most every day
And seeks to join the LOA
So when I hear her words so sweet
I eagerly arrange to meet

And when those vicious orcs attack
Tis good to know she's got my back
Her combat skills are sharp and fine
More deadly than any song of mine

But the real joy at each day's end
Is knowing I have such a friend
To quest and fight with, side-by-side
And represent our guild with pride

So guildmates, let us raise a toast
From Erudin to the Felwithe coast
To Legasi, we offer all our praise
And hope for the happiest of birthdays!
