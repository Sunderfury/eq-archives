I love finding a frosty ogre lord (I know its arctic, but I still calls 'em frosty) and cleaning house with it. For sheer fame, I just find a pair of them (I know where a pair spawns close together) and let 'em at each other.

And I keep finding these glacial staff thingies. Speaking of which, anyone know how much they sell for these days?

The monsters don't spell cast (except Ice ele's) and they usually stick with their provokes (except Ice ele's and snow ele's). Overall, its a great dungeon for the mage or bard, but horribly bad for the warrior... unless rat men is your thing, then there's this great spot by the entrance.
