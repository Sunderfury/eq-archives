Pea Pod An 'Ow He Gots His Name
by Azlana

Dis da story of Pea in da Pod.

Dere was dis ranger man name be Sunmoon an he growd up someplace an wanda round lots shootin thins wit his arras.

He be lookin fer family join up wit an den he be finding our family of LOA.

He mets lotsa peoples from da family an dis one VERA smart warrior lady named Azlana. She thinkin he be good person but his name be too long fer her ta say all time. So she, bein so smart, say ta self dat she be thinkin o’name fer im.

Long time go by an she no see im. Den one night dere be peoples from family, Aanlain, Amarin, an Timbo helpin her get outta da hell level. Dis ranger man come by say ellos. When she see im he be all in green. She thought self ‘he be so cute an lil an green he look like a pea in a pod’ . So was at dat moment she say self ‘Dat it! He be Pea Pod!’. She say on gulidsay dat from now on dis Sunmoon ranger man be knowd as Pea Pod.

She wen sleep dat night vera happy cuz not only she finally give Pea Pod his new knick name but also cuz is actually THREE names. Peoples can call im Pea Pod if wanna be formal. Den dey can call im Pea or Pod ifn dey likes also.

Good thin she be so smart or he jes woulda jes gots one names.

An dat be end o’story bouts how Pea Pod got his name.
