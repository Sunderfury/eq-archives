Azyana's Journal, Entry Seventeen
by Azyana

My golden one came to me last night knowing I was so close to my next circle that he wanted to assure I would attain it as safely as possible. He cast so many spells upon me that I felt like a tank.

He carefully brought to me undead making sure I would not die in the process. I have made progress on my own but in groups died a lot so I think he just didn't want to see it happen right now. I would sit an meditate in between and tried to talk with him but he just seemed so distracted that after awhile I gave up and decided to just enjoy the fact that we had gotten the chance to spend more than a few minutes together this day.

He came out of the deep thought that he had been in to tell me that it was time to meet his mother. Oh my, something that I had wanted to do now is coming up so close. I had learned some things the past few days that I had wanted to write down but now my mind is so distracted that I can't just seem to bring all my thoughts together.

His mother. He said that she had grown her beard back. Me with out one. What will she think? At least I have attained my next spells and maybe she won't think me too young for him. Oh my, what am I going to do? What am I going to say? He best not leave us alone because I will surely be tongue-tied.

I need too walk around a bit to clear my mind.

*closes her book and puts everything away *
