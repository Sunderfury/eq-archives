Akalabeth
by Argil

Aid to the needy rendered when asked,
Kinship of spirit beneath the skin,
Assistance to guildmates, regardless the task;
Loyalty brings us through thick and through thin.
Aloud we call for our long-winded bard;
Bravely he strikes up a resounding chord.
Ever we quest for our lost tome and shard
Truth is our shield and virtue our sword,
Honor above all, forevermore.
