Mara and the midwife arrived at the tower in record time. Mara turned the wagon and horses over to a waiting stablehand and quickly followed the midwife inside. Mara noticed that Niffer hadn't moved from her place beside Crystal, and was still holding her hand and dabbing the sweat from her brow with a cool cloth. Crystal's breathing seemed a bit more labored then it had been before, and Mara quickly ran to her side. Calmly and smoothly, the midwife set them to tasks... fetching sheets to tear for bandages, boiling pots of water, and preparing teas. Once the girls had left the room, she sat beside Crystal, placing her hand on her stomach, she said, "this will just take a moment, I need to examine you." Crystal quietly nodded in response, to tired to say much.

Mara and Niffer returned with the cup of tea and other items the midwife had asked for... in unison they both asked, "how she is?" The midwife smiled reassuringly, "all seems to be progressing well, luckily there is not much bleeding though we will have to monitor her closely. She needs to conserve her strength, there is no telling how long before the labor progresses to transition and delivery." The young girls visibly breathed a sign of relief.

Taking the cup of tea, the midwife held it the Crystal's lips. Sipping slightly, Crystal cough, "I think you're trying to kill me you old witch," she managed to say jokingly. The midwife smiled, and held the cup to Crystal's lips silently insisting she finish the whole cup.

A commotion at the front door was heard, and within seconds Kondor appeared in the bedroom doorway, his face grave with concern, and his clothing covered in the dust from the road having ridden his horse at top speed since the messenger had reached him. The midwife quickly reassured him that all was well, and that she had just given Crystal something to help her sleep. Smiling with relief, he knelt beside Crystal and took her hand, kissing it softly. Crystal opened her eyes and smiled, whispering "I love you." Kondor bent forward to kiss his wife's forehead, "My dearest, I love you with all my heart and soul. Rest now, you will need your strength." Nodding slightly, Crystal closed her eyes again as the affects of the tea eased the pain and she drifted quietly in to sleep.

Softly the midwife directly every one to leave. As they all gathered around the fireplace the midwife instructed them, "Now all of you get some food and some rest, we have a long day ahead of us."
