Sunday, June 02, 2002

Goings on in Camelot


Must post more often, the Enosh-spam is just getting too intense... Anyway, here's some news from the land of Albion.


Well, not much has been going on in the way of major events recently, people have been ill, been away, been to the gathering, etc etc so we haven't had anything like a big guild-event like last report. However, we are coming along very nicely as a guild, with a large number of up-and-coming members soon to be reaching the higher levels so we can field a decent force of combatants for Realm vs. Realm combat. Currently I'm trying to organise more events that the Alliance as a whole can take part in. We've got a good community spirit going in the Alliance, but we need to start working together a bit more in mine, and others, opinions.


As to DAoC as a whole, well Mythic will be introducing the Holy Grail of player crafters soon, by finally allowing crafters to enchant their items and make them vastly superior to the dropped stuff. Of course, there is still ongoing 'tweaks' to the game, or nerfs if you listen to some people, that are changing the game. I think we're all having a lot of fun in the DAoC Chapter, and we've really got some good friendships going on, both in and out of game. We're a good bunch of people, honest, even if Cazz smells and doesn't shave, and Phyleus keeps wanting his bottom smited...
