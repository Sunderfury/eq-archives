Monday, September 30, 2002

Asheron's Call 2 PUBLIC Beta

As reported in the AC forums and on GameSpy's website, Asheron's Call 2 has entered the public beta phase. Head over to FilePlanet and download the current beta install. One thing to note, due to last weeks character wipe, the development team is allowing all new characters a jump to level 20 instead of 2 for completing the starter quest. Note that this gift of an instant mid level character is only good until this Friday's patch.
