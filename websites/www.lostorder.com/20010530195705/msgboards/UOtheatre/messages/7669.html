My trouble wasn't with a bard character though.

My dex-monkey/warrior type decided to pick up poisoning. So he wound up with the following skills:
100 fencing
100 tactics
100 archery
90 anatomy
75 healing
75 poisoning
95 alchemy
and the rest in resist

Now since I do tend to attack player-mages, resist is an extremely valuable skill. I need to keep it and improve it. But I can't have the resist and keep my other skills.

So I found a couple people who do nothing but make potions cheap (having no "mule" space left). I made a lot of the potions I could use for the next few months. I think I'll wind up making several kegs of deadly poisons too so I could try to finish my poisoning with my own supplies. And once I'm stocked up, I can drop alchemy for resist and poisoning.

If it comes down to it later, I can alter another of my characters to add alchemy, but for my warrior, the ability to kick tail is more important.
