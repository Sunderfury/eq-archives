Hucaluk Goes to Town

Me troll. Me no like desert. Desert hot. Me hot. Me want to go back to swamp. Nice and cold and dark in swamp.

Me see orcs up ahead. Maybe orcs know way back to swamp? Me ask them.

Not good idea. Orcs smash Hucaluk on head. World go dark.

But me wake up in swamp! Me glad to be in swamp. Me smile and jump and clap. Then me run from alligator.

Me tired of running from alligators. Me want to go back to Grobb. Other troll run by. Me follow troll. Maybe troll going to Grobb?

Other troll run for long time. Big swamp! Me lost. Me hope other troll not lost.

Me hear scary howl. Kobold run up and bite other troll. Me point at other troll and laugh.

Then other troll fall down. Other troll dead. Kobold not dead. Kobold look at me. Me no laugh no more. Me run away.

Me run fast. But me no run fast enough. Kobold bite Hucaluk on rear end. World go dark.

Me wake up in swamp. Me sick and tired of swamp. Mean things live in swamp. Try to hurt Hucaluk. Hucaluk no want to be hurt. Hucaluk want to go home.

Other troll run by. Me ask if he know way to Grobb. He say follow. Me follow. Me go to Grobb at last!

Me follow other troll to path. He point and say that way to Grobb. Me thank him. He good troll.

Me leave swamp. One day me return. You wait and see. One day me be powerful. Me no run from alligators. Alligators run from me! One day me wear alligator tooth necklace!
