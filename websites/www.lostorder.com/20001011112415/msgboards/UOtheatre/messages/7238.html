GRAND OPENING ANNOUNCEMENT - MYTHNDALE TAVERN & SHOPPE

On Saturday, August 12th, 8 p.m. to 11 p.m. eastern MythndaleTavern & Shoppe will have its grand opening. Located in Mythndale Village on the Chesapeake Shard (Trammel), nestled in the woods just off the Western Road outside Britain on the way to Trinsic (28o 33 S 14o 28 E).
Website (with map): http://www.members.home.net/astralwind/myth/ Come meet the citizens and staff while enjoying an evening of merriment and fun.

Agenda for the eve of the 12th:

8 p.m. - Door prizes, free furniture painting (BYOF bring your own furniture or purchase it from shoppe vendor), sales on finely crafted items and rare imports
9 p.m. - Entertainment
10 p.m. - Fashion show & auction (bid for a date with your favorite lady or brave knight)
11 p.m. - Drawing 50/50 (winner gets half the gold from auction)

Mythndale is a small village but already full of history and mystery. Its residents take a lot of pride in their home and serving the community. The abandoned mansion that resides just to the south of the tavern is said to be haunted, with strange sounds being heard at night and dark shadows lurking in its hallways.

The tavern is open year round - free food and beverages when staffed. A vendor is available for your refreshment needs at other times. Tavern is staffed (all hours eastern time): Sun. 8-10 p.m.; Mon. 9-11 a.m.; Tues. 8-10 p.m.; Wed. 8-10 p.m.; Thurs. 8-11 p.m.; and Fri. 8-10 p.m.

A variety of events and contests will be held weekly, as well as larger ongoing quests and adventures that will be scheduled on a regular basis (a calendar for the events will be posted on the Mythndale website)

The Tavern offers the finest wares crafted by grandmasters and exotic imports from around the world including amour & weapons, leather armor, archery supplies, magical ingredients (reagents), potions, scrolls, runes, furniture, house add-ons, magical items, rares and much more.

Other unique features include:
First Floor: Complete Rune Library for all your traveling needs, a fully equipped Workroom
Second Floor: Game Room & Hot Tub for relaxing after a hard day of adventuring, a Training Room for brushing up on weapon skills
Third Floor: Temple, Library, Meeting Room, Comfy Beds
Roof: Tavern featuring the finest foods and drinks in the land

Many Services are also available:
Free: Blacksmith Repairs; Furniture Painting; Item Identification
For Hire: Treasure Hunter; Weapons Poisoned; Dungeon Tours; Wedding Planning & Receptions
