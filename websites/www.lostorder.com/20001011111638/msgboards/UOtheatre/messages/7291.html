Alas, my friends, I tire of the world of Brittania. Her citizens nay appreciate my songs nor dirty limricks. Although I have so recently become a grandmaster bard, peacemaker, mage, meditator, evaluator of intelligence, and so on, the real fun of life came from interacting with others. Everything, from fighting evil (or good, he he) to relaxing in towne and chasing fair maidens (or whatever suits your fancy), those of us who truly live are oft forgotten. The people of the lands seem strange automatons to me. I shall sight several curious behaviors: When fighting evil, they seem to call out "mine!" and "wanna trade off?" or "heal" (without a "please, good sire" *shakes head*); secondly, in the depths of the darker hells, things are still "cool"?; thirdly, by the time I have composed a ballad or lymrick for someone, they have run off.

It has been more then five months since someone actually paid me (compliments, equipment, love, or gold) for a song, and I write each song for a specific occasion.

Indeed, friends, I tire of the land, and shall sadly depart. I am composing a farewell song in three parts (funny, satyric, and finally sad) and post it soon.

Anyone on Nappa that is VIRTUOUS and would like blessed black clothing (tunic, shirt, skullcap), blessed thighboots, about 300k, and a bag of 1000 of each reagent should let me know. PLEASE do not spam this board! I will post my email with my farewell song. The goods will go toward someone of virtue only! (I also have some stuff to give away on L.S...)

Yours in search of a land of true roleplaying souls,
Trinculo
