I wanted to pass along that there is a critical problem with Neverwinter Nights and the first patch as seen here and here that stops creatures from using their special attacks. For example, "You are beset by a Ancient Huge Red Dragon!! He will now chicken-scratch you to death, his breath is 'out of order'. Thank you, move along". I have gone the extra bit to test and you can back up your characters, uninstall, reinstall, (not patch) and play without problems (thus far) and the creatures will retain their lethal and fun-to-watch attacks. Tuesday, June 25, 2002

AC2 Beta Starts!

Posted on AC2 Warcry and other fans sites, as well as the official AC2 website:

Asheron's Call 2 Beta Has Begun!

June 25, 2002

We are pleased to announce that the first wave of AC2 Beta invitations were sent earlier today. These invitations went to randomly selected members of our AC1 player community. Those invited will receive a series of emails with information on how to join the Beta and the official AC2 Beta Community site (at: http://groups.msn.com/AsheronsCall2BetaCommunity).

Over time we plan to continue ramping up entrance into the AC2 Beta, and will announce when the next round of invitations are sent--hopefully soon! For more information on the AC2 Beta, be sure to read the official FAQ and Beta Security Announcement.

Thank you.
