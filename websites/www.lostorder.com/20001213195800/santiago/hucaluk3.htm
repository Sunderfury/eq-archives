Hucaluk in Love

What me do now? Me want to be powerful. Me want to be hero of all trolls! But me have to fight to get powerful. And when me fight, me always lose.

Me ask bartender for help. He say talk to Kaglari. Me ask who Kaglari is. He say she priestess of tower. Me ask what temple. He say troll tower. Me ask where troll tower is. He say go to cave. Me ask for ale. He say get lost.

Me go to troll tower. Me climb up stairs and stairs. Me see big lake of steamy hot fire. Me no want to fall in!

Me look up. Me see throne above big lake of fire. Me see most pretty troll Hucaluk ever see. Me heart go boom boom. World goes dark.

Me wake up and run up stairs. Me run to pretty troll. Me ask who she is. She say Kaglari. She ask what I want. Me heart go boom boom. Me ask if she can make Hucaluk powerful. She say yes. She give me magic scrolls. She say go read and come back later.

Me try to learn magic. But all me can think of is Kaglari. Me write poem instead:

Kaglari eyes is orange
Kaglari skin is green
Kaglari is smart and pretty
Kaglari should be queen

Hucaluk not smart but strong
Hucaluk hits and never misses
Hucaluk a happy troll
If Kaglari give him kisses

Me take many hours to write poem. Poems hard! Me no like to think. But me like Kaglari.

Me run up stairs and give poem to Kaglari. But she no like poem. She call Hucaluk stupid troll. She push Hucaluk over ledge. Me fall into big lake of fire! World go dark.

Me wake up in swamp. Me sad. Me write another poem:

Kaglari eyes is orange
Kaglari skin is green
But you no fall in love with Kaglari
‘Cause Kaglari very mean

Hucaluk strong but not smart
Hucaluk fall in love with Kaglari
But Kaglari throw Hucaluk in fire
And now Hucaluk very sorry

But me have magic now. Me powerful! Just you wait and see. One day me become hero of all trolls. Then Kaglari be sorry she threw Hucaluk away.
