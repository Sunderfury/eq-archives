Successful Relic Raid - Palomides Round Table Alliance
As reported by Namoric, senior member of our DAOC guild

I wanted to expand on just exactly what happened.

The call to arms started going off around Friday night. Folks getting the word that Dulcette of the Silver Daggers was planning a relic raid for Sunday morning, 10.00 am eastern. I logged on a bit early Sunday morning hoping to get to the merchant keeps and stock up on ram parts. Alas, all the merchant keeps where taken and NOT owned by Albion. So what I did was made what parts I could and bought a bit more. I logged Chao and Namoric in at about the same area and started the groups going.

Before we all dispatched and headed off, we had two full groups of Alliance members, and one 1/2 group.

Talk about an early morning turn out. I was getting Worried about Phyleus showing up until Sleeping (who was being played by Phy's X-Roomate Skitz) called Phy to wake his sorry arse up. Thank God we were running behind, cuz Phy made it on the last port, along with a BCK member whom I was holding a place for.

This whole time Kenderboy was out scouting the area and got ganked by a Shadowblade. We didn't make it out in time to rez him and he Auto-Released. Which sucks cuz he took 1/2 our ram with him. We didn't want to leave the Portal Keep and give the enemy any notice that ANYONE was there.

We got the the enemy Relic Keep and much to our HAPPY surprise: the outter door was still broken from a failed raid earlier in the week. Only 2 doors to break through. With machine like precision (we're talkin LOTUS type precision here man) we broke through the doors and into the relic keep all the while fighting off massive guard spawns - these guards are purple to a 50th level character. Only AFTER we secured out relic back and made it through the Mile Fort Gates (one of the last bastions of defense for the enemies if they want to stop us) do the tells start coming: Hibernian Groups forming at their Border Keep....

Oh crap .. HURRY ... See... We didn't have ANY of our relics and we can't capture any other relics until ours is safe and secure. Hibernia had ALL the strength relics and we wanted to take them as well once we had ours. Which means we have to hold our position with fast guard spawns and enemy reinforcements until our relic is safe back home. Guess what ... WE DID. As soon as the Scabbard of Excalibur hit it's resting place, Dulcettes groups snarfed up the remaining two Relics right at the same time the main group of Hibernian reinforcements show up... WE WALK RIGHT THROUGH THEM. They were no match for the well oiled machine that was Albion.

Also during this time, some Midgard Enemies showed up, hoping to stop us before we got back home to reclaim THEIR relic without having to do any of the work. Guess what... it wasn't happening. There were several groups left at key areas just to make sure this type of thing didn't happen.

Then: Disaster....

Dulcette, who was carrying the spear relic, went LD. She was on the Pad to Port back over to Albion then LD. The rest of the group got in the port... but no Dulcette... Oh S*** where is the relic? Search.... No Relic... Wait... guess who has the relic? YES

It was Given to AKALABETH to make the final run back home. given to our LVL 50 Armsman Phyleus to make the final leg of the journey. With groups forming in Castle Sauvage to protect the route to our Relic Keep, the Alliance groups proudly made our way to Castle Sauvage to put the THIRD and FINAL strength relic where it belongs... in Albion!!!

Phyleus - in a gesture that has earned him the respect of almost every guild and player in Albion - handed the Spear BACK to Dulcette, the raid leader, to be placed in its home.

The Palomides Round Table has become one of the most respected alliances in the realm. We're not the biggest nor the most RvR active - but we hold those values that make the Albion Realm so great: Honor, Integrity, Trust, Loyalty.... and Respect.

/salute
