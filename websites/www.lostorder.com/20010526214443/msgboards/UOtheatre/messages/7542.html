An amazing coincidence?

Posted by Dedigan on October 16, 2000 at 17:05:37:

So Niall and Kat decide to pay a visit to fire dungeon. Niall (me) is an archer, a fencer, and an alchemist. Kat is a mace fighting bard. On the way there, we kick the tails out of a hoard of fire elementals and efreet. Then we entered the dungeon.

The first bit was rather easy for us, we've hunted together before and our skills work together nicely. We reached the paved chamber that's routinely covered in liches and fire-thingies. We fought valiantly, and we died. We recover a bit, return, and died again. Then Dedigan came in to save the day. It took a while. In fact it took a long while, but Dedigan and Kat managed to recover what the liches didn't loot. In the process, we left a trail of dead liches any undead hunter would admire, but most had decayed by the time we finished leaving their gold in the ether.

Now's the coincidence part. After leaving such a trail of liches, I checked the last lich we killed for gold, not even thinking that it may have a bit more than expected. I pull up the bag and suddenly I've gone from weighing in at 180 stones to 457 stones. The line of liches progressively looted the previous one dry until at the end of it all, we had over 9,000 gold and wands to spare. We also retrieved everything the liches looted from ourselves (Yay! I got Niall's reagents back!).

I guess liches really are good looters, except they don't recall away to their houses after each looting.

We had to gate back to town since neither of us could move with all the stuff we carried, but all turned out well in the end.
