To A Cherished Friend
by unknown

I had wondered through
This world all alone
Never feeling your touch
Never hearing you song.

But then we met
In that hot desert sun
And I knew in my heart
That you were the one.

My life was empty
And dry like that land
But that has all ended
For you are my true friend.

The joys we share
The light you bring
Tis enough to make
This poor ranger sing.

So I put down these words
That to yours can't compare
But I want you to know
I will always be there!
