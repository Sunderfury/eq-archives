Hucaluk's New Armor

Ok so me not king uv all trollz.  It wuz good dreem, doe.

Me wake up on ground.  Me hav big bump on hed.  Dam doze dum froggeez!  Me hed herts now tanks to dem.  But day be sorry.  Me go bonk dem on da hed.  Den day see how it feelz.  Day not like it wen me make dem into froggee stoo.

Ok so me go run throo swomp.  Me like da swomp.  Iz nise an dark dair an fool uv nise tings to eet.  Like rats an snakes an froggeez an sumtimes a nome or a elf.  Elfs tayst good but yoo shood peel off da armer first or yoo get sik in da tummy.

Ok so me go run throo swomp like me sed beefor.  Me see all deez udder trollz.  Day hav all dis armer.  Me wunder wair day get it.  How day get so rich?  Me ask dem, “Hay!  How yoo get so rich?”

Well day no anser Hucaluk.  Day too bizzy killin all da froggeez.  Dats good koz me no like da froggeez.  But me want to bonk sum froggeez too!  Me say, “Hay yoo dum trollz, let me bonk sum froggeez too!”

But day no lissen to Hucaluk.  So me go bak to Grobb.  Me sad koz me never gonna be king uv all trollz if dis keeps up.

Den gess wot happened!  Me bet yoo never gess.  So me tel yoo.  Me saw da most bootiful woomin in da hole world!  Yup, yoo herd me.  Even mor bootiful dan Kaglari!

Me turn around an dair she wuz.  Like a big green aynjel.  She had deez dark ize dat wuz like poolz uv swomp wudder.  Her noze wuz big an her teef wuz sharp an she had big eerz.  She had udder tings dat wer big too but me not say wot doze wer or me get in trubble.  Less just say dat wen she wokked she jiggled an bounced an por Hucaluk’s hart went boom boom boom.

Den gess wot happened!  O why me even ask yoo?  Yoo never gess.  So me tel yoo.  She sed, “Hullo, Hucaluk!”  Yup, dat iz wot she sed.  She noo hoo Hucaluk wuz!

She tokked for long time.  Me tried to lissen but it wuz hard to lissen wen she had deez nice big jiggly bouncy parts to look at.  She wuz nise troll doe.  She sed her name wuz Putoona.  She giv me all dis nise armer an sed I kood hav it.

She had dis liddle nome with her.  She call him Woozle.  Me not no why she had dat liddle nome.  Maybee for brekfest or sumthin.

But den da gard came along an skwoosht dat poor liddle nome wit hiz foot.  Putoona wuz sad.  She sed she had to go get a noo wun frum sumwair.  Me just sed ok an wocht az Putoona run off into da swomp.

An dat wuz da last time me ever saw dat bootiful troll.  All me hav to rumember Putoona iz dis noo armer wich iz good koz now me kan go an get ruvenj on doze dam froggeez!  Gess wot Hucaluk hav for brekfest now?  He hav froggie stoo!  Mmmmm!
