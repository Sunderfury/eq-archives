Ode to Our Lady of Frivolous Merriment
by Orbison Windjammer

*picks up his mandolin and begins playing*

Oh, wonderful Lady, hold your head high
It breaks all our hearts to see that you cry

Surely you know, you're the heart of this faction?
You bring us all joy, with each little action.

You work and you labor both night and day
All for the love of the LOA

And in spite of the trials you have to face
You always come through with style and grace

And never have doubt, that the things that you do
Inspire so many to look up to you

And rest assured, Lady, wherever you lead
We will all follow, attending each need

I speak for the guild, who all share this sentiment
Bless you , our Lady of Frivolous Merriment
