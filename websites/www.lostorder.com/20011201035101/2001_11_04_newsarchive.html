Tuesday, November 06, 2001
Several things to mention
LOA is having a blast in Dark Age of Camelot. We have about 40 members and friends involved in the fun thus far. We currently range in levels from 8 to 21. We are making every effort to create a trade system, better grouping organization and preparing our members eventually for Realm vs Realm combat. If you are interested in learning more about LOA's presence in Dark Age of Camelot, please visit our forums and jump right in (click here for forums). One of the very great things we have experienced is that our allies from other games are also playing on the same servers as we are. We have had the pleasure to group with BCK and STC.

I have heard word that our Asheron's Call chapter leader purchased a cottage in Dereth. With housing now in AC, our members will most likely have a place to gather and socialize (much like our village in Ultima Online). Congrats to Enosh for procuring the cottage and sharing the place with fellow members.

Several members of our guild received notices in the mail regarding Shadowbane Beta 3. We updated our profiles and are now awaiting the next step. I have to admit that DAOC took the wind out of my SB sails... but after seeing new screenshots of Shadowbane, I am looking forward to giving it a test drive. I can tell that a hard decision will be facing me in the coming months... I love Dark Age VERY MUCH. The game is just incredible and tons of fun to play. Shadowbane has HUGE potential to be equally incredible. Decisions... Decisions...

That is all I have time for today. I hope to post again soon with more updates and events that are taking place in LOA.
