Wood Elf

Razi's View
The Wood Elves in EverQuest represent the standard view that most people have of Elves - forest dwelling, animal loving, tree hugging style. They reside deep within the Greater Feydark forest, in a town of wooden platforms and huts suspended high above the ground, a town named Kelethin. They have all of the expected Elven features - the long flowing hair, the pointy ears, the sharp and distinctive face. It is only in this way that they are in common with their cousins whom live nearby in Felwithe, the High Elves. Wood Elves are generally much more down to earth and get stuck in a bit more in battle than the High Elves, who are predominantly a magic using version - similar in magical abilities to the Erudites. The Wood Elves are very nimble and agile, and are at home on the forest floor amongst the trees.

Their home town, Kelethin, is a true spectacle. Comprised of many wooden platforms held high above the forest floor, many an unwary person has fallen to their, rather painful, deaths on the ground below. The Wood Elves are suited to their true nature of wandering the forests and protecting the wildlife in the area, especially with the Druid class. Although not the strongest of the races, Wood Elves are primarily based upon their high Agility and Dexterity, which means that the idea is not to hit hard and often, but to hit decisively and dodge incoming attacks. The classes reflect this strategy as each class is generally aimed towards inflicting injury whilst not being in direct combat (Rogue's backstab, Bard's music, Ranger's and Druid's magics). There is of course the path of the warrior, and this is certainly an option with the Wood Elf, but if you weren't necessarily in love with the Swyrel type, you would be better off with a different race for that path.

Wood Elves are between the small and medium size categories, so are fortunate enough to have the choice between small and medium sized armor. Also, being of the Elven race, where would they be without the Infravisions - a standard with all Elven races. They share a continent with their fellow kin, the High Elves who are a short distance to the East, as well as the Dwarves and Gnomes. The main port to Antonica is not far off. The WE are generally known as being a polite and well mannered race, not as 'stuck-up' as their brothers in Felwithe. They spend the early part of their lives patrolling the Greater Feydark forest, killing off evil and protecting the wild life.

Bottom Line
Of the two 'good' aligned version of Elves, the Wood Elves are more physical and agile in combat than the High Elves who concentrate on the magical side of things. They are the typical elven representation, living amongst the trees and foraging for food in the bushes (a skill which is available early on). As their typical nature demonstrates, they are mainly made up of Rangers and Druids, protecting the forests from any evil that dares to enter. A word of warning, should you choose to live amongst the trees, be careful where you step when you are 50 ft above the solid ground!
