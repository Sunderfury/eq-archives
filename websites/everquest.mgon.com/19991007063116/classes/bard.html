Bard

From 989: "The Bard’s stock-in-trade is his ability to play songs. His songs are actually spells of a sort, and their magical affects last as long as the Bard continues to sing and play the correct instrument. They have some passing knowledge of the Warrior ways (mainly for self-defense), but their main focus is always their art, and so they will never be as skillful as Warriors."

The Bard class at first may not seem attractive to most players. Especially the ones who intend to 'Go Solo' But for the social person, Who enjoys grouping, This is definitely a fine choice of class. Although not the best class in hand to hand combat, Bards as their name suggests use song to make a living. The most interesting thing about this is that their songs require no mana, Their tunes can be played indefinitely and quite often affect all members of a party, or all mobs in the vicinity, they can also even run while playing their tunes while other magic users have to stop and then cast. Bards will be welcomed into groups for this reason, One will increase the group performance, With two or three the group would absolutely rock. Skill wise Bards get some of the warrior and rogue skills, They are the only non-rogue class that can pick locks.
