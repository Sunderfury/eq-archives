EA Falls
Reported by: Matt   0 Comments | Add  

Electronic Arts Inc said fiscal third-quarter earnings fell 5.3 percent on sluggish demand for games. Net income was 88 million dollars compared with .9 million since last year. Sales rose a lower-than-expected 6.6 percent to 640.3 million from 600.7 million.

It's the first time earnings during the holiday season have declined for Electronic Arts in at least the last 10 years. The decline seems to be due to the lower than expected sales of software due to the low amount of PS2s available.
