Age of Sail II In Stores
Reported by: Zhou   0 Comments | Add  

Talonsoft announced that it's new real-time naval combat game, Age of Sail II, started shipping to stores.

Age of Sail II, the sequel to the 1996 Age of Sail, allows players to pick from the navies of 11 great empires between the 1775-1820 time frame. Done fully in 3D, the game also has a camera that allows precise control of groups of ships in combat situations. Three dimensional coastal landscaping presents a stratgetic problem for would-be admirals to overcome. Gamers are also able to play each other via the Internet or by a LAN connection with a maxium of 16 players.

Age of Sail II has a suggested retail price of 39.95(US). Click here to visit the official Age of Sail II website.
