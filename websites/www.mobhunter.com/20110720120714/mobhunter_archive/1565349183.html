All Quiet on the Weekend Front

by Moorgard on 2001-09-08

Absor talked about corpses a bit over the last couple days, clarifying how much time you have to res your dead arse should you meet your maker.

To be clear, if a naked corpse can receive an exp res (i.e. not a duel or sacrifice corpse), you have the following time:

Level 1 - 5: 30 minutes
Level 6+: 24 hours of online time, or 1 week of offline time, whichever comes first.

After some discussion, Absor admitted that in the latter case it's actually not one or the other. Your corpse will be around for 7 days, but being online accelerates the timer. So you could actually be online for 12 hours and offline for 3.5 days and your corpse will go bye-bye. So don't do that.
