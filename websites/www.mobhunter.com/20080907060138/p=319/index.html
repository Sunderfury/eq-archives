It Begins Again

At long last, another season of the NFL is underway. It may not have been the most thrilling game to kick things off, but it was a relief to get past the preseason nonsense and see the starters play with some gusto.

Of course the real season begins on Monday when my beloved Vikings trounce the hapless Packers on their way to winning the Superbowl. Go Vikes!

(That’s the beauty of the first week of the season–anything is possible!)
