Handy Acronyms

The blokes at Rock, Paper, Shotgun posted a list of PC gaming acronyms packed with several chuckles. The one that made me laugh out loud was the definition of RPG:

While many games ask you to play a role, the only ones that earn this title are those that ask you to play a role that would socially ostracise you if other people found out.

Yeah, good stuff.
