Roger is the toughest to please. He watches a ton of movies but thinks very few of them are worthwhile. Shwayder is the opposite, liking (or at least not complaining about) most things he sees. The Role of Story in Virtual Worlds

I love good stories; most of us do. It’s probably this love that ultimately inspired me to become an English major, hoping that I would someday have the good fortune to be able to tell stories for a living.

When I announced last year that I was transitioning from community manager to game designer, I wrote a post (archived here) explaining that one of my main reasons for doing so is that I enjoy telling stories:

“After being allowed to express my creativity by working on some of the game’s background lore… it became clear to me that I wanted to spend more of my time telling stories to an audience, and that’s ultimately what moving to design will allow me to do.”
