A Brief Work History of Steve Danuser

(More comprehensive version available upon request – mail to: sdanuser at gmail.com)

Overview

Creative writer, game designer, world architect, evangelist, storyteller, and leader. Specialist at originating and nurturing games and intellectual properties from inception to launch. Director-level experience within high-pressure creative environments to foster and manage teams. Able to establish, enforce, and evangelize vision for products and IP across multiple projects and studios.

Experience

38 Studios, LLC, Providence, RI – November 2006 – May 2012

  • Key Accomplishments

    • Interfaced and collaborated with best-selling author R. A. Salvatore and artist Todd McFarlane to champion their story and artistic vision

    • Architected modular world and history timeline capable of supporting multiple game titles and ancillary products within continuity of Amalur franchise

    • Oversaw integration of Amalur IP into Big Huge Games RPG engine, resulting in successful launch of Kingdoms of Amalur: Reckoning in 2012

    • Directed creative and narrative design for MMORPG, codenamed Project Copernicus

Director, Story & IP Development – Overseer of IP across Providence and Baltimore Studios (12/2011 – 05/2012)

Creative Director – Established and drove creative vision for the Project Copernicus MMO (06/2009 – 12/2011)

Lead Content Designer / Director of Community – Built content design team for Project Copernicus / set vision and goals for community department (11/2006 – 06/2009)

Sony Online Entertainment, San Diego, CA – August 2003 – October 2006

  • Key Accomplishments

    • Served on leads team for EverQuest II MMORPG, launched successfully in 11/2004

Game Designer – Created lore for the EverQuest franchise and built content for EQ2 live game and expansions

Community Manager – Created a vibrant and healthy MMO community, setting standards for community management
