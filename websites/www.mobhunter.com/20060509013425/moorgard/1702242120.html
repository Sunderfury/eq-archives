Another interesting tidbit shows up farther down the page in the story about the Statue. We all know that the English language is in a constant state of evolution, but this might be pushing it a bit too far. Tigole has taken the incident that resulted in the guild Conquest getting disbanded and has made a new word. Apparently, "Conquest-ed" is now a synonym for being exploited.

I kind of like this new word, though I think it better to go with the simpler form of "Conquested." The capital letter and the context of the word in the sentence should be enough to make the meaning clear.

Thanks for increasing our vocabulary, Tigole!
