I Have Always Depended on the Kindness of Strangers

Over the last couple of weeks, I’ve gotten some very nice messages from people. First, sending condolences over the loss of my kitty, Max, and this week offering congratulations on the announcement of my move to design. Hopefully I responded to everyone, but if I missed any, please accept my thanks.

It’s easy sometimes in a job like this, in which I’m so often communicating with those who are angry or unhappy about something, to lose sight of the fact that the vast majority of people are generous and kind. As members of a high-tech global society prone to isolating ourselves behind keyboards and video screens, we forget that there are feelings and emotions we all share.
