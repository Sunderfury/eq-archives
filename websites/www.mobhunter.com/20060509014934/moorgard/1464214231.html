More Views of Luclin

by Moorgard on 2001-06-27

Looks like pictures galore are surfacing of Shadows of Luclin--though there's not a ton of new stuff to see.

Voodoo Extreme posted three new screenshots, and GameSpyDaily posted an impressive twenty-five of them.

You'd think that with all these new shots we'd be seeing, well, something new. But almost all of the character shots are just different views of the same models we've seen for a while now. Granted, there are some impressive NPC and landscape shots, but we really want to see character models, after all.

However, with the release of Anarchy Online today, you can see why Verant would want to ensure that people are still paying attention.

And we really shouldn't complain. Any new Luclin shots are like free sample day at the methadone clinic for addicts like us.
