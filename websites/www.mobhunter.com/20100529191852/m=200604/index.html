It’s the Little Things

I hit level 70 the other night in EQ2. That’s quite a landmark for me, since I wasn’t close to 50 yet when the first expansion came out and barely hit 60 in time for Kingdom of Sky. So allow me to honor this moment by geeking out a bit.

It was a really great ding, you see. A friend and I were duoing heroic ravasects in the hive. We were fighting one when a roamer added, and it was hitting us pretty hard. A few fancy bruiser moves later and we were down to one mob, but my health was plummeting. I was sure I was just about to meet my demise on the next hit when the bug fell over instead, and I was awash in a big fancy particle swirl proclaiming my feat.

On the Road Again

Early (too damn early) tomorrow morning I board a plane that will eventually lead me to Atlanta for FanFaire. As you might expect, this will likely cut into my posting a bit.

I always have a great time at FanFaire. Though we tend to go in with a certain wariness that we might be swarmed by angry players, people who attend are virtually always extremely kind and fun to talk to. By the end of the event, despite several late nights of too much drinking and not enough sleep, we always feel energized by all the nice things folks say to us.

I’ve never been to Hotlanta before, and it’s unlikely I’ll have time to see much of the city, but hopefully I’ll get a chance to look around a little at least. Ideally surrounded by people who owe me lots and lots of drinks.
