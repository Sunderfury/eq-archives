I R Not Mentally Irregular

Good news! The American Medical Association is not ready to conclude that video game addiction is a legitimate mental illness. This means that when your mom comes down into the basement to yell at you about how much time you spend playing WoW, you can show her proof that it’s not an addiction. At least, not yet.

Bad News! Those of you who, like me, were planning an elaborate workman’s comp scheme to live on easy street thanks to your “illness” will just have to wait until the AMA comes to its senses. Hurry up, already!

…And We’re Back

The site has been kaput for the better part of a week. There wasn’t a lot I could do about it since my irresponsible site admin (Shwayder) was selfishly trotting about the globe as you, my loyal readers, had to face a world without my wisdom. Let the wailing and gnashing of teeth subside, for your favorite website is back.

While we were apart, I was sent further proof that I am a man ahead of my time. A recent poll shows that I’m far from alone in my opinion that words such as “blog” and “blogosphere” are offensive. No doubt my influence has spread across the globe. And since the poll was written about on chinadaily, you know it’s true.

Thank goodness I can now stop posting responses on other people’s boards and get back to writing on my own.
