Solidarity!

Why the lack of updates lately? Because as a charter member of the Bloggers Guild of America (BGA), I have chosen to support my brothers and sisters by refusing to break the picket lines. Until our demands are met, I must withhold my wisdom from the world and refrain from posting.

Well, except for this post. Which doesn’t count. Seriously.

Shame, shame, SHAME upon all those bloggers who refuse to support our cause! Our demands will never be met if we don’t stick together.

On a related note, the management of Moorgard.com has informed me that unless I agree to start posting again, I will be replaced by a scab writer. But fear not, loyal readers, I’m sticking to my guns. We won’t let The Man push us around!

If there are any artists out there who can help me make up some picket signs, please drop me a note.
