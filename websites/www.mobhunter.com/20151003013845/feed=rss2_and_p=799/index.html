I like the personal quests in GW2 but once those sort of ran out, I just felt like I was wandering from spot to spot, doing heart quests, collecting vistas or talents while waiting for an event to trigger/start.

It sounds like there was a lot to do, but it wasn’t things I enjoyed for a length of time. Vista jump puzzles? Bleh. Talent/trait battles? Pretty much a stomp the NPC with all the other people there to get their trait (in the odd occasion it was more about clicking on a spot). The events, themselves, weren’t all that spectacular and were pretty repetitive.

Don’t get me wrong, in no way am I saying WoW questing is better – it isn’t. I just don’t think GW2’s mechanism warrants the credit it is getting… unless you’re an explorer type. It really caters well to people that like exploring.
