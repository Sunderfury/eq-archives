Minnesota MMO Blog Explosion!

Being up in the Minneapolis area for the week taking care of the finalÂ moving details, it seemed like a great time to get together with some of my fellow gamers/bloggers/deviants who hail from the Land of 10,000 Lakes. That’s right, peeps, Minnesota has the greatest gamer community around!

Kendricke (he who dances with great vigor and talks really loud), Brent from VirginWorlds (he who podcasts on the drive home from work), Cuppycake (she who has an RSS feed implant at the base of her skull), and I met up at the Mecca of Minnesota, the Mall of America. Though we started off at a very nice sushi stand, neither Cuppy nor I are big on raw seafood. (Beer, however, is another story.) So after the sushi place kicked us out (not sure if it was closing time or the loud jokes about doing inappropriate things to farm animals), we moved on to the one placeÂ we knew our rowdy crew would fit rightÂ in: Hooters.

Kendricke and Cuppy provide hints at some of the quotes that came up last night. Brent is probably still wondering what the hell he got himself into. Suffice it to say that we covered a wide variety of topics, both gaming related and so totally not. Don’t bother asking — we’re keeping most of them as inside jokes we can treasure for years to come.

I had a total blast, and it reminded me how much I love hanging out with my fellow gamers. We’re all freaks, but freaks with a common love: alchohol MMOs!
