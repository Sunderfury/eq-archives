Anatomy of a Move, Part 1

Step 1: San Diego to Phoenix (Friday). Sore from packing that morning made the short drive less enjoyable than usual. Stayed in Phoenix a few days and drank beer. Tried to go to the Heart Attack Grill but failed due to the fact that they aren’t open on weekends (/boggle).

Step 2: Phoenix to Denver (Monday). Long-ass drive. Staring at the wasteland that is northern New Mexico makes the mind do stupid things, such as dream up cool bard mechanics. I arrived at Shwayder’s mom’s house, where she was waiting with lasagna and olive bread. <3 Shwayder’s mom, h8 Shwayder.

Step 3: Denver to Lincoln (Tuesday). Yes, Nebraska, to see my sister and brother-in-law. Though Nebraska is not exactly thrilling to drive through, it still rates higher than eastern Colorado. Yeesh.

Step 4: Lincoln to Minneapolis (Wednesday). I shall head home for a few days of R&R. Probably drop off a few things, pick up a few more things. Such is the nature of moving.

More to follow.

Armchair Critic: Jackass Number Two

This weekend past I journeyed to the local cinema to take in a screening of a recent film called Jackass Number Two. I knew very little about the movie before sitting down in the theater, though it is my understanding that it is a sequel to an earlier production. After being transfixed for the better part of two hours as I drank in the sociological commentary of this film, rest assured that I will be seeking out the first chapter in this epic saga with all due haste.
