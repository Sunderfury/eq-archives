Marching Off to WAR

That is to say, trying to.

I’m currently waiting in a queue so that I can jump onto Volkmar and join the rest of the Casualties with my marauder named Moor. He’s kind of like a monk with a mutated arm.

Bit of a bummer to be waiting in line when it’s only collector’s edition people who should be able to play. I guess that either speaks to the number of units sold or the capacity of the servers.

Why aren’t you people watching football instead of adding to my queue?
