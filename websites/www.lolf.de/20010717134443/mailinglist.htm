Come on, if you can play EverQuest, you are bound to have an email address, even if you don't read it regularly. You need to create an account on eGroups with that address. Then go to our eGroups page and subscribe yourself to the mailing list. You will get one mail from the mailing list owner, and you have to reply to that mail (or something like that).

Now you can ignore the mails that go to your email address, and just read the mailing list on their web site. No need to check your mail every hour.
