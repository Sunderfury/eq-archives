VII ) The Age of the Tangleroot June 2001 - August 2001

As you have heard the leadership was passed on to Meethos Tangleroot.

If you have not heard of him I try to explain what kind of Halfling he was. Whenever you needed a port, help in a quest he was there for you. Whenever you been in a sad mood, he cheered ya up. Meethos was a natural leader, a great person and he had really great goals with us. The Legions morale was down, the weight to take over the leadership from master Xzandar was unbelievable.

Meethos had to fight many problems. While old legionaires like Darvan returned home, many legionaires left us. Meethos, as a high level druid, leaded us into a few completly awesome raids beyond our imagination at this time. Together with our great Templar Kaisersos they lifted us up into the ranks of a small raiding guild.

Epic Quests had been implemented into the game and a new land, Velious, had been discovered. I remember a raid to Velketor with Gotrik (before they changed Velketor's Labyrinth to Karnors 2) - it was a VERY thrilling raid. One step forward and we all would be toasted by a huge AE Trap. (Gotrik found this out later). He just told us that he has a bad feeling at the moment and that he prefers to port out now.

Meethos, as our leader, set us high goals. One of this goald was Kaisersos' Epic Ragefire Camp. The whole guild worked together on this, we built shifts and cleared all those Fire Giants away. We had all been put under an enormous pressure. We achieved the goal but we payed a high price for it. This camp left a deep scar in the guild and splitted us. It was a time where the guild had not the best time.

Meethos gaev his best but I felt that he put to much pressure unto his own shoulders and lifted us up to a *too* high position, for which the guild was not ready yet. Things had been said that no one wanted to say and people had been hurt and emotions went high.

In this time I moved houses in RL. I thought all had become good again. Due a mistake I had almost 3 weeks with no phone connection in my new flat. After 2 weeks my phone rang...
