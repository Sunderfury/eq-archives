IRC

We *also* have a regular chat channel on IRC, in the DALnet. To connect to this channel, do the following:

  • Read www.irchelp.org if you are unfamiliar with IRC.
  • Install some IRC client.
  • Point the client to irc.dal.net or irc.eu.dal.net (Europeans), port number 6667 (which is the default in most clients).
  • Join the channel #lolf.
  • If you like to use THIS chat, then go ahead and register your nickname with DALnet (optional) and post your decition on our forums. :-)
