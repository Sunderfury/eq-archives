V) The Age of the Next Generation (July 2000 - October 2000)

I am sitting in my room in Ak'anon and trying to write the next chapter in our guild history for all of the upcoming generations. Dim candlelight surrounds me as I stare at the wall with a pencil in my hand. There are all my notes and the pictures that I took in my journeys and it's hard to bring all the stories together. All the names of friends I made in the Legion come back in my head. Well for the guild the last months were the best time ever. Friendships moved closer together and we are really like a big family.

Well, as you know the guild had several leaders. The guild was huge once, crumbled down, grew again and in a few weeks we start our first Kedge Raid. What a great thing! After master Xzandar came back from his exile we were "cured". The ones who joined just for twinking or power leveling fell away. We did our first Runnyeye Raid and it was a really great thing. We stormed the citadel (25 members!) and had a lot of fun although it was the first time that a few members died during an event (it happened before but not in that high numbers). Bad thing too was that we had no high level cleric at this point for ressurection. A wood elf tried to spam our guild in Misty Thicket during the event, he killed halfling guards. So myself and the fearless Eddward (who guarded me) showed him real Little Folk power and he fled (after a short discussion, he really ran away).

The Applepies Clan grew again and we have 6 members now. Filastil, Filumtil, Muppan, Illerion, Ikipu and myself are wearing the surname Applepie now and we spread fear and terror over all tall and evil races =:-).

And I am so happy to see another Clan coming up. The Tangleroots are close friends to the Applepies. Meethos, Meebork and Gillain are the members till now. I see the last weeks that not only low or mid level players joined, we have many members in the 40s and 50s who joined us the last months, this gives us more possibilities in questing things and do really high events like the Hole Event. Tototot joined, a longtime friend of mine and one of the best brewers I met (you have to taste his Ginesh!) and I like his laugh (Har har har). Players come and goe and we had many new ones who brought many new ideas and spirit in the guild but we had losses as well. Dameont, an old Necromancer gnome and former guild leader quitted the guild. He is seen rarely in Norrath but if you meet him, remember that he was once one of us and give him a friendly Legion hoooo! My full respect goes to Haradmer and Ticky for the Idea of the "Santa Ticky" events on Saturday. And to Winzigklein for the ambitions to start our guild lottery. We had our first war in this time also against the Dark Legion. We kicked them through the whole Arena, mass over might. Lets end this chapter of our history with the walk through Droga. The fearless Applepie Filastil showed us around and the raid was a full success (until he tried to cast a spell and gated the whole high level group out at the end of the raid, accidently ..eheheh - imagine the faces of the rest of us when our live savers just ported out).
