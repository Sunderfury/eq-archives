World Clock

Please activate Java to see a chart of the clock around the world.

This applet is from http://www.arachnoid.com/lutusp/worldclock.html.

How this is intended to be used:

1. In case of an emergency, that is, the EQ servers are down, check the official network status. You will find an entry from "today" with a time, say "07:10". That entry will read something like "All servers are currently down for scheduled patching. Estimated down time is 7 hours." or something like that.

2. Now we know that the servers should be up at 07:10 plus 7 hours, that is 14:10, or for those used to am/pm, 02:10 pm.

3. Now take a look at the applet. Your own time is highlighted with a blue bar (make sure that it displays the right time). Verant uses the PDT/PST time zone, which is also called "GMT-8". So, by looking at the applet, in the line with "GMT-8", you see what time it is currently in Verant's building. You can assume that the servers will soon come up when you see something like "14:10" or "02:10pm" in the line with "GMT-8".
