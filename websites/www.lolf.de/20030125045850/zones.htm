What is this all about?

On this page, the members with the greatest knowledge about the zones will be listed.

If you like to learn about a new zone, you can ask these persons about it (for example to give you a walkthrough to the zone, if their time permits; or for helping about a new raid you want to lead).

On the other hand, being on this list obviously brings fame and glory to you if you are listed here. :-)

Being listed with a symbol means that that person will lead a guild raid in that zone if asked (pending that a good date is found).

On the third hand, if you see that something vital is missing here, you may choose to learn about that zone for the betterment of the legion!

Please note that to keep the list to a small size, only a few people will be listed for each zone.

Send your zone mastery to filumtil@pluto.camelot.de.
