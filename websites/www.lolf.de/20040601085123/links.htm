Good current information and news about EQ; this page displays current posts from Gordon Wrinn (Abashi) and is a sugested reading before every EQ session. All information is accompanied by discussion boards, this especially makes the spell sections a huge pool of knowledge.

The static information in this page seems to be comparable to EverLore; but the site seems to be much faster. It's has not much original content, but specializes in very good (moderated) link sections for each topic.
