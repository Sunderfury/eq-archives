Who is the Legion Of Little Folk?

The legion of little folk is made up of all us small folks ready to take over Norrath on the Povar server of EverQuest.  We are a big family and help each other over anyone else.

We believe that there is power in numbers and that in numbers we can conquer all... Legion hoooooooooooo!!!!!!!!!!!!

Is the Legion a roleplaying guild?

Depends on the definition of "roleplaying". :-)

Excessive twinking and powergaming is not part of our guild. But we don't force everyone to be "in role" all the time, either, using fancy language etc.. We usually find a solution in the middle of those extremes. There are times of playing out a role (they are most fun, but also most hard to sustain), and there are times of talking about the game itself or even (seldomly) things in real life.

Is the Legion a "newbie" guild?

The Legion has many newbies, if you count everybody below level 20 as newbie, and it is especially friendly to those members. But it is definitively no newbie only guild; almost all members stay in the guild even when they reach high levels, and quite a few high level members have joined the guild. We currently have at least two members above level 50, quite a few in their 40's, and many 30's.

How big is the Legion?

There is a member list yet; at the bottom you find the count of all registered members. There may be a selected handful of members who haven't yet registered themselves. In November 2000, the Legion consisted of about 150 registered members, at least 90 of them really active.

Why doesn't anyone answer my tells about joining?

If an officer or member is not answering your tells one of three things is happening:

  1. He or she is busy fighting
  2. Afk (away from keyboard)
  3. Doesn't want to speak with you. This most probably means that you are one of the large people... ;-)

What is a guild?

A guild is a group of people that band together with the same goals and interests in mind, kind of a club or gang. We treat each other like brothers and sisters and do whatever we can to help the guild.

How do I list all online members of the legion?

Use the following command to list all members of the guild that are currently online:


         /who all "legion of little folk"
or 
     
         /who all GUILD
Best put it on a hotbutton (click on "Socials", then right click on one of the small buttons).

Who manages this web site?

The Legion home page was founded by Dameont, one of the former leaders of the guild, who now plays only occasionally. It has been vastly extended and is now being actively maintained by the person behind Filumtil (filumtil@pluto.camelot.de). All requests concerning the page (especially screenshots and stories) should be sent by mail. You can also use /tell in the game to talk to me. But it is NOT enough to post something on the discussion board since I cannot guarantee that I'll read everything there in time.

Please give me up to 5 work days until I process your mail.

I want to include my image from the member listing on the message board, what is the URL?

It does not work. Images from homepage.com can not be used on ezboard (or any other server except homepage.com). You have to find some other server on the web where you can store your image and reference it from there (ask a friend, often students have free web server access).
