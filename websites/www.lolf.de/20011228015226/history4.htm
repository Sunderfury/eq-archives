IV)The Age of Awakening (April 2000 - Juli 2000)

One of the main problems were that there were no officers available if you needed them. So Xzandars first step was to lift up a handful of legionaires into the officer rank of the guild. New members joined and the guild started to grow again. Very quick! The new leader spent many hours to quest items with members of the guild, you all know him and I stop my hymns of him know (I try...).

I remember one night when Xzandar, Wezzin (my father) and I tried to get my enshroudment robe... we camped for the globe of shadow in Guk and tried to kill the Nocta Priest for 5 hours. And the globe didnt drop. Another gnome joined us and we tried to help each other. I was so tired I thought I'd fall from my chair. At 4am I gave up. I had to work the other day and I told Xzandar that I thought that this globe will never drop. So we left Guk very depressed and tired. We entered South Ro and Xzandar told me, that the other gnome looted the globe one minute ago. In that moment I felt a strange pain in my stomach, in other words I felt like the an idiot. 10 minutes later! But he was not angry with me, not one bad word.(funny sidenote: next time we tried it took around 10 minutes. I sneaked throgh Guk, saw the priest, gave Xzandar a tell he was bound in Guk, he gated there, killed the priest and I looted the globe.) Argh! Again such a long story from me hehe, nevermind, back to the Legion.

One day i hunted in Mistmoore and i met Ducati Bombati. He was an Officer of the Gnomeless Savage guild (gnome only) - we sweared friendship and an alliance between our guilds to combine the efforts of the small races. A few weeks later we had our first mass event in Crushbone together with them. We camped Dvinns Tower and I can remember in funny memories that someone entered Crushbone and shouted "someone camping Dvinn ?" The second shout was "yes, about 50 people" and then the screen was spammed with our HOOOOO and the Gnomeless Savages' TAHOO! battlecrys :). We had several Events like the one in Blackburrow or more Legion only battlegroups in Kunark.

I often visited Steamfont Mountains to recruit new members, I think it was a very good time for the guild. Yes, i believed in mass over might at this time. Friends of mine joined Everquest, Filastil and Filumtil, and the idea of the Applepie Clan was born. A dark shadow crawled upon the guild. When I entered the guild the guild was completly different. I remeber names like Bertlov, Wezzin,Grimburn, Vivictus and Kanje, Terondaf, Liara or the powerfull Darkan Doubleblade to name just a few.

We had a strong basement of members. All this vanished because we recruited to many members and a few bored the rest of the guild of stupid questions about powerleveling and twinking. To get the strong basement back that we had once Xzandar made a hard decission. He broke the first rule "mass over might" and stopped the mass recruitment for the first time. I think it was a hard but wise thing to do that. We didnt recruit over one month but I made a good experience in this time. All the ones that were really interested joined us after the stop (they waited weeks like me). Ikipu and Binadur for example.

And Xzandars plan worked out. We came to new power in the guild. A few left but these are the ones who are not interested in the guild so we didnt care about them =) In the month we didnt recruit master Xzandar went to Kunark on exile. He was there to work mental on new ways to strengthen the guild and himself. After weeks of meditation and silence he came back with new power and is now leading us into the next age.
