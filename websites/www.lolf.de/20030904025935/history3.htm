III) The Age of Darkness (Dezember 1999 - April 2000)

Schiro disapeard and gave the leadership to Yulonda. Well she was not a officer but i think the other officers knew what difficult task it is to be a guild leader. She agreed but she could not stop the many members leaving the guild. There was no way to stop that and the guild shrunk to a minimum of players. I think she was very enthusiastic at the beginning of her carrier but she saw that she was not able to keep the binds together. She lifted Leitbur up in the officer ranks. Sad thing she was bugged and Leitbur waited over on month before he could invite members! She was leader for about 2 months and left the guild for private reasons, though she is still in the game. She gave the leadership to an old gnome who most of you know.

Dameont gave his best to keep the guild together, but the guild was in my eyes at a dead point. All the known recruiters left, just a few stayed in the guild. The homepage lied there, but it was not updated since Dezember 1999.

I hope its ok when I change back to my personal story. I tried to enter the guild for about two weeks, it was just horrible, there where no recruiters online or too far away - over the whole two weeks! I often talked to Saberman at this time, he was no officer at this point and the first thing he told me was something like "what, you want to enter this guild? i think about quitting". Hehe! Good old Saberman!

So joined the Legion of Little Folk in Highpass Hold. Leitbur was the officer who invited me into the Legion. I will never forget this moment (I died during the process). A gnome called Xzandar offered me to be my mentor shortly after I joined the guild, he was the 1st officer of the Legion. Five days after I joined the Legion I was on my first event . The whole active guild, I think we where six members, met in Kelethin and stormed Crushbone. Dameont joined us there, of course we had a lot of fun. Well life went on in the guild, more or less active, I entered the 20 Lvl.

Then all changed from one day to the other. Xzandar told me that he would be going to be guild leader soon and he wants me to be an officer in the guild (me? I was stunned!) and I refused, because I didnt felt able to do handle such a great task, but after another talk with him, I agreed. Dameont did a really great job in the three months that he was guild leader to keep the few that stayed in the guild together. He build up the homepage as we know it for example, but theres another world in front of the monitor that needed him. The new leader changed a few general things and so we moved on into the next age, the age of awakening.
