What is this all about?

On this page, the members with the greatest skills in various aspects will be listed.

If you are in need of something, you can ask these persons about it. As you are in the same guild, you'll probably get the service for free or for a lower price than usual.

On the other hand, being on this list obviously brings fame and glory to you. :-)

On the third hand, if you see that something vital is missing here, you may choose to learn that skill for the betterment of the legion!

Send your skills to filumtil@pluto.camelot.de.
