Note on the time zones: for every event, MET (middle european time, Europe/Berlin in some charts) and PST (pazific time, the same as used by the EverQuest page) are given. To get from MET to PST, subtract 9 hours, i.e., 11:00 MET is 02:00 PST. If in doubt, the FIRST time given is always the correct one (I could make an error when calculating the PST time). All times are given in 24-hour format, i.e. 22:00 instead of 10:00pm, 10:00 instead of 10:00am. For more information and for a conversion tool, see the Time Zone Converter.
