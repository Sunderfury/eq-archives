Tuesday, August 10, 2004

Caution Gnome Slayer Spotted!!

Once again we ventured to the plane of innovation to help our friend Nitram Anizok power up his latest creation. We succeeded in subduing Xanamech Nezmirthafen after he went on the rampage and we were rewarded with a Pulsing Phase Emitter and The Weapon's Driveshaft.

However after the dust had settled Nitram was nowhere to be seen, after careful examination of the crime scene it appears that he was mercilessly slain by one of our own. So if ya be a gnome dont turn ya back on Blipplekortz the gnome slayer!!!

Yea Yea, we believe ya when you say it was an accident...

Tuesday, July 20, 2004

Xanamech's battery runs out...

A 18 [edit Feppsi: 3*6=18, not 3*6=36 :-) ] strong monday raid went to POI to have a word with Xanamech.
 
He was expertly tanked by bunguk and was quickly dispatched by the rest of the raid.
 
He dropped the 41% haste collar and the war only agro proccing hammer.
 
We had to run a bot in to claim the hammer - we just could not let it rot.
 
Just as we thought we were done when the junk beast spawned right outside xanamech's lair, so he had to die as well, and now his power source will be recycled in some new and devious tinkered toy that feppsi will be constructing.

Monday, July 12, 2004

Monday Raid Visits Arch Lich

Scouts reported Rhag 2 to be up so we headed over to ssra and made short work of clearing to rhag 2 + despatching him.

We then fought up to Arch Lich Zudanes level and managed to clear a path to his room with only a couple of hiccups.

We attempted to use the hit and run tactic on the arch lich, but he saw through our plan and stunned our mt and spun him arround so he didnt know which way to run to safety.

Our rampage tank was pretty well covered for heals until the MT bit the dust, then things got a little more tense and we had to order a retreat. Luckily we managed to camp out one of the clerics so CR was easy - thank heavens for perma-rooted mobs :)

Umbral Plains Upturned.
