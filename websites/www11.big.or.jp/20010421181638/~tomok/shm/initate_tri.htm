Initiate Symbol of Tribunalげっと手順！

･Shaman/CLR用のQuestを紹介します。
･このアイテムはTribunal信仰のSHM専用です。つまりBarbarianだけですネ。
･Paineel(CazicThule信仰)/Neriak(Innoruuk信仰)に関しては、入手手段やアイテムも違います。
･TrollSHMかHum/DefCLRなあなたはゼヒ、Neriak版のこのQuestを完了して下さい。ManaコストナシでDarkness系のSpellを発動できる熱いアイテムが手に入ります。

･手順だけの説明です。ストーリーは追っていませんのであしからず。

　

1)Halasの有名(笑)裁縫屋さん「Cindl」に会いに行き、こう言いましょう「what mammoth hide parchment」。
　→Mammoth Hide Parchmentげっと

Cindl says 'Jinkus must've sent ye fer sum muir mammoth hyde parchment fer 'is posters.
Here, tayke it, frree o' charge, as a donation t' th' church an' t' th' will o' th' Trrybunal as well.

　

2)次に「Greta」を探します。Cindlの近くに居います(屋外)。こう言いましょう「what ink」
 　→Vial of Datura Inkげっと

Greta Terrilon says 'Jinkus must 've sent ye fer some of me special ink made frrom the pigment of th' datura flower.
Ye may tayke some frree o' charge as a donation t' th' Church and t' th' will of th' Trrybunal as well.

　

3)HalasのShamanGuild内に居る、Jinkusを捜し、先ほどげっとしたMammoth Hide ParchmentとVial of Datura Inkを渡しましょう。
　→ Wated Posterげっと

Your faction standing with ShamenofJustice got better
Your faction standing with MerchantsofHalas got better
Your faction standing with CircleOfUnseenHands got worse
Your faction standing with CoalitionofTradeFolkIll got worse
Your faction standing with EbonMask got worse
You gain experience!!

　

4)QeynosBankの外に立ってるBarbarian「Dun」を捜し、先ほどげっとしたWanted Posterを渡しましょう。
　→List of Qeynos Most Wantedげっと

Dun says 'Tis good that Jinkus has sent you with this poster.
There has been talk of a northman fugitive in the city.
Take this list back to Jinkus so that our brethren in Halas are aware of the many criminals that have fled Qeynos and are still at large.
Your faction standing with Banker got better
You gain experience!!

　

5)再びHalasのShamanGuild内に居る、Jinkusの元へ行き、List of Qeynos Most Wantedを渡します。
　→Initiate Symbol of Tribunalげっと〜〜〜〜〜！

Jinkus Felligan says 'Y're learnin' t' serrve th' Church well, young Initiate Chuppa .
Ah grant ye yer holy symbol and th' blessing o' the Trrybunal thot They may grrant ye wisdom in serving Theirr will.
Your faction standing with ShamenofJustice got better
Your faction standing with MerchantsofHalas got better
Your faction standing with CircleOfUnseenHands got worse
Your faction standing with CoalitionofTradeFolkIll got worse
Your faction standing with EbonMask got worse
You gain experience!!

　

6)おめでとー！首に装備して右クリックすると「Summoned:Hammer of Wrath」が手元に召還されます。

　

7)余談とか。
このQuest。OggokやGrobbのはスデにアップグレード版が発見されています！Tribunal版もたのしみー！
