■翻訳タイム

･強奪した本をやくしてみましょうか。いつもの怪しげな訳です(汗)
･あいかわらず、トカゲ語で書いてあります。

･1冊目。

Neh Ashiir's disappearance will always be cloaked in mystery. We of Torsis have a saying that goes "Neh's chances". We all know this is used to define an unknowable? variable but not many know of its relation to the event in the early years of our fair city. For as many people as there were in Torsis there were that many theories regarding Neh Ashiir's disappearance. Others say there were political tensions between the queen and her lord, so much so that she had to leave to escape death. And for good measure a few pointed to the stars and said that people from below the sky took her. One thing all agreed upon however is that the relationship between the royal couple was decaying. Political scrutinizers noted small personal habits between the two that implied a separation of intimacy during their public appearances. Many even tie the highly publicized Crusade of Kirn into the disappearance of Neh as Kirn left the city only a few weeks before. It is recorded in many texts that the visage of Rak Ashiir reflected his despair over his wife's vanishing although it is a well known fact that it was mostly an act played by the King to keep up his illusion of nobility. Perhaps one day we will find the truth but at this point it would mean little to the public.

･Neh Ashiirの消失は、ずっと神秘のベールに包み隠されている。
･Torsisに住む我々はそれを"Neh's chances(Nehの賭け?)"と呼ぶ。
･全てを知ることはできないことを我々は知っている。しかし、都市のイベントと関係があることは分かっている。
･Neh Ashiirの消失に関しては、Torsisに住む人の数ほど多くの意見がありまった。
･あるものは彼女が死から逃れるために去らなければならないほど、王妃とLordの間に政治的な緊張があったと言います。
･そしてあるものは、星を指差し、空からの人々が彼女を連れていったと言います。
･しかしながら、皆が口をそろえているのは、国王夫妻の関係が壊れていたということである。
･政治に関心のあるものたちは、政治の場に出席する間、彼らの仲たがいを暗示する2つの小さな癖に気付いた。
･Kirnがほんの数週間前に都市を去ったので、 皆はNehの消失にこれを関連付けてさえいる。
･彼の気高さを演じつづけていたが、彼の妻の消失により、Rak Ashiirの顔が絶望を反映していたことは数多く記録されている。
･恐らくある日、我々は真実を見いだすだろう。しかし、その時点でそれは一般の人々にとってはあまり意味がないことだろう。

･3冊目

Throughout the history of the High Scale it was common for the knights to practice complete celibacy. It wasn't until the controversial Kirn was given the hallowed title that contract between the sexes became tolerated. While Kirn was the mightiest of the High Scales he was able to achieve many great victories in the name of our Lord of Fear. One not sure battle know as the Bone Rending when Kirn lost his fabled whip, Thriaxis' Tail, as was still able to defeat scores of his enemies using his hands only. Before this battle many believed Kirn could not fulfil his duties as a lead and patriot for the people.
However Kirn's might and his lecture on the use of females in religious crusade and duty convinced the public that celibacy had its place in divinity but non-celibacy also had a place and value within the church. A shrine depicting Kirn among many women was constructed in remembrance of his controversial reign although his corpse was never brought back for burial as it was lost far across the ocean among distant brothers on faith.

･HighScaleの歴史を通じて、騎士は完全な独身でいることが習慣だった。

･HighScaleで最強だったKirnは、我々の主Lord of Fearの名において、多くの偉大なる勝利を手に入れた。

･しかしながら、Kirnの力とCrusadeでの女性修道士の起用に関する講義は、人々に独身主義が神性なコトを確信させた。しかし非独身主義も教会の中で同じく、場所と力を持つようになった。
