I dont know much about this, but here is what i found out in 30 minutes. I know that is not that big job to write your own Spell analyser software.
The spdat.eff file contains the information of the whole spell that exist in everquest, if there are new/changed spells you will see it the update programm.

The first two lines contain the name of the spell, the second two lines contain the object that casts it (i think) its allways PLAYER_1. The third two lines contain a zone name, this is maybe for special spells which can only cast in the specific zone or only where cast the randomly.
The lines "Text" contain the text that will written to the chatwindow on events like spell is castet, spell rus out or spell if gone. This block contain three lines maximum.
