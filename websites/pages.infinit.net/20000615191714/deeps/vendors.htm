Where to buy

Here is a list of all major cities their brewing locations. Much need to be completed here, so please write me if you have some info to add.

If you look closely, there are NO specialty vendors in Brewing in all of Faydwer. So, brewing is a bit harder there, you'll need to travel to Freeport to get supplies.

Vendors are listed in an west to east order.

This page will be under construction for some weeks.

Erudin
Location of Brewbarrel: In the Blue Hawk beside the Erudin Library
- Bar supplies: Inside the same bar
Qeynos

Location of BrewBarrel: The Fish Ale (back room), The Crow's Nest
- Brew vendor: The 2 ladies in Voleen's Fine baked goods
- Bar supplies: Fish Ale, Crow's nest and other bars
Halas
Location of Still: McQuaid's Dark Stout (east part of city) and McDonald's fire Cider
- Bar supplies: Inside those bars
Highkeep

Location of BrewBarrel: Kitchen, upper lvl and there's one In the Tiger's Roar in HighPass
- Brewing vendors on upper lvl and in Golden Rooster and Tiger's Roar, in HighPass
- Bars supplies are outside the Keep, in Highpass
Rivervale
Location of Still: Behind the Fool's gold outside
-Bar supplies in Fool's gold
-No brewing vendors
Neriak
Location of Still: Maiden's Fancy in the Third Gate section.
Grubb
No BrewBarrel: go to Oggok
- Brewing vendor: Gunthak's Beltch (local bar beside Warrior guild). Vendors are Gartonka & Graktok.
- Bar supplies: same vendors
Oggok
Location of Still: Meet and Drink Tew Bye
- Bar supplies: inside the same bar
Freeport

Location of BrewBarrel: Hog Caller Inn
- Brewing vendor: Kristin Charcoal (NW part of West freeport)
- Bar supplies: Hog Caller Inn
Tassle's Tavern: in North Freeport market, near the Jade Tiger Den
East Freeport: Trader's Holiday, on the way to docks
East Freeport: Seafarer's Roast (the bar on the docks)
