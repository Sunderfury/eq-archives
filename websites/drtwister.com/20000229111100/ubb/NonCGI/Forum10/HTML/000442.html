Golem Burial Ground - 50.9S, 67.3E

Rocky Crypt - 50.8S, 69.2E

Lots of golems, close to each other.

I'd suggest going there sometime when it's not busy, like early morning, and learning how to kill them extremely fast, I'm war/life so I bludgeon vulner III and then shock wave II, but you might want to do different. Maybe throwing in a magic yield will make you work wonders on them, I don't know..

With all your magics, you don't have melee defense, so you'll have to kill the golems quickly or not at all. I suggest buffing and using some other melee player to attract the golems attention, then killing the golem while it goes after the melee. You'll piss off a few of the warriors, melees, and etc, but you'll be getting exp.

Don't bother draining through walls or harming through them, you won't do nearly as much damage as a war mage that will vulner and then take a golem out, drawing it away from you in the process.
