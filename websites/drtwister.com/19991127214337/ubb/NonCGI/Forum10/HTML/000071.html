5. Jewler- You can now find or buy jewlry. What is a person could make jewlry. Lets say Docter Bombay the jewler takes

might be wanting to look up the jewler for a 5000 pyriel item for 5300 instead of trading in his 6000 pyriels for 5000 pyriel
note.

just made! It looks like the two cl***es that seems to get left out alot is Archers and Vagabonds. So, how about letting the
archers make bows. As his skills grows, so would he ability to makes different type of bows. The different types of bows
could arrage from just looks to effectiveness and weight rating.

7. Wands- When I say wands I do not mean the one that comes with the instant unlimted fireballs. I'm merly talking about a
different looking wand in someone hand. As a maker of wands, you skill would allow you to make different lookin wands that
look more stunning to other people. Imagine what you would think as you see tha 30 level war mage walk into town with a
