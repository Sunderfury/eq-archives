Here's a walkthru of V.Fort.

First off, You gota kill everything at the entrance, all the Virindi, and Tuskers.

Then, Facing away from the portal, go up the right ramp. Select the wall switch thru the wall, and hit it. Go into the room, hit that floor lever.

Run down to where the Virindi Master spawned, Hit the Left Switch.

Now Run up the ramp to the upstairs, and hang a right. There'll be a door there, leading back. Kill the Tusker Guard and Virindi there, and go thru the 2nd door.

Remember, these switches are timed, so dont dawdle around, or you'll get stuck somewhere and have to repeat the whole process.

When you get past the 2nd door that the Tusker guard was guarding, There's a Floor lever. Hit it to open the doors down the ramp, and run your ass off when you get into the room to the far right wall.

There's at least 10 Virindi and 2 Tusker Slaves in this room, if you run by em fast enough, you can get up the ramp to the bridge overhead.

This is a great spot for low level archers, I took some level 15 to 20's here and they got Great exp off the Virindi (When they didn't get nuked).

From here, after you kill all the virindi, go up the ramp on the other side of the bridge, dont hit the first switch, it just opens a portal out... Walk around until you find the floor lever, hit that.

Run back down, go thru the door across from where you entered this big room and hit that floor switch.

Now go back and to the right facing away from the bridge, there's a door that opened there.

This area is safe, there's nothing that spawns there. Going back and to the right leads nowhwere, the left fork leads to the top of the "Puppet Pit".

Here, you'll jump down and have to kill a dozen odd Virindi Puppets. There are 4 Floor item spawns. Thats It.

There is a second doorway, up the ramp and to the left from where the Virindi Master spawns, but We could never figure out how to get in.

Basically, the Lever to the left at the entrance does nothing that we can find.. The right lever by the master opens the door to that lever. The switch back above the Virindi Bridge opens the gate to the portal out.

We had someone stand by that door, while the rest of us ran through, killing everything and hitting all the levers. The door never opened.

Oh, and we searched all the walls for hidden passages, no such luck.

So.... Who knows whats back there.....
