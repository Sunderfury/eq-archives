Not positive about this one, but I've seen this happen a couple times:

You're on one of the team-PvP servers as a dark elf.

You fight a barbarian who is using a Guise of the Deceiver. When he is dead, you cannot loot his corpse ("You cannot loot this corpse at this time"). He didn't die from a pet or DOT, he died cause you hit him for 60 points with your sword.

I believe when you use the Guise, members of the evil team cannot loot your corpse.

If this is what's causing it, it may also be true that dark elf enchanters using Illusion: Human cannot be looted by members of the human team, etc. It's as if when it checks to see if you're lootable it checks what species you APPEAR to be rather than what species you are.

This is specific to the team-PvP server (Tallon Zek and Vallon Zek), but nevertheless is a quite abuseable feature. I can think of 3 times so far where other-team characters were killed by me and were unable to be looted and all three were under the effect of the Guise when they died.

A quick fix for this may be to make Illusion spells vanish when a player dies, so that their corpse will appear as it should rather than as the illusionary form. (Of course, this would also be consistant behavior with the way illusions work in every movie in existance... Illusionist dies, illusion goes away.)
