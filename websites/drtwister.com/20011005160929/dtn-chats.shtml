Internet Relay Chat

The Dr.Twister Network server is available by connecting to irc.atomicrhino.com with any IRC client. Our server is designed to give players the opportunity to communicate with other players about the games they play on a daily basis. Several channels are available to talk about online games, share tips and tricks, discuss problems and hear the latest rumors about what is happen in the land you play in.

Scheduled Events

We regularly have scheduled events open to the public to chat about specific topics. We invite key members of the production teams for the games you play who visit to answer your questions. These moderated chats are announced well in advanced however a schedule of regular group chats are available for each individual game.

Bug Chat™

The Dr.Twister Network is the leading source for fixing bugs in Multiplayer Online RPGs. Our exclusive Bug Chat™ was designed to allow players to openly communicate with the development teams the problems they experience while playing their games. This opportunity allows us to see exactly what is going on around us, and gives the developers an chance to express the reasons behind problems and give the players an estimated time for fixing the problem. To find out additional information regarding the scheduling of a Bug Chat™, check the daily news.
