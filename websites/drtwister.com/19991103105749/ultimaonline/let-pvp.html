PvP Switch?

Hello again good Doctor *hehehehe*

There have been plenty of posts lately on a PvP switch for UO, far too
many than I can stand. Now is the time to enlighten the less bright
players on PvP switches.

UO already has a PvP switch and a damned good one at that. It's called
Guildwars and Orver/Chaos in UO..maybe they should have called it PvP
for the less intelligent of players. If you join a guild that is at war
with many guilds or an Order/Chaos guild you've toggled your PvP switch
on!. You are now at great risk to be attacked, gang-banged and killed
ANYWHERE! at anytime....you are no longer safe in towns, guards won't
help you, it's quite difficult to attempt to buy regs while you notice
"MastaKilla" spearing you with a deadly posined pitchfork. Your guard
has to be up at all times, you need to have your escape planned before
you venture anywhere when you are a character in that situation.

Now say you are a humble player not interested in PvP and you don't
choose to get involved inPvP at all, you get angry when Captain Red and
friends drop you faster than networks drop a bad TV show. Well guess
what? this game is not just about YOU and your wishes, needs and wants,
if you assume that then your attitude is just as selfish as those PK's
you complain about.

You assume a risk when you venture out of town guard zone..everyone
knows that, and OSI has that fact posted everywhere. There are plenty
of non-guarded monster laden zones players can go to and not run into a
single PK. When is the alst time you saw a PK in Destard? the 5 level of
Despise? anywhere in T2A? they simply don't exist....the odd opportunist
may try to kill you because you turn grey, but that is a flaw in the
reputaion system not a flaw of PvP.

PK's should be viewed like I envision OSI intended them to be, another
hazardous obstacle players encounter when they venture to the more
dangerous areas of the land. This may be a less than stellar real life
example but when you live in the city do you expect to be mauled to
death by a grizzly bear? and if you go off to the mountains and a
grizzly attacks and maims you, would you go running to your congressman
and demand that all bears be removed from those mountians as they are
infringing on your right to enjoy the wilderness...you surely didn't
choose to be mauled, the bear if to blame not yourself for you should be
free to do what ever you please as long as nothing prevents you from
doing what you want, you should be able to assume no risks when
traavelling anywhere (I hope you caught the sarcasim there)

PvP in UO has been designed rather well, I can't believe I just
complimented the Design Team, you have safe areas should you choose not
to be involved in PvP, yet you should be aware that in UO as inlife if
you venture to more dangerous areas of the land you should be prepared
for bad things to happen and take the appropriate precautins to prevent
them.
