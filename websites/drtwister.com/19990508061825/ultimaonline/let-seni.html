Goodbye

Posted by James, aka Senith on April 07, 1999 at 13:57:25:

I'd like to say thank you to everyone who's been by my side the past few months. And I would like to apologize to anyone who thinks I'm "bailing out"...

But I'm not. I've lost faith in a lot of folks on the Support Team, due to their reactions to the incident and the actual conversation that sparked it. How people can ignore the comments made and villify me for letting the public know what was said is beyond me...but I desecrated one of their idols, and I guess people want to believe in something.

So I'm quitting. The Counselor program is no longer a viable place for me to exist; it is unpleasant to work with folks who PM me with death threats and insults. I lost my shot at Seership, due to my sponsor pulling out after the incident, even though I had the support of a very large player base.

And then there is OSI. My posts have ALWAYS been polite, save the Iron Will conversation. I have never directly attacked Origin, and have always respected their efforts.

But I'm leaving. And I have nothing left to hide. So here are my opinions; take them as you will...

Necromancy was never intended to work; it was thrown together quickly to keep players from switching to EQ. Is this an opinion without actual proof? Yes and no. I have plenty of circumstantial evidence; the release date of testing being right before the release of EQ, the pulling of testing right after, the odd coming of Evil/Good BEFORE necromancy was perfected, and the return to the drawing board. If this isn't enough...then someone else should explain it to the customers. Everyone WANTED necromancy...what is the Evil/Good nonsense for when something else was already on the table that the people wanted?

It is my opinion Test Center and Abyss serve as places for players to do QA work without getting paid. OSI knows that players will report what they find, or exploit them religiously until it becomes known...so they enlist the help of hundreds of people to test their patches without rewarding or paying them.

The funny thing is...these people are PAYING to test. Their $10.00 a month is going towards TESTING for OSI. Does that seem a little askew to you?

And the same goes for Customer Support. They don't have enough staff to do the job....so they bait people with the fancy blue robes, the "respect" that comes with it (unless you count the 80,000 people that constantly say "he's just a Counselor; he can't do anything"), and the "abilities". Look at that; a couple hundred UNPAID support staff. And what is the reward for this?

Nothing. Nada. Wow, you can get an item on your paper doll. Oooh, you can talk to the GMs. And? The GMs don't tell you anything you don't already know, and the items on your paper doll are pointless, considering the Counselor character is unable to do anything save answer questions and serve at "weddings".

And now they want to add new powers, like squelch and jail. Funny, this will cut the GM queue in half, and allow yet more responsibility and work to fall onto the shoulders of UNPAID and UNREWARDED volunteers. Does THIS seem wrong to you at all?

Anyone offended by the statement above? If you are, then you are beginning to recognize there is some truth in it, and you are just fighting the realization. Think about what I wrote above before you dismiss it as nonsense....REALLY think about it...

I'd like to thank everyone who donated to the tower fund, and hope you all make use of it. With the wipe coming, it won't matter soon...but I hope it serves as inspiration for others to build something similar in the future.

Don't follow anyone blindly...strike out your own path. And NEVER back down from your words, or hide behind a false name; I may have caused myself a lot of problems by posting with my Counselor name, but it brought me to a lot of realizations.

What will I do with my time now? I'm quitting my night job, and signing on as manager full-time at Decanter Fine Wines, a wine shoppe in suburban Columbia. I'll be taking Tang Soo Do again, and see if I can finally put together a few songs in my little home studio I've built up. I'll be turning my computers into servers for Hotline (check out www.hotlinesw.com), so if you know what that is, or find out later, check me out at the Phantom Server on the tracker: tracked.group.org. And I'm getting involved with a Matrix fan club (yes, the movie was that frickin' good). Will I play EQ? I don't think so...it doesn't interest me that much.

The only things I will miss are the people, and my position at Stratics. Being a reporter allowed me to throw some words around and write some okay stuff; it seemed to be appreciated by the few that read it, and my superiors seemed pleased. I hope someone applies at Stratics for my position when I'm gone...it's a good gig, and the folks there are real nice (especially Beans).

I'll be conjuring up a final post, about the opening of the Arcadium and my leaving...so if you want to stop by, feel free. It should be up by tomorrow morning, if nothing odd happens.

Well, that's it. I'll reply to a few comments in the thread, but I'm not going overboard. Anyone who knows me well enough can ICQ me or e-mail...I'll be here...

My accounts have been handed over to my fiance's little brother. So you may see Nessos running around, but Senith has been deleted, and all the items transferred to someone who will manage them responsibly.

And does it matter? It's all disappearing in a week anyway, and I'll be forgotten soon after...

I wish you all the best of luck! And dammit, get out from in front of that computer and go ride a bike!

-James, aka Senith

P.S. If someone from the Support Team is reading this, it's been 3 days since I e-mailed Kal and still no response. So I assume this is a "we don't want you back" response. And if someone from Stratics is reading this...you'll get my official resignation shortly, along with my final post.
