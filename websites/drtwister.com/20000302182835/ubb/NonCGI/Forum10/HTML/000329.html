#2: This is a bug a few people already know but I'm going to post anyway since I didn't find a post about it when I ran a search. This is the chest tie bug. To tie a chest to yourself so that no matter how far you move away from the chest no one else can access it but you. This trick takes timing and a little practice to get it right, I suggest doing it in town where it's safer. First, open a chest. Next, double click on the chest to close it and then double click on it again. Do the two sets of double clicks real fast so that your sending the info to open the chest before it actually closes. Now here comes the tricky part. AS you are hitting the double click for the second time, the command to open the chest, start backing up. If down right your character will be backing up as the chest is closing, you will get maybe a few feet away and then the chest will begin to open. When it opens your character will automatically stop moving backwards if your holding the downarrow key. When you character stops the chest will be open, BUT there will be no item bar to look at. If this happens you have done the tie correctly. You can now walk away to any distance, even portal out and no one can touch the chest until you log out. Be careful tho, sometimes when the chest respawns it will auto close even if it's tied to you, so periodically checking the chest is a good thing still.
