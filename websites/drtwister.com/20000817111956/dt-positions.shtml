Description of Positions

Reporters
 Help to report on daily information for a specific game. Read emails, generate content for site and communicate with Site Administrator. Donation of your time can be from as little as a few minutes every other day.

Site Administrator
 Handle daily updates and maintenance of the site for a specific game. Help to generate content, handle interviews, establish contacts with the Development Team and communicate with network administration. Donation of your time should be daily.

Forum Moderators
 Ability to read and monitor all content within the network forums. Handle and maintain long or off topic threads, monitor and report on abusive posters, and delete, report on spam.
