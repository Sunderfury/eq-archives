Dr.Twister's E3 Diary 2001

Well I must say, this year's E3 Expo was the same as every other year, well except for the fact that I managed to stir up a few good stories while walking around as Dr.Twister. It was also the first time we had the opportunity to see more than just one Massive Multiplayer, which during previous years we would litterly have to search dark corners to find someone to talk about EQ.


The plan was pretty set this year. Introduce myself to as many of the game developers as I could, offering the opportunity to work close with the site in the upcoming months. As you may be expecting, some took it to heart, others ran and some just flat out wouldn't speak to me.

Below you will find several fun and interesting stories as well as a few previews of games that I had the opportunity to see. As one story explains, my reputation lived up to the hype and I was denied seeing a few that were being shown behind closed doors. Of course, if you want legit game news, you go there, but that's not why you come here now is it?

Enjoy!
