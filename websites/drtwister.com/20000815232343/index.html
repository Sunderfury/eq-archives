Wednesday, August 9, 2000

Online Pirates screenshots
Posted: Wednesday, August 9, 2000 by J.

German game makers neoSoftware (note: heavily into Flash 4) have released new screenshots of a new massively-multiplayer game in development, Online Pirates (note: pretty skimpy on details.

GA-RPG has the screenshots, mostly of impressive-looking ships "shot" from an arial view while they're in harbor.

"Do you love playing online games but are through with running around in another fantasy scenario? Do you think you're tough enough for living in the 18th century? Then Online Pirates is the game for you. Imagine walking in the shoes of a swash-buckling pirate, and being able to command your own fleet of ships. You'll not only be able to sail the ships, but it will also be possible to change the features of the ships - such as weaponry, rigging, stowage, etc."

It's supposed to come sometime in 2001. I know at least one guild that's looking forward to it.

Diablo 2 Expansion
Posted: Wednesday, August 9, 2000 by Dr.Twister

PlanetDiablo has updated their Diablo II Expansion Information section with a few new additions, one of which is very exciting! First of all, there is a mystery with an NPC of Act III, named Natalya. After completing the last quest of that Act (killing Mephisto), she completely disappears from the town of Act III! Darkness, a Diablo II site, recently discovered the sound file that includes what Natalya would have said if she was still in the game after you defeated Mephisto. It includes details that match the rumors we have heard about the Diablo II Expansion, supporting the possibility of their truth. We have this sound file available for download on our Diablo II Expansion Information page, in two formats: WAV and MP3.

Next, Natalya seems like the perfect example of the Assassin character class. While she does not identify herself directly as an Assassin, she does say that she is part of an ancient order of hunters who seek and slay corrupted sorcerers. This not only supports the possibility of an Assassin character class, but also could mean that if that class is included in the Expansion, it will probably be female.

You can find PlanetDiablo's Diablo II Expansion Information section at the URL listed below:

http://www.planetdiablo.com/diablo2/expansion/
