supporting UO, going to sleep. I would spend all day doing a 'volunteer'
job, for free, while all my friends were out putting in less effort and
getting money for their efforts. I never expected to be paid, although I

could not escape on my own. It even went so far as to dreaming about UO,
and doing calls in my sleep! I hold no grudge against this individual, only
