-Sidenote to Shiner: Your method is too complicated for creature. What you might not have realized is that whenever the sum of the gem and the potion are the same (same herb and talisman too) the exact same 3rd taper is used. And for the most part the same 2nd taper is used as well, but of course there are a few that break the rule. So when figuring out your pattern, all you need is two positive spells where the sum of the gem and the potion equal 1 and 2, then you know your pattern. To figure out the negative's, you should only need to know one of those and be able to shift the tapers accordingly. So by researching only 3 creature self level 5, you get all the creature self level 5's .
