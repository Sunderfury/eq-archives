Proposal for Merchant Economy by - RLSteeves

Hail Good Fellow,

I would like to submit the following ideas for consideration based
on practicality and building the merchant economy and other issues. In
making the following requests I have tried to examine both sides of the
coin as far as possible uses good and bad.. Now that in UO you seem to
want to keep a balance between PKs and the average player I feel there
are some real nice ideas to enhance the game and most do not required
new artwork .Please review the following..

1) Make Cooking Kettles like the one that spawn craftable items.

Criteria:
They are made as an item and not a deed
They can be placed in any location and do not allow blocking (you can
walk through it)
Minimum skil required to make 65 Smithing 40 Lumberjacking (for the
fire logs)
Materials required 125 Ingots and 10 logs
Weight 12 stones
Pros:
They could be carried and when meat is found the person would be able to
cook the meat.
Nice decoration
Another item that merchants could sell and smithies could make.
No new artwork

Cons:
Would make a nice trap scenario. Place one of them for effect and a few
regular chest with items in them. Then pace a Trapped chest in the
bunch.. Now that’s mean :)

2) Make Braziers craftable

Criteria:
Make item as a deed that wil remain in place until all charges are used.

When item is made it will have a set number of charges from a random
list of possible spells.
It will not be able to block movement of players or creatures
Minimum skill required to make 65 smithing 85 magery
Materials required 100 ingots 25 sets of reagents for the appropriate
spell that will be cast on the brazier (random)

Pros:
Great decoration.
Help to build merchant trade
Could be used while in high spawn area if spell is useful.
No new artwork
A continual supply would be needed
Economy would be enhanced

Cons:
Ruthless would have access to 25 spells (proposed number of spells)
without using mana but so would their victim

3) Make rescue runes or scrolls available

Criteria:
Could be sold by Healers or made by Mages by casting recall on an
unmarked rune stone
They would be for one use only and destroyed upon use.
When made they would pick a random Healers shop and recall the user or
his target to a random healers location in a town guarded zone.
Selling price 75gp
Not considered an aggressive spell
Newbie type item non stealable or lootable
No magic ability required to use

Pros:
Ghosts would be able to get to a healer.
A quick escape from Pks for newbies
Target a Red Pk and send him into a town zone : )
None could be waiting to ambush the sendee as the location they recal to
is random
Gives Mage and item to craft and sell

Cons:
They could be used to thin down a large crowd if you wanted to attack
one player or monster

4) Secure Metal Chests Craftable

 

Materials 75 ingots 10 cloth (lining)
Skills required 50 smithing 30 tailoring
Made in form of deed to be placed
Only one allowed per character
Accessible by placer ONLY
Deteriorates like a house
Refreshed like a house
Unable to block movement of characters or creatures
Able to be redeeded
Sold at shops for 750gp (a small goal for a newbie to reach)
Able to be placed in town limits but not near any dwellings (same space
limit as with a small house)

Pros:
Would give a newbie a way to at least store his items until he could
make friends or own a house.
Would solve the new mining dilemma (colored ore and Pks killing miners
to get what they can not. The need for the colored ore has created a
monster)
Yet another item to build marketable item inventory
Same weight limits as all other chests

Cons:
Could be used along with cooking kettle in the trapped chest scenario
Looters would have a place to store 400 stones of goods without having
worry about what they could carry. Come back later and get items inside.

5) Make colored leather armor craftable

Criteria:
Skills required would be the minimum for each piece as already exists
plus an additional 5 points to be able to dye it

Pros:
A nice addition to the tailoring trade
Items would be more in demand in the market place
Help to build the Tailoring trade economy

Cons:
None I can see at this point

6) Fur Coats craftable and required in cold areas

Criteria:
They would be required wear in all areas where snow or ice might be
found
Not wearing on would result in large amounts of stamina loss and small
amounts of strength loss
They could be made from furs as found on the used to be fur bearing
animals
Minimum skill required to make them 75 tailoring or 80 to dye them as
well
The higher the skill the better the protection from the cold
Weight 10 stones
Materials 15 furs

Pros:
Again building the tailoring trade
A more realistic approach to fur bearing animals
More items to add to the economy more money turned over
A more realistic approach to cold weather climates

Cons:
New artwork?
Less you can carry

7) (Now for the good one) Stone Throwing Catapults Craftable

Criteria:
Skills required to make 85 Carpentry, 75 Tinkering, 50 Smithing
Materials 250 logs, 10 gears 20 springs (this one will get them ? )
Unable to block travel
Made in form of a deed
Deteriorates after 2 hours from time it was set
Can not be moved once set
Fires exploding metal balls , flame sacks , stone balls(new items
craftable see below)
When fired a targeting cursor appears and damage is dealt in a circular
pattern outward to 4 tiles either in all directions. 100 at center down
to 25 percent on the outer edges ( Something simialr to earthquake or an
expanded flamestrike)
If a player is targeted it will through the metal ball about 2 screens
away before it falls to the ground same with a monster
Can not be rapid fired 1 minute timer
Pros:

Great for castle or home defense
Another item to be crafted and sold
They disappear after 2 hours of being setup
Great for guild wars

Cons:
Not good for close range as you take damage if in the target zone
Massive amounts of damage dealt 80hps at center down to 20 at edges with
metal balls or 60 at center and 15 on edges with flame sacks
A Pks dream but he cant move it..
Expensive to shoot (But what a riot to watch)
