Here are a few small things that need to be implemented in the release to make this a much better game:

1. Wizard Spell Filter. Good Lord, talk about Spam-Hell. Everytime someone casts a spell they 'talk'. Well if you are in an area where they are fighting, then you get spammed to hell and everything is scrolled right off your screen. Put a filter in and that will allow us to hold conversations and add to a much more enjoyable gaming experience.

2. When you start to run, the screen tilts. When you stop, it reverts back to normal. This makes running in first person, and even in 3rd person, very unpleasant. Motion sickness city. Also, if i adjust my screen so i can see somehting, I have to redo it while I am running as well. Pain in the butt to keep changing it.

3. When adjusting what we are looking at, slow it down. Jeez.. i barely touch the key to look up and i am all of the sudden staring at the damn moon.

4. When we use auto targeting, PLEASE dont let it auto target it to something underground. Jeez.. nothing like casting 4 spells before you realize that you are injuring gr***.

Just a few suggestions that I am sure no one who matter will read or give a ****. BUT they are the differences between a good game and a great game.
