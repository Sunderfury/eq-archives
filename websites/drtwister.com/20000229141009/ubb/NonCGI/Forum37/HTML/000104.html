Hello all,

I have discovered a possibly easier way to understanding your third taper pattern/shift. This method requires that you only have to research 1 spell per class (herb, gem, and talisman stay the same) on all 144 combinations. The second spell of the same class can possibly be researched by < 64 combos. This saves a lot of time. The rest is then very easy to follow. I have found this to work with everyone I talk to and hope it helps you as well.

The taper matrix (Hope ya can understand it ) :

Taper shift of 1 : Red, Grey, White, Brown, Violet, Indigo, Blue, Turquoise, Green, Yellow, Orange, Pink

Taper shift of 2 : Red, White, Violet, Blue, Green, Orange

Taper shift of 3 : Red, Brown, Blue, Yellow

Taper shift of 4 : Red, Violet, Green

Taper shift of 5 : Red, Indigo, Orange, Brown, Green, Grey, Blue, Pink, Violet, Yellow, White, Turquoise

Taper shift of 6 : Red, Blue

Taper shift of 7 : Red, Turquoise, White, Yellow, Violet, Pink, Blue, Grey, Green, Brown, Orange, Indigo

Taper shift of 8 : Red, Green, Violet

Taper shift of 9 : Red, Yellow, Blue, Brown

Taper shift of 10: Red, Orange, Green, Blue, Violet, White

Taper shift of 11: Red, Pink, Orange, Yellow, Green, Turquoise, Blue, Indigo, Violet, Brown, White, Grey

Taper shift of 12: Red

These direction of patterns are based off of Shiner's Web page numbering system.

Suppose you all are wondering what the heck does that do for me.

Lets use my Level V war magic as an example. Let's Assume that all I knew was Shockwave V. We All know that spell has a potion number of 1. And the taper for that is Green. Know we want to figure out Whirling blade V, which is potion number 2.

From the table above, we find green. It can be seen that there is no possible way that my taper shift is 3, 6, 9, or 12. That eliminates 48 possible taper combinations that we have to research.

Also from the table it can be seen that Whirling Blade V taper can be Yellow, Orange, Red, Grey, Brown, Violet, Blue, Turquoise. Still quite a few, but it saves us some time. After trying all these combos, I find that my taper for Whirling Blade V is Turquoise. That matches a taper shift of 11.

You now know that Flame Bolt V, which has a potion number of 3, is Blue. And so on throughout the taper shift of 11.

Hope you all followed that.

What's nice is if you know one of violet. The possible tapers are limited to only 4 or 48 possible combos.

Later,
