Thursday, August 17, 2000

Dark Zion shuts down
Posted: Thursday, August 17, 2000 by J.

Dark Zion is no longer in development, as Wombat Games announced Wednesday.

"Due to lack of funding and publisher interest, we have stopped development of Dark Zion. The office has been closed and the employees are looking for jobs."

Founded in Sept. 1998 by ex-Ultima Online programmers Rick "Wyrd" Delashmit and Jason "Stormwind" Spangler, Wombat Games was reportedly started so the two could do their own MMORPG, their way. In April, Dark Zion was announced.

Wyrd was found making the rounds at E3, searching for a publisher. DZVault and loyal message board fans did all they could to pump up the project, but apparently it wasn't enough.

There might be some tail-end comments available on this, so keep your eyes open.
