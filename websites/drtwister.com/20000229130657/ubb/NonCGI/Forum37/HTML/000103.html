Here's the easiest way I can explain it....


Counting from the Vendor inventory we can number the tapers and potions.

Taper Potion

Grey = 1 Vitiriol = 1
White = 2 Verdigris = 2
Brown = 3 Turpeth = 3
Violet = 4 Stibinite = 4
Indigo = 5 Realgar = 5
Blue = 6 Quicksilver = 6
Turquoise = 7 Gypsum = 7
Green = 8 Colcothar = 8
Yellow = 9 Cobalt = 9
Orange = 10 Cinnabar = 10
Pink = 11 Cadmia = 11
Red = 12 Brimstone = 12

In the begining you have to have at least learned 2 level 5 spells for this to work and you need these spells to be 1 potion difference from each other.... For example Frost Bolt 5 and Lightening Bolt 5 are each one potion away from each other. Lightening Bolt 5 using Cobalt ( 9 ) and Frost Bolt 5 using Colcothar ( 8 ).

From this table we will achieve the 3rd Taper in Level 5 and 6 spells making it much easier to learn all the spells knowing what your taper rotation is.

Frost Bolt 5 = Colcothar as potion and I found that the 3rd Taper in it was Grey ( 1 ).
Lightning Bolt 5 = Cobalt as potion and I found that the 3rd Taper in it was Red ( 12 ).

Seeing as how Grey is #1 on our chart and Red is #12 on our chart we have a difference of 11.... that is my taper rotation.

The next step gets alittle tricky if your not too good with math. I use the handy dandy calculator that Microsoft so nicely supplied with Windows 98.
So lets say that I want to know what ShockWave 5's 3rd taper is ...all i have to do is the simple math. We have to compare Shockwave 5 to one of the known level 5 spells we already got. I'm going to use Frost Bolt 5 as a example..

We need to know the potion difference between the spells.

ShockWave 5's potion is Vitiriol which is #1 on our list.
Frost Bolt 5's potion is Colcothar #8 on our list.

Next all we need to do is subtract the potion numbers.

ShockWave 5 = 1
Frost Bolt 5 = 8
= - 7
(Dont worry about having a negative number it still works)

We ended up with a -7 as our potion difference. Take the -7 and multiply it to your known taper rotation -7 * 11 = -77 then you have to add the known 3rd taper from Frost Bolt 5 to the -77 so -77 + 1(Grey) = -76 then all we have to do is add 12 (You probably are asking where the 12 came from.... We have a total of 12 tapers in the game.) to the -76 until we get a number between 1 and 12 in the positive..

-76 + 12 = -64
-64 + 12 = -52
-52 + 12 = -40
-40 + 12 = -28
-28 + 12 = -16
-16 + 12 = -4
- 4 + 12 = +8

Stop right there.... we got a positive number between 1 and 12. #8 is equal to Green on the list.... So Shockwave 5's third Taper is Green.... all you have to do is alternate the second taper on the create spell screen until you successfully cast the new spell.

*Note* When trying to find the potion difference always have the unknown spell on top of the subtraction tree.
*Note* If when you subtract the potions and end up with a positive number then you have to subtract 12 not add 12 from your total.
