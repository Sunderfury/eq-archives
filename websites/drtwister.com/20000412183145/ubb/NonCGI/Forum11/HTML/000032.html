I quit EQ a while back ago, but before I left I decided to try something. Ya know the black sash quest, for monks in Freeport? Well, the monk guild leader, is supposed to hand em out, but the quest has not been implemented. No problem I said. Now, my necro was 30, so the higher the better in this situation. Cast something at the guild leader who is on the second floor. *** Warning- after doing this one or two times, your faction with the entire Freeport area, including merchants and guards will be WASTED*** A darkness spell is well deserved to slow him down if he doesn't resist it! However, your standard issue lifesuck spell will be doing the majority of the work. Now, run out very very quickly, before all the other monks mob you and run up to the top of the theatre of the tranquil with the guild leader in pursuit. Sit, and cast lifesucks at him as long as it takes to kill him. He takes FOREVER to kill, as it took me half an hour straight of medding and casting to kill him. Estimated 10,000+ HP's. After this, loot him, get his sash, and sell it to a high lvl monk as I did. Is an EASY 500+ PP a pop, but after one or 2 times. They will KOS on sight, so, do it at yer own risk. And my price is only an estimate, as I wanted to sell it quickly. Also, I think i ended up aquiring the only one on Xegony =)
