I was there the other day. A couple of warnings before you go see the white rabbit. It is passive until someone examines it. The second someone checks the rabbit stats, the fun starts. If you've seen the Monty Python movie where the white rabbit literally flies from person to person slaughtering everyone in sight, this is what happens. The White Rabbit's stats are:

Level: 503
Armor: 200
Melee defence: 717
Missile defence: 717
Melee skill: 717
Magic defence: 583
Run: 700

Notice the run speed of 700. You can not run away from this thing. It's beside you before you can blink. It looks like it's flying at you. It will hit you for both piercing and electrical damage.

I would question your sanity if you attempted to melee it or shoot at it. This may be possible, but the only way I have seen it killed is with life magic from behind the wall. With my level 38 mage fully buffed to 240 life magic, there was no hope of killing it. I tried for 40 minutes. I needed the help of a level 47 mage to finish it off.

What does it drop? Nothing but the white rabbit corpse. It restores one stamina point when eaten. I've asked this of many people - it never drops other loot.

Bring death items. Have fun!

Bulz

OK heres the skinny on the white rabbit.

1. Exp - It varies depending on when you last hit the white rabbit with what ever skill you use to kill him. If you dont know when you attack a monster you get exp applied to your skill. You then have to wait 15 mins before you can exp to that skill by attacking that particular monster.
Its spawn rate is 14 minutes so you can kill it inside of the 15 min time window but generally you get about 50,000 to 65,000 exp.
