LadyMOI needs your help!

One of the major things I would like to do over the next few weeks is to
take an accurate pulse of the feelings of the UO Community. You guys
make up one of the most remarkable groups that exist on the net,
spanning a number of different websites, usenet groups, and even
different games. I spent a couple of days trying to get a good grasp of
the issues that are on the various bulletin boards last week, and I
found I didn't have the bandwidth to get it all down. But I want to know
what you think. I need to know what you think.

For those of you who haven't had any contact with me yet, my name is
Carly Staehlin-Taylor (aka LadyMOI), and I'm the Online Community
Manager (aka Minister of Information) for Lord British Productions at
OSI. I initiated my work here with the Ascension team, and have since
begun to migrate more attention over to the UO team. We've got a
definite need for better communication right now, and I want to help
make that happen.

What I'm looking for is for anyone who'd like to help me in this
endeavor. There's a *lot* of discussion about UO out there... on the
web, on usenet, on irc, even in the game. It's impossible for me to keep
an eye on it all, that's where you come in. If you would be willing to
monitor these boards, and send me little updates about the hot topics,
as well as a summary of what the players are saying (likes, dislikes)
along with representative quotes taken from those boards, you would be
doing me a great favor. Once I get them, I'll be synthesizing these
reports into one that will be delivered on a weekly basis to not only
the development team, but also to the management team here at OSI. It is
my hope that we'll be better able to understand your needs, concerns,
likes, and dislikes as a result. That can only be good for everyone involved.
This isn't to indicate that the developers will stop showing up on the
boards. In fact, I'd like to encourage more of that. But these reports
that you send me can be used to help focus our attention on the issues
that you, the players, think are important.

Once I get an indication of how many of you would be interested in
helping me out in this way, I'll try to develop a structure that makes
sure that none of you have to do work that someone else is doing. If we
get enough interest, we can get blanket coverage of all the boards, and
create accurate pictures of the feelings and attitudes that exist.
Initially, I would like to ask that you just start doing these and I'll
use what I get. If the interest is very great, I'll spend some time
creating a structure for us to work within.

If you are interested, please begin creating these summaries and send
them to me directly at ctaylor@origin.ea.com with the subject heading:
Community Report, , . I will be looking forward to working with anyone
willing to help in this project.

Thanks so much,

Carly aka LadyMOI
