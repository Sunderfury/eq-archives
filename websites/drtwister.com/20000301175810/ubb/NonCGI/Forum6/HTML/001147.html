I discovered this insipid pathing bug myself when killing aviak guards in Lake Rathe.

I thought about trading it... but the people asking for trades are frauds anyway.

This works on the diving board, where there is one guard. (spawn point is always camped) no idea whether other Aviaks behave the same. It works for anyone with DOT spells. But if you have a necro you don't need my help. (I play a Shaman)

Hit a guard with a DOT spell, root the guard If you have stackable DOT's try to get more on, then run as far away from him as you can.

If root holds the Guard will get go back on the board ... (!) It's funny to watch!

You can sit down and med and time the DOT and then go back. Whenever you start approaching the guard he may run at you, so be ready.
