In an effort to help the Development Team (WolfPack), a Shadowbane DevBoard has been created. This message board will be hosted by GamerzX who is not affiliated with any of the Shadowbane websites and shall be moderated by the staff members of the Shadowbane websites (such as SB Stratics, SB Authority, etc).

Note that this forum was just started today but is readily taking shape. WolfPack Designers (the Shadowbane Team) will be designated by a highlighted prefix of WP before their names. The rest of the people you see with highlighted prefixs fall under the following categories:

COS - Crossroads of Shadowbane Personnel
Dr.T - Dr.Twister's Network Personnel
SBA - Shadowbane Authority Personnel
SBS - Shadowbane Stratics Personnel
SBV - ShadowVault Personnel

The reason this forum was created was to give the developers and the soon-to-be gamers of Shadowbane a central place to talk. That way, the developers can spend more time answering questions than jumping from forum to forum to answer the same ones over an over again. Rumor is that this might even become the official Shadowbane DevBoard... but mind you, at present, that is just a rumor I heard. So, stop by the Shadowbane DevBoard and start the discussions already!
