Ok this is about your Kaladim troubles and how you couldn't reconnect at the character select screen.

On rare occasions, I've had the whole zone crash on me, not the whole server. While you get disconnected you arent really being booted from teh server only the zone. When you attemp to log back in the zone is in the process of being reset so to speak. This explains why it took you a few minutes to log back in. If that ever happens again take note of the message under your character picture. It might read "Zone not Available" if I remember right. Hope that helps.
