Making Ornate Chain Armor

Ornate chain is not much better than banded, making it a rather useless armor type. Despite that, here are the recipes if you wish to use them. I would suggest making splintmail instead if you wish to have a better alternative to banded for a non-plate wearing character.

The recipes for ornate chain armor are as follows:

visors, gorget, and bracers One high quality metal ring, Smithy hammer, specific chainmail pattern, flask of water, silver bar
vambraces, gauntlets, mantle, helm, boots, and beltTwo high quality metal rings, Smithy hammer, specific chainmail pattern, flask of water, silver bar
Tunic, cloak, and leggingsTwo high quality metal rings, Smithy hammer, specific chainmail pattern, flask of water, silver bar
