Making Banded Armor

Banded armor is the bread and butter of the smithing trade for the most part, though it is starting to be pre-empted by Fine Plate.

Banded armor is made in a standard forj by combining the appropriate mold with a flask of water and the appropriate number of metal sheets.

Metal sheets are made by combining two small bricks of ore and one flask of water in a standard forj.

The Recipes for banded armor are as follows:

Boots, gorget, and bracers 1 sheet of metal, appropriate mold, one flask of water
Sleeves, gauntlets, mantle, helm, mask, belt, and cloak 2 sheets of metal, appropriate mold, one flask of water
Tunic and leggings3 sheets of metal, appropriate mold, one flask of water

The ore to make the sheets is found in Grobb on a troll named Okun, I believe, and in Neriak in the Blind fish.
