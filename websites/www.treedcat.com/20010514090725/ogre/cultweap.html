Making Ogre Cultural Weapons

Ogres can make two cultural weapons; the ogre war mace, and ogre war hammer. There is a majik, and non majik version of each. The stats on the weapons are 10 damage, 40 delay for the mace, and 19 damage, 56 delay for the hammer. The imbues add strength for Rallos Zek, and Stamina/wisdom for Cazic Thule.

These weapons require blood temper which is very hard to come by since one of the components (elf blood) is very rare. This temper is made by brewers.

Ogre Cultural weapons are made from adamantite sheets which can be purchase in Neriak 3rd gate.

These weapons must be made in the Oggok forj. They will not work in a normal forj.

The recipes for Ogre Cultural weapons are as follows:

Ogre War mace 1 sheet of adamantite, mace head mold, Oak shaft, ogre swill
Imbued ogre war mace1 sheet of adamantite, mace head mold, Oak shaft, blood temper, imbued jade or amber
Ogre War hammer 1 sheet of adamantite, hammer head mold, Oak shaft, ogre swill
Imbued ogre war hammer1 sheet of adamantite, hammer head mold, Oak shaft, blood temper, imbued jade or amber
