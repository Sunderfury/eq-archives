Making Fine Plate Armor

Fine Plate armor is the staple for the grandmaster smith. It should not be attempted till one hits at least 170 skill in smithing.

Fine Plate armor is very expensive to make since at about 75 charisma it costs 25pp for each medium quality block of metal.

Fine plate requires the use of a smithy hammer, which can be bought at plate mold vendors, and a regular forj. Do not try this in a cultural forj as it will not work.

Fine plate also requires a leather padding which is made through the tailoring skill.

The Recipes for Fine Plate armor are as follows:

Medium quality folded sheet of metalOne block of medium quality ore, one flask of water, smithy hammer.
visors, gorget, and bracers 1 medim quality folded sheet of metal, appropriate plate mold, one flask of water, smithy hammer, leather padding
vambraces, gauntlets, mantle, helm, boots, and belt2 medim quality folded sheets of metal, appropriate plate mold, one flask of water, smithy hammer, leather padding
Tunic, cloak, and leggings3 medim quality folded sheet of metal, appropriate plate mold, one flask of water, smithy hammer, leather padding

The ore to make the sheets is found in Rathe mountains on a female troll just past the beetle monolith. If you bear left instead of right at the monolith, you will come to the camp.
