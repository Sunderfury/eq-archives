Jeb Lumsed AKA a sarnak imitator

  • Location: (-1200,-4000)
  • Zone: Burning Woods
  • Level: 54
  • Spawn Info: 72 hours after reset, does NOT despawn
  • Up After Reset: No

If you have your epic and are at least level 66, you may speak directly to Jeb Lumsed to begin the Epic 1.5 quest.

You: 'Hail, a sarnak imitator.'
a sarnak imitator: 'Much time has passed since the creation of the Staff of the Serpent. The power of discord is seeping into our fair world, and only the most powerful of us shall stand to force it back. The time has come for a new tool, and a new breed of enchanter. Are you prepared to meet this challenge?'

You: 'I am prepared.'
a sarnak imitator: 'I have recently received this ancient tome; it contains knowledge that may prove invaluable to the construction of a new staff. Unfortunately, time has had its way with it. I need you to find the missing pages so we can get to work. There should be ten, unless they have been destroyed by time and decay.'

You have received Tattered Illegible Tome!
