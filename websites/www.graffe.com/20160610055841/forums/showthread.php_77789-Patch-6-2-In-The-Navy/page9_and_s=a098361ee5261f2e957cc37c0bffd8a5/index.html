    Re: Patch 6.2: In The Navy.

    Originally Posted by Polynices
    Wow, what a hilarious climb down from their previous stubborn insanity. Nice of them to make us grind out 3 entire reputations on foot before we can fly. I wonder how painful *that* will be.
    The whole thing is a joke, a last-ditch effort to stop the wave of sub losses. Note how this is coming out before the Q&A, like they couldn't afford to wait until then to deal with this? And even with so many people quitting that they had to change, they still felt the need to put a massive amount of grind in there to keep those subs occupied with busywork. How much busywork? MMO-Champ told us how much.

    Explore Draenor: currently, 15% of players sampled by MMO-Champ (1.8 million players that have logged in since May 1st) have this complete. This requirement, I get, as it's somewhat consistent with the way flight points are handled.
    100 Treasures: Somewhere between 6% and 43% completed (the achievements are for 50 and 200). Note that, by saying that this will be the future template, they are turning this unwanted jumping puzzle shit into a mandatory part of the game.
    Loremaster: 22% complete. Hey, all you people who can't stand questing, or who leveled using some of the other methods in game (instances, PvP), nose back to the grindstone!
    Securing Draenor: 3.7% complete. At least you have the option now of purchasing the missions with garrison resources, so you're not at the mercy of RNG.

    What about the three reps to Revered part? We'll need about 21 K rep to go from Neutral to Revered, or 17,500 rep with a Trading Post. What does that entail? Well, there is a rare mission that gives an item that gives 1000 rep to all reps in the patch, but as a rare mission you can't count on getting it. So what else?

    Order of the Awakened: http://ptr.wowhead.com/guides/reputa...awakened-guide

    So you get 1500 points from the first quest for them, and 1500 per day thereafter. 11 days, where each day you need to loot 10 total treasures/rare elites in Tanaan. It's a bit stiffer than the Timeless Isle quest - remember, kill five rares in a week? - but thanks to Handy Notes feels doable.

    Hand of the Prophet (Alliance): http://ptr.wowhead.com/guides/reputa...-prophet-guide

    Quick, but very grindy. Every day, you'll get a guaranteed 1000 rep by doing the equivalent of three apexis grinds and a "kill roughly 20 things" quest. You could also get a freebie 250 rep from another daily that could be completed while you do the other stuff. You also get spotted some rep as you do the intro to Tanaan stuff. Looks like you'll get 1700 at first, and a few 1000-point shots from garrison campaign stuff. But, to be clear: to progress this rep in a meaningful way, you will be doing THREE APEXIS GRINDS PER DAY.

    I saved the best for last.

    The Saberstalkers: http://ptr.wowhead.com/guides/reputa...stalkers-guide

    Hoo, boy. Full-on grind. Four rare elites offer 600 rep each, mobs offer 30 rep each, mobs are elite and, in general, cannot be soloed (mandatory grouping, yay!). There's also summoned bosses that can be done once per week for 3500 rep, but they take stuff that you want to save to buy the mount for the rep, and give me a "summoned guy on Timeless Isle" vibe, meaning it's a raid/huge collection of players thing.

    So, if you go full-blown into this stuff, maybe after two weeks you'll have what it takes to fly. It's too bad they won't actually implement flying until well after the patch, at an unknown time likely to be convenient to the shareholder's call.

    Screw this cynical crap. Letting it lapse, not looking back.
