    Shouldn't they be bigger fans of TNG/DS9/Voyager? Shouldn't they be bigger fans (or more empassioned, for or against) about the second trilogy of Star Wars? Shouldn't they be playing GURPS or Heroes Unlimited as opposed to D&D?
