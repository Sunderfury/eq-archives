    The events and stuff you do in this game... flows, for lack of a better word. I had gotten a waypoint for my personal story - went to it, got distracted... and an hour later, I show my wife how I've ended up on the completely opposite end of the map, JUST because I followed various events and hearts that I thought was fun.

    I recently switched over to playing a warrior, after levelling my necro to 32. Completely different way of fighting and seeing stuff. And I love them both. Can't wait to play a mesmer too...

    We need to repeat this: This has no sub. You're paying single-player money for a fully fledged MMO, where YOU keep the control over how much cash you put in.
    I've invested money in gems, to get extra bag slots. I know that CybermanX have purchased the same bag slots by exchanging in-game gold to gems, thus saving real money.

    And do you want to know the number one reason why I'll be playing in 6 months ?
    Because combat is actually fun. I'll smack a mob for no other reason than enjoying the sounds and animations of my MASSIVE HAMMER
    The game rewards you for smart play - you don't have or NEED a healer, if you are good at using interrupts, stuns, knockbacks, knockdowns etc.

    My jedi never felt as badass as my hammer-wielding warrior, or my necro with his whole party of pets.
    Oh, and they're level 11 and 32. When was the last time you had a level 11 character that you though was badass in a level 1-80 game ?
