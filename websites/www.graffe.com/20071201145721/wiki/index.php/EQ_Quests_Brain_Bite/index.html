[edit] Concussion

Concussion is a very important wizard spell. It reduces hate from a mob. Even a resisted concussion supposedly reduces hate.


Concussion (752)

Mana Cost:25
Cast Time:2
Resist:Magic
  • Decrease Hate by 400

Description: Attacks the mind of your target, causing them to forget some of the damage you have done to them.

Classes: WIZ/37

[edit] The Quest

The Quest to obtain Concussion depends on your faction with the Firiona Vie Outpost and the Overthere Output.

[edit] Good Quest

This quest is given by Despondo, the wizard trainer, in Firona Vie (-4000, 2619.50).

You say, "Hail Despondo."

Despondo looks a bit troubled. "Welcome, Welcome adventurer, I am the wizard tutor Despondo although few need tutorage around here. It would seem that this is no place for a student of any sorts. The lands of Kunark are quite deadly."

You say, "Whats wrong?"

Despondo says "I was bound to this post by General Veredeth. I came to find the lost souls of my friends, but am needed here. I must find a fellow wizard to [rescue their souls]" You say, "I will rescue their souls."

Despondo says "You will need the [three bottles of entrapment]. if you can find their remains in Kunark they you will give the bottles to the body and their soul shall enter. Return all three bottles and I shall give you the wizard spell [Brain Bite]"

You say, "Where are the three bottles of entrapment?"

Despondo says "I will need three gems to prepare the bottles. I am afraid I left my currency within the Freeport Vault. You will have to get the gems I need: a peridot, an onyx and a star rose quartz then I will give you the bottles."

You say, "What is Brain Bite?"

Despondo says "Brain Bite is a concussion spell created by my former friends whose souls are now lost to Kunark. The blow to the inner brain of an enraged foe may just cause the loss of memory and lower their rage. If you want a copy you have to [rescue their souls].

You say 'Where are their souls'

Despondo says 'I have had visions of their sourroundings. (nice Verant typo). Hampton is in a forest of trees which rain oranges. Ryla fell within a great fortress of armored dog men. Mardon met his fate high in a palace above the jungle leaves.'

Give him the gems and receive 3 seperate bottles of entrapment. Identifying them, they are labeled Morden, Hampton, and Ryla.

[edit] Evil Quest

This quest is given by a Dark Elf named Dyth X`Teria in the Kunark lands of 'The Overthere'. He can be found in the docktown at +3137.13, +2266.10, -49.97. He is inside a small stone building.

You say, 'hail Dyth X`Teria'

Dyth X`Teria appears to be the local master of wizadry. His garb clearly comes from the dark city of Neriak it has an emblem of a black sun. Speak!! I am dispatched from Neriak to this land by order of the king. I shall research [new spells] and aid the adventuring wizards. My knowledge of wizardry is offered to all in hopes of gaining information of Kunark.

You say, 'What new spells?'

Dyth X`Teria says 'Within this lost land could lay the knowledge of extinct civilizations. I am ordered to seek this knowledge for the empire of Neriak. So far all I have discovered is a way to harness a spell of my own design. I call it, [Brain Bite].

You say, 'what is brain bite?'

Dyth X`Teria says 'If you wish to own a copy of MY spell, Brain Bite. I would be glad to give you one. All I ask is that you [gather a few souls] for me. You say, 'I will gather a few souls'

Dyth X`Teria reveals three ornate bottles. While I finished my research of Brain Bite, I encountered three [interlopers] who wished to steal my spell. I had the Dragoon garrison track them down and dispatch them. Unfortunately, I forgot to instruct them to trap their souls with these bottles. Do you want to [take the bottles] and finish the job.

You say, 'who interlopers'

Dyth X`Teria says 'The garrison report was sloppy to say the least. They informed me that Hampton was slain near ruins within a jungle infested with raptors. Mardon was slain within an ancient city found deep in a jungle. Ryla was slain in a hidden fortress guarded by fierce armored wolves who walked on their hind legs.

You say, 'I will take the bottles.'

Dyth X`Teria says 'The bottles also require a gem. You will fetch me the three gems and I will prepare the bottles and give them to you to finish the job. I need an onyx, a peridot and a star rose quartz.

Give him the gems and receive 3 seperate bottles of entrapment. Identifying them, they are labeled Morden, Hampton, and Ryla.

[edit] The Skeletons

  • The beings that need to be found are called "a human skeleton".
  • To get the souls, do not kill the skeletons but hand them the correct bottle (use Identify to find out which bottle is which).
  • Some race/diety combinations are KoS (kill on sight) to the skeletons. If you are KoS to the skeletons, you will need someone to charm them while you hand them the correct bottle (charming can be done by an enchanter or a level 49+ necromancer with Cajole Undead). Once you hand the skeleton the bottle, the skeleton will vanish and in your inventory will be a new bottle called "Bottle of Swirling Smoke".

[edit] Hampton

This is the easiest soul to get. Hampton is found in the ruined city within Trakanon's Teeth. Location is +270, -3800. Hampton cons indifferent to most light races but be sure to con Hampton regardless. If you are KoS to him, get an enchanter to charm him while you hand him the correct bottle.

[edit] Ryla

Ryla's soul is in a jail cell in Karnor's Castle. The 'a human skeleton' for Ryla's soul cons indifferent to most light races but be sure to con Ryla regardless. If you are KoS to him, get an enchanter to charm him while you hand him the correct bottle. Check this map for the quickest, easiest, safest path to get to Ryla's skeleton. Before you do anything ask a druid/ranger in the zone if they see 'a human skeleton' in their tracking list. If the human skeleton is not on track, just hang out in the zone and he will spawn eventually (he spawns for approx 20mins and then despawns for 20mins). If or when the human skeleton is up, continue with the following steps:

With the path indicated on the map, you will only encounter around 2-3 mobs on the way to the cell.

  1. When you get to the Prison area, there will be about 6 mobs in the room (one in each cell); once again do not panic. Ignore everything in the room and go in the cell where the human skeleton is (see map).
  2. Hand her the RYLA bottle and run back out the way you came. Jump over the rail into the moat and swim towards the ledge where you gravity fluxed yourself.
  3. Cast Gate to safely get out of the zone without training anyone.

[edit] Mardon

Found in The City of Mist. Mardon is a level 40-45 wizard and is aggro to everyone. The location of Mardon is +136.00, -641.00, +76.97 upon the second floor. To retrieve Mardon's soul, you will need a player to accompany you who is able to charm a high level undead (i.e. Enchanter, Necromancer or Bard). Take a look at this map to see where 'a human skeleton' spawns. (Note: the path on this map is outdated due the gravity flux trick no longer working). A human skeleton in City of Mist has many place holders. Ranging from Plague Bone Skeletons to Fog Golems. Just keep re-doing the following steps until you get the 'a human skeleton' spawn instead of one of the place holders. Same routine on this one.

  1. Have your partner CHARM the 'a human skeleton' npc.
  2. Hand over the correct bottle (use identify on the bottle) to the 'a human skeleton' while he's charmed to get Mardon's soul.

[edit] Hand In

Once you have all 3 bottle of swirling smoke. Head back to the quest giver (Despondo in Firiona Vie or Dyth X`Teria in the Overthere) and hand all 3 bottles at the same time. You will be rewarded a Scroll of Brain Bite for retrieving all 3 lost wizard's souls. Congratulations!
