When you finish a map (think Stranglethorn Vale for relative size) which includes finishing all the hearts, skill point challenges, points of interest and vistas you get a few random items and a big chunk of experience. The vistas themselves give you a neat aerial view of the surrounding area (the camera moves and shows you the area).

    Jumping puzzles are done for the sake of fun, but you are also rewarded with achievement points and a chest with multiple items and some coin. They can be completed once a day for the chest. Jumping puzzles are never required for map completion with one exception I can think of because a vista is at the end of it.
