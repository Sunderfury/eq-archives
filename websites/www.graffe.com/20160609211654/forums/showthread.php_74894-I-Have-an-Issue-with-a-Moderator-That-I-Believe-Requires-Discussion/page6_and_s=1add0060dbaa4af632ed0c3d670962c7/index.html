I abused this right, sorry about that, no excuse. In addition, since I was a moderator, I felt it was my responsibility to actually read everything that is written in a thread, so putting people on ignore was rather counterproductive to my responsibilities.

    Final point, I found some of the reactions to these last few days rather revealing. The intentions and emotions that you've projected onto me have been largely totally wrong, I use this place as a sounding board for the most offensive and insane things that tumble around in my head, because if I brought this stuff up with people I work with and hang out with in real life, they'd probably lock me up, and rightly so.
