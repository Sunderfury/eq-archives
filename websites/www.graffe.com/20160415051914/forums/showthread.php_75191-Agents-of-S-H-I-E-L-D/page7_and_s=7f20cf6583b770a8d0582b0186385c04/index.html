    Re: Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

    I'm still watching, but I keep forgetting to come check this thread out, as I tend to be behind on episodes. It's gotten really good. Back when I last posted some of you guys were remarking how it had picked up but I still wasn't feeling it, but I am now. It's too bad it's still not resonating with others apparently.

    I was pretty impressed with the makeup work for the "getting shot" episode from a few months ago by the way. I've seen a lot of people pretending to be shot on shows like this, but they really rarely go through so much trouble in making you believe it. Have to say though, the character was pretty dumb to walk right into it.

    This latest episode was pretty fun in a "what's going on!" sort of way. I'm kind of amused that Whedon has graduated from killing characters you like, to having characters you like do horrible things. I can't say I didn't see it coming, but it was still pretty f-ed up.

    I've had fun speculating about the Clairvoyant, and I guess it's just as well I never got around to posting about it since I was apparently dead wrong.

    Spoiler for not really a spoiler I guess:

    I thought maybe Coulson had an alter ego of some sort after the procedures done on him. Something about him had apparently changed, and they tried to turn him back into a decent human being. Well what happened to that other personality?
