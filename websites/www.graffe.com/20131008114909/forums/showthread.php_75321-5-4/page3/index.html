    Re: 5.4

    So the fansites got invited to an event at Blizzard yesterday. Bullet point recap:

    http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/...-Posts-TCG-Art

    Some points that stand out for me:

    Timeless Isle, and many of the other future activities for the game, will be more like the Barrens stuff and the dinosaur island thing. As someone who doesn't touch the Barrens and has never touched dinosaur island, this sucks. I find that this stuff typically is balanced around people in DPS specs, just like Brawler's Guild was, and my favourite alt is a Disc-spec priest.

    The world boss will require you to have the legendary cloak to engage him. Because when you think about a world boss, the words "exclusive access" are the first to come to mind.

    Originally Posted by Article
    The devs weren't happy with how the Glory of the Raider mounts were working out, as you had to kill Heroic bosses and kill bosses in ways that were incompatible with killing them on Heroic difficulty.
    I weep for the poor heroic raiders that had to spend one week slumming in Normal to get their mounts. #firstworldproblems

    Heroic scenarios are working out really well. A lot of players are completing them successfully and the design is closer to the initial vision for scenarios.
    My guild has one person who does H Scenarios daily. One. Everyone else caps valor on day one, thanks to the broken +50% valor buff. I could always spend my time in a city, with my thumb up my ass, spamming General channels trying to find random people to do these scenarios. I just wish there was some kind of tool in game that could find random groups of people to pair up with me for them... yeah. Seriously, if this "make friends, schedule your playtime, waste time looking for content" stuff is the new normal, I'm out. The other folks I know who used to be big alt players have all left or cut back very significantly on time played, and I'm not going to continue playing a game where I feel like the creators of the game don't respect my play style at all.

    Patch 5.2 was supposed to be the last major thing the quest team had to work on before moving to the next expansion, but there is so much storytelling in Patch 5.3 and Patch 5.4 that they got pulled away to work on those. Most of the Patch 5.4 quest team work is done now, so they are back on the next expansion.

    Instead of seeing a huge pool of randomized quests, we might see more story based daily quest chains like Patch 5.1 added.
    So after 5.0, no new five mans. After 5.2, no new quests (planned). This isn't WoW, this is EQ 2.0: spend time in the world, kill massive numbers of mobs, and hope you see the rare drops that allow you to progress your character. But, one positive thing: the 5.1 story quest model worked really well for me. I'd love to see more of that with future reputations, where every day or two as you progress your rep you see story updates. I thought the Isle of Thunder was a big step back in that regard, with progress tied to other people on the server (and in a very non-intuitive way, where people who brought out a bunch of alts when they could to help progress were actually hurting it unless they brought them out every day).
