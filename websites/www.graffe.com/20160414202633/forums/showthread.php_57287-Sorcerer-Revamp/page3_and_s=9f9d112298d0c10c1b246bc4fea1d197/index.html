    Having an AOE specific damage-based hate redirection would actually make the sorcerer's AOE damage usable in many instances.

    A 1 minute reuse timer, and only having it effect AOE abilities, would allow a sorcerer to blast very hard early on in a fight to hate dump for a tank, then proceed to blast away sparingly with aoes.

    This would be a strong boon for grouping, and also for certain raid encounters.


    I don't think that it should effect direct damage spells, or else certain abilities that the psionicist has or will have might be undercut.


    AOEing is the sorcerer's unique area where they should utterly dominate, and any abilities to lower their aoe hate generation should be explored.

    The only reason sorcerers to not utilize their AOE's is that they usually gain excessive hate from them, or that the aoes for some reason are still not more cost effective against multiple foes.
