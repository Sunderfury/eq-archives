    Take Rallos Zek, for example - outside of the pullers and the kites for the final phase how exactly was there any difficulty for anyone in that fight? And did you know you can trivialise the *entire* kiting phase through Bard Tricks TM?

    It was hard because of the numbers required, of the artificial limits on access, the random unavoidable wipes, and the limitations of the UI at game at the time. Put the same fight into WoWand LFR groups will be killing it in the first week. The was a reason the entire top core of raiding in EQ shifted to WoW - the raids were simply *better*. More involving, more challenging and more interesting.
