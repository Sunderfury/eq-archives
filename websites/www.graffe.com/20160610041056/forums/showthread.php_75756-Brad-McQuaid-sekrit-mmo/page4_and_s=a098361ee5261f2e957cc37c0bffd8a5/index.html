    It was really strange watching it. I felt like I was watching an EQ group, but playing in a different world. They even were using a lot of EQ sounds as placeholders.     It was funny, near the end of the stream they took on an area boss (at level 7) and wiped. When they all respawned at their bind points, the boss was still aggro'd on them and came across a huge part of the world to kill them again and again
