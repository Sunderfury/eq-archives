    Well, try Guild Wars 2.
    They don't have that WoW setup.
    Actually my friends complain that GW2 provides them with all the abilities at level 5-10-ish (level 1 in the Mist), and then there is nothing to play for.
    Personally I think it's fine and refreshing to have all your weapon skills at level 1, if you wish.

    Also if PvP is your thing, you can create a level 1 character, enter the Mists, and join in on an equal level straight off.
    Or do WorldvsWorld as level 1 and then gain levels through that, if you want Xp to matter.

    PermaDeath could be fun if it's done right. In the old standard Pen & Paper roleplaying games, there were permadeath. Nobody ever thought that was wrong.
    But in an online MMORPG, it hasn't worked at all so far, and a working setup would probably have to be a lot different than what we have seen now. At least if they want more than a handful of players.

    At any rate, I am not going to argue to GW2 is revolutionary is every way, but if people stop playing and expecting it to be like WoW, it actually has shaken up the MMO template quite a bit.
