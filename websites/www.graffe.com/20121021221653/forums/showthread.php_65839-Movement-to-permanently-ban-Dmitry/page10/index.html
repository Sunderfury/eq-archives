    Re: Movement to permanently ban Dmitry

    No-one wants to contact Maledict...

    Anyways, the nature of the War account won't feature into the discussions on what to do with Dmitry. If it is him, it doesn't matter - we already know he will evade bans and suspensions, he's been doing it for years. doing it again doesn't make it triple bad or something, it's just part and parcel. If it isn't him, then the person wasting their time trying to set it up is just wasting their time - if they want the account, feel free to PM me or email me.

    Fundamentally, someone is using a new account that evades the regular IP tracking to be posting. If it is Dmitry, then it remains suspended. If it isn't Dmitry, then whichever normal poster it is who wants the account can PM me and I'll release it. As per usual - the identity of the person who owns the account wouldn't be revealed. In the same way various folks have switched accounts and identities over the years, I don't think it's particulary relevant that the account details became public knowledge.

    Note - the reason the account is suspended is because if we don't suspend it, then suspensions and bans don't mean anything. Anyone can post through a new account on an IP scrambler, we can't track it. Someone gets suspended and a new poster appears in their defense using an IP hiding mechanism - we either accept than suspensions / bans mean nothing and remove them from the toolbox of the mods, or we deal with accounts like that in the same manner until it's shown they aren't the suspended person. (which is easy enough).

    Re: Movement to permanently ban Dmitry

    At the moment, it's rpesumed to be dmitry, hence it was suspended as he's on a 7 day hiatus from the board.

    If it isn't Dmitry but a new poster, or a regular poster wanting to hide themself, then they can contact me and get it unlocked. I haven't told anyone the ID of snuffle, I'm not going to blab this ID either.

    If it is Dmitry, then the account will be unsuspended when his suspension goes.

    If it's someone trying to get Dmitry into trouble - then no luck, it's not going to feature in the discussions around him.

    Fundamentally, it is not an ideal situation - but the technological limits behind IP tracking and the circumstances mean that suspending that account is the only thing that makes sense. Poster is suspended, within a day a new account shows up hiding behind an IP disguise defending said poster - either we suspend that account as well, or we remove the suspension completely and don't suspend anyone full stop.
