I'm almost done with Jade Forest and the only pieces I've replaced were my Mainhand and Offhand combo (Heroic Sword from Rhyolith and Normal offhand from Baleroc) with an epic caster staff from a rare. I found that this particularly rare drops the epic staff so I checked the area and managed to find him alive. Got the drop on my second kill.

    But I'm enjoying the game for now. I don't think this will be a long term return but I'm curious to see the zones, dungeons and try out LFR when I get there. I realize I came back at a very bad time for Mages but since I'm still leveling the nerfs haven't affected me. I level as Frost anyway which is our top spec at the moment. And besides, I HATE Arcane and only used it while raiding for guild progression since the DPS couldn't be matched despite being a terribly designed spec.
    There are other worlds than these.

    Re: 5.2 on PTR

    Valley (the next zone up) has ilvl 399 rewards, with the occasional blue 419; same with Krasarang (although the rares there drop BoE ilvl 440 pants on occasion). Kun-Lai is 414/434. Depending on your pace, you should ding somewhere around Kun-Lai, either in it (if you're only leveling while rested and taking advantage of stuff like holiday quests), or in a zone after it.

    In terms of appearance, I think I liked Valley the best. Rolling hills, open farms, lots of bamboo huts with friendly NPCs. It also has the farm thing in it. Kun-Lai is also pretty neat, since it's supposed to be the Tibet of the continent.

    Do the dungeons as you level, once for each, to see what they're like and how they fit into the story. The heroics at 90 aren't much different. The neat thing to try is the scenarios, since who you get in a group can change how you do them significantly. I also find them more fun. If you really want to try something addictive, get into pet battles. Enjoy your time back, however long it lasts!

    Re: 5.2 on PTR

    You'll open up two new dungeons at level 87, and then an array of dungeons at level 90. Nothing in the levels in between.

    I'll add that gearing up at 90 is extremely trivial now: Besides the 5-mans, you can run the "old" LFR's for 476 and 483 gear.
    I got three drops in an evening, they have REALLY upped the droprates.

    You gain 120 justice per boss in 5-mans now (if you're in a highlevel guild). That translates to a LOT of points, very fast - those can be converted to honor points, and you can buy 476 gear for those.
    Remember that PvP gear works just as well for PvE now.
