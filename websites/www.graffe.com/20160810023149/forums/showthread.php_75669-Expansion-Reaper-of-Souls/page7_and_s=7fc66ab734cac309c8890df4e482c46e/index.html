    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Man, that last boss in Act V is some ridiculous crap. Took way too long stubbornly trying to beat him on Torment 1. On some attempts nearly the entire playing area was filled with his stupid death clouds. The bright side to the weird bloated HP scaling was that my dots (as well as the witch doctor dot length passive) actually showed some use. But dots weren't enough to beat him, and while trying different things I discovered something other people probably figured out a long time ago. Tip for anyone else with a witch doctor:

    There is a passive that gives you 100 mana regeneration over 10 seconds if you use certain spells. Now, the two offensive spells that make use of this cost 100 and 200 mana each, so you might be like me and wonder what's the point? Well I set spirit barrage (100 mana) to my main attack slot during one of my attempts, and while kiting him I noticed my mana regen getting so fast that I could spam cast the spell even without mana voodoo. As far as I can tell, the passive regen stacks with itself. It can sometimes be hard to get going, but you can just unload with this spell and do a good bit of damage.

    Right now I'm running spirit barrage, soul harvest, spirit walk, voodoo, fetishes, and piranhado. I do terrible aoe damage, mostly just using pirannas and soul harvest then letting the wizard finish them off, but I burn anything unique down extremely fast. I'm tempted to drop fetishes as being overkill, but most witch doctor aoes cost mana, which makes it harder to get the spirit barrage deluge going, and the little guys do a good job blocking monsters.

    I'm up to 5m toughness. I think my damage is 220kish. 300kish with soul harvest. 450kish with voodoo. I'm currently using a 3k dps two hander, but I'm hoping to find a good 1 hander soon as I also have a cool legendary offhand which summons demon dogs without needing to use the skill. Even with loot 2.0 I didn't really start finding stuff I could use on my witch doctor until 70.
