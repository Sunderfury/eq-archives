[edit] Thestra

[edit] Level range 12-16

Location: Veskal's Exchange

Description: Plenty of quests are available in the Exchange Outpost. The surrounding areas include the Verdant Lurker Headquarter's (as detailed in the AE guide), a Graveyard and spider caves.

[edit] Level range 15-20

Location: Renton Keep

Description: For the unadventurous, Renton Keep represents the top spot on Thestra for these levels. A small fortress sitting in the middle of the large farmlands of Central Thestra, it has several quests that ask you to kill nearby bugbears and ants. (And one where you help bring in the harvest!) The area tends to be crowded with people, because of its easy access, and finding a group is rarely difficult.

[edit] Level range 16-20

Location: Silverlake

Description: A small picturesque village situated in a scenic location towards the North of central Thestra. A quest from Renton Keep will lead you here, and if you make it past the dangers of Trengal Keep enroute, and past the halfling brigands, you will find a perfect place to spend some time from 16 and onwards. A plethora of quests, perhaps the most important of which will give you a very good hand-held weapon (needs level 17, and starts at the farmer at the edge of the village by the path to Renton Keep). Some quests will lead you towards Cairnworth Hall, where a Friendly Ghost will give out additional quests to locate Relics in the 4-dot dungeon below.

[edit] Level range 18-20

Location: Misthaven Crossing

Description: The chunk is located just North of Veskal's Exchange, and is the center of vampiric activity on Thestra. There's a small village in the center of the chunk with a few quests, which will send you to liaise with the Vampire Hunter and others.

There's also a small building with undeads around it that's good for idle grinding, directly South of the village.

I found the monastery especially nice for a sorcerer around level 18+. Similar in layout to the Verdant Headquarters, the only thing that detracts is the relative low number of mobs. At level 20 I could clear the place out just in time for the next spawn cycle. If there's anyone else in the place, you will fight for spawns.

[edit] Level range 20-24

Location: Shoreline Ruins Description: At the far North of Thestra, just East of the Deebs, you have a heavily forested area with a good number of decent quests and places for XP. Travelling along the path, you will come upon an outpost village that is in trouble with a local keep and undeads roaming the nearby bay. Quests are also available at the guardpost on the path.

The keep is OK for a bit, but the respawns are too slow to stay for long.

Much better is the undead ruins (Stormwynd) down by the bay. At 20 it was a challenge to find pulls that were manageable (mobs are 2 dot level 21/22/23), but great fun. Good xp too, with the altar not far away for the inevitable many deaths.

[edit] Qalia

[edit] Level range 10-14

Location: Qa Riverbank

Description: Plenty of quests available at the Neamsog Bunker. Missives available at Neamsog Bunker as well. The missives are close by, convenient, and easy to do. Quests available at the Neamsog Bunker lead you to other points of interest in the chunk. For example, several large Ksaravi outposts and villages to the north, and a dungeon to the north west (Azebaj Hive).

[edit] Level range 26-30

Location: River Valley

Description: This is a contained zone with about nine hundred mabillion very high end exp quests. It is also the zone that leads to the River Palace Dungeon which is at the far end and is worth taking you screen shot camera to see. There are two evac points in the zone, both up on the river path - one near the mines entrance and one near the River Palace.

At level 26-27 you will need a partner, by level 30 you can pretty much solo all but a handful of quests.

To start the process go to the mining camp outside the CIS building. There is a quick delivery quest that opens up a bunch of quests in the camp. (I think it starts in the big tent or maybe it's outside in the little tents but you have to go see the guy in the big tent... you'll figure it out.) Make sure you run around and get them all. Near the cave entrance above the camp (on the path behind the big tent) are a few more quests. Get those too.

Go through the cave. (follow right wall or left wall, everything is yellow/nonviolent till near the end of the cave). At the back of the cave are ruins. There are nasty angry girly tree chicks at the end. Invis or kill past the evil girly chicks to get out into river valley.

River valley itself has two drop down areas one on the right as you zone in has the best stuff for level 26 or so. Lots and lots of fast spawning two dots to solo and there are quests associated with your kills.

One the right drop down are higher level quests in a camp in the middle of the right side.... bunch of nutbag drug addicts in there.

On the left on the drop down is a tent. You can see it off the left ledge. Three guys in the tent that give a puss load of more quests that are sort of level 27-28 with a few 29-30s.

If you have invis, then there are a few run around and gather quests that will net you about a half level once you kill the chromatic lizards (which by the way drop scales the color of their skin).

A handful of the quests appear to be broken. HOWEVER THE FLOWER QUEST IS NOT BROKEN!!!! DO NOT SELL THE JEWELRY FROM THE FLOWER QUEST (THE REWARDS) you need to have it in inv to get the next part done. I do remember an escort quest in the crazy guy camp was not working.

This is small group/solo content and at least on Woefeather there were lots of people around to do this with.

One thing. There are literally 4 quests that involve the fricking frogs. Don't do the frogs until you have the black lotus quest (level 27 I think), one or two from the tent guys and one from the crazy guy camp on the right. There are frogs that spawn near the tent guys but the best place for them is on the right side (as you face river palace) and near the river palace end of the right side. There are literally twenty spawn points there.

I did nearly all the quests in there I think. Send me (Dmitri) a tell if yah get stuck.

Things to know :

- Duo with a priest/paladin/disc/sham = best solution because somes mobs have sick resist rates (see 31 or 32 beetles/mosquitos when your 28).

- The Chest and Legs can BE COLLECTED with ZERO kills. Exploration = 4+2 items to loot, all of these items are on the ground. Move around, you'll see a red exclamation mark on some items on the ground. Pick them and you get nice Legs/BP

- Absolutely amazing Tank gear (70hp earrings/mask). No real caster loot in the rewards (nice 25int +42Spell staff)

- LFG for Infineum mines, and solo/duo in River Valley Great solution from 25 to 32-33

- Cash if you are lucky is obsene (go farm the Adventurers & Sylvan in duo). I got about 5 gold in 3 days selling stuff & with cash items. 8 Arcadian swords at 20 silver = bingo

- Train Dmitry on every ocasion (most important of all)
