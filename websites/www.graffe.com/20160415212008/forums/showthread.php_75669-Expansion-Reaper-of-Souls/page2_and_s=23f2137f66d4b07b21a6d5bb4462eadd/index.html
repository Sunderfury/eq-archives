    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Originally Posted by Wool
    Did they ever explain their reasoning behind the ridiculous original balancing of them by the way? It seems like it would have made more sense to have them scale to your level.
    It's an over-reaction from Diablo 2 hardcore players (The guys with 1000+ hours like me, not the people with Hardcore Characters). At the end game, you would have your cookie-cutter spec, and you needed specific cookie cutter gear. It was always a specific set of legendary items you needed to have the best items in the game. So those players wanted ultra rare random gear that could be better than the Legendaries.

    So they set it up so that Legendary items were just mediocre Rares. Because of how much loot was available on the AH, it meant that those ultra rare pieces were super common, enough so that Legendaries would just get burned, to be crafted into one of the few good craftable Legendary items. Once they changed Legendaries by adding more of them to the game with new effects, and changed it so that appropriate Legendaries dropped (so you weren't competing for 1 of 3 level 63 Legendary items in Act IV).

    It was one of the major failings of Jay Wilson: Catering to what the 0.1% said they wanted, not what was fun. I think if you're going to be successful as a game maker, you have to listen to the problems most people have, and ignore the solutions offered from almost everyone.
    "Complaining is the modern metagame" - BNet forums
