    Re: GW2 or Rift or WoW

    Auction houses are marked by the gold fuzzy coin emblems with a scale on them - there aren't that many in each city, and cities have multiple layers so its easy to miss them. (especially the human city).

    Hollow squares are points of interest - you get a small amount of xp for each one you go to, and you need to go to all of them to 100% the zone. 100%ing any zone gives you a reward - generally some level appropriate gear for that zone and some tradeskill stuff + decent experience + coin.

    Vista's generally just rely on you finding a fairly simple route around to get too. You need to grab each vista in a zone to 100% it as well. The game also has a large amount of much more complicated jumping puzzles you can do for rewards as well that aren't required for zone completion.

    Re: GW2 or Rift or WoW

    For a starting mesmer build doesn't hugely matter because your locked to the minor trait lines until level 40. Also at these levels the stat gains from the trait lines are actually significant so my advice would be to put points into the lines that increase your damage for levelling - that's power, crit and crit damage for mesmers.

    (Despite what you might think we are actually not that great at condition damage for PvE, especially at lower levels).

    Re storylines, the choices you make at creation determine your storyline up until level 30 I think, at which point you follow the same storyline as every other character. For example, my mesmer did the human noble storyline up until level 15, then did the 'dead parents' storyline. So there's some replayability in new characters it it drops off later on.

    On that note I will have to be negative as well - the personal story because utterly terrible once you hit level 30 or so, and doesn't ever recover. I won't spoil it for you, but its simpy junk after a while.
