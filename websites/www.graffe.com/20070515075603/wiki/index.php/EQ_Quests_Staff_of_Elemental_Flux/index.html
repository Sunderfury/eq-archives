[edit] The Staff

The Staff of Elemental Flux is the upgrade to the Staff of Temperate Flux. You must complete that quest before this one.

[edit] The Quest

This quest begins in the Lesser Faydark with Mithious Scratch located in the upper area of zone close relative to the Greater Faydark zone line (no exact loc)

He tells you he can make your staff better for 100pp. Hand him 100pp and your Staff of Temperate Flux. kabam!

Mithious Scratch breaks your staff of Temperate Flux!

He then sends you to find the set of instructions he forgot and lost. These reside with Nathen (located in Erudin in the inn near the Library. First floor, last door on left). He asks you for a automated quill (geerlock) and a piece of parchment. Upon turn in you are rewarded with a little instruction book item (can't read it). Returning it to Mithious results in him asking you to bring him the ingredients for the spell. He needs a Blue Diamond and a Powerful Solvent. The Blue Diamond is the common item sold for around 300pp, the Powerful solvent hinted at my Mithious is from Alchemist Bauer who is locaed in North Freeport in the Jade Tiger inn on the second level third door down. As soon as you hail him he attacks, he hits for nothing harder than like 90's on my weak ac self, and was killed with one shot of Thundaka.

Now comes the fun part. After breaking your staff, and insulting you by sending you off on errands you finally have all pieces. Turn in the broken staff, the diamond, the instruction book, and the solvent to Mithious.

He starts a power hungry rant about now having the power to stand up to the Temple of Solusek Ro! And he is not giving you your staff! So you annhiliate him, loot the staff and laugh as he fades to dust. Fight was easy solo, with 200+ in most resists he didn't land anything, and he has under 10k health. Magic was resistant (at least that one Thundaka I cast) and Strike of Solusek hit just fine and blew him up nicely)
