    I don't know how he did it either, but I have guesses. Amplifiers have pre-sets on them that are programmable and accessible by a push of a button. Sometimes between riffs you can notice a little movement of his right foot or see him glance down. That is probably him pushing a pedal and switching pre-sets on his amp. I have an amp with four such pre-sets, its pretty wild. You can go from a clean bubbly style to punk thrash sound in a heartbeat. He also adjusted the top string up or down for some songs, used effects like a slide (the little tube on his finger) and used the wammy bar (little rod that hangs off his guitar). He was amazing.
