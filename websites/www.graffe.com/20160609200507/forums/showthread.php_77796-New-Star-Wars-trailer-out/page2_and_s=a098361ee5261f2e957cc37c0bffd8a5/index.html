    I think people are over-thinking the Revan stuff. While it's possible they share inspiration, it's not really that iconic a design. It's a Jedi guy with a cool mask. There's no reason to think the similarities go any further, as indeed, the context of KotOR's story would make this problematic even if you did want to do such a thing. At the most, I might believe the producers were specifically drawing inspiration from some of the cooler designs that already exist.

    I would also like to point out that the KotOR timeline takes place so many thousands of years before the movies, that there is no reason to address the Old Republic content at all in relation to the new stuff. Most of the Expanded Universe takes place during the same era as the movies, so there are very clear cases of overlap to take into account. The Old Republic is practically a different thing.
