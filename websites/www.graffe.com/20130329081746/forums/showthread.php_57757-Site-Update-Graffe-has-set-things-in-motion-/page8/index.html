    Another small update. After reading the relevant threads a couple of times sifting for themes and points of agreement it has struck me that there are two issues wrapped up in rules discussions. (1) What rules should we have? (2) How should those rules be enforced?

    Under (1) fall questions like, should we have a no personal attacks rule?

    Under (2) fall questions like, should mods enforce the no personal attacks rule when they see one, or only when someone reports it?

    In the interests of finding as much common ground as possible I think it would behoove us to try and seperate these out, since some disagreements over (2) type questions seem to be masquerading as disagreements over (1) type questions in the discussion. Expect the new rules discussion posts I make tomorrow to reflect this distinction.
