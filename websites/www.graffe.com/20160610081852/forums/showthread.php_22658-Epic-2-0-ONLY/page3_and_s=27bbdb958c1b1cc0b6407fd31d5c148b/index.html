    Wiz 2.0 Epic Completed.

    Congratulations to Sectre D`Phlith of Cestus Dei, Veeshan, on being the first wizard to complete the 2.0 Epic for Wizards Server wide.

    The last piece of information we had was

    Grathnok Gilforgan says 'Your resolve and strength of will astounds me. You have returned to my people, the most sacred artifact we have on this world, and for that, you have earned my gratitude. As promised, I will give you the information that I have on the discordant globes. These globes can only be collected in one area, where the magic of discord constantly fluxes and an eerie sense of pain and anguish resides. Beware, it is an extremely dangerous area, protected by many of the Muramite Army's best and highest ranking members. Good luck to you, I hope you achieve all that you aspire.'
    A few weeks ago, the Globe Of Discordant Energy was found to drop of Warden Hanvar in the Tower of Anguish. This is a full raid event.

    After obtaining the Globe, head back to Talwyn Flamecaller in Lavastorm Mountains. Giving him the Globe Of Discordant Energy resulted in the following information

    Talwyn Flamecaller says 'Your first task is to take the right half of the orb to the highest point achieved by a wizards spire on Norrath. On the western prong, you will find a small tear in space. Place the orb inside this tear; it will absorb the pure essence of teleportation. The second task will take you to the highest point achieved by a wizards spire on Luclin. Find another tear in space there and absorb that essence as well. Return to me with both halves imbued with the proper essence and hand them to me, along with your staff.'
    He also breaks the Globe Of Discordant Energy into 2 different items

    Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and
    Broken Orb of Discordant Energy

    So based upon the information provided, there are two spires that must be visited, one in Norath, and the other in Luclin.

    The first spire is located in Dreadlands. Proceed to the massive spires just south of the port in spires. Get some form of Levitation, and proceed to scale the North West Spire. When you reach the top (a long way up), you will see 5 glowing containers each named "Always Work".

    Place the Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and hit combine to yield Infused Orb of Discordant Energy.

    The next step is to proceed to the highest spire in Luclin - in Mons Letalis. Once there, go through the tunnel upto the platform, and then levi and scale the NW Spire again to the top where there is again a container named "Always Work". Place the Broken Orb of Discordant Energy and hit combine to yield Infused Orb of Discordant Energy.

    From there head back to Talwyn Flamecaller in Lavastorm Mountains. Hand him the two Infused Orb of Discordant Energy with your 1.5 Epic totally un-augmented.

    Talwyn Flamecaller looks at the item for a moment before speaking, 'You have returned to me with an item I seek. I shall hold onto it until you return with the rest of the items.'
    Talwyn Flamecaller looks at the item for a moment before speaking, 'You have returned to me with an item I seek. I shall hold onto it until you return with the rest of the items.'
    Talwyn Flamecaller says 'With these Infused Orbs, your path to becoming a soldier of Solusek's legions has been completed. You have surpassed even my expectations!' he says. You hear Talwyn chant in low, almost inaudible tones. His hands glow bright with power as he assembles a weapon worthy of Solusek himself. 'Take this staff as a symbol of your loyalty to Solusek and weild it proudly, wizard!'
    and returns to you the completed Epic 2.0, Staff of Phenomenal Power whose picture can be seen below, as well as 10 aa's
