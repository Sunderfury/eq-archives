    Re: Bat v Supes: Dawn of Justice (trailer)

    I thought the tonal shift between the two trailers was very interesting. In the first one, it sort of explored the darker side of Superman and presented Batman as the guy that has to stop him. In this trailer, it instead showed the darker side of Batman and presented Superman as the nice guy needing empathy. It's almost as if each trailer is from the point of view of each of the two characters.

    As for the Avengers comparison, maybe DC doesn't need that formula as much as I thought they did. With The Avengers, you had a set of heroes that anyone who isn't a comic book fan may not know much about. Everyone knows Batman and Superman though, thanks to decades of movies and tv shows. You don't really need another origin story for them.

    For what it's worth, I liked both trailers and look forward to seeing this, eventually.
