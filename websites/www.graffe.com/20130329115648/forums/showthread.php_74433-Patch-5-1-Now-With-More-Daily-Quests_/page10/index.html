    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    I have an alt, Sprickett, that hit 90 three days ago. Today will be her fourth day of daily quests at level 90. She's had double rep for all reputations. This is what I've seen:

    On day three, she was 17000/21000 Revered with Order of the Cloud Serpent. Today, the fourth day, she'll be Exalted.
    Anglers rep will take just under two weeks to go to Exalted, at 3300 rep per day. This is without having done the Dread Wastes Anglers quests.
    On day three, she hit Honored with Golden Lotus and opened up the third set of dailies. I expect I'll be Revered by Friday, so in a week or less.
    Today, I did my Tiller's quests before camping (so four rounds). She's 6300ish/21000 Revered, and has 12 plots to farm (harvesting these plots alone is 1320 rep per day).
    After three days she's 5500/12000 Honored with Shieldwall. She's had a new storyline quest to do every day, despite only doing the Beast daily one of those three days (so she's missed 880 potential rep).

    I have not done Dread Wastes on Sprickett yet. However, I recall that after the normal quests in the zone one ends up somewhere in Honored with Klaxxi. With double rep, which applies to normal quests as well as dailies, if you normally end up at 1500 rep or more into Honored, Sprickett will end up Revered and able to do the final few paragon releases (which will put her even further into Revered). This will also open up the full set of dailies, which for her should be about 2000 rep per day. She'll end up Exalted in roughly a week after completing the zone quests, I'm guessing.

    As you can see, the double rep is amazingly fast. I honestly don't believe that alts need tabards, and I say this as someone who will be completely snowed under by daily quests on alts if they prove to be onerous. I could see it for mains, though. If the devs wanted to put some kind of limit on it, my suggestion would be to have the tabards only work until Revered, when double rep begins and when all gear on the vendors is available. If people want the vanity items at Exalted, they can do the dailies, at double rep. Even for the worst reps, that would be ten or eleven days from Revered to Exalted, which is reasonable.

    As for the token thing, I'm one of those people who still has over 1400 of the things banked, so I'm not worried about running out any time soon. But for people who use double rep to make it to Exalted, and then stop running the quests, I could see problems cropping up. I'm not as negative about linking them to pet battles as you are, Mileron. It was intended that they come from a solo activity, questing, so I can see why they'd want to keep it that way. It's interesting that they want to give regular XP for pet battles, though. I guess it was so addictive that they found they were slowing down their leveling too much!

    EK Safari done. Just need Scourged Whelpling, Silithid Hatchling, and (next summer) Qiraji Guardling to be a zookeeper. Starting to work on the leveling achievements by rotating in various pets as I grind for blue-quality pets. Got my very first stone to improve quality. Congrats to my newly-blue Perky Pug!
