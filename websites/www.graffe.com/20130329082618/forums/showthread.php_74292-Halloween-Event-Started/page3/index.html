    Re: Halloween Event Started

    This is only tangently related to the Halloween patch, but I saw my first skritt burglar last night.

    Was in Mount Maelstrom, when I noticed a large chest just sitting out in the open. Confused, I looked around for the event or boss monster guarding it. Seeing nothing, I run up and trigger it, and a skritt jumps out! He starts cackling like a maniac and runs off, the chase is on!

    He pretty much trains you through a ton of different enemies, and every now and then dropped little glowing bags you can click on for treasure. He also had a few other surprises along the way, most of which I didn't notice because I was frantically trying to kill him.

    In the end, he gets to a circle of mushrooms and starts gibbering a spell. At 10% life, he teleports away before I can kill him and I fail the event. I'll get you next time Skritt Burglaarrrrrrr! :shakefist:

    Spoiler for achievement surprise:

    One of the surprises while chasing him, was him turning me into a creature, snaring me, and taking away my abilities. Basically I got polymorphed. I then got an achievement (in the explorer pane) "a waddle to remember". The flavor text says something about "stomping around as a baby siamoth".

    I don't know if the achievement is specifically linked to the event or not. It makes me wonder if the Skritt burglars have specific locations, and specific achievements you get for chasing them in each spot. Or maybe it was a coincidence.
