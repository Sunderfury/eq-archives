    Re: 5.3 on PTR (Soon)

    Originally Posted by Dawlin
    I still need some other consistent venue than dailies to get the lessers. Yes, I know that outdoor mobs will have a chance of dropping them.

    <Insert rant about dailies for extra chance at raid loot here>
    It took me around 45 minutes per character to get 90 Lessers, doing pet battles.

    Alliance directed, though I'm sure there's a Horde version that's possible
    Must be level 90
    Team of rare pets, level 1 (preferably with attacks that are strong against critters)
    Stormwind Mage District
    All level 1 wild pets there are single battles, and are all critters (rabbits and squirrels)
    You will easily level each pet to 6, and be eligible for a chance of Lesser charm drops (2-6 can drop, since your team is within the 5 level range)

    I leveled ~15 pets to 6 this way easily (and made 450+ Lessers) in about 3 hours yesterday.
    Also got the "catch 50 rares" and "win 1000 pet battles" achievements while doing so
