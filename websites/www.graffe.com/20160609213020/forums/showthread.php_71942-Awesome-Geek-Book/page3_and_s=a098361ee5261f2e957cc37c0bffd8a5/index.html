    I've said this before and I will keep saying it as long as needed: Comparing any movie to its source material is bound to cause angst, so why bother? Just wait for it to come out.

    Anyway, I have no problem casting a shapely hottie as Art3mis or any other character in the book, including Parzival, because characters are incidental to RP1. It's just a trip through '80s nostalgia list with gee-whizbang special effects, which is why Spielberg is the wrong choice for director. The overall point of RP1 would best be handled by someone like Michael Bay, Zack Snyder, Chris Nolan or Bryan Singer, who have SFX chops and the right ages to feel or at least understand appreciation for the era's nerd culture. Their main weaknesses (shallow characterizations, lack of subtlety, clumsy handling of actors in some cases) won't matter at all in this case.
