    #1 - Sorcerer
    #2 - Druids and Necro tied (necro will be able to deal more damage over time but druids will have larger "burst DPS)
    #3 - Rogue
    #4 - Psionicist



    What i really wonder is what type of dps this is.


    Everyone always seems to think of dps as a singular value, when really there can be different types.


    Is this a crude list of the "Average" dps, or a max potiential, or efficient dps?



    I hope we can eventually get a rough list on how he expects more of the classes to relate.


    Assuming that aggro based on distances is changed to -1% per meter, meaning that casters at 25m have an inate -25% hate, while melee classes have a -30% hate from this buff, i would think that the 3 methods of dealing damage would work out as such:

    (note, the 5% difference in aggro plays in with the strengths and weaknesses of each archetype, so that they deal closer to the same damage. Casters on normal and burn fights have a damage advantage over fighters, but this comes at a slightly higher risk of pulling hate, while fighters only really have a damage advantage on efficiency fights since they never run out of endurance, but they have the anti-hate advantage in every fight.)


    Burning:

    Obviously casters should have the highest "max" potiential, but be hindered from constantly maintaining this max potiential by energy and aggro issues. The offensive fighters should generally not be able to spike their dps as high as casters, but they should have less chance of pulling hate while they spike their dps.

    Efficient:

    On long fights where casters have to adapt to a fighting style that does not drain their resources or cause excess hate gain, Fighters should start to get much closer in terms of dps, perhaps passing casters, due to their slight dehate advantage as well as their endurance being an infinant, unending resource.

    Average:

    On an average fight that contains elements of both of the above, the dps ranking should probably be very similar to what has been described as the "what it should be after the changes" list.



    Casters more total damage potiential, slightly less hate mitigation.

    Fighters more efficient damage potiential, less max damage potiential, slight hate mitigation advantage.


    I think that will keep more with the tradition of Eq, as well as make more logical sense, rather than be "dead even" in hate generation and damage.

    Regardless, both of these differences should be SLIGHT, not drastic, as in a sorcerer/druid/necro would out dps a rogue by a little, not by double.
