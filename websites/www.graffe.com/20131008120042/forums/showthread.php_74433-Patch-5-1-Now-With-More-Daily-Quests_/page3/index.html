    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    More nerfage: the Spirit of Harmony vendor now only gives you two Golden Lotus for a Spirit, down from three.

    Even more nerfage, but in a good way: the spawn timers for the blue ground spawn items for "Is Another Man's Treasure" have been reduced. Significantly. Very significantly. Based on observations with a guildmate last night (when he went from 9/20 to 18/20 and I went from 9/20 to 15/20), we're talking "same respawn as a Pandaria silver elite" repop time. Get the locations from the comments on Wowhead about the achievement, and get hunting, because I'm not sure that ilvl 450 BoA weapons are supposed to be that easy to obtain.

    Cleared Molten Core solo with D, which was nice - I was worried that somehow I'd be killed as cloth. Then I saw that the MC bosses had less health than some of the Golden Lotus quest mobs, and laughed my way to two new pets. BWL was not as kind - couldn't solo Razorgore. Has anyone here had any success at that? I've heard others say it can be done, but it's looking grim on my priest.

    Important point: if you're doing quests in Krasarang, and you see a "Someone on your faction has captured an animal" message, get back to your base's animal pens. When the animal is released by someone, anyone who deals damage to the animal will get a daily quest to hand in once the animal dies. You can only get one animal-related quest per day, even though there's three different animals, and it gives 200 rep. If nobody is catching animals, you can buy a cage for 30 of those tokens off the vendor, and it starts a quest chain. Pick crab - all the others have stupid drop rates. I'd consider banking my tokens for the rat cage (which releases a bunch of battle pet rats, unique to that item, at your base), but someone had done that the first day I arrived and I managed to grab a rat already.

    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    Per blue

    Viscidus:
    You only need 20 frost attacks to freeze him.
    After that you need 15 physical attacks in 15sec to kill him.

    Twin Emperors:
    The healing got reduced. You only need 16k dps to kill the bosses before the enrage timer kicks in.

    C'Thun:
    The one-shot ability got removed. You can kill the tentacles inside the brain and then go for
    the boss again.

    Al'ar:
    He is not immune to fire damage anymore.

    Reliquary of Souls:
    Essence of Desire casts Spirit Shock every 10sec, the debuff only lasts 4sec.

    Gunship Battle:
    If all members are using the cannons and no one is outside, the battle doesn't reset anymore.


    Verified myself that C'thun can be soloed just a few minutes ago

    The "punt to room level" "dust swirly" does ~15% HP damage per tick (40-50k at 90).
