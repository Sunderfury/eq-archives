    Re: Returning player questions

    I think when you reach level 20, you get an ability called 'Zen Pilgrimage'. Then you will get a class specific quest to use it to go to the Peak of Serenity in Kun Lai. Once a day, you will get a daily quest (as well as a monk specific quest every 10 levels that will grant you an assortment of gear... mostly belts and staves). That daily quest will give you a bonus to XP gain of 50% for one hour. But you will need to remember to use Zen Pilgrimage to travel to the Peak of Serenity to do the daily. You won't be prompted to go there again.

    This is how it worked for my panda monk.
