    Re: Gun control has been inadvertently achieved

    Originally Posted by Merrick ap'Milandra
    How does the internet gun business work across state lines?
    You pay for the gun, then the seller gives you a reciept with their contact information and sales #. This you then take to your local gun dealer who then faxes his dealers paper work to the selling store, who once makes sure the paper work is proper, sends the firearm to your local dealer. Once it arrives locally, your local dealer runs the required background checks and if there are no issues, the sale is completed. Local dealers charge around $35 for a FFL transfer. I did one back in October this way to get a hard to find .22 rifle for my 12 year olds birthday.
