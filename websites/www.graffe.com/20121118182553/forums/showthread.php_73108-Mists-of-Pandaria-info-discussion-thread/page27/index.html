    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    If you type "/pvp on" and then type "/pvp off" you will stay PVP enabled as long as you are attacking someone in PVP PLUS five minutes.

    So if you're standing in Stormwind, and type /pvp on, then /pvp off, and stand around for 5 minutes, you'll become non-PVP.

    But you need to stay PVP-enabled to capture objectives.

    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Originally Posted by Macfudd
    I think I misunderstood how achievements were merging.
    Some of them are working that way, some of them are not. There's no guidelines on which will be which, just have to merge and see. I was very surprised that Bloody Rare and Frostbitten were account-level, since the requirements for them aren't something as simple as a number. Remember that you have to log all the alts on to get stuff to merge.

    Originally Posted by Mileron
    Some of my mounts "feel" faster than they did pre-patch.
    They likely are faster. A couple of the old guild perks were removed and new ones added to take their place. The two new ones I remember is +10% mount speed and +25% flight point speed. There's other possible reasons why your mounts are faster (my Unholy DK now has On A Pale Horse baseline, for example), but that one is the most likely.
