    From Allakazam:

    the mission named is "Rescue Fibblebrap" and its devided into 3 parts. to start it, say "i will do it" to him... if you got high enough faction, you will be able to pick a mission

    part 1 - instanced in corathus creep... when you zone in, 1 person will receive fibblebrap's memory thing. have to fight thru mines to kill alpha corathus, and turn in the alpha meat to the named at the end. and he will give you fibblebrap's gem 1. when you are done, return to bellfast, and 1 person needs to turn in both the gem and memorysphere

    part 2 - instanced in stoneroot falls - have to get to the named warlock and kill him. can loot fiblebraps gem 2. turn it is to belfast

    part 3 - 2 instanced zones in the hive. in the 1st zone, clear to the named - who is in the room to the west and north. when the named starts to run, the earhquake will start. let him go, and clear to the south room, where the door to the next instanced is. zone in, and clear mobs near zone in. Then from the zone in ledge start pulling mobs around the named (he will be way down bellow you). heavy CC is needed, since pulls will be 5+... when you cleared as much as posible, jump down near named, and kill him... cant pull named right away, cause he is tathered to his spot, and will return once he reach zone in. when the named is dead, loot fibblebrap's gem 3. 1 person needs to turn it in for progression and exp

    part 4 - need to find a npc that will give a task to find some powerful alchemist and he also mentioned something about some power gems...
