    Re: Warlords of Draenor

    The art reveal for garrisons was today:

    http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/2...ng-a-garrison/

    Nothing terribly special, although this caught my interest:

    Originally Posted by Blue, bolding mine
    For the profession hubs, we wanted the player to be able to experience leveling up their chosen professions not only with skill points, but also visually through their Garrison. As the player progresses, each profession hub will level up, and the associated building will become more visually impressive. We treated each profession hub as an opportunity to describe the NPC who works there—how neat or messy they are, how they might work, and how they’ll grow with you as you level up your Garrison. We also thought it would be fun to throw in small hints of different races that might be associated with certain professions, such as draenei for Jewelcrafting or dwarves for Blacksmithing, to tie in the many races that give the World of Warcraft its depth and history.
    That gives some idea about the tie-in between the garrison and professions. I also thought it was cool that there's a mage tower as one of the building options. What would make that REALLY cool is if the mage tower's library had some of the clickable books you can find throughout the world, and as it levels it gains more books.

    Re: Warlords of Draenor

    Lots of new WoD info, including a major Alliance lore spoiler (it's blacked out on the page, you have to highlight it to read it):

    http://www.mmo-champion.com/content/...e-and-Spoilers

    Observations:

    -that spoiler better not be the end of the story. That would be a very shitty lore thing to do, in a game that's done a lot of bad lore things in the past.
    -that first video is so painful to watch. You can't judge DPS because the guy plays like crap (didn't put poison on your mainhand? really?), you have no idea about stats because the guy never bothers to show the character panel, you don't know if the new racials have been done yet because he never looks at abilities (gnomes are one of the races that need a racial changed because of hit and expertise being removed). Total fail, sir!
    -in the second video, we see the racials for orcs: the old ones that no longer make sense are gone, nothing has replaced them yet. The guy making the video still blasts past new stuff and spends minutes lost in the new base. You can see fairly significant rep gains (20 per) for killing mobs, and get a better sense of how quickly combat goes against even-cons.

    The third video is the one that worries me. In it, we see very clearly that the "squish" hasn't been applied to old content. One of those grey yaks (the little ones in the forest) on Timeless Isle nearly kills the guy's fully-geared level 90 orc with one special attack. Anyone want to guess if the "squish" will be applied correctly to old content, or will that be botched? You can also see that the non-intro zones are unfinished, or at least the maps for them are. Interesting item: the rep bar with Frostwolf Orcs on the map of Blade's Edge Mountains. Can Alliance players earn rep by killing mobs? Can it be done more quickly than "20 rep per mob kill, you start at max Hated"?
