[edit] Specialization FAQ

[edit] Why do I want to specialize?

Each Specialty receives their own three piece Bind on Pickup set. This set has its own 3 piece set bonus. In addition, each specialty will create twice as much of their cloth per combine as someone else with the same ingredients. If its important to you, please note that Primal Mooncloth is used to make 20 slot bags, and making 2x Primal Mooncloth at a time is a huge savings.

[edit] Can I change my specialization at a later time?

Unfortunately, at this time there are no mechanisms to allow you to change your specialization.

[edit] What level tailoring do I need before I can specialize?

350 is the required tailoring level. When you reach the required tailoring skill, visit Shattrath's Lower City, and all of the Tailoring Representatives are in place. Each will offer an individual quest.

[edit] What is the Quest for Spellfire Tailoring (Fire+Arcane)

[From Krona/Byronius]Get the quest from the Spellfire tailor in Lower City - Shatt. She gives you a item to go summon a elemental in Netherstorm. You can summon the elemental anywhere in Netherstorm. No requirement. I summoned it right outside of the Consortium Bio-Dome. Level 70 NON-Elite.

Kill the mob, loot the essence and head back.

Turn in, and whalla, your Spellfire spec quest is complete.

[From Maledict] It's important to note that the elemental you summon from Spellfire tailoring hits like a *truck* - it's shadowbolt is over 3K damage. This is not an easy quest to solo.

For added fun - one appears every time you create Spellcloth as well!

[edit] What is the Quest for Shadoweave Tailoring (Shadow+Frost)

The quest for Shadoweave Tailoring sends you into the Shadowmoon Valley to Netherwind Pass, and the Altar of Shadows present there. While it is guarded by several level 70 beasts, demons, and blood elves, if you sneak along the route of Fel Lava, you'll only run into a couple beasts. I (Nadiar) made this run at level 64.

[edit] What is the Quest for Mooncloth Tailoring (Healing+MP5)

Travel to the Mooncloth vendor in Shattrath. She will ask you to travel to the Cenarion Refuge, where you stand in the Moonwell and use the sample of Primal Mooncloth you are given. After this, travel back to Shattrath and complete the quest.

[edit] What are the set bonuses?

  • Shadoweave: 2% of your damage dealt from Shadow and Frost spells is healed. Consists of Shoulders, Boots, and Vest. +179, +66 stamina.
  • Spellfire: 7% Intellect added to your damage. Consists of Belt, Gloves, and Vest. +172dmg +69 crit rating
  • Mooncloth: 5% of mana regen while casting. Consists of Belt, Shoulders, and Robe. +275 healing 22mp5

[edit] What is the cooldown on making each piece of cloth? Is it shared?

All three types of cloth have a 4 day cooldown that is not shared.

[edit] How do I skillup past 360?

Your only options here are to craft Arcanoweave Boots if you can get the pattern, Soulcloth Gloves if you can get the Soul Essence (this is the best option), or Imbued Netherweave Boots. The Imbued Netherweave Boots will cost you a large fortune, but are the cheapest option available to nearly everyone. I'd recommend getting the Arcanoweave pattern.
