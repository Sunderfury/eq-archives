    You know, I don't have time to find it, but when we were discussing the new rule set the vast majority of people felt that we didn't need to have rules about everything. (Yes, I am calling Dmitry on hypocrisy here.)

    Many of the conventions covering mod behaviour are like this. There is no specific rule that says "mods will not harass posters for making reports" or "mods will not take it into their hands to change the historical practice of anonymous reporting by fiat". There is no rule that says that "the technical admins will not moderate non-spam in the absence of a immanent threat to the board", but Nadiar still abides by that guideline.
