    Re: Game of Thrones: Season 3 Starts Soon!

    Originally Posted by Bonlainy
    Wouldn't you think GRRM put something in the agreement with HBO (or whomever it is with) not allowing them to write their own conclusion? Maybe he did leave it open for them, but it seems he wouldn't want them to do that if he plans at least two more books in the series.
    I can't imagine HBO agreeing to produce the series if GRRM required them to stop if they caught up with the books. When they purchased the rights, it allowed them to use the world and characters and anything else covered by the copyright, but it didn't make them beholden to every last detail. They're free to make up an ending of their own - or a beginning or a middle, for that matter - but they stick to the canon because that's kind of the point of an adaptation.

    D&D have frequently mentioned discussing as-yet-unpublished portions of the story with Martin, and Bryan Cogman mentioned in a recent interview how the writing team spent several days discussing the possible course of the story in the last two books. If the HBO series goes on that long, then with or without any more books from Martin they'll have at least an approximation of the canon plot. And honestly, I'm rather looking forward to what the series does with AFFC and ADWD, given the ability to reassemble the threads chronologically and axe out all the fluff and filler sidestories Martin has used to inflate the last two books.
