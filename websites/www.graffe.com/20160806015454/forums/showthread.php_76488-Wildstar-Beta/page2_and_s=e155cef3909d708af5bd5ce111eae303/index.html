    Re: Wildstar Beta

    I've played it for 9 levels on 2 characters and I like it. It is highly stylized, and the combat thing is very consolish. As an explorer I experienced a ton of jumping puzzles which - combined with the console style combat - made a d-pad worthwhile. The story aspects are average for the industry which isn't much, but I found them mostly enjoyable, if slightly confusing. Overall I found the game to be a nice hybrid between pure level / class based systems like WoW and skill-tree load out games like TSW.

    It's a fun romp through a theme-park MMO, and a nice departure from the more Sandbox feel of Landmark. If I could justify the money I'd pre-order and play for a month or two at least. I don't know anything about dungeons, raids, pvp or housing, but the concepts look cool.
