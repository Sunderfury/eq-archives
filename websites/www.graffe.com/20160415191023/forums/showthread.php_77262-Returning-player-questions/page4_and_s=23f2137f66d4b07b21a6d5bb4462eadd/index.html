    Re: Returning player questions

    Unfortunately I'm not the best person to answer this question, for a number of reasons.

    Most of the main storylines are interwoven throughout the respective expansion continent.

    Take Wrath for example.

    The story doesn't just encompass Arthas - it also covers the Titans and the origins of Azeroth.
    Arthas' story can be found in Borean Tundra then to Dragonblight to (maybe a little Zul'drak) to Icecrown and those raids.
    The Titans weaves through Howling Fjord, Sholazar, Storm Peaks and Ulduar raids.

    I didn't finish the quest lines in any of MoP, but that was when I really started raiding, albeit via LFR, so I didn't have the "lore lead-in" on any of them.
