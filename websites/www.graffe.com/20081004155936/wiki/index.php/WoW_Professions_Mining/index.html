[edit] Mining

Mining is a gathering profession; unlike herbalism in mining there are not very many things you can gather. Mining has its "assistant profession" of smelting. Once you mine the ore you can smelt it into bars; some bars you can combine to make another type of metal.

[edit] Minable Ore

Once you learn mining at level 5 (or any level afterwards) you gain the ability to smelt copper. You never need to train to be able to mine a mineral vein, you must only have the skill required to mine it. You must train to be able to smelt the ore into bars though. Each type of mineral has its associated stone, thus mining copper will always yield rough stone. Its also possible to mine uncommon (or, in the case of Outlands ore veins and deposits, rare) gems from each type of mineral.

Also keep in mind that you can find some of these veins in special habitats. For instance, if you find a Mithril Deposit in an insect nest its going to be "Ooze Covered Mithril Deposit" This does not change the skill required to mine it or the possible rewards. Exception to this would be the ones in Zul'Gurub (Hakkari Thorium Vein) that contain Soulderite about 30% of the time.

[edit] Smelting

Smelting each ore into bars is trained at the same skill level that you can mine them. Smelting requires a forge. Smelting copper is learned upon training mining. Most smelting is grey (doesnt provide skillups) exceptions to this are Copper (green at 70) Tin (green at 75) Bronze (green at 115) Silver (green at 125) Gold (green at 185).
