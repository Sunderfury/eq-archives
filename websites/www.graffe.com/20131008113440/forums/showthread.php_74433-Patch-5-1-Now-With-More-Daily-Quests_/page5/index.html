    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    Shez makes a good point for one market segment - those who were rushing to have a BiS item and had lots of cash and had lots of other valor upgrades to buy. They will move on now as they either see an alternate upgrade through raiding, weren't the kind to spend 20k on a slot, or already have it. The next wave is more limited - there isn't as much of a burning need to have a BiS item if you aren't pushing the raid curve and if you didn't have tons of cash a few weeks ago, you aren't likely to have just finished saving 20k for one item. But as an inscriptionist, I feel you, the ink/herb nerf hurts the supply of the components a lot and I suspect the material costs will establish the price of the end product with a small profit, rather than the premium the trinkets were providing before. Especially after people burn through their stockpiles of herbs and inks from the glory days of omg so many tea leaves.
