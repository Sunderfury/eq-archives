    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    Tell you what, the Grand Commendations have to be one of the best items added to the game this expansion.

    My human rogue hit 90 on Sunday, having done 2100 into 3k Neutral with Klaxxi (prior to my having the Grand Commendation.)

    Bought the Commendation on Monday, started the rest of the Klaxxi quests with my rogue today, and after 12 hours (granted there were other things done in there, so I wasn't solely doing Klaxxi quests) I'm over 8k into Revered.

    If I had waited til next Sunday to use the Faire rep boost, I would have gained approximately an extra 2700 rep, equivalent to 9 dailies. So I could have saved myself a day and a half or so.

    Alternately if I had done other quests before starting the Klaxxi chain I would have gained an additional 2100 rep, again equivalent to a day and a half.

    Not that it's important either way, I'll probably be exalted by next Sunday.

    I just wish the other reps were as easy to start as Klaxxi. I still hate the GL dailies, which is why I'm not doing them every day.


    As far as Winter Veil goes, with 10 characters running the Greench daily each day since it started, I've ended up so far with 17 extra Clockwork Rocket Bots and 2 of the Miniature Tree daggers. Plus uncounted racers, gorillas, rag dolls, red rider rifles, and zeppelins. And a crapload of free guild exp and JP.
