    Re: Warlords of Draenor

    I can live with no flying to max level, thats something that we've become accustomed to. But not being able to fly at max level is actually something that would convince me to not play the game. I'd almost certainly keep my subscription, but instead I'd log in, do whatever was available literally at the feet of my characters, then log off and play something else.

    Without flying, the game becomes a lot less enjoyable to me. Travelling is wasted time- it's not a fun activity because I'm not actually playing the game - its the wow equivilent of my trip to work. And I do the same thing I do on the train to work - I read on my kindle. Countless times I've had my girlfriend ask what I'm doing and I've said 'Playing wow' and shes replied with 'but you aren't even at your pc' or 'but you're reading?' and then I try to and explain that part of playing the game is getting to where I can do something. And she replies with 'It doesn't sound like a fun game'.

    Replace that with a ground mount ride and I prop the kindle up next to the monitor and try and read while steering out of the corner of my eye. And if I aggro something and get dismounted - thats only added fustration. There's only two outcomes, either enough things aggro'd that I die, or I manage to survive the encounter. Both just delay me from getting to do the activity that I actually want to do in the game. And then because of these delays the total time I spend doing something I like is reduced, and I'm getting less things done for the hours I put into it.

    And seeing as they also removed fast corpse runs and reduced the number of convenient graveyards in Mists then that just adds even more fustration. I LIKE that when I finish a quest in an area and find that the mobs have all respawned behind me that I can fly back and turn in. The alternative of killing yet another dozen bears to get clear of the bear habitat is just wasting my time. Do people really enjoy those quests of 'Kill x at the bottom of this cave', so you wind your way in, kill it and then slowly wind your way back out killing all the respawns?

    Originally Posted by Ikeya
    Maybe they'll make flight paths not suck this time? You know, they'll be direct and flight points will have multiple routes to other flight points as opposed to just one or two?
    Heh, we all wish but I doubt it. Each flight path has to be individually coded to guide the player along the path from A to B. If you tried to do (relatively) direct paths from any given flight point to any other given flight point, for N total FPs you'd have N(N-1)/2 paths to code, and that's going to get big fast. What I'd like to see is code that takes you along the most direct flight path, whether you have the intermediate points or not. You shouldn't be able to stop at the intermediate points (and they'd need code to prevent people who disconnect from landing at an intermediate FP they don't have), but it's not like the griffon you're on should skip them all because you don't know they exist yet.
