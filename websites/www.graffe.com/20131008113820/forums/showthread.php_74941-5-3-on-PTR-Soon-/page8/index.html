    5.3 on PTR (Soon)

    For tanking in lfr - run the fights early in the week. Tuesdays have worked best for me but they're not bad on Wednesday/Thursday either. The higher dps players and better healers run these days and any tanking mistakes will be less noticed.

    Read a quick lfr tanking guide for when to taunt on the debuffs etc.

    Tell the other tank in a whisper you're familiar with the fight but never tanked before and ask for advice. If he knows you're new he can take a second to tell you when to taunt if necessary before you get someone killed!

    With those I jumped right into tanking and never had any problem.
