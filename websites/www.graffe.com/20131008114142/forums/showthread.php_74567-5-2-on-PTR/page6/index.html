    Re: 5.2 on PTR

    The farm work orders are a nice rep bonus, but they're a kick in the pants, because unless you prepare correctly, you can only do them every other day.

    For example, the slacker that I am, my paladin and mage only had 2-3 options for work orders. GL, OSW, Klaxxi, and OSW, Klaxxi.

    I started two on each character, finishing Work Orders 1. Have to wait a day for the plants to grow, finishing Work Orders 2.
    The next day (the same day you harvested to finish WO2), you plant more for another repeat of Work Orders 1, but because you already turned in Work Orders 2, you can't get the actual quest again.

    So realistically, you can only work one rep at a time if you dedicate your whole plot to it.

    And you have to have unlocked that faction's dailies - which means no boosting AC or SP until you actually hit Revered GL.
