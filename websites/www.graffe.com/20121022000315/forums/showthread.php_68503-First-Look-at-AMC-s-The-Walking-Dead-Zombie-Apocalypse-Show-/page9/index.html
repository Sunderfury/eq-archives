    Re: First Look at AMC's The Walking Dead (Zombie Apocalypse Show)

    I liked the last episode alot. It did some important things that the Graphic Novel failed to do early on, I thought:

    1) Established the relationship between Dale and Andrea. Both are favorite characters of mine, but their connection has never been very spelled out.
    2) Showed how Rick ended up in the hospital bed alone, and Shane's involvement
    3) Gave the survivors a definitive break from the "real world." Its over, now, there is no going back.
    4) Defined the walkers medically (why the heck do we not use the word zombies?).
    5) Developed Shane more as a character.

    As to the timer, you know that whole sequence could only work in fiction. What are the odds that they show up right when the whole thing is going to blow and the scientist is basically suicidal? Pretty good if you write it that way. The computer just included the fact that it was going to conserve X% power in its estimate.

    Now I am sad we may have to wait until October 2011 for the next episode. This was a pilot project, by all appearances, but man is it fun TV.
