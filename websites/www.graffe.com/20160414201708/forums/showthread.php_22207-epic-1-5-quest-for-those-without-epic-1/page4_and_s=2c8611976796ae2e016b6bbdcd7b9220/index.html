    Summary of the Test of Intelligence

    Compiled and organized: will update as we all solve pieces of it. . .

    *
    One riddle's answer:
    the empty jug

    Katta, in the bar "nectar of unity" a spawn on the table "an empty jug"

    *

    Riddle: the bask through the woods near a crypt

    In the cave heading to Crypt of dalnir(? right spelling) crypt through Warsliks Woods, not the opening before dalnirs crypt, but the one thats a dead end with the brutes in it, "a meditation basket" lies in one of the corners...

    *

    Riddle:
    emerald staff that is near a runestone in a maze;

    confirmed to be Emerald-tipped Staff in Maze of the Beholder (Gorge of King Xorbb) p221 n1165

    from EK - go first right, second left...all the way to the dead end, behind the rock.

    *

    Riddle: The discarded Pipe slowly sinks into the cauldrons bed.

    you will find this in Dagnors Cauldron at loc -864.57, -914.21 underwater.

    *

    Riddle: Though the cloth sits in the burning flames of the eye of the maiden, it does not become harmed.

    Red Cloth. neg 31, neg 713. In maiden's eye.

    Its in the giant fire by UP.

    *

    Riddle: This valuable token is worthless in the prison of the planes.

    Platinum Token pos 267, neg 174 in poJustice.

    in the Genni, Golikan, dumann the quick room.

    *

    Riddle: These bones sometimes wash up on the shores of the gulf

    Gulf of Gunthak
    They're in the boat stickin out of the water in front of that dwarf, go inside (above water) and you'll see a little bone looking item that blends in real well with the inside of the ship.

    More reports: Someone found the bones on the boat deck, but under water at loc n 34.38, N 513.26, N 64.36

    *

    Riddle: This Sash can be found near the Spires of the deadly mountains

    White sash in Mons Letalis, loc was 24, 624, 207. up where the Lord spawns. The greaters do see invis: be careful.

    *

    Riddle: this turban was lost in the endless sea of tranquility

    Worn Turban" Lore no drop all/all : no ID to it.

    This item was reported in the Fishing Forum at EQTC, confirmed asthe Wizard epic piece.

    Wizards, get your fishing poles and start fishing in PoT!

    *

    Riddle: This ceremonial sword can be found under the dragon in the lands of burning rock.


    The Golden Sword can be found under the dragon statue near the druid port-in.

    There have also been reports of the sword spawning by the other Dragon statues in Skyfire as well. One was spawned at the Dragon Fire Temple / Cleric Epic Dragon Fight statue in NE Skyfire.

    *
    endgame:

    Talwyn Flamecaller says Excellent work, Wizard! There was once a time when i thought these puzzles could not be solved, but through preseverance and knowledge, you have passed the test of intelligence.

    Reward: Rune of Greater Desiccation.
