    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    The keys are used for the Uber Quests
    There are 4 Keywardens (you've probably seem them), in Acts 1-4. I believe at 70, any of them T1+ can drop the pattern, but it used to only be Nekarat.

    Keywarden Act & Zone Key
    Odeg the Keywarden Act 1 Fields of Misery Key of Bones
    Sokahr the Keywarden Act 2 Dahlgur Oasis Key of Gluttony
    Xah'Rith the Keywarden Act 3 Stonefort Key of War
    Nekarat the Keywarden Act 4 The Silver Spire Level 1 Key of Evil


    Using the dropped pattern,you take all 4 keys, and create one of 4 Infernal Machines. You enter the house (Heretic's Abode) near the healer and merchant in Act 1, and click the Portal Device on the ground, and it opens a rift to the corresponding realm.

    Inside you have an Uber Fight. They're more bad ass versions of previous bosses.
    Machine Realm Item Drop Ubers you Fight
    Bones Discord Leoric's Regret Leoric and Maghda
    War Turmoil Idol of Terror Siegebreaker and Zoltan Kulle
    Gluttony Chaos Vial of Putridness Rakanoth and Ghom
    Evil Fright Heart of Evil Diablo and randomly summoned other Ubers (2x)

    Now you take all 4 of the item drops, the pattern you buy in Act 2 from Squirt, and you can make a Hellfire Ring. It's basically like any other ring, but it increases XP by 45%, and has a chance to cast a ring of fire for 200% weapon damage when attacking.

    I don't know if the ratio for the Item Drop is the same as Legendary Mats.


    edit: Oh, and the Hellfire Ring has no level requirement to equip.

    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Drop toughness for damage (for example, swap those resall gems for straight int). I run about 3-4mil toughness and have zero issues surviving in T1; I've found that killing an elite pack faster is honestly better "survivability" than trying to just live through their damage output. Swap out some of the armor/vitality for damage stats (crit, critdamage, frost damage, frozen orb damage- I stay away from CDR, attack speed, etc for the most part since frozen orb spec tends to be AP starved anyway). What is your crit%? Crit% and critdamage scale together- you can stack all the critdamage in the world but if you have no crit%, it won't do anything. I've found once you've hit the 30+% crit% range you'll find that your paperdoll damage starts zooming up.

    Edit- here is my wizard's profile: http://us.battle.net/d3/en/profile/a.../hero/33303347. I only have 3 legendaries (and one of them is a L60!) so it doesn't take an incredible amount of gear to do well in T1. In-game with full buffs I'm sitting at 930k damage and 3.8mil toughness.
