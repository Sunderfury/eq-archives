    I got carried away this year. I am usually fairly responsible, and only had a short list of games to buy. This year though....

    I got four of the five games I planned on buying: (I skipped DmC because the discount wasn't good enough and I don't have time for it yet.)

    Fez
    Dust: Elysian Tail
    Blood Dragon
    Rayman Origins

    I got four more games on whim.

    Hotline Miami
    Scribblenauts
    Reus
    Sleeping Dogs (through Amazon)

    I got two games after nagging friends into agreeing to play with me:

    Payday: The Heist
    Orcs Must Die 2

    I got one game after sending it to a friend as a joke (only cost a dollar) and deciding I wanted it too:

    Organ Trail (Oregon Trail parody)

    And I got DLC for Borderlands and Strike Suit Zero. I might also get Poker Night 2 if it gets a flash sale.



    Luckily most of these are short games, I've already beaten a few of them. Sleeping Dogs is the one game out of the bunch I shouldn't have gotten, as it's the only one I don't see myself playing soon.

    Based on what I've played so far, Hotline Miami is easily recommendable at $2.50. It was more fun than I thought it would be. Fez is pretty good so far too, and Reus is interesting although not as deep as I expected. It's a step above a flash or iPhone game (like those Kairosoft games), but not as good as a classic PC game. For a dollar, Organ Trail is worth a laugh, but gets old.

    Do not buy anything that is not a daily, flash, or community deal. Games rotate between those three deals and do not get lower (except by pricing error) than what they offer. If on the last day, something never got a higher discount: then it is safe to buy.

    I could have sworn I posted this warning this year, but I can't find evidence that I did so. Probably wouldn't have mattered anyway, but I apologize nonetheless. Every year on various forums people shout this warning at the top of their lungs and people still get burned. Next year you will be the one warning people, and the one shaking your head in regret as someone gets burned anyway.
