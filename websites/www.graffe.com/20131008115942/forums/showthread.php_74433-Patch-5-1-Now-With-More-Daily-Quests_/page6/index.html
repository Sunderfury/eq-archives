    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    Ok, things I've noticed tonight that I need other people to confirm:

    (1) The +5 skill quests at Darkmoon Faire were reset at some point this week. If you did them early in the week, you can do 'em again.
    (2) The seeds at the farm that grow herbs have been changed. Someone on another board posted getting 10/16 Golden Lotus, so I did a round of plantings yesterday. Result: 7/16 Golden Lotus. I'm doing a round of ore seeds today, to see if the trilium chance has changed.
    (3) I noticed something odd about MoP battle pets, specifically, pets that spawn in groups (like three of something, or a mom and some kits). This is what I observed:

    Engage one of the group. The front pet will have quality X, then you'll have secondary pet A of quality Y and secondary pet B of quality Z. I would forfeit the match, and engage the second group. The front pet of that group would also have quality X, and the two secondary pets would still be A and B with qualities Y and Z. Forfeit, go to next group, same deal, for all the members of the group. What made this stand out as unlikely to be a coincidence: I did one set of four (mom, three kits) where I saw the same blue quality secondary pet in all four groups, and later on saw the same blue quality secondary pet thing in a group of three. The implications are that you only need to check one of the members of a group to know what all the members will be like, and that if you see a blue in one member of a group, all of the members of the group will contain a blue.

    Originally Posted by Delores Mulva
    (3) I noticed something odd about MoP battle pets, specifically, pets that spawn in groups (like three of something, or a mom and some kits). This is what I observed:

    Engage one of the group. The front pet will have quality X, then you'll have secondary pet A of quality Y and secondary pet B of quality Z. I would forfeit the match, and engage the second group. The front pet of that group would also have quality X, and the two secondary pets would still be A and B with qualities Y and Z. Forfeit, go to next group, same deal, for all the members of the group. What made this stand out as unlikely to be a coincidence: I did one set of four (mom, three kits) where I saw the same blue quality secondary pet in all four groups, and later on saw the same blue quality secondary pet thing in a group of three. The implications are that you only need to check one of the members of a group to know what all the members will be like, and that if you see a blue in one member of a group, all of the members of the group will contain a blue.
    This has been the case since launch. The result might not have been intentional, but it was programmed this way. The way it works is this, any pets that spawn at the exact same instant will actually be exactly the same. Same quality, same stats, same breed, same secondary pets, ect. This is most noticeable in pandaren, like you said, with mothers / kits (the front pet will vary in name, but not quality or stats). But it can be seen elsewhere in the world, like on server starts. Previous to 5.1, if you went pet hunting on server startup, you may see 30 of whatever pet it is up, but only actually have 3-4 different combinations, because they spawned in 3-4 different batches.

    The wondering festival is the same way. There may be 10-15 hatchlings that spawn, but they will all be exactly the same. Which means you literally only get 2 chances a week at a rare, even if your server is deserted and you're the only one there.

    5.1 has changed this slightly though, in that pets do not pop on server start anymore. It's been randomized. But the underlying code is still there.

    Note on value: on my server, currently dusts are 1.7 g each (using Undermine Journal for pricing). Windwool is 2 g each. Herbs are 4 g each, Golden Lotus 100 g. Exotic Leather is also 1.7 g each.
