    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Adventure Mode:

    It's a lot more limited than I was expecting and is more like random dailies.

    Pro:
    Usually quick
    Often generate using random dungeons/rare areas
    Often see a lot of additional elite packs/rare mobs
    Decent rewards - upwards of 7 million XP and ~100k gold at 70/Hard for all five + final bonus
    Random Bonus rewards (double gold, double shards, etc)
    Once you finish Act V on any character, doable at least as early as 11 (I waited until I got Town Portal spell)
    Seems to be a lot more Cursed events present

    Con:
    If you disconnect or exit the game for any reason (even DC) your list of bounties will clear/be reset.
    Large packs of mobs
    Still going back to the same places



    Cursed Chests:
    I've gotten a number of "elite pack" spawns out of Cursed events
    I've even had a boss-boss spawn from one - Ghom, in Fields of Slaughter/Act 3 (while on a Bounty).
    I've also had a couple just "bug out" and give me a timer with no activity, and only give me the single chest.
    Still, if you have a decent cleave, they're quick exp and loot.

    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Originally Posted by Mileron
    Discovered you can start Adventure Mode (once you beat act V with any character) at least at level 11. (I got my Crusader to getting the Crown before I tried.)
    You can play Adventure Mode with all characters once unlocking it with 1 character. I played Act 5 up until right after the "Her?" boss on my monk, and am just leveling on Adventure Mode now.

    Originally Posted by Wool
    Ugh, why does some stuff have so much HP? Was that one of the criticisms in that earlier beta post that I dismissed? It's really annoying! Trash is bad enough, but it took me 20 minutes to kill that first boss!
    There are a few different things going on.

    1) You're supposed to play through on Normal if you don't have max or near-max level of gear. On normal, their health isn't too crazy.
    2) If you were over level 61, the gear is currently broken. At level 65, you're getting level 61 gear with level 61 affixes on it, instead of level 65 affixes. Some items reset at level 66, so you start getting those slots as upgrades, and a few items are in between levels, and they roll with those appropriate affixes.
    3) The bosses level with you, so you're never significantly more powerful than they are, but between outleveling your gear and #2, you're not leveling "with you." Before you likely out leveled the bosses you were fighting, which made them significantly easier to defeat. If you go back and level now, the bosses all feel like they have a shit ton of HP.

    I accidentally reset my monk a few days before the expansion and forgot about it, so I played through the entire game last night. To make it go faster, I had it on Normal, but the bosses still had quite a few HP and took awhile to kill. I didn't think Urzael felt much harder than Maghda.
