    Originally Posted by DmitrytheWizzy
    I will bite on the build Z. I am level 21 and mostly pistol pistol but not married to that.
    Rifle is your choice of weapon and Power is the stat your gear wants to have. For traits 10 in Explosives and 10 in Firearms. For explosives take Burn chance on critical hit, for firearms take Rifle recharge by 20%.

    For healing you can use any but I prefer med kit for having the swiftness steroid every 20 seconds to get around the map faster. For my three utilities I use Toolbelt, Grenade, Rifle Turret.

    I actually only use toolbelt and Rifle turret for their F1-F4 abilities due to how amazing they work with Rifle abilities. Grenades/Dropping the turret is there for veteran mobs or multi pulls or events where i need to tag a bunch of mobs. Toolbelt is there to switch to use the #2 to add cripple when kiting or #3 for a crowbar whack with confusion.

    The average pull goes like this: Auto attack, as they come fire off the throw wrench (F2), as they get closer use Rifle #3 for the close range damage and bleed, immediately hit Rifle 4 for the double knockback, here's where you fire off your Turret F4 because it's completely off the GCD and can be used while knocked down or CC'ed. The wrench hits back on its return by now and then as the mob closes in hit them with a Rifle 2 net and auto attacks or if it has a fair amount of health still feel free to double hit them with a nice big jump shot damage.

    You can single target incredibly fast with the setup and sometimes I use the toolbelt kit completely for some fun. Smack them with wrenches (the third hit of the #1 combo is like 3x the damage of the other two), crowbars, and use a big magnet to pull enemies to you.

    Also don't forget to overcharge your turrets if you use them, the extra abilities are usually good!

    Re: Ohhhh yeeeeah!!!!!

    I think the spamming thing is just you guys getting used to the combat. I did the same thing at first, but now am comfortable enough to advise against spamming. Here's why:

    Most abilities have a situational component. You will want to use them to react to a specific situation. Even the damage specific abilities (which generally you can feel free to spam) have situations in which if you use them too early you will deprive yourself of some benefit you need later. Combo fields for example, or an interrupt/snare.

    Additionally, don't forget that you can swap weapons in combat. I've found that I never really stick to just one set of five abilities, I pick weapons that synergize and switch between them in combat, instead of spamming all the abilities on a single weapon.

    I'm not fully comfortable with it yet. It's so fast that it still seems like in mass groups spamming is inevitable, because the debuffs and buffs are so short that it is hard to coordinate them anyway. If everyone is generally playing smartly, it might work itself out.

    Re: Ohhhh yeeeeah!!!!!

    You cannot equip new weapons during combat, but you can have two sets equipped and the ` key will swap between the sets. Elementalist and Engineer never get this ability as elemental attunements and kits take the place of weapon swapping... and if you're not getting comfortable with the Elixir Gun and/or Bomb kit you're doing it wrong! Note that the Engineer kits will be in ability spot 7-9 depending on what you assign them to, but the ` key drops you out and back to your regular weapon; this is also true for any temporary weapon/item that replaces your ability bar in the game.
