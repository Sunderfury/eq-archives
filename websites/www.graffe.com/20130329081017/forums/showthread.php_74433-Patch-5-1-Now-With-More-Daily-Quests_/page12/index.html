    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    Well, first off, level that terrible turnup to 25. It is by far and away the best capturing pet. And it lets you have one team to capture any pet vs several teams of varying levels.

    Remember 25's dont soak xp anymore, so leveling pets just need to be in for one full round. The EK and Kalimdor trainers you can use anything. But start with EK since 80% of the time their first attack does no damage.

    This is the order I do the rest. I use different pets on antari / payne, but then i stick with one till 25, switch to the other.

    Bloodknight Antari
    Fast rabbit | Rocket chicken (but any mechanical should do).
    I start against the fel flame, leveling pet starts, swap in the rabbit.

    Major Payne
    Fast Fluxfire Feline (he's useless if he's not a s/s breed) | Any pet that can take the flower
    I start with the leveling pet, if stun is first, he takes an additional hit. If hit is first i swap him out. FFF can then super charge / windup and one shot the bear. He should have enough health to do the same to the mechnopeep.

    Aki - Vale
    Anubisath Idol | Anything
    Anub can pretty much solo this entire team. I start against the dragon, reflect the weather, kill him. Switch the weather to sandstorm against the cricket. Can swap the leveling pet in anytime during the cricket, they'll take no damage. Anub will be half health after the dragon, but regen during the cricket.

    Earth Spirit
    Any fast rabbit | Any fast rabbit
    Between dodge and Burrow 2 fast rabbits can easily take this team. If you time them right you can take zero damage against the rat. I use Flurry on the first rabbit and Scratch on the second.

    Air Spirit
    Any 2 dragons. Although I prefer Spirit of Competition | Dark Whelpling if you have it.
    Spirit can solo the first 2 using the heal whenever the moth cacoons. The final pet, the elemental is still hard, but usually darkness is enough to keep his healing in check and get him down before you die.

    Hyuna - Jade Forest
    I again use Dark Whelpling | Spirit of Competition but start with dark whelpling this time.
    Active darkness, not so much for the effect, but it's good damage. Kill first pe without liftoff. Use liftoff to avoid burrow on second pet. The turtle can take awhile, but spirit can heal / lift off each of his head buts to stay at full health.

    Farmer Nishi
    Ragnaros or any wave attack pet
    I swap in both leveling pets, then use rag's wave attack till the back line is dead. Then kill the flower.

    Mo'ruk - Krasarang
    Anubisath Idol | Grunty (or any pet that can take the turtle)
    I start against the worm. Anub should be able to get both the first 2 down. but even if he doesn't, the moth should be low enough for the 2nd pet to finish it.

    Water Spirit
    Anubisath Idol | Shimmering Wyrmling
    Sandstorm pretty much negates the wave damage. I start against the fish, reflect the submerge. You'll soak his delayed whirlpool, but it's ok. Swap pet in at the very start of the 3rd pet while he puts up delayed attacks, then swap in the Wyrmling, even if anub is still alive. Reflect back the whirlpool, kill the spirit.

    Shu - Dread Waste
    Spirit of Competition | Fluxfire Feline
    If healing on cooldown and using liftoff to avoid whirlpool, Spirit should be able to take the first 2. I'm not sure what would work if you dont have him.

    Zusshi - Townlong
    Magical Crawdad | Fluxfire Feline
    Crawdad takes out the elemental easy enough, and some of the snail. When he dies get the leveling pet in. Use FFF pounce to finish it off. Taking some hits doesnt matter, as long as FFF is in when the final pet comes out he gets 3 turns to fully wind up and one shot the final pet.

    Fire Spirit
    Anubisath Idol
    He can easily solo this whole team. I start against the dragon, use sandstorm when he's in the air since he's resistant to it anyway. You can reflect every liftoff. Make sure to reflect / swap in a soaker for any double conflags. Swap in the leveling pet during swarm. If the opportunity arises, reflect the final pets 2nd dot.

    Yon - Kun Lai
    Anubisath Idol
    Again, he can solo the whole team. Start against the rabbit. Put up sandstorm, reflect all burrows / liftoffs possible. Swap leveling pet in during 2nd pet's stampede.

    Sandstorm is good damage, so I cast it pretty much on cooldown, not just to keep the weather effect up. But sandstorm pretty much decimates any team with small frequent attacks as opposed to single massive attacks.
