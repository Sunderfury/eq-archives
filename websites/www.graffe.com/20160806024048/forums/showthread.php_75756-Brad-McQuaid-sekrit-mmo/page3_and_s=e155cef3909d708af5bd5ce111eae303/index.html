    Re: Brad McQuaid sekrit mmo

    Originally Posted by DmitrytheWizzy
    No game will ever again have the epic adventure of traveling from the Greater Faydark to Qeynos. It took me three real life evenings. Highpass felt like reaching a settlement in the wilderness after the death and dark of Kithicor. Then when you pass through the first Karanas and see the giant bridge??? I can still remember the moment I first found the road south into Qeynos. It felt like I had been on an epic journey.
    Shit yeah but it was the opposite for me. I started out in prosaic Qeynos, totally classic medieval castle city, with typical temperate continental climate and royal-european themes. Qeynos hills (qeytoqrg) was a nice little zone that was outside the newbie yards but still pretty safe and comfortable, with icy Everfrost and Halas connected through Blackburrow. The barbarians up there made for a nice little west coast community. Everything was all humans (and Erudites and Barbarians). I think I encountered one Ogre in my entire newbie experience, he'd run all the way from Grobb wanted a bind near the edge of town.

    Starting out in the vast plains of Karana, by the time I got to highpass it felt so remote, on the edge of dangerous Kithicor forest (such a great way to split the continent). Then passing through the commonlands and Freeport, which seemed so much more dry-climate and cosmopolitan than Qeynos. Arriving in Butcherblock felt like I'd finally arrived in the magical land of dwarves and elves and gnomes. My first time there I saw an Emerald Drake, there was never anything like that on Antonica. Greater Faydark itself was the commerce zone on my server, so arriving there felt like arriving in a big city.

    Apart from the coolness of Highpass and other landmarks (the NK Wizard spires, the EK Bridge, Kithicor, the EC Tunnel, the FP/BB ship) the coolest part of the journey was seeing the gradual change in PC characters you encountered along the way. East of Kithicor you'd see a lot more Dark Elves, Ogres, and Trolls. BB and GFay was full of dwarves and high/wood elves and gnomes.
