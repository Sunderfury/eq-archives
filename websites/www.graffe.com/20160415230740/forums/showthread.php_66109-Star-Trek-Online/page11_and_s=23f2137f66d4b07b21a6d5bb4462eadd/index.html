    Re: Star Trek Online

    With the sale of Cryptic, a lot of the original designers left the building.
    The new ones/those that remain are still pretty good, but seem overbusy trying to fix some older things and get F2P ready at the moment.

    That said...

    Today marks the "early access" for STO's Free2Play.

    Originally Posted by blogpost
    Starting tomorrow (Thursday, January 5th), anyone with a lapsed subscription to Star Trek Online will be able to log into the game and play as a Silver member. This means that any account that has in the past subscribed to the game, even if only for the initial month, can come back and play for free!
    F2P Features Matrix and FAQ

    Recent changes since my last post here:

    +Major space graphic revamp, where the "astrometric overlay" in sector space is now optional toggle
    +Major space skill revamp
    +Major ground skill revamp (went live with today's patch)
    +Removal of a LOT of the different tokens (exploration tokens of multiple tiers, admiral tokens of multiple tiers, etc)
    +Introduction of the Duty Officer system
    Spoiler for DOff System:

    The Duty Officer and Assignment system is an optional mini-game themed around the logistics of managing the full crew complement of your ship.

    We have several goals for the system:

    Provide significant non-combat gameplay that fills the desire for less overtly aggressive styles of gameplay.
    Create an engaging logistical mini-game.
    Provide a supplementary or even alternative character advancement mechanic - this will be useful for both factions, but in particular should somewhat ease advancement issues in the KDF.
    Establish a new set of parallel achievement goals and rewards in the form of the Commendation part of the system to give more goals for players who have hit cap.
    Provide alternative and supplementary mechanics and support to an array of frustrations apparent in existing systems such as the process for gathering anomalies, gaining high quality bridge officers, the Diplomacy grind, the lack of a parallel Diplomacy system on the KDF side, lack of usage for commodities, need for more Energy Credit sinks, and so on.

    +Additional timed events that take place at certain hours, including crafting events, Foundry bonuses, Borg and Mirror Universe invasions, a Galaxy circuit race, and a tactical simulation at Starfleet Academy (and probably on Q'onos for Klingons)
    +The last Feature Episode included the Remans and Romulans
    +The crafting system was made easier, but also with an additional Energy Credit sink with the inclusion of new vendor-buyable "non-replicatable materials" required in addition to vendor-buyable schematics
    +Mission Replay is now accessible from the Mission Menu, instead of having to travel to your bridge to do it
    +The Special Task Force "Event/raids" have been mostly fixed (from what I read in the forums) and have new gear rewards, including several ship item sets
    +The new Enterprise was revealed. Odyssey Class.

    Since I really don't partake of the end-game STF event/raids, my only complaints still include UI quibbles that I've mentioned before.

    The Graffe channel is still open... I'm the only one in it of late, though.

    Re: Star Trek Online

    Tomorrow starts the 2nd Anniversary Celebration, including quests from Q, and the introduction of the Enterprise F.
    Federation characters of level 5 and up will be able to take part in missions to acquire their own Odyssey class starship, which is only usable at level 50. (Until then it will be an inventory item that you'll probably want to bank to use at level 50.)
    According to the stats posted the Odyssey could be considered to be a science support craft or possibly an engineering tank, though without actually having access to it (yet) I can't say how I'd play it. It'll be a space whale, though.

    Not as bad as the new Bortas, which the Klingons are getting in response to the Enterprise's launching. Klingon characters can get that mission starting at level 21 (as soon as you get out of the Klingon tutorial, I believe) but also cannot use it until level 50.

    These ships will be available for free to characters who complete their introduction missions from Feb 2nd to Feb 6th. After that, they haven't indicated when or how the ships will become available again.

    Also, the Dominion/DS9 Featured Episodes will be starting later this month.

    Re: Star Trek Online

    I haven't been playing enough to keep up with this much, but seeing as Season 11 is starting today, I thought I'd post.

    Today starts a new era in exploration, now that the Iconian War is over.

    It's a New Dawn.

    Release notes: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...detail/9618603

    Spoiler for patchnotes:

    New Features:

    Featured Episode: Sunrise

    The new Featured Episode Sunrise has been added to the Featured Episode tab in the journal.
    This episode can be played by captains who are level 10 or higher.
    For more details, please visit the Sunrise blog at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...ode%3A-sunrise



    Admiralty:

    Admiralty is a new system which allows a level 52 - 60 captain to send out any starship that the captain currently has access to for assignments which will reward valuable assets and advance influence and prestige.
    The starships that can be used are all currently commissioned ships in the captains roster.
    This does not include starships still in rewardpacks or ones which are unclaimed in the ship store.
    Captains will only ever have access to a single Admiralty Ship per class of ship they have access to.
    Fleet and Mirror versions of existing classes are counted as separate classes.
    Each Admiralty ship has the following characteristics: Tier, Rarity, Stats, Special Abilities, and Maintenance.
    These characteristics will be used to determine which ships go on what assignments.
    There is also a chain of assignments called Tour of Duty which lead to the largest rewards each campaign has to offer.
    With the launch of season 11, players will have access to two campaigns each with their own set of rewards:
    United Federation of Planets
    Klingon Empire
    The Admiralty system can be accessed in the Duty Officer and R&D Systems UI as a new tab.
    For more details, please visit the Admiralty blog at: https://www.arcgames.com/en/games/st...miralty-system



    Badlands Battlezone:

    The Badlands Battlezone is a new zone for all level 60 captains and is accessible from the Alpha Quadrant.
    Fight the Terran Empire who is opening portals into the Badlands to stage an invasion into our universe.
    Completion of objectives in the Badlands Battlezone will award new Terran Empire marks, used for advancing through the Terran Task Force reputation.
    Participation also awards Expertise and Dilithium.
    For more details, please visit the Badlands Battlezone blog at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...ds-battlezeone



    Counterpoint:

    Counterpoint is a brand new PvE Space 5-player cross faction queue.
    Fight the Terran Empire as they invade our universe in an attempt to take Deep Space 9.
    Counterpoint will have Normal, Advanced, and Elite difficulty options.
    For more details, please visit the Counterpoint blog at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...A-counterpoint



    Assault on Terok Nor:

    Assault on Terok Nor is a brand new PvE Ground 5-player cross faction queue.
    Fight the Terran Empire inside Terok Nor and find a way to send them back to the Mirror Universe.
    Assault on Terok Nor will have Normal, Advanced, and Elite difficulty options.
    For more details, please visit the Assault on Terok Nor blog at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...t-on-terok-nor



    Terran Task Force Reputation:

    This new reputation is available for level 50 captains in the Reputations panel of the character status window.
    The Terran Task Force Reputation System offers high-end rewards for Captains to use against the Terran Empire of the Mirror Universe.
    Earn Terran Marks by completing any of the following content:
    Counterpoint
    Assault on Terok Nor
    Tasks in the Badlands Battlezone
    In addition to Terran Marks, Mk XII set gear requires Terran Gravimetric Inducers.
    Earn Terran Gravimetric Inducers through higher difficulty versions of any queued event which offers Terran Marks.
    For more details, please visit the Terran Task Force Reputation blog at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...rce-reputation



    Cardassian Struggle/Dominion Domination Revamp:

    Lost and Found: This is a level 35 episode which brings the player to meet Commander Sarish Minna on Deep Space 9.
    Spoils of War: This is a level 35 episode where Sarish Minna sends the captain to the Badlands to find out what the True Way is up to.
    Jabberwocky: This is a level 35 episode where Sarish Minna sends the captain to return the Orb of Possibilities to Bajor.
    The Factory: This is a level 35 episode where Sarish Minna sends the captain to stop the True Way and the Dominion from joining forces.
    The New Link: This is a level 35 episode where the captain must stop Laas from deceiving the other Changelings in the link.
    These episodes replace Badlands through Cage of Fire in the Cardassian Struggle/Dominion Domination arc.
    Episodes Second Wave through Boldly They Rode remain unchanged.
    Delta Recruit Changes:
    These changes will not interrupt progress for Delta Recruits who have not completed the Cardassian Arc.
    The Delta Recruit objective that was placed in Crack in the Mirror will now be placed in Spoils of War.
    The Delta Recruit objective that was placed in The Long Night will now be placed in The Factory.
    Objectives that require playing the entire arc are now adjusted to not require the episodes which are no longer available.
    For players who have already finished Crack in the Mirror or The Long Night, credit for completing the Delta Recruit objectives will already be granted for Spoils of War and The Factory.
    For more details, please visit the Cardassian Revamp Blogs at: http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...vamp-episode-1 and http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...vamp-episode-2 and http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...vamp-episode-3 and http://www.arcgames.com/en/games/sta...vamp-episode-4



    General:

    Items in a rewardpack no longer go into the overflow bag when opened if there are already items in the overflow bag.

    Resolved an issue where some Bridge Officers could not be commissioned and were missing some information such as ones from the Federation, Romulan, and Klingon tutorials and Fleet Embassy.
    Kit Modules can now be changed in Space.
    Resolved an issue when in a team, canceling the beam down option would prompt both players to select their own set of Bridge Officers.
    Removed the duplicate Federation Minefield queue that Klingon faction captains had access to.
    Resolved an issue which was causing collision to be missing in non-spherical zones on maps.
    Resolved an issue which made the Rock and Roll Animation look incorrect when used on ships which can saucer separate.
    Resolved an issue which was causing the grenade explosion FX to be missing on some weapons such as the:
    Klingon Honor Guard Pulseweave Rifle
    Phased Biomatter Burst Rifle
    Adapted MACO Phaser Pulsewave Rifle
    Implemented new loading screens for New Dawn.



    Content:

    Updated the level scaling for the Delta Quadrant and Iconian War episode arcs:
    The Delta Quadrant episode arc is now scaled to be level range 50  56.
    The Iconian War episode arc is now scaled to be level range 56  59.
    The following queues are being retired:
    Mine Trap
    The Breach

    Dyson Sphere Space, Undine/Voth Space Battlezones: Resolved an issue that caused captured points to read "Federation Supply Crate" instead of "Capture Point."
    The Vault: Ensnared:
    Rescued ships now warp out.
    Resolved an issue where rescued Warbirds wouldnt fight.
    Undine Space Battlezone: Resolved an issue that caused the Duty Officer Assignment contact in the starting area to only display for one player at a time.
    This contact will now only display for captains who are high enough level to have Duty Officer Assignments.
    Broken Circle: Immobilized player during briefing so as not to potentially break mission progression.
    Midnight:
    Resolved an issue where occasionally, the bombardment cutscene would not play depending on the dialogue choices made.
    Resolved an issue where some rewards from Midnight were not Bind on Pickup.
    Resolved an issue in the Dyson Sphere Space Battlezone that would cause a Federation capture point to not drop back to 2/3 completion if players failed to activate the rift-destroying device after it powered up.
    What Lies Beneath: Resolved an issue where KDF Captains Bridge Officers would attack the Devidian portal.
    Atmospheric Assault: Resolved an issue where the wrong contacts would appear in some mission steps.
    Coliseum: Resolved an issue where Slamek would sometimes get into a state where he would not help the player escape the Coliseum.
    Cutting the Cord: Resolved an issue which was causing Temek to speak an incorrect line.
    Ram Izad Patrol: Resolved issues with the flow of dialog.
    Resolved an issue that made it difficult to target Starbase 234 in the Romulan patrols of the H'atoria sector.
    Resolved an issue which was causing the IKS Kang to have fewer HP/Shields than intended when on Advanced Difficulty.
    Adjusted the DS9 and Bajor Sector Spawn Points to try to keep players from spawning inside the sun.
    Resolved an issue where there were some spots in the ground of the Dyson Sphere where players could fall through.
    Changed a typo in description of "Bug Hunt" queues: "elminate" -> "eliminate".
    Resolved an issue on the Tesseract interface: "Complete Half of Romulan Story Arc" now uses correct icon which was previously using a Starfleet chevron instead of Romulan icon.



    Systems:

    Resolved an issue where the Reactive Armor Catalyst R&D task, unlocked by completing the Broken Circle episode, was showing an incorrect icon.
    Resolved an issue that was preventing "EPS Flow Regulators" and "Shield Emitter Amplifier" from appearing in their respective Console Crafting Recipe options.

    Resolved an issue that was preventing Industrial Replicators from ever showing up as NPC drops.
    Resolved an issue that was causing the Level 18 Duty Officer Reward to be given at level 11 for Federation characters.
    Resolved an issue that was causing the "+CritChance/+CritSev on Melee Attacks" Security Officer Duty Officer to not always increase crits correctly.

    Updated Omega Task Force reputation starship shield reward project names and descriptions to correctly reference the name of the item that is awarded.
    Resolved an issue where the Iconian Resistance space shield tooltips indicated more Shield Resistance than the item actually had.
    The tooltip read 20% while the actual resistance was 10%.
    Increased the resistance to 15%, and updated the tooltips to match.

    Added a disclaimer to all Specialization scaling passives: "Must have at least 1 point spent in this Specialization, to gain this bonus."
    This is a text change only.
    The "Install Replicator" Duty Officer Assignment on Nimbus will no longer take up a Duty Officer Assignment slot.



    Powers:

    All Medium space turrets have had their innate damage buffs reduced by 50%, but have gained one additional weapon.
    All Heavy space turrets have had their innate damage buffs reduced by 70%, but have gained two additional weapons.
    All Heavy Torpedo space turrets no longer have access to High Yield, and have had their Torpedo Spread ability reduced from Rank III to Rank I.

    Biotech Siphon no longer restores health when using the Zephram Cochrane Shotgun.
    The cooldowns on "Subspace Wake" and "Team Fortress" consoles have been reduced from 3 minutes to 2 minutes.
    The cooldown on "Resonant Dissipation Matrix" console has been reduced from 3 minutes to 2 minutes.
    Resolved an issue where Reactive Armor Catalysts were preventing Shields from taking damage.
    The Delta Alliance Unimatrix Shield Array's Unimatrix Shielding proc now has a 2.5% chance to trigger, and 30 second duration.
    Resolved an issue that would cause a Borg Elite Tactical Drone to occasionally spawn with a knockback power.
    Prototype Bateret Sedative and Prototype Ketracel Stimulant now correctly trigger cooldowns on other ground devices when used, and can no longer be used during combat.
    Ferengi Rapid Fire Missiles now correctly trigger both "Overwhelming Force" and "Torpedo Barrage" Starship Traits, when using High Yield.
    Resolved an issue that was preventing Timely Intervention, a Command Kit Module, from ever becoming available for use, regardless of teammates' health values.
    Weapons with the [Snare] mod will now only debuff the target, instead of also affecting self.
    Corrected an issue that would cause the starship trait Weapon System Synergy to trigger on the 11th stack of Weapon System Synergy instead of on the 10th as intended.
    Resolved an issue with Sensor Analysis that was causing Shield Drains against affected foes to be diminished once 6 stacks had been reached.
    Shield Drains should again be properly enhanced by Sensor Analysis, regardless of number of stacks on the foe.
    Updated description of Subspace Fold power to match actual distance travelled.
    Updated description of Torpedo Spread 2/3 templates to now display the correct number of targets the powers hit in the long description.

    Console - Universal - Hazari Shield Drone: Added +Max Shield Hit Points and +Shield Healing as a passive bonus.
    Console - Universal - Resonant Dissipation Matrix: Added +Exotic Damage and +Shield Healing as a passive bonus.
    The Kara Advanced Warbird [T6] starship's hangar pet launch abilities should now have their recharge times affected by Auxiliary power levels.
    The Fleet Ha'apax Advanced Warbird [T6] starship's hangar pet launch abilities should now have their recharge times affected by Auxiliary power levels.
    The Kolasi Siege Destroyer [T6] starship's hangar pet launch abilities should now have their recharge times affected by Auxiliary power levels.
    The Fleet Guramba Siege Destroyer [T6] starship's hangar pet launch abilities should now have their recharge times affected by Auxiliary power levels.
    Updated description on Aquarius Escort to include further explanation of console synergy.
    Resolved an issue where the torpedo nodes for the Constellation class Heavy Cruiser were not appearing in the correct place.
    Added a new set of nacelle options for the Constellation class named "Constellation Beta".
    These nacelles are available for players who own the Constellation Class Heavy Cruiser Refit.
    The Kamarag displays the correct UI when upgraded to a [T5-U].



    Character:

    Revamped the Cardassian ground enemy group to be more in line with canon.
    Updated female Klingon boots with the same upgrades to shape, skinning, and poly count that were implemented previously on male Klingon boots.
    Adding more usable costume parts in the category Armor  Iconian.
    Tribal Tatoo - 11 added for male and female human and aliengen.
