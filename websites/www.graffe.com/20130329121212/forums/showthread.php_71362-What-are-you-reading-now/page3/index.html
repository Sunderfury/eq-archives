    Re: What are you reading now?

    Finished the Millennium Trilogy. It was a great story, and I'm glad it had a definitive ending. His prose is absolutely awful, even if I make allowances for the translator being poor, there is just a lot of information that isn't really needed, and a huge volume of data that dates the series. Example: in the first book one of the characters discusses their iMac with 9.6 GB Hard Drive and 1GB RAM. In the second and third book they repeatedly reference Lisbeth's Palm Tungsten T3 with 64 MB of memory and 400 MHz processor. The best it gets is in the third book they usually just reference "Tungsten T3." It's like he wanted to get advertising royalties from Apple and Palm for every book sold. If the system references were slightly more generic it wouldn't be as glaring, even though anything except a tablet at this point would seem antiquated (certainly no one uses a PDA anymore).
