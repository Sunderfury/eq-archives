    Re: November Event

    Even on the servers that worked, it took a ridiculous amount of waiting before each part of the event started. I didn't have any trouble with lag or invisible enemies, but playing follow the zerg isn't that fun anyway. I'm not sure one time events are ever going to be a good idea. I did enjoy watching entire zergs be downed at once during the island event though. When the giant crab starts spinning like Sonic the hedgehog it's time to move out of the way.

    Mixed feelings on the island itself. It seems decent enough, but the mobs are way too tough. Normal trash is harder than veterans in other zones. The giant veteran crabs are harder than champions. This place was supposed to provide variety away from Orr, but I actually saw people griping and saying they were going back to Cursed Shore. Sure everyone says they are sick of risen, but they are hypocrits too. If all the majority of the playerbase cares about is a path of least resistance grind (which they will then complain is a grind, despite it being something they time and time again choose to do), then they aren't going to leave a place where you can kill 50+ enemies insantly every 10 minutes in favor of a place where you fight one single enemy for 15 minutes.

    It was hilarious in a sad kind of way. After the initial events were over, almost everyone left. Sure there were enough people still around to do events in a normal zone, but the mobs are much tougher here. I was actually near the end of an escort event with about a dozen other people. Fighting a single giant crab, no problem. A second one aggros and people start dying. A third one aggros and everyone says screws it and leaves mid fight. They just decided it wasn't worth the trouble. It was frustrating because I had almost killed my own giant veteran crab, but couldn't do it alone. Short of a reddit thread claiming these drop precursors at a high rate, the place will be a ghost town once the event is over.

    I think the highlight of the patch is the new dungeon. I played a few fragments today and it was really good. Much better than the explorables I've done so far.
