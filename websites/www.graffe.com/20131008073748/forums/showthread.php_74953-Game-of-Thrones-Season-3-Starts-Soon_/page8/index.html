    Re: Game of Thrones: Season 3 Starts Soon!

    There is a list of episode titles floating around the web. Episode 9 this season is titled "Rains of Castamere", so that's surely the first of the two big weddings. My guess is that the second big wedding won't be until next year. Otherwise, it'll just be too rushed.

    As for future seasons, I can see this show going 8-9 seasons. The ratings are higher now than ever, and growing each week so HBO is happy. The producers can cut out a lot of fluff from AFFC/ADWD, and combined the two books into two seasons. Then if GRRM hasn't finished by then I think the current show runners can bring the show to a good end. They have discussed the future plot extensively with GRRM, they love the story (so they care about doing it well) and they appear to be capable writers.

    On the other hand, there is an *outside* shot that GRRM can finish the series in time. Maybe the last book comes out at the same time as the final season on HBO? He is presumably quite a ways into book 6. At this point, things are starting to fall into place for the ending and the "setup" will be mostly done. All the major characters should be ready and he knows how he wants it to come out. I would think the last two books would go more quickly than the last two did, so there is at least an outside chance he could get them done quickly.

    Here's my own guess/wish at a timeline:

    2014 Season 4 (2nd half of book 3)
    2015 Season 5 (1st half of AFFC/ADWD)
    2016 Book 6 releases - Season 6 (remainder of AFFC/ADWD)
    2017 Season 7 (Beginning of Book 6)
    2018 Season 8 (Remainder of book 6)
    2019 Book 7 releases - Season 9 (12 episodes - with 2 hour finale; producers work from advance copy to get scripts ready)

    Hey, I can dream
