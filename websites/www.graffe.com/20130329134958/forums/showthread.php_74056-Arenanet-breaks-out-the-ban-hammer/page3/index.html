    Re: Arenanet breaks out the ban hammer

    Players absolutely did know this was not the developers intent. The weapons cost 21000 on every vendor in the game bar one. On one vendor, one vendor alone, they cost less than the very first karma vendor you meet at level 1. They were cheaper than the previous tier by a factor of thousands, they were cheaper than the same tier by a factor of thousands and cheaper than the next tier by a factor of thousands. If you bought tons of those weapons you knew damn well you we're exploiting a bug.

    Normally in an MMO the result would either be that you would keep the loot after it was fixed, or it would be rolled back. For once a company did what they all say they will do, and actually punished people appropriately for abusing an exploit in the game.

    And it was appropriate - if you just bought a few weapons, nothing happened. If you bought up to 50, you got a 3 day suspension. Over 50 and it was a ban that they have now converted to a 3 day ban. People were buying thousands of weapons at a time, and I for one am glad that just for once a games economy doesn't start out with a small group of people having a huge unfair advantage because they knowingly cheated.
