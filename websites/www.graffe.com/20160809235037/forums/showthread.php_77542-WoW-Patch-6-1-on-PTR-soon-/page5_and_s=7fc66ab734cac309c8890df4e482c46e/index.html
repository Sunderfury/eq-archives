    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    He has a unique one, Mentor. If a follower is on a mission with someone with Mentor, they are treated as if they have the same level and ilvl as the follower with Mentor (if that would be a boost). So, as an example, you could take a 91 with Harrison on a level 100 mission and they'd get full XP (confirmed as per Wowhead comments), or if you got Harrison to 675 all followers with him would be treated as 675's. Mentor is one of those abilities that you can't knock off a character with something else, which is good because it's amazing.
