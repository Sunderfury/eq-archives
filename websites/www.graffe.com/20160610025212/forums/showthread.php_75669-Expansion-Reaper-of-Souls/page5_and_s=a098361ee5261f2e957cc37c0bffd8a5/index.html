    Re: [Expansion] Reaper of Souls

    Wow, I'm impressed. I didn't think anyone would actually be able to get in and play last night. We had a couple hitches and a bit of a false start when Blizzard announce Act 5 was ready when it was not. Had to relog to get it working, but not before we beat Diablo on Torment again first. Why didn't I lower the difficulty first? Shut up, that's why. Then we wasted 5 minutes trying to get Auriel to take us to the next act.

    Only played an hour or two because one of us is a responsible adult (hint, it's not me), but I like what I've seen of Act 5 so far. There is just so much stuff to do crammed into such a small area. You are never not killing something. I never found time to farm some upgrades for my WD, but it starts off pretty easy anyway. The only time I ever died was in a rotating furnace event with endlessly spawning skeletons. And died I did.

    What's funny is I'm still getting garbage drops on my WD (he's cursed), but even only upgrading like 3 or 4 bits has massively inflated my stats. I went from 38k damage and 380k toughness, to 85k damage and 1mil toughness.

    P.S. Singularity is pretty awesome. Crusader is kind of neat (played one to 12 after my party disbanded).

    And congrats to the resident lunatics.
