    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Originally Posted by Solesk
    Here's the problem, since you only get 7 tokens a week....
    You get seven tokens per week from doing the daily. You likely start off with several tokens before you start making 300 stat food, unless you raised all the Ways and not just the one you need. You can buy tokens with Spirits, if that makes economic sense on your server. You can exchange 20 fish for a token, and fishing up 20 fish is not exactly time-consuming. If you have alts, they can effectively transfer tokens to your main by buying the soy sauce and mailing it (each level 85 alt can get seven tokens without consuming any mats aside from sliced peaches and rice pudding mats, by opening up all the Ways). Seriously, tokens are not the problem, unless you're one of the very small minority that does a stunning number of attempts per week. The real problem is the meat. You have to kill to get it (which means competing with other players) or exchange at a ridiculous ratio, and they just nerfed the drop rates on some types of animals (goodbye, guaranteed Wildfowl Breasts).

    Originally Posted by Solesk
    Even now, when everyone is leveling characters and mobs are killed at their highest rate of the expansion, there is not enough meat to go around.
    Everyone is using their own meat right now. The catch-up quests for Cooking at Halfhill allow everyone to participate, and it's content that people want to do. Honestly, I could set up at Halfhill and spend all day trading veggies and meats with people. It's busy 24/7. I'm beginning to wonder if meats will ever become a "sell" item, given that roughly around the time people stop using them to raise all the Ways they'll also be done with the dailies that bring them in contact with meat-dropping mobs, for the most part. And with all the dailies to do every day, I can't see people continuing to do quests for reps they've capped unless there's some other benefit.

    Originally Posted by Solesk
    But they have seriously messed up certain parts and I can only imagine it's on purpose to artificially slow people down.
    Of course it is. Consider the Klaxxi dailies, prior to Revered. You get seven of them per week (unless your dailies are in the area that doesn't have a dropped daily item), 130 rep each. 7 x 130 = 910 rep. That's an odd number... 910 x 1.1 (+10% rep guild perk) = 1001 rep. I guarantee you they balanced that stuff around everyone getting 1000 rep per day, and deliberately assumed everyone would have the guild perk and backed it out to make sure 1000/day was the rate. You don't do that unless you really, really care about delaying people.
