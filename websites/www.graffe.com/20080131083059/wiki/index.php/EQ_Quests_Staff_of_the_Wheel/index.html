The quest for the Staff of the Wheel results in two usable item rewards: 

    Staff of the Wheel
    MAGIC ITEM LORE ITEM NO TRADE
    Slot: PRIMARY
    Skill: 1H Blunt Atk Delay: 100
    DMG: 10 Dmg Bonus: 16
    INT: +10 HP: +10 MANA: +10
    WT: 1.0 Size: LARGE
    Class: WIZ
    Race: HUM ERU HIE DEF GNM FRG DRK
    Slot 1, Type 4

    Star of Eyes
    MAGIC ITEM NO TRADE
    Charges: Unlimited
    Effect: Identify (Any Slot, Casting Time: Instant)
    WT: 0.1 Size: TINY
    Class: WIZ
    Race: HUM ERU HIE DEF GNM FRG DRK

The quest also results in two no-trade runes, Rune of Frost and Rune of the Astral for use in creating the wizard spell, EQ: Spells: Wizard: Ice Comet.

[edit] The Quest

Spoiler Warning:

The following contains material that may spoil game content.

The quest starts by talking to Sulgar within Everfrost. To find Sulgar, zone out of Blackburrow and head for the North wall (loc +3033, -2393). He is standing next to two Barbarians. If you cannot interact with him, petition a GM to correct your beta-neutral faction. If the GM will not help you, Merloc Kole of The Rathe says that all parts of the Tishans Kilt Quest will up your beta-neutral faction.

Also, you can try asking about 'Tarton Wheel' (leaving off the apostrophe and the s in 'Tarton' and see if that helps) Once you have the rods, go back to Sulgar and ask him for the chest (Be sure to get the chest last for it is a NO RENT item. Once you log out, the chest and everything in it will disappear) which you need to combine the rods. Upon a successful combining of the rods, you make two items. One is the Staff of the Wheel and the other is the Star of Eyes. Either of these items you can keep (many use the staff for it's intelligence bonus) or return both to Sulgar in trade for the runes.

The staffs can be found at the following locations.

[edit] Azia

In Erud's crossing on the deep part of the ocean floor past the shipwreck infested with undead, location -1162, +2340 (one user suggested levitating to find the coordinates first, then diving for it). Use the best of your shields, load your fades, shadow steps and gates. Acquiring this rod can get ugly. Seems that 'Killer Sharks' (level 40) inhabit this area and make it very difficult to acquire the rods. Some say the sharks see through invisible and some say they cannot. - you have been warned -

[edit] Beza

In Everfrost, within the underwater caves, it is in the "house" all the way back and right in the cave, where three Ice boned skeletons guard it in the center of the room. To find the hole in the ice that leads to the underwater cave, goto +2420, -4060. You may or may not need enduring breath for this swim depending on your swimming skill. Jump in the water and swim North. Once you hit dry land inside the cave, keep right the whole way until you reach the "house". (mob info correction by Talisin Sa`Lidar)

[edit] Caza

Lake Rathe, Underneath the first island you see is an underwater tunnel leading to a stone tower, its on the top floor of the tower (need enduring breath for this one). This is guarded by water goblins that may attack unprovoked. Be careful for some say they can see through invisiblitly also. - you have been warned -

[edit] Dena

Dagnor's Cauldron, right outside the entrance to Kedge Keep. If you face with your back to Kedge Keep, it is directly diagonal left, in a corner. Loc -890, -740.

[edit] Ena

Lower Guk in the Reanimated hand room. This one is the only one you need a group for, unless you are brave and kamakaze it. Warning: depending on how your video card / colors are set up, the floor may be the same color as the rod in this room and it makes it very hard to find with the Hand and the dar beating on you.

[edit] Fana

Oasis is correct but the tower is underwater next to the above ground tower, this one is on the top floor of the tower as well. This is guarded by water goblins that may attack unprovoked. Be careful for some say they can see through invisiblitly also. - you have been warned -

[edit] Geza

Highpass Hold is correct, it is in the water near the waterfall closest to the Tiger's Roar Inn, there are two waterfalls there, the rod is in front of the one closest to the Inn.

[edit] Heda

Erudin is wrong, it is within the Qeynos Aqueducts, in the shark pit there. Some High Elves with the wrong faction may have to deal with an NPC by the name of Riggin Vix, in which they may be KoS to.

[edit] Izah

Neriak is correct, but it is in the water in front of the bar called "The Blind Fish". The bar has four glass windows that are underwater and the rod spawns right in front of those windows. (If you do not know Neriak or are not welcomed there, I suggest creating a newbie Dark Elf to get familiar with the waterway area, the guards, and the location of the rod before attempting to grab this one. There are MANY guards that patrol the bridge area but you will be safe in the water - Graffë)

[edit] Jaka

It is in Unrest but not near a mob that drops dwarven armor. It is in the water outside to the right of the mansion. Near where the the Gnome Spelunker is. Instead of going into the tunnel that leads to the Spelunker, there is a very shallow tunnel that is to the right. Within that tunnel lies the rod.
