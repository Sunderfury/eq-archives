    Test Notes for Sorcs...

    - Sorcerer - Energy regeneration on Taqmir's Bolts now lasts for the correct amount of time.
    - Sorcerer  Increased the range on Shocking Grasp to 25m.
    - Sorcerer  The particle effects for Incinerate, Inidrias Inferno and Taqmirs Barrage IV no longer appear AE.
    - Sorcerer  Asayas Insight should now increase spell accuracy as well as spell school skills.
    - Sorcerer  Opal Focus should now have its buff effect of making you immune to movement impairing effects work more often now. If you find an NPC spell that it does not work on, please notify your class lead or report it. Thank you.
    - Sorcerer  Asayas Scorn has been added to trainers at level 46.
    - Sorcerer  The functionality of Chromatic Halo has changed. It is now a rune that lasts for 2 minutes or 1000 damage. It has a 2 minute refresh and is instant cast.
    - Sorcerer  New forms have been added. You should now get Icy Focus, Fiery Intensity and Unfocused at level 10.
    - Sorcerer  Increased the radius on all Sanctuary spells to 10m.
    - Sorcerer - Changed the funtionality of Flame Spear. Now has a 3 second cast time, no ranged condition and deals extra damage on critical strikes. The base damage has been lowered. A 20 second refresh has been added.
    - Sorcerer  The Ring Conjure abilities now function differently.. These abilities now summon focus items into your inventory. These items can be activated instantly from your inventory. Once they are activated they apply the buff they previously had. They now have a 5 second refresh timer.
    - Sorcerer  Lowered the energy cost of Seradons Falling Star
    - Sorcerer - Lowered the refresh of Seradons Falling Star to 22 seconds.
    - Sorcerer - Chaos Volley has been completely reworked. It is now a nuke that deals damage to you and your opponent and gives you and your group 2% spell damage for 20 seconds. It stacks up to 3 times, increasing the damage with each cast and the damage buff up to 10%.

    And the full notes can be viewed here..

    http://vgplayers.station.sony.com/la...pdates_test.vm

The psionicist revamp continues several new abilities such as meta-mind, paralysis field, and others as well as reworked spells. Look at the length of both of these revamp lists. How is it possible to add these new abilities yet not implement the anti-magic aura? That doesn't make sense.

    They even added/fixed fluff form spells for the druid AND (non-fluff in this case) the necromaner yet cannot do the same for the sorcerer despite having asked the players about this.

    The much purported trade off between dps and utility becomes an academic and even erroneous point if both are suboptimal; it is possible to be good at both are lacking at both as the class balance right now clearly demonstrates. Neither will be truely where they should be if this revamp is allowed to materialize if the current form. The longer it takes to fix the class problems the less likely it is that the final changes will be skillfully crafted and effective.
