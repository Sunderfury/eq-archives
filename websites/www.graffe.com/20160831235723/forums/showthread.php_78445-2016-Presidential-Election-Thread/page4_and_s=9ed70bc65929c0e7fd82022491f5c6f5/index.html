"Yah but that was 150 years ag..." SHUT THE FUCK UP. Culture is collective consciousness passed down over generations - you still scream like a little girl when a spider lands on your arm. Black men and Black women have this weird codependent thing going on, where there's still this underlying cultural expectation of breed and dash, on both sides. To cope with this in an environment that will create the least-broken children possible, the Black woman has to learn to let certain things go for the betterment of the family. So when he cheats or moves on after knocking her up she will still feel that the expected red-hot flash of rage and hatred and jealousy and all that, and then she has to take a deep breath and try to do what she thinks is best for her and her kids. So she justifies and forgives and moves past as best she can.

    That's the lens through which, I think, Black women viewed Hillary's decision to stick around. I think they 'get it', especially since (and this is God's truth) most of us view Bill as a Black man anyhow. They see her forgiveness as amazing inner strength, not weakness, and are culturally drawn to her through shared experience. I'd be very interested to see whether she has the grip on younger Black women that she has on older ones, given that the Lewinski thing is ancient history to them. South Carolina may tell.

Black pastors LOVE them some Jewish people, so it has nothing to do with anti-Semitism from that source.
