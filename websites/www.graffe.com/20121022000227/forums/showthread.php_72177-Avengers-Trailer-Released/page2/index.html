    That's a lame excuse. Comic books are all about the characters. Of course you can't have a movie where every single hero gets the amount of attention they got in their own movies, but there's plenty of room for surprises and development so long as you start with a smart script and use special effects to support the story rather than writing a story to support the special effects.

    You can have story-driven, character-driven content but it's harder if you're just plugging gaps in the action with standard-issue banter and obligatory exposition.
