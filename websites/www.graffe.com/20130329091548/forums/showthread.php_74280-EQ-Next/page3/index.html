    Re: EQ Next...

    Originally Posted by Delores Mulva
    How many people would love an MMORPG about a theme park filled with carnies trying to kill you, though?
    I think the real question is, WHO WOULDN'T?

    All players make a young kid that has different abilities based off of the classic Goonies Archetypes, and you have to rescue your parents from crazy, drug addled carnies. Some of the carnies want to make you play their sick twisted games (crazy larger than life versions. Ring Toss has you running around holding a giant bottle trying to catch huge rings the carnie is shooting into the air), but others just want to kill you. Since you're kids you don't get actual weapons, and have to knock them out, or trick them into taking themselves out.

    Classes are entirely based on what kids want to be when they grow up: Fireman runs faster, climbs faster, takes less damage from fire. Police Officer is immune to fear abilities and can use pop guns (stuns/mini-fear). Doctor heals everyone in the group with him. Astronaut can use gadgets like cell phones to bypass security, or activate/deactivate rides, and is immune to poison and disease (because they have a helmet).

    Primary gameplay is Mario-like Exploration Puzzles, and getting required keys to advance through areas to challenge bosses and level up. Cinematics involve switching the game from the crazy shit going on in the game (childrens imaginations) to what's actually happening, which is a perfectly normal carnival, and probably some crazy blending of the two. Since most bosses require alternative ways to take them out that isn't killing them, the different classes have different solutions available to defeating a boss.

    If you "die", the graphics change you back to seeing everything as a normal kid, but your friends can come over and "patch you up" which pulls it all back.
