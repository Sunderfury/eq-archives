    I warned some of the returning players in the guild about how addictive pet battles could be. They all said no problem, we want to level anyways. Next thing I see is pet battle achievement notices in guild chat. They should have listened!

    I tried to do the "do all scenarios on Saturday" achievement today, and couldn't get credit for any of them. I thought it might be a "9 am reset" issue, since some of the scenarios were started and finished prior to 9 am, but two were done after 9 am and I didn't get credit there either. Looking at the comments on WoWHead, it looks like it's a known bug. Just in case anyone else was thinking about doing this today, don't! (And lol at how easy the scenarios were. When a smite Disc priest and a hunter whose DPS isn't much better than the priest's can do a scenario from start to finish without the third person, you know things are easy.)

    A reminder to everyone: Darkmoon Faire starts at midnight tonight. It's important to mention this because of the "plus to profession" quests at the Faire, which could save some of you significant sums in mats. Also note that, even though it doesn't improve any of the "Way of the..." scores, the +cooking quest will allow you to get credit for 600 Cooking if your guild still needs that for heirloom pants. I confirmed that today when the returning players decided to spend gold now to get those legs, and as the most advanced Cook I had D do the Stormwind cooking daily to get the final point for 600.
