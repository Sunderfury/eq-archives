    I broke down last night and got a group together to do this task. It was frightfully easy. Granted we are Anguish geared, so I am sure that had some to do with it. But, after doing it, im sure that lesser geared people could handle this with some skill and thought, and a little time watching and figureing out the zone. If you already did your spell missions in this zone, its pretty much the same as far as the clearing part and the roamers. I will say, if you dont have an fd puller, you need to be careful or you will get stuff falling in the pit and agroing that whole tunnel to the zi. Keep a decent tank, switch out my druid for a lower end geared cleric, and you prolly got it made.

    Group make up was: me, shaman, druid, war, bst, twink bot mage.

    Wizard pulled. Most of the time all pulls were singles, with a few doubles at times. Took a while to clear this way because of roamers etc. I spent time waiting to assure single pulls etc whenever possible. We used mage pet pull to pull one of the 2 shiliskin in the corner at the bottom right, because I kept getting agro from the guardian. Then we just root cc'd the other one while we burned down the guardian.

    We found the shroom and after he got his courage up, he ran out of the room, soon as he was past the door, 2 shiliskin spawned and rushed us. we dispached them easily, and the shroom went right to the zi, and that was it.

    No robe this trip, but a decent earring that was actually an upgrade for someone in my group, and it was NOT the twink mage bot either. I will keep doing this though till I do get a robe. Always wanted a mana pool click item but passed on them every time before because they were useless.

    Well then!

    I managed to finish mission 70.5 last week. The first attempt we had 2 shadow knights, 2 druids, an enchanter and a wizard. We did fine until we got to the blade and we wiped. I chalk this one up to ignorance upon the group since no one really had any idea of what was going on.

    So a few days later I brought another group consisting of, a warrior, a shadowknight, a wizard, a bard, and 2 druids. On the first pull the two druids got owned within seconds and then the warrior died. I gated us to undershore to save the rest of the group. After we gathered outside and went back in and from there, everything went fine. We had a few close calls but no deaths. It was pretty easy when we got into the groove of things. All of the people in this group was from my guild except one druid. We are all qvic/MPG trial geared (I just recently came back so my gear sucks at EP levelish.), and so we are pretty well off.

    Overall, I think that the missions will pose as a great challenge to causuals, but it is possible to do if they think about it and get crafty with things such as pet pulls. I think that they will scale now since I see that they adjusted the difficulty a bit.

    On a side note, I don't think that Gal was complaining or looking for hand outs. After experiencing these missions first hand, I think they would be difficult to do if you aren't geared correctly, but doable. Further, I think that he was just expressing his concern and frustrations over trying mulitple times. I think it is safe to say that most of will tend to get frustrated after trying to do something several times that takes a long time. He also isn't blessed with a set schedule. Because of this, he won't be able to join a raiding guild. I understand his plight. FINALLY! I think he will be able to gear up on his own doing trials and DoN's. I don't know much more about him as his magelo is not updated. I just feel kind of hurt that he was attacked for what he was frustrated with. It is apparent that he actually TRIED these several times. Personally, I'd rather have someone come to me and say they are frustrated because they TRIED and FAILED rather then "Oh, it looks too hard! Make it easier!". Someone who tries is always someone who whines and complains without going through it has a stronger argument since they actually went through the problem.
