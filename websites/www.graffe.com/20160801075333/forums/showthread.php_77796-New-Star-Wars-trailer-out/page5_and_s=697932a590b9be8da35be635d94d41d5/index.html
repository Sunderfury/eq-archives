    Re: New Star Wars trailer out

    The acting performances, especially from the four new main characters (Rey, Finn, Poe, and Kylo) were spectacular. The atmosphere was wonderful, and I loved the return of so many location shoots, physical sets, and practical effects. The CGI was generally excellent.

    The plot was rehashed, half-hearted, hollow, and disappointing. It felt like they spent so much time trying to address the failings of the prequels (dialogue, acting, crude/ineffective humor) and insert shout-outs and homages to the original trilogy that no time was left for rational world building or developing an interesting primary conflict*. And so they just said "Uhh, let's retell A New Hope!"

    Spoiler for *:
    Unless you consider Kylo's situation the primary conflict of this particular movie, in which case they just failed to develop the overarching conflict of this new trilogy.


    I have tickets to see it again tomorrow in IMAX, and I'm hoping it will be improved with a second viewing.

    Re: New Star Wars trailer out

    Good movie. Won't hold up forever, but you should see this one in theaters given the atmosphere around star wars. The characters are great, and the moviemaking is great.

    Spoiler for Plot concerns:
    Kylo Ren and Rey are the primary plot point. Starkiller base is just there to be a tool to reset the galactic balance of power in favor of the bad guys so that it doesn't feel like the new republic just crushes the first order under their boot. The writing/direction involving Kylo Ren is kind of driven to show you he's a pretty pathetic child, so it's supposed to feel stupid. Still, there are very important places where Abrams could have shaved 15 seconds from the movie here and there involving him, and it would have been better.
