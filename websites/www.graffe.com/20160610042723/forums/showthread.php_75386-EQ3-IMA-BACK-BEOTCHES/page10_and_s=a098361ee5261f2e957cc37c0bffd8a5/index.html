    Re: EQ3 - IMA BACK BEOTCHES

    Game play is greater than graphics. I'm guessing you can't have the photo realistic graphics and do what they wanted to do with destructibility and creation. Remember, the world has to rebuild itself or it won't be long before all the top levels of the world are gone. The other part is a lesson from WoW. Don't aim for the current top end computer technology. Aim for the middle so you can get the game in as many hands as possible. It would be really cool if they built in a way to ramp the texture quality up every few years, but I doubt it.

    Also, I would be very surprised if this game wasn't earmarked for the PS4 as well as PC. Given the graphic quality, I bet the PS3 could handle it.

    The only "holy grail" left that they didn't mention, at least for me, is the ability to form a relationship with an NPC (no, not that kind of relationship you sickos). Something where you can be friends with an NPC and he'll crack jokes and not repeat the same things over and over again. I guess we are a ways away from that kind of AI though.

    Re: EQ3 - IMA BACK BEOTCHES

    Originally Posted by Philo
    All of this and no gnomes? This game is going to be fucking fantastic.
    Game does sound and look good (to be honest, the graphics of the human female reminded me more of Bioshock Infinite Elizabeth than a WoW human). Music they played was good. They showed telegraph moves similar to Wildstar, graphics similar to Wildstar, only everything slightly better. The minecraft and destructable enviroments seem too good to be true. So many possabilities with that. However, I am very interested to learn more about the game mechanics.

    How do the leashing mechanics of the mobs work. Trains? (one of the devs shouted "there are no more trains" as they played the group demo.

    What are the death penalties?

    How do group mechanics work? At the group demo, one guy shouted "stasis" and all the mobs had circles around them and did not move. Mass enchanter ability? Mezzing back? How does pulling work. Can monks FD?

    WHERE ARE THE DAMNED PET CLASSES?! GIMME MY MAGE

    How are mobs regulated? Are they like old EQ, where power was nearly always in favor of the mob? Or will the game go more towards how games have evolved now, to where the player is usually vastly stronger than the mob? Very, very rarely, if ever, will you die while paying attention in WoW, GW2, or any other mmo since EQ, to an equal level mob. Even the "elite" versions. In EQ blue con mobs could roflstomp you. The combat they showed seemed to tilt it in crazy favor of the player, mowing down mobs. But that might be them just showing off the skills and whatnot.

    They do get extra bonus points for right off the bat showing a night day cycle where the night actually changed the visual perspective to other than a blueish tint (wow and every game after it).

    EDIT: As to races, they probably use mocap for the animations, and as such, same as Vanguard, all body types will be humanoid in shape. Expect gnomes and the other races to be various sizes of what we have seen so far with traits. Doubt any one of them will have bulk or bodytypes that go away from the lean norm.
