    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Blue confirmation on exactly what happens with duplicate pets when the pre-expansion patch hits. And it's even better than what they said before: all pets merged into your pet journal, the three of a given pet and 500 pet total limits will not apply to this process, you can cage them and sell them at your leisure. Considering I'm over 1250 total pets post-merge (less any pets that don't stack post-merge, like store pets or guild pets), I'll need the time:

    http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/2...-mailed-or-no/

    There was another Blue post that talked about players with multiple accounts tied to one B.Net account. Remember ages ago when I wondered if those accounts would share pets? Answer: they do. However, only one such account can engage in pet battles at a given time. So no logging on multiple accounts to battle yourself unless you have multiple B.Net accounts.

    Blue has also made a blog post about Spirits of Harmony. In MoP, there won't be a bunch of elemental tradeskill items like there have been in prior expansions (Essence of Fire, Primal Fire, Eternal Fire, Volatile Fire, etc.) Instead, there will be one item: Spirit of Harmony, and one sub-item, Motes of Harmony (10 motes = spirit). Motes and Spirits are BoP, and drop from mobs everywhere in the world and from gathering nodes. You can also grow them on your farm - you get 12 plots of land at top level, each one can churn out one Mote per day if you plant the right seed. Spirits are used for the same sorts of things that Chaos Orbs currently do, although more are required (the epic crafted gloves, BPs etc. require eight Spirits). There's also vendors in the world that will take one Spirit and give you back one stack of tradeskill items (ore, herbs, leather, enchanting mats, rare inks) or other items (one Iron Paw token for Cooking, for example).

    http://blue.mmo-champion.com/topic/2...rfect-harmony/

    Just like in Cataclysm, the idea of restricting high-end tradeskills with BoP items is lame. I get that you can grow them on the farm, but it's still a silly restriction. It's especially bad for gathering alts, who can get these things but have a limited set of things to do with them. I hope they're made BoA sooner rather than later, so players can at least shift them around to an alt that can use them.

What stops someone like yourself from creating a dummy B.Net account, handing an alt on it a bunch of cheap vendor pets, and doing the same thing?

    There's some good news on the staves, though. You can't make the MoP BoA epic staves, no. But you can make the level 81 BoE staff and the level 85 BoE staff. At 75, would you have even been able to raise your skill cap to get the new recipes? Isn't that usually level-restricted? As an aside, the mats requirements on the BoA epic staves is insane. Take the INT staff. Ignoring vendor items:

    8 normal inks
    2 Spirits of Harmony
    3 Rain Poppies (normal herbs)

    to make the blue one, which is required to make the epic one. The epic one also takes:

    20 rare inks
    20 scrolls of wisdom
    5 Spirits of Harmony

    The scrolls are made when you do your glyph research. They require three inks each. So total mats for an epic staff:

    68 normal inks
    20 rare inks
    3 herbs
    7 Spirits of Harmony

    That is a ton of herbs to mill to get that many inks (or many Spirits of Harmony handed in to buy them). But back to the original point - yes, stupid to have professions be linked to BoP items like this. It was dumb with Chaos Orbs, it's dumb now, and I say that as someone whose crafters will all be level 90 with gardens growing these stupid Spirits.
