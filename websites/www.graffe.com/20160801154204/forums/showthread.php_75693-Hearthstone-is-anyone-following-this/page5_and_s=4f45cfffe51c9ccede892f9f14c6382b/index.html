    They don't really introduce you to the new mechanics (ones not seen in the original Jaina sequence) very well with the practice decks. You might not notice what Overload does for the shaman deck when the AI uses it, for example. But the expert AI can teach some things to card game newbies, like timing a rush (the AI has board clear spells, you do not) and working card advantage.
