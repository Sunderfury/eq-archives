    Frost Nova nerf

    Frost Nova has been changed on Player Test Realm so that it breaks as soon as it takes more than a certain amount of damage from a single spell. So far it seems that a non crit will leave the spell up, and if you're naked, a critical spell still has the same chance not to break, however when geared, a critical spell will break Frost Nova, Freeze, and Frost Bite every single time. We need more testing to figure out the new "break" formula, but regardless, this is a huge fucking nerf.


    Needless to say, I'm pretty unhappy with this, as it makes the Frost tree the weakest PvP tree, and seriously lowers frost DPS on trash mobs. It also makes Ice Lance a useless toy, since now you'll never get it off on a Frost Bite (by the time the proc shows up, you're usually already halfway done casting another Ice Bolt).


    Please note: Its on PTR, none of the CM's are talking about it, and its not in the patch messages. It *could* be a bug, but it really seems like a nerf to something that is only overpowered when you get a huge burst of luck.
