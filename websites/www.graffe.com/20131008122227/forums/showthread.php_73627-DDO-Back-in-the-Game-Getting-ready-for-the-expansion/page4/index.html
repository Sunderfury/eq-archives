    Get someone you like to 8 before august. The stones go *poof* sometime in august.

    Part of the reason you're dying a lot straight off of the boat at 7 is that the 7 boat gear isn't really much better than the 4 boat gear, ESPECIALLY the monk gear. It's completely lacking in necessities like Moderate Fortification, +Balance, poison/disease immunity, and instead of those +2 sneak attack goggles, they should have included the Trapblast goggles from Shan To Kor (+4 Reflex Save). I also would have skipped those boots from Tangleroot and chosen the Black Wolfskin Belt from tangleroot instead (+3 Con) But alas, they did not ask me to make the gear sets for Vet II Status. :P

    Getting off of the boat at 7 on a new server was particularly difficult. It took me about a week to get to 8 (roughly 4 hours of play time, which is FAR more than it should have given how well I know the quests I was running) because I just kept getting roflstomped by things that I wouldn't have worried about at all with 75% crit/sneak attack immunity and poison/disease immunity (Those traps only SEEM mildly annoying until the stat loss is the difference between the next 4v1 melee encounter. Truly silly way to die.)

    I actually recommend taking your 7 and running some 4-5 quests with the cheaper hirelings of the same level (as opposed to level 7 hirelings). Even if you're saving the greater tome of experience from the expansion pre-order, some of the loot in those is good enough as to be upgrades from your level 4 boat stuff.

    If you go into Cannith Crafting at all, the Sharn Syndicate quest is a good source of Bound to Account items and they're all fairly quick runs. These currently STAY bound to account after you deconstruct them and add shards for new stats/bonuses, so you don't have to make the same item for each and every alt.

    That being said, starting at 1 *will* gear you up naturally in a fairly efficient manner.

    Heard some good news on the ddovault the other day - apparently ALL characters will get 1 free lesser reincarnation in the upcoming future. Probably not for a few months, and nobody gets free greater, true, or +x types, just a basic free "can't change any of your class choices per level" lesser reincarnation, but this is GREAT news because with the upcoming AC changes almost everyone is going to need to re-spec.

    I myself will probably be waiting until January to use it so I'm sure they've ironed out ALL of the major build-breaking bugs before I decide on something, but I'm fairly certain that's going to come in handy, especially for casters.

    Oh, Dmitry, Expansion launches the 25th. I don't know if you have anyone ready to Reincarnate but absolutely DO NOT under any circumstances do so until it's verified that it works. Whatever code versioning system they use regularly re-introduces old bugs on major patches and there was one a few patches ago that completely hosed you, left you stuck on the boat, and unable to continue levelling after a TR. It took them about a week to iron out.

    Also, don't put any bound to character items in "bound to account" collection (collectibles, gems, ingredients) bags after the expansion launches. Those recently made the bags permanently BtC even after the BtC item was removed so if you toss your bags through the shared bank, they bound to the first person to pick them up.

    Oh, and possible upcoming bug they may not fix in time for launch day. If you switch weapon types (swords to bow, for example) ALL of your gear bonuses disappear until you unequip and requip each piece on the beta server, so if you suddenly seem a lot squishier come monday, bust out a extra few toolbars and put two pieces of gear for each slot on it so you can swap in combat...or don't change weapons.
    Those are all of the major gotchas I know of for now concerning the patch. This one is going to be a bit bumpy, though, with the massive physics engine overhaul. They tried to do a LOT for this and hit some roadblocks with the code along the way. Since they're refusing to push back the release date, it's going to be...interesting, i the chinese sense of the word, but I figure it'll all come out mostly okay in the end.

    It has so far, anyway.
