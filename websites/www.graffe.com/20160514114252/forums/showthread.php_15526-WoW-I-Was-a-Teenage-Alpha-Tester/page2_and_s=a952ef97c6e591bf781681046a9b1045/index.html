    Well, in each push, you were kinda limited by what levels and classes and races you could play, so some common quest items appeared, such as these shoulders (well, not common, but seen more than once on higher level guys).     One last thing before I hit the sack for real:

    Whiz warned ya: the Succubus level 20 Warlock pet.

    Each race (except gnomes, who apparently use the human quest) has their own quest for how to get the Succubus pet, which must be lured to this plane through an object representing pure love, which they feed on. Then, it must be mastered in combat and subjugated, when you then learn how to summon her in the future. Each summons requires a Soul Shard, which I'll talk about at another time.

    Right off the bat at 20th, she can be taught to cast a "de-taunt" spell, which transfers aggro from her to another target. This is another reason why it's a Good Thing to have a decent amount of Stamina and Hitpoints as a Warlock. She also has a wicked "Lash of Pain" attack, which is basically a backstab with her whip, on a timer. This ability can be learned at a more effective damage amount in later levels.

    At 25th, she can be taught "Seduction" (single target mez ability), at 30th she can learn "Lesser Invisibility."

    Here she is, although you can't see her pointy tail in this pic. She has the best idle animations; she checks her nails, or often just feels the need to slap herself on the ass.
