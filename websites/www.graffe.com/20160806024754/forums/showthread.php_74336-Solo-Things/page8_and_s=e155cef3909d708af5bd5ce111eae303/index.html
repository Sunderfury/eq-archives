    Took about five hours, with some goofing around (like doing some exploration achievements on the Eastern Kingdoms alt and checking for the Unborn Val'kyr on the Northrend one), and about 15 minutes of putting alts in the right spots to speed things up: one in Westfall to do the southern EK trainers up to Searing Gorge, one in Twilight Highlands to do TH/Hinterlands/Eastern Plaguelands, one in Hellfire Peninsula to do Outlands, one in Dalaran to do Northrend, one in Uldum, and one in Winterspring to do Winterspring/Hyjal/Deepholm.
