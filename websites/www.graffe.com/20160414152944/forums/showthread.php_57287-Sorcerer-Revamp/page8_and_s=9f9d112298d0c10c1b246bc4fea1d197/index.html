    First time poster, long time reader...

    While I would love for us to become #1 in DPS again, our class is still lacking. I also play a Psi and Necro, while my wife has a Druid. My Sorcerer is the least fun to play. Two reasons mainly: Spamming CV and he doesn't really bring anything else to a group.

    - Psi has pretty decent DPS in longer fights thanks to DoTs while also having good crowd control, agro management, and group buffs.
    - Necro has good DPS, great pets, CC in a weird way, and FD is a very powerful tool.
    - Druid has great DPS, great buffs, pets, heals, and clickies for groupmates.

    I just don't see what else we bring to a group right now besides damage, and thats the thing thats bothered me about Sorcerer for over a year now (when I started playing those other classes). Here are some wish list items I'd like to see for our class:

    - Flame Shield (changed): Surrounds your defensive target with flames for 15 seconds. Any opponents attacking your defensive target will be vulnerable to fire damage and will be damage for X amount.
    - Ice Shield: Encases your defensive target in ice. Target will not take any damage for 6 seconds. Cannot be used again on the same target for 2 minutes.
    - Summon Prometheus: Calls the god of fire to fight by your side. He does tons of fire based damage and lasts 30 seconds. Has a 15-minute refresh timer.
    - Summon Shiva: Calls the god of ice to fight by your side. She heals the group by X amount and randomly revives ONE fallen comrade in the group, then disappears -OR INSTEAD- She freezes all opponents within a 10m radius of your defensive target for 30 seconds. Upon awakening the opponents have an ice-based DoT that lasts 20 seconds and does X damage. Has a 15-minute refresh timer.
    - Conjure Life Shard: Conjures a life shard that can be used to revive a fallen comrade. Should almost wipe out your energy pool and have a 4-hour refresh timer. Not tradeable.

    I have a lot more crazy ideas but just wanted to share these for now.
