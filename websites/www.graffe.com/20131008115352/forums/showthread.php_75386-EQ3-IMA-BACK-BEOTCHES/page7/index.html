    EQ is dead, let it rest in pieces. I had some good memories, but most of that was exploring early in the game. You really can't do that in most games these days. What made EQ fun about that is when you would zone, you didn't know what level zone you were in. My very first character was a dwarf warrior and I remember running around butcherblock. There was this altar with three orcs there. I had no idea what I was doing, so I ran over to check it out. Not a good idea because the two chased me and I felt like I was getting away and the oracle killed me with a spell. Took me forever to find my corpse. My dwarf fell into the pool in front of the warriors guild in Kaladim. I couldn't figure out how to swim at all. I tried twice to get my corpse and died both times. So after playing that character for a week, I started a new one. He rotted in that pool.

    Oh yeah, I can't forget that I was playing in that little box at first. I had no idea you could go full screen at the time.

    It really sucked because my second character was a wood elf druid. Using that little box, I fell from the trees many times. Again it took me forever to find my corpse until one time I couldn't figure it out. Then came character number 3....LOL.

    Yeah.....good times, good times.
