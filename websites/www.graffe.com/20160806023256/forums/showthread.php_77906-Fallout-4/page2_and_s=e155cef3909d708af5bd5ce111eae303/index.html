    basically, the "Enclave", the "Elite", knew the world was f'ed so decided to escape to Mars
    They then use the vaults as tests for social experiments, to see what long space flight would do, and recycling systems etc

    the vaults were never designed to "preserve America" at least not by those at the top, hence in Fallout 2 they were going to wipe out ALL Humans/mutants who weren't "pure" (anyone exposed to radiation or the FEV virus) and tried to do it again in FO3

    Fallout 1 needed a water chip, deliberate experiment to bugger them up by not having enough replacements
    Other vaults opened to early to see psychological and radiological effects
    the "Mormon" vault of New vegas didn't have enough clothing, another was set up so gambling would be used as a base for community decisions, another had WAY too many weapons
    Vault 15, iirc in Los Angeles was deliberately overpopulated with various racial/social mixes to see what that would do and helped bring about the various gangs (Vipers, Jackals and Khans)
