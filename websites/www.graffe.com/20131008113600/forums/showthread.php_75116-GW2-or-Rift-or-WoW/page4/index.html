    Re: GW2 or Rift or WoW

    I've play a lot of Rift (pre-expansion) and GW2. Currently playing GW2 because it's free. Though I enoyed Rift more as an MMO experience.

    While combat dodging is fun in GW2, I enjoyed Rift's dynamic events more. And Rift has a traditional tank/dps/healer group system where GW2 everyone heals themselves and dps themselves. In GW2, there's also FAR less reason to group outside a dungeon and it makes for very little interaction among players at lower levels.

    Rift has the added feature of being able to shift into a tank or healer or dps role depending on the need or mood. So you aren't always stuck doing something you don't want to do. In game Achievements are better and more diverse in Rift.

    Also there isn't a ton of content in GW2 right now. You can do most things with one character in a few months. Including attaining all the best gear, where Rift is two years old and has much more end game content.
