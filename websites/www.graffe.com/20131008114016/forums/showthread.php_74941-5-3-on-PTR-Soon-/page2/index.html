    Re: 5.3 on PTR (Soon)

    What they could do, to make it easier to itemize, would be to change the way stats work with regards to affecting different specs.

    Ret Paladins need Strength.
    Holy Paladins need Intellect.

    Why not make Holy Paladins' spellcasting stats benefit from Strength? Sure, they would need to figure out what to do with Spirit, but it would remove the need to have one whole specialized set of Plate gear (kind of like the specialized Int mail for Shamans).
    They could do something similar for Monks and Druids for Agility.

    But it's probably too late in the game for an itemization change so radical.
