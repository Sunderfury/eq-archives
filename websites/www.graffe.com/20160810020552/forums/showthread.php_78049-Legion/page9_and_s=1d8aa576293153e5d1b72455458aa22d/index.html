    Some classes/specs do, some don't. My interest lies mostly with tanks, so let's use them as a yardstick:

    Bear/Prot Pally are very good right now. Mechanics are in place, the core of the specs work. They're the tanks that are used to tank the +3 mythic dungeons with crazy affixes.

    I haven't been following Nobble too much on twitch, he's been playing the zone quests a lot, which figures since he's a lore nerd^2.
    What I've seen of the zones and quests looks good - nothing overly broken.

    Re: Legion

    Have they confirmed that the max bid for the BMAH is the same as the gold cap? It happened to be the same previously, but that doesn't mean that the code says "max BMAH bid = gold cap", as opposed to "max BMAH bid = 999999", or that they'll change it.

    If you're not one of the people running a given market on the AH, this is very bad news. Previously, traders could afford to compete strongly on prices, because so long as they had enough gold for a cap bid they could guarantee that they'd get whatever they wanted. Unless someone has been storing items for sale in the bank in addition to capping out each alt, nobody currently has enough gold for day one cap (they might - 11 alts x 999999 - but very unlikely). That means nobody can guarantee getting what they want, and they will want every gold they can get to reach that position of safety again. AH prices are going to go up, and stay up.
