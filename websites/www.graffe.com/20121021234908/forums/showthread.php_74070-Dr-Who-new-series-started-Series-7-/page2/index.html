    Re: Dr Who, new series started! (Series 7)

    When Davies ran Dr Who, it was possible to speculate and sometimes get things right. Things pretty much were as they seemed. Since Moffat took over, I find that is increasingly difficult if not impossible. He knows we all do that and he intentionally plants seeds to mess with the viewer. Not only is everything not as it seems, but it is a safer bet that everything is definitely not as it seems.

    For example, he is leading us to believe that in just 4 episodes Amy and Rory will complete their transformation into Daleks and that will be the end of them. However, that is far too easy, in fact, while their demise will surely tie back to this last episode one way or another, it will likely be as a result of something that seems so insignificant right now, that nobody could possibly ever guess it.
