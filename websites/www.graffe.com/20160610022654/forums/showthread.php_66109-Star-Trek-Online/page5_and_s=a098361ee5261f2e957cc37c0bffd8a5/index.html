    Re: Star Trek Online

    Well now that I've played it over the weekend, here is my mini-review on it.

    The game is fun. Which is a shock because I was expecting a giant suckfest. The core structure is there, and works. The game needs another 6 months to a year to flesh out the details and shine it up. Some parts of the UI are buggy, some missions, some features in the station, graphic bugs, etc etc etc. I will NOT be buying it now. I'll wait till this summer or fall to see if they actually refine the game. The idea of a 12 month or lifetime sub is a joke at this point. The open beta is level capped, and you can't play the Klingons, the fleet(guild/raid) encounters disabled. So I'm getting the feeling that the game does not have a lot of depth to it. So far for me it's been very repetitive, kinda like city of heros mission system. Fun, but the same thing over and over. I can't see anyone playing it beyond a month or two at most, unless they REALLY turn up the development of content post launch. (yeah right)
