    Re: What's Cool in GW2's Exansion? This really is a cool Game

    All classes can do some sort of healing or damage reduction, it seems.
    One example I experienced recently was with my new Necromancer fighting this boss in parts of the Halloween content. The boss summons tons of skeletons (scales to the number of players in the fight) and they have Retaliate which means that there can be tons of debuffs (DoTs for instance) flying around. Now the Necromancer can have a setup where you can take debuffs from your allies unto yourself. The Necromancer can also get another ability that lets your transfer debuffs on yourself to your enemies. I believe you can also do it the other way around, as in taking buffs from enemies to yourself and turn them into something useful (debuffs for enemies or buffs for allies).
    Alongside the above you do damage too of course, so that way you can play your Necromancer as a "healer" if you will :-)
