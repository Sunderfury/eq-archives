    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Good news, everyone! The pets all merge. You can't cage them yet, and there's a fair number of "unique" pets that you only get one copy of (like those Spirits of Competition, sigh), but you'll still have them come MoP release for resale. And more good news: in addition to the expected account-wide achievements, Bloody Rare and Frostbitten (the silver elite killing achievements) are also account-based. I finished Bloody Rare today with one kill, and am down to two kills for Frostbitten. My account score is 9385, so I picked up "It's Over 9000".

    The bad news: some of the new rotations are very clunky. Some will likely just take practice - I'd put the new Arcane Mage rotation here, with the "stacks can only be cleared by ABarr" mechanic. But on my tanks, and on my Demonology warlock, I felt very much like I was playing the interface and not the game. Resource management 4tl.
