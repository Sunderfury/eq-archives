    Go to the cauldron that the aliens are surrounding. Go INSIDE it and SIT and /say I have come for your gems. This will not work if you are standing or outside. You need to levitate and use the rocks near by to get inside.

    A voice booms from the center of the cauldron. 'These gems are sacred, important to our survival, you shall not have them'

    If you are a non-wizard and try it u get 'Unfortunately my friend, you are not a student of the sorcerous arts, and cannot be trusted with information so vital to our cause'

    Spawns: Elite Sun Revenant Level 75 Wizard Sun Revanant. He spawns just south of the plateau and makes his way up to the cauldron where he stay. He does circle around the cauldron a bit. He IS Kill on Sight. He DOES see invis. He is immune to run speed changed. Summons.

    He was casting spells on me:
    Elnerick's Entombment of Ice
    Shock of Magic
    Draught of Fire (this being his favorite)
    Annul Magic
    Talaka

    His AEs:
    Greater Sand Cure of Knowledge
    Greater Sand Cure of Life

    For those too lazy to look, they are PBAES with -325 resist adjusts, prismatic.
    One is -1000mana a tick+decrease spell haste 50&%, one is -1000hp a tick+decrease atk by 500. The AE range is only 150. The can stack with each other. They lasted for 18 seconds. Remove Curse, Pureblood, and RGC did NOT remove it. However, annul magic removed it.

    He PROCS Torment of Body

    1: Decrease HP when cast by 3000
    2: Decrease Hitpoints by 300 per tick
    3: Accuracy Amount(-25)
    4: Decrease Effective Casting Level by 100
    5: Increase Curse Counter by 24


    I got hit (on a necro with max Def aa) doubled. He hit for 1483 and 456. Necro has 1122 AC.

    He then flurried:
    1483
    1541
    1696




    I was level 65, so it doesn't seem like level matters in spawning him.
