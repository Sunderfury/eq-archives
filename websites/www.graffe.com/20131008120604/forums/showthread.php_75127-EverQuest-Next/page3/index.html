    Originally Posted by Droigan

    Basically I want the world to be Dark Souls the MMO Make a damn MMO that fits the audience who likes those games. Some people hate them, that is fine. But considering how popular Demon and Dark souls became, should think there is a viable segment to make a mmo for those people.

    Bring back the ability to make mistakes (granted not to break the game until New Game+ like you can in Demon/Dark souls). I still remember my very first EQ experience. Logged in, not really familiar with the hotkeys. First thing I did was autoattack the trainer you start next to in Freeport. BAM, dead. Respawn outside the gate of West Freeport. I was human. And it was DARK. Could not see anything. A passing mage happened to give me a summoned ultrastone I think it was. Suddely I could see the zone. The pawns, the rats, the snakes.... It was magic.

    Remember darkness in mmos? Ever since wow, dark = slightly grey/blue hue. Kitchitor woods? Remember that zone? Took a decade until the fear of that zone was recreated in a single player game, Tomb of the Giants in Dark Souls.

    Seems like games today are autotuned to hold your hand in the background, not letting you make mistakes. You are not ment to attack that NPC, so you can't. Why can't it just obliterate me and let me feel stupid?

    And before people go "oh dark was stupid", difficult things were stupid..... then the game would not be for you. Every single other mmo is.

    /sigh
    Minecraft actually re-creates a lot of these feelings - there's genuine danger in that world.
    There are several good servers out there. Pretty damn hardcore, some of those people.
