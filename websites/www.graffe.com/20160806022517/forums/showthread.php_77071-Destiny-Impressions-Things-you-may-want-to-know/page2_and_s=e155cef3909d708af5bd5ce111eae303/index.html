    Originally Posted by FilanFyretracker
    PvP servers would honestly not make sense setting wise considering all guardians are supposed to be fighting for the city so tossing an FFA server out there would not benefit anything. It does have the crucible which can at least be written in as training.
    What the fuck are you talking about? PvP is supposed to be a major part of the game. They have 3 end game PvP factions and only 1 PvE faction. And the PvP portion of the game is almost completely unplayable at any serious skill level. No dedicated servers, terrible maps (the outdoor moon level is the worst map ever), and the PvP doesn't have any matchmaking. They redefined matchmaking to mean you didn't have to form your own groups, rather than the game actually trying to build a quality match. Halo was amazing because it balanced the groups based on player skill in a ton of different facets, including gametype, map, and recently played game.

    Destiny is balancing games the same way CoD balances games. It takes the number of points earned in a match and tries to even them out based on the players. A 10:15 k:d ratio is better than a 9:1 k:d ratio, so you end up with terrible teams and an absolute shit quality match.

    Its awful because Halo had the best matchmaking of all time. Microsoft had a research team working on making sure the algorithm was as perfect as possible, but most of the algorithm is based around Glicko, which is open source. I would have hoped for at least a Glicko implementation, instead we got something that isn't even as good as a random scramble.

    I'm going to help you guys out. He isn't talking about mmo style pvp servers. He is talking about the way the game actually hosts matches.

    On any good PC shooter you will have a dedicated server hosting the match, creating an even playing field. Quite a lot of console games save money by actually making someone's console host the game. This often results in players not all being synced and goofy lag that frustrates people. It's honestly minor unless you are really into it, then it's catastrophic.
