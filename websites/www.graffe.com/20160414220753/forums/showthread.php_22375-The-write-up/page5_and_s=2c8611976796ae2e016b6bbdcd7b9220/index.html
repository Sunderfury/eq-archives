    Why on earth would you use epic 1.5 over the Time wand? Yes, I have both.

    I've been expermienting with epic 1.5 on raids and 6 person groups for a week now.

    The 10% resists makes no difference on raids. Why? Becuase 10% lower then a mob that has already been debuffed by the usual classes makes no difference. None. zero. nadda.

    In a 6 person setting, the odds that you aren't going to have some type of debuffer are small. Even if you do, you have 2 choices.

    let's say he's fire resitant..

    1. You can continure to use fire, use the epic effect, which will last for ONE mob (talking lvl 68 + mobs)

    2. Or you can be smart, switch to a DD that the mob is not resistant to (say cold or magic) and not worry about the one in every 10 crap.

    This is an completly USELESS effect.

    Now for the second effect.

    4% less agro. On the surface doesn't seem to bad.

    Myself and one other wizard from my guild that also has 1.5 did some expermients. I would have the epic equipped, and he would not. We would both be casting the same DD spell on the same mob. We would both chain cast without usuing concussion, and counted the casts it took to get summoned/agro.

    It was the same amount of casts EVERY TIME. I'm not talking about one night, this was over a period of 6 nights of raiding. We also tried casting concussion. Both of us had to cast concussion the same amount of times to loose agro, regardless of if we had epic 1.5 equipped or not.     So this effect is useless as well. note: the effect in itself is not useless, but the 4% needs to be increased to make it worth anything.
