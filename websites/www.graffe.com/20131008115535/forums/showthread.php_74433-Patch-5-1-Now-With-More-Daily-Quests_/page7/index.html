    Re: Patch 5.1 - Now With More Daily Quests!

    This is my Frostbolt macro:
    Code:
    #showtooltip Frostbolt
/console Sound_EnableSFX 0
/use 10
/cast Icy Veins
/use 13
/use 14
/script UIErrorsFrame:Clear()
/console Sound_EnableSFX 1
/cast Frostbolt
    The two /console lines turn off, then turn on, the "donk donk donk" sound when you try to use an on-cooldown item/spell.
    The /script line removes any item cooldown errors that appear.

    /use 10 - glove tinker for engineer
    /use 13 and 14 - trinkets
