    Re: 5.3 on PTR (Soon)

    The metas for Loremaster are account-wide. One person can do one zone, another does another, they both count towards progress on the meta. Note that if you care about the individual achievement scores of the different alts, none of them will get the points for the metas unless they individually do all the work. But if all you want is the title, tabard, etc. you don't need to do it all on one character.
