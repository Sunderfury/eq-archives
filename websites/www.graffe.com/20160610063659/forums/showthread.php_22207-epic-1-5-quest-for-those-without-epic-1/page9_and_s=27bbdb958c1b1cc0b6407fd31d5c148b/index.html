    Golden Sword in Skyfire

    This sword can be found under ANY OF FOUR dragon statues in the zone. Looting the item spawns it randomly at the 4 locations. I found mine under statue in SE corner.


    1.) Statue above Veeshans peak
    2.) Druid port in statue
    3.) NE corner statue (cleric epic)
    4.) SE corner under statue

    Save yourself some grief and check them all!

    Good luck
