    Re: Censorship of Myngil

    Okay then, some thoughts on the overall topic.

    i) Thread titles need to be safe for work - that's long standing, and there's no real question of that.

    ii) Usernames should be covered by the same rule - for the same reasons. You see them even when casually browsing the forums, and you don't have to go into a thread to see them.

    iii) Forum titles can only be seen once you have entered a thread, so they come under the same normal rules as everything else. Tits is an okay word, albeit it a slightly odd one to see under someone's name all the time. We can't use the NSFW arguement here, because you see that language used all the times in the content of threads. It would be silly to moderate a title for a word that you can use in the threads themselves just as much. (Side question for the philosphers - why would "Iron Fist of Oppression" be okay, when it implies violance and physical attacks, but "Iron tits" is not safe for work despite the fact most straight guys like boobs?")

    iv) Re. Dmitry - I'll PM him about it, but generally I have always asked people to not attack individuals in their signitures and forum titles. It creates an inherently uncivil atmopshere, it adds absolutely nothing, and it's basically just there to cause drama. Call yourself all you want, but naming and attacking other posters in that way is just plain unhealthy.
