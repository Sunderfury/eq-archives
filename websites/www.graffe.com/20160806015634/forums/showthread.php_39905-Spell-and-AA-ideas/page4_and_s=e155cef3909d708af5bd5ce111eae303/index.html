    What i'd like to see....

    Spells.
    1) Directional Shadowstep
    - it's an upgrade, so don't screw with it and make it long recast and stuff. no through the wall crap or anything expected.. just a great escape/travel spell we should have always had.

    2) Snare upgrade,
    - less resistable and longer duration - I don't care if it's AoE, or single.

    3) Harvest upgrade.
    - aa or spell..

    4) Reverse Coth would be nice.
    - target player and teleport to them.

    5) Disintegrate Upgrade ( lvl increase )

    6) Dot Absorbtion shield.

    7) Reflect line upgrade .. actually making it useful.. was a great idea .. just not enough.

    8) Huge Ether Skin upgrade.
    - most trash hits harder with one swing then this protects us for...it use to be a great line to absorb enough to get a root, or get abscond off .. now it's barely enough to realize you're about to die. I don't care if mana regen on it increases much .. but the amount it absorbs should be 3+ times what it is.

    9) Hasbeen
    - this spell summons a random warrior to your side to tank a mob and out dps you at the same time.

    AA's -
    1) Aggro reduction over-time ability.

    2) FD upgrade... less mana use or no increased mana use to get the benefit.

    3) Mind Crash upgrade - more aggro reduction.

    4) army of xzul - 3 ranks - each gives an additional sword.

    5) Unleached Destruction
    - passive
    - when a wizard dies with more then 85 percent of their mana unused - they have a chance to DD the target that killed them - 3 ranks (3/6/9).. each rank increases the chance ( 25/50/75) and also increases the DD damage ( 10k/20k/30k ) - resistable, magic based, can't be focused.

    6) moral booster - jester type avatar that continuously lies to all wizards in range - tells him how great their dps is. Randomly flips off rogues.

    Re: Spell and AA ideas

    Lots of excellent ideas from a lot of wizards.

    1. Not really a spell or AA but would like to see and upgrade to Staff of Elemental Flux if possible Lower Element 3: -18 to fire/cold instead of -12, zero cast/recast as is currently.

    Personally, I think that this is one of our important items; and I'd like to see the quest more challenging than previous. My thought was to somehow make this a solo only quest (think Gandalf turning white or something) and with some real challenges. Preferably crawling a dungeon and not running all over the world. The instance challenges should be proper invis (including ITU), handling several mobs, small quarter root/snares etc.

    2. I also liked the idea of wands in WoW. I didn't use them a lot except really long encounters. Something not overly too powerful, maybe along the lines of solist's icy wand but upgraded and 3 seconds cast time.

    3. I haven't played for a while and so I'm not sure about rains but if it's not been addressed before, I'd like rains to really be 3 waves regardless of the number of mobs (used to be 1 wave if 3 or more mobs were hit; 2 waves if 2 were hit and 1 wave if three were hit). If that's overly powerful (and I realize magicians will benefit from this as well), maybe make this an AA that increases the number of mobs per hit per wave by 1, 2 and 3.

    4. PBAoE root will be cool as well. Targeted AE-root even better.

    5. Make items castable from within backpacks (snare dagger, staff flux, etc.) I'm currently almost always down by two or three bag slots because of that.

    6. A reagent bag (like a potion belt but with lots of slots; should allow 100 components of each reagent easily) to store peridots, opals, TL bits, Fuligan Soulstone of Innoruuk, etc.

    Thanks,

    - Petel
