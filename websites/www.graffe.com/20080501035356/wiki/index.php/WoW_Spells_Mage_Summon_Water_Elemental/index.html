WoW: Spells: Mage: Summon Water Elemental

From GraffeWiki

Jump to: navigation, search

Summon Water Elemental is the final talent, as of the Burning Crusade expansion, for frost mages that requires the mage to have previously spent 40 talent points in the frost talent tree. The water elemental has no cast time, requires 16% of the mage's base mana to summon, lasts for 45 seconds (or until killed), and is on a 3 minute cooldown.

The water elemental is controlled in the same fashion as other pets in WoW, via a pet bar. The water elemental has two types of attacks, a waterbolt (aka frostbolt) and a targetable frost nova (25 second cooldown). The water elemental gains a portion of the mage's +frost damage stat that adds to its own damage.
