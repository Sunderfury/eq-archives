    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    I've actually been using WoWuction for this sort of thing:

    http://www.wowuction.com/us/bronzebe...nHunter#tabs=1

    You can search by item name, and it shows the average on your server and the US Alliance server average. It takes all items with the same name and lumps them together, so if you see an item is expensive it's not just because the "of the monkey" version is really good or something. I take all greens with US Alliance price over 100 g and put it up for roughly 10% below US Alliance. In general, when I look at the item it turns out to be a transmog item - boob tunic, assless chaps, the only item of a given colour for a given armour type, etc.

    Dawlin's system is much better for that, but I mention mine because of the other thing the web site has: the bargain hunter list. It posts lists of items on your AH that are selling for a fraction of their US Alliance average. I like to bring up the list from time to time, buy up the stuff under 10%, and repost it at around 80-90%. It usually sells quickly, especially because my server tends to be an "expensive" server, with prices higher that average.

    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    Originally Posted by Dawlin
    How long did it take for you to complete the BRF step ?
    I'll use my achievements to figure it out. I completed Chapter II, the Highmaul ring, on January 21st. That was prior to the release of BRF: I have my Slagworks achievement on February 18th. So Feb. 18th to April 5th, seven weeks of raiding with the staggered release schedule slowing me down. Now, I did get runes from work orders and the follower mission between the 21st and the 18th, so that distorts things a little. Let's figure out how fast it would go now!

    A full clear of all four LFRs is 9x9 + 27 = 108 runes. You get 18 runes per week from the follower mission. From my adventures in mining, I suspect that you get one rune per two days' worth of work orders (it took a little over two rush orders per rune). So mine plus garden is 7 more per week. If you have two profession buildings churning out stuff, that's another 7 per week. Doing follower missions, you get roughly five rush orders per week, which is four days' worth of work orders, or 2 more runes per week. 108 + 18 + 14 + 2 = 142 runes per week. That all adds up to about the same time I experienced with the staggered release, seven weeks.

    Now, the real question is how quickly my alts will get through it. I have yet to run BRF on one of them since I got the ring, and I'm not sure I'll get the bonus because it might trigger off the Chapter III achievement and not the actual ring, and I don't have that yet (I didn't realize I had to hand in one of the quests to open up the Blackhand quest, and I did it after I'd killed Blackhand this week). I plan to do that first thing this upcoming week, which will take me over 20,000 achievement points, so then I'll do the alt BRF runs and see how many runes they award.
