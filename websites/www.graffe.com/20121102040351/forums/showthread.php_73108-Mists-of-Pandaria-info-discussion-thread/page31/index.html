    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Found them. Lazy bastage I am.

    Classes

    •Many tooltips have been corrected to account for recent class balance adjustments.
    •Druid
    •The 25% speed increase granted by Cat Form should no longer stack with the speed increase of Dash.
    •Hunter
    •All hunter aspects have been removed from the global cooldown, and they again share a 1 second category cooldown
    •Paladin
    •Holy Shock’s base damage has been reduced, and its spellpower coefficient has been increased.
    •Warlock
    •Havoc’s cooldown has been reduced from 45 to 25 seconds.
    •The missile effect of Chaos Wave should no longer display erratically when cast on a moving target.
    Bug Fixes

    •Well of Eternity
    •The spell graphic for Arcane Bomb during the Queen Azshara encounter has been reduced to a reasonable size.
    •Ulduar
    •The Silence and Pacify effects from Conservator's Grip are now removed from players once they step within the appropriate area radius of a Healthy Spore mushroom.
    •Dragon Soul
    •Player pets should no longer experience pathing issues when attacking Deathwing's Arm Tentacles.
    •Congealing Bloods now move at slower base speed.
    •Master Looters can again assign loot to players in other groups within a raid.
    •The Refer-A-Friend Summon should again function as expected.
    •Worgen players on pre-Cataclysm accounts should be able to accept and complete all Gilneas quests.
    •Players should again be able to teleport out of the Firelands Hatchery in Hyjal if they have completed Aessina's Miracle.
    •Players should be able to use the new Fishing spell where appropriate.
    •Crossing coalesced zone boundaries should not duplicate profession recipes.
    •Players should no longer inappropriately receive the "Requires master riding skill" or "Requires artisan riding skill" errors when attempting to mount certain mounts.
    •Items in the Auction House UI should no longer display in reverse order when sorted.
    •The PvP node capture bar should be back in expected locations such as Wintergrasp, Tol Barad and Venture Bay.
    •Players should no longer be placed into battlegrounds that they have downvoted while queueing for a Random battleground.
    •Flying over Wintergrasp when no battle is active should no longer cause a forced dismount.
    •Accepting a quest that flags you for PvP combat should no longer prevent you from accepting further quests.
    •Resolved a Mac issue related to CPU usage.
    •As he deserves, Crithto should now be attackable by both Alliance and Horde players.

    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Leveling a Horde alt for the "one Alliance and one Horde at max level" achievement has been funny. Instances are faceroll easy, and considering how my AE ability (Blood Boil) spreads my DoTs and does two to four times the damage of my single target strikes, I easily beat people three levels higher than myself on the damage meter. The only thing that comes close to killing me is Alliance guards when I forget I'm a goblin and fly towards Alliance quest hubs.

    A reminder to everyone playing: tomorrow afternoon the Theramore scenario will be activated on US servers. It'll be level 85 until MoP release, then it'll be bumped up to level 90.

    So as I was looking through my giant list of pets, trying to figure out how to prune out as many as possible, I noticed that some pets had the same set of abilities and same type as other pets, but strictly better statistics. For example, the Darkmoon Cub is a Beast with the Cat set of abilities (Claw, Rake, Devour, Pounce, Screech, Prowl). It also has better stats in every respect (health, power, speed) than all of these cats: Bombay Cat, Calico Cat, Winterspring Cub, Orange Tabby, Silver Tabby, and Panther Cub. So, great, I can cage all of those down to one of each and just use the Darkmoon Cub if I want a cat (I have more than the three maximum one can put on a team). What else can I do that with, I wondered?

    That's when it got weird. When looking at the stats on some of my pets I started to notice that pets of the same type (it seemed like all such pets were purchased off vendors) could have different stats. For example: in my pet journal, I have Blue Dragonhawk Hatchlings with (health/power/speed) 151/10/11, 163/9/10, 151/9/12, and 154/10/10. So which do I sell when MoP comes out? To make matters worse, the other pets with the same set of abilities and same type as the Blue Dragonhawk Hatchling also have multiple stat combinations: I have 151/11/10 and 148/10/11 Gold Dragonhawk Hatchlings, Red Dragonhawk Hatchlings with 148/11/10, 145/10/12, and 145/11/11, and Silver Dragonhawk Hatchlings with 147/9/12, 148/10/11, and 145/9/13. So what started off as a simple exercise in comparison turned into a convoluted set of rules (eliminate the two 151/10/10 Blue Clockwork Rocket Bots, which are always worse than Lil' Smoky's 156/10/10, but keep the Blue Clockwork Rocket Bots with 148/10/12....) Argh!

    Worst part: I told my buddy that I was doing this, so he asked me to send him an e-mail with the results. He hates complicated stuff, and if ANYTHING gets deleted that shouldn't I will take the blame. Double ARGH!

    Originally Posted by Backster
    for some reason I seem to run about 75% as fast as everyone else around me I don't have a walk setting on or something do I?
    Many classes have abilities that passively increase their runspeed. There's also enchants you can get for boots that increase runspeed, and a lot of PvP server players get them. If you're used to how far Blink will take you, you can keep up better by Blinking. Just don't Blink past the group into the middle of the next set of mobs! There is a walk setting - it's usually set to the slash key on your number pad - but it's stupidly slow, so it's much more likely them moving quickly.

    The only way I land spells on half the yard trash before it dies is with insta-casts
