    Re: DDO Back in the Game Getting ready for the expansion....

    Yeah, what I did was to take about as much as I would have paid for a year's subscription and use that money to unlock things...each year. That way I get to keep it once I own it all without paying another dime except for when they release new questpacks/races/classes/whatever.

    If you only buy points when they go on sale and then only use those points when the stuff you want to buy goes on sale, you can get the whole shebang for quite a bit less.

    That said, Dmitry is right and it IS easier to just pay monthly for quest access. You get 500 free turbine points a month as well, so when you stop paying for access, you can pick up some of the stuff you want to.
