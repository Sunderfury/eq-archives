    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    Originally Posted by Backster
    Why do you want more than one of a pet? (srs question). They tradeable?
    Come next patch on Tuesday, all pets you have on all your characters (with very few exceptions) will be combined into one "pet bank". Any of your alts can access any of the pets, and (again with a few exceptions) you'll be able to "cage" the pets and sell them on the auction house. This is part of the preparation for the pet battles thing, which will be activated in MoP.

    The issue is this: suppose my alts all currently have a copy of the same pet. The pet bank normally won't allow you to have more than three copies of the same pet in it, and no more than 500 pets total. So what happens on Tuesday to all these extra pets? Originally, the answer was, "All the extras will be caged and either dumped in your inventory or (if there's no room) mailed to you." Ok, sounds good. Then, on August 15th, the devs said that as a one-time thing, when the patch happens, all the extras will be put in your pet bank. We would be able to go over the 3 of a given pet limit, and the overall pet limit would be 1500. This would just apply for the merger, and we wouldn't be able to get any new pets until we got below 500 total (and couldn't get another copy of a pet we had until we were at 2 of that pet or fewer). Hey, great, my mailboxes won't explode on Tuesday! (I have one alt over 150 pets, eight alts over 125 pets, one alt over 100 pets.) Then, yesterday, another dev posted and said this:

    Originally Posted by Blue
    4. All standard pets will be merged, with a limit of three per type. Players who currently have more than three of the same pet across their characters will see a total of three after the migration. Examples of standard pets: Orange Tabby Cat, Brown Prairie Dog, Crimson Whelpling.
    So does that mean I'm going to lose any pets beyond three that I've accumulated? Today, one of my alts obtained a nifty pet that the other nine already had. If I had learned it, according to this dev post, I'd have "lost" it on the patch. A lot of players are quite upset, since they've been playing under the assumption that any pets they learned on their alts would be "refunded" come patch day. For me, this could represent a massive loss of gold from pet sales. I have eight Spirits of Competition across my alts (old PvP pet, cannot be obtained any more), and I've been getting offers from people to pay 100,000 gold or more each for them once they become tradeable. So just that one pet being limited to three will cost me half a million gold.

    The dev who made the post responded to some posts today. He's avoiding the "can I go over 3 with the merge, what happens to the extras, doesn't this contradict everything that's been said in the past" questions. It's not a good sign.
