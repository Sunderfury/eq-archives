    Originally Posted by Morhion
    thats a good idea and i can actually see it happening; but not at 50/100% probably more like 15% 30%
    I actually didn't mean it in percetage.

    E.g.: Firebane uses a resist adjustment of -300. Add to this the -100 of Prismatic Focus and you have an adjustment of -400 which would be around 30%

    The idea was focussed around our big nukes. We all love it if you cast Ether Flame and get resisted on it or same for ACF or Corona Flare (whichever you use). Our big nukes all have only a resist adjustment of -10. Even if you add to this -50 from Pyromancy you only have a resist adjustment of -60 (add to this the Flux staff for -72 if you are keen).

    As our DPS comes only from nukes (pets can be neglected mostly), every partially or fully resisted nuke lowers our DPS significantly. Having a resist adjustment of -110 (or -160 with Pyromancy) would at least help us in this department. However it wouldn't affect the ingame implemented mob resists too widely aka if a mob is very resistant to fire your best choice would still be to use another nuke type or lures.

    Not saying this should be implemented like this but fact is that our resist adjustments definitely need work/improvement.
