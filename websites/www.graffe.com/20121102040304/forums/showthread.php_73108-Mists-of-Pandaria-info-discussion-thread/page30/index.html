    Re: Mists of Pandaria info/discussion thread.

    That's for after you get Brain Freeze, which I think is level 77. Once you have Brain Freeze, you'll want to sit on your Fingers of Frost procs until Brain Freeze also procs, so the instant Frostfire Bolts also get the FoF buff. You only use Ice Lance to burn a stack of FoF if you hit 3 stacks.

    Until you get Brain Freeze, though, there's no reason not to hit Ice Lance every time Fingers of Frost procs.
