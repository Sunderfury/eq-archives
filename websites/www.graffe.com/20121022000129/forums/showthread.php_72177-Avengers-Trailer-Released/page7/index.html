    Originally Posted by Mileron
    The movie rocked; the theatre, even on Memorial Day, was still rather packed (at least half full).
    I just saw it, and the theater was still packed (easily 65%-70% full and most of that was the rows near the front). Was awesome. Everything it should have been. TONS of characterization. Not deep of course and some doesn't up under close scrutiny, but the action is really good at persuading you not to scrutinize too closely. Point is: it's there. No hero introduction scene is wasted, every hero is challenged individually and as a team. Side plots are brief and all support the main plot, which is ultimately pretty straightforward. Action scenes are fun, funny, and exciting. Not usually too tense but that's ok, this movie is supposed to pump you up not stress you out.

    Though Loki was disappointing it didn't really hurt the movie (unless you're, say, a huge Loki fan). He was still an effective villain even if not all he could have been.

    Luckily the scene where the aircraft carrier becomes an airship was really loud, because I was laughing hysterically. (Still cool though)
