    Re: 2016 Olympians Will Be Competing In Poop Water, And The IOC Doesn't Care

    So I decided to see how much your TV coverage of the Olympics sucks. Here in Canada, the main Olympics network (CBC) has made a deal with the two sports networks (TSN, Sportsnet) to share coverage so as many events are covered live as possible. Tomorrow, this is when the three networks are showing Olympics stuff (times are PST):

    CBC: 4 am to 3 pm, 3:05 pm to 8 pm, highlights show from 8:05 pm to 10 pm.
    TSN: 5:55 am to 8:30 pm.
    Sportsnet: 5:20 am to 9 pm.

    Remember, they've split coverage so if you flipped between the three networks, you'd see different sports on each at the same time. Now, what you get:

    NBC: 7 am to 2 pm, 5 pm to 9 pm, highlights show from 9:35 pm to 1:30 am.

    So you get 11 hours live and four hours highlights per day. We get 16 hours live, two hours of highlights on CBC, 14.5 hours live on TSN, and 15.5 hours live on Sportsnet. I'm sure some of those live hours on TSN/SN are actually highlights, but... wow. I saw that NBC was actually airing Jeopardy instead of live Olympic coverage.
