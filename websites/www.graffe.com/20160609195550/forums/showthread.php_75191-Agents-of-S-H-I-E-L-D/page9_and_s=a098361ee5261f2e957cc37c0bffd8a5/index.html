    Re: Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.

    I think we will see Ward again. Not sure what will happen with Fitz. I don't think he is gone either. Maybe Skye will develop the ability to heal people or something.

    I thought it was a solid closing and it completely reboots the SHIELD aspect of the MCU. SHIELD was getting pretty unwieldy anyway. As an entity, I don't see how an organization like that can exist. Who pays for it? How does the US allow an organization that large with that much power exist on its territory? I'm sure those are the same questions you could ask of SHIELD in the comic books as well, and I've always written it off as a "It's comic book world, don't think about that too much and deal with it". Still, I like the idea of a smaller organization. Plus, with HYDRA out in the open there is a constant bad guy out there to fight.

    I also really like how the show reacted to the movies and also fills in the space between the movies so now when new movies come out they won't have to waste screen time extrapolating changes in the world.
