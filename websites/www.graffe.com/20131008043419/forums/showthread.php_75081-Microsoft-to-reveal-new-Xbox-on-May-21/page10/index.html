    Re: Microsoft to reveal new Xbox on May 21

    People often forget that typically as soon a console releases it's hardware is obsolete, it limits developers and the high bar for the console as far as graphics and computing is set fairly quickly. Part of the strategy that MS is employing with XO is that they want to take a portion of the rendering and heavy computing to the cloud. This in effect makes it so that the hardware of the console is less important, and it give developers the chance to continue to advance the games, because the hardware limitation is not set at console release time. The cloud computing and offloading of rendering to the cloud is one of the greatest not talked about features of XO. However it does place a dependency on having a good network connection for highest end specs as the consoles age and more of the computing is happening offline.
