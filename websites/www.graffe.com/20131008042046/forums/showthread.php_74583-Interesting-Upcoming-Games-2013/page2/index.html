    It's been rumored for awhile, but 2K officially announced a slight rebranding of the XCOM shooter. It's now called The Bureau, with XCOM saved for the subtitle. It is also now about a predecessor of the XCOM program in the US government that worked to conceal the truth about aliens. So it's sort of like being the bad guys in all that old Men in Black fiction. And while this part isn't new, they made sure to point out that it is now a third person tactical squad based shooter, instead of a bioshock-esque fps.

    Comes out in August, NA gets it 3 days sooner but everyone gets it in August.
