    Re: Solo Things

    Did Naxx on Sunday (10m) and (technically) finished AQ40.

    Got just one of the pets from each.

    As Ret, Retalented after Plague and Spider to have Sacred Shield instead of Selfless Healer.

    The only ones in Naxx that gave me troubles were Gluth and Thaddeus.
    Patchwerk and Rasuvius were both Cake as Prot (Ret Gear + sword and board)
    Realized how much I use Exorcism as a puller as Ret.
    4HM were cake, I think I did them as Prot.
    Grobbulus was also easypeasy, as Prot I put on attack and Insight and actually got myself a drink to come back to him half-dead.

    I don't remember what spec I was for Gluth, but I know I was Ret for Thaddeus.

    Gluth: Basically a war of attrition. Started with best burst I could, then switched to Seal of Insight for the (increasingly smaller) heals. Kept powered Execution Sentence on him and burned AW and pet whenever I could. Tried the "kite til Mortal Strike debuff wears off" strat but didn't do well since he moves so fast (even with Pursuit of Justice), though I did get lucky enough to get off a few powered WOG. I ended the battle with under 25khp.

    Thaddeus: I forgot there was no break between the pre-bosses and the big guy so wiped the first time. Came back, saved up some HP for heals before I jumped to his platform.

    Sapph: Cake, his iceblock was annoying (can't even break out of with with EMFHS or Bubble, so that extended the fight by a good minute)

    KT: Having only been at his doorstop once before, I never paid attention to the fight, so ran around the room gathering the alcove adds. Bad idea. I took down a hundred or so before getting smushed. When I got back, I stood in the middle and took them as they came. He made a nice splatter too.

    AQ40:
    Viscidus I had to do as my DK. Couldn't get past the Twins as the DK, putting out only 4kdps as blood (85) and 12kdps as Unholy.
    So went back in as the paladin and finished C'thun. Now that you don't insta-die in the stomach, he's easily curbstomped.
    Ended up buying a dozen Elementium Ingots on the AH for under 500g so I'll pick up some tasty rep and nice mog items after this reset.

    Tried Ruby Sanctum. The East and West dragons both stomped me good, both as Ret and Prot.
    Once more into the Obsidium breach, and again can't get Sarth below ~400k solo, and that's with the best burst I can manage.
    Forgot to do VoA.
    Onyxia is still a quick curbstomp.
    And I've also finished MC on my mage, rogue, dk (naturally saw the eng drops my mage has wanted for years), warlock, and shaman. Forgot to run the hunter. No bindings this week.

    Can't wait to rerun some of these with the DMF Carousel rep buff.
