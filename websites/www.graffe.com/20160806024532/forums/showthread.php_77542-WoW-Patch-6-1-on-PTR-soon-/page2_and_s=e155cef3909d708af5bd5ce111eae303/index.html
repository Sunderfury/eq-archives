    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    Ask and ye shall receive: the new build is up on the PTR, and the elemental transmutes are in. New stuff:

    The Alchemy trinket can be upgraded to ilvl 680.
    The elemental transmutes are in. 10 elements plus four daily CD stuff to 10 elements.
    The big money transmute is in: 50 daily stuff plus some crescent oil to one Savage Blood. I have to start doing the daily CD!

    Profession missions are now unlocked by having that profession's building. So far, it looks like the reward from them will be those Rush Order things that complete X work orders when used. Followers that have the related profession give boosts to success on those missions.
    One more tier of crafted item upgrade items was added.
    Scavenger was nerfed down to 100%.
    A new follower ability, Treasure Hunter, that boosts gold by 100% was added.
    New cooking recipes that are better than the barn feasts have been added.
    There's at least one item that gives you a new follower mission when used.
    Exploration Missions tend to give Archaeology frags/stones as rewards now. There's also more apexis rewards from missions.

    Lumber Mill and Trading Post no longer cost resources to build, but the gold cost is going up.

    There's a lot of new stuff for the Darkmoon Faire. Many of the dailies have had toy-granting achievements added, like "score ten rings in the turtle ring toss". The big race that was in beta is also going in.

    I see reference to something called Prime Glyphs, but no other info is given.
