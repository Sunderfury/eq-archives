    Re: Movie / TV Prop collecting (mostly Star Trek)

    *update.

    I've been searching for a Star Trek Next Generation Female Science uniform for many years now. In fact when I decided to start collecting Star Trek costumes it was *the* costume I most wanted to own. Little did I know how uber rare they are to find. VERY few were ever sold, and the owners of those costumes just flat out refused to sell them. I'd done research and tracked down 9 or so owners of these costumes and they just wouldnt concider selling them.

    But all this changed about 6 weeks ago. I was contacted by a well known collector who wanted to let me know another prominant Star Trek collecter was selling off her collection of +200 items to raise money for a new house she wanted. I'd contacted her in the past, knowing she had a fantastic Science Blue costume, but she wasnt willing to break up her collection of TNG costumes. The person who tipped me off, already had one of these in his collection and wanted to help me out by giving me the heads up. Knowing it was coming up for sale well before anyone else, I was able to start saving up money to make sure I could give it an honest run in the auction.

    When the auction was finally annouced and the catalog released, I immediately put in a max absentee bid. I figured first bidder wins in the case of a tie, so I might as well go for broke! Normally I snipe but I wasnt going to take a chance in this case. Over the course of the next month, I raised a bit more cash and raised my bid a week before the auction started.

    Come auction day, I was on the edge of my seat. There were a good 450'ish items in the sale, the star trek items started around #220. The sci blue was around 390. I had to sit there and watch some truely awesome items sell... in some cases really cheap! The DS9 Q and Sisco boxing costumes sold IMO very cheap, iconic and a funny scene "Q: You hit me! Picard never hit me! Sisco: I'm not Picard", Nog's starfleet uniform was epic and sold for a reasonable price. Picard Nemesis Stunt costume sold... and a ton more DS9 items. FINALLY it got to the Sci Blue and I was in the lead. It got down to "last call" and the price jumped up to my previous maximum. The wait continued and it got down to last call again, price jumped up again just below my max. Gaaahhhh! Stress was killing me. It got down to last call a 3rd time and I sat there in dismay when another bid came in! This time it was at my max... I was still in the lead but I knew one more bid and I was done for. Again it got down to "last call", going once... going twice. People in a chatroom I was in were all typing "END IT!!! END IT!!!" hoping I would win... and suddenly I see *SOLD*!

    WOOOOOOOOOO!!!! I won it! More than I wanted to spend, but IMO still a fair price. It makes my other 2 TNG costumes with ALOT more being a full set now. I was also happy to see the "Voyager" and "first contact" costumes I already own, selling for large increases over what I bought them for. While a fun hobby, this is also a partial investment. Some of my costumes are up 30-40% over my purchase price! Very cool, and my wife lets me spend alot more on it as she's seeing the gains.

    I used the mannequin I had the command red on so unfortunately I can't do an away team trio picture until I get a new mannequin. Its interesting with the sizing, how much they vary. The Gold Ops is the same costume size as the Sci Blue and Command Red, however it is the smallest, fitting them mannequin snugly. The Blue is bigger on the same mannequin but I don't think it would fit the other model size 4 mannequins I have. The command red is really huge on the mannequin so I think I'll get a bigger mannequin for the rest and do some shuffling to see what fits best on what.

    Anywho, here it is! The rare TNG female Science Uniform.





    In person, its a bit more of a greenish teal, but under bright lights / flash it glows blue. Same with the Command Red costumes, they are a deep dark maroon in person but under light the light up a bright red. Its interesting the choices in fabrics and colors they have to use in filming and working under super bright lights. You can see the Insurrection Troi and Worf in the background. House is cluttered still due to the flood we had in the other side of the house. I'm laying carpet back down as we speak.
