    Something to note. The pet stone bag that contains a random pet level stone (a) now can produce aquatic stones, (b) has (from what I saw with 45 bags today) about a 30% chance of containing a second stone. So unless you've completed leveling about half of the pet families, the bags are a better deal on average. It's also worth noting that you can bank the extra stones for families you are done with, so if you get a pet of that family later you can level them with "worthless" stones.
