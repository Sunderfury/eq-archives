    Re: DDO Back in the Game Getting ready for the expansion....

    Been running more with my druid lately. Got to 10, closing in on 11. Solo classes are just amazing. Between me and my wolf I can do even level stuff on normal solo that would eat a melee for lunch. I got to do the first Vault of Night raid with a PUG and got like 50k xp for it. Was a lot of fun and loot too.

    Levelling is pretty much a pick your path thing. If you want to solo, you can certainly do it. There are rewards for repeating things on hard and elite. Best bang for the buck though is to find someone doing elites at your level and knock em out. That gives you huge stacks of xp.

    Back to soloing, druid and artificer are just sick. Warforged artificer especially. Self heals are the biggest thing for solo. Self and pet if you're a pet user. Artificer gets ranged dps (caster or crossbows your pick) and all the rogue skills for the fun of it.

    True Reincarnation is going to be the ultimate decider for me. If the passive feats are account bound I'm going to go hog wild and have a ton of toons on all servers to bang stuff out with. I get burnout fever if I have to play one toon all the time. If they're character bound I'm going to focus on my druid. Get to 25 three times, then ranger three times, then artificer. The passives for druid are +2 to all pet and hireling skills (+6 total) and ranger is +2 damage to ranged weapons (+6 total). Put those two together and my artificer will be an unholy wrecking machine. Focusing on one toon will lower operating costs quite a bit as well. The +3 to all stat tomes from the store cost $34, so doing that is not going to be possible for all toons. Also other things like bank space bag space and the collectible bags that cost $$$ will make one toon as a prestige character the most feasible either way. Meh.

    If anyone wants an invite let me know. Right now I'm getting guild to 20 asap so I can get us an airship. First one is only 50k, which I already have.
