    I got a set of 377 blue pvp gear, ran normal dungeons till I ran out of upgrades from justice gear and then managed to get through a few heroics without getting kicked out too often and got some heroic points gear.     Something my brother and I have both found very annoying, we can't get upgrades from Justice point gear or random normal dungeons, but we get kicked for not hitting this 15k benchmark that everyone seems to think is needed for heroics and told 'come back when you have better gear'. Massive issue in wow with loud people looking down on anyone who can actually use gear from content they are running.

    I've been doing LFR, though frustratingly have only won a single item in the last 3 weeks. Its been good fun being able to do some raid content finally. That’s pretty much all I’ve seen of raiding except some of Naxx and Sunwell with guild doing old content runs.

    The thing I’m now finding really frustrating is trying to gear up for pvp. Getting only 100 conquest a day is very slow and grindy and getting steamrolled in arena with 377 blues while slowly building up honour gear seems pointless. Would be nice to get 100 conquest for the first 10 battleground wins a week, much like heroics. Instead I find it faster to do 1 battleground, then run 7 random normal dungeons and convert the justice to honour.
