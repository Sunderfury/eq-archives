    Re: Mark Hamill's Trickster Return Spotlighted in New Flash Photos

    The legends of tomorrow season finale is shaping up well so far also.

    I'm up waaaay too late catching up on the last episodes here, but it looks like the people in charge of DC's television franchise are REALLY making an effort to do good things and work together.

    The best part of it is that none of it is really dependent on any other show, so if you don't like all of them, you can still keep up without losing the plot.

    These guys have the spirit of episodic comics pretty well locked in.

    Nothing of it is worthy of an "epic" trade paperback, but it's all a consistent story evolution without re-invention for going on four seasons now (starting with arrow), which is a massive departure from what the printed/e-comic franchise is doing these days, from what I can tell.

    Some of the shows have more interpersonal drama than others, but they're ALL still capturing the comicbook action when the tacks turn brassy.

    As a diehard DC fan who now pretty much ONLY plays DC Universe Online as an MMO, they're doing a GREAT job on the TV side with regards to capturing the feel.

    DC was never Marvel and Marvel has plenty of its own merits, but I'm enjoying the crap out of the multitude of shows here.
