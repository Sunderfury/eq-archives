    Re: Halloween Event Started

    Took me about 2 hours to complete. As an Elementalist, I have the trait to gain movement speed while Attuned to air. That made a HUGE difference. Because of the extra speed, you need to be a bit more careful on tight jumps but you make up that lost time on the straight ways.

    If you're not an elementalist, the biggest piece of advice I can offer is to go into the Instance and if there are a lot of Charr or Norn characters, exit out to the character select screen and come back in. Odds are you'll get placed into a new overflow server. Keep doing this until you find one with few or no fatties. After I discovered this trick I was able to finish the puzzle within a couple of attempts.

    The rest of the Halloween stuff is pretty cool and I'm really enjoying it. I'm looking forward to seeing the new stuff coming up.
    There are other worlds than these.

    Re: Halloween Event Started

    You can get to the minigames by looking for blue and red crossed flags(think keg brawl queue up) on the minimap in lions arch/newbie zones where the festive stuff is and talk to them to queue up. You can also get to them by going into the portals in the newbie zones and talking to the boat guy.

    dulfy has a map for one of them http://dulfy.net/2012/10/27/gw2-luna...n-event-guide/
