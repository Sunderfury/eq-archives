    Re: WoW Patch 6.1 on PTR (soon)

    I got a really good phishing e-mail today. Claimed my credit card used to pay was expiring before the next billing date, please go to account management (conveniently linked, but not told "click here") to change or use prepaid time. The e-mail had the right graphics, told me "if your battle tag isn't (correct battle tag) don't respond", had the correct noreply@blizzard.com e-mail address source, and even came within a couple of days of guessing my renewal date (I'm on three month, so not as easy as one would first guess). So be on the lookout for stuff like this.

    The amount of crap to do in a day seems worse now, not better. That could be the "first day rush", although I don't see how I'm getting the new daily stuff done on top of everything else, especially given that I need to arrange for multiple alts to go over to someone else's garrison to even get the quests (solo content gated behind group dynamics, hooray). The consumables for mining just remind one of what a waste of time mining is in the first place, and I imagine the coffee buff was dropped to a three minute duration just to prove to us that mining takes longer than three minutes. I can't imagine getting anything done without Master Plan, and until I get some of my inventory mods back (Auctioneer/TSM/Altoholic) the crates are piling up in inventory. Fingers crossed that the mods that keep me playing get updated soon.

    PS. Anyone else notice that the flight paths actually got worse, not better? The path from the garrison to the far Nagrand FP now has more zigs and zags than a Charlie Brown t-shirt, and the flight from my Gorgrond outpost to Frostfire is so screwy it takes you right through a mountain peak. Hopefully that's one of the many things fixed in this morning's one hour maintenance.
