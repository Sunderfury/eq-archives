    The camp invasion was replaying a scene from the graphic novel, so I may have overlooked problems with it. But it was displaying a plot point that is maybe kind of subtle but has been touched on already. If you fire a shot, the zombies will hear it and eventually gather at that place. It may take them a while, days, but a group of "walkers" will eventually show up. This isn't just a thing where you have to be right near a big group of them and then they come running. They can hear a shot from miles away and they'll start a-shuffling.

    Also it would be easy to get complacent if you haven't seen a zombie for a while. The campers are still learning how to cope, in many ways, and aren't all zombie experts by a long shot. Honestly, I was also happy to see them start getting weeded out.
