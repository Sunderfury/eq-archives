    Re: July 23 GW2 Update Two Mini Games, Voting on Future Fract, PvE Survival Game

    If you make the exotics it will likely you cost the same as it would to just buy them off the trading post since ectoplasms are expensive (though crafting your own might be more rewarding). But you can also get exotics by spending karma in the temples in orr. Some info on the temples: http://wiki.guildwars2.com/wiki/Temple and status on whether the temples are open or not: http://www.gw2state.com/temples.html You can always guest to another server where they are open to buy the gear.

    You can also buy exotics with the tokens you get from running dungeons in explorable mode (http://wiki.guildwars2.com/wiki/List_of_armor_sets scroll down to token armor sets for more info)

    And lastly you can buy exotics with tokens from Wvw. http://wiki.guildwars2.com/wiki/Invader%27s_Armor this is the invaders set but I am 99% sure you can buy a set with berserker stats if you plan to use it for pve. Try to wvw on friday nights if you want a lot of tokens since that is when there will be the most zerg verse zerg battles. Stack Vitality/Toughness and use weapons that let you hit lots of targets you only get loot if you tag a player before he or she dies.

    If you are not going for the win, but rather just points, then just stay in the middle and kill people. Map you 1 skill to an extra mouse button if you have one and just run in, grab a weapon (pistol is best) and just fire randomly twice. that is two kills for 10 points. Run into the main paddock and get as many arrows as possible in the next few seconds while trying to stay out of line of sight. If you get traps immediately drop them. If you get rations eat them only behind cover. If you die, respawn at the start (jump off tower and die or if on beach just act like a maniac and try and die fast. If you can fight it out in the padock area and die first just collect motes. There are always a million of them. I usually had a few kills, a lot of salvages in the first few minutes and thena bunch in the wisp time. I usually got 50 points with no effort. also, it's pretty fun to spawn karkas.

    PS best way to get exotics is to buy a cheap (non best stat set) off of the AH and then run path one of CoF for tokens for your offensive set. Once that you can run CoF for money for your sigils. In a week of casual play you can have great sset for fractals built.. Fractals gets you rings. Do your dailies for your laurels for your other non armor slots.
