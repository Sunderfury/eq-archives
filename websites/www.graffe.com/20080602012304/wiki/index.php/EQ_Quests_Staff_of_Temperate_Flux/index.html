[edit] The Staff

The Staff of Temperate Flux is a very important wizard item.

  1. It lowers a mob's fire/cold resistances by 6 each. Very nice while soloing or being in a group without someone who can lower fire or cold defenses. 6 points is enough to notice a difference. With this stick I've had fewer full resists and fewer partial resists (of all mobs within my ability to hit, of course).
  2. It also makes a great pulling device because it aggro's without damage.
  3. The effect cannot be multiplied and doesn't stack with other fire/cold debuffs. As a debuff, it helps all in your group, not just you.
  4. It can be resisted, but is rather pointless since you can just click away until it takes effect.
  5. When kiting, it is a great distance-judging device, since you can click it on the run.

[edit] The Quest

Original Quest submit by: Telor Strifesage

This quest is initiated by speaking to Gardern in the Temple of Solusek Ro. He issues this quest along with the Weeping Wand. For the Staff of Temperate Flux, he asks you for a Heart of Fire from an Inferno Goblin Wizard from Solusek's caves, a Heart of Frost from a Goblin Wizard in Permafrost, a Rod of Bone from Stone Skeletons in Lake Rathe, and finally a Staff of Ro. It is arguably one of the most important quests a wizard will ever do.

  Important Note: This quest is no longer multiquestable

[edit] Heart of Fire

In Solusek A, I was near the Solusek's Champion when a wizard spawned that had this. The hearts are semi rare. Wizards and Shamans share spawning points so by killing shamans you increase your chances of finding a wizard with one of these. They only drop from Inferno Goblin Wizards. If you want to save yourself a headache, go into Solusek A during peak hours and OOC that you would like to loot one in return for some platinum.

[edit] Heart of Frost

In Permafrost, I did an OOC that I was looking for a heart of frost, someone found one on a wizard that spawned in the Preacher's room. I wasn't there long, got very lucky. I was told a few things about this from a very trusted guildmate, she found the heart of frost on more than just wizards (being a 50 Bard, she would go in and kill all the npc's that weren't camped for fun). Apparently, wizards have them much more frequent but hearts can be found on anything. I know first hand that, the best place to find wizards is while camping for the preacher. I was unlucky that the preacher was camped, but lucky that a person there was kind enough to let me loot the heart. I also know, first hand, that my heart was found on a frost goblin wizard.

[edit] The Rod of Bone

The Rod of Bone is found on Stone Skeletons that sit inside an eerily lit tower on the shore of Lake Rathe. I got to this tower by entering Lake Rathe from South Karana and I swam keeping the zone wall on my left side. I should note that this Tower is located on a high shore that you'll find shortly after passing the zone entrance to Rathe Mtns. A solo aviak guard spawns on a perch on this highland. Many 20-24 people camp that as it easy exp. (I know first hand) so be careful. The stone skeletons, to the best of my knowledge, are magicians who conjure earth pets. Two of them with pets con'ed green to me at 35. They were green but high enough to aggro me when I cast on one of them. I had a wizard with me and we needed to kill 3 sets to get 2 rods... not too shabby.

[edit] The Staff of Ro

The Staff of Ro is obtained in Solusek's Temple. Romar Sunto tells you he will give you a Darkwood Staff, also known as a Staff of Ro, if you bring him a Lambent Stone. Lambent Stones are found on Sand Giants, Hill Giants, and Griffins.
