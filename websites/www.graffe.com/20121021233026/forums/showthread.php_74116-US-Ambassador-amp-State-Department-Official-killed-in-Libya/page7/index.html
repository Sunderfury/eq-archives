    Well, what exactly would the US do if Pakistan openly harbored terrorist groups who carried out attacks? It cost hundreds of billions of dollars and thousands of soldiers lives going into Afghanistan, and Pakistan would be a hundred times tougher to fight.

There are no long term solutions I can see except to stop sticking your head above the parapet being the world's policeman, and wait a generation or three for the animosity to die down.

Best case scenario it would take decades to calm down. Worst case scenario is a local nuclear exchange and perhaps even the major global powers such as China and Russia getting involved, shutting down world trade and bringing a new economic depression that makes the current one look like a picnic.
