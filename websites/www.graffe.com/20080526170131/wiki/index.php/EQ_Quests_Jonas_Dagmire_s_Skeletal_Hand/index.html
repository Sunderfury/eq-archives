[edit] Jonas Dagmire's Skeletal Hand

This is a long quest, though most of it is fairly straightforward to complete. The time it may take will vary depending on how lucky you are with the RNG since there are a lot of random drops involved and a couple rare spawns that need to be killed. The whole quest can be soloed by a 75 Wizard, most of it without too much of a problem, though some parts may be much easier if done in a group.


All of the following quests will need to be completed in any order to finish the aug. Doing the quests for each island unlocks bone pieces as quest rewards which are combined to complete 6 subparts (4 fingers, thumb, backhand). Those 6 pieces are then combined in order to complete the final aug. Presently you must do the quests in the actual Buried Sea zone only. Even though the Missions may drop the same pieces off the same mobs, you do not seem to get credit for them at this time.

[edit] The Combines:

These combines are EACH performed in a Bone Joint Compound that costs 10 doubloons from the Pirate vendor on Jardel's Hook. This container is used up so you will need a total of 7 containers to complete the aug. 5 for the fingers, 1 for the backhand, and 1 for the final combine.
