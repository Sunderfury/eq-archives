[edit] Investigating the Combine Ruins

Expansion: The Buried Sea
Zones involved: The Buried Sea,The Barren Coast
Given by: Brynden Snow

Quest Steps:

  1. Kill 10 firemaw tiger
  2. Kill 10 sharpfang raptor
  3. Kill 10 darkclaw basilisk
  4. Collect 10 Combine Stone(s)
  5. Return 10 Combine Stones to Brynden Snow

Rewards:

Faction with Blacksail Smugglers
Experience
Reward depends on how many quests you have done in the series this belongs to, may be 1 of 3 possible bone pieces or 75PP. Bone pieces are used for the quest for Jonas Dagmire's Skeletal Hand
