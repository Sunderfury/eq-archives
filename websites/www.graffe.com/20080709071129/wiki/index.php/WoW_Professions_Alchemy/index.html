Alchemy

Alchemy is a production professions. Unlike the armor production professions, you'll never run out of customers with this skill due to the consumption character of potions.

Alchemy is excellent for an alt, as all the production materials are tradeable, but can have its advantage for a main: there is a trinket for Alchemists only which increases the net gain from health/mana potions (Alchemist's Stone, all stats +15, +40% effect from health/mana potions).

[edit] General

[edit] Kinds of potions

Potions might be categorized in three groups:

  • Instant effect potions: The all-times favorite health/mana potions are in this category.
  • Duration effect potions: Potions with an effect that lasts over the duration, but not over deaths. Not only stat-increasers are part of this group, but also some regeneration potions that let the user sleep while the potion does its work.
  • Flasks: These resource dumps have the strongest effects and last two hours ever across deaths. Pre-BC, the Alchemy labs (necessary to combine flasks) were only in level 60 dungeons. With Shattrah there's finally a place even your level 40 alt can use to combine the flasks. (You can't walk through the Dark Portal before level 58, but you can move a character of any level to Shattrah with the help of the local Warlock airline...)

[edit] Skilling up Alchemy

[edit] Herb sidenote

Interesting to know if you don't buy your herbs from the AH: Some herbs have **very** specific locations to gather. Most notably for that in lower levels is Blindweed, which is all over Swamp of Sorrows... and not much elsewhere.

[edit] Endgame

[edit] Flasks

Flasks are the 'masterpieces' of the Alchemist. It takes a great deal of herbs to produce them and one of the most rare herbs: the black lotus. This herb is only in the highest zones available, with only a handful of nodes per zone and the respawn time being measured in hours flasks are neither for mass production nor mass consumption. The most often produced flask is probably the flask of the titan for the main tank of a raid.

[edit] Transmutations

Alchemy is the skill necessary to transmute elements into other elements. It requires a philosopher's stone as reagent (not consumed on use) which the alchemist has to create themselves once. Most common is the transmutation of Arcane Crystal + Thorium bar into an Arkanit bar on a cooldown of 48 hours. The second in line is probably the transmutation of Undead essence into Water essence (and from there into other essences), as Undead essence is a semi-common drop in some level 60 dungeons, whereas Water essence itself (like all 4 elements) is a rare drop from Elementals. Essence transmutation is on a 24 hour cooldown.
