ATTRIBUTES

STRENGTH

  • Strength determines the weight you can carry before becoming encumbered.

  • The higher your strength, the larger the weapon you can wield in combat without losing your stamina.

  • Strength has a direct impact on your attack value. Increase your strength, and you increase your ability to force that blow through your enemy's defences.

STAMINA

  • Stamina impacts directly on the number of your initial hit points and the number of hit points you will gain upon reaching each new level.

  • A high stamina allows you to continue a tiring activity for a longer period of time. You can run farther without a break. You will be able to swing that overweight lead staff of thumping more times without becoming exhausted.

AGILITY

  • Agility influences armor class. An agile troll will lead a longer life than a slog-footed one.

DEXTERITY

  • Dexterity is said to influence your chance of striking an opponent in melee combat.

  • Dexterity should increase your chances with ranged weapon attacks as well.

  • I have seen no direct impact of dexterity on character abilities. A higher dexterity does not increase your attack value as I believe it is intended to do.

WISDOM

  • Wisdom determines the shaman's mana and shaman spell success.

  • Wisdom increase a character's ability to save versus spells that impact the mind.

  • Wisdom cannot be raised with any shaman spells. All the physical attributes and even charisma can be raised this way. Because of this, I recommend the starting shaman place as many bonus attribute points into wisdom as is allowed.

Intelligence

  • A high intellingence allows a troll to learn skills at a faster rate this his less-gifted peers.

Charisma

  • Charisma effects how NPC's react to your character.

  • A higher charisma will lower prices at merchants.

  • Everyone hates a troll anyway, so why worry about it?
