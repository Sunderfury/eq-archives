EVERQUEST NEWS

  • Everquest Stratics For an in-depth examination of Everquest, you can't beat Stratics. I consider it number one of the "Big Three".

  • EQ Vault An excellent site to watch for up to the minute news from the EQ community.

  • Crossroads of Norrath Third of the "Big Three". Crossroads seems to have a roleplay issue focus.

  • Official PvP Headquarters Message boards, links, and PvP quest info await the strong of heart at this Everworld site.

  • The Neriak Maurader An excellent newsletter detailing the PvP action on Rallos Zek from the dark elf perspective.

  • Evil Quest Information site dedicated to supporting all that is evil in Everquest.

  • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm Amazing work! This site is a huge collection of data and a hub of links from which any subject in Everquest can be thoroughly explored.
