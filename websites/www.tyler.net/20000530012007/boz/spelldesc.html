SPELL DESCRIPTIONS

Spell descriptions in italics are from sources outside Bohica's life experiences. Each definition of this type will be followed by its source. As Bohica tests these spells, the italic definitions will be replaced by his findings.

Level 1

Strengthen
This spell adds 10 strength points to the target of the spell.

Burst of Flame
Burst of Flame does direct fire damage to a single target. Maximum damage is 5 pts.

Minor Healing
Heals 10 points of damage on a single target.

Flash of Light
The spell is not available from shaman vendors. I had to buy it from a cleric spells vendor. It blinds a target for 10 seconds or so, making it miss 99% of the time. If you move back, the blinded target runs away. Flash of light is also a great way to practice Divination. - Danlor, Barbarian Shaman on Tarew Marr

Cure Disease
Removes disease from spell target.

Endure Cold
Adds a maximum of +20 to cold resistance. The maximum effect is reached at the 10th level of casting. - Detritus, troll shaman

Dexterous Aura
Increases target's dexterity. Maximum addition is 10 pts and reached at 6th level.

Inner Fire
Added 15 hit points and 1 ac point to target when cast by 5th level shaman. Adds 16 hp and 2 ac pt when cast by 6th level shaman. Adds 17 hp and 2 ac pt at 7th level. Added 19 hp and 2 ac at 9th. Further tests pending.

Sense Magic
This spell is supposed to be used to detect magic on items. Most magic items clearly state function without this spell.
Sense Magic is most useful in practicing divination. At level 14 the shaman has access to Serpent Sight, which is quite appreciated when touring with humans. Casting a level 14 divination spell when your divination skill is around 5 means a lot of fizzling. - Detritus, troll shaman

Level 5

Gate
Returns caster to home point. This cannot be cast on others.

Sicken
Short term disease with a small amount of initial direct damage to target. Causes 8 pts of damage at 7th level. At 9th level, 8 pts were still the maximum. This spell reduces the troll regeneration rate. If this is consitent with other races, it has a minor damage-over-time effect. Further tests pending.

Cure poison
Removes poison from spell's target.

Summon Drink
Creates one water. This water is stackable. It can be traded to other players. It cannot be sold to merchants. It dissappears upon death or disconnect. It may be placed into bank, but will vansish from the bank upon death or disconnect.

Endure Fire
Adds a maximum of +20 to fire resistance. The maximum effect is reached at the 10th level of casting. - Detritus, troll shaman

Feet Like Cat
Adds a maximum of +20 to target's agility. This spell reaches it cap when cast at level 12. - Quwem

Drowsy
Slow target attack speed

Fleeting Fury
Adds +15 str, +20 dex, and +5 ac to target.

Spirit Pouch
Creates a magical pouch that stores up to four items of any size (tested with 2 handed staff). This bag can be traded and banked. It cannot be sold to merchants. The bag and all of its contents will dissappear upon death or disconnection.

Scale Skin
Snake scales are a necessary reagent to cast this spell. Adds +5 ac at 6th level. Adds +6 ac at 9th. Tests pending.

Frost Rift
Causes direct cold damage to a single target. Maximum damage potential is 15 pts. - Quwem

Level 9

Light Healing
Healed 28 pts at 9th level.

Summon Food
Summons a single loaf of dark bread. This bread will disappear upon death or log-out. It can be traded to other players.

Spirit Sight
See invisible self and others - EQ Vault

Cure Blindness
Anit-blindness - EQ Vault

Endure Disease
Adds a maximum of +20 to disease resistance. - Detritus, troll shaman

Sense Animals
When I used it the spell rotated me toward the nearest animal. Try casting it with your back to an animal. You'll turn around. - Sluglug

Winged Courier
BUGGED To cast winged courier, you must first target the player you wish to send an item to. Next, you left click on the item to be sent to pick it up. Finally, while holding the item, you click on the winged courier spell icon to send the item to the targeted player. This is currently broken. The item will disappear, the targetted player will get a message that they received the item. The item will NOT be in that player's inventory. When the sending shaman zones, the item will reappear on his cursor. Winged courier will only work when the caster and receiver are in the same zone.

Serpent Sight
Infra vision - EQ Vault

Tainted Breath
Poison spell offering initial damage strike followed by damage-over-time effect. At level 9, it does 13 points of initial damage, and then 10 ticks of 3 damage. At level 10, it does 14 initial and 11 ticks of 3. At level 11 it does 15 inital, and I assume 12 ticks. At 12, it does 16, and probably 13 ticks. - Jered W.

Spirit of Wolf
I'd estimate this spell adds about 25% to the targetted player's top running speed.

Spirit of Bear
Add to stamina - EQ Vault
