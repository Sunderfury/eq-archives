Thursday, February 22, 2001
Thx nerf!
Last night we faced off with the Dain again... with an even stronger force than last time. We expected a tough victory, seeing as we'd have to compensate for the AI and hate changes.

The actual result? Our best attempt got the normally wussy (in Velious terms) Dain to 50%... and even that involved a massive res-rush.

What happened, you may ask yourself? Did the mighty Ownage, Inc. lose their glory? Was I, the mastermind of Da`Kor tactics and strategy, Koryo the Tactician, absent? Did our warriors forget to equip weapons?

No, my dear friends, our loss was quite simple. Our healers employed two strategies. The first involved healing the tanks that were taking massive damage from Dain's 425 quadding stubs. The result : all healers dead in the first minute of the fight. The second strategy involved "being careful when they healed" (I think someone checked Abashi's post on a 2nd comp and suggested we employ The Wise One's advice). The amazing result : all the melee died!

I know you are all expecting wise words from me as an answer to the question "What now?"
Well, I think I can say with confidence... fuck if I know, game is fucking broke.
- Kzzern
