The above form will bring you into the emarr IRC channel by using a Java applet. Java applets can be annoying and they're also very limited, and so is the CGI client. For the full IRC "experience" I suggest that you download a real IRC client  (i.e. mIRC). I will list the steps how to connect to the emarr IRC channel with mIRC:

1) Install the mIRC client
2) Start up the mIRC client and go to options.
3) Click on "Connect" on the left bar, and enter your nickname. 
4) In the same menu, at the right of "IRC servers" click on "Add".
5) At "description" and "IRC server" fill in: irc.stratics.com
or one of the following servers:
stratics.truefear.net
dhp.stratics.com
compu.stratics.com
frws.stratics.com
intworks.stratics.com
6) The standard port (6667) is correct and does not need to be changed.
7) Select the "irc.stratics.com" server from the server list and click on the "Connect to IRC Server" button.
7) once connected, type "/join #emarr"

Now you have found our secret hideout :P


IRC Tips: 
Tip 1) If you're planning to visit stratics' IRC a lot, you will most likely want to register your nick on this server, meaning nobody can use that nick but you (imposers suck monkey balls). To register your nick, do the following (note: this can be done with the applet as well!):

/nickserv register <your password here>

Next time you log into IRC, you have one minute to identify yourself or your nickname will be changed:

/nickserv identify <your password here>


Tip 2) mIRC has an "Perform on connect" menu, it'll save you a lot of time if you would use this. Follow these steps:
1) Go to options
2) Click on the minus just before "IRC". 
3) Click on "perform" in this submenu.
4) Enable "On connect, perform these commands"
5) Enter whatever commands you use to get to #emarr

These are the commands that I am using to give you an idea:
/nick Ogmuk
/msg nickserv ident <my password>
/join #emarr
/join <other channels you visit at stratics IRC>


Tip 3) Most used IRC commands (besides the ones I already named):
Emote : /me <your emote here>
Changing your nick (e.g. when you go afk) : /nick <new nick>
Leave IRC with a message : /quit <message>
