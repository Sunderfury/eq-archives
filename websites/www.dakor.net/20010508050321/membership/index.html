Thoughts on Membership
Da`Kor operates as a cohesive unit.  Each and every person has an important role in the guild.  Those not fulfilling this role are removed from the guild, it is really that simple.  We're not here to screw around with our friends, have heart to heart talks, or fight half assed.  If you are looking for that, you won't find it here.  Simply put, we are out to have fun by dominating the challenges of this game.

If you are loot driven or a whiner, stop reading right here.  Join a support group and don't waste your time and ours.

We only look at people who are 100% willing to devote themselves entirely to our guild.  During the many weeks for the consideration and sponsorship process, it is up to you to talk with us, group with us, and prove you are Da`Kor quality.  You are expected to bust your balls if you want to join Da`Kor, because we bust ours for our members.  If you aren't putting in 100% effort to get in our guild, then why should we put any effort in for you?

If you're interested in joining Da`Kor because you want to learn to kick ass first and ask questions later, that is a good start.  If you want to be in Da`Kor because you see something different and special about us, and simply won't settle for anything less, then you are on the right path. Our attitude is not meant to be elitist, nor is it meant to offend other guilds.  There are a lot of other skilled players and guilds on Erollisi Marr - Da`Kor is not for everyone.

If you think your kung fu would benefit our kung fu, send an email to recruiting@dakor.net.  Include your character name, level, who from Da`Kor knows you, accomplishments, and any other information you feel we should take under consideration.  When we receive your email, it will be posted to a private Da`Kor board where members can read it.  You will not receive a reply back.  It is now up to you to send tells to members looking for groups.  Please note that the consideration and sponsorship process can take over a month.

We do not consider people who are concurrently applying for other guilds.  If you want a chance at joining, you must be willing to commit to Da`Kor.
