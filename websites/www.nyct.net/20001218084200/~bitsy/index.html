ABOUT THE HALAS QUEST

Go to the tavern in Halas, the smaller one the left, I forget what it's called. There will be a woman standing outside, hail her, follow the lead text in the brackets until she tells you that she wants all three pages from her brother's diary. He killed three bards. Proceed directly to 222.94, 1002.97 and 38, 89.5. This is where the skeletons spawn. They conned yellow to me at level 10. There is a Vengeful Soloist, Lyricist, and something else, I forget. Loot the pages. Hand them back to the woman. She will give you a new brew book (recipes below).

Question on: order for the pages?

When I looted I think two of the pages stacked immediately, so all I had were two pages to hand her instead of three. I handed them to her at once and she first said 'now I need one more' and then immediately 'thank you here are his recipes' and handed me the brewbook you see below. If you got a different book or response, please email me.

NEW: If you don't give her all the pages she gives you a list of possible brews. See complete list above.
