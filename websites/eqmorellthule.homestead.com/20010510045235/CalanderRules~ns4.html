These guidelines are pretty much just common sense, but many people have requested to be put up, they are designed to allow every guild an equal and fair opertunity to do raids without conflicts. Most guilds already follow these basic guidelines when raiding. But please read, understand, and adhere to these guidelines. When you are done reading them, click the confirmation link at the bottom to go to the calander. Please do not violate these rules. If they are violated too much I will have to make the calander restricted so only appointed people can post. Please dont ruin it for everybody.
1. Please do not schedule more then 2 weeks in advance. This is just for things like planes and dragons etc.

2. Please do not chain schedule on the same plane/dragon or raid-worthy dungeon week after week after week. Also, please do not attempt to reserve the same plane, dragon, or raid-worthy dungeon any sooner then 3 days later. IE: If you schedule to raid Plane of Fear on Monday, please do not try to do Plane of Fearany sooner then Thursday. Please feel free to schedule raids on other planes/dragons or raid worthy dungeons however.

3. Whatever Rotations are in place need to be clear to anyone who wants to attempt certain spawns (i.e. VS). Rotations are established by the guilds that are capable of doing the harder spawns. The guild leaders discuss and have in place a fair rotation already on a few spawns. If you want to try something new that falls into the "agreed upon rotations" you will need to contact ALL of the guild leaders involved in the rotation and see where your "spot" is.

4. Scheduling conflicts: All Planes have a set re-pop time, some longer then others. DO NOT SCHEDULE to go in the day before another guild without knowing the re-pop times AND making sure you are not interfering with the next raid. Basically, please be OUT of the plane in enough time for the guild that scheduled FIRST to have a full re-pop.

5. Obviously the Server Resets and Patches are a huge factor in the game. If your scheduled raid is screwed by a patch or reset.. This does NOT give you right to attempt the raid at the next available opportunity IF it interfiers with another guilds plans. Please cancel your raid and try again at the next available non-conflicting time.
