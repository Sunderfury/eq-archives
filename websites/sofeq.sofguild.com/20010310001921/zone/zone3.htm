A Guide to Oasis of Marr

 written by Aileron D´Ailell


How do you get to 20?  The Oasis of Marr is an extremely good zone to get those few levels from 12 to 20.   Here are some tips to make your hunting here easier.....

low level mobs - aqua goblins, caimen, crocodiles, desert madmen estimated level 10-14

mid level mobs - ghouls, dry bone skeletones, an orc warrior, an orc priest, estimated level 14-17

high level mobs - a orc warrior (bad grammar = bad mob), deepwater caimen, deepwater crocodiles - level 18-22 mobs

Things to avoid - Sand Giants!!!  Spectres!!! and Lockjaw (level 25 giant crocodile)

Where to hunt - There are 3 or 4 good spots to hunt depending on your level.  

At 10-14, there is an NPC camp in the center of the zone by the lake.  This is where you can fight aqua goblins, caimens and crocodiles.   Stay behind the campfire at all times to avoid wandering sand giants.  The aqua goblin camp is underwater hehehe, so your lurer should be at least 55 in swimming to survive =)  The camp has around 4 to 5 goblins and they will usually all come after ya.  Kill any aqua goblin shaman first and BE CAREFUL when targetting them because you might inadvertently target one of the human NPCs in the camp and well... either you take a faction hit by killing them or zone quickly =)  They are low level NPCs (around 10), so they're not that dangerous.  Oh and DO NOT USE Area Effect  spells as they will also damage the NPCs around you and they will retaliate.  Around the camp are wandering caimen and crocodiles, these are       good xp mobs but poor loot.  Aqua goblins carry bows and arrows, and the occassional bronze weapon and raw-hide armor.

At level 15 to 18, the best spot is the NW corner of the zone.   This is where you fight orcs.  Remember, AN orcs are levels 15 to 17 and A orcs  are level 20.  So take note of it =)  There is also a 4-orc camp at the SE corner of the zone, this is a good hunting spot and also close to a zone (South Ro).

At level 18 to 22, the best spot is along the shore between the first and second pillars.  You can hunt deepwater caimen and deepwater crocodiles here.  They are easy kills if you have a good group because they usually fight alone and others won't join in the fun.

Special Notes: Sand Giants now wade in the water so the pillars aren't a safe place to rest anymore.  Lockjaw used to be a threat but now, plenty of people kill that kill him for the lockjaw tunic (magic leather armor) and the much sought after Glowing Crook Staff (humongous 2HB weapon,  except when wielded by dwarves, halflings and gnomes hehehe)  Spectres can be found in a tower at the center of the lake in the center of the zone.  They don't wander unless someone is foolish enough to attack them.   They are level 35+ I believe.

Oasis of Marr is a good place to hunt.  Some say it's a poor loot zone because you just hunt crocs, which is not true.  I made about 200plat in this zone to purchase my banded armor.  Orcs carry good loot and you also get the occassional jewels from desert madmen too =)

How to you get here you ask?  From Freeport go to the South Gate and go south and zone into North Ro.  Go east to the beach and follow the  shoreline going south or alternatively, from the East Commonlands/North Ro tunnel, go south along the mountainside and the zone border is just after the 3-Dervish Cutthroat camp.

Safe journeys to all,
