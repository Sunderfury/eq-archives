Journey With Angels

written by Zackary


Journey With Angels
Dedicated to the memory and spirit of a true
friend and Soldier, Kilandra MacLeod

What love is there to be found in a friend,
One who stands o’er six feet ten?

A person whose size, could not contain,
The size of neither spirit nor that of the heart within.

The love she received and gave was a light,
That was cherished each day unto the night.

The Errant Knight, of whom we knew,
Her smile was the wind upon which angels flew.

And upon the day, she left our land,
We held her close as she took our hand.

Her journey to help those in need,
A noble task in which she believed.

And in the noble fashion of a true Soldier,
She went out and smiled on less fortunate others.

One day we thought, she might return.
And of her Journeys we hoped to learn.

But alas we found, the gods had a plan,
Which would keep her ever from our lands.

Upon their shoulders they raised her on high,
To carry out their wishes and with the spirits fly.

And though we ever shall miss her so,
Our lives are better for knowing her soul.

Forever will she live, in all our hearts,
And always and ever, will she be a part.

A nobler friend we may never know,
Her spirit lives on for us to show.

Though the days may be dark and the path poorly lit,
Watch for her light in the heavens, and you won’t be adrift.

For though it seemed her Journey had merely begun,
Forever after, it will never be done.

Go forth dear sister, watch o’er us and the lands.
Protect us in our travels, and hold us in your hands.

Never will we forget you or your love,
And you we will remember when we look above.

Fly now with the Angels, on wings of air,
May your Journey be safe and your days all fair.

-Written by Chris Peterson
AKA Prince Zackary Stoutheart, SoF-EQ
December 15, 2000
