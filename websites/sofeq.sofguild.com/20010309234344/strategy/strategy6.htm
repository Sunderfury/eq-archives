Small Rawhide Armor

 written by Reanne


If you are looking for small raw hide armor..  I have the place for you..  If you are in the Kaladim area..  There are orc oracles and Cents there that often carry small raw hide as treasures! *I got every stitch of my raw hide armor there for free*  There is a tower where they spawn..  This is a pretty popular place, with only three spawning in rotation..  But most ppl will welcome someone into their group and split the looting process..  

Ok, where this place is..

If you are coming from the Greater Faydark zone..  follow the path just past what is referred to by other players as,  The two houses..  You can sell here and buy some items,  unfortunately food and water is not offered here..   Just past the two houses, still on the path..  you'll see a lil ravine.. or whatever you call it..  an indent =D  to your right..  follow the mountain on the right.. you'll pass a pretty cool look'n white/yellow pillar..  keep to your right.. nothing spawns here as of yet, that we all know of..  you'll soon come to a huge gray pillar, Orc Tower..  One orc spawns in the back.. and two right in front of the pillar..  IF!  you get into trouble.. the only place for you to run to for help is either back to the greater Faydark zone.. *run to the road and go left* or..  run to the path and go right to the guard shack..  it's a lil jaunt.. so be sure you're not about to die..  

Now.. to the left of that white/yellow pillar is a bandit camp..  good exp there..  but mostly you just get their tongues *yuuuuck*  as treasure..  these are for some quest that I have yet to figure out..  I just put them in the bank and give them to ppl who are looking for the tongues..  The bandits have names.. first and last..  and yup.. they are a good fight.. and tend to gang up on you.. so do not go there alone..

The only things you really wanna try to avoid in Butcher Block are the two Ogres that spawn from time to time..  Corflunk and Zarchoomi..  you'll hear someone shout Cornflake is out!!  If you are at orc tower, you are pretty safe.. most ppl will lead him or his sister Zarchoomi, down the path  to be killed by the guards..  These ogres are between level 6 and 9, respectively..   but pack a mean punch..  As treasures,  they always give a Lore item..  their head ;-)  Yup, another quest item.  

Another place in BB zone *butcherblock*  is the Chessboard. This place is really cool look'n!  Yuuup.. looks just like a chessboard.   only it has huuuge Undead Pawns, Bishops, Rooks, Knights and the King..  *skeletons*   Treasure here really stinks..  But..  it is nice exp and about the only place for a level 10 plus, to get decent exp without great fear of death..   it is best to group up here..  as it too is a busy place..  

Another thing you can kill that is worth the effort sometimes.. are Krag Elders..  if it has a weapon..  it just might be bronze  =)   I have gotten several bronze weapons from them..   not magic users,  they just wack away at you and of course.. stun is one side effect..  The Lowland Basilisk are toughies..   so be warned..  they have stone breath and tend to use that stunning spell a LOT..  they bite hard too..  even if it cons a weaker blue to you.. expect a good fight.. but most times you get their tongue as a treasure..  *what is it with dwarves and tongues?*  and that sells for at least one gold =)
