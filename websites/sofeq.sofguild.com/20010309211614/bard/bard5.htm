What Are Friends?

written by SuziJ


What Are Friends?

What are friends for?
To tell secrets and so much more.
A shoulder to catch a tear,
An arm to grab in fear.
An ear that listens through it all,
Someone to catch you when you fall.
That special someone everyone needs,
That doesn’t know the meaning of greed.
They give of themselves so freely,
Not someone sinister or seedy.
They are strong when they have to be,
And tender when it suits to a tee.
What are friends for?
To share with and so much more.
Friends are there, for Gray times and blue,
Friends are forever and true.
They are what makes us all go,
I’m glad you’re the friend I got to know.

 By: SuziJ Gemstone
