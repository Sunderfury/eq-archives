Soldiers of Fortune - Allies
The Soldiers of Fortune officially recognizes the following guilds as comrades in the world of EverQuest. These guilds have shown that they share like minded goals and ideals with SoF.

Each of these guilds has formed a mutual alliance with SoF to further enhance the gaming experience of both guilds and their members. All of the following alliances have been voted upon and ratified as official by the High Council of SoF.
