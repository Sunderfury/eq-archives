Soldiers of Fortune Guild Rank Structure

SoF is a Medieval based RPG Guild.  The Queen is the ultimate authority. The High Council, her advisors.
Be it understood that this is a Monarchy - NOT a democracy.

*This rank structure was revised, voted on, and accepted by the High Council on April 9,2000.*
