Sunday.

02:09 Those were the good old days

I got bored tonight, and decided to log into my old, old stomping grounds: BatMUD. I dunno, I played for an hour or so, mostly catching up on news gone by for the past year, but it wasn't really all that fun. Not like it used to be.

Probably my experiences with EverQuest and the Guide Program therein have sort of jaded me with respect to text-based MUDs.... not to mention that I'm a bit bored with EQ anyway, and have only logged in twice in the past couple months, only playing for maybe an hour once and five minutes the other time.

But it's like reliving part of one's past, logging in there, and here's the fingers to prove it.

The character I created this time was named "Dachannien" (not coincidentally, the same name as my Guide character on EQ and the name I go by on CWRULog). So here's the finger to use as a baseline.

It was created Sun Nov 26 07:00:48 2000 and it is 1h, 40min and 14s old.
It has been on for 1h, 43min and 6s, not idle.
It has killed: A newbie wizard, 356 exp
My most recent character, "Kalay", I played for the bulk of my undergrad career (at least, until EQ came out). In fact, you can see in that character's finger info that EQ was indeed the reason I haven't logged in there in ages.
Kalay Alkayis is a level 32 mortal of the Cromagnon race.
He was created before 1997 and he is 55d, 10h, 49min and 43s old.

That's right, I spent 55 days on this character between January 1995 and May 1999, although by May '99 I had already abandoned him for EQ. That's okay, because I had between 20 and 30 days on my guide character, not counting the immense amount of time spent in weekly meetings and performing administrative tasks for my server.
