A little technical background into the definition of a burrito might also be helpful. So, click on the definition of the burrito from the two choices below.

---1--- a flour tortilla rolled or folded around a filling (as of meat, beans, or cheese) and usu. baked.

---2--- a usu. fried tortilla that is folded or rolled and stuffed with a mixture (as of seasoned meat, cheese, and lettuce).
