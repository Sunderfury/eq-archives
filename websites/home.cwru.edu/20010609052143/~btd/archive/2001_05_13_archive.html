15:53 Whole lotta nuthin

That's pretty much what I've done this week. Nothing. Well, I take that back. I've played a lot of Icewind Dale, and probably by this weekend I'll have finished it (and hence will be writing a review that nobody will read because nobody visits my weblog).

That reminds me. Terence tells me that to get weblog hits you have to jump through a lot of hoops. A whole lot. He says he gets a bunch of hits, but it took him six months and a lot of commentary on other people's lame weblogs to get to that point. What's the point? I mean, when I wrote "Where's My Burrito?" I'd get perhaps one or two e-mails about it a week. I even got links from various other sites that liked it. But it didn't involve me writing up a bunch of blather about how much cooler I am than you because I have a real job working for a web design company or an ISP, which gives me the time to write up the aforementioned blather. (I don't even have a real job.)

So, I'll be writhing in the Pit of Despair, watching my anime and playing my games, while other people discuss Felicity and Friends and interior decorating and Julia Roberts being a great actress, and then wonder aloud why people confuse him for a gay guy.
