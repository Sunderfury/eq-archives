Loot Rules (CURRENT and in process of changing)

All warriors present prior to the first pull of a raid /random to determine loot order for that night. Prior to this roll, and warrior needing 3 pieces or less of indicolite can elect to name an item they would most like to loot (ARMOR).The other warriors then vote, if a majority vote in his/her favor, that person only loots if the piece they selected drops. If the vote is majority against, the warrior in question will random for placement.

The Last Piece rule still applies, with one modification. Many warriors do not need or want certain pieces of indicolite armor. Therefore, any warrior may select 2 pieces of armor that they DO NOT want. These 2 pieces do not count against them when figuring last piece eligibility.

If more than one warrior asks for the same Wish List or Final Piece, they will random roll to decide the order for that piece.

Wish List and Final Piece claims must be made prior to random rolling for Loot list placement. Basically, this is done to avoid the list and last piece rule becoming a means to avoid dealing with an unlucky roll. For example, if someone needing boots, gloves, and sleeves doesnt claim "Wish List/Last Piece" right until after they place last on the random list for the night. Under our system, once you commit to a certain path, you are stuck with it.

Also, once you claim Last Piece rights, you never again loot another piece of armor unless it is rotting on a mob and no other warriors are present to take it.

A single bracer is all that is counted in cases of determining last piece eligibility, unless the warrior in question wants 2 bracers.

Helms will be a random roll for all those that wish to have it. As of this draft, Helm does not count against you as far as loot turn is concerned. Helms are not randomed when the 'wish list' or last piece rules are in effect.

All warriors that join a raid after the first pull are placed at the end of the list in order of arrival. Any warrior needing only one piece who join a raid after the first pull are subject to a 2 hour limit before they can loot, unless the majority of the warriors in the zone agree to waive it.

All Warrior Only God weapons will be distributed by way of Random Roll among all warriors present who have been in the zone for at least 5 hours and plan to take part in the fight against the god in question. No drop weapons are to be randomed for PRIOR to fighting the mob to avoid confusion.

Current policy for Soul Leech and Spined Dragon claws is this: It is up to the leader of the raid in question whether Soul Leech and Spined Dragon Claws will be limited to Shadowknights and Rangers, or if warriors will be allowed to roll for them.

In the Plane of Fear, due to the nature of the place, the warrior looting system will apply UNLESS the raid leader wishes it otherwise with regards to god loots. The raid leader should be followed if you intend to take part in the raid, although unfairness will be noted.

At no time will any warrior be allowed to posess more than one god weapon. If a warrior has bloodfire and wishes to upgrade, they can random for another weapon provided they agree to random bloodfire to the remaining warriors should they win something better. For this to happen, all warriors present must agree.
