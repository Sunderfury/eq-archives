Feerrott Trade Supplies

Companion zone to Oggok, this hunting area has no signs on buildings, so /loc entries and merchant names are all you have to go by.

Baking

  • bread - Innkeep Morga (1139, -134), Innkeep Gub (1140, 1435)
  • spices, vinegar, sauce, garnish, frosting, flour, milk, recipe books - Bup (1171, 122)

Fletching

  • groove nocks (3), bow staff (5), whittling blade, planing tool, standard bow cam, twine, string (2), A Bowyers Guide - Fugla (964, 1256)

Smithing

  • needle & thimble molds - Murga (1139, -134)
  • lg armor molds - Mugu (974, 1239)

Tailoring

  • lg. sewing kit, How to Sew (3), quiver & backpack patterns, Tailoring book - Murga (1139, -134)
