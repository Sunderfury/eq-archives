Qeynos Trade Supplies Locations

City assessment: life is good here for most tradesfolk. The exceptions are smithing and pottery, for which I've not found all items needed. The Qeynos area is the best area yet for those who wish to pursue fletching, spell researchers have materials on hand, and there is an abundance of pelt-bearing animals in the hunting areas for tailors. Bodies of water abound for the fisherfolk, and jewelers and cooks will find their needs well met as well.

Baking

  • flour, mixing bowl, bread, baking spirits - Karn Tassen, Voleen Tassen (Voleen's Fine Baked Goods, S. Qeynos)
  • oven - inside Voleen's Fine Baked Goods (S. Qeynos)

Brewing

  • barley, hops, rice, malt, yeast, wine yeast - Heidi Grainsifter (Voleen's Fine Baked Goods, S. Qeynos)
  • bottle, cask - Fellesha Varshen & Raheim Varshen (Bag N Barrel, S, Qeynos), Basanio Weekin & Gahna Salbeen (street vendors south of Fire Prides, S. Qeynos)
  • bottle, grapes, shot glass, cask - Nug Rellash. Earron Huntlan, Tasya Huntlan (Lions Mane Tavern & Inn, S. Qeynos), Sunsa Jocub, Fish Ranamer (Fish's Ale, S. Qeynos), Sabnie Blagard (Crow's Pub, N. Qeynos)
  • brew barrel - Lions Mane Tavern (S. Qeynos), Crow's Pub (N. Qeynos), Fish's Ale (S. Qeynos)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Captain Rohand (Mermaid's Lure, S. Qeynos), Tanlyn Galliway, Sneed Galliway (Galliway's Trading Post, N. Qeynos)

Fletching

  • groove nocks, fletching kit - Danon Fletcher (behind Nestiff's Wooden Weapons, S. Qeynos)
  • (more major fletching supplies in Surefall Glade)

Jewelcraft

  • silver/electrum/gold/platinum bars, Legacy of Jewelcraft, jewelers kit, star ruby, fire emerald, sapphire - Ziska Ironforge (The Jewel Box, N. Qeynos)
  • 5 gemstones - Hanlore Escaval (The Herb Jar, S. Qeynos)
  • 18 gemstones, jewelers kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Svena Ironforge (The Jewel Box, N. Qeynos)

Pottery

  • kiln, pottery wheel - inside Bag N Barrel, (S. Qeynos)

Smithing

  • forge - outside Tin Soldier (S. Qeynos), outside Fire Prides (S. Qeynos), behind Ironforge's (N. Qeynos)
  • (molds found in nearby Qeynos Hills)

Spell Research

  • quill, parchment, various tomes - Hanlore Escaval (The Herb Jar, S. Qeynos)

Tailoring

  • patterns, sm. sewing kit - Fhara Semhart (Fhara's Leather & Thread, S. Qeynos)
  • sm. sewing kit, quiver & backpack patterns - Iala Lendard (street vendor outside Lions Mane Inn, S. Qwynos)
  • sm. sewing kit - Solani Dayadil (street vendor south of Fire Prides, S. Qeynos)
