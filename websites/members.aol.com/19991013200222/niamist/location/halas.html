Halas Trade Supplies

City evaluation: Cooking, fishing, pottery and tailoring are great here, with the caveats listed below. Smithing seriously needs imported goods from other lands as does fletching, and you can totally forget about becoming a jeweler without relocating, or taking repeated shopping trips. Many thanks to Lucian on the Bristlebane server for locating a few "missing" items for me.

Caveats: Many of the cooking recipes that are "common" recipes to start with in other lands are simply not feasible in barbarian lands -- there are no bats, rats, fire beetles and the like to give up the needed pieces/parts for things such as "Edible Goo", "Batwing Crunchies" and "Fish Rolls."  That being said, there are other things you can make, with a bit of financial output.  Tailoring is great, with quite an abundance of wolf and bear pelts to practice on -- however, since advanced tailoring needs items made from a smith, and/or higher quality pelts than are available in the area, it will require some imported goods.
