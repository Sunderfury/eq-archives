Rivervale Trade Supplies

City evaluation: Fishing, basic tailoring, and basic cooking can be performed by those who live in Rivervale and travel to the next zone, Misty Thicket.  All other trade skill materials are either incomplete or nonexistant.  As a halfling, you must either travel or hire someone to bring back materials in order to perform other trade skills.

Baking

  • oven - /loc -188.69, -157.58
  • (cooking supplies in Misty Thicket)

Brewing

  • brew barrel - next to Meeka Diggs (near Fools Gold), on second floor of Fools Gold
  • bottle - Wibble Bramblebush, next to dock

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Fiddy Bobbick and Wibble Bramblebush, next to dock

Fletching, Jewelcraft

  • none found

Pottery

  • pottery wheel - /loc -159.08, -77.75
  • clay and other supplies in Misty Thicket
  • pottery wheel & kiln - /loc 391.65, -468.49 (near deputy Kapop)

Smithing

  • forge - next to druid's guild
  • armor molds in Misty Thicket
  • none of the other smithing materials, including the basic metal and file molds are to be found

Tailoring

  • armor patterns, small sewing kit - Twippie Diggs (near Fools Gold)
  • small sewing kit - Meeka Diggs (also near Fools Gold)
