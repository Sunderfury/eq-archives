Alchemy - by Sharanna (Veeshan Server)

The elder shaman (level 25 or above) has the ability, if he/she cares to exercise it, to make many magical potions. While the skill is still new to the lands, and hence prone to various quirks and such, there is high hope for it. Each potion requires that the shaman mix two or more items in a medicine bag. A legendary shaman by the name of Jonas has had 5 volumes of his Letters published in certain spots, that outlines the ingredients needed for each potion. These letters are generally available in the same cities as carry the medicine bag and some of the needed herbs. Many of the components needed for this skill, however, must be scavenged from creatures in the wilds.

Shamen who are interested in additional information, including "recipes" are encouraged to visit the Shaman's Crucible site
