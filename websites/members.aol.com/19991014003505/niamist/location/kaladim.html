Kaladim Trade Supplies

City Evaluation: Trade skills appear to be excellent in this area. In this and the nearby Butcherblock zone, you can find the only known book on brewing, plus one of the few locations for smithing ore. The only thing I have NOT found are the ingredients needed for brewing. All other trade skills look good.

(See also Butcherblock Mountains listing for items in the next zone)

Baking

  • oven - outside Gurtha's Ware (S. Kaladim), outside Greybloom Farm (N. Kaladim)
  • (other supplies in Butcherblock)

Brewing

  • Brewers Limericks (book), cask - Dura Darkfoam, Hanamaf Darkfoam (Pub Kal, S. Kaladim)
  • bottle, cask, grapes - Tumpy Irontoe (Irontoe's Eats, S. Kaladim)
  • bottle, cask - Aarina Ratsbone (unmarked bldg., east section, S. Kaladim)
  • brew barrel (Pub Kal, S. Kaladim), inside Greybloom Farm (N. Kaladim)
  • grapes - Tisin Greybloom, Serenk Greybloom (Greybloom Farm, N. Kaladim)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Doranafi Ratsbone (street merchant near cleric guild, N. Kaladim)

Fletching

  • groove nocks (3), fletching kit - Alanury Stormhammer (Staff & Spear, S. Kaladim)

Jewelcraft

  • silver/electrum/gold/platinum bars, Legacy of Jewelcraft, jewelers kit, star ruby, fire emerald, sapphire - Aanina Rockfinder (Everhot Forge, N. Kaladim)
  • 18 gems, jeweler's kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Bonaf Norkhitter (inside mine, N. Kaladim)

Pottery

  • blocks of clay (3), firing sheets (3), glass shard, Basic Pottery book, vial sketches (3) - Dooni (Gurtha's Ware, S. Kaladim)
  • pottery wheel, kiln - beside bridge, east section of S. Kaladim
  • pottery wheel - near mine entrance, N. Kaladim
  • kiln - near bank, N. Kaladim

Smithing

  • forge - outside Redfists Metal (S. Kaladim), outside Everhot Forge (N. Kaladim)
  • (molds found out in nearby Butcherblock, ore in Steamfont Mountains)

Tailoring

  • patterns, small sewing kit - Kalameky Darkfoam (Tanned Assets, S. Kaladim)
  • small sewing kit - Gurtha Yaptongue (Gurtha's Ware, S. Kaladim)
