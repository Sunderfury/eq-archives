Kithicor Forest Trade Supplies

Zone analysis: this is one of the through-routes to get from Freeport to Qeynos and/or from Rivervale to the rest of the world.  Most found merchants are along/near the road, and as long as you stick to the path, you should be safe shopping -- not that there is a lot here, but one never knows ...

  • balm leaves, elf leaf, larkspur, dragonwort - Elsbyth (-768. 3081)

Baking

Brewing

Tinkering
