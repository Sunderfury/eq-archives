South Karana Trade Supplies

Zone evaluation: A bowyer's paradise, and not much more than that. There's also an aviak merchant at the top of the aviak village that sells foodstuffs, bandages and throwing weapons. However, unless you're a dwarf, or have sneak skill and can trade with him from behind, he won't deal with you.
