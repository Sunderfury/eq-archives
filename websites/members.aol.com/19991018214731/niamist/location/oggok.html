Oggok Trade Supplies

(See companion area Feerrott for items available in the next zone.)

City Evalaution: Baking and Fishing are the only two skills that can be performed with travel only between Oggok and Feerott.  However, travel to the neighboring city of Grobb allows you to also try out Brewing, Jewelcraft and Tailoring. Fletching is not a recommended skill for any of the evil races unless all you want to make is bows, as arrow-making supplies are only found in areas where you will be KOS.  Pottery will require travel to multiple zones (Grobb has clay, but you need to go to Neriak for firing sheets, and to Oasis for sketches). Not enough is known about the shaman alchemy skill to know if more items are needed than what is included here. Note that neighbor zone Feerrott has one of the best collections of items for bowmaking that I've seen outside of Surefall Glade.   My thanks go to Krobuu of E'Ci for some corrections and additions to the below information.

Alchemy

  • various herbs, medicine bag- Garakbar (unlabeled room with skull floating in the middle of it)
  • medicine bag, Letters of Jonas (volumes 1-5) - Rora Dirtstamp (unlabeled room with skull floating in the middle of it)

Baking

  • bread - Chef Dooga (Meet and Drink Tew Bie), Crunga & Erung (unmarked shop down hall from armor merchants) - note that Dooga will KOS non-ogres, so beware
  • oven - next to Crunga & Erung in unmarked shop down hall from armor merchants
  • (main cooking ingredients in Feerrott)

Brewing

  • cask, bottle - Brokk Boxtripper (near Priest of Discord)
  • bottle - Angrog (next to tunnel leading to Feerott)
  • brew barrel - next to Clurg (Meet and Drink Tew Bie)
  • (main brewing ingredients in Grobb)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Angrog (next to tunnel leading to Feerott), Sinsaal (inside first tunnel on left in the wall of tunnels)

Fletching

  • groove nocks, fletching kit - Praak (Bouncer Keep)
  • (main bowmaking supplies in Feerrott)

Jewelcraft

  • peridot - Hrak (entry to shaman guild)

Pottery

  • pottery wheel, kiln - inside first tunnel on left (facing the wall of tunnels)

Smithing

  • forge - inside Metil Armer
  • (molds in Feerrott)

Tailoring

  • patterns, small sewing kit, How To Sew (3) - Juk Hidebeater (Lether Armer)
  • small sewing kit, How To Sew (large size only) - Duuga Tearstopper (Lether Armer)
  • (lg sewing kit in Feerrott)
