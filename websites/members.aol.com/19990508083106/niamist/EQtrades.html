Hail and well met, citizens of Norrath!  EverQuest is a role playing world rich with variety and details. One of my favorite aspects of it is trade skills. Not only can you customize your character with them, but these skills provide extra opportunities for roleplaying ... and in-game cash.

Do you want to learn a trade skill?  Do you want to sell the fruits of your labor to other players?  Are you trying to find specific items to pratice your craft? Or are you just trying to find someone who sells player-crafted items for a reasonable price on your server? If the answer to any of these questions is "yes", then I hope this site will be of assistance to you. As the game changes and grows, so too will this site, so remember to check back often.
