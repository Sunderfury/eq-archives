Here are a few of my favorite EQ links. More sites will be added as I have more time to explore

General links:

  • www.everquest.com - If you're totally new to EverQuest, you'll want to visit the main Sony site for the game for good background information, hardware requirements, etc.

  • www.eqvault.com - This is a great place to see current EQ server status, when you can reach the site (they've had some server problems of their own). They're great on posting the most recent news about the game, player guides, maps and more. Their message board may be slow, but it's quite active, and a good source for information.

  • eq.stratics.com - This is the best site I've seen yet for depth of coverage on a myriad of topics, up-to-date news, and more.

  • Everguide - a bit of news, some guides, some stories ... this site is quickly growing. I can't wait to see what they're like once they're up to full staff!

  • EQ'lizer - chock full of information on quests, abilities and much, much more. Some would call these cheats, so if you want to learn things the hard way, don't visit this site.

  • EverCrest - a semi-daily comic strip that any die-hard EQ'er can empathize with

  • EverQuest Map Shop - some amazing maps of the various zones. While the majority of the site is Japanese, it is a must visit for anyone looking for good maps.

Specialized links:

  • Spiritwalkers Guild - located on Veeshan and Tunare, we're expecting this to be a great guild for roleplayers.
  • Everquest Monks - containing information specific to monks, I hit this site several times a week to participate in their message board discussions
  • Everquest Enchanters - great information on spell research for enchanters, plus, more importanly for those who have been begging me to provide "recipes" for jewelry, a listing of jewelry combinations
  • The Shaman's Crucible - includes a lot of stuff for shamen, including some hot-off-the press information on alchemy
  • EQ Research Study - again, a site that provides "recipes" for spell research, among other things. They also have some very active message boards for those trying to get niggling questions answered, trade words for runes, etc.
  • EQ Trade Skill Guide - Tinkering - gnomes, be not dismayed with the tinkering skill. This site contains in-depth information on what currently works, and how to make it. This is the site that spurred me to put up a tinkering page, and start listing out items that I'd ignored before.
  • Oggok - The Ogre City has much information of interested to all you misunderstood ogres out there.
