Felwithe

City evaluation: Fishing, jewelcraft and tailoring look to be readily do-able. Basic fletching is also possible. Baking does not seem readily do-able, other than edible goo and fish rolls, unless someone finds a vendor who sells spices and such. Smithing, pottery and brewing seem like bad ideas for a high elf unless you're willing to travel a lot. 

Baking

  • oven - next to Bait & Tackle (N. Felwithe)

Brewing

  • cask, bottle - Merchant Issynal (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe)
  • cask - Merchant Muselender & Barkeep Songwhisper (Tovaniks Venom, N. Felwithe)
  • brew barrel - basement of Tovaniks Venom (N. Felwithe)

Fishing

  • fishing pole, bait - Rolyn Longwalker (Beyond Faydark, N. Felwithe), Merchant Sorintal (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe), Fishmonger, Jassa (Bait & Tackle, N. Felwithe)

Fletching

  • groove nocks, fletching kit - Merchant Longarrow (Faydark's Bane, N. Felwithe)
  • (many more fletching items in Kelethin)

Jewelcraft

  • 18 gems, jewelers kit, Legacy of Jewelcraft - Merchant Tyslin (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe -- also same exact merchant located in building just inside entrance to S. Felwithe)
  • metal bars (silver, electrum, gold, platinum), Legacy of Jewelcraft, jewelers kit, star ruby, fire emerald, sapphire - Merchant Silspin (next to Bait & Tackle, N. Felwithe)
  • some gems - Merchant Lyssia (Enchanters Guild, S. Felwithe)

Pottery

  • pottery wheel, kiln - behind Faydark's Bane

Smithing

  • none found (molds and books in Kelethin)

Tailoring

  • patterns, sm. sewing kit - Merchant Tissan (Faydark's Bane, N. Felwithe)
  • sm. sewing kit - Merchant Silvenspin (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe), Merchant Moonthread (building just inside entrance to S. Felwithe)
  • (large sewing kit in Kelethin)
