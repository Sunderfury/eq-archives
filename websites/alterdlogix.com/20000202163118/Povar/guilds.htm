Guild Name: Circle of Marr Home Page: Circle of Marr GM: Kaylis
The Followers of Mithaniel Marr believe that valor is what separates civilized beings from the beasts. His followers live by a strict moral code that prizes truth, honor, and charity. We believe in giving and helping all that we can and not charging people with gifts of SOW, Buffs, unless willingly donated by the person, then that is up to the discretion of the people involved. More and more women are joining in this facinating game and it is also our goal to help them along as well to make the gaming experince a good one.

Guild Name: The Faded Home Page: The Faded GM: Aquamoon
Creed: The Faded represents a union of many races and classes who seek to maintain the balance between light and dark in the world. The members of this group put aside their differences in race and religion in order to seek the peace and safety that comes from unity across the world. Recognizing that fanatics in the name of light represent as great a threat to this order as the widespread rule of darkness, The Faded will intervene in matters to prevent either the light or the darkness from gaining too much power, but most often we will neither aid nor oppose either side, preferring to remain aloof from the conflict.

Guild Name: Blades of Law Home Page: Blades of Law GM: Caoimhin Hammerhand

Summary:  Our guild is driven by the principles of the four blades: the dagger (loyalty), sword (honor), axe(justice), and scythe (duty). We exist to return the world of Norrath to a state of harmony. For too long the powerful of the world, both good and evil, have recklessly and ruthlessly dominated our lands. All races and classes are welcome, but racial hatred is not tolerated. We as Blades realize that good and evil will always exist in Norrath, and that all have their place as opposing and balancing forces.

Guild Name: Nighthawks Home Page: Nighthawks GM: Jamilyn Skyheart
The Nighthawks are a role-playing guild. We are a group of people that seek to help others in need, despite what the cost may be to ourselves. We do not seek to be the greatest, the largest, or the most well-known guild of Norrath. We do seek, however, to serve the causes of good, and eliminate evil; to aid those in need; to ensure that darkness does not have a final
victory.

We are not exclusive. Though we are a role-playing guild, role-playing is not mandatory. What is mandatory is that each Nighthawk serves the common interest of good, in his or her own way, and that they do not just help others when put on the spot, but seek to be of help when possible. They must be loyal to their fellow Nighthawks, and treat them each as a brother or sister. There is no guild fee, no quota. All that we ask is that you have the heart of a hawk -- the Nighthawk.

 

Guild Name: Symphony of Darkness Home Page: Symphony of Darkness GM: Soliel Delahaine
A day is a few hours of turmoil. A man can experience events in these hours of tumult that forever Change him. For better or worse, dawn always comes to a man, beckoning a new day. Today was another day, yet something different occurred. Something, one can not touch with words. Something profound (I hesitate to say such for I know not yet whether this will Change my life or those of others).

Dawn would not show her face; rather she was cloaked in rain. If a man wishes to seek something in himself, he only must look into the rain. Something enigmatic about the soul always becomes clearer in the droplets. You can look at the rain, stare into it and peer at your reflection and watch it crash into the ground and shatter. Your reflection shattered. Cracked. Each time your reflection will destroy itself and Change direction. Nature itself was Chaotic. Chaos. Change.

There is something that you can hear in the thunder, a message mayhap. The sound Changes. Change. The message of thunder signals something to everyone. If one walks the roads or passages during a tempest, a sight not known to anyone can be seen. Total darkness. Then light. Darkness, in its most violent form, breeds light. Nature has poetic justice.

The panther that live in the wilds of Norrath, wakes in the darkness. His eyes glisten before he kills. Light can be found, even in the predator's eyes; eye's that can lead into the creature's soul. Before he commits the. Chaotic. event the light of his soul glows brightly.

Destiny is a thing of Chaos. It is a myopic view to think that destiny is to be followed. For like nature, one's destiny is a thing of Chaos and Change. One must mould Fate to his needs, and only then can he claim mastery of the forces of Change.

Listen to the wind, can you hear the sounds of nature, of chaotic whistling? Do you hear the sounds of discord? A symphony in the darkness, can you hear it calling? It is asking you to join it...

 

Guild Name: Symphony of Light Home Page: Symphony of Ligh GM: Aesculapius Lux

BE IT SO UNDERSTOOD, that by the Joint and Plentiful powers of the undersigned, this Charter does hereby establish and empower the fellowship guild henceforth known as  The Symphony of Light; And further that this aforesaid guild has been formed to promote the principles and beliefs of the more enlightened races of   Norrath. These beliefs are founded upon six primary principles, which are held dear to all members. Honor, Justice, Valor, Love, Harmony and Liberty.  These principles shall drive the lives of all members and transcend our racial and religious differences, to provide members a basis on which they may work together to ensconce these principals in all peoples of the enlightened races.

In our understanding of the limited capacities of the lesser races, it shall be a goal of this fellowship to liberate whatever lands might be in the control of said lesser races, and to exterminate their infectious pestilence from all the lands of Norrath. 

Further, it shall be a goal of this fellowship to seek out and destroy all traces of the black art of necromancy and the foul heresy it has spawned throughout the lands. 

Further it shall be a goal of this fellowship to go forth into the lands to discover the secreted artifacts of power of ages before, and to secure that power to aid in our ongoing struggle with the darkness. 

Our Sigil shall be a beacon of hope to all in need of aid, and a symbol of great fear to our enemies. All shall know when in the presence of the Light, as even a child knows when the sun breaks the horizon, scattering the darkness before it, that the peoples and principles of Norrath shall be preserved.
