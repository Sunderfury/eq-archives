I have been involved in dog rescue for many years now. I placed ex-racing greyhounds in homes, and I have often found a stray on the street and found a home for it. Even friends and relatives have been known to drop off a dog to me so that I'll find it a happy place to live. This page has pictures of the dogs and cats I have fostered and a little story as to his or her personality. Currently I foster for CAWS, and I am very grateful for everything they do for me. Please come back often to see how the fostering page is going. Thanks!


The above picture is of Tucker. Tucker is a smooth tri colored collie who is up for adoption as of June 2000. He is a very sweet boy though has most likely been abused. He is very shy and afraid, but is very loving and likes attention.


Tucker was found in Ogden in a hotel parking lot. He now has all his shots and has been neutered. I hope he will find a loving, great home.
