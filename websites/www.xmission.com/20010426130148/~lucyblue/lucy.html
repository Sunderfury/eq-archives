Hi, I'm Lucy.
My full name is Elsinore Lucilla Aurelius, and I hope to be a champion one day. I am a smooth blue collie and I was born on February 15, 1999. I flew to my new home in Utah from the Chicago O'hare airport. It was rather scary, and when my new Mom picked me up I was shaking and didn't want to come out of my safe crate. But my new home was nice, I now live with Kip and Levi. Kip teaches me lots of stuff, like using the dog door and going up and down the stairs in the back yard. I like to pick up all the fun things at home, but my Mom says I can't carry them all around. She wants me to play with only things she calls 'toys,' but I can't figure out which are toys and which are not. I'm sure I will soon, but in the mean time I'll just try everything. Levi is my new little brother, he was born in November 1999, and is lots smaller than me, but I like to wrestle with him!

My Mom took me to puppy kindergarten, and it was fun. I got to play with lots of other dogs. Then she gave me treats to make me do funny things, but it's not so bad as long as I get the treat!

Here are some pictures of me. My Mom will probably take more and make them look better in the future. But for now she likes to play with me in the yard and in the house, and take me and Kip for walks. It's fun. She says when I'm older she'll take me to agility and obedience classes. Those sound like fun, too.

I am growing up pretty fast, and Mom is surprised at how big I am. These pictures were taken in September 1999, I am 7 months old. I love to sleep on the couch!

This is me when I was three months old, and Mom let me jump on the bed. Now I get to sleep on the bed with her.
Here I am on May 13, 1999. Staring at the camera.
I like to lie on the bed with my head hanging off the side.
