Herm Franks Park

Herm Franks Park is located at 1300 South and between 700 East and 800 East. During the winter months many people take their dogs after work to this park to play. Please note, the park is not fully fenced and there is a great deal of traffic that travels past.

However, for the careful dog owner and the well behaved dog, the park is a great area for people and dogs to socialize and run around and play. The park has a few baseball diamonds, and I have been told that when people are playing, they would prefer dogs going to some of the other parks.

Lucy and Kip had a great time when we visited. However, I was nervous about the traffic and lack of fencing, for they could get distracted if I'm not careful. Levi was too little to do much dog socializing, so he waited for us in the truck. He wasn't too happy about it, though.

The park has a nice central location and many people have a good time visiting. Enjoy!

PLEASE NOTE:
As responsible dog owners we must preserve the privileges we have with our dogs. To do so we must follow some basic rules:
Always pick up after your dog.
Always make sure your dog responds to voice commands.
Always make sure your dog is not aggressive towards other dogs or people.
