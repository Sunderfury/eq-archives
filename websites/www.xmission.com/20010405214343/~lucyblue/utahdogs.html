Welcome to Utah Dogs.

This web site contains links to information regarding dogs and their people in Utah. Included are off-lead areas, places to camp, links to rescue organizations, clubs, and other interesting information.

Please visit the Utah Dogs Web Forum for interactive information about dogs and their people in Utah. It will only work if you post!

This site currently has limited information, but does cover much of the current off-lead areas open to those who live in Salt Lake City and the surrounding areas.

This site is going through design changes. It was last updated on 15 September 2000. In the next few weeks all the pages will be changed to reflect the design of the first page.

The webmistress encourages anyone who has pictures or stories to please Email them. Thank you.
