Ilsik Haucil Charter

The rules of Ilsik Haucil are simple in design; however at times they can be difficult to follow. Such is the burden of all true moralities.

  1. Treat all members of Ilsik Haucil as family, with respect. Ilsik Haucil was formed around several groups of friends who became a large family of friends. Each new addition to our family is tested to ensure that they will be a welcome member of the house.

  2. Decisions that affect the entire house will be decided by vote. All members will be notified of the proposal and asked to vote within a set number of days. The majority decides. Invitations and promotions are a vote for the officers, as are rewards. The only time the Speaker can vote is when filling in for an absent leader.

  3. Members shall treat all players with dignity and respect , unless a player acts in a manner that earns our enmity. That player will then be ignored or shunned.

Player limit:
Ilsik Haucil has a player cap. There are two important reasons for this cap.

  1. To try to keep guildwars fair with our sister-guild, the Dark Alliance, who has the same player cap. This cap will be raised; however, the player cap will never exceed 50. This player cap is for players, not characters.

  2. To keep the house a tight-knit group of friends, a house where everyone knows your name. Is this elitist? Perhaps, but it also makes for an exceptional house.

Ilsik Haucil is only as good as its members. Its honor and renown are the sum of the whole, and both are held high in the realm of Mithaniel Marr.

Valkith avu Winden Haucil, First Speaker for House Ilsik Haucil
