Millcreek Canyon
3800 South Wasatch Blvd

Millcreek Canyon is offically off lead only on Odd Numbered Days. During these days you can take your dog up the canyon and let him loose on the various trails.

In the Winter time the Canyon is closed about half way up, and you and your dog can walk up the snow packed road. You may be sharing the road with skiers and snow shoers so please be considerate. During the Summer months you can drive all the way to the top and hike trails such as the one going to Dog Lake.

There is a fee to go up the canyon.. I believe it is $2.25 as of this writing though may change in the future.
