Red Butte Gardens

My Sister and I visited Red Butte Gardens one Saturday afternoon in March, and what did I discover there? Many people taking their dogs along a nice path up the mountainside... off lead. So, later that same day I piled Lucy and Levi into my Toyota Pickup and we headed back for some pictures.

There were more bikers there, and walkers, than dogs when we returned, but it was a nice day. The entrance to the Gardens is close by, but there is a packed dirt path close by that people walk with their dogs. The path heads to the south of the entrance.

Red Butte Gardens is located nearby the University of Utah. Taking Wakura Lane to the east you will find the road winds around a bit and then you will find the gates to Red Butte Gardens. In the summer the Gardens host the Summer Concert Series. It is a beautiful place to watch nature.

There are many trails which snake up the hillsides around the Gardens. On a nice day there are many people, bikers, walkers, and joggers about, as well as a number of dogs.
