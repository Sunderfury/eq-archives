Memory Grove
Near Capitol Building

Memory Grove is an official off lead area located near the State Capitol Building. There is a nice trail which parallels a stream. Dogs are not supposed to walk in the water, though. ;)

This is a nice easy trail which climbs a little ways up the side of the mountain behind the Capitol. During the summer there can be found dogs during the day, sometimes during lunch time. There are bags near the beginning and ending of the trails for people to pick up after their dogs.
