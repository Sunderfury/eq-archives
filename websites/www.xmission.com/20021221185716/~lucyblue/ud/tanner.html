Parley's Nature Preserve
(Tanner Park)
Not Officially Off Leash!
2600 East Heritage Way (2700 S)

Parley's Nature Preserve is not officially an off leash area. Many people bring their dogs down the gully located where I-80 and I-215 meet. This is an excellent place to bring your dog and I do hope it will become an official off leash area. (Please take note that at certain times of the year you may be subject to a ticket if you let your dog run off leash)

Parley's Nature Preserve has a nice stream running along the full path going down into the gully. Many dogs and people can be found splashing in the water. Dogs love to run through the bushes and meet other dogs. This is a very popular off leash area and you will most likely find many other dogs and people especially on the weekends.
