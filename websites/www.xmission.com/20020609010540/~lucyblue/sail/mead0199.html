Lake Mead, Nevada
October 16, 1999 through October 23, 1999

Vessel:
Moon Shine - Catalina 22

Crew:
Cynthia Sorensen, David Oswald

[ 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 ]

On Saturday, October 16, Dave and I hopped into my 1990 Toyota pickup and started our journey to Lake Mead, Nevada. We left Salt Lake City, Utah, at 9:00 a.m. and drove all day until we reached the lake. These pages will be a record of our trip, a map showing where we went, and pictures of our adventures. On this page there are pictures of Cathederal Bay, the bay we stayed in the first and last nights of our trip. There will be more to come.

In addition to sailing around on Moon Shine, we also utilized the Sea Cruiser 2000 for various difficult tasks. It is a sturdy little boat and carried anchors and people well. The picture on the above left is Dave laboriously rowing to shore and checking the anchor lines.

The above pictures are from Lake Mead, but there will be more to come.
