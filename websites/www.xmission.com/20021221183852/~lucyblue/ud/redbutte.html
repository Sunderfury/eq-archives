Red Butte Gardens
University of Utah

Red Butte Gardens is located near the University of Utah. Going to visit the Gardens you will see a trail heading off to the south. Following this trail is a spiderweb of paths that go up the mountains.

Many visitors to the UofU and also SLC travel up to see Red Butte Gardens. Please keep your dog under control if you visit this area. There will be many hikers walking beside you. Also please clean up after your dogs as this area gets messy fast.
