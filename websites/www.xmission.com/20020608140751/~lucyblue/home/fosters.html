I have been involved in dog rescue for many years now. I placed ex-racing greyhounds in homes, and I have often found a stray on the street and found a home for it. Even friends and relatives have been known to drop off a dog to me so that I'll find it a happy place to live. This page has pictures of the dogs and cats I have fostered and a little story as to his or her personality. Currently I foster for CAWS, and I am very grateful for everything they do for me. Please come back often to see how the fostering page is going. Thanks!



Sydney

Sydney is a shy, cute, Shepherd Mix. I think she has some Rottweiler in her, too. I got Sydney on February 13th, 2002, as the person fostering her took a 3 week vacation. She is very sweet and is getting along with Lucy and Levi great! I hope she finds a good home soon.



Rosie

Rosie is a red healer mix I fostered in the summer of 2001. Rosie had so much energy she tired Lucy and Levi out. She will need a home who can keep up with her, and who also has the time to work with her. She is very sweet and loving and will follow anyone from room to room.



Tucker

The above pictures are of Tucker. Tucker is a smooth tri colored collie who was up for adoption in June 2000. He is a very sweet boy though has most likely been abused. He is very shy and afraid, but is very loving and likes attention. Tucker was found in Ogden in a hotel parking lot. He now has all his shots and has been neutered. Tucker found a wonderful home with a loving family, and is now very happy!



Bria

This is a Bria, she is a lab mix, with one blue eye and one brown. She taught Lucy how much fun barking is... but not fun for the people. :) She was born in about March of 1999. She is a very sweet girl who I fostered in August of 1999. She was adopted by a very loving couple and lives in a fun new home.
