Lake Mead
October 10, 1998 through October 18, 1998

Page Four

We camped in our tent again on Friday night after stepping the mast down and trailering the Ghost in the dark. It went very well once again.

We left on Saturday morning and drove for 11 hours to get home. We went slow, making sure the Grey Ghost was secure on the trailer. We travelled well and were all feeling much better. The vacation was much too short, as vacations always are.

The boat's shadow kept scaring us on the drive home... why is that boat following us? It was a great trip, maybe next year we can get more sailboats to come with.
