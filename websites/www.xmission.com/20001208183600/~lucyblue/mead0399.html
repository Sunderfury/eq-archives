On Saturday, October 16, Dave and I hopped into my 1990 Toyota pickup and started our journey to Lake Mead, Nevada. We left Salt Lake City, Utah, at 9:00 a.m. and drove all day until we reached the lake. These pages will be a record of our trip, a map showing where we went, and pictures of our adventures. These pages will be set up in the next couple of weeks as I get time and as I get the pictures developed. Please come back and check these pages out! For now there are only a couple of old pictures here to hold my place so I can remember how to code the HTML!

The above pictures are from San Francisco and are temporary page holders until I get pictures of Lake Mead on here.
