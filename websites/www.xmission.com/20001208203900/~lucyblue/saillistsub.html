The Utah-Sailing Email List


Utah-Sailing is a discussion group (emailing list) dedicated to the sport of sailing in Utah.
If you're an enthusiast we'd love to have you as a participant.

Here's how the list works. People post a message to the list by emailing utah-sailing@lists.xmission.com. That message goes out to all members (ie. subscribers) of the list. Members can then respond to the message by also posting to the list. The result is an email based discussion group focusing on sailing in Utah. Join the list and listen in or participate in the discussion!

Utah Sailing Mailing List Info

Once you have submitted your subscription request, you will be emailed a confirmation notice. You must follow the instructions in that notice to complete your list registration. This extra hurdle is intended to eliminate prank and other erroneous subscriptions.

You may post to the group by sending email to utah-sailing@lists.xmission.com after your subscription is complete.

If you have questions regarding the Utah Sailing email list, send email to lucyblue@softhome.net

Note: Your name and email address are stored quite securely on XMission's email-list server. The list's administrator is the only person who has access to these addresses. As the list's administrator, I can guarantee that your email address will not be exploited in any way including for use by spammers as a result of subscribing to this email list.
