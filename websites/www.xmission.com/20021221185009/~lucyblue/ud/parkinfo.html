Off Leash Park Information

When taking your dog to an off leash area please make sure you pick up after your dog and are courteous to non-dog people who you and your companions meet along the way.

For more information regarding off leash areas in Millcreek Canyon please visit Millcreek Fidos. For links to past newspaper articles regarding Millcreek Canyon issues, including off leash dog days, please visit Save Our Canyons Millcreek Archive.

For excellent rules regarding off-lead areas, please visit Logan Dogs Rules Page, and for tips please visit Logan Dogs Tips Page.
