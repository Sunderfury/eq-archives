San Francisco Bay
March 13, 1999 through March 20, 1999

Vessel:
Grey Ghost - Ranger 22

More pictures of Angel Island above. Cynthia wanted to buy the island and make a home for ourselves, but she weren't sure it was for sale... nor the price. It is probably a little above her price range.

Above left, Cynthia sailing on the Ghost, wanting to get some sun but finding overcast skies instead. There was not much sun on the trip, but we bundled up in 5 layers of clothes and wore our life jackets too, we were warm enough. Aaron, above right, is relaxing against the mast.

All good things must come to an end. Trailering the Ghost was pretty easy, we are getting quite good at it since taking it both to San Francisco Bay and Lake Mead.

We hope to go on many more sailing vacations. The more practice, the better we'll get. In addition to sailing on the Great Salt Lake this summer we hope to plan a charter sometime in the next few years to a warm place like the Carribean or New Zealand. Anyone up for the ride?

The above picture was taken in Saint George when we returned from Lake Mead. It shows a good perspective of how large the Grey Ghost is compared to Cynthia's little 1990 Toyota Pickup. She's a good truck, though, and pulls the boat with no problem.
