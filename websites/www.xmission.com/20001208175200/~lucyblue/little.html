Little's Home Page

Hi, I'm Little. I came to live with my Mom, Kip, Lucy, and Levi in January 2000. The person who used to have me thought I was a calico siamese mix. But after my Mom did some browsing on the web, she thinks I'm mostly Ragdoll. But she's not sure which. It doesn't matter, though. I like to follow her around and I like to meow a lot. The dogs are okay, but I'm not too sure about them yet. They just want to play and I'd rather snuggle.

I spend most of my time in the kitchen, separated from the dogs by a gate. I get the kitchen and the downstairs, while the dogs look at me funny. But I keep them away. Sometimes, when I'm feeling brave, I'll sleep on the couch while they are walking around, but I'm not too sure about them yet.
My Mom's never had a cat before, so I'm showing her all the ropes. She feeds me canned food but sometimes I don't like the kind she gives me. I just don't it eat, and she's pretty good at giving me some better stuff. I like the kitchen table, and I'm usually either on top of it or snuggled below on one of the chairs.
