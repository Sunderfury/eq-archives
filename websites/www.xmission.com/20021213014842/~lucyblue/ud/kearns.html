Atherton Park
4500 South 815 West

Atherton Park is a fully fenced dog park. In the summer it has running water and plastic kiddy pools in which dogs can play. There are some benches you can sit on to watch the dogs run around.

During rainstorms and after thaws the dirt ground can get somewhat muddy, but this doesn't usually detract from the fun the dogs have running around. There is a double-fenced gate to enter and exit the park so dogs are prevented from escaping onto the street. There is parking near the park, and easy access from 4500 South.
