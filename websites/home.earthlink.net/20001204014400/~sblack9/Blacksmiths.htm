Welcome to the Smiths of Norrath homepage!

From here you will be able to place orders for all your banded needs. Please review the price lists and check for special deals on your server by clicking the appropriate server name.

Please note that this site is still under construction. At present our supply of smiths is relatively low but we are growing daily. If you are a smith capable of making banded and you'd like to join us than please send us an e-mail.

News

11/3/99

Sorry for the lack of updates everyone. I've been quite busy.

I've gone through and added a few new smiths to Brell and Karana servers and fixed a few bad e-mails here and there with this last update.

What I really need to make note of is the change to the Price List. The Price List is no longer a static list and is instead a suggested retail price that is given in a range. I simply ask that no smith in the union go beyond either the minimum or maximum value set. Another point of note for the Pricing List is that I have made a note that smiths who receive orders via e-mail should give a complementary 20% discount to all orders. Now of course if you are already at the bottom of the price range with your banded than I am not sure how willing you are going to be to give an addition 20% discount for your wares...with that said the discount is up to the individual smith. I personally will offer a 20% discount for e-mail orders just because they are far easier for me to fill. The price ranges should allow for smiths on older servers to stay competitive in the market.

I must make a quick note concerning banded gauntlet molds. They can now be found on all vendors that have banded armor molds and are now craftable in all sizes. Thanks goes out to all smiths who sent me information concerning this change.

Good luck with your smithing everyone!

--Sorcia Blade
