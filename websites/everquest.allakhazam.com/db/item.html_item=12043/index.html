    To clear up whether this can be placed in the ammo slot:

    I was confused because my eyeglass did NOT go in the ammo slot, whereas the picture clearly shows that it does. Furthermore some authentic Magelo profiles have it in the ammo slot.

    I thought maybe I needed to do the gnome's quest to change it. So, preparing to do that, I removed the aug from it and voila! Apparently having an aug on an item block it from being equippable in ammo slot.

I have no reason to believe the 5% jewelcraft bonus is any different. I guess the folks with the eyeglass in their ammo slot on Magelo, just want to prove they do indeed have it.

    The ONLY thing that makes a difference on your success rate is your current modified skill. So, if you have a skill of 200 and you have a geerlock jewel setter (+5%) equiped, your current modified skill level will be 210. That number will be compared to the trivial value for the item you're trying to make in order to determine if the combine works.

    Your stats are ONLY used to determine if you're going to get an increase in your skill from the combine. For jewelcraft, your wisdom or intelligence score (whichever is highest) will be used to figure out the skill improvement.

    INT Increases your chance of getting a skill up. Once your skill is 250, you can still make this (or anything else) with 50 INT. :p

    SKILL + DEX Increases your chance of a successful combine.

    Geerlok increases your chance of making items that trivial close to your skill. It DOES NOT artificially increase your skill.

    For example. You have a skill of 200 and are trying to combine something that trivials at 199. The Geerlok gives you 5% more chance of combining, it DOES NOT artificialy give you a skill of 210. The cap on Tradeskills is 250. Not 252, 262 or any other number people try to convince you with.

    It dosen't take a genius to work this out. If it did artificialy increase your skill, you wouldn't be able to get a skill increase if you had it equipped at 238+, because you would already be over/at the 250 cap. :p
