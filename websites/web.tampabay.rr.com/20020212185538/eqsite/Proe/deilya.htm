Deilya


Deilya, Wood Elven Ranger, worships Tunare.


Will be changing alignment to Discordant Neutral.

Deilya was orphaned as a child; her parents were viciously killed by the Crushbone Orcs, a fact that has burned a hatred of Orcs into her soul.  She was taken in by a kindly Ranger who was among the force that drove back the orcs that day.  Within the Faydark's Champions guild, she learned a love of nature, animals, Tunare, compassion towards life but a fierceness against her enemies.

She was intrigued by the entertainments of Kelethin, staged by the Songweavers guild, and it was at one of these recitals that she met a young Half-Elf by the name of Daelin.  Eventually, she came to respect the young fellow whose lust for battle seemed to match her own, and they began spending more and more time together.  Short journeys to Ak'anon to view the frothy, wiry-haired minotaur at the zoo were undertaken at times, where the two would analyze the beast, looking for the most effective ways to fight its kind.  From these voyages to Ak'anon, she became friends with a few gnomes, including a young Wizard and Cleric. 

Now that she has completed her training, she has earned the rank of a Ranger, and plans to undertake her crusade against the Orcs alongside her close companion Daelin, who has become strangely tight-lipped lately whenever he starts extolling the wonders of battlelust that Rallos Zek can command, as if he suddenly thinks better of the conversation for some reason.  /shrug
