QUEST WALKTHROUGH [COMPLETE VERSION]

This is the most complete version of the faq. It as full spoilers and complete details on every segment of the quest. If you find this walkthough to be missing anything, please contact me by email [crydee@monsterweb.org.]

Although it is possible to do portions of the quest out of order, this walkthrough assumes that you complete each part before moving on to the next section. Just in case you have done portions of the quest out of order, I have made note of what items you would have if you have been doing the quest in the exact order listed here and which of those are required to complete that part of the quest.

NOTE: If at any point in the quest you need to use sneak to deal with an NPC, you will need to use sneak again at all later encounters. Although I will suggest using sneak the during the first encounter with a potentially problematic NPC, you will need to remember to use it again for later encounters. This mostly concerns Kinlo Strongarm and Mairee Silentone.

Part 1: Obtain the Shiny Tin Bowl

Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods, Kunark

Telin Darkforest can be found near the zone to Skyfire Mountains. The easiest way to find him is to head to the North East part of the zone, towards Skyfire, and you should eventually pick him up on tracking.

You say, 'hail Telin Darkforest'
Telin Darkforest looks at you serenely. 'Hello, wanderer.'
You say, 'what are you doing in this forest'
Telin Darkforest says 'Mighty this place must have been, once. Now scarred by hatred and suffering. I have been sent here seeking answers to problems not yet understood.
You say, 'what answers'
Telin Darkforest considers you for a few moments before speaking. 'A great evil walks across the land. The hand of some demon we don't yet know is twisting and polluting forests.'
You say, 'what evil'
Telin Darkforest says 'If I knew that, I wouldnt be here looking for answers. I would be elsewhere, looking for solutions. For now, I suspect much but know little.
You say, 'what do you suspect'
Telin Darkforest looks at you and sighs. 'The lands are changing. Beasts, large and small, have turned upon those who watched over them. Druids and rangers, worshippers of both Karana and Tunare, have reported a feeling of hatred throughout their once peaceful lands.'
You say, 'perhaps it is Innoruuk'
Telin Darkforest raises his eyebrow slightly and a subtle look of surprise creeps across his face. 'Innoruuk? Perhaps. Aye. It must be. Perhaps I knew and didn't want to consider the possibility. It must be this though, and some action will have to be taken.'
You say, 'what action should I take'
Telin Darkforest looks at you consideringly and then scribbles something quickly on a small, worn paper. 'You seem powerful enough. Perhaps you can help those who seek to protect nature's peace. Take this note. Deliver it only into the hands of Faelin Bloodbriar. Where you will find her, I cannot say. She is a friend of mine and a wanderer. She has been seen from one end of the lands to the other. Seek her out, and quickly.'

    Go to Teloa. Give her the Frayed Braided Grass Amulet and she will lead you to the druid gathering
    Kill the Dark Elf Corruptor and Reavers that spawn and loot the Fleshbound Tomb
    Go to Althele. Give her the Fleshbound Tomb to get the "Earth Stained Note"
  • Go to Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket. Give her the Earth Stained Note to get the "Shiny Tin Bowl"

Part 2: Create the Hardened Mixture

  • Go to Everfrost and forage "Chilled Tundra Root"
  • Go to Greater Faydark and forage "Ripened Heartfruit"
  • Go to Innothule Swamp and forage "Speckled Molded Mushroom"
  • Go to Misty Thicket and forage "Sweetened Mudroot"
  • Place the Chilled Tundra Root, Ripened Heartfruit, Speckled Molded Mushroom, and Sweetened Mudroot in the Shiny Tin Bowl. Hit the Combine button to create the "Hardened Mixture"

Part 3: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #1 [Ancient Pattern]

  • Go to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Target him and say ‘I will take the artifact' to get the "Crushed Pot"
  • Go to Farios Elianos in Felwithe [B]. Give him the Crushed Pot to get the "Grocery List"
  • Go to Merchant Nora in Felwithe [A]. Give her the Grocery List to get the "Bag of Provisions"
  • Go to Farios Elianos in Felwithe [B]. Give him the Bag of Provisions to get the "Receipt"
  • Go to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Give him the Receipt to get the "Ancient Pattern"

Part 4: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #2 [Platinum Speckled Powder]

  • Forage a Rose of Firiona in Firiona Vie.
  • Go to Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glade. Give him the Rose of Firiona to get the "Wood Painting"
  • Go to Human Skeleton in Frontier Mountains. Give her the Wood Painting to get the "Silver Chained Locket"
    [Note: Spawns around NEG 2100, POS 2000]
  • Go to Niera Farbreeze in Surefall Glade. GIve her the Silver Chained Locket to get the "Platinum Speckled Powder"

Part 5: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #3 [Enchanted Clay]

  • Obtain a Jade Reaver from City of Mist
  • Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim [B]. Give him the Jade Reaver to get the "Enchanted Clay"

Part 6: Create the Runecrested Bowl

  • Combine the Ancient Pattern, Enchanted Clay, and Platinum Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel to make a Runecrested Bowl. [Note: This requires no skill in pottery to do and any pottery wheel should work.]

Part 7: Obtain the Softly Glowing Stone

  • Give Ella Foodcrafter in Misty Thicket the Hardened Mixture and the Runecrested Bowl to get the "Softly Glowing Stone"

Part 8: Epic Preparation

  • Go to Jaeil the Wretched in The Hole. Give him the Shiny Tin Bowl to spawn Jaeil the Insane.
  • Kill Jaeil the Insane and loot the "Soulbound Hammer"
  • Go to Mairee Silentone in Eurdin. Give her the Soulbound Hammer to get the "Dwarven Smiths Hammer"
  • Go to Island Four of the Plane of Air. Kill the Essensce Tamer to get the "Swirling Sphere of Color"
  • Go to Mairee Silentone in Eurdin. Give her the Dwarven Smiths Hammer and the Swirling Sphere of Color to get the "Hammer of the Ancients"

Part 9: Obtaining Earthcaller

  • Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim. Give Kinlo Strongarm the Hammer of the Ancients, which causes Usbak the Old to spawn, who gives you a "Small bit of Mithril Ore"
  • Give Kinlo Stirngarm the Small bit of Mithril Ore to get the "Refined Mithril Blade"
  • Kill Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate to get the "Shattered Emerald of Corruption" [Note: This also a random/rare drop from any of the boss mobs.]
  • Go to Xanuusus in North Karana. Give him the Refined Mithril Blade and the Shattered Emerald of Corruption. You now have Earthcaller.

Part 10: Obtain Swiftwind

  • You will need to redo Parts 1 and 8 of this version of the quest. This involves re-obtaining the Shiny Tin Bowl so that you could get the hammer again.
  • Kill Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle to spawn Venril Sathirs remains
  • Cast the spell "Dance of the Fireflies" to Summon a Firefly Globe
  • Give the Firefly Globe to Venril Sathirs remains to spawn the Spirit of Venril Sathir
  • Give the Spirit of Venril Sathir the Level 49 Cleric Ressurection spell to respawn Venril Sathir
  • Kill Venril Sathir to get the "Pulsing Green Stone"
  • Go to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie. Give her the Softly Glowing Stone and the Pusling Green Stone to get the "Warmly Glowing Stone"
  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Warmly Glowing Stone to get the "Ancient Longsword"
  • Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim. Give Kinlo Strongarm the Hammer of the Ancients, which causes Usbak the Old to spawn, who gives you a "Small bit of Mithril Ore"
  • Give Usbak the Old the Ancient Longsword to get the "Refined Ancient Sword"
  • Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Refined Ancient Blade, which causes Faelin to spawn. Telin gives you the Ancient Blade back
  • Give Faelin the Refined Ancient Sword to get Swiftwind.

The quest has now been completed!

POSSIBLE FACTION ISSUES

These are listed in the order in which you may run into a faction issue.

Plane of Growth
Although the Plane of Growth is not used for the quest, it does need special mentioning.

Telin Darkforest [Faydark's Champion]
Telin Darkforest is on Faydark's Champion faction. Any of the Kelethin Ranger quests will give you faction with him. You can also kill orcs in Crushbone.

Faelin Bloodbriar and Foloal Stormforest [Soldiers of Tunare]
The only way to increase faction with either of these two NPCs is by doing quests for either the Kelethin Ranger or Druid guilds.

Giz X`Tin [unknown]
He is mostly be on one of the Dark Elf Factions and will probably be less than indifferent to you. If this is the case, cast Spirit of Wolf on yourself [to be able to keep up with him] than sneak up behind him so that he will be indifferent to you and then give him the ring. If you give him the ring, while he /CONs dubious [or lower] to you, you will not get the coin to continue the quest.

Dark Elf Corruptor and Dark Reavers in East Karana [Dark Elf Warrior]
Killing them will not impact faction with the Neriak Guards. This faction will only be noteworthy to any Dark Elves [and to a lesser extent Trolls and Ogres] that may wish to assist you with killing these.

Alrik Farsight [Keepers of the Art]
To gain faction with Alrik Farsight, visit the Magician Guild in Felwithe [B] and give Niola Impholder Bat Wings until she is amiable to you. If your faction with Niola Impholder is so low that she doesn't give you faction when you turn them in, stand behind her and use sneak to make her indifferent to you.

Kinlo Strongarm & Usbak the Old [unknown]
If your faction with these dwarves is less than indifferent, you will need to stand behind them and use sneak in order to be able to deal with them.
