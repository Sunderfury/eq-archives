The winds begin to blow, an eerie wind, a cold wind, a wind that bites through to your joints and puts pay to the fire of your soul.

Gather around fellow Norrathians, yes all of you… For I tell you evil is afoot tonight.

The wind seems to howl from the four points of the compass. It seems to whisper…… I hear it more clearly now …. It seems to say.. “There is evil in the land, and the land must be cleansed. The land can be cleansed by fire or by that which makes the burden of our souls lighter, that which makes life seem less harsh. Either way the evil must be purged or the darkness will advance and poison all it touches. The choice is yours.

The land will select its champions, the land will find the way. But it is your purity and courage that will save the day, or lose it and damn us all.” I scream out into the cold, bleak night “HOW!!! Tell me how!!!, we have tried to vanquish the evil, but it is undead. It turns away our greatest clerics and laughs at our finest warriors and mages.”

My question is greeted by a stony silence. I scream out again at the Gods: “We continue the struggle and I fear many more brave souls will perish in the fight, but we stand strong and try to force the evil from the land.” The wind whispers again… “You must take both paths, for only one might succeed and you dare not fail. The other path is an ancient path. It requires forces of good from all points of the compass. It requires that evil is fought with that which it fears most – ridicule, because it preys on fear, and joy, because that is what it tries to destroy. It will take four to act as the corner stones of a great upsurge of strength. It will take offerings from the land, it will take many, many questers all set on the same path. My priests will appear to assist you in your cause. My priests will light the way. And, I the wind of Norrath will instill knowledge in many others to help the questers on their journey.”

The wind went silent. The with one last howl it said: “All of Norrath must be united, 100 questers will not be enough, you need hundreds to join together to defeat the evil.” The wind went quiet.

Was it a dream?? I know not, but we must prepare. If the dream be true, many will be given knowledge and priests will appear to guide us on the true path. In the meantime, we try to best the evil with force of magic and brawn. May the gods give our champions strength and may the waters of luck runs deep and true.

Savin, troubled by the omens.

Terrak Innoran
Huntsman of Innoruuk
