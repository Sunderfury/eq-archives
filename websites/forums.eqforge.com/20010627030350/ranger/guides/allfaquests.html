Bard Quest: Sining Short Sword

This is a summary of the Bard epic quest for the Sining Short Sword.

Quest Start

Talk to Baldric Slezaf in Dreadlands, and he will send you to get Page 24, Page 25 and a Lute made by Forper Fizfla.

Page 24 [Top Half]

  1. Go to West Karana. Talk to Konia Swiftfoot to get a torch.
  2. Go to Misty Thicket. Give the torch to Fajio to get another torch.
  3. Go to South Ro. Give the torch to Andad Filla to get another torch.
  4. Go to Lake Rathetear. Give the torch to Misty Tekchita to get a ring.
  5. Go to West Karana. Give the ring to Konia Swiftfoot to get the top half of Page 24.

Page 24 [Bottom Half]

  1. Talk to Braenar Swiftsong. Say 'what doll' to him to get an invoice.
  2. Go to Sol A. Give Marfen the invoice to get a doll.
  3. Go to the Estate of Unrest. Give the doll to Serra to get a note.
  4. Go to Braenar Swiftsong. Give the note to Braenar Swiftsong to get another note.
  5. Go to West Karana. Give the note to Maligar to spawn Maligars Enraged Dopplgan.
  6. Kill Maligars Enraged Dopplgan to get Maligar's head.
  7. Give Maligar's head to Braenar to receive Mahlin's Bongos.
  8. Go to West Karana. Give Mahlin's Bongos to Braenar Swiftsong to get the bottom half of Page 24.

Page 25

  1. Go to Skyfire Mountains. Kill Edrak the Old to get the Chromodrac Guts.
  2. Go to Burning Woods. Kill Nezekezena to get the Red Wurm Guts.
  3. Go to Rathetear Mountains. Kill the Blackwing to get the Hudge Oynx Drake Guts.
  4. Go to South Karana. Give all three guts to Kelkin Mekia to get Page 25.

Forpar's Lute

This is awarded in several parts.

Forpar's Lute [Head & Neck]

  1. Go to Ocean of Tears. Kill Quag Maelstrom for the Alluring Horn.
  2. Go to Butcherblock Mountains. Give the Alluring Horn to Vedico to get a note.
  3. Go to Forpar. Give Forpar the note. He will give you a note and tell you what items you need next.
  4. Go to Kedge Keep. Kill Phinigel Autropos from the Phinigel Spine.
  5. Go to Plane of Fear. Kill an Amygdalan Tendril for the tentacle
  6. Go to Karnor's Castle. Kill the Drolvarg Warlord for the Petrified Werewolf Skull.
  7. Go to Forpar Fizfla. Give him the note, Phinigel Spine, tentacle and Petrified Werewolf Skull to receive the Head and Neck of the Lute.

Forpar's Lute [Body]

  1. Kill Nagafen [in SolB], Talendor [in Skyfire], or Zordakalicus Ragefire [in SolB] for a Large Red Dragon Scale.
  2. Kill Lady Vox [in Permafrost] or Gorenaire [in Dreadlands] for a Large White Dragon Scale.
  3. Obtain two Metal bits from any player blacksmith.
  4. Go to Forpar Fizfla. Give him the two dragon scales and the two metal bits to receive the Body of the Lute.

Forpar's Lute [Strings]

Go to Sebilis. Kill Trakanon to get the Trakanon Gut Strings, which are the strings of the lute.

Forpar's Lute

Give Forpar Fizfla the Head and Neck, Body & Gut Strings to receive Forpar's Lute.

Recieve the Singing Short Sword

Go to Dreadlands. Give Baldric Slezaf Page 24 [Top Half], Page 24 [Bottom Half], Page 25 and Forper's Lute.

Clerc Quest: Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh

This is a summary of the Cleric epic quest for the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh.

Quest Start

Talk to Shmendrik Lavawalker in Lake Rathetear, and he will send you to get Lord Bergurgle's Crown.

Orb of Frozen Water

  1. Hail and talk to to start the quest.
  2. Kill Lord Bergurgle to get Lord Bergurgle's Crown.
  3. Give Lord Bergurgle's Crown to Shmendrik Lavawalker to spawn Natasha Whitewater and Shmendrik Lavawalker attacks you.
  4. Kill Shmendrik Lavawalker spawn A Spirit of Flame.
  5. Kill A Spirit of Flame to get the Damaged Goblin Crown.
  6. Give the Damaged Goblin Crown to Natasha Whitewater for the Ornate Sea Shell. She will then despawn.
  7. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Ornate Sea Shell to Omat Vastsea to get the Coral Statue of Tarew.
  8. Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro. Give Coral Statue of Tarew to A Seeker and a Plasmatic Priest will spawn, who immediately attacks.
  9. Kill the Plasmatic Priest to get the Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe.
  10. Go to Solusek Eye [Sol A]. Kill Lord Gimblox to get Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring.
  11. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe to Omat Vastsea to get the Orb of Frozen Water. Natasha Whitewater will spawn.

Orb of Clear Water

  1. After receiving the Orb of Frozen Water, give Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring to Natasha Whitewater to get another Ornate Sea Shell.
  2. Go to Burning Woods. Give Naxton Deepwater the Ornate Sea Shell to get Message to Natasha and spawn Ixiblat Fer.
  3. Kill Ixiblat Fer to get the Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer.
  4. Go to Chardok. Kill Overking Bathezed to get the Singed Scroll.
  5. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer and Singed Scroll to Omat Vastsea to get the Orb of Clear Water. Natasha Whitewater will spawn.

Orb of Vapor

  1. After receiving the Orb of Clear Water, give Message to Natasha to Natasha Whitewater to get the Shimmering Pearl.
  2. Go to Nagafen's Lair [Sol B]. Give Zordak Ragefire the Shimmering Pearl to spawn Zordakalicus Ragefire, who will attack immediately.
  3. Kill Zordakalicus Ragefire to get the Heart of Zordak Ragefire.
  4. Go to Timorous Deep. Give the Heart of Zordak Ragefire to Omat Vastsea to get the Orb of Vapor. Jhassad Oceanson will spawn.

Receive the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh

  1. Give the Orb of Frozen Water, Orb of Clear Water, and Orb of Vapor to Jhassad Oceanson to get the Orb of the Triumvirate. Avatar of Water will spawn.
  2. Give the Orb of the Triumvirate to the Avatar of Water to get the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh.

    Monk: Celestial Fists

    This is a summary of the Monk epic quest for the Celestial Fists.

    Quest Start

    Talk to Tomekeeper Dahl in Erudian and say 'What rare finds.' to start the quest.

    The Book of Celestial FIsts

    1. Go to Skyfire. Kill any of the named mobs to get the Book of Immortals.
    2. Go to Erudian. Give Tomekeeper Danh the Book of Immortals, and say 'Who is Lheao' to get Danl's Reference.
    3. Go to Timorous Deep. Say to Lhaeo 'I have found the book Immortals' to start the dialogue with him.
    4. Give the Robe of the Whistling Fists and Dahl's Reference to get the the Celestial Fists Book.
      [Note: see Robe of the Whistling Fists Sub-Quest below for information on obtaining it.]
    5. Go to Trakanon's Teeth. Hail and talk to Kaiaren.
    6. Give the Celestial Fists book to Kaiaren to trigger Kaiaren to argo, and you will get a different Celestial Fists book.
    7. You can either kill Kaiaren or Feign Death.
    8. Give Kaiaren [his true form] the Celestial Fists book, then say 'I got it from Lheao' to start dialogue with him.


    Celestial Fist of Fire: Eejag

    1. Go to Lavastorm. Find a Fire Sprite and say 'I Challenge Eejag' to it to spawn Eajag and the Fire Sprite will despawn.
    2. Eejag will shout across the zone and challenge you to come to the large fire pit near the Temple of Solusek Ro.
    3. Go to the large fire pit and you will see an Eejag in the fire pit.
    4. Kill Eejag to get the Charred Scale.
    5. Go to Trakanon's Teeth. Give the Charred Scale to Kaiaren's true form. The scale is returned to you and you are given more information.


    Celestial Fist of Air: Gwan

    1. Go to the Plane of Sky. Give the Charred Scale to the Presence [on Island 2] to spawn Gwan.
    2. Say 'I am prepared' will cause him to argo.
    3. Kill Gwan to get the Breath of Gwan.


    Celestial Fist of Earth: Trunt

    1. Go to the Mines of Nurga. Give the Breath of Gwan to a Sleeping Ogre to spawn Trunt.

    2. Go to North Freeport. Give the Tiny Gem to Bonndl Felligan.
    3. Bonndl Felligan will leave the sewers, follow him. He will stop and speak to you.
    4. Say 'You can buy booze' to Bonndl Felligan, and he leads you to the Greater Spirit.
      [Note: You can respawn the Greater Spirit at any time by saying 'You can buy booze' to Bonndl Felligan.]
    5. Hail and talk to the Greater Spirit. Say 'What heyokah' to get an Opaque Gem.
    6. After you get the Opaque Gem, say 'I will walk the path of the Heyokah' to continue the quest.


    Test of Patience: Broken Arrow from Abe

    1. Go to Erud's Crossing. Hail Ooglyn and give her the Opaque Gem when she asks for it.
    2. Ooglyn will tell you to follower her to around NEG 1600 2400 [underwater.]
    3. Ooglyn will tell you to wait there and then leave you.
    4. Eventually Srafen the Soaked will spawn. Hail and talk to him.
    5. Eventually Dillon the Drowned will spawn. Hail Drillon the Drowned, and talk to both him and Srafen the Soaked.
    6. At some point during the dialogue Froham the Forgotten will show up. Hail and talk to him.
    7. When the start to wander off, do not follow.
    8. Eventually Abe the Abandoned will show up. Hail and talk to him.
    9. When Abe the Abandoned stops talking, wait till he starts talking again. Say 'I will give them the arrow' when that trigger phrase shows up to get the Broken Arrow from Abe.
    10. Wait some more, and Abe the Abandoned will tell you to follow him and wanders off. Do not follow him.
    11. The Greater Spirit shows up. Hail and talk to the Greater Spirit to get the gem.

    7. Go back to West Karana. Give Marr's Promise, Woe and Envy to the Wandering Spirit to get a gem.
    8. Talk to the Wandering Spirit some more. Say 'what Shield of Falsehood' to get the Shield of Falsehood.
