General Guidelines

When making effective use of Harmony it behooves you to be grouped with one other player. This both decreases downtime and increases your chances of survival if something should go wrong. I grouped successfully with multiple players of magic using classes and you might want to stick to these instead of melee.

Part 1: Selecting the Target

The target you select when using Harmony may not matter too much, Harmony is area of effect. However, you should be careful which target you draw. My instructions deal with Dervs., they could be modified for anything else. Draw targets in this order:

1 - If there is a MoB away from the main group, get that one first (This keeps it from jumping in when not wanted)
2 - If you have a choice of MoBs (2 blues and a yellow), select the tough one (This gives you the best chance of just drawing one. The tough one has the best chance of resisting Harmony and it is best to get it out of the way first).
3 - If there is only 1 MoB, don't bother with Harmony, save your mana.

Part 2: The Draw

Drawing the target is a pretty simple process. Here is the way most draws will work:

1 - Target all the MoBs and find the toughest one. Keep that MoB targeted.
2 - Have your partner back off a safe distance from any spawn points.
3 - Get within spell range and cast Harmony on the MoB. You will not see the Harmony icon. That's fine, it is working (Just make sure that your spell was acually cast and was not interrupted. There is no graphical or sound difference between a successful Harmony and a interrupted Harmony)
4 - Now, run up and hit the MoB with your weapon. (Some may say to use a spell, I have had better success when I use a weapon instead)
5 - Back up from the MoB to draw it past your partner, keep an eye on the other MoBs while backing to make sure they stay put.
6 - Kill the MoB using as little mana as possible while not letting anyone's health fall too low.
7 - The remaining MoBs are weaker, draw them one by one using the same system.

Conclusion

I found that Harmony helps to allow your group to do things that it normally could not due to the number of MoBs involved. However, Harmony is of no help at all if you and your partner cannot take out 2 of the mobs in question (due to the risk of a bad draw) Nor is Harmony castable

I wish you luck. If you have anything to add, please let me know.

***An important note, while the spell has an area of effect and a duration it is NOT area of effect for the whole duration.  That is, it hits all MoBs in range of the spell target when you cast the spell and all those MoBs will keep the Harmony icon for the duration (No matter where they go) HOWEVER any new MoBs that spawn or walk close will not get it.
