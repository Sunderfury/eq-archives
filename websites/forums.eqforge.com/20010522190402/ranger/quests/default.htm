The JaggedPine Crook

The quest can be found in the Surefall Glade area from Corun Finisc. In order to complete the quest, you must track and kill a VERY rare spawn of a Gnoll Hunter. In my entire playing time, I have seen only one and after slaying the beast, received the top of the staff.

Most often, you will hear others in the area shouting for the part of the staff they do not have, or they are willing to depart with their portion for a tidy sum (Usually around 3 to 5 plat)

Once you have succeeded in getting both parts of the staff, hail Corun Finisc for him to put it together for you, give him both pieces at the same time and he will reward you with the JaggedPine Crook.

Damage 5, Weight 8.5. +1 Strength, +5 HP, +5 protection from fire and a Magical Attack.

Trueshot Bow

This quest takes place in Kelethin. To begin, speak with Trueshot and he will give you a pack to collect 4 items: treant wood from Freeport, dwarven wire from Kaladim, spiderling silk, and a microservo from Steamfont.

The Treant Wood can be obtained by speaking with Jyle Windshot, but he will ask for a small lantern first. Be warned, there are TWO Jyle Windshots in the area and one of them will try and rip you off for the wood.

The Dwarven Wire can be tricky to get as you have to have a dwarf speak with Tantor Everhot. Once you finally do speak with him, you'll find he desires a Tumpy Tonic. Tumpy Irontoe is an infamous bartender in Kaladim. Great you say, this isn't that bad, but the problem is to make the Tonic he requires Kiola nuts (make sure you buy two because you will need two tonics total.) Kiola nuts, to my knowledge, are found on the Sister Island in the ocean of Tears. In addidtion, you'll also have to provide Tumpy with a flask of store-bought water (not foraged.) Give the two completed tonics (separately) to Tantor and he wil give you the Dwarven Wire.

The microservo can be found on various rogue clockwork beasties in the land of Steamfont. They commonly roam around the windmills in the zone.

While in Steamfont go ahead and kill a few spiderlings to obtain the spiderling silk neccessary.

Once you have attained a high enough level (not sure if it is related to level or fletching ability) you will receive a Treant stave and instructions on how to make the bow. With this stave, you can make the infamous Trueshot Bow!

Stats are 20 DAM, 45 Delay and 100 Range. Keep in mind, there are bows better than this, but it doesn't cost you nearly as much for the stave as say a darkwood stave... PLUS, it is a Ranger only weapon!

Gnasher Furgut

This is the quest involving Gnasher Furgutt (level 8 or 9 gnoll in Qeynos Hills.) Kill him, take his head and the note (Note: The note will only appear during the deep night hours 9PM to 4AM or so) on the corpse to Surefall Glade, and give to the ranger behind the guild (standing by himself, his name is Larsk Juton.) He will give you faction/experience/cash.Then when you give him the note he gives it back to you and tells you to go see Captain Tillin in S. Qeynos (same guy you give gnoll fangs to).

Give Capt Tillin the note and he gives you cash and an Amulet of +5 charisma, then he calls the executioner to go kill the guy that was dealing with the gnolls. You can follow her and watch the execution if you want but its not necessary.

Orc Hatchets

Talk to Dill Fireshine in the ranger guild (Faydark's Champions) in Kelethin. He will ask you to retrieve orc hatchets from the orc pawns and orc hatchetmen and bring them back to him. When you bring two hatchets and give them to him at the same time he will reward you with 4 - 5 tarnished weapons that you can use or sell.

(Note: You used to give him one hatchet at a time and would receive a miscellaneous item worth about a gold maximum.)

Pixie Dust

Talk to Ran Sunfire in the ranger guild in Kelethin. She will tell you how the pixies are setting forest fires and ask you for your help. If you take on this mission you will receive a pouch that holds 8 of the pixie dusts. Fill this up and give it back to her. You will be rewarded with a random piece of patchwork or tattered armour.

Note: Pixie dust stacks and you must remember to separate out 8 individual packets of the dust in the pouch for the quest to work.

Orc Runner

After completing the orc hatchet quest, Dill Fireshine will mention something about [crushbone allies], he will tell you to go to ButcherBlock and stop the Orc runners and will reward you for returning their pouch to him.

at 7th level orc runners con between white - red
