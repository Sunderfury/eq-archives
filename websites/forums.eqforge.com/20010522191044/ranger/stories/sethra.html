Stories from Norrath


Sitting on the wall of Misty Thicket watching the mist that it is named for swirling slightly in the breeze. I am safe in the knowledge that nothing can hurt me here. I begin to think about new places to visit and new enemies to hunt. The last few months have provided great times, ridding the Thicket of the Crushbone orcs and earning my squad ring has provided me with many pleasant, and some not so pleasant memories. Unconsciously I twist the hard-earned ring on my finger and run my hand over the scar on the back of my hand. A present from one orc who didn’t like the bite of my sword. A small price to pay I muse.

My thoughts are interrupted by a prickling sensation just behind my eyes, signally someone wishes to get in contact with me. I concentrate and hear my friend Endareth’s voice, "Hail fellow Keeper!" he says, "Come join me in Lavastorm!"

How fitting I think, a new hunting ground just when I needed it

"Hail Keeper" I say, "I will meet you there"

Shaking myself out of my riviere I pick up my two handed sword and sling it across my back and start jogging towards Rivervale. Stopping only once to heal and buff a halfling newbie.

Once I hit the Commonlands I begin to realize how hot the sun can be as sweat begins to trickle down the side of my face. In the Thicket the mist keeps most of the heat away. I stop for refreshments at a small village keeping a sharp eye out for a nasty dark elf called Gix. Too many times I have nursed wounds from his hands. One day he will pay.

I continue to the entrance of the Nektulos Forest stopping only to recast my protective spells. Upon entering the forest I, immediately get a sense of foreboding. A cold chill runs down my spine making me shiver.

"What is this place?" I ask Endareth who has come to guide me to Lavastorm

"Dark Elf Territory", he says, a dangerous glow in his eyes "Watch your back"

Another chill runs through my body as I consider what he is saying, this is dangerous territory for a Wood Elf, Dark Elves lust after my blood.

"Lets go", I say, "The sooner we leave this place the better"

Endareth nods and begins to murmur the words for Spirit of Wolf and Invisibility, I chuckle and once again thank my lucky stars for a friend with such power; nothing will hurt me now!

Ah, to become complacent is the worst possible think I could have done. Being ignorant of this forest I do not realize the Stone Guardians that patrol the forest can see through the Invisible spell. Being curious of these creatures I get closer to this strange looking beast, Almost before I realise it, he has turned and seen me. He charges towards me and gives me a stunning blow to my head, cancelling my invisibility. While trying to clear my head he hits me again, just under the ribcage. I hear a sickening crunch and I lose my breath. Somehow I manage to gather my senses and start running, luckily they are slow creatures and I manage to escape another blow, but I am visible now and very nervous.

I keep running towards Lavastorm, keeping a sharp eye out, my breath is coming in ragged gasps and every time I take a breath a sharp pain shoots though my chest.

Up ahead I see the entrance to Lavastorm Mountains, hope for respite surges through me. Just when I think I'm safe, Dragoon T’Sane jumps from behind a rock. "Wood Elves Die!" he screams. Weary from my run, bleeding from the wound on my head and pain shooting through me from every breath, I desperately run to the gate of Lavastorm.

Too late I hear the whistle of a sword cutting the air. I scream in agony, indescribably pain shooting through my head, but not for long. I sink to my knees, darkness surrounds me, the last sounds I hear is the victory cry from T’sane.

When I awaken I am in Rivervale, a long run from Lavastorm, Endareth contacts me "Sethra! Are you ok?" I bite back a sarcastic remark, knowing it’s the stress. "No", I reply, "I died."

I once again start jogging back to Nektulos Forest, jumping at every crack of a twig. I am without weapons or amour and still feeling the after effects of my reincarnation. To add to my nervous state, I wander too close to a Skeleton camp, something I would never do if I was in a normal state, and a dread corpse chases me to the Forest entrance.

Once I enter the forest I sit and catch my breath, my heart is beating so loud I’m sure everyone can hear it. I must calm myself before moving, fear is my worst enemy.

Again I make my way to Lavastorm staying high on the edge of the mountains, out of sight of the enemies on the ground. I reach Lavastorm and see my mangled corpse lying on the ground. Anger surges through me… later I tell myself. I start begin to retrieve my belongings. Out of the corner of my eye I see movement. A guard; I heave a sigh of relief, I can retrieve my belongings in safety. I continue to gather my things when the sound of running feet makes me look up.

The guard is running towards me, with bloodlust in his eye, my blood. Oh god not again, I think. My heart in my mouth I drop my things and run towards the Lavastorm gate. Pain shoots through my side as the guard finds his mark, and again through my leg as he opens a deep cut, his sword scraping my bone. Half walking half crawling I stumble across the gate, the guard screams in anger, he can go no further.

I stumble to the ground, exhausted. I am alive but only just. I quietly sob to myself, my body, cut and broken. It will be days before I recover enough to retrieve my belongings, Gingerly I lay myself down on the warm earth and sleep. Dreaming of revenge

--Sethra
