Stories from Norrath


Last week I got together a full group and headed across the Plains of Karana to the Lair of Splitpaw. My group consisted of myself (a Half-Elf Ranger), an Erudite Wizard, a Half-Elf Bard, a human Bard, and a half-elf warrior.

We went straight down to where Shaman Lenrel spawns and promptly offed her and entourage, but ending up with only a Snakeskin Mask to show for our efforts. After healing up with the bards singing the Hymn of Restoration, I noticed that the evil Gnoll Necromancer Granex Drah was on the tracking screen.

We then proceeded to take care of Drah and his pet along with the mother of all Gelatinous Cubes, which was much higher level than a normal cube and decided to show up during our battle with Drah. To make a long story short, we alternated between killing Lenrel and Granex Drah whenever the spawned but that all ended when the High Shaman Grisok spawned instead of Lenrel. We foolishly believed that it would be the same battle, just a different name...

Maybe it would've been but Grisok carries a Splitpaw Captain in his entourage. For those of you who don't know those are TOUGH! In mid-battle our Wiz decided to do what he could and nuked the captain. Well that got a pissed-off splitpaw captain charging after him, and since he is a wiz, I ran after him taunting the captain, figuring the warrior and bards could handle Grisok and a few shamen and nobles until we finished off the captain. While I am still chasing the Captain, I notice the message that a group member has died flash across the screen immidiately followed by the warrior and one bard running by us with a few nobles and Grisok on their tail and leap into the water.

The wizard and myself are still battling the captain and Grisok and his goons come after, so into the lake we go as well. Miraculously, not a single gnoll follows us and we simply swim over to a ledge and heal up. I suppose the rumor about Splitpaw Gnolls not being able to swim is true and it is very valuable as we proceded to whomp the captain and his buddies just swimming away whenever we were near death. Happily I haven't died in Paw since discovering the water trick. And now have a few Snakeskin Items and Gnoll heads in my inventory.

Rogosh of Surefall
Tunare Server

(Editors note: To all you young Rangers out there, this is an especially important lesson. The Gnolls of Splitpaw DO NOT swim... well, I did see one flailing helplessly one day trying to get OUT of the water, so I promptly ended his misery.)
