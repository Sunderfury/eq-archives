Arrow Making

Pricing Arrows:

The economy of Norrath is a free enterprise one. You can sell your arrows for whatever you want. However, I would suggest pricing the arrows at from twice the materials cost to three times the materials cost. Once you fail on an expensve bow you will understand why I suggest this. Now that store bought arrows are not all that good, the demand for fletched arrows has gone up. Especially in the 150 range arrows.

The arrows that I use and make for others, the most are the 1, 2, and 3 damage 150 range arrows. If you take a close look at the table above you can figure out the components needed for these arrows. I will give you an example to help you along. A 150 range 1 damage arrow can be made with: Field Point, Wood Shaft, Round Fletch and Small Nock. So, as soon as the #4 arrow listed in the table above becomes trivial, these 150 range, 1 damage arrows should be easy to make. You will use the Parabolic Fletch from arrow #3 and the Small Nock from #4 arrow with the cheapest point and shaft, Field Point and Wood Shaft.
