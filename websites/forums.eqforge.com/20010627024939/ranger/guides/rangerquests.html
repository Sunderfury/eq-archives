Orc ImpalerThis quest is started by Morin Shadowbane after you complete the Short Sword of Morin quest.
  1. Go to Brynn Fynndel in Lesser Faydark, and tell him "Morin sent me." He will tell you to kill the Shadowed Leader for the Shadowed Codebook. [Note: This step is optional.]
  2. In Lesser Faydark, kill the Shadowed Leader [spawns at POS 1800, NEG 400.] This mob is on a 20 minute timer, and the placeholder is a Shadowman. I recommend using Wolf Form when camping this mob if the Brownie Scouts are up. Wolf Form should neutralize your faction with the brownies. When you kill the Shadowed Leader, you will get a Shadowed CodeBook [Lore & No Drop] from him.
  3. Go to Brynn Fynndel, give him the Shadowed CodeBook, and he gives you a note to take to Gildara Myllar.
  4. Go to Gildara Myllar and give her Brynn's Message. She gives you Gildara's Ring [No Drop, SVM+5] and tells you to kill the Ghoul Boss.
  5. Go to the Haunted Shrine in Lesser Faydark. There are three spawn spots at the shrine, and all undead mobs are placeholders for the Ghoul Boss, who looks like a regular skeleton. Kill the Ghoul Boss to get the Ghoul Boss Notebook [Lore & No Drop].
  6. Return to Gildara Myllar. Give her the ring back and the Ghoul Boss Notebook. She gives you the Sealed Ghoul Boss Notebook to take to Morin Shadowbane in Kithicor Wood
  7. Go to Morin Shadowbane and give him the Sealed Ghoul Boss Notebook. He will give you a message to take to Brynn Fynndel.
  8. Go to Brynn Fynndel. Give him Morin's message and he will give you Orc Impaler.

Rain CallerThis quest is started by Maesyn Trueshot in the Kelethin Ranger Guild after you complete the Trueshot Longbow quest. The key phrase to trigger the quest is ''What is the next incarnation?''
  1. Go see Kinool Goldsinger at the Felwithe Enchanter guild, and say 'Where rain caller?' He gives you some background information on Trueshot and Rain Caller. He tells you that he needs your Trueshot Longbow, a Treant heart, Faerie Gold Dust, and a 3000GP guild donation. He then sends you to see Princess Joleena for the Fairy Gold Dust. [Note: This step is optional.]
  2. Trueshot Longbow: If you do not have Trueshot already, see below for details on the Trueshot Longbow quest.
  3. Treat Heart Sub-Quest:
    1. Go see Jyle Wyndshot on the second floor of Hogcallers Inn in West Freeport. Give him Small Lanterns until he gives you the Treant Heart. You can also go to South Karana and ask the evil folk killing the treants if you can loot one. [Note: Rangers should never attack or kill treants. The faction hits alone should deter you from doing this.]
  4. Faerie Gold Dust Sub-Quest:
    1. Go see Princess Joleena in Lesser Faydark and say 'What is Fairy Gold Dust?' to her. She tells you that her wing was stolen and that if you help her recover it, she will give you the Fairy Gold Dust. [Note: This step is optional.]
    2. Go see Sanfyrd Featherhead in Ak'Aknon and say 'What fairie wing?' He will give you a case of hair tonic and has you get some hair tonic for him.
    3. Go to West Freeport, in the newbie area you will find Ping Fuzzlecutter. Buy 10 Fuzzlecutter Formula 5000 from him place them in the crate and combine. [Note: Make sure that you place one Fuzzlecutter Formula 5000 in each slot
    4. Return to Sanfyrd Featherhead in Ak'Aknon and give him the crate of Hair Tonic. He then decides that he wants you to get his toupee repaired and gives you a Tattered Toupee.
    5. Go see Ping Fuzzlecutter and ask him "Can you repair a toupee?" He tells you that he needs 2 Clumps of Gorilla Hair and 1 lock of golden tresses from the Golden Haired Mermaid. [NOTE: This step is optional.]
    6. Go to Cazic Thule and kill the Hulking Gorillas. Each of the gorillias will drop one Clump of Gorilla Hair.
    7. Go to Kedge Keep to kill the Golden Haired Mermaid. You will need approximately one group of people in the high 30s to low 40s to complete this. She drops the Lock of Mermaid Hair.
    8. Go see Ping Fuzzlecutter. Give him the Tattered Toupee, 2 Clumps of Gorilla Hair, and the Lock of Mermaid Hair. He gives you the Mane Attraction.
    9. Go see Sanfyrd Featherhead. Give him the Mane Attraction and he gives you the Glimmering Fairy Wing.
    10. Go see Princess Joleena. Give her the Glimmering Fairy Wing, and she will give you the Faerie Gold Dust.
  5. Go see Kinool Goldsinger. Give him Trueshot Longbow, a Treant Heat, Faerie Gold Dust and 3000GP. He will give you Rain Caller. [Note: You need to turn in 3000GP. [Note: Giving him the equivalent amount using other coins will not work.]

    2. Go to Greater Faydark and forage "Ripened Heartfruit"
    3. Go to Innothule Swamp and forage "Speckled Molded Mushroom"
    4. Go to Misty Thicket and forage "Sweetened Mudroot"

    3. Go to Merchant Nora in Felwithe [A]. Give her the Grocery List to get the "Bag of Provisions"
    4. Go to Farios Elianos in Felwithe [B]. Give him the Bag of Provisions to get the "Receipt"
    5. Go to Alrik Farsight in Timorous Deep. Give him the Receipt to get the "Ancient Pattern"
  4. Part 4: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #2 [Platinum Speckled Powder]

    2. Go to Merdan Fleetfoot in Surefall Glade. Give him the Rose of Firiona to get the "Wood Painting"

    4. Go to Niera Farbreeze in Surefall Glade. GIve her the Silver Chained Locket to get the "Platinum Speckled Powder"
  5. Part 5: Runecrested Bowl Ingrediant #3 [Enchanted Clay]
    1. Obtain a Jade Reaver from City of Mist
    2. Go to Kinlo Strongarm in Kaladim [B]. Give him the Jade Reaver to get the "Enchanted Clay"
  6. Part 6: Create the Runecrested Bowl
    1. Combine the Ancient Pattern, Enchanted Clay, and Platinum Speckled Powder in a Pottery Wheel to make a Runecrested Bowl.
      [Note: This requires no skill in pottery to do and any pottery wheel should work.]
  7. Part 7: Obtain Swiftwind & Earthcaller

    2. Kill Venril Sathir in Karnor's Castle to get the "Pulsing Green Stone"
    3. Go to Foloal Stormforest in Firiona Vie. Give her the Softly Glowing Stone and the Pusling Green Stone to get the "Warmly Glowing Stone"

    11. Give Usbak the Old the Ancient Longsword to get the "Refined Ancient Sword"
    12. Give Kinlo Stirngarm the Small bit of Mithril Ore to get the "Refined Mithril Blade"
    13. Go to Telin Darkforest in Burning Woods. Give him the Refined Ancient Blade, which causes Faelin Bloodbriar to spawn. Telin gives you the Ancient Blade back
    14. Give Faelin Bloodbriar the Refined Ancient Sword to get Swiftwind.
    15. Kill Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate to get the "Shattered Emerald of Corruption"
    16. Go to Xanuusus in North Karana. Give him the Refined Mithril Blade and the Shattered Emerald of Corruption.

Trueshot LongbowThis quest is given by Maesyn Trueshot in the Kelethin Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger this quest is 'What are Trueshot Longbows?'
  1. Hail and talk to Maesyn Trueshot. He gives you a pack to gather the materials.
  2. Material 1: Dwarven Wire
    1. Go to the Ocean of Tears and buy four Kiola Nuts.
    2. Go to Tumpy Irontoe in Kaladim. Give him a Flask of Water and Kiola Nut to get a Tumpy Tonic. Repeat this until you have 4 Tumpy Tonics.
    3. Go Trantor Everhot in Kaladim. Give him the 4 Tumpy Tonics to get 2 Dwarven Wire.
    4. Place only one of the Dwarven Wire in the pack that Maesyn Trueshot gave you. Save the other one for later.
  3. Material 2: Treant Wood
    1. Go to Innkeep Nasumi in East Freeport and buy a Small Lantern.
    2. Go to Jyle Windshot on the second floor of Hogcallers Inn in West Freeport. Give him the Small Lantern to get Treant Wood. [Note: If he gives you a Treant Heart instead, save this for the Rain Caller quest that you will do later.]
    3. Place the Treant Wood in the pack that Maesyn Trueshot gave you.
  4. Material 3: Spiderling Silk
    1. There are plenty of Widow Hatchlings in Greater Faydark and Spiderlings in Steamfont Mountains. Either of these will drop the Spiderling Silk. You will only need one Spiderling Silk.
    2. Place the Spiderling Silk in the pack that Maesyn Trueshot gave you.
  5. Material 4: Micro Servos
    1. Go to Steamfont Mountains and kill the Rouge Clockwords for Micro Servos [No Drop.] You will need two of these.
    2. Place only one of the Micro Servos in the pack that Maesyn Trueshot gave you. Save the other one for later.
  6. You should now have 1 Dwarven Wire, 1 Treant Wood, 1 Spiderling Silk and 1 Micro Servo in the pack. Hit the Combine button.
  7. Return to Maesyn Trueshot and give him the pack. He will give you a Treant Bow Staff and tell you the secret to making the Trueshot Longbow.
  8. Purchase a Fletching Kit if you do not already have one.
  9. To Make a Trueshot Longbow, you need to combine the following in a Fletching Kit:
    • 1 Planing Tool
    • 1 Treant Bow Staff
    • 1 Micro Servo
    • 1 Dwarven Wire
If you fail to make the Trueshot Longbow, you will retain the Planning Tool. All other ingrediants will need to be re-obtained. You need a minimum Fletching skill of 100 [Master] to make the Trueshot Longbow. However, having a fletching skill that is higher will increase your chances of sucess.Trueshot Longbow

Ivy Etched BootsThis quest is given by Leaf Falldim at the Kithicor Ranger Guild. The key phrase to trigger this quest is 'I will earn the Ivy Etched Boots.'
  1. Mistmoore Granite
    Go to Mitmoore and kill Fouting Gargoyles, Jeering Gargoyles or Leering Gargoyles for Mistmoore Granite. The granite drops very rarely off of the Gargoyles outside of the castle. You have a better chance of getting the Granite inside of the castle. You need two pieces of Mistmoore Granite to complete this quest.
  2. Bar of Ronium
    1. Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro. Talk to Lord Searfire to get the Cauldron.
    2. Mistmoore Granite: You should have this item already from the above step, so just place one in the cauldron.
    3. Sands of Ro: Go to the Oasis and kill Sand Giants until you get the Sands of Ro, then place it in the cauldron.
    4. Soil of Underfoot: Kill faries until you get four fairy dust. Give the four Fairy Dusts to Priestess Ghalea in the Cleric guild, and she will give you the Soil of Underfoot. Place it in the cauldron.
    5. Melatite: Go to SolA to the Gnome area. You can kill the gnomish robots in this area for the Melatite, which is a rare drop. However, some people will kill the robots for whatever reason, sell the Melatite to the Gnome merchants, and you will sometimes them selling it. Place one Melatite into the cauldron.
    6. Enchanted Bar of Platinum: You can either by these from a player enchanter, or you can buy two Bars of Platinum and have an enchanter enchant them for you. Place two Enchanted Bars of Platinum in the Cauldron, and make sure they are not stacked.
    7. Hit the combine button on the cauldron and you will receive a Bar of Ronium.
  3. Give Leaf Falldim the Mistmoore Granite, a Bar of Ronium and 2000 Gold Pieces to get the Ivy Etched Boots. [Note: Giving him the equivalent amount using other coins will not work.]

  4. Go to the jewlrey shop in Erudin and buy a Star of Odus
  5. Give Leaf Falldim the Quiver of Kithicor - full and the Star of Odus to get the Ivy Etched Leggings.
