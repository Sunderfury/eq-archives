Kithicor Woods

To many this is just the dark zone to cross when going to/from Highpass or Rivervale, but it is actually a goldmine of experience for the fledging ranger and his fellow adventurers. If you can stand the perpetual darkness that is *grin*

So, what can you fight in Kith then?

For low levels, sub 10 that is, you have:

Moss and darkwood snakes, giant bats, giant scarabs, giant spiders, bears and wolves. As a Ranger you will find these with relative ease and as long as you stay clear of the Undead ruins and Shralok Orcs, nothing in Kith will attack you on sight. Wether you want to hunt bears and wolves are of course up to you, a good ranger might do this to protect the travellers of this woods since they are quite agressive to non rangers. The loot from these monsters are quite good for this level.

After 10th level you would like to take a look at the 3 undead ruins just north of the lake where you can find:

Skeletons, Zombies, Lesser Mummies and Ghouls. The two westernmost ruins usually has 3 to 4 undead which change everytime they spawn and the easternmost (stonhenge) has up to 9 zombies at one time and occasionally a werewolf. A good thing is to bring a cleric or two along.

While waiting for the Undead camps to respawn, you and your stalwart group can pass the time by fighting the Large Pirahnas in the lake. They are 13th to 15th level so be prepared and make sure you have magical aid to breathe, there is nothing more terrifying that to run out of air while a pirahna the size of a bus is gnawing on you. Remember to fight very close to the shore, that way you wont sink while fighting and you have a very short distance to "run" if needed.

When you finally have reached 18th level most of the things (except Ghouls and Wherewolves) will be green to you. There are however the Shralok Clan of orcs in the SW corner. There are usually 3 of them plus their Captain and Chief, Gan'Shralok. These are about 15th to 23rd and they spawn relatively slow, but i know of a few "loners" that has gone all the way from 18 to 22 on these orcs. As a note, i have never found any special loot on these guys, they are probably just there for the story of HH siege. You can find Mucktails in East Karana too.

The loot is not the best but the Ghouls occasionally carry bronze weapons.

At last, why would you bother with this zone when Dervs give much better xp and loot for intermediate levels? Well one thing, you will be alone.
