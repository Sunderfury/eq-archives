Blood Ember vs Umbral Armor 
Xilith T'Vyn of Tunare
Umbral
Umbral Armor is dropped in the Plane of Fear off of Amygdalan Knights.  It can also randomly be found on Phoboplasms in the Plane of Fear, and in the Plane of Hate (rarely) off of Haunted Chests.  Both Phoboplasms and Haunted Chests drop random pieces of every type of armor.  Both the Plane of Fear and the Plane of Hate require you to be level 46+ to even zone in.  All Umbral Armor is nodrop, but not lore, so it is possible to accrue an extra set.  Umbral Armor has very good AC, and plenty of stats and resistances.

What to use?
At this point, you should remember that 47 AC from equip will actually be about 70 AC when you wear it.  In general, while your resists shouldn't be neglected, the AC is more useful.  However, you can get a full (or near) set of Umbral relatively quickly.  There are more Amygdalan Knights in Fear than any other mob, and SKs are a minority class.  You can probably get 1-4 pieces of Umbral on each Fear trip, and it will be a long time before you can get some pieces of Blood Ember, such as the breastplate and greaves.

So first, I would recommend getting your Umbral from fear.  This will give you a good base to slowly replace with Blood Ember.  I would suggest wearing all the Blood Ember you can get for a general configuration, and augmenting the resists you lost with other items such as the Granite Spaulders from the Hole, Necklace of Superiority from Charasis, or a Platinum Diamond ring.  I'd keep the Umbral with Resists around for use in special situations that warrant a particular resist, i.e. dragon raids and such.
