The Orc Impaler

The phrase to get Morin to talk is [another service,] not another task as many have posted.

The sequence of events is:

1) Get quest from Morin
2) He sends you to Brynn in Lesser Fay
3) Brynn tells you to kill the Shadowman Leader a roaming spawn ( 1797,-400: Lesser Fay)
4) Leader drops the Shadowed CodeBook NO DROP LORE. ( does not poof on camping )
5) Return Book to Brynn. He gives you a message for the spy Gildara who spawns near the undead tower in Lesser Fay
6) Give message to Gildara and she gives you her ring and tells you to kill the Ghoul Boss
7) Ghoul Boss is a new spawn at the undead tower in Lfay that randommly spawns in place of one of the 3 undead. For me he was at the spawn on top of the hill
8) Kill Boss, get the Ghoul Boss Notebook NO DROP LORE
9) Return Notebook to Gildara with her ring
10 ) She gives you the Sealed Ghoul Boss Notebook NO DROP LORE and tells you to take it to Morin
11) Give book to Morin and he gives you a set of Sealed Orders for Brynn back in LFay
12) Give the orders to Brynn and one nice shiny new Orc Impaler for you.

Ghoul Boss conned blue at 45, is a melee only MOB that hits in the 70's and 80's against an AC 800 ranger.

The Impaler is a spear (range 40) so it can go in your range slot. All in all very nice little weapon.
