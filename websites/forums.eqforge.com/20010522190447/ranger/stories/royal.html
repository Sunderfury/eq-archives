Stories from Norrath

This started a few nights back when I decided my Combine Long Sword was just not holding up against the my foes quit as well as I would like. I needed something with more punch to bring the evil I was facing to justice. After much debate I decided that I needed to quest for a mighty two handed sword called the Bone Bladed Claymore. As most already know this sword is the property on one evil Troll Shadow Knight that makes his living in the lowest depths of a dungeon called Befallen. To accomplish the task of dispatching this vile creature I would need help, and lots of it. I called on my closest brothers at arms, the holy Cleric, Walbort and the most honorable Ranger, Rolin. We have fought many battles together and I knew I could count on them when things got ugly. We all meet at the entrance to the infamous Befallen, we had all been here before, and we have all had our souls separated from us here also. But we are older this time and wiser. We started by having Rolin call upon his God to give us the agility of the cat, Walbort's gave us the ability to move past the undead without being seen, and mine endowed us with Holy Armor and Centered our minds for the fight to come.

All set to bring or holy might down on this place of evil we entered. The stench of the undead was almost unbearable, we used our keys that where retrieved in earlier missions to gain access to the lowest level of this god forsaken place. To our surprise we found others down here sending these creatures home to Innoruuk. We moved past the false wall and to the chamber of death where the Troll does his work. Once again we found plenty of help around us keeping the area cleared. This allowed us to concentrate on our mission to separate this unholy Shadow Knight from his instrument of destruction and turn it into a sword to slay his evil brethren. When we came to the chamber we found that the Troll Shadow Knight was off visiting with Innoruuk, (hatching some evil plan) and we would have to wait for his return.

As we waited others would come to challenge the Troll but they would yield to us, they knew our purpose you true and just. After waiting for nearly two hour, (in real time) Rolin picked up the ugly Troll Shadow Knight on his tracking, he must have smelled him out, and he yelled, "Shadow Knight!" . We all ran to engage the evil beast and the battle started with a roar! Rolin was the fist to get to the Shadow Knight so it was really wanting to dispatch him, try as it may it was all in vain, this night belonged to the just and pure of heart. The Troll was no match for this veteran group, he fell quickly and his greatly feared Harm Touch did him no good. The rest of his horde fell quickly once their leader was slain.

I removed my Bronze platemail helm to take a good look at the slaughter that lay before my comrades, and I. I knelt down and removed the Bone Bladed Claymore from the dead Troll, then made a vow that from this day forth this sword shall be used only for good and righteous cause.

It was a lot of fun getting the BBC, I owe it all to Walbort and Rolin, Thanks guys!!

Royal

Dwarven Paladin of the 23rd Order
