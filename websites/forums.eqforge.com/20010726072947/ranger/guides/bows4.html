Bow Making

Pricing Bows:

The economy of Norrath is a free enterprise one. You can sell your bows for what ever you want. However, I would suggest that you at least price your bows at around twice the materials cost. Once you fail on an expensve bow you will understand why I suggest this.

Another factor to keep in mind, just because you never fail at the easier bows is no reason to sell them cheaper than the 2 times materials cost. You will fail enough on the high end bows to teach you that lesson.

Summary on Bows:

Never, never let anyone tell you that bows and the archery skill are worthless. Every player that waited until they were a level 40 or higher to start using their archery skill, and bought a high-end bow from me, has commented that they should have started using a bow at level 1. A Darkwood bow with its range of 150 with arrows that also have a range of 150 allows the archer to shoot and pull mobs from a distance that almost equals the range of any damage dealing spell used to pull mobs. And, bow kiting was not affected by the rules changes Verant put in place to hinder the other types of kiting.
