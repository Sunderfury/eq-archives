Bow Making

Advanced Bow Making:

The final stage of bow making is the addition of cams to increase the speed of the bow even more. But you still have the trade off of losing a little damage. Using a cam decreases the base damage of the bow by another 1 point and decreases the delay by 5 points. With a Darkwood Stave you will be able to use 2 cams to get a 2 point reduction in damage and an 10 point reduction in the delay. You should never use a cam with a stave that is less expensive than the cam. For best results use 1 cam on oak staves and 1 or 2 cams on Darkwood staves.

Adding cams adds complexity to the bow and the failure rate will go up. At level 200 I have failed many times on dual cam darkwood bows. As a matter of fact I once failed 4 times in a row on a dual cam darkwood bow. That cost me almost 1200 plat.
