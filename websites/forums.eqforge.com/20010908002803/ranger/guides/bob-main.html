I want to make some justifications for the values I listed. Most of them are purely mathematically derived, but some rely on judgement calls.

If we compare the FBSS, the Swirlspine, and the Hangman's Noose, we can arrive at a comparative value for haste vs. AC.

FBSS 20% haste 0 AC
Swirl 15% haste 4
AC Noose 16% haste 5 AC

So, if a player is willing to upgrade from FBSS to either of these belts, they are valuing 1 AC at either:
Swirl: 4 AC = -5% haste or 1 AC = 1.25% haste
Noose: 5 AC = -4% haste or 1 AC = .75% haste split the difference
1.25% haste < 1 AC > .75% haste or 1 AC = 1% haste


Next, pure math tells us that 1 AGI = 1pt. The reason:
5 agi equals 1 point of VISIBLE AC.
1 point of ITEM AC = 1.55 points of VIS AC
So, if two items existed, one with 12AC(19 vis AC)
and the other with 11AC(17 vis AC) and 10 AGI(2 vis.AC),
they would be exactly equal, ie. both would result in 19 visible AC points.


Next, we talk about hit points and stamina.
At level 50, the level this list is geared towards, you receive roughly 20 hps from 5 STA. Therefore if we assume the value of hit points is 1 AC = 10pts = 20hps = 5 STA
we can see that 1 hp = .5 pts and 1 STA = 2 pts

I chose to have Wisdom have an equal value to Stamina. However, Wisdom produces FAR fewer mana than STA does hps.
At level 50, a ranger gets 8 mana per 1 WIS.
Since 5 WIS = 1 AC, then 40 mana = 1 AC
Therefore 1 mana = .25 pts.

STR and DEX. These both have an effect on how much damage you can produce during battle. DEX increases the chance for procs and criticals with your bow. STR increases the MAX damage you can deal. I value highly these as well, but less than hps and mana, so they each received the arbitrary values of 1 STR/DEX = 1.5pts.

CHA and INT are completely useless in combat situations for rangers, therefore they equal 0 pts.

Resists in general are highly situational. They are COMPLETELY useless against a pure melee mob, nothing to resist see.. However, they are VERY important against most casters. I personally maintain a set of resistance gear that I switch to when needed, but I don't wear it most of the time.

Resists.... Magic has long been set aside as the most desired and hardest to get resist. It gets 1 SVM = .75pts

Fire/Cold are the next most useful and easier to get than Magic. 1 SVF,C = .5 pts

Poison and Disease are the red-headed stepchildren of resists. They are only worth .25 each.
