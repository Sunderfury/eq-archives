Stories from Norrath

After clearing camp and removing all traces of occupation I don my pack, string my bow and fade back into the brush just off the trail. This had been a fine camp with a lovely display of wisps in the night. Must remember this spot. While foraging for some berries I hear a shout in the distance. I settle and listen to the forest... waiting for it to tell me what the commotion is about.

The first sign is an alder’s leaves, moving ever so slightly against the wind, then the fall of a pebble from an outcrop. I ready my bow and loosen my weapons in their scabbards. Over the rise comes running a dark skinned maiden looking over her shoulder behind her. I can't see him yet but the ground is starting to vibrate with each footstep so I know he will be coming around the hill in the distance soon.

As the maiden runs past me on the path I reach out and pull her into the camouflage of the brush. She turns on me with a swift stab of the dagger, which I barely dodge as I back up to show her I mean no harm. "M'Lady, We must not waste any time, your friend will soon clear the rise and then we will both be in trouble. May I assist you?" I say while I bow quickly. With her breath coming quickly, I did jump out of nowhere, she seems to size me up and find me acceptable. "I would kindly accept your assistance kind sir. But.." "I know you have an admirer following you." I chuckle and then hurriedly begin casting around for the items and upon finding them I camouflage us both to the best of my abilities. " Just stay completely still and do not attack what ever you do." About that time the ground shakes and several trees loose leaves and all the dead limbs of the previous winter. Then I can see him. I wonder about my decision to help this obviously Noble Lady.

I had no idea a Hill Giant could reach the heights of the one walking towards us. I pull us back from the path a way farther and just in time as the giant almost steps on our previous blind. Minutes seemed much longer as we wait for the Giant to wander down the slope and we loose him in the tree.

"M'Lady, methinks thou should choose thine admirers more carefully in the future." I jest to loosen up the tenseness hanging in the air and to calm my nerves. "Now introductions seem to be in order. I am Gyde of the Rangers of Surefall. At your service." Now I let loose with a flourished bow hoping not to make a fool of myself. "I thank you for your timely assistance, Noble Ranger. I was close to collapse. It is a long run from King Xorbs Gourge. My name is Titian. Could I perhaps join you on your way to the Northern Plains of Karana?"

While the Northplain was not my original destination I suddenly felt a need to visit a druid kinsman at the Ring in Northplain. "I would consider it an honor to accompany you." I see a smile on the Lady's face as I move to pickup the packs that were dropped when the Giant walked by. I have been on the plains for a while so it wouldn't hurt to have some company on the trail, even if it were not going to my final destination. I think my Stepmother would call that rationalizing. She liked using large educated words around me. She was always trying to push me to follow the rest of the family into the Druids Circle. She often used Saban, my stepbrother, as an example. I pointed out to her our situations were different, which usually headed off any further comments.

I pull my thoughts back to the beautiful lady as we walk down to the trail. She stands taller than I do by a handspan or more, with dark skin and black flowing hair. She wears a robe of shear silk, which would get me into serious trouble if I were to pay any closer attention. At a certain point in the trail I see the game trail that leads off into the woods towards the bridge. While it is not shorter there is less likely a chance of meeting up with the recently departed Giant on this tree strewn path that on the larger well traveled path. While walking we talk of our recent travels and I learn of her homeland of Erud where she is travelling. I had heard of it from my guildmaster in Surefall before but had never met anyone from there before since I loved life on the plain and was rarely seen inside of the walls of Qeynos. It had been long since my last trip to any inhabited place so I found the conversation with Titian stimulating.

Rounding a hill we come upon a pride of lions that are on the hunt. I quickly camouflage the lady and head her in the direction of the bridge. About that time the Matriarch of the pride sees me and heads my direction. I strike a few blows, half heartedly to be sure. I would hate to have to harm this fine lion but I must delay him while the lady makes her escape. When I decide that she has enough of a head start I turn and with a leap jump over the head of a gorge hound that was coming up behind me hoping to suprise me. Now I was in for a run.. I still have a good run to the bridge where the animals will not follow for there is too much traffic and they are generally afraid... unless extremely agitated. I feel the lions claws strike my back almost knocking me to the ground. Then I hear the hound making lunges at my legs, which inspires me to run just a little faster, but I am reaching the limit of my endurance and soon will fall.

About to the bridge, I had given up hope of reaching it and decided to turn and give a good account of myself when M'Lady Titian appears out of camouflage and casts heal on me. With this little extra we make the run to the bridge and our pursuers fade back into the forest thwarted. "Now I am indebted to you M'Lady" I say with a smile. "Nay Noble Ranger. For you were willing to give your life that I may survive, I am in your debt." Titian smiles as she tends my wounds. We sit and talk while we are resting occasionally laughing about our experiences around Norrath. While we wait friends that are going to Erud also arrive inviting her to join them on their trip. Titian looks my way sadly, "M'Lord I hesitate to leave you. You have helped me so much this day." I stand and bow kissing the gloved hand of the Noble Lady Titian, "It truly was an honor to accompany you and should you ever come this way again I will be here to guide and protect you, M'Lady." Soon after the departure of Titian and her friends I find myself moving .. walking up the trail.. to a spot I know of that has a beautiful display of wisps in the night...
