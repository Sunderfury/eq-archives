The Ranger's Reference 21-May-01 01:27 PM

Submitted by: Sunmoon

Many, many thanks to DeadZed for compiling this excellent resource! An explanation from him:

The Ranger's Reference

It's set up to make a pamphlet from ordinary size paper which you fold in half to form the pamphlet. Once you open the document, go to File-Print and tell the program to print ODD pages. After the odd pages are printed, flip the stack of papers over and tell the program to print EVEN pages (you may have to print differently depending on how your printer prints. This was the method that worked with my printer).

The pamphlet contains: Skills & Disciplines Reference, Spells Reference, Commands and Emotes Reference, A few armor quests , Crydee's Epic Quest walkthru (short version) , Fletching Guide , Baking Recipes, Brewing Recipes, Pottery Recipes, Sdaasiil's Armor Reference (abbreviated), Sdaasiil's Weapon Reference (abbreviated), 2 Hand Slash Weapons Chart, Dropped Bows Chart, Some Basic Game Interface References, Kunark and Velious Zone LOC guide
