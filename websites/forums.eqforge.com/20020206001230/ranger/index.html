Combat Fury AAXP skill is bugged 28-Jan-02 11:52 PM

Submitted by: Coryn

Combat fury is currently bugged for the classes that could score critical hits previous to the Luclin expansion. This includes rangers for bows, with anecdotal evidence suggesting warriors are affected for both melee and bow attacks as well. Paladins' holyforge disc also is adversely affected.

Combat fury currently ignores the natural chances of these classes to critical hit.

According to tests, a ranger with 200 dex has a natural archery crit rate of around 7%. Since CF1 only gives around a 5% chance to crit for archery, the effect is actually a reduction in archery effectiveness.

Warriors have noted a parsed difference of 3.2% melee crits with 255 dex, falling to 1.5% with CF1. Combat Fury 2 and 3 should reduce or eliminate the discrepancy, but currently the skill goes directly against the CF description in the AAXP window.

As this affects three classes, hopefully we can bring this to SOE's attention and get it fixed very soon.

Thanks to Myrrh Imbris for the tests and excellent statistical analysis found here. Also thanks to Kruelty of the Karana server for sharing evidence that the bug affects warriors as well.
