Bow Making

Now that you have some skill at making arrows you may want to start trying your hand at bows. I would suggest waiting until your reach fletching level 35 or so before starting to make bows. However, I would like to say that I waited until I reached level 200 before I started making bows as a serious source of plat. You can gain fletching level while making bows as well as when you make arrows.

Introduction to Bows:

The simplest bow is made from a stave and a string. Just two parts are needed to make a simple bow. The stave dictates a base value for 3 aspects of a bow. A bow has a Damage value, a Delay value and a Range value. A stave gives you a base Damage, a base Delay and a base Range. The range of a stave can never be changed but the other 2 aspects, Damage and Delay, can be modified. The 5 different staves currently in the game are Hickory, Elm, Ashwood, Oak and Darkwood. In succeeding sections I will detail how you as a fletcher can change the final damage or final delay of a bow.
