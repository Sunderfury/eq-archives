Leaders within the Black Sun House also wear colored sashes to
indicate their status within the Black Sun. The High Commander and
Commander wear blue sashes and Squad Leaders wear black sashes.

You will also have a Squad Leader who you will report to regarding any
queries you may have, to get addition equipment if needed etc. Any
issues you may have needs to be taken up with your Squad Leader. If
he/she is unable to help you then he will refer you to the Commander
then High Commander.

Some squads will be composed of adventurers and some will be composed
of non-adventurer characters whose purpose is to support the House.
Non-adventurers will be assigned a Work Leader who will act as their
coordinator.

Some characters will be assigned solo roles, outside the Squads.
Examples may be Scouts or even Spies that operate better alone and
will report directly to the High Commander or Commander.
The Black Sun House encourages it's members to create worker
characters in addition to their adventuring characters to provide
monetary and skill support to the House.

REGULATIONS

We have a few rules, not many, but they are set in stone, those who stray
most then once will be warned then dismissed... I know this sounds a bit
harsh, but these rules are a guide to make you understand what the clan
stands for.

Loyalty, Comradere and Strength are the main ideals that Blacksun House
Members stand for.

1. Honour and Respect for your members and other players on UO.

The Black Sun clan is a very honourable clan, we treat others in the
clan with respect at all times, with fellow travellers we also show
them that same respect. (Player killers excluded). One rule I use in
real life as well, and it's a very simple one:
Treat others as you would like to be treated yourself.

2. Adventuring issues.

When adventuring with fellow members, always watch out for those who
maybe getting in trouble, help out where ever possible, this is the
basis of the Black Sun Principles. Remember, a timely heal that saves
a stranger may make a friend for the Black Sun House!

3. Fallen comrades/Adventurers

If someone dies in your presence, even a non-members, don't loot them.
(Pkillers are an exception.) If possible, gather their equipment and
store it in one bag. Then tell the ghost your icq number so you can
arrange a meeting place. I have made many friends by doing this.

People who loot other players maybe asked to leave the clan.
In addition, Black Sun members must follow the regulations of our
parent Crimson Sky. One of these regulations is the wearing of guild
colors.

 

GUILD COLORS

As a new member of the Black Sun House of the Crimson Sky, you will be
issued a gray cape and a Crimson robe. The gray cape indicates your
probationary status within the guild.

Once proven, your gray cape and crimson robe will be exchanged for a crimson
cape, indicating full membership.

In addition, please check the Black Sun web sight for additional house
dress code. Black Sun House members will wear a black colored kilt to
indicate membership of our House within the Crimson Sky.

MEMBERSHIP FEES

You will also be required to pay guild membership fees
to help support guild aquisitions (equipment, houses, castles etc.).
This fee is %10 of your income. eg, if you adventure and make 1000gp
you will pay 100gp to your Squad Leader or to myself or Alydar if your
a solo member.

The guild will provide you with access to guild resources (equipment,
forges, etc.)  And also free repairs through one of the Clan Blacksmiths.
(Note: you maybe asked to join a mining party once a week, either as a
miner or guard)

To help you progress as a member. I can personally provide you with runes to
all the major dungeons and cities.
