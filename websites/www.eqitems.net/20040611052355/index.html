Welcome to the new EQItems.net!

Posted on: March 27, 2004 05:17pm EST



After months of work, we have finally upgraded our databases to support all of the recent changes to EverQuest. The zone, NPC and spell databases are not yet online and we still have to go through a lot of item screenshots that were submitted to us in the past, but the item database is online and working. We will be adding the list of NPC's and zones where items are found or quested in the near future.

We are in beta testing so please let us know if you run into any problems.
