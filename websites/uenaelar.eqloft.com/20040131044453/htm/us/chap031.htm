THE PLANE OF FEAR :
I log with UENAELAR. Arrived in Everquest, I receive a message from my guild : "Come to join us, we are in the plane of fear". Cool !! The access to the plane of fear is placed in Feerrott, it is guarded by 5 or 6 spectres. It is enough for a sturdy person to pass through the monsters and to dive into the gate of teleportation to reach the plane. I dive…
I arrive in the plane for the first time, it is a sort of jungle with a red sky… The arrival is placed not far from our camp, I join so my companions easily … I stayed on the plane with my guild 5 hours. Here, all the monsters are only level 50-55 !!! Here are some of them :
  • Nightmare, an enormous black horse with the red eyes gives me a buck. I was ejected in more than 200 metres of the camp !!
  • A scareling, small red imp, gives me a blow for 441 life points !!
  • A gelatinous cube attacks us, the rests of the last meal are seen through its body.
  • For the first time, I get acquainted of Gorgones, giant Gorillas, monstrous Zombies, Warriors octopuses and quite white spiders…
  • The god CAZIC THULE lives in this plane. From time to time, he shouts the name of a present player in the zone and it is the immediate death of him !! Here, the resurrections are common…
  • The Amygdalan knights are SK as me. They drop sometimes powerful armors reserved for the SK. Soon can be, I would have the chance to possess one…
The exit of the plane is done by teleportation, we reappear in the Lavastorms, tired. But some were able to find powerful weapons or armors…
 
THE FIRE GIANTS :
Solusek B is the zone where is Lord Nagafen, the red dragon. To reach its cave, it is necessary to cross the Kobolds section that you know already, the one of the Spiders and one filled with Fire Giants. This evening, our guild launches a raid on the Fire Giants… We are about twenty chars level 48 - 50. The Kobolds section is fast crossed, the poor Kobolds who dare to attack us are cut in pieces within less than 15 seconds… The Spiders section do not put any problem neither, in the way we kill a Noxious Spider and a Stone Spider… We are now all gathered at the entrance of the Fire Giants section, unknown for me. The enchanters begin to cast and we are all levitating. I ask FLUBB why and he answers me that is it a surprise… Ready ? Go !! We all charge… Wouahhhh !!! A very small bridge steps over a river of lava, just a little wrong step and plouf, it is the warm bath ! I understand now the utility of levitation… At the end of the bridge, wait us two fire giants, colossus that look like harem guards from the "thousand and one nights". The battle makes fury, one of ours succumbs under the powerful knocks of the monsters, but we are finally victorious. Divine Grace : 2 - Giants : 1 We rest while a cleric resurects the unfortunate. Somebody asks around if we have monks or SK to pull the monsters… The accounts are fast made : A monk and a SK… Guess ? And me here is playing the hero in the bottom of a dungeon filled with Giants !! The principle is simple : Halfway the monsters, I cast my Feign Death to feign the death. The monk go ahead to pull the giants (sometimes 4 or 5). He returns then back (like RUN LIKE HELL) and feigns death at my spotl (it is an innate capacity for the monks). The giants roam around us during some moments and go back home one by one. When the last one stays there alone, I get up and I pull it towards the group… This method works well enough, but the least error is sanctioned by the death… (2 times for me). We arrive in the fortress of the giants. It is very beautiful, but the furniture is a little high for me !! We kill the King TRANIXX, the king of the giants as well as its bodyguard, The Warlord SKARLON. I do not remember the loot we found on them, but the lads look like satisfied… Finally, we uncork in the entrance of NAGAFEN's lair… Hé hé hé, I see appear the interest in your eyes… I reassure at once the members of the PCRD (Protection committee of the Red Dragons), Nagafen is not here. It was killed by an another group yesterday and it has still not all respawned… We teleport us in the lavastorms and I am going to sleep, it is all the same 5 AM in Paris...
