ALONE IN THE DARK
It was during a night like many others in great divide, cold and snowy. Uenaelar was roaming near the river that heads to Thurgadin when he saw something very strange. there was a dwarf here, alone back against a large black monolith. As he was coming closer to him, he heard the dwarf saying with a little voice :
"Don't come closer dark elf, I prefer being alone tonight"
Then, as Uenaelar wasn't moving, the dwarf started to speaks about a tale :
The story of a guild, created by a group of friends in order to travel across the world and having fun. The state if mind in the guild is opened one, friendly and mutual aid oriented. Secretly helped by powerful allies, its members can sometimes get magic items they wear with pride.
Then, the guild grew up, it accepts any good intentioned fellows. It try to get an organization, raids are planed. Well small raids, no ideas of dragon slaying. Just a way to have fun and to find an item if there is the possibility. There is no obligation for anyone to come, only the ones that wanna do it. The guild is young, experience less, these raids are sometimes successful, sometimes deathly.
Day after day the guild grow up, and now raises the 50 members. Problems appear, some members dispute each other in public, other ones criticize the organization during raids. And slowly, the initial spirit is lost, the first fights start just about items, some members see in the guilds a way to get new wealth. The eyes focused on the xp that takes place instead of the wink of a friend, a several members act in low level raids, and became angry because if the xp-lost it made.
The dwarf explains that he feels guilty, guilty for have been unable to see that previously. The heart broken, he has left his guild. Tonight, he is here, alone, he is far from his friends.
Uenaelar leaves without saying anything. He feels the night darker, colder. A frozen hand takes his corpse, Tonight he has seen a dwarf crying…
