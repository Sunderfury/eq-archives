About EQMerchant.com

    EQ Merchant is your one stop shop for everything related to Everquest tradeskills.  Here you will find links, consolidated and up-to-the-date information, and guides that will help you get up to speed on all the things a merchant in everquest is interested in.  Good luck, enjoy the site, and let me know if you have any questions or suggestions.

09/22/2000 - Barky

    Well, I’ve spent the past 4 weeks browsing around the entire web, looking at the numerous message boards (some with amazingly short message retention times) and looking at the web sites like Allakhazam’s, EQ Casters, and EQ Traders for the new blacksmithing information.

    I kept on telling myself, I wish there was on place that I could go that would tell me everything, give me a message board to discuss it on, and integrate that message board content back into the main site.  Hence, the idea for EQ Merchants.  I’m a tradeskill junky by heart, from Ultima Online to Everquest and I hope that this resource will be useful to other people interested in tradeskills.

    DISCLAIMER: I just want to make it clear that a ton of research was done by the people on Test Server, by the people who are with the various web sites, by the people who submit information to them, and by the people who post to the various message boards. This site was meant as a place to bring ALL of those resources into one place.  Very little in this site was originally researched by me, or originally found by me in Everquest.  I owe kudos and gratitude to the people from EQ Casters Realm, Allakhazam, KC’s Message Boards, Muttonchopps, Agathorn Silverbow, Dekered Steelheart, Countt and all the other people who have posted to boards that I have gotten information from. Also, all the formatting, and display of the information has been created by me.  There will be no copy-and-pasted information here.

    I want this site to be a good resource for everything related to Everquest Trade skills, but my main interest is blacksmithy.  If you’re interested in making a guide for another trade skill, then by all means please send it to me. I’m doing this in my spare time, and since I have a software company to run, I might be a bit stretched at times - but I will try my best to keep this site updated as much as possible!

    Bear with me as I start to collect information and put it in all the categories. If you see something that is not correct on the site, PLEASE e-mail me and let me know.

    Things that I’ve gotten up so far:

    • Dyes and art of coloring Fine steel plate armor
    • Crafting Fine steel plate armor

    I should have more information up by the end of the weekend.
