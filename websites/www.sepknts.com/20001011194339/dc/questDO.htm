Rivervale
Shakey the Scarecrow's Stuffing Quest
Belt of the River /MAGIC LORE ITEM /AC 5/ +5 Str/+5 Sta/ -10 Res Cold/ Effect: Summon Drink. Classes: D, C, Ro, Wr. Halfling ONLY
Wee Harvester /MAGIC LORE ITEM / 10 Dmg / 45 Dly / +1 Str, +1 Sta, +3 Wis/ Effect: Summon Food.
At the Tagglefoot farm in Rivervale, hail Shakey the Scarecrow.  One of the nearby NPC's will begin to converse with you.  The NPC will tell you that Shakey hasn't benn himself and needs to be fixed.  

Step 1: Get some Hay- Travel to the miller Farm in West Karana.   There are 3 ways to get the hay you need:

  1. Kill Scarecrows who carry hay as loot.
  2. Look around the cornfield, sometime the Miller's drop hay.
  3. You can sometimes buy the hay from one of the Millers

Step 2: Get a flask of the Blessed Oil of Life- 

Step 3: You must have the hay cursed by the High Priest of Cazic-Thule named Roror.  Roror wanders the Feerrott.  Hail him and say "curse my hay".  He agrees to curse your hay for 66 gold pieces.  Give him the 66 gold and hay at the same time, he will give you back a sack of cursed hay.

{WARNING: It seems that our happy go-lucky Roror has changed recently.  From all reports his faction was changed from Beta Neutral to the faction of the leezards in CT.  There supposedly is a way to gain faction with the leezards, but I haven't heard anything about how this is accomplished yet.  Roror is pretty nasty, I watched him hand a 52 Ranger his... arse.  Said ranger happend to be my brother and he says Roror conned blue to him.}

Step 4: Return to Rivervale and go to the forge near where Shakey is.  Combine the Blessed Oil and the Cursed Hay in the forge.  The result will be a sack of Writhing Hay.

Hand in the Writhing Hay and you will receive either a DRUID ONLY item or a DRUID USABLE item.
