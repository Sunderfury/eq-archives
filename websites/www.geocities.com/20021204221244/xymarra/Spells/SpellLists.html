These spell lists are generated from the speff.dat file in my EverQuest game directory using the EQ Caster spell data file parser. The spell locations database was mostly derived by visiting all spell vendors on Norrath and Luclin and parsing the log files with the EQ Log parser program. The remaining information was gleaned from Casters' Realm, Graffe's Wizard Compilation, and The Mage Compendium.

Because these data files are generated by programs and not by hand, there are some errors that I am aware of, but am not able or willing to repair. In particular, when two spells have the same name, they share an entry in the locations database. Also, there are spells that appear in the spell data file that are not actually implemented in game. With that in mind, if you come across an error in the locations listed for any spell, please let me know.

For the sake of brevity, not every location is listed with every spell. If a spell is generally available at the guild halls for a class, other sources of the spell are not listed. Other classes that can research a spell are only listed if the spell is not for sale anywhere and cannot be researched by that class. Shadowhaven is particularly annoying, since some classes have most of their spells there, some have a seemingly random subset, and some have only the new Luclin specific spells; thus, Shadowhaven is only listed explicitly for spells that are not generally available in guild halls. Many low level Cleric, Druid and Shaman spells and most Beastlord spells are found in Shadowhaven.

Durations Key

Every spell has two descriptions of the duration, one fixed and one variable by level. The actual duration of the spell is the minimum of these two values, unless the fixed duration is zero, in which case the duration is the variable duration. Durations are listed in ticks (a tick is six seconds). Many spells vary in duration by one or two ticks.
