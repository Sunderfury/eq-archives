Necromancer Research

      At this point, a necromancer's life becomes a bit more intruguing. You're finally level sixteen, and you have to research to get that starving creature that hangs around you bigger. Along with nearly ever pet spell from this point forward, you also have to research some very vital spells that will decide your fate in Norrath.

      We here at Necromancers Realm feel for you, so we've decided to give you this table outlining what components you need to research with, as well as how important it is to have these spells, based on our infinate wisdom about necromancy.

      The first thing that needs to be done is going to your guild, wether it be in A'kanon, Cabilis, Freeport, Neriak, or Paineel. Now you need to purchase what is called The Book of Dark Bindings. This is a vital part of your research, as you cannot research without it. The next part of research involves getting Words. Words can be found on nearly every creature in norrath that is above level sixteen. The tricky part is finding out which creatures actually drop the specific words that you need for that awesome spell you need.

      Research is often times not needed for acquiring a spell, because some of them can be purchased from vendors across Norrath. These spells that can be purchased, will be noted as such. You do, however need to research, because there are a good many spells that cannot be found anywhere but on the sets of words needed to create the spell.

      The only drawback to research is that when you have the words that you need, you place them in the Book of Dark Bindings, and hit the combine, only to discover that unfortunately you lacked the skills to make the spell and the words vaporize. Now not only do you not have the spell you were trying for, you're out words that took you hours to acquire. Fear not, there is a way to improve your research skills and not use those invaluable words.

      There's 2 ways to go about this now. First if you're of good alignment, meaning that people other than your townsfolk tolerate you. If so, then you may be able to acquire what is called a Concordance of Research from North Karana. For those of you who are of Evil alignment, there is a place you can go as well.

      The Concordance of research is acquired at the gypsy camp in North Karana. You must speak to Mrysila, about the concordance. She tells you that if you bring her lightstones and greater lightstones, she will give you one of the volumes that you seek. It is a good idea to keep giving her the stones until you recieve all of the volumes that she has. There is also a book that she gives you that lists all of the components to make the runes which are made in the Concordance. This allows you to increase your research skill, but not lose the words that are used to make your spells.

      For those of you with Evil alignment, you must go to the Southern Desert of Ro, and seek out a Dark Elf Sorceress, if you talk to her, then she will assist you in learning the skill of research, and will give you the tools that are required for increasing your skills. You basically have to do the exact same things that the good alignment people have to do. Give her a greater lightstone for the Concordance of Research, and give her lightstones for a random text, telling you the combinations of components that you have to have to make the runes you need to practice with.

      Keep in mind, with both of these methods, the runes that you produce must be combined in your Book of Dark Bindings for you to actually increase your skill. Combining the runes in the Concordance does nothing, and you have wasted your time.

      Below is a table outlining each of the particular words that you need for your research of certain spells. You can create the spells above your current level, this is often recommended if you are hunting creatures that are dropping words that are above your level. Keep in mind that the higher the level of the spell, the higher your research skill needs to be. So often times if you find words lying on the ground or at a merchant, check to see if they are within your range of research. We recommend that you do not research any spells that are two spell levels or more above you. Meaning that if you're level twenty, we recommend that you only research the level twenty, twenty-four, and twenty-nine. Going above this level, will result in failures more times than not. Your research skill can go to two-hundred, so we recommend that you do the Concordance quests to get you started.
