Wednesday, 05-23-2001


News About Next Patch

    I know we're beating a dead horse here, but just to let everyone know, don't let any corpses lie before the patch on the 30th. We know as necromancers, you all want to see as many corpses on the ground as possible, but please try and keep Norrath a clean place for the patch, as you may not be able to get back what was on your corpse afterwards.


      Posted by Zenatal

Thursday, 04-12-2001


Necromancer's Realm Back In Action

    Well, it's been quite the long time since we've had a news update here at Necromancer's Realm, and we apologize for letting things slip through the cracks. As many of you have noticed, we've actually started updating some of the previously un-updated sections of the site.
    We're looking to add more strategies, and only you can help us out. Click Here, to go to our Strategy submission page, and send us a strategy of your own experience, or that of a friend.
    We're also looking into some of the new Velious quests, so if any of you have knowledge about them, feel free to Click Here to submit your knowledge of these quests.
    There is a ton of things going on around the Everquest world at the moment, and we're going to try and keep everyone updated as to how it affects us Necromancers. Keep an eye on the site for additions, and breaking news. And for those of you who have various sections of the site bookmarked, please make sure that you're bookmarks point to the correct places on the site, we did some moving around of our files, and just want to make sure that you get all the new stuff we're going to be offering in the next couple of weeks. Thank you for your continued support, and enjoy what we're going to be doing for you all.

      Posted by Zenatal
