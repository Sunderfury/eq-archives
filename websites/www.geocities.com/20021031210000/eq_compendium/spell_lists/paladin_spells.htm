Paladin Spells

General:

          Paladins are a hybrid class whose spells should be used to assist rather as than as a primary attack or healing.  As a Paladin, spells often take on Clerical properties, having some of the very same types of spells as Clerics but will never be as powerful than Cleric spells.  If you have any other information, please contact me.
