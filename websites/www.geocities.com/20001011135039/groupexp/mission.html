Our Mission

And the Disclaimer

Here at ExperienceQuest, our mission is simple:

  • To provide information to players that is as accurate as possible.

  • To dispel myths and rumors about penalized Race/Calss Combinations.

To these ends, this site presents data obtained from web in an attempt to shed some light on experience gained when soloing and when grouped, as well as the effect that grouping has on a player's ability to gain levels.

Bear in mind that because the data used to present this information was obtained from third parties (principally HackersQuest), we can not and do not guarantee the accuracy of the information presented here. Furthermore, we do not use, nor do we encourage the use of ShowEQ. And finally, this information is not presented to discourage grouping, nor is it presented to "bash" Verant, Inc.

Finally, having said all that, the experience model presented here seems to closely approximate the actual behaviour of the experience system used in-game (according to all the information currently available).

Ok, so get on with it!
