So how is group experience awarded?

Apparently, when grouping with other players, experience is awarded according to the following formula:

pExp = mExp * (tpExp/tgExp)

where:

  • pExp = amount of experience player receives

  • mExp = experience value of the MOB

  • tpExp = total current player experience

  • tgExp = total current group experience

In other words, a group member is awarded an amount of experience proportional to the contribution of the player's lifetime experience to the lifetime experience of the group as a whole. Consider the following example group fighting a level 8 critter:

In the left three columns is the information about the composition of the group. The fourth column shows the current lifetime xp of each player, with the total group lifetime experience at the bottom; and the last column shows the ratio of each member's lifetime experience to the group's total. The ratio in the fifth column represents approximatey how much of the MOB xp will be awarded to each group member.
