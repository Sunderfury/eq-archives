Evil Necro Fun

When done leveling to 16 in Oasis go get your new spells. Then hop on the boat that leaves Oasis docks. Get off in Timouros Deep. Head left along shore to Ogre villiage and hop on the "Ghost Ship" there. This takes you to The Overthere in Kunark. This Outpost is evil run and you have free access to bank, merchants, etc. To the east of the Outpost you can hunt anything that cons yellow or less. Run to the guards for help so stay close to the Outpost. This place will level you like a newbie zone does for 1-5 from 16-20. Use the spell strategy from the Oasis guide.The loot here runs about 1-5plat per kill. Also most blue kills gave me a full blue bubble of exp. Just make sure to con everything before attacking. The Kunark rhino ranges in lvl from 16-35. If its red, leave it alone!

Another note. Even though they con ready to attack. Only the Sarrnaks have actually attacked me unprovoked.

Happy hunting.
