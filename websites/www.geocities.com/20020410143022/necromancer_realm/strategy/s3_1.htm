Solo Stragegy in Ill Omen, and South Karana


On mobs near the lake side, towards the MILL, in Lake Of Ill Omen. After getting your lvl24 pet spell, go to South Karana and solo at the Aviaks until 26 before returning to Lake Of ILL Omen.**note** while in South Karana there are a fwe mobs that may attack you, so be VERY careful in the ways you travel across and watch for shout warnings in the zone. Loosing tons of XP due to medding attacks is...stupid. At 26 return to Lake Of ILL Omen and solo along the BACK DOOR. There is usually a group, you can sometimes snag a sarnak that wonders off to the side or other places. Also Lake Of ILL Omen is a great place to pick up your research words. Stay at lake of ill omen, unless boredom drives you out then you can go back to South Karana, either way solo til 29 and get your new and improved DUEL WEILDING pet%, along with a new snare type spell & higher life drain & higher DoTs & even a pet heal. I am currently soloing aviaks and named mobs, paladin/treants/druid, in South Karana and just made 30. I cannot wait to get my new Mana for HP transfer at 34. Good luck to you.
