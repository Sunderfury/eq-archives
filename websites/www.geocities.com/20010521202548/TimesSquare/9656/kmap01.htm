FINDING YOUR LOCATION

Finding your location within the GBC compound is simple. Each GBC Information Kiosk, like the one that you are at now, is clearly marked with a number. Just locate that number on the Kiosk Location Map below to determine your present location.

The Main Compound Map is also shown for comparison. More information can be obtained by clicking on the MAP button, at the bottom of this page.
