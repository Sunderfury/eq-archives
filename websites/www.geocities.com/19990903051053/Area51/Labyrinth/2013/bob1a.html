Robert Barton-
"Decorator & Designer"

Robert Barton is a very talented designer and decorator.

Robert began his career as a decorator at an early age installing and decorating aquariums. He then studied Interior Design in a Fine Arts program offered by McGill University in Montreal. From there he did some minor work in the film industry and freelanced as a visual display artist for the retail sector.

He moved to Los Angeles where he worked on several theatrical productions. He also began designing costumes and masks with 9 stores selling his product.
He eventually went on to becoming an Art Director for Disney Studios.

Tired of the crowds and smog, Robert returned to the tropics and began designing and decorationing special events and show productions for major vacation resorts around the world.

Currently Robert freelances as designer/decorator/planner/performer in the Special Event Industry in beautiful Vancouver. His work has been featured in all the major hotels and venues in both Vancouver and Whistler.
