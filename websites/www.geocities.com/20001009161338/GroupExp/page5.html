So, what does this all mean - really?

You are, of course, free to draw your own conclusions about the information presented here. The bottom line of all this appears to be summed up in one word: GROUP. Verant has stated from the beginning that this game was not designed for soloing, but rather to promote socializing and group play. The experience system, as it appears to work, promotes this ideal not only by making soloing a difficult activity ("looks like an even fight" - yeah, right, hehe), but also by structuring experience in such a way that a group of friends that adventures together on a regular basis should level at approximately the same rate, thus allowing and even encouraging us to form friendships/regular adventuring groups.
