Blacksmithing Guide

 

General:

          For all blacksmithing, a forge is required, and can be located near most smith shops.  Also, everything that you need to make these items (molds, ore, water, etc) can be located in or near smith shops.  Just put the components in the forge, and hit the combined button.  If you have any information on Blacksmithing, please contact me.

 

Things to Note:

·        Blacksmithing is an expensive skill, with some spending between 150 to 500 pp.

·        Banded Armor pieces are not a good item to practice on when trying to raise your skill level

·        When asked to add more than one of the same item, keep the items unstacked

·        Blacksmithing can be very helpful to many different classes, and can help further other skills

·        Freeport is agreed to be the best place to blacksmith, as it has vendors that sell all the items necessary to blacksmith, and multiple forges. 

·        Fine Plate Armor seems to be the only type of Armor that can be Dyed

 

Racial Smithing:
