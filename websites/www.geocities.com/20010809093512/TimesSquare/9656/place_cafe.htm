FRONT St. CAFE- - 134s 203e Front & 6th Street

The Front Street Cafe has the honor of being the oldest building in the Compound. When the Compound was first renovated by the GBC, the Cafe was actually moved nearly an entire city block, piece by piece, to its present location.

Conveniently located near the main gate, and sporting a retro "50s" atmosphere and music, the the Cafe is the perfect place to get a chilidog and cherry coke. Suprisingly, the Front Street Cafe also seems to be a popular hangout for the higher-class mercenary crowd, making its clientel rather colorful at times.
