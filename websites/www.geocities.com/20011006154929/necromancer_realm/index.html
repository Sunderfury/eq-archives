Friday, 08-30-2001



Woody at /GU Comics Closes Shop

Alas, it is a sad time in the everquest community. Our good friend Woody at /GU Comics is closing up shop. The Website is still up, so you can view the archives. We at Necromancer's Realm, would like to thank Woody for all his hard work, and for making us laugh at ourselves. /GU Comics will be missed. Here is a letter from Woody giving us a little more info.

After more than a year and some 10 million+ page views, I feel that GU
Comics has run it's course. There are a ton of people I'd like to
simultaneously thank and say goodbye to; but for the sake of brevity,
I'll do that else where and at another time. I would like to thank
Everlore at this time though. They were wonderful hosts for my little
web comic. I appreciate all the hard work they put into keeping GU live.

You guys own. Thanks.

And to the fans... I love you guys. At the darkest moments and the
lowest point you were there to lift me up and support my work.
I enjoyed the time you shared with me, and I hope I brought atleast
one smile to you. And, hopefully helped you realize that EQ is a
game... and that it's okay to take yourself lightly.


Thanks again and maybe I'll be seeing you around.

Woody Hearn - Former Cartoonist GU Comics

We'd like to wish Woody the best of luck in his future ventures.
--Thank you, from Necromancer's Realm
