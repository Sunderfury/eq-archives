Robert Barton-
"A Man of Many Faces"

Robert Barton is a very talented character actor with creative eccentricity.
Robert has performed his characters for hundreds of people on a daily/nighty bases at vacation resorts and corporate functions around the world.

For over 15 years he has practiced and perfected his art of masquerade. Once in makeup and costume a metamorphosis occurs and new persona is born complete with voice, mannerisms, and attitude.

Robert can customize a character(s) for any event. His "Meet & Greet" style along with his "Roaming Interactivity" and improvisational strengths is the perfect complement for any function: A talent not to be overlooked.
