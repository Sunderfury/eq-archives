Ok, seems to work for newbies - but I'm Level 50-something...

In a player's 50's, he or she will find that the experience system works in much the same way it did when they were a newbie. Consider our newbie group a few months down the road when they are in their 50's fighting a level 60 critter:

The situation looks much the same as it did 50-some-odd levels ago. In terms of total xp, the Troll SK is still at the top of the heap, as expected since he requires so much xp to level. As well, the Halfling Warrior is still at the bottom, since he requires so much less xp than the Troll. And in fact, you'll notice that the slopes here aren't quite as steep as they were when we were newbies. Remember the curve from level 1 to 60? And do you remember how steep it got toward the end? You are now seeing the effects of the xp formula as it applies to high level characters. Quite honestly, in 50 kills they have made very little progress toward gaining the next level; and in fact, it would require a little over 1,000 kills (as a group) in order for all these players to level (this is an estimate, of course).
