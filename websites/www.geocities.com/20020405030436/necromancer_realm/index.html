Monday February 4, 2002

Everquest Legends Servers Open Up

    Everquest is moving into a new Era of gaming. The Everquest Legends servers are dedicated to the player. Players get their own website dedicated to their player. An interactive map that shows where each character is located, and where the guildmembers of that player are. Complete websites for guilds, including a forum and calendar of events for the players to use. All this for the measly price of $39.95/month.
    Personally, we at Necromancer's Realm, feel that this is a bit overpriced. What do you thing? Click Here to discuss it in our forum. We want to know what you think.
