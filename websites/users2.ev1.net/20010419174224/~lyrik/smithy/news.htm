2001-01-01 - The New Year

Whoot!  The new year is here...it can only go up from here =)  Hehe.  Well...I've gone through and gotten a *LOT* of screenshots and put in hyperlinks in the spreadsheet so that you can actually see the items...should help a lot of you getting started.  It also shows you that an item in question actually *DID* exist in the game at one point.  All in all...I think it's a good addition.  I'm always accepting screenshots for new items.  If you do, please do it in the .JPG format...also cut and paste a pic of the item as it appears in your inventory on the upper right hand side.  Microsoft Paint is great for this!  You can get to it by going to START->PROGRAMS->ACESSORIES->PAINT.  Prices are still messed up in all of the spreadsheet, but I fixed a lot of the sheets (Tailoring got a second sheet for cultural tailoring) and the Alchemy sheet is looking pretty much like I want it to.

2000-10-28 - Welcome to my web page

This is my first attempt to make a web page, so please be patient with me =). I created this place for two reasons. One was to give a place where all trades people in EQ could share my spreadsheet. I have been working on it for a few months and am pretty proud of it...hehe. It still has a way to go, but it is very useful. I would appreciate a note for anything on it that is factually incorrect. I have tried really hard to keep it up-to-date and correct, but it ain't easy =). The second reason I made this is because my favorite site for blacksmithing (KC's Realm) has not been touched in months and the message board software he was using *cough*sucks*cough*. I also wanted a messageboard that was more conducive to experimentation and for getting information around to other smiths. I might be making similar pages for the other skills, but I will never combine it with this one...I want to preserve the individuality and don't want to homogenize it so that it is simply a messageboard for all trade skills. Also...on the ezboard...if you click on the pictures I have used on all the messageboards, there are links to the source of the picture. In some cases you can *buy* these items from the site. I don't get any money for this...just thought it was kinda cool =)
