List of known bugs in EQ smithing

1. No known way to enchant MQ ore found (breaks enchanted seafarer recipes).
2. Field Plate Boots reported as broken.
3. Jade reported to be not working on Northman ring armor (affects making Northman Chain for Rallos Zek).
4. Normal Ogre Splint does not allow Ogres/Trolls to use.
5. No known working recipe for Elven Chain boots (should use 2 rings, but doesn't).
6. Dwarven Smithy Hammer lost when making non-enchanted Brellium Chain Jointing.
7. Iron Oxide (EQMerchant) - only 1 resin version of this dye works.
8. Russet Oxide (EQMerchant) - only 1 resin version of this dye works.
9. Permafrost Crystals (EQTraders) - 1 resin blue version doesn't seem to show up on BP (and possibly other armor pieces.
10. Large Fine Plate Visor - Recipe appears to be broken...does not work with one sheet like other fp visor recipes.
11. Forges in Halas are reported as being broken for dying armor...person reporting this stated that they were making 1 resin blue fine plate pauldron.  They did the same thing in Freeport and it worked fine.  Person was sure they were not using the cultural forge (reported by Brazarg on KC's Realm).
