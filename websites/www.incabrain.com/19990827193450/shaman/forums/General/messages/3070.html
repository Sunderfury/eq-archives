This line of spells casts very quickly, gives you a big boost to STR and AC, which helps you hit harder and get hit less, and sets up a very nice hp regeneration which does stack with the regeneration spell. So instead of zoning and losing the exp for that tough mob, you can actually finish it off yourself. Not to mention you can use it to regenerate your health quickly while running around.

I think of this line of spells as a good panic button while soloing, like spirit of cheetah.
