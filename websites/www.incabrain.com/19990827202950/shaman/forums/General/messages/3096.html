I ask you, what shaman out there actually use this line of spells?

I use them rarely.

If we were ever going to trade in a set of spells in favor of some other spells, this would probably be the ones to lose, though. They are pretty minimally useful. They give a penalty of majorly negative mana regeneration, but do not confer that big of a benefit.

I only use them when soloing, and only when I'm not able to kite. When I solo in Guk, for example, I'll cast Frenzy as a last resort, with the last of my mana. It helps, but probably not as much as it should.

In groups, I believe it's a lot better to sit back and meditate up some more mana. For me, Frenzy's prime use is extra health regeneration, and in a group, this isn't likely to be helpful.

Thing is, I can't imagine EVER using a spell like Rage at level 44. 20 extra health per tick is nothing when the mob will be double hitting you for upwards of 100. As it is, soloing indoors involves use of Root and DOTs, with melee accounting for maybe a quarter of the mobs health or less. At level 44, I can't imagine being able to melee even a tenth of their health away (especially since we have no good weapons), not unless Rage confers double attack and about 100 points of weapons skill (which it doesn't).
