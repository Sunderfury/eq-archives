I think the magic resist is ok the way it is. Lowering it would make it too easy for solo casters especially in the case of DoTs. Shamans and enchanters may be able to help the cause with some resistance debuffs which can help greatly allowing the other casters to fufill their normal roles. But the real answer is that the casters take on an alternative role against creatures that are deep reds. If you want to take on reds the answer lies in being support for your tanks. Direct casts on the creature itself are usually ineffective, but buffing, healing, temp buffing, and damage shields are still effective. Shamans are one of the most adept at doing this too because we have some great utility spells and are pretty well rounded.
