I ask you, what shaman out there actually use this line of spells? By the time we get the spells our melee abilities are fading fast. What good are these spells? (+STR, +AC, +hp regen, - mana drain)

In a group, these spells take precedence over SoW for me. Why? I cast them on my tanks. Even with the wimpy lvl 5 Fleeting Fury, my tank buddies could see a noticable effect. It gets better. Cast Burst of Strength on a Warrior that is Berserker and watch the fur fly. My tank pals no longer scream for heals, but they -always- let me know when the 18 second timer runs out. Everyone always says they can't tell the difference with buffs. Well this one of those buffs that make you sit up and say "wow!" The effect is nothing short of amazing.

For solo use, uhh... no. I'm a group creature. I can't solo my way out of a paper bag.
