One way to reduce the dangers of not being able to zone from Erud's Crossing (without getting on the boat that is) is to bind to Qeynos and gate out. With SoW build up your distance from any pesky wisps by running around the island a couple of times, then cast gate. Not as convienient as zoning because you have to wait fer the boat, but better than dying. I did this several times between 11 and 14.

This is probably obvious, but I thought I would share anyway...
