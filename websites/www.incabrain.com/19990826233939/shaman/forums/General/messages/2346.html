I generally use slower weapons that give more damage, and I generally do the hit+slam, cast spell, repeat cycle. If I do my casting RIGHT after the hit+slam, I have less of a chance of my spell being interrupted. If I wait too long, and I have auto attack on, it appears that it wants to have the attack happen a set number of seconds, and the spell won't go off because my attack will go off again and interrupt it. I have, however, had times that my spell DOES go off, and it seems to then make the attack for that round NOT happen. It's pretty strange.
