It may not be the 'best spell in the game' but I like Frenzy (and Burst of Strength) spells. Frezy is specifically usefull for long battles when you run out of mana. User your last 25 or so mana to cast Frenzy. At level 19 it makes a very noticable difference. You didnt mention this but it also increases attack speed--with my delay 50 Bloodforge hammer, my attacks exactly keep up with those of wolves in East Karanas. This is _much_ faster. Plus with strength bonus I hit for full damage almost everytime I hit and AC bonus reduces hits. Last but not least HP regeneration is faster so I sometimes end up the fight with more HP that before I cast Frenzy. All in all a good deal at level 19.

For those at higher levels that say they prefer to meditate in fights when OOM, not much point to these spell. I am not to those level so I cannot say. But if ya happen to be soloing for some reason meditating is of course not an option (unles you run fer a while).

If you are not in fights where you use all your mana, again there is not much point. In most fight I dont get to the point of using the spell. But when I do I am _very_ glad to have it. Besides it is fun to almost be able to tank again for a few brief seconds : )
