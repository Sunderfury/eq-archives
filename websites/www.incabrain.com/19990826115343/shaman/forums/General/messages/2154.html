The trick to not being pestered for Spirit of Wolf is to roleplay, persistently say no, or a combination of the two.

As any who have encountered me on Tunare know, Rumbul is 99% of the time strictly in character. So if someone asks em for a "SoW" my usual response varies from "SoW? Wut dat?" to "Rumbul needs him mojo fer bashun."

As Detrytis pointed out, but the time you break the level 30 threshold (I'm 35), the mana is more valuable than the platinum, and more often than not I don't even have it memorized.

I too buff the majority of the trolls I pass but the point is this - do not feel bad if you turn people down when they ask for a Spirit of Wolf blessing from you. As Rumbul would say..

"It nub Rumbuls job ta makes yu run faster wit da Wulfie Feets mojokah. If Rumbul use da mojo on evryone els wut me gots leff fer bashun?"

Give it a while, you will soon find that people do not bother you as much (there is always that insistent/annoying shaman wannabe out there somewhere) nor do they begrudge you for it.
