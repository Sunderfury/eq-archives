This comes back to an ancient unresolved debate. "Does your swing interrupt your cast?" I have always been of the opinion that it can.

My primary evidence for this si when I am standing immobile, not the target of teh the MOB, not even being attacked, I cast a spell, and I will see "Your casting is interrupted." The only thing that happens is my hammer comes swinging down. And yes, it sometimes connects.

I even once saw the same and "You regain your concentration and continue casting." But I saw that ONLY once.

So, first to answer your question, your timing on your swing does not reset. Hotkey your melee attack to see it grey and blink when you try to swing.

But I say it not only can swing while you are casting, but your swing can interrupt your spell. There are many others here that disagree.
