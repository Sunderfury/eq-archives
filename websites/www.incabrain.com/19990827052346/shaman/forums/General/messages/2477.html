If it connects, you will (a) interrupt spellcasting almost 100% of the time and (b) often "freeze" your opponent for about 3 to 4 seconds, meaning you get a free hit in combat, or the creature stops while running away.

This is a racial ability, and is available from level 1. In my experience it's a lot easier to stun much lower level opponents.
