I always start my spell immediately after I see the monster has attacked and hit or miss in my text window. This seems to be the most accurate way to time spells, because EverQuest seems to START the attack graphic when the attack pass/fail success decision is made.

I think this is a crumby game design. I think the graphics should be timed to indicate success or failure when the weapon blow lands. It is so cheesey to see "You crush monster for 25 hit points" and THEN see your guy start to swing. It is about as cool as when the monster says "hit by 15 non-melee damage" :)

Because of this, when I keyed off the graphic for casting spells, I always seemed to have much lower success rate than when I simply waited to see the attack text and then hit my hotkey. BTW, hotkeys make such a huge difference in casting the spell rather than going up to the left panel and clicking on something.
