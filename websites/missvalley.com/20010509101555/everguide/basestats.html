Base Stats

The following is a list of the base starting stats for each race. The following information should assist you in picking out the best race/class combination. This is important as some class/race combinations can be more potent than others. If you are a role player this information may not matter much to you as benefits are outwieghed by the enjoyment of playing a particular combination. However whether you are a role player or a hack and slash type this information should be of interest to you as it will let you know what the starting abilities of a given combination will be. Using this chart in combination with the descriptions of each stat you should be able to get a very good idea of the combinations strengths and weaknesses which does affect gameplay considerably. The main attributes for the classes are highlighted in red for you.
