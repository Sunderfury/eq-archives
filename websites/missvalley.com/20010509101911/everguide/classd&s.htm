Class Descriptions and Strategies

Welcome to our Class Descriptions and Strategies collection. Here we provide an indepth look at each class and some of the ways to get started in that class. We here at EverGuide feel that getting good advice about such a complex game shouldn't feel like and IRS audit - highly stressful and time-consuming. Instead, we strive to provide you with the best EverQuest advice available online. So, look around and see what we mean.

All advice is obtained from experienced RPGers and is not manditory in order to succeed at Everquest. However, since our writers do know what they're talking about, we highly suggest at least looking into the options that they lay out.
