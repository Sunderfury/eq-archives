Juli's Animation
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Summons an animated sword as a pet to protect the caster.
Area of Effect: targets self, summons a "pet".
School of Magic: Conjuration

Cost in Mana:

 24

Component/s/ Needed:

 two tiny daggers

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
HighPass Hold
Tips: Still not a very powerful pet, I rarely used the first or second level enchanter pets.  I often found it far more rewarding to work without a pet, however I have heard enough people claim they found their second level pet to be of sufficient help to warrant the potential experience drain to know that mine was a personal choice. 

These pets like to taunt your attacker, drawing it off of you and allowing you the ease of casting without disruption from damage.  At this level the pet does not have sufficient life to survive long as a damage shield for you, however, by judicious use of Fear and damage over time (DoT) spells, like Suffocating Sphere, you can find our pet quite effective.  Pets can do one thing far better than players, which is run and hit things without lagging.

Words of caution, unlike normal pets these can not be commanded to attack or defend you.  They are not true pets in that sense, rather, they will defend you after you first take damage.  Unless you take damage in a fight it will not aid in combat, so if grouped with others and you want your pet to help you need to get hit at least once.

Perhaps a bug, perhaps intentional, but if you allow your pet to get the killing blow it takes  portion of the experience from the kill.  If you, or a group member, gets the killing blow your animated pet does not seem to take a share of the experience from the split.
