Once guilded, no member of Midnight Sun remains an individual. He or she becomes a representative of the Guild and a member of our family. As such, we adopt, upon indoctrination into Midnight Sun, the following codes of conduct:
  • EverQuest Code of Conduct
    All members agree to read, understand and adhere strictly to the codes of conduct (also known as the Play Nice Policy) put in place by Verant. This policy is posted on the EverQuest website at : www.everquest.station.sony.com
  • Player/Guild Disagreements
    Don't engage in arguments with other players over /shout, /ooc, or on message boards. This makes the guild look bad and is generally annoying for everyone. If you feel it necessary to discuss differences then take it to /tells. If you have a problem with another person or guild in game, please contact a member of Midnight Sun's Internal Affairs group ( Lilipal, Faedra, or Altaadine) online and allow them to handle the situation. Some guilds have bad reputations and we do not wish to have one as well. Hostility and insults in a public channel reduces you to the same level of immaturity we all find deplorable, and it will not be tolerated in Midnight Sun. If a dispute occurs on a Message Board, post the URL on the guild website and a member of Internal Affairs wil investigate it. We refuse to be involved in the flaming that occurs on some other guild message boards, so allow one of these Officers to post an official statement.
  • Loot Policy
    Don't hog loot! Whenever you are in a group you are representing us as a guild. Don't be greedy and give everyone a fair chance to loot. Be respectful of the loot order that has been decided on by the rest of the group. If on a raid, be aware of the raid loot policy and follow it! Midnight Sun will post the loot policy for each guild hunt/raid at the time the raid is posted to our Raid forum or Event Calendar. As a general rule we follow NEED before GREED.
  • Guild Channel
    Don't over-use guildsay. This is a great channel for asking questions, seeking advice or looking for a group, but don't have active conversations with a single person, because the text can get excessive and be an irritation to others. If you have to talk to a single person, take it to /tells. Never use foul or abusive language on guildsay.
  • Guild Participation
    Do your best to attend all guild functions. If you are online during the time of a guild event, you should do your best to attend. All events will be scheduled well in advance and there is no excuse for not knowing about them.
  • Guild Website
    Check the website regularly! Guild members and leaders often post news and events on the guild forum. Guild members are expected to visit the guild website at least every other day to browse the website and see what is going on. Upon acceptance to the guild, new members should setup a EzBoard account with a name as closely resembling their character name as possible.
  • Teleports/Resurrections
    We request that all members attempt to find a resurrection or teleport within the zone prior to asking for one in guild chat. Midnight Sun members have always been extremely helpful and we must learn to NOT exploit this option that they make available. If you can not find a teleport or resurrection , then ask for one in guild chat but please do not continuously spam your request if not granted. We will NOT tolerate harassment in any shape or form. Those who have the ability to resurrect, teleport, summon corpse are not servants. People have the right to ask, but on the same hand people have the right to say NO when in a group or just plan busy without fear of a guilt trip. This is a matter of respect for your fellow members.
  • Guild Roster
    All members are automatically entered into the roster upon acceptance to the guild.  It is YOUR responsibility to keep the information on the roster updated.  This can be done by posting level changes, character additions, and corrections on the DING! forum on the website. It is VERY important that those attempting to schedule large scale raids know the level and class of the members of the guild so please kept this as current as possible.
  • Alternate Characters
    Alternate characters may be guilded; however, the number of allowable guilded alternates are in a direct relation to the level of your main guilded character. Members with mains under level 25 that were in place before the guild level limit was activated are allowed 1 guilded alternate character until level 35. At that time they follow the same alternate limit as the rest of the guild. There is currently no minimum level requirement for guilded alternates.

Main guilded character is level 25-35 -> 1 guilded alternate character allowed
Main guilded character is level 36-45 -> 2 guilded alternate characters allowed
Main guilded character is level 46-60 -> 3 guilded alternate characters allowed
  • Officers
    Officers are voted on by the other officers or appointed directly by the guild leader based on the position and circumstances.  All decisions on current and future officers are the sole discretion of the Guild Leader. Officers have the ability to guild members that have met the guild requirements for membership.  Deguildment is allowable by an officer with consent of the Guild Leader or with the concurance of at least 3 other officers if the Guild Leader is unavailable.  
  • Guildmember Interaction
    If you have any problem or dispute within the guild, do not bring this discussion to guild chat, but instead contact Ladihawk or Lilipal via /tell or email. Constructive criticism is fine, but please post it in the Members Only area or deliver it via /tell. An officer can always be made available to mediate or settle disputes between guild members in group or outside the game in guild or private chat.
  • Alliances
    Alliances with other guilds will be determined by a vote amongst the guild officers.   If there is a 75% majority vote, then an alliance with the guild in question will be established. Guilds expressing an interest in forming an alliance with Midnight Sun should be directed to post their interest on the guild website or instructed to contact a guild offiicer.
Questions regarding Midnight Sun's Code of Conduct can be directed to any guild officer or posted on the guild website. Ideas for consideration as policy should be sent directly to the Ladihawk and Lilipal.
