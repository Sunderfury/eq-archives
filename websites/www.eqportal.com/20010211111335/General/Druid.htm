DRUID  

The best travelers and protectors of Nature. They can teleport around the world or run across it and handle virtually any terrain. They are solid in the offensive spells department as well and have a great number of utility spells and abilities. They will never heal as good as a Cleric but they will often contribute more in combat and certainly travel. Considered one of the easiest classes to solo with. Their armor and weapons are somewhat weaker then a Cleric but still quite respectable and better then an Int based caster.
