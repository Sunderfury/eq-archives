March 10, 2001 - Clockwork Breastplate made!

For everyone who doesn't know, Meshugugget of Luclin has made the first clockwork watchman breastplate! Grats go out to her and Kraket, Mutt, Wyrian and Sbik for helping to gather components. She said it took about 30 attempts of vanishing devices and breastplates for her to make one. She successfully made it with a skill of 156.

Clockwork gauntlets have also been made, but I do not yet have a screenshot of them. Wyzskarlet of Brell was the first person to make these and let anyone know :) The gauntlets are a +30 strength buff. That means boots are going to be dex.

Also, Knok Verst of the Karana server has informed me that Clockwork Watchman Armor is activateable at level 20.

February 21, 2001 - Site updates

Okey everyone, doing some site updates today, yay!

Mostly GPS and component locations were updated. Also, I have been informed that Rebreathers are trivial at a skill of 177. Since this is the closest number I have, I'm going to update the recipe section (I'm sorry to whoever e-mailed me this, I can't find it anywhere.)

Also, I have heard from someone who parsed the spell data file that guantlets and boots cast +30 strength buff and a +30 dex buff (Steam Overload and Mana Infusion).. don't know which does which yet.

January 29, 2001 - Vanishing Device made!

It is my great pleasure to introduce the finished gnomish vanishing device! Thanks go out to Wyzskarlet of Brell Serilis, and to Krilan for supplying the eye and keeping the finished product. This item was not trivial at 154 skill. The item is what appears to be a secondary slot item, with 5 charges of invisbility cloak. Also, the item appears to be ALL/ALL as well. I've added a link in the recipe section to the stats of the item.
