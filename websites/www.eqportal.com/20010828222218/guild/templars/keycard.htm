his quest originates in Ak'Anon and improves faction with the gnomes; resulting in some silver, experience, and a minor item.

  • Get a keycard from the gemcutters in Ak'Anon by telling them, "I am a new warrior".

  • Give a keycard to each of the four C-Class clockworks scattered throughout Ak'Anon. You will receive a blackbox from each as you give them the keycards.

  • Once you have aquired all four blackboxes, take them as a set to the gemcutters (give all four of them to the gemcutter at once). You will receive faction, experience and some coin. In addition, the gemcutters will send you off to deliver a new blackbox to a gnome at the windmills.

  • Take the new blackbox to the gnome at the windmills and give it to him. You will receive faction, experience, some coin and a note to return to the gemcutters.

  • Take the note back to the gemcutters and give it to them. You will receive faction, experience, some coin, and they will send you off to find and destroy two malfunctioning rat robots that travel around Ak'Anon.

  • Find the two malfunctioning rat robots (Duster X and Duster V) and kill them. They are both level 6. Be sure that you DO NOT kill them within site of any guards or you will become dust for them to clean up!
