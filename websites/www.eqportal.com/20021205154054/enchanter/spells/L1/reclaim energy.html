Reclaim Energy
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Removes your summoned/animated sword/shield pet.
Area of Effect: The only effective target is your summoned pet, though you can target anyone.
School of Magic: Conjuration

Cost in Mana:

 5

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 SHD, NEC, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Wuggan Azusphere (1104, -1022)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Early on this spell actually recovers less then or the same energy as it takes to cast and seems to have no appearant value.  It doesn't reclaim casting components, only mana.  It is not until later that its true value becomes appearant.

First reason:  The pets we summon have some variance in level.  There are times you may wish to summon pets until you get the strongest available pet (you can determine its relative strength with /consider).  Casting invisibility or zoning will effectively remove your pet, but one is wasteful or mana the other is wasteful of time.  Reclaim energy is quicker than either and will save on mana in the long run.

Second reason:  Given that our pets are not true pets and can not be commanded, they may occasionaly proceed to attack something that attacks you, despite the fact you may not want to kill what's attacking you.  Case in point: gnomes are allowed in Neriak to begin with.  Orcs do not like gnomes and will attack them on sight.   Orcs on Faydwer are aligned with the Indigo Brotherhood (warrior guild and guards of Neriak).   Thus, killing enough orcs will preclude a gnome's ability to safely walk the streets and forests around Neriak.  While its a simple matter to mesmerize the orc and keep it from killing you, once your pet is defending you, you will be unable to call it off and prevent it from hitting the orc and removing the spell.  As a result, without quick intervention, either the orc will win out and kill you in the end, or the orc will die and you will take a faction hit against the Indigo Brotherhood.  To resolve this you need a quick way to destroy your pet before you can effectively mesmerize the orc and go on your merry way without altering your faction standing in Neriak.  If you successfully manage to use this method to escape faction hits, I would advise zoning as soon as possible as well.  As soon as someone or something else decides to kill the mob you've fooled, it still remembers that you participated and you will still receive faction adjustments.  Zoning is the only way to avoid this.
