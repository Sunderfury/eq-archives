MONK  

Monks are excellent at avoiding blows and they hit very fast. A single blow from a Monk is not likely to be as dangerous as that from the others in this group. However they hit far more often then anyone else and that damage adds up very quickly. Besides a few special attacks they also have an ability to heal themselves and can pretend to be dead to sometimes escape combat. The weakest thing about a Monk is that they do not have the ability to carry much weight before hurting their defenses. A monk should make friends with Magicians, Shamans and Enchanters for buffs and spirit pouches to carry things.
