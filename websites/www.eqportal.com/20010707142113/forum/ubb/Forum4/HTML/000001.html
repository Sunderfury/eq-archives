Here are a few suggestions for soloing at these levels..

35: I know what you mean, couldn't find a good spot to hunt at that level. I finally asked a more experienced druid, and she suggested shins at the Lower Guk entrance area. You may need to wait a level or two, but I found the live side a great spot for experience. Just put up thorns, DOT them, and let them beat on you awhile. And it helps if another group is there to take care of multiple frogs. The undead side is too crowded, so you may find yourself short on things to hunt there.

14: 14 was when I left Faydwer for the first time.. and ended up dying more times than any other level =)
I initially hunted in Unrest, then shifted to the Karanas for awhile. I never really hunted in Oasis, but I've heard it's a good spot.
