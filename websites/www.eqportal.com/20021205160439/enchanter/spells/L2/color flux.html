Color Flux
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Area of Affect stun centered on caster.  (does not affect the caster)
Area of Effect: All creatures within a short distance of the caster. (be wary of nearby guards)
School of Magic: Divination

Cost in Mana:

 20

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: In theory this is a good spell for stunning multiple creautres if you get attacked by more than you can handle.  I've heard other people say a good strategy is to color flux when you get attacked by 3-4 things then use the spare time to either gate out or, if you think you can handle them, begin in with some other combat tactic (charm, mesmerize, etc).   Playing solo, I've yet to get this to work correctly.  In a group, I've managed touse it much more effectively since I'm not  being beaten to a bloody pulp the whole time I'm casting.

The duration of the delay appears to be about 2 seconds long.
