With the advent of the RaceWar servers, PvP has really brought on a new flavor.  This section will be divided into three parts.  The first is for Blue Server PvP, the second for Race War PvP, and the third will the the infamous Rallos Zek PvP.  The divisions are so because strategies can vary considerable depending on the environment.

Blue Server PvP:

Being PvP on a normal blue server can be both exciting and dissapointing.  For one, other players who are blue can't help you out directly very much, and many hold a certain distaste for you simply because you are PvP.  These are the people who think PvP is against role-playing, but I would argue that with them.  Killing other people is part of what people do.  For gaming purposes, I feel it can make for great sport, and a challenge like no other you will find in the game.

The exciting part about going PvP on a blue server is the fact that there are so few of you out there.  Count on no more than 2 dozen people of all the 1500+ that regularly sign on to that server being PvP, but look closely at EVERYONE!  As you journey through Norrath fighting, looting, buying and selling, all will seem perfectly normal.  Then one day you'll open a door and "GASP!!!! A RED!!!!".  Your pulse pounds as you scramble to pull up your combat screen, click on them, con them, turn invisible..... Who are they?  Have they seen you?  Can they see invisible?  Will they kill you?  You finally get a con on them..... and they're EVEN!!!  As a Wood Elf rogue it quickly dawns on you that a wretched Dark-Elf scum stands before you, so engrossed with the merchant that he didn't even see you... and with cat like precision you drive your dagger into his back!  He is numb for second, wondering what just happend, just long enough for you to swap weapons and begin meleeing him to death.  The evil necro tries to fire off a few spells, one lands and burns a bit, but without his precious pet you dispatch him quickly...

Well, that's my big line for going "Red" on a blue server.  Take it or leave it.  Here's just a few tips and general things to know when PvP'ing on a blue server.
1) Keep a list of all people you run into who are red.  Write down the name, class, race, and level if you can get it.
    Whenever you zone and/or periodically while in a zone, do a /who all on them and make sure they aren't around if
    they're close enough in level.
2) Find a way to make friends with one, you'll need them.  Giving them stuff could do it, perhaps simple logic, but you
    NEED a friend.
3) Stay invisible as much as possible, if you have SoW keep it on constantly.
4) Check your other camera angles regularly, while in combat and not.  Knowing they are there before they strike may
     be your only chance of survival.

Race War Server PvP:

The racewar server has brought a whole new style of playing to the PvP scene.  Now, as many of you know, the races of Norrath are divided into four teams
    Team 1 = Humans        Barbarians     Erudites
    Team 2 = Wood Elf      High Elf        Half Elf
    Team 3 = Dark Elf        Troll             Ogre
    Team 4 = Gnome         Halfling          Dwarf
Conveniently for rogues, we can be on any of these teams without suffering a huge penalty because of our wide variety of races.

Many people argue that this is THE most fun style of PvP play, and with good reason.  You get to kill those other races that always annoy you, there's an 8 level differential to PvP, and you get that whole group morale/team thing that gives you a sense of purpose.

Some things about playing on a Race War Server.
1) Just because you're in your newbie garden doesn't mean you are safe.
2) If you're going to be an elf, you're going to have to get REAL used to Crushbone and plan to spend a LOT of
     time there.  It's really the only place you can go to level from about level 8 on, unless you are brave enough to    
     leave your other elven brethren.  
3) Elves don't have a safe dock to get to Antonica.  To some, this could be a good enough reason to be a Human or
    Barbarian rogue, just so that you will have ready access to all the fruits of Antonica at the lower levels.

I'm still fairly new to the Race War scene (as most of us are) so I don't have the greatest insight to it.  Drop me a line if you have anything you think might be usefull to add, or want me to try and dig something up.
