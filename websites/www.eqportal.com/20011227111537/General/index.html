Hosting and Link information

 


I have many links here so let me tell you the bias and procedure I have in place for them. This only reflects my policies and each site webmaster hosted here will do as they choose.

1. Priority goes to sites hosted on this server. If your here then the site must be up so why bounce you around? Also I have a persoanl bias towards those sites helping us reach the community goal. I require no ads so this is not done to make money.

2. Next priority are the noncommercial and better sites on the subject at hand. I define commercial sites as those that are run to make money. Either money for the webmaster or for that webmasters ISP. Plenty of great people are supporting EQ with thier own money and sweat so they earn my respect and linking rights more then the ones who consider this another get rich quick scheme. Ads are also annoying because they are off topic and distracting.

3. Shotgun game sites where EQ is but one of many that information is displayed for. Were playing EQ here not UO, Diablo or anything else. Nothing against those games but I'll let someone else foot the bill for games I do not play. Offtopic information is also distracting and may make it harder to get the information your looking for. Except for the GamesDb most of these are commercial anyhow and fall under........

4. Commercial sites are the last thing I want to link to.
However in some cases those are the best info sites for the topic your interested in so those sites will have links from here. Otherwise let them pay for thier own advertising as long as they exist to make money off you. You will find they mostly support other commercial sites anyhow so tis only fair to have at least one site focus on the true fans of the game.
Note that this is MY personal opinion only and I do not object to making money, capitalism or trade. I only object to paying for stuff I don't want and advertisements in general. This is not a company policy of refplace but a personal bias of the owner.

Some of you may wonder why I created this service and what makes it so special.
This Hosting service which is EQPortal.com wants to create a safe home for EQ Fan sites without forcing either the webmaster or the visitor to endure a bunch of off topic ads. So the webmaster or person who runs a site gets free hosting and you the visitor and EQ Player (myself included) get a better web experiance. One additional principle this service is founded upon is that EQ is about groups and players who run more then one charecter. That means you want more then just an Enchanter or Druid site but a site that has everything for all your charecters and even server specific info.
That is the Goal of EQPortal and the reason for its existence.
