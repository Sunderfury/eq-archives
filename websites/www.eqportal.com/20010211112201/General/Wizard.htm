WIZARD  

The easiset of this group to play, they are the artillery of the world. They have some nice perks such as Root to hold the target in the Bullseye and Fade to get themselves out of the bullseye. They are second only to Druids in travel and have the plane gate spells which no one else has. They also enjoy some modest protective spells but in the ability to dish out a lot of damage in a hurry they are unexcelled and quite infamous.
