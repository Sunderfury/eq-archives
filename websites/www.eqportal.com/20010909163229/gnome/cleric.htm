Guide to be a Gnomish Cleric

 

Hail and welcome to the guide to be a gnomish cleric!

 

 

First off, if you’re serious in creating a gnome cleric, you have come to the right place, This is the guide ONLY for creating a gnome cleric. If you are not interested in making a gnomish cleric, git! Now, let continue..

 

 

Stats:

 

Put all of the points until you hit 23 points in WIS and put 7 points in STR. You will have 72 STR and 100 WIS.  100 WIS is a good start, don’t let anyone tell you gnome clerics will suffer because they lack of WIS.

 

Deity:

 

There are three kinds of deity you can choose, PlagueBringer, BristleBane, and Brell Serilis. I personally chose Brell Serilis, it’s a good side, BristleBane is neutral, and PlagueBringer is evil.  Remember the difficulties you will have if you chose to be in the evil path and the easiness in the path of good and neutral.

 

Starting:

 

Alright! Now you’ve chose your name and ready to proceed! You will start in a town called Ak’Anon, which of course, it’s a gnomish town! From my experience, I spawned right by the town gate where the big sign reads, Welcome to Ak’Anon.

 

There are 2 cleric guilds in Ak’Anon, One is located with Necromancer guild for the evil clerics. The good cleric guild is located near the Bazaar merchants where the bard NPC by the ramp toward the bank. Go across the bridge, go down the cliff, once you get out of the tunnel like cliff, turn left, go up those stairs, you’ll see a nice glowing house, ( pretty impressive I must say..) Go in, make a turn to the door in your left, you’ll see your guild leader on the stairs, give her your note and grab your guild tunic and start scribbing the spells and memorize them.

 

Hunting:

 

To start out hunting, make sure you buff yourself with what you got, and head out, make a right you’ll see large rats, decaying gnome skeletons, kobolds and spiderlings. Hunt the skeletons and rats til you hit level 2, once you’re level 2, hunt kobolds, their loots are decent and randomly get bag containers which is a good money making.. sell em to buy yourself some spells. Hunt kobolds until you’re level 3, then start hunting spiderlings, beware, they can gang up on you and lead to a quick deaths to any newbies. Once you hit 4, try to buy all of the level 1 spells as soon as possible and head out to the kobold camp 1. Kobold camp 1 is found near  the zone to Lesser Fraydark. To get there, from the entrance to Ak’Anon, head out straight on the path, until the road forks, head left, you’ll get into a valley full of newbie critters, stay up hill, don’t go down. You’ll eventually see the camp. You could solo those kobolds, or get in groups. Their loots are great also. Once you hit level 5, it’s time for you to buy your new spells. Head back to your guild and buy your level 5 spells.  Get Ward Undead and Spook the Dead spell as soon as you can! Head out to your newbie hunting area where you hunted rats and such. Now, memorize ward undead and spook the dead. Ward undead inflict 38 dmg per cast. You could beable to cast it 5 time with full mana. Pull a skeleton that’s by the dragon bone or by the gears near the Minotaur Cave.  Cast ward undead on any skeleton, they will con yellow or red to you, but you can do this, and cast spook on one, one skeleton will flee, just cast ward undead repeatly until it drop dead. Hunting with this method, will shoot you from level 5 to 9 in no time. Personally, I stick there til I hit level 14.

 

 

Once you’re level 14, it pretty much opens up more hunting areas for you to hunt, and you get another upgrade in your spells. Yep, more spells to buy!

 

 

Conclusion:

 

This conclude my guide to be a gnome cleric! I wish you all gnomish clerics the best.
