Alliance
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Adds faction standing with targeted NPC's faction.  This spell is no-resist.
(if you succeed in casting, the target will not resist or become angry)
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 35

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1101, -983)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: This is the first in a series of such spells.  The target can not resist, so feel free to use it frequently on shop keepers.  The spell is most commonly used to sell your goods for a slightly better price or to buy supplies at a slightly lower price.  There is, however, a litmit to how good a price you will receive and once you reach it this spell won't affect the pricing any.

Adding faction standing can have other beneficial affects.  Some quests are not available to you until you reach a cetain faction standing.  This spell only adds a limited amount, but it may make the difference.  Some beings will also attack you if your faction standing with them is low enough, this may also make the difference between being attacked and being merely sneered at.

The duration on this spell seems to last a while.  The best way to tell is compare shop prices before and after casting it.  The duration seems to be over 5 minutes for the most part, long enough to run back and forth between shop keepers when performing tasks like buying trade-good supplies, creating trade-good items to increase skill, and then selling back to regain some of the monetary cost and start over.  I've managed to run through several platinum and quite a long "training" period while only casting this spell once.

Zoning seems to remove the affects.  Raising faction standing affects not only the target, but their entire factions opinion of you.
