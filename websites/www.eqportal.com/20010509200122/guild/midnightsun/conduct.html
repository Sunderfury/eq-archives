Don't Steal Kills....EVER. This is an activity you should never be apart of in any form or fashion.. period!

Don't engage in arguments with other players over /shout or /ooc. This makes the guild look bad and is generally annoying for everyone. If you feel it necessary to discuss differences then take it to /tells.

Don't hog loot! Whenever you are in a group you are representing us as a guild. Don't be greedy and give everyone a fair chance to loot. Be respectful of the loot order that has been decided on by the rest of the group. Don't loot out of turn!

Don't over-use guildsay. This is a great channel for asking questions, seeking advice or looking for a group, but don't have active conversations with a single person, because the text can get excessive and be an irritation to others. If you have to talk to a single person, take it to /tells. Never use foul or abusive language on guildsay.

Do your best to attend all guild functions. If you are online during the time of a guild event, you should do your best to attend. All events will be scheduled well in advance and there is no excuse for not knowing about them.

Check the website regularly! Guild members and leaders often post news and events on the guild forum. You should spend a few minutes at least every other day to browse the website and see what is going on.

Be helpful to other members. If you can provide a service or information that another member needs, you should be willing to do so. Helping each other is what we are here for, and favors to others are always looked on in a good light by other members and officers.

Be a good representative of the guild. This is an open ended responsibility, because all members should know what it means to be a good EQ player.

Have FUN!!! This is the most important aspect of the game. The challenge and excitement of the game should always stay fun. If it isn't, then you're doing something wrong.
