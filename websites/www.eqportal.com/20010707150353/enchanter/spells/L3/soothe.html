Soothe
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Reduces the range at which the targeted being will respond to intruders or assist companions.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 30

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 CLR, PAL, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1101, -983)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: This spell replaces the lull spell with an improved version of the same affects.  Soothed monsters can be more closely approached without triggering the attack if they are hostile.  Soothed monsters also have a smaller reaction range allowing you to attack their nearby companions without triggering them to assist.  You'll have to play with the spell some to determine what the safety range is on this.

While it is a good spell to seperate groups of monsters so you can attack them one at a time, it will have no effect on a being once combat has already begun.  If you wish to stop a combat you will have to use spells like mesmerize.
