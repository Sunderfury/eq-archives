In the Raves Department.......
Kudos to Verant for Kunark!
Overall the Graphics are superb. I have to turn my Clipping (Options menu, Display third page or so) almost to the bottom or the frame rate kills me (My character actually) but even with this the place looks good and every once in awhile I adjust it to the max. for a good look around. Eventually I'll upgrade to more and faster memory and that should fix it. These zones are huge and only Field of Bone seems crowded. However it has gotten somewhat better now that more people are pushing off and higher level. This is because of two good Quests as well as it being the original Bind zone. The zones are apparently running on much better servers as well since I rarely see the lag problems I would expect in other outdoor zones like the Karana's with that many people.

One of the joys of Kunark is that faction is now more of a factor and people are starting to have to decide what to do based on consequences for their actions which enrich the RP aspect of this game. Oh, and so far I have not seen any Iksar Guard killing!
I do hope to find the Sicken spell to be locally obtainable however instead if us having to buy our 5th level spell from travelers. Higher level spells are fine to travel or quest for but I believe one where your corpse decays to fast to retrieve it are a bit out of line.
This is going to make me unpopular, but I do think that Iksar monks are too powerful. I have one at 5th level now and a human at 12. The difference between the two at the lower levels is very apparent. Combine regeneration, better AC, infravision, and better stats and we have what I believe to be a superior race/class combo. One anonymous poster said that this only works at lower levels and that Human monks do more damage then Iksar monks. As he was using a fake e-mail address I don't credit it much but perhaps some of you have noticed a difference?

Warriors, Shadow Knights, Necromancers, and even Shamans seem to have enough counterbalances to be fair.

This site is dedicated to EverQuest and is hosted by Refplace for the purpose of providing free to low cost hosting facilities for those fans who wish to host EverQuest related sites. It is not for commercial purposes and hence no ads are used or required by the Hosting company or administrator.

To have your site hosted here or simply linked please contact the Administrator

This idea was brought forth by Stonedreamer of Fenin Ro who recognized that there are many sites out there with good information and advice but that sometimes finding these sites was hard and time consuming. As the Library of his home in Ak'anon once did he has sought out authors far and wide and attempted to bring many of them under one roof so all can benefit. Classes can talk to and learn from others who share their spells and skills. We can see what our traveling companions needs and abilities are from their peers and share with them what we need and can offer. Thus EQPortal has been created in the spirit of Teamwork.

Those who have their sites here are hoping to benefit more then just their Class, Race or Trade much less themselves and should be commended. Stonedreamer and Refplace in no way assumes credit for their hard work. We merely provide a building for them to gather in.
