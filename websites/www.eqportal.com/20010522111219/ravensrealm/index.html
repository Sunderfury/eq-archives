Monday May 14, 2001

RAVEN COUNCIL

Waunder announced on the message boards that we will be once again be looking for members for the Ravens Council. 

The following will be the duties assigned to the council:

  • *  The Raven Council will be made up of six (6) members.
  • *  Four (4) members from the flock and two (2) from the Raven Officers.
  • *  In case of a tie vote, the Guild Leader will have the seventh (7) and final vote.
  • *  The Council is not responsible for punishment of its peers.
  • *  The Council will be responsible for hearing accused violations of the Charter and determining if a violation of the Charter has been committed.
  • *  If the Council deems a violation has been committed, the case is turned over to the Guild Leader for punishment.
  • *  Punishment may consist of, but not limited too, a verbal warning, suspension from the guild or permanent removal from the Ravens.
  • *  A Council Member may step down after three (3) months of service. At that time, the guild will vote on whom they want to replace the retiring Council member.
  • *  The council is the primary body for holding and passing out the Treasury.
