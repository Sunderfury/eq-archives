Strengthen
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Temporarily adds strength to target.
Area of Effect: single targeted creature.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 10

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 SHM, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the enchanter's earliest buffing spells.  Strengthen may come in handy to do an extra point of damage now and again, though it has little real effect at early levels.  I tend to get more milage out of it when I am encumbered with excessive loot from hunting expeditions and need the extra strength to carry more and move faster.  Then again, it never hurts to cast strength on everyone in your party allowing each peson to do that little extra damage in combat.  Later on, an enchanter's casting slots are often filled with more effective combat spells and this one seems to fall by the way side.
