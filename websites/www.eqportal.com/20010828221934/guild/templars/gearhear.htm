his quest orginates in Akanon. Improves Gnome faction, lowers fairy faction.

  • Talk to the main warrior guild master, he will want you to kill a defective robot named Gearheart. He will tell you that Gearheart has been captured by the faeries and you must travel to Lesser Fay to slay him.

  • Travel to Lesser Fay. Gearheart randomly spawns in one of two locations, the first is in the middle of the fairy village and the second is near the Steamfont zone by the observatory. If he is in the fairy village DO NOT attack him unless you are able to kill all the fairy guards that will come to his aid! Wait until he wanders off (hardly ever happens) or until he spawns at the other location.

  • Once you locate Gearheart in a safe location, kill him. He is level 17. Once slain, he will drop Gearheart's gearbox.

  • Take the gearbox back to the warrior that started the quest and give it to him. He will give you a good gnome faction adjustment, experience, some coin, and a random piece of raw-hide armor.
