Failed Thermal Cloak Recipes


IMPORTANT NEWS

The search for the Tattered Gnomish Cloak has finally ended! The first Thermal Cloak has been created! See the Golden Gear Awards section for those that were involved with this great discovery. To find out where to obtain the Tattered Gnomish Cloak and how to make the Thermal Cloak, please see the Ingredients and Recipes sections.


Abashi responded to our question regarding the Thermal Cloak, and stated that the Tattered Gnomish Cloak is not in Norrath at this time. Unfortunately, he did not directly state if this also meant that the Thermal Cloak could not be produced, however most Gnomes are following the assumption that this is also true, given that the Tattered Gnomish Cloak is an important ingredient according to Gimblestan.

Good news fellow Gnomes! A Bard just recently delivered a letter to me from that Gnome of all Gnomes, Gnomad! The letter was faded and had obviously traveled a long distance. I'm actually amazed that mail gets through at all now that the Bards are relying on adventurers to run it from depot to depot. Hmm, perhaps I should start work on a Mail Delivery Clockwork...but I digress...

Within the letter, Gnomad states that he has gone through his archives and has managed to find his old list of Thermal Cloak failed attempts. Along with the enclosed list, Gnomad wished us all good luck. I have posted this list in its entirety below.

Regarding the failed attempts, many Gnomes and other races have written in suggesting that a belt be used as part of the recipe for the Thermal Cloak, as Gimblestan himself had written that he put his firewater on a belt. This sounds like a great suggestion, and I can only believe that it was not an option when Gnomad and other Gnomes first set out to recreate Gimblestan's work. I was not a part of this and cannot comment. My only thought would be that a suitable belt was not available in the world, but I would appreciate it if a more seasoned Gnome could shed some flameless light on this. UPDATE: See the Norbert item below for some thoughts on the belt.

Contributions to the Creation of the Thermal Cloak
Many Gnomes have written with their attempts and suggestions. Below are their ideas, presented in reverse chronological order.

NEW: A number of people have written in suggesting that the twice woven cloak found on the faeries in Lesser Faydark be used in the construction of the Thermal Cloak. To date, this has not been tested out. [UPDATE: Gyro of Solusek Ro wrote in to let us know that the twice woven cloak is used in a Temple of Ro quest].

NEW: Gephetto Strombolli of Cazic-Thule wrote in:

Its possible and i think, tho farfetched, the firewater belt and the cloack are two seperate items. Possible you have to wear the belt and the cloak as individual items. Only a thought, figured i'd add mine since in order to figure that out weWILL have to work together on it.

NEW: Antiginous Schmiginous of Bertoxxulous has made small tattered cloaks in the past. The recipe was 1 ruined wolf pelt and a cloak pattern from High Hold Pass.

NEW: Vaire of Xegony has heard that tailor patterns for a small tattered cloak may be found in Kelethin.

Konaki Jiiji wrote in that in looking for a Tattered Gnomish Cloak, he, um, inspected a couple of Gnomes. The first was the Gnomish Spelunker found in Unrest, and the second was the Gnomish Mage found in Cazic Thule. Neither Gnome had any items on them after inspecting them three times.

Byraxis of Innoruk wrote in with a new attempt using a Torn and Ripped Tunic. Byraxis' attempt may be found at the bottom of this section. (UPDATE: Byraxis foudn that the Torn and Ripped Tunic is given to Gnome Warriors. Byraxis tried the Tunic with one and three rods with any luck. This is likely a dead end).

Gixx Fizbane wrote in that by turing in Orc Belts to Canloe Nusbeck of the Warrior's guild in Kaladim, you will receive small tattered armour. In the past, Gixx has receive a small tattered cloak, but at the time Gixx hadn't thought to use it for the Thermal Cloak.

Tynk Mindrifter has given 250 bone chips to Gunlok Jure of the Paladin guild in Kaladim. It was rumoured that Gunlok gave out Tattered armour. Indeed, Tynk received (as well as some other items) small tattered leggings, gloves, tunics, and sleeves, but so far, no small tattered cloak.

Nadiya Nightshade of Rallos Zek has suggested that medium sized cloaks be used when attempting the Thermal Cloak. Nadiyra's reasoning is that most other quests that require a piece of clothing use a medium sized one as the resultant magical item fits all sizes.

Tynk Mindrifter of Mithaniel Marr has let me know that Gunlock Jure in Kaladim randomly gives out small tattered cloaks as a reward for turning in four bone chips. Tynk will try to confirm this.

Brelag of Tunare wrote in with a attempt by combining a small bronze splintered cloak, 2 rods and firewater. The attempt failed to produce anything. I've added this information to the bottom of this section.

A non-Gnome wrote in suggesting that the +2 INT Gnome only cloak found on Innoruuk in the Plane of Hate may be the Tattered Gnomish Cloak.

Malthus Melodius of Cazic Thule has heard that the farie courtiers in Lesser Faydark may sometimes carry a Small Tattered Cloak. He has hunted a few of them but has come up with nothing to date.

A Gnome on Rodcet Nife wrote to say that within the week, someone in Oasis of Marr shouted a question as to what to do with a Tattered Gnomish Cloak. Perhaps it has finally been added to the world.

Norbert Gearsmith of E'ci and other Gnomes have suggested that a belt not be used in the making of a Thermal Cloak. Gimblestan always capitalized the ingredients when describing how to create an item, and the belt is not capitalized.

Norbert Gearsmith of E'ci has written in with some more attempts, this time with a leather cloak. You may find this information that the bottom of this section. Thanks Norbert!

Pendrill of Rallos Zek has suggested using a material other than a patchwork cloak, such as leather. This does bring up a great idea of trying other fabrics, such as damask or gossimer. Earenhylle of Rallos Zek (a Dark Elf) has suggested using reinforced leather armour as it contains steel boning. To create it, use a large sewing kit and combine: Cloak pattern, High Quality Bear/Cat/Wolf pelt, and 2 Steel bonings. Thanks for the suggestions!

Mallv just wrote in to let me know that he has tried a ruined wolf pelt, a cloak pattern, and a number of rods in a medium sewing kit. So far, no luck. His list of attempts is below. He'll be trying 3 rods sometime soon. Thanks Mallv!

Dakirn of Tunare just wrote in to let me know that he has tried a small patchwork cloak and a metal rod in a small sewing kit (the small sewing kit has two slots) as Gimblestan mentioned sewing the rods into the cloak. Unfortunately, this did not work out. He will be trying out a medium kit sometime soon. Thanks Dakirn!

Not long after I wrote in the compendium regarding Gnomad's letter, I received mail from Dakirn of Tunare. He has been trying to recreate Gimblestan's work recently, and has graciously passed on his list of failed attempts. I've added his list to the end of Gnomad's.
