Gate
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Returns the caster to the bind point (where you return when you die).
Area of Effect: self only.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 70

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 CLR, DRU, SHM, NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, central room. (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the early joys of being a spellcaster.  You can explore without fear of getting truly lost once you have this spell.  If you get overburdened with gear and are moving at a slow crawl, you can gate home and you are already that much closer to selling off excess gear.  Later on, when you get Bind Affinity, you can even set your bind point directly to a key location, such as a shop to sell gear, a tavern for that quick drink after a tough fight, or just near the actual location you'll be working in case a mishap causes you to need to seek out your corpse.

The casting time on this spell, as well as the comparatively high mana cast early on, makes this a spell you'll not be able to use frequently in mid-combat.  At least, this is what I've heard and been told many times.  Granted, this is true if you're being beaten on by some disgruntled monster, however, being an enchanter means having more options at that critical point.  All it takes is a single, successful mesmerize spell to buy you the time to gate, provided you have the mana for it.  Failing that, I have successfully gated out of combat a few times even while being the unhappy target for someone elses aggression.

Gate will carry you across zone borders back to your bind point, however, if you are gating within the same zone the affect is nearly instantaneous.
