Haze
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Adds AC to the target for a while.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Abjuration

Cost in Mana:

 25

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Our second buffing spell that can be cast on others (the first being strengthen at level 1) its one I always kept readied at lower levels.  Later on when you have more life and access to stronger pets that can take a beating you may find you don't bother with spells like this, but early on those extra few armor points can be crucial to surviving a fight.    When grouped, your companions will also particularly enjoy this spell.
