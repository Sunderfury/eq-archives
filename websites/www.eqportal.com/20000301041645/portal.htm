This site is dedicated to EverQuest and is hosted by Refplace for the purpose of providing free to low cost hosting facilities for those fans who wish to host EverQuest related sites. It is not for commercial purposes and hence no ads are used or required by the Hosting company or administrator.

This idea was brought forth by Stonedreamer of Fenin Ro who recognized that there are many sites out there with good information and advice but that sometimes finding these sites was hard and time consuming. As the Library of his home in Ak'anon once did he has sought out authors far and wide and attempted to bring many of them under one roof so all can benefit. Classes can talk to and learn from others who share their spells and skills. We can see what our traveling companions needs and abilities are from their peers and share with them what we need and can offer. Thus EQPortal has been created in the spirit of Teamwork.

Those who have their sites here are hoping to benefit more then just their Class, Race or Trade much less themselves and should be commended. Stonedreamer and Refplace in no way assumes credit for their hard work. We merely provide a building for them to gather in.
