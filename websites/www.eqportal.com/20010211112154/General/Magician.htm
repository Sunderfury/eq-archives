MAGICIAN

Magicians are the most self sufficient of this group and welcome in many parties. They can conjure up many items, including food and water, weapons, armor and even bandages. The ability to summon bandages makes it cheap to increase their skill and is a nice backup to the Clerics. They are best known for their pets and they have the best set of pets of all classes. A Magician can solo pretty well at the lower levels but parties like them for the restocking ability. Later on they can conjure temporary magic items that make Enchanters Drool such as Rings of Flight and a Ultravision stone.
