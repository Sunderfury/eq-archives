Tinkering Recipes

Below is a summary of the known Tinkering recipes. Thanks to Tynk Mindrifter of Mithaniel Marr for the prices. They are based on an amiable faction and a charisma of 150. They have also been round to the nearest coin. For the original source, please refer to The Library.

IMPORTANT NEWS! NEW TINKERING BOOK DISCOVERED!

A new tinkering book has been discovered on the island of Velious! The book is located on "igloo" island, talk to Adinel Jailbar, a new merchant selling new items!

Thanks goes to Clockwork Tinkers Union (ctu.gnomish.net) on Cazic-Thule on being the first to e-mail in the new recipes! They have been awarded a Golden Gear Award, which you can find here.
