Golden Gear Awards
Welcome to the Golden Gear Awards! King Ak'Anon and I wanted a forum where the achievements of a few Gnomes in the art of Tinkering could be showcased and encouraged. With the King's blessing, the Golden Gear Award was born.

These awards have been created to honour those Gnomes that have contributed to the Tinkering Community, usually in the form of releasing information that many Gnomes keep to themselves. Through their efforts, the Tinkering Community has continued to grow and prosper. *Fulcrum cheers*

Below are the recipients of the Golden Gear Award, and the statues that have yet to be awarded.

The first award is a special one, and it is unfortunate that it must be awarded posthumously. Many Tinkers know of Gnomad's work. For those Gnomes too young to have heard of him, Gnomad created the first repository of Tinkering information. It was the source of Tinkering knowledge, and the place all Tinkers went when a question needed answering. It was upon Gnomad's departure from Norrath that I have attempted to fill the void with the Compendium.

(April 4, 2000) Tynk Mindrifter of Mithaniel Marr is the first Gnome to receive an award for Furthering Gnomish Pursuits. I've created this new category to showcase those that have really helped out the community. Tynk has passed on a lot of information that has been incorporated into the Compendium including costs for ingredients and recipes, and for most of the information in the Gnomish Curios section. Tynk's curiosity has taken him throughout Norrath in search of all things Gnomish, and enjoys sharing this information with the rest of community. Congratulations Tynk!

The remaining awards are self-explanatory. Congratulations to all!
