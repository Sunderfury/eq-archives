RANGER  

These are scouts and Jack of all trade types. The utility spells and abilities of this class make them very powerful and flexable. They sacrifice some in the armor and weapons department for their versatility but are valuaed companions. Tracking in particualr gives Rangers an early headstart in finding opponenets and friends quickly. In combat they continue to show their versatility by having a balance of offensive and defensive spells. Being a Ranger is about having a number of choices and options that outstrip any other fighter class.
