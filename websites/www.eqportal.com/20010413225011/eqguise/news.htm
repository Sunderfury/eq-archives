Ok, iv been asked this question many times....Why don't you send your pictures and ideas to Verant? The answer is that I would say a good 98% of the pictures i make...for example...armor pictures, verant can't make. See when i make my pictures, i can select certain areas of the armor/robe and change them to be 1 color, then select the rest of the robe and change them to be another color. Verant cannot do that. They have to change the color of the whole texture. And on some of my pictures i put on an extra texture on to there...such as stars on a robe, or fire on a robe. Verant cant do that either, they would have to make a whole new texture in the global memory file...which would slow down loading time and cause problems with texture memory on crappy video cards. Also, another good example is what i did with the Monk Kunark armor...the visible picture...they cant just tint it black...because the color of the skin would change as well. For example...take a bloodstained tunic on a troll male. It has holes in it...and where the holes are...the skin is supposed to be colored green, but its colored red (like the tunic is).

If Verants graphic engine didn't suck, they could do layerd transparentcys and things like that to make much cooler looking things. But they are limited by their engine, and minimum specs. If everquest were and offline game, they wouldnt be so limited, and if they didnt use zones, they wouldnt be so limited. Now i could expirement with color combos and contrast by doing the whole thing at once like eq has to do...but they wouldnt look very good. i think verant has found every color that looks decent. but maybe ill give it a shot.
