BARD

A rounded class that can do a bit of everything but not as well as any specialist. They are able to affect more people at a time with their songs/spells and have some ability in combat. Bards rarely need to travel alone and can make a whole party shine. Soling is a bit hard for Bards but then they rarely need to.
