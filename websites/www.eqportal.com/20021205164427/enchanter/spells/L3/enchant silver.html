Enchant: Silver
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Enchants a plain bar of silver to make it enchanted silver.
Enchanted silver is used in the jewelry crafting trade.
Area of Effect: a single silver bar.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 80

Component/s/ Needed:

 a single silver bar

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Neriak only, in the library in 3rd Quarter
Tips: The spell seems to be no-resist.  If you successfully cast it, the silver bar will always be enchanted and become enchanted silver.   Enchanted silver does not stack with normal silver bars.

How to cast:  Pick up the silver bar with the cursor by clicking on it.  It should replace the cursor with the silver bar.  Now exit out of your inventory screen so you can see your memorized spells (in the spell gems) and click the spell gem for enchant silver.  If cast successfully you will see the silver bar / icon blink as it is replaced with the enchanted silver bar.  You can now store it away or place it directly into your jewelry crafting kit with a stone, hit combine and attempt to make jewelry.  Want to know more about jewelry?  Check out the Tal'Kor Jewelry Academy.
