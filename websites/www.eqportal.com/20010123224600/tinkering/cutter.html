Gemcutter
With the location of Diamond Dust revealed (thanks to Derkal Pentolite of The Rathe), the Gnome Tinkering community can now focus on the purpose of the Gemcutter. Unfortunately, Gimblestan was a little vague on how to exactly use the Gemcutter. Here are some quotes from Gimblestan's Volume XXXIII:

"...I was mindful to pickup a few pearls for my friend Simpkin the Jeweler."
"He just finished the initial design on his GEMCUTTER and was interested in using it to make those wonderful pearl anaglyphs he was so fond of."
"...his Firewater powered apparatus was able to make precise cuts on even the tiniest gems..."

There has been confirmation from Abashi that no items exist that may raise one's skill, so the Gemcutter will not make you more proficient at creating Jewelry. A number of Gnomes have written in with their attempts to use the Gemcutter. Their comments have been listed below. Please, if you can add to this list, write in!

There have been rumours in the past that the Gemcutter is not yet fully functional. As my attempts to obtain an answer from Abashi in the past have failed, I will post a message to the public forum. Please check out the Community Posting section for more information.

Diamond Dust
While the location of Diamond Dust has been revealed by Derkal Pentolite of The Rathe, I've decided to leave the Diamond Dust section in the Compendium. Below are the names of Gnomes that gave their time for the benifit of the Tinkering Community.

And one never knows if there may be multiple locations where Diamond Dust can be found...

Locations Checked
Ogre in Lake Rathe
Surastik of Cazic Thule reported that he gave a diamond to a gem-crushing Ogre in
Lake Rathe. The Ogre did not crush the diamond and had to be killed to get it back.
Gnome in Rathe Mountains by Inn
Surastik of Cazic Thule reported that he gave a diamond to a gem-cutting Gnome in
the Rathe Mountains by the Inn. The Gnome accepted the diamond and did nothing
with it (even after talking to him about gem-cutting). Being a vendor, it was not
possible to retrieve the diamond. Thanks for the sacrifice Surastik! I have heard that
this Gnome is part of a 50th level Shadowknight quest.
Solusek A Gnome Merchant
Flintnilf Ironori reported that he has checked the Gnome merchant in Solusek A and
has verified that he does not sell diamond dust. In addition, diamond dust is not found
on any of the clockworks or other Gnomes there.

relation. The Ogres in Lake Rathe (see Locations Checked section above) will apparently
crush dufrenite. Cogg of Rallos Zek was going to check this out.
Dusters in Ak'Anon
A Gnome that wished to remain anonymous stated that Diamond Dust could be found on
the bodies of the Clockwork Dusters in Ak'Anon. To date, no Gnome has confirmed this,
the probable reason being that there is likely a faction hit from killing Dusters.
