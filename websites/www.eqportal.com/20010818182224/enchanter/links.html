For what its worth, these are the EQ links I have bookmarked and have found to be the most useful. I considered adding every enchanter or EQ page I could find, but every site I've seen that does this ends up with a great number of bad links after a few weeks, so I'm limiting this to Enchanter links and EQ links that have proven to be stable and I continue to use. Each of these links will spawn a new window for the listed site leaving you free to compare information to what I have here if you wish.

Other Good Enchanter Links

EQ Portal Ok, so you're already here.   This will spawn another window.
EQ Casters Good spell caster information.
Mythiran Tower A site dedicated to spell-research.

General Everquest Links

Questor Primarily a Japanese site, but the maps speak for themselves! This link goes to the English version.
AllaKhazam's Magical Realm Excellent quest database, item listings, faction lists, and more.
EQ Items An excellent item database.   It includes search parameters that will customize the output to just what you need.  Hasn't been updated much lately.
EQ Trader's Corner An excellent site for trade skills.  Lists of recipes, trivial levels, and where to get supplies.

Everquest The Official Site, where it all began
EQVault The Vault Network, Active news updates, good basic info.
EverQuest Express A online newspaper for EQ.  Older issues have excellent Q&A.
Everquest Stratics Active news updates, good basic character info.
EQ'lizer Has an ever-growing equipment list..
EQ Maps Excellent site for maps and zone information.
Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium The best site for tinkering information.  Fulcrum picked up where Gnomad left off.  Home of the GPS (gnomish positioning system).


Have a site you'd like to see here?  I currently feel I have enough general sites so, unless its spectacular or has something to offer most others dont, I probably won't add it.  If it is that great or has useful enchanter information then, by all means, please contact me.    -Muddletupper.
