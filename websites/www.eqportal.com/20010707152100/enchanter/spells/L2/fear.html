Fear
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Causes a monster to become afraid and run away from the caster.
Be warned, it will return and may bring back others if it passed by them.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 40

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 CLR, SHD, NEC, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: The most popular use for this spell involves having a pet.   The common tactic is to get your target's attention with a damage over time spell (Suffocating Sphere at this point), let the thing hit you once so your pet (Juli's Animation or Pendril's Animation at this point, though I'd suggest it be Juli's) then cast fear.  As the target runs from you both your DoT spell and your pet continue to damage it.  Constructive use of such spells can allow an enchanter to solo things that /con yellow.

The next most common use for this spell is to simply chase something off for a short time.  This can allow you time to cast gate and escape or cast some other preparation spell.

The biggest problem with fear is that it isn't permanent.  Once it wears off, your victim will come chasing after you drawing any nearby companions with it.  Thus the DoT / Fear combination is not something that works overly well in dungeons.  It seems to be a rather nice combination, however, on open plains where you can see for a fair distance.
