Warrior's Tournament
Server Name: Veeshan
Name: Warriors' Tournament
Location: Freeport Arena
Start Date: 10/16/99 2:00:00 PM (PST)
Date Added: 10/9/99 6:55:19 AM (PST)
Date Updated: 10/9/99 6:55:19 AM (PST)
Description: 

All warriors between level 10 and level 
20 are invited to attend an organized bashing 
in the Freeport Arena! 

The entrance fee is 4pp. Money will be 
refunded if there are not enough participants. 
Competitors will be paired with equal or
near-equal opponents for one-on-one battles. 
Magic buffs are not allowed, but boosts 
from magic items such as rings are allowed. 
In case of an odd number of competitors, 
the weakest warrior will be moved up a bracket automatically. 
Looting is strictly prohibited. The first 
place winner will be awarded 50% of the entry 
fee money, the second place winner will get 25%. 

If you are a caster who can bind or dispel 
competitors, your help would be greatly appreciated. 

This event will only take place if at least 8 
competitors have signed up by October 16. 
Please remember to sign yourself up if you wish 
to participate; late entries will not be accepted. 

Unsportsmanlike conduct or broken rules will 
result in BAD things. You have been warned. >=D
