Suffocating Sphere
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Causes damage over a period of time. (called DoT)
24-31 points of damage total & lowers target's strength.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 20

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the best battle spells you'll have for a while.   The biggest drawback of a DoT (damage over time) spell is that you will need to actually survive long enough for it to achieve its full potential.  You'll either want to draw your target's attention with this spell or, at least, cast it early on in combat.  Its biggest benefit is that it has a decent damage ratio, exchanging a lowered mana cost versus damage done for its slower effect.

Its also great in groups.   Frequently the mage in a group casts a few spells then meditates for mana while the rest continue to pummel the target to a pulp.  Unless you're mana needs to be reserved for other spells right away, I find this is a nice way to assist in the thrashing even while meditating.

How effective is it?  I found myself soloing black bears at about level 6.   Perhaps not a great feat, but I was doing it without a pet.  All I would do was blast it with Suffocating Sphere once to get its attention, beat it with my stick until the spell wore off, then cast it again.  Typically it woul only take two of these spells and a little time to finish off a bear.

Get used to using this one too as you won't see your next DoT until level 12 when you can aquire the 4th rank spell Choke.
