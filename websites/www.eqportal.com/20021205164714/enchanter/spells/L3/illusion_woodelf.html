Illusion: Wood Elf
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Gives the caster the appearance of a wood elf.
Area of Effect: Self-only
School of Magic: Divination

Cost in Mana:

 10

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - just outside Mechanamagica, near ramp up to it. (1121, -1037)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Illusions can help you make your way into places you are normally disliked.  I seems that am npc's reaction to you is based upon the following factors.  Your faction standing (based on what you kill), your race, your class and your religion.  You can adjust faction standing by being selective about what you kill or don't kill.  Later on you can also temporarily increase faction standing with spells like Alliance.  it is with the Illusion series of spells you can adjust what race you appear to be to them.  Most common uses for this affect are to allow passage through towns that consider your native race to be KOS (Kill on sight).  A good example would be a drow/dark-elf attempting to walk thr streets of Felwithe.   Provided they haven't been killing elves or things that lower their faction standing with the elves, this improves their chance of walking the streets without getting jumped by every guard and shop-keeper in sight.  (what about class and religion?  Well, we're stuck with those and can't fool people)

Some illusions also give benefits as if you were the race you appear to be.  Examples would be improved healing rate, the ability to bash, etc.  Wood elf  seems to have no real benefit.
