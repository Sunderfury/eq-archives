Eye of Confusion
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Temporarily blinds the target.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 25

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1101, -983)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Not the best combat spell, but far from the worst as well.   While it will temporarily blind the target, the most this seems to do is stop it from attacking for a moment until someone hits it and it attacks them, probably with a lower chance to hit until it can see again.  The duration of the spell seems to be about 10 seconds.  I can only conjecture that monsters can not target you when blind the same way you can not target them when blind.  This means that spellcasting monsters will only be able to target whoever hits them first with the auto-targeting when blinded.

Casting this on a creature to get its attention frequently causes it to run away until the spell wears off.  Once it does wear off the being comes chasing after you to attack you.  Fear is a much more effective way to accomplish this affect.

I rarely hear of enchanters keeping this spell slotted, however, if I find a particularly good use for it I'll be sure to post it.  I'd love to hear of one.
