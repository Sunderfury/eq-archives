Cancel Magic
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Removes magic effects from target.  Seems to target each spell affecting target and has a chance of removing each.  You can not selectively choose which spells to affect and which to leave so this will remove both beneficial and detrimental affects.
Area of Effect: A single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 30

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 CLR, PAL, RNG, SHD, DRU, SHM, NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, central room (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Good for removing magical affects with a duration, like root.   At your current level you probably won't be fighting to many spell-casting beings that cast duration affect spells, but there are some.  Most magic you encounter will come in the form of damage spells with instant effects that this will not counter.

This spell has no effect on non-spell-based icons like poison, disease, and rabies.
