See Invisible
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Allows you to see invisible, hiding, camouflaged or otherwise unseen beings.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being, requires consent (must be grouped)
School of Magic: Divination

Cost in Mana:

 25

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 DRU, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, center room. (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Most useful for keeping a group of invisible people together.   Perhaps you wish to lead someone through HighPass safely?  The easiest way is to cast see invisible on both of you then invisibility on both.  In this manner you will both see each other and be able to follow or be followed where other monsters will not see you.

Besides, if you're like me you just like to know everything that's out there.  I don't like having invisible/camouflaged people running around talking at me when I can't see them.  The thought of invisible monsters nearby is just plain creepy.  I haven't seen any yet, but I believe there should be, after all ghosts shouldn't always be visible.
