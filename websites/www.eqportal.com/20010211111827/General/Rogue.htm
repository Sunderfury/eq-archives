ROGUE

Shadowy and stealthy these guys can sneak into or out of a fight. They are not able to stand toe to toe with a warrior but let one get at your back and your in deep trouble. Many of their abilities are not fully implemented but they show great promise out of combat. Rogues have the ability to gain wealth fairly easily as well and in several ways.
