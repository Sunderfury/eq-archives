Greetings,
I am a level 17 druid and perhaps narrowmindedly focused all my combat abilities into 1 handed blunt weapons. I played around with scimitars until I was about level 5, when I was able to buy a Fine Steel Warhammer. Growing up in the Faydark, druids are practically weaned on the stories of Crushbone, and the Screaming Mace that is found there. Seeing no future in slashing weapons, I concentrated on this goal.
Sadly (note sarcasm here), I've just come into posession of an Obsidian Scimitar, and my theory of no future for slashing weapons quickly proved incorrect. Dutifully, I returned to my guild and poured as many points as possible into my slashing skill. Now however, I am wondering "What other weapons might be out there, that so well compliment a druids style?"
Any recommendations of weapons and thier corresponding skills would be highly appreciated. Good hunting, my friends...

Greetings Diarmuid,

I am a druid in his 30th season on the Prexxus server. I too had started off using the 1h blunt. i quickly changed ove to 2h blunt as most or the better steives were of this class. After about my 16th season, I aquired an Observer Staff, this is a great 2h blunt staff, but it took up both my hand slots to use. After I gained the Testiment Of Vanear, a +10 wis +10 mana NO DROP book, I quickly switched back the the 1h blunt. I now use the Screaming mace in all my battles, But my Melee has come to be a bit less effient then in my younger seasons. I now rely mostly on my castings to help me win a battle. Snare, Swarm, and DDs have become my most trusted battle allies. In most groupings, I am often called upon to heal or buff, little to no "nuking" or Melee.
I still do however, use my melee skills in some battles that either i run low on mana, or have come close to finishing off the prey and wish to keep my skills toned up. This is just my version of what is good for a Druid to use. There are others who have an opinion as well. the best advice I can give you is this, do what feels right for you, we are all different in our own way.

Hello, glad to see someone using this message board =)

After 50 seasons, I've come to the conclusion that proficiency in a particular weapon class is not real critical. When I originally started, I put points into 1hs because I liked to wield a scimitar. Later on I saw posts about 2hb weapons being better, and I figured I screwed up. Oddly enough, I have never really used a 2hb weapon aside from my first worn great staff. I've always felt better carrying a shield, and I have yet to see any adverse effects =)
Now, I wield my Paw and CGS when grouping, and frequently when soloing. The only use for my melee skills is to finish things off, and since they usually start to run with 500+ hp, I've found it better just to blast and med rather than pummel it to death.
