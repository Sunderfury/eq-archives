Lull
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Lower Target Frenzy & Reaction Radius.  This means you can come closer to monsters without them attacking.  It also means the affected monster may not be triggered to attack you when you attack its nearby companion.
Area of Effect: Single Targeted Creature.
School of Magic: Alteration.

Cost in Mana:

 10

Component/s/ Needed:

 --

Classes:

 CLR, PAL, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Lulling can be used when you need pass near a normally aggressive monster.   It will not make the monster non-aggressive, but it will give you a better chance of slipping by without it noticing.  You'll have to play with the spell to determine your effective "safe" range from the monster.

This spell has no effect on the target once it is already engaged in combat.  It is meant to be a preventative spell, not a combat spell.
