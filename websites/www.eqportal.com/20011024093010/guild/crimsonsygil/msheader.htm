Patch Message 09/25/01

** Spell Changes **
- We have removed the changes to bard song stacking that were made with the recent emergency patch. We will be reviewing those changes for possible update in a future patch.


** Item Changes **
- The Shaman Epic effect has been returned to its previous effect (and it should conflict with fewer Damage over Time spells, as was the intention for the change made last patch).


** Quest Changes **
- Ivory Sky Diamond is now dropping as it should.

- Fixed an error in a Stonebrunt quest where an NPC was handing out the wrong reward.


** Miscellaneous **
- Added some code that might fix a bug with female hair and Erudite hoods that caused them to fail to appear sometimes when a helmet was removed. This would cause some characters to look as though the back of their head was missing at times.

- Mouselook will now work a little differently when in the first two third-person camera modes (overhead and chase). They will work similarly to first-person mouselook (i.e. your character's heading will change when you move the mouse left or right, the left and right movement keys will cause your player to strafe, and the camera angle will pitch up or down when you move the mouse forward or back). These changes should make those first two third-person camera modes more useful.

- The EverQuest Team

Hail, FotP!

I am attempting to organize a guild swap meet/yard sale/bazaar, or whatever you want to call it. It seems that most people end up with a bag or two in the bank full of stuff they keep meaning to give away or get rid of somehow. We could each clean out our bank vaults and bring all of the old stuff you can't find a use for, and trade/give/sell it to other guildmates. This would be a good opportunity to twink a character, or trade something useless to you for something useful. It's a form of recycling. This would also be a good opportunity to bring any tradeskill made items you want to sell (potions, armour, etc.) Trades folk could take orders if they want to offer services. I will probably try to set the date for a weekend, and extend an invitation to our alliance guilds as well. I hope people think this is a good idea, and want to come join in at this event

I am thinking September 29 would be the best day for this, I will post again when it is decided.
As an added note, I want everyone to feel welcome to this event, even if you don't have anything to give/sell. If you are low lvl and have nothing to spare, bring stacks of batwings to trade for things you need, this is mainly an opportunity to clean out our banks and put these items to good use.
