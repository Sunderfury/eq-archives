Pendril's Animation
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Summons an animated sword as a pet to protect the caster.
Area of Effect: targets self, summons a "pet".
School of Magic: Conjuration

Cost in Mana:

 12

Component/s/ Needed:

 a tiny dagger

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
HighPass Hold
Tips: At lower levels I found this pet to be of little use.  It tends to die way to quickly to provide much use and the cost of the component (10-15 coppers a piece) is more often outweighed by the need to buy spells and cloth armor early on.

At higher levels (rank 2 at the earliest, certainly at rank 3 and on) the pet becomes much more useful if you spend a lot of time soloing.  These pets like to taunt your attacker, drawing it off of you and allowing you the ease of casting without disruption from damage.

Words of caution, unlike normal pets these can not be commanded to attack or defend you.  They are not true pets in that sense, rather, they will defend you after you first take damage.  Unless you take damage in a fight it will not aid in combat, so if grouped with others and you want your pet to help you need to get hit at least once.

Perhaps a bug, perhaps intentional, but if you allow your pet to get the killing blow it takes  portion of the experience from the kill.  If you, or a group member, gets the killing blow your animated pet does not seem to take a share of the experience from the split.
