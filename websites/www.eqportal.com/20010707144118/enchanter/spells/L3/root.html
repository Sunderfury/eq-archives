Root
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Prevents the target from moving from the spot they were standing on when affected.
Area of Effect: a single targeted being.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 30

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 CLR, PAL, SHM, NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, center room. (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the common spellcaster spells, it is also one of the most commonly used.  The most common uses are to hold a target at bay while the caster blasts it with direct damage spells.  The next most common use seems to be to hold the target at bay while the caster makes a hasty escape to the nearest zone transfer or nearest guard.

While these options are certainly valid, enchanters have more than this simple option.  For escaping, why use root when we can use mesmerize much more effectively and not only stop the creature but make it forget you were ever fighting it?  For combat, this spell will suffice for a while, but next level you will receive whirl till you hurl which not only immobilizes the creature but effectively stuns it for 10-15 seconds keeping your pet/warriors from being damaged while they beat on it.  The only real advantage to root later on is for keeping a creature at bay while you blast it while soloing.
