Steps to Joining Moment of Clarity

    One Sponsor:  We are invite only.  A member of MoC (not in a trial period) has to sponsor you to the Officers of MoC.  Do not post or seek sponsors prior to getting approval from the officers.  The Applicant should be level 45 or higher to be considered for membership.  Some exceptions may be made.

    Officer Approval:  The MoC sponsor will propose the applicant to the guild officers (via posting on the MoC message board in the "New Recruits" message area).  The officers will decide if we need that particular person, class, level at the moment. It will be a simple yes/no within a few days time.  The officers will discuss the application.

    Five Sponsors:  Upon officer agreement, an officer will post a call for sponsors in our "New Recruits" message area.  Here MoC members can read about the person, hear the recommendation from the original sponsor and choose to sponsor the applicant or not.  The applicant will need 5 MoC sponsors (who are not in a trial period), and not have any outstanding "no" votes.  If there are "no" votes, then the officers and guild will talk about it.  If the issues cannot be resolved, the application is terminated.

    Trial Membership:  Once the applicant gets 5 sponsors, they are invited into the guild for a trial month.  During the trial month they should go above and beyond and show us why we need them.  They will participate in guild raids/events, read/post to the guild message boards and be a very active member.

    Final Voting:  Once the trial month is up, the applicant's full membership will be voted on by guild officers.  If the applicant receives a "no" vote, the issues will be discussed among the guild officers.  If the issues cannot be resolved, the applicant is removed from the guild.

 Removal from the guild

 Sad as it is, sometimes people simply cannot get along and personalities clash, or people act in ways that are unacceptable to guild guidelines. We don't want any boat rockers, greedy people, wingnuts, or people that don't put in 110% effort. Guild officers will decide on who will be removed from the guild.
 

Please note: The above "new member" rules were borrowed from the guild 'Affliction' and slightly modified.
