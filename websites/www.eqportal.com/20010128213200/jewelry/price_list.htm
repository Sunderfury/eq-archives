Jewelry Price List

Massive Disclaimer:  Due to popular demand, here is a list of some approximate selling prices for the various jewelry items. Please keep in mind that:

  1. These are my approximate selling prices. They will assuredly vary from jeweler to jeweler and server to server based on market demand. (They even vary with me, depending on how bad of a jewelry night I'm having. *winks*)

    Please, no angry emails. I am not attempting to set standard prices for jewelry. This is simply to give new jewelers an idea of where to start.
       

  2. The prices listed here are for individual items. I typically give a discount when someone buys, for example, a complete set of wisdom items or a pair of earrings or rings.
      

  3. All items are rounded to the nearest platinum for simplicity's sake. That explains the similarity in price, for example, between a platinum malachite ring and a platinum lapis lazuli necklace. Both gems are less than a platinum so, rounded up, their price is the same.
      

  4. The drastic price difference between platinum jewelry and other metals is based on the fact that 1) platinum bars are much more costly than other bars and 2) platinum jewelry is never trivial, so the fail rate is significant, even with maximum skill and decent wisdom.
