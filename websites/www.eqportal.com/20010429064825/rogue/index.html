10/2/99

 Site has gone live today!!!! This is the product of countless minutes trying to learn all I can about this mysterious class that few seem to want to play.  The mission of this site is to become the one center of all Rogue activity on the Internet.  I will continue to provide as much in depth info as I can on all aspects of playing a Rogue, including equipment, races, skills, pvp, and the infamous poisons.  Soon I hope to include some more interactivity to try and establish an actual "Rogue Community" here. 

Any info/advice/gripes are quite welcome and I will incorporate as much info as I can get.  So please drop me a line if you got any info to help your fellow brethren.  After all, us Rogues only got each other! and money. :^)
