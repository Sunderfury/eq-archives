Enchanted Armor & Weapons

One of the original selling points of the enchanter class in Everquest was that we would be able to make enchanted items.  Alas, when the game finally did come out we found that our ability to enchant was greatly hindered by the fact that there was nothing enchantable nor spells for us to enchant with.  The sole exception at the time, was out ability to enchant bars of silver, electrum, gold and platinum from which magical jewelry could be made.  A nice little tid-bit, but not what we had hoped for.

After months of game play, and months of players bugging them to let enchanters make enchanted items, Verant finally capitulated to our desires and has started adding enchantable gear.  So far there is only one item known, Wu's Fighting Gauntlets.   We would hope the list of what we can make should grow and, when it does, I'll further divide these pages up so it is easier to find what to make and how.  Until then, here is the information on Wu's Fighting Gauntlets.

Wu's Fighting Gauntlets
Magic Item, AC: 4, WT: 0.1, Class: Monk, Race: Human
Notes: They act as a light source as per greater light source.  Allows monks to punch for magical damage.

How to make them:  They require a master tailor (trivial level unknown at this time) to combine:
1 silk swatch, 1 greater lightstone, 1 glove pattern, 1 vial of viscous mana,  & 4 heady kiola into a large tailoring kit.  The heady kiola must not be stacked, they must be placed in seperate slots.

Breakdown of Components:

Silk Swatch: created by combining 2 spider silk (not stacked) in a tailoring kit.  Trivial at tailoring 17.
Greater Lightstone: sometimes found on willowisps and will-o-wisps.   Wisps are only damage by magic spells or weapons.
Glove Pattern: bought from tailoring merchants.
Vial of Viscous Mana: An enchanter made item, created from the 16th level enchanter spell Thicken Mana.
Thicken Mana requires a poison vial and a pearl as components of casting the spell.
Heady Kiola: brewing made item.  Made from 2 kiola saps, water, & bottle combined in a still.  Trivial at brewing 46.
