Tinkering Community Posting
The Tinkering Community came together to pose a number of questions to the gods. Over time. this set of questions became known as Posting 16681. On March 1, 2000, we received an answer after over a month of campaigning.

The support within the Community was grand, with many Gnomes and non-Gnomes replying and posing additional relevant questions. It was also through this effort that the Community Recipes section of the Compendium was born. Many replied to Posting 16681 with new Tinkering inventions.

While our work is not yet finished, we have managed to get through. You may find the official reply followed by the original set of questions below.

 

Subject: Abashi - Tinkering Community Questions

Ahhhh! Here it is! I was writing a reply to it earlier when the thread disappeared. Anyway, what I was going to say was:

Tinkering can definitely use an update and I'll see if I can get it on the list. I'd like to see more useful items out there that come from Tinkering, but the problem is avoiding things that tread on a class ability to any great extent.

As to a couple of other questions that I've seen in here, the tattered gnomish cloak isn't in game anywhere at this time, and I here that the gemcutter has *some* use, but not to the extent that some suppose.

-Gordon

 

Subject: Abashi - Tinkering Community Questions

Greetings Abashi!

I am the owner of Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium, a site that seems to recently have become an official voice for all Tinkers of Norrath. Many Gnomes have written in asking a series of questions that we don't have answers for yet. We are hoping that you may be able to track down the correct source of Tinkering information and pose these questions to them:

1. Thermal Cloak
The building of a Thermal Cloak has puzzled Gnomes since the beginning. Gimblestan's recipe for the cloak was not as clear as his others. The Gnome community would like to know if the Tattered Gnomish Cloak does indeed exist in Norrath, and if it is called just that. It would also be good to know if it can be purchased, obtained from a quest, constructed by a tailor, or loot from a kill.

In addition, could you confirm that it is indeed possible to build a functional Thermal Cloak? As you may view from the Thermal Cloak section of my compendium, Gnomes have tried a number of combinations without any luck.

2. Diamond Dust
With the recent discovery of Diamond Dust in Norrath, Gnomes have begun to create Gemcutters (see the following section). Question: Why is Diamond Dust a Lore Item? This prevents a Tinker from carry more than one at a time, and greatly hinders a Tinker's ability to create Gemcutters.

3. Gemcutter
Now that a handful of Gemcutters have been created, they have been promptly lost in attempts to make use of them. Could you please confirm that Gemcutters are indeed functional as intended? Unfortunately, their use is a little vague (Gimblestan was not as clear as he could have been).

4. Gnomish Fireworks
With the recent update to Gnomish Fireworks, they are a hit! Would it be possible for the firework launcher to disappear after it's use? It is a pain to delete a launcher after it has used up it's single charge.

5. Tinkering Components
Would it be possible to make Tinkering components stack? As there is only one location in the world where most components may be found, it is quite cumbersome to have an entire bag filled with a single metal rod in each slot, etc. As other trades have stackable items (being a Jeweler too, I know that gems and metal bars may be stacked), could you please investigate the possibility of Tinkering following suit? This change would make many Gnomes happy.

Thanks for all your help.
Fulcrum of Fennin Ro
