Weaken
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Temporarily decreases the target's strength.
Area of Effect: a single targeted creature.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 8

Component/s/ Needed:

 --

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the enchanter's first debuff spells, this spell helps decrease the amount of damage a monster will do.  Unfortunately the decrease in strength is minimal and only marginally affects the amount of damage done (perhaps decreasing it by a point or so) it doesn't hurt to cast this spell in combat if the opportunity arrises.   Doing so may save save a life, as sometimes a victorious fight leaves the person being beaten upon with a scant few HP at the end and this may make the difference.   Its also a good way to practice your Alteration skill by using this in combat.
