Tashan
Spell Rank: Rank 2 - learnable by enchanters at player level 4

Spell Effect:

 Lowers targets resistance to magic.
Area of Effect: A single targeted being.
School of Magic: Abjuration

Cost in Mana:

 15

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
(Freeport?)
(Neriak?)
Tips: One of the banes of every spellcaster is a creature's ability to resist the spells cast upon it.  Of all who cast, only enchanters can make the job easier by lowering their victim's resistance to magic.

Another great fact about Tashan is its incredibly quick casting time and relatively low mana cast.  Given a choice between drawing a target's attention with a spell that costs 20-30 mana and may still fail or using Tashan, many enchanters opt for Tashan.  Granted, it still may fail, but its quick enough you can get another shot off before they hit you if you are at maximum range.   I rarely see Tashan fail.  Once it does succeed, all your other spells have that much greater a chance at succeeding.  And lets face it, sometimes in over-crowded areas where people aren't too woried about being polite, claiming ownership over who has rights to kill something comes down to who draws its attention first.   Not many spells come close to comparing to Tashan's casting time of roughly 1 second.
