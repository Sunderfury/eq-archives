Minor Shielding
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Adds 10 HP and 5 AC to the caster.
Area of Effect: self only.
School of Magic: Abjuration.

Cost in Mana:

 10

Component/s/ Needed:

 --

Classes:

 NEC, WIZ, MAG, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, central room. (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the enchanter's two starting spells (the other being shallow breath).  This is a common mage spell, thus it may not be found in your local Enchanter's Guild, rather it will probably be wherever the "common" mage spells are located.  Early on it  you'll find you tend to keep this spell up at all times as it is your best defense (nomal cloth AC aside).  Later on, higher level versions of this spell replace it, however, you may find you already have your 8 slots invested in different spells depending on your style of play.
