Getting Started
The first thing you will need to do to get started in Tinkering is to be a Gnome. Other races may attempt to Tinker, but they have very little in the way of skill and will never advance. Some Gnonmish scholars believe that it has to do with their abnormal height. The second thing you will need is a Toolbox, as pictured to the right. With this toolbox, you will be able to take a number of items and combine them together to make something new. You may obtain the toolbox from a vendor in one of the windmills at Steamfont for a couple of platinum.

Lastly, you will need to be 16th level. At 16th level, you will start with a Tinkering skill level of 50. I've heard others state that you may Tinker prior to level 16, it's just that you cannot raise in skill level until you are 16th. I personally didn't start my Tinkering career until 16th level.

There was a time when it was not possible to advance in Tinkering until you were about 25th level. Then the world changed and you could raise in skill prior to 25th level, but there was a skill cap. Today, Gnomes have shown that there may no longer be a skill cap for Tinkering. This is great news as it puts Tinkering in line with the other trade skills in Norrath.

Once you are ready to try Tinkering, you will need to know the various recipes for making Tinkered items. You may obtain these recipes by buying various books in Steamfont and Ak'Anon, or by referring to the various sections of the Compendium. All you do is place each of the items into the toolbox and hit Combine. It will either work or fail. As with other trade skills, you may raise in skill by creating items. Don't be surprised to spend hundreds of platinum for each point increase!
