Warriors by Tralin Fuzzlenutterstine

Gnomes make the best warriors. There is not one person that will agree
with me. They have no hps, and no str. And where is the problem? I duel
a lvl 28 tweaked out Orge warrior, with my Lvl 28 gnome warrior. He was
using a mith 2 hander, i was using a PGT, and ykesha with haste sash. He
has over 1000 hps, i have just about 1000. I won. He kept missing, and i
when he did hit i riposted. Not to mention my PGT would proc on an
average of every 6 swings. First thing that is important with a gnome
warrior is AC! Then DEX DEX and more DEX! More dex, more crits and
procs.
