Shallow Breath
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Causes 4-6 damage, decreases strength, and decreases agility.
Area of Effect: a single targeted creature.
School of Magic: Alteration

Cost in Mana:

 7

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: One of the enchanter's two starting spells (the other being minor shielding) it is our only real attack spell early on.  It doesn't do much, but never underestimate its use.  Early on an enchanter may do little to no damage with a weapon, however the ability to cause 4-6 damage comes in quite handy and it hardly hurts that you are decreasing your targets strength and agility at the same time.  The decrease to strength and agility is not cumulative for multiple castings of the spell.

This spell is most effective early on as a means of catching the attention of desired targets.  In the rat-race that constitutes the first few levels of play it can be difficult to keep up with a monster long enough for your meager combat skills to allow you to actually hit a monster and get its attention.  With this spell you can target your victim, turn on auto-attack, blast it with shallow breath and wait for it to come to you.
