Welcome to the new Unearthed Arcana. We have a new layout and design. If you wish to have your banner advertised on this web page please email Sailex or Kilandar. As you may notice, none of the links are working. This is because this site is under heavy construction and will be constantly. Check this page for current news.

About this page:
1. This page is dedicated to Dark Elves in the realm of Norrath.
2. Currently we are planning to have 2 classes represented here. Wizards and Shadow Knights.
3. We will have guides, spell tables, items, and other info pertaining to dark elves and these 2 classes
4. Hopefully we can add more classes but that will come at a later date.
5. There will also be a culture section known as "The Oracle" which will have stories, poetry, bios ..etc.
6. Feel free to stop in at our message board for free discussion on dark elves.
7. If we get to know you and like you then we will give you access to the Salafi's domain
