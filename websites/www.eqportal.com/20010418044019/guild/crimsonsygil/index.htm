/say

You stand before a once rather plain blockish building which recently undergone some expansion and at least one new coat of paint. Three steps take you to the double front doors, which are never locked. Beyond is a short hall, lined with trophies of past battles, hand woven tapestries and portraits of heroes from the past as well as the present. A trail of glowing orbs leads you in the direction of a low rumble. As the distance between you and your destination eases the rumble is recognized a conversation, many, many conversations intertwined with laughter and general merriment.

  "Welcome, and well met traveler"

  You are greeted warmly, the non-descript man offers to take your cloak.

  "You have discovered the guild hall of Fellowship of the Phoenix, if ye be a member then you know your way around and I am wasting flowery words on you. However if this is your first visit the feel free to poke around. If you don't belong someplace then you will not be able to get in, otherwise make yourself at home."
