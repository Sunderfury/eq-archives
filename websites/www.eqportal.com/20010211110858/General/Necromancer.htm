NECROMANCER

This formidable class is perhaps second only to Wizards in doling out the damage. The difference is that where a Wizard dishes out a horrifying amount very quickly the Necromancer doles it out in a more spread out fashion. Instead of mighty bolts of power and rains of death a Necromancer hits with disease and poisions. All the while their pets are acting as a buffer and doing additional damage. They even get a few just plain cool spells, however dealing with others is much more difficult for this class.
