Minor Illusion
Spell Rank: Rank 1 - learnable by enchanters at player level 1

Spell Effect:

 Change the caster's appearance to that of nearby object.
Area of Effect: self only.
School of Magic: Abjuration.

Cost in Mana:

 10

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'Anon - in the Mechanamagica, near Juline Erncaller. (1093, -976)
Felwithe
Freeport
Neriak
Tips: A nice utility spell, many monsters don't seem as likely to notice you when you have minor illusion cast on yourself.  I like to use this when looting my corpse in a relatively dangerous zone.  You can't move while under this effect, but you can turn it off whenever you like.

It can also be more effective than invisibility in PVP.  While invisible, some people can still see you plain as day.  If you happen to look like a nearby object you may go completely unseen to the casual observer.   Need a nearby object to mimic?  Carry an extra small object, like fishing grub.  Drop them and they become a bag on the ground.  Now when you cast minor illusion and become the nearest object (the small bag) you are quite hard to be seen from a distance, especially in darker zones like the Faydark.  Its also useful to note you can turn, cast and pick up objects without losing the spell effect.

An interesting effect: if you are minor illusioned and are charmed by a monster, you may still be able to cast spells on others, but you will  be unable to move from he spot thus your party members can flee if need be.  This can prevent you from physically attacking other party members as well as preventing the monster charming you (an evil eye perhaps?) from sending you off after other targets.
