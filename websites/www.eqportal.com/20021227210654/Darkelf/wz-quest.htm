Quests Items Spells Guides

Quests are something that Dark Elf wizards and wizards in general have had a lack of. There are a few quests out there but I will only supply the worth while and fun ones that Dark Elves can do. The following quests come from the new zone "Temple of Solusk Ro." These quests come from the test server and have just been implemented the general servers. I recently went to the zone and confirmed that these quests have not changed since being implemented.

 

Wizard Items: Gardern and Vilissia will make several items for wizards:

Staff of Temperate Flux: (Gardern) He needs a Heart of Fire from an Inferno Goblin Wizard in the Caverns of Solusek, a Heart of Frost from a Goblin Wizard in the Caverns of Permafrost, a Rod of Bone from a Stone Skeleton by the shores of Lake Rathe and a Staff of Ro.

Weeping Wand: (Gardern) He nees a Silver Wand from a Silvered Guard in the Temple Cazic Thule, A Bloodback Wand from the Mountains of Rathe, Twice-Woven Silk from a Faerie Guard in Faydark, and a Scepter of Tears from the Tomb in Castle Mistmoore.

Tishans Kilt: (Vilissia) You must take a Shadowed Ball Shadowed Men and bring it to Trankia in the Everfrost Mountains. Not sure what else is required.

Acumen Mask: (Vilissia) She needs a Glowing Mask from a Skeletal Monk in the Caverns of Guk, A Patch of Shadow from a Shadowed Man, a Darkbone Skull from a Darkbone Skeleton in the Estate of Unrest and a Bonechipped Mask from a Goblin Headmaster in the Ocean of Tears.

Runescale Cloak: (Vilissia) She needs a Lizardscale Cloak from the Temple of Cazic Thule and the three Runes of Scale. All three can be found on Crocodiles - Saltwater Crocodiles in the Caverns of Guk, Deepwater Crocodiles in the Oasis of Marr and Firescale Crocodiles in the mountains of Lavastorm.

Here is the Staff of the wheel quest.

Tarton's Wheel: This is the quest to get the components for the Wizard Spell Ice Comet. There are 3 NPC's standing along ice wall near Starn bearjumpers place. Talk with the human wizard Sulgar and he will tell you that he is looking for tartons wheel. (you must have good faction with beta neutral to get this quest - unfortunatley, I have no idea what you can do to raise this faction). He gives you a glowing case and a tome of hints that spans all over the 3 continents. The case is to hold the spokes of the wheel and the tome gives you hints on where to find the pieces. Be aware that the case is a no rent item and will disappear when you log out, so don't put any rods in it until you are ready to give them to Sulgar. Tartons wheel apparently lets you travel through the planes. You combine the pieces you find in the box and get The Staff of the Wheel (a 1HB weapon) and the star of eyes (a gem which casts unlimited identify spells). When you give them to him, he will give you the runes of frost and astral needed to research the ice comet spell. (Then pray your research doesn't fail). There are 10 pieces that you need scattered across the face of Norrath. The names of the 10 rods are: Azia, Beza, Caza, Dena, Ena, Fana, Geza, Heda, Izah, Jaka. Be aware that the rods spawn very rarely, are no drop items, and are just laying there for anyone to pick up (They are just called "a rod", and identify as wizard only) so they will be very difficult to get to in time. Of course, if you are not a wizard, please leave these rods alone, since, as a no drop and thus nonsaleable item, they are of no value to anyone other than a high level wizard on this quest. The locations are as follows:

(1) [azia] On the ocean floor in Eruds Crossing past the zombie boat where the zombie sailors hang out. (location - neg 1162, pos 2340);

(2) [beza] In the underwater cave in the Everfrost plains. It is in the "house" all the way back and right in the cave, where 3 dry bones guard it in the center of the room;

(3) [caza] Lake Rath. Underneath the first island you see is an underwater tunnel leading to a stone tower. Its on the top floor of the tower. (You need enduring breath for this one);

(4) [Dena] Dagnor's cauldron, right outside the entrance to Kedge Keep. If you face with your back to Kedge, it is directly diagagonal left, in a corner.;

(5) [Ena] Lower Guk in the Reanimated hand room. This one is the only one you need a group for, unless you're brave and kamakazi it. Warning, the floor is the same color as the rod in this room and it makes it very hard to find with the hand and the dar beating on you;

(6) [Fana] Oasis of Marr in the underwater tower next to the above ground tower, on the top floor;

(7) [Geza] Highhold Pass in the water near the waterfall closest to the Tiger Inn. There are 2 waterfalls there. The rod is in front of the one closest to the inn;

(8) [Heda] Qeynos Aqueducts, in the shark pit;

(9) [Izah] Neriak in the water in front of the bar called "the blind fish". The bar has 4 glass windows that are underwater, and the rod spawns right in front of those windows;

(10) [Jaka] Unrest in the water outside the right side of the house near where the the gnome speulunker is. Instead of going into the tunnel that leads to him, you go a little right of that entrance and there's a very short passage where you can find the rod. (Submitted by Shaidar)
