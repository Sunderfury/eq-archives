Latest Luclin News
Hello All,
We apologize for the problems some of you have experienced getting into EverQuest after the
launch of Luclin. As of just a bit ago, we patched a new graphics DLL that fixes the problems
some of our users (AMD users in particular) were experincing crashing to desktop. This does not
mean that all crashes to desktop have been fixed, but this code had the potential for a lot of side
effects, and fixing it is a big step forward.
In the coming week you will see us concentrating on fixing many of the problems that remain. We
are also hard at work on optimizations for both framerate and loading time. Load time is a particularly
big target for us to work on, and we have some promising results in development. Our goal is to have
that code out to you as soon as possible, but it needs to really be tested properly before we will feel
comfortable doing that.
In addition, we're also working on server side stability in a few zones, most notably Hollowshade Moor.
These should be taken care of very rapidly as well.
Thanks very much for your patience.
The EverQuest Team
