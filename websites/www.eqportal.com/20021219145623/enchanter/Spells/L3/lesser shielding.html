Lesser Shielding
Spell Rank: Rank 3 - learnable by enchanters at player level 8

Spell Effect:

 Adds AC, HP & magic resistance to the caster.
Area of Effect: self only.
School of Magic: Abjuration

Cost in Mana:

 25

Component/s/ Needed:

 -none-

Classes:

 NEC, MAG, WIZ, ENC

Locations Found:

 Ak'anon - in Mechanamagica, center room. (1075, -1002)
Felwithe
Freeport
Qeynos
Neriak
Tips: Not a very powerful spell, but anytime we can grant ourselves extra armor, hit points and a better resistance to magic it doesn't hurt.  This spell is simply the next step up from the minor shielding spell and they do not stack.  The duration is long enough that you can cast it, remove the spell to memorize another one, and only have to occasionally rememorize it to refresh/recast it.
