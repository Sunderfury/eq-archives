February 25, 2000 - Territory Section Updated
Greetings! With the Collective of Old Fennin regional forums currently down, I've had some time to add to the site.

I've added some detail to each of the regions located within the Kingdom of Ak'Anon. I hope you enjoy reading them. You may find them in the Territory section. I am hoping that everyone that decides on the Ak'Anon region will also decide on a region within the Kingdom. You could pick one for your birth, and another for your current residence (or they could be the same).

Once the government is in place, I believe we should do a Kingdom census to get a feeling for the population. Perhaps some of the questions would include:

  • Name
  • Sex
  • Occupation
  • Marital Status
  • Religious Beliefs
  • Guild Affilitations
  • Birthplace
  • Residence
  • Education
 
February 23, 2000 - New Site
Greetings and welcome to the new Kingdom of Ak'Anon site.

Within this site you will find a proposed system of government for the Ak'Anon region. I present it for your consideration and look forward to hearing your comments. Please be sure to post all comments to the Ak'Anon forum.

First off, after you've taken a look, it would be great if we can discuss the ideas located within. To do so, be sure to head over to the Ak'Anon forum. Let me know what you like and what you don't. It would also be interesting as to where you see yourself within the Kingdom.

I'd like to also stress that I don't see the Kingdom of Ak'Anon as only a Kingdom for Gnomes. While it is true that Gnomes will likely make up the majority of the populace, the non-aggression ideals of the Gnomes allow for other races to participate in the government. All that would be required is to swear allegiance to King Ak'Anon.

Perhaps you're a Wood Elf that has moved to the Territory of Lesser Faydark to study at the Observatory. With your studies now over, you've decided to make the Territory home, and run for Regent.

Or you're a dwarf that has worked in the mines of the Solusek Eye Territory for most of your adult life, and consider it your home. With the recent move to a Parliamentary system, you have decided to run for Regent, or perhaps as the Lord of the Ak'Anon Order of Miners.

Hopefully some thoughts to get the discussion started...

-Fulcrum
