Steps to Joining Moment of Clarity

    One Sponsor:  We are invite only.  A member of MoC (not in a trial period) has to sponsor you to the Officers of MoC.  Do not post or seek sponsors prior to getting approval from the officers.

    Officer Approval:  The MoC sponsor will propose the applicant to the guild officers (via posting on the MoC message board in the "New Recruits" message area).  The officers will decide if we need that particular person, class, level at the moment. It will be a simple yes/no within a few days time.  The officers will discuss the application.

     Removal from the guild

     Sad as it is, sometimes people simply cannot get along and personalities clash, or people act in ways that are unacceptable to guild guidelines. We don't want any boat rockers, greedy people, wingnuts, or people that aren't willing to help. Guild officers will decide on who will be removed from the guild.
