8/26/99
I received this from Morphius on Quellious server:
I have found a vendor that sells all the 16th and 20th level PET spells and a few others. The spells are available by a High Elf vendor on Mudtoe Island in the Ocean of Tears. The island can be reached by taking the ship from Queynos to Kaladim. When the ship makes its single stop at Erollisi Island exit the ship and walk off the dock to land. With the dock at your back turn left and follow the coast of the island. As your get near the end of the island you should see a small boat waiting at the edge of the water. Once you are near the boat look seaward towards the West and you should see the only visible island off in the distance (on a clear day). You can jump in the boat or swim it doesn't matter and head for the island. As you approach the island you will see a medium sized stone building at it's top center. If you don't you probably are on the wrong island. Walk up to the building and inside is the Vendor. Be careful. There is a cyclops on the island. I never went near it so I don't know if it is aggressive or not. Most of the islands in this area are dangerous.
