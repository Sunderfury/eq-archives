Provinces and Territories
 
The Kingdom is divided into a number of political regions, the newest being the Solusek Eye colony. A list of these regions is as follows:
  • City State of Ak'Anon
  • Upper Steamfont Province (includes territory just outside Ak'Anon and the Windmills)
  • Lower Steamfont Province (includes territory beyond the Windmills)
  • Lesser Faydark Territory (includes dwellings in LF, and some property beyond)
  • Solusek's Eye Colony (includes the Solusek Mining Company)


City State of Ak'Anon
     
Ak'Anon Entrance   Royal Observatory   Royal Palace   Merchant Courtyard

The City State of Ak'Anon is the Capital of the Kingdom of Ak'Anon. The Royal Palace and all of the guild halls reside here. The Royal Observatory headed by Fodin Frugrin, located just outside the entrance to Ak'Anon, is considered a part of the city.

The Capital is known for the many clockwork creations that fill all corridors. The clockworks work in a variety of roles, including that of guard, merchant, cleaner, and sweeper. You'll also find many a Gnome working as a mechanic (under the direction of the Order of Engineers) tending to the various machinery that keep the city running. Other locations of note is the great Library, which houses the sum of Gnome knowledge, and the renowned Ak'Anon Zoo.

The City of Ak'Anon is made up of Gnomes from all walks of life: from scholars to Tinkers, warriors to clerics, merchants to adventurers. And while much of the city is safely traveled thanks to the clockwork guards, most residents would warn of the lower depths around the abandoned mines and scrap yard.

The city is also home to the last postal stop of Faydwer. Once a week, mail for the provinces and territories is placed within the Cargo Clockwork at the Royal Observatory. From there it is delivered to the Windmills of Upper Steamfont. Residents of the two Provinces and Lesser Faydark Territory may then travel to the Windmills to retrieve their mail without requiring a trip to the Capital.
Upper Steamfont Province
   
Observatory   Windmills   Dragon Bones

Upper Steamfont Province is the wealthiest region outside of the Capital City. The great market place just beyond the entrance to the underground city falls within this region's jurisdiction.

The observatory located east of the market place, headed by Phiz Frugrin, is second only to the Royal Observatory. The observatory makes up the main complex of the University of Upper Steamfont. The University is also known for its archeological dig of the great dragon bones.

By far the most prominent structures are the great Windmills. Managing to pick up even the slightest breeze, there are no other structures like them in Norrath. They are maintained in a joint effort between the Ak'Anon Order of Engineers and the Eldrich Collective. The Windmills also house the Tinkering branch of the University of Upper Steamfont, where apprentices help out with the daily Windmill maintenance.

Most Gnomes that live within the Upper Steamfont Province are merchants or Tinkers, many of them quite wealthy. The region surrounding the University is always bustling with students.
Lower Steamfont Province
Observatory

Many consider the Lower Steamfont Province to be more rural than it's more populate neighbour. Life here is generally slower and more relaxed. The main attraction is the observatory that is headed by Tindo Frugrin.

Many Gnomes that live in Lower Steamfont tend the land to produce a variety of grains. Lower Steamfont is also a popular vacationing area for Gnomes that want to leave the hustle and bustle of their everyday lives for the serenity of the plains and foothills. Some of the wealthier Gnomes own cottages here.

The distance of the province from the Capital does have its downsides. For one, the Watchmen rarely make patrols through the region. Sometimes the Kobolds raid the farms for goods, and the occasional wild puma wanders through populated areas.

Some of the younger Gnomes tend to leave Lower Steamfont to attend university in Upper Steamfont (when they can afford it), or, for the more fortunate ones, to apprentice at one of the guildhalls in the Capital. The observatory also acts as a college for those that cannot afford the more expensive universities.
Lesser Faydark Territory
Observatory

The newest of the Kingdom of Ak'Anon regions on the continent of Faydwer, the Lesser Faydark Territory is a wild place, filled with large beasts and bandits. Few Gnomes call the territory home. Most that do work at or help in the construction of the observatory headed by Trudo Frugrin.

The territory was born when a large passageway was discovered through the Steamfont Mountains. There have always been smaller passageways, but none the size of this. Once Gnome miners widened the entrance (using controlled amounts of Firewater), it was possible to move building supplies and trading carts through. Construction of the ever-important observatory began, and treaties were formed with the two other races that call the Lesser Faydark home: the Brownies and the Faeries.

Some of the other races in the Lesser Faydark have opposed the construction of the observatory feeling that it will be used to spy on them. So far, this has not slowed its construction (although the reported over spending may). To help alleviate these concerns, people from all over Faydwer are invited to spend some time at the observatory. It is the only observatory where the visitor has complete access (other observatories have restricted access).
Solusek Eye Colony
 
Fort Entrance   Fort Interior

Following the closing of the mines within the City of Ak'Anon, another source of ore and gems was required. While the Dwarves were willing to trade for their mine's output, the Kingdom of Ak'Anon required its own internal supply. Captain Bipnupple was dispatched to find that supply.

His search took him to the Lavastorm Mountains. He discovered an entrance to a dual cave system that became known as Solusek's Eyes. Discovering that the caves had enough ore and gems for the Kingdom but were already inhabited by a Goblin clan, a treaty was struck. Word was sent back to the City of Ak'Anon regarding the discovery, and supplies and miners were dispatched to the newly declared colony.

Not long after construction of the colony began, the Goblins violated the treaty and declared the Gnome excavated regions their own. Captain Bipnupple ordered the construction of a fort to protect the Gnome miners while they work. Tinkers were also instructed to lay traps in passageways leading to the fort to deter Goblin raids.

Most Gnomes that live in the colony are either miners or Tinkers (that work at the creation of clockwork guards), with the majority dedicating their time to mining. It is hot work excavating near the lava pits, but most are not deterred, with the prospect of the riches at hand.

Other Gnomes make their livelihood through the transportation of ore and gems back to the Capital.
