ENCHANTER

Enchanters are probably the hardest class to play. Even the game designers seem to be confused on what Enchanters can do and how they work. The official word is that thier role is to Enchant poeple, places and things. Only 1 spell is known to enchant a place (Sentinel) and the only thing they can Enchant is metal bars for the making of Jewelry. They have the lowest offensive punch and the weakest defenses of all classes. So if Verant does not have the answer then what can an Enchanter do and do well? In combat they work best at changing the circumstances and pacing of a fight. They can confuse or charm a monster and speed up or slow down the combatants. If the party has to run the Enchanter can most assure them of a succesful escape (Druids are admittedly better in some circumstances). They have the best buffing and debuffing abilities of this group though are not as strong as Bards or Wis based casters. Where an Enchanter really excells is out of combat. They are the best Tradesman in all of Norrath. With decent starting Int to quickly learn a craft and the Cha to buy and sell at good prices this should be obvious. Going beyond this however they have the ability to assume the guise of any PC race and modify factions and thier Charisma so that they can reliably buy and sell at the best rates anywhere in Norrath.
