Thank you for your interest in joining Midnight Sun!

First let me tell you alittle about our guild and our beliefs before you decide if this is indeed a guild that you wish to be apart of....

Midnight Sun IS:
Here to have fun, to show initative, to have integrity, to have honor, to be helpful to others, to promote the better side of EQ, to be there for one another, to make new and lasting friendships, and to make Norrath a better place for all.

Midnight Sun IS NOT:
Here to power level you, to be your pesonal taxi, to "twink" your characters, or to provide you with "phat lewt". If this is what you are looking for in an EverQuest Guild, then you will do better looking elsewhere.

 

Step 1 : Grouping/Sponsorship
The first step in seeking membership in Midnight Sun is to get to know some of our members ingame. Seek our members in your level range and make a point to party with them. The more members of the guild that you party with the better, as that increases your chances of meeting the sponsorship requirement. Another good way to meet our members is to visit the Events Calendar and come to any of the scheduled activities. Midnight Sun's events are always open to non-members of the guild. In order to join Midnight Sun, you will need to have at least one current member willing to sponsor you.

 

Step 2 : Knowledge/Understanding
Once you have partied with some of our members and feel that this is a group of people that you could enjoy spending time with, you need to go over our policies on the CONDUCT page here on the website. If needed, we welcome posts to our Public or Application Forums where any questions regarding the guild will be answered.

 

Step 3 : Posting Application
After you have met the above two requirements, you are ready to post your application and desire to join here on the guild website. Head on over to the Application Forum and post the following information in order to be considered for membership:

  • Character Name
  • Character Race/Class
  • Character Level
  • Email Address
  • Brief statement on why you want to join us

 

Step 4 : Sponsorship Acknowledgement
Once your application has been posted on the Applications forum on the website, your sponsor(s) will need to post on the same thread stating their sponsorship of your membership.

 

Step 5 : Guilding
After your sponsor has posted on your thread in the Applications forum, you can be guilded ingame by any officer of the guild. At that same time the officer will provide you with the passwords to the members only forums on the site. Be sure that you have read and agree to the Guild Rules of Conduct found on the Conduct page before accepting membership.
