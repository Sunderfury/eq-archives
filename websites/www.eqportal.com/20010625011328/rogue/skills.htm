I'm not going to go into all the basic skills, that info you can get off any site.  Below are the Rogue specific skills and as much information on them as I can find.

Skill Level Function Skill Cap Full Description
Sneak 1 Move slowly, quietly, and won't disrupt Hide. (Lvl + 1) * 5 Used in Conjunction with Hide you can move while invisible. Drawback is that Sneak makes you move so slow.  Not 100% rogue, but close enough for the list.
Hide 3 Turn Invisible. (Lvl + 1) * 5 Used in Conjunction with Sneak you can move while invisible.
Dodge 4 Avoid getting hit.   Rogues are not about withstanding hits, but rather avoiding them.  Just spend one point on this and it increases by itself.
Pick Locks 6 Open doors without a key.   Almost as useless as Sense Traps at this point due to a lack of places and situations to use it.
Pick Pockets 7 Steal money and loot from NPC's. Can make you a LOT of money. (Lvl + 1) * 5 Be careful, if you're caught the NPC will engage you and they will be VERY hard to deter.  Even if you never strike back it will take several hits and taunts from another player to get them off you.  The more you practice the less likely you are to get caught and more likely to get something.
Sense Traps 8 Allows you to determine if there is a trap nearby.   Still considered worthless due to the extremely limited number of traps in the game. 
Backstab 10 Strike your enemy from behind and do an enormous amount of damage.   This is the signature combat skill of the rogue.  Best results are obtained from behind and you must use a piercing weapon.  You'll want to max your piercing skill at each and every level without fail.  Max your piercing before you use any other weapon or you will regret it.  Also save up some practice points for this, 10 to 15.
Archery 10 Use a bow and arrow to inflict damage.   Rogues are one of the few classes that can use a bow.  Even though we now get critical hits with throwing weapons, the bow and arrow still have much more damage potential (and are cheaper!)
Parry 12 The ability to use a secondary weapon to deflect blows.   What can I say?
Safe Fall 12 Leap from high places and prevent getting hurt.   This skill as wonderful strategic potential.  It could very well be your only escape from some bad situations.  Use it to jump out of a window or off a ledge to get a head start on your pursuers.  Prime it with about 5 practice points then start jumping off stuff.  Small stuf at first of course.
Dual Weild 13 Use a second weapon in combat.   With this and Double Attack you are capable of landing 4+ blows per round (using fast weapons).  Just put 1 point into it and start swinging.  One bit of warning, be weary of damage sheilds with this, they can be lethal to say the least.  See the strategies section on how to deal with them.
Double Attack 16 Get a second attack in per round.   Several places have noted this as a level 15 skill, but according to my trainer, it's a level 13.  Researching.  Use a slasher as your secondary weapon and leave your dagger, etc. in your primary for backstab.
Apply Poison 18 Apply poison to a weapon.   A difficult skill to master, and store bought poisons really don't seem worth it.  If you do want to use poison, practice this as much as possible before you get to creating your own.  Your own poison are very difficult to make, and you don't want to waste any more of your efforts than you have to.  Be sure to check out the poison section to learn more.
Make Poison 20 Create very lethal poisons.   Again a very difficult skill to master.  Requires a lot of money and time.  Luckily rogues are inherintly wealthy if they use their skills accordingly.  Check out the poison section for more.
Disarm Traps 20 Disable a trap.   Notice a trend here?  Redundancy annoys me so I won't.
Instill Doubt 22 Makes your enemy run away.   This skill will help you get out of a bad situation by make your enemy turn tail and run.  Currently researching the effectiveness both on NPC's and in PvP.
Disarm 27 Removes your opponents weapon from their hand.   This was an absolutely beautiful PvP skill, but now is just marginally effective.  It use to drop their weapon on the ground where you could pick it up and take it.  Now it just puts it back in their inventory.  Few players will go for more than a round or two before getting it back in their hand.
Riposte 30 Parry plus a counter strike.   Allows you to deflect a blow and turn it into one of your own.  Researching more on this.
