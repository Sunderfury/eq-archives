  From Russia with Love, straight to the Dain!Ttnaig Frostreaver, May 14, 2001


For months now people have wondered why the fuck we've never killed Dain, we had no fucking idea either to be honest. The good Dain came through for us today, making up for a few months of us leaving him alone. He rewarded us with the first coldain 10th ring in Everquest, along with a few new bad ass items, Congratulations to Bentos!

We have no problems with the Michael Jackson's of dwarves, our good friends the Coldain. Dain Frostreaver issued a plea to Triton today, requesting our assistance in defense of Thurgadin against the Frost Giants. Though we do use Kael for armor quests, we just don't give a shit about the frost giants to let them live. For the Glory of Dain, ATTACK!

The quest was a fucking blast, four hours of combat. It's nothing like your normal quests too, picture a real time strategy inside of EQ. You're constantly dealing with mobs flanking you, also the dwarven hero doing the ring quest is in complete control of the dwarven armies, their positioning is vital to intercepting and combating the giant armies. Really the quest is fucking insane, by far the BEST one implemented in everquest.



We picked up some nice items as well, first off is the Lance of Narandi. The rewards given by the dwarves after his death were bad ass as well, Narandi's Faceguard, Choker of the Wretched, and Narandi's Crown. And of course, the ultimate ring.. the coldain 10th ring.

 

Quick and to the point today, the quest was a fucking blast and provides good incentive to finish a lot of them. Screen Shot time!
