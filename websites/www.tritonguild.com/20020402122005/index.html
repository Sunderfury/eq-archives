RAID SCHEDULE
Triton Only


  Ssra Temple - For Rent - Contact Triton  LoLoL @ Rombus, Mar 31, 2002

Almost forgot to mention that we won't be moving the website for another week or two. Ignore my update about expecting downtime this week. ;)

Following what was perhaps the gayest display of fucking Triton luck ever yesterday, we returned to Ssra to show the High Priest exactly what we were made of.  Hours of preparing (read: we let Athlene setup CoH and she takes for-fucking-ever to zone) pushed us to the edge (thx bugged walls), but in the end Triton was victorious.  Grats on gaining another server-first and becoming one of the few guilds server-wide to drop the High Priest (/shlong). 

Loot was, well... who cares!  We finally owned the High Priest (2 deaths, though I'm still debating if Nitton dying counts) and showed him just what we're made of.  Ok, fine.  Let me just say that as usual typical Triton luck prevailed.  Grats Grimdoom on the Shield of Ssraeshza and Gaven on the Bone of Bertoxxulous.  No rare spells, a copy of C3 and Greater Familiar (/shock!).  I have to ask myself what a FT6 shield with pretty much no other stats and a 2HB Shaman only weapon are doing on a mob of this caliber... Verant, smack yourself please! 

All-in-all it was an awesome victory for Triton.

After HP died we were all pretty pumped so we decided to take a crack at the Blood for phat lewtz.  This turned out to be not only the most entertaining part of the night but also the most frustrating. 

Ok, we get into the Emperor's throne room and start looking around.  No problem, we can do this... get ready, play with him for a bit, then fight him.  Bingo, no problem... Blood down, completely schooled.  Shak races the other Ice Wizards (who naturally got EXP) over to the corpse and starts clicking like mad~ boom... 100m/hp Cleric hammer and a Ssra Bracer of Honor (8 all stats / sv. all + 20 ac).  Kast on Cleric thing, Kanyu on Bracer.  This is where things get all fucked up.

We're sitting here, about 30 second passes and all of the sudden king fag Ssra pops and begins to RAPE our raid.  27 to 2 in 10 seconds, no joke.  Fucker tore us up like there was no tomorrow.  Minael and Ivy are FD so we'll be ok.  Minael logs in after /q'ing out and is instantly DT'd (the message for the DT is bugged btw!).  Ivy gets warped right under Ssra for no apparent reason.  She works up the courage to get up and dies instantly as well.  Ooooooown!  Not only does the entire raiding party have to be summoned, but the corpse of the Blood also ended up going poof.

To sum it up: Blood died, Emp popped before the corpse could become public, he bent us over his lap and spanked us, Bloods corpse rotted.  GG Verant!

G A Y!
