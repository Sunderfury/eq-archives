Scarab Armors

小型種族装備限定の緑色PlateのQuestです。
初心者オススメ

South KaladimのWarrior Guildにいる金髪Dwarfに話し掛けると・・・

You say, 'hail Vacto Molunel'

Vacto Molunel says
'Well met Gafus. I am Vacto Molunel. If you are seeking to outfit yourself with the finest weapons in all of Kaladim, I am the one to see. I also posses a [rare talent] you might find useful.

You say, 'what is rare talent'

Vacto Molunel says
'Peaked your interest did I? Well, you see, I am one of the few Dwarves who posses the knowledge and talent to craft a unique type of armor entirely out of Scarab Carapaces. While my specialty is making [Scarab Helms], I have also been convinced to make [Scarab Breastplates] from time to time. All of the pieces I craft are to Dwarven proportions, but I have had some Gnomish and Halfling customers as well. They come from all over for my armor.

Scarabから獲れるCarapaceでArmorを作ってもらえるようです。
GnomeやHalflingでも作ってもらえるみたい・・・って事は小型種族しか装備できなさそう。

Small Scarab Helm
AC4 , CHA-1

You say, 'what is need to make scarab helm'

Vacto Molunel says
'Interested in a Scarab Helm are you? Well, because my talent is so unique and my time is so valuable I am afraid I could only make you a helm if you were to provide me with the two Scarab Carapaces necessary to accomidate a head such as yours. I will also need a payment of 5 Gold Pieces. The Butcherbloks are crawling with worker scarabs that have just the right size carapaces for our needs.
