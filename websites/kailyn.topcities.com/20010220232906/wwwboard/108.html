Also, I have seen posts by people who were in the early Betas. They say that your ATK value used to be divided into two numbers:

Hit % / Damage

IE, one number was a measure of your ability to hit, the other was a measure of how much damage you could do.

Strength raised the Damage number.

But, at some point, they decided that the two number setup was too complicated, and instead changed it to one number, with an arbitrary "/ 1000". This was to avoid confusion. :)

Since we have seen in tests how much Str actually raises damage (someone said 100 Str was about 1 more point average damage per hit?), I think it is safe to say warrior classes should only really worry about the To Hit part of the ATK value. And that part is not effected by Str.
