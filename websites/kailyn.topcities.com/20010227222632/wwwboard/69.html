If you're fighting something that you can hit with the proc, I agree wholeheartedly. Even at a 50% resist rate, dual Yaks edge out dual Waks. However, when you hit a 60% resist rate, they are even. Beyond that, the Waks are better. (All of this is my opinion, backed by a few calculations, of course.)

So, it depends upon the anticipated resistance rate which to use (assuming you have the luxury of both). For general purposes, I'd choose the Yak because I believe that you can hit most MOBs with the proc, even most planar MOBs.

However, if you know you're tackling Cleric houses in Hate or some other very resistant MOB, it seems to me that you'd switch in the Wak.

BTW, I don't think this applies to just Waks. The Amygdalan Tendril is another debated weapon. It's better than a Yak without procs, but not as good as a Yak with procs. I tend to use a Yak in my primary hand and the Tendril in my off-hand. But, if we've got a Spite Golem or Cleric running at us, I just bring up inventory and flip them--only takes a second.

Just an opinion...
