EQ parse incorrectly reports total hits
[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ EQ Melee Board ]

Posted by Draxin on April 23, 202000 at 16:14:03:

I have heard time and time again that STR had no effect other than weight carried. Even though you can see
your ATK number increase when you increase your STR. My statement was that STR does increase your chance
to hit. The basis that most people have been using for their conclusion is EQ Parse. Unfortunately EQ Parse
incorrectly reports total hits. The hits that are not reported are those that the monster Parries, Dodges and Ripostes.
ex.
[Sun Apr 23 12:00:29 2000] You try to slash a crag spider, but a crag spider parries!
[Sun Apr 23 12:00:40 2000] You try to slash a crag spider, but a crag spider dodges!
[Sun Apr 23 12:01:05 2000] You try to slash a crag spider, but a crag spider ripostes!

In order for a mob to use a defensive skill it must first be hit, just like with us. Verant has stated this before.
