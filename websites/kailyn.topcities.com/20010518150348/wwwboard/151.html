Nice to find a site for warriors. Thought I would pass on a little Kunark info. In the Lake of Ill Omen, in the NW of the zone around 3300 positive, 3000 positive is the entrance to a large gobbo cave. Inside is a named gobbo, Lord Gorelik. He is a high level shaman who spawns a lot. He carries the Mithril Staff stats 28 damage 45 delay warrior only. I have my druid bound outside the entrance and check when I log in to see if he is there. So far he has been there every time. He is too hard for my level 45 druid to solo, but with a few friends I have managed to get a staff for my warrior (lvl 49) and a few more for my warrior freinds. Lord Goreliks spawn rate will probably be nerfed soon but for now he is just waiting for you to come and get your staff )
