Dex proc is item based. It is not a generalization of if I have 100 dex I will proc once in every x amount of swings.

Most warrior/sk/pally proccing weapons have a low "minimum dex proc" compared to Rogue weapons.

For instance, the 75 dex may get your Yak to proc, but good luck seeing more than one proc a night with a 75 dex weilding a gnoll hide lariat.

I think there may also be a variable in there that is based upon the level required to get it to proc in the first place.
