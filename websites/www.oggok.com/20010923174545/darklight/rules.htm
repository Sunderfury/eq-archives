Guild Rules
[07.18.2001] Last Update

General

RolePlaying...

Try to remain incharacter (IC) at all times, though situations may warrant otherwise. Get involved with the guild, create a unique character history and perspective for your chacacter and his ideology. Avoid zone flaming and chat not related to game events. If you have something that needs to be said not in a game manner then preface your comment with OOC (Out Of Character) Remember, /shout for incharacter zone messages and /ooc for out of character zone messages.

Proper Language...

Swearing, RL racial slurs and off color jokes will not be tolerated. We want this guild to be fun but still functions as maturely as possible. Insulting persons for RolePlaying uses is acceptable but do not direct any of them at the player, only his character. Things such as 'Whats your LOC?' should be said in OOC.

Use of /anonymous...

The command /annoymous should only be used in cases of extreme harrassment, undercover work, and enchanter illusion spells. This command will remove your guild tag. Using /roleplay should be used for things like moving the guild from zone to zone and for battles. Generally leave /roleplay turned off the remainder of the time because it prevents the /who all Guild from functioning. Hopefully Verant will fix this problem in the future. If this happens the entire guild shall move to /roleplay.

Twinking and Exploits...

This guild will not engage in such activities as hand offs, game AI manipulation for level advancement, and mule characters. Verant does not tollerate the use of exploits, cheats, and 3rd party programs and so this guild will not either. In extreme cases such as character corruption, some actions may be deem reasonable but all three members of the Triad must agree.

Guild Interaction

Treat all Members with Respect...

Your character and another guild member's character may have opposing views but all out hatred and anamosity amongst guildmembers will not be allowed to continue. All the members of this guild are brothers in arms. Treat each other with respect and friendship. An army divided shall fall.

Follow the Chain of Command...

The officers are not unjust in their dealings and will not show favoritism les they lose all rank and privileges. However, you will be required to follow their instructions. In the guild, the Triad is the top line of command, followed by the commanders and then the leutinents.

Use of Tradeskills...

Tradeskills take money to learn and master, especially if you have several failures in a row. For fellow guild members you can charge a fair price for your services. No one is expected to pay cost for products, but neither should they have to be what one not in the guild would be charged. If you want to sell your services at cost for guildmembers that is your right. Members should never expect this kind of action or assume that becuase they bought the components that a labor charge will not be applied.

Use of /guildsay...

The guild channel is a way for all members to stay in constant contact no matte what zone they are in. Avoid usint the guildchannel as your personal chat room. Spying Information about our enemies and their strengths and weaknesses shall be collected only by ingame means. No members shall be allowed to create 'spy' characters of opposite races. If it is found out that you are either running a spy or are a spy for one of the other teams you will be immediately removed from the guild and put on our Hated Enemy list.

Killing Other Players...

We are evil, feel free to back stab, root and overly bash those of opposite teams and do not show them any honor in battle. Run from battles you can not win, but do not leave your brother behind to die. This is one of the worst offenses you can comit against the guild.

Non-Guild Interaction

Do Not Camp Newbie Respawn Points...

You can acquire a number of kills by attacking people as the respawn points in newbie zone. This activity should be viewed as cowardice. The slaying of the helpless and defenseless does very little to spread the darkness. We must destroy the strongest of the lights forces, not their underlings.

Dealings with the Dark Races...

If another character is not part of our guild, but is part of our team then treat him with the honor do is race. We do not want to promote discention among our peoples. That goes against our guild charter.

Dealings with Non-Dark Races...

DO NOT group with members not of the Dark Race Team. DO NOT assist them unless you have a good roleplaying reason. Slavery may be a case where grouping and dealing with them would be considered appropriate. Of course, if you wish to sell them a fine tattered cap for 4p then be all means gouge the prices. DO NOT sell any really good items to those not of the Dark Team that may later be used against us.

Guild Wars...

We shall make several enemies as we spread the darkness on Norrath. Remember that your character hates the other guild's characters, not their players. The /guildwar command will not be necessary but only the Triad shall retain the power to declare official guild wars. Guild Officers may need to meet with opposing guild officers from time to time to discuss mutual items such as where to hold battles and things of this nature. This will all be done in OOC.

Languages Forbidden to Teach...

Dark Speach, Troll, Ogre and Teir'Dal must never be shared with those not of the Dark Team. This is considered a major offense against the guild and against your entire team and race. However, take all opportunities those weak minded fools offer to learn their languages. DO NOT exchange one of our languages for one of theirs.

PvP Rules...

You will never ever pull the plug while Zoning. If caught doing this. It will lead to a warning next action will be dismissal from the guild.

You will always show honor to your victim. By not attacking while looting or running out of the zone while being escorted. You will leave the Zone after getting your body if you have been killed by a PKer. You wil then have to wait atleast 10 mins to return to that zone to fight with that Pker. Its this that allows them to feel voctory in battle. Its a form of respect. And yes not all PKers deserver respect but we will still show that we are honorable.
In battle with another player you will not bag unless you see the other player is naked. This is the only way Bagging is ok. If you are caught being naked you will be warned. To expand on the equipment rule. If you have something they can loot exp. Banded, magic jewelry, or anytype of armor. This is acceptable. We do not premote naked gankers. If any question please ask a Traid Leader or the Guild Leader to clear it up for you.

Punishment

Minor Offenses...

Each case will be different. Hopefully there will never be a need of punishment. Most minor offense shall be dealt with in a RolePlaying fashion that may include such things as being stripped of all awards and rank or being outcast from guild activities for one week.

Major Offenses...

Everyone gets one warning. After that your character shall be removed from the guild. None of the guildleaders want to waiste their gametime babysitting. Several repeats of a minor offense shall be considered a major offense.
