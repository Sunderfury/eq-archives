Oggok is the center of Ogre 'Learnun' Almost every trade skill can be practiced inside the city limits, but the supplies must be imported from other lands. The location of most trade practice areas are listed below.
Trade Help

Items
Location
Smithing Forge Inside the Metil Armer is Oggok's forge. Molds and metal will have to be purchased outside of Oggok. There is an Ogress in the Feerrott that sells armor molds, but for the lower items to practice on you will have to acquire them from other sources. Ogre blacksmiths are in short supply so the right Ogre stands to make alot of shinies.
Pottery Kiln Inside the first chamber of the left-most cave if you are facing them. Clay and design sketches will have to be purchased outside of Oggok. The best place for these materials is in the Darkelf Merchants in South Ro Desert and Grobb.
Brewing Brew Barrel Clurg has a brew barrel in his tavern, the Meet and Drink tew bye. Alcohol components will need to be purchased outside of Oggok. Some of these components are for sale in Grobb.
Tailoring Patterns & Kit The Ogre running the leather armor store has both sewing kits and patterns. Large kits and non-armor patterns can be purchased from an innkeeper in the Feerott. She also sells the molds necessary for creating a sewing kit.
Alchemy Pouches & Herbs Inside Oggok, past the armor stores, is a small shop with a flaming skull in it. An Ogress there sells the components for Alchemy.
Cooking Oven The oven is in a little shop in a dead end hall, north from the floating skull. Bup, an Ogre merchant in Feerrott, sells cooking ingredients, but not supplies like jars, etc.
Fletching Bow & Arrow Parts Praak sells some components as does a merchant outside of Oggok, in the Feerrott. However they are mainly for bows, arrows parts will need to be purchased in Non-Ogre lands.
