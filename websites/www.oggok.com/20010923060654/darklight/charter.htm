All Must have a Beginning...

The story of the three and the founding of the war began with the coming of nightfall, and with it a great darkness. Black clouds filled the skys over Antonica, cutting off what little light the moon and stars could afford. But the darkness was more than just the shadows of clouds for the flickering of torches could not penetrate far into the night's embrace and even magic seemed less effect against it. A great wind began to howl, accompanied by thunder and lighting, yet the storm brought with it no rain for it was not the work of Karana. Some began to stir in Norrath that night, a feeling that all could sense. Good, evil, and even the indifferent.

The first was called from the swamps in the lower regions of Norrath. A troll hunter of renowned prowess and battle skill. The dark wind found him unable to sleep, something in the back of his mind pulling at his consciousness calling him deep into the swamp. Something was waiting for him, what it was he did know. Abandoning his home he set out for this unknown. Far into the darkened swamp he did travel, so far that the terrain became unfamiliar to him yet the call pulled him deeper and deeper. Abruptly the troll stoped, standing at the edge of a murky pool. The wind danced on the surface of the water causing it to churn violently and something else as well. Slowly a figure rose from its depths. The troll dropped to his knees, knowing the embodiment of his deity and creator, the Faceless One, stood before him. The dark avatar reached out a hand, beckoning the troll to approach. The warrior had served his lord all his life and did not hesitate to once again follow his bidding. Something changed though, the water around the troll's legs began to thicken as he walked, soon he was unable to move his feet. The more he struggled, the tighter the liquid's grasp became. Then it began to make its way up the troll's body, its thick black tentacles wrapping around his arms and chest. As the fluid constricted around his throat the troll looked up to see the emotionless face of Cazic Thule and he attempted to cry out, but there was no air left in his lungs to scream. Then life gave way to death.

The dark wind howled as it moved ever farther, spreading north across all of Norrath. A jungle, this one's inhabitants larger even than the trolls. It was here the second was called, a lone ogre, his hands stained with the blood of many light-dwellers. He was a student of history, one of very few who kept the old ways. A warrior who knew of his past and where his people came from. As he felt the wind's touch, something in his soul told him destiny was nigh. Looking out into the jungle, he began to run. To run down many pathes he had never seen before, being drawn away from the safety of his home. As he ran, the Ogre began to feel more like the prey than the hunter. As he neared the extent of his stamina and will power something stirred in the darkness. At first the Ogre saw nothing more few large trees. Slowly the realization came over him that the object was not a group of trees, but the silhouetted legs of a larger being, taller than the ogre, taller even than the giants. The Ogre felt immense power and, for the first time in his life, great fear. Before him stood the Great Warlord, Rallos Zek. The Ogre was so struct with awe at seeing the father of his race that his muslces froze in place. The figure stretched one mighty hand and, with a single crushing blow, the ogre was knocked to the ground in death.

The wind blew on, meeting its final distention far to the north in a forest already thought haunted by many. In the shadows of a tall tree knelt a dark elf. He was a priest of unquestionable faith and loyalty. In silence he meditated, listening to the dark night, knowing that things beyond the normal were taking place. Dread filled his heart as he reflected on the omens he had bore witness to in his dreams the past four nights. He was pulled from his trance by a flash of light, a small floating bauble that glowed blue in color. After a moment, almost as if waiting just long enough to catch the dark elf's curiosity, it began to move away. Quickly and quietly he jumped to hs feet and gave chase. At first he was careful to not make any sounds as he moved, but the floating light moved faster and faster from before him. It did not take long before he was forced to give up all stealth and secrecy to run so that it would not move out of sight. Hours passed, yet the dark elf was so enthralled by the sphere that he did not stop in his pursuit. Eventually the globe stopped moving forward and began to ascend high into the air. The darkelf fell back and covered his eyes when the glowing ball flashes bright as day. Quickly it faded back to a glow again, revealing itself to be a staff in the hand of the Prince of Hatred. The preist stood motionless, unsure if he should kneel or run away as fast as his feet could move. The decision was made for him as the entity pointed one finger at the darkelf's chest. Energy flashes forth knocking him to the ground. The darkelf felt ice form in his veins, the cold sending a shock through his frail frame and a burning pain in his chest. He could fill a cold grip around his heart as it began slow, his body growing colder and colder. The pain was unbearable but then death bid a welcome release to the darkelf.

As life ends for the three, so does the waking dream begin. Through death the three were removed from the primary plane of existence and taken to the realms beyond. There they each received a vision, different for all yet the message remained the same. Innoruuk the Father of Hate, Rallos Zek the Warloard, and Cazic Thule the Faceless One struck an unholy alliance. A war was coming, one in which the gods themselves would be involved. A great war in which no one would be spared, triumph or death the only possible outcomes. The dark three are not prepared to lose this war and so, even as the light dwellers squabble among themselves, they chose their generals to make ready the troops. Great turmoil is to come upon the realtive peace that had selted on Norrath and even the so called gods of light will be fighting amongst themselves. The three will use this opportunity to take hold of Norrath and any who would dare stand in their way shall be destroyed. The gods then returned Troll, Ogre, and Dark elf to their former lands although not their former existance. Each was given new life and new purpose. They must prepare the way for the great shadow to cover the light and spread its harmful rays to all who do not bend their knees in subjugation.

-- Goals of the Darklight Brigade --

Bring ruin and desctruction to the light dwellers!
The time has come for Norrath to feel the embrace of darkness. The unholy alliance of the three dark gods shall send fear and trembling into the hearts of all. (The Darklight Brigade shall not faulter to destroy the other teams).

Make a defense for the darkness!
The light dwellers are aware that the day of their defeat is coming and they will try and stop the darkness knowing full well that they stand in the path of the inevitable. (The Darklight Brigade shall stand ready to defend their dark brethren and their lands).

Spread the words of darkness and anguish to all!
Those that remain unaffiliated can not become martyrs. Those that remain in ignorance can not know why their lives were forfeit. All must know of the coming day, all must be prepared for its arrival, all must know they face the dark alliance of Innoruuk, Rallos Zek, and Cazic Thule. (The Darklight Brigade shall promote RolePlaying).

Destroy those that are traitors to the darkness!
Ogres, Trolls and Dark elves must stand together to prepare the path. Those that make alliances outside of the darkness must be destroyed so that a pure people can stand ready when the war comes. (The Darklight Brigade shall hunt our and execute turncoats).

Make ready for the coming dark war!
Only those that are strong shall survive the great war. The dark races must train themselves in combat. When the great day arrives the must make their stand firm on the side of darkness for the war shall be among gods, but settled by mortals. (The Darklight Brigade shall have fun being evil).
