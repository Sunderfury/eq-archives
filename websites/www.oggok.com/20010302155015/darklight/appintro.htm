Becoming a Member

Step One:

Read through the entire Charter and Guild Rules, Make sure that you not only understand them, but you agree with them as well. If something does not sit right with you, then e-mail our guildmaster. Ignorance has never been an acceptable reason for failure to comply with guild ideologies.

Step Two:

When you're finished reading those pages, fill out the application if you are still interested in joing. Try and answer all questions to the best of your ability and honestly. Just because you decide to say "No" on any given question does not mean you will automatically be barred from entering into the guild. This application will be sent to our guild leader who will review it and contact you via e-mail. Make sure you use your real e-mail address and name. Rest assured, the Darklight Brigade does not engage in spamming.

Step Three:

A GM or Triad Officer will arrange a time to meet with you for an online interview. This will be a question and answer period, both in character and out. Feel free to express any concerns or question to the guildmaster at this time. If, after the discussion, you are still interested in becoming a member you will be promoted to an Initiate. An Initiate is a not a full guild member, but rather one in training. You will be invited to adventure and mingle with all guild members. This testing period gives both you and our members a chance to make sure the guild is a good fit for for you, and you and good fit for us.

Step Four:

After a brief period, usually one to two weeks, you will either be admitted into the guild or denied. All Initiates are added as members at guild meetings and must swear an Oath of Loyalty to the guild and to the forces of darkness. This will be a public oath in front of the entire guild.
