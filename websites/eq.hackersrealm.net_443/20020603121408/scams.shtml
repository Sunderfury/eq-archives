scams [this section is best if viewed with a screen resolution of 1024x768 or higher]:

Note: If a program claims to do any of the following, it is a scam: Edit the amount of money you have, edit the amount of health/mana/stamina/exp you have, or edit the stats of items in any way (i.e. make it faster, or remove the no-drop tag). Those things are not really possible to hack.

eqhexhack [*homepage]
This program has been posted on a number of message boards, newsgroups, and auction sites. It claims to do many things, such as platinum and the ability to edit your level. However, none of those things are possible, and are certainly not possible with this program. The author was charging $100 for the program. Those who ordered it reportedly received rouge versions of TuxTrainer or IceTrainer burned on a CD-R in a bag, and a note stating that the other things were fake.
cpr2plat [homepage]
This program was posted too a message board by a person called "Encoded_Ice". It was supposedly meant as a joke. It claims to be able to covert the copper in your inventory to platinum, however it will always give you an error message and attempt to make your screen go black to lock you out of your computer.
Trainer for sale!
Numerous trainers have been posted on auction sites like player auctions and ebay. Some posted by a person named "EQ33T" and also goes by the name of "Anime3k" and more recently goes by the name of "Digitalworkings", who is the reason why we no longer release our offsets. Him, and people like him used our offsets to release trainers for sale in the past. Most trainers on auctions sites are fake altogether.
