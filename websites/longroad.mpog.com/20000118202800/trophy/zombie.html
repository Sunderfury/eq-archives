The Long Road Trophy Case

Western Plains of Karana and Kithicor Forest
To a young adventurer, the Zombie can be an awe-inspiring and life-ending fight. Its lifeless body performs at lightning speed and with unearthly strength. Amongst its undead kind, however, the Zombie is actually a weaker opponent as it does not possess the enhanced necromantic protections of the Ghoul or Ice-Boned Skeleton.
