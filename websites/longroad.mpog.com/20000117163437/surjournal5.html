The Rescue

Day Twenty - Blackburrow

I awoke on bare rock badly bruised. I remember seeing the gnoll pup standing in the middle of hollow tree and yapping at me. There was no need to alert Fyodor of this, it was only a pup after all. As I lunged for him he sidestepped and I fell through the false earth. I don’t know if "false earth" is the term I should use but it seems to fit. Now I am all alone down here. Karana help me, I fear the worst. Fyodor’s words haunt me now, "They are a crafty lot". Why didn’t I listen?

Shouting out would be a very bad idea at this point. If the Gnolls were not aware of my presence before they would be if I yelled. Besides, I am ranger and rangers track. Surely I can track my way out of here, can’t I? As I turned around my heart stopped. The room was scattered with corpses. Dwarf, gnome human, elven, and even troll bodies littered the floor. Would I meet the same fate? I started the journey out.

Staying close to water’s edge I made my way around a bend and saw a door on a ledge up above me. It was accessible by a wooden bridge across the water. I started to sneak across, tracking anything that was close. As I reached the other side I crept up to the bridge and snuck across. I listened to the door and heard no sound, placing my hand on the door I started to turn to the knob when a hand reached around and clasped over my mouth, jerking me back and almost off the bridge. I went for my sword but I was on my back before I knew what hit me. Looking up I saw the weary face of Fyodor, and he looked horrible.

"Have you any idea what is on the other side of that door? The gnoll commander and his two personal elite guards!" We continued down the waters edge till we reached the end. At this point we dove under the waters surface and swam through a small opening. As we emerged on the other side I grabbed Fyodor’s arm and halted him. "I am tracking two patrolling Gnolls" I exclaimed. We snuck behind them and finished them off with ease. We looted their corpses and found coin minted in Qeynos. What were Gnolls doing with Qeynos coin?

We fought our way out of Blackburrow’s pit and finally rejoined some other adventurers that were fighting some burly Gnolls and a couple of guarding Gnolls. Still, I find no evidence of a conspiracy. Fyodor’s training has become invaluable. I have made numerous kills without him, though I am sure he kept a close eye on me all the same. We camped on ledge over looking a pool. We alternated keeping watch but neither of us really slept. Tomorrow, we continue the search.
