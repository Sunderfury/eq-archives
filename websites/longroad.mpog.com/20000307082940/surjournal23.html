Chaos in the Crags

Day Ninety - Highhold Pass

Walking to the orc-controlled pass and join up with other mercenaries was just a short distance from where the bandit battle took place.  Immediately we happened upon to humans that were apparently grouped together.  Sanchez and Cyprusdraco were a friendly enough pair from Freeport.  Sanchez was a healer and not too shabby a one at that.  His skills in combat were not near those of Jhared’s but they served him well.  I had barely had time to introduce myself when a wave of orcs attacked from a ridge.  They were upon us at lightning speeds and well armed. 

The orcs in the misty thicket were unorganized and not well equipped, that was not the case here.  I suppose that secretly I had hoped that these would be the pawns and centurions that I had fought before but I was very wrong.  These orcs stood a good two feet above me in size.  I drew my bow and let two arrows fly as they advanced.  Sanchez cast a spell rooting one in his tracks but the other two were still advancing.  I replaced the bow with my tomahawk and swung with all my might.  I buried it deep inside the orc mercenary’s gut as Cyprusdraco dropped another one behind me.  The third mercenary, now free from Sanchez spell advanced on his positioned and called for support.  An Orc Warrior crashed down from hiding and destroyed a young elven mage not more than four paces from me.  His yellow mage robe smeared with his blood.  I turned to attack but my shaman friend had already taken the beast down.  I drew the barbed whip that Isabo had given me and snapped it into the orc mercenary’s flesh as it began to pummel Sanchez.  Surprisingly, I was able to control it very well with my left hand.  With my right I continued to attack with the tommy.  Cyprusdraco laid the killing blow to the orcs skull.

We looted the orcs and found several pieces of armor and a bronze weapon.  The strange part is that these orcs were carrying currency.  Whoever was behind this was paying well.  We congratulated one another on a fine job and settled into position for the next wave.

The day continued on much the same way.  All total our grouped killed 13 orcs.  Unfortunately, only two scalps were salvageable but the items these creatures were carrying would be a worth a lot to a vendor.  We decided to go back to the keep and celebrate with some ale.  I was told that they might even have some of the outlawed Blackburrow stout.  I wish Fyodor were here to witness this.  I think even he would be proud of me.
