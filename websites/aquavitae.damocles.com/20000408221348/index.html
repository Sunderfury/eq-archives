04.01.2000 - Guild Leadership Structure

Well Jedis started asking questions... then Galil opened his mouth about something... so Baps and I knocked heads a few times and came up with a few new positions on the chain of command tree. There are now 3 new positions for officers in the tree. (Actually there's 4... Malabu was the High Priestess but the position was given to Alyana not too long ago as Malabu resigned from EQ) The positions of Seer, Lieutenant and Advocet are open. Each of these positions will be assigned by the leader of the branch they are from. If you would like to be considered for one of these positions contact your branch leader (Baps, Lamma and Adman respectively). If you are a leader and you don't know what to do, talk to me. And if you are a leader and you THINK you know what to do, talk to me before you promise anything to anyone.

In addition to these changes the position of Lord has been changed to Captain cuz Captain of the Guard sounds better than Lord of the Guard. (I considered Master Chief as well... but...)

Leaders, these are YOUR branches. If you would like them changed, tell me and we'll make it happen.
