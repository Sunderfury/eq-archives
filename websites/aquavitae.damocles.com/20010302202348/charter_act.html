Aqua Vitae Rules and Codes of Conduct:

Aqua Vitae believes in freedom for its members. This freedom granted on our members is to allow them the choice of only minimal participation in AV. We want people to join and get what they seek from this experience, not for us to gain what we seek from them. This is not a dictatorship, it is, and hopefully will forever be, friendship. We encourage active participation, involvement, and comments but no member will be alienated for not doing these.

The officers of AV are here to provide support for our members and to create a more enjoyable playing environment for not only our guildmembers but also the rest of the residents of Norrath. Please feel free to contact any of them with complaints, issues, problems or compliments whether you are a member or not.

All of AV realizes that each individual in this game has their own right to enjoying their time and we by no way own a "camp", a "quest", or any locations or creatures in Norrath. If an AV member is denied the right to something of the like listed above then no member will take matters into their own hands. We believe the best way to reach others is not by threatening them or alienating them but by demonstrating effective cooperation.

No member will practice deliberate training. Accidents happen however and the guild will not suspend a member because of this without investigating the incident(s). Training constitutes anything that the trained person believes it is. AV members realize it is within their power as a human being to resolve these conflicts with simple reasoning and compromise.

All members understand that their actions and comments will now carry the weight of our guild's name as well as their own. They also understand that their actions reflect upon their Mentor who are also responsible for their Ward's actions.

AV membership is based on past actions and intentions. Comments about our members are welcome, however, they will not be prosecuted unless they contain threatening language, profanity, sexually explicit depictions or other extreme abuse. Our guild allows and expects individual freedom so the views or ideas of any single member may not necessarily reflect the supported views of the guild itself. We work as a group and think as a family, we are not a collective.

Removal from the guild will require a unanimous vote from the officers and final approval from the guildmaster.

Any type of profanity, demoralization, threatening language, sexually explicit depictions or personal attacks will not be tolerated over guild chat. We realize people become frustrated so minor infractions, so long as apologized for, can be overlooked but continual disregard for the rights of the others can constitute removal or suspension from the guild. Guild chat is here to help us communicate not to spread hate propaganda or used as a trading post for the mistreatment of others.

- Snydar, AquaVitae GM
