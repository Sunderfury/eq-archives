As many of us know, kunark is a very difficult place to travel. Both because of the size of the zones and the high level mobs that seem to be EVERYWHERE and 99% see invis.

I wanted to make some guidelines for people travelling kunark but I've decided that some of these should be guild RULES. Yes, it's that important.

  1. Avoid travelling alone. These zones make you feel like you're lvl 5 again trying to cross North Karana. You WILL get stomped.
  2. Use invis. Most mobs see invis but some don't. And it never hurts to try.
  3. Sow is a MUST. Both to escape and to cut down the travel time across these huge zones.
  4. Avoid using the Kunark Gates. The teleport spot is relatively safe, but you can't get ANYWHERE without going thru that damn tunnel. The tunnel that leads out of the portal area is packed with mobs that have been trained back to the druid circle and wizard spires. Just getting thru this tunnel usually requires a well balanced group of 40's. Once you pass thru this tunnel, you have the entire dreadlands to cross. This is a huge mountainous area filled with Giant sized creatures. Rather nice place to hunt if you can keep up with the pace, but terrible to try and travel across. I can not ever recommend crossing the dreadlands from the portals to get to the Firiona Vie Outpost. Use of this gate should be limited to those who want to stay in the dreadlands for xp, but even then you're better off staying near one of the zones. Unfortunately this means taking the boat. Trust me, you're better off waiting on the boat than crossing the dreadlands. It takes a lot less time and you will spend less time regaining lost xp.

A side note on evac. When you evac, you're done for the night if you have tanks with. They will have to wait for the boat to get back, or cross the dreadlands. You might as well take everyone to the dreadlands and cross together as a whole group even if the casters are bound in Firiona Vie.

Tanks, speak up. Would you rather die where you stand and be res'd in a few minutes or be evac'd back to the mainland and spend 30 minutes waiting for a boat? Let your group know so they can plan accordingly. Casters can gate themselves out if need be... Just make sure you let everyone know which you prefer. They'll probably evac you anyhow but...

Enjoy Kunark everyone, but be careful, it's been an xp sucking experience for many people, but I don't think I can go back to the old lands...
