Joining the Order of Erudin

If you are an Erudite, High Elf, or Human and not a practitioner of the necromantic form, or you are of another race that we respect and strongly feel we should accept you in our Order, then please feel out this form. Allow up to four days for response, but most of the time response will be same day.

Also, we highly recommend you download mIRC (Internet Relay Chat) and join some of our members around #EverQuest, #EQ4 and such on EFnet (irc.chat.org). We will mostly be around there until we feel it is time to set up our own IRC channel.

* Indicates a required field

What is your ingame nickname going to be?

* What is your IRC nickname going to be? (Or, if you don't use IRC, your usually used nickname)

* What is your ICQ number?

* What is your email address?

How many hours do you think you can dedicate to the Order a day?

* What class will you be?

* What race will you be?

* Which phase are you in? (most of OoE is in Phase 4).

* Why do you want to join the Order of Erudin?

Do you have any other comments on the guild, the site, or just anything else in general?
