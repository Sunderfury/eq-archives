Good guilds! Neutral guilds! Let us unite...

Posted by Tohd Ohmnius (209.214.67.166) on January 03, 1999 at 15:20:17

I had a daydream that all neutral and good guilds would one day adventure together in friendship. That we would all Role-play to our fullest and have a lot of fun. That evil would tremble before us.
Then I blinked back to Reality and realized I had just dreamed everything that was present within the Alliance of Virtue. True, the name (Virtue) sounds UOish, but we are HARDLY copying UO. We only wish friendship and RPing to spread and evil to die. Besides, Names are not as important as what is on the inside. The AoV stands ready to welcome all.

  • Join the Alliance. Protect your Virtues. Ally!
