Current Skills in EverQuest

Listed here are the current skills in EverQuest. This information has been cross-checked through several sites to confirm accuracy, and is not directly 'copy and pasted' from any site in any way, shape, or form.

abjuration
Dealing with offensive magic mainly, this spell might prevent one from heat or cold.

alcohol tolerance
This determines one's ableness to resist the side effects of alcohol.

alteration
A spell that may cause illusion. This spell may make something appear to be as it is not.

apply poison
You may poison certain weapons that inflict damage on the receiver and sometimes poison them, thus making them sick... depending on the poison's ability.

archery
One's accuracy with the bow. The art of bowcraft.

baking
To bake; cook in dry heat, especially in an oven.

backstabbing
This skill is for the Rogue. The Rogue's skill in this determines their ability to strike back behind an enemy.

bash
To knock an enemy down, to interrupt spellcasting. Limited to only some races.

beg
To demand something. To perform pesty requests in an attempt to receive usually gold or items.

bind wound
This spell may only be used once a day. It heals.

brass
A skill for the Bard, this allows Bards to perform musical performances via brass.

brewing
To produce alcohol.

block
A Monk skill, this will block certain attempts at hitting you.

blunt, slashing, and piercing weapons
The art of weaponry. Determines ability in the weapon categories of blunt, slashing, and piercing.

channeling
For the magic user. To decrease the amount of time necessary to cast a spell.

common tongue
To understand the common tongue, and to speak it.

conjuration
To summon something from somewhere else in Norrath.

disarm
To temporary remove an enemy's weapon.

disarm traps
To halt a trap's effectiveness.

divination
To detect things invisible and to conceal things visible.

defense
To defend yourself from most attacks.

dodge
To avoid a hit. To dodge.

double attack
To move with haste and strike simultanously.

dragon punch
For the Monk, this skill performs a rather dangerous attack.

dual wield
To weild a weapon in both hands. Limited by class.

eagle strike
For the Monk, this skill performs a rather dangerous attack.

elder elvish
To understand and speak elder elvish.

evocation
For the magic user, this skill deals with offensive magic. Such as fireballs and the such.

feign death
To fake death. Pretend oneself is no longer living in the world of the living. To pretend oneself is longer on the physical plane.

fishing
To seek those who are fish.

fletching
The creation of arrows from correct materials.

flying kick
Limited to the Monk, this skill performs a rather dangerous attack.

foraging
To forage food.

hand to hand
To fight with the hands, to fight weaponless with one's bare fists.

hide
To conceal oneself from the eye.

jewelcraft
To recognize jewels and their value.

kick
To kick. This determines the damage a kick will inflict, and where.

meditate
To memorize spells quicker, to recharge mana.

mend wound
While this risks damaging oneself, this can heal oneself at other times.

offense
One's skill in offensive practices.

old erudian
To understand and to speak old erudian.

one hand blunt
Determines skill with blunt, one handed weapons such as the mace.

one hand slashing
Determines skill with slashing, one handed weapons such as the sword.

parry
To resist an attack dealt by a weapon.

percussion
For the Bard. To drum.

picklocking
For the Rogue, to pick a lock without a key.

pickpocket
To rob another person.

piercing
Determines skill with piercing weapons.

pottery
The ability to create items made of clay and the such.

riposte
This is a special attack. Limited classes.

round kick
This is a rather dangerous attack for the Monk.

safe fall
To prevent an unsafe fall. For the Monk.

sense heading
Although this has a risk of being incorrect, this ability allows oneself to determine their direction (north, south, etc.)

singing
For the Bard... the ability to sing.

smithing
To smith items.

sneak
To sneak upon another without their knowledge.

steal
For the Rogue. To steal from another without their knowledge.

stringed instrument
For the Bard. To play a stringed instrument.

swimming
To swim through water.

tailoring
To make cloth, sometimes armor.

taunt
Force your enemy, via anger, to ignore his surroundings.

throwing
Determines skill in the use of throwing weapons, such as certain axes.

tiger claw
An attack for the Monk.

thieves cant
To understand and speak the language for thieves.

track
To seek footprints... to track another.

two hand blunt
Determines ability to use two handed blunt weapons.

two hand slashing
Determines ability to use two handed slashing weapons.

wind instrument
For the Bard. The ability to play wind instruments.
