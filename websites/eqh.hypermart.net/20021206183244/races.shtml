Races

There are 12 races from which to choose from. Not every race has the same choices for classes. For example, a troll cannot be a wizard, but a High Elf can. A High Elf has its own limitations, , too, but its restrictions and its choices represent its general ability: magic. For the list of what race can be what class, go here.

Race: Description:
Barbarian Native to the northern lands, these people are the less social and more rugged version of the human with their warlike culture.
Dark Elf With dark blue skin and white hair, this evil relative of the High Elf and Woodland Elf worships evil gods and lives under the ground, and thus can see in the dark.
Dwarf Short, strong and agile, dwarves are a smaller version of the human. Never has one come across a dwarf without a beard.
Erudite The Erudite, or the "High Man," is a relative of the humans. This beautiful race is the opposite of the barbarian, usually nearly basing his life on becoming as beautiful as he can. They are very intelligent and usually excellent in magics.
Gnome A gnome is a small humanlike creature, much like the dwarf. Yet, gnomes live underground and rarely come back above. They have brown skin.
Half-Elf Their ears not as pointed as an elf, a Half-Elf is the race inbetween a humand and woodland elf.
Halfling These people are very light and quick and resemble humans to an extent.
High Elf The smarter, somewhat superior Elven race has whiteish skin and most High Elves excel in magic.
Human The human is the most important race, the standard race.
Ogre Stupid, ten feet high, and strong, this race is very agile.
Troll Green, stupid, eight feet tall and strong, this race is also agile.
Woodland Elf With pointy ears, these relatives of the High Elf are much more agile, and equal their kin in intelligence.
