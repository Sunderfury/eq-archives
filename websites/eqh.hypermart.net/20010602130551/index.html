Tuesday, December 22nd, 1998

On 'Buffing' Up Characters in Beta
There has been some worry about GM's buffing up characters for beta testers. Why, and if it will happen in final. Here is what Gunther, a V.I.P. on the EQVault message boards has to say on this issue:

"There is a periodic need for a group of high-level characters to test a specific game function, or dungeon. Testers are chosen and are given 'buffed' characters to use for these tests. They are not allowed to use these characters in normal play, and if caught doing so will be banned. These tests are always done in the company of GM's or Guides. In final, there will be no 'buffing' allowed, and 989 is developing very strict and no-nonsense guidelines for the GM staff that will prevent abuse of powers (who wants to lose thier job and paycheck over buffing a buddy in a game?).

Rest assured, that the other games of this genre have been observed, and the lessons learned by those games are being taken into account by 989 in the development of customer service policies and guidelines for Everquest.

Gunther"

He also mentioned that 989S has coders working with very good 'hackers' to prevent such practices from being done in the final game. May Quellious protect thee,
