The Order of Erudin's Ranking System

Below is how we rank our members. All information is subject to change, and the names probably will. Also, see the individual ranking system we have incorporated for certain members who accomplish an impressive feat.

Guild-ranks

Guildmaster
There are more than one of these. One of the goals of these people is to prevent members from feeling 'inferior.' We want to make it really clear that the 'leaders' in our guild will not go around acting like punks or telling people to do this and that or insulting people's opinions. Everyone in the guild has their say in things, and every word is considered. All members have equal power. Basically, the only thing the leader does is maintain the web page (one of them does) and they usually are the ones thinking real hard for new ideas. :)

Lord of the Arcane
This is the rank after the previous one. This rank is quite harder to earn than the ones below, and may or may not always be the highest rank. It is, however, the most respected rank, generally. So, therefore it is more difficult to earn. Some, but not nearly all, of the things we look for are as follows:

  • High dedication.
  • Help out (see above).
  • Almost always with the guild ingame and IRC.
  • Show to be very good at what you do (ingame).
  • Much more.

    Sorcerer
    This is the rank achieved after Initiate. As mentioned below, this rank is rather easy to attain. We will keep a close watch on these members and promote them. This rank is usually kept for a few months, but I really can't say without knowing the person. The following type of things are what we watch for (keep in mind that this list is not near complete, we also want you to be a nice person, of course, and that is not listed. ;)

  • Achieving higher skill levels.
  • Show dedication.
  • Be around often, at least in IRC. (see above)
  • Help recruit (note: this does not mean mass recruit. Only recruit people you know and trust).
  • Much, much more.

    Initiate
    This is the title that new members have. This is only here so we, the entire guild, may evaluate new members to ensure they are valuable. Most of the time a person will only have this status for around a week or less, and rarely will someone be rejected promotion out of this rank. It's simply to make sure the person isn't a jerk or something.

    Skill-ranks

    Neophyte
    (1-4). Members with ingame levels 1-4 will be known as 'Neophytes' in our skill ranking system.

    Apprentice
    (5-10). Members with ingame levels 5-10 will be known as 'Apprentices' in our skill ranking system.

    Novice
    (11-20). Members with ingame levels 11-20 will be known as 'Novices' in our skill ranking system.

    Expert
    (21-35). Members with ingame levels 21-35 will be known as 'Experts' in our skill ranking system.

    Adept
    (36-40). Members with ingame levels 36-40 will be known as 'Adepts' in our skill ranking system.

    Master
    (41-49). Members with ingame levels 41-49 will be known as 'Master' in our skill ranking system.

    Lord
    This is for our level 50 members.
