Classes

On the table below is a list of the EverQuest classes you may choose from and a description. To find out what race can be what class (their restrictions), click here.

Name Description
Bard The bard's ability is to play his songs, rather than be a skilled warrior. Some of his songs, if played correctly and with skill, may have a magical effect on certain people/things.
Cleric The cleric may heal with ease and power if he is skilled, and he may call upon dieties to do some actions. The cleric is very religious.
Druid The druid lives outside and animals will not attack him, for he is a man of nature.
Enchanter The enchanter may inscribe magical effects on items, people, and other such things.
Magician While a magician is not as skilled at offensive magic, the magician is a master at summoning magics, perhaps to aid him. The magician is still skilled in offensive magic.
Monk Monks may turn themselves into a weapon and defy armor. They are very religious, but not as skilled as a warrior in combat, at least not with an actual weapon.
Necromancer Necromancers may perform magics that involve death, such as summoning a ghoul of sorts to fight for him, even though it is dead.
Paladin The paladin is another holy person. They are skilled warriors, but also hold some cleric abilities.
Ranger Rangers are skilled warriors and live outside, with nature.
Rogue The rogue is commonly a good fighter and is usually swift (as is required in order to be a good rogue), but he excels in stealing, picking locks and snooping around.
Shadow Knight Shadow Knights worship evil gods and, much like necromancers, can cast deathly spells, as well as fight well.
Shaman The shaman is fairly skilled in casting offensive spells, but also holds some cleric powers.
Warrior The warrior is the master of hand-to-hand combat. They excel in fighting with swords, maces and other such things.
Wizard Wizards are the masters of offensive magic, having no match. They are not as skilled in fighting, but very skilled in magic.
