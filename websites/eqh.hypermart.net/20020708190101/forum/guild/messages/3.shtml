Greetings, Our Tall Brothers

Posted by Cattaputta (207.54.160.209) on January 01, 1999 at 14:54:47

Greetings to our tall brothers from the far side of the world!

I am posting here because I see our guilds have much in common. We are the Gnomes of Norrath, an all-Gnome guild made of good and neutral Gnomes of all classes except Necromancers.

As one of the few other truly intelligent races of Norrath, we look forward to sharing the secrets of magic with you. Unfortunately, you are burdened by you height, but we should be able to find many ways to work together nevertheless. We can always use more healers and clerics to travel with, something which with your wisdom I suspect will make you quite good at. Also, you may need rogues and warriors as travel companions, something hard-to-hit Gnomes excel at.

Feel free to visit our page listed below. We currently have about 24 Gnome Iniate Members, and 6 Honorary Members of various races.

Cattaputta Caputta
Gnome Magician
Gnomes of Norrath, Guild Leader
