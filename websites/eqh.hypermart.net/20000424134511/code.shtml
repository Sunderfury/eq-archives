Guild Code

our mission: Our mission is to achieve supremecy in the art of the arcane. To master the arcane arts, to learn all there is to know. We seek knowledge. Power. We will conquer those who oppose us. We are the Order of Erudin.

Our code. What we believe. Who we despise and why. All perspectives are from Erudite Wizard Seginus and Erudite Enchanter Morgaelin's point of view, but the opinion is shared among members.

Basic Code
   We are strong believers of the old Erudite ideals. We respect all of our fellow Erudites except Necromancers. Our Order will welcome any Erudite of any class besides the Necromancer. Why would an Erudite, of all our superiority, choose to be such scum? With this far beyond my comprehension, we choose not to allow such scum in our Order. We believe practitioners of the dark magic should be shunned from our society. In any case, this is not so. We can, however shun them from our Order. *smile*
   Not unlike all true Erudites, we seek knowledge. We seek mastery in all forms of true magic. Our idea of 'true magic' does not include Necromancy, of course, for we believe that in order to be a true magician, you must be skilled. In order to be a true Necromancer, you must be foolish.
   In order to receive this mastery, however, it takes work. We have believe the Erudite race is the greatest form of magicians. As we have before, we will continue to become the most powerful race in the lands of Norrath. Through effort, patience, and discipline, we will achieve that goal.
   Most of us serve Quellious, The Tranquil. We believe Humans and High Elves are not incapable of achieving these same goals, therefore we welcome them in our Order.

Racial Differences:

Barbarians
The barbaric form of Humans. Running around with petty weapons making a feeble attempt to act like a normal person. Utterly disgusting, in my opinion. Nearly naked, too. Sad, sad creatures are the barbarian 'Humans,' if you call them such.

Dark Elves
Unlike the Barbarian, we deeply hate this race. A poor excuse for an elven race, Dark Elves, with their blue skin, are purely evil. Necromancer, most of them. Dabbling in death. They reject High Elven authority, and choose to be outcasts. Who is to say it was not these evil scum that murdered some of my brethren at one point in time? Why should we, or any organization for that matter, allow these murderers to live in the somewhat peaceful environment they currently live in? Think about that a while, and you'll come to our very same conclusion.

Ogres
Tall. Very fat. Very ugly. Stupid and smelly. These words describe the Ogre near perfectly. Unfortunately for them, however, that is not all they commonly are. Evil. They are commonly evil. And like Dark Elves, they are murderers. Dead murderers, if encountered.

Trolls
Very similar to the Ogre, Trolls are evil. You know what evil means by now. Unless, of course, you are an ignorant Barbarian, in which case I demand you leave... for your sake.

Class/Profession Differences:

Necromancer
Evil, murderers. We do not consider this a tolerable form of magic. Necromancy is for the foolish.

Shadow Knight
The opposite of the Paladin, these so called 'Knights' serve evil. Again, murderers. Murderers who will not be tolerated. Let us pray for them. Pray for them that they change their ways, for some day the world shall be called 'Erudin,' and foolish 'Shadow Knights' shall be executed when spotted.

Shaman
While not disposed of on sight, these pitiful excuses for magicians are a disgrace to the forms of magic. Let us be glad these fools are not evil... for their sake.

Racial Similarities:

High Elf
The High Elf is our brother. We allow them in our Order, if they are willing to give up their previous beliefs.

Human
The Human is our brother. We allow them in our Order, if they are willing to give up their previous beliefs.

Gnome
Intelligent, yes. Small, yes. Good magic users. Gnomes are a valuable race in Norrath.

Dwarf
Usually a fellow servant of good, these small little creatures pack a punch. Useful. Great healers. A valuable race in Norrath.

'Neutral' Races and Classes

Woodland Elf - Charismatic, cousins of the respected High Elf. For this reason, we consider the Woodland Elf a somewhat valuable race.

Half-Elf - Cousin to the elf. For that, we don't hate them.
