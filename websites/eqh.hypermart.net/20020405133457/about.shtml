When was this page created and why?
This page was first created in November, 1998 as a general EverQuest site. It was then transformed into the guild web site in mid December, and then transformed into a general site again. This time, however, there is a guild site and a general site. :)

Is the Order of Erudin
Yes. Our original plan was to only allow magic using classes in our guild. But, unfortunately, EQ *does* have some things that just are not compatible with doing that. For instance, if we did that, we'd never be able to survive. A good party is necessary. And besides, some of us wanted to be other classes. :) We will be a roleplaying guild. This does not necessarily mean "thee," "thou," however, for we believe old english is not all that is required to roleplay.

Why did you choose Erudite for OoE?
We chose to be Erudites after much changing of decisions. :) The reason, however, was mainly because we like their background, roleplaying-wise, and thought it would be fun to roleplay that type of person. We also like being practically alone on our continent, heheh. :)

Doesn OoE you allow other races? Why?
That depends. If you are one of our 'respected' races or somesuch and don't mind the long distances apart (no matter where you are - it's across an ocean unless you're Erudite) or are just feeling *really* special, then go apply and specify that on the sign up application. Keep in mind, though, that we are looking for Erudites and probably won't have more than a few other races if any.
