Who, what, is the Order of Erudin?

our mission: Our mission is to achieve supremecy in the art of the arcane. To master the arcane arts, to learn all there is to know. We seek knowledge. Power. We will conquer those who oppose us. We are the Order of Erudin.

Restrictions
We are a race-restricted guild. Restrictions are as follows:

  • We are not going to officially run/launch this guild until Phase 4!
  • You must be Human, High Elf, or Erudite to join the OoE.
  • No necromancers.
  • You mustn't perform evil actions or follow evil dieities.
  • Neutral beliefs are okay. Neutral/good is preferred.
  • You must show maturity in your actions.
  • We are "neutral" on the subject of roleplaying. We believe maturity is a necessity for our members, but feel we cannot roleplay well every minute of our time. Some of our members may roleplay, others will not. All, however, will be respectful.

    High Elves and Humans
    High Elves and Humans have a story to them. They are to "give up" their Elven or Human culture and join the Erudite beliefs. They need not leave their home cities permanently; Qeynos, Freeport, or Felwithe they may remain bound to. They simply must believe what we believe.

    Race Distances
    We realize that each race is on a different continent. This is okay, because we believe that it will be rare for us to recruit a level one member. At the game's release/phase 4, when we all meet up, we will simply have to find each other. We realize this is difficult, but it is only a one time thing. As for death and returning home upon such, it is, again, simply an inconvenience. If our guild is to be a guild of true friends, such inconveniences will not hinder our friendship. Simply because family does not live in your same city does not make them no longer family. :)

    Our Goals
    Our goals are simple, yet so complicated. We seek knowledge, and power. We seek magic and force. Warriors. Magicians. Paladins and Clerics. We seek all that is to know, and we follow all that is in the true culture of the Erudite. True Erudite. Rebels(necromancers) need not apply.
