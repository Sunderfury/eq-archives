Cleansing Ocean
Hall of the Deepwater KnightsのPAL GM Laoni Reistaに話し掛けます。
You say, 'hail Laoni Reista'
Laoni Reista says 'Very good to make your acquaintance ○○○. If you are a [new knight], then let it be known, for I am seeking young knights of Deepwater.
You say, 'I am a new knight'
Laoni Reista says 'You do seem a bit young. We shall test your mettle. Within this Temple you shall learn to swim as fast as the swordfish and attack with the bravery and skill of the shark. Are you ready to [assist with the cleansing of the ocean]?
You say, 'I will assist with the cleansing of the ocean'
Laoni Reista says 'You will go to the waters near the harbor. We have heard of Qeynos' rogue guild attempting to smuggle our valuable Vasty Deep Water from Odus by way of swimmers. We require proof of their involvement. It is said they often carry special coins. Return one of these coins to me.
というわけで、海に行って怪しいrogueを倒します。
NPCは、港を出た辺りの海の底を泳いでいる場合と、 港を出て右のほうに泳いで行ったところの岩場の上に 立っている場合があります。
泳いでいる奴は倒してもcoinを手に入れられませんでした (そもそも、サメに襲われて勝手に死ぬ)が、 岩場の上にいる奴は今のところ100% coinを持っていました。
どちらも倒すとQeynosのRogue Guild、Unseen HandsのFactionが下がります。

岩場の上にはたまに高レベル(level 16から見てRed) のDwarfが立っている時もあります。 こいつはcoinを持っているのかどうか知りません。

というわけで、倒して手に入れた A Nicked Coin をLaoni Reistaに渡します。
Laoni Reista says 'Good work. you have shown these rogues who are the better swimmers. Now we have proof of their involvement. You are a fine addition to the temple. Take this small reward. Go and serve Prexus.
