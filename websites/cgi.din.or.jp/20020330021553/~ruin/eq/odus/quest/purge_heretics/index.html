Purge Heretics
MAG GM Markus Jaevinsに話し掛けます。
You say, 'hail Markus Jaevins'
Markus Jaevins says 'Greetings young one. It is always good to see a new face here in the Palace of Erudin, the perfect city state, free form [crime].
You say, 'what is crime?'
Markus Jaevins says 'Oh yes. We here in Erudin have no crime. Actually it is a very low crime rate. Most of those crimes are comitted by the defective brains of [Heretics] or inferior non-erudite races.
You say, 'What is Heretics?'
Markus Jaevins says 'The Heretics are our sworn enemy. Their origins are rooted within our people. They are the mad Erudites who have chosen the dark side of magic called necromancy. We, the magicians, caught one snooping in the palace just last week. He is now our [prisoner].
You say, 'what is a prisoner?'
Markus Jaevins says 'The infidel was memorizing our spell to take to the rest of the Heretics scum. He will be finally [purged] today. Could you please go to the cells here in the palace and ask Jail Master Lius to give you the heretics remains.
[purged]についていろいろ聞いても何も得られませんでした。 まぁ、とりあえず指示通りにLiusに会いに行きます。
You say, 'hail Warden Lius'
You say, 'What is Heretics?'
Warden Lius says 'Do not speak that name loudly!! The Heretics are the sworn enemy of all good Erudites. Long ago they chose to study the forbidden black arts. Such wicked people must be destroyed. They are a living mockery of all Erudites. They will all die!!
You say, 'Give me the heretics remains'
Warden Lius says 'I am afraid that the heretic prisoner has escaped. There was a skirmish on the palace grounds so I want to assist. When I returned to my post he was gone. I don't know how, he was locked away in the first cell, across from that no-good, thieving gnome, and it is impossible to break the lock with magic or not. We will find him soon. Tell Guildmaster Markus he has escaped.
またMarkusのところへ。
You say, 'prisoner has escaped'
Markus Jaevins says 'Escaped!! This is not good. The Sentinels will not find him in time. You must find him. When you find him release his soul and rid his body of evil any way you can. Bring me proof and all will be well.
ということで、逃げた囚人を倒しましょう。
