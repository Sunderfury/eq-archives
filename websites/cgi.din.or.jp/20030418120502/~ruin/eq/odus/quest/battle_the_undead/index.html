Battle the Undead
Temple of Divine Lightの CLR GM Lumi Stergnon に話し掛けることで始まります。
You say, 'hail Lumi Stergnon'
Lumi Stregnon says 'Peace and tranquility be with you ○○○. Are you a [new acolyte of peace] or are you just here to [pay homage] to the child of tranquility.
ここで、[pay homage]の方を選ぶと
You say, 'I pay homage'
Lumi Stregnon says 'Then respect out Temple and keep your prayers silent.
となって終わってしまいすので気をつけましょう。 (別に無視すればいいんですが)

ということでもう一つのキーワードで続けます。
You say, 'I am a new acolyte of peace'
Lumi Stregnon says 'Then you shall learn the ways of Quellious and learn to do as told by higher ranked members. It is time for you to do the lesser duties of a young priest. Which will it be? [Retrieve the Peacekeeper Staffs] or [Battle the Undead]?
[Retrieve the Peacekeeper Staffs]の方は 別のQuest に繋がっています。
You say, 'I battle the undead'
Lumi Stregnon says 'You shall venture to Toxxulia Forest. There has been an increase of skeletons. I do not know where they come from, but I do know that we shall send you to destroy them. Take this box. Return it to me when you have filled it with the bones of these undead creatures. May Quellious' light guide you.
You must place items in this object in order to create something new.
ということでEmpty Boxをもらいます。 そして指示通りのItemで箱を埋めてcombineすると、 A Box Of Bonesが出来あがります。

あとはこれをLumi Stregnonに返せばOK。
