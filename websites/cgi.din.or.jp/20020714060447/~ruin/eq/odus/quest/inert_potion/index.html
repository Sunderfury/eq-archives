Inert Potion
Erudin PalaceのGuild Tower入り口の池のほとりにいる ENC GM Toresian Fhabelに話し掛けます。
You say, 'hail Toresian Fhabel'
Toresian Fhabel says 'Welcome my friend. We are the Craftkeepers. We study the circle of magic called enchantments. What circle do you [study]?
You say, 'I study enchantments'
Toresian Fhabel says 'Then I welcome you into our circle. It is good to see new blood. I have a favor to ask of you. Do you [wish to assist Toresian] or do you not have the time?
You say, 'I wish to assist Toresian'
Toresian Fhabel says 'Thank you. It is a simple task. Take this potion down to Viles of Vitality and give it to Slansin. He will need it for their stock. Now be on your way ○○○.
というわけでInert Potion を受け取ります。 これを届けましょう。


と、ここで謎が。Slansinという名前の男が二種類います。 片方は普通のMale Venderなのですが、もう片方は右のような格好。

普通のVenderの方にPotionを渡すと、
Slansin says 'Thank you. I have been expecting this. We require it for mixing.
Your faction standing with Craftkeepers got better
Your faction standing with HighCouncilofErudin got worse
Your faction standing with Heretics got better
Your faction standing with HighGuardsofErudin got worse
GoodのFactionが下がってHereticsのFactionが上がってます。 でもENC GuildのFactionは上がってますね・・・

Leather装備の方に渡すと、
Slansin says 'THa!! What a fool! I have stolen another potion to add to the crate. I shall make a fortune!! The real Slansin is in back. Now it is time for me to leave.
Your faction standing with CoalitionofTradeFolkIII got better
Your faction standing with CoalitionofTradeFolk got better
Your faction standing with CarsonMcCabe got better
Your faction standing with CorruptQeynosGuards got better
Your faction standing with TheFreeportMilitia got better
怪しいがぎりぎりEvilじゃない系統のFactionが上がります。 台詞的には悪っぽいんですが・・・？

ちなみにLeather装備のほうはLv20からGreenで、殺しても何も持っていませんでした。 殺した時、上記５つのFactionが下がりました。
