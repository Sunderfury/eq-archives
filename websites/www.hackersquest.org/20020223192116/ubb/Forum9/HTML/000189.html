So, maybe it isn't that über duping bug, but it does make a hefty amount of money.

Run battlesuit missions on Full Money/Bad.

Check the pick up loot (it will say 'They hid [enter item here]) and then regular reward, either may yield a battlesuit.

Battlesuits can sell to the store for a lot of money. A QL 150 one will sell for about 500k. If you are clan, sell them to omni-terminals. (they pay 33% more) You can use any trade skill terminal in an omni-store, and weapon/armor and such.
