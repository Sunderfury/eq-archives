I've tried to do something similar with my expected results. Closest I was able to get to recreating maps for use in other games was by using the zone exporter tool on the main page to extract the .wld files, then used a program called 3D Exploration (awesome program) to convert them to objects (obj files), then imported them into 3D studio max and level editor. All texture data is lost which is to be expected. I made a few maps and by the time i brought them up to scale, it was just too immense for quake which is idealy used for indoor dunegons. Crashed a couple times trying to compile it and some i just quit cause they were gonna take too long. I dont have half life, but i know theres some outdoor action there so maybe their engine would be a little more supportive of large open areas.
