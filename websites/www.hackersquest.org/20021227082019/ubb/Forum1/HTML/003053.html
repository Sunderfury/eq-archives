Decaying Skeleton 		posted 07-03-2001 09:15 PM         
Janarix,

I would certainly agree that the password will be sent as a one-way encode fo itself - ie MD5 etc. any other way would be VERY stupid of them.

But the username will most likely be reversible. In the old days the username was sent as plaintext, so would not be encoded along with the password. Also, it would be very inefficient for them to have to perform a database table scan on a binary field to find out what user was logging on from their encoded hashes. Much easier to reverse the encoded username and use the stored password hash to check the authenticity of the login.
