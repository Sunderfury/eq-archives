I dont know if everyone already knows about this. THis isnt an exploit, but just something that comes in usefull when you die and forget to go to the reclaim booth.

All you have to do is:
*make sure you save first*

1: Put everything in your inventory in the bank
2: buy about 20 bullets or enought to fill your whole inventory, and split them up one by one.
3: When your whole inventory is full, just terminate youself by using /terminate and your reclaim booth will be full.
4: Now dont take any of the bullets out of your reclaim, and everytime you die, your belonging will be right back in your inventory because your reclaim is full.

I dont know if everyone already knows about this, I just thought it might help someone.
