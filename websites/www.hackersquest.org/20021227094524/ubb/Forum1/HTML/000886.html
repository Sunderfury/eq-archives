Decaying Skeleton 		posted 08-06-2000 12:03 PM         
The PoD is factionless and actually too easy to kill, i soloed him in gfay (using a pathing bug) with my 54 ench. Here is how its done (so far only pet classes can do this, im still working on figuring out how non-pet caster can do it)

1. make a pet, buff it up, do your shit.
2. pull the PoD in gfay with a dispell or something.
3. the PoD will stay agro on you, never even thinking of your pet.
4. now, do NOT let him hit you (he hits for quad 356 dmg), this is avoided by strafing up and down the small hill in front of the lift a little away from the guards. He will keep running towards you, but when you strafe to a side, he gets caught in a pathing bug and runs back towards the lift and up the hill. when he gets too close, strafe down again, and he runs back.
5. repeat until he is dead, he will never agro on your pet, but i dont think a charmed mob will be able to touch him, since he is lvl 50 and prolly not meant to ever be killed.

The xp isnt really worth it and the drops are kinda crappy. He drops a robe of discord which has same stats as FBR, and a no drop staff (stats i cant remember, but not too good).

All of you are WRONG. It is VERY simple.
1.Charm a wolf
2.Run in front of PoD lift and wait for your pet to get there.
3.Target the PoD and tell your pet to attack
4.The wolf will run back and forth on the hill. This is where I used to get stuck, but do this. Go next to your pet and say "/pet guard me". It will follow you and walk slowly to Guard Brookrock. When you get near him, Brookrock runs and you should run back to the PoD then. The fight is underway.
