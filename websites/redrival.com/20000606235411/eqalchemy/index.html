ALCHEMY

--Mimsa Starphire--High Priestess of The Tribunal of the Six Hammers Aes Sedai of Black Tower Xegony

Alchemy is the art of making potions, available to shamans at L25. The intelligence stat may improve the rate of success. Potions are made by placing the components in a medicine bag (available at the shaman's guild for about 2p) and clicking the COMBINE button. Potions cannot be used while moving. Lesser (or minor) potions have a shorter duration than their regular or greater equivalents. Potions will not stack with each other; however, each seems to stack with regular buffs, and a few potions interact provided stats do not overlap. Drinking another identical potion will cancel the effects of the previous. Potions have the same limitations as the spell; i.e., shrink can only be used in a dungeon, SoW can only be used outdoors.
