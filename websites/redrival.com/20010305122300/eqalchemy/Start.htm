STARTING OUT GUIDE

Alchemy is a shaman only skill,that can be obtained from lvl 25.I recommend training 10 points to the skill before starting to make potions.You need to buy a Medicine bag and Herbs.To make a potion place the Herbs into the bag and hit combine.You dont need bottles or other items.The easiest potions to make are the lesser and minor potions.For example:Potion of lesser stability
Buy Sage Leaf herb and Lucerne herb from the herb merchant,place them in the medicine bag and hit combine.Some recipes require 3 herbs to make a potion.
