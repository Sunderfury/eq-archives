The Soerbaird Present a Tribute to Innoruuk

Author: Isara duan'Alainn

9/10/2000 12:30pm PST

As their hatred spreads across the land,
With the blessing of the Father's hand,
So shall the Soerbaird sing songs of praise,
To His children's proud and noble ways.
Long live they; dark of skin, of pious heart.
For Innoruuk's kin will ne'er depart,
So long as light spreads across the land,
They will fight, blessed by His hand.

The Soerbaird are pleased to present the second in a series of concerts dedicated to the deities of Norrath. Come with us as we weave songs of darkness and praise to Father Innoruuk and his children.

What: The Soerbaird Tribute to Innoruuk
When: September 13, 2000 at 7PM, PST
Where: Neriak (in the cavern behind the waterfall), Fennin Ro

The Second Annual Soerbaird Comedy Hour!

Author: Tamboran

8/15/2000 9:00pmPST
THE SECOND ANNUAL SOERBAIRD COMEDY HOUR!!

WHAT: A comedy concert by the finest bards in Norrath!
WHEN: Thursday, August 17th at 10PM EST
WHERE: Just inside Ak'Anon's gates, Fennin Ro
WHY: To make you laugh!

Join The Soerbaird and friends for a concert that will make you double over laughing! Tamboran Ratamacue, one of the few Feir'dal to make a Teir'dal laugh (and live to tell about it!) hosts this evening of comedic ballads and storytelling about Norrath.

The First Annual Soerbaird Comedy Hour, held in Surefall Glade back in January, received over 60 attendees, along with many of Norrath's VIP's. Let's hear what they had to say about it!

"Heed the wishes of Tunare and leave the bears of Surefall Glade undisturbed!" -Corun Finisc

Uhmm, yeah, nevermind. Learn about fallen comrades, overbearing lovers and drunken warriors and as we sing and drink well into the night!

So, bring your mug and your laughing muscles to Ak'Anon, as we hope to present to you a night you'll never forget! And we hope it's because of the songs...

NOTE: Due to some confusion about the last show, I want to point out that this is a CONCERT with SONGS...There will not be any room for people to just hop on stage and tell a joke. If you have a song about Norrath, contact The Soerbaird on their forums, and we will work you into the setlist.

ANOTHER NOTE: We will not be responsible for the actions of the Ak'Anon security force during this concert. The location of this concert is just inside the main gate inside Ak'Anon, in that open valley area. Guards and clockworks DO patrol the valley.

-Tamboran Ratamacue

Ollave of the Soerbaird, and all around nice guy. Really. I'm not kidding. Don't look at me in that tone of voice...
