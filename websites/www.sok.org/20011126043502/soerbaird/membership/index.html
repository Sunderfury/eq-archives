About the Soerbaird Academy

All bards who wish to enter the ranks of The Soerbaird must first be accepted into the Soerbaird Academy. Being a student does not earn you the title and privelages of being a Soerbaird--it means that you meet the most basic qualifications of barding that The Soerbaird seek to uphold. A student is tasked with proving his or her worth by completing the graduation requirements set forth by The Soerbaird.

Once all the requirements have been met, the accomplishments of the student are taken before the Soerbaird Council for a vote. If the council deems the bard worthy, then the student earns the right and responsibility of being a Soerbaird.

Academy membership guidelines

The Soerbaird seek to maintain a high quality in its membership. Therefore, consider the following before applying to our academy:

  1. The Soerbaird will be composed only of professed bards. No other professed occupations will be allowed to apply.
  2. All students will be expected to take the Soerbaird Oath of Pantheism. We of The Soerbaird wish to remain neutral and unbiased in the affairs of Norrath, and intend to respect all gods equally in our work. We require all students to take this oath, that your bardic work will not be affected by bias towards any one deity, but will proclaim the truth about each as best you can.
  3. The Soerbaird must pay a tuition of 10 platinum to enter the Academy. Upon graduation from the Academy, a monthly fee will be exacted from each member according to their station. For those of you with questions about the tuition or fee, please mail Jythri.

    The initial fee for joining the guild is only 1 platinum. The remainder of the 10 platinum tuition is due upon your reaching 10th level.

  4. Each applicant to the academy must provide the following:
    1. Name (or names) of your patron, if you have one. The patron is the person who sponsors your position in the Academy by helping you with funding.
    2. A ballad,story, or short history that you have written about Norrath or its people.
    3. A summary of your personal history and lineage.


    To ensure that your application is processed as rapidly as possible, please include this information when you apply.
