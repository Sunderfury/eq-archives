- Powered Clockwork Talisman -  

You must first complete the first part of this quest for the Small Clockwork Talisman. Tabben Bromal will then be ready to offer further information.

You say, "I am ready"
Tabben Bromal says, "Very well. You'll need to gather up a dense hammered casing for the outer shell; a crystalline carapace will act to protect the face of the device; and it requires a fiery power source to propel the rotor mechanism. The device is then attached around the neck, much like the one you are wearing. The only difference will be the strands of living chain that will keep it from slipping from around your neck. Place those four items and your talisman in that box and return it to me. We should be able to get this thing built for you without much fuss at all, should all things go well on your end of the task, of course."

Gather the following items:

1) Crystalline Carapace -- Drops from A Crystalline Arachnae in the Plane of Valor

2) Strands of living Chain -- Drops from A Parylyx Hatchling in the Plane of Torment

3) Dense hammered Casing -- Drops from Daiku in the Plane of Tactics

4) a fiery Powersource -- Drops from Sentries and Protectors in the Tower of Solusek Ro

Combine the 4 items along with the Small Clockwork Talisman in the container and hand it to Tabben.

Note: Gate effect requires a Quintessence of Knowledge as a reagent. These are crafted by alchemists.
