March 6, 2003  --  New Spell Quest Found

Posted quest information for the new level 44 cleric spell Temperance. I have a few additional notes to mention however. Firstly, the NPC will -only- respond to clerics, which is good since the spell is -no drop- :) This is a buff spell, offering an additional 800 hp and 48 ac to your target. The mana cost is 550 per cast, and lasts 1 hour and 40 minutes. These specifics will be added into the spell listing soon.
