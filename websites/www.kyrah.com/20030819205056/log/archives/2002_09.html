September 9, 2002  --  More Cleric Hammers

September 6, 2002  --  Epic Quest Changes

The last patch introduced a new system for getting Ragefire to spawn. You still need to head over to Skyfire Mountains, but instead of sitting and waiting at the dragon statue, you'll be doing a bit of work. First find Warder Cecilia, hailing her will give you the "Box of Binding". Now start killing everything you can until you have obtained 4 pearlescent fragments. Combine these fragments in the box and hand it to Warder Cecilia. You will receive a Seal, and the human form of Ragefire will spawn. Complete according to the original instructions (found in the quest section of this website).
