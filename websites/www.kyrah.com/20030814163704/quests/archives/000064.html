- Shining Star of Light -  

Go to the Temple of Life in Qeynos and speak with Astaed Wemore. He will tell you that he is in need of a cure for Lempeck Hargrin, who is very sick. To make the potion, he needs some Honey Jum, which is made by Kizzie Mintopp in Misty Thicket if you bring her 3 honeycombs (dropped off bixies) and 30 gp. Bring the Honey Jum back to the Temple of Life, and give it to Astaed. In return you'll receive a potion. Bring this to Lempeck. Apparently there are two possible potions that you can receive, one is the correct version and the other is wrong. If you give him the wrong one, he will attack you and you'll need to go back to Misty Thicket and try again. If you give the correct potion, you'll be given a rusty scythe (Lore Item, any rusty will not do.) Go back to Aeston and give him the scythe, and you will be rewarded with the Shining Star of Light. Faction increases with the Priests of Life, Knights of Thunder, Guards of Qeynos and Antonius Bayle. Faction decreases with the Blood Sabres.
