TEMPLAR'S BOOTS
You must have ally faction in Kael to complete this quest. To be more specific, the quest NPC is on Kromzek faction. You must pass Tormax faction guards, and possibly Kromrif. You only NEED ally to Kromzek to receive the item, but would probably find it difficult getting to the quest room without at least dubious Tormax faction.

Vylleam Vyaeltor can be found in the quest room behind King Tormax's throne. He requests 3 crushed flame emeralds and ancient tarnished plate boots.

The crushed flame emeralds drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The ancient tarnished plate boots is a rare drop in the Temple of Veeshan, dropped anywhere EXCEPT the east wing (HoT).

Give the 3 crushed flame emeralds and the ancient tarnished plate boots to Vylleam and you will receive Templar's Boots in return.
