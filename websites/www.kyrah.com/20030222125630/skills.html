- CLERIC SKILL LISTING -

All clerics have various skills, as shown in the charts below. These can be trained by the guild master in any cleric guild, though it is wise to only spend points where they are needed since it may become costly and most skills naturally raise through gameplay. Visit the library section if you are seeking information about alternative advancement skills, which are exclusive for 51+ players.
