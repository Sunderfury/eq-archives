- BOOTS OF THE RELIANT -  

Blaize the Radiant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Reliant," she will tell you that Turgan the Reliant once lived in Kithicor Forest until there was a fire, at which point he may have moved to Lake Rathetear.

Go to Kithicor Forest (it is advised to do so during daylight) and head to -90, 3300 where you will find a tree stump. You can jump to it by climbing up the fallen tree beside it. On top of the stump spawns the Ignot of the Reliant. It appears as a small bag.

Now look for a burned down cabin also in Kithicor. The exact loc is 1700, 1100. In the left corner of the back of the cabin spawns a diary, which appears on the ground as a small bag.

Head over to Lake Rathe. If entering from South Karana, turn left past the gnolls. You are looking for Turgan, who now lives in a cabin at 3500, 150. Give him the diary in exchange for the Icon of the Reliant.

Purchase 1 bar of platinum and have a player enchant it. Give the enchanted platinum bar, the Icon, and the Ignot to Blaize in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Boots of the Reliant.

Note: Be aware that the spawn times in Kithicor may be several hours. Just keep coming back and looking if you do not succeed the first time!
