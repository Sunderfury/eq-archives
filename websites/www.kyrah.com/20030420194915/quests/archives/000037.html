- AKKIRUS' CHESTPLATE OF THE RISEN -  

You must have ally faction to Claws of Veeshan to complete this quest.

In Skyshrine there is a wyvern named Fardonad Fe`Dhar (high up in the city, main quest room) who promises to make you a new breastplate if you bring him 3 pieces of black marble and unadorned breastplate.

The pieces of black marble drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious, or can be purchased from other players. The unadorned breastplate is a very rare drop in Temple of Veeshan, specifically the east wing which is known as the Halls of Testing.

Give the 3 pieces of black marble (unstacked) and the unadorned breastplate to Fardonad, and you will be rewarded with Akkirus' Chestplate of the Risen.
