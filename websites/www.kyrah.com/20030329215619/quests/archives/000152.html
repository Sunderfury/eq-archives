- ELEMENTAL PLATE ARMOR -
Find Bor Wharhammer in the Plane of Tranquility.

You say, "Hail, Bor Wharhammer"
Bor Wharhammer says, "Greetin's t'ye Kyrah! Isn't the area 'round 'ere so nice an' quiet? Such a departure from me old days; slaving o'er the forge t'create masterpieces! T'be 'onest, at times I do miss the old forge, but after I created me finest breastplate, it seemed as if nothing else I made could ever live up t'it. So I left me home to'wander the land an' see what I could learn o'the world. I 'ave learned quite a bit from the elders 'ere an' the skilled craftspeople in New Tanaan. I 'ave even devised a type o'emblem that will impart the magic o'tranquility into the user t'create planar armors from pieces o'energy found in the planes."

You say, "what planar armor?"
Bor Wharhammer says, "From me understanding, the very fabric o'the planes is so strong out 'ere, it can be found in raw form the deeper ye traverse. Anyone who ventures far into the planes finds that they must wear excellent armor t'protect them from the savage environment. The very essence of planar energy can be focused into a form that can fit our shape. As ye progress deeper into the planes, ye will find many molds an' patterns with which t'base a set o'armor. If ye want t'know 'ow t'craft the various types o'armor, I can instruct ye about plate, chain, leather an' silk."

You say, "what plate?"
Bor Wharhammer says, "Ahhhhh Kyrah! The fine rigid armor that can stop a shaft from piercing yer heart! Too bad it be so cumbersome an' difficult t'move about in. To construct a piece o'plate armor, ye need t'combine a plate mold, an emblem, various amounts o'sheet metal an' use one o'those crafty Tanaan smithin' 'ammers all within a furnace touched by Ro. Ethereal metal sheet construction is another matter. Ye will need t'combine two bricks o'ethereal energy, an ethereal temper an' a Tanaan smithin' 'ammer within a Tanaan forge. I would seek a skilled craftsperson t'make the metal sheets fer ye; the emblem will enable ye t'craft the final armor piece no matter what yer skill be."

You say, "what emblem?"
Bor Wharhammer says, "Well Kyrah, the emblems dinnae be easy t'craft but I will gladly give ye one fer the price of 500 platinum pieces. They allow a planes traveler with no craftin' skills t'create many fine pieces o'planar armor in a special, magical kit I also 'ave an' will throw in with the price. The kit acts as a focal point fer the wild magic energy o'the Planes. Ye will only be able t'use each emblem an' kit once when ye create the piece, 'owever I dinnae be goin' anywhere soon! Just venture back when ye need another an' dinnae ferget the coin!"

You say, "what ethereal temper?"
Bor Wharhammer says, "Temper be the juice o'smithin'. T'make an ethereal temper, ye will need t'combine two celestial essences along with soda an' an emerald tealeaf. A skilled brew master can distill the power o'the raw ingredients fer ye."

Here's what you need to gather:

1) The most difficult part of this quest is obtaining the Elemental Plate Molds. They drop from various named MoBs in: Vegarlson, the Earthen Badlands, Reef of Coirnav, Eryslai the Kingdom of Wind, Doomfire The Burning Lands. They are no drop.

2) In a brew barrel combine 2 Celestial Essence (no fail combine of 1 Celestial Solvent + 1 Research Component with a mortar and pestle) + 1 Soda Water (merchant item) + 1 Emerald Tea Leaf (PoK merchant item) = Ethereal Temper (brewing skill 220). The temper is droppable and may be combined by another player.

3) In a forge combine 2 bricks of Ethereal Energy (drops in Plane of Valor and Halls of Honor) + 1 Ethereal Temper (from the step above) + 1 Tanaan Smithing Hammer (sold in Plane of Knowledge) = 1 Ethereal Sheet of Metal (smithing skill 220). You will need 1-3 sheets depending on the armor piece you are creating. Sheets are droppable and may be combined by another player.

4) Give Bor Wharhammer 500pp and he will give you a Mystical Forge of Ro with a Cleric Emblem inside.

Now you have all your components! No smithing skill is required for the final step. Combine in the Mystical Forge of Ro the following: Cleric Emblem, Elemental Plate (Slot Name) Mold, Tanaan Smithing Hammer, and Ethereal Sheets of Metal (quantities below).

1 Sheet: Bracer
2 Sheets: Gloves, Helm, Sleeves, Boots
3 Sheets: Breastplate, Leggings
