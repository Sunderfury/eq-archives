- GUANTLETS OF DRAGON SLAYING -  

Go to King Tormax in Kael Drakkel.

You say, "Hail, King Tormax"
King Tormax says, "Greetings, High Elf. You know who I am, but I only vaguely know of you. My purpose is simple, I will rule these lands like my father, and my grandfather before him. All tasks but two are inconsequential to me."

You say, "what tasks?"
King Tormax says, "The tasks are simple, High Elf. I wish the death of the other 'powers' of this frozen waste land. If you are able to destroy either of my mortal foes, bring me proof of your exploits and you will be known as the hero of Kael Drakkel."

You say, "what powers?"
King Tormax laughs deeply, "I speak of the foolish old dragon Yelinak and that pitiful Dain Frostreaver."

With a raid force, kill Lord Yelinak in Skyshrine and bring his head back to King Tormax to receive your reward.
