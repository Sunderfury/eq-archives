TEMPLAR'S CROWN
You must have ally faction in Kael to complete this quest. To be more specific, the quest NPC is on Kromzek faction. You must pass Tormax faction guards, and possibly Kromrif. You only NEED ally to Kromzek to receive the item, but would probably find it difficult getting to the quest room without at least dubious Tormax faction.

Vylleam Vyaeltor can be found in the quest room behind King Tormax's throne. He requests 3 crushed onyx sapphires and ancient tarnished plate helmet.

The crushed onyx sapphires drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The ancient tarnished plate helmet is a are drop in the Temple of Veeshan, dropped anywhere EXCEPT the east wing (HoT).

Give the 3 crushed onyx sapphires and the ancient tarnished plate helmet to Vylleam and you will receive Templar's Crown in return.
