- TEMPLAR'S GUANTLETS -  

You must have ally faction in Kael to complete this quest. To be more specific, the quest NPC is on Kromzek faction. You must pass Tormax faction guards, and possibly Kromrif. You only NEED ally to Kromzek to receive the item, but would probably find it difficult getting to the quest room without at least dubious Tormax faction.

Vylleam Vyaeltor can be found in the quest room behind King Tormax's throne. He requests 3 crushed lava rubies and ancient tarnished guantlets.

The crushed lava rubies drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The ancient tarnished guantlets are a are drop in the Temple of Veeshan, dropped anywhere EXCEPT the east wing (HoT).

Give the 3 crushed lava rubies and the ancient tarnished guantlets to Vylleam and you will receive Templar's Chestplate in return.
