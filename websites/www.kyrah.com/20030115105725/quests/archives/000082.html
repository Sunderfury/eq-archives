KEY TO OLD SEBILIS
Bring a group, including a tracker, to Trakanon's Teeth. You are searching for the Froglok Hunter, who drops the Medallion of Nathsar, and the Froglok Forager, who drops the Medallion of Nathsar. They spawn instantly after one another, so getting the pieces for several players at once does not take much time.

Among some ruins, which are guarded by undead iksar, is a building where Emperor Ganak can be found. This is at approximately +3000, -3000. He will con indifferent. Hand him the two medallions to receive the Trakanon Idol, which is the key to Sebilis.
REWARD ITEM INFORMATION 
Trakanon Idol
NO DROP
WT: 1.0  Size: SMALL
Class: ALL
Race: ALL
