- Pure Blood Scroll -  

Find Grennik Neltrin in the Gulf of Gunthak.

You say, "Hail, Grennik Neltrin"
Grennik Neltrin says, "Hello, Kyrah. Have you been exploring the island? I came here to discover the secrets of the Broken Skull trolls but I have yet to even explore the island. Rumors that their high priest Nadox held the power to cure any disease have been drifting around Norrath for some time. I've come to see if the rumors are true. Unfortunately the island is much more dangerous than I had imagined and I haven't found anything useful. If you find anything having to do with the troll High priest, please bring it to me."

1) Combine in a brew barrel: "A Blackened Vial" (dropped from Broken Skull Initiates/Adherents in the Crypt of Nadox) + "Broken Skull Clan Blood" (dropped from Furious/Vehement Blackhands in the Crypt of Nadox) + Gunthak Gulf Water (dropped from An Erudite/Ogre Marauder/Pillager in Gulf of Gunthak) = "A Black Potion"

2) Combine in a brew barrel: "A Black Potion" (from prior combine) + "Pure Blood" (dropped from Luggold Initiate/Abominations in Crypt of Nadox) = "A Glowing Black Potion"

Bring "A Glowing Black Potion" back to Grennik Neltrin and you will receive the Pure Blood spell.
