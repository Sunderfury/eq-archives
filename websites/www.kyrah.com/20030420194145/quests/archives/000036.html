- AKKIRUS' BRACELET OF THE RISEN -  

You must have ally faction to Claws of Veeshan to complete this quest.

In Skyshrine there is a wyvern named Fardonad Fe`Dhar (high up in the city, main quest room) who promises to make you a new bracer if you bring him 3 crushed opals and an unadorned plate bracer.

The crushed opals drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious, or can be purchased from other players. The unadorned plate bracer are a rare drop in Temple of Veeshan, specifically the east wing which is known as the Halls of Testing.

Give the 3 crushed opals (unstacked) and the unadorned plate bracer to Fardonad, and you will be rewarded with Akkirus' Bracelet of the Risen.
