GREAVES OF FORBIDDEN RITES
Go to Thurgadin. You must have at least kindly faction to receive the quest.

Loremaster Dorinan can be found in the cleric guild/library. He asks you to bring him 3 chipped onyx sapphires and corroded plate greaves so that he may fashion a new pair of pants for you.

The 3 chipped onyx sapphires are dropped of various 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The corroded plate greaves is a rare drop from giants in Kael.

Hand the 3 chipped onyx sapphires (unstacked) and corroded plate greaves to Dorinan and you will receive the Greaves of Forbidden Rites.
