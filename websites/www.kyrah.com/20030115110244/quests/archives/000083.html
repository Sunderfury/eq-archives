KEY TO HOWLING STONES
A froglok found within the Swamp of No Hope's village named Durgaz offers to give you a key to his master's lair if you bring him two items:

1) Jade Chodakai -- This is a spear dropped by Chancellor of Di'Zok in the Lake of Ill Omen sarnak fort

2) Xalgozian Fang -- Dropped by a vampire named Xalgoz within Kaesora

Upon turning these in you'll receive the Key to Charisis, which is Howling Stones.
REWARD ITEM INFORMATION

NO DROP
