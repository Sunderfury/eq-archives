- EMOTE COMMANDS -

Players can express how they are feeling through emotes. Simply type "/em" followed by what you'd like your character to do. For example "/em has never been here before" would show with your player name in place of "/em". Below are some commands that you may wish to use in your emotes.
  • %m –- Returns the character’s pet's name (when they have a pet)
  • %t -- Returns the current target.
  • %s -- Returns the subjective gender-specific pronoun for the target (He, She, It).
  • %o -- Returns the objective gender-specific pronoun for the target (Him, Her, It).
  • %p -- Returns the possessive gender-specific pronoun for the target (His, Her, Its).
  • %r -- Returns the race of the target (only works on Player characters).
The emotes below are formatted into the game already for you. You simply type "/" before the emote name.

Some of the premade emotes have visible actions that go with them. You can play around with these by typing something along the lines of "/dance say does a victory dance!" Whatever you typed after "say" will show after your name as your character performs the action.
