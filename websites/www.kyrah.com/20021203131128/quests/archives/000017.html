GREAVES OF THE PENTIENT
Blaize the Radiant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Pentient," she will tell you that Astrid the Penitent spent a lot of time in Upper Guk and hid the ignot underwater there, even though she was not able to swim. She also spent a lot of time on Kerra Island, where she lost her life to the Erudite heretic named Maugarim.

Go to Everfrost Peaks and find the frozen lake in the center of the plains, make sure you have an enduring breath potion or item with you. There are several holes in the ice, find one and swim to 2996, -5771 where you'll find a small bag. Pick this up, it is the Ignot of the Ardent.

Next go to Kerra Ridge (located next to Toxxulia forest). Upon zoning into Kerra, cross the bridge and at the fork turn right. You will come to the small Kerran castle. Go inside and find the room behind the throne. Maugarim spawns in the small room on the left. She is around level 27-31. Kill her and loot the Icon of Penitent.

Purchase 1 bar of platinum and have a player enchant it. Give the enchanted bar to Ostorm (NPC in Sol Ro Temple) and he will return a magnetized bar to you. Give the magnetized bar, the Icon, and the Ignot to Blaize in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Greaves of the Pentient.
