- JOURNEYMAN'S BOOTS -  

Find Hasten Bootstrutter in Rathe Mountains. He has three possible placeholders: A Drake, Maldyn, Brother Zephyl.

You say, "Hail, Hasten Bootstrutter"
Hasten Bootstrutter says, "Stopped I have done. Greet you I will. What business do we have? Hmm? Speak up!!"

You say, "what Journeyman's Boots?"
Hasten Bootstrutter says, "Journeyman's Boots are what you seek? Ha!! I have those upon my feet and disinigrate will they if my life is taken. Extra pair have I and [rare trade] can we make."

You say, "what rare trade?"
Hasten Bootstrutter says, "I call for three things and Journeyman Boots are yours. I call for a [Shadowed Rapier], call for the [Ring of the Ancients] and call for Gold.. 3250 Gold Coins!! This the trade that I call."

You say, "what shadowed rapier?"
Hasten Bootstrutter says, "Shadowed Men, foes to all, have appeared. Many lands do they walk. Invisible are they, but items they wield can be seen. Seek them out and return to me a Shadowed Rapier. Return it with haste before 'poof' goes the rapier!! No time to camp have you."

You say, "what ring of the ancients?"
Hasten Bootstrutter says, "Ancient Cyclops do march the land. Long have they lived and crafted the Ring of the Ancients have they. I must have one and you shall go and fetch one for me. Seek the lands of the oldest Cyclops clans. Seek the plains, seek the island in tears and seek the dunes for there is one who is last. His clan was blown from the sands."

Bring back the following items for the trade with Hasten:

1) Ring of the Ancients -- Drops from "An Ancient Cyclops" who has two rare spawn points. The lower level version of this MoB spawns in the Southern Desert of Ro. You will need to kill off any wandering MoBs to cycle the rare spawns. This is a difficult camp not only because it takes a lot of time and work, but because there are often others vying for the chance to claim the cyclops once he spawns. The second location is in the Ocean of Tears upon an island between sister island and seafury island. Placeholders spawn every 7 minutes.

2) Shadowed Rapier -- Drops from "Shadowed Men" which have many camps throughout Norrath: West Commons, South Karana, Nektulos Forest, Lavastorm Mountains, Lesser Fay, Innothule Swamp, Kithicor Forest. The rapier is a random drop considered fairly common. It is -no rent- however, so this step should be completed on the same day you intend to turn in all quest items to Hasten.

3) Gold Coins -- Not a drop, but a request by Hasten to bring him 3250 coins. Make sure the amount is in gold pieces, he will not accept platinum.

You will be rewarded with a special pair of boots that allows swifter running speed (about the same as the run 3 ability). The boots can be clicked from a general inventory slot or worn (though, they are normally not worn).
