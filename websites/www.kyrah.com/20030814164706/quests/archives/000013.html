- Chestplate of the Constant -  

Gavel the Temperant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Constant," he will tell you that Althuryn the Constant was slain by a pair of goblins, one has fled to Runnyeye and the other to Dagnor's Cauldron.

In an underwater tunnel within Runnyeye, you will find the named goblin, Sludge. Kill him for the Ignot of the Constant.

Head over to Dagnor's Cauldron and bring a tracker with you, this may take some time. You are seeking a goblin named Bilge, who can usually be found in the area between Butcherblock zone and Unrest zone. Loot the Icon of the Constant from him.

Purchase 2 bars of platinum and have a player enchant it. Give the enchanted bars to Genni (NPC in Sol Ro Temple) and she will return galvanized bars to you. Give the galvanized bars (unstacked), the Icon, and the Ignot to Gavel in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Chestplate of the Constant.
