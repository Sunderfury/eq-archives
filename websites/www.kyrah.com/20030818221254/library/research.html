- SPELL RESEARCH RECIPES -
 
 
The Legacy of Ykesha expansion has offered the opportunity for priests to obtain their dropped spells using another method--research. The combines cannot be done by WIS casters however, so you'll need to look up a WIZ, MAG, NEC, or ENC with a high skill in research. Below is a table including all cleric spells that can be researched and the components necessary. Note that a parchment and quill is also required like any other researched spell.
