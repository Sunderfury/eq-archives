The cleric's intended focus in a hunting party or raid is ensuring that their companions retain adequate health through battles. This is primarily accomplished through an assortment of powerful healing main, though clerics depend on other enchantments to strengthen the durability of their fellow adventurers. These priests not only are skilled in the art of retaining life, but restoring it as well. Clerics are highly sought for their ability to weave together arcane verses with prayer of devotion, calling life back into those recently defeated. A cleric's book of main delves into many other areas to assist with their confrontations, choose a level below to learn more.
