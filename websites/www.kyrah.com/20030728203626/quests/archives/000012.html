- BRACER OF THE REVERENT -  

Gavel the Temperant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Reverent," he will tell you that Regis the Reverent was in love with a gypsy girl who you must find to begin the quest.

Your first stop is the gypsy camp in Rathe Mountains, not far from the Lake Rathe zone. The loc is 4000, 1500. Here you will find a gypsy named Lianna Ferasa. Ask her "What has become of Regis?" and she will tell you that he was slain by Zahal the Vile. If you kill Zahal, Regis the Reverent will rise from the dead immediately after with Lianna's flute. She wants you to kill the zombie of Regis and bring the flute back to her for the Icon of the Reverent.

Zahal is a ghoul who spawns in North Karana about every 4 hours, near the guard tower. The loc is -1800, 200 but once he spawns he wanders around. Zahal is around level 27-30. Often, Zahal is killed by NPCs. Regardless of who kills him, Regis will spawn afterward. Some choose to simply wait at the spawn point for Regis and ignore Zahal altogether assuming that he will be killed off anyway. I cannot confirm that this is the best method. It's up to you.

Once Zahal is dead, Regis will spawn in front of a tree around 1500, -1550. Binding here is a good idea so you can gate there after killing Zahal. Regis is around level 28 and drops the Ignot of the Reverent and the flute.

Go back to Lianna in Rathe Mountains and give her the flute for the Icon.

Purchase 2 bars of platinum and have a player enchant them. Give the 2 bars (unstacked), the Icon, and the Ignot to Gavel in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Bracer of the Reverent.

Note: This is the only quest armor item that is NOT lore (though the icon and ignot ARE lore). Therefore you can do the quest twice to get two bracers.
