AEGIS OF LIFE
In the Qeynos Temple of Life, Find Camlend Serbold. He'll ask you to bring him the Pestilence Scythe and heart dropped by Lord Grimrot in South Karana. The confusing part about this however, is that there are actually two versions of Lord Grimrot. One is a human, who is strong and a rare spawn. He drops the scythe. The other is a skeleton, who is weak and common. He drops the heart. With warmly faction, return the two items to Camlend to receive the Aegis of Life, which can be worn in either the secondary slot or the back slot.
