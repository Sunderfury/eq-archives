- Akkirus' Guantlets of the Risen -  

You must have ally faction to Claws of Veeshan to complete this quest.

In Skyshrine there is a wyvern named Fardonad Fe`Dhar (high up in the city, main quest room) who promises to make you a new set of gloves if you bring him 3 crushed lava rubies and unadorned guantlets.

The crushed lava rubies drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious, or can be purchased from other players. The unadorned guantlets are a rare drop in Temple of Veeshan, specifically the east wing which is known as the Halls of Testing.

Give the 3 crushed lava rubies (unstacked) and the unadorned breastplate to Fardonad, and you will be rewarded with Akkirus' Guantlets of the Risen.
