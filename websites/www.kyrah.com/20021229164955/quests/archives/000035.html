AKKIRUS' BOOTS OF THE RISEN
You must have ally faction to Claws of Veeshan to complete this quest.

In Skyshrine there is a wyvern named Fardonad Fe`Dhar (high up in the city, main quest room) who promises to make you some new boots if you bring him 3 crushed flame emeralds and unadorned plate boots.

The crushed flame emeralds drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious, or can be purchased from other players. The unadorned plate boots are a rare drop in Temple of Veeshan, specifically the east wing which is known as the Halls of Testing.

Give the 3 crushed flame emeralds (unstacked) and the unadorned plate boots to Fardonad, and you will be rewarded with Akkirus' Boots of the Risen.
