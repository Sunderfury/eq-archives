July 17, 2003  --  WARNING! Pre-Order Bug!

Upon logging on Everquest today, players will be offered the option to pre-order the latest expansion, Lost Dungeons Of Norrath. However, apparently the process is bugged and reports claim loss of AAXP, flags, levels, expansions, and more. Though it seems GMs are working to process petitions and fix characters, as you can imagine, it's not a good idea to take the risk! I'd strongly advise waiting till this is all sorted out before attempting to pre-order LDoN.
