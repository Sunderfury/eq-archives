- ENCHANTED RING OF TORDEN -  

In the Bastion of Thunder, you'll need to hunt in each of the four wings to collect the following spheres:

1) Sandstorm Sphere -- South Wing

2) Lightning Sphere -- West Wing

3) Tornado Sphere -- North Wing

4) Blizzard Sphere -- East Wing

They drop off random giants. Once you have the four spheres, you'll need to loot a "Ring of Torden" which drops off named giants. Use this container to combine the spheres, which will return "Enchanted Ring of Torden" to you. This is the key to the BoT towers.
