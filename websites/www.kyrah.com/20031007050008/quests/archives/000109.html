- Ring of the Shissar -  

A key is required to enter the chamber of Emperor Ssraeshza in the Temple of Ssraeshza. It is also an all/all ring. Follow the steps below to complete the quest.

1) Camp the basement, killing named taskmasters until you get a pouch (yes, this requires a general inventory slot).

2) Camp the 1st floor until Commander Zazuzh spawns. Loot the idol.

3) Camp the 2nd floor until Commander Zeruzsh spawns. Loot the ring.

4) Camp the Imperial Guard chamber, killing guards until one of the following spawns: Advisor Zekuzh, Arbiter Korazhk, General Kizuhx. Kill and loot the insignia.

5) Combine the idol, ring, and insignia in the pouch to create "Ring of the Shissar" which serves as a key to the Emperor's room.
