GRAND HAMMER OF THE HOLY RITE
Find Legionnaire Kayn in the Twilight Sea.

You say, "Hail, Legionnaire Kayn"
Legionnaire Kayn says, "You must have been speaking to Derron, a mightier cleric ye couldnt find. Except for Donal the Wise of course. So tell me are ye also a cleric like Derron?"

You say, "I am a cleric"
Legionnaire Kayn says, "Ah then you must want the mask, cloak, gauntlets, talisman, girdle, and hammer that I be holdin'."

You say, "What about the hammer?"
Legionnaire Kayn says, "To get the hammer you must bring me a moon jewel, a mark of salvation, a light etched emerald, and the writ of distance."

The pieces drop from various mobs in Luclin. Once you have collected them all, return to Kayn for your reward.
