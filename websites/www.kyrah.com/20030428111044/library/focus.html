- FOCUS ITEM INFORMATION -
 
 
Focus Levels:
  • Focus I -- Works on all spells up to level 20
  • Focus II -- Works on all spells up to level 44
  • Focus III -- Works on all spells up to level 60

Focus Effects:
  • Improved Healing + 20% Healing
  • Extended Range + 15% Spell Range
  • Extended Enhancement + 15% Spell Duration
  • Reagent Conservation - 15% Chance of Reagent Use
  • Improved Damage + 20% Spell Damage
  • Enhancement Haste + 30% Spell Haste
  • Mana Preservation - 10% Spell Mana Cost
  • Spell Haste + 15% Spell Haste
  • Affliction Haste + 33% Spell Haste
  • Extended Affliction + 20% Spell Duration
  • Affliction Efficiency - 25% Mana Cost
  • Summoning Haste + 30% Spell Haste
  • Reanimation Haste + 30% Spell Haste
  • Summoning Efficiency - 25% Spell Mana Cost
  • Reanimation Efficiency - 25% Spell Mana Cost

Spell Haste Restictions:
  • Does not affect Complete Heals
  • Does not affect spells with less than a 3 second cast time
  • Does not affect spells that last less than 72 seconds in duration
DD Damage Restictions:
  • Personal Area of Effect Spells are not effected.
  • Target Area of Effect Spells are not effected
  • Damage over Time Spells are not effected
  • Dragonsbane and Giantsbane are not effected
Affliction Restictions:
  • More ticks of damage done for standard amount of DoT damage per tick
  • Does not effect spells which last less than 36 seconds
  • Does not effect non-damaging duration debuffs such as Snare, Harmony, Root, Malo, etc
Duration Restictions:
  • Does not effect Invulnerability spells
  • Does not effect Complete Heal spells
Summoning and Reanimation Restictions:
  • Only works on summoned Pets
  • Does not work for charming Pets
