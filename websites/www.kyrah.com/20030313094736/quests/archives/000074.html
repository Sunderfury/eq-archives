GAROU BONE CLUB
Look for Belix Misteyes in the Iceclad Ocean near the Tower of Frozen Shadow. He will tell you about the many dangerous creatures in the area and if asked, he will tell you the most dangerous is Garou. He will reward you if you bring him the skin of Garou along with a cougar bone.

Kill Garou for the skin and a named black cougar named Midnight for the cougar bone. They are both rare spawns most often seen near the Tower of Frozen Shadow.

Hand the two items to Belix to receive the Garou Bone Club.
