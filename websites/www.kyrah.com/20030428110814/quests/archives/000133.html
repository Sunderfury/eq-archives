- BANGLE OF DISEASE WARDING -  

Grimror Plaguebringer is an ogre who wanders around the Plane of Disease.

You say, "Hail, Grimror Plaguebringer"
Grimror Plaguebringer says, "Grimror no have time ta talk. Gots stuffs ta do."

You say, "what stuffs?"
Grimror Plaguebringer says, "I wuz sent here by Zulort to, err. . . To gadder some alchemy type stuffs. Him have big plan, and need me to travel lots."

You say, "what big plan?"
Grimror Plaguebringer laughs. "Me not tell you dat! Grimror keep secrets secret. You gonna help Grimror or no?"

You say, "I will help Grimror"
Grimror Plaguebringer says, "Dat good! Grimror been getting compon ents for dayz now and Grimror still not finushed. You gonna get dem tings fur me?"

You say, "what about dem tings?"
Grimror Plaguebringer says, "Grimror need bile, dat stuff es strong here! But Grimror keent seem to git eet all. Bile comz from da leetle bugs. Keel little bugs, and geeve Grimror dere leetel bodiez and Grimror give yuuz anyting dat yuuz want. Dere be four leetel buggiez dat Grimror still neez, seegeen, scaveen, genereen, and harroon. Yuuz bring Grimror dere skinz and Grimror geeve you anyting yuuz want."

Translation: Kill A Harrion Larva, A Generian Larva, A Sengian Larva, and A Scaevian Larva. Return their biles to Grimror to receive Bangle of Disease Warding, which serves as a key into the Crypt of Decay.
