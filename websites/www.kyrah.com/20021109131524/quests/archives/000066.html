BONE TALISMAN
Errak Thickshank can be found on Kerra Island, in a hut near the water. He tells you of a sick friend and asks if you can collect some ingredients for the cure. He will reward you with a Bone Talisman upon return.

You will need:

1) A giant snake fang, found in various zones from any giant snake

2) An aviak charm (no drop), from aviak avocets in South Karana

3) A tiger skin, dropped by tigers. The easiest to camp is in the Ak'Anon zoo
