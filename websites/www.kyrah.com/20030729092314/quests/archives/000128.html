- CLOAK OF GREATER PERNICITY -  

Go to the top floor of the library in the Plane of Knowledge.

You say, "Hail, Aid Fimli"
Aid Fimli says, "Greetings Kyrah! I wish I had time to chatter but my duties to the list and the Grand Librarian keep me extremely busy."

You say, "what duties?"
Aid Fimli says, "I am the aid for the Grand Librarian and he has me cataloging tomes on the master list. I spend so much time working on the list I haven't had time to go collect these items from the planes my uncle in Ak'Anon keeps pestering me over. He wants to upgrade his scrapyard back in Ak'Anon, and says only these items will do. Would you be willing to do me a favor?"

You say, "I would be willing to do you a favor"
Aid Fimli says, "Wonderful! If you would gather the following; a bundle of superconductive wires, silicorrosive grease, a gold tipped boar horn and a shard of pure energy, then take them to my uncle Sanfyrd in Ak'Anon. I would really appreciate being able to get back to my work uninterrupted."

Collect the items below:

1) Bundle of Super Conductive Wires -- Drops from random MoBs in the Plane of Innovation

2) Silicorrosive Grease -- Drops from random MoBs in the Plane of Innovation

3) Gold Tipped Boar Horn -- Drops from boars in the Plane of Tactics

4) Shard of Pure Energy -- Drops from lightning giants in the Bastion of Thunder

Find Sanfyrd Featherhead in Ak'Anon (+2020, -230) and hand him the four items. He will give you a note to bring back to Aid Fimli.

Aid Fimli says, "Great! I see you got the items to uncle; perhaps he will stop badgering me now and let me be in my work. Hmmmmm, Uncle mentions in his letter that he worked out a way for a gnome to make a giant set of crab crackers. He states it is useless in Norrath but I may be able to make some use of it out here. I guess it wouldn't hurt to share it with you and file it away in the library. To create the cracker combine two metal rods, firewater and a knuckle joint in a toolbox. Interesting indeed, anyways here if your reward; I hope you can put it to good use."
