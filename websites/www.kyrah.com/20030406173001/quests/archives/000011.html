- TUNARE WARDEN -  

Once you have completed the first and second part talisman quest, you may begin the third.

Find Yeolarn Bronzeleaf in the Felwithe cleric guild.

You say, "I am ready to become a Warden of Tunare."
Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says, "The crate that the you recovered from the Courier contained this black candlestick that radiates an aura of great magical power. I request that you deliver the candlestick to Lady Trilani who is studying with the High Men in Erudin. Perhaps she can divine the nature of the candlestick and offer aid in defeating its power. The remaining contents of the crate have been given to Crusader Swiftmoon to be delivered to a gnome in steamfont that is purchasing them on behalf of the Eldrich Collective. When you return be sure to present your Disciple Symbol to me with anything that Tilani may ask you to deliver."

Bring the candlestick to Tilani Palrone in the Erudin Palace.

Tilani Palrone says, "I sense a great evil power in this candlestick. I will need you to concoct a divinatory aid for me before I can discern more. Take this suspension and brew it in a brew barrel with one white hellebore (dropped by Gnolls in Split Paw or SK), a pouch of the red dust created by the Fire Peak Goblin Wizards (random goblins in Solusek's Eye), and the caustic substance used by the Dark Offerers in Mayong Mistmoores wicked castle."

Faction standing with Keepers of the Art, King Tearis Thex, and Faydarks Champions increases. Faction standing with The Dead decreases.

Upon giving the brewed result to Tilani, you will receive Un-Animation Powder which you must bring back to Yeolarn along with your second talisman.

Yeolarn Bronzeleaf says, "Praise Tunare!! I will have our sorcerers examine this power immediately to see if we can reproduce it in quantites enough to eliminate the undead plague. I award you the rank of Warden of Tunare, the All Mother smiles upon you Kyrah!"

Faction standing with Clerics of Tunare, King Tearis Thex, and Anti-Mage increases. You receive the third talisman and experience.
