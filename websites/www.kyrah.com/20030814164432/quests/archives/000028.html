- Bracers of Forbidden Rites -  

Go to Thurgadin. You must have at least kindly faction to receive the quest.

Loremaster Dorinan can be found in the cleric guild/library. He asks you to bring him 3 crushed opals and corroded plate bracer so that he may fashion a new bracer for you.

The 3 crushed opals are dropped of various 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The corroded plate bracer is a rare drop from giants in Kael.

Hand the 3 crushed opals (unstacked) and corroded plate bracer to Dorinan and you will receive the Bracers of Forbidden Rites.
