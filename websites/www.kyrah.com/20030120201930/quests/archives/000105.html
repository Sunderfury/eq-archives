BEGINNER MAGIC MANUAL
Find Acomor Lothwol in the Plane of Knowledge.

You say, "I am willing to learn"
Acomor Lothwol says, "The use of magic can be a taxing ordeal on the body, and quite often may leave you in a weakened state. Fortunately, I happen to know of a rather rejuvenating meal that will grant new power to your condition. If you are not familiar with the recipe for [Hobgoblin Surprise], I can tell you. What you must do is bring me some as well as your Planar Traveler's Manual. I hope you won't be long, for I am beginning to get quite hungry."

The Planar Traveler's Manual is a quest in itself, find the steps HERE.

You say, "what Hobgoblin Surprise?"
Acomor Lothwol says, "Don't be put off by the name, it's actually quite tasty. To make Hobgoblin Surprise, get a pie tin, some bread, add some hobgoblin meat and brown gravy. Cook it in the oven and enjoy the results. Mmm mmm, it waters my mouth just thinking about it."

It is believe that this cooked item requires a baking skill around 250. Once you've managed to create it and have your manual, hand them both to Acomar.

Acomar Lothwol says, "How delicious! You have done well here, I can't remember how long it's been since I've tasted a meal so fine. Take this book, it will certainly help you in your use of the magical arts. When you are ready, Cadelid Etord will give you your next lesson, so be sure to speak to him. Do not lose your book or else he may not appreciate you coming to class unprepared."
