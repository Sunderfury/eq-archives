- WATER SPRINKLER OF NEM ANKH -  

Ready to take the epic plunge? Yes, literally you will be starting underwater so make sure you have some form of enduring breath on hand! This thorough walkthrough should help you out along the way :)

Step One: Obtain the Orb of Frozen Water
  • Go to Lake Rathetear
  • Go to Island with 3 Aviaks loc (2800, 150)
  • Swim under Island
  • Go to cave entrance loc (2831, -912, -221)
  • Go to Aquagoblin Tower (note: Wolfform = no aggro)
  • Kill all 4 Aquagoblin on top of tower - Do not use AE – Deep is tough
  • Kill Lord Begurgle in Lake Rathe - 28 minute spawn timer
  • Loot "Lord Bergurgle's Crown"
  • Give it to Shmendrik Lavawalker 3600, 0 - 15 minute spawn timer
  • Let Natasha Whitewater kill Shmendrik
  • Kill "a spirit of flame"
  • Loot "Damaged Goblin Crown"
  • Give it to Natasha Whitewater
  • Receive "Ornate Sea Shell (I)"

  • Go to Timorous Deep
  • Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227)
  • Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
  • Swim to the other side
  • Head right keeping the on your left
  • Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307)
  • Enter the Hidden Oasis
  • Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
  • Give "Ornate Sea Shell(I)" to Omat Vastsea
  • Receive "Coral Statue of Tarew"

  • Go to Temple of Solusek Ro
  • Go through the big door and left
  • Go to balcony and up some stairs
  • Make a left there is a room with chair loc (326,38,29)
  • Practice route to the zone or have CoH’r
  • Have puller come with you
  • Announce you are pulling the Priest
  • Give Coral Statue of Tarew to "a seeker" (male). Note: Spawns 9am, Despawns 9pm (game time).
  • Kill Plasmatic Priest
  • Loot "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe"

  • Go to Solusek A
  • Go the room has pit in it loc (-796, -366)
  • Kill Lord Gimblox - 19 min. spawn, PH "Lg Fire Goblin" or "fire goblin shaman"
  • Loot "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring"

  • Go to Timorous Deep
  • Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227)
  • Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
  • Swim to the other side
  • Head right keeping the on your left
  • Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307)
  • Enter the Hidden Oasis
  • Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
  • Give Omat "Blood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robe"
  • Receive "Orb of Frozen Water"
  • Find Natasha Whitewater in building behind Omat
  • Give "Lord Gimblox's Signet Ring" to Natasha Whitewater
  • Receive "Ornate Sea Shell (II)"
Step Two: Obtain the Orb of Clear Water
  • Go to Burning Woods
  • Give "Ornate Sea Shell (II)" to Naxot Deepwater loc (3400, -2150)
  • Receive "Message to Natasha".
  • Kill Ixiblat Fer
  • Loot "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer"

  • Go to Chardok
  • Kill Overking Bathezid Di'Zok
  • Loot "Singed Scroll"

  • Go to Timorous Deep
  • Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227)
  • Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
  • Swim to the other side
  • Head right keeping the on your left
  • Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307)
  • Enter the Hidden Oasis
  • Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
  • Give "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer" and "Singed Scroll" to Omat Vastsea
  • Receive "Orb of Clear Water"
  • Find Natasha Whitewater in building behind Omat
  • Give "Message to Natasha" to Natasha Whitewater
  • Receive "Shimmering Pearl"
Step Three: Obtain the Orb of Vapor
  • Go to Nagafen’s Lair
  • Go towards the Fire Giants Castle
  • Across the ramp there is a ledge Under that banner, Zordak Ragefire will spawn
  • Give "Shimmering Pearl" to Zordak Ragefire
  • Receive "Swirling Pearl"

  • Go to Skyfire Mountains
  • Go to LOC p3200 n3200 - Big Dragon statue, he spawns in front
  • Hail the Warder
  • Receive "Box of Binding"
  • Kill random mobs in Sky Fire Mountains, obtain 4 pearlescent fragments
  • Combine 4 pearlescent fragments in the "Box of Binding"
  • Hand box into Warder
  • Receive a Seal
  • The human form of RF will now spawn
  • Kill Zordak Ragefire, the human level 55
  • Kill Zordakalicus Ragefire, Dragon Level 60
  • Loot "Impure Heart of Ragefire" NO DROP Container
  • Place the "Swirling Pearl" inside Heart along with "Pearlescent Seal"
  • Click Combine, Container becomes "Heart of Ragefire"

  • Go to Timorous Deep
  • Go to Island loc (-11567, -2227)
  • Swim through cave entrance at loc (-11530, -3728)
  • Swim to the other side
  • Head right keeping the on your left
  • Swim through the other cave entrance at (-11336, -1307
  • Enter the Hidden Oasis
  • Find Omat Vastsea on Pier
  • Give "Heart of Zordak Ragefire" to Omat Vastsea
  • Receive "Orb of Vapor"
Step Four: Obtain the Orb of the Triumvirate
  • Find Jhassad Oceanson on the beach by a tree
  • Give "Orb of Frozen Water", "Orb of Clear Water", and "Orb of Vapor" to Jhassad Oceanson
Step Five: Obtain the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
  • Be patient, Avatar of Water end up beside Jhassad
  • Give "Orb of the Triumvirate" to Avatar of Water
  • Receive the Water Sprinkler of Nem Ankh
-- List Compiled by Torriana of Brell Serillis
