AKKIRUS' VAMBRACES OF THE RISEN
You must have ally faction to Claws of Veeshan to complete this quest.

In Skyshrine there is a wyvern named Fardonad Fe`Dhar (high up in the city, main quest room) who promises to make you some new armplates if you bring him 3 jaundice gems and unadorned vambraces.

The jaundice gems drop from random 40+ mobs in Velious, or can be purchased from other players. The unadorned vambraces are a rare drop in Temple of Veeshan, specifically the east wing which is known as the Halls of Testing.

Give the 3 jaundice gems (unstacked) and the unadorned vambraces to Fardonad, and you will be rewarded with Akkirus' Vambraces of the Risen.
