VAMBRACES OF THE FERVENT
Blaize the Radiant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Fervent," she will tell you that Jovan the Fervent lost his Ignot in a game with an aviak known as Gull Skytalon. Jovan started drinking after that and now spends his time in Highpass.

Go the aviak island in the Ocean of Tears. The island loc is 2000, 4000 and the easiest way to get there is to take the boat to sister island (first boat stop from Freeport) and then swim (or levitate if you can get it cast on you) to get there. Look for the named aviak, Gull Skytalon. He is around level 35. Kill him for the Ignot of the Fervent. If you do not see him, kill the aviak placeholders until he spawns.

Aquire 1 Tumpy Tonix. You can get this from a master brewer or from the NPC named Tumpy Irontoe. Tumpy is found in Kaladim, in a shop called Irontoe Eats. He will make the Tumpy Tonix for you if you bring him 1 flask of water and 1 kiola nut. To get a kiola nut, go to sister island and purchase from the shop straight out from the dock.

Bring the Tumpy Tonix to Jovan in Highpass, he needs it as a cure for his hangover. You can usually find Jovan upstairs in the sawmill. If you are facing the entrance to Highhold, turn right to find the sawmill. Jovan will reward you will the Icon of the Fervent.

Purchase 1 bar of platinum and have a player enchant it. Give the enchanted bar to Vilissia (NPC in Sol Ro Temple) and she will return a vulcanized bar to you. Give the vulcanized bar, the Icon, and the Ignot to Blaize in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Vambraces of the Fervent.
