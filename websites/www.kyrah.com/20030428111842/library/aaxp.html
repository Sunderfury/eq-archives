- ALTERNATIVE ADVANCEMENT EXPERIENCE -
 
 
The Alternative Advancement system was introduced with the Shadows of Luclin expansion, breathing new life in lvl 60 players who thought their experiencing days were over. You don't need to wait until 60 to begin though, the first set is trainable at lvl 51. You can also choose the percentage of experience that will be placed in the AAXP pool, for example, you may set AAXP to 50% and the rest of your experience gain will go into the normal XP bar.
  • At level 51, your General Archetypes are unlocked. These are abilities that any player may advance in.
  • At level 55, your Priest Archetypes are unlocked. These are abilities that all priest-based classes may select.
  • At level 59, your Cleric Archetypes are unlocked. These are abilities exclusively available to the cleric class.
  • At level 61, your Planar Advancement are unlocked. Available to all classes, must level to 65 to fully advance.
  • At level 61, your Priest Planar Abilities are unlocked. Priest classes only, must level to 65 to fully advance.
  • At level 63, your Cleric Planar Abilities are unlocked. Cleric class only, must level to 65 to fully advance.
Note that planar abilities require the Planes of Power expansion. Below is a chart with a list of all abilities for clerics along with how many points must be obtained to train in them. Be aware that some skills require training in lower level archetypes. This information can be found in your AAXP box. Just click the skill name for more information.
