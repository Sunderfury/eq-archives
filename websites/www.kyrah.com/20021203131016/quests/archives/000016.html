GUANTLETS OF THE ARDENT
Blaize the Radiant in the Temple of Solusek Ro gives this quest. If you say "Tell me about the Ardent," she will tell you that Theo the Ardent who was at war with with a necromancer named Babbinsbort. He brought an expedition to Everfrost, but drowned when the ice beneath his feet gave way.

Go to Upper Guk and find the Scryer spawn room. There are two pillars of water here, and the right one contains the Ignot of the Pentient (-200, 450). It appears as a small bag.

Your next stop is Befallen, where you will find Babbinsbort on the third floor. Kill him and loot the Icon of the Ardent.

Purchase 1 bar of platinum and have a player enchant it. Give the enchanted bar to Genni (NPC in Sol Ro Temple) and she will return a galvanized bar to you. Give the galvanized bar, the Icon, and the Ignot to Blaize in the Temple of Solusek Ro for the Guantlets of the Ardent.
