- SPELL REAGENT INFORMATION -
 
 
Some cleric spells require a gem to cast. These are most often the symbol buffs, but a few other spells also have a reagent requirement. Below is a listing of these spells for reference. Note that many level 44 clerics continue to use the Transal symbol to avoid high peridot costs. Normally the Pinzarn buff is not worth the cost until 50+. It's also a good idea to discuss with your group how to arrange reimbursement for your component costs. Most players will have no problem agreeing to offering the group cleric a larger cut of the cash drops, but if they don't, you shouldn't feel obligated to cast these buffs on them :)
