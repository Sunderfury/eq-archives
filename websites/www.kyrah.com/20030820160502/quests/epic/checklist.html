Supplies You'll Need: Aside from plenty of patience and bugging your friends for assistance, there isn't much necessary to bring along on this quest. Three came to mind though, all of which are available as potions:

Enduring Breath -- Required for the Lord Bergurgle camp, also helpful with Timorous Deep traveling
Invisibility -- Will help you get around certain areas, such as Sol A & B (when soloing to avoid wasting time)
Levitation -- Not really necessary, but will make Timorous Deep runs A LOT easier and faster
Necessary Forces: The chart below is meant to give you an idea of what kind of force you'll need to defeat the MoBs included in this quest. It is based on 50+ players, lower levels may need a larger force. Many variables come into play of course, so how many companions you'll need assisting depends on their level, equipment, skill, etc.
