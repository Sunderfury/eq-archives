- COLDAIN PRAYER SHAWLS 1-2 -  

This quest begins in the cleric guild (which is also the library) of Thurgadin. Each one will raise faction with Coldain and Dain Frostreaver IV, lower faction with Kromzek and Kromrif.

1) Burlap Coldain Prayer Shawl
Hail Loremaster Borannin. After he greets you, say, "I seek to perform duties for the crown." Borannin will instruct you to bring him 4 frost giant toes. These drop randomly from the giants in the Great Divide. Once you have all 4, hand them to Borannin for the 1st shawl.

2) Cloth Coldain Prayer Shawl
After completing the first shawl quest, say, "What task?" to Loremaster Borannin to continue. He will give you a box and tell you to fill it with 10 frost giant heads, which once again can be found in the Great Divide. Once filled, hit the combine button (result: Preserved Kromrif Heads) and bring it back to Borannin along with the burlap shawl. In return you'll get the 2nd shawl.
