- VAMBRACES OF FORBIDDEN RITES -  

Go to Thurgadin. You must have at least kindly faction to receive the quest.

Loremaster Dorinan can be found in the cleric guild/library. He asks you to bring him 3 jaundice gems and corroded plate vambraces so that he may fashion a new set of armplates for you.

The 3 jaundice gems are dropped of various 40+ mobs in Velious or can be bought from other players. The corroded plate vambraces are a rare drop from giants in Kael.

Hand the 3 jaundice (unstacked) and corroded plate vambraces to Dorinan and you will receive the Vambraces of Forbidden Rites.
