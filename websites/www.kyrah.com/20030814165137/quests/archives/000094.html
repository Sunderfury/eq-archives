- Orb of Clear Water -  

The Orb of Frozen Water must be completed to begin the second part of the quest. You should have an "Ornate Sea Shell" from Natasha Whitewater.

Go to Burning Woods with a raid force (important classes: warrior with defensive discipline, shaman to slow, enchanter to tash). Usually the groups park at the zone next to Skyfire. Bind yourself nearby, then head for +3400, -2150. An iksar wearing a bright aqua-colored robe spawns here. Her name is Naxot Deepwater. It is believed that she is on a 24 game hour cycle, so if you do not see her, you may need to wait a little bit.

You say, "Hail, Naxot Deepwater"
Naxot Deepwater says, "Beware these woods! The sarnaks claim this land as their own and wicked creatures walk beneath the burning foliage."

Hand her the Ornate Sea Shell and in return you'll receive "Message to Natasha".

Naxot Deepwater says, "Praise the Triumvirate! Natasha sent you just in time! Those twisted sarnak summoners are summoning Ixiblat Fer as we speak! We must stop Ixiblat while he is still weak or all of Norrath may be set aflame! Please do me one favor, should I perish to this beast of fire. Give this note to Natasha when next you see her, and if you should perish and I survive, I will make sure the waters never forget the reflections of your deeds this day."

This is when Ixiblat Fer will spawn. He is a giant level 62 fire elemental. Ixiblat hits for a max of 575 and is immune to fire based spells. Gate back to your raid while a tracking puller with sow grabs Ixiblat. Make sure the main tank fights in front while the other fighters are behind him. Once he falls, claim his "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer".

Next stop is Chardok, again with a raiding force. You'll be taking on the royals area, namely fighting your way to Overking Bathezid (level 65 with a 2 hour respawn). Loot "A Singed Scroll" from the corpse.

The scroll message is as follows:
Greetings Overkind Bathezid. The time has come for you to return the boon owed to me from so long ago. I have need of your most powerful summoners to bring Ixiblat Fer into the mortal realm and reign fire upon my enemies. Your old friend, Zordak Ragefire.

Head to Timorous Deep and give Omat Vastsea the "Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer" and "A Singed Scroll". He will reward you with the "Orb of Clear Water".

Omat Vastsea says, "I commend you on your success over the fire elemental lord, Ixiblat Fer. The fact that the Plasmatic Priesthood has mustered up the power to summon such a being means drastic actions must be taken to stop the fanatic cult of Fennin Ro. I wonder what kind of favor Zordak Ragefire performed for the sarnak summoners to convince them to assist in the ritual that brought forth Ixiblat Fer. The dragon Iksar hybrid abominations are not beings who ally with others easily. All I have seen in my divinations of the High Plasmatic Priest are riddles, and signs that the one who slays him may be awarded an Orb of the Triumvirate. Seek Zordak Ragefire, cut out his heart, place within it the swirling pearl and return the heart to me."

Natasha Whitwater will again spawn at this point. Give her the "Message to Natasha" and she will give you a "Shimmering Pearl".

Natasha Whitewater lowers her head in mourning. "It is a shame that fate has decided one so young and from such a background should perish, but we must not attempt to change what was destined to be. Naxot sacrificed herself selflessly so that others may be spared the horrors Ixiblat would have brought to Norrath. May the waters of Norrath forever remember her reflection. I thank you for delivering Naxot's message to me and give you this pearl imbued with the power of the Triumvirate. Should you find Zordak Ragefire, speak with him before all else then hold this pearl before him. When the pearl begins to swirl it's enchantment will have taken effect."
