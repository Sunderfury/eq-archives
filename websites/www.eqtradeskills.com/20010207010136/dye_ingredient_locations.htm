Dyes - can be made by anyone, using the above extracts, and combining them in a medium clay jar (made by a potter, this is a combinable container. You lose the jar on combine)

  • dark color - 1 resin, 1 extract
  • base color - 2 resins, 1 extract
  • light color - 3 resins, 1 extract

Dyed Plate Armor - Supplies

To create and use dyes, you will need several items.   You will need the extract itself, which is detailed more in the next section.   You will also need Resins, Jars of Acid, Jars of Lacquer, Empty Vials, and Medium Clay Jars.   In addition you will also need standard Water Flasks, same as you use for blacksmithing.

You can purchase Resin, Jar of Acid, Jar of Lacquer and Empty Vial from two vendors at this time.  First vendor is in High Keep, by the Pickclaw Goblin mines.   This is the same vendor that sells Medium Quality Ore.  The second vendor is in Steamfont Mountains, in the Windmills.  Again, it is the same one that sells Medium Quality Ores.  It is important to note that the Empty Vials must be purchased here.  Ones created by a potter will not work.

How to dye your armor:

Take a completed piece of fine steel plate armor, place it in the forge along with 1 dye, 1 jar of acid, 1 jar of lacquer and combine. Once dyed, the color cannot be removed. You Can fail on this part of the process (it's a skill check versus your blacksmithing skill), read your combine message carefully. The armor will be returned to you, un-dyed, and you lose the dye, lacquer and acid.

Where to find components

Evergreen Leaves in Greater Faydark:

  • Visual cue: these look like lighter green slanted squares on the ground. Since the ground is green, these can be easy to miss if you're not scouring carefully.
  • Locations: (63, 166); (596, 200); (638, -96); (-411, -57); (962, 814); (-191, 7); (90, -400); (30, -2); (403, -440); (555, -234); (669, 774); (717, 748); (197, -483); (200, 400); (427, -321); (-384, -30); (630, -101)

Jack-o'lantern Fungus and Sarcoscypha fungus in Lesser Faydark:

  • Visual cue: these look like small colored mushrooms (red and blue) on the ground
  • Locations: on the ground in the faerie camp, generally around the bigger mushrooms

Permafrost Crystals in Permafrost

  • Visual cue: these look like red lumps on the ground.
  • Locations: On the ground in the dire wolf pens in Permafrost, as well as on the ground in various spots of the "Goblin Castle"

Oxides (Iron and Russet) in Steamfont:

  • Visual cue: these look like red lumps on the ground.
  • Locations: On and around the huge gears on the ground near the minotaur caves.
