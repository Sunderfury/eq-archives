Welcome to Raul's Tavern!  

 

This is a complete guide to brewing so far as we know it.  There are a few recipes that NO ONE knows or no one is sharing so these are all the published known recipes out there.  If any one knows the recipe for the following drinks please e-mail me at oke@mediaone.net so I can update this page.

Well as you know by now, brewing is not really a profitable skill but a lot of fun to do and who doesn't like having a buddy that can run out and make a few brews for a little everbash.  Well here are all the recipes I know at this moment, I will be updating these pages as I get more info and trivial levels.  I hope this helps you on your brewing quest and keep your eyes open for new combinations.  Verant just increased the brew barrel size to 10!!!

   
The best place I have found to brew is in HighHold Pass, there is an inn and a tavern right next to each other by the river that you can pop back and forth and get all you need.  I got to skill level 127 in just 2 hours there! (That was a few patches ago so that may be changed now).

Rumor has it that if you have your brewing handbook on you, your chances for success are much better.  I cannot verify if this is the case but I'll try anything if it has a chance of decreasing my number of fails!

Anyway enough babbling, here they are and enjoy yourself out there!

  and YES!!! we finally have Morning Dew in the game!!  It can be foraged in Greater Faydark and Lesser Faydark.

Brewing Material locations 

New brewing recipes for smithing!

Misty Acorns can be found on the ground in Misty Thicket

Oak Bark is foraged in Kithicor Forest and Warslik's Woods in Kunark also a ground spawn in Butcherblock

All tempers are trivial for Brewers at 135

Earthen Temper = Dwarven Ale + Lava Rock + 2 drops of Mercury 

Moonlight Temper = Morning Dew + vial of swirling mists + 2 essence of moonlight 

Royal Temper = Rain Water + Griffene's Blood + 2 drops of sunshine  

Scale Temper = Vial of Sarnak Blood + Vial of Froglok Blood + Vial of Iksar Blood + Bloodwater

Shadow Temper = Neriak Nectar + Elf blood + 2 essence of shadows

Blood Temper = ogre swill + Elf blood + Human blood + Dwarf blood

Frost temper = Brandy + Ice goblin blood + 2 essence of winter

Sea Temper  =  2 Mugs of Sea Foam + Saltwater Seaweed + Bottle of FishWine
