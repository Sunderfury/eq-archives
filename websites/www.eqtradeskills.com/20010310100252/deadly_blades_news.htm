March 28, 2000

I am working on getting things in order, I need to know who is still in the guild and who is leaving or has left.  I have made Chevalier De Blanc an Assistant Guildleader and we will be meeting tonight to make some major decisions about the guild.  I am planning on keeping it a rogue/bard guild but we will form alliances with other guilds so we can get a nice mix of classes to group with.  The big thing is communication right now.  A lot of our current/past guild members do not communicate information and with a guild especially it is VERY important.  If you have looked at the roster you know what info I need to update the database.  I also need to know what trade skills you have mastered.  I am redoing the roster so I really do need to know who is going to participate and who is along for a free ride.  If a free ride are your intentions, you are in the wrong guild and may want to seek that elsewhere.  If you are serious about being in a guild, The Deadly Blades will be more than happy to call you brother or sister and work together, for the whole and not individuals.  If you could let me know either way I would appreciate it.  I can be reached at raul@eqtradeskills.com or if that address is not working oke@mediaone.net 

The merits and awards database is almost done.  I will be posting that shortly and well as the hierarcy of the guild so everyone knows where they are and where they can go.  I am also trying to add member pages, send in a description and a picture of your character you want up on the web and people will be able to access your profile from the roster page.  I hope to have it up by the weekend.  Hope you all are doing well, for those of you staying, thank you for sticking this out through some rough times and I promise things will get better.  For those of you leaving, I wish you all the best and if you decide to come back one day, you are always welcome to return.
