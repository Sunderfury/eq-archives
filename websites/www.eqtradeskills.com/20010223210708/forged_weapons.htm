Raul's guide to forged weapons

well here it is!  The unofficial guide to forged weapons.  The forged weapons were added to Everquest in the October 13th, 1999 patch and was one of the first and only improvements made to the blacksmithing skill since the release of EQ.  Three new items were added, a morning star, bastard sword and two-handed sword.  All very similar to their fine steel counterparts.  These are all trivial to a master blacksmith so I am not sure when they become trivial.  Any input would be greatly appreciated so I can update this page.  As far as mold locations go, you can buy them from merchants who sell other molds, (see the materials location guide).

Well, on to the good stuff.  You can read about the new weaponsmithing book called:  Dirge of the Fireforge. 

The first weapon is the forged morning star.  It is a 1H-blunt weapon.  It can deal 8 damage and has a delay of 38.  It weighs 9.0

You need 1 hilt mold, 1 spiked ball mold, 1 sheet metal and 1 flask of water.

+ + + = (trivial at 75)

Next up is the forged bastard sword.  It is a 1H-slashing weapon.  It deals 7 damage and has a delay of 33.  It weighs 6.5

You need 1 pommel mold, 1 hilt mold, 1 dual edge mold, 1 sheet metal and 1 flask of water.

  + + + + =   (trivial at 75)

Last but not least is the forged two-handed sword.  This puppy deals 12 damage and has a delay of 44.  It weighs in at 11.

You need 1 pommel mold, 1 hilt mold, 1 heavy steel blade mold, 1 sheet metal and 1 flask of water.

+ + + + =   (Trivial at 91)
