Conium Darkblade in Dagnor's Cauldron

Conium is a dwarf rogue located on the island in Dagnor's Cauldron.  He is located at the highest point of the hill.  I was level 31 when I was doing this but lower levels may have some problems.  There are aqua goblins and rats running around the island.  The goblins left me alone but the rats always attacked me.  They were diced up pretty easily but stay on watch while you are doing this part of the quest.

This is the note I got from Lon the redeemed below.  I hailed him first then handed him the note.  Notice I have an open slot in my main inventory.  Make sure you have one open because Conium will hand you an axe.
