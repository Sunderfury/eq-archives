The Hemo Encologist

This is another tough item to get for the quest.  The Hemo Encologist is located in the inner courtyard of Castle Mistmoore.  It is right next to the fountain room.  This is dangerous because she has lots of friends with her in the courtyard.  I got really luck because a nice troll by the name of WAUKE killed the Hemo for me and let me loot it.  She had 2 blood spirits on her and bronze legs.  Unfortunately there were no other rogues in the zone to get the other spirits so I think they rotted  =(
