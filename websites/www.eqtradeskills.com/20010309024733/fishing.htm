Fishing in Norrath

 

Zone-specific Fishing Catches:  (you need a skill over 100 to catch some of these)

  • Cabilis - Fetid Bass (sized from tiny fetid bass to humongous fetid bass)
  • City of Mist - Ikfish
  • Firiona Vie - Fetid Bass (sized from tiny fetid bass to humongous fetid bass), sewer catfish
  • The Karanas - Thunder Salmon
  • Karnor's Castle - Wulf Fish
  • Lake of Ill Omen - Illweed reed, Wet Clay
  • Nurga - Cave Guppy
  • Overthere - Danak Grouper
  • Ocean of Tears - Saltwater Seaweed; Gunthak Mackarel
  • Swamp of no Hope - Krup Guppy
  • Toxxulia Forest - Greengill Salmon
  • Timorous Deep - Timorous Tuna
  • Warslik's Woods - Evergreen Salmon

There are also new fishing flies you can tie in a tackle box that you get on the Brother Island in OOT!  You can also purchase dry hooks and stuff there as well.  The island is the stop right before the Sister Island on the way to Freeport from BB.  The tackle box costs around 1PP.

Fishing materials locations in OOT

fishing pole, bait - Endan Halson (inn, Zachariah Reigh's Island - first stop from Butcherblock to Freeport) 

tacklebox, fishing pole, fishing bait, catch-a-lot bait, dry hooks, treble hooks, and circle hooks - Sanian Shearsin (Zachariah Reigh's Island, building at -2103, 7796) and Craven Jonsbrow (Zachariah Reigh's Island, on the beach by the dock -2256, 7596)

I don't have a lot of personal experience to add to this yet so here is what someone had to say about it;

Okay, I started doing the flyfishing.  So far, it's a major
letdown, money-wise.  But it's a ton of fun as a low-level
time-killer. :)

Some notes:

-The tacklebox is a 4-slot container.

-A failed mix (meaning correct ingredients but not enough skill)
will return the hook.  (i.e. "You have fashioned an alternate
item")

-With a 75 Charisma, selling price on all three types of
Squeaking Nibblers and all three Night Swoopers was 2cp.  Yup, a
lousy 2cp.  ::sigh::


-With a fishing of 45, I tried making a handful of them.  All
were trivial before 45:
  -Squeaking Nibbler Dry Fly
  -Squeaking Nibbler Treble Fly
  -Squeaking Nibbler Circle Fly
  -Night Swooper Dry Fly
  -Night Swooper Treble Fly
  -Night Swooper Circle Fly

-When they were fished with, they were consumed when a fish was
caught. (as per normal bait)  Given their cost and energy, I was
hoping they would be re-usable.


So far, I can't find a use for this skill, except for sheer fun.
 The money is pure crap - right around a silver per hook, plus
fur and thread, and there's a return of only the two copper.

The ~biggest~ bummer was that the Fishing Flies don't look any
different from regular hooks.  I mean, if they at least ~looked~
like flies, I'd be all over it.  But no luck.

As soon as I can piece together the rest of the fur/wing
components, I'll send more details.  Have you heard where to
pick up the recipe book?  The book on the NPCs that sell the
components is labeled "About Seafarers Ringmail" or something
similar...

thanks!
-Scott =)
