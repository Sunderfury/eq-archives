Code of Conduct


The Amber Order is a guild founded under the guiding influence of Quellious, The Tranquil. We are not a religious guild devoted to Quellious, specifically. Rather, we welcome members of all races, classes, and religions in the hope that the guild, as our common bond, will strengthen our mutual understanding and lead us, and Norrath, to a more peaceful existence. The concepts of "good" and "evil" are often relative ones.  Exploration and understanding of each may be necessary for us to properly understand each other and thus end conflict between peoples and within ourselves. Our primary goal is to explore Norrath fully, to learn the nature of all the places and entities in this world so that we may find the true path to peace.

We have no requirements on members other than that they be able to participate in the guild peacefully and help us pursue our goals. To that end, we must understand what it means to participate. First, we must protect the knowledge and experience we gather, thus we must protect the guild and its members. Race, class, PvP status, and religious beliefs are secondary to the protection of the knowledge we each hold as a part of our "reservoir of enlightenment." Thus, in The Amber Order as in no other place (yet), you may see a Paladin coming to the aid of a Shadow Knight, and vice versa. We believe in Peace, yet we must protect our bothers and sisters for we do not fully understand the role each of us may play in eventually unraveling the mystery of mutual understanding that will bring peace to Norrath.

Similarly, senseless destruction and killing rob Norrath of knowledge. Once lost, we cannot know the contribution someone might have made to our enlightenment. Who knows? Maybe the key to bringing understanding and peace is carried by just one individual. If we allow indiscriminate destruction and killing we may have lost a path to enlightenment. Consequently, as members of The Amber Order, we seek to aid those in trouble and promote peace by sharing our knowledge with everyone. However, we recognize that death and conflict are natural extensions of Norrath's current state of ignorance. We cannot forcibly bring peace to Norrath, we must enlighten it.

So, we will help when we can, and when feel that we can do so constructively. We are not warlike, nor are we a vigilante group. However, we will go to war, or participate in vigilante actions, if we feel that some level of destruction or killing threatens Norrath's enlightenment.

Finally, (out of character) The Amber Order expects that its members will conduct themselves as "good citizens" in the context of an Online RPG. We will avoid of foul language (since minors may be playing), shun the use of "cheats," be prudent of our use of "broadcasts" (/ooc & /shout), and generally try to enhance everyone's enjoyment of the game, rather than seeking fun in disturbing others.
