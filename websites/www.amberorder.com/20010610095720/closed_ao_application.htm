We are based on the E'ci server

If you aren't already considered a friend of the guild but you want to join, it will require both effort and patience on your part.

When the Knights and others of Amber wish someone to join us, they will ask you to use this web-based form to submit your application.  Your application is emailed to all the officers of Amber Order, and assigned to one for review and processing.  If approved, you will be contacted and invited to join.

The Prospect will then serve as an Initiate until they either become truly accepted by the guild and get promoted to Squire or are politely asked to move on.

So, if we know you and want you to join then chances are we'll either have asked you about it or we soon will do.

If we don't know you but you still want to join then your best bet is to take a look at the members page and try to get to know us in-game, and be patient.

 

Applications for Membership in Amber Order are not

being accepted on this site until further notice.

We have reached our goals for size for the time being.  Exceptions may or may not be made for exceptional people/players, and will only happen in-game. We strive for camaraderie, fun and goodtimes, not size, not uberness, and not hardcore.
