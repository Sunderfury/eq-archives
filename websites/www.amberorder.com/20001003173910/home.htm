Welcome to the homepage of The Amber Order guild on EverQuest's E'ci server.  If you want to know more about us, you've come to the right place!

Check the links above to read our Charter, check our members Code of Conduct, read our discussions, look at our members profiles. If you like what you see and are considering joining, fill out an application and we'll get back to you in game.

Amber Order has set up a dedicated Roger Wilco server for use by the guild while playing on E'ci. You can join us in the chat by pressing here!

There is also a fledging attempt at publishing a calendar for Amber's officers to publish events in an organized format.  Check it out, but for now the official event list on our message board is the place to watch.

We also have an IRC chat channel!! We've added a Live Chat page to the site, so if you don't know what IRC is, or don't have an IRC program, or really just don't care but would like to chat with your AO pals, CLICK HERE!  If E'ci ever goes down, this would be a really good page to visit--hint, hint!
