Me find dis poim somewhere - by Petunia

Puff da magic trollie lived by da swamp and frolicked in da murky mist looking fer stuff to clomp.

Little Inkie Sha'Dal loved dat trollie Puff and brought him lungs and jellied brains and udder hummie stuff

Together dey would travel on a boat with plumpling slaves Sha'Dal kept his flesh-hooks warm while Puff tortured da knaves

Noble kings and princes would fill their bloody steins Pirate crews would lower their heads while Puff pulled out their spines

A trollie clomps forever but not so Inkie boys bloody heads and hummies dead make way for other toys

One black night it happened Inkie Sha'Dal came no more And Puff dat mighty trollie he ceased his fearless roar

His head was bent in sorrow green scales dey fell like rain Puff no longer went to play and cause da hummies pain Without his life-long friend

Puff's heart was cold and frig'd And Puff dat mighty trollie sadly slipped under a bridge

-Petunia
