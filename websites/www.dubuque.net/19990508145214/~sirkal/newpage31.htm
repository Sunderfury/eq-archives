As you can see from the following information, Per AC point cost increases at a dramatic rate. 23 AC points of Cloth costs 3,857 copper pieces for a per AC cost of 167. The 110 Plate AC costs 5,425,660 copper pieces for a average cost of 49,324 copper pieces. So it makes sense to move up 1 level at a time. When you have all the Cloth pieces available, then move to Leather and so on. For that 1 Steel Mask with a AC of 5, you can get 32 complete Cloth armor sets with a AC of 22 each. That's a 704 total AC vs. 5 for the Mask.
