Faction. No matter how you play, whether you are a roleplayer or powergamer, factions play an integral role in your daily life in Norrath. Factions can affect how NPCs react to you in a number of ways. Low factions with someone can cause them to not do business with you, or even to attack you on sight. On the other hand, having a high faction standing with someone will give you their respect, protection and a discount when buying and selling. There are many faction groups in Norrath, and finding which ones certain people belong to and how to raise your standing with them can be quite a challenge. And who knew that killing that gnome in Crushbone would make the Gem Choppers hate you? I sure didn't! But I do now. And that, I'm assuming, is why you're here; to find out whether killing that dark elf in East Freeport will make all dark elves hate you, or just a select few. So, with that in mind, go ahead and browse.
Note: This site currently deals only with factions from battle.
