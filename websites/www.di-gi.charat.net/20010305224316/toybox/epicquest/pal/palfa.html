Paladin New FA Quest : Fiery Diffender



始まり：Plane Of FearにいるIrak Altilに話し掛ける所からクエストが始まります。
You say, 'what sword?'
Irak Altil says 'Hands of a twisted brother no longer living.

You say, 'what shield'
Irak Altil says 'Earthly children of the god of hate.
Keeping stand over his dark brothers and sisters.

You say, 'what breastplate'
Irak Altil lets loose an unearthly howl as it gleams with sickly light.
'Upon she who lured me from my post. In that place where hatred festers. Painful corruption.'

１．これらのitemを集めます。(剣は3、盾は2group程度で取れるようです。lvl55平均）
・剣：the Holeにいる"Keeper of the Tombs"から（pop間隔　2hours程度）
・盾：Nektulos ForestにいるKirik Vil（DarkElf）から（pop間隔　数日）
・胸当て：Plane of Hateに（a bansheekill後）出現するThought Destroyersから


２．次のそれらのitemを浄化する過程に入ります。

Irak Altil says 'The sword must be dipped in water pure.
Light of clean spirit to cleanse the shield.
A gem of clarity unlike any other for the breastplate.
All must be cleansed and returned to the temple for my soul to be freed.
Only the temple can cleanse, only they can touch the artifacts.
Take the sets of two to the temple.
When they are all three cleansed seperately take them to he who will grant me atonement.

・盾の浄化：FelwithにいるElia the Pureに盾を渡し、Gleaming Crested Shieldを受け取ります。
・剣の浄化：WestFreeportにいるpeasant womanに"I will take it"と話しかけて、itemを受け取り それを近くにいる彼女の兄弟に渡すと、a bucket of pure waterがもらえます。（鑑定で Blessed by Sacrifice）
剣とその水を、Erudinのthe Peacekeepers guildにいるReklon Gnallenに渡すと、Gleaming Crested Swordにしてもらえます。
・胸当ての浄化：Kaladimのbank近くの地下にいるJarkに"some"と話しかけます。会話を進めると夕食が欲しいらしいです。
Pal/Clrギルドの前の泉の所にNella（彼の奥さん）がいるので、"dinner"と話すと夕食がもらえるのでJarkに渡します。
するとPure Crystalがもらえます。（鑑定：Blessed by Compassion）
　胸当てとCrystalをErudinのthe Peacekeepers guildにいるReklon Gnallenに渡すと、Gleaming Crested Breastplateがもらえます。

浄化した剣、盾、胸当てを、Erudinのthe Peacekeepers guildにいるReklon Gnallenに渡すと、mark of atonementがもらえます。

Reklon Gnallen says 'I never thought our order would see these artifacts again.
With the return of these relics we can now put at ease a scar upon the history of our order.
This cleansing will atone for his failure.
Your soul must be pure to have given so freely of yourself.
Go now, take this crested token of our order.
If you wish to free his soul you must undertake another sacrifice.

You say, 'what sacrifice'
Reklon Gnallen says 'Only this crest and the sacrifice of something that symbolizes your strength and purity will release his spirit into the cleansing flames of our lady Quellious. Go now. Thank you Khael.

３．Fiery Difender
・先述のIrakに、mark of atonementとFAを渡すと、Fiery Difenderがもらえます。
