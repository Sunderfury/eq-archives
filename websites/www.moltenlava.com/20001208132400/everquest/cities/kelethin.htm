City Name:  Kelethin
Native Race:  Wood Elves
Guard Alignment: Good
Guild Masters: Bard, Druid, Ranger, Rogue, Warrior

Kelethin is a city found deep within the evil, yet enchanted, forest of Greater Faydark.  It is just a short run from Felwithe and even closer to Crushbone Clan.  Being so close to Crushbone's entrance, though, makes it a very dangerous place for new characters starting out, which is why so many of them take the long journey to Butcherblock or even Freeport.

Kelethin is a city completely built within the tree tops of Greater Faydark.  It uses an ingenious design of pulleys and elevators to bring people to and from the ramps.  Once in the city, everyone must be sure to walk slowly since there are no rails on the edges, so inexperienced players will fall to their death instantly.

One thing to note about Kelethin is that it is the darkest of all the outdoor cities.  The forest of Faydark creates a roof over the city, so that hardly any light passes through it.  In fact, all of these images here have been given a major gamma boost so that you can see the buildings easily.   However, once you can see the buildings, you will notice the ornate designs of gold all around the corners and edges.  Inside the buildings, there is usually a hearth of some sort, surrounded by more paintings, carvings, and creative designs in the walls.   The Wood Elves really know how to decorate wood.

Another thing you will notice about the visibility while in Kelethin is the haze and fog that never seems to lift or burn out.   The building on the left in the adjacent image is only about 100 feet from the character, and the building on the right is only about 130 feet away (once again, there is a gamma boost of about 1.4).  Obviously, the fog really kills visibility in Kelethin.   The eye candy, though, is definitely worth the added danger because it is absolutely stunning!

As home of the Wood Elves, the Kelethin merchants specialize in the ways of nature.  There is a bowyer and fletcher, a potions shop, and a place to buy your leather and chainmail armor that a Wood Elf would need while fighting against the evil orcs.  There is also a bank which is conveniently located so that people coming from Crushbone after a long battle can empty their inventories and cash in their gold!
