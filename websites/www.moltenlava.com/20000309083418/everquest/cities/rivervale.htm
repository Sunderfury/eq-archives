City Name:  Rivervale
Native Race:  Halflings
Guard Alignment: Good
Guild Masters: Cleric, Druid, Rogue, Warrior

Rivervale is the cozy little home of the Halflings (a.k.a. Hobbits).  All around town, you will see sheriffs of the town who act as the guards.  They will be sure that the Mayor's laws are always carried out while wearing their awesome cowboy hats and Zorro masks!

The most popular halfling in all of Rivervale is by far the Mayor.  Rumor has it that he has been elected for the past fifty years for the same position.  In fact, the people love him so much that they erected a castle in his honor.  The castle can be found directly to your left when you enter from Highpass Hold.  Within the castle is an inn, the bank, and the Mayor's quarters.  He is rarely found there, though, because he always walks around the village casting spells and greeting visitors.  Right outside the castle, you can find a market place where you can buy and sell leather armor, weapons, clothing, food, and other provisions.  The women in the town run the merchants, unlike the other towns which are all patriarchal societies.

Another landmark to look for in Rivervale is the river and all of its wonders.  The fishermen of the village (Halflings mainly eat fish because their village is found within a mountain) all work near the dock which can be found on the other side of Fool's Gold.  Jump in the river near the docks and swim to the other side to find a huge waterfall!  This is a great place to go fishing for an early attempt at making money or restocking your food supplies.

Another important part of Rivervale is the magic area.  Within the halfling-holes where all of the halflings live, you can find some that are trying to sell scrolls and other magic items to the potential Druids and Clerics of Rivervale.  All of the Cleric and Druid merchants are on the same road located to the right of Fool's Gold or straight out from the Mayor's Castle.
