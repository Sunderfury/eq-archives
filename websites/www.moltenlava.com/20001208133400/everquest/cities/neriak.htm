City Name:  Neriak
Native Race:  Dark Elves
Guard Alignment: Evil
Guild Masters: Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Rogue, Shadow Knight, Warrior, Wizard

Neriak is the evil, yet enchanted underground city of the Dark Elves.  The twisted and strange halls deeply reflect the twisted and strange ways of the Dark Elves.  In fact, the city was created for and by the Dark Elves so that no one but Dark Elves can travel through it (not that anyone could get past their guards).   Even then, they often get lost in the maze they call Neriak.

The most obvious part about Neriak is the glowing graffiti stained all over the walls.  The magical paint they used to create these effects glow and are magnified by the Ultra Vision all Dark Elves possess like a black light.  Just by taking a look across any of the walls, you can't help but notice these glowing relics of artists long dead.  They offer a real sense of history and mystery to Neriak that can only be appreciated by the Dark Elves themselves--yet they still don't know their meanings.

Neriak is by far the most eye-pleasing city.  In fact, just the joy of walking around the city could be reason enough to create a Dark Elf!  Between the graffiti mentioned before, the fires burning all around, the massive underground sea, and the golden statues conveniently placed throughout the city, Neriak is unbelievable!  In fact, it is a hell of a lot of fun just hanging out in the Wizard's guild watching the guildmaster practice his skills.  I was personally lucky enough to watch the column of ice a Wizard created in the dark halls of Neriak, but, unfortunately, the screenshot didn't capture in time!

The most important part of Neriak is the Necromancer and Shadow Knight guild hall.  It was obvious that I was heading there in my journey when I noticed some ghouls and skeletons walking around smiling warmly at me.   They considered me their friend, so they must have been the evil conjuration of another Dark Elf.  I then followed the tunnel until I found a Japanese-looking building with evil red carvings up and down the walls.  Inside, the Shadow Knight guildmaster can be found with his evil assistants selling their twisted goods.

Dark Elves, surprisingly, have a strange love of alcohol.  There must be at least three or four pubs found within this seemingly civilized city (in an evil sense).  There are also a few shops that specialize in creating magical potions to alter the strength, intelligence, resistance, and other areas of the Dark Elves.  Apparently, they specialize in creating any type of beverage.  However, the casual person cannot visit Neriak due to their evil guards.
