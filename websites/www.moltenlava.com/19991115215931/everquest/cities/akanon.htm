City Name:  Ak'anon
Native Race:  Gnomes
Guard Allignment:  Good
Guild Masters: Cleric, Enchanter, Magician, Necromancer, Rogue, Warrior, Wizard

Ak'anon is a complete city of gadgets, gears, and robots.  This is because Gnomes are adept in the skill of tinkering and inventing devices to making their lives much easier.  The city was built under the Steamfont Mountains, and they have harnessed the steam to power their gear-driven machines.

All of the buildings in Ak'anon are made of iron with strong bolts holding them together and built on the solid rock floor of the tunnels they have mined.  They haven even found a primitive way to capture and use electricity to light their city.  The Gnomes are much more advanced in technology than any of the other races.

One of the most amazing parts of Gnomish culture is the use of androids to do their mundane work like sell items and bank.  Their clockwork creatures do everything from keep the streets clean to guide lost souls.  By asking the guide (to the left) where you would like to go, just ask it and it will bring you there!  Yes, that's right, they will literally walk to the place you asked, and all you have to do is follow.   They have also created clockwork spiders to act as guards in the city, and clockwork rats to act as janitors.

Ak'anon is one of the most visually stunning cities in all of Norrath.  There are many different places where you can climb up and get a great view of the very active city beneath you.  Also, Ak'anon's building of the magical arts is the most beautiful building I have ever seen!  It glows with the yellowish-green marble, hundreds of columns, and amazing entrance.   However, due to its location, I was unable to get a screenshots of it!

Ak'anon is also one of the most diverse cities around (besides the Human cities, of course).  Since it is home of many different classes, almost anybody can find something of use in Ak'anon.  The Gnomes specialize (though not as well as the Dwarves) in all types of small armor, spells of nearly all classes, and unique clothing.  Though it is in one of the corners of Norrath and almost completely secluded, it is still well worth a visit.  In fact, I made it a point to go there almost every day for a while because it isn't very far from Crushbone (though further than both Felwithe and Kelethin).  This is because the home of the Gnomes is one of my favorite cities.
