City Name:  Kaladim
Native Race:  Dwarves
Guard Alignment:  Good
Guild Masters: Cleric, Paladin, Rogue, Warrior

Kaladim was once an underground mine created for stripping the mountain of its beautiful crystals until it finally grew into a city that could flourish on its own.  That is when old Stormhammer brought his tribe of dwarves to create a stronghold in the mountain.  It was through his leadership that he won the respect of his people, and earned the rank of King.   The people then went on to create the giant statue standing over the entrance in his honor.  The Stormhammer line has been ruling Kaladim for centuries now.

Dwarves are known for their great skill in creating armor and weapons of utmost quality.  The most famous forge in Kaladim is the Everhot forge which sells armor from chainmail to incredibly expensive Shiny Brass Plate.  The skills have been passed down from generation to generation so that this art hasn't been lost.  If you are a Barbarian looking for armor, though, you are in the wrong place because they only sell small equipment.

One of the most-used buildings in Kaladim is the bank.  This is mainly because it is located in the heart of the city--no matter where you want to go, you'll most likely pass the Bank.  However, be wary when visiting the bank since it is also the convenient location of the Rogue Guild!

Kaladim also has a very homely feel to it with simple little houses and small shops where a husband and wife will buy and sell all sorts of interesting goods.  These little buildings can be found all throughout Kaladim.  However, what Kaladim lacks, though, is any sort of magic.  Sure, it has a Cleric's guild, but a Magician or Wizard will not find any shops they will be interested in.
