Name:  Bat
Level: 0-2
Loot:  None
Experience:  Fair
Faction Change:  No
Melee Damage Max:  6
Magical Damage Max:  N/A

Bats are very easy creatures that a level character can almost always beat, regardless of class.  They are there to help improve your skills.  However, be patient when fighting bats because it will be a long battle since it is hard to hit them, and they will rarely hit you.  They make great practice, though.  If you get into trouble, you can easily run to the guards.

Name:  Deathfist Pawn
Level:  1-3
Loot: 0-5 Copper, Occasional Rusty Weapon
Experience:  Good
Faction Change:  Yes
Melee Damage Max:  10
Magical Damage Max:  N/A

Deathfist Pawns are the hardest creature you will find in East Freeport.  They usually call their friends into a battle that they may begin to lose.  They are also very quick on their feet, so don't expect to run, plus guards will not kill them.  Only fight one if you know you can win or run to the zone line.

Name:  Fire Beetle
Level:  1-4
Loot:  Fire Beetle Eye
Experience:  Good
Faction Change: No
Melee Damage Max: 9
Magical Damage Max:  N/A

Fire Beetles are a very nasty form of beetle that will put up a very tough fight for a newbie.  Usually, when one is attacked, two or three more will join in the fight.   It is best to keep a third-person view when fighting them so you can watch your back.  The fire beetle eyes sell for a silver and change, but they are more useful for assisting you to see since they make their holder glow in the dark.

Name:  Giant Rat
Level:  0-2
Loot:  Occasional Body Part
Experience: Fair
Faction Change: No
Melee Damage Max: 7
Magical Damage Max: N/A

Giant Rats are very comparable to Bats.  They usually will not attack you when they see you, but they will fight back when provoked.  They can have a fairly strong bite at times, but they will usually hit for much less.  Their body parts usually sell for around 7 copper.

Name: Stone Snake
Level:  0-2
Loot:  Fangs, Snakeskin
Experience:  Poor
Faction Change:  No
Melee Damage Max: 5
Magical Damage Max:  N/A

Stone Snakes are a fair bit easier than rats and bats, but not by much.  They are purely melee, fight to the death, and will be killed by guards.  There isn't too much more to say about them except that their body parts sell for 5-9 copper.
