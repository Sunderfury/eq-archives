City Name:  Grobb
Native Race:  Trolls
Guard Allignment:  Evil
Guild Masters: Shadow Knight, Shaman, Warrior

Grobb is what the dirty, rotten Trolls call their home.  The place is completely in shambles and is so infested that nothing other than the Trolls could ever live there.  It is no wonder that Trolls are all green and slimy!

Grobb itself is a complete swamp with small rivers flowing through it and giant willows growing in all sorts of crevices with vines crawling up them.  The rivers that run through Grobb, though, are not sanitary by even the Ogres' standards.   They are completely green, cannot be seen through, and infested with creatures that no one has ever seen before.  The rivers have sent their plague-ridden bacteria on the ground and walls around the rivers so that the entire village is green and decaying.

The few living things found in Grobb reflect the mood found within--depressing and hopeless.  That is why the only types of trees in Grobb are weeping willows.  Just by looking at the tree to the right, you can get the feeling found in Grobb with the tired, old tree hanging like that, the brownish-green hills in the background, and the purple-hued sky.  It is quite different from the happy feeling found in Rivervale!

If Grobb has anything, it has a primitive culture of some sorts.  There are a few guards found in broken platemail (no doubt created by Dwarven Slaves), walking around Grobb.  Also, here and there, a new Troll can find a gathering of Warrior Trolls surrounding a Shaman.  However, the most spectacular sight in Grobb is the torture stand where they all gather around and burn Dwarves and other captives alive.

It's quite hard to mention the different types of buildings in Grobb since they all look the same.  In fact, I'm not quite sure the Trolls even know what their role is inside those buildings.  There are a few merchants scattered around, a bartender (which can be found to the right), and a clan chieftain, which won his position by brute strength.

The chieftain's advisor, though, is a wise shaman--well, wise as far as Trolls go.  In fact, Grobb even has a bit of magic to it, believe it or not.  The Trolls draw their magic from the evil spirits of nature.   They are able to steal the strengths of the creatures (serpents, wolves, etc) and use it to their own bidding.  They even have a spell that can cause roots to grow out of their enemy's feet causing them to be stuck where they stand.  That is what the shaman above is preparing to cast.  This is why you should reconsider any plans you may have of visiting Grobb!
