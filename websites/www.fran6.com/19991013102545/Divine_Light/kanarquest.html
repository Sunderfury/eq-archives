Windaire's Quest

I went on your quest as a level 17, Barbarian, Warrior. First of all, let me say that you can not do this quest without the help of a realy good magic user and or a shaman (healer). In order to get to the cavern, you need someone that can do the water breathing spell (which you need 2 fish scales apiece for to complete the spell). Then you have to find a hole in the ice and jump in and make sure you going in the right direction to get to the cavern entrance which you can see under the ice. Just getting to the entrance is perilouse to start. At the entrance, you will meet at least 1 goblin diver, sometimes as many a 3. They weren't to bad, but fighting under water is an experience in its own right. Then the hard part begins. There is not a lot of NPC's that will attack you in there, but when you start to attack them they can do many levels of serious harm with magic. All that we found in the caverns was Ice Bone Skeletons that range from blue to red and a level 40 Necromancer who needless to say we didn't bother for fear of becoming one of his subjects, bones and all. We went down as a party of 4, a shaman, druid, warrior and myself. All have to have at least one magic weapon or use magic to kill everything except the Divers and the Necromancer.

--- Windarie
