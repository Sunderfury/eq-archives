Ranger-wearable Armor

The impromptu armor guide for Rangers

Well here's a couple points on the different types of armor listed, in order of ability to obtain.

Well you have your plain ol' banded, available from any smith.

Next we have blackened iron. This has the aperance of black chain type armor. It has the same AC as chain, but weighs more. So it's something like ring armor. You should be able to get this from the home of the goblins near Rivervale around lvl 20.

Then there's Blood Stained armor. This has the appearance of red chain. There's only the tunic and mantle(shoulders) available. The tunic is 13AC +4WIS +4AGI and the mantle is +3STR +10SV Magic and weighs about the same as banded I think. You should be able to get this in your mid 20's with a good group. It's in an area of undead on Faydwer.

Then we have rubicite. Prolly the first plate armor you'll be getting. It has the same AC as normal store bought steel plate I think, and is red in color. The brestplate has an effect called rubicite auora which is supposed to double HP regen rate. You should ba able to start getting this in your high 20's all the way up to mid 30's for the brestplate. Be prepared to deal with the crowds and Kill stealers in the area. You can find in in the home of the lizzards on antonica.

Now next we have silvered plate. Looks just like normal steel plate. So far all I know of are the bracers(7AC and I think +7SV Poision) and the greaves(stats unknown, but more ac than the rubi greaves and some + stats) I'm assumming you can get a full set of silvered plate, but have no idea. You can find it in the home of the froglocks on Antonica. And you'll need to be in your 30's to 40's to get it

Now the next stuff is vaguly heard of, and I'm guessing on a bit of it. There's a black enamelled brestplate, EQlizer states that it has 25AC, but I have seen one before, it has the appearance of black plate. Then there's blackened mail gloves(+5STA and +50mana) Now these would be great for a Ranger, with 50 more mana, that's another Ignite and a half there. I think these are supposed to show up as black, but it didn't look like it on the person I saw them on. Green splinted plate mail is prolly the best armor in the game, on one knows where it is, or what it's stats are.
