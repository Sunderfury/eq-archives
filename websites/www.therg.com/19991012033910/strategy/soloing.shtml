A soloing guide for Rangers

Well get used to traveling first off, and second you have to be able to memorize the environment. The main thing that helps me is I can learn a place and set it vaguely to memory the first time I go, and the second time I go I pretty much have it set in my memory. The first key is to know twist turn and spawn place in your surondings. That being said here is where I went and when.

14-17, 22-24: paw - I cannot remember exactly what lvls stuff was in here. I belive that at first the guards would give me exp and are fairly easy to get one at a time. There is also a shaman that spawns by himself that should give exp. There is a gap because at 22 you can pull the bosses and solo them rather than the trash types.

17-19: Lavastorm - Rock derv's are good exp, as are the lava crawlers, and drakes. Be sure to fight at the zone line though, keep your fire res spell up and save some mana to snare a wandering fire ele if they happen to float by so you can run.

19-31: Upper Guk - Probably one of the most versitile places out there. It is hard at 19, but you should be able to pull a few Tuk's solo and get good exp. Then move up to gaz, and once the gaz warriors stop attacking you can go on to the special npc locations and get exp that way. This place you REALLY have to know well though to know where the solo spawns are. If you have to fight 2 at a time, snare one and run the other back to the entrance.

25-31: Cazic Thule - Trains are real nasty but with your cammo on you should be able to pull defenders, warders, and sent's from the maze fairly easy.

30-31: Runnyeye - The warlord room is a nice place to solo and get exp and decent loot. You have to learn how to break up the spawn though. Pull two out by snareing one and fight the WL and zone. then come back and finish the trash. This might be done at 29 but I think my damage shield tiped the scales.

25-31: Najena - plenty of stuff in here to solo. TT's, Ogre's, and my favorite is BoneCracker. Just remember to keep them snared. You won't be able to do BC till lvl 30 or so, that is when you can pull him without his friends.

22-25: Nektulos forest - I like pulling the Dark elf guards and guardians to the zone to lavastorm and fighting them. Not real great exp but there is that revenge factor from all those deaths from Dvinn and Dorn. This is all I can think of right now. There is prolly more but I am sleepy and it is a monday. The limits are at 31 cuase that is where i am and i don't know if it will be easier or harder at 32.

This is mainly what I did, it might not work for everyone. Dag(soloist at heart as well)
