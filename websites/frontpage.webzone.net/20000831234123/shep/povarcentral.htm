GAME VOICE
- Sketsa @ 09:31 PM CENTRAL
Add the power of voice to your games.

Your level 50 Dark Elf just got PK'd by a backstabbing Rogue!   Have you ever wanted to tell your opponent what you really thought of his tactics?   Well, here's your chance to do it in real-time!  The SideWinder Game Voice allows you to communicate within games (over the Internet or LAN) and to take advantage of voice activation of game commands via a stereo headset connected to an eight-button control unit.  You can also choose between broadcasting your voice to the entire group or directing your communications to private team channels.  Game Voice software makes finding and connecting with your friends quick and easy, due to the integrated buddy list based on Microsoft Messenger Service technology.  While online or off, you can take advantage of Game Voice's voice command functionality.  You only need press a button and then speak commands such as "attack," "conjure," "teleport," or "heal" to respond swiftly and intuitively to any situation you might find yourself in during gameplay.

For more information on this device and to download a free, limited-feature chat-only version called Game Voice Share go to:

http://www.gamevoice.com/

Since Game Voice requires at least two players, Game Voice Share means you can always talk with teammates and opponents alike, regardless if they have the full retail version.   The downloadable Game Voice Share is a limited-feature, software-only version of the full retail product, and it requires the user to have a microphone and speakers -- or preferably a headset -- in order to use it.  Game Voice Share is free for unlimited use.
