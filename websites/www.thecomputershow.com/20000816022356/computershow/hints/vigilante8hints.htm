  1. NEWS RELEASE FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE *

    BRADYGAMES' EXCLUSIVE OFFICIAL STRATEGY GUIDE FOR VIGILANTE 8*: 2ND OFFENSE WILL HELP YOU RULE THE STREETS

    INDIANAPOLIS, Indiana - October 21, 1999 - BradyGAMES announced today that it will publish the only official strategy guide for Vigilante 8(tm): 2nd Offense. As players travel back in time they'll need all the help they can get to learn how to crush rival gangs. The strategy guide contains over 120 four-color pages of highly detailed information that's absolutely necessary to get the most out of this challenging game. Secrets, hints, maps, and everything else necessary is included in the guide to make it the most complete on the market.

    "Vigilante 8 is the latest strategy guide in support of our long-standing relationship with Activision," said Lynn Zingraf, Vice President and Publisher of BradyGAMES. "This strategy guide will help players get the most out of the game, regardless of where they choose to play it."

    Included in the Vigilante 8: 2nd Offense Official Strategy Guide are descriptions of all vehicles, including additional attributes upgrades, and power-ups earned by destroying other cars. Also listed in the guide are bios for each character as well as their special moves and fighting combinations. It's easy to get the competitive edge against rival gangs since complete information on them as well as all locations is provided. But perhaps the most important thing the guide offers is strategies and tactics for the game's numerous modes of gameplay. Arcade mode, Quest mode, Cooperative mode and Versus mode are all given thorough attention so players can be their best in each mode.

    AVAILABILITY AND PRICING Vigilante 8: 2nd Offense Official Strategy Guide (ISBN #: 1-56686-953-6) will be available wherever software and/or books are sold when the video game is released. The guide will have an MSRP of US$12.99, CDN$18.95 AND UK£11.50.

    Headquartered in Santa Monica, California, Activision, Inc. is a leading worldwide developer, publisher and distributor of interactive entertainment and leisure products. Founded in 1979, Activision posted revenues of $436 million for the fiscal year ended March 31, 1999.

    More information about Activision and its products can be found on the company's World Wide Web site, which is located at http://www.activision.com .

    For the latest demos, hints, and cheats, visit BradyGAMES at http://www.bradygames.com .
