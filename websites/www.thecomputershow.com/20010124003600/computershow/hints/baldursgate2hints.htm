Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Dual-Classing: Rangers can dual to clerics and druids can dual to fighters (and vice versa). Bards, monks, sorcerers, and paladins can't dual. Characters need at least 15 in the prime requisite(s) of your first class and at least 17 in the prime requisite(s) of your second class according to AD&D rules. Baldur's Gate 2 seems to have more stringent requirements. A Human Ranger (Stalker) can dual to a Cleric. In order to do this, the ranger must haveWisdom of18. Wisdom of 17 wouldn't dual class for some reason. A Kensai/Mage has to have an intelligence of 18 to dual class from the Kensai to the Mage. Intelligence of 17 didn't work for either. Many characters can only dual class after switching to the 18 on widom & intelligence.
  2. Character interaction: Viconia and Edwin will fight and insult one another. Viconia and Keldorn will fight for real and Keldorn will kill Viconia eventually if left in the same party long enough.
