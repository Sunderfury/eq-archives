688(I) Hunter Killer Hints

    A military simulation with the complexity of 688(I) Hunter/Killer is difficult to defeat without inside information. That's why we created the official Jane's Player's Guide For 688i Hunter/Killer. The Player's Guide is packed with must-know intelligence based on the simulation's technology and presented in a clear, concise manner. It's got the practical information you need to successfully complete missions on every level:

    Strategies and Tactics: Actual U.S. Navy tactics for sensing, tracking targets, maneuvering, hiding and attacking. Game Mechanics: Practical information available only from the designers of 688(I) Hunter/Killer, such as vessel and weapons performance, damage effects, scoring, and AI tendencies.

    Mission Walkthroughs: Point-by-point walkthroughs of every mission in the game, with detailed maps showing the locations, headings, speeds, depths, tactics, and proficiency levels of all friendly and enemy ships, subs and aircraft.

    Interviews: Fascinating insight into the minds of 688i's creators.
