    Prima and SquareSoft Release Exclusive Strategy Guide for Final Fantasy Tactics

    Prima Entertainment, the world's leading strategy guide publisher, announced it is shipping the exclusive official strategy guide for Final Fantasy Tactics. Final Fantasy Tactics: Prima's Official Strategy Guide will be on the bookshelves just prior to the release of the game itself slated for January 30th.

    Final Fantasy Tactics is being released on the heels of the unprecedented success generated by SquareSoft's Final Fantasy VII. The game is a unique blend of game genres, combining the style of Final Fantasy VII with the tactics of a sophisticated strategy game. The result is a highly captivating game-play experience. Considered by some to be the biggest role-playing game of 1998, Final Fantasy Tactics is highly anticipated by Final Fantasy fans.

    "We are excited to be publishing the exclusive official strategy guide to SquareSoft's next Final Fantasy product. To capture the unique Final Fantasy universe, Prima's creative team went to great length to produce a strategy guide that combines clarity and completeness with great aesthetic and visual qualities. We believe that we have been able to capture the mystique and intrigue of the Final Fantasy world in an entirely novel way," said Debra Kempker, publisher of Prima Entertainment.

    Prima Entertainment is the world's leading publisher of strategy guides for computer and console games, now with more than twelve million strategy guides in print. Renowned for its consistently high quality, The Rocklin, Calif.-based company has captured the hearts and minds of game players as "The Most Trusted Name in Games™" and continues to dominate the field it created in 1990. Founded in 1984, Prima Publishing, the parent of Prima Entertainment, has three divisions including Entertainment, Lifestyles and Computers & Technology, and offices in the United States and United Kingdom.

    For more information on Prima's Final Fantasy Tactics strategy guide, visit Prima's web site at www.primagames.com.
