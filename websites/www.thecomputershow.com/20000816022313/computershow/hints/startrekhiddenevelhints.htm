    Strategy Guides

    BEAM UP BRADYGAMES' EXCLUSIVE OFFICIAL STRATEGY GUIDE FOR STAR TREK® HIDDEN EVIL AND SAVE THE UNIVERSE ONCE AGAIN

    INDIANAPOLIS, Indiana - November 3, 1999 - BradyGAMES announced today that it will publish the only official strategy guide for the latest computer game in the Star Trek series - Star Trek® Hidden Evil. Trekkers will savor over 200 pages of information in the guide that helps them unravel the mystery surrounding the Ba'ku and their home planet. Gamers who are unfamiliar with Star Trek will embrace this strategy guide as a reference tool to help familiarize them with the various characters, equipment, and other intricacies.

    "Our strategy guide for Star Trek Hidden Evil is the latest of several Star Trek strategy guides from BradyGAMES," said Lynn Zingraf, vice-president and publisher of BradyGAMES. "Star Trek is definitely one of the biggest, if not the biggest, science fiction sagas ever created. We're delighted to provide gamers, whether they're old or new fans of Star Trek, with everything they need to get the most out this game in terms of their score and their enjoyment of the game."

    Saving the universe is no easy task. That is, unless you have the Star Trek Hidden Evil Official Strategy Guide from BradyGAMES. The guide includes a complete walkthrough of the game along with detailed mission objectives, in-depth character biographies, item lists, tools, and Star Trek terminology. Solutions to every puzzle are provided as well as descriptions on how to effectively use tricoders, phasers, communicators, and the "Vulcan nerve pinch." Unless you have the brain capacity of Data, you'd better get the strategy guide to help navigate your way to victory. As Captain Picard would say, "Make it so." AVAILABILITY AND PRICING Star Trek Hidden Evil Official Strategy Guide (ISBN #: 1-56686-951-X) will be available wherever software and/or books are sold when the computer game is released.
