Summary / Solution / Truth

As I return from a convention and cruising the parties, I'm reminded just how painful I find it to be alone.

During the evening I was given a reminder from a drunken philosopher named "Kirk". He introduced himself and simply said "Stop watching. Enjoy yourself." and moved on.

Standing, watching, soloing as it were, I ponder the similarities in life.

EQ helps us break down the barriers that we build up in the real world and may have difficulty overcoming.

EQ is a fantasy. The people there are also a fantasy. However the people, and feelings, behind the characters are very very real.

Remember, EQ can never replace what your missing in life, but it can help you to understand what your really missing.

I just hope you have more luck finding what it is that your missing than I.
