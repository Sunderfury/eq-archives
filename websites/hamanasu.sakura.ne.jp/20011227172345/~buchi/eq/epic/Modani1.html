〜 Modani Qu'Loniの章 : Chardokの皇子 〜
Written Date: 2000/11/17

目的:Chalice of Kingsを受け取る

FelwitheのJoren Nobleheartに、ChardokのPrince Selrach Di'Zocから
入手できるthe Head of a Princeを渡す




しかし、初っ端からとんでもないものを要求してきますねぇ(^^;

コレの為に行った訳ではないのですが、GuildでChardok行ってきました。
KoroとPrinceとQueenのDropが目的だったみたいですが。

PrinceRoom、皇子様の護衛に8匹付きだったそうで（苦笑
QueenRoom前の雑魚をPullすれば、環状通路を一周して帰って行くし。
なんというか、かなり鬼なDungeonでした。
LV59とLV56ENCが居れば、４匹くらいまでは捌けるみたいでしたけど(^^;

さて、無事Princeを殺して頭をGetできたなら、Jorenに渡すことによって

これが貰えます。右手専用です。


Joren Nobleheart Log

You say, 'hail Joren Nobleheart'
Joren Nobleheart says 'I hope you have been sent by the King. There is an urgent matter at hand.

You say, 'I have been sent by the King'
Joren Nobleheart says 'Our sources at the outpost have learned that the Chardokian royal family
had a part in the disappearance of Firiona Vie.
I have been ordered by King Tearis Thex to locate individuals familiar with the new lands
and to order them to the gates of Chardok Mountain. Do you accept the king's order?

You say, 'I will accept the king s order'
Joren Nobleheart says 'Gather your forces and march on the gates of Chardok.
You are to strike deep into the home of Overking Bathezid.
Your goal is not the overking himself, but his only living heir.
His son's head will be the price to pay for their envolvement in this kidnapping.

[Head of a Prince]を渡します
Joren Nobleheart says 'Vengeance has been fulfilled. However, the King still has a heavy heart.
Not until Firiona has been returned can this sadness be lifted.
But in appreciation of your efforts.
I am honored to present to you the King's Chalice.
You gain party experience!!
[Chalice of Kings]を受け取ります
