Event Information
 Date: Monday, November 05, 2001 At 7:00:00 PM
 Zone: Plane of Hate
 Organiser: Swysh
 O'McSwyshy had a farm ee ii ee ii oo - Armour Farm
 Description:
 This is an armour farming event for those who want to get armour, i am also opening addmissions to UK people as well.

The intention is to get Hate armour for the current batch of people who need it, if successful im gonna try and run this as a semi regular event.

At this point i am considering just 4 groups, this is subject to change.

The rules:

Level 47+ only.
All armour will be rolled equally, people with an armour bit drop out until everyone has an item or you are the only person who can use it after everyone else.
Last piece rule is in effect, you must be able to prove this to me on request.

Exception: The Shaman Breastplate, if it drops, is defaulted to Talili in compensation for the one that rotted when she was up before.

50pp will be needed for hate stones and a minimum of 10 peridots.
All other items will be rolled as per ability to use.

Plannar Gems: Puller gets gem of choice, i am hoping to get a good puller, i am also hoping Siixie will be up for learning to pull hate.

All other gems will be zone lottoed.

I am not intending to interact with epic mobs on this visit, do not come expecting to get bits from the hand or other mobs.

Swysh Lizzychops, Legionaire - Cabilis expeditionary forces
