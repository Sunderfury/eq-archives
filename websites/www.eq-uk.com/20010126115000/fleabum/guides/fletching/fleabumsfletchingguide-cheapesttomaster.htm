Cheapest Guide To Master

Here you go, just as I was asked, this is the cheapest, no frills guide to get to grandmaster fletcher. This is way I got to grandmaster fletching in 6 or 7 hours with about 1000pp. Note this was done on a Trollie Shamaness with 140 wisdom and 100 intelligence. Your experiences may differ, but that's not my problem ;)

Firstly you will need a Fletching Kit and the Guide to Fletching (not required but might get better results with it so its worth the few gold to buy it). Next you'll need the components of the arrows you are going to make.

I suggest you DON'T put 20 skill into this at the guild, mainly because the low level skill gains are fast, and it doesn't cost much if you fail.

The cost in the table will vary for you, this is due to various factors, like charisma, faction, race. So treat the cost as an approx. value. Also note that each try for an arrow, creates 5 arrows (Silver Tipped create 10).
