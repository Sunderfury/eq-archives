Reinforced Leather

As reinforced armour size is based upon the pelt used, see the Other Items page for an explanation. Reinforced armour is the the last of the armours that require blacksmith items to make, but it produces good quality armour. All reinforced armour requires one high quality pelt, a number of bonings and the relative pattern to make.
