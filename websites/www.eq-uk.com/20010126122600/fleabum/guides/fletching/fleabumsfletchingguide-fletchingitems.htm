Fletching Items

In this section I will cover various things, like quivers, bows, arrows and fletching kits.

Arrows

Arrows are made from various components. For each arrow you need 4 items, an arrowhead, shaft, flight and nock.


Bows

Bows are made from various components. The bows you create require a bow stave, and string. Optional extras are cams, planing tool, and whiting knife.


Fletching Kit

This is the most used item in fletching, well it would be, you can't fletch without it ;). Can be bought from most of the bow vendors.


Quiver


Quivers at this time are pretty useless. They hold 4 slots of arrows and automatically feed the ammo slot. The problem is that backpacks also feed the same ammo slot and hold 8 slots of arrows. So save yourself a wad of cash, buy a backpack instead. Maybe if Verant make quivers go into the ammo slot, they would become viable, but as usual Verant don't have a clue ;)
