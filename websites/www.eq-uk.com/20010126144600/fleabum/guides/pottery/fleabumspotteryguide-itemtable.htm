Item Table

Pottery is produced in two basic stages which are :-

(1) In the first stage you combine the appropriate sketch and a vial of water with clay and other materials on a pottery wheel.

(2) The if you succeed making the item, you then take the result and combine it with firing sheets in the kiln to make a finished product.

A good guide to remember this by is, firing sheets are the only non made ingredient to go in the kiln and everything else goes in the pottery wheel.
