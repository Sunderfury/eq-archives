Raw Silk

Raw silk armour is created by combining a number of Silk Swatches with the relative pattern. The raw silk armour is good for the caster types as its light (0.4) and relativity cheap (2-4pp). The armour is useable by Enchanters, Mages, Monks, Necromancers, and Wizards of any race.
