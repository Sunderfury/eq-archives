Studded Leather

As studded armour size is based upon the pelt used, see the Other Items page for an explanation. Studded armour is the the first of the armours that require blacksmith items to make, but it produces medium quality armour. All studded armour requires one medium quality pelt, a number of studs and the relative pattern to make.
