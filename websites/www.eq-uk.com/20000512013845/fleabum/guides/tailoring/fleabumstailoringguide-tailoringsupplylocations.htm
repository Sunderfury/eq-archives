Fleabums Tailoring Guide

This guide has been developed from my experiences with the trade skill of tailoring. If you find any mistakes or have anything to add then mail me fleabum@hotmail.com and I will get it updated.

Tailoring Supply Locations

Ak'Anon

  • small sewing kit - clockwork tailor at -1312, -915

Butcherblock

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), How to Sew (3) - Alga Bruntbuckler (-1183, -1951)
  • patterns, small sewing kit, How to Sew (small) - Fugan Mumfur (-1946, 2878: south of docks, on edge of water)

East Commonlands

  • sm sewing kit - tailor in each of the 3 inns on the main path out from Freeport to W. Commonlands and in the inn near the orc camp

East Karana

  • small sewing kit, How to Sew (medium sizes) - Merchant Silvia (shop at 1315, -2547)

Erudin

  • patterns, sm. sewing kit, How To Sew (med)- Demicla Tanner (street vendor)
  • sm. sewing kit, How To Sew (med) - Galbasi Weaver, Monita Weaver (street vendors)

Everfrost

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), - Seria O'Danos (in cave between Everfrost and Halas)

Feerrott

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), How to Sew (3), Tailoring book - Murga (1139, -134)

Felwithe

  • patterns, sm. sewing kit - Merchant Tissan (Faydark's Bane, N. Felwithe)
  • sm. sewing kit - Merchant Silvenspin (Shop of All Holds, N. Felwithe), Merchant Moonthread (building just inside entrance to S. Felwithe)

Freeport

  • patterns - Winlar Tanner, Sanhan Tanner, Sissin Tanner, Winsa Tanner (Leather & Hide, E. Freeport) Note: Winsa also seems to carry ruined, med. quality and high quality cat pelts.
  • small sewing kit - Svinal Wyspin & Lystin Wyspin (The Emporium, N. Freeport), Wahnig Nooz & Jallen Nooz (unlabeled building South of bank, N. Freeport)

Grobb

Halas

  • small sewing kit - Greta Terrilon (street merchant near MacPherson's Bloody Blades)
  • small sewing kit, How To Sew (Med/Large Sizes) - Mac (Macs Kilts)

Highpass Hold

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), Tailoring book, How to Sew (3)- Dyona Rossook (Serpent Supply)

Kaladim

  • small sewing kit - Gurtha Yaptongue (Gurtha's Ware, S. Kaladim)

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), Tailoring book, How to Sew (3) - Merchant Gaeadin (-116, -130)
  • How to Sew Medium Sizes, small sewing kit - Merchant Linadiane (-142, -545)

Kithicor Forest

  • patterns, small sewing kit, Tailoring book - Lena Leatherspinner (-325, 3879)

Misty Thicket

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wristband and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2) - Tipa Lighten, /loc 581.00, -2201.00
  • small sewing kit, leather armor patterns - Sonsa Fromp, /loc 672.00, -2210. NOTE: only folks who can't get past the guard and into Rivervale (i.e., ogres, trolls, dark elves) should buy from Sonsa. Her prices are considerably higher than those in town.

West Commonlands

West Karana

  • large sewing kit, mask, gorget, belt, cloak, wrist, and shoulderpad patterns, backpack, whip & quiver patterns (2), How to Sew (3) - Alysa (425, -4362)
  • small sewing kit, How to Sew (Medium) - Silna Weaver OR Minya Coldtoes (847, -1824; NW corner of the zone, a tent behind the buildings) -- these women seem to take turns manning the booth, and sometimes nobody will be there.
