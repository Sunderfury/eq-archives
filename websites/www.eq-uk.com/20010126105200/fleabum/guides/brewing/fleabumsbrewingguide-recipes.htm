To actually make the drink, you put the ingredients into the brew barrel and click on the combine button. If you succeed you will get the finished brew, otherwise you may get a message saying that you made an alternative item, and get some of your ingredients back. Some of the recipes make more than one of the item, Ale for example makes three from one combine, look on the Trivial Table for how many of each item are made.
