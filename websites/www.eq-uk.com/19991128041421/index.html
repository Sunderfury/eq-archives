 Paw to be upped! Server Update!
23 November 99 04:50 PM [PST] - Khelor
November 23rd, 10:00am
----------------------

In observation of the holidays, the Verant Technical Support department will be closed on Thursday the 25th, and Friday the 26th. Customer Service (Game Masters) will be lightly staffed on Thursday the 25th as well.

*Paw Update*

Within the next two weeks, the Paw zone will become a mid-level dungeon. Any characters under level 25 that are bound to Paw may want to bind themselves to an area outside. No GM assistance will be given to low level players bound to Paw after the transformation takes place.

*Scheduled Server Update*

The EverQuest Servers will be coming down on Wednesday, December the 1st, for a scheduled update. The estimated downtime is approximately 6 hours.

- The EverQuest Team
