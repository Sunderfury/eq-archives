Handmade Backpack (90)
   1 High Quality Bear Skin
   1 Backpack pattern
Note that these are trivial at 90; attempting to make these with a much lower skill will result in a high failure rate.

Wu's Fighting Gauntlets (88)
   1 Glove pattern
   1 Greater Lightstone
   1 Vial of Viscous Mana
   1 Silk Swatch
   4 Heady Kiola
Vial of Viscous mana can be made by an enchanter of level 12 or higher. It requires 1 Pearl and 1 Poison Vial. The poison vial can be made by a potter and is trivial for potters at a skill of 148. Use regular (not lined) poison vial.
