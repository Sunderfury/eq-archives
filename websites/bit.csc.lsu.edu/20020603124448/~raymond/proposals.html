Falstaff Spacesuit Fantasy Football League

Proposed Rule Changes

(Compiled from various sources)

Division Names
Add Section 1.03 to read:
Teams in their corresponding divisions will decide on their division name.

Incomplete Drafts
Add Section 3.04.a to read:
In the event that a team does not complete their drafting of players by the deadline for free trades on draft day (see section 5.03.b ), they will be charged one move for each unfilled roster position in order to complete their draft.

Team Names
Add Section 3.06 with subparagrahps to read:

Section 3.06 Each team must present the Commissioner with a team name prior to the draft in draft order.

Section 3.06.a Teams may register a team name with the commissioner at any time prior to the draft. Registration of a team name reserves the name for that owner so that other owners may not use part of that team name for their team name.

Section 3.06.b Team names may not be related to any previous team name.

Section 3.06.b.i Teams may not have team names related to another team of the current season.

Adds and Drops
Change Section 5.04 to read:
(a) There will be a primary and secondary deadline for dropping and adding players.
     (i) The Primary deadline will be the first deadline of the week. All add/drop requests will be
     put into a holding bin by the commissioner until this primary deadline.
          (a) The primary deadline will be midnight Wednesday.
     (ii) The Secondary deadline will follow the primary deadline. All add/drop requests will be
     processed immediately on a first come-first served basis.
          (a) The secondary deadline will be midnight the night before kickoff of the first NFL game.
(b) Teams may only add players from the available pool of players. A player being dropped does not become part of the player pool until the following NFL week.
(c) When requesting a move to drop/add players prior to the primary deadline, a team may include alternate picks in the event that another team picks up their selection.
(d) In the event that two separate teams request to add the same player to their rosters, the team with the worst record shall be awarded that player.
     (i) Tiebreaker will be the least points scored.
     (ii) Second tiebreaker will be least total points scored.
     (iii) Third tiebreaker will be the team with the highest standard deviation of total points scored.
(e) Teams may continue to drop and add players until the end of the season.
(f) Any player dropped after the trading deadline will not be put into the available player pool.
     (i) The commissioner must maintain a list of players ineligible for pickup.

Scoring Changes
Change section 6.03.h to 2 points per interception.
Change section 6.03.i to 2 points per fumble recovery.
Add section 6.03.j to read: 1 point per sack.
Add section 6.03.k to read: 7 points per shutout.
Change section 6.04.a.i to 2 additional points instead of 1.
Change section 6.04.b to read: 2 additional points instead of 1.
Change section 6.04.c.i to 2 additional points instead of 1.
