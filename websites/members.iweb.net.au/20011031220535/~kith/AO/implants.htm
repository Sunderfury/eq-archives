Implants and Nano Clusters

Implants and the nano clusters that are installed in them offer a great way to customise the skills of your character. They will become essential at higher levels for the extra skills and abilities they provide. This document is a repositry for knowledge about implants and nano clusters as I research it.

Feel free to send me updates or corrections to the guide at cxf@dingoblue.net.au

In Game Books

There are at least two books that cna be purchased in-game that describe inplants and nano clusters. The introductory book, Implant Creation Introduction Manual can be found on the implant vendor in general stores. It makes reference to another book which I have not been able to find.

Implants

Implants are pieces of hardware installed into various parts of the body. There are implants for the eye, ear, head, right/left arm, right/left wrist, right/left hand, chest, waist, legs and feet. Implants are not much use unless they actually do something. For implants to be effective you must install nano clusters into them.

Nano Clusters

A Nano Cluster is a small collection of nanos that can be installed into an implant. Once installed the nanos go into action and impart various benifits to the user. These benifits include skill increases, ability increases and Armour Class increases. There are three levels of nano clusters; Faded, Bright and Shining.

These are the cheapest version of clusters. They typically cost between 350 and 950 credits and offer the lowest increases.

Nano Programming (Installing Nano clusters)

To install a nano cluster simply hold the cluster on the mouse and right click it on the desired implant. There are a few things to know about installing clusters.

  1. To insert a Nano Cluster into an implant of quality N the cluster must be of quality N-1or greater. Thus only nano clusters of quality 9 or more can be installed into an implant of quality 10.
  2. The result of the combination will have the quality of the lowest of the two. Thus if the implant is quality 20 and the cluster is quality 40 then the resulting implant will be quality 20 and the cluster will impart benifits of a quality 20 cluster.
  3. All implants can contain three nano clusters; one Faded cluster, one Bright cluster and one Shining cluster. You can view the clusters installed in an implant by shift clicking the implant.
On top of those restrictions installing nano clusters into implants makes use of the Nano Programming skill. The higher the cluster/implant qualities the higher the skill required to install the cluster into the implant. One very useful thing to know about the implant requirements is that they only need to be met at the time the implant is installed. Once its installed you can fall below the requirements without adverse effects. This means you can use buffs, tutoring or other means to get your skills up immediately before installing the implant.

If your treatment skill is greater or equal to the treatment skill of the implant then it can be installed, or removed, without any assistance (NOTE: this is actually a bug. Lately I have attempted this to get a message "This operation requires technical supervision..."). Simply drop the implant into the appropriate slot and it will be installed. If your treatment skill is less then the treatment requirement of the implant then you will need to make use of a surgery clinic.

  • Fixed Surgery Clinic: This is a robot controlled surgery clinic that can be found in general stores and in backyards. It costs 300 credits to use and you get 30 seconds in which to install all of your implants. One benifit to using a robot clinic is that it boosts your treatment skill by 100, making it easier for you to meet the install requirements.
  • Portable Surgery Clinic: You can use a portable surgery clinic to install implants. These clinics are purchsed from general stores and you can get them in various qualities.
