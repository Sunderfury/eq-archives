Woodsman's Armor

- 対象レベルは40前後

Shadow of Luclin の Twilight Sea のKatta ゾーン近くの小島に Jern's Rest という名の宿屋があります。そこにいる Alton Daleson から ranger 装備について話を聞くことが出来ます。

You say, 'Hail, Alton Daleson'
Alton Daleson says 'Welcome to Jern's Rest! I'm the owner and proprietor of this inn, its been in my family for generations. My great grandaddy, Jern, built it back when Katta Castellum was first being built. If ya be wantin' of anything dont hesitate to ask.'
You say, 'What armor?'
Alton Daleson says 'His armor has also been in the family for generations, if ye be a ranger I dont have much use for it running this inn. Ill just need something in return for it.
You say, 'I am a ranger'
Alton Daleson says 'Excellent! If you are interested I have the Woodsman's Helm, Woodsman's Mail, Woodsman's Vambraces, Woodsman's Greaves, Woodsman's Bracer, Woodsman's Boots, and the Woodsman's Axe. If you are interested in the rest of his armor youll have to ask my wife Ellen, shell have some errands you can run for her.
You say, 'What helm?'
Alton Daleson says 'For the Woodsman's Helm you will have to acquire these items a cloud jewel, a mark of leaves, a frost covered leaf, and some sea grass.
You say, 'What mail?'
Alton Daleson says 'To get the Woodsman's Mail you will have to acquire these items a sky jewel, a mark of forests, a frozen vial, and a tro jeg brain.
You say, 'What vambraces?'
Alton Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Vambraces you will have to acquire these items a meteor jewel, a mark of mountains, a tro jeg eye, and a solstice rune.
You say, 'What greaves?'
Alton Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Greaves you will have to acquire these items an astral jewel, a mark of oceans, a pouch of fire stones, and a cat skin pouch.
You say, 'What bracer?'
Alton Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Bracer you will have to acquire these items a moon jewel, a mark of lakes, and some lightning dust.
You say, 'What boots?'
Alton Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Boots you will have to acquire these items a star jewel, a mark of timber, and a gilded steel rod.
You say, 'What axe?'
Alton Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Axe you will have to acquire these items a sun jewel, a mark of the naturalist, some ancient pottery, and a fossillized skull.


また、この宿屋を出て北側の桟橋に Alton の妻、Ellen Daleson からも ranger 装備について話を聞くことが出来ます。

You say, 'Hail, Ellen Daleson'
Ellen Daleson says 'Oh hello there, I'm in charge of the docks here and wife to Alton.'
You say, 'What armor?'
Ellen Daleson says 'Oh you wish to get the armor that Alton's great granddaddy used to wear. If you are a ranger just let me know.
You say, 'I am a ranger'
Ellen Daleson says 'Very well if you want the Woodsman's Mantle, Woodsman's Mask, Woodsman's Cape, Woodsman's Gauntlets, Woodsman's Choker, or Woodsman's Belt. I have some errands that need to be done.
You say, 'What mantle?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Mantle bring me a sun jewel, a mark of rivers, and a temporal sack.
You say, 'What mask?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Mask bring me a cloud jewel, a mark of stone, and a platinum chain.
You say, 'What cape?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Cape bring me a sky jewel, a mark of creatures, a wondrous stone, and a marble statuette.
You say, 'What gauntlets?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Gauntlets bring me a meteor jewel, a mark of animals, and a tattered old card.
You say, 'What choker?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Choker bring me an astral jewel, a mark of ambidexterity, and a small ancient sculpture.
You say, 'What belt?'
Ellen Daleson says 'If you want the Woodsman's Belt bring me a moon jewel, a mark of the environment, a gold pointer, and a broken ancient blade.
