Runed Scout's Armor   → 性能比較表
Quest NPC  : Argash
Faction Needed  : Coldain, Kindly +
Factions Raised  : Coldain, DainFrostreaverIV
Factions Lowered  : Kromzek, Kromrif
まずは Corroded Chain と呼ばれる Core Parts とそれを作るための Gem を手に入れる必要があります。Corroded Chain は Kael Drakkal で入手することが出来ます。基本的に 以下の Kromzek faction に所属している gaint から入手できます。
  • Lieutenant （Lv47） - bank 周辺の Watchman の rare pop。
  • Trooper （Lv50-54） - king royal 周辺や arena 周辺に pop。warrior type。
  • Legionnaire （Lv51-55） - king royal 周辺や arena 周辺に pop。warrior type。
  • Adjutant （Lv53） - king royal 周辺や arena 周辺に pop。cleric type。
  • Veteran （Lv57） - king royal 周辺や arena 周辺に pop。warrior type。
  • temple guardian （Lv60） - arena 奥の Derakor the Vindicator の護衛。
  • Armor of Zek （Lv55） - arena 奥の Statue of Rallos Zek の護衛。
  • guardian of Zek （Lv66） - arena 奥の Rolls Zek temple に pop。
  • Sentinel （Lv57, Lv66） - king royal 周辺に pop。
基本的にこれらの giant から drop します。Kromrif にも同じ surname を持つものもいますが Kromrif 所属の giant からの drop はありません。また、Kromzek 所属でも、Watchman、Sergeant、Guard、Senior Guard など giant からの drop もありません。高レベル giant ほど drop 率、rare parts （BP など） を落とす確率が高くなります。

Gem の組み合わせ、また入手先情報はこのページの下かこちらを参照してください。

You say, 'hail Argash'

Argash says 'Greetin's, Milky , you look like a Wood Elf who knows the value of a good bow when ye see one. I'll let ye in on a li'l secret, the bow is the greatest of all weapons as it allows you to inflict maximum punishment on yer foes with minimum risk. How can ye possibly go wrong? Or better yet, if'n ye be a ranger, I can see my way clear to making you a coif, tunic, sleeves, bracer, gauntlets, leggings, or boots that I'm sure ye would enjoy.

You say, 'What coif?'

Argash says 'To create a piece of armor to protect your sensitive skull, I will require three pieces of crushed coral as well as a corroded chain coif.

You say, 'What tunic?'

Argash says 'To forge a spectacular chain tunic I will require three flawless diamonds and a corroded chain tunic. With the gems and my skills I may be able to draw out some of its magical powers.

You say, 'What sleeves?'

Argash says 'Protection for your arms will come at the price of three flawed emeralds and a set of corroded chain sleeves.

You say, 'What bracer?'

Argash says 'For the bracers, I will require a corroded chain bracer as well as three crushed flame emeralds. Return to me when you find such things Wood Elf.

You say, 'What gauntlets?'

Argash says 'Protecting your hands is very important. I can forge protection for your hands if you bring me a pair of corroded chain gauntlets and three crushed topaz.

You say, 'What leggings?'

Argash says 'A set of corroded chain leggings might be salvageable if you were to find three flawed sea sapphires. With the right techniques and ingredients almost anything is possible.

You say, 'Whar boots?'

Argash says 'We use our feet so often, should not they be protected as well as any other portion of our bodies? If you seek fine protection for your feet, return to me with three pieces of crushed black marble and a set of corroded chain boots.


Forest Stalker's Armor   → 性能比較表
Quest NPC  : Gragek Mjlorkigar
Faction Needed  : Kromzek, Ally +
Factions Raised  : Kromzek, Kromrif, KingTormax
Factions Lowered  : ClawsofVeeshan
まず Ancient Tarnished Chain と呼ばれる core parts とそれを作るための Gem を手に入れる必要があります。Ancient Tarnished Chain は Temple of Veeshan の west、north area の Lv60 以上の named 以外の敵が drop します。west area の敵は ClawsofVeeshan 所属、north area の敵は一部を除き GuardiansofVeeshan のため、すべての人が KoS になっています。

Gem の組み合わせ、また入手先情報はこのページの下かこちらを参照してください。

You say, 'hail Gragek Mjlorkigar'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'Greetings to you. I seek one who is a protector of nature. A ranger is what you call them I believe. Are you a ranger?

You say, 'I am a ranger'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'I have not seen your kind in quite some time. Do you seek aid in your never-ending fight against those who do nature harm? There is no need to answer for I see within your eyes, the commitment and the conviction. None the less, the tasks I set before you shall be none the easier. Are you ready to submit to my will?

You say, 'I am ready to submit to your will'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'That is good for there is nothing like the wrath of nature, woodlands warrior. And Natures wrath shall you be, with this armor to protect you in your struggle. I shall forge a coif, breastplate, sleeves, bracers, gauntlets, leggings, and boots for you.

You say, 'What coif?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'This coif shall protect you from battle and the elements. What I require of you is this. Three crushed coral pieces and a ancient tarnished chain coif.

You say, 'What breastplate?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'Even as the hardest tree in the forest requires protection, so shall this breastplate protect you. Bring these components for me, an ancient tarnished chain tunic as well as three flawless diamonds.

You say, 'What sleeves?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'I require an ancient tarnished chain sleeves and three flawed emeralds. Do this so that I may grant you these special sleeves as a gift for your diligence.

You say, 'What bracers?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'This shall help shield your forearms from any stray blows. Bring me an ancient tarnished chain wristguard and a trilogy of crushed flame emeralds.

You say, 'What gauntlets?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'Bring me these items, for I will not grant you your pair until then. A pair of an ancient tarnished chain gauntlets as well as three crushed topaz.

You say, 'What leggings?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'A pair of leggings you seek? I seek something as well. Bring me a pair of an ancient tarnished chain leggings and three flawed sea sapphires. Go now. The struggle continues as we speak.

You say, 'What boots?'

Gragek Mjlorkigar says 'For the boots, I shall require a pair of an ancient tarnished chain boots and three crushed pieces of black marble.
