SWEET TOOTH IS BACK AND BADDER THAN EVER IN ALL-NEW TWISTED METAL 4
-- 989 Studios’ Next Installment In Multi-Million Selling Twisted Metal Series Takes Automotive Mayhem To New Extremes --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 13, 1999 – 989 Studios‘ announced today that Twisted Metal® 4, the latest installment in the multi-million selling Twisted Metal“ series, is scheduled for release this November on the PlayStation“ game console. Twisted Metal 4 will blaze onto the vehicular combat scene with all-new demolition derby-style gameplay action.

"The fourth installment in the series that created vehicular combat, Twisted Metal 4, expands on all of the breakthrough features that have made the Twisted Metal series legendary in the car combat genre," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "With all-new twisted characters, sprawling mutli-level game environments, advanced gameplay features and an expanded presence by Sweet Tooth himself, Twisted Metal 4 will be an explosive addition to the impressive Twisted Metal franchise."

Players crash through explosive gameplay environments using a host of all-new revved-up vehicles. Each car has a unique weapon and special attack that can be customized. Equipped with heavily-armored vehicles, players must patrol and explore each of the vast 3D environments destroying anything that crosses their path. Using advanced gameplay physics, Twisted Metal 4 allows players to race up a ramp, land a death-defying jump and sideswipe other cars while on two wheels. Bizarre and twisted weapons help the players reach their goal.

Twisted Metal 4 also features a new theme, updated vehicles, deadly new weapons and different battlegrounds. In addition, new combo moves are faster and easier to use and the environments are more interactive than in the previous incarnations.

Twisted Metal 4 Key Features

  • New gameplay features include: unique storyline, battlegrounds, weapons, vehicles and characters
  • Unique and larger level designs to explore
  • Improved graphics and vehicle physics
  • Blazing fast gameplay rendering at 30 frames per second
  • Lots of hidden characters and vehicles
  • Total game customization
  • Multiplayer madness with Multi-Tap support
  • Optional Death Match "Only" Levels, made especially for wide-open homicide at full throttle
  • Supports Analog and Dual Shock‘ gamepads
