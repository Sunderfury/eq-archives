This year 989 Sports creates an all new 3D Game Engine that delivers incredibly detailed player models that include actual sizes and faces of all of your favorite NHL® players. Add in real NHL® arenas that come to life in amazing architectural detail, and you’re playing one of the most realistic hockey games ever to grace the PlayStation.
With the motion capture of former NHL® goalie Kelly Hrudey, you can make every Goalie Save possible including: Glove, Stick, Blocker, Kick, Butterfly and Stack the Pads. You can even Leave the Crease to chase down loose pucks. Having motion capture of NHL® forwards including Scott Young you can skate Backwards, Forwards or go for the Lean Turn or Crossover. Speed down the ice and stop on a dime. Your arsenal includes every Check imaginable along with the Drop Pass, Give-and-Go, One-Timer, and of course the big Slap Shot.

The Broadcast tandem of New Jersey Devils’ Broadcaster Mike Emrick and ESPN2/Hockey Guru Darren Pang provide fast paced play-by-play and insightful color commentary. With amazing Camera Zoom Ins and Cut Aways we bring you right down to center ice. Get Up Close and Personal with Hip Checks that rock the boards. Gaze up at the arena’s rafters and see the championship banners from each individual team.
The best Hockey Artificial Intelligence has players and teams executing and reacting On-the-Fly just they way they do in the NHL®. All new Icon Switching™ on defense and improved Icon Passing™ place control of the NHL®’s most lethal lines in the palm of your hand. Deke your opponent with the Fake-Slapshot or go Top Shelf with a Back Hander. It’s up to you!
The players’ choice is clear and it is NHL FaceOff ’99. From Darren McCarty to Chris Draper, from Olaf Kolzig to Joe Reekie the realism and the heart and soul of the NHL is captured for all to see. It’s ready to be unleashed when you play NHL FaceOff ’99.
The most updated Home, Road and Alternate 3rd jerseys are available for all of the NHL® players and teams including the expansion Nashville Predators and International Teams. We’ve got all the rookies and free agent signings as well. Suit up your team, grab your authentic goalie mask and battle for authentic NHL® Awards including the Conn Smyth, Hart Memorial, Presidents and Vezina trophies.
