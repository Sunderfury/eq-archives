989 STUDIOS’ HIGHLY-ANTICIPATED ONLINE ROLE PLAYING GAME, EVERQUEST™, WILL HIT RETAIL SHELVES IN MARCH 1999
-- Enthusiast Press Already Heralding EverQuest As Game of the Year --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., January 13, 1999 – 989 Studios announced today that its highly anticipated PC title EverQuest™, a massive online role-playing game, will be available March 16, 1999. Already heralded by game reviewers as an extraordinary evolution in online role-playing games, EverQuest features a multitude of adventures and quests and is "The dream role-playing game," according to PC Gamer.

"EverQuest will be a defining title in the online role-playing game genre because of its unique free roaming environments and graphically immersive story line that progresses according to the player’s individual decisions," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "EverQuest is the most detailed and visually stunning role-playing game ever developed."

EverQuest is being launched with a substantial marketing campaign that includes broad-based print and online advertising, retail-specific promotions and in-store merchandising. Retail pre-sell promotions will begin in February 1999.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®.
