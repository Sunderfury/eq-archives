Bust A Groove is an addictive competitive dancing game where ten funky characters compete in an all out dance-off to determine who has the freshest moves. By rhythmically pushing buttons in sync with the beat of the game’s music, players perform a variety of dance moves to hip-hop, disco and techno tunes. Bust A Groove is the only dance videogame to employ state-of-the-art Motion Capture Technology with specially choreographed dancing action developed exclusively for the PlayStation® game console.

Have you always suspected your friend had no rhythm? Test out your theory in 2-player mode or perfect your groove in Practice Mode. No matter what you look like on the dance floor, everyone’s a smooth operator in Bust A Groove!

 
Kick It Down With These Fly Features!
  • 10 Unique Dancers To Boogie With – Male, female and alien, all out of control dancing fanatics. Four hidden characters for added thrills.
  • Realistic Dance Action – The only dance videogame to use Motion Capture Technology for a truly authentic dancing experience.
  • Exciting Play Modes – Single Player, Two Player, Dance Edit and Practice Mode.
  • Musical Variety To Swivel Your Hips To – Dance to hip hop, disco, techno and much more!
  • 12 Unique Environments – Rich graphics and wild dance floors ranging from a ‘70’s disco club to city rooftops.
  • Combo Moves for impressive routines with maximum rhythm.
  • Unique Special Attacks – It may not be a mosh pit, but you can still sock it to your competitor when you fall short on the funk!
