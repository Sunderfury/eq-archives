NFL GameDay™, the best selling football franchise for the PlayStation®, is back and loaded with all-new features and player graphics. Year in and year out, NFL GameDay is looked upon as the most technically advanced sports videogame on the market. NFL GameDay™2000 is no exception. All-new player graphics have each player scaled to actual height, weight and body style in unprecedented detail. Team specific playbooks with more than 1,200 new plays (over twice the amount of last year’s game) designed by 32 players allow you to call and execute the plays NFL coaches call from the sidelines. More than 200 new motion captured moves from 13 NFL players create the most fluid animations ever seen in a sports video game. New Training Camp mode allows you to sharpen your skills on offense and defense. A new General Manager mode places you at the helm of a NFL franchise. Make general manager and coaching decisions over multiple seasons. A never before seen telestrator, state of the art play editor, new TV action cameras with Dick Enberg's play-by-play and Phil Simms commentary make this the ultimate football videogame.
