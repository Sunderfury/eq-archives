Rally Cross™ 2 is an intense off-road racing game of control and lightning speed. In this exciting action-packed sequel, you can rip through all new treacherous terrain, including steep mountains, sandy deserts, tropical jungles, panoramic coastline, mud, snow and ice. Choose from one of ten all new rally style cars and trucks, or pave your own road by modifying the rally cars for optimum speed and handling. You can even run yourself ragged on a custom course you can create with the game’s Track Editor. Rally style racing, improved rally car handling and control, and several thrilling play modes guarantee you hard core competitive fun in the race for the 1998 Rally Cross 2 World Championship!
New 3D Game Engine–All new 3D car and diversified course models provide the most realistic rally-style racing experience ever. Cars feature real-time lighting and mud and physical damage is accumulated throughout the race. Reflections are even visible on the cars’ exteriors. The new engine also incorporates improved car physics and authentic off-road terrain.
Innovative Track Editor–Design your own off-road course by combining seventeen different track features including double jumps, mud pits and 90 degree turns.
10 All New Rally Style Cars and Trucks–Each car is individually built with its own unique characteristics including speed, handling, brakes and more.
8 All New Diverse 3D Courses–Longer and wider courses offer higher speeds, more obstacles, increased gameplay and exciting racing action. Race in jungles, deserts, mud, snow, ice and more.
Car Customization–Choose from various tire types, suspension settings, gear settings, paint styles and more.
Rally Style Racing–Lose control of your rally car near water and risk disqualification by submerging your car.
Dynamic Play Modes–Practice, Time Trial, Single Race, Season, Head On, Mixed and Suicide.
Replay Feature–Relive the excitement of the race from several different camera angles.
3 Difficulty Levels–Test your racing expertise as a Rookie, Veteran and Pro.
CD Quality Sound–Recreates the ultra-realistic rally racing environment.
