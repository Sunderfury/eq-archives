In order to be the Xtreme Champion you must master "PHAT" tricks like "360 Indy Grabs" on skateboards, "Tabletops" on BMX Bikes and "Grinds" on In-Line Skates. All the tricks and combos the pros pull off are in 3Xtreme. Earn points by landing tricks so you can upgrade to the best equipment available.

See the Tricks!

  • While holding down your buttons throughout your tricks does increase your score, pulling off multiple tricks one after another will send it through the roof.
  • Hold down the "Circle" button to give you better handling through turns.
  • Hit the "Square" button at the top of a ramp to get more air.
  • You don’t have to place first on every race, just in the overall competition. You may want to concentrate on passing through gates and performing tricks so you can purchase better equipment. If you have the best equipment available for the circuit, your chances of placing first greatly increase.
  • Hold down the "X" button to accelerate and let go as soon as you achieve top speed. You won’t slow down until you go up a hill or hit an obstacle. This allows you to concentrate on performing your tricks.
  • Although passing through the gates is nice, it is also very risky. You may want to bypass them and put all your effort into finishing in first place.
  • Don’t stop performing tricks just because you have the best equipment available in your circuit. Your points continue on with you as you progress through the circuits. The more advanced circuits have much better and more expensive equipment.
  • If you are holding your own in the races, you may want to bypass the less expensive equipment, and save your points to purchase the best equipment available.
  • Stay away from the walls, they will slow you down immensely.

No points for bailing. Pull off as many tricks as you can but give yourself some time to straighten out and land.
