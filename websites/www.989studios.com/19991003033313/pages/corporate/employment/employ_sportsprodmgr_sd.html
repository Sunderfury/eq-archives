EMPLOYMENT REQUISITION

Sports Product Manager

989 Studios is a subsidiary of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc that focuses on Sony PlayStation 32 bit and 128 bit CD ROM game development. We are located San Diego, CA and have our Corporate offices in Foster City, California.


Job Opportunity:

A Product Manager position combining leadership qualities, personnel management and guidance skills, and the demonstrated ability to develop and implement the company’s Marketing programs. Duties will include the development of company marketing requirements for specific product lines and other various duties as assigned.

Recommended Experience:
  • Responsible for managing all marketing materials surrounding the launch of products such as television ads, print ads, packaging and website materials.
  • Oversees the development of business plans and product positioning in the marketing place.
  • Oversees market research, monitors competitive activity and identifies customer needs.
  • Interfaces with product development to develop new products or enhance existing product(s).
  • Receives assignments in the form of objectives and establishes goals to meet objectives.
  • Provides guidance to subordinates to achieve goals in accordance with established policies.
  • Establishes and recommends changes to policies which affect subordinate supervisors, customers and/or functional peer group managers.
  • Interactions normally involve matters between functional areas, other company divisions units, and the company.
  • Direct reports include: Assistant Product Manager and/or Marketing Coordinator


Requirements: Bachelor’s degree in Business or Marketing. Up to three years experience in the videogame industry. MBA is a plus. Ideal candidate would have extensive hands on experience with sports games as well as other game genre.

Available: Immediately

Compensation/Benefits: 989 Studios offers a competitive salary and a generous benefits package that includes Medical, Dental, 401K, Vision plan and additional perks.
