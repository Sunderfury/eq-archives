EVERQUEST™ ONLINE ROLE PLAYING GAME EVOLVES INTO A WHOLE NEW WORLD
989 Studios Puts The Destiny Of The Characters
And Ultimately The Storyline In The Hands Of The Players

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 28, 1998 – Designed up to be a massive online role playing game capable of supporting more than a 1,000 players per server, EverQuest™, available this Winter from 989 Studios, will feature a multitude of adventures and quests with an interactive storyline that evolves according to a player’s individual decisions.

"With free roaming environments, superior graphics and an evolving storyline, EverQuest will be the most comprehensive and versatile role playing game available on the Internet," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "EverQuest’s evolving storyline puts the power of the character’s destiny and, ultimately, the Quest, where it belongs, in the player’s hands."

The adventure begins in Norrath, the enormous world of EverQuest, where players can choose to begin the game in any of the five continents, with more than 30 adventure zones and 12 cities. Each of these continents comes with its own diverse species, economic systems, alliances and politics. Throughout this adventure, players form groups, strategic alliances and gather magical artifacts in their perilous and exciting quest to conquer the world of Norrath.

On their journey, players have the opportunity to earn experience points, discover hidden treasure and acquire new skills. With more than 40 unique skills and innate abilities per character, the player can become the noblest of knights or the most vicious elf. For those who are magically inclined, there are more than 100 spells in five separate magic classes to protect them as they explore the rich and vast world of dungeons, towers, crypts and evil abbeys throughout the game’s many dimensions.

Adventurers can explore the world of Norrath alone, but are encouraged to form alliances to acquire knowledge, solve puzzles and travel to strange, sometimes dangerous lands. Players can also interact with characters controlled by other gamers -- some good, some bad -- as well as computer controlled non-player characters.

Unlike other Internet role playing games, EverQuest allows players to view the 3D environment from a number of different camera angles, and is optimized to support the latest 3D graphic accelerator technology from 3Dfx Interactive™ and Microsoft“ Direct 3D™.

EverQuest Key Features
Supports more than a 1,000 players simultaneously per server

Immerse yourself in beautiful 3D environments

Provides SVGA (640x480) 16-bit color scenery of dungeons, arctic tundra, vast deserts and even a completely underwater environment

Player killing (Pkilling) is available to those who wish to participate in this mode, while those who do not wish to participate in Pkilling are immune

Choose from 12 races and 14 classes, including dwarves, ogres, trolls, rangers, wizards and rogues

More than 40 unique skills and innate abilities per character

Collect thousands of magical items, including artifacts of great power

Hundreds of spells to learn in five separate magic using classes

Optimized for the latest 3D graphic accelerator technology, EverQuest supports Voodoo Graphics™ by 3Dfx Interactive™ and Microsoft® Direct3D™
