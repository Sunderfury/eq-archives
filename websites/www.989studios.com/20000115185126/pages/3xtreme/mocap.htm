Andy MacDonald, 1998 X Games Gold Medallist in the skateboarding vert displayed to us some of his SICK moves that won him the Gold in San Diego. Through motion capture technology MacDonald’s movements and tendencies were captured, then incorporated into 3Xtreme giving the game the most realistic, fluid animations possible.

BMX Biker Chris Potts and In-Line Skater Todd Grossman showed their stuff while pulling off killer tricks and stunts. With motion capture by three different professional Xtreme athletes 3Xtreme hands down offers gamers the most comprehensive, realistic Xtreme style racing available. Check it out for yourself!
