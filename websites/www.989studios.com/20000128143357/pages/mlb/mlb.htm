As we prepare to enter into the next millenium, last year’s best playing baseball game on the PlayStation has moved into the 21st century. If you love baseball, come join Vin Scully along with Dave Campbell from the booth as well as Mo Vaughn and the rest of your favorite players from the field in MLB 2000 from 989 Sports.
Perennial All-Star and Triple Crown threat Mo Vaughn brings his lifetime .304 batting average, 230 career homeruns and 752 career RBI’s to California this year to play for the Anaheim Angels and represent 989 Sports on the cover of MLB 2000.


Number of players: 1 or 2
Periphrials: Memory Card and dual Shock™ Analog Controller
