Sideswipe enemy racers, hang a hard left at the vertical grapple, and speed your way to the finish line! With Jet Moto 3, 19 treacherous, obstacle-ridden race environments and 11 all-new beautifully detailed jet moto bikes promise to take you on the ride of your life and clear your sinuses to boot. With multi-player action and several season race options, Jet Moto 3 offers the ultra-realistic racing experience…as long as that experience includes crashing into a burning volcano at 120 MPH.


Number of players: 1 or 2
Peripherals: Memory Card and Dual Shock™ Analog Controller

Available Now
  • The rush is back!
  • Race any of the 11 sleek, photorealistic jet hover bikes. Old favorites like The Max, Wild Ride and Vampeera team with new riders such as Angel and Convict.
  • All-new 3D racing engine with MotoPhysics™ delivers ultra-realistic, tack-sharp 3D graphics at a blazing frame rate. Experience outstanding handling and even an occasional horizontal and vertical grapple!
  • 19 treacherous, bone-smashing environments to conquer, including an ancient Inca city, a subterranean graveyard and the gut-wrenching floating SkyPark.
  • Defeat the competition and earn the right to go freestylin’ in the wildest, most thrilling stunt tracks ever designed.
  • Each rider/bike boasts his or her own performance ratings for acceleration, top speed and handling.
  • Experience weather effects including snow, rain and night racing.
  • Challenging AI jostle and fight for position by crowding or side-swiping other players.
  • Rip it up or get wasted in 1 Player modes (Single Race, Season, Circuit and Practice) or 2 Player modes (Single Race or Circuit) with 4 season difficulty levels: Novice, Semi-Pro, Pro and Expert.
