SEQUEL TO THE DOUBLE MILLION SELLER TWISTED METAL™ SERIES, TWISTED METAL™ 3, TAKES ROAD RAGE TO THE EXTREME
989 Studios Crashes Onto The Scene With New Muscle Cars And Earth Shattering Gameplay

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 28, 1998 - 989 Studios announced today the newest addition to the popular, double million seller Twisted Metal® series, for the PlayStation® game console. Available this November, Twisted Metal™ 3 puts gamers in the ultimate automobile combat simulation.

"Twisted Metal 3 delivers more of what Twisted Metal fans love: revved-up cars, heavy machinery, explosions and plenty of power," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "This title uses our Tru Physics™ technology which allows gamers to experience realistic skidding, powerslides and vehicle collisions."

Twisted Metal 3 gives players the option to choose from a variety of updated, heavily-armed and heavily-protected vehicles. Gamers use these vehicles to move through Los Angeles, London, Washington D.C., Chicago, the North Pole, Egypt and other locations, as they blow up every opponent and obstacle in their path.

Twisted Metal 3 also features eight new battlegrounds, 12 different vehicles and a host of new weapons including the Speed Missile, Rain Missile and Mortar. In addition, new combo moves are faster and easier to use, and the environments are more interactive than in the previous incarnations.

Twisted Metal 3 Key Features
Extremely realistic, four wheel physics model using "Tru Physics™": creates realistic car body lean and shocks, skidding, powerslides and vehicle collisions

State-of-the-art, fourth generation 3D engine: 30 frames per second, gouroud shading, colored environment lighting and all 3D environments (Even the weapon pick ups are 3D!)

White knuckle two-player action: link cable or split screen (horizontal/vertical selectable in game)

All new three to four-player battles via link cable

New combo moves, like Freeze Blast, Rear Attack and High Jump, plus many more that are faster and easier to use

Special multi-player arenas

Awesome visual effects, explosions and car damage

Secret areas and hidden characters

12 vehicles (updated and highly detailed): Thumper, Road Kill, Hammerhead, Mr. Grimm, Warthog, Spectre, Outlaw, Firestarter, Club Kid, Auger, Flower Power and Axel

Bosses: Minion, Dark Tooth and the all new Head Hunter

Game environments: Los Angeles, London, Washington D.C., Chicago, North Pole, Nevada’s Area 51, Egypt and Calypso’s Blimp


989 Studios, formerly known as Sony Interactive Studios America, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with a second development studio in San Diego, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™ and Twisted Metal™.
