WHAT:
989 Sports is visiting the Minnesota Vikings training camp to give the team a sneak preview of the all-new version of the most popular football videogame for the PlayStation® game console - - NFL GameDay™ 2000. Available in-stores early September, NFL GameDay 2000, has a reputation for being the most realistic because of the involvement of all-pro players like John Randle, Terrell Davis, Mike Alstott, Charles Woodson, Akili Smith, Ryan Leaf, Hardy Nickerson, Jason Sehorn, Jimmy Smith and Jerome Bettis (just to name a few). 989 Sports incorporates actual player movements into their videogames through motion capture technology, as well as enlisting numerous marquee players to consult on the NFL GameDay 2000 playbook.
