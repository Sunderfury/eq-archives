Offense
1. To hit the One-timer shot, press "X" and then "Square".
2. Hold down the "X" button for the Give-and-Go.
3. To take a slap-shot, hold down the "Square" to wind up all the way.
4. To take a snap-shot, hold down the "Square" to wind up half-way and release it to shoot
5. To leave the Drop pass, press "Triangle".
6. To get a Speed Burst while skating, hold down the "O".
7. To Fake the shot, hold down the "Square" to wind up then tap "Square" during the backswing.
8. To Redirect a shot, hold down the "Square" then press "X" during backswing.
9. To use Icon Passing™, hold down "L2" and press either "X", "Square", "O", "Triangle", or "R2".
10. To change offensive strategies, press "R1".
11. To change lines , press "R2"
12. To Change to one of 3 Offensive Strategies On-The-Fly, Hold down "R1" and "Triangle".
13. In a Normal Offensive Strategy, your players are cycled.
14. In an Aggressive Offensive Strategy, your players try to screen the goalie and set up rebounds.
15. In a Positional Offensive Strategy, your forwards stay in their lanes with one ready to backcheck.
16. To Change to one of 3 Breakout Strategies On-The-Fly, Hold down "R1" and "O".
17. In a Normal Offensive Strategy, your forwards will not cross.
18. In a Aggressive Offensive Strategy, your forwards will cross in front of you.
19. In a Conservative Offensive Strategy, your forwards will cross in behind of you.
20. To increase scoring opportunities, attack the net after taking a shot to knock home a loose puck off of a goalie’s save.
21. To increase your chance of scoring from outside, try to use another player to screen the goalie.
22. Look for your forwards to set up around the net for better scoring opportunities.
23. Try to move the puck up ice by passing instead of carrying it all the way with one player.
24. Try to "Juke" the goalie on breakaways by moving the puck from side to side before shooting.


Defense
1. To switch players on Defense, press "X".
2. To switch to your goalie, hold down "X".
3. To Hip Check, while skating backwards hold "O" and D-pad left or right.
4. To Shoulder Check, hold "O" and D-pad left or right.
5. To use Icon Switching™, hold down "L2" and press either "X", "Square", "O", "Triangle", or "R2".
6. To change defensive strategies, press "L1".
7. To assign Icons, press "L2"
8. To change lines , press "R2"
9. While manually controlling your goalie, press "Square" to have your goalie make a save.
10. To have your goalie gather a puck in or cover a loose puck, press "O".
11. To Change to one of 3 Defensive Strategies On-The-Fly, Hold down "L1" and "Triangle".
12. In a Normal Defensive Strategy, your man stays a little off of the man he is guarding.
13. In an Aggressive Defensive Strategy, your man plays tight on the man he is guarding.
14. In a Conservative Defensive Strategy, each man is responsible for a zone.
15. To Change to one of 3 Forechecking Strategies On-The-Fly, Hold down "L1" and "O".
16. In a Normal Forechecking Strategy, 1 forward harasses the defensemen in the opponent's defensive zone.
17. In an Aggressive Forechecking Strategy, 2 forwards harass the defensemen in the opponent's defensive zone.
18. In a Conservative Forechecking Strategy, you attempt to trap your opponent in the neutral zone.


Fighting
1. When Fighting, press "Square" to duck.
2. When Fighting, press "X" and D-pad right for a Right Cross.
3. When Fighting, press "X" and D-pad left for a Left Cross.
4. When Fighting, press "O" and D-pad right for a Right Uppercut.
5. When Fighting, press "O" and D-pad Left for a Left Uppercut.
6. When Fighting, press "Triangle" for a special move.

Options
1. Create a free agent player with the type of skills that can help your team to the playoffs.
2. Make trades in order to fill holes in your lines.
3. Release players who are not making the cut.
4. Sign Free agents to add leadership or key ingredients to your team.
5. Use a memory card to keep track of individual and team records.
6. Use a memory card to save a game or a season in progress.
7. In Instant Replay mode operate the Free-Cam by holding "L1" and using the D-pad.
8. In Free-Cam mode, press "Triangle" to raise the camera.
9. In Free-Cam mode, press "X" to lower the camera.
10. Try to allocate your player’s points in a way that will create a 99 overall player.
11. At any time during a season, press R1 to end the season.
12. To better your skills, start at Rookie and then progress to Veteran and eventually All-Star.
13. For more continuous gameplay, try turning OFF different option in the "Options" menu.
14. Utilize the "Quick Start" option to start an Exhibition game quickly without having to set up the game.
15. During a Multi-Team Season, games can be simulated for the entire league or just a particular team.
16. Use Transactions to change the makeup of your roster with trades, by creating and releasing players and by signing free agents.
