CYBERSTRIKE® 2 BRINGSFUTURISTIC COMBAT SIMULATION TO THE WORLD WIDE WEB
989 Studios Unveils Sequel To Best-Selling Multi-Player Game

FOSTER CITY, Calif., November 17, 1998 – 989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, announced today the release of Cyberstrike“ 2, an online action-strategy game. The sequel to Cyberstrike, one of the most popular online games, Cyberstrike 2 places an emphasis on teamwork and cooperation between players to defeat their enemies.

"Cyberstrike 2 stretches the boundaries of Internet gaming by allowing players to decide the fate of their Clan," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "Cyberstrike 2 offers intense game play and infinite replay with an enhanced 3D engine that supports real-time lighting effects, explosive pyrotechnics, and indoor and outdoor environments." With the help of the game’s advanced 3D engine, game players will be able to play across entire city landscapes. The game also provides seamless motion over city streets, in-and-out of buildings and through nuclear reactors.

Cyberstrike 2 also features single-player and multi-player battle modes. In the single player-mode, players can join one of two Clans in 50 separate single player missions, each with its own storyline and challenges. Utilizing the multi-player mode, gamers from around the world can meet on the battle fields of the Internet to form four teams of up to eight. This means a total of 32 players can play in each customizable, fighting environment.

Cyberstrike 2 also allows players to customize their individual CyberPod with more than 60 different offensive and defensive modules, including shield generators, surface-to-surface missiles and deadly accurate rapid-fire machine guns.

Cyberstrike 2 Key Features

  • Offers intense game play and infinite replay
  • Enhanced 3D engine supports real-time lighting effects, explosive pyrotechnics and seamless indoor and outdoor environments
  • Battle deadly opposition in detailed 3D arenas and breathtaking landscapes in beautiful SVGA (640x480) 16-bit color
  • Wage war across different styles of terrain from canyons and industrial waste land to post-apocalyptic cities
  • Customize each CyberPod from a selection of 60 different offensive and defensive modules
  • Two different Clans to choose from, each with its own story line
  • Twenty distinct single player missions per Clan, for a total of 50 missions
  • Detailed movies depict the backgrounds of each Clan
  • Provides extensive multiplayer support, via multiple Internet servers, allowing four teams of eight players for a total of 32 players per combat environment
  • Features CD quality music by the Fatman and Team Fat
  • Optimized for the latest 3D graphic accelerator technology, Cyberstrike 2 supports Voodoo Graphics‘ by 3Dfx Interactive‘ and Microsoft® Direct3D‘
