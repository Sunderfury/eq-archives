Aztec Island
This shortcut actually has 2 parts to it. The road will turn left immediately after the second tunnel. Instead of going left with the road, continue straight through the opening in the side of the mountain (it is very obvious). If you have enough speed and you jump at the end of this hidden path, you can make it to another hole in a mountain on the other side of the road. Completing both parts of this shortcut will save you an enormous amount of time.

The Metro
This one is pretty difficult. You will have to be at top speed in order to reach it. Make your way to the second cement walkway (the ones with the yellow and black arrows on the side of it) on the right side of the tunnel. It’s just after a left turn. You will see a bridge suspended in the air in this section of the tunnel. You can use the front of the cement walkway as a ramp and jump up to the bridge. Follow the bridge’s path through a hidden tunnel to shave off a few seconds of your time.

Characters, Courses, and Equipment must be available in a Season before they will be available in any other mode. Example: You cannot use Spunkmeyer in Exhibition until you can use him in a Season.

The equipment you unlock will correspond with the type of equipment you are using.
