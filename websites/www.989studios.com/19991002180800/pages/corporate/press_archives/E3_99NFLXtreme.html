NFL XTREME 2 TAKES ARCADE-STYLE FOOTBALL TO NEXT LEVEL WITH REAL NFL PLAYER MOVEMENTS AND TAUNTS
-- 989 Sports’ NFL Xtreme 2 Delivers More Outrageous Taunting, Hard-Hitting, Fast Action, Arcade-Style Football To The PlayStation --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 13, 1999 – 989 Sports™ announced today that NFL Xtreme™ 2, the sequel to last year’s popular NFL Xtreme™, will bring even more arcade-style five-on-five football mayhem to the PlayStation® game console this July. Licensed by both the NFL and Players Inc, this year’s NFL Xtreme 2 is equipped with more than 350 real NFL player animations, outrageous taunts, lightning-quick gameplay and high-impact blows.

"This year, the NFL Xtreme 2 development team recorded the real sounds and voices of numerous marquee NFL players," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "Dozens of real taunts will be in the game, making NFL Xtreme 2 the most entertaining arcade-style football videogame on the market."

The NFL players recorded for NFL Xtreme 2 include a virtual who’s-who of All-Pros, such as Broncos RB Terrell Davis, Packers WR Antonio Freeman, Rams RB Marshall Faulk, Vikings DT John Randle, Buccaneers DT Warren Sapp, Falcons CB Ray Buchanan and Bills PT Ted Washington.

With all of the NFL teams and players, unlimited passing and no rules, out of bounds or penalties, this is a new way for gamers to play football. Incredible animations intensify the gaming experience: gamers can throw tackles, perform insane hits and select from more than 150 celebrations. In addition, NFL Xtreme 2 offers lightning-quick play selection -- three times as fast as last year’s version of the game.

The stunning graphical presentation in NFL Xtreme 2 boasts a 400-polygonal player model (four times the amount of polygons used to create the award-winning NFL GameDay™ ‘99 player models), delivering gigantic and exceptionally-detailed 3D players that look like they are ready to jump out of the television.

NFL Xtreme 2 also incorporates actual NFL player movements which were motion captured to enhance the gameplay realism. Players motion captured for NFL Xtreme 2 include Pittsburgh Steelers RB Jerome Bettis, Oakland Raiders WR Tim Brown, New York Giants CB Jason Sehorn, Seattle Seahawks LB Chad Brown and Tampa Bay Buccaneers FB Mike Alstott and LB Hardy Nickerson.

NFL Xtreme 2 Key Features:

  • All 31 NFL teams and stadiums (including the Cleveland Browns and Tennessee Titans)
  • Arcade style, five-on-five, open eligibility, no rules, hard-hitting football videogame action
  • First downs at each 20-yard line and midfield
  • Numerous NFL players were recorded to provide authentic taunts on big plays
  • Individual scaling, weight and height on each player
  • More than 150 celebration animations
  • Players celebrate after touchdowns, sacks, big hits and first downs
  • Ball carriers high-step, execute spin moves, hurdle, stiff-arm, shoulder charge and front flip over the pile
  • Gigantic linemen and linebackers with bulging biceps and thighs, sleek receivers and DB’s with tall, wiry builds
  • More than 350 animations from real players at 400 polygons per player (3D graphics)
  • High resolution game presentation, including player shadows
  • Play in rain, snow, wind, blizzards and in the dark
  • Water/snow/grass splashes with every stride of the players
  • Injuries (players limp when injured)
  • New lightning-quick play selection
  • All-new unlimited passing behind the line of scrimmage
  • Outrageous animations include: jerseys being torn away, front and back flips, throw-tackles, stiff-arms, one-handed catches, juke moves, high steps and shoulder charges
  • Dynamic camera angles and views
  • Realistic sound effects include ringing bells, a freight train, cuckoo clock chiming and players’ grunts and screams
    Play in Exhibition, Season or Tournament modes
