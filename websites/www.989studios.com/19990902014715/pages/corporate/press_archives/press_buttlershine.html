989 STUDIOS AWARDS ADVERTISING ACCOUNT TO BUTLER, SHINE & STERN

FOSTER CITY, Calif., January 20, 1999 - 989 Studios™, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, known for its top-selling PlayStation“ videogame franchises titles NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Cool Boarders“ and Jet Moto™, has named Butler, Shine & Stern its agency of record."Our selection of Butler, Shine & Stern reflects our belief that effective and innovative advertising can communicate the leading-edge technology that will continue to differentiate 989 Studios’ videogames from other software companies," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "Butler, Shine & Stern is a very well respected, highly creative shop that demonstrated to us that they understand our business. We look forward to them helping us achieve our goals."

Butler, Shine & Stern was chosen following 989 Studios’ agency review, which involved a handful of undisclosed firms. The agencies were invited to present a strategic advertising plan in support of 989 Studios and 989 Sports™ titles. 989 Studios 1999 North American advertising budget is 15 million dollars.
Greg Stern, President of Butler, Shine & Stern noted: "This was a perfect pitch situation. We had the opportunity to date before getting married by first working on a few projects with 989 Studios. We enjoyed the collaborative working environment this client creates, their appreciation of distinctive creative work, and we think their games are among the best in the industry."

In April 1998, Sony Computer Entertainment Amercia Inc. (SCEA) announced the formation of dedicated software publishing unit and the company’s new name -- 989 Studios. As an independent software publisher, 989 Studios is working to strengthen its brand identity with all of its top-selling titles. Retaining Butler, Shine & Stern, an advertising agency separate from SCEA’s, helps solidify the company’s independence. 989 Studios continued to retain TBWA/Chiat Day, SCEA’s advertising agency of record, throughout 1998.

"We were fortunate to be able to work with TBWA/Chiat Day during our transitioning period," said Fox. "Chiat’s highly successful print and television campaigns helped build brand recognition for 989’s top-selling franchise titles and we appreciate their dedication to our business." Butler, Shine & Stern, based in Sausalito, CA, is an advertising agency that provides strategically focused, award-winning and effective creative advertising and integrated marketing solutions. In addition to 989 Studios, their clients include Borders Books & Music, E-greetings Network, Jamba Juice, Millers Outpost, Seagram Wines, and R.A.B. Motors.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders“.
