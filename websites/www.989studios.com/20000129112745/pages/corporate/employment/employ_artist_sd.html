989 Studios is a subsidiary of Sony Computer Entertainment America, Inc. that focuses on Sony PlayStation 32 bit and 128 bit game development. We are located in San Diego, CA and have our corporate offices in Foster City, CA.

ANIMATOR LEVEL I

Primary Responsibilities

  • Utilizes traditional art training, and knowledge of animation and computer graphics software to produce animation for software products.
  • Learn to animate models in and 3D
  • Learn to animate texture maps.
  • 2D sprite animation.
  • Participates with product development team to help visualize and develop game designs.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree or Certification in Art. Illustrator/Animation background with some knowledge of 3D art and/or animation techniques a plus. Exposure to 3DSM or Alias preferred. Must show a strong ability in traditional art and creativity. This is an entry-level position.

ANIMATOR LEVEL II

Primary Responsibilities

  • Utilizes traditional art training, and knowledge of animation and computer graphics software to produce animation for software products.
  • Animates models in 2D and 3D
  • Animates texture maps.
  • 2D sprite animation.
  • Participates with product development team to help visualize and develop game designs.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree or Certification in Art or equivalent. Must have solid knowledge of character animation in 3D Studio Max, Alias/Wavefront or similar package. Must have prior experience in game animation, and a minimum of one game cycle required. Ability to animate within game limitations (i.e. frames, engine ability). Must have a basic understanding of Modeling.


MODELLER LEVEL I

Primary Responsibilities

  • Environmental and basic character modeling.
  • Application of texture maps.
  • Participates with product development team to help visualize and develop game designs.
  • Revise more complex models.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree or Certification in Art or equivalent. Must show a strong ability in traditional art and creativity. Experience with set design or sculpturing a plus. Basic modeling experience on 3DSM or Alias a plus. This is an entry-level position.

MODELLER LEVEL II

Primary Responsibilities

  • Model complex animatable characters.
  • Create efficient geometry densities to work within the game environments.
  • Applies lighting techniques.
  • Participates with product development team to help visualize and develop game designs.
  • Creates game specific programmable objects.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree or Certification in Art or equivalent. Must show a strong ability in traditional art and creativity. Experience with set design or sculpturing a plus. Modeling experience on any software packages is necessary. Must have strong game restrictive modeling skills. Must have effective texture map application, scene and environment lighting ability.


TEXTURE MAP/CONCEPTUAL ARTIST LEVEL I

Primary Responsibilities

  • Creating conceptual art.
  • Creating texture maps.
  • Learns basic concepts of modeling.
  • Participates with product development team to help visualize and develop game designs.
  • Learns restrictions of texture size, texture depth and v-ram.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree or Certification in Art or equivalent. Must show a strong ability in traditional art and creativity. Strong illustration skills and color work is a plus. This is an entry-level position.


TEXTURE MAP/CONCEPTUAL ARTIST LEVEL II

Primary Responsibilities

  • Creating and optimizing texture maps for models and v-ram.
  • Create texture maps which work with animated geometry.
  • Greater modeling and application responsibilities.
  • Participates with product development team to help develop game.

Requirements: Must have a minimum of an Associates Degree of Certification in Art or equivalent. Must have knowledge of computerized painting software (i.e. Photoshop, StudioPaint, Fractal Painter, etc). Must have prior experience with bit depth reduction techniques. A minimum of one game cycle is required. Must have an intermediate level of modeling skills and advanced texture map application techniques.
