989 STUDIOS DROPS INTO THE PIPE WITH PROFESSIONAL
RIDERS IN ALL-NEW COOL BOARDERS 4
-- PlayStation’s Best-Selling Snowboarding Videogame Series Goes Big This Year With All-New Moves, Riders, Courses and Boards --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 13, 1999 – 989 Studios™, makers of the best-selling Cool Boarders® snowboarding videogame series, announced today that Cool Boarders® 4 will be available for the PlayStation® game console this November. With the involvement of numerous world famous pro snowboarders, several major snowboard manufacturers, countless realistic maneuvers and fast arcade-style gameplay, Cool Boarders 4 is the videogame real snowboarders like to play.

In Cool Boarders 4, gamers can emulate pro riders’ moves due to the inclusion of more professional riders and board manufacturers than any other snowboarding videogame on the market. Gamers will be able to hit the slopes as known snowboarding professionals such as Jim Rippey, Ross Powers and 12-year-old phenomenon Shaun White of Burton®, as well as Forum®’s J.P. Walker and Salomon®’s Michele Taggart -- just to name a few. "The Cool Boarders development team continually pushed the envelope to deliver the most intense snowboarding videogame each year," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "By involving numerous world-class riders in the development process of this year’s game,

Cool Boarders 4 will be the most incredible snowboarding game ever to hit the PlayStation. Gamers, snowboarders and snowboarding enthusiasts will be impressed with its ultra-realistic graphics and smooth gameplay."

With a newly-advanced 3D game engine, Cool Boarders 4 adds many new features that let the gamer experience the true feeling of snowboarding. The new "Trickmaster Mode" allows riders the opportunity to learn and perfect their own tricks on long downhill courses filled with jump after jump. "Multiplayer Tournament Mode" lets gamers challenge their friends on any event to see who can nail the best tricks and get downhill first.

Riders can choose from dozens of authentic snowboard manufacturers such as Burton, K2®, Salomon, Ride® and Forum. Each board is based on its actual performance ratings. Gamers can also create custom riders by choosing different heads and bodies, dressing them up in name brand apparel from today’s hottest companies – Billabong™, Levis® and Arnette® -- and shredding down the slopes on a fully customized snowboard.

In addition to the Downhill, Half Pipe, Slope Style, CBX and Big Air Stadium events, Cool Boarders 4 offers new Special Events on each mountain for the ultimate snowboarding challenge. Riders can snowboard out of bounds into bushwhacking territory while outrunning the Ski Patrol, or burst through fresh powder and maneuver through tight courses while dodging a fast-moving avalanche.

Cool Boarders 4 will be available in November and features Burton pro rider Jim Rippey on the package front.

Cool Boarders 4 Key Features:

  • Play as one player or compete head-to-head in two-player mode
  • Choice of numerous professional snowboarders, such as: Jim Rippey/Burton, Shaun White/Burton, Ross Powers/Burton, Chris Engelsman/K2, Michele Taggart/Salomon, Chad Otterstrom/M3, Andrew Crawford/Morrow and J.P.Walker/Forum (just to name a few)
  • Select from dozens of authentic snowboards from many different major snowboard manufacturers such as: Burton, K2, Salomon, Ride and Forum
  • State-of-the-art enhanced 3D game engine features polygonal characters, unprecedented courses and real-time rendering to provide the most realistic and entertaining snowboarding experience
  • Perform thousands of snowboarding tricks including "hand plant the half pipe"
  • Gamers can create custom riders by building features from the ground up, including body style and apparel; pick out clothes from today’s hottest companies—Billabong™, Levis®, Arnette® and more
  • Fully create and customize snowboards by tweaking style and color
  • In addition to the Downhill, Half Pipe, Slope Style, CBX and Big Air Stadium events, each mountain includes its own Special Event to treat only the most skilled boarders
  • New "Trickmaster Mode" teaches the gamer how to perfect his/her tricks and ride like the pros
  • Five challenging mountains plus a hidden Special Event on each
  • The popular fighting feature has been improved so the hits are harder, the action more intense and the competition more fierce
  • New "Multiplayer Tournament Mode" lets the gamer challenge his friends to see who can nail the best tricks and get down the hill first
