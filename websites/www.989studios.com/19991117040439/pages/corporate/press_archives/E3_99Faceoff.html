989 SPORTS TO DOMINATE HOCKEY VIDEOGAME CATEGORY WITH ALL-NEW NHL FACEOFF 2000
-- The Fastest Hockey Videogame On The PlayStation Returns For Another Year Of Hard-Checking Action --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 13, 1999 – 989 Sports™ announced today that NHL FaceOff™ 2000, the fifth edition of the highly-touted PlayStation® hockey videogame series, will be available this October. NHL FaceOff 2000 has all the NHL teams and players, authentic two-man commentary featuring New Jersey Devils® play-by-play announcer Mike Emrick and ESPN’s Darren Pang, a dramatic new TV-style presentation, interactive crowds and enhanced Artificial Intelligence.

"NHL FaceOff 2000 complements 989 Sports’ strong heritage in capturing the realism and excitement of sports action in videogames," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "The NHL FaceOff 2000 development team has raised the bar this year by bringing the hard-hitting energy and intensity of hockey, plus an eye-popping TV-style presentation, right into the hands of the videogamer."

Last year, NHL FaceOff™ 99 was the first hockey videogame to have motion capture animations performed on ice – it featured moves from St. Louis winger Scott Young and former San Jose goaltender Kelly Hrudey. This year, NHL FaceOff 2000 has built on the success of the NHL FaceOff ™ franchise to include more than 150 new player animations. Every subtle and personalized move is perfected to amazing detail.

New I.C.E. AI™ (Intelligence Comprehension Execution Artificial Intelligence) has players playing and reacting just as they do in the NHL. Icon Passing™ and Icon Switching™ bring incredible realism and control to players as they battle for the puck in offensive and defensive zones. The new "Puck Halo" will make the puck easier to see and play with in the corners and in front of the goal. Players can sprint across the blue line on a breakaway, glide in and unleash a slap shot that hits the post or goes too high and shatters the glass.

With the use of actual blueprints, arenas are re-created in amazing detail. The new 3D player models capture every nuance with real texture-mapped faces that are true to the facial features of NHL stars. Increased camera angles, pop-up stats and in-game panels work in cadence with the authentic audio of play-by-play announcer Mike Emrick and color commentator Darren Pang, creating the best TV–style presentation available for a hockey videogame.

NHL FaceOff 2000 incorporates all 28 NHL teams for the 1999–2000 season (including the Atlanta Thrashers™) with updated rosters and the ability to play through an entire season. Gamers can track more than 30 individual and team stats and compete for any of 15 NHL awards, including the Hart Memorial, Art Ross, Vezina, Conn Smythe, Presidents and the Stanley Cup®.

NHL FaceOff 2000 Key Features:

  • Unrivaled 3D game engine
  • Realistic 3D player models based on player size, weight and facial appearance
  • NHL arenas derived from the actual blueprints and presented in lifelike detail - each arena’s characteristics are included, even the championship banners and Jumbotrons™
  • Interactive crowds with team chants, yells and special animations such as hats being thrown onto the ice after a hat trick
  • Eye-popping TV-style presentation featuring two-man commentary, including New Jersey Devils’ acclaimed announcer Mike Emrick and ESPN’s analyst Darren Pang
  • Seven different game-play camera angle option settings and countless cut-aways, zoom-ins, pop-up stats and in-game panels
  • New "Puck Halo" makes the puck easier to see and play with in the corners and in front of the net
  • New I.C.E. AI (Intelligence Comprehension Execution Artificial Intelligence) allows players to play and react just as they do in the NHL
  • Motion captured animation performed on ice provides the most realistic hockey action available; players motion captured include St. Louis Blues winger Scott Young and former San Jose Sharks goalie Kelly Hrudey
  • More than 150 player animations
  • Refined Icon Switching™ on defense and Icon Passing™ on offense translate into the most realistic hockey control available
  • On-the-fly strategy control lets the gamer change his attack with the touch of a button
  • Complete roster management with player creation, trades and free agency
  • Full season and game statistics available in every offensive and defensive category
  • Every player and team for the 1999–2000 season is included – even the Atlanta Thrashers – in addition to eight international teams
  • Personalized goalie face masks from around the league
  • Create and manage your own team with complete rosters -- draft, trade, release and sign free agents
