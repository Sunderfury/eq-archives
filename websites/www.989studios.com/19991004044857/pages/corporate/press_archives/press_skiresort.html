TOP U.S. SKI RESORTS PARTICIPATE IN "COOL BOARDERS® 3 VIDEOGAME CHALLENGE"
-- Best-Selling PlayStation® Snowboarding Videogame Takes to The Slopes --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., December 7, 1998 – 989 Studios announced today the start of the "Cool Boarders® 3 Videogame Challenge." Employees from top ski resorts throughout the U.S. will compete against each other on 989 Studios’ Cool Boarders 3, which is the most realistic snowboarding videogame available for the PlayStation® game console.

Employee rivalry will consist of 16 players from each resort competing on Cool Boarders 3. Bracketed tournament-play will take place throughout the winter season in each resort’s employee lounge, resulting in one finalist per resort. All finalists will receive a copy of Cool Boarders 3 and bragging rights as the local Cool Boarders 3 videogame champion.

"With its early sales success, Cool Boarders 3 has already set the standard for realism in snowboarding videogames," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "So, there is no better place to further demonstrate the realism of Cool Boarders 3 than at the resorts themselves. The ‘Cool Boarders 3 Videogame Challenge’ will provide an opportunity for the best snowboarders from the best resorts to pull off all the snowboarding tricks they’ve already learned on the mountain, plus a ton more that they’d be afraid to actually try."

With its advanced 3D game engine, Cool Boarders 3 lets the videogamer feel every rock, bump, ice patch and jump, while competing on 34 courses over five tenacious mountains. Players can select from 22 authentic snowboards, which include sixteen Burton“ Snowboards and six Ride“ Snowboards, each created with real performance ratings for acceleration, control, responsiveness and flexibility.

To further increase the realism of the Cool Boarders 3 experience, professional snowboarders’ signature moves have been motion captured to provide the most realistic snowboarding game animations possible. This includes 35 real jaw-dropping snowboarding maneuvers and thousands of combos like Front Side 540’s, Back Side Ally-Oops, Back Flips, Tail Grabs and 360’s.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®.
