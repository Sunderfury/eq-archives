SYPHON FILTER™ IS AN EXPLOSIVE THRILL RIDE WITH FAST-PACED ACTION AND SOPHISTICATED THIRD-PERSON GUNPLAY
-- 989 Studios Propels Gamers Into the Deadly World of Counter-Terrorism --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., February 16, 1999 - 989 Studios announced today that Syphon Filter™, an exciting 3D action-adventure videogame, is now available for the PlayStation“ game console.

Syphon Filter features intricate gunplay and spine-tingling action in a fully-integrated 3D environment, providing gamers with a glimpse into the secret world of counter-terrorism.

Players must navigate through interactive environments, dodge high-speed subways, deadly terrorists and apocalyptic explosions. Adding to the action is the carefully designed third-person perspective that drops the player into Special Agent Gabriel Logan’s world with eye-opening realism.

"Syphon Filter is an amazing videogame conglomeration that contains heart-pounding gameplay, the hard-hitting action of a blockbuster movie and the suspense of a great spy novel," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "As the gameplayer, you must use stealth maneuvering and expert marksmanship, while making decisions on-the-fly to unravel the intricate web of conspiracy."

As operative Gabriel Logan, the player’s mission is simple: stop an act of biological terrorism on U.S. soil and infiltrate the organization responsible for creating a virus that threatens to destroy mankind.

To help gameplayers accomplish the mission, Syphon Filter features sophisticated third person gunplay and three targeting modes: default mode, manual mode and target lock mode. Gameplayers can also choose from an arsenal of weapons, including a night vision sniper rifle that allows them to dispose of the enemy with one shot. Syphon Filter is a 989 Studios production, developed by Eidetic, Inc.

Syphon Filter Features

  • An arsenal of weapons include silenced pistols, infrared rifles, grenades, sniper rifles, tasers and shotguns
  • Counter-Terrorism, special reconnaissance and direct assault mission objectives add gripping intensity to the action-packed plot
  • Real-world, real-time, open 3D environments
  • Explore exciting and vast environments, including 20 challenging levels
  • The environments are not mere backgrounds; they are an integral part of the action. Subways come flying by at top speeds, glass shatters, street lamps blow out and explosions light up the sky
  • An advanced communication device houses Gabe’s weapons inventory, mission objectives and game options
  • Carefully designed third person perspective
  • Advanced animations deliver fluid motion. Gabe can kneel, crouch, climb, roll, throw, pick up, jump, walk, run and move across places hand-over-hand
  • Awesome special effects add enormous detail: breathe in cold air and view the effects of gunshots, shattering glass and fiery explosions
  • Supports the Dual Shock™ Analog Controller for the ultra-realistic experience
  • Features 989 Studios’ proprietary interactive midi sound engine: music increases in intensity as action picks up

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®.
