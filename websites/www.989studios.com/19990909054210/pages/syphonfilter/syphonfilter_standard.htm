Game Features:

  • Carefully designed third person perspective drops you into real-world 3D environments.
  • Counter-terrorism, special reconnaissance and direct action mission objectives add gripping intensity to the action-packed plot.
  • Multiple weapon targeting modes: sniper, manual look/aim and target lock.
  • Twenty vast levels to explore deliver the challenge of split-second decision-making.
  • Utilize an arsenal of sophisticated weapons, including silenced pistols, explosive grenades, sniper rifles, infrared rifles, tasers and automatic artillery.
  • Advanced animations deliver incredibly fluid motion, allowing Gabe to keep enemies in target while kneeling, crouching, walking, rolling, climbing, jumping and running.
  • An advanced communication device houses Gabe’s weapons systems, mission objectives and game options.
  • Tons of vertical gameplay increases the challenge and excitement of navigating environments.
  • Awesome special effects add enormous detail: expelled breath puffs, gunshot effects, shattered glass, dynamic real-time lighting, fiery explosions and more.
  • Hailing bullets take full advantage of the Dual Shock™ Analog Controller for the ultra-realistic experience.

Get ready to save the nation in February 1999.
