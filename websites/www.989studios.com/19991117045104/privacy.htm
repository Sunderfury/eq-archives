Privacy Policy
989 Studios is committed to respecting the privacy rights of all visitors to our web site. We also recognize that our visitors, and the parents of children visiting our site, deserve a high level of assurance that any personally identifying information they choose to share with 989 Studios will not be used or transferred without their express consent.

This Privacy Statement and the certification seal located to your right confirms that 989 Studios is a valid licensee and participating member in the Entertainment Software Rating Board’s Privacy Online Program: ESRB Privacy Online. To protect your privacy to the maximum extent possible, we have undertaken this privacy initiative and all our web sites have been reviewed and certified by ESRB Privacy Online to meet established online information collection and use practices. As part of the privacy program, we are subject to frequent audits of our sites and other enforcement and accountability mechanisms administered independently by ESRB.

ESRB Privacy Online is a third-party seal provider whose mission is to protect consumers’ online privacy and make the Internet a secure, reliable, and private place to share information and conduct business. ESRB Privacy Online promotes and enforces established principles and guidelines for fair information practices that include requirements of full disclosure, notice, and informed consent.

Whenever you visit a web site that displays the ESRB Privacy Online certification seal, you can expect to be notified of:
  • Exactly what personal information may be collected and by what means;
  • Who, if anyone, is collecting you personal information;
  • How this information may be used, including, without limitation, with whom (if anyone) it may be shared;
  • The consequences, if any resulting from your refusal to provide personal information;
  • How you can access and, if necessary, change the personal information a company maintains;
  • How a company ensures data security, quality, and integrity; and Where and how to ask questions or file complaints.

If you have any questions, complaints, or comments regarding our privacy statement or policies, please contact Sony Consumer Services Department at (800) 345-7669. Collection of Information
989 Studios does not require that visitors reveal any personally identifying information in order to access our main site.

Although information may be required to participate in certain promotions offered through our web sites, participants provide information on a voluntary basis only. Generally, we may wish to collect personal and/or demographic information about you in connection with your participation in our online contests, sweepstakes or other promotions. Such personal information may include name, address, telephone number, e-mail address, and date of birth. Such demographic information may include your age, gender and video game-related information.

Personally identifying information, which 989 Studios collects for a particular promotional purpose from either on-line or off-line methods, will be saved and used only for that purpose, unless the participant chooses to "opt-in" and have his or her information included in 989 Studios’ database. The inclusion of such information in 989 Studios’ database is free and grants to the participating individual certain rights which may include, but are not limited to, discount offers, game news, demo discs and more. No information will be shared with outside parties.

Policies - Children 12 Years Old and Under
989 Studios will not knowingly collect information through our websites from people age 12 and under. We encourage parents to take an active role to protect the privacy of their children and to prevent the inappropriate use of information about their children. Parents should instruct their children never to give out real names or any other identifying information over the Internet without first getting permission.

Cookies
Cookies are bits of electronic information which a website can transfer to a visitor's hard drive to help tailor and keep records of his or her visit at the site. Cookies allow website operators to better tailor visits to the site to the visitor's individual preferences. The use of cookies is standard on the Internet and many major web sites use them. You can choose to set your browser to notify you whenever you are sent a cookie. This gives you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. Please note, however, that 989 Studios does not presently use cookies. If 989 Studios were to use cookies, it would be to help provide its visitors with a better experience on our websites.

Links
989 Studios may create links to the websites of its promotional partners or affiliated parties. We will make every effort to link only to sites that meet similar standards for
maintaining each individual's right to privacy. However, many other sites that are not associated or authorized by 989 Studios may have links leading to our websites. 989 Studios cannot control these links and is not responsible for any content appearing on these sites.
