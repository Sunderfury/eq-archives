989 STUDIOS BRINGS IN THE HOLIDAYS WITH EXCITING GAME PROMOTIONS
Bust A Groove™ and Twisted Metal® III Videogame Promotions Are Currently Underway

FOSTER CITY, Calif., December 21, 1998 – 989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is spreading cheer this holiday season with nationwide Bust A Groove™ and Twisted Metal® III PlayStation® videogame promotions. Both promotions will run from December 1998 through January 1999.

Club-goers’ across the nation will be groovin’ with the official Bust A Groove "Club Night" promotion. DJs at popular dance clubs across the country will challenge entrants to "boogie-to-the-beat" in a dance contest at participating club venues. Participants in the Bust A Groove "Club Night" promotion will receive Bust A Groove promotional items and can enter-to-win a PlayStation game console, a copy of Bust A Groove and other valuable prizes. Sponsored in conjunction with top radio stations and dance clubs, the Bust A Groove "Club Night" promotion venues, participating radio stations and contest dates include: Club USA in Los Angeles, hosted by Power 106, on December 20th; Bahama Beach Club in Boston, hosted by Jam’n 94.5, on December 23rd; and, The Ice House in San Diego, hosted by Channel 93.3 on December 31st. Doors for the venues will open at 9:00 p.m.

Consumers can also satisfy their sweet tooth this holiday season with the Twisted Metal III "Sweet Tooth" Sweepstakes by entering-to-win the infamous Twisted Metal III pink polka dotted, combat-ready ice cream truck. The ice cream truck is a true-to-life replica of the "Sweet Tooth" ice cream truck in the Twisted Metal videogame series and will be awarded to the consumer whose entry card is drawn at random. Consumers can enter-to-win at participating retail outlets nationwide, including: Babbages, CompUSA, Fred Meyer, FuncoLand, Its All About Games and Montgomery Ward.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®.
