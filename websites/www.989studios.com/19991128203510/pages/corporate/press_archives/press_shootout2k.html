989 SPORTS GIVES THE PHOENIX SUNS A

BASKETBALL VIDEOGAME –
NBA SHOOTOUT 2000

NBA ShootOut 2000 Touted By NBA Players As
Most Authentic Basketball Videogame

WHAT:

989 Sports, a leader in sports videogames, is visiting the Phoenix Suns to give the team a sneak preview of the all-new version of its popular basketball videogame for the PlayStation® game console - - NBA ShootOut 2000. Available in November, NBA ShoootOut 2000, has a reputation for being the most realistic because of the involvement of pro players like Jason Kidd, Brevin Knight, Trajan Langdon, Charles "Bo" Outlaw, Robert Horry, Jerry Stackhouse, Latrell Sprewell and Paul Pierce (just to name a few). 989 Sports incorporates actual player movements into their videogames through motion capture technology, as well as enlisting numerous marquee players to consult on the NBA ShootOut 2000 playbook.

WHERE:

America West Arena
Pacific Room/Post Game Press Conference Room
12:00 p.m. – 1:30 p.m.
Friday, November 5, 1999
