PRICE DROPPED FOR EVERQUEST: MOST SUCCESSFUL
ONLINE ROLE-PLAYING GAME EVER
-- EverQuest Now Available for a Suggested Retail Price of $39.99 --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., December 7, 1999 - 989 Studios™, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, announced that it has lowered the suggested retail price (SRP) of its best-selling online role playing game EverQuest®, just in time for the holiday season. Effective today, EverQuest, which currently has an active subscriber base of more than 150,000 players, will be available for $39.99 SRP.

"In less than one year, EverQuest has become the most successful online role-playing game of all time, selling more than 250,000 units," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "With EverQuest now available for less than $40, holiday shoppers will be able to join the hundreds of thousands of players who currently enjoy the world of EverQuest."

A massive online role-playing game, which has hosted more than 40,000 simultaneous users, EverQuest features a multitude of adventures and quests with an interactive storyline that evolves according to a player’s individual decisions. In the game, players can explore three vast continents, with 70 adventure zones and 12 cities.

The Station@sony.com is the official provider of EverQuest, with software updates and news about the game, message boards and other community features, as well as an easy-to-use billing system for EverQuest’s monthly subscription fee of $9.89.

Verant Interactive is an independent development studio that develops cutting-edge online games. Based in San Diego, California, Verant Interactive has a staff of over 70 employees who consist of the development teams responsible for the online games Tanarus and EverQuest.

989 Studios – EverQuest Price Drop

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the entertainment software industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto®, Cool Boarders® and EverQuest®.
