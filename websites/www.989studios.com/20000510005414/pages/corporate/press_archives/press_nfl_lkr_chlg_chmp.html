***SATURDAY, JANUARY 29***

CHAMP BAILEY CROWNED
989 SPORTS’ NFL GAMEDAY 2000
LOCKER ROOM CHALLENGE VIDEOGAME CHAMPION

WHAT: Throughout the ’99 – ’00 NFL season, NFL teams participated in 989 Sports’ NFL GameDay™ 2000 "Locker Room Challenge™," which is a football videogame tournament for all NFL players. Earlier tonight, at the 989 Sports NFL Players Party in Atlanta, Georgia, the NFL GameDay 2000 "Locker Room Challenge" participants competed against each other to decide which NFL player was the NFL GameDay 2000 Champion.

The following eight NFL players competed in the NFL GameDay 2000 "Locker Room Challenge."

  • Champ Bailey, Washington Redskins (DB)
  • Charlie Batch, Detriot Lions (QB)
  • Jacquez Green, Tampa Bay Buccaneers (WR)
  • Derrick Alexander, Kansas City Chiefs (WR)
  • Warrick Dunn, Tampa Bay Buccaneers (RB)
  • Lawyer Milloy, New England Patriots (DB)
  • Damon Huard, Miami Dolphins (QB)
  • Randall Godfrey, Dallas Cowboys (LB)

Champ Bailey beat the following players to win the NFL GameDay 2000 Locker Room Challenge:

  • Round 1 Against Randall Godfrey (17-0)
  • Round 2 Against Damon Huard (2-0)
  • Round 3 Against Warrick Dunn (14-10)
