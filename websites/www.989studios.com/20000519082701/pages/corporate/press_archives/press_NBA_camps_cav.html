*BASKETBALL ALERT* * BASKETBALL ALERT*

TUESDAY, OCTOBER 12, 1999

989 SPORTS GIVES THE CLEVELAND CAVALIERS A SNEAK PREVIEW OF ITS
ALL-NEW BASKETBALL VIDEOGAME –
NBA SHOOTOUT 2000

NBA ShootOut 2000 Touted By NBA Players As
Most Authentic Basketball Videogame

WHAT:
989 Sports, a leader in sports videogames, is visiting the Cleveland Cavaliers to give the team a sneak preview of the all-new version of its popular basketball videogame for the PlayStation® game console - - NBA ShootOut 2000. Available in November, NBA ShootOut 2000, has a reputation for being the most realistic because of the involvement of pro players like Brevin Knight, Trajan Langdon, Jason Kidd, Charles "Bo" Outlaw, Robert Horry, Jerry Stackhouse, Latrell Sprewell and Paul Pierce (just to name a few). 989 Sports incorporates actual player movements into their videogames through motion capture technology, as well as enlisting numerous marquee players to consult on the NBA ShootOut 2000 playbook.

WHERE:
Gund Arena
Family Lounge (adjacent to the players’ locker room)
2:30 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.
Tuesday, October 12, 1999

BROADCAST OPPORTUNITIES:

  • Exclusive demo by 989 Sports of NBA ShootOut 2000
  • Cleveland Cavaliers playing NBA ShootOut 2000
  • Talk to the many Cleveland Cavaliers who are videogame fans
  • Behind-the-scenes making of NBA ShootOut 2000, including player interviews and NBA ShootOut 2000 gameplay footage (footage available upon request)
