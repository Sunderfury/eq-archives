SYPHON FILTER DOMINATES CHARTS AS
BEST SELLING PLAYSTATION TITLE FOR PAST EIGHT MONTHS

FOSTER CITY, Calif., October 26, 1999 - 989 Studios™, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, reported today that Syphon Filter™ has been the best selling videogame for the PlayStation® game console since its February 1999 launch, according to the most recent September TRSTS Report produced by the NPD Group.

"Syphon Filter is a prime example of how 989 Studios mixes innovative new titles with our existing lineup of industry-leading franchise titles," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "Syphon Filter has been the best-selling PlayStation title for the past eight months and will soon join the ranks with our other million sellers like NFL GameDay™ , Cool Boarders®, Twisted Metal® and Jet Moto®. All told, 989 Studios has more million-selling hit titles than any other PlayStation publisher."

The unique gameplay in Syphon Filter navigates players through interactive environments, dodging high-speed subways, deadly terrorists and apocalyptic explosions. Adding to the action is the carefully-designed third-person perspective that drops the player into Special Agent Gabriel Logan’s world. As operative Gabe Logan, the player’s mission is simple: stop an act of biological terrorism and infiltrate the organization responsible for creating the "Syphon Filter" virus.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®.
