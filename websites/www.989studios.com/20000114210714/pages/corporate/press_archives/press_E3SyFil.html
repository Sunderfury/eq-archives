SYPHON FILTER™ IS AN EXPLOSIVE THRILL RIDE WITH FAST-PACED ACTION AND SOPHISTICATED THIRD-PERSON GUNPLAY
989 Studios Propels Gamers Into the Deadly World of Counter-Terrorism

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 28, 1998 - 989 Studios announced today that Syphon Filter™, an exciting 3D action-adventure videogame, will be available in November exclusively for the PlayStation® game console. Syphon Filter features intricate gunplay and spine-tingling action in a fully-integrated 3D environment, providing gamers with a glimpse into the secret world of counter-terrorism.

Players must navigate through interactive environments, dodging high-speed subways and apocalyptic explosions. Adding to the action is the carefully designed third-person perspective that drops the player into Gabe Logan’s world with eye-opening realism.

"Syphon Filter is an enthralling videogame that combines heart-pounding gameplay with the hard-hitting action of a blockbuster movie and the suspense of a great spy novel," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "Players must use stealth maneuvering and expert marksmanship, all while making decisions on-the-fly to unravel the web of conspiracy."

As operative Gabriel Logan, the player’s mission is simple: stop an act of biological terrorism on U.S. soil and infiltrate the organization responsible for creating a virus that threatens to destroy mankind.

To help gamerplayers accomplish the mission, Syphon Filter features sophisticated third person gunplay and three targeting modes: default mode, manual mode and target lock mode. Gameplayers can also choose from an arsenal of weapons, including the sniper rifle which allows them to dispose of the enemy with one shot. Syphon Filter is a 989 Studios production, developed by Eidetic, Inc.

Syphon Filter Features
Real-world, real-time, open 3D environments

Vast environments to explore and brain twisting puzzles deliver the perplexity of real life split-second decision making

An arsenal of weapons include silenced pistols, infrared rifles, grenades, sniper rifles, tasers, shot guns and much, much more

The environments are not mere backgrounds; they are an integral part of the action. Subways come flying by at top speeds, glass shatters, street lamps blow out and explosions light up the sky

Carefully designed third person perspective

Advanced animations deliver fluid motion. Gabe can kneel, crouch, climb, roll, throw, pick up, walk, run and move across places hand-over-hand

A personal communicator device houses Gabe’s weapons inventory, mission information and game options

Awesome special effects add enormous detail: breathe in cold air and view the effects of gunshots, shattering glass and explosions

Features 989 Studios’ proprietary interactive midi sound engine: sound increases in intensity as action picks up


989 Studios, formerly known as Sony Interactive Studios America, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with a second development studio in San Diego, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™ and Twisted Metal™.
