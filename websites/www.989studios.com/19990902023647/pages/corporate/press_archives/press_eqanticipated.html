989 STUDIOS’ EVERQUEST™ BECOMES THE MOST ANTICIPATED ONLINE PC VIDEOGAME OF 1999
-- Retail Pre-sell Orders Have Already Reached Record Amounts --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., March 8, 1999 – 989 Studios announced today that its highly anticipated PC title EverQuest™ has broken industry record numbers for pre-sell orders and has become the number one PC pre-sold game on EBWorld.com. Heralded by game reviewers as an extraordinary evolution in online role-playing games, EverQuest features a multitude of adventures and quests that break the mold in the online role-playing game genre.

"We have already received phenomenal feedback on the pre-launch of EverQuest from all of our retailers and are obviously very pleased by the high volume of pre-sell orders," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "EverQuest is literally the most detailed and visually stunning role-playing game ever developed and since we are limiting the initial build of this game to ensure no server issues, consumers should take advantage of the pre-sell opportunities to make sure they can secure a copy of EverQuest before the retail launch on March 16."

The retail pre-sell promotions that began February 1 allows consumers to either pre-purchase or put a deposit on their copy of EverQuest at select retail outlets across the nation. To date, more than 8,000 consumers have reserved a copy of the game. With one week remaining before EverQuest’s retail launch, 989 Studios is estimating that the pre-sell list will exceed 10,000 units. After consumers have purchased EverQuest at retail, there will be a fee of $9.89 per month for access to the ever-changing world of EverQuest.
EverQuest allows thousands of gamers from all over the world to adventure solo, at great peril, or join forces with online allies to fight off enemies together. The Station@sony.com will be the official provider of EverQuest software updates and news about the game, message boards and other community features, as well as an easy-to-use billing system for EverQuest’s monthly subscription fee.

989 Studios, a Sony Computer Entertainment Group Company, is a wholly-owned division of Sony Computer Entertainment America Inc. and is the third-largest PlayStation® software publisher in North America. Headquartered in Foster City, California, with additional development studios in San Diego and Santa Monica, 989 Studios develops software for the PlayStation game console, personal computer and online markets. 989 Studios is a recognized leader in the videogame industry, with top franchise titles such as NFL GameDay™, Twisted Metal®, Jet Moto™ and Cool Boarders®
