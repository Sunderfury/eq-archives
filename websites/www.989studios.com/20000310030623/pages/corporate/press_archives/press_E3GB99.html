NCAA GAMEBREAKER™99: INCREDIBLE GRAPHICS & GAMEPLAY DELIVER THE BEST COLLEGE FOOTBALL VIDEOGAME ON CAMPUS
With Play-by-Play From Legendary Announcer Keith Jackson
To Capture True Feel Of College Football


FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 28, 1998 - 989 Studios announced today that NCAA GameBreaker™99, the third edition to the highly-touted college football videogame series, will be released this October. With beefed-up 3D graphics and legendary college football announcer Keith Jackson calling the play-by-play, NCAA GameBreaker ™99 sets the standard as the best NCAA football videogame for the PlayStation® game console.

NCAA GameBreaker ™99 features new 3D player and stadium models that generate incredible polygonal graphics and ultra realistic gameplay. Players are scaled to their actual height and weight, with motion capture animation utilizing Washington State grad Ryan Leaf, USC grad Jason Sehorn and Purdue grad Mike Alstott, to bring bone-jarring collisions and acrobatic catches to life. A new TV-style presentation features audio play-by-play by the voice of college football announcer Keith Jackson. "As in the real world of college football, NCAA GameBreaker ™99 includes numerous 'GameBreakers’ who can turn the game around every time they touch the ball," said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. "NCAA GameBreaker ™99 will excite gamers with all the realism and subtle details that make college football fans so fanatical. Plus, with Keith Jackson, all the realism of college football is in the game." The flavor of college football is evident throughout the game. All 112 Division I stadiums from "The Horse Shoe" at Ohio State to "The Swamp" in Gainesville are re-created in amazing detail. More than 2,200 plays are at gamers’ disposal and reflect characteristics of all NCAA Division I team playbooks. A new addition to NCAA GameBreaker ™99 is "Blue Chip Recruiting," which allows players to build their favorite team into a national championship contender. NCAA GameBreaker ™99 features superior gameplay to accompany its amazing graphics. "On the fly" defensive shifts and Total Control Passing™ allow gamers to respond to different game situations. The "Pummel" button makes a statement early in the game by giving gamers the ability to deliver hard-hitting tackles.

NCAA GameBreaker ™99 Key Features
New polygonal players scaled to actual height and weight

All 112 NCAA Division I stadiums are exact replicas created from their actual blueprints

Utilizes motion capture animation of eight former college standouts and now NFL Pro’s Ryan Leaf, Mike Alstott, Jason Sehorn and Hardy Nickerson

TV-style presentation with legendary Keith Jackson calling the play-by-play

Authentic collegiate play formations with 2,200 different plays designed by college coaches

True-to-life "GameBreakers" can change the outcome of a game every time they touch the ball. This year there’s twice the amount of GameBreakers!

Total Control Passing™ allows players to overthrow or underthrow receivers depending on the coverage

New "Pummel Button" enables players to launch a particularly vicious hit

"Blue Chip Recruiting" feature allows gamers to sign top recruits and build
national powers

Wind, snow and rain all affect gameplay, while uniforms show mud and grass stains depending on field conditions

Tracking of game and full season statistics in every major category
