989 SPORTS’ SUPERCROSS CIRCUIT BRINGS AUTHENTIC MOTORCROSS RACING TO THE PLAYSTATION GAME CONSOLE
-- SuperCross Circuit To Incorporate Fast-Action Gameplay, Premier In-Game Sponsors And Champion Motorcross And Supercross Racers --

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 13, 1999 - 989 Sports™ announced today that it will release SuperCross Circuit™ for the PlayStation® game console this November. Capturing the razor-sharp racing action of authentic Motorcross and Supercross racing, SuperCross Circuit will be developed based on official riders, tracks and dirt bikes.

Featuring some of the industry’s hottest Motorcross and Supercross racers, SuperCross Circuit’s champion racers to date include: Mike LaRocco, Larry Ward, Greg Albertyn, Ryan Hughes, Steve Lamson, John Dowd, Jeff Emig, Casey Johnson, Sebastien Tortelli and Jimmy Button. SuperCross Circuit will also feature authentic Motorcross and Supercross style racetracks that will add to SuperCross Circuit’s realistic racing action. Tracks in the game to date include: the Metrodome, Pontiac Silverdome and Sam Boyd Stadium.

"As with all 989 Sports titles, SuperCross Circuit will deliver unparalleled authenticity and realism," said Jeffery Fox, vice president, marketing. "With professional riders, licensed bikes, authentic tracks and unprecedented gameplay features, SuperCross Circuit will let gamers experience the fast-paced action of Motorcross racing from the safety of their couch."

SuperCross Circuit allows gamers to race like the pros on 16 challenging Supercross stadium tracks and 12 death-defying outdoor Motorcross courses. Using authentic Motorcross bikes, gamers will test their mettle by handling a 200-pound bike and navigating hairpin turns, bumps, straight aways and 80 mph free-rides. The game adds to the fast-paced racing experience by emulating real-life track conditions over the course of the race – wet courses dry with time, puddles accumulate water, and ruts emerge and enlarge during the race.

SuperCross Circuit Key Features

  • Champion Motorcross and Supercross racers include: Mike LaRocco, Larry Ward, Greg Albertyn, Ryan Hughes, John Dowd, Jeff Emig, Casey Johnson, Sebastien Tortelli, Jimmy Button, Doug Henry, Tim Ferry and Steve Lamson
  • Twenty authentic indoor and outdoor racing courses including: Glen Helen Raceway, High Point Raceway, Motocross 338, Red Bud Track & Trail, Unadilla Valley, Kenworthy’s Motocross Park, Washougal Motocross Park, Spring Creek Motocross Park, Broome-Tioga Sports Center, Steel City Raceway, Pontiac Silverdome, Metrodome, Qualcomm, Astrodome and Sam Boyd Stadium
  • Create or edit your own track with the revolutionary "Track Editor"
  • The game emulates real-life track conditions over the course of the race – wet courses dry with time, ruts enlarge during the race and puddles accumulate water
  • Technologically advanced physics engine recreates realistic dirt bike physics and handling
  • Features a Motorcross "Proving Ground" that allows racers to test their handling, jumping, passing and other important racing skills before competing in the Series Championship
  • Bikes feature real-time lighting with Gouroud shaded texture polygons that allow the bikes to show the accumulated effects of mud and physical damage during a race
  • Eleven bikes, each with their own handling and acceleration characteristics
  • Racers can customize their bikes with several types of tires, suspension settings and gear settings depending on track and racing conditions
