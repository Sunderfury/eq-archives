Anaheim Angel first baseman and spokesman for MLB™ 2000 Mo Vaughn was brought on board during the game's development process. Mo lent his batting and fielding movements to the game through a motion captures session. He also worked directly with the product development group in order to tweak the game's Artificial Intelligence. A big gamer himself, Mo brought to MLB 2000 that unique element of gamer and pro athlete that makes 989 Sports™ titles the most authentic sports gaming experience available on the PlayStation® game console.
