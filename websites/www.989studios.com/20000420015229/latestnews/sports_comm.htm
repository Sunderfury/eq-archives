Jason Kidd, John LeClair and Jim Harrick star in 989 Sports Brand TV Commercials:
In September, Phoenix Suns’ Point Guard Jason Kidd and University of Georgia Bulldog Head Basketball Coach Jim Harrick were in Los Angeles to be apart of the latest TV commercials from 989 Sports. One of the commercials features a quick post-game interview with Kidd. The 989 Sports anchor rushes up to Kidd and asks a question using his very best hip lingo, only to get nothing but a bewildered stare from Kidd. Embarrassed the anchor signals for the cameraman to cut. Jason Kidd is the cover athlete for NBA ShootOut 2000, and worked with 989 Sports during the development of the game (see above). The second commercial featured an exclusive interview with Jim Harrick. Unfortunately, it appears that Coach Harrick is unaware that he is live and makes some choice comments about the 989 Sports’ anchors. Coach Harrick worked with the Product Development team on NCAA® Final Four® 2000 (see below). The third commercial was shot in Philadelphia at the Flyers practice facility on October 5th. This spot features a post-game interview with John LeClair. Once the 989 Sports anchors cut to the interview, it is apparent that the 989 Sports anchor conducting the interview was not ready for it to begin. All three commercials are set to begin airing in late November and are very funny.
