NFL GAMEDAY™ '99 THE BEST SELLING FOOTBALL VIDEOGAME SERIES, COMES TO THE PC
989 Studios Goes Deep with New Football Release

FOSTER CITY, Calif., May 28, 1998 - 989 Studios announced today that the best-selling football videogame series for the PlayStation® game console will be available for the first time for PC gamers this September. NFL GameDay™ '99 will be the first PC pigskin game to feature 3D polygonal graphics, which provides an incredibly life-like NFL environment.

“Pro Athletes and videogame enthusiasts have raved about the NFL GameDay series since it first appeared on the PlayStation,” said Jeffrey Fox, vice president, marketing, 989 Studios. “ Similarly, NFL GameDay ™ '99 will set the new standard for football videogames on the PC. The realism that a 3D polygonal environment adds to gameplay is truly remarkable.”

Bringing the NFL experience to PC owners, NFL GameDay ™ '99 incorporates amazing graphics, unparalleled football realism and superior gameplay. New 3D players capture the true appearances of hundreds of NFL stars. Every subtle and personalized move has been motion captured in amazing detail as ball carriers put their hand down on the turf to regain their balance, linebackers hand out vicious tackles and receivers make spinning one-handed catches.

Revolutionary stadium models were created from actual blueprints with each stadium’s character brought to life. A TV-style presentation incorporates player and coach “zoom-ins” and “cut-aways” as well as commentary by renowned sportscasters Dick Enberg and Phil Simms.

Players can choose from all 30 NFL teams with updated rosters and take their squad through an entire season culminating with the Super Bowl™ and Pro Bowl™. Gameplay features a realistic simulator that utilizes advanced artificial intelligence making the computer a formidable opponent, while an enhanced Total Control Passing™ gives players even more offensive command.

Another NFL GameDay ™ '99 feature exclusive to Windows™95 gameplayers is the ability to connect to the SISA.NET match-up service at no charge. By connecting to SISA.NET, 989 Studios’ free match-up service, players can challenge other NFL GameDay ’99 owners from around the world.

NFL GameDay™ '99 PC System Requirements

CPU: Intel Pentium® 166 required. Intel Pentium® 200+ recommended with a 3D graphics accelerator card

Memory: 32 MB RAM required

CD-ROM: 4X CD-ROM required, 8X recommended

Sound Card: 100 percent Sound Blaster compatible

DirectX: Supports Microsoft DirectX 5.0™ drivers

3D Graphics Card: Supports Microsoft Direct3D™ and Voodoo Graphics™ by 3Dfx Interactive™

Input Devices: DirectInput compatible devices supported. Microsoft Sidewinder® Gamepad

Modem: 28.8 Kpbs required, 33.6 Kpbs or higher recommended

Internet Play: Internet access required for internet play
