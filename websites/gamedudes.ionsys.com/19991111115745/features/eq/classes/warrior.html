The warrior class, affectionately known as the "tank", is an essential part of any group. With more hit points and melee skills than any other class, the warrior is at the forefront of every party, taking the damage to protect the weaker casters from harm. Capable of using all armor and weapons (with the exception of some class-specific magic items), the warriors can outfit themselves as they choose.

Barbarian: Product of the extreme north, barbarians are a hardy race with some excellent strengths for the warrior class.

Dark Elf: The Dark Elf has the lowest strength and stamina of all above races. Not a recommended choice.

Dwarf: Dwarves make excellent fighters, other than the occasional flying beast he may be too short to reach

Half Elf: A decent, but not superb choice. There are no glaring weaknesses, but no obvious strengths either.

Human: much the same as the half elf, the human is neither a good or bad choice.

Halfling: again, not the best choice for a warrior-type due to low strength and stamina compared to other available options.

Ogre: overwhelming strength is the ogre. s claim to fame. It can be difficult to play due to being an evil race, but is still a superb choice for a warrior.

Troll: Very high warrior stats combined with the high regeneration rate of all trolls makes this perhaps the best choice of all, if you don. t mind being shunned in many cities.

Wood Elf: Yet another fairly weak choice for a warrior due to low strength and stamina.
