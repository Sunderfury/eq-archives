Portfolio

Problem Cause Solution
Demographic Information, Academic Records, or other Official University Data is not available. Peoplesoft is down Some of the select lists are populated with data from Peoplesoft. If Peoplesoft is unavailable—generally 4 - 6 am Monday - Saturday and before 12:30 pm on Sunday—then this data will not populate.
Some or all advisees are not listed in the Advisor View, or a advisor does not have the "advisor view" button in the View section. The advisee/advisor relationship are not set up in Peoplesoft. Some one in the college office needs to make the the advisor the "advisor of record" for the missing student(s) in Peoplesoft. This can't be changed within Portfolio.
Unable to log in even though the Internet ID and password are correct. The Internet ID is not a student or a staff/faculty account - an account with an associated student or employee ID in Peoplesoft. Only students and staff or faculty members have regular access to the portfolio system. Other people may get guest access if information has been shared with them.
