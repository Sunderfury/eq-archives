Playstation NTSC

These are the materials you need to provide for a Playstation NTSC T/O. All forms are to be filled out by the Developer/Producer unless noted otherwise.

  • 1 VHS Gameplay Tape
  • Symbol File (From the developer)
  • Instruction Manual
  • All available screen text
  • All available cheat codes
  • ESRB Rating Certificate
  • Software Submission Form Downlaod (sftsub.zip 117KB)
  • Global Technical Requirements checklist v. 1.5 (filled out from section 6.0 to 20.4) Download (trc15.zip 260KB)
  • QA Guidelines checklist Download (chklist.zip 16KB)
  • Assessment Report (If a resubmission)

Playstation PAL

These are the materials you need to provide for a Playstation PAL T/O. All forms are to be filled out by the Developer/Producer unless noted otherwise.

  • 2 VHS Gameplay Tape (in PAL format)
  • Symbol File (From the developer)
  • 2 copies of the Instruction Manual
  • 2 copies of all available screen text (in all applicable languages)
  • 2 copies of all available cheat codes
  • Global Technical Requirements checklist v. 1.5 (filled out from section 6.0 to 20.4) Download (trc15.zip 260KB)
  • SCEE Publishers QA Checklist
