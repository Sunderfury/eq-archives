test Turnover Requirements

      Before a title can be submitted for Turnover there are materails that need to be supplied. The materials needed varry depending on platform and territory. All paperwork needs to be filled out and all other materials need to be supplied prior to sending a turnover/submission request. All materials should be sent directly to a Turnover Technician. Without these materails a title can not be submitted to the third party liceser until all required materials are ready.
