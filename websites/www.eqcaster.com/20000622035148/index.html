Welcome to EqCaster!

I am Vaslin Slickfinger, level 42 Scout of Tunare on Tarew Marr. Being a sneaky rogue as I am, I was very curious about poisons and how they worked. After many months of decyphing data, I finally had a complete list of poisons and their effects on creatures. But another friend of mine, Dreald the Magician, had some more questions about his new spells, and once again, I dug down and grabbed some magician information as well.

And I decided to share this information with all of Norrath! So, for everyone that practices the fine art of the arcane, I give you EqCaster. A wonderful little utility program that will show you complete spell information and weapon effects for all the spells of this land.

If you would like to comment on an incorrect spell, please write me at vaslin@eqcaster.com and I'll see what I can do. There is certainly a myriad of spells and making sure that the data is correct is very difficult. Most of the information must be assumed and of course, I'm only a wood elf.
