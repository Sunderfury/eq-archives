Welcome to EqCaster!

I am Vaslin Slickfinger, level 53 Scout of Tunare on Tarew Marr. Being a sneaky rogue as I am, I was very curious about poisons and how they worked. After many months of decyphing data, I finally had a complete list of poisons and their effects on creatures. But another friend of mine, Dreald the Magician, had some more questions about his new spells, and once again, I dug down and grabbed some magician information as well.

And I decided to share this information with all of Norrath! So, for everyone that practices the fine art of the arcane, I give you EqCaster. A wonderful little utility program that will show you complete spell information and weapon effects for all the spells of this land.

I also present EqOption! A downloadable program that will edit your color and default settings automatically!!
