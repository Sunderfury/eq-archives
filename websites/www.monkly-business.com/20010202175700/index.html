    January 31, 2001.

    Monk Stuff
    Submitted by Zadkiel

      No update today, just some Monk news.

      Absor opened the thread on whineplay on Monday (2 days early) and it has been busy indeed. A compilation post from the messageboards here was posted, and a lot of support was given to it.

      Unfortunately, the way the EQ board system works means that giving a link is difficult, as they lock each thread as it reaches 250 posts, so you might find this link is to a locked thread, but anyway, click here to view the current thread as of today.

      On page 3 of this thread, Absor posts his first set of replies, and.... I have to say, I am dissapointed. The man seems to have NO CLUE as to what a monk is, what Verants own 'vision' is for us even, he freely admits this:

        I'll have to find out what the, um.., Vision™ is for this class

      Whats up with that? This is the man charged with sorting through hundreds and hundreds of posts about the monk class, identifying the wheat from the chaff, and presenting the ones he decides are worthy of mention to the tuning team.

      So, what does he pick out? Someone suggested monks should get critical hits on our throwing (doh!)...

        Interesting idea. This can also be used to increase monks damage a bit. That it, IF it needs to be increased. But if anyone is going to be the master of throwing, it may as well be monks.

      Not only does that show a clear ignorance about how monks work, but if I was a rogue reading that, knowing this guy was in charge of their class too, that would scare me.

      On a more fun note, It is the final of the Monk 'Best of the Best' tournament this Sunday, starting at 12pm PST. Yours truly has been granted permission to attend as a representative of this site, so expect an article early next week with full pictures and commentary.
