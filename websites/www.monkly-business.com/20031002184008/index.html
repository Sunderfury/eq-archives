    Vhoghul Sends me Mp3's of some of the Forum questions
    Submitted by Lenardo on 9/29/2003 12:51:41 PM

      Thank Vhoghul for those files-if anyone else has the WHOLE thing send em to me so i can listen to the overall tone of the forums-which i heard was fairly angry. Now as to the Details of said files:

      Warriors & aggro generation/Taunt:

      The eq team is almost ashamed that the issue has not been resolved and addressed, but it will be DEFINATELY addressed when the melee imbalance fix is implemented.

      Melee imbalance

      Again the EQ team apologized for letting the issue take so long BUT they said they have a team working on it now and it will be addressed soon. They are looking at revamping disciplines etc like the "future plans" says. whether or not it is enough waits for the changes to be implemented.

      Itemization

      Current itemization in eq benefits the hybrid more than the pure classes. they will look into it and might change the way they do itemization to make pure melee items.. IMO they should.

      The Line of Sight code for EQ is being completely Rebuilt as I type this.

      "Pure" Instancing (LDoN style) for the Plane of Time

      Currently is not being looked into, that MIGHT change in the future.

      BARDS - Fading Memories might be changed, to what exactly, they are not sure, According to the panel(S.H. said it) the team that designed the AA skill did not anticipate the tool it would develop into.

      According to Kinma at the fan faire it was Revealed that they can set FD success rates according to a ZONE, so you actually CAN fail more in XX zone than YY zone, and it is INTENTIONAL.

      As i get more Info i'll post it.
