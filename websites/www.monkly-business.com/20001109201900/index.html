    November 3, 2000.

    Monk Epic Quest Complete on Test Server
    Submitted by Zadkiel

      Reports have come in that the epic quest has been completed on Test. Some people doubt the validity of the information, but I have my reasons to believe it as true.

      Here is the big scoop, the final item is NOT a weapon, it's a pair of gloves.

        Celestial Fists
        AC: 15
        STR +20, STA +10, AGI +10, DEX +10, HPs +100, Sv vs Disease +10, Sv vs Poison +10, Sv vs Magic +10, Sv vs Cold +10, Sv vs Fire +10
        Effect: Celestial Tranquility
        Weight 0.1

      The good news is that being gloves, the particle effect is on both fists. I dont have a picture as yet, but reports are it's a nice looking effect.

      The bad news is that it isn't a weapon. Rogues and Warriors both got weapons that increased their damage output by a decent amount. Seeing as most monks 50+ will be max hasted from being grouped with enchanters/bards most of the time anyway, the haste effect is largly meaningless, so all we get is some AC, HPs and Stats, effectively a minimal (from the str) increase in damage output.

      2 notes need to be made:

      1. The quest is still not completable on the live servers, and will not be until the next patch

      2. Before you go totally mad over this, lets wait until the effect is fully tested. Yes I know what the effect does as listed in the spdat.eff file (12ac, 40str, 40% haste) but Verant have played games before, the effect may be completely different, lets wait and see.
