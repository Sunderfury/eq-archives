    Lenardo on Planetside
    Submitted by Lenardo on 3/31/2003 4:01:19 PM

      BARF..

      Seriously though, this game looks fun but unfortunately I get motion sickness and FPS type games tend to make me ill. Spiritual, Baelish & I walked in on the tail end of the planetside demo (we were on break from eqlive forum 1- and wanted to hit the eq2 forum) where 2 of the dev's were playing and the third was answering questions. The forum went over by ~20 minutes.

      I was impressed with the clean graphics, the fast game play, the smooth animations of the pc's it was all very impressive and it looked like it is going to be a lot of fun to play, especially if you are into Co-operative team play fps games.

      Unfortunately due to motion sickness susceptibility I doubt that I will be trying the game beyond any beta testing I might do. They are about to go into open beta and put the stress test on, but they are looking for each server to have 3-5 thousand people playing at a time.
