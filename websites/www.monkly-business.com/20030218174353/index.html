    Snow Days
    Submitted by Lenardo on 2/18/2003 11:18:46 AM

      Gotta love Snow Days - the East Coast got slammed sunday and monday and i got to spend lots of time outside removing the ~2 feet of snow from my driveway. and to shovel a spot for my dogs to "go" since they are shetland sheep dogs and stand abit over 1 foot high...

      So i Spent yesterday on Test, first leveling my Froggie, then - lo and behold a GM ... i sent a tell- pardoning the intrusion and inquired as to the possibility of them doing template chars(i was polite - ie i was sick of doing newbie crap). and got my beta char(55th monk-FEMALE though). had fun with said gm as we worked on stuff in that GM's zone. nice looking zones, interesting npc models. You are going to be HOWLING at the undead troll females once you see them in action for the first few times... its also fun to be with the zone designer and critique/make suggestions as you kill things in his zone.

      With a week to go until this goes live, i'll be spending more and more time Delving into the depths of this area, i like it so far but being kos is a pain, which is why i am a monk. sneak and invis baby...hmm wonder if i can get in good with the pirates so i can save on the invisibility potion purchases.(no that is not a hint i just don't know - Yet)
