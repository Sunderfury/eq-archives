Immortal Charter

 I created Immortal for the sole purpose of having fun and to create a good playing environment for not only my guild members, but for the Xegony community as well. As the guild expands we will do our best to uphold this "fun" clause for everyone involved but it will get ever more difficult. Unfortunately times will arise when this will not be the case. It is in these times when we as a guild will do our best to come to some sort of ending on the situation. Each situation will be dealt with as a separate entity. I will handle each account as civilized as I can, and since I am the  judge, I will do my best to listen to both sides of the argument. My decisions will be based on a per situation basis. Some "like" problems will have completely different outcomes and punishments. If this is the case, do not quote me on anything. I am a human, and I reserve my right to change my mind and opinion as I see fit. Sometimes I feel like a nut, sometimes I don't.

As the leader of Immortal I am bound by my creation and will be thwarted at every turn. I will do my best to make the correct decisions along this road called life, but have patience with me for I too will make mistakes. I am not perfect, nor do I claim to be; but I  will do my "best" to make the "best" decisions I can. Sometimes I will make the wrong decision, and sometimes I will make a the right one, but in the end I feel that they will all be more positive than negative. When I do oops, I soon will  know and will learn how to avoid this problem or solve it better in the future. Some cases will have no right answer. It is at this time that I will also do my best to chose the lesser of two evils.

They say that your only as good as the company that you keep. If  this is the case then I'm a King. My officers and I have done our best to keep this guild full of good people. People that would help others in time of need without any thought about the consequences of  what may become.

Agla Magus Sator
Founder
