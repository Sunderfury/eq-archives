Are YOU Immortal?

Immortal has been a guild of exceedingly high standards of conduct since our Founding by Agla almost 18 months ago. In everything we do, we strive to represent ourselves well. And when we start debating amongst ourselves on prospective new guild members, the same ethical standards to which we hold ourselves are our first and most essential criteria.

Immortal recruits very selectively because we are like family. Our doors are by no means closed to prospective members, we just like to get a feel for you first, so we know and so you know if Immortal is for you.

If you’d like to be Immortal, just make yourself known to us. Email Silvana here, visit our message boards with the link to the left and make a post, and take a look at the officers on the members page so you can contact them in game. We look forward to hearing from you.
