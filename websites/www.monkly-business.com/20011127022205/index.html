    Luclin requirements ramped up
    Submitted by Yang on 11/24/2001 11:53:16 PM

      Well well. Our buddy Absor posted the new system requirements to get the full force of Shadows of Luclin, and boy are they harsh:

        During final testing of EverQuest: The Shadows of Luclin, set for release December 4th, we have found it necessary to increase the minimum specifications for your hardware platform. Here we will explain how these changes may affect your system's ability to run this expansion. The new specifications are listed below:

        Required Specs:
        Windows® 98/2000/ME/XP
        Pentium® II 400Mhz or greater
        256 MB RAM
        16 MB Direct3D compliant video card and hardware T&L (i.e. Nvidia GeForce/GeForce 2)
        DirectX 8.0 compatible sound card
        28.8 K+ Internet connection
        4X speed CD-ROM
        450 MB+ hard drive space

        Note: Although technically below the minimum specs, players with 128 MB of RAM will still be able to run Luclin by turning off the new character models.

        Recommended Specs:
        Windows® 98/2000/ME/XP
        Pentium® III or greater
        512 MB RAM
        32 MB Direct3D compliant video card and hardware T&L (i.e. Nvidia GeForce 2 or greater)
        DirectX 8.0 compatible sound card
        56.6 K+ Internet connection
        16X speed CD-ROM
        1.5 GB+ hard drive space
        Note: DirectX 8.0 or greater is required. System requirements subject to change as the game progresses.

      You can see more detailed reasons for the requirements HERE. Looks like I'll need a new video card...at least I've got the RAM covered, sigh.

      -Yang
