    Nvidia & Ati introduce new Cards
    Submitted by Lenardo on 10/24/2003 10:56:50 AM

      Both Nvidia and ATI have introduced new cards that are or will be available in the coming week or so.

      Ati has Introduced the XT version of the 9600 and 9800 both are very Minor upgrades to the Pro series. so if you are looking for a new card by ati now is the time to look at the dropping prices of the 9600 and 9800 pro.

      Nvidia has introduced 2 new cards the fx 5950 ultra and the fx 5700 ultra. While the 5950 is a minor upgrade to the high end (Gainward has a version that is a good 10% faster(it is liquid cooled)), the 5700 ultra in most benchmarks is a significant upgrade to the 5600 ultra. and takes over the "mainstream" segment very well for that company. both the nvidia 5700 ultra and the 9600 xt score Very similarly in most benchmarks- ati does fsaa better, nvidia does anthro better.

      Nvidia has Also introduced the successor to the Detonator Driver Series - The Forceware Driver Series Version 52.16 is now Released and Available for Download from Nvidia.

      As a side note remember a few months ago over the flak Nvidia got over optimizing its drivers for benchmark programs?

      WELL, apparently ATI's latest drivers(3.8) are being investigated for the same thing(for aquamark i believe and possible unreal)using default 16 bit textures when the higher res ones are requested by the benchmark...Nvidia's current drivers reflect their new "no benchmark specific optimization" while showing probably the best image quality that they have in recent memory.

      Also S3 and XGI are going to be releasing Their new Graphics cards shortly so the winter is going to be VERY fun for the Video card upgrade department.

    Doom 3 to be Framerate CAPPED
    Submitted by Lenardo on 10/23/2003 2:11:13 PM
