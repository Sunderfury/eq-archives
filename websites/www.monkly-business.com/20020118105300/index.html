    Guides and Tactics Instructions changed
    Submitted by Bodi on 1/17/2002 8:33:54 AM

      I have changed the 'rules' for posting in Guides and Tactics slightly, and I just want you all to be aware.

        Submission Guidelines Jan 2002

        If you would like to submit an article to the site, or have any comments on existing content, then email them HERE.

        Guides MUST be posted here first before they are submitted to the main site, allowing you the guide writer to add comments passed on by other players.

        We would like guides to follow a simalar format, with samples shown below. If your guide (more related to Hunting guides) already exsists, PLEASE DO NOT POST A SEPERATE one. Post it within the same topic.

        eg. 'Solo guide: Quelious Monk 1 to 30' written by Zinj, does not require a seperate 'Solo guide: Quelious Monk 1 to 30' written by Bodi, no matter how much he likes to see his name in lights.

        Sample Thread headings:

        Solo Guide: xxxxxx Monk (level) to (level)
        Level Guide: (Continent): (level) to (level)
        Quest Guide: -Quest-

        THIS FORUM IS HEAVILY MODERATED !

        Do not complain if your post has been moved or even deleted if you do not read these instructions. Bumps and questions will be deleted or incorporated into threads depending on relevance.

        No content commentary will also be deleted from posts here, and also the cropping of peoples sigs within threads (one per person and possibly only the person who created the thread), and large ones to be culled to allow easier printing.

        If you find your hints and tactics posts 'missing'; its very likely a Moderator has consolidated a parallel thread. (ie you have posted the same subject as someone else but have not checked back to see whether it has been done before.) This is because all like information placed in one thread is a lot easier to find and to use than digging through a couple hundred different more or less identical threads. So, if you're going to post, please check to see if someone hasn't posted it already, or if it can go under a thread already opened on that topic.

        This forum is delibaratly not Read Only, as sometimes people post worthy extra information to the original post, so will remain open to all to add further comment.

        Do Not Ask Questions Here

        Please obey the board rules and only post guides here. If you have a question to ask, please ask it in the relevant forum, NOT in here.

        Other information

        This forum is for people to post guides on how to do something --rasing a trade skill quickly, raise difficult factions, recomendations for equipment/resist gear/luggage, what quests to do when.

        And a final word to say that Your post could be deleted or moved, if in the wrong forum, depending upon the whim of the Moderator at the time.

        On Behalf of
        Monkly Business Admin and Moderators.

        Note: This board is in 'construction' and may look more messy than it should as I try and consolidate and condense everything, sorry for any inconvience caused.

      I hope at the end of this, we will have a Guides and Tactics forum will be easily searched (as we all know EZBoards is crap) to find what you need.

    Ghoulbane and Soulfire to be CHANGED
    Submitted by Lenardo on 1/15/2002 3:05:37 PM

      From the Everquest Developers forum: from the looks and tone of the post, its probably going to be retroactive as well.

        We have reviewed Ghoulbane and SoulFire and will be making some changes to these items with the next patch.

        Ghoulbane will become No Drop and SoulFire will require the proper Race/Class to activate.

        We wanted to make you aware of these changes before we made them. These weapons are really intended to be holy weapons, and these changes should make them exclusive to Paladins. We will also be reviewing the quest to make certain that it is working after the patch.

        Alan
