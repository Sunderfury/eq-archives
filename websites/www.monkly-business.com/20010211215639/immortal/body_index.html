Kelorek`Dar Gets His Turn

1-22-2001 11:25 PM PST

The Usual Suspects (Immortal, JD, CoV) got together again today, this time to take out the smelly sea beast in Cobalt Scar. We promptly relieved him of his head, which can be seen below.

As it turns out, he did a pretty decent job of stiffing us on the good loot, but we had a good time. We’ll come back again soon to sample the other goods and services the big lug so generously provides. Drops included 3 Kalorek`Dar Spine Razors, a Sea Dragon Scale, and 4 spells.

Shortly after the raid, a few of us were seen sporting some new neon-lime breastplates, courtesy of the late Wuoshi, may God rest his soul, Amen.

~Kite Forprez

Wuoshi Eats Our Dust

1-21-2001 10:25 PM PST

While completing the Phenocryst Crystal quest for Dantes and Graffe, we decided to launch a spur of the moment raid on the Bane of the Wandering Lands, Wuoshi.

So after summoning 28 brave souls to the scene, Immortal, Jade Dragon, Circle of Vengeance, and Undaunted showed old Wuoshi the business as only we can. Congrats to the winners of the 3 scales, 3 daggers, and 2 spells. The fight was wild and fun, and we’ll be doing it again with our friends real soon.

~Kite Forprez
