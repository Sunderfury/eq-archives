Name: Orc Slaver
Location: Crushbone
Loot: Slave keys - rusty weapons - up to a
couple Gold
Level: 9-12
Faction: +AllizeVolew
-AllizeTaeew
Abilities: None
Description
These guys terrorize the slaves in Crushbone and will sometimes kill them. They drop the keys
that will free the slaves and if you give a slave a copper or something they will tell you which
key they need. Their boss is the Orc Taskmaster which can be much tougher to defeat - but he
is a rare spawn so you probably won't see him.
