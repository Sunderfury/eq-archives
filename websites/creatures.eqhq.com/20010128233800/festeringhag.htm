Description
This is one of the deadliest creatures I have ever encountered. If you do not take them
out right away - their spells will blow you away in a couple hits. If their are two together -
the puller is in deep trouble as you can die in as few as 10 seconds. Three is death -
as simple as that - you will be toast the second they start casting. Their weakness is
they have few HP so just hammer them with nukes and you can drop them quicker than
they can drop you. Gossimer Robe is nice but a very rare drop.
