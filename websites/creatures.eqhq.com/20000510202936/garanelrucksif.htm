Description
The one time owner of the Estate of Unrest you will find him deep in the basement of the house with a
long path of tough critters surrounding him. His loot is great though and he either drops a Jagged Band
- or rarely Dwarven Work Boots. The boots are nice and can be sold for around 1000pp. It's hard to get
him all by himself so be prepared to take a hag- gdb- or terrors with him.
