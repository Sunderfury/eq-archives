Location: Unrest

Level: 10-13
Faction: +AllizeVolew

Abilities: None
Description
These can be VERY annoying in Unrest. They tend to train easily if you are just out of their
XP range and can be a real pain if you get too many on you at once. Depending on when
you first go to Unrest this can be one of the first things you fight.
