Name: Large Skunk
Location: Butcherblock
Loot: Skunk Scent Gland
Level: 3-4
Faction: +AllizeVolew
-AllizeTaeew
Abilities: None
Description
Once you get to level 3 or so you should be able to take these easily.
Best for the semi-rare scent glands they drop which are worth a couple
gold which can seem like a fortune back then. Watch out for the Skunk Spray
which will lower your charisma. Don't try to buy or sell anything until this effect
wears off.
