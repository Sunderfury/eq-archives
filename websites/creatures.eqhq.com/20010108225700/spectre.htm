Description
Nasty creatures who can hit for a lot of damage in a short period of time. Their only downfall
is that they are VERY slow and can be easily outrun. They usually gather in numbers so you
will have to deal with more than one. If you can just stay out of range and cast on them you'll
be able to kill them and get great XP.
