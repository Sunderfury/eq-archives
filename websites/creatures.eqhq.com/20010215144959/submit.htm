Submission Guidelines
Keep in mind the following things when submitting information and pictures:

1. If you're sending more than one picture at once, PLEASE try to ZIP them
up so I only have to download one file at once.

2. Take pictures in 800x600 resolution or higher, anything less and the quality
degrades.

3. Try to have a light source in the picture. Use a lightstone of other item if it is
dark outside so the viewers can clearly see the monster.

4. Try to get just the monster in the picture close up from the front. This is not
always possible but something to shoot for.

5. Always send the information you know with the pictures and only send
information in on monsters if you have the pictures of them.

6. Convert the pictures to JPGs and try to cut out the monster so there is nothing
else in the picture. This is not required but it will help me out tremendously and
I will have a lot less work that I have to do.

7. Send all pictures and information to chrono91@aol.com.
