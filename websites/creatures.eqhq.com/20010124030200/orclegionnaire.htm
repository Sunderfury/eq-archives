bracers and shoulderpads - cloth armor

Description
These span more levels than most creatures so make sure you con them first. Generally the ones
inside the castle are the tougher ones and the ones that spawn around the slaver area are easier.
The ones in Lesser Faydark spawn in groups around camps and are in the middle of the pack
difficulty wise. I think there is a Dwarven quest that involves the shoulderpads but I'm not sure..
