レベル44のスペルTashaniaはQuestをこなすことによりゲットできます。
TashaniaはMobのSV Magic-65の、ENCにとっては欠かせない魔法です。

Temple of Solusek RoにいるRomar Suntoと話しCoinPouchを貰いましょう

hail Romar Sunto.

I am Romar Sunto, servant of Solusek Ro. I am searching for the [Coin] of the mighty enchanter [Tash] - if you are interested in helping me to acquire it, I will [reward] you with a powerful spell.
I am also dispenser of [Darkwood Staves] - sometimes also called the Staff of Ro.

what is coin?

Tash had a collection of ten antique silver coins that were left in different cities across the world.
If you were to collect all 10 coins, I would give you a [Coin Pouch] that would let you combine them into the maser Coin of Tash.
If you are interested, I will sell you a copy of the Tome of Tash, detailing where the coins were reportedly left, for a mere 50 gold.

what is coin pouch?

I will lend you this coin pouch ・put all 10 antique silver coins into it and combine them into the Coin of Tash.

what is tash?

Tash was a mighty enchanter and master jewelcrafter.

what is reward?

I will reward you with a scroll for the spell Tashania ・for personal use, of course.

10 SlotのCoinPouchを貰えたはずです。

以下はコインのある場所。Ak'Anon以外は全て水の中に落ちています

※変身しただけではguardに見破られることがあるのでInvisiを併用することをおすすめします。

10個揃ったらCombineし、「Coin of Tash」にします。

Coin of Tashにしたら、3箇所でEnchantして貰いましょう

1　HighKeep図書館にいるTarn Visilinに渡してEnchantしてもらう＞Glowing Coin of Tash
2　Neriak図書館にいるMizr N'Marに渡してEnchantしてもらう＞Gleaming Coin of Tash
3　Erudin図書館にいるRaine BeteriaにEnchantしてもらう＞Radiant Coin of Tash

Radiant Coin of TashをTemple of Solusek RoにいるRomar Suntoに返却すると、Tashaniaが貰えます。
