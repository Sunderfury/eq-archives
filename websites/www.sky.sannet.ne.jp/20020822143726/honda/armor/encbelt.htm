Sphinx Hair Cord (AC: 4 CHA+5 INT+5 SV DISEASE+10 SV COLD+10 SV FIRE+10)

島に居るMobを倒すとCoreやItemをDropします。
ENC用のCore1つ、Item2つをゲットしたらQuest Roomに行ってみましょう。
各島へ移動するにはMobから出るItemをKeyにするわけですが、Quest RoomへのKeyは1st Isleに居るKey Masterが売っています。
EncのQuestをくれるEffは名前にEnchanterってついてるのでわかりやすいです(w


'Hail, Enchanter Jolas'
Enchanter Jolas says 'Greetings. Have you come here to test your powers of enchantment? '

'I want test powers of enchantment'
Enchanter Jolas says 'I am most honored to be able to help you. Please choose from one of my instructors, Lelulean or Enderbite. '

'I am choose lelulean'
Enchanter Jolas says 'Lelulean it shall be. Take this book and read it. When you are finished, hand it back to me and I shall summon Enchantress Lelulean to test you. '

Enchanter JolasからBookを貰え、返却します
 Enchanter Jolas says 'Farewell. '

LeluleanがPOPします
Lelulean says 'I am here to do your bidding, Enchanter Spirit! '

'Hail, Lelulean'
Lelulean says 'Welcome. I am Enchantress Lelulean. Do you wish to test your powers? '

'I wish test my powers'
Lelulean says 'Great, let us waste no more time! I have three tests from which you can choose. They are illusion, metamorphism, and deception. '

'I wish to test of illusion'
Lelulean says 'Illusion it is. Proceed upward through the sky and return to me a crimson tessera, a darkstone emerald, and a finely woven cloth cord. This will prove your abilities to me and I will reward you with a cord of sphinx hair. '

Leluleanにcrimson tessera、darkstone emerald、finely woven cloth cordを渡します
Lelulean says 'Good work. I hope you find this as useful as I find the items you retrieved for me. '
You gain experience!!
