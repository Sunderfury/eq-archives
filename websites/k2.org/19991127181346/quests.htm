Temple of Solusek Ro Quests:

The Harvester: Vurgo will make you a Harvester. You must first bring him a shadowed scythe from a shadow man. He will then ask you to get a fungus eye from the Mortuary Fungus in Unrest (Basement Fire Pits) , a shadowed knife from the goblin headmaster in the OOT(Oracle Island Headhunter PH), and a fire opal(55PP in the Temple). 

Necromancer Shadowbound Armor: Syllina will make shadowbound armor for necromancers. She makes 3 kinds: boots, gloves and a robe. In addition, she will tell you how to make shadow silk for 50gp. This is made through the tailoring skill, so you either have to learn tailoring or have to buy it from a tailor. I keep getting conflicting reports on which sceptor she wants for which quest. I think that it is set to vary randomly from person to person and she will ask for a different sceptor each time. Best bet is to ask her first. The scepters need to be identified to see which one they are, and they seem to appear in both Mistmoore and Guk. I have also been told that you need to give her the items in the order she lists them, but can't confirm this. 

    ShadowBound Boots: shadow silk(Combine 1 silk swatch(Combine 2 silks), 1 gather shadows scroll, 1 shadow wolf pelt), sceptre Mistmoore, eye of shadow (from shadow men), and a skeletal toe (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns);


    ShadowBound Gloves: inky silk(Combine 1 silk swatch(Combine 2 silks), 2 gather shadows scroll, 1 shadow wolf pelt), sceptre from Lower Guk, hand of shadow (from shadow men), and skeletal finger (from giant skeletons in rathe mntns); 

    Robe of Enshroudment: large shadow silk(combine 1 silk swatch(Combine 2 silks), 1 gather shadows scroll, 2 shadow wolf pelt), werebat wing (from unrest), mask of shadow (shadowmen), and globe of shadow (Nokta Shaman in Guk Spawns Right of the Back LG zoneline down the 2 planks (Vis Shaman is the PH)). 

NOTE: You seem to be able to use the eye of shadow in place of the fungus eye.  Shadow Wolves roam EC quite frequently.

Pet Word Quest

You can get 12 words from the 3 Sisters (X`Ta Tempi, Timpi, and Tompi) in Neriak Foreign Quarters (-10, -45).

Tempi will give you the following words:

Possession: From Unrest-Barbed Bone Chips, From Mistmoor-Dagger Charred, and a Bloodstone
Haunting: From Unrest-Darkbone Chip, From Mistmoor-Dagger Ebon, and Jasper
Collection Beza: Globe of Darkness, Eye of Kor, Amber
Collection Caza: Eye of Guk, From Spectres-Globe of Fear, and Jade

Timpi will give you the following words:

Detachment: From Unrest-Dull Bone Chip, From Mistmoore-Dagger Charred, and Bloodstone
Rupturing: From Unrest-Festering Cloak, From Mistmoore Ebon Wand, and Jasper
Suffering: From Spectres-Globe of Fear, From Guk-Iced Bone Chips, and a Star Rose Quartz
Requisition: From Plane of Fear-Eye of Fright, Stone of the Wraith, and a Pearl

Tompi will give you the following words:

Allure: From Unrest-A Charred Pearl and Ruby, From Mistmoore-Charred Wood Wand, and a Bloodstone
Dark Paths: From Unrest-Sphere of Unrest, From Guk-Eye of Urd, and a star Rose Quartz
Obligation: From Spectres-Globe of Fear, From Guk-Eye of Jin, and Jade
Acquisition Beza: Fetid Skin, Scare Straw, Turmoil Warts, and a Pearl
