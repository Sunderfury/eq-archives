1/29/2000

Welp folks I am glad I no longer play EQ after what they did to NEC pets in the last patch.  We no longer get a lowered attack delay by giving our pets daggers.  This means the higher level pets are now nerfed to about 50% of what they once were.  Giving our 44th/49th level pets the ability to cast a random spell proc in battle will never compare to seeing it hit x4 for 55 damage ever couple of seconds.

The Necromancer info on these pages will remain static.  I no longer play EQ.  I will however make the occasional update for ShowEQ.

This link sums up my current interest in EQ: http://www.mpog.com/pvp/archive/2000/pvp20000129.gif

EQ was great for its time but it has become quickly dated.  The next company that comes out with a similar game with better graphics and learns from the mistakes (Crappy Guild System, Extremely Repetitive Gameplay, Lack of a Viable and Challenging PVP Solution, Inability to play an Evil Character) the Verant has made.  I'll be there.  I hacked my way up to 50th and got bored (I always get in trouble when I get bored).  In the mean time I'm having more fun in life outside of the narrow EQ environment.

If anyone has good data for NEC pets from ShowEQ to submit I'm going to post the old data and compare it to the new HP/Level Pet info so send it on in.

I also posted the Monster Data from the Plane of Air in the General Forum.
