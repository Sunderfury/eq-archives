July 22nd: GUK RAID FOLLOW UP
The following is a field report from the teams who fought in upper guk, after will be a brief report from the lower GUK teams.
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

UPPER GUK battle report:
-------------------------------

The Upper Guk team lead by myself, Thundermare Cloudstride, went fairly decent for the majority of the time. We waited for all to arrive, and proceeded to the Summoner and Priest spawn area, which is a bit deep into Upper Guk. I was hoping that after camping this spawn numerous times, we would collect an Amethyst Bracelet (5AC, 3 wis, 15HP) off the priest or possibly a Brazier off the Summoner (the Brazier allows you to summon a fire elemental of approximately 16th lvl regardless of class).

The two groups were as follows: Team One - Thundermare (leader), Thyloc, Iluminata, Anarkyx, Kingheavyj; Team Two -Tricon (leader), Gortticus, Divoran, Ilania, and Regorr.

[ image 1 - image2 - image 3 ]
images courtasy Anarky

We were able to make it up to a safe area to buff and rest right before approaching the room. After buffing, we were ready and charged the area. Unfortunately I had killed the most frogloks in my life compared to the others in my group so naturally, everything aggroed on me. I thought *I'm dead* as my health bar faded before my eyes. However, my group was able to heal me quickly and draw everything away. Iluminata tried mezzing everything, but there was so much confusion, that it did little or no good. However, 6 vs 10 is not fair, and their corpses quickly piled near our feet. We slayed them all within about two minutes.

This was our first battle, and I was actually rather surprised we were able to dispatch of them so quickly. After the battle, we quickly med (thanks to Iluminata's breeze spells) and were ready again for another battle. I, unfortunately, had not researched the spawn time of the room, so I had no idea when they would pop. It happened to be after about 10 minutes that the room spawned again. We slayed them, with ease, in a short amount of time. The loot on the Priest was to be sneezed at except for the rod it carried which our chanter quickly picked up for a future quest of hers. After looting, Iluminata quickly placed herself on one of the four poles surrounding the room claiming it her throne and making us her slaves (now I wouldn't have minded that if she only had her high elf illusion on...heheh).

While we were resting, a 44 level druid named Kandavane, on his way to Lower Guk, passed by and questioned what we were all doing here. After replying we were having a raid, he buffed most of us with his high level skin spell and bid us farewell.

Our next few battles went fairly well and we had the area camped fairly well. But we had other places that we had planned to camp. So after medding, we quickly went off to camp the Nokta Shaman who drops the Globe of Shadows (a quest item needed by our good friend Tricon. Alas, we should have stayed for we knew not what lied ahead.

We decided not to invis ourselves and instead fight our way to the next camp sight. The cons were generally blue, even, and the rare yellow as we passed through. We quickly dispatched of each foe, but no thanks to our tanks who proceeded to attack everything Iluminata mezzed (she was rather angry with our group's performance near the end of the raid).

Finally, after a few battles and meds in between, we reached the spawn area of the Nokta Shaman. We had to destroy it's placeholder first, however. After quickly dispatching of them, we proceeded to rest and med. While healing our wounds and cleaning up the bodies from the floor, a player named Raamus approached us. We greeted him and gave him buffs. He thanked us and went on his way.....

No more than a minute later did our friend come back....with friends. Behind him were about 4 froggies. They were all black to me, but I thought with ten players on our side, we wouldn't have a problem. I was wrong. As soon as Raamus came, the frogs immediately aggroed on our poor enchanter and she was quickly slain...so fast, we never noticed it since we knew not what was going on. After seeing our comrade fall, we quickly went to slay the beasts. Alas, I guess our chanter did some good in our battles because they were very difficult without her. More frogs joined in as they spawned from both adjacent rooms. The battle lasted about 5 minutes, and then it was finally over, the room filled with the stench of blood.

No sooner had we dispatched of all the frogs when the room we were in finally spawned...Nokta included. Our casters were out of mana, including myself, so I decided that instead of medding, I would get my hammer out. Because I had no mana, I was unable to snare any of the frogs, who quickly ran and got their friends. The battle raged on and on. I don't know how, but we managed to be the victor, with no more deaths.

Tricon looted the corpse of the Nokta Shaman only to find a Fine Steel Morning Star there. Alas, our friend would not get his quest item after all. We all decided it was best to move on to the ancient croc room before "Raamus" came back with another train. Aye, all watch out for Raamus...he doesn't think before he does things. I guess some people are like that.

We had to fight our way out of the rooms to the big area with water to reach the ancient croc. A few of our members had left, and we were down to seven. The battles were tougher now than before, as well as slower. But we eventually came out to be the victor. Finally, we approached the water and made our way through the underground water cavern to the room were the ancient croc spawned...only to find it being camped by a 40 mage. Alas, I despise people who farm for items off of rare spawns that people like myself just want to camp to get the item for myself. But I digress, and we decided to move back to the summoner and priest room and find the rare items we wanted.

Again, we had to fight our way through to the room. The battles were long, but we prevailed. We were almost to an area close to the room where we could med and rest, and I decided we should dispatch of the few frogs left near the cliff area. Alas, that was a mistake I made which cost many lives. There happened to be more frogs than I thought over that cliff. About 3 of them attacked us at first. But we were just about out of mana, and I could not cast a snare on them all. As a result, we had runners, which brought more to the area where we were fighting. More and more joined the fray, and as we thought we had taken out the last one, another two came to make sure we were disposed of.

The battle raged on and on. Tricon and Ilania lost their lives during the battle at some point. I thought we were surely doomed, but Iluminata, her sweet loveable self, was able to mez all but one as we focused our attacks on a single opponent. With our nukers gone, we had to rely on brute strength and agility to get us out of the mess. Somehow, we finally prevailed and proceeded to a safe area to meditate.

Tricon quickly called out my name, and I proceeded to invis and get him. After looting his corpse, I heard the cries of Ilania. I knew our group needed to call it a night, so I decided we should just invis, drag our corpses to the entrance and leave. Alas, we had received no items that we were hoping for, and had many deaths. On the up side, we did receive a good bit of experience (myself gaining a yellow bubble). With that, and a lack of sleep that night, I camped.

NOTE: Before the raid, early people were treated to a wonderful language session by Dennli. It was all rather amusing, and drinks were passed out too. All in all, this was a good raid in general. Though not a whole lot of exp was gained and no rare items were receieved, the important thing was we were together and had lots of fun!

Thundermare Cloudstride
