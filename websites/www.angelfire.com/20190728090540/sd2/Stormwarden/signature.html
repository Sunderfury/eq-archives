What is a signature?
A signature is a graphical image that expresses your characters style, and attitude. In part its a picture that represents your character. It is called a signature because it is displayed at the end of a message you post on a message board. The image is a personal calling card of sorts that is unique.

I use Photoshop 6, and additional packages for fuller graphical design.

About this Service
- I make and design Signatures specificly for Everquest Players.
- Each signature is unique
- Content is only limited by imagination
- Each Everquest Signature is 500pp each
- I keep an original on file in case changes need to be made (ie change of guild)

How to ORDER
1) email dennlitrueheart@hotmail.com and provide me with the following:
2) .jpg image of your character, or arrange an ingame meeting. ( i can do this for other servers as well)
3) an explaination of what you want to see graphicaly:

  • background: A particular location in EQ, a design, color, pattern, etc
  • color scheme: ie) a dark elf would probably want shadows, while a druid would want more earth tones
  • text: name, guild, diety, officer, caption...
  • size, long, short, tall, circle, framed, octagon, etc...
