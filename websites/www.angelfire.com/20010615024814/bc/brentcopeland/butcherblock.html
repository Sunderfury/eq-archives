Ogre Heads:
WHO STARTS Byzar Bloodforge, in Kaladim Warriors Guild
HOW IT STARTS Ask Byzar, "What about Corflunk" or "What about Zarchoomi"
QUEST INFO Kill Corflunk or Zarchoomi, Turn in heads to Byzar
ITEM(S) RECEIVED If you have good faction you will usually recieve Bloodforged Armor
FACTION HITS +Stormguard, +Kazon Stormhammer, +MinersGuild 249, +Merchants of Kaladim -CrakenkWarriors
NOTES This will lead to the Trumpy Irontoe quest for the Bloodforge Hammer

Orc Runner #1:
WHO STARTS Lord Jyleel, basement of the Paladin Guild in Felwithe
HOW IT STARTS Hail him
QUEST INFO Kill the orc runner(Runs from GFaydark to Crossroads, sometimes in BB, and sometimes seen in Nectulos). You will get a letter to take back to the Paladin.
ITEM(S) RECEIVED Quest for Dvinn's Heart
FACTION HITS +Champions, +King Tearisthex, +Clerics of Tunare,+Soldiers of Tunare -Crushbone Orcs (Big faction hit on orcs)
NOTES When you do the orc runner quests the faction hit from them is pretty severe. You will eventually see the statements from the orcs you face change to something along the lines of your crimes are legendary among us and then you are prime target. This also affects Dvinn and Emperor Crush as well.
