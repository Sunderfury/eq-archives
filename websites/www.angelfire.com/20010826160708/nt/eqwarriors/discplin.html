Warrior Disciplines

Disciplines are new abilities obtained post level 50, to activate type "/dis ****" replace the **** with the name of the discipline you want to use. Only one discipline can be used at a time(I think)

They are as follows:

Level 52 - Evasive: you get hit less by enemies but also hit them less.
Duration - 3 minutes
Re-use delay - 15 minutes

Level 53 - Charge: when activated you will never miss an attack.
Duration - 14 seconds
Re-use delay - 30 minutes

Level 54 - Mighty Strike: all your hits become critical hits or crippling blows if you're in beserker frenzy.
Duration - 10 seconds
Re-use delay - 60 minutes

Level 55 - Defensive: you take less melee damage(reduced instead of avoided) and do less damage.
Duration - 3 minutes
Re-use delay - 15 minutes

Level 56 - Furious: you riposte every attack that hits you.
Duration - 9 seconds
Re-use delay - 60 minutes

Level 57 - Precision: greater chance to hit your target but you become more vulnerable as well.
Duration - 3 minutes
Re-use delay - 30 minutes

Level 58 - Fell Strike: all your hits will be at least 4x base weapon damage.
Duration - 12 seconds
Re-use delay - 30 minutes

Level 59 - Fortitude: you will not get hit by melee damage while using this discipline.
Duration - 8 seconds
Re-use delay - 60 minutes

Level 60 - Aggressive: you will do more damage every hit but will also take more damage
Duration - 3 minutes
Re-use delay - 27 minutes
