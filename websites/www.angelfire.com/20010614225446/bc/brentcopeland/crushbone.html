Screaming Mace:
WHO STARTS Dwarven smith in Crushbone/Elven Priest in the same Hut
HOW IT STARTS  Hail smith, and say "The King is dead", then ask "What about ringmail"
QUEST INFO Dwarven smith will ask for the Dwarven Ringmail Tunic(found on Emperoro Crush), you will get a dwarven mace, after you give him the tunic.  Then talk to the Elven Priest, say "The King is Dead".. He will ask for a bracer.(give him Bracer of Battle(found on Orc Warlord).. You will be give a prayer cloth of Tunare. Give both mace and cloth to the priest....
ITEM(S) RECEIVED Screaming Mace
FACTION HITS ?
NOTES Might require the Shiny Brass Shield(Supposedly you can do this quest without it)
