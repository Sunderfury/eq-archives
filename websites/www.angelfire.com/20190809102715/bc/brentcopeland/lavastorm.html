Fire Goblin Runner: :
Talk to someone in the Warrior's guild in Neriak. He will ask you to go to the Lavastorm Mountains and look for a Fire Goblin Runner who is supplying an Eurudite. If you kill the Fire Goblin Runner and return his Runner Pouch to the warrior you will receive some experience, money and possibly a decent item. (Submitted by Thrymm)

Sir Lindeal's Testimony: :
: Talk to Loveal S´Nez in the Lodge of the dead in Neriak 3rd Gate to initiate this quest. The forces of evil have been plagued by the good paladin Sir Lindeal long enough. He is located in the Lavastorm Mountains and is clearly not well liked by his evil bretheren. They want you to kill him and return his testimony book. Returning it gets you a nice item (perhaps a damask robe). (Submitted by Lord Vormulak).
