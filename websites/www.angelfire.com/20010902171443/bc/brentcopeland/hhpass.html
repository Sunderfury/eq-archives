Scalp Collection: :
Captain Ashlan and Volunteer Delharn collect orc scalps. (They always seem to respawn - I have never seen anyone else here). Captain Orben and a volunteer collect gnoll scalps. They give you 1-6 gold and you will raise faction with these groups: Corrupt Qeynos Guards, Freeport Militia, Highpass Guards, Highpass Merchants and Kithicor Residents. You will not lose any faction. This is a great way to improve faction with the shadier groups without losing any with the good groups. (Submitted by Valhakar Sarajin
