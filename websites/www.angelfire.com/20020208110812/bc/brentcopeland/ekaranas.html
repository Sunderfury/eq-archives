Clothspinner Sisters
WHO STARTS Clothspinner Sister who hangs around Qeynos
HOW IT STARTS Hail the Clothspinner sister who wanders around the entrance of Qeynos
QUEST INFO She will give you a letter to deliver to her sister in E. Karanas. You will find her in the gorge(under the rocky ledge that you cross over if you are goin UP the gorge). She will give you a monogrammed cloth. . A guard will then attack her. Kill the guard, and she will give you a veil to take back to Milea Clothspinner. Give the original sister the veil and cloth.
ITEM(S) RECEIVED Depending on faction. You will get a decent item and a note to deliver to the temple of life.
