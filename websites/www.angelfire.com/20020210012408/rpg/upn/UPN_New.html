Instructions for Joining the Guild

To first join the guild :

1.  You must be at least Level 20, or have another character in the guild of at least Level 20.

2.  Then if you are a new member you will need to contact an Officer for the invite, it is recomended that you post a message in the new members section of the Forums to say hi to everyone and let the rest of UPON know you.





If you are a new member be sure to check the Rules section.
