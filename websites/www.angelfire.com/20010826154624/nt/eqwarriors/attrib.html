Attributes

There are four main attributes important for a warrior, these are:

Strength - determines carrying capacity(for heavy armor + weapons + loot). Increases max and average damage a bit.

Stamina - determines how many hp you get per level and how long you can fight before you become fatigued.

Agility - chance for creatures to miss or for you to avoid hits(probably). Keep this stat over 75 and you're fine.

Dexterity - increases chance for weapon effects to go off and how quickly melee skills are learned.

Other Attributes

Intelligence - higher int might help with faster skill gain, can be beneficial if you want to use ALL weapon skills. Better off not throwing any bonus points into this one.

Charisma - affects prices for items you buy from npc's..also not worth any bonus points since most of your armor/weapons will be from players or as loot from something you killed. Increases ability to resist mind altering spells such as charm.

Wisdom - Supposedly helps your attempts to successfully create something with trade skills.
