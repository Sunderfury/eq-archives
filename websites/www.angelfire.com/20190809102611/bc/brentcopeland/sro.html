Bandit Tongues: :
 This may not work anymore. In South Ro along the zone edge at 1100, 1428 is a 1-tent camp populated by a neutral Orc "Ortallius" and Wizard "Rathmana Allin". Ortallius wants proof that you killed bandits. Give him a bandit tongue for faction, experience and a reward.

Evil Research: :
 If you are an evil magic using class, go to South Ro and talk to the female Dark Elf who sells the smithing stuff. Walk up and 'Hail' her. You may need a good faction to get this quest. She wants regular lightstones for the books that give combine instructions and a greater lightstone for the book that actually combines the ingredients. The book you get for the lightstone is random and seems to be book one far more often than book two, so bring plenty of lightstones. You get some good xp for doing this and it increases faction with DarkBargainers, DreadGuardInner and DreadGuardOuter. I have been told the combine items don't yet work, so this is more for the xp and faction than anything else. (Submitted by Maldaven)

Ortallius' Cutthroat Rings: :
 Hailing Ortallius and talking to him reveals that as a child he was saved from Dervish Cutthroats by Rathmana and he now offers a bounty for the return of any 3 Cutthroat Rings. Give him at least three rings for a few minor items. Your faction will increase with Solusek. (Submitted by Zanshin)

Rathmana's Business Offer: :
 Talk to Rathmana about his [business]. He will tell you to give him the item and 20 gold. Nobody seems to know what item he is talking about. (Submitted by Zanshin)

TOP
Rathmana's Travelling Offer: :
 Talk to Rathmana about the [traveling offer]. If you give him 30 gold, he will allow you to reach into his scroll bag and take 1 scroll. The scroll you get is random. It is often an illegible scroll, which is basically worthless. Sometimes you will get a high level (level 20 or so) scroll worth quite a bit. (Submitted by Zanshin)
