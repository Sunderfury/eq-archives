Kila






Kila was born in a town she doesnt remember by parents who tried but were still only an assasin and a thief.



She grew up in Britain wrestling the ideals her parents set for her against the skills they taught her through example. Born from this struggle came a fluidity of thought. A Duality of Spirit. A conscience that dips into the dark and the light.



She may sometimes do the unexpected and is often reckless in her ferver but her loyalties are clear.



She got to test these loyalties in the town she picked to spend her young adulthood in. Trinsic. A mixed town. Soft and nuturing at times, but mostly harsh and unforgiving and even crude.







She was starting to depend too heavily on the skills she learned as a child, not stealing exactly, but chancing upon unscroupulous situations and taking full advantage of them. In this way she survived the days and nights in Trinsic.





One day she chanced upon a pack horse on a visit to Britain. Not needing a pack horse as much as money for food, Kila attempted to sell the companionable beast. After many unsuccessful trips to various stabling houses, she spied a moongate opening up. Full of frusteration and her usual abandon she took a deep breath, held it, and rushed into the gate before it closed.






After the tingling subsided and the pony neighed nervousely down her neck, she realized that her eyes were squeezed shut. Kila opened them onto the streets of a foreign town. Vesper she was later told. It was here that her life changed forever.





In Vespar Kila met Getafix. Getafix took a hand to help her sell the pony. It was Getafix who introduced her to the others in The Black Tower Guild. And, a week later, Kila became a member of this guild. And they became her family.
