Diseased Rat Livers:
WHO STARTS Larkon
HOW IT STARTS Go around behind the Library Mechanimagica. Talk to a gnome caster in a yellow robe, named Larkon. Given the choices, tell him that he's losing his mind.
QUEST INFO This will lead you to talk to a gnome in the hut just outside Ak'Anon, next to Cargo Clockwork. When you tell the second gnome that Larkon sent you, he'll give you an "airtight box" to collect 10 rat livers. Fill it up, hit 'combine', and return it to Larkon.

Series C Black Boxes:
WHO STARTS Manik Compolten in the gemchoppers hall
HOW IT STARTS Hail Manik
QUEST INFO He will ask that new warriors take keycards and go retrieve blackboxes from series C clockworks.  He asks that all four boxes be returned simultaneously, meaning that you must return after getting each one to get another keycard.  This quest is performed by wandering around Ak'anon and giving the keycards to Clockworks with a C in their name.  the locations of these clockworks are:  XIIC)  On an island in the middle of the lake. (You'll have to swim to get to it.)  XVIIC)  Just outside the Mines of Malfuntion.  (He's the newbie area 'guard'.)  XXVIC)   In the palace near King Akanon's room.  XXVIIC)  Near the tunnel that leads to the Mines of Malfunction. (In the paddle wheel room.), or at the top on the Akanon Wall. 
ITEM(S) RECEIVED ?
FACTION HITS +King Akanon, +Eldritch Collective +Gem Choppers-Dark Reflection -Meldrath
NOTES Plus, you get the 27C Blackbox Quest.  As an aside I have been told to be sure to ask Manik about the Asylum of the Mad and about Meldrath.

Telescope Lenses:
WHO STARTS Tobon Starpyre, the Wizard Guildmaster
HOW IT STARTS Hail Tobon
QUEST INFO He wants you to collect four spare telescope lenses from four observatories around the Steamfonts. He gives you a rolled up note with the names of the four Royal Observers Three of the Observers Steamfont Mountains while the last is located in Lesser Faydark.  These are supposedly the four lense locations :

1) The gnome royal observer just outside zone line in Lesser Faydark will give  you one if you ask. 

2) The gnome royal observer in one of the observatories in Steamfont will also give you one if you ask 

3) The gnome royal observer in another observatory tells you it was stolen by a Dark Elf female, Zenita DRin.  She is in Freeport in the secret tunnels beneath an inn by the docks. You can get the lense two ways.  Kill her - she is approximatley level 15 and hard to kill.  Or you can play cards with her (if you have an OK faction with her) by continuously trading her a bottle of something you can purchase upstairs for a draw of a card until you manage to draw a King.  (This one is confirmed) 

4) The final royal observer in Steamfont tells you his brother, Bidl Frugrin, went to Kelethin to do some observations with the spare telescope lens.  He says he has likely found the local tavern and has gotten drunk, wandering the forest by day, and hanging out in the tavern by night.  At night, he can be found in the tavern next to Trueshot Bows in Kelethin and happily gives you the telescope lens.

ITEM(S) RECEIVED Level 8 spell and around 1pp
FACTION HITS +Eldritch Collective, -DarkReflection, -TheDead, +GemChoppers, +KingAkanon
NOTES ?

Toko's Spellbook:
WHO STARTS Tergon
HOW IT STARTS After returning the spellbook to Tergon, he tells you about another Quest:  "Now I have news of a larger matter. It has to do with a [defector].
QUEST INFO It seems a gnome magician by the name of Toko Binlittle has gone and left the guild. He joined forces with the [Pirates of Gunthak]. Find him. He must be destroyed.  Our secrets cannot be known. Return his spellbook to me and I shall give you the [Elemental Grimoire]."  From what I know the Pirates of Gunthak are from the Southern Gulf of Gunthak. It seems that they have been operating within the Ocean of Tears, leagues from their own territory.  The Elemental Grimoire contains the knowledge which will  advance your techniques in research.  With it you shall learn to research spells and scribe them for your own spellbook.
