The time is nigh for ghosts, werewolves, witches and more to once again roam freely through the lands of Norrath. Join your fellow adventurers during the Day of the Dead (October 31st) and see what is in store for those brave enough to log on. Do you dare board the ghost ship Deep Tomb for a most perilous ride through the ages? Will you fight back the rising plague of lycanthropy as were-creatures take control of the night? Or simply enjoy the spirit of the ghostly season and partake in a romp of trick-or-treating? Regardless, know ye that the lands of EverQuest will be most perilous, and you shall be taking your life in your hands and must face the consequences if you seek out fame and fortune during this day.

The rumors are spreading. What do they portend? Why have the clerics of Mithaniel Marr cloistered themselves in deep commune with their deity? What power do the Lanys cultists actually hold over the undead? Do the Gypsies truly believe that the increase in supernatural energies mark the return of the Combine? Is a cult of "Snake" worshipers really starting to take food in Norrath? Is it true that the veil that separates Norrath from the planes is weakening? When will The Prophet once again return to the lands of Norrath to bring with him times of great evil and decay? All these rumors and more will be made clear in the coming days. Sharpen your blade. Memorize your spells. You have been summoned to adventure.


As the great wizard Al`Kabor continues his research of lost magic of the Combine Empire and the moon of Luclin glides across the skies, the world of Norrath enters a turbulent period in its history. In the coming weeks, join your fellow adventurers as untold challenges and events arise to test even the heartiest of souls. During this Time of Turmoil, experience dozens of GM and Guide hosted quests and events to discover new secrets and unique treasures, promising to elevate the honor and fame of your EverQuest character.
