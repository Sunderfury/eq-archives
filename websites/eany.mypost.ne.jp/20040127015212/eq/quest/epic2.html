Step 2

またもやTimorous Deepにもどり、Omatに先ほど預かったOrnate Sea Shellを渡しましょう。

Omat Vastsea says"So,you are firend of Natasha's? That is good to see. She is a very wise and gifted woman. The Riptide goblins have adopted a new king but are still in a vulnerable state untill his subjects accept his rule. The Plasmatic Priesthood is aware of the weakened state of the Riptides and has convinced the Fire Peak goblins to strike against them. Although I do not agree with the mannerisms or actions of any goblin clan,such a war would be detrimental to all who are cought in its path. Lord Gimblox of the Fire Peak clan has been meeting with a member of the Plasmatic Priesthood in the Temple of Soulsek Ro. Locate the Plasmatic Priest, hand him this statue to hinder his powers, then eliminate him and bring his robe."

この時、OmatからCoral Statue of Tarewがもらえます。

Temple of Soulsek RoにいるPlasmatic Priesthoodに、これを渡すと力が弱くなると。そして彼を殺して、ローブを持ってきなさいと言うことですね。さっそくTemple of Soulsek Roへ行ってみます。目的の人物は、Temple2Fの、NPC(SeekerもしくはKeeper)が3人いる小部屋です。LOCは(p326,p38,p29)。部屋の入り口から見て左側のNPCが、Humanの女性だったらそれはPHです。間違ってもそいつにItemを渡さないように。Game時間の夜9時にDespawnし、朝9時にまたSpawnします。Lv55でHumanの男性がSpawnするまで待ちましょう。

You say "What is Solusek Ro?"

a seeker says"Solusek Ro is the Master of Fire and the Keeper of Power."

You say "What is Plasmatic Priest?"

a seeker says"You speak of things that should not be spoken! It would be in your best interest to leave this temple at once and return to lands less consumed by flame!"

渡すと本性を現し、Seekerからa Plasmatic Priesthoodと名前が変わって襲ってきます。CLR Typeで、CHとDD、そしてAEを撃ってきます。Stun連射で一切CastさせないようにすればRoomでやってしまって構いませんが、自信がない場合はSeekerの状態のままZONE際までPullし、PullerはZONE→他の人がHailで足止め→Item渡してKillが安全でしょう。AEを使われてしまうと、周囲にいるSeeker・Keeperに当たって、KSされる可能性があります。Lv55という割には強くなく、55平均のFull Partyで割とあっさり勝てるでしょう。

A seeker shakes violently as his hand closes on the coral statue and flames dance in the depths of his eyes! The statue begins to emit a reddish glow then shatters in a burst of fire and smoke! The flames in the plasmatic priest's eyes vanish with the explosion and only madness remains!

"The Triumvirate thinks that destroying my spirit of flame will stop me?!! I will kill you all now, the goblins will continue to war, and when my spirit is restored I will ensure that Ixiblat Fer consumes all of Norrath in his flames!!"

a Plasmatic Priesthoodを殺すとBlood Soaked Plasmatic Priest Robeをlootできます。

次はSoulsek Aに行き、Lord GimbloxというGoblinを殺します。こいつは(n796, n366)にPOPします。King Roomのすぐ側にある、真ん中が低くなっているRoomです。King Room方面から向かっていって、左側のGoblinがPHになります。かなり弱っちょろいやつで、RootしてDD2発撃てば瀕死になってくれるので、Stunなり殴りなりで殺しましょう。

LakeのLordよりもCampは容易ですが、なかなかPOPしてくれないので根気が必要です。こいつを殺すとLord Gimblox's Signet RingがLootできます。RobeとRingを取ったら、再びOmatの元へ戻りましょう。

a Plasmatic PriesthoodからlootしたBlood Soaked Plasmatic Priest RobeをOmatに渡しましょう。

Omat Vastsea says"You have proven yourself to be one who is willing to take responsibility for his own actions. Such a trait is desired by all delties to be present in their faithful. I pray that you had the wisdom to slay Lord Gimblox as well as the Plasmatic Priest in order to prevent further advancement of the incursion against the Riptides. Priestess Natasha awaits inside the inn for Lord Gimblox's ring, make haste for she is a busy woman and will soon depart."

するとOrb of Frozen Waterがもらえます。Questにおける超重要ITEMですので、大切に保管しておきましょう。そばのINNの中に、またもやどこからともなくNatashaが現れているので、Lord Gimblox's Signet Ringを渡します。

Natasha Whitewater says"Thank you, XXX , for your services thus far. The Triumvirate missionaries are, so far, impressed. However, there is yet another threat rising from the Plasmatic Priesthood that may have dire effects on all of Norrath if it is not stopped. An Iksar member of the Triumvirate missionaries has discovered that the Plasmatic Priesthood has somehow convinced the sarnak summoners of Chardok to assit them in summoning Ixiblat Fer to Norrath. Take this shell to Naxot Deepwater in the Burning Woods so that she knows I have sent you. We ask that you return with the Sceptre of Ixiblat Fer as well as evidence to show that the Plasmatic Priesthood is dealing with sarnaks. High Priest Omat will await your return. I Must depart now. I pray for your success."

NatashaからOrnate Sea Shellを受け取ります（前にもらった物と全く同じです）。Prismatic PriestにStatueを渡した時に言っていましたが…こいつはSarnakのSummonerに依頼して、世界を焼き尽くすためにIxiblat Ferの召還を依頼したと。それを阻止するのが次のお仕事になります。Burning WoodsにいるNaxot DeepwaterにOrnate Sea Shellを渡して、Ixiblat FerをKill→ChardokのOverkingをKillというのが流れです。

Ixiblat Ferは世界を破滅させうる力を持っているわけですから…しっかりと戦力を集めて、Burning Woodsへ行きましょう。
