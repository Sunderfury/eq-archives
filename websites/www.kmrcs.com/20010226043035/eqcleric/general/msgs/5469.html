You run down to the Rathe mountaints, and talk to Lianna, who is in the gypse camp(not too far away from the rathe lake zone). I asked her "What happen to Regis?", and her reply was that Regis was kill in North Karanna, close to the northern treeline to the east, and was slained by Zahal the slayer. When you kill Zahal Regis' body is suppose to come up, where he died at, and should have Lianna's flute on it. You are suppose to get the Flute and take it back to Lianna, and I think she will give you the icon and ingot for the reverent. Which with those two pieces and three enchanted platium bars you trade in for a piece of the cleric armor.
