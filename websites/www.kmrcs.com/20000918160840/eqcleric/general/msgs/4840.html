2 clerics

1 warrior

Bards can do limited melee, warriors take the damage and taunt, and monks (pallys if in unrest) deal out damage like crazy . Enchanters can also do some direct damage, one cleric can do direct damage, stun, etc, one cleric can heal...
Just watch those undead baddies fall down dead, hehe
