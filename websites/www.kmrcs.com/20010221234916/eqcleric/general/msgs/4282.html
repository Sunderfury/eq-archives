In order of how much I play them:

1. Cleric - 34, human
2. Warrior - 16, human
3. Shaman - 20 (almost 21, barbarian

Retired characters include:
16 gnome necro, 8 human magician, 10 half-elf warrior

I think the most satisfying is my 16 warrior. I've had a lot of fun with her lately, especially since I can get most of the crafted requirements myself. Mmmm... dual wielding Langseaxes.. she's my killing machine =)

I get so tired of medding that I really was against starting another caster. Plus I had my shaman already (who was my first character). Now I just use my shaman to run around and do quests and menial tasks for me (love that SOW!).

The biggest problem is equipping so many characters. Two characters is fairly easy but more can be difficult, especially if you want them to be VERY well equipped. The warrior with crafted is not so difficult but then you've got to worry about jewelry, weapons, ect.. gets to be difficult on the bank account. I hope my main character isn't suffering too much.

Anyway, try a tank, you'll like it!

P.S. Try a monk, they don't even need equipment until 10 or higher.
