I believe there is a level restriction on doing the quest. I successfully did the quest 4 times at lower levels (under 20) and was able to get it. Once I levelled past 21, I have attempted the quest more times than I can count and have not received the smasher since. It's a fairly good weapon to use at lower levels, but is non magical which tends to be a major pain. If you are a patient one you can generally sell them for a decent amount of cash. I also believe that faction does not play a part in getting the smasher. I received my first smasher after completing the quest about 30 times. Then I did the quest about 3 more times and received another. I went and levelled inbetween the 2nd and 3rd time and then came back and did it again and got it 2nd try. The last time I received the smasher, I think it took about 5 tries to get it. Best advice is to plan to spend a good 2-3 days working at it. Keep working at it and you will get it eventually.
