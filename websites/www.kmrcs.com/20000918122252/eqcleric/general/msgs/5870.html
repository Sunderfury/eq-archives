Atone is more similiar to the Enchanter Spell Memory Blur than Mesmerize.

If you attack a normally non-agressive monster -- then stop attacking and cast Atone -- the monster will return to its non-aggro state. Atone cancels the hate list and the damage list.

If your group is attacking 2 monsters and you cast Atone on 1, it will calm down for a fraction of a second, see you are attacking its friend, and resume attacking your group.

Atone is a great spell and I keep it memmed all the time. But it is only useful in the right circumstances.
