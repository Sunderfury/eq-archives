At 15 maybe you are rather broke. If I were you I will..

1. Full your spell book - always the priority, 70pp maybe
2. Golden Jasper earring - ~70pp a pair and you can use this til you 50
3. Lizardscale mantle or cloak - ~40pp serve till 35/50 i think
4. Shining Brass Shield - ~30pp nice shield save you from root
5. Screaming mace - ~100pp but i'll go get it myself :)
6. Saving - better hold some cash for your 19 spells.

Bronze.. you can get some if you find them real cheap, such as 25pp - 5pp depends on bp or gorget. Don't buy them high as you will get A LOT of them when you are 25 to 30.

Ob staff? 5 wisdom and the mana is nice but 2h blunt isn't the final way to go. Don't worth to invest all your money in a future bank junk.
