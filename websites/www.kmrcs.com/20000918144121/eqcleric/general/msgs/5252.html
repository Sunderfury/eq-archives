At your level you don't really need to group yet and you can solo quite a bit.

In the late teens to twenties is the time to start grouping. The mid twenties level has the most zones in the game where you can get exp so you shouldn't have alot of problems finding a group.

I will say one thing though, that will pretty much change at higher levels. There just aren't enough high level places for people to play and even clerics will have a hard time grouping in overpacked zones. Hell nothing like logging into SolB and see over 70+ people.
