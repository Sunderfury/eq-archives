Just curious if this has happened to any of you -
Last night I was running around in Oasis doing my normal routine of find a deepwater caimen or croc and then root and smite/melee till dead. I came over the top of a sand dune and saw some person running away from a deepwater caimen (blue to me and they were 3 levels less than I). I watched for a sec and saw that this person was getting pounded on so naturally I jumped in the fray and started beating on the croc to get it off them. I didn't click on the other person to see what their health was, but figured I could help them to kill the croc and they would get the xp. Well I didn't realize that this person was so low on health and a few secs later the person falls over dead, well I was kinda shocked but kept fighting the croc (The croc was about 3/4 full health when I started beating on it - so I figured the other person was still ok for health wise). Anyway, needless to say I finished off the croc and went and sat down to heal/med. A few minutes later I get this blasting message from the person that
died saying "Why the H*LL didn't you help me? I yelled for help 4 or 5 times and you didn't help. Why didn't you heal me?" I replied back saying I didn't see any of your yells for help. I had just come over the hill. Then this person replied back and said "B*LLSH*T, I saw you standing there watching me as I got pounded. Why didn't you even heal me?" I (getting kinda annoyed at this person's attitude by now) replied back saying I'm not your personal healer - If you had yelled for help and I had seen it, I would have helped - I didn't see any yell message or anything - you didn't specially "say" ask for a heal or anything. I did try to help you by beating on the croc. This person then replied back "Well you are a cleric - look up a definition of a cleric - you are supposed to heal people" Well at this point in time I got fed up and replied back with "I'm not your personal healer. I will help people that are in need if they ask for help - with your attitude you are never going to get any help" Then
with that I /ignored this person.

Out of curiousity would any of you have handled this differently had you been in this situation?
