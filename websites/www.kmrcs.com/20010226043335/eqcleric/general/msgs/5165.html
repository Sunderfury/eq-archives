I keep a supply of cat's eye agate and a supply of bloodstones. The bloodstones (Symbol of Ryltan) only get used on me when soloing or on pullers/tanks when I think we might be going in over our heads.

The cat's eye agates are comparatively cheap, so when asked to cast a symbol buff on someone outside of the above circumstances, I give them Symbol of Transal (unless they want to pay for a bloodstone) - that way, they get their buff and I don't feel like I'm burning cash.

When I get Symbol of Pinzarn, I'll probably do the same with it (requires a Jasper) and Symbol of Ryltan.
