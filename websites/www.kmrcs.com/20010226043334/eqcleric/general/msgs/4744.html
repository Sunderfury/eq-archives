Posted by Kaldaur on October 10, 1999 at 10:26:23:

I have had a few reports of people logging off immediately after death in frustration and when getting back on they are unable to be ressurected. Now either the time limit is 2 hours RL or there are some bugs with the time limit... anyone else have more info on this?

On another note... I personaly hate the new limit. I feel that the limit needs to be increased to 4 hours or for the first 2 hours the person can be ressurected from anywhere.... after that they have to be in the same zone as their corpse for ressurection to work.

One of Verant's 'reasons' for the time limit change was to prevent people from getting a 'teleport' effect by leaving corpses everywhere and getting revived to that spot. Having to be in the same zone as your corpse after the 2 hour period would effectively negate the 'teleport' effect and give us a longer period for ressurections. This second period would need a limit as well inorder to prevent the massive piles of corpses laying around but I feel an additionaly 2-4 hours on top of the first 2 hour (res from anywhere) period would not be unreasonable.
