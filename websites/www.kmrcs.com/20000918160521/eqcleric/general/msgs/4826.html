They do tend to have a bit more strength and offten use a two handed weapon rather than one handed, which means they tend to do a bit more damage when they do hit. They also will generaly have more hp due to there high stamina. Cleric races tend to have a bit more adjility and/or dexterity which counters this although is less obvious.

Armour is a mute point all priest classes lose hp quickly. At low - mid levels clerics have the advantage of wearing plat. At mid - high levels shamans could also wear plate (ruby, etc) but most clerics and shamans go for wis enhancing items (or at least a combo of wis and ac).

: I'm a bit curious about how Shamen are better at meleeing than clerics? Our weapons are the same, and we have the same caps on our weapon skills. Obviously an ogre is better at melee than a high elf, but race issues aside, shamen are no more viable in melee than clerics are.

: We wear better armor than they do, and our HP and AC buffs offset their ability buffs to a great degree. Druids are more viable offensively than clerics (except against undead), but I don't think that Shamen are.
