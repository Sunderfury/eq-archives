Any tips on where you've been hunting lately and if
getting exp to 24th came easily or was difficult?

I'm currently making the push from 23 to 24 in OOT
fighting Gargoyles. They are pretty easy to solo
and drop decent loot. Works MUCH better if I can
have a 17th-ish friend around taking care of the
skelies there for me. Saves time and we both get
good exp.

I've been told Giant Skels in Rathe Mtns are good.
Also heard that there are too many baddies in Rathe
Mtns to risk hanging around the skels.

I've been told that "yard trash" in Mistmoor is good.
I've also been told that there is no place you can
hang out in the "yard" in Mistmoor and not be standing
on the train tracks.

I've been told to go back to Unrest a million times.
Don't beat that dead horse. Unrest sucks unless you
have a group and can hang out inside the house. Pullers
and trains there have gotten me killed more times than
I care to discuss. I'll put Unrest back on my list
after I hit 24 and get my new spells.

And lastly I've been told that Pickclaw Goblins in
Highpass are good, although I have a hard time finding
a group who wants to hang out there and they look
a little hard to me to solo (mostly yellow).

Ideas or suggestions?
