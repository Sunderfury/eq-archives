Posted by Goss on October 17, 1999 at 05:48:51:

So, I've been on Kerra Isle for 17 hours now. I've been taking turns with other clerics and druids on Mitty's spawn. I've personally killed Mitty 26-30 times? I've lost count.

During this agonizing camp, I have not, nor have any other players looted the gold ring from Mitty. SHE HAS NOT DROPPED IT ONCE! One of the players petitioned a GM. The GM said the spawn was working. A druid I have come to know well during the camp has said he has killed Mitty around 50 times and has not looted the gold ring.

Is this what camping an item for a Quest is all about? Is this what Verant intended? I've heard horror story after horror story from other players about quests. Now I have my own. I am very discouraged to do any more quests. Hopefully this is an isolated incident, although I assume most of you have horror stories of your own. The only good thing about this quest was I got to meet some really nice clerics and druids while camping.

I have currently given up on camping Mitty and the PAW quest. It is just not worth it to me to camp a mob for such a long time.
