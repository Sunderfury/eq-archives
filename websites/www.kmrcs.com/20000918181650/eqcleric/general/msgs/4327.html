Sounds like your group got a Cleric and Paladin classes mixed up. =)

I agree with the posts on this thread that nuking/melee does
depend on the situation and the class makeup of the group. I
have only used Wrath when we need to kill a mob Very quickly.

What I usually have mem'd are 2 stun spells (holy might and Stun;
the Force spell takes up too much mana and casting time is long).
I alternate between the stun spells, and let the melee/wiz classes
do the damaging.

The only time I "nuke" is usually with Undead. Again this depends
on the group makeup. If the wizzies are lower level or we don't have
a caster in the group, then I Nuke for a bit. Being Human, I usually
have the lowest mana in the group, so mana efficiency is essential and
my nuking ability very very limited (which can be a good thing =) ).

In short, you're (we're) not a Melee/Nuking class. Though Handolmer with
that nice Hammer of butcherblock can probably now be classified as such. =)
90% of my time is usually stun, heal, med.

Sad as it sounds, there will be a lot of groups out there that will always
fault the Cleric in the group for their deaths, regardless of the Truth of
the situation.

We are Healers, and we are Scapegoats. We take upon our shoulders the
burdends of Death, and the anger that comes after it.
