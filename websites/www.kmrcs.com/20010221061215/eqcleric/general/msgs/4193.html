I think I went from my upper teens through the low twenties in Unrest. It does have a learning curve, but as I gained in levels, it also got to be more fun. I started out with the yard trash and moved into pulling from the first floor. Then it was pulling from the second floor. And for a while I would camp the basement. I have had deaths at each of those major milestones, but it didn't deter me.

I wouldn't shun a zone because of a quick death. Try it out for a few hours and then shun it. =)
