This would not just be for clerics, but perhaps shaman as well. (I dont' think druids need this at all.. Their skin buff is about equal to courage line, but much burn much more mana, cast slower, and are not efficient for group buffing. Should stay that way =) )

We should receive a group version of buffs, one rank after we get them. This wouldnt' go for all buffs, like maybe not the resist magic/fire/etc buffs, but for the courage line, holy armor, symbol, skin for druids, +stat buffs for shamans, etc. There should be a reduction in mana spent, and regents used when these are cast in a full group, and of course they would make it a lot faster to buff a whole group up, save mana, reagents, time, and mouse clicking.

: Group resist disease - 250 mana

Group resist magic is an enchanter spell, no one else gets it =0

Other group spells:

Level 14: Group Invigor - 100 mana
Level 24: Group extinguish fatigue - 175 mana

The courage line is generally kept up on EVERYONE.. caster, tanks, all party members get it. It is a quick, low mana cost HP and AC buff. It makes sense to me to put in group versions of this spell that always has to be cast on 6 people, one after another. These group versions would be VERY well used..

The armor line, well, these are not efficient buffs to begin with. Generally, self buffs, and on tanks. If there isn't much risk to the party, holy armor buffs are often never used. You spend so much more mana for what you get than you do on the courage line. The only use for this, in an all tank party, or before VERY hard fights where you need every buff you can get. (Taking on cazic thule in the plane of fear for example =) )

The group symbols would be more used in later levels. Before then, only before a difficult battle. At high levels, these would be great though, the reagent cost for all but Naltron isn't considered much. At level 50, group pinzarn would be a very well used spell. Naltron is very costly in both reagents and mana (315 mana, 12pp reagent).. Pinzarn is used much more often. Another thing, Paladins get Pinzarn.. Effectively giving them the best overall symbol since naltron is so costly to use. Clerics need a group version of it =)

Group fatigue spells for kedge keep, and other underwater adventures =)

Shamans should probably get some of the same. Their stat buffs need group versions, same endure/resist group spells. No need for other group spells by them though in my opninion. Druids I don't know.. they dont' need anything else =) Wouldn't bother me much if they got the group endure/resist spells however =)


Suggestions & comments welcome =)
