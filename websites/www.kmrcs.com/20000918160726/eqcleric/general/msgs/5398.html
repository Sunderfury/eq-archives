The best groups I've ever been in included an enchanter- not for clarity at all. I've been in a group with one enchanter, on cleric (me) and 4 melee classes- never had a problem.

Enchanters, when played by a skilled one, allow your group to take on one MOB at a time. They can also charm one of the other MOBs to become an extra for your party- still attacking one MOB (this gets a little harder as mes starts wearing off). This "pet" also takes no experience away from the group. Now, throw in a wiz/shaman/druid/mage and remove a melee class- Tashan line of spells reduces the magic resistance of the MOBs you are fighting, so that red that you just got ahold of and the prementioned DD/DOT classes can only get a couple of spells to stick: Tashan/i/ia and BING almost (or all) the spells are now hitting for max, or near max, damage.

Try grouping with an enchanter for awhile (there are enchanters don't play their class strenghts, just like there are clerics who don't play to our class strenghts- so you have to find one that really likes playing to the class strenghts instead of casting DD spells), you'll really enjoy the decreased healing you do :) I always want one in my group.
