Mystery of the Disappearing Corpse

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Gorgrim on October 14, 1999 at 09:10:13:

Anyone have this happen to them before? Is this a
known bug, like the zone bug?

I was in W Freeport, and had time to kill waiting for
some friends to get online, and I tried to do the quest
where you kill the halfling named Shintl Lowbrew in
one of the inns. Well, my Smite got partially resisted
and only did around 4 damage, and Shintl takes off like
a halfing out of collapsing sandhole. As I chase her
down, I keep getting "Your casting was interrupted"
and she makes it out into the street. I chase her past
the Academy of Arcane Science, and as she rounds a
corner, I follow her to find myself face to face with
a Militia guard. At level 15 (and that just barely)
I'm no match for him, and I don't run very far....

So I run back from my bind point in North Ro to where
I died. I see Shintl Lowbrew still standing a short
distance away, so I know I'm in the right place, but...
no corpse. I did /corpse and ran all over the
surrouding area trying to summon... no luck. I found
a bard, and she said it was curious, but her Corpse
Song kept leading her into a wall. So I'm thinking
that somehow I died and fell into a wall, and now I'm
trapped in the terrain. At this point a GM Guide
answers my petition, asks a few questions (Are you
sure you're looking in the area you died? Have you
asked a bard?), then said he was escalating it to the
GMs, please be patient as the new patch has them going
a little bit bonkers right now...

So, here I am, back at level 14 (I *HATE* it when that
happens), no corpse, naked. As I'm sitting down in a
funk to memorize my spells and summon some food and
water for the wait, someone shouts for a bind. I figure
while I'm not doing anything, might as well do it.

So I ask where, and he says he's at the West Gate, but
he's KOS with the guards, can I please come outside the
gate, turn right and follow the wall a little ways. So
I do that, and as I'm running through the newbie zone,
what do you think I pass? My corpse! How on EARTH
did it get way out here?

Anyway, I got my stuff, PLUS 7pp for the bind. But I'm
still shuddering at the thought that I might be in a
place MUCH more dangerous sometime, and die, only to
return and find my corpse isn't where I died....
