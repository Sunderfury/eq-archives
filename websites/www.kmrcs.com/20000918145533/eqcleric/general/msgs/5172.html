: I tried to avoid most of the Derv camps, because they're overly competitive, & many rude people tend to hang out here.

: Oasis hunting crocs CAN be just as bad, but seemed somewhat better. But, there's there's the no loot issue.

: There's a spot in Oasis, over near the NRo zone, where there's a pretty good Orc spawn that can be worked to 20L. Just be warned, there are 2 different types of Orc Warriors.

: An Orc Warriors - probably start being green at 16, but you'll still find some blues.
: An Orc Priest - These were sill blue as I approached 20. They are clerics, & can be quite nasty
: A Orc Warrior - These bad boys hit hard, fast, & can desimate a teenage party that is not prepared for serious battle. They're still blue to my 27L wizard.

: Plus, you'll get the other Oasis stuff,,Shriveled & Crypt Mummies,,, Dry Bone Skeletons, but you must watch out for Sand Giants. If you hunt, & pull your mobs to the NRo zone, you should do fine.

Yes! There is a ledge in the corner on the same side as the S. Ro zone, but past it (as far from the beach as you can go). Pulling to here is absolutely fabulous for teen parties. My best group experiences ever were on that ledge at lvls 14-18.
