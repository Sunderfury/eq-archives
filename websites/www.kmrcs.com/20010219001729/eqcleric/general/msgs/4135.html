You may also want to consider helping put one or two of the mobs down quickly by throwing in a few nukes. If you can reduce the number of mobs in play before you need to heal, you get a lot less mobs getting mad at you.

Also keep both DA and DB memorized. The anti tougnt effect and the few seconds of inv has kept me in the battle quite a few times, and on a couple of occations I have had to use both in one fight. Try to heal you tanks up as much as poss before using it though unless you have a backup healer (not being able to heal your tanks becouse you can't cast isnt a situation you want to get into unless there is no altenative). Also set up a hot key to let your party know that you can't cast and can't be cast on.
