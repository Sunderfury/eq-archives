February 4

A few news items and the inevitable comment from yours truly

  • Now the Enchanters are doing a walkout. Seems like so few are happy in Norrath these days.

  • On the Druid SLN nerf (from the Everquest Gameplay forum):

    Question: 

    Just been using the new improved skin like nature following patch. Thanks for upgrading this (not sure why ... but whatever). However please test this thing a bit ?

    Cast on warrior with no buff SLN does 2dmg each hit
    Cast Thorns on warrior with no buffs does 24dmg each hit
    Cast SLN and Thorns on warrior and does 22 (!) dmg each it

    Reversed the sign or something I suppose !

    Answer:

    This is a bug that has been corrected in the spell database. The database allows a spell effect in this case to be added as either a positive or negative number. Several of the shield spells had their signs switched, and it doesn't show up as a bug until those shields are stacked. It'll go in in the next patch, where they will stack correctly.

    -Gordon

  • On this whole thing about the Battle Cleric I have a few comments: 
    • In my opinion the term Battle Cleric refers to tactics, not armor.  Battle Cleric, in my mind connotes an offensively oriented cleric, not one wearing plate armor.  
    • All clerics, for obvious reasons, feel that a high AC is important.  Some, however, feel that mana is more valuable to the group and therefore sacrifice AC for Wisdom.  This difference is especially noticeable among higher level clerics due to the ever-lessening viability of a cleric successfully tanking (i.e. soaking up damage).  The high level mobs (45+) generally just hit too hard.
    • Try to understand the other player's perspective in these arguments.  The perspective of a level 25-35 cleric will be very different than that of a 45+ cleric.  Both people may be right when relating to their own experiences.  It's just that the game changes for clerics so much as we increase in levels.

Battle Cleric?

ROFL!!!  Read the post from Abashi below please.

Cleric Armor
A couple of us have been talking about class-specific armor over the past couple of weeks and will bring it up in a tuning meeting soon.

As far as cleric armor is concerned, I'm ok with the stats, but less so with the AC. The idea of making a cleric choose between being a Wisdom Cleric and a Battle Cleric seems to be a good trade-off, but the AC advantage should be pretty good if they choose Battle Cleric.

January 24

How many of you find the game almost unplayable?

No, I'm not talking about the Cleric Temple Quests, instead I'm referring to the frequent crashes and severe overcrowding that has occurred after Christmas.  For the last few weeks zoning for me and many others has become an adventure; I have yet to keep a log but I crashed at least 5 times while zoning last night.  I don't know if this is a networking issue or server capacity issue, but the game is really starting to feel unstable.  And these issues are definitely Verant's; I use real-time voice connections to my other group members and that doesn't go down, just the game.

Also, while Fennin Ro is generally the most crowded server, Karana is definitely in the running for a money position.  We are frequently hitting 2400 and yesterday peaked at around 2700.  I have no idea where all the new people are hunting, but it's getting extremely difficult to find hunting places.  Remember when I was excited about Kedge, Kithicor, and Paw?  Yes, Paw is open, but after having spent Saturday night there completely sweeping the top 1/2 of the zone (sweep as in nothing alive) the loot (or experience at 45) just isn't there.  I expect we're going to return and sweep the bottom and experience the same disappointment.  Oh, and the pathing is pretty bad too, making the zone more dangerous than it should be.  Kithicor is now dead in the day time, but even before was not really a good place to hunt for experience (too many roamers).  Kedge I admit I haven't spent enough time in.  We need new zones and we need them soon.

January 4

Future changes for this site

Over the course of this month I will be moving this site.  It's popularity has grown to the point where it is no longer feasible for me to pay for it's maintenance out of my pocket (i.e. bandwidth over-utilization charges).  It will have a new address, http://eqcleric.gameglow.com and will become part of the Gameglow network. 

It will also have banner ads.  The Gameglow people are hoping to one day make money from these sites and banner ads is part of the price sites such as mine must pay to get free hosting.  Oh, don't get me wrong, if the $$$ start rolling in I'll get a piece of the action also, but at this point I'm not quitting my day job.  While I'm not totally enamored of advertising either, it's quite common and I've learned to just tune it out.  I'm certainly not advocating you ignore any advertising displayed on this site (far from it), but I've seen enough messages about this issue over the course of EQ Cleric's existence to know how some of you feel.

Do not send me e-mail offering donations (unless you are talking three figures or greater).  I'm really not interested in collecting $5 a month from various people.  Don't get me wrong, I think it's wonderful that people make those posts and send me those e-mails, it shows that they really appreciate this site.  However, I'm going to go with the normal advertising model and hope that covers the costs for this site's continued existence.

I will try to ensure that all the old messages from the forums are carried over, but at this point will not guarantee it (only because I have yet to do all the research).  That is my number one goal for the move of this site, so rest assured I will work on it extremely hard.

As always, I would like to thank all of you who come here and help make this place the wonderful community for clerics that it has becom.

Rayne, Karana
