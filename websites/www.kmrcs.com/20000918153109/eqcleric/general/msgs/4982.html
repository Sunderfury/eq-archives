I feel your pain brother. As I posted below I believe that druids and shamans
are a better choice for the levels below 30. Clerics are still a good choice for
a party (don't get me wrong) but a druid or shaman is better.

I am however surprised that you have that much of a problem finding
a group at high levels. I assume you are high level since your going to guk and
SolB looking for a group. Perhaps you are taking the wrong tack. Try starting a group.
"Level XX group looking for a tank and a nuker..."
do a /who all xx yy to find people in your range and start /telling.

If your not in a guild join one. Any guild would want a high level cleric...

best I can suggest.

on soloing it can be done at most any level. If you will give your level perhaps someone
on the board could point you to a good soloing spot. Of course clerics can't solo like Druids or
Shaman at the mid high levels (until 44). Most of my soloing is doing experience giving greens.
The best option for exp is a 2 person group. Get a tank of a bit lower level. (trust me I always see
high level tanks sitting on their butts) and hit a target either outside or by a zone
in a dungeon. I have found it a lot easier to get a small group than a full group to go dungeon exploring.

good luck and hope you stick with it
