Unrest is fine for you, but getting past the guards at the docks on both ends is a bit hard. Easier if you can get a teleport to Faydwer (especially by a druid).

So Ro undead are not a bad idead either, if you want to solo or group for the orcs in Oasis (hated doing crocs- no loot).

Weapons- FS is okay, Cold Iron is better, RTS is not too bad an idea at your level (I never did get 2HB over bad).

As for practice points, I have tons saved up. Start a trade skill or just keep em. You want to make sure you save some fo when you get Specialization, but other than that I've rarely used any.
