Hamar on the Bloodforge Hammer

The Bloodforge hammer quest begins by giving the two ogre heads from Zarchoomi and Corflunk to Byzar Bloodforge in Kaladim warrior guild.  Both ogres are found in Butcherblock.   Byzar will ask you to take a little trip to kill a dwarf by the name of Trumpy Irontoe and bring back his head. Trumpy is found in or around Fish's Ale tavern in Qeynos.   Fish's Ale tavern is near the unused dock.  If you try to kill Trumpy outright you will be slain by his bodyguard Bruno Barstomper.

Hail Trumpy, he will say he wants some drinks.  I believe he wants honey mead, I forget.  You can buy these right there in the tavern.  I had to give him 5 drinks before I got a response.  He then says something about some guy.  Ask about the guy.  He says something about another guy, ask about that guy and Trumpy will say he didn't say anything about that guy and run out of the tavern.  I followed him around behind the arena, rooted him and killed him.  During the fight guards walked up and didn't interfere.  Loot his head ( I got it first time I killed him ) and return the head to Byzar Bloodforge and you receive the hammer.

When you kill Trumpy your faction with some evil groups will raise and your
faction with some good groups will lower.

Hope this helps, sorry it is so vague.  I was doing this at around 3AM so
some details are kinda vague.

Hamar, Karana

 

Additional notes on the Bloodforge Hammer quest from Morepheus Deadbane

Just as Hamar said, Trumpy Irontoe hangs out in Fish's Ale tavern in Qeynos.  He was very easy to find for me. I imagine as this quest becomes more well known, we will see a lot more people hanging out in that tavern, hehe.

Trumpy asks you to buy him drinks. He does not respond to anything else further until you buy him Honey Mead (like Hamar said) and give him one. Trumpy will really like you now, saying you're not as worthless as you look, tells you to buy yourself a drink and get him another one, too. I had to give him 4 drinks before he started talking about the other characters, and you also get faction and experience (faction with the thieves guild, probably). The first character Trumpy talks about is Kane, saying he will have to tell Kane about you. Ask him about Kane, and he will tell you Kane runs the town and then he says something about Sragg. Ask him about Sragg, he will then say he didn't say anything about Sragg, he is obviously afraid of Sragg and his group for some reason, and he takes off and runs out of bar. Follow him out of the bar, if you kill him in the bar, the bouncer in the bar (who isn't really Trumpy's bodyguard, he is just a bouncer that doesn't like people killing the bar patrons, heh) will make short work of you. Once Trumpy walks out of the bar (he walks slowly, he is quite drunk by now), kill him.

When you kill him, your faction with evil groups (ie, Kane Bayle) and the Corrupt Guards of Qeynos goes down and your faction with the Guards of Qeynos and the Merchants of Qeynos goes up (I guess the merchants don't care much for Trumpy and his nefarious ways).

The head on Trumpy Irontoe is just labeled "Dwarf's Head".

Mikaela Tristram on the Staff of the Observers

The first step in the quest is to go to runnyeye and kill the monster known as Borxx.  Borxx is a big evil eye and spawns way deep in.  But once spawned, Borxx wanders around the entire area, so there's no telling where to find it.  That's what is time consuming.....trying to find Borxx to kill it.  What we did was just stay put instead of running our arses all over and wait for Borxx to come to us.  Of course, then you run the risk of another group killing it.  So  we decided to camp the spawn spot. That way, if someone did kill it, we'd be there when it respawned.  Now don't be confused by the big evil eye that sometimes has spawned when you first come in runnyeye.  That one is different. The one you want is called Borxx.  Now, after you kill Borxx, loot just ONE, and I do mean just ONE, lens off it. Make SURE you leave a lens on the corpse or the quest won't work.

Two people can loot Borxx, one lens apiece.  Just so long as the same person does NOT loot BOTH lenses.

You will need at least one other person with you to do this.  Give the lens to the other person, then create a male gnome enchanter agnostic mule character with VERY high charisma.  Put all training pts into his charisma.  He must have a high faction for this to work.

Go to the Steamfont Mountains.  Near Ak'anon there are a bunch of windmills...pass the windmills and continue in the valley from there, and along the left side of the mountain there are two observatories.  The NPC you want there is BRON FRUNGA.  Make sure your gnome has the lens at this point.  Have the gnome enchanter Hail Bron Frunga.  If your gnome enchanter's faction is high enough, she will tell him about the lens.  Do what she says and give her the lens and she will give you the observer's staff.  It is most definitely worth the trouble.....the stats on it are terrific.

Mik

Additional notes on the Staff of the Observer

The location of Brona Frugrin is 1800, -200.  When hailed and asked the appropriate questions, here's what she has to say:

Well met Fandiren (which is the name of my mighty agnostic Level 1 Gnome Enchanter with maxed out Charisma). What brings you out this way? Are you interested in becoming an Observer? No, you look like the adventuring type. The wilds of the Steamfont Mountains is as far as my body goes. But through my [duties] as an Observer, my mind travels the cosmos.

Well, I am quite an accomplished enchanter but most of my time now is spent crafting the magical lenses that enable us to see beyond the ceiling of Norrath and into other realms and dimensions. Say.. In your travels have you encountered any [Evil Eyes]?

Evil Eyes are dangerous creatures of great magical power. You will know one if you see one. There is a rumor that somewhere on Antonica there lives a powerful Evil Eye by the name of Borxx. I believe that with the Lens from her eye and some expert tinkering I could create a device that will enable me to observe the gods themselves in their native planes. If you were to bring me this lens the Eldrich Collective will reward you greatly.

Rayne

 

Hobbletoe on the Bullsmasher Quest

It is a quest that can be done entirely in Ak'Anon.  You need to have a good faction with the Gemcutters (or Gemchoppers, who ever the warrior guild are) as they are the ones that actually give you the hammer. Manic Compten (I think that is his name, the spelling should be close) is the one that you need to give the completed blackbox of the head defective clockwork named Red V.  Red V spawns deep within the Mines of Malfunction between 12AM and 6AM game time (usually twice within this span).  He needs to be killed quickly or else he will run for the door and be killed by a clockwork just outside the door.  When he dies, he will drop some scrap metal, occasionally, some sprockets or gears, and of course, the incomplete Red V blackbox.  To complete the blackbox, you need to get Blackbox Fragments off of the Obsolete Clockworks in the caves or from Runaway Clockworks that I see often by the Dragon skeleton to the West of the city gates.  Fill up the incomplete blackbox with the fragments, click on combine, and give the completed blackbox to Manic in the Gemcutters guild.  If your faction isn't high enough, you will get something else (I got a Round shield once), some money, and factions. It is a nice, nice hammer.  Dmg 6, Dly 20, Weight 5.0 (I think), +2 Str, +2 Agi, +2 Dex Lore Item.  I sold mine today for 50pp because I could summon a hammer to use, and the quest isn't that hard.  I think that Red V is like a 5-6 level MOB.  If you'd like a little more info or anything, let me know, and I'll see what I can dig up for you.

Hobbletoe - 12 season of service to Brell Serilis, Duke of Below

 

Xarina on the Shining Star of Light Quest

If my stats are right the Shining Star of Light is about as good as the Hammer
of Striking which is a level 24 spell.  1HB, Dmg 6, Magic, Delay 28, Wt 5.0,
PLUS it casts Cleanse (purges disease, etc.) CLR PAL only.   You can obtain this
quest from Aeston Wemore outside The Temple of Life in Qeynos.  When asked if
you will be staying near Qeynos or traveling a far, choose a far.   He will tell
you to go to Rivervale and find Kizzie Mintopp, Kizzie is just south of the
tavern in the middle of Rivervale.  Tell her that "I am from temple of life".
She will then tell you to find some guy called Honeybugger outside in Misty
Thicket and find out how to get honeycombs, because she needs them to make Honey
Jum.  (You do not have to see this guy..just  give Kizzie the items)  Bring her
3 honeycombs and 5 gold (don't forget the gold like I did!!! :-P ? honeycombs
can be obtained in the Misty Thicket quite easily by killing bixies). Kizzie
will reward you with some Honey Jum. Take the Honey Jum back to Aeston in
Qeynos, he will reward you with a Potion of Life. Take the Potion to Lempeck,
(he is between the river and the Miller's farm.) you will  receive a rusty
scythe (Lore item version).  I read if your faction is not high enough with the
priests of Life (probably at least amiable) he may attack and kill you.  My
fraction was not high enough for Aeston to talk to me, but at least I was not
attacked (working now to raise that fraction!).  Take the scythe back to the
Aeston Wemore, and you get the shining star.
