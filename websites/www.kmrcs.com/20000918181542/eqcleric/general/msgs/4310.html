The druid teleport sites and the wizard teleport sites are different. Even though they may have many of the same zones to teleport to, the sites are different.

Druid circles look like stonehenge (kind of) with druids and sometimes treants around them, while the wiz sites look like miny pyramids with stone columns that bend towards the center on each corner.
