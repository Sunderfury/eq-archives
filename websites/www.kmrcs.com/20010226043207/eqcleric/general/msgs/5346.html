Do you die a lot or something? What kind of weapon and armor are you using? Do you play more than 5 minutes a day?

I recently got a warrior to 13th in 14 hours of play time. I'm 15th now and plan to be 20th by the end of next week. I still have a full time job and don't play every day(most though).

The trick is to know where to hunt and what to hunt. If you want a really nice zone for that level, go to Misty Thicket. Solo, don't group. Start grouping after about 10-12. Get yourself into some banded armor. Get an RTS(Runed Totem Staff).

Fight only blues. If you are in a group take on whites and yellows. Sit down to heal and get mana. Run to the guards if anything goes sour.

Use tab to quickly target yourself for heals during combat.

Ask higher levels for spare equipment, but don't beg.
