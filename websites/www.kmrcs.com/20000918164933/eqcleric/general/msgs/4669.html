Posted by Akane Milak on October 08, 1999 at 14:25:50:

I had a very successful night in a very odd group. We had 2 enchanters, 2 wizards, a monk, and me. One tank? Yep!

The tank would pull, everyone would nuke = lots of dead lizzies. We would ping pong the lizzies back and forth between the wizzies and me (Wrathing like mad). If we got too many, no problem! Assist on the monk and mez the others. We were unbeatable!

One thing I've noticed is that the PURE classes such as pure tanks (monk/warrior), pure casters (wizzies/enchanters/necros), and pure healers (clerics) can be very effective, if not more effective than several hybrids. However, you have to have at least a few of each for the group to work. Hybrids are great when you don't have enough pures or to round out the party (can't be those shaman buffs for example).

The monk was telling me that he was with 5 casters in the Upper Throne Room in Cazic! Several necros, wizzies, and enchanters, and him! He kept himself healed with mend and feigning when necessary. In fact, the monsters were usually too busy running around at each caster that blasted it to worry about beating on the tank. If one caster got too hurt, the others would taunt with spells. I was quite amazed that it worked at all.

Anyone else have good experiences with strange groups like this?
