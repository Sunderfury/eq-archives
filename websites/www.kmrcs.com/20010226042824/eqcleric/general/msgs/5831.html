Stared a new cleric on saturday night, and have made 8.5 levels in about 13 hours (would have been a bit more but I kept getting impatent waiting for my mana to regen at level 7 and tryed beeting on mobs without enouth mana ;))

Level 1 through 5 should be easy in any Newbie area. From 6 onwards you have to stretch your legs a bit more.

If your soloing always take on blue cons (unless your confident you can win or are close to the guards/zone). Taking on blue cons at this level is very easy. Just keep yourself buffed and melee it (though in the ocational DD to keep your skill up), heal and repeat. If you stick to blues you can take on unlimited mobs with no/little down time.

The only possible problem you are likly to have is overcrowding in the newbie area. If it rearly is that bad make the move and try a differant newbie area. Ive done areas in Halas, Rivendale, Butcher Block & faydark, they are all good spots.
