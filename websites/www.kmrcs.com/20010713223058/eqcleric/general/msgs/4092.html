If you don't mind completely sacrificing AC, there's also the gatorscale leggings (+4wis) dropped by the Ancient Croc is UpperGuk, he's an easy kill, you could solo him, just be prepared for a LONG camp (in 36 hours he spawned 3 times for us) All of this stuff you could get yourself, if you put in the time, i personally won't buy anything anymore, there's not a lot of stuff i can't round up a group to go get for me nowadays.
