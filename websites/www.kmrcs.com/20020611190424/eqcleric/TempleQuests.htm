Virtue of the Reverant
Reward is the Bracer of the Reverant AC 7, +5 Wisdom, Weight 3
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Reverant Kill Zahal in North Karana to spawn the corpse of Regis who drops the Ingot and a flute
2. Icon of the Reverant Give Lianna Ferasa (Rathe Mountains) the flute and she will give you the Icon.
3. Two enchanted bars of platinum  
To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Reverant"
Gavel says: "Regis the Reverent fell in love with a gypsy girl named Lianna Ferasa who lives in the Rathe Mountains. Ask her what has become of him. Bring me the Ingot of the Reverent, the Icon of the Reverent, and 2 Enchanted Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."


Virtue of the Constant
Reward is the Chestplate of the Constant AC 18, +5 Stamina, +5 vs Magic, Weight 7.5
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Constant Kill Sludge in Runnyeye.  He can be found by going down to the river between the Warlord and King rooms, go left underwater.  He's at the end of the underwater tunnel.
2. Icon of the Constant Kill Bilge in Dagnor's Cauldron.  He's a mid 30s shaman, has SoW, and is heavily camped due to his other drops (Kedge Totem - +18 Wis, and the Reed Belt - +5 Wis).
3. Two Galvanized bars of platinum For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Genni you will receive one Galvanized bar.
To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Constant"
Gavel says: "Althuryn the Constant was brutally slain by two aqua goblins. The one called Sludge fled to Runnyeye, the one called Bilge lives in Dagnors Cauldron. Bring me the Ingot of the Constant, the Icon of the Constant, and 2 Galvanized Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."

 

Virtue of the Devout
Reward is the Shield of the Devout AC 9, +5 Str, +25 Mana, +25 HPs
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Devout Dropped by Minotaur Sentries in the Minotaur Caves in Steamfont
2. Icon of the Devout Dropped by the giant Glacial Bear under the river in Everfrost Peaks.
3. Two Vulcanized bars of platinum For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Villisia you will receive one Vulcanized bar.
To obtain the quest say to Gavel the Temperant: "Tell me about the Devout"
Gavel says: "Nebbletob the Devout was once a slave in the mines of Meldrath, the Minotaur Sentry there was particularly cruel to him. He was in the expedition to Everfrost when Brother Theo drown. When trying to rescue Theo he came upon some caves under the river and narrowly escaped death when a great white beast attacked him. He never saw what the beast was, but it shredded his pack where he kept his Icon. Bring me the Ingot of the Devout, the Icon of the Devout, and 2 Vulcanized Platinum Bars and I will forge them into a reward for you."

 

Virtue of the Reliant
Reward is the Boots of the Reliant AC 9, +6 vs. Disease, Weight 4.9
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Reliant StumpWatch '99 -- The ingot spawns on top of a stump in Kithicor Forest.  To get on top of the stump run up the log next to it and jump.  The /loc of the stump is (-90,3300).
2. Icon of the Reliant CabinWatch -- A diary spawns in a burned out cabin in Kithicor Forest.  The /loc of the cabin is (1700,1100).  Give the Diary to Turgan in Lake Rathetear who will give you the icon.
3. One enchanted bar of platinum  
To obtain the quest say to Blaize the Radiant: "Tell me about the Reliant"
Blaize says: "Turgan the Reliant lived in a cabin in the Kithicor woods until there was a great fire. Searching the burned cabins there may turn something up. He always loved Lake Rathetear as well. Bring me the Ingot of the Reliant, the Icon of the Reliant, and 1 Enchanted Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for you."

 

Virtue of the Ardent
Reward is the Gauntlets of the Ardent AC 10, +5 Agility, +5 vs Cold, Weight 3.8
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Ardent Item spawns on the bottom of the river in Everfrost Peaks, /loc 2996, -5771.
2. Icon of the Ardent Kill the necromancer Babbinsbort who spawns on the 3rd floor of Befallen (/loc -800, -184)
3. One Galvanized bar of platinum For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Genni you will receive one Galvanized bar.
To obtain the quest say to Blaize the Radiant: "Tell me about the Ardent"
Blaize says: "Theo the Ardent was in constant war with a gnome necromancer in Befallen named Babbinsbort, but he never could defeat him. Poor Theo drown on an expedition to Everfrost when the ice of the frozen river gave way underneath him. Bring me the Ingot of the Ardent, the Icon of the Ardent, and 1 Galvanized Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for you."

 

Virtue of the Fervent
Reward is the Vambraces of the Fervent AC 9, +5 Dex, +5 vs Fire, Weight ??
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Fervent Kill Gull in the Ocean of Tears (Aviak Island, /loc 2000,4000)
2. Icon of the Fervent Give Jovan (Highpass Keep) two Tumpy Tonics
3. One Vulcanized bar of platinum For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Villisia you will receive one Vulcanized bar.
To obtain the quest say to Blaize the Radiant: "Tell me about the Fervent"
Blaize says: "Jovan the Fervent was always prone to vice. He lost his ingot in a game of Kings Court with an aviak named Gull in the Ocean of Tears. After that he became a drunkard and it has been said he lurks around the bars in Highpass Hold. Bring me the Ingot of the Fervent, the Icon of the Fervent, and 1 Vulcanized Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for you."

 

Virtue of the Penitent
Reward is the Greaves of the Penitent AC 11, +5 Int, +5 vs Poison, Weight 5.6
Items Required: How to obtain:
1. Ingot of the Penitent Spawns in the right-hand water pillar in the Scryer Room (/loc -209, 458) in Upper Guk
2. Icon of the Penitent Kill the Erudite heretic Maugrim in Kerra Ridge.
3. One Magnetized bar of platinum For each Enchanted platinum bar you hand Ostorm you will receive one Magnetized bar.
To obtain the quest say to Blaize the Radiant: "Tell me about the Penitent"
Blaize says: "Astrid the Penitent often visited the frogloks of Upper Guk. She once told me her ingot was hidden underwater there. I found that strange because she couldn't swim. She was also a good friend to the Kerrans of Kerra Ridge, where she lost her life fighting against the Erudite hereitic Maugarim. Bring me the Ingot of the Penitent, the Icon of the Penitent, and 1 Magnetized Platinum Bar and I will forge them into a reward for you."
