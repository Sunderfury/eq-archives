Sheesh... lighten up. I said in the first few
words of my original post this was intended to be
HUMOROUS.

As for the situation you describe... first of all,
if the guy you ressed has a level 39 cleric with
him, why do YOU need to res him?

Second, group/friends/guild all come first. If I
am grouped, I'm not going ANYWHERE to do ANYTHING
for ANYONE outside my group. My mana is reserved
for protecting my group before it will be spent on
anyone else, regardless of what condition my group
is in now. That can always change in a flash, and
while you're running off to be helpful, your group
gets trained and dies.

Third, this game is all about COMMERCE. I can do
whatever I want as far as charging goes. If I lay
down a law that I will only resurrect someone for
1000pp, that's my prerogative. I won't do many
resurrects, will I? No. That's fine. I, and I
ALONE will decide when I will cast ANY spell for
someone else's benefit, and that goes for healing,
buffs or any other spell. I have absolutely NO
duty to you or anyone else, simply because I chose
to play a cleric. No one has the right to invoke
some imaginary rule that I "must" help them because
I'm a cleric. If you know anything about history,
the last thousand years or so in organized religion
featured such wonderful things as letting your
parishioners "buy" their way into heaven by giving
to the church (OK, so that's Christianity... can't
speak much for corruption in other forms of
religion). Just because you see the world through
rose-colored glasses doesn't mean that your view
is right.

My post was only a humorous suggestion to stop what
many people refer to as "res begging." This, if
you don't already know, is where players will look
around for high-level clerics and send annoying
messages asking for a resurrect. From what I've
heard, more often than not, when the cleric gets
there, the person will have a STACK of bodies for
you to resurrect.

Some clerics will run around and do this out of
the kindness of their hearts, or out of some sense
of obligation. I won't, and I'll tell you why.
Because I'm paying $10 a month too, and I'm not
paying it so I can be someone else's life support.
When I die, I slog back to my corpse and play on.
It's not the first time I've lost a level because
I was stupid and died, and I haven't yet, and never
will, beg for another cleric to resurrect me. If
other people would just carry on, instead of pull
the old "woe is me" bit every time a little bit of
misfortune came there way, a LOT fewer people would
complain about this game. Lighten up and enjoy
the game for a change.
