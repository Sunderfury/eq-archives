This from Nezzar Enkahmin of Rodcet Nife :
"Greetings, I am emailing to let you inform players of the
possibility of the Specialization Removal Quest being inop. My
character's name is Nezzar, I play on the Rodcet Nife server,
and am a level 31 wizard. I followed the quest, giving the ruby
to Ostorm like he had asked. He then said that my
specialization is now at 49 and I got faction adjustments 415
and 416. He then asked for the 50 gold pieces I owed him. I
paid him and went on my way. Went to guild, tried to train in
it, but it said that it was maxed. While fighting, during a med
period, glanced at my skill levels and noticed that my
abjuration was average while my evocation was at below
average. The skills were not reset. Now, the only thing I can
think of is the possibility of the 50 gold pieces being to nullify
this effect. I am not sure of this, the 50 gold pieces were in
brackets, but only after doing the quest, giving him the ruby, I
was in a conversation with another at that time and lost the
NPC talk so I could not bring up that dialogue with the NPC
Ostorm. If anyone out there is going to do this quest, have
them ask about the gold pieces and what they are for before
they pay for it. It may be that it cancels the effect. Warn the
others. I have submitted a feedback after I noticed it, (I had
done a petition, but the 2 GM's online got off line before
answering my petition), and I am waiting on an email from
Verant if they respond. Thank you"
