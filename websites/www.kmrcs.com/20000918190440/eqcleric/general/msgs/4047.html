I leveled from 12 to 19 in South Ro solo. some of it was tough going at 12, but there are plenty of solo undead running around for you to spank.

Shrivelled Mummy
Crypt Mummy
Sand Mummy
Ghouls
Skelletons
Dry Bones skelletons

Not to mention two relatively uncamped Dervish cut-throat camps, dune taratuntulas and wandering orcs in the desert part that will give XP up into level 19.

the zone is fairly empty (unlike oasis) easy to get to a zone (unlike befallen) and well worth your time as far as loot and XP goes for those levels.
