Posted by Laocorn on October 14, 1999 at 19:15:15:

I have a theory about why some people seem to lose their connection at least once every time they play (like me) and why others almost never lose their connection (like my brother).

My brother and I both play on the Fennin Ro server out of Qeynos. He is a Monk and I am--obviously--a Cleric. I carry a bunch a gear with me at all times. I have spells, a weapon, shield, food, bags, armor, etc. The point is I am always carrying a ton of items at all times. I tend to lose my connection to EQ at least one to three times every time I play EQ. It never fails; it ALWAYS happens.

My brother, who is a Monk, carries almost nothing with him. He has only food, milk, and a fire beetle eye--that's it. He has lost his connection, as a Monk, to EQ a grand total of TWO times. Both on seperate occasions and both far apart. He can go long stretchs and connection losses are never a problem.

We both use the SAME computer, SAME Cable Modem, SAME EQ account, and the SAME ISP provider.

So why do we have such very very different EQ experiences? I think it's tied into how much your character is carrying! I think the less your character carries the less likely you are to lose your connection. Why this is I still haven't figured out, but from what I've seen, using myself and my brother as test subjects, this is the answer I've come across.

Any comments? :)
