Posted by Chikung (povar) on October 07, 1999 at 09:28:12:

Okay, I know there are manna calcs out there to let you know how much manna you have at a certain level (factoring in +WIS items), but many a time during EQ play, I have seen an item auctioned, say, a +3 WIS earring and I can't log out to see if buying this earring would be better than, say, a +10 manna earring that I already have equipped. So, I have gone through the manna calcs (yes, I know there is a formula you can use during the game, but, like I said, I'm obsessed!)
to provide a Quick n' Dirty list for how much bonus manna you get for ea. +1 WIS.

Levels 1-4 +1 WIS= 0 manna
Levels 5-9 +1 WIS= 1 manna
Levels 10-14 +1 WIS= 2 manna
Levels 15-19 +1 WIS= 3 manna
Levels 20-24 +1 WIS= 4 manna
Levels 25-29 +1 WIS= 5 manna
Levels 30-34 +1 WIS= 6 manna
Levels 35-39 +1 WIS= 7 manna
Levels 40-44 +1 WIS= 8 manna
Levels 45-49 +1 WIS= 9 manna
Levels 50 +1 WIS= 10 manna

So in the above example, it would be better to keep that +10 manna earring equipped until level 20 (+3 WIS x 4 manna Bonus = 12 bonus manna)

**It is apparent that in real life I suffer from a tremendous *lack* of WIS, as I have spent excessive work hours printing out maps, reading strategies and doing manna calcs...
