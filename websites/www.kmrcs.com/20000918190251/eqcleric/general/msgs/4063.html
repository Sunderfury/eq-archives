There is a way to at least break even... My normal group consists of 2 clerics and a druid, each with a manastone. If we're full health but not full mana, we'll each click the stone 3 times (gives about 60 mana each) and then the cleric with the most mana casts Word of Health. This restores the entire group's health, while giving those with less mana a boost, with no net cost. (180 mana spent, 180 mana gained.) Add more casters with stones, and you start making a mana profit.

I think WoH in general is a very underrated spell... In a full group, you can do over 600 healing for only 30 more mana than greater heal. But I have never seen anybody outside of my normal group ever use this spell.
