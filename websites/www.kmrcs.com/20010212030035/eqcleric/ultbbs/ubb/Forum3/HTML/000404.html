I sent the folowing email to Gordon Wrinn about the armor quests:

Just before the Cleric Sit-In, Brad McQuaid sent the following message to
the organizers.

I will discuss your points with the game designers responsible for the
cleric quests and see what they have to say.
--------------------------------------------
Brad McQuaid
Producer, EverQuest www.everquest.com
Vice President, Verant Interactive, Inc.
---------------------------------------------


Later, Gordon Wrinn posted this message on the Official EQ message boards.


Abashi
Station Member posted 12-08-99 11:35 PM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
------
We are aware of some disparities regarding the difficulty of obtaining
some of the class specific armor. You should start to see improvement with
the next patch.
-Gordon


The next patch was performed on Dec 14th. With these items in mind, I have
a couple questions.

1. Has the analysis of the quest armors and quest dificulties been
completed?
2. If completed, have the changes been implemented?
3. If changes have been implemented, are further specific changes planned
or are you done for now?

No spoilers requested. I just want to know if you are still making
changes in that area or if you are finished. I ask because I have not seen
any changes in the Cleric Armor or in the quests for them.

Thanks for the info,
Crestus
Rodcet Nife

P.S I posted this on the Official EQ gameplay message board. Please feel
free to reply there if you wish.

When I got back from lunch, this reply was in my email.

Hello Jack,

We're still discussing issues related to the rarity of class-specific armor
components for several classes (clerics included). I jumped the gun a
little when I said "the next patch", as only a few spell-related items were
to be patched on December 14. The rest of the data, which includes updates
to class-specific armor rarity, among other things, won't be going in until
after the holidays.

My apologies for getting everyone excited prematurely.

-Gordon


----------------------------------------------------------------
Gordon Wrinn
Internet Relations Manager
Verant Interactive, Inc. www.verant.com - www.everquest.com
----------------------------------------------------------------

I am so excited that I was answered and that they are still working on it. I was just afraid that they might have been done.
