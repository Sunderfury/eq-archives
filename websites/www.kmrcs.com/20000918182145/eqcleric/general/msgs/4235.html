: 1. I feel that this is a really cool weapon. As is the Bullsmasher (for low levels). I really hope that Verant will come up with some better weapons for the "taller" races, especially at low levels.

Yeah you could say its a cool weapons. Its just the best weapon found in EQ so far. And even though i am a dwarven cleric, i think this weapon is far more suited for a dwarven paladin than us clerics.
In our hands i dare say it would be pretty much useless at high levels. Also note that there will only be this one (per server). Its a unique weapon. So no reason get in a tizzy cause your race dont have one. And i would refrain from jealousy until you see the entire list of unique items avaliable to GMs.


: 2. What do you guys think of these big quests/dragons dropping only a small amount of loot? My understanding of this quest is that only ONE person/server has this weapon. Granted, this weapon REALLY kicks arse but where does that leave the other people that tried this quest? Or how about when 40 people gather to kill a dragon and the dragon drops four items. What about the other 36 people? Then the exp ONLY goes to the "killing" group. I think here needs to be better sharing of these loots and items for such tremendous events. Maybe more "less" powerful weapons?
For one i hate the fact that there is not some form of larger grouping method. So many monsters and even zones require more than 6 players cooperating. It makes no sense not to have some way to allow more than 6 players to group.
I would rather see singular powerful rather than more less powerful items. But thats a personal preference.
