Posted by Citra on October 11, 1999 at 08:49:13:

I would go with:
Cleric
Enchanter
Paladin (some warrior but paladins do well)
Wizard
and fill up the remaining slots as you wish. I prefer hybrids or pure melee...or a second cleric.

The reason for this order is simple. You could do without a cleric under lvl 24. But after 24 having a cleric is really like having 2 warriors. You see a mob will usually pick one target and beat him until he is dead. If the cleric can sustain the warriors life to last 3 times as long...all 3 warriors and the wizard can continue in the battle.

Enchanters increase all casters by 50% or so with clarity and tanks by 40% or so with alacrity as well as having stuns, mez, and nukes. Basically they don't have a lot of power but they increase the groups efficency alot. If three mobs attack and you only take damage from one mob at a time...And you are 40% faster and have more mana....

You need a puller. You need a tank. It's nice if they can take some heals and buffs off the cleric and LoH in a hard fight. Or stun an enemy caster. Thats why I choose paladin.

Wizards...sometimes when you run against an enemy caster the best practice is to meet fire with fire (or ice if you are in solsek *smile*)
