I was bored on Saturday night and I was near Ak'anon so I went to the mino caves and played Helper-Dwarf to the little folks hunting there.

The folks hunting there were in their mid-teens at best. There were 3 in the mines when I got there and I started casting valor on everyone and helping out when they pulled too much. When the mino guards came out I'd kill them off and everyone was terribly happy. It was a good time for all. I like doing this and I try to make it a practice of having an evening like this at least once a week in different places (Befallen, BB, etc.). I stay full in character and the night is better for everyone.

I asked those there (the crowd grew to about 15 under my guidance and they were eventually killing guards with just a few saving spells from me) that if they should see an icon/ingot to let me know and I would be most appreciative. Everyone agreed and oddly enough there were no clerics in this group.

Well, this went on for well over 2 hours. Not a single sentry spawned during that time. So even though I did the right thing and waited an inordinate amount of time, and I used that time to improve the lives of fellow players, I still have a total of ZERO to show from the new quests.

I have visited Everfrost, Rathe Mts, Lake rathe, Kerra Isle, Mino caves and every single location of every quest piece known. And after all of those hours,I have zilch.

Then I se the new items posted on the sites. And I find out teh stats on the breastplate. And I see that I wouldn't trade my bloodstained tunic for both the breastplate and the boots together. So I am officially out of quest mode. Not gonna waste even 10 seconds on these quests anymore. I only have one piece of armor that I would trade for the new armor is my gloves. My only piece of non-magic armor, steel.

I prefer my bloodstained tunic, dwarven workboots, gatorscale legs, azure sleeves, adamintine epolets, runed crown and 2 mithril bracers to the new armor. My armor is better than the quest armor with the exception of my steel gloves.

I play a dwarf and the fun part of this outfit is that I look like a newbie running around except for my bloodstained tunic. It all looks like simple leather or cloth. I can't wait to move to a mithril tunic and then I'll look like a pauper. Only the chainmail look of the runed circlet shows that I have anything nice. I hope that they make a new high +wis headpiece that looks like nothing or even leather. Even thinking of petitioning to have my last name removed.
