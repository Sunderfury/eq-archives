I agree 100% with what has been said. I am currently just a few kills away from lvl 20 (must resist urge to solo in Permafrost!) and have been fighting in Permafrost for the last level or 2.

Our main group consists of a lvl 20 Ogre SK, lvl 18 Bard (don't leave home without one!) and 2 clerics.

Being a HE Cleric, I can't lift my own arms, so tonight I went in with a Skull of J'Hentra and a Chiton Shield. No weapon. I had Healing and LH, Stun, Holy Might, Root, Blinding Flash or Cancel Magic (depending on upcoming MOB), Smite and DA memorized. It worked great!

Only once did we have to run, and that was when both of our roots were resisted twice, and my smite only did 4 damage (wee!), and the gob ran off and grabbed 4 more friends after we just killed a train of 5, with one red to the 20 SK.

If the two cleric had meleed and burned, we would not have killed 3 of them total, and would have died. Also, by staying outside of the fight, you can see if more 'friends' come into the fight. The tanks don't always notice that 2 more are beating on them when 3 already are.

One more tip for the night. When you know you are going up against casters that can heal, make sure that you have 2 stuns memorized. When you see that blue come out of their hands, stun them! Nothing is worse than seeing a MOB get off a G Heal in a huge melee.
