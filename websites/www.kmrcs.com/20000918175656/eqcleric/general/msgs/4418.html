Well, if you are already bored at level 12, I hate to say it
but you might want to try something different. I'm surprised
that you're bored already, as I found the options of playing
a cleric were amazing, especially in a group! But as you get
higher in level, you're likely to find that things get even
more boring because you fight less and med more.

But to really give it a fair shake, you need to play past
level 14, because that's when your life as a cleric really
begins. Everything up til then is just training.
