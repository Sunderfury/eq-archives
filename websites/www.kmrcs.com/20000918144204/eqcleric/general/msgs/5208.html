Hmm... level 6 was a long time ago. I soloed until around 8 or 9, then got into a 2 person group most of the time.

You can still melee pretty darn well at that level, and having medidate helps. You shouldn' have much downtime either with just 2 people, and the XP should be good if you take on high blues and whites.

I grew up in G. Faydwer, so I can't give any MOB tips at all at that level.
