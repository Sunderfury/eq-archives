There is a gnome rogue in Rathe Mountains named Hasten Bootstrutter. If you hail him and ask him about the journeyman's he tells you he wears a pair, but they will disintigrate if you kill him. However he has an extra pair which he will give you for a [rare trade].

Ask about the rare trade and he tells you he wants a Shadowed Rapier (from shadowed men, remember this is a no rent item), a Ring of the Ancients (from Ancient Cyclopses found in plains, island in tears, or dunes - probably a rare spawn)and 3250 gold peices.
