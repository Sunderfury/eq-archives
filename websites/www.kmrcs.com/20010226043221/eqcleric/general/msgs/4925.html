Yea Flash of Light can be very useful in interrupt mobs healer. Once you see "a orc priest starting to cast a spell", cast the FoL to it at once. Since FoL is a fast spell you can mostly interrupt their casting. You can also hot key the spell so it comes handy and fast enough.

Spook the dead can also save life from those nasty ghoul root. However you probably don't have the spare slot to mem it. If you have a polly let him mem it.

Make sure you know how to use Divine Aura and Root too.
