Having played a wizard into the high 20's & then abandoning it... AND now working Tomma to this point as well, there is one VERY OBVIOUS difference between say,,Wizards Shieldskin, & a Clerics Symbol.

Wizards Shieldskin Series = A layer of magical skin, that absorbs dmg. Once enough dmg has been dealt to the wizard, the skin drops. In many fights, this can be just 2 or 3 hits from the mobs that a high 20s wizard would be fighting.

Clerics Symbol Series = This is an actual BUFF, that increases the targets ACTUAL HP's. It lasts as long as it's designed duration, & then drops.

So, wizards actually get shafted here.

I've started a new routine when joining a group, or adding a new member. I give them my "SCHPEAL", hehe. I explain to them that, while I am in touch w/ Mithaniel Marr, I am NOT clairvoyant. If they need buffs, they need to speak up & say so. I don't offer Symbol, unless it's a good friend,, OR if someone either asks & offers to pay or hands me gems.
