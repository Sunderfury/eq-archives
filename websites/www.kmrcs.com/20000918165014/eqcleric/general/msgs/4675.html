In mistmoore, in the graveyard, we'd pingpong anything & everything. Party? 2 Wizards, 1 Druid, 1 Cleric, & 1 Ranger.

With a group like this, the key is,, killing mobs before they could really hurt anyone bad, but the Cleric was always there, just in case. But, primarily was used for offense.
