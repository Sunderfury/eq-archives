Just picking up on this point.

: Of the 4 abilities that aid in shortening a combat or running away:
: (DOT, Debuffs, SOW, and Dmg Shields) a cleric has none and a Shaman or Druid has 3 of the 4...

These are not the only ones,other ones (Cleric specific)

Root- buys you time to run ,(Druids have snare line shamen also have root)removes a mob from battle for a while

Fear- Makes the mob run from you

Lull- reduce battle odds

Buffs-increases the amount of time you can remain in battle,and thus the chances of winning.

Flash of light-blinds mob buys time to run

Stun-Most effective against casters but also good against hard hitting mobs.

DD- Comparable efficiency at some levels.

Guess the formula just changed ;)
