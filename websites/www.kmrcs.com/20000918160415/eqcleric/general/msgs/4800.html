I used to read the rants about how clerics are useless and laugh. A lot they know, I would say. But now I'm just not so sure.

I'm 18th level and have come to the conclusion that soloing is almost impossible, yet I'm also finding it almost impossible to find a group. Groups would rather have druids or shamans, who can heal AND fight (let's not forget SOW) and bards who can heal AND fight etc. Basically I get the feeling I'm gonna be stuck at this level for a long long time.

I don't want to give up on my cleric (I have a considerable amount of time invested in him), and yet, I don't wanna be stuck at level 18 for 2 months while I solo greens and plead for a group.

Night before last I was actually told by a group that they "don't want someone who can't melee to suck up the experience earned by those who can."

I'm worried.
