Plate Armor and Abashi
Posted by SGoldenheal at 18:56 PM

Abashi are Clerics a Plate wearing class? If yes then why cant we wear any of the following armor items, none of them are "class specific" and in some case's non-plate wearing class's can wear.
  1. Mithril Greaves War,Pal,Rng,Shd,Brd,Rog-why Rng and rog and not Clr?
  2. Mithril Vambraces War,Pal,Rng,Shd,Brd,Rog-same as above
  3. Darkmail Guantlets Brd,Pal,Rng,Rog,Shd,War-same as above you have all the plate wearing class's except Clr. And you have 2 non-plate wearing class's.Granted this item may be counted as mail not plate. But still clerics can wear Mail and Rog's cant.
  4. Silver Plated Bracer War,Pal,Rng,Shd,Mnk,Brd,Rog
    same as above all plate wearing except Clr and 3 non plate class's.
  5. Chestplate of the Darkflame War,Pal,Shd <--I think this one is missing Clr. i have heard some lame rumors that we cant wear because its evil well #1 Pal can wear and #2 there is such a thing as an evil Clr.
  6. As a side note we can wear some things with the same name as some of the above such as Mithril Breastplate,but we cant wear #1 or 2 above. We can wear Silver plated Greaves but not #4 above


Being a plate-wearing class isn't supposed to mean that you can wear every piece of plate armor out there. There are still items that are restricted to the melee classes (and yes I'm including the bard in this description, though they're really in a class all their own). You'll notice that even though Shamans can wear chain, they aren't able to wear every piece of chain that rangers or rogues can wear.

-Gordon
