New Quests: They aren't punishment, they are a reward!

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Juul Ekiloretu on October 15, 1999 at 23:01:02:

So as I was afk doing dishes while Juul sat in a burned out cabin in Kithicor this afternoon, I had a rather startling revelation (and no, I wasn't drinking the dishsoap!).
The new cleric quests are not a punishment, they are a REWARD!
Now, I am sure you are shocked, dismayed, and startled at these words ... be so kind as to allow me to prepare my rather convoluted, but entirely logical explination!
As a cleric, I am sure we have all wished for the ability to solo - something that is rather difficult to do if you aren't near some undead of near your level.
Guess what? Now CLERICS can solo!
Juul has become better at sitting in a burned out cabin for tedious hours that she is paying for(255)!
An added bonus!
For everyone tired of "will you res xxxx please?" spam, you can take your /annoy flag OFF!
While you are sitting in a burned out cabin, no one will ask you for a res! That's right - no one! Why not you ask? Because no one other than other people suckered into questing are there!
As a matter of fact, we get to reflect on the state of our dedication to our particular god while we sit for hours, something that is SURELY benifical for any cleric!
If you just hit level 30, a burned out cabin provides you with HOURS of uninterupted pleasure while you work on your specilization!
Now do you see? Verant is doing us a FAVOR with our new quests! How else could they appease all the clerics with solo-envy!
And, as another benifit, they have made the game harder for other players because all the clerics are sitting in a burned out cabin!

Oh, and an aside to cruel rangers: don't camo and drop your damn fishing grubs in my burned out cabin. It isn't funny at all =(

THANK YOU FOR THE GREAT QUESTS VERANT!
I have seen the light, and it is good and pure!
