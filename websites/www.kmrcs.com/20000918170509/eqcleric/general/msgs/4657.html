: Dont get what all the fuss is about recently. Whatever Race or equipment you are/have makes vertuly no differance in the big scheme of things.

: High Elves have a bit more mana, but lack in certain other areas. Dwarves, Halflings & DE have a good wis and some other good & usefull stats. Gnomes and Eurudites are possibly less suited to cleric, stat wise, but can still be fun and forfill there roll as a cleric as much as anyone can.

: Equipment & Quests. There are very few quests worth while doing (except for the newbie quests, and every charicter I have started has had some nice newbie quests) & those which are worth while, can be done by just about anyone as far as I have seen. Equipment is nice, it can help a lot but even a naked cleric is better than no cleric.

: No one is ever going to turn round and say we dont want you in a party becouse your a cleric (unless there roleplaying possibly); or becouse you dont have such and such equipment.

: Any Cleric particuly at higher levels is more than popular and needed regardless of race considerations. As a cleric for the last week I can't remember grouping with anyone less than yellow con to me (mostly they have all been red). If mana & equipment was more important than class, being a lower level with average equipment, I would not have been able function effectivly. As it is I have no trouble at all.

: It is our spells that make us popular not how much AC or mana we have, or what starting stats we start out with. A totaly stat messed up gnomish cleric with no wisdom items at all will still get a party and be able to heal better than any other class in the game, buf as well/nearly as well as any other class, nuke, clam, stun, etc ....

: Play your cleric and enjoy it for what it is, a great class, regardless of race, quests (never found an intresting quest yet, hunt/camp certain item mob - hand in loot).
