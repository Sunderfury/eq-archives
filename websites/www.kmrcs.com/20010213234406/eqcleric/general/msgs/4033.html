Well damn, that kinda makes me feel cheap as a cleric having a skill I can't use. God forbid they make my res spells only work to the party im currently in.

Here's a little scenario which I will say is very common. So lets say my friend is doing a hell level (40 or 45) and they die. They lose a full bub of XP like you do on hell levels. It takes REAL long to get a full bubble on the hell levels, sometimes many nights. You will want to res them saving them many nights of gaming, and getting the corpse back may take a long time. This 2 hr rule hinders my ability to res people.

Make it a day or so and I'll be happy but 2 hrs is just outright ridiculous. This change hinders my role as a cleric, plain and simple.
