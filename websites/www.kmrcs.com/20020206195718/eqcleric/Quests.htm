Staff of the Observer

The Staff of the Observer is a 2h-blunt weapon possessing the following statistics:
12 damage, 35 delay
+5 to both Wisdom and Intelligence
+30 Mana
Charges of the Sentinel spell (can warn you of enemies approaching while you meditate)
10 Weight

The steps to obtain the staff are:

  1. Seek out Bron Frunga in the Steamfont mountains.  She can be found near Ak'anon (look for two observatories).  A very high faction with the gnome(s) is required to obtain the staff.  If your character is unable to obtain the quest from her, it has been suggested to create a gnome character (male, enchanter, agnostic with maxed out charisma).  Hail her to obtain the quest.
  2. Seek out and kill the named evil eye Borxx, who can be found in Runnyeye.  While he has a definitive spawn spot, he does wander around the zone.  Another party member can loot the other lens, but it is imperative that the character who is going to give the lens to Bron only have one.
  3. Give the lens to Bron and you should receive the staff.

Note: It is not necessary to actually do step 1 before steps 2.   However, you should perform step 1 with the character who is going to turn in the lens to ensure that the character has a high enough faction to obtain the quest, and therefore the staff.

Screaming Mace

The Screaming Mace is a 1h-blunt weapon possessing the following statistics:
8 damage, 35 delay
It occasionally casts a Yaulp (+10 str, +22 ac for 18 seconds)
Emits light like a greater lightstone

The steps to obtain the mace are:

  1. Obtain the tunic from Emperor Crush and a Bracer of Battle from the Warlord.
  2. Go to the slaver hut with the Dwarven Smith and Elven Priest.
  3. Hail the smith and give him the tunic.  He should give you a dwarven mace.
  4. Hail the priest and give him the Bracer of Battle.  He should give you a prayer cloth.
  5. Give the priest the mace you received from the smith and the prayer cloth.  You should receive a Screaming Mace.

Note: Several popular sites state the the Shiny Brass Shield is also required for the quest, but it has been posted several times on the EQ_Cleric mailing list that it is not necessary to turn in the shield to obtain the mace.

 

Bloodforge Hammer

The Bloodforge Hammer is a 2h-blunt weapon possessing the following statistics:
13 damage, 50 delay
+10 Magic Resist
13 Weight

The steps to obtain the hammer are:

  1. Obtain the heads of Corflunk and Zarchoomi (two ogres who terrorize between around the x-roads to Greater Faydark zone area) and give them to Byzar Bloodforge.  He can be found in the Warrior's Guild in Kaladim.  Give him both heads at the same time.
  2. He will then ask you if you are willing to take a little trip for him, and if you reply affirmatively he will ask you to go to Qeynos and kill Trumpy Irontoe.  Trumpy can be found around the docks.
  3. Once you kill Trumpy take his head back to Byzar to receive the hammer.

Bullsmasher

The Bullsmasher is a 1h-blunt weapon possessing the following statistics:
6 damage, 20 delay
+2 Str, +2 Agi, +2 Dex
5 Weight
Lore Item

The steps to obtain the hammer are:

  1. Obtain the quest from Manic Compten in the Gnome's Warriors Guild (Gemcutters? Gemchoppers?).  You will be asked to return the completed blackbox of the head of Red V, a defective clockwork.
  2. Kill Red V, who can be found deep within the Mines of Malfunction between 12:00 AM and 6:00 AM game time. Note that he must be killed quickly or he will head for the door and be killed by a clockwork just outside.
  3. He will drop some scrap metal, sprockets or gears, and the incomplete Red V blackbox.
  4. To obtain the remaining Blackbox Fragments kill Obsolete Clockworks in the caves or Runaway Clockworks near the Dragon Skeleton west of the city gates.
  5. Fill the incomplete blackbox with the fragments, press combine, and give the completed blackbox to Manic.  If your faction is high enough you will receive the hammer.  If it is not high enough, you will receive another item and faction.

Shining Star of Light

The Shining Star of Light is a 1h-blunt weapon possessing the following statistics:
6 damage, 28 delay
5 Weight
Special effect: Casts Cleanse (purges disease, root, etc.)
CLR PAL only

The steps to obtain the staff are:

  1. Give Kizzie Mintopp 3 honey combs and 5 gold.  She gives you Honey Jum.
  2. Take the Honey Jum to Aeston Wemore outside The Temple of Life in Qeynos.  He
    gives you the Potion to cure Lempeck
  3. Take the Potion to Lempeck (he is between the river and the Miller's farm in
    West Karanas)
  4. Lempeck gives you a rusty scythe (lore version)
  5. Bring the rusty sythe back to Aeston

    Note: you must have your faction up to do this quest..
