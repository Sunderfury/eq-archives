Why my warrior LOVES a good cleric

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Axebane on October 11, 1999 at 11:18:13:

Ok, I played a cleric till teens, but found that I just did not have the healing touch in me to be a good cleric. I like melee too much.

with that said, I will always attempt to get a cleric in my party. Let me correct that, a good cleric in my party.

I am only at 12th level with my warrior, but I was in Steamfont yesterday and hooked up with another dwarf.

Myself (dwarven warrior) and this dwarven cleric just make a wonderful team.. Reasons why warrior/cleric are a great combo

1. Warrior can take the beating better then anyone.

2. I dodge, parry and overall get missed quite a bid

3. Add cleric buffs for hp's and ac

4. Cleric is smart and does not heal until I am 1/2 hp.

5. Allows cleric to deal out some melee damage while waiting.

6. Cleric has enough hp and armor to take some hits while I taunt em off

7. Runners! Hate em and they are deadly. Cleric stuns em or nukes em.. Thank-you

8. Healing! Mana downtime is much less then hp downtime

9. Find a few undeads (gears near mino cave are great) and you just let cleric drop some nukes while you melee. Since MOB dies so much faster, I don't have to soak up the damage.

10. Oh yeah, my warrior does pound it out pretty good. With my fast dwarven axe I can hit, bash, kick and critical them.

So overall, very nice combo.... Any warrior that does not want a cleric and takes a shaman/druid over them is just plain NUTS
