I agree, Try So. Ro undead, and you can also try Oasis..
I made great xp here by finding deepwater caimens
and regular crocs.. deepwater crocs will prob
con red/Yellow/white to you, so I avoided these
for the most part unless I had a tank.

But just root and nuke with smite... can only
take out when, then med up and find another.
Nicest things about the crocs are they
don't gang up on you and don't KOS.
Bad thing is next to nothing for loot.
You will sometimes get words of deepwater
stuff (go figure, must be something to read
after he finish's munching on somebody) and
these you can sell to players for a bit of cash.

If this gets boring, go to So Ro and do undead.
Sometimes there is undead in Oasis too that
works well as a change from crocs.
Just watch out for specs/sand giants and you'll
do fine.

That's how I made 19 from 18 though was in Oasis,
and pretty quickly too!

Good luck!
