I would go to the zone spot in S. Ro that borders Oasis.
Sit on top of hill then do Sense Undead. Con what you find
first lol, but most of the dead ones should be blue/green.

Occasionally DB's will con yellow to you. Don't try those...
Buff yourself up with much HP's and Endure Fire and Magic.
Pull the mob to base of that hill and ward/expulse to death.
Best to use Expulse to pull with, then use ward in battle as it
takes less time to cast. If you ever get in trouble back up to
top of hill as there is some sort of barrier that all the
undead fear and they run away.... You need to zone to oasis
and back though to make sure they don't go get a bunch of friends
and whack you up. Good exp for most part and we can solo
undead real good and there is little chance of dying being
so close to the zone.
