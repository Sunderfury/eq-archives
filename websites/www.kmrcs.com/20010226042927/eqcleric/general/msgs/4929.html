Atone = Forgiveness

Main use: A mob attacks you, you root, move away, and Atone. If it says he calms down, that means he's no longer Aggro for you and will not attack after the root wears off.

Alernate use: A shaman makes a pet. You kill the shaman and the pet is left. Atone the pet and it dissapears because it calms down but has no master to support it. Slashes its poor self for 32000 damage.

Thats mainly it. Have fun!
