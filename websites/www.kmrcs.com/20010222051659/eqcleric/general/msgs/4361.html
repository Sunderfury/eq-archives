It is a slow process. Once you get to 14 you will have
Expulse Undead and Healing. These will be awesome to you.

Wait till you get to the high teens and low 20's that
is when you really start to question things. I haven't
gotten a new burn, undead burn or heal since 14.... And I
won't until 24 on 2 of those 3 gripes...

So 14 is fun, but after 14 if you are bored then you may
want to reconsider. Only good thing is once you hit 14 you will
be able to fight much harder things and join higher groups.

Stick it out until 14 and see what you think...
