I have seen them sell for 200 to 400 plat on 'The Rathe'. I usually sell or trade them based on a 300 plat value.

Depending on your level, it may or may not be a good weapon for you. Up to level 35, I found the Observer Staff to be the best weapon for the Wis bonus and the mana boost. However, I see most high level clerics with a Charred Shield and Gem-encrusted Sceptor.

The mace is a decent combat weapon but by the time you can actually acquire it for yourself, you dont really need it. I really if ever go in to melee at my level.
