My opinion that some of the high end stuff may not be for sale.
Sorry, if that disappoints you but things like BS Tunic just don't
seem to be for sale much. People want to trade these for equal value
not PP. Once people get to a certain level PP isn't as valued
as the rare drop items..

I would equip with a darned good mace first if I were you. Screaming
Mace is darn good and usually 100-125pp. Then look for Bronze
armor in your size. Barbed is also good as it's a little less
AC (Maybe 1 less than Bronze), but it's much lighter and it causes
damage when you get hit. Get any +wis jewelry you can buy also
Maybe a ruby veil +4wis/str or good earrings/rings.

Then with whatever is left buy these 19 spells Daring, Spirit Armor, Holy
Might and endure magic if you have money. Endure Magic last.

Just think you'll have 4 levels and you'll have all the key 19
spells already bought and paid for :). I have not bought any
other 19 spells as I have heard they are all not worth the cost.

Currently I'm almost 21 and have BS Mantle, Barbed Arms, Apron belt
(can't remember name, but it comes from unrest), savants cap, ruby veil and
a screaming mace. You can get all that stuff (except for veil) for about 300pp.
Plus probably your key 19 spells...

Good luck...
Walterscott
