Poll - Camping

 

Issue: Verant has recently proposed a new rule, the yield rule, which is intended to prevent high level characters from camping low level mobs for which they no longer gain experience if lower level characters that would gain experience are also there.  The yield rule would allow the lower level group to ask the higher level character/group to yield the camping spot, with the one exception being for mobs that contain No Drop items such as the Journeyman Boots.

Here are a couple of alternatives that have been proposed:

1) All items should be completely randomized to mobs of the appropriate level.  For example, instead of the Goblin Preacher always dropping a Runed Circlet or a CIMS, any mob between 24th and 26th level could drop the circlet.

Pro: Would eliminate item camping.

Con: Would not eliminate camping, some places are better for experience than others.  Could also make it much tougher to find a particular item since there would be no fixed place to acquire it.

2) Leave the current static mob/item table as it is, but add new items to the game and let them be found on random mobs appropriate to their level.

Pro: Would add an element of surprise to the game (e.g. killing a level 30 mob becomes much more exciting now because it might drop anything).

Con: Would not eliminate the current camping issues which are the cause of the proposed yield rule.

Please select one of the following choices:

I like the game as it is and do not want Verant to make any changes.
I like the Yield Rule as proposed by Verant and feel it should be implemented.
I think all items should be random and tied to the level of the mob.
I think there should be a mix of static mob/items and random items based upon the mob's level.
I have no opinion.
