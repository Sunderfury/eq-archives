I had the same problem with my Earthlink cable modem. I called Earthlink's tech support and they determined my ISP number was either
1) assigned to someone else at the same time or 2) "stolen" by someone. (Sorry I'm not much of a techie so I didn't underand all the
details.)

Anyway Earthlink gave me a new ISP and since then I rarely get booted--done to once every 10 hours of playing time or so.
Previously I was getting booted at least once or twice an hour.

Earthlink tech support was great in resolving the problem. Give them a call.

Good luck.
