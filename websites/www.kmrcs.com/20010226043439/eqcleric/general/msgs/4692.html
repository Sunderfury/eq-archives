Posted by Tomma Trueheart on October 08, 1999 at 15:53:05:

Hey folks,

I'm a semi-new contributer to this list. I had only started playing Tomma, when saw that there was a Cleric Petition going around. I was mostly playing my Wizard at the time. However, I do recall reading the petition, & wonder WTF is this bullshit?

The ONLY valid complaint I felt y'all had, was still being stunned during the duration of Divine Aura line of spells. After all, how can you be knocked senseless, if your invulnerable???

Now that I've played a Cleric well into her 20s, I've changed my thinking in regards to valid changes to the cleric class. I still think that getting stunned while Divine Aura is cast, simply doesn't make sense. But, I have a new thought, & is a good one I think too. Give Cleric's Bash. It's a natural fit. Most Cleric's use the mace/shield weapon combo anyway. Let us put that shield to use! It's not like Bash does enough dmg to be a balance altering difference anyway.

Also, have it be a base skill ratio like our current melee skills are. (4 * level) + 5 = max.

Also, it's amazing that our dodge skill is capped at VERY GOOD. This is purely stupid. This should also, fit out max skill cap of (4 * level) + 5 = max.

Again, I reiterate these 2 changes would not change the cleric class tremendously. However, it would just let us do our jobs a little better. Our jobs is simple, keep everyone alive.
