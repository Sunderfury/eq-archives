And they have SoW and better dmg nukes and melee. I do however feel cleric buffs still surpass shaman and druids at low lvls. For example who cares about str when the str bonus doesn't happen until lvl 20? Yeah so you can carry more =)

I thought shaman and druids (all priests) had the same fighting skills (max at 4*lvl+4)? If so then why doesn't anyone ever complain about shaman or druids being lousy fighters and useless in melee like everyone says clerics are? I, personally, think that melee is fun and I will continue to do so probably the entire time i play this character.

Also, i've heard that clerics get a bonus to their healing spells, so a cleric greater healing is better than a druid/shaman greater healing.. is this true or just a rumor?

I personally group with a dwarven warrior (me being a dwarven cleric) and we have great fun together and make a good team. i've heard that some warriors like shaman or druids better but i don't understand their reasoning.. it's usually SoW that they are after. Why would a warrior want SoW in battle? I don't know about higher level MOBs but the ones that i fight right now don't move around that much, and when there is a runner we certainly don't need SoW to catch it (at worst just DD it or try to root.. both of which clerics can do in a pinch). I would think that creating a good bond with a cleric would be especially important to a warrior because they get better heals faster.. i'm only lvl 14 right now and my brand new heal spell still only heals one bubble on my 13th lvl warrior friend. In 5 levels (19) i doubt it is going to be too teribly impressive... but a druid or shaman doesn't even GET the spell until then.

On top of that, the cleric buffs seem more combat oriented than either druid or shaman.. AC and HP rather than straight stats. You're right on about str bonuses under level 20.. what's the point? Besides.. clerics have reckless str if you REALLY want to carry that extra few pounds back to town.

I'm not saying that druids and shaman are bad classes.. they are very good at their own thing.. but when it comes to healing and combat buffing why would any self respecting warrior accept a substitute?
