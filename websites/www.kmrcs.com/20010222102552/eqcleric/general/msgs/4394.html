: Explain how you would power level a cleric?

Easy, your talking to an enchanter (with a guild behind her as well, it's even easyer)

Cast Clarity on Cleric
Fetch red mob for cleric
Debuff mob for Cleric
Keep stunning Mob
Finish off mob when lower than half hp

Cleric buffs himself fully
Cleric stands in front of it and cast/melee till dead.
Cleric heals himself (for what little damage he/she has taken)

med & repeat till 30+

With a mana stone and enouther healer you could reduce the downtime.

With tweeked equipment, you could reduce downtime and killing time.

Fairly simple, but some skills may need a bit of work after the clerics been leveled. Also sounds fairly borring if it was going to be done seriously ;)
