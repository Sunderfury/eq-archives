The stun line of spells (i.e. Stun, Holy Might, Force, Sound of Force) have an all or nothing saving throw. There are no partial resists with stun. That is why it is resisted more often.

All or nothing spells always have a higher resist rate than others.

Another reason is because the spell stuns, and does damage, meaning that the monster get's a saving throw vs the damage as well as vs the stun.
