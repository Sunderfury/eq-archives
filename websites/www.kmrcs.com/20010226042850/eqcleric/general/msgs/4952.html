Was passing through and saw this post and here's some bits from someone who plays one.

Shamans get the following things to add to melee:

Generally higher natural phsyical stats (although we are in envy of the high wis of many clerics)
Stat buffs
Debuffs - Combat Speed, AC, Str, Resistances
Speed Buffs - SoW, Quickness, etc
DoTs
Frenzy line - Regneration and attack speed. Self Only
Pets - high lvls only

The Raw melee skills are exactly the same though. The idea of Shamans being a tank mage is only through the intelligent use of assistive spells which druids are probably better at anyways.
