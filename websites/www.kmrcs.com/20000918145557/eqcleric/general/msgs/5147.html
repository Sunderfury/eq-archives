Depending on where you are located Freeport area or
Felewithe area. I would go to a Derv camp and group.

3 Camps in N. Ro and they drop good loot and experience.
Not much else to worry about, other than an occasional
Sand Giant.

If you are near Felewithe go to Lesser Faydark and hit
the Sisters Nybright camp. They are like dervs but offer
equally as good as loot and exp. Dervs till 17 are good
though.
