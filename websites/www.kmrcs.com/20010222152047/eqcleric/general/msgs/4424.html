I know you don't want to hear this, and I'm sure others may disagree,
but I would suggest scrapping and restarting before you get too high.
You can certainly play a cleric with low wisdom, but you're handicaping
yourself. 25 wis is 50 mana at level 10 and 250 mana by level 50. That
might not seem like much, but by the time you get there you may wish
you'd chosen differently.

And as for strength, My base is only 60, and this has not kept me from
wearing the absolute best armor available for my level. It just means
that I didn't rush out and get bronze in my teen levels, I went for a
mix of banded/bronze/magic instead. The only time I really had a weight
problem was around level 16-17, when I was fighting far away from any
vendor, and experimenting with bronze armor. After that armor gets
lighter and your strength will go up, plus you'll also get those handy
weight reducing containers. And on top of all that druids, rangers, and
shamans have good +str buffs.


: I just started my first cleric earlier today. I chose Teir'Dal, because I want to have that cool-looking plate someday, and I like to RP evil characters. I put 25 points into Strength (to 90) and the last 5 in Wisdom (which brought it to 98). I realize that at higher levels I will most likely want higher Wisdom, but I also want to be able to wear heavy armor so I can do alittle tanking if the need arises.

: I have played Teir'Dal before, so I knew how bad Nektulos is as far as newbie zones are. So, first thing I do is visit the Necromancer and Thief guilds in Neriak. Why? Because I can get 2 6-slot bags for free! With my 2 mini-backpacks I head to West Freeport. There I slaughtered as many snakes as I could for scales and fangs. When no snakes were available, I attacked Decaying Skeletons, Bats, and Rats (in that order). By the time I had reached level 3, my inventory was full. I hauled everything back to Neriak, sold everything but the snake scales, fangs, and eggs, and headed to the thieve's guild. There is a quest there to turn in 3 scales and 3 fangs for a Dark Ebon Shield. Although it is lore, so I could only recieve one, I noticed that I was receiving nearly 4/5ths of a bubble of experience each time! By the time I had used up all my scales and fangs, I had earned 4 bubbles off that quest alone 8).

: Anyway, I headed off to the Cleric guild, trained the 10 points I had saved up into 2hb (1hb is nearly maxed, and HtH is 5 or 6--I plan on trying to keep them [or at least 1hb and 2hb] maxed), and bought all of my Rank 1 spells--yes, ALL of them 8).

: After scribing them, I headed back to West Freeport and played around awhile before camping. Clerics get some nice spells! A red wolf attacked me, so I Flashed it and ran away 8). And now I don't have to run up to crits because I have Strike (I think... It's the Rank 1 DD).

: All in all, having a blast 8)
