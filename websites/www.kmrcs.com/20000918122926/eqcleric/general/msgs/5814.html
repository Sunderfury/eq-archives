Well with a group of almost all spellcasters it's tough because nobody wants the mob to attack them since they all have very low hp's. What I usually do with a group in that case in root/nuke the mob and only save 2 bubbles of mana for heals at the end. After my nuking is done I usually keep rooting the mob to keep it away from the other spellcasters.

However if you had another tank to taunt the mobs away from you and the other spellcasters it probably would've went alot smoother. But the fact that mobs gang you when you heal is just part of a cleric's life I'm afraid =( If there's a situation where I'm postively guaranteed to die then sometimes I just won't heal. Should I die just because I'm a cleric? I think not. This of course can be different if you're able to cast divine aura and make it out of there but sometimes even DA won't save you.
