Go with the +WIS because:
1. It will grow with you.
2. Less fizzles
3. Less trade skill failures
4. Cooler to say you have a higher wisdom =)

But the counter points for +MANA are:
1. Usually cheaper
2. Better for low-lvl twinking
3. Usable by INT and WIS casters
4. Better once WIS is over 200

And don't forget the other stats on the item. If it is a tie on mana vs wis but one is 6AC and one is 3AC....well there is a tie breaker...
