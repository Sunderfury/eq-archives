WIS vs. Manna

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Chikung (povar) on October 06, 1999 at 15:36:34:

For awhile I've wrestled with the question of whether to equip with the savants cap (+3 WIS) or stick with my Zahren's Coronet (+3 STR, +25 MANNA).

I've heard that ultimately, a Cleric will always want +WIS over manna items, as over time, the manna from WIS will win out.

I did a series of tests on a few manna calcs, and it appears that at level 21 (my current lvl), I receive +12 manna from the cap (Net -13 from the coronet)
At 40, I receive +24 manna from cap (net -1)
and at the zenith of our careers, level 50, I would get +30 manna (net +5).

The AC and STR bonus (along with the +25) manna should win out against +3 WIS everytime.

Of course, hopefully, there will be some bigger and better +WIS items as I grow up (g)

Anyway, hope that helps any other Clerics suffering from WIS/Manna decision paralysis...
