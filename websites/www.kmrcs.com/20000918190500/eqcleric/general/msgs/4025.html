To be honest, Befallen will be either too boring
(if you stay on the top level... everything will be
pretty green to you at 12), or too deadly (if you
get the key and try to play around on level 2). I
still don't bother going back now that I'm almost
15, the benefits are not worth it. Aside from
the massive trains that people are wont to pull
to the zone from the bottom of the dungeon, most
of the second level stuff will be too rough to
handle solo or with a small group, and are very
aggro, and also linked. If you're not careful,
that lone Greater Skeleton down the hall will
manage to pull it's Crypt Mummy and Dry Bones
friends from just around the corner when you hit
it. I've died one too many times in Befallen
between 9 and 14, and I plan on going back maybe
after I hit 20 or so, and I feel I can handle most
of the second level mobs a little better.

You can take the solo hunting route in the Commons,
especially if you look for things like plains cats,
pumas, and some bears... lions and lionesses are
probably a little rough for you at 12. Wisps are
definitely good hunting, but are sometimes hard
to find.

Still better, get yourself two tanks at the same
level (monks are outstanding for this) and go to
Oasis. You will have to get used to the heal/med
routine for this, but then it's good practice for
later life as a cleric. With two pure tanks (I
guess a paladin would be OK too, and help a bit
with some of the healing), you should be able as
a group to keep pulling whites and yellows almost
non-stop. At 14 I made half a level in two hours.
The only problem with Oasis crocs is no loot. If
you're short on cash (and your level 14 spells
will cost you upwards of 30pp in all likelihood),
Oasis is probably NOT the way to go. I rather
liked the Chessboard in Butcherblock, but again,
no loot.

Another possibility is Steamfont. If you hunt in
the area between the windmills and the Minotaur
caves, you should be able to solo the earth
elementals, young ebon drakes, and harpies. A
second person makes the down time a lot better,
whether it's a tank or a caster (I've done both).
These mobs should be white or blue to you at 12,
and they will often drop nice loot, like random
pieces of jewelry or gems. Also, there is a guard
(when he's not being kited by high-levels) right
behind the first windmill, and it's not very far
to run if you're in trouble, and you can sell at
the second windmill. Personally, I hunted
Steamfont pretty heavily between 12 and 14.

Good luck!
