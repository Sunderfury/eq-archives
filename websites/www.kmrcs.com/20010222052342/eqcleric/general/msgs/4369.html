: The following was the ceremony I wrote and used numerous times when I acted as the priestess in another online game. All of my couples seemed to enjoy it. I've modified the names so it fits with EQ. I suggest you let the bride plan all the particulars and go along with whatever she wants within reason (grin). Crowd control is sometimes a problem so prepare a "/shout" macro to calm things down and lean heavily on those who don't comply -- usually your best tool is humor, so use it liberally. The brides get real upset and the grooms go ballistic when things go wrong. I sometimes gave small gifts to the bride and groom, too, and if I knew the players personally, would give a very brief talk about them (relating funny hunting anecdotes, etc.).

: Good luck to you!

: HERE IN EVERQUEST, HOWEVER, IN ADDITION TO LOVE, HONOR AND SUPPORT, THERE ARE EXTRA OBLIGATIONS:

: , DO YOU PROMISE TO ALWAYS HELP RECOVER 'S CORPSE, NO MATTER HOW DANGEROUS IT MAY BE?

: , DO YOU PROMISE TO BE GENEROUS WITH THE CONTENTS OF YOUR BANK ACCOUNT IN THE EVENT THAT MEETS WITH AN UNTIMELY DEMISE?

: , DO YOU PROMISE TO OFFER THAT UNSTINTING SYMPATHY AND SUPPORT, AND THOSE LITTLE COMPLIMENTS SO NECESSARY TO THE FEMALE HEART, WHENEVER MAY NEED THEM?

: , DO YOU PROMISE TO OFFER THAT UNSTINTING PRAISE, SUPPORT AND APPROVAL, SO NECESSARY TO THE MALE EGO, WHENEVER MAY NEED THEM?

: DO YOU PROMISE TO SHARE WITH YOUR SPOUSE THE SPOILS OF ALL YOUR HUNTS?

: DO YOU PROMISE TO ALWAYS AVENGE THE DEATH OF YOUR SPOUSE?
