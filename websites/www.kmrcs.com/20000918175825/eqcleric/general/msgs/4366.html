If you like to group then stick with the Cleric, each level you gain increases your value to a group. The life of a cleric may be dull at times but the one aspect you can always count on is being the center of the social interaction involved in this game.

One of the true benefits of being a cleric, is that unlike many classes we do not taper off at higher levels. A cleric really begins to shine at level 44, when we get a 510 point DD and a 585 point undead spell.
