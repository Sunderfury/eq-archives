Poll - Retribution

 

Issue: The July 27 patch reduced the damage of the level 44 DD spell Retribution by about 10%.  Verant claims that this was to fix a typo.  Before you vote, please look at the information gathered by Ballder.  He provides a detailed comparison of the top DD spells from each class.

Fellow Clerics,
    I thought you might like to know how the other casters DD spells all stack up against one another.  Notice there are 2 kinds of DD spells, shock and bolt.  A shock spell is a spell that will track regardless of movement. A bolt spell you must aim and if the monster were to move you would miss it. Anyways at lvl 50 here is how the classes stack up.
 
Wizard:  Ice Comet 1120 (bolt 49th)
Wizard:  Rend 783 (shock 49th)
 
Mage:  Lava bolt 800 (bolt 49th)
Mage:  Shock of Swords 615 (shock 44th)
 
Druid: Starfire/Ice 615 (shock 49th)
 
Enchanter: Discordant Mind 600 (shock 49th)
 
Cleric: Retribution 516 (shock 44th)
 
Necromancer:  Ignite Bones 494 (shock 44th)
 
Shaman: Frost Rift? 415 (shock 44th) (not sure of this name as its fairly
new)
 
Ballder, Fennin Ro
