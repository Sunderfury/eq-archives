I usually play with the same group, so had not really had this problem before the past week or so...
Since my normal group of friends was not on line this past week/weekend due to rl constraints, I wandered several zones looking for a group to get in. As a level 30 cleric, I had been expecting to just enter a zone, announce I was available, and get in a group. Went in Solusek A, Najena (hoping to get at the fbr for a friend), forget where all else. Saturday and Sunday basically logged on, tried the lavastorm dungeons and camped again to try a few hours later. Do other clerics on Tunare have this same problem? It wasn't that the zones were that crowded with clerics -- in SolA there were 18 people and 1 50th level cleric the one morning -- of course, 6 of them were above 40, just farming the good items before a yield rule gets implemented. It seems like the groups are all using druids for healing -- saw lots of them...
I really wanted to hunt sol a, since there are some items there I really want to get my hands on -- but should I be looking at other dungeons if I want to find a group? (And please don't say Unrest... I have had enough of that place for a long time...)

Suddenly, my rogue (the class that is broken worse than clerics) is a lot more attractive -- can always find a group then, ironically :(
