You are not half tank, you are not even quarter tank (but are close). Even though we may have quite a few HPs, and can wear plate does not mean we are tanks. The fighting class has the skills to keep them alive : Parry, Ripsote, Dodge, etc. Yes we do get dodge but it stopped increasing at good. You can tank for very limited amounts of time. I am a Dark Elf Cleric lvl32, and the only time I tank is when there is no other tank class around, and the rest of the group are casters who can kill a mob quickly. Actually I dont even really tank, I just pull, root the mob they are attacking, tank the others and just keep healing myself while the casters do the work. Works fairly well most of them time, but I have been known to die from this. We are not tanks, but can fill the roll for very limited times. You are not half warrior, you are 100% Cleric a pure casting class that has good hps and can wear plate armor.
