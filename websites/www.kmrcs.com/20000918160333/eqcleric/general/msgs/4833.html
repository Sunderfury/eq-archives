Where to start?

I've played Warriors, Wizards, Clercs & so on...

I've worked each class well into thier 20's or better, too.

Root, whether its being cast by a Wizard, or Cleric,, has always been a sticky issue. Enstill isn't much better.

I must agree w/ the other 2 who've posted. I use Holy Might endlessly, & IMHO is easily one of my most used spells. Holy Might is the 19L stun spell. It had approx 10 sec stun period, & hits for 30 points of dmg, + 1 point per level.

It gets resisted at most,, 10% of the time. Not near as bad as root. These 10 seconds, is more than enough time, for you (or yer wizard) to get off a final blast,, or yer tanks to finish shredding the target.

PLUS, I use it actively during melee sessions.
1) If the mobs are stunned, they're not beating on my tanks.
2) I AM doing SOME dmg.

1 more thing on root,, some mobs inherently have higher magic resistance than others. Some are easier to root than others, as a result.
