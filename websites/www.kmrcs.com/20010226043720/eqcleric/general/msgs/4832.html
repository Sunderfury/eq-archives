Try not to heal to early & dont heal to full hp (unless you rearly need to for some reason).

Have the tanks hit/toughnt all the mobs if possible.

Keep DA and DB memorised at all times. Once you have healed (try to leave this until the tanks have hit/toughnted the mobs a few times) and the mobs go frency hit one of the above and back out.

Set up a hot key saying "Invulnrable cant cast or be casted on" and one saying "Get this mob of me" or somthing similar.

Use the 1st one several times once you hit DA/DB, nothing worse than somone burning mana or even lay on hands to no-effect when you cast this spell.

DA/DB should get the mobs off you (act's like an anti-toughnt) if it doesnt hit the other button until the tanks get them of you. If that tanks can't get them off you get your group out of there by running, evac or Gate; A dead clerics no good for anyone, especialy not you.
