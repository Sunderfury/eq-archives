“We are suppose to be the very best healers in the game but how can we do any healing if we're all sitting on stumps waiting for bags to drop from the sky every 7 hours or so? “

I understand that people who have been playing Clerics for a long long time might be bored with just levelling and want to go quest for some new armor…..but really, to read this board lately you’d think that a person Can’t BE a Cleric without this precious new armor. If questing for the new armor is so hideously boring that you’d rather be out in a group healing …then do it! I promise that your Cleric will not dry up and go poof just because he’s not wearing the new armor :p They didn’t remove existing Rubi so if you had it…you still have it ….if you didn’t have it then why MUST you have this new armor?

I get the distinct feeling that maybe verant didn’t make Cleric specific armor with the thought that EVERY Cleric should have it. With the way Verant set this up, I doubt that nonclerics would ever be camping spots for cleric armor to sell…..and I bet Verant doesn’t want anyone buying their Cleric only armor from other players either. As it stands, you won’t be getting the special armor AND levelling at the same time like you would getting Rubi…..I’ll bet that’s exactly what’s intended.

No doubt this setup is all about anticamping, and the quests may well end up tweaked in the future if the initial deployment isn’t accomplishing what Verant intended. If the rules are different for other classes maybe that’s just to see what works best. Granted it would be nice to see all this done longer on the test server but maybe there just aren’t enough players there to get an accurate read on the effects.

I hope I don’t sound condescending about the whole topic, that is not my intent…I understand the desire to have the new armor….I just don’t see it as ruining my class if I don’t have it. I’m having much more fun just playing my char than you are having obsessing over this new armor.

Lydia
