Cleric Armor Quests- why bother?

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Aldara on October 17, 1999 at 05:30:09:

All I really have to say is now I'm sorry I actually spent time playing the game casually than doing a powergaming camp-a-thon on rubicite.

I'd like to have fun by earning some nice items for myself. I'm not saying I don't enjoy just hanging out with my friends and killing some evil, but part of the fun is getting some nice stuff for your character. Unfortunately for clerics, there isn't any. I was hoping these new quests would bring that into the game, but they don't.

So far, we've gotten this:
Boots of the Reliant: 9AC, +6 sv vs Disease (compare to Rubicite boots: 10AC, wt 3.6). Well, these boots will cost you 2 bars of enchanted plat- say 250pp with enchanter fees. If I showed up in greater fay one night selling boots like that with these stats for 250pp+ I'd get laughed at. Bronze boots are 8AC, BTW, and small ones are lighter.

Gauntlets of the Ardent: 10AC, +5AGI, +5sv vs cold. (compare to Rubicite gauntlets, AC 11).

Vambraces of the Fervent: 9AC +5DEX +5 sv vs fire. (compare to Rubicite vambraces, AC10).

Chestplate of the Constant: 18AC, +8STA, +5 sv vs magic. (Rubicite chestplate 19AC).

Greaves of the Penitent: 11AC +5 INT +5 sv vs poison. (Rubicite leggins 12 AC)

All these items are HEAVIER that the small bronze I'm wearing as a High Elf. Now DEX maybe i can understand. But why would I trade any of my WIS items for the above?

All I have to say is I'm still waiting for Verant to add some GOOD quests/items for clerics, like they have for Paladins (special swords), or shamans (midnight mallet, mortificator's staff). Because they sure haven't yet, for a class that is so one dimensional it's getting dull.

Or to quote Geoffrey Zatkin in his answers to the recent cleric petition- "clerics are the only class that get Superior Heal"- well, maybe the absense of what else he had to say is what's important- i.e. he had no other statements as to why anyone should play a cleric, when they could be playing a druid. I have level 40+ tank friends, and they don't need Superior Heal- just me and Greater Heal- to group on on stuff that con's yellow to them. I'm glad GZ didn't insult us by also pointing out that we get to wear good armor, because every cleric in Norrath knows by now that is total bullshit, when virtually any class was getting rubicite, and when rubicite gets removed, for clerics it is replaced with junk.

Anyway, to summarize, except for maybe the boots (and possibly the Bracers of the Reverent which are rumored to be +WIS, though basically the same as runed mithril bracers apparently), I'm not going to be wasting my time on any of these "quests".

I'd sure be really sorry if I had actually done the quests, bought a ton of enchanted platinum bars and ended up with the above items. Sure glad I checked the cleric boards first so i know I don't have to waste my time. Now I can go about my normal business of staring at my spellbook for 80% of the time I'm logged in.

I guess should've been a druid. Actually, I should've been a ranger, since their armor is actually decent and goes for about the cost of a ruby, which you can buy at a vendor rather than having to hire out an enchanter. I wonder why Rangers get better treatment, BRAD?

Actually, don't get me wrong. I like being a cleric. I just think Verant missed a great opportunity to cut EVERYONE a break here and give us something to do other than kill stuff. When I was level 1 all I wanted to do was to help others. Deep down I probably still do, but right now I think if/when I get to level 49 (if I don't quit this game first), I'm gonna charge 500pp per res and /wave at people's corpses when they can't pay.

In a slightly bitter mood,
Aldara
Level 31 Cleric of Tunare, Xegony
