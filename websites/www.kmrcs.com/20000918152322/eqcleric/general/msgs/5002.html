Why do disillusioned clerics constantly say this? My primary character is a tank, and I darn well know that this is NOT true.

The ONLY times in the entire game that you are matched in healing are 9-14 and 19-24. You get Healing 5 levels before the other classes. You get Greater Heal 5 levels before the other classes. At the point where they get Greater Heal, you get your first Res spell. It may not mean much to you, but it sure as heck can save me long, painful run. The other classes never get another healing spell after GH, but that is where it just starts to get good for a cleric!

To address the other things you brought up, it seems like you never found a regular group. That is unfortunate, the game dynamics not only favor grouping, but regular groups with well matched capabilities. I feel for you - personally, I don't want to be stuck at level 35+ without a regular group of friends. I don't think this issue has anything to do with being a cleric, though - it has to do with Everquest, game dynamics, the friends you have made, the level of your character, and the server you play on. It's about _people_, not classes.

In short, the cleric does NOT suck. It is such an integral part of the game that my tank never wants to be in a party without one. THERE IS NO SUBSTITUTE!

If you think wizards, druids, or shaman are so great, try one. I've tried them all, and they are great - but in comparision to a cleric they just different, not better. There is no better way to gain some appreciation for your character, though, than to start another one. I recommend it highly.

Beril
