I have not spent a lot of time in the Qeynos area, but my advice would be to move east and get yourself bound in RiverVale. Granted it a long trip and full of danger, but Misty Thicket - the area outside of RiverVale has a lot of stuff around that a young cleric around your level can solo well. There are 3 gobblin camps that you can kill to get xp and loot. I've practically filled my bank box with large backpacks that have been dropped from gobblins. At about 8 gold per backpack that is quite a money saver or maker (however you want to look at it). There is also a orc camp that you can get a partner and do. There are young kodiaks running around that are a bit higher level that can give good xp. Also a good thing about RiverVale it's located in a pretty good area. If you have a bit of cash you can get some decent items from people passing through the area. West commons & east commons is fairly close. As is runneye and the gorge. East karanas is near, Highpass & highhold keep are both near Rivervale. It's a pretty good point to go to and you can get to fairly good levels soloing stuff around these areas.

Good luck and happy hunting to you.
