I think the point is that they are not 'better equipped' since all of their equipment is now lying on a corpse in the middle of a zone that was dangerous (but manageable) to them when they were fully equipped. Granted, this isn't as much of a problem with the caster types (tho it is still a problem), but the warrior who is armorless and weaponless is at a serious disadvantage as is the party who is relying on him.

This game definitely rewards 'zone border campers'. Since, IMO, the key to levelling is not getting killed (as opposed to getting *great* xp), I've almost completely stopped going deep into dungeons unless I have a great party. I much prefer to stay in outdoor areas now - the xp and certainly loot may not be as good, but the risk of death is greatly reduced and in the long run, I come out ahead.
