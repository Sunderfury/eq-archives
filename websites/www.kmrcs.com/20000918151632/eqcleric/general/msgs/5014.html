Seems like in just about every thread on this board someone makes a statement about clerics not being able to solo. I don't get it.

I know there is downtime after soloing, but I know from playing the warrior that it's no worse than theirs.

Soloing my be inconvenient (because of downtime) but it is by no means, IMO, impossible or even that difficult.
