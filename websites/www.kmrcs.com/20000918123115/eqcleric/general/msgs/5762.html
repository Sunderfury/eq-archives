Regis the Reverent
-------------------------
The gypsy is in the gypsy camp in rathe mountains (big surprise). You ask
her "what has become of regis" she tells you a guy named zahal the vile
killed him in N Karana. She says you must go kill zahal and her lover's
(regis) body will rise from where he was killed which is at the north tree
line and to the east. She also asks you to bring her the flute off Regis
body if he has it. So off to N Karana I went with a ranger buddy of mine. We
tracked zahal the vile down to the north west of the gypsy camp. He was a
ghoul by the way. After we killed zahal and checked his body (only guts)
then we high tailed it to the north tree line and then went east. Cast sense
the dead to help you find him. We killed him and got the ingot and the
flute. You take the flute back to Lianna Ferasa in the rathe mts. She gives
you the Icon for the flute.
