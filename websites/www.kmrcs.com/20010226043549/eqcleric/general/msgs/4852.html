I have a lvl 36 enchanter and a lvl 21 cleric.

I have the same damn problem. Both are equally important to a group. Clerics are easier to play since you don't rely on the intelligence of others so much. However clerics will get blamed for death more often.

I can tell you a low lvl enchanter and high lv enchanter are 2 whole different things.
At high lvls you rarely do dmg. You clarity anyone with mana and speed up the tanks and mez the extra's. So if you are loving the whole nuke deal and having pets....bye bye. The reason is like clerics are nukes are slow & inefficent. However ours also get resisted more and taunt the mob more (and we have less armor). Our pet does not engage until we get hit. Then it dies shortly afterwards only living long enough to break your mez and make the sleepers mad at YOU. Or we die as the mobs engage (few hit points no armor) or it wanders off and makes friends with 6-10 natives. Jewelcraft is an expensive trade I think there is still a market for more though.

Clerics med as much as enchanters and again at high lvls neither melee or cast dmg spells. Simply buff and heal. Thier heal spells frenzy a mob almost as much as an enchanters DD spells. But the clerics have more HP and AC to cope with it...and the tanks protect them more.

It is a really tough decision which unfortunately I am unable to make myself.....both are classes I don't think any 30+ group should be without both are equally rare since many give up because they hate to med and want to do dmg.

I hope thast helped....
Citra/Bismol
