The new board

So, does everyone like it?  I'm starting to become pretty happy with it personally.  From a web administrator's perspective it's a dream; the package just flat out works.  I can setup new forums in minutes, maintenance is non-existent, it's fast.  Damned nice package.  I still find the bubble up thingy annoying, but if they won't show us threaded messages, it pretty much has to be that way.  I have a few suggestions:

  • Put the person's name you are replying to at the beginning of your message.  It helps in the absence of threads and subjects (damn I wish we could use subjects).
  • Start new threads on old topics after they start getting large.  I notice some of those topics are over 20 messages now.  I personally tend not to read those (too hard to figure out what I haven't seen yet).
  • Use the Search engine.  It's nice, and it's fast.  I've stopped panicking when I've become unable to find a topic.
  • Pay attention to the different categories.  Most people are, but not all.  If you have a serious rant to make, go for it =).  Just put it in the Rant's forum.  I'll probably be pretty lenient on those (insults and gratuitous language are still not allowed).
