After seeing the stats of the cleric-specific quest armor,
I am severely disappointed at the major difference in
stats between our cleric's armor and those of others.
There is not a single point of wisdom in any of the
pieces that i have seen...and thats 5!!
Other classes like warriors and rangers...their pieces
have their most important stat...strength...e.g.,
Ranger tunic has both wis +3 and Str +3
...what do we get for our tunic...some resist poison and
dex or something...another of our pieces has intelligence..
what is up with that? Where is the wisdom!!
The Warrior gloves give +5 STR...our comparitively..our
tunic with is amazingly (sarcasm) great unneeded stat
buffs..is very pathetic.
I hope you people will also share my view..and please
send feedback to Verant to do a little tweaking for
our case. That is, unless you are happy that our armor
gives no wisdom and only provides the stats that aren't
nearly as important!
