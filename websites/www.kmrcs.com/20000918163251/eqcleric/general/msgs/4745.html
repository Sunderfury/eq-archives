: Hello, I am a level 16 cleric, and I was wondering a couple things.
: 1)Is there anywhere i can hunt for ok exp, and fairly good loot?

Depends if you are talking solo or group. If you
want to hunt solo, you will pretty much be stuck
with very little loot. At the mid-teens, all the
good loots (that we can get our hands on, anyway)
are on things like dervs and the orcs in Oasis
(who usually drop bronze items, as well as being
decent XP). Those are hard to take solo, though.
Grouping with just one tank can make it a lot
better.

: 2)Will I eventually become un-broke(if I may coin a term)?

Hmm... depends on what you consider un-broke. I
have just about all my level 14 spells (all the
ones I care about), decent equipment, and over
25pp in the bank, at level 15. I don't consider
myself to be broke at all.

: 3)what items should I start looking for?

Largely depends on what you have and what you
want.
