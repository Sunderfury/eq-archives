Play the way you want to. Obviously you will need more wis - but you have to remember as a cleric your spells come first. When you are close to levelling to a new spell level, go out and purchase all the spells you want/need. Then if you have any money left over afterwards, you can look to buy some of the other stuff. An unprepared cleric is not a good boat to be in. Ex: If you spent 150 pp for some armor or a weapon or something that seemed nice and then didn't have enough money to buy that much needed spell, it was a waste. Concentrate on spells first, then save a bit of money to buy some of the nicer armor pieces like +3 wis earrings, +6 rings and so forth. These items are pretty expensive (unless you know somebody who will make them for cost), so start saving.
