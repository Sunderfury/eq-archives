If you didn't program it in, program it in. It may not be worth it to you, but it is to us - its OUR time and effort you're wasting. OURS.

If you implement this, you are asking for real trouble. Those players who have no problem KSing from you will certainly have no problem taking advantage of something like this.

What Verant needs to do is provide their GMs with the *tools* to discover what they need to know. Maybe they already have them and the GMs are just, for the most part, lazy - but I get the impression that their tools are really lacking. Being able to quickly view a saved position (to verify inventory for example), reload a saved position, determine (via logs possibly) the events of a certain location over the last X minutes, etc. Give the GMs the tools to allow them to determine what the correct course of action is in each case, and maybe they will be less likely to sit on their hands and take the course of least resistance when situations arise. I work in this industry (game development) as a programmer and I would never develop a game like EQ without developing a mature toolset to monitor it - it's simply shortsighted and irresponsible.

My 2 cents...
