Deep dungeon parties require several groups.

Imagine a bard in each and an enchanter in every other group alternated with a shaman(since you can't mez dragons or Fire giants and such all the enc needs to do is clarity and alacrity). Add a cleric and shaman in each group and you are kicking some butt...especially if the tanks have FBSS.

Why? Bards speed attack and afterwards mana and health songs.

Stacks with Haste effects on executiones axe, flowing black silk sash etc...

Stacks with enchanters celerity and clarity

Stacks with shaman quickness for some odd reason (or so said a recent post I read).

So 2 warriors with 4 speed effects on them and good buffs backed by a cleric, wizard, bard, and either shaman or enchanter. Imagine the dmg those warriors would put out!!!

That would truly be an awsome sight.... Each warrior would have to be hitting maybe 3x speed. On top of the buffs STR, AGI, DEX, STA, center, holy armor buffs.

Give the pets the same buffs and 2 FS daggers with a 19 delay....
