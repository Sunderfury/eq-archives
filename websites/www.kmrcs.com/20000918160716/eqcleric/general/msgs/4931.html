Ideal Group:

1 Cleric
1 Warrior
1 Wizard
1 Bard
1 Ranger
1 Shaman

Cleric: reasons are obvious to everyone who frequents this board.

Warrior: There is no better tank or can dish out melee hurt like a Warrior. Warriors may have a hard time soloing due to their lack of versatility, but in a well balanced group, they do what they do BEST.

Wizard: DD and Evac. any questions?

Bard: I had a hard time choosing between a bard or enchanter for this spot. Bard won in the end. Why? Manasong is faster mana regen than Clarity. Skillfull bards may also "stack" multiple songs to produce simultaneous effects ie. manasong, healsong, etc.... Bards have the ability help tank when needed (if both warrior and ranger are occupied and a gob is beating on the cleric/wiz). More versatility.

Ranger: Their ability to track EVERYTHING in the zone. Pull, Tank, Snare, Sow.

Shaman: one of the best DOT dishers in the game, offensive buffs, Chloroplast, Canabalize, additional healer in crisis.
