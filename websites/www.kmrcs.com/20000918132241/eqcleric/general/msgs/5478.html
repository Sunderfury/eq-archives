I'd like to thank Castore for pointing me in the right
direction for this quest. Part one involves getting the
diary that is in the burnt out cabin. The location of
the cabin is 1700 x 1100 in the Kithicor Forest. When you
enter the cabin, the diary will be in the back left corner
on an area that looks like grass.

The diary tells you the Ingot is on an old stump in a ring
of trees/stumps. Location is -90 x 3300 also in the forest.
You need to run up the ramp and jump to the stump. I fell a few
times trying to make the leap, but did finally. The Ingot
appears as a small bag on the stump.

With Ingot in hand, off to Lake Rathe to find Turgan the
Reliant. He can be found in a cabin along the shores of
lake rathe at 3639 x 145. Here is where I am currently stuck.
When Hailed, he says "what is it that you want". I have tried
every combination I can think of....even upper and lower case
responses. Nothing so far has worked....and I'll be darned
if I'm gonna hand over the diary and find out it vanishes with
getting the icon.

With the spawn times of the diary and ingot set at 7 HOURS, I'm
going to take my time and figure this one out. If anyone else
finds the answer, please post here. Thanks.
