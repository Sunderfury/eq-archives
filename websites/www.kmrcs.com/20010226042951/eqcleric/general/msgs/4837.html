I am currently level 24 (just hit yesterday). I don't see why everybody seems to think that the cleric class sucks so much. I have had no major complaints with my class. I have soloed & grouped with little difficulty all the way until now. I started off soloing until around level 12 then I grouped for a short period of time, then I went back to soloing and kept switching. If I could find a group in the area I was at, I grouped. If I couldn't find a group (which wasn't very often) or didn't feel like grouping, I went to places I knew I could solo. Maybe it took longer to level soloing the easy blue stuff, but oh well. I get compliments all the time from groups that I'm in about how well I heal and how I take pride in my given profession. For those of you that are having problems, maybe you need to take a step back and re-examine yourself and see what you are doing wrong.
