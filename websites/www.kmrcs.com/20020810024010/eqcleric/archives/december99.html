A thought or two on Paw

I'm really interested in other people's experiences in that place.  I've been there a half dozen times, and must admit I have mixed feelings about it.  On one hand, I love the fact that it is relatively empty.  After numerous nights of 90+ players in LowerGuk (makes things a tad bit crowded) it's nice to have a zone with less than 20 players in it.  On the other hand, I think the reason it has so few players is the risk/reward ratio is really really bad.  

The behavior of that dungeon is very different now than it was before the revamp.  It's one thing to leave numerous clusters of four or more mobs up when they are level 9 or so and do not aggro once green.  It's another to do it when they are all >20, usually >25-27.  It is quite frequent to open a door and have 7 or more mobs pour out at you.  Granted, there are ways around this but the drops really don't justify the effort.  An 8 dmg, 31 delay 1hs sword is really not very special when handed to a level 40 Warrior.  And a lower level group might have some difficulty acquiring it.  

Pathing is very screwy in this place.  For example, I tried to do a pull (I was the tank, hehe) and Pacify failed on the greenie, all 5 came (nice for a level 39 spell eh?).  I took the mobs away from the group and dove into the water (we were working the snake pit near the Judges/Executioner spawn).  I then swam to the middle of the pond, where the pillar is.  At this point I had 4 Gadulian (or however it is spelled) snakes on me.  No problem, I gated out (with 200 spec alt you'd be amazed what you can gate out through).  The snakes then teleported up to where my group was.  Nobody tried to heal me or anything yet the snakes went there.  Very odd.  When I come back in all four snakes are at the zone line (huh?).  An hour later they are still there.  I sat invis'd and watched them sit there for 5 minutes with nobody going through, yet they never moved.

The point of this is Paw could be a great dungeon, but more work must go into it.  I have not been there enough to claim to have all the answers, so I am very interested in hearing other people's experiences with it.  Please post any thoughts/experiences about Paw on the General Board.

December 13

Splitpaw Revamped

Finally!  I had an opportunity to visit the re furbished Splitpaw Saturday night and it was a blast.  While at level 40, many areas of the zone were green, it was all blue deeper inside.  Furthermore, everything was blue or higher to a level 33 in our group.

We didn't get an opportunity to camp all the named mobs, but we did manage to pick up a few items:

  • Gloves, AC4, +3 Dex, +2 Wis and, best of all, wearable by clerics (among others)
  • Vac Ra Vim - 1hs, 8 dmg, 31 delay, no drop
  • Snakeskin mask: AC3, +5 vs Magic, +5 vs Poison, quite disappointing actually, but it was an additional drop to the sword listed above.

Most importantly, none of the items listed above were from named mobs.  Instead they were from various mobs throughout the zone.  I'm reasonably certain that the sword, for example, is dropped in more than one place.

I expect to have a much more complete report in a few days.
