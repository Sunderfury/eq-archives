At lower levels there are lot's of places to hunt, as you progress past 30 these areas were hunting is possible get fewer and fewer. The problem you have is not a Cleric issue it is a combernation of the serer maturing and fewer places to effectivly hunt.

Since hitting 30 and reluctantly leaving my regular party (combernation of outleveling and a few factions/disruptions) I have been hitting areas like Cazic, Lower Guk and Sol B.

On my server this hasn't been a problem for me. Primaraly this is becouse I am a cleric (and there must be fewer clerics than on your server).

I can pritty much wounder into Lower Guk and get a group within minutes or even seconds. A simple shout of "anyone need a cleric" often gett's me 1 to 3 tells almost Straight away. One most occations there have been tank's/druids/casters looking for groups before and long after I have found a party.

Sol B is a bit less succesfull, but I am, or at least was the last time I was up there, still fairly low for a lot of the stuff in that area. I only get a group here about 75% of the times I have been here.

If you are having problems on your server it is becouse you are seeing the same sort of problems as these other classes are seeing on my server.

The only suggestion I can think of is to try and find a good guild which hunts together on a regluar basis, try a differant server, or start a new chariacter.

Your post seemed to suggest that it was a a cleric problem, which is simply not the case.

For me personaly it just seems to be getting better, the people I have been meating and grouping with give me tell's to join there groups, and I have been included in a number of guild hunts (not my guild) when they have been short of clerics.
