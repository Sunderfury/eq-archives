There are a few in the Western Commons, in desert side corner where the desert meets the grass, or if you come from Kithicor, take a right and follow the zone wall up to the corner.

Be warned though, we had a group of 5 (Enchanter, 2 Warriors, Druid and Cleric (me)) - the first spawn there were about 3-4 of them...went down pretty easy. Next spawn was 5...went ok as well, a little more hurt though. The spawn after that was 6 - 2 Necromancers and 4 cone guys (Think they're clerics) and all hell broke loose. The druid died, as well as the two tanks (All over lvl 32) so unless you got a all 30+ group, be careful...
