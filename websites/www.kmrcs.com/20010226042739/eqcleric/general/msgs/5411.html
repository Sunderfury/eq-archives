You have the right lense, but you have to get really drunk in real life and give 500pp to Bandayd Woundmender who stands in a burnt down house 6 hours a day and give him the evil eye lense. You will receive a Cracked Staff and your faction increases with "Bandayd Woundmender and the Slayers of Slaw". Then you walk up to the Gnome in Steamfont and click on him and press 'A' with the cracked staff in your hand. He will award you with some Exp loss and a teleport back to your bind point. You will then have the observer's staff.
