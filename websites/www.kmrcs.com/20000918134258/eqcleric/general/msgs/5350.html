Putting the "Jest" back in Everquest... or simply QUEST for EVER.

[ Follow Ups ] [ Post Followup ] [ Message Board - EQ Cleric ]

Posted by Psomm, the Mentalist... on October 14, 1999 at 13:19:00:

They tell you there are new quests - something I honestly feel will eventually work... just not the SAME WAY they've BEEN doing it.

1. Here are all the zones one must visit to complete the quests: Kithcor, Lake Rathetear, Befallen, Everfrost Peaks, OOT, HHP, Upper Guk, Kerra Ridge, Runnyeye, Dagnors Cauldron, Meldrath mines. (this is BEFORE you get re-directed (diary, Lianna, etc)).

This, in itself, is enough to keep most on-the-ball-high-level clerics WITH a druid busy for a several weeks or more (even if the re-spawn was within 5 minutes). We dont need to wait 6 - 9 HOURS PER Ingot or Icon or Spawn or whatever. It's simply a time waster and I think that was EXACTLY what they had in mind - waste an exorbanant amount of time questing in hopes that common camping points got some relief.
If this was not their intention and they simply wanted to make more quests for us, then they need to pay attention; the biggest complaint from EVERYONE all the time is the ridiculous spawn times and worthless waiting around. I'd rather clean my refridgerator than sit online for 6 hours (possible getting disconnected or having someone else nab the spawn) waiting for a quest item that may or may not be worth it. Not to mention it may be broken and ALWAYS non-refundable by the guides if it is so.

My solution:

1. Make quest items some of the best items in the game and make them time-efficient so they become FUN and worthwhile. Thats all we want - fun. Fun. Fun. Not boring or irritating to a fault.
Then even if they are nerfed or take forever, we MAY forgive and continue to quest.

2. EMPOWER GM's. If somone reports a problem in a petition, assume they are telling the truth and do what you can as opposed to the standard of doing as little as possible.
If you need to, make a "check" next to every petition so you know how many petitions a person has had in their EQ life to better determine the few who will "Get one over on you". If you didn't program it in, program it in. It may not be worth it to you, but it is to us - its OUR time and effort you're wasting. OURS.

Psomm, the Mentalist...
