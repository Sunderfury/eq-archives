Depends on what kind of earrings, rings you have.
At 24 I currently have silver rings +2 wis ea., and gold
earrings, +3 wis each. I also bought a blackened Iron
necklace for +3 wis, a Savants Cap +3 wis, silver
ruby veil +4 wis, and Lizardscale Mantle +3 wis.

Most of these items are approx 20-30pp per item other than
the veil or mantle. Veil ran me 180pp and mantle was 50pp.

Depending on your armor type you may want to try
and get some bronze items too if you can't find the wisdom
items.

Tip on finding a good Jeweler, I did a "/who all enchanter".
Picked the highest level enchanter and asked if he could
make me certain items. He said he could and I bought them from
him. Very nice as he was accomodating to me at a meeting
place and time. He was pleased too at the ease of doing
business, no running through zones advertising, etc.

So if I were you I would try that first. Instead of running
zone to zone shouting/auctioning for items maybe there is
an enchanter in the town near you who can make the items you
need quick.

Hope that helps...
