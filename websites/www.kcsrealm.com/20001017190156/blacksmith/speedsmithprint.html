Fedaykin's Guide to SpeedSmithing!




The sweet thing about this guide is that you don't need metal bits. At all. This has been designed with speed in mind, but it is extremely expensive. Which makes it perfect for all those high level rich paladins!!

-K.C.

First off, this guide is the quick and dirty way for a higher level player to become a master smith within short period of time. In order to achieve master smithe you will need around 300-400pp. If you are a young smith and cannot afford this, I suggest you read the smithing guide on this page and make alot of bits =)

This can all be done in Freeport.

Day 1) Train 20 points in to smithing.. this makes bits trivial. (or at 21 I believe)

Start making steel bonings until trivial.

Place the items into the forge as shown. Click COMBINE.

+ + =

Day 2) Make Sheets of metal until trivial.

Place the items into the forge as shown. Click COMBINE.

+ + =

Day 3) Make Gorgets until Trivial.

+ + =

Viloa.. around 350pp later you are a master smith. (I did this with my paladin and his INT is only 75).

Each one of my RL days were EQ sessions that lasted about 1.5 Hours.

Well, I hope you enjoyed my guide, now GET TO WORK!!
