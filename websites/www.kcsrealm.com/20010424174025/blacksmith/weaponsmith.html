Last updated: 05/13/00

As of 10/13/99, You can now create weapons in Everquest! This is a great step, and we have been waiting for this for a very long time. I am posting everything I know on the subject, but I still need some info. Like when it becomes trivial, when you can first start making it, etc. I made all three items yesterday on the first try, so I am assuming they are trivial to a master smithe. I will include my standard picture step-by-step guide to making weapons! I am also looking to see if they have any other weapons! If you have any of this info, please SUBMIT it.

 

I now have the complete text of the new weaponsmithing book:

Dirge of the ForgeFire

It is a nice example of bad poetry on the part of Verant, but its better than nothing!

  1. FORGED MORNING STAR

    This weapon is made using 1 water, 1 Hilt Mold, 1 Spiked Ball Mold, and 1 Metal sheet. All of the molds are found in the blue import shop in east freeport. A few of the molds are also found at the Trader's Holiday. The metal sheets are found at the Armor Shop.

    Place the items into the forge as shown. Click COMBINE.

    + + + =

    This weapon has a damage of 8, a delay of 38, and a weight of 9. It is a non-magic 1 hand blunt.

  2. FORGED BASTARD SWORD

    This weapon is made using 1 water, 1 Metal Sheet, 1 Pommel Mold, 1 Hilt Mold, and 1 Dual Edge Mold.

    Place the items into the forge as shown. Click COMBINE.

    + + + + =

    This Weapon has a damage of 7, a delay of 33, and a weight of 6.5. It is non-magic 1 hand slash.

  3. FORGED TWO-HANDED SWORD

    This weapon is made using 1 water, 1 Metal Sheet, 1 Pommel Mold, 1 Hilt Mold, and 1 Heavy Steel Blade Mold. This is made the same way as the FORGED BASTARD SWORD, but with a Heavy Steel Blade Mold instead of a Dual Edge Mold

    Place the items into the forge as shown. Click COMBINE.

    + + + + =

    This weapon has a damage of 12, a delay of 44, and a weight of 11. It is non-magic 2 hand slash.

    If you have any questions or information about the possibility of magic weapons, please post on the message board.

    K.C.
