On Sizing and armor:

Some armor types must be sized to your client. There are three sizes of armor: Small, Medium, and Large. Some races can wear more that one size of armor

Barbarians, Ogres and Trolls wear Large Armor
Barbarians, Elves, Erudite, Half-Elves, and Humans wear medium armor.
Dwarves Elves Gnomes, and Halfings wear small armor.

Each piece of Banded Armor can be sized can be sized to a small medium or large client. The size of the armor is determined by the mold used. Each section of banded armor requires a sheet of metal and a mold appropriate to the section of the body. Each also requires a flask of water to cool it.
