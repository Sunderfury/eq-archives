ANGELOWAR

Angelowar and I met in the desert just outside of a cutthroat camp. We joined forces to kill the cutthroats and then moved on to the Oasis for a short time before going our seperate ways. She is an excellent fighter and although she is a member of the dark race she didn't act evil and seemed to be compassionate and caring. I found her to be extremely attractive and very beautiful and I would ask Tunare, the Great Mother, to keep her safe and help her to stay a force of good in the world.
