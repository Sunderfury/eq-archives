Clock Ruins
The Clock Ruins of Ak-Anon, located in the South East of the continent Faydwer. The ruins are quite a sight to behold, giant gears and clock towers have become part of the landscape here. The area is infested with undead and other creatures. It is also located near the Minotaur Mines, so you should be careful when travelling in and around this area.
