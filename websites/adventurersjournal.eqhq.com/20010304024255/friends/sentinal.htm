SENTINAL
When I met Sentinal I was wary of grouping with him. He and another magic user (alas, I cannot remember the name of the other) had asked me to hunt with them and quite frankly I thought that being magic users I would have little or no use for them in battle. I have never been more wrong in my life. Sentinal showed me what a good magic user can do and it was an experience that will stay with me forever. He showed no fear as we fought anything that came our way on the Plains that day, and his fellowship and camaraderie were both very much appreciated.I learned that day not to make quick judgements about people and it is a lesson that I will never forget.
I am glad to call him my friend and would be honored to travel with him again. Stay well my friend and may Tunare strengthen you.
