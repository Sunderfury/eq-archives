TAKANA
I met Takana very early in my travels and since then we have adventured together quite a few times. She has always been a source for good times, quick with a joke and always in good spirits. Her light hearted attitude never fails to keep me in a good mood and her great beauty (both outside and from within) never fails to keep me enthralled. She also has a great inner strength and any adventuring group would be lucky to have her along. I am deeply honored to call her a friend and look forward to further adventures that we might share together.

I pray to Tunare, the Great Mother, for her continued safety and happiness.

I have recently found out that Takana has been taken to the great hunting ground in the sky. I know that she is happy and in the loving arms of the Great Mother.
