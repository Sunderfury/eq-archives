KIRIANI

I met the ravishing Kiriani in the desert, we sat and talked for a while on one of the outcroppings of rock along the coast. Her intellect is matched only by her great beauty and although we never fought side by side, I am quite sure that she is one of the most capable women I will ever meet. Regardless of the short time we spent together I consider her a friend and look forward to the day when I might gaze upon her lovely face again and share another discussion or perhaps travel together for a time. Until then I pray to Tunare, the Giver of all beauty, for her safety and happiness.
