Great Wall in the Misty Thicket
The wall runs through the middle of the Misty Thicket and acts as a barrier to the many beasts that otherwise might find thier way into the homes of the halflings behind the wall. There is only one way through and it is usually guarded by halflings. The Misty Thicket is a relatively low level area, although the farther you get from the wall the more dangerous it gets. Stay on guard and keep your eyes open and you should be alright.
