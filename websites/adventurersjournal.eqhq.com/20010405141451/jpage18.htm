July 5
During a recent outing with the guild in the Steamfont Mountains, Lady Siniana and I shared a few quiet moments together beneath a shady tree. I was showing the Lady some of my writings and she was showing me some of hers when a gust of wind blew many of our papers. Somehow one of Siniana's Papers got mixed with mine when we were collecting them, my heart leapt when I read it, the poetry was beautiful and I was left speechless. To know that she has feelings for the likes of me is beyond all of my hope. Still, I will not press her for I do not wish to damage anything we have. I thank Tunare for the blessing she bestows upon me and for bringing this lovely woman into my life.
The paper that I found is below, I have changed nothing, the words are hers;

 

JOURNAL ENTRY
Tomorrow, the guild is going hunting in the Steamfont Mountains and my heart is racing at the thought of who will be there. Yes, the handsome Lord Temyez, oh how he makes my heart melt. I have had these words running through my mind all day. I wish I could just tell him how I feel. Well, maybe someday I can tell him all of this....

GREEN EYES

In the lingering heat,
Starry suns circling Overhead,
your quiet Embrace left love
Dancing across my Rosy lips.
Your Callused and tender Fingers gripping my
Arms to fear I may Run away.
"No," I Assure you, "it Would be childish To flee."
I envision Myself drowning in
Your oceans of green,
But their color Wraps me in a Blanket of security.

As my soft fingers trace
The hardness of your arms' Flesh,
I think of your Strength.
Moonlit walks Along a dark beach,
Hearing your heavy breath On my ear.
These arms, Ready to protect me from All harm.
The night grows Sweeter with tender touches
And promises blazing our Love to a brighter flame.

Kisses drenched with honey
Rain wet my lips against Yours,
And our bodies press Closer,
Molding into a Single heart.
Your skin Searching mine
For love's Warmth against bitterness.
Strong jaw line, granite, Yet passionately contoured,
So that it may not injure My face with closeness.
Your oceans of green clearing My muddy brown skies.

Tokens of love received in
Handfuls of wind tossed by
Your callused hand,
Rippling flesh reaching to
Hang onto mine.
Whispers Of love under umbrella suns
Smile to kindle the highest Of all burning flames.
I Love you so much, detailed Perfection, longing to be held.

We beat through one red heart,
See through your oceans,
kiss My rosy lips. I am protected By your arms, you, by my warmth.
Fires crash through green, our Flesh binds as one.
I love you Caresses each heart as it is
Blown from mouthful promises.
Love together an eternal flower.
Hold me please, green eyes, for I love you so much.

Siniana
