Runnyeye
Runnyeye, home to the runnyeye goblin tribe. It is a rather small area located in the mountain caverns between the Beholder Maze and the Misty Thicket. It's a good place to get some good experience, but be careful as it can also be a deadly experience. There are many goblins roaming the tunnels, warriors and casters alike, evil eyes also can be found here. It is a fairly high level area where some very good items can be found. You won't go far here unless you are level 10+ and have a decent party.
