             Walking around Fironia Vie was like walking around in a full sized city. It was hard to believe that the outpost had only recently been established. There were shops everywhere, where all manner of goods could be purchased or sold. Many people traveled through here on their way to the new lands of Kunark. It was here, in the wilderness of these new lands of Kunark , where Temyez thought he may find his Love, the Lady Siniana. She had been abducted in the days past by some unknown assailants and brought here, or at least that was what he had been told.

He pulled the tattered letter from his pocket and read it again for millionth time,

The one you call Siniana is such a strong spirit, I am sad to say she lost much blood whilst we tried to write this note. But what prettier ink than that of a paladin’s blood? However, if you wish to find her, you must come to Kunark and seek her. She witnessed an event that she shouldn’t have, and therefore, she must remain with us to ensure our secret is safe. She doesn't wear a mask, yet sometimes it seems that way. When you first look at her you could actually cry, as some people do. Everything about her is beautiful, but I can't tell you. You must find her yourself.

Someone, who claimed to have Siniana, left this letter at the Guild house, why they left it was unknown. The letter itself seemed to answer the question of why she was taken, but it left many questions unanswered. If she had witnessed something she should not have seen, and they wished to cover it up, why would they leave a letter telling where she might be found? If they wished for someone to come looking for her, what then were there intentions toward her? These and other questions had been plaguing the ranger since he had first boarded the ship in the Butcherblock Mountains. And since he had arrived here in Kunark he had been going from place to place asking questions, trying to find someone who might have the answers, someone who may have seen the Lady, or know who wrote this letter. The answers to his questions had all been the same, "No, haven’t seen her" or "Sorry, can’t help you." All during this searching he is tormented by the vision that he had during his passage here. What did it mean this vision? The woman from the vision had said that the answers he seeks are within. What did that mean? He sure didn’t feel like he had any answers. She had also said that his heart was a prisoner? He didn’t know what that meant either; his heart didn’t feel like it was a prisoner. Hurt, yes. Upset that he could not find Siniana, sure. But a prisoner? No he didn’t think so. He was certain that this vision had something to do with what was happening lately, but he didn’t know how, and it seemed that he wasn’t getting any answers anywhere.

Stepping through a doorway, he finds himself at a tavern he has been to many times in the last couple of days. "Well" he thought, "There are no answers here in Fironia, so I will head out soon, to search the lands as best I can" He decided that would be the best course of action considering that he wasn’t getting anywhere here. Walking up to the bar he asks the bartender for some water to fill his canteens. The bartender takes the canteens and, grumbling something under his breath, walks to the back to fill them up. While he is waiting for the water he looks around at the other inhabitants of the barroom. At the end of the counter a bar wench smiles at him and winks, he turns away in disgust. A couple sits quietly at a table out in the middle of the room eating lunch. The only other occupants are two old timers sitting just a seat over at the bar. The two old men are talking and he vaguely overhears some of what they are saying. "……… treats me so bad I don’t know why I stay wit her" says one of them. "Well now, don’t git yerself all in an uproar Teddy" replies the other, "No, no, I’m sik of it, I’m not goin back there for a few days, I’m gonna let her see what she’s missin" says the first one. The second man sits back and takes a swig of his ale, then he says, "So then, where will ya be goin to?" The first man looks up and scratches his beard, "well, I think I’ll head up and do some huntin out nere the green." The second one looks at him, "Ayuh, is that right?" "Yes" replies the first man, "Spend a few days hunting out there on the green" Temyez loses track of the conversation as he feels a tap on the shoulder, "Here’s your water" says the bartender with a frown. turning to take the water, he hands the bartender a few silver pieces "Thank you" He says. The bartender nods his head and walks away. Heading out the door he smiles about the two old men talking about woman troubles. He couldn’t understand why some people get so upset, I mean he was looking forward to spending his later years with Lady Siniana. Perhaps settling down and getting a little farm somewhere, stop all this adventuring and just be together with her and nothing else. And here was this old man all upset with his woman and talking about leaving her for a hunting trip out on the green. Suddenly, he stopped in his tracks and the world seems to slow down. For a moment he is transported back to a vision that he had just the other day, the floating woman in his vision had said "Your heart is a prisoner", "Your heart is a prisoner of the green, find your heart." A prisoner of the green? He hadn’t known what that meant, now these two old men had used the same term, "The Green." Turning around he ran back inside the tavern, and up to the two old men sitting at the bar. "Excuse me", he half shouted to them, "Pardon me, I don’t mean to interrupt, but what was that you said a few minutes ago about going hunting?" The two men looked at him strangely for a moment, then the one spoke up, "Well, I believe I said I was agonna go on a huntin trip up near the green." "Yes" Temyez cried out smiling, "Yes, that’s it, the green, that’s what you said." "Ayup, that’s what I said" the old man replied with a slight frown. "Yes" Temyez cried out again, "I mean, I’m sorry, what is the green? Where is it? Is it a place? Is it on a map?" "Hold up ther, sonny" said the old man, "The green aint on any map, it’s a place that we old timers refer to, sumbody done gone and give it a fancy name now, but to us it will always be the green, but I kin show you on a map where itis, If’n, you like?" Reaching into his pack Temyez produces a map of Kunark and unrolls it onto the table. The old man looks at him kind of funny, "Yer kinda uppity aint you sonny?" Temyez looks at him and smiles "Sorry about that" the old man looks at him for a moment longer then points to a spot on the map with his finger, "Here itis" he says matter of factly. The next few minutes the old man tells him about the area and traces it with his finger, pointing out different features of the land and such. He tells him about the many old ruins that are located out there and the rumors that there are Ilksar in the area. Temyez thanks the men wholeheartedly and runs out of the place. Running towards the bridge to the mainland. When he reaches the bridge he pulls out the map and holds it up to take a look at it. The area that the old man had traced with his finger is quite large, but it gives him an idea of where to start looking. "I am coming M’lady, I am coming." He says with a grimace, then starts across the bridge, heading into the wilderness.
