Minotaur Caves
The Minotaur Caves are located in an Eastern region of Faydwer known as the Steamfont Mountains. The caves are the home to the Minotaurs, a race of half man, half bull creatures, and is one of the few places where you will encounter them. The Minotaurs are highly aggressive and will attack most adventurers on sight. They are fairly high in level, 15 to 20, and can put up quite a fight. I have also heard of a higher level monster known as the Minotaur Hero who occasionally shows up here and goes on a rampage killing everyone he encounters, unless of course you are able to kill him. Beware in the area of the Minotaurs
