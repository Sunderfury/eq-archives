Athene
I met the beautiful Athene on the western plains very early in both our adventures. We fought together in a small group for a few days. I doubt that she even remembers me, however I shall never forget her as she is one of the most beautiful women that I have met. She is a skilled fighter and a great contributer to the welfare of all group members. I hope someday to meet her again, and adventure with her throughout the realms. Until then, I Pray to the Great Tunare for her welfare and happiness.

UPDATE - As luck would have it, or perhaps fate. I have once again met up with this lovely Lady. As a matter of fact she is now my Guild Sister and I am very honored to have an opportunity to get to know her once again. She has managed to become even more beautiful since I last saw her so many years ago in our youth, and her skills have increased one hundred fold. I had always wished to be reunited with her someday and The Great Mother has granted my wish.  I still recall how my heart jumped so long ago when I first saw her and got to know her a little, and I am reminded of that fact regularly as it still beats faster when I see her now.
