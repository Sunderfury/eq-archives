Steamfont Mountains
The Steamfont Mountains are located in the Southeastern region of the continent Faydwer. They got their name from the erupting geysers of steam that rise up through the land. The area is filled with somewhat lower level creatures, although more dangerous monsters can be found in isolated areas. The area is home to the City of Ak'Anon, the city of Gnomes, and the Minotaur Caves.
