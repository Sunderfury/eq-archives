Butcherblock Mountains
The Butcherblock Mountains are located on the continent Faydwer. The port is located here as well as the city of Kaladim, the entrance to Dagnor's Cauldron, and the Greater Faydark can also be found in this area. It is a fairly large zone where you will encounter many low level creatures and adventurers. There are many things to see in this region as well as many merchants and shops. So, go and explore everything, but as always be careful.
