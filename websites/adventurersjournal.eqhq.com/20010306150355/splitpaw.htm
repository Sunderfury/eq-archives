Split Paw
Split Paw or Infected Paw, as it is otherwise known, is the lair of the white knolls. It is located on the Southern  Karanas Plains and I think it's a great place to hunt for a couple of reasons, first there are very few people there at any one time, second the money, weapons and items are very good. I recommend at least level 9 for hunting in this dungeon, I didn't start hunting here until level 10. Any items that you find here can be sold at the Centuar village located to the Northeast or the Aviak village located to the South.
