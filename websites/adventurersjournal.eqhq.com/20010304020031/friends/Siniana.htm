Siniana
The Love of my life! She is everything to me, my Love, my Life, my Soulmate. I first met her in Surefall Glade when she swore her allegiance to The Holy Order of Tunare. The very first time I saw her I knew something was special about her, I believe that I fell in Love with her at that very moment. From then on my heart belonged to her, I was unable to look at any other woman in the same way.
Since then our Love has grown strong and everyday it seems to get even stronger. I would gladly do whatever she asked of me, even if that meant laying down my weapons and giving up my travels forever. To be with her I would give all I posess and then some. I Love her completely and unconditionally.
She is a strong woman, self reliant, and very proud. She is able to take care of herself and knows the ways of battle well. She has a compassion and Love for others that I am constantly amazed at, freely giving of her time and/or belongings to help another person. She has an internal beauty that is matched only by her physical appearance. She is truly the definition of beautiful to me.
Everyday I thank Tunare for bringing us together and ask for Her blessing upon our Love.

Recently, The Lady and I have been seperated from each other. I will, however leave this page as it is in memory of what we once shared
