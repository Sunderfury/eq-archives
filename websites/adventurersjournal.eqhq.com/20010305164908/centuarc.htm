Centuar Camp
The Centuar Camp is the home of the Centuars, a half man - half horse creature. It is located on the Southern Karanas Plains and is a good place to sell items retrieved from the Split Paw dungeon. They don't seem to sell much here beyond fletching supplies and a few other odds and ends. They seem nice enough, although I wouldn't want to anger one of them.
