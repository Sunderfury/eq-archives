September 14
What a wonderful day, I have finally achieved level 22 in the ranger’s guild. All Praise Tunare, She continues to look after me always. I have spoken to my guild master and received the instruction needed to use a new stronger set of spells. With these spells I will be able to progress much faster and travel much safer. Some of the spells available to me now are ‘Skin like Rock’ which will give me more protection and ‘Light Healing’ which will allow me to regenerate my health much faster. A spell called ‘Enduring Breath’ which allows me to breath underwater and a more powerful attack spell called ‘Ignite’. I truly look forward to using these new spells in battle and in my general travels throughout Norrath. I also received further instruction in the skill of foraging, although I am fairly good at this skill already, the better I get the more food I can find in the wilderness and the longer I can travel without stopping at merchants. I would advise any young rangers to build this skill as much as possible for it becomes invaluable later in your journeys.
Now it is time to focus my energies on obtaining all the pieces of the Ivy Etched Armor. I have two of the components for the bracer and need only save enough platinum to purchase the gem needed and I can at least get one of them. After getting both of the bracers I will probably travel to Castle Mistmoore once again to try to collect the piece needed for the Helm. Soon, Tunare willing I will be wearing all of the Ivy Armor.
