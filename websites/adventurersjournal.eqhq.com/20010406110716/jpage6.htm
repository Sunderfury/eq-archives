December 5
I have reached level 20 and I immediately went to the local rangers guild. The ranger guild trainer told me that I have earned the honor and privilege of taking a Surname for myself, I will take the name of my father and his father before him, the name of D'argos. From this day forward I shall be known as Temyez D'argos, and I will do my best to bring glory and honor to the name, as did my forefathers.

It was a beautiful sunny morning when I finally left the rangers’ guild in Kithicor Forest, after two days of training I was glad to be out of there. I had arrived here after a long, extended stay in the wilderness and although glad to be back in a town again I couldn’t imagine staying here for any long period of time. Upon my arrival I had gone to see the trainer immediately, I felt that I had enough experience to learn the secret of the double attack and the trainer had agreed with me. We had spent the last two days training in this devastating new tactic and I felt good, refreshed, and ready to try out my new skills in battle. Before leaving I decided to stop in to the weapons and armor shop to see if they had anything new. I was looking over the various weapons when a guild trainer approached me and asked if I would be interested in earning a magical weapon. Always eager for a chance to get magical items of any kind, I told the trainer that I would be interested and asked him to tell me more about this magical weapon. He told me about the sword, what a weapon, the short sword of Morin, a one handed sword that was not only masterfully crafted, but also magically increased certain abilities of the wielder. This sword would fit perfectly into my arsenal, and I told the trainer that I would be willing to do almost anything for such a sword. The trainer began to tell me of the dangers that have crept into the forest, the undead creatures and shadowmen that have increased their presence on the outskirts of the woods. In order to defend the forest against these threats the rangers of the area needed information on the whereabouts and plans of these creatures. I was told of a messenger, a ghoul messenger that travels between the forest and an undead camp on the Karanas. This messenger carries the information that the rangers need, if I could intercept this messenger and relieve him of this information, then return it to the rangers of this camp, I would receive the sword as a reward. Excited about this opportunity, I told the trainer that I would find this messenger and return with the information he needed. I headed out of the ranger camp feeling uplifted and focused on obtaining this sword, looking west I lifted my head and said a silent prayer to The Great Mother, then began to move silently through the forest. My trip to the plains of the Karanas was uneventful and as I came down the ridge from High Pass Hold into the eastern edge of the plains I was once again in awe of the beauty of the place. The wide open expanses, the freshness of the air, the complete uninhibited view of the sky, of all the many places I have been the plains are by far my favorite, and not just because my home is so near, its just that I love the openness of it. As I entered the plains I began to search for signs of this ghoul messenger but found nothing, and decided that I would continue to head west searching for signs. As I moved west I began to see signs of creatures passing and when I arrived at the westernmost area of the plains I almost immediately picked up signs of the ghoul and began tracking him. I had been tracking him for some time when I finally spotted him far ahead just going over a ridge, I paused to cast a camouflage spell on myself so I could stay concealed, and picked up my pace so I could catch up with him. I got within about twenty feet and just followed him for a while, he had no idea that I was there and I watched him intently, looking for any signs of weakness, studying his movements. He was very fast, and he changed direction constantly, probably to keep from being followed easily. He looked very formidable and I was weighing my options, deciding if I was capable of defeating him, just as I was leaning towards not attacking him my camouflage spell wore off, and I became visible. The creature turned instantly and attacked me, I had been caught unprepared and took serious damage before I could even put up a defense. The creature flailed away at me with his fists and I knew almost immediately that I was in serious trouble. Here I was just minutes into the battle and I had been wounded badly and worse yet my blows did not seem to effect the beast at all. The ghoul cast a root spell on me and I found myself unable to retreat as it continued to beat on me, it seemed that I was doomed. Suddenly an arrow slammed into the creatures thigh, and it screamed in pain. Another ranger leapt into the battle and started hacking away at the backside of the beast. What luck I thought, possibly this other ranger would be able to draw the ghoul off of me, and I could retreat and heal, but as the battle continued it became obvious that was not to be. The ghoul could smell death and continued to beat on me until I fell, a defiant battle cry escaping with my last breath. Sometime later I awoke, cold and alone, in an isolated area in the mountains of High Pass Hold. The Great Mother Tunare, in Her infinite wisdom had restored me to life so that I may continue my battle against the evil of the land. I know not why She deemed me worthy to bring back from death, I do not question the decisions of The Goddess. I only know that I will thank Her endlessly for the gift of life and will continue to serve Her in whatever capacity that I can. I do not know the name of the other ranger who joined my battle with the ghoul, nor do I know the outcome of his battle, I pray he was victorious, but that is a matter between him and Tunare. Someday I will return to hunt this ghoul again, someday I will earn the sword that was promised to me, someday when I am stronger and wiser. For now I will mend my wounds, learn from my mistakes, and perhaps, just perhaps find some lovely woman to help ease my pain for a night or two.
