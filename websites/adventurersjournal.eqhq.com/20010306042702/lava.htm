Lavastorm Mountains
The Lavastorm Mountains located in the North Eastern region of the continent Antonica is a rough and barren place. Creatures such as Fire elementals, fire Imps, fire goblins, fire drakes, basilisks and dervishes can be found here. This area can be extremely dangerous not only because of the creatures, but also because of the many pools of lava which is an extremely hazardous substance, falling into a pool of lava can mean a very quick death with very little chance of corpse recovery. When hunting here you should be extremely careful. The Temple of Solusek Ro and the entrance to Najena can be found in this region.
