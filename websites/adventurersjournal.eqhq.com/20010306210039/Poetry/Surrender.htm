Surrender

Soft moans of pleasure...
A sweet breeze upon my neck...
Velvet kisses upon my lips...
Beneath me a blanket of soft green grass...
In the distance the sound of water...
Perhaps a water fall...
For I cannot think...
The passion in your eyes seem to devour me whole...
My self control...
There is no more...
For I surrender to the fire in your touch...
The taste of your lips...
The passion in your eyes...
Below the midnight sky...
I make myself whole as I surrender to you...
Giving all and taking all that's returned...
