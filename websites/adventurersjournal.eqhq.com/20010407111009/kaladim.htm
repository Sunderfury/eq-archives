City of Kaladim
Kaladim, the city of dwarves, is located on the western side of the continent Faydwer. The city itself is located inside of a mountain and is home to a cleric's guild, a warrior guild, and a paladin guild. It also houses a couple of inns and bars and a bank for those adventurers who might need to make a deposit or withdrawal. The dwarves themselves seem friendly enough after you get past their rough exterior and the city can be a welcome sight for a tired and hungry traveler.
