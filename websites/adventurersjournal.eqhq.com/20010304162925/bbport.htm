Port at Butcherblock Mountains
The port in the Butcherblock Mountains must be passed through if you are to travel from the continent Faydwer to Antonica. There are merchants inside the wall and guards posted at the gate. Be prepared to kill some time by fishing or by practicing some other skills for it can be quite a wait for the ship sometimes.
