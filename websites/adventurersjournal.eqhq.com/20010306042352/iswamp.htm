Innothule Swamp
The Innothule Swamp located in the lower South Eastern corner of the continent Antonica is a dull, smelly, and foul swampland. Being a swamp it consists mostly of water with small islands and areas of land. Here an adventurer will encounter the Frogloks, a race of frog men and many creatures of the swamp such as snakes, alligators, rats, kobolds, fungusmen, and of course frogloks and the occasional undead. The entrance to the Feerott and the ogre city of Grobb can be found here as well as Guk, the realm of the froglok. The Innothule Swamp is a low level area mainly for very young ogres and adventurers, but you should still exercise caution when traveling here.
