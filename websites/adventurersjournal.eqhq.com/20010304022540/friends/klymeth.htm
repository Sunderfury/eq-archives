KLYMETH
I first met Klymeth outside the city of Kelethin. He wanted to start a guild and we spoke at length about it, deciding that night that we would start "The Holy Order of Tunare".
Since that day he has become a great friend to me. Both encouraging and thoughtful his compassion for others knows no end and I have seen him sacrifice himself to save another. I value his friendship above all else and thank Tunare for bringing us together.
I pray to the Great Mother that she always watch out for him and keep him safe.
