Haemish
I met Haemish a long time ago and from the very first time I met him I felt an instant connection with him. Beyond being just another Ranger he became a good friend and confidant, someone that I could talk to when I needed to, someone that I could fight beside with honor. I am proud to be able to call him a friend and although he has moved on to other adventures and now belongs to another Guild, I still consider him to be a Brother to me and I always will.
I thank Tunare for bringing someone of such character and honor into my life as an example to me of how one should live.
