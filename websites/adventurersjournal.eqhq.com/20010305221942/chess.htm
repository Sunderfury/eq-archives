Chess Board
The Chess Board is located on the continent of Faydwer in the ButcherBlock Mountains. It is somewhat of an enigma and the spawning area for various undead creatures. No one is quite sure why this monument of a huge chess board and pieces exists, but nonetheless it is quite a sight to behold. However be advised that the area is inhabited by the undead.
