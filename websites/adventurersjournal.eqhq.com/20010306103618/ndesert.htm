Northern Desert of Ro
The Northern Desert of Ro, located South of Freeport and the East Commonlands is a vast wasteland of sand and sand dunes. It is home to dervish cutthroat camps and areas haunted with the undead, occasionally a sand giant will roam the southern half, be wary. It can be a dangerous place because it is easy to get lost in the dunes and with the hills you can be attacked by surprise very easily. There is a fishing village and an Inn to the North to buy and sell supplies. The tunnel to the commonlands is also located here in the Northwestern area. As always I would advise you to be careful in the Desert.
