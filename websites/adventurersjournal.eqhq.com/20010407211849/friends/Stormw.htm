STORMWEAVER
I met Stormweaver at the second tower on the western plains. We hunted together for a few hours and did very well against every manner of beast. He seemed concerned for my health on a number of occasions, during battle he would tell me to back off when he saw my health going low and for that I thank him. I believe him to be a good man, genuinely concerned for the safety and well being of others. May the Supreme healer, Tunare, Mother of all, keep you well while you travel.
