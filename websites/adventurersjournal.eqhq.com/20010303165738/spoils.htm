Qeynos Sewers Spoils
  

Magical gloves for monks can be found in the sewers of Qeynos on froglok tads that any newbie can kill. There are several entrances to this zone (one of which is by the monk guild). These gloves can be worn to allow magical attacks by monks.

For those that don't know, there is an easy quest that you can do in South Qeynos and the Qeynos aquaducts. If you enter the catacombs through the water between the bank and the mages area (as opposed to the water entrance directly in front of the bank) you'll be in an area that spawns a named gelatinous cube named Cuburt (aggressive and about level 5). Other things that spawn from that same spot are normal gelatinous cubes (non-aggressive and about level 5-6), mangy rats (aggresive and around 3rd level), and sewer rats (non-aggressive and around 2nd level). The actual spawn point for all of these is a ledge just above the froglok spawn point. You don't have to swim through any of the aquaducts to get to this point, it is along the main walkway you reach when you pop into the aquaducts through the above mentioned entrance. About 20% of the time, Cuburt drops a tattered bloody dress, which is your main goal. You take this back out of the aquaducts and to the guy named Zamel who stands by the bridge over the water in front of the bank (again, this isn't the same way into the aquaducts that you took). Zamel will give you a +3 INT earring (not lore or anything like that) about 3/4 times (the other times he gives you a piece of hematite), plus some faction and a few silvers. You can also supposedly take the dress to some guard commander and you have a chance for a magical amulet of some sort (this is already documented on many of the quest sites), but the +3 INT ring alternative hasn't been well documented. Also, the frogloks down there by Cuburt's spawn point don't hurt your faction with Guk frogloks, and occasionally drop netted equipment, making it a good spot for monks starting in Qeynos to hunt. For anyone on the racewar servers, this will be an especially good place to hunt, as monks could get netted gloves from here (something that normally will be very tough for them to acquire). - Unknown

Ak'Anon Spoils
     

Complete Bull Smasher Quest
You MUST have a very high standing with the gnomes of the Gemchopper hall, especially Manik Compolten. Go to the Mines of Malfunction enter through the doors and go straight, keep going until you hit a room that has a corridor to the left. Go through the corridor and you will come into a room with 4 doors (including the one you came in from) go right and you should pass by a room that as 3 spellcasting trainers. You will end up in the room where red V spawns. Kill him to get the box. You will also need 4 fragments to combine within the box. To get the fragments you need to kill obsolete clockworks (the fragmets can be found on the gnome clockworks and the spider clockworks within the mines) Once you get 4 of them combine them in the box you got from Red V . This Will give you a blackbox. Give the blackbox to Manik Compolten in the Gemchoppers Hall and he will give you some cash, faction, and an item. The item can be any one of the following; rusty weapons, patchwork armor, a lore item named Iony's Absorber (seems to be broken) and every once in a while a Bull Smasher.

Ravenscale Armguards / Lizardscale Belt
Tae Ew Archon
The Archon spawns at the top of the "Temple". Turn right and you'll see a ladder on your right and a door straight ahead. Go through the door and you'll enter a maze. The maze is a little complicated, but suffice it to say that if you're invis and hug the left wall, you'll eventually find outself going up a short set of stairs into what's known as 4 Corners (4c). 4c is a large square area that has 2 lizard spawns at every corner, and recently they've added higher level roamers patrolling this area. Go around past two of the corners and you'll see stairs. These stairs lead into the temple. The temple layout is fairly simple (a square path with a set of stairs at each level), and the Archon is at the top.
Common Drop : Lizardscale Belt (AC2, HP+10, MANA+10)
Rare Drop : Ravenscale Armguards (AC10, DEX+5, +5 vs fire, ROG)
