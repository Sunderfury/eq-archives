SERENA
I met Serena on the western plains and we shared a wonderful day of hunting and exploration. She was new to the area and was fascinated by everything she saw. I showed her as much of the plains as she wanted to see and I was inspired by her excitement and curiosity. I have not seen her since that day although I hope to meet up with her again. She seemed to have a good heart, and I pray to Tunare that she is able to maintain the level of excitement and curiosity she had on the day that I met her, and share it with everyone she meets.
