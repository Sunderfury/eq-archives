Halfling Village
This halfling village is located just beyond the Great Wall in the Misty Thicket. It is a good place to sell some loot or buy some goods for travelling, it is also a fairly safe place to rest a bit. It is a good place for low level adventurers to stay close too, both for the safety and the hunting.
