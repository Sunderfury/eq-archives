July 18
Once again I have found some papers belonging to the lovely, gifted, and beautiful Lady Siniana mixed among mine. I know not if they are there by accident or if perhaps the Lady secretly puts them with mine so I might find them. If she does hide them, I pray that she learns soon that she need not hide anything from me, for I Love Her completely just as she is. My body burns for her and her touch when I read this and think of her. I pray she forgives me for publishing her private journal entries and I hope she understands why I had to. The writings below are from her, written in her hand, and not changed in any way;

Journal Entry - May 20th
Today, the guild is battling in Solusek A, for our beloved Brother Conwen, and alas, during the battle my spirit has left me. So, while I wait for Brother Klymeth to revive my soul, I am going to finish this piece I have been working on for dear Temy. It is so quiet and peaceful here in Butcherblock that I think I will be able to give it to him today. I certainly hope that he likes it, for I have been adding to it since the day I have first made his acquaintance. Oh Tunare, please let things continue to grow between us so that we may have the strongest bond of love ever noted in Norrath. My heart truly does only beat for him if you shall make it so....

I'll Be...

I'll be your eyes, if you can't see
I'll be your legs if you can't stand
I'll be your breath, if you can't speak
I'll be the one to hold your hand

I'll be your words, if you need the truth
I'll be your warmth if you feel too cold
I'll be the light if you think it's too dark
I'll be your youth if you feel too old

I'll be you, if you don't have the strength
I'll be your sun, if you're scared of the rain
I'll be your tears so you don't have to cry
I'll be the one to heal your pain

I'll be your power, if you feel too weak
I'll be your applause if you feel ignored
I'll be the shoulder you can lean on
I'll change the subject if you feel bored

I'll be here, if you need somewhere to run
I'll be here, because you made me see
I'll be here, because I love you so much
I'm here, and I always will be

~Lady Siniana D'artanian~
