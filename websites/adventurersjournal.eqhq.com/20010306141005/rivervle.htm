Rivervale
Rivervale, the city of halflings, located in the middle of the continent of Antonica. You will find the city between the misty thicket and the kithicor woods. It is a pleasant little city to visit and rest after a long journey, the halflings seem nice enough and you can buy many supplies and weapons here. There are docks for fishing and a pub where you can drink an ale and listen to a stories of fellow travellers or tell some of your own.
