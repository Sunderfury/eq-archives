Qeynos
Qeynos, a wonderful city, you can meet many different people here and there are many quests waiting for the curious adventurer. You can find just about anything you need inside the city, from food and supplies, to weapons and armor. The city offers many taverns and inns, an arena for battles and duels, and the docks where you can catch a boat to another continent. If you are a friendly person and good race you have nothing to fear here. However if you are an evil race you will be killed on site by the guards, it may be better for you to stick to the underground of the Qeynos sewer system.
