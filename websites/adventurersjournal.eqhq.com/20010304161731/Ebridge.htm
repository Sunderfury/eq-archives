Bridge from North to East Karanas
This is the bridge that connects the Northern and Eastern Plains. Very dangerous areas to be in, some of the beasts of the area are griffins, evil eyes, reavers, giants, and other assorted high level beasts. The zone border is directly in the middle of the bridge, and many adventurers stay close to the ends of the bridge while fighting the creatures in case there is trouble. There are no guards in the area, so you must use extreme caution when traveling here
