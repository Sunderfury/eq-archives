Question: I screwed up my colors, how do I put them back to default?

Answer: 

  1. Run Ultimate Editor and click "Load Old Default Settings" or "Load New Default Settings" under the file menu. 
  2. Click "Save INI" under the file menu and choose your game directory to save the INI to and click 'Save'. 

 

Question: I don't like some of the text colors in my eqclient.ini, how do I change a message color?

Answer: 

  1. Run Ultimate Editor and click "Load INI" under the file menu and choose the eqclient.ini in your game directory and click 'open'. 
  2. Click on the 'Edit' button next to the text you want to change. 
  3. Pick a color in the color dialog box and click 'Ok'. 
  4. Click "Save INI" under the file menu and choose your game directory to save the INI to and click 'Save'. 

 

Question: My brother/sister/mom/dad/freind/roommate/etc uses my computer to play EQ also, we don't have the same color preferences, is there a way to save our color schemes?

Answer: 

  1. Yes. Run Ultimate Editor and click "Load INI" under the file menu and choose the eqclient.ini in your game directory and click 'open'. 
    Click "Save INI" under the file menu and choose a location and new filename (eg: save the settings to a file called 'myeqclient.ini' in your game directory) and click 'Save'. 
  2. Make the changes that your brother wants and click "Save INI" under the file menu and choose a location and new filename (eg: save the settings to a file called 'brothereqclient.ini' in your game directory) and click 'Save'. 
  3. Now all you have to do before you play EQ is to load the proper settings that you want (click "Load INI" under the file menu and choose either myeqclient.ini or brothereqclient.ini in your game directory and click 'open') 
  4. Save them over the eqclient.ini in your game directory (Click "Save INI" under the file menu and choose the eqclient.ini in your game directory and click 'Save')
