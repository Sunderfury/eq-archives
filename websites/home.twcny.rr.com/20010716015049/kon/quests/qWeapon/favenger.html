Fiery Avenger (Outline)

NPC Text Log

**It is recomended you read the text log also to understand the story. That way if you screw up somewhere, its not so hard to figure out**

Step 1: Kill Dragons and get a Burnt Book and a Frost Book. (You need DarkBargainer faction for this. You can let a DE friend or Enchanter friend loot the books for you if you want.)

Step 2: Go to the Library in Neriak, and give the Burnt and Frost books plus 1000pp to the female barkeep, located in the Blind Fish Tavern of Neriak. You will get the Book of Scale.

Step 3: Go to the Ocean of Tears, and give the Oracle your Book of Scales. He will give you Miragul's Phylactery.

Step 4: Go to the underwater caves of Everfrost, and find the lich of miragul. Have him charmed, and give him the phylactery. (Your faction MUST be indifferent or higher.) This will despawn the lich, and the live one will pop out. Kill him and get the Head of Miragul and Miragul's Robe.

Step 5: Aquire a Ghoulbane and Soulfire.

Step 6: Go to the Plane of Air. Look for Dirkog Steelhand, and pay him 500pp. This will make Inte Akera spawn on the 4th island of PoA. (I don't think they are on the same island, so you might have to have someone left behind as you make your way to Island 4)

Step 7: Give Inte Akera your Soulfire, and get a blessing of nobility.

Step 8: Give Inte Akera your Ghoulbane, and get your blessing of sacrifice.

Step 9: Give Inte Akera:

Blessing of Sacrifice
Blessing of Nobility
The head of Miragul
The robe of Miragul

You will receive your Fiery Avenger.
