Dark Bargainer Faction

DarkBargainer faction is mainly for paladins who have looted the Burnt and Frost books for their epic weapon quest. (Alternatively, you can let a non-kos to neriak friend loot them)

If you looted both halves of the books your self, you need this faction to get them combined in Neriak to get the Book fo Scale.

Faction Raising Quests

You can raise Darkbargainer faction by doing the research quests in South Ro.

Turn in regular Lightstones and Greater Lightstones to the female dark elf smithing things in the Sro DE camp. If she is KOS to you or wont accept the items, you can have a high level enchanter charm the npc to force them to accept your lightstones.
