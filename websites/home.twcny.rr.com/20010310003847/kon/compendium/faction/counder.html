Clerics of Underfoot

Miners Guild 249

These are the followers of Brell Serilis. There are three factions pertaining to dwarven paladins... Clerics of Underfoot, Paladins of Underfoot, Miners Guild 249. Dwarven paladins inherently get Paladins of Underfoot faction and there is no known way to raise it. Faction Raising Quests: The best way to raise Clerics of Underfoot and Miners Guild 249 faction is by bone chips. Give bone chips to Gunlok Jure in the Kaladim Paladin guild. Doing this quest raises factions with Clerics of Underfoot, Kazon StormHammer, and Miners Guild 249.
