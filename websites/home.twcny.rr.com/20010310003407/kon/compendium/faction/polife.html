Priests of Life

Knights of Thunder

This is the main faction for Paladins and Clerics of Rodcet Nife/Karana. Faction Raising Quests: The best way to raise faction with these guys is to get a friend and have them kill Koalindi fish in the pond beneath the Rodcet Nife Temple. Loot the fish, and return the scale to Enic Ruklin. You can also give 2 gold to Lashun Novashine and/or 2 bone chips to raise Priests of Life faction. Doing quests for either of these groups will raise faction with both.
