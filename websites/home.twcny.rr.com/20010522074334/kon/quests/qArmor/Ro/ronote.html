Note about Ro Quests

All Ro quests start in the Rathe Mountains in the paladin camp. To make Ro Armor, you need to combine the Armor Mold you quested for and a bar of Ronium in a forge.   

Tips

To obtain Ronium, click on the Ronium link on the armor quest page.
Make SURE you combine them in a forge, and not a stove!!
Double check all components before you hit combine. It really sucks to have hours worth of work go down the drain because you weren't careful.

How to Make Ronium

   Go to the Temple of Solusek Ro and look for Lord Searfire. Tell him "I wish a bar of the alloy." He will then give you instructions on how to make a bar of ronium. To make ronium, combine these components in the container Lord Searfire gives you.

2 Bars of unstacked enchanted platinum
1 Mistmoore granite (from gargoyles in Mistmoore castle, not ones outside the castle)
1 Sands of Ro
1 Soil of Underfoot (Obtained by giving 4 fairy dust to the head of the cleric guild in Kaladim)
1 Melatite (Drops from gnome clockworks in the gnome mining town of Solusek A)
