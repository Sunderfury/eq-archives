Mithaniel Marr

Mithaniel Marr is Erollisi Marr's brother. They both are credited for creating the Barbarian race, and the human and erudite races that emerged afterwards. Mithaniel Marr followers regard truth, honor, and chivilary above all else. They aspire to be heroes to the opressed, and believe all of Norrath should be bathed in the light of Marr. Followers of Mithaniel are accepted among all the goodly races and religion of Norrath. Devoted followers will have trouble with the Freeport Militia as they take sides in the power struggle that is going on in Freeport.
