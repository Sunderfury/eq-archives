Outline of the Quest

**Note** Its recomended you read the detailed quest info to really appreciate the story aspects of this quest. That way if you screw up one part you don't have to read a skimpy outline and wonder what you did wrong.

Prior Requisites: The Fiery Avenger

Solo Steps

Step 1: Go to Freeport, near the Monks guild. A peasant woman will spawn there sometimes, hail her and say "I will take it to him." Look for Joshua in the bakery nearby and give the water to him. You will get pure water in return.

Step 2: Go to the mines of Kaladim. Look for Jark, hail him and say "I will get your dinner." This will spawn his wife, Nella Stonebraids. She can be found in front of the Paladin/Cleric temple. Ask her "What Dinner?" She will give you Jark's dinner. Return that to him and get a pure crystal.

Group/Raid Steps

Step 3: Go to the plane of hate. Look for the thought corruptor, and loot the tainted breastplate off it. Thought corruptors usually spawn right after a scorn banshee dies.

Step 4: Go to Nektulous. Look for Kirak Vil and kill him. He is about level 56 with a very high regen rate. You need about 2 groups of level 55+ to kill him. There is a strong Neriak faction hit, so tell the people who don't want faction hits to zone out before he dies. Loot the tainted shield when he is dead.

Step 5: Head to the hole, and kill the Keeper of Tombs. Info about the Hole can be found here. This site is for Shamans, but a lot of the tactics is the same. Really good maps of the hole there also. Loot the tainted sword when the keeper of tombs has been slain.

Final Steps

Step 6: You can do these parts solo. Look for Elia the Pure in Felwithe, near the pond in the back. Give her ONLY the tainted shield. She will cleans it in the pure light she is in, and give you a gleaming shield in return.
Next, go to Reklon Gnallen in the Quellious Temple of Erudin. Give him the Tainted Sword and Pure Water, and get the Gleaming Sword. Next give him the Tainted Breastplate and pure crystal, and get the Gleaming Breastplate.
Next: Give Reklon Gnallen the Gleaming Sword, Shield, and Breastplate, and get a mark of atonement.

Step 7 (Final): Go to the plane of fear. Look for Irak Altil, he should be an indifferent npc to you. (If he's not, you will have to have an enchanter charm him).

Give Irak your Mark of Atonement first. If it is not eaten or refused, give him your Fiery Avenger. If the mark is eaten or refused, you did something wrong or the quest is bugged.

He will thank you and give you your Fiery Defender.
