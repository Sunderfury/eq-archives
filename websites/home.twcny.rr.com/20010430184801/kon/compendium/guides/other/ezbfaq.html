EZboards Guide/FAQ

This applies to any EZboard.

1) How do I get signatures and personal icon portraits and stuff? 

On the upper left screen of whatever EZBoard forum you are in is a login link (or My Control Center). Login there, and edit your preferences from your Control Center.

You can also resize your personal photo (usually max size is 60x60) by adding a height and width line to the end of your personal photo link. 

i.e in the box where you put your picture link:

http://www.zyworld.com/takeII/interslayer.jpg height=100 width=80

2) How do I make nifty signatures for each of my post?

Also in your User Preferences is a box for your signature. Whatever you type here appears at the bottom of *all your posts* You can use any HTML codes here.

Basics:

If you want to type your name, then want the next thing on a new line, type a <br> after your name. The <br> tells the computer to start on the next line down.

You can include images or links also.

Image Link:

<img src="Your URL here">

 Links:

<a href="your link here>Name of link</a>

You can also include java applets etc, but those increase loading times and you'll probably end up getting flamed for it =p

3) How do I bold, underline, italicize, include links or pictures in my posts?

On some boards, you can just plug in the HTML for that and have it work. Most boards use the EZcodes.

To Bold:

[b]Bolded Message Here[/b]

 Italics:

[i]Italicized Message Here[/i]

 Underline

[u]Underlined Message Here[/u]

Pictures:

[image]Picture Link Here[/image]
