Ro Vambraces

Marianna says 'Rodcet Nife welcomes you into our noble camp. I am the keeper of the [Vambraces of Ro]. Marianna says 'The mold will be offered to you when you have performed a task for the Temple of Life. The oceans near our home are host to a plague.. the plague sharks!! They have been infected with a deadly malady which has been turning up in the Qeynos Hills. Kill the sharks and bring me two of their rotten shark portions as proof.

Detailed Quest Information

(Highlight under this to read)

In Erudins Crossing (The zone the boat takes you to between Qeynos and Erudin), there is a plague shark. The best way to kill it is to wait for the Seaking boat to enter the zone from Qeynos, and wait by Tolo on the isle. The plague shark will follow seaking, and you will be able to kill it. You must have a friend to do it, as the plague shark is very strong. You must also be able to kill it within the time limits of the boats docking at the isle, or when the boat leaves...so will the shark.
