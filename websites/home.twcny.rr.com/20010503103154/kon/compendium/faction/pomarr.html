Priests of Marr

The Priests of Marr is the main faction for Paladins and Clerics of Erollisi Marr.

Faction Raising Quests

The best way to raise faction with the Priests of Marr is to give milk to Mojak Hikspin, who fishes by the lake in West Commonlands. Giving Mojax milk will spawn a npc named Duggin Scumper, a thief. Kill Duggin and return the note to Eeyana Naestra in the Hall of Truth in North Freeport. 

If you dont want to run back and forth from WC to NFP, you can just keep giving milk to Mojax. You can also do just about any Knights of Truth quest to raise Priests of Marr faction, and vice versa. 

Also, you can go FP Militia Guard hunting to raise Priests of Marr faction.
