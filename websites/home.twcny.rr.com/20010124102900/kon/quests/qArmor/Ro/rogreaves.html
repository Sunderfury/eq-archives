Ro Greaves

Elisabeth says 'Very good to meet you, Interslayer . I am the keeper of the [Greaves of Ro]. Please feel free to rest and recuperate here. We shall see that you are safe from the evils of the Rathe Mountains. Elisabeth says 'Go to the countryside of Freeport. There, you shall seek out any nightfall giants . They have terrorized the countryside for too long. They have protection from common weapons. Rely on magic. I failed in tracking them down. You shall succeed and when return any single head to me, you shall be awarded the mold for the greaves.

Detailed Quest Information

(Highlight under this to read)

The nightfall giants spawn between the druid ring and bandit camp in West Commonlands. Kill the giant and return 1 head to Elisabeth for the mold. The giants often spawn at night.
