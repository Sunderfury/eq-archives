1) take him to the most remote part of the zone i can find along coast .. and stand in betweeen him and the camp in case he loses aggro ...( remove weapons and hit duck to avoid riposte for us paladins)
2).. once it turns about 5pmish to 6 game time . i return the the camp with the PH .. 
3)root and either have a cleric/enc/whatever mezz or i camp out can come back to clear aggro.. 

i have not failed using this method 
i have actually been afk for about 5 of the respawns for the entire time .. using a stopwatch/timer

hope this helps alot of you to avoid aviaks/bone collectors/whatever ... good luck and god speed ...
