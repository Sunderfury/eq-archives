Quellious

Quellious is the god of tranquil, the peacekeeper. They strive for enlightenment, and wish to share it with others. By sharing this knowledge with others, they believe universal peace can be obtained. 

Quellious is one of the smallest religions of Norrath, and their followers are accepted among any goodly nations or religions. They have no abnormal enemies or conflicts, other than the usual factions that hate all paladins.
