Remember to hit clear form when you chose a new armor slot.

Interpreting your score...

Scoring Goals: (This is due to change because of expansions)

Level*100
 (For example, at 45 the goal is 4500)

From 1-45, you're more or less "golden" if you reach this number.

From 50 and beyond this number should be your minimum.

Scoring Explanation:

A survey was taken, and stats were ranked by how many points they should be worth. With 15 points per AC as a base. Points are generalized and do not take into account race, like how High Elves need str, or weight penalties.

1 AC= 5 Agility
Wisdom 8 Pts AC 15  Pts
Strength 7 Pts Magic 7    Pts
Stamina 6 Pts Fire/Cold 5    Pts
Dexterity 4 Pts Poison 4 Pts
Agility 3 Pts Mana 1.5 Pts
Intelligence 1 Pts Disease 1 Pt
Charisma 1 Pts HP 1 Pt
