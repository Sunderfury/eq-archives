The Ultimate Damage Prediction Guide

By: Beldukil

Maximum = ( WeaponSkill + Strength ) / 100 * WeaponDamage + LevelBonus

Where
LevelBonus=(Level - 25)/3 for 1H weapons or (Level - 25)/1.80 for 2H weapons.


This gives us formulas for calculating the important numbers for both the primary hand and the offhand:

MinimumMainhand/2h = 1 + LevelBonus
MagicNumberMainhand = 2*WeaponDamage + LevelBonus
MaximumMainhand = ((Strength + WeaponSkill)/100)*WeaponDamage + LevelBonus

AverageDamageMain/2h = 0.4*Minimum + 0.4*MagicNumber + 0.2*Maximum

*There is a debate whether this should be weighted .45+.45+.1*

DPS Mainhand = Accuracy%*(AverageDamage/(Delay/10)) *(1+DoubleAttack%)

And for the offhand, we have:

MinimumOffhand = 1
MagicNumberOffhand = 2*WeaponDamage
MaximumOffhand = ((Strength + WeaponSkill)/100)*WeaponDamage
Average Damage = 0.4*Minimum + 0.4*MagicNumber + 0.2*Maximum
Damage Per Second Offhand
= Accuracy%*(AverageDamage/(Delay/10)) *(1+DoubleAttack%) * Dualwield%

For more details on this and the data used to derive them, see boards.stratics.com/cgi-b...apsed&sb=5 

Also, while the Double Attack formula commonly referred to on the Eqlizer page appears right, the Dual Wield formula is wrong, at least for warriors. The correct formula for Double Attack and Dual Wield for warriors are:

Chance to Double Attack = (Skill + Level)/500
Chance to Dual Wield = (Skill + Level)/400

This is correct for a warrior, but Rangers may use a different constant for Dual Wield instead of 400. The Ranger who posted on Eqlizer said that this number was actually 600, meaning warriors dual wield 50% more often than Rangers. Anyone have a log they can parse to back up or correct this?
