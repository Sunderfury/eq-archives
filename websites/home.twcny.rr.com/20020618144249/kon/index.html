Knights of Norrath
 
Welcome to Knights of Norrath! In this site, you can find information about paladin spells, skills, disciplines, and alternate advancement info. You can also find information about the Paladin epic quest for the Fiery Defender.

There's also an Armor Rating calculator that rates the equipment you have on, and a weapon damage calculator. Check 'em out!

Also be sure to visit the boards. Paladins of Norrath is always full of activity, from equipment discussions to newly discovered quests.
