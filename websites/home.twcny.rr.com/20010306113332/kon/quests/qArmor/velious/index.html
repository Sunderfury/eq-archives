Paladin Velious Quest Armor

First off, you need to have at least indifferent faction to have the quest NPC talk to you. Battlepriest Daragor gives you the quest, and is located in the right tower of the Battlepriest building in Thurgadin. The battlepriest building is the building with all the dwarfs training for battle, not the monastery with the praying dorfs.
