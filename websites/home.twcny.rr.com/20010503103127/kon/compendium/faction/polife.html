Priests of Life/Knights of Thunder

This is the main faction for Paladins and Clerics of Rodcet Nife/Karana. 

Faction Raising Quests

The best way to raise faction is to get a friend to make a level 1 char, and have them kill Koalindi fish in the pond beneath the Rodcet Nife Temple. Loot the fish, and return the scale to Enic Ruklin. 

You can also give 2 gold to Lashun Novashine and/or 2 bone chips to raise Priests of Life faction. 

Doing quests for either of the temples will raise your faction for both.
