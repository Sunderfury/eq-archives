Ro Breastplate

Abigail says 'Greetings! I am the holder of the secret of the [Breastplate of Ro]. You may rest here. You are quite safe within this camp of paladins. Abigail says 'The Breastplate of Ro mold will be awarded to you. First, you will perform a test of strength. Go to Faydwer. There you shall seek out and destroy the Teir'Dal who carry the dark cauldrons!! They have been venturing into our lands and capturing many Koada'Dal and Fier'Dal for cooking purposes! Bring to me two of their dark cauldrons.

Detailed Quest Information

(Highlight under this to read)

Abigail is talking about the Mistmoore Cooks that spawn in the kitchen of the castle in the Mistmoore zone. Kill them and return to cauldrons for the breastplate mold.
