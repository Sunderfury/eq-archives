Freeport Militia

Corrupt Qeynos Guards

These factions are the factions for the evil guards of Qeynos, and the Freeport guards. 

Faction Raising Quests

The best way to raise faction with the militia is to go to the erudin castle and do the Inert potion quest. 

Look for Toresian Fhabel on the third floor. He's waiting for someone to take an Inert potion to the Vials of Vitality in the first floor of the Erudin Castle. 

Take the potion, and give the potion to a npc named Slansin. He can be found in the basement in the Jewlers shop. 

The fastest way to do this is to have someone attack slansin and drag him upstairs to Toresian, root him, then camp out. That way you can turn in the items very quickly and maybe not get carpal tunnel syndrome.

Note: To do the inert potion quest, you need faction with Toresian, which can be obtained by killing Elial Brooks in Tox forest. This is also the only way to raise DW knights faction.

If your faction maxes out and you want to raise it more, you must go to Misty Thicket, and give bandages to Honeybugger to lower faction with CoalitionTradeFolkIll. Once your faction is lowered sufficiently with these guys, return to erudin and redo the inert potion quest. 

You can also raise FP militia faction by turning in monster parts in High Hold Pass or HH Keep.

In the High Hold Keep you can turn in left goblin ears to Captain Linarus. 

In High Hold pass, there are captains coresponding to either side of the zone that you can turn in orc scalps or goblin parts that come from the monsters nearby.
