Spell Tips

  • Invigor - This is a nice spell to have up when you get tired from a long fight, or are using a pretty heavy weapon. Others in your group would probably need you to cast this on them as well, freeing up a spell slot for a caster.

  • Root - This is a paladins best friend; one of the most useful spells you will ever get. Root can be used in many situations, like when you need to get away from a mob, or need to do crowd control.

    • Crowd Control with Root:

      • When pulling, if you get more than 1 mob, root the mob along the way before you hit your camp spot. This buy's time for the group enchanter to mez. Either way it spaces out incoming mobs for easier handling.

        This technique is especially useful as you get higher up in levels and are able to take more damage from monsters, allowing you to literally root small trains into one spot.
