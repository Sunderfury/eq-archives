Calculator and Scoring Explanation

Scores are based on what people posted according to their level. I excluded people were obvoiusly twinked.

The max score you could probably get with the best items in the game is around Level*100, give or take a few hundred points.

When interpreting your scores, think BIG. When your score is like in the 3000's or whatever, having a 20 or 30 point difference is like...next to nothing ;) If you had 3500 dollars and someone else had 3550, would the next guy be that much richer?

So basically, you're OK armor wise if you hit the average score listed.

Disclaimer:

This calculator is a rather lose ranking system. Its not a definitive way to rate armor because there are just too many variables such as race, personal preference, the situation, etc. So don't throw a fit if you don't score as high as you should/want ;) Also, this does not take into account weapon delay ratios. Its purely a armor/stat rating system.

Future Additions:

An option to have the calculator give a 'weighted' total for races that are strength-challenged will be added.

Calculator Info:

A survey was taken by 70 paladins. They ranked how many points stats and stuff were worth, with 15 points per AC as a base. Points are standardized for general cases, and do not reflect special cases such as Erudites or High Elves who require extra Str, or Dwarves who value extra agility.
