Deepwater Knights

The Deepwater Knights are the Erudin Paladin and Cleric faction for followers of Prexus.

Faction Raising Quests

The only way to raise faction with the DW knights is to kill Elial Brook at the docks in Toxxulia Forest. He spawns every 10 minutes and is about level 7. He is a Necro and is often with pet.

Once your faction is at indifferent or amiable, you can probably start doing quests directly from the Prexus temple. 

Killing Elial raises faction with:

Deepwater Knights
The Craft Keepers
Crimson Hands
Gate Callers.
