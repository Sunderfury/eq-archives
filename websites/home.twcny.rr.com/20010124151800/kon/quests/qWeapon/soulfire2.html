Soulfire

Detailed Quest Info

It is very easy to skip or mix up steps in this quest. I suggest you do it from start to finish and not skip any NPC's, Items, subquests, or instructions mentioned.

Kalatrina Plossen asks you to take a note to a spy in the Militia Hall in WFP. Take the note to Guard Ayle in the back of the Militia Hall. He's on the first floor, out in the back in an outside foyer area.

When you give the note to him, he asks you to give another note to his father. The text is a little misleading, but in the end, you are suppose to give the note to Groflah Steadirt. Groflah is on the top floor of the weapons shop in EFP.

After giving the note to Groflah, he tells you to meet him in the Seafear's Roost tavern in EFP. Give the note to him there, and he will tell you the story of Zimel's blade. Apparently, Zimel is now a prisoner of the Militia under the arena in WFP. When you ask the prisoner about Zimel, he wants "Bog 'n Goo, and Blanket too!" What he means is he wants some Edible Goo, Bog juice, and his blanket. Edible Goo and Bog Juice are made from Baking.

In the process of this, hopefully a begger named Tykar Renlin will spawn at the bar. Give him 4 droms champagne to recieve Zimel's blanket. While you are meeting with Groflah in EFP, wait for Tykar to spawn too. Get the blanket, and return to Zimel with hi Bog, Goo, and Blanket.

Zimel now give you a key to a strongbox in High Hold Keep. Take the key to the banker, and give it to her. She gives you a note with instructions that it is to be delivered to Brother Hayle in the dungeon of Paw.

Brother Hayle is located in the jails of paw. When you give the note to him, he says you must return to him:

This note (The one he hands back to you after turning in the note from the bank)
The hilt of Soulfire
Testimony of Truth
Brilliant Sword of Faith. 

The hilt of Soulfire is located in Mistmoore off the Shadow Knight troll Xicotl.

The Testimony of Truth is obtained through this quest.

Click here for information on how to get the Brilliant Sword of Faith.

When you hand in the last set of items, MAKE SURE you turn in the note Hayle returned to you after giving him the note you got from the banker.
