The Peacekeepers

*Not Confirmed*

The Peacekeeper faction is the main faction for Clerics and Paladins of Quellious. Faction Raising Quests: This is one of the hardest factions to raise. If you are not an Erudite who follows Prexus, you need to raise factoin with the DW Knights to do  Prexus Temple quests to raise faction with the Peacekeepers. After you do that, you can raise peacekeeper quests by doing a bone chip quest with Lumi Stergon. He will give you a box to combine bone chips and return that to him.
