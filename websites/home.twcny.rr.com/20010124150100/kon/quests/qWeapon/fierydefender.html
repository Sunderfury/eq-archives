Epic Quest

Fiery Defender Pictures and Stats

The quest starts in the Plane of Fear. A npc named Irak Altil is a wandering indifferent, undead zombie.

Irak Altil Log

You say, 'what are you mumbling about?'

Irak Altil turns its rotted eyesockets towards you. 'Fallen light, upon this ground I fell. Left to rot, cast out. My holy arms stripped and my soul cast out of the halls. Twisted, failed, disgraced.'

Zivilyn says, 'are you disgraced' 

Irak Altil says 'Disgrace upon my soul for failing in my duties. Disgrace of my brethren. 

Zivilyn says, 'what are your duties' 

Irak Altil breathes his words from his skeletal mouth as if wind through a valley. 'Sword, shield, breastplate. Twisted to Innoruuks foul will. Must be retrieved and cleansed else my soul is to wander for all time.' 

You say, 'what sword?' 

Irak Altil says 'Hands of a twisted brother no longer living. 

Zivilyn says, 'what shield' Irak Altil says 'Earthly children of the god of hate. Keeping stand over his dark brothers and sisters. 

Zivilyn says, 'what breastplate' [ed. note: actually the keywords is "breast"] 

Irak Altil lets loose an unearthly howl as it gleams with sickly light. 'Upon she who lured me from my post. In that place where hatred festers. Painful corruption.'

Reklon Gnallen

Now, the next npc is Reklon Gnallen, who is in the Temple of Quellious in Erudin.

Reklon Gnallen says 'Hail to you friend. Seek you knowledge or atonement in this temple of peace? 

You say, 'What temple of peace' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'Dedicated to the god of peace, Quellious, is this temple. It is here that her clerics and paladins pray and receive inspiration. 

You say, 'What paladins' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'As the clerics are the warm hand of healing, the paladins are the stalwart shield of protection, and in times of grave need the sharp sword of justice. To our post must we always remain vigilant else dishonor might befall us. 

You say, 'I wish to remain vigilant' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'To a paladin the upholding of his duty, or honor to his temple is foremost. To fail in his duty to stand at his assigned post in time of need is a great disgrace. Few are the paladins who have done so, and their spirits have been outcast from the god of peace.'

You say, 'What outcast' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'Harsh though it seems, a true paladin must always remain vigilant and dedicated to his cause. If he does not then he might find himself fallen from grace.'

You say, 'Who has fallen from grace?'

Reklon Gnallen says 'There are a few who have done so. Only a few that come to mind. Such stories are useful to remember as reminders of our beliefs. 

You say, 'What stories' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'One is the story of the paladin who fell due to his weakness in the duty of his guard post. 

You say, 'what peasent woman' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'Many hundreds of years ago did this story begin. There was a paladin, whose name is lost to all but our oldest leaders, who was among the greatest examples of honor. It was his post to guard the chambers to our most important halls. His fall was a creature in the guise of a poor peasant woman 

You say, 'What sacrifice' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'Only this crest and the sacrifice of something that symbolizes your strength and purity will release his spirit into the cleansing flames of our lady Quellious. Go now. Thank you.

The Tainted Items

Irak Altil failed his duty as a guard for the Paladins of Quellious and was cast out from his Temple. To release him from exile, you need to purify and return a tainted sword, shield and breastplate. 

The Tainted Shield

Irak Altil says 'Earthly children of the god of hate. Keeping stand over his dark brothers and sisters. Light of clean spirit to cleanse the shield. All must be cleansed and returned to the temple for my soul to be freed. 

The tainted shield drops off Kirak Vil in Nektulous forest. He wanders the zone and is about level 56. Very tough, high regen rate. You need at least 2 groups of level 55+ to take him down. 

The Light of Clean Spirit is not a physical component you turn in for the shields purification. Just give the tainted shield to Elia the Pure in felwithe. She is by the water just before you enter the inner city zone. When you hail her, she says that she is standing in "Pure light" so since she technically has it already, you dont have to give it to her with the shield.

The Tainted Sword

Irak Altil says 'Hands of a twisted brother no longer living. The sword must be dipped in water pure. The tainted sword drops off the 'Keeper of Tombs" in The Hole. 

The Pure Water comes from doing a simple sub-quest of helping a peasant woman give her sick brother some water. The peasant woman is located in the small pond by the Monk guild in West Freeport. Ask her "What water?" Then "I will take it to him." Her brother is in the bakery nearby, named Joshua.

The Tainted Breastplate

Irak Altil lets loose an unearthly howl as it gleams with sickly light. 'Upon she who lured me from my post. In that place where hatred festers. Painful corruption.' A gem of clarity unlike any other for the breastplate. The tainted breastplate drops off the thought corruptor in the plane of hate. Supposedly they spawn by killing Scorn Banshees. Supposedly, the best chance of getting a thought corruptor to spawn is within the first few hours of hate when the first scorn banshees are pulled. If we all do hate the same way on the servers, then there's probably one or two scorn banshees that are PH's for thought corruptors. The Pure gem is done by doing a subquest in Kaladim. In the mines (take a right as you go in, across the bridge), find a miner named Jark.

The Purification

Irak Altil says, 'Only the temple can cleanse, only they can touch the artifacts. Take the sets of two to the temple. When they are all three cleansed seperately take them to he who will grant me atonement.' 

What Irak means is only the temple (Quellious temple, which he worshiped) can cleanse the artifacts. The sets of 2 that he speaks of is the set of the sword and pure water, and the set of the breastplate and pure gem. 

Give the sets seperately to Reklon in Erudin and he will purify them.

To purify the Tainted Shield, just give it to Elia the Pure in Felwithe.

The Crest

Next, turn in all three purified items...the sword, shield, and breastplate to Reklon.

Reklon Gnallen says 'I never thought our order would see these artifacts again. With the return of these relics we can now put at ease a scar upon the history of our order. This cleansing will atone for his failure. Your soul must be pure to have given so freely of yourself. Go now, take this crested token of our order. If you wish to free his soul you must undertake another sacrifice.

You say, 'What sacrifice' 

Reklon Gnallen says 'Only this crest and the sacrifice of something that symbolizes your strength and purity will release his spirit into the cleansing flames of our lady Quellious. Go now.

 

Turning in the crest and a Fiery Avenger to Reklon does NOT finish the quest.

The End

The last step is to turn in the Mark of Atonement crest and your symbol of Strength and Purity to Irak Altil.

Supposedly, Irak Altil is a triggered spawn, but that is yet to be confirmed. Either way, he's up there in fear somewhere ; ) If you are lucky, you'll see him.

When you see Irak, he should be an indifferent conning undead skeleton. Turn in your crest first, then your symbol of strength and purity...your Fiery Avenger.

Irak will then give you your new Fiery Defender, and run off to sacrifice him upon the altar of Cazic Thule.
