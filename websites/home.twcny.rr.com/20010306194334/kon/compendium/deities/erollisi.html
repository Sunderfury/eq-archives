Erollisi Marr

Erollisi Marr is sister to Mithaniel Marr. They both are credited for bringing to Norrath the Barbarian race of Norrath, which led to the emergence of the human and erudite race. "The dream of every follower of Erollisi is to die in selfless defense of someone or something they love. Many Paladins hear the true calling of their hearts and follow Erollisi Marr." Followers of Erollisi are accepted among all the goodly races and religion of Norrath. Devoted followers Erollisi Marr followers may have trouble with the Freeport Militia as they gain experience and faction, and take sides in the struggle for power that is going on in Freeport.
