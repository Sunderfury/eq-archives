Quests - Fiery Avenger

Soulfire Fiery Avenger Fiery Defender

The Fiery Avenger is the second step towards the Paladin Epic Weapon, the Fiery Defender.

Prior to doing this, you must have obtained your Soulfire.

Step 1: Obtain the Burnt and Frost Books from Dragons around Norrath.

There is an NPC (Either in the Rathe Mts or in Qeynos) that will combine these books for you for 1000pp. The books will combine to make the Book of Scale.

Next, go to the Ocean of Tears, and give the Oracle your Book of Scale. He will give you Miragul's Phylactery.

Step 2: Go to the underwater caves of Everfrost, and find the lich of miragul. Have him charmed, and give him the phylactery. (Your faction MUST be indifferent or higher.) This will despawn the lich, and the live one will pop out.

Kill him and get the Head of Miragul and Miragul's Robe.

Step 3: Aquire a Ghoulbane and Soulfire.

Go to the Plane of Air, look for Dirkog Steelhand. He can make Inte Akera spawn on the 4th island in PoA. These guys aren't on the same island, so you have to leave someone behind with Dirkog to spawn Inte when you hit isle 4.

To get Dirkog to spawn Inte, pay him 500pp.

Step 5: Give Inte Akera these items in this order: (1 at a time)

  • Soulfire, and get a Blessing of Nobility.
  • Ghoulbane, and get a Blessing of Sacrifice.

 After getting your two blessing, give him (All at once in the 4 trade boxes)

  • Blessing of Sacrifice
  • Blessing of Nobility
  • The head of Miragul
  • The robe of Miragul

Congratulations! You now have your Fiery Avenger!
