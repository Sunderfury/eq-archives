Temple of Solusek Ro

This faction is required for quests given in the Temple of Solusek Ro, most notably the paladin Armor of Ro quest component: Ronium. 

Faction Raising Quests

The best way to raise Temple of Sol Ro faction is to seek out Rathmana in South Ro (NW corner of zone), and give him 30 gold. 

Alternatively, if you're a dervish hunter, you can turn in 4 dervish rings to Ortallius, the orc who is next to Rathmana. 

Also, if you hang around Mistmoore a lot, you can raise faction there if you camp in the graveyard. There is a flower that spawns in the crypt, which you can give to an outcast Shadowman named Synthii,  who sits behind the crypt building. Be careful of the invisible pit traps inside the crypt! 

The flower spawns every 10 minutes or so.
