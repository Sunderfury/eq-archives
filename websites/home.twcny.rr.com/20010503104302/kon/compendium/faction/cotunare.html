Clerics of Tunare

The Clerics of Tunare is the paladin and cleric faction in Felwithe. 

This is the main faction for High Elven paladins. 

Faction Raising Quests

The best way to raise faction is to give muffins to Pandos, a dwarf in West Freeport. You can find Pandos on the 2nd floor of the West Freeport Inn. 

Give him muffins (1 per trade slot) and you will raise faction with:

Clerics of Tunare
Faydarks Champions
King Tearis Thex
The Soldiers of Tunare. 

 

Alternatively, you can give bone chips to Yeolan Bronzeleaf in the cleric hall of Felwithe.
