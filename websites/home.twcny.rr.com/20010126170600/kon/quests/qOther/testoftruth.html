Test of Truth

The test of truth is given out to paladins of level 9 and under. If you are above this level, just follow the steps anyway and the quest will still work.

Merko asks you to give a note to Altunic Jartin in East Commonlands. Altunic can be found in the last house to the left on the road to W. Commons just before the zone point. He sells various shields, and often wanders the East Commons zone.

Give him the note, and a npc named Squire Narl will spawn. He is a tough mob, and if you are level 9 or below you will probably need help.

After Squire Narl is dealth with, give Altunic the head. Altunic will now ask that you give him a medium cloth shirt. No other shirt will do. After you give him this shirt, he will give you the Token of Generosity. DO not turn this in to Merko yet.

After you have finished this first leg of the quest, return to Merko. Tell him "I have received the token of generosity."

Merko will commend your actions, and ask that you turn into him a Spider Venom Sac. These can be bought off vendors, and are drops off Giant Spiders nearby Freeport. Turn the sac in to Merko to receive the Token of Bravery.

Once you have both tokens, turn them both in at the same time to Merko. Once you do this, run to the sewers (Turn around backwards and face the river, then follow it left) and look for Guard Wella. Kill her, and loot the item and turn that into Merko.

Merko will reward you with your Testimony of Truth.

Notes:

Some paladins who are heavy on RP might think this is an innocent slaughter of a poor guard. The guard does say if you let her go, she will change her ways. It turns out that Sir Lucan D'lere ordered her to murder Merko's wife, and her death is simply swift justice for her evil deeds.
