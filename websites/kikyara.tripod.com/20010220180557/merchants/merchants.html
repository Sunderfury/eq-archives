Toxdil
Position: West Freeport Tunnels (620,200)
Book: A old and delivered Book
Wares: Mortar&Pestle, Poison Vial, Lined Poison Vial, Sealed Vial, Suspension, Constrict Suspension, Larent Suspension

You can find Toxidil easily when you stand infront of the West gate of Freeport, hug the left outside wall. At one of the corners ahead is the entrance.

Toxdil will make poison for you. Give him 2 Snake Poison Sacks (unstacked) and 20 gp (NOT 2pp!). You will get some experience and a flask of snake poison in return. The poison is not very strong but one of the easier ways for rogues at level 18 to get their hands on a real poison flask.

Toxdil has also a very important role in the Burning Rapier quest.
