Nyheter från Verant
Maax 14:30

Detta mail har sänts ut till de större EQ-sidorna. I brevet beskrivs lite om vad som hänt de senaste dagarna och vad som komma skall:

Hello All,

Well... we're quickly discovering that Murphy was an optimist.

We had some problems with the hardware on the primary login server. To answer the parade of questions: Yes, we do have redundant hardware/mirroring. This problem occured and we had to use our backup system, but of course nothing is ever easy and bringing the other system up took quite a bit longer than it should have. Now, I think if we had that problem again we could do it in about 5-10 minutes.

Here's a list of some of the things we have in place to insure our future downtimes are minimal:

1) We're going to more evenly distribute servers with our 2nd ISP (Cerfnet)

2) We have another pipe coming in from another major ISP it will be here soon. That will make our 3rd ISP.

3) Our downtime schedule will be strictly adhered to. This will be posted to our website. We will also be doing a better job of communicating during the downtimes. Frankly a lot of us end up being involved when there is a problem, and it always seems like we've just about got it up. We'll do better.

Tomorrow we should be bringing up the first of a about 3 new servers. It should't require any downtime.
We do apologize for the inconvienence we have caused you all,

John Smedley
President and CEO

Verant Interactive, Inc.

EQ Extreme och EverHack
Maax 14:20

2 st s k Exploit-program har dykt upp till EverQuest. Det första dök upp igår med att någon postade en länk till var man kunde hämta hem EQ Extreme. Det blev ett stort ståhej runt detta och John Smedley meddelade Verants attityd mot dessa program:

Use it and you will be banned. No exceptions, no 2nd chances. It's quite simple for us to log the use of this program and we have begun doing so.

Senare kom det dock fram att detta program endast bestod av några ofarliga Visual Basic rader och det enda som gjordes var att starta EQ på din dator. Då kom Brad M med detta:

Apparently the program was a hoax. We took the program seriously, and due to the huge public response, John posted our policy on the matter (which is also in the terms of service information one agrees to when launching EverQuest and something I've been saying for over a year).

Then because we have to take all such programs seriously (the same way we take reports of exploits and duping seriously, even though most are not valid), we investigated it.

Senare på kvällen kom det rapporter om ytterligare ett hack-program, EverHack. Detta program ska göra det som EQ Extrem påstods göra och lite till. Troligen är detta också falskt. Ingen ytterligare info.

Det senaste från John S
Maax 09:30

Detta postades på EQVault för några timmar sedan. Skriver lite om nya servrar(!) och annat gott:

Hello All,

Well.. it's been a much better day ;)

We had around 15,100 on at our peak this evening around 7:45pm PST. During that time we were checking both the UUnet AND Cerfnet pipes all the time and they came up clean.

Does that mean that YOUR individual packet loss will be good? for 95% of the people the answer is yes.. for some of you there will be problems, but at that point it's most likely either your ISP or something else on the net. We had some reports in select areas of problems (some @home users and a few others). These seem to be limited to particular places or ISP's... that's what I call normal connectivity. Remember, we put that packet loss meter in the game for the specific reason of helping diagnose people's problems. One other
comment I wanted to make is that when I've gone into the servers and broadcast asking how people's packet loss. Over 90% said it was between 0% and 5%.

Some people have asked whether or not the servers are causing packet loss. The answer to that is no.

Some additional items of note:

1) Tomorrow morning at 7am PST we will be doing routine maintainence (from now on things will be scheduled and put up on the downtime section of our website at www.everquest.com

2) Occasionally when you log in you see the message "Login database temporarily unavailable". This is a known bug and we are working on correcting it. Give it a minute to recover. We expect to have this bug nailed tomorrow and will keep you informed.

3) Additional Servers - Yes, we ARE adding other servers. They are going up tomorrow. One thing we want to encourage is people on some of the busier servers to start up characters on these new servers if they have low enough level characters on the old ones and don't mind. If you want to stay where you are, that's fine too. - We will be adding 2 additional Servers tomorrow. We plan on adding more in the near future as well.

4)Patch - We've been so frantically working on the UUnet problems that we had decided not to patch until they were solved. If we can ask for your help in playing on our Test Server we really want to make sure the new Music stuff for the other cards is working ok. It has tested out well internally, and it's up there now for you to play with.

5) Bugs, Bugs, Bugs - We are smashing a lot of bugs, and the new patch will have a lot of bug fixes. We are particularly focusing on crashes when Zoning and logging into the game.

That's all for now.

John Smedley
President and CEO
Verant Interactive, Inc.
