Cookies, plain and shaped, are trivial at 102. Those are the ones made with a Clump of Dough, Frosting and Spices (shaped ones include a cookie cutter of some variety). Chocolate Cookies are not trivial 'til sometime after 142, as are all the recipies involving chocolate. For those you add in Winter Chocolate...no reports on shaped chocolate cookies, mostly because the brownie parts to make Winter Chocolate are so hard to come by.

Can anyone say what the actual highest level is in Baking, yet? Anyone hit trivial making stuff with Winter Chocolate?
