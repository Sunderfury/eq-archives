I really hope Verant doesn't release an expansion anytime in the near future. There is just no way the servers could handle it. I mean look at it now, a good majority of the time it is unplayable on the weekends. Imagine when the server volume increases by 25% all the time when an expansion is released. That may be a conservative estimate. The numbers jump 25% when there is just a GM event scheduled. I guess the performance isn't that critical as long as we continue to renew our subscriptions. If I were running the EQ servers and I made just as much money spending $150K a month as I made spending $210K (I have no idea of the real figures. I am just giving an example.) I would continue spending as little as I could to maximize profit. Don't get me wrong EQ is a fantastic game when it works. It just seems to me that it is working less and less every week. I remember when I was thinking, "Geez, 7% lag. This is ridiculous!!". Now I am happy if we can stay around 25% lag. I have run the same setup since beginning EQ so I don’t believe the lag is related to my hardware (PII 350, 96MB RAM, cable modem). I have tried playing on several different servers, but the difference in performance is marginal. I guess to sum it up, I wish we could focus on the problem at hand, instead of focussing on game mechanics and expansions. I would prefer to play a character that has a few buggy spells, rather than not being able to play at all.
