I am reasonably sure, if you are VERY patient, that you can make a large profit baking in Neriak (for evil races). You can buy fish off the guy right by the fountain for around 2-3 sp. It just takes a long time. Probably easier to kill stuff.

Well, baking can be fun AND profitable.. not to mention a nice break from the hack n slash and a bit safer than making cash while getting your butt kicked. HOW MUCH profit you ask? Well, given the right stats, and the right skills... you can make QUITE the decent profit.. but it takes time and effort and patience. I know a magician (good friend of mine) who after donning charisma items, getting his fishing skill and baking skill over 100 has managed to turn a profit of about 20pp in less than half an hour. I have started down that path and can see where he is able to do that... how? easy. Fish (fish are free), Kill bats (free bat wings) make fish rolls.. 100% profit. As for the others.. well.. with high skill and good charisma.. Fish(fish are free) and buy sauce.. good cha can lower your cost... sell filets.. high cha can increase your return. Also remember that spices are cheap, milk and flour are cheap, snake eggs are free and cookie (unshaped) sell for almost 6sp a pop. So thre is another option. Have fun, take care and may Tunare guide your travels.
