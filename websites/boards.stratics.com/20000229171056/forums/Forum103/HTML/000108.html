The trick for a bard/mage is to build your bard skills first,lock them, then work on strength. Your Strength building skill will be Wrestling with no tactics. Tactics is pointless if you only have the Wrestling melee skill. Your mainly interested in avoiding being disrupted not inflicting damage. My Bard/Mage has stats of str/dex/int = 95/30/100 using this method. He is GM Provoke/music/magery/wrestling...

alvinleong,
Ive found that Int is really tough to build but as long as you use only magery skills (magery, ei,med) and strength skills your str and int will go up and your dex will go down. Its just slow. If you have some points to spare you could either try Spirit Speak to get your Int back up then mark it Down when you are finished.
When I finished Gm'ing in Music, Provoke and Magery my stats were a pitiful str/dex/int = 49/76/100. Luckily for me the Skill Lock patch just went into production so I Locked Barding skills , Wrestling up, Tactics Down and proceded to GM in Wrestling on my Polar bears. The bad part about stat gain at the Stat Cap is that gains are easy to control buy what skills you use but loses (due to gains) are uncontrollable. In fact they appear to be random. Anyways, when I Gm'd in Wrestling my stats were something like 95/53/77. As you can see I lost an equal amount of int as i did dex. It took lots more wrestling and Magery use after this for my skills to finally settle down at 95/30/100 and I'm still trying to get my dex down to 25.
