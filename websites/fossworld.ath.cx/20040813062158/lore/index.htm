ROZTOV'S LORE LIBRARY - ONLINE VERSION

Hail Adventurer!

So you look tired, sit down and I will get you something hot to drink. Put your feet up and choose a book to read. I have organised my library into sections as you can see. The first section is historical, the second section is in-game lore and the third section is for in-game stories and tales. Take your time, the world can wait, for a while at least!

Using the magic available in this library I have highlighted key words in all the books here that will lead to other references if you would like to read them.

If I ever figure out how to add the Google search feature I will put it here too :)
