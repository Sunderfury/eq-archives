Strength - Your Strength will not affect you at all at low levels. Since you wont be doing more damage and you wont be able to carry more then 14 pounds without taking penalties. However starting level 20 or so you will start doing a bit more damage then Monks with less Strength.

Stamina - Will increase your max hp depending on your level and also let you jump up and down more before getting tired.

Agility - Your Agility affects your AC, 3 agility = 1 ac. Getting AC items is a much more effective way to get AC than +agility items. For example a serpentine bracer will give you 4 ac while a symbol of loyalty to vox will get you 12 ac. Agility is also supposed to help with dodging.

Dexterity - Suppingly helps with hit % and riposte/parry/duel wield/double attack. Hard to say.
