In the Freeport and Qeynos guilds, you can get quests for Sashes or Headbands.

Both the Sashes and the Headbands give the same bonuses.

White  +1 Stamina +1 Charisma

Yellow +1 Stamina +1 Charisma +1 Agility +1 HP

Orange +2 Stamina +1 Charisma +1 Agility +2 HP

Red  +1 Dexterity +2 Stamina +2 Charisma +2 Agility +2 HP

Purple +1 Dexterity +2 Stamina +2 Charisma +2 Agility +3 HP +1 AC

Blue: +1ac, +1str, +2dex, +3sta, +3cha, +2agi, +3hp (Not in the game yet, you can get it by killing the Monk guild master that is suposed to give out the quest).

Black: +2ac, +2str, +3dex, +4sta, +4cha, +1int, +3agi, +4hp (Not in the game yet, you can get it by killing the Monk guild master that is suposed to give out the quest).


They each Weigh 0.2 pounds.

If your faction is high enough, you can get the sash quests from the Freeport guild and the qeynos headband quests as well. If you go from Freeport to Qeynos for example, you can kill Gnolls for faction. However you can get your faction up instantly, by getting a guild note from another Monk that started in Qeynos (you can just create one, drop in on the ground and pick it up with your Freeport Monk). After you give the note to the Qeynos guildmaster, your faction will go up enough and you could get the Headband quests as well without having to kill hundreds of Gnolls.

Freeport Sash quests

White Sash - You will need to get 1 bone chip, 2 orc scalps (don't stack them) and 1 snake fang. you can find all of these components in the freeport newbie zone near the west gate.

Yellow Sash -Your white Sash, and bring a Giant Snake Rattle (found on Asps and Darkwood snakes), Chitin from a Desert Tarantula in Northern Ro and a Deathfist Slashed Belt which can be found on Orc Centurions in Ecommons.

Orange Sash - Legionnaire Bracer from Orc Legionaries, one Greater Lightstone from a wisp, one Cutthroat Ring from the Cutthroats in Northern Ro and your Yellow Sash.

Red Sash - Marnek's wand (found in lower levels of Befallen), the Sapphire of Souls (in Najena), and the orange sash. 

Purple Sash - ??? E-mail me

 

Qeynos Headband Quests

White - you need four Gnoll Scalps from Gnoll pups.

Yellow - you need three Putrid Rib Bones from from Putrid  Skeletons in the Ruins of Qeynos and the White Headband.

Orange - you need two Gnoll Pelts from Black Burrow, one Gnoll Skin from Gnoll Elites or Gnoll Commanders in Black Burrow, and the Yellow Headband.

Red - you need the Orange Headband, the head of Shen, the skull of Dareb, and the head of Drah, all found in Paw, a zone in the middle of South Karana.

Purple - you need the Red Headband, the Dagger of Marnek from third level Befallen, the skull of Jhen'Tra from the Gnoll Embalmers from Lake Rathe, and Zaharns Coronet from a level 31 High Priest Zaharn in Permafrost.
