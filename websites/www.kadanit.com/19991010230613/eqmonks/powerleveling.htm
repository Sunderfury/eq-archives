This page contains tips, tactics and strategies on how to level as fast as possible. If you have something to contribute, please E-mail me.

 

Up to level 10

When powerleveling, you should wear the best armor available (obviously), without going over 14 pounds. What does that mean? Get yourself some food and water, and a lightsource. And a set of cloth armor. Now replace some of the cloth armor with Leather armor until your weight is 14. Now you're at max efficiency for your level. When hunting don't loot anything that will make you go over 15 pounds. You wont make much money, but you will level up faster. Also, at level 10, it's best to fight mobs that /con blue rather then mobs that /con black/white. The best place to hunt at level 10 is Misty Thicket, you can find information on how to get there in the Popular zones section.

If you see a High level Shaman, Cleric or Druid in your zone (14+), ask him to buff you, most will agree. You will be suprised how much these buffs help.

Levels 13-15

Kill shivered mummies in North Ro, you should group with a Cleric (because those shivered mummies will give you rabies and Clerics kick ass against undead), preferably one that is lower then you in level. Shivered mummies respawn very fast in North Ro, and are pretty easy for their level.

Effective grouping

A Monk's best friend is a Shaman. A level 20 Monk and Shaman can take on mobs that /con yellow. Why is that? well, Shamans have awesome buffs. They can buff your Dexterity, Agility, Strength, Stamina, AC and hps. You will be much more effective this way. You will hit for more, get hit less and miss less often. Also, with the added buffs, you will get hit very little, so even though Warriors have more hps then you do, you will last much longer And the Shaman can heal you while you are fighting.
