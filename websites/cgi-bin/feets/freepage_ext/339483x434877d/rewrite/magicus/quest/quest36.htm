Qeynos

Aenia and Behroe

In North Queynos there's a blue house which holds a daughter and her father. Only thing is they never leave. Why? Well first because the father hasn't gotten his shop up yet and more importantly because his daughter, Aenia, is not allowed to leave the house. This is because he doesn't want her around such rapscallions as those lowly bards, especially one certain one named Behroe Dlexon, who works nights at the docks compiling the watch report. Well it turns out that Aenia and Behroe are in love and if you go see him he tells his tale of woe. He is upset because Aenia's father will kill him if he goes near her. The lovesick fool has written a ballad that he will ask you to sing to her for him. You need to /say to Aenia the entire sonnet. Make sure you don't make a typo or it won't work. Aenia will swoon and gave you a love letter to take back to Behroe (note that Behroe only spawns at night - you might have to start the "watch reports" quest to spawn him). Take the love letter back to Behroe and he gives you a nice low-level item. (Usually a Shaw of the Wind Spirit). For completing the quest you get the following faction: LeagueofAntonicanBards, KnightsofTruth and GuardsofQeynos get better; RingofScale and MayongMistmoore get worse.

The Assassin

This quest is sometimes given to you after you complete the Nitrates quest in Surefall Glades. Return to surefall and give the note to Gerael for status and few coins. Gerael returns the note to you and asks you to give it to Captain Tillim in Qeynos. He asks you to also to warn the target (Gash Flockwaller) that [They are Trying to kill him]. Go to Qeynos and give the note to Captain Tillim. You are rewarded with some faction and told that Gash stays in the Lion's head INN. Going to the Lion's head inn. Gash Flockwaller should be in a back room during the day. Talking to him and repeating the key phrase as [they are trying to kill you] gives you a clue about the unkempt druids - that they carry [black wood chips]. Somewhere in this the Assassin will attack - his name is Raffel Minnmorn. At this point the quest may be broken. One time I beat the assassin with Gash's assistance - as a result the body dissappeared and I couldn't loot it; the second time I got a ranger/druid mob together and we splatted him, but since I hadn't gotten the wood chip comment yet all that I got from him was a rusty dagger. Raffel con's black to a 10th level ranger, but he has a slew of druid spells and shouldn't be solo'd unless you are pretty strong or lucky. So the ending is still unclear.

Bandit Sashes

Chesgard, in the house of thunder, is willing to reward those who help eliminate the bandits in the Karanas. Bring him a bandit sash and he will give you xp, faction, some decent gold and an item (often bronze). You get the following faction changes: Knights of Thunder, Priests of Life and Guards of Qeynos go up and Bloodsabres goes down.

Bandit Spectacles

Gehna Solbenya, in the house of thunder, says she will reward you for the return of the binder spectacles sometimes dropped by bandits in the Karanas. Turn them in for xp, gold, faction and an item (usually a level 9 spell).

Bard Reports

The Bard Guild's needs your help in keeping an eye on the town, if you wish to accept their assignment. Talk to Juseanus and he will give you two scrolls to deliver. Take one to the North gate and another to the docks. You may be given scrolls to return as well. Each delivery is worth a little bit of experience. Also, the following factions will improve: LeagueofAntonicanBards, KnightsofTruth, GuardsofQeynos. RingofScale and MayongMistmoore go down. Because you can do this quickly and get a faction hit for each delivery, this is a good way to improve faction.

Bat Fur

There's a shop called "The Herb Jar." If you sit there for a while Caleah Herblender says to one of her co-workers, "We're out of bat fur again?" Ask her about bat fur. She will then tell you she is a wizard of the circle of three, and that they need components to develop new spells. She asks for your help in obtaining ratwiskers, a beetle eye, and a piece of bat fur so that she can work on a spell she is trying. Giving all 3 items at once gives you the following faction(she attempts to cast a new spell, but fails): +OrderOfThree, -BloodSabers, +GuardsOfQeynos, -OpalDarkBriar. You don't get any other reward other than xp and the satisfaction of helping out the wizards guild.

The Bayle List

This a long and involved quest, and can be started in a number of different places, but revolves around Qeynos, Surefall and Freeport. The details I was given are still unclear in spots, but it ultimately resluts in a high powered shadowknight weapon. It involves getting all three parts of the Bayle List. Each part can also be gotten separately for a different reward, as listed on the separate quests. (1) For Part one of the list, talk to Commander Kane in the Shrine of Bertoxxus in Qeynos Catecombs and he will ask you to deliver blackburrow stout to Barn Bloodstone who is outside the Golden Rooster in Highpass. Make sure your faction withBarn is at least indifferent. To raise, do the Letter to Janam/Letter to Rebby quests in the East FP. If Commander Kane doesn't talk to you at first, just Hail him and say "The disease is spreading." Then he will bemuch more manageable. Doing so results in part one of the list. (2) For part two of the list, you need to kill a bear. As background, you can talk to Runethar Hamest in the Temple of Thunder, who is investigating corruption among the guards of Qeynos. He asks you to go to Surefall Glade, find Frenway Marthank in the Druids' Guildtree, and ask him for "The Bayle List." When you do, Frenway is in a state of depression. His son -- who happened to be carrying the very list you're looking for -- was killed and devoured by the great grizzly bear Mammoth, who lives deep in the caves of Surefall Glade. As a Druid, Frenway is forbidden to harm the bears of the Glade, but he wants vengeance. If you can find and slay Mammoth, you will get a the Bayle list II. Basically, all you have to do is kill the bear (he is about level 20) and skip the rest. (3) For part three, talk to Raltur Caliskon in the Dismal Rage in Freeport and he tells you to assist Sir Lucen Delere in any task he has. But to return any interesting information to him and not Delere. Ralture will then tell you to kill Lady Shea and get the Bayle List. However, Lady Shea will tell you that she had the list stolen from her by Dyllin Starshine, who is bound for Qeynos. He can be found in the smuggler's camp in Highpass. If you have a high faction or use your influence spell on him, he will give you the list. You can also get it by killing him, but you will take a bad faction hit from that that might cause you to not be able to complete the final part of the quest. (4) Take all three parts to Rathmana Allin in South Ro along with 20 gp and he will give you back a translated list. If you give this to Pietro Zarn, the shadowknight guildmaster in the East Freeport Sewers, he will usually reward you with a Rage War Maul (you may need very high faction with him to get it - thus you shouldn't kill Dyllin unless you started extremely high). It is possible that this is used to obtain an even better shadowknight weapon.

Blackburrow Stout Shipment

The Fish's Ale bartender may mention that he needs someone to pick up a shipment for him. If you accept, you are given a note to give to Gnasher Furgutt. Hail Gnasher, give him the note, and he will give you a shipment of blackburrow Stout. Take it back to the barkeep, and you get some experience and a free bottle of Stout. (See Gnasher's head quest for the other side of this)

Blank Scrolls

The Wizard's Guild needs blank scroll sheets. Talk to Kazlo and he will ask you to go to Surefall Glade to see if the druids have any blank scroll sheets. There may be other ways to get them, but Den Magason (wanders around the dock area) will sell you blank scroll sheets for 2 gold a sheet. You will not get your money back when you turn them in, but you will get some xp, faction and some minor coin.

Blessed Oil Quest

This starts in Western Karana by talking to Brother Estle, located on the road just as you enter the plains. Talk to him and he will tell you that he is waiting to get some blessed oil, perhaps to slick down the other monk he is always fighting. Go to the Temple of Life and talk to one of the priests, who will give you the oil to deliver to the good monk. Take it back to him for some money, experience, faction with the Priests of Life and occasionally the Prayer bead quest.

Blurred Map

Tabure Ahendle, by the arena in Qeynos, is concerned about a map her apprentice had on him when he was shipwrecked while crossing over from Erudin. It is possible that you will have to perform another quest before she tells you this information. On the island in Erud's crossing, you can kill zombie sailors who will occasionally drop a blurred map. Give this to her for some coin, xp and a minor item and the following faction changes: raise Steel Warriors, Guards of Qeynos and Knights of Truth and Lower Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia

Bone Chips

There's an NPC named Lashun Novashine. He's found either on the runway leading up to the flying saucer, or out wandering around in the newby area. You'll see him shouting out "Cease this endless conflict ... " If you give him two skeleton bones, he will give you exp and faction and sometimes cast a healing spell on you. You get the following faction adjustments: +PriestsofLife, +KnightsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.

The Bonethunder Staff

Runethar Hamest in the Temple of Thunder is investigating corruption among the guards of Qeynos. He asks you to go to Surefall Glade, find Frenway Marthank in the Druids' Guildtree, and ask him for "The Bayle List." He also tells you that if you bring him this list and the Thunder Staff, he will give you a special reward. Go first to talk to Frenway in Surefall. When you do, Frenway is in a state of depression. His son -- who happened to be carrying the very list you're looking for -- was killed and devoured by the great grizzly bear Mammoth, who lives deep in the caves of Surefall Glade. As a Druid, Frenway is forbidden to harm the bears of the Glade, but he wants vengeance. If you can find and slay Mammoth, you will get the list. Return it and the Mammoth's hide to Frenway and he will in turn give you the Bayle List II. There are more than a few people in Surefall Glade who will strike you down for attacking the bears, so be very careful. Once you have the Bayle list II, you need to get the thunderstaff. You can get this by killing Drosco the Zombie in the Qeynos Sewers and bring the order of thunder he drops back to Wolten Graff in the Temple of Thunder. He will give you the thunderstaff. Take that and the Bayle list II back to Runethar, and he will reward ytou with you very own Bonethunder Staff.

Breakfast for the Guards

Two of the guards apparently missed breakfast. One named Wenbie speaks to you of [Voleen's Bakery] and the other, Guard Lasen, complains that even when it rains, he gets thirsty. Give a Muffin to Wenbie, and a Flask of Water to Lasen, and you get some small coin, faction, and some XP from each of them. It is possible that you may get a brass ring (+1 char waist item) if you give Wenbie enough muffins. You get the following faction changes for this quest: Antonius Bayle, Merchants of Qeynos and Guards of Qeynos get better, Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Circle of Unseen Hands gets worse.

Broken Lute

One of the bards in the bards guild in Qeynos gives a quest. He gives you his broken lute named the lisero to take to another npc bard "somewhere in Karana" named Vhalen Nostrolo. Vhalen is located in South Karanas near a small pyramid, and when you give him the lute, he gives you a note which you return for a reward (sometimes a bard spell scroll).

Brother Trintle

Tell Suuspa Clanim in the Temple of Life in North Qeynos about Brother Trintle attacking Brother Estle and she is shocked and expresses the thought that Trintle must have been swayed by the words of Bertoxxulous. If you kill Brother Trintle in West Karanas you discover that indeed he is now a follower of Bertoxxulous. He has on him Broken Prayer beads, which when given to Suuspa will result in the following faction adjustments: +PriestsofLife, +KnightsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.

Camlend's Scythe Quest

The quest is given by Camlend Serbold at Qeynos' Temple of Life. You are supposed to bring him the Opal Scythe (found on Lord Grimrot in SK). The catch is that Lord Grimrot spawns in two versions- a weak one (level 25 or so) without the scythe, and a BIG level 35 with the scythe. Both have harm touch and all the other appropriate SK abilities. The big version can solo a cyclops quite easily, and has no problems making mincemeat out of anything less than a full 35'ish group. The reward for turning in the scythe to Camlend is actually far less than the value of the scythe, so it isn't really worth doing. Note: some people have claimed this is how you get the oaken scimitar, but many others have disputed that claim, so I wouldn't recommend trying this.

The Clothspinner Sisters

You will occasionally see an NPC running around outside the gates named Nerissa Clothspinner. Talk to her and hear her tale of woe. She will tell you that she misses her warrior sister, and will give you a letter to deliver to her sister somewhere in the Karanas. The sister is located in East Karana in a cave. Deliver the note to her, and she will thank you and give you a monogrammed cloth to take back to her sister. She will then be rewarded for talking to you by being attacked by a guard and killed. Bet that makes you feel good. If you kill the guard and the sister lives, she also gives you a veil to give to the sister. When you give the original sister the cloth she will send you with a note to the Temple of Life. If you give her the veil, you get a decent item as well. There you may or may not be rewarded, depending it seems upon your alignment and religion. If you are of a more evil bent, she may give you a note to deliver to Rihtur Shadowbane in the Shadowknight guild which provides some xp and the following faction adjustments: positive Bloodsabres, corrupt qeynos guards and Opal Darkbriar and negative Guards of Qeynos and Priests of life.

The Crate

Guard Weleth keeps complaining about some crate he has. Tell him 'I will take the crate' and he will give you a crate of arrows to take to Nesiff. Deliver the crate and you will get faction, experience and a note to give back to Guard Weleth. When you return the note you get more faction and exp. As an alternative, after getting the note from Nesiff give it to Lt. Dagorok instead of the original guard. You get the following faction changes: Faction changes for giving the crate to Nesiff: MerchantsofQeynos got better, CircleOfUnseenHands got worse, AntoniusBayle got better, CoalitionofTradefolk got better, GuardsofQeynos got better; Faction changes for giving the invoice to Weleth: GuardsofQeynos got better, AntoniusBayle got better, CorruptQeynosGuards got worse, CircleOfUnseenHands got worse, MerchantsofQeynos got better; Faction changes for giving the invoice to Lieutenant Dagarok instead: CorruptQeynosGuards got better, GuardsofQeynos got worse, KaneBayle got better, CircleOfUnseenHands got better, BloodSabers got better.

Crush the undead

Tell Wolten Grafe in the Order of Thunder that you will [crush the undead]. He will give a 6-slot box to fill with bone chips. Combine the bone chips and give the resulting box back to him to increase the knightsofthunder, priestsoflife, and guardsofqeynos, and reduce the bloodsabres. You also get some minor items (food, water), small change and some experience.

Cures

Tonmerk Plorsin, in the flying saucer at the Temple of Life is a healer and will perform the following tasks for a price : [cure poison] 1 snake fang; [heal] 5gp; [cure disease] bandages and 1 bog juice.

Cure for Lempeck Hargrim

This is the quest for the Shining Star of Light, a 1HB cleric/paladin weapon. The quest starts with Astaed Wemore, the same cleric in the Temple of Life who gives the blessed oil quest. Apparantly Lempic Hargrim is sick and needs a special cure. He will tell you to go to Rivervale and find Kizzie Mintopp, and tell her that "I am from temple of life". She will then tell you to find some guy called Honeybugger outside in Misty Thicket and find out how to get honeycombs, because she needs them to make Honey Jum. Bring her the honeycombs and she makes the Honey Jum for you. You then need to give it back to the cleric who gave you the quest. He will make the Jum into a potion for Lempeck Hargrim of West Karana. Take the Potion to Lempeck, and receive a rusty scyth (Lore item version). Sometimes he goes crazy and attacks you at this point. I have seen two theories on what makes him attack you. One theory is that if you don't get the medicine there within a certain period after you get it it is too late for him and he attacks. The other theory is that you have to give it to him in the daytime, since lycanthropy is a nocturnal disease. I would suggest you get sow and do it in the day just to cover both. Take the scyth back to the Aeston Wemore, and you get the shining star. Quickest method to complete the quest without all the talking: 1) Give Kizzie Mintopp 3 honeycombs and 30 gp for the Honey Jum. 2) Take Honey Jum to Cleric in temple of life (the one who gives the quest) and get Potion in return. 3) Take Potion to Lempeck Hargrim of West Karana and get LORE ITEM RUSTY SCYTH - NO DROP in return. His house is near the river bank, between the fields. 4) Take Rusty Scyth back to cleric in temple of life for the shining star of light. You also receive the following faction changes: Priests of Life, Knights of Thunder, Guards of Qeynos and Antonius Bayle get better, Blood Sabres gets worse.

Diseased Animals

Priestess Caulria in the temple in North Qeynos (the one in leather near the back wall) gives the quest, which requires you to look into the occurrence of rabid wolves in the area. Rabid wolves and bears are found in the Qeynos Hills. Return a 'diseased wolf pelt' to her for faction bonuses, experience and a 'cure disease' spell that she casts on you to purge you of that annoying PlagueRatDisease and Rabies. There are also rabid grizzly bears in the Hills, and if you return one of the pelts to her, you get a spell scroll in addition to the faction bonuses. You do not get a cure for this quest, so make sure you keep a diseased wolf pelt to give to her after the bear pelts, as rabies is annoying and takes an age to wear off. Sometimes, fairly rarely, you get a 'fleshy orb' on one of the rabid creatures. Give this to her, you'll get faction bonuses, some cash, and she'll give you back a lore version and ask you to take it to someone in Freeport (Lorme Trethadore or something like that)(Note there is a Lorne Tredore in the academy, but this doesn't seem to be the right person). Not sure what you get for that yet. You will also get the following faction adjustments: +PriestsofLife, +KnightsofThunder, +GuardsofQeynos, -BloodSabers, +AntoniusBayle.

The Donations

One of the Knights of Thunder gives you a donation box and asks you to find six residents of Qeynos to donate to the temple. Here's a list of where to get the six donations: 1) Lanhern Firepride - Only at night when she is in Fish's bar. Give her an ogre swill for one; 2) Hansl Bigoon - Either at his house, or the office of the people; 3) Kane Bayle - Guard House; 4) Enil Runik - Temple of Life; 5) Guard Leopold - Qeynos Hills, wanders; 6) Guard Cheslin - Qeynos Hills, wanders - Frequently dead. There are other people who respond to "donation", but don't seem to cough one up: The cloth armor salesperson in front of the Lion Mane Inn and Tavern (she says to go away) in South Qeynos. The two gents who make bags in North Qeynos. A bard in the Bard's guild (says he donates to his own cause) in South Qeynos. Also in South Qeynos, a merchant by the Grounds of Fate who only makes three things, axes I believe. He laughs because of the taxes being so high. Oh, so do the bag makers. Your reward is usually a spell scroll.

Drosco The Zombie

The keepers of Bertoxxulos' shrine are annoyed by a zombie named Drosco who is wandering the aqueducts and threatening their security. Talk to one of the Necromancer Guild Masters and he will ask you to eliminate this pesky zombie. Kill Drosco and return the letter and amulet you find on him for experience, faction, money and an occasional spell, as well as another quest. These factions change when you kill Droscoe: BloodSabers and Corrupt Guards of Queynos go down and Order of Thunder goes up. When you turn in the items, the faction change is the exact opposite.

Drosco the Zombie, part 2

The amulet and letter that Drosco carries can also be taken to the Temple of Karana (Temple of Thunder) in South Qeynos. The amulet can be given to Wolten Grafe, the Keeper of the Medallions of the Order of Thunder, for xp, faction adjustments and sometimes a Thunder Staff. (There is a female in the temple who also will reward you for the amulet with a few coppers). It could also lead to another quest. The Priest may decide to give you a scroll to Give To Eestyana Naestra, who is in the Hall of Truth in Freeport. The reward for this part of the quest is usually a nice item (sometimes a prayer bead necklace). These factions change when you kill Droscoe: BloodSabers and Corrupt Guards of Queynos go down and Order of Thunder goes up.

The Escaped Catman

This is the quest for the Hero Bracers. You must do the first part at least once. It is possible that once you have done the first part, you can just get the shackles each time you do the quest. You also need to do the Steel Warrior initiation quest before doing this. You probably need a high faction with the steel warriors to complete the quest. Here are the details: [1] Go to the basement of the arena in South Qeynos and talk to Grahan Rothgar. He will ask you if '...you have a report of the [escaped catman].' Ask him What escaped catman. He will give you the story and ask if you want to '...[retrieve key seven].' You can continue the quest anyway, but you will get nothing for it; no faction, no xp, no item, no nothing. [2] Grahan will tell you to go to Kerra Isle (in Erud's Crossing) and kill the catman named Nomala. Yes, there is a catman named 'Monola' but that is the wrong catman. The correct catman is named 'Nomala'. He is not as high level as any of the other cats but tends to gather a mob. Best strategy to kill him is in Step 3. [3] Go to Kerra Isle. Nomala will spawn by the fire. Once killed, he will not spawn again for another hour or two, so figure on taking a while to do this. Anyway, he will stand there for a bit, then walk off toward the shore down by the dock. Milla is standing around close by, but if you wait about one minute, she will walk off and leave Nomala all alone. Kill him - but don't let him run, kill him quick. Best bet is to hit him once then run a few feet down shore away from their village. He does not always have the key. [4] Return the key to Grahan and you get xp, faction, an item (along the lines of a bronze short sword), and a second quest. [5] The 2nd Quest: He wants you to go hunt down '...a [second escaped gladiator]' Fine, sounds easy... nope. He wants you to return the shackles of the Minotaur Hero of the Steamfont Mountains. [6] Go get em and bring em back. Minotaur Hero + Shackles = Hero Bracers.

Fabian's Strings

Sansa Nusk in the Bard's Guildhouse is trying to make lute strings for Fabian. This is a difficult task because Fabian uses special strings made out of Troll Gut, and Sansa is all out. Sansa will ask you to bring her troll gut so she can make string for Fabian. Troll Gut can be found on a ghoul named Zahal the Vile in North Karanas. Give her the troll guts and 50 gp and you will get the strings. However, I don't know where fabian actually is to complete the quest.

Faren's Tacklebox

On the docks in Qeynos (not the boat dock, the other ones south of there) is a fisherman named Faren. Guard Beren will tell you that Trumpy, a dwarf, keeps pestering his poor brother Faren. Corshin thinks Trumpy is a friend of Commander Bayle. Fish with him for awhile & eventually Trumpy Irontoe will walk up and begin to taunt him. Faren will then say: "Hey! My [tacklebox]! You rotten dwarf!" or something similar. If you ask him about the tacklebox, he will explain that Trumpy just knocked it into the bay, he can't swim and could you please get it. Jump in (you have practiced swimming, right?) and look around. What fooled me the 1st time is the tacklebox doesn't look like a tacklebox, of course, it looks like dropped loot. Get it and return it to Faren for some exp, good faction bonuses some copper and a Hurrieta's Bloody Tunic, which can be sued for the Hurrieta's tunic quest listed below. As a side note, you can also talk to Trumpy and he will give info on the Bloodsabres - for free, without buying any drinks.

Fisherman Convert

A guild trainer upstairs from the Deepwater Knights guild in Erudin will ask you to convert a fisherman in Qeynos. She tells you that the fisherman will break his pole and give it to you to return back to her for fulfilling the quest. This fisherman is located in the pond of N. Qeynos. Dive into the pond and go into the sewers. In the sewers, there is a fisherman near the "main open area" of the aquaducts (he is not there all the time). Talk to him and he will tell you that he is willing to go back to worshipping the God of Prexus if you will give him your guild tunic (I assume you must still have your guild tunic to fulfill this quest). Give him the tunic and he will give you his broken fishing pole. Then take the broken pole back to the guild trainer and she will give you experience, faction standing and an item, possibly an orange ring.

Frostbite's Fish

Talk to Enic Ruklin at the Temple of Life and he will say he has to deal with a [problem]. It turns out a pesky barbarian and his dog Frostbite visit Qeynos on occassion. Frostbite has a tendency to eat the sacred Koalindl fish from the Temple. Enic must find Frostbite and retrieve the corpse of the fish. Frostbite can be found at the Barbarian fishing village in the Westren Karanas. Watch out! Ulrich McMannus is very fond of the mutt and will insta-kill you if he sees you harming Frostbite. Also, frostbite does not always have a fish on him when you kill him and killing him causes severe faction drops with several barbarian factions. Deliver the fish corpse and you will get some decent gold and sometimes a nice item as well (I have been told of prayer beads and a Band of Rodect Nife from this quest). A note on this. You can also complete this quest by killing Koalindl fish yourself or through a newbie mule character (which is the better method), taking the severe faction hit, getting killed by the guard, and turning in the fish corpse to Ruklin.

Gharin's Note

In North Qeynos there's a young man named Gharin who hangs around the clock tower area. He's bummed because he has to deliver a note to Surefall Glade when he'd rather be drinking at the tavern. Get him blitzed and he'll hand over the note. Deliver it for some change and the following faction: (Part 1 - Receiving the Note) GuardsofQeynos, AntoniusBayle and MerchantsofQeynos get better, CorruptQeynosGuards and CircleofUnseenHands gets worse. Going Postal

This is a massive quest involving most of the cities in Norrath. See the full quest description in Ak'Anon and the reference table at the end of this document.

Holy Armor

Daedit Losaren in the Temple of Thunder can cast the Holy Armor spell on faithful followers of Karana. He tells you that he needs someone to gather the components -- 4 Fire Beetle eyes and 4 Kerra Isle beetle eyes -- in a box which he provides. The trick to it is to have ONE kerra isle beetle eye in each of the 4 slots left after the fire beetle eyes go in. Giving him the box results in the Holy Armor Spell Scroll. He will also cast the spell on you if you give him a donation of 10 gp, and you will get some xp and faction adjustments.

Honey Mead for Trumpy

The annoying dwarf Trumpy Irontoe is thirsty. Buy him 4 honey meads (the runt is greedy) and you will get some xp and the following faction adjustments: increase: CircleofUnseenHands, CorruptQeynosGuards, KaneBayle; decrease: MerchantsofQeynos, GuardsofQeynos.

Hurrieta's Tunic

After completing the Faren's Tacklebox quest, you recieve Hurrieta's Tunic. Hurrieta is the mother of the Ironforges in Qeynos. Ton Firepride HATES the Ironprides. Take the tunic to him. Surprise, surprise, Ton is a secret supporter, if not member, of the Bloodsabres. He tells you to take the resulting Hurrieta's Bloody Dress to the Bloodsabre leader (forget his name...I think Garuc). Take the dress to him, and get your final reward. The reward varies from a rusty weapon to a Rat Shaped Ring. Overall, your faction with the general good guys goes down, bad guys goes up. I am guessing that there is an alternative quest to deliver the tunic to the good guys.

Indaria's Doll

Indaria, located in the white building behind the bank, apparantly had her doll eaten by sharks. Kill sharks in the sewers or by the docks until one of them drops a doll and return it to her for some experience and a nice item (usually a Velvet Choker).

Karana's Blessing

Genna Solbenya in the Temple of Thunder needs someone to bring Karana's blessings to the faithful. She will give you a special flask. You need to take it to the Plains of Karanas and ask various NPCs if they are followers of Karana. If they say they are, ask if they need Karana's blessing. If so, let them drink from the flask. Once the flask is empty, return it to Genna for a reward. Although she says to take it to several people, I have been told that you only need to take it to one person - Minda, or something like that - in a farm along the river in WK. After you complete the Prayer Beads Quest, The High Priestess might then ask you to take a LETTER to Brother Hayle asking him to come home immediately. Lord Grimlot's Scythe

The quest is given by Camlend Serbold at Qeynos' Temple of Life. He wants you to bring him the Pestilence Scythe and heart found on Lord Grimrot in SK. Lord Grimrot spawns in two versions- a weak skeletal form (level 25 or so) that drops the heart, and a stronger human form (level 35 or so) with the scythe. Both have harm touch and all the other appropriate SK abilities. The big version is very magic resistant, can solo a cyclops quite easily, and has no problems making mincemeat out of anything less than a full 35'ish group. You need to kill both forms to get the items. If you bring him back the scythe and the heart, he may reward you with the Aegis of Life, a very nice shield. Here is the catch. You need to have an insane amount of faction to complete this quest. High end warmly is required. Furthermore, the amount of faction needed to get the quest is not sufficient to get the item so you will need even more faction or run the risk of him just taking the scythe and giving you nothing in return. If you really want this item, I would suggest you do one of the lower end quests at least several hundred times to raise your faction, and then cross your fingers and hope it is enough, since there is no way to know if your faction is good enough until you actually give him the items.

Sneed's Rat Infestation

Sneed of Sneed Gallaways Trading Post apparantly has a rat problem, and it's not his customers. After talking to him and hearing his sorry story, go upstairs and kill the mangy rat and bring his head back to Sneed as proof of your deed. He will give you a few coppers and an iron ration as your reward. Yippy.

Steel Warrior Initiation

This quest can be done around level 6 or so (lower with a group). Over the course of it, you will receive several faction changes raising Steel Warriors, Guards of Qeynos and Knights of Truth and Lowering Corrupt Qeynos Guards and Freeport Militia. Go to Brin Stolunger in Warriors Guild S. Qeynos, tell him you are a [young steel warrior]. He will give you a ten slot arena sack and tell you to collect 5 bat wings and 5 snake scales and combine them inside to create a full sack. When you give it back to him, you get a few copper, xp, faction and a note that you are to take to Axe Broadsmith in Qeynos Hills. (He's in the hut by the pond) When you give him the note, he will tell you to kill the [strange skeleton] across the pond. When you kill it, bring it's skull to him as proof, and you'll get more coppers, xp, faction and a letter of recommendation to take back to Brin Stolunger. When you return to Qeynos and give the letter to Brin, you get MORE coins, xp, faction, and another note. This one you take downstairs to the animal pits below the arena, and give it to Grahan Rothkar who tells you it's time for your Final Test. He will give you a key to open one of the animal pens so you can enter and fight a young lion. When you kill the young lion, bring it's pelt and meat to Grahan and you'll get MORE copper, xp, faction and TADA... Strongbear Bracers. And he welcomes you to the Steel Warriors!

The Tattered Pouch

In Southern Qeynos, there is occasionally a beggar walking around. He is usually located under the port authority dock. His name has something to do with Rats. Anyhow, he begs you for 2gp's to spare his starving life as he's not eaten in days. Giving him this will Raise some factions while lowering your merchants standings and it will open The Tattered Pouch quest. After offering him the 2gp (you can use any combination of money i.e. Gold, Silver and Coppers to make the 2gp) he will tell you about a mysterious pouch he happened to pick up off the street and for an additional 3gp's, he is starving after all, he will give you the pouch. At this point, once again, the same factions raise and lower, and he tells you that Crow is known for being a fencer, and quickly changes that to Crow being familiar with the local goings on and says he knows a good deal about "missing" items. After leaving the filthy bum, high tail it to Crow's bar. Hand him the Tattered pouch and this quest is done. You get experience, some money, a beer, and all good factions lower, all bad factions raise. Turns out "honorable" crow is really working for the bad guys.

Taxes

On the second floor one of the buildings in a small group of buildings to the EAST of "The Lion's Mane Tavern" and to the south of "Nesiff's Wooden Weapons" and the "Bag and Barrel", you will find a man named Vicus Nonad with a bad cough. After talking to him for a bit, he will tell you that he is a tax collecter and needs you to make his runs for him. He will give you a box, in which to place and combine the taxes, and a scroll with the names of the people. To get the taxes from the, simply hail them and say "Please pay your taxes." Doing this will give you a very small lowering of faction with Qeynos merchants. Upon going to Mira Sayer, In Qeynos Hills, and asking her to pay, she will begin to cry. When you ask her why she is crying, she will tell you a halfling named Flynn something or other stole her tax money. He is in Crow's Pub in Qeynos. Go to him, hail him, and ask him if he stole Mira's tax money. He will affirm this, and tell you to say "I am a gnoll loving weakling and am not worthy to comb your feet" <grin> After doing so, he will shout it, and the entire zone will hear what an idiot you are. He'll give you the money, combine it, and give it to the tax man. You'll get a couple silver, and an item (sometimes a silver stud and/or a Brass Ring). A tip for those of you who need an extra way to hold items. If you keep the tax collection box, you will have a 10 slot box to hold items, and you can get this as low as level 1. Not bad.

Tayla Ironforge

Brohan Ironforge tells you that his daughter Tayla is missing and was last seen near Nektulos Forest. Bring him proof that she is safe and he will reward you. Apparantly Tayla is working at a brothel named the Maiden's fancy in Neriak. (You gotta love that one). Not sure what she will give you if you talk to her, but I have been told that she won't talk to humans.

Temple Blankets

Chesgard Sydwen (near Temple of Thunder) will ask you if you are with the [Temple of Thunder] If you say yes then ask you to take blankets to Follower of Karana. You visit residents of Karana and ask "Are you a follower of Karana?" If they are then ask "Do you require Temple blankets?" If they say yes, you give them the blankets.

Tomer's Rescue

You probably need high faction with the monks to do this quest. Tomer Instogle is a Monk hurt near the western walls outside of Qeynos city. He will tell you that he has been hurt and needs to get back to his guild in Qeynos. Leave him and go to the Silent Fist Guild in N. Qeynos City. Talk to Seta Bakindo in the back room. Ask her about Tomer and she will ask you to escort him back. Go back to Tomer and tell him "I will escort you back". He will ask you to hold his backpack for him and will follow you. Take him back to Seta and tell her "I rescued Tomer". Give Tomer back his backpack for Faction and Exp. This raises faction with Silent Fist Clan and Ashen Order.

Trumpy Irontoe's Head

After showing your prowess by killing the zombie Drasco, you will be asked to seek out and kill the halfling Trumpy Irontoe. Seek him out and bring back his head for some experience and yet another quest. Warning, you may have to kill Trumpy several times before you manage to chop off his head. Also, Trumpy seems to be a good friend with Commander Kane, who will get very upset if you keep killing his little buddy. The quest is worthwhile for leading to the next quest, but be aware of the potential consequences.

Trumpy Irontoe's Skull

After bringing the head of Trumpy Irontoe to the Necro Guildmaster in Qeynos, he will cast a spell on it and hand you back his fleshless skull to deliver to Lord Grimrot, who is supposed to be in a field of skeletons somewhere in the Plains. Grimrot is located in South Karana, surrounded by some tough and dangerous skeletons. You may have to wait a while for him to show up so be prepared to fight or hide. The delivery of the skull will get you a decent amount of experience and gold, as well as an occasional nice item.

Unser's Call

This quest is initiated by Unser in the Hall of Sorcery. He asks you to collect for him 2 bat wings, 1 snake scale, & 1 rat whiskers for his Unser's Call. Collect these things for him and you will get XPs and a nice ratings bonus.

Vasty Deep Water

If you go talk to one of the Paladins on the top floor of the temple of divine light, he will tell you to fetch a book from the library. This starts either the library book or Vasty Deep Quest. The librarian tells you she doesn't have it but that a person on the top floor knows the person who last checked it out. When you ask him, he tells you that the person who checked out the book is names Modorro and that he was executed in Qeynos for crimes against the state. He tells you to go talk to his sister who is in the Erudin Palace. Once there, she says to meet her just outside the hotel in town to talk to her. When you meet her, she explains that her brother is still alive. She'll then tell you how Moodoro got corrupted by a halfling named Flynn Merrington. They used to sell the valuable Vasty Deep Water, till Moodoro got caught. Here's where there is a catch, part of getting to the quest requires asking a question that is not in brackets. Some people may miss it that way. Nolusia says that she created a doppleganger to die in Moodoro's place. You must ask Nolusia 'What is a doppleganger' or 'What about the doppleganger?' Even though it is not in brackets. She'll tell you it died in Moodoro's place and he relocated to Qeynos, and that she is afraid that he's started up again because of Flynn. Lanken Rjarn knows, and they're both afraid that the High Council will learn of it. So she wants to murder Flynn Merrington. She wants you to return a bottle of Vasty deep water to her, so that she can poision it to give it to Flynn. If you say yes, she tells you to find out how they get their Vasty Deep Waters and to bring a bottle to her and she will poison the lip you are then to take it to Flynn not Moodoro and that will end everything. You can get Vasty Deep Waters by talking to the Bank manager and asking him for it (it may be that only an enchanter can use it for this quest though). He says your guild must have sent you to get it. Bring me a guild coin and I will give you the water. Go back to the temple and ask for a guild coin which will be given with the message remember this is not currency. The coin is a lore item that gives +10 mana.
