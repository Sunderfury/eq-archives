Plane of Sky

These quests are still buggy and many do not yet work. Finish them at your own risk. Each Cass has a set of quests. I believe that most of the items are obtained within the plane itself, and many of those items disappear when you log out. They are broken down below by class. The quests are initiated generally by talking to the person listed. You then talk to the person listed with the quest itself to get that particular quest.
