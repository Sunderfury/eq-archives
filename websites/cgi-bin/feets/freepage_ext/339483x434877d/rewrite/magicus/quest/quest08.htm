The Clothspinner Sisters

You will occasionally see an NPC running around outside the gates whose last name is Clothspinner. Knowledge of this quest is still developing, but here are the basics. Talk to her and hear her tale of woe. She will tell you that she misses her warrior sister, and will give you a letter to deliver to her sister somewhere in the Karanas. The sister is located in East Karana in a cave. Deliver the note to her, and she will thank you and give you a monogrammed cloth to take back to her sister. She will then be rewarded for talking to you by being attacked by a guard and killed. Bet that makes you feel good. If you kill the guard and the sister lives, she also gives you a veil to give to the sister. When you give the original sister the cloth she will send you with a note to the Temple of Life. If you give her the veil, you get a decent item as well. There you may or may not be rewarded, depending it seems upon your alignment and religion.

Milea Clothspinner

A Cleric Guildmaster at the shrine of Bertoxxulous tells you about the dalliance between Commander Kane and Milea Clothspinner. He wants her eliminated. Venture to Eastern Karanas to the cave where Milea makes her home, do her in, and return with proof of her death for faction and experience. Be forewarned that this quest will not make you friends with Commander Kane.

Sir Morgan's Armor

At the zone border of Eastern Karana, by the qeynos road marker is a knight, Sir Morgan, and his squire. Sir Morgan needs money to upkeep his rusty armor. Give him 5 gold and you will get a random item. Some of these can be pretty nice. Items I have been told he has given out include the following: Patchwork cloak; Patchwork boots; Rusty Weapons; Silver Earring; Bloodstone; Halfling knife; 1st level spell scroll; Bronze Dagger; Belt pouch; Damask cap; Mountain Lion Cape; Highkeep Flask; Snakeskin Mask; Drom's Champagne.
