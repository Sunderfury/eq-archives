Runnyeye warbeads

In the mines of Kaladim, Diggins says he'll "maybe just maybe" give you a Piercing weapon for 4 Runnyeye Warbeads. I don't know if he follows through with his promise.

Runnyeye Warbeads No 2

In the alternative, you can return these to the sheriff at the top floor of the bank in Rivervale. You will get a reward for every 4 warbeads you give him. Make sure you don't give him broken warbeads as this will not cause him to give you a reward. You get experience, faction and 1-3 silver.
