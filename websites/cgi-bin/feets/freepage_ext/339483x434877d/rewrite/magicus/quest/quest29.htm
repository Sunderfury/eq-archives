Neriak

Book of Turmoil

In the Library at the 3rd gate is a scribe named Lokar To'Biath. If you give him a bottle of red wine he will give you some exp and it will improve your faction with the Dread Guards Outer/Inner and the Dark Bargainers. He will then tell you he mentions his sister and her vow. If you ask Lokar To'Biath about his sister's vow..."What vow?" He says a 70 gold tithe to Innourok may help. He then gives you the note to his sister , who is disguised as a female barkeep in the Blind Fish Tavern. When you find her, you can ask her about being a scribe of dal and about the book of turmoil. Give her the note from her brother and she gives you a note written in Tier'Dal, the note says that she will give you the book if you return to her the head of her brother, because he has betrayed her secret, and the note. Go back and kill him and take his head and the note back to the barkeep. (You will also sometimes get a bloody robe, and AC 2 no drop lore item, but I'm not sure what to do with that). She will give you the book of turmoil. This in turn can be given to the Oracle in the Ocean of Tears as part of the Tome of Ages quest. (you get a glowing torch as a reward).

Death of Lyda Nasin

It is possible that you have to complete the message intercept quest first to get his quest. The guild master of the Ebon Masks in Neriak sends you to freeport to speak to the same dark elf who spawns at night down by the docks. She gives you a mission to kill Lyda Nasin, the daughter of the Owner of the Seafarers Roost. She spawns on the second floor of the Seafarers in the morning, however Dark elves are KOS if they enter the bar (with the new ability to move and stay hidden perhaps the thieves can now complete this one...) In any case an easier way to kill her is to hire someone to go upstairs and tell her that a ship has docked. She will come outside to the pier where she can be killed. You get faction, some cash, and a minor item.

Fire Beetle Eyes & Mammoth Tusks

An NPC on the top floor of the Necromancer Guild Hall named Noxhil wants young Teir'Dal to assist him with some [menial duties] that he has. If you choose to help, he will give you a box which you need to fill with 10 fire beetle eyes and combine. When you hand the combined box in you get a few coppers and he mentions [other components]. When you ask him about the other components, he then tells you to get 4 kerran fire beetle eyes and a bull elephant tusk. There are no bull elephant tusks in the game. In order to do this quest, you give him a Mammoth tusk instead. Mammoths are found way over in Everfrost and the Kerran Beetle Eyes are found in Erud's Crossing, so you will have to do some hiking to get him his stuff. Also, Bull Elephants, which are found in South Karana, do occasionally drop mammoth tusts as well (who knows why). When you give him the Mammoth tusk and the combined box of Kerra Island beetle eyes, you will get some experience and a reward. I received level 12 necro spells each of the three times I did the quest. He then tells you that you seem to be of sufficient level to assist [The Dead]. Here the quest series is broken, and I could not get him to tell me his third quest.

Fire Goblin Runner

Talk to Narex T'vem in the Warrior's Guild in Neriak. He will ask you to go to the Lavastorm Mountains and look for a Fire Goblin Runner who is supplying an Erudite. The runner often zips right through the area by the zone entrance, and is a fairly low level. If you kill the Fire Goblin Runner and return his Runner Pouch you will receive some experience, money and sometimes a Featherweight Pouch. He doesn't give the quest to higher levels, but still gives the reward for the pouch.

Going Postal

This is a massive quest involving most of the cities in Norrath. See the full quest description in Ak'Anon and the reference table at the end of this document.

Halfling Raider Helm

A Guard in Foreign Quarter will announce that the indigo brotherhood is looking for some to kill halfling raiders, located in the camps in Nektulos. He will direct you to go talk to the brotherhood for more information. A warrior in the guild will tell you he will pay you 1 GP for every Halfing Raider cap you return to him. Get some gold, experience, and faction increase/decrease. I am told you need to give them to him 4 at a time. You may also get a Footman's Voulge.

Honeybugger Assasin

Paziu Puno (rogue guild neriak) wants Lil Honeybugger dead and promises a "king's reward" for her head. She lives in Misty Thicket in the Halfling territory just outside of town. Unfortunately, she rooms with her big brother Joogl Honeybugger who never seems to leave her side, and he is probably around level 10 or so. Since you need to be below this level to get the quest, you had better get some help in killing her. When you kill Lil, she drops a halfling head (which is what you're supposed to bring back to Paziu Puno for your "kings reward"), a bandage (so you can reverse the faction hit you take from killing Lil by giving it to Joogl later), and a Case of Jum Jum Juice (lore item)(which I guess you can give to the treant). Here's where this quest gets funny. After sneaking/hiding through Rivervale and making the run back to Neriak, you need to give the halfling head to Paziu Puno. He then tells you something to the effect of "Foolish young elf. This was no contract from King Naythox. I wanted Lil Honeybugger killed before she gave birth to my child. Only a naive dark elf would set foot in Halfling territory. Here's your great reward, garbage from other guild exploits." You get some exp, a few cp, a minor item, and the usual rogue dark elf faction hits (better with Ebon Mask, worse with guards of qeynos, guardians of the vale, and wolves of the north). Never trust a dark elf.

Kerran Beetle Eyes

(All Classes) This quest is initiated in Neriak. An NPC in the Necromancer Guild will ask you to venture to far away Kerra Island on the continent of Odus and collect beetle eyes for him. If you choose to accept this rather onerous task, you will get a box which you need to fill with eyes, combine and return for experience and a small reward. The beetles are located on the island with the Kerran village in Erud's Crossing. Be aware that the local Kerrans don't take kindly to your killing their beetles.

Messages for Neriak

In the Necromancer Guild hall you will find a NPC who needs your help as a courier. (the same one who asks about fire beetle eyes) He wants you to seek out three individuals and ask them if they have any messages for Neriak. Your reward for each completed message is some money and experience. The first messenger is located in the Western Commonlands. The second person on the list, Cytodl Krish, is on the second floor of the Inn that is NE of the entrance to the High Hold Keep. I'm not sure where the other one is.

Moss Snakes

Talk to Hekzin G'Zule in rogue's guild and he will say he is working on some new type of armor. He needs someone to go collect 3 moss snake skins and 3 teeth. Bring them back to him and increase your popularity with guild and receive some minor items (usually one or more rations or iron rations). However, you may receive the Dark Shield of Ebon. Faction changes are as follows: Raised: Ebon Mask; Lowered: Wolves of the North, Carson McCabe, Qeynos Guards, Guardians of the Vale.

Orc Pawn Picks

One of the Dark Elf Warrior Guild Leaders in Neriak Commons by the name of Trizam N'tan gives the quest to return 4 Orc Pawn Picks to him for faction, money and occasionally an Item. The faction changes are +IndigoBrotherhood, -EmeraldWarriors, -SteelWarriors.

Preserved Meat Delivery

In the Oggok tavern there is an ogre chief named Dooga. If you talk to her enough she will give you preserved meat to deliver to an ogre named The Gobbler in Neriak. Take him his meat and he'll give you a nice item (I have been told of an ogre butchers axe and Ogre Butcher Gloves). Your faction will increase with Neriak Ogre.

Ring of The Dead

Information on this is sketchy. There is a female NPC in the Necromancer Guild who will talk to you about a Ring of the Dead. I'm not sure if she is the one to talk to to get the ring. I have been told that she will recharge it if she really likes you, but won't give you the ring. The ring casts bind affinity.

Rogue Message

Eolorn J'Axx in the rogue's guild needs you to take message to person in Freeport. The message regards receving the take of the loot from local rogues. Delivering the message gets you exp. You have to kill/avoid the shark at the docks in east freeport and pick up the locked chest under the shark. Returning with the chest gets you exp, coins an a minor item. Better like sharks if you go on this one. Faction increases with Ebon Mask and decreases with Wolves of the North, Guardians of the Vale, and Carson McCabe.

Sir Lindeal's Testimony

Talk to Loveal S´Nez in the Lodge of the dead in Neriak 3rd Gate to initiate this quest. The forces of evil have been plagued by the good paladin Sir Lindeal long enough. He is located in the Lavastorm Mountains and is clearly not well liked by his evil bretheren. They want you to kill him and return his testimony book. Returning it gets you a nice item. I have been told of people getting a damask robe and a Cloak of the Undead Eye from this.

Teir'Dal Courier:

Hail Seloxia Punox and he will say that he has a little test for the young warrior. He wants you to deliver a report to Ambassador K'rynn in the Ogre city of Oggok. Tell him you will venture to Oggok and he gives you a note. Take it to K'ryn (yes it is spelled differently) for some xp and change. He spawns near the Shaman's Guild, and is not always there. He will then give you a report to deliver back to Seloxia. He will reward you with a minor item.

Thex Mallet

Eigentlich gibt es keinen Grund, diesen Teil der Quest durchzuführen, doch damit unseren jungen Freunden der Hintergrund der ganzen Geschichte etwas deutlicher wird, erzähle ich Euch auch diesen Teil:
Sprecht mal mit Lovehill, einem ziemlich finster aussehenden Kerl in der Nekromanten-Gilde in Neriak, nachdem ihr die Sir Lindeal's Testimony-Quest zu Ende geführt habt. Er wird Dir einen Brief in die Hand drücken, denn Du schleunigst an Kizdon Gix in die Western Commonlands zu überbringen hast. Hast Du ihn gefunden und ihm den Brief überreicht beauftragt er Dich die 3 Teile des Mallets zu finden und an Lovehill zu übergeben. Außerdem bekommst Du einen weiteren Brief, der an seinen alten Schüler Lrodd geliefert werden soll.
Der Satz, den Du an Kizdean Gix richten mußt um den Brief für Lrodd zu erhalten muss genau der folgende sein: "utalk adarev otcin"
Nun, leider ist der unglückselige Lrodd bereits tot, aber man kann sein Skelett im 1. Level von Befallen finden und dieses freut sich auch über den erhalt des Briefes. Du bekommst sogar die Information, dass Du für das Mallet nach Windstream suchen musst.
Um es einfach zu sagen: man findet die benötigten 3 Teile auf 2 verscheidenen Gegnern. Die Klinge und der Kopf des Mallets findet mal auf Elfenskeletten. Die Stem hält Commander Windstream, welcher im 3. Level von Befallen sein Unwesen treibt. Aber seid auf der Hut, Commander Windstream ist ein Zombie, der mindestens der 20. Erfahrungszirkel hat. Alles was nun zu tun ist, nachdem alle Teile gesammelt wurden, die Klinge und den Kopf in das Stem (ein Container im übrigen) zu packen und zu verbinden. Viele Leute entschliessen sich das entstehende Thex Mallet zu behalten und benutzen. Solltest Du Dich aber entscheiden, das Mallet an Lovehill zu übergeben und ist dabei Deine Faction mit Ihm sehr hoch (mindestens amiably), dann erhälst Du vielleich den Ring of the Dead. Es soll sogar ab und zu den Reaper of the Dead geben...

Yegek's Test, part 1

Yegek B'Larin, an NPC beside the Arena area in the Neriak warrior's guild building, will give a bag with 6 slots when told "Seloxia Punox sent me". He instructs you to fill it with 3 bone chips and 3 fire beetle eyes, combine it, and return the bag (and also reminds you to keep your starting shortsword). Doing so results in a faction raise with the Indigo Brotherhood, and faction drops with three good guilds (The Emerald Warriors and two I can't remember), pocket change, and a minor item (often a torch, but I got plenty of iron rations from this one also). You may then get part two of his quest.

Yegek's Test, part 2

When Part 1 has been completed, Yegek may ask if you are ready for the [second test]. If you say you are, he will request your starting No Drop shortsword back (you DID keep it, right?). Give it to him, and a Teir'Dal Traitor appears in the arena. You have to fight this NPC to complete Quest #2. Note that Yegek says you must do it without a blade, but you can use a weapon. He is hard to kill, but can be done by levels 4 and above. The easier way is to enlist the help of a high level player and let him kill him and then you can loot him and get his head. Return the head for a Sullied Two Handed Sword.
