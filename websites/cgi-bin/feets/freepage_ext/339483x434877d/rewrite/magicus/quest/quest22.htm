Kithicor Forest

Ged's Gloves

There is an NPC named Ged Twigborn who hangs around another NPC and they will wander the forest at times. I usually see them in one of two places: either along the same wall as the zone to the commonlands, or by turning on the path off the main road from the sign pointing towards Freeport. Ged will make you some gloves for a price. He makes ratskin, wolfskin, bearhide, or lionskin gloves. All require some money and a specific pelt. You need to be amiable with him to buy lionskin gloves. The Ratskin Gloves cost 6 gold and a giant rat pelt (ac 3 , negative 5 charisma), the Bearskin Gloves cost 25 gold and a high quality bear skin (ac 5), the wolfskin gloves cost 13 gold and a high quality wolf pelt and the Lionskin Gloves cost 96 gold and a high quality lion skin. I have been told that a tiger spawns some times in the south west corner of Kith forest near the orc spawn and gives a high quality skin, and that they are also dropped by highland Lions in NK. The gloves are good for druids who can't wear metal.

Grim's Tiger Revenge

If you enter Kith from West Commons, then follow the right wall like you're going to Rivervale, Grim Oakfist is in a house with an innkeeper near the right wall, not too far from the ranger guild. You must have higher than indifferent faction with the Ashen Order for Grim to give you this quest. He says there are three named tigers in Kithicor that I believe killed his family. He is here for revenge but cannot hunt them due to the rangers all around Kithicor. He says he will reward you if you bring back the pelts of these tigers. They come in different parts of the woods, one even keeps roaring. They spawn after you talk to Grim. So the easiest way to find these tigers is with a tracker-- but killing these tigers gives a hit to ranger/druid faction. The tigers are around level 25, but they hit in the 40's and each one drops a strange or unusual tiger pelt. When you return the three pelts to Grim he thanks you and gives you a Collar of Neshika.

Ivy Etched Armor

In the ranger camp in Kithicor, you can obtain several pieces of Ranger only armor:

Ivy Etched Boots: (Leaf Falldim)

  1. Ronium (details on making Ronium can be found at the end of this document);
  2. Mistmoore Granite (from gargoyles in Mistmoore);
  3. 2000 gp.

Ivy Etched Bracer:

  1. The bottom of the rune of ivy (from Orc named Redwind in Everfrost);
  2. a watery ring (from a tidal lord in Dagnor's Cauldron) and
  3. 1 fire emerald

Ivy Etched Gauntlets:

Leaf wants to reassemble the legendary Kithicor Bow. Hunt Maldyn Greenburn (a wayward master archer) in the Rathe Mountains by unkempt druids and retreive Broken bow part A, part of Morins Bow of Kithicor. Part B drops from Slaythe the Slayer in Lower Guk. Bring Leaf both parts and he will give you the gauntlets. There seems to be no way to instead combine these yourself to get a new bow.

Ivy Etched Helm:

  1. Top of the rune of Ivy (from a deathly usher in Mistmoore)
  2. Cyclops charm (from Tarskuk in Rathe Mtns) and
  3. 1 Sapphire

Ivy Etched Leggings: (Leaf Falldim)

Leaf needs the ”Quiver of Kithicor” and a Star of Odus, which can be purchased in Erudin. To get the quiver, go to Kelethin and speak with Leutenant Leafstalker who tells you to collect 6 different named arrows (Acknowledgment, bravery, defiance, gallantry, fortitude and recognition) from the Outcast Brownies in Lesser Faydark (Kill Pixe Tricksters and Pranksters to get the spawn). Then go to OOT and to Seafury Isle. Kill the Buccaneers until Wiltin Windwalker spawns (he is a very rare spawn) and kill him, hoping that he drops the Quiver. (I have also been told that it may be a faster spawn if you don't kill the buccaneers). Get the Quiver and combine the 6 Arrows. Return the Quiver to Leaf in Kithicor along with the star.

Ivy Etched Sleeves:

  1. Heart of fire (from an inferno goblin wizard in Solusek's Eye);
  2. left rune of ivy (from griffon grimfether in Karanas) and
  3. 2 star rubies

Ivy Etched Tunic:

  1. Skull of meldrath (from Steamfont);
  2. Right side of the rune of ivy (from brownie named Thistle Underbrush in Lesser Faydark and
  3. 2 rubies.

Morin's Sword

Morin Shadowbane, the multisexual ranger guildmaster of the forest, sends you on a Quest for the new Ranger only weapon, the Short Sword of Morin. There are two ways to get the sword. (1) If you trade in an Ebony Bladed Sword to Morin, you will get a Short Sword of Morin, a Ranger only weapon; or (2) She will also tell you that she will give you her personal sword if you find and kill the Ghoul Messenger, who is located "between Kithicor and the Karanas" and bring back his note, or whatever he drops. The messenger spawns somewhere in Western Karana, is tough to anyone not in his 30's, and is always running. He drops a map of Kithicor. Take that map back and you will also receive the sword. You get the same sword for doing this as for trading the Ebony Bladed Sword.
