Fire Goblin Runner

Talk to Narex T'vem in the Warrior's Guild in Neriak. He will ask you to go to the Lavastorm Mountains and look for a Fire Goblin Runner who is supplying an Erudite. The runner often zips right through the area by the zone entrance, and is a fairly low level. If you kill the Fire Goblin Runner and return his Runner Pouch you will receive some experience, money and sometimes a Featherweight Pouch. He doesn't give the quest to higher levels, but still gives the reward for the pouch.

Sir Lindeal's Testimony

Talk to Loveal S´Nez in the Lodge of the dead in Neriak 3rd Gate to initiate this quest. The forces of evil have been plagued by the good paladin Sir Lindeal long enough. He is located in the Lavastorm Mountains and is clearly not well liked by his evil bretheren. They want you to kill him and return his testimony book. Returning it gets you a nice item. I have been told of people getting a damask robe and a Cloak of the Undead Eye from this.
