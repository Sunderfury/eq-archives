Rathe Mountains

Dragon Armor

Karam Dragonforge will make a special Dragon Armor for you. You need a high faction with him to get the quest, but I have been told that wolf form will work.

Red Dragonscale Armor:

To acquire the Armor, one needs to have one red dragonscale (from Nagafen), one vial of swirling smoke, and 1000pp. He is located at the Human Inn in Rathe Mountains.

White Dragonscale Cloak:

To acquire the armor, you need a wedding ring and a wedding present for his wife. For a ring, he wants a platinum and diamond ring. For a present he wants, a black sapphire and platinum necklace. He also needs the white dragon scale.

The Etched Stone

I am told this quest is for the level 49 shadowknight Lifedraw spell. There is a necro npc named Kazann something. He cons blue to a level 50, so is very high level. He will not even talk to you if you are not level 50. He tells evil aligned characters to give him 2000pp! and he will give a stone called a hyacinth to be etched by a gnome shop keeper in Rathe Mtns. He tells you that the gnome does not like the necromantic arts, so you will have to conceal your identity when dealing with him. The necro npc tells you to bring back the hyacinth, a gold necklace like the purest snow (the White Gold Necklace found on the Froglock Noble in Lower Guk), an orb of the purest crystal (the Crystalline Orb from the Stone Spider in Nagafen's Lair), and the blood of xenyari the druid. He tells you that the blood must be voluntarily given by xenyari, so killing her will not do. To get the blood you must bring Xenyari Lisariel a Prickly Pear. The prickly pear drops off of an ice terror, one of Lady Vox's guardians. Be careful and place it deep in your inventory or you might eat it before you return it. When you bring her the pear, she says "Prickly Pears! I just love Prickly Pears!! Thank you so much for getting these for me. I really, really appreciate your gift and I shall not OWW!! I hate when I stick myself with the needles. Oh look, I am bleeding now. Sigh. Can you hold this note for a moment please? My hands are full and I must clean up." You will get a "Bloodied Piece of Parchment. Now take the parchment, necklace and orb back to the gnome, and he will ask for another 1000 pp before giving up the etched rune, which is called a Faceted Hyacinth. Take the Faceted Hyacinth back to the Necromancer and he will give you an amulet and no instructions on what you need to do with it. (the amulet can be kept if you want as it gives +7 int and +7 agil when worn). However, if you take that back to Xenyari she will be struck dead and the amulet will be changed. You need to take that back to the Necromancer once again. Make sure you have a group of friends along because when you give him the amulet a level 53 zombie called a monstrous zombie will spawn, and you have to kill it and give its heart to the Necromancer to receive your spell. Good luck.

Journeyman Boots

Hasten Bootstrutter in the Rathe Mountains wants several items in exchange for his extra pair of Journeyman Boots. First, he asks for a shadowed rapier, from a Shadowed Man, which will poof when you camp, so get this last. Second, he asks for a Ring of the Ancients, which is found on the Ancient Cyclops in South Ro. Finally, he asks you for 3,250 Gold Coins. He also talks like Yoda, and runs really fast, and doesn't spawn that often. He claims that the boots will disintegrate if you kill him, so I assume that he doesn't just drop them when he dies (and he is really tough to kill).

Monks of the Whistling Fist

It is likely that you need to be a tailor to do this. Brother Qwinn in Southern Karana seeks the return of the code of the whistling fist, which was stolen by him. It is dropped by Targin the Rock, a rare spawn in the Sol B throne room. Bring him the Code and a purple silent fist headband and you will get a special needle. Brother Zephyl in Rathe Mountains wants the Idol of Zan Fi, which is in the possession of Brother Raster, who spawns in the Mino Elders room in Lower Guk. Returning this Idol, plus a red sash, results in a special tailoring pattern. These two items need to be combined in a sewing kit with a swatch of shadow silk (tailored) and a Scroll of Jonathans Whistling Warsong, which is bought from the Bards Guild, to make a monk only item called the Robe of the Lost Circle.

Paladin Armor of Ro

Supposedly the drop rates of the items has been increased lately to make this quest easier to do. I am still not sure how you can raise the Deepwater Knights faction if you start out to low to get the low level Erudin quests. This is an extremely involved quest. For each piece of armor, you need to do three things.
  1. You need to get the mold for the item. You do this by fetching the quest items and turning them in to the NPC who gives the item quest. You need to develop a high faction with the NPC's Guild before he or she will give you the quest. [NOTE - It may be that faction is no longer important since a recent patch.] You can get the quests immediately from those who view you warmly [or perhaps amiably]. If your faction is not high enough, they will request that you go to their guild and talk to someone there first before getting the quest. Those below amiable will not talk to you. Below are the people who start the quests and the methods of getting the molds for each item. All molds are no drop lore items, so whoever turns in the items must finish the quest.
  • Boots of Ro: Given by Paladin Nicolas [Dwarf Paladin]. Faction needed with the Dwarf Paladin's Guild [Clerics of Underfoot]. Bring him the plans for the Brazen Brass Kilij from a Sand Giant in South Ro and he will reward you with the mold for the boots.
  • Bracer of Ro: Given by Paladin David [Erudite Paladin]. Faction needed with the Erudite Paladin's Guild [Deepwater Knights]. Bring him two Goblin Nets from the elusive goblin net masters/deepwater goblin netmasters (the waters of Lake Rathe) and he will reward you with the Bracer Mold.
  • Breastplate of Ro: Given by Paladin Abigail. Faction needed with the Clerics of Tunare in Felwithe. If you are not given the quest outright, go ask the leader of the church in Felwithe if you are a [honored member] and if he says yes you can do this quest. Bring her 2 Dark Cauldrons (from the cooks in Mistmoore. approx level 31 shadowknights) and she will give you the mold for the breastplate.
  • Gauntlets of Ro: Given by Sentry Joanna. Faction needed with the Temple of Marr in Freeport [Priests of Marr]. If you are not given the quest outright, go ask Gygus Remnara at the Temple if you are an [honored member] and if he answers yes you have good enough faction for the quest. Bring her the locket from Sentry Alechin of Qeynos [approx level 31. you can kill him or as a guard or werewolf (SK) and get it] and she will reward you with the mold for the Gauntlets.
  • Greaves of Ro: Given by Paladin Elisabeth [Half Elf Paladin]. Faction needed with the Hall of Truth in Freeport [Knights of Truth]. If you are not given the quest outright, go ask Theron Rolius at the Hall am [I an honored member], and if he says yes you have good enough faction for this quest. Bring her a head from a Nightfall Giant (approx level 35. West Commonlands) and she will reward you with the mold for the greaves.
  • Helm of Ro: Given by Paladin Thomas. Faction needed with Temple of Thunder in Qeynos [Knights of Thunder]. If you are not given the quest outright, go ask someone (not sure who) in the Temple if you have proved your nobility and if he answers yes, you have good enough faction to do the quest. Bring him 2 skulls from the undead cyclopses (approx level 35. plains of thunder/Karana - kill regular cyclops to get them to appear) and he will reward you with the mold for the helm.
  • Vambraces of Ro: Given by Paladin Marianna. Faction needed wtih the Temple of Life in Qeynos [Priests of Life]. If you are not given the quest outright, go ask High Priestess Jhanda am [I an honored member] and if she says yes you have good enough faction to do this quest. Bring her 2 rotten shark portions from plague sharks (a rare spawn found in Erud's Crossing. approx level 30, but around killer sharks which are level 40 or so) and she will reward you with the mold for the vambraces.
  1. Once you get the mold for an item, you need to talk to Lord Searfire, the Dwarven Smith in The Temple of Solusek Ro. He must consider you warmly before he will deal with you. He will then give you the Sol Cauldron and tell you how to do the next step, which is acquiring the ingredients to make the metal ronium. These are: Melatite (Dropped by MB and MC Clockworks in Solusek's Eye. You can also sometimes buy them from the Gnomes in Solusek's Eye since many players sell them if they find them), Two Bars of Enchanted Platinum, Mistmoore Granite (from Gargoyles in Mistmoore), the Sands of Ro (from sand giants) and Soil of Underfoot (Get from Priestess Ghalea in the Kaladim cleric's guild for 4 fairy dusts [need very high faction for this]). Put them in the cauldron and hit the combine button and you will get a bar of ronium. I have been told you no longer need a blacksmithing skill to combine these, but cannot confirm this.
  2. Once you get the ronium, you need to combine it with the mold in a forge to make your item. Any forge will do. You need to have a decent blacksmithing skill for this. (this might have changed in a recent patch so that blacksmithing is no longer necessary). It seems like a skill over 100 will almost always work and skills as low as 50 will usually work, but after doing all that work you want your skill to be as high as possible.

Solvedi Scimitar

Solevdi Aldeberan, in the druid circle, will present you with your very own Solvedi Scimitar, a druid 1HS sword, if you give him a Gaint Laceless sandal (found on the giant Timbur the Tiny in NK) and a Lionhide backpack (found on Groi Gutblade in SK). Many will debate if it is worth the trade.

Tbrinn's Quest

Tbrinn Ember offers you a weapon if you bring him Gazhak the Berserker's Mithril Plated Girth and King Xorbb's Slime Crystal staff. Return these and he will give you a Sap Sheen Staff, a Druid only 2HB weapon.
