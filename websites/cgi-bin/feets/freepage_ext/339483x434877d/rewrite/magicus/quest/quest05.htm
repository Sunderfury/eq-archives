The Falchion

Solltet ihr euch bereits bei Lord Jyleel bewährt haben indem ihr ihm einen Brief von einem Orc Runner besorgt habt wird er euch vielleicht fragen ob ihr euch selbst als ehrenhafter Paladin des Hofes beweisen wollt. Und das fragt er einen jungen Magier... der hat sie doch nicht mehr alle...
Solltet ihr dem zustimmen, schickt er euch los um euren Mut zu beweisen. Als Beweis genügen ihm 3 Gegenstände die man diversen finsteren Gesellen abnehmen kann.
Zuerst wäre da mal das dunkle Herz von Ambassador Dvinn aus Crushbone, ein ganz übler Geselle.
Als zweites will er den blauen Kopf eines Orc Prophet, ebenfalls aus Crushbone.
Zu guter letzt der schwierigste Teil, die Supply Crate, welche sich im Besitz eines gewissen Brarm auf dem Sirenenfelsen im Ocean of Tears befindet. Allerdings ist zu Brarm zu sagen daß er nie allein ist, er wird immer von 2 Dark Elf Dragoons begleitet welche sich etwa im 22ten Erfahrungskreis befinden. Zudem ist er dort nur sehr selten anzutreffen, in den Zeitmaßstäben einer anderen Welt könnte man sagen daß er nur einmal am Tag so um 5 Uhr Morgens (GMT nehm ich mal an).
Wie auch immer, wenn ihr ihm die drei Sachen bringt gibt er euch dafür ein Falchion of Koada´Vie welches nur von Paladinen geführt werden kann.

Orc Belts

Turn in orc belts to Canloe Nusbeck in the warrior's guild for experience, faction and minor money and items. You can turn in as many as four at at a time. The general consensus is that four items will just be treated like giving one item four times, so it is just a time saving device. You can find these by killing orcs in Crushbone. Faction changes are as follows: +Stormguard, +Merchants of Kaladim, +Miners Guild 249, +Kazon Stormhammer, -Cracknek Warriors.

Orc Legionnaire Shoulder Pads

Turn these in to Canloe Nusbeck in Kaladim. As with the orc belts, you can find these by killing orcs in Crushbone. You can turn in as many as four at at a time. I have been told that you have a chance at a better item with two at a time, but that more than two will not help. I am not sure if that is true or not. You now need at least two to get something. As of now, you can turn these in even if your faction is too low for him to actually give you the quest. You will get some decent weapons (sometimes even bronze ones worth several pp each) and items for these. Faction changes are as follows: +Stormguard, +Merchants of Kaladim, +Miners Guild 249, +Kazon Stormhammer, - Cracknek Warriors.

Screaming Mace

You need to get a Dwarven Ringmail Tunic (found on Crush) and a Bracer of Battle (found on the Orc Warlord) to complete this quest. You are also supposed to have a Shiny Brass Shield (found on the Orc Trainer), but you should be able to do it without one. Then, go to the Slaver hut with the Dwarven Smith and the Elven Priest. First talk to the Dwarf smith, who talks about armor and asks for the Shiny Shield. If you have the shield you can give it to him and he will ask for the tunic. Give it to him, and you will receive a dwarven mace. Alternatively, if you don't have the shield or don't want to give it up (Why would you), you need to say to the smith "the king is dead" and then ask him "what about ringmail". He will then ask for the tunic and give you the mace. Once you have the dwarven mace, talk to the Elven priest and say "the King is dead". He will then ask for the Bracer. If you give it to him, he gives you a Prayer Cloth of Tunare. Then give both the mace and the prayer cloth to the Priest, and he gives you back a Screaming Mace, an 8/35 magical weapon that occasionally yaulps.

Slave Keys

You need to give a slave a copper and he'll tell you what key will free him, and you can use keys on slaves for faction. When you kill an Orc Slaver, sometimes they will give keys. The keys always are the same for the particular race/sex combo of a slave, but different slaves can spawn in the same places. Each key has a number to go to a lock on the slaves. When you match the key with the lock (slave) you will get xp and some cash. The number/lock combinations are as follows:
Key # 15 goes to Relton Brenclog
Key # 16 goes to a Male Dwarf Slave
Key # 17 goes to a Female Dwarf Slave
Key # 18 goes to a Male Wood Elf Slave
Key # 19 goes to a Femake Wood Elf Slave
Key # 20 goes to a Male High Elf Slave
Key # 21 goes to a Female High Elf Slave
Keys 22 and above have no uses.

Unfinished Molds

Several Slaves give you unfinished molds in exchange for other items. How they keep these items as slaves is a mystery. The three items that get molds are a Taskmaster Earring, Brass Earring and the Shiny Brass Shield. I am not sure what you do with the molds.
