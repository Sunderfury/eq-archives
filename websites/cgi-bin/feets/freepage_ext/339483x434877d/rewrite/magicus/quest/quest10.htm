Erudin

Barnacle Breastplate

If you talk to Weligon Steelherder, the guildmaster of the deepwater knights in Erudin, he will talk about some deepwater tasks, then on to a diseased shark. He asks for you to kill a diseased shark that is endangering the life of the ocean, fill the bag with the sharks remains, and the remains of 3 of its young. If you go to the docks, and kill the diseased shark, you will get shark items, plus some shark skin that is a lore item. The Diseased Shark is about level 10. Save the shark skin lore item, and kill the young baby sharks that spawned. Each young shark has a baby shark remains no drop lore item, which you need for the quest. Place the diseased shark skin lore item, and three of the remains (seperately in the last 3 slots of the bag), and combine. The bag becomes a no drop lore item, which you return to the guildmaster and recieve a BLUE chestplate. The chestplate is only 8ac and sv 3 heat and cold resistances. However, it looks very spiffy, and is great for parties, gatherings, and weddings =) It's a darker blue than crafted armor too. Supposedly, if you are a high enough level and have good enough faction, he will then give you the quest which results in the Deepwater harpoon.

The Bridge

Weligon Steelherder is concerned about a plan afoot to build a bridge linking Antonica and Odus. Apparantly there are three engineers who have conceived this plan, and if he can only eliminate them, Odus will be safe from the influence of the lesser races. He wants you to bring him the plans and the head of the three engineers who put them together, and promises to reward you with your very own Deep Six Cutlass. The list with the engineers names on it can be found in a house In South Qeynos just off the square with the clock tower, opposite the tax collector's house. When Hansl Bigroon leaves the house, he will drop the list. It lists the names of the three engineers. First is Trantor Everhot. This dwarf can be found in North Kaladim going between the mine and Everhot's Forge. Second is Joogl Doobrough, who is a gnome at the Steamfont Windmills. Third is Purchin Oddsbot, who is on the grounds at the Academy of Arcane Science in West Freeport and occasionally can be found in Highpass. You will take some serious faction hits for killing these three.

Catfish Tail

Talk to one of the Paladins standing below the Temple of Quellious. He will ask if you want to "face fear" and when you say yes, he will tell you to go to Kerra Island and kill a Cat Fisher, and bring back his tail for a little money, experience, faction and an item (usually a brass ring). This quest will not be given after level 12.

Clear Water

If you are a paladin, you can get Clear Water and/or a guild coin by talking to the Bank manager and asking him for vasty deep water. He says your guild must have sent you to get it and asks you to bring him a guild coin and he will give you the water. Go back to the temple and ask for a guild coin ,which will be given with the message remember this is not currency. The coin is a lore item that gives +10 mana. Give this to the banker and he will give you the water and some experience and some faction., I'm not sure what you do with the water, so you may just want to keep the coin for the mana boost.

Erud's Tonic

This is an item needed in several quests. To get an Erud's Tonic go to the Hawk's Inn in Erudin and buy an Innoruk's Kiss of Death (LORE ITEM). Go to the top floor of the library and give the Drink to Sinnkin Highbrow, the bookworm. She'll give you the Erud's Tonic (no drop lore item) in exchange.

Fisherman Convert

A guild trainer upstairs from the Deepwater Knights guild in Erudin will ask you to convert a fisherman in Qeynos. She tells you that the fisherman will break his pole and give it to you to return back to her for fulfilling the quest. This fisherman is located in the pond of N. Qeynos. Dive into the pond and go into the sewers. In the sewers, there is a fisherman near the "main open area" of the aquaducts (he is not there all the time). Talk to him and he will tell you that he is willing to go back to worshipping the God of Prexus if you will give him your guild tunic (I assume you must still have your guild tunic to fulfill this quest). Give him the tunic and he will give you his broken fishing pole. Then take the broken pole back to the guild trainer and she will give you experience, faction standing and an item, possibly an orange ring.

Going Postal

This is a massive quest involving most of the cities in Norrath. See the full quest description in Ak'Anon and the reference table at the end of this document.

Heretic battle

Markus Jaevins, the Magician Guildmaster, will ask you to get the heretic's remains from Jailmaster Lius. Lius will tell you that the heretic escaped. The heritics are the various Necromancers wandering around Tox Forest. It may be that any of the heritics will sometimes drop the bones that he is looking for, but the one who always does is Elia Brook, who hangs out next to the dock. Kill him and return his remains for experience, faction and a small reward. When you kill the heritics, your faction raises with High Counsel of Erudin and High Guard of Erudin, and lowers with Heritics. Turning in the head raises the Peacekeepers and lowers the heretics.

High Guard Battlestaff

After you finish the Peacekeeper Staff quest in Erudin, Lumi will comment that you are ready for more advanced duties, she says "We have heard that there is a person in the mountain keep called High Hold who is hiring mercenaries to attempt to obtain a High Guard Battlestaff. We are missing one of our staffs and believe this person has it. Go to Highpass High Hold and find this person and return the High Guard Battle Staff to me." She is supposed to give you a container to put the staff pieces in, but I have been told she does not do it, so this may be broken. Go to Highkeep and talk to the banker. She says she will clear out old strongbox accounts if given the key. The key is gained from an npc named Tallus Holton, who is located in East Karana. Attacking him also draws his bandit cronies so I suggest a team approach or a level 25 to 30 individual. If you kill him, you get a staff segment (a lore item) and a Highkeep Strongbox Key. Give the banker the key and she gives you a few gold,a little experience and a faction adjustment plus one more staff segment. Combine them in the box and return them to Lumi to get a High Guard Battlestaff (Not sure of the stats, but I think they are Eru Pal Cler only, 5/33, +10 char)

Ice Goblin Beads

Talk to Josper Kenshed in the wizards guild and he will ask you to go to Everfrost and fill a bag full of ice goblin beads for him. Return to the Wizard's Guild in Erudin and take the bag to Josper Kenshed. He will say, " Well done my young apprentice. I call you apprentice, fore you are nothing but a spark to my fire. This is the final component for my greatest creation... AHA!! I call it.. Iced Tea!! Never again shall I boil under the hot sun. As for you, take this. It should serve you well. Now go away. There is no Iced Tea for you." You will get a faction increase with the Crimson Hands, High Council of Erudin and the High Guards of Erudin and a decrease with the Heretics, experience, a few gold and a Servant's Staff.

Ilanic's Scroll

Josper Kenshed in the wizards guild also mentions a [special matter]. Ask him about it and he will ask you to find the skeleton Ilanic and ask him [where is the scroll?]. Apparently, he sent his apprentice Ilanic to test his new spell icecapades, which others call column of frost, but only gave him half the spell and it backfired and he has been lookin for Ilanic for awhile now. He says Ilanic wanders Tox forest. He says when you bring the scroll half back to him he'll give the complete spell. I'm not sure where you find Ilanic, but the half scroll can be found on a Kobald caster in Tox forest, which is a pretty rare spawn. For completing the quest you will get: The spell Column of frost, exp, good faction with The Crimson hands, The high council and the High guard of Erudin, and Bad faction with the Heretics.

Inert Potion

On the third floor of the castle, Toresian Fhabel is waiting for someone to take an inert potion down to Vials of Vitality, on the first floor of the castle, and give it to Slansin. There are two people who you can give the potion to. Some times the correct person (with a single name, not a surname) 'spawns' in the shop and if you hand the bottle to him, you gain with several Erudin Factions. Usually (it may now be always) if you run this quest then "A Gate Bandit", [now also named Slansin?] will spawn in place of the person you are supposed to give the bottle to. If you hand the bottle to the bandit, the following factions go up: Coalition of Trade Folk Ill, Coalition of Tradefolk, Carson McCabe, Corrupt Qeynos Guards and The Freeport Militia. The best part of these faction changes is that it raises lots of hard to raise factions without lowering any others. You can do this over and over again to improve all these factions at once. This bandit is about L10 or 11, and it is possible that killing him starts a new quest by talking to one of the NPC's in the shop. Also, the bandit tells you that a friend will avenge his death [of course that is standard language], and if you take the boat to Qeynos after doing the quest, a guard will sometimes attack you when you get off. I don't know what happens if you defeat the guard.

Kobald Shaman Paws

Lernena Shelyrak of the Cleric's Guild apparantly has a feud with the Kobald Shamen. Some sort of religious dispute I guess. She is a bloodthirsty sort and wants you to return with the paws of her enemies. Go kill Kobald Shamen in Tox forest and return with three paws and you will be rewarded. This quest will not be given after level 12.

Library Book

If you go talk to Rarnan Lapice on the top floor of the temple of divine light in Erudin, he will tell you to fetch a book from the library. This starts either the library book or Vasty Deep Quest. The librarian tells you she doesn't have it but that a person on the top floor knows the person who last checked it out. When you ask him, he tells you that the person who checked out the book is named Moodoro Finharn and that he was executed in Qeynos for crimes against the state. He tells you to go talk to his sister who is in the Erudin Palace. Once there, she says to meet her just outside the hotel in town to talk to her. When you meet her, she explains that her brother is still alive. Her brother is at Crows (It seems he is sick and is in need of some Erud's Tonic. This is a side quest). Ask him about The Testament of Vanear. He says yes he has it and will sell it to me for 2 gold. If you give him the 2 gold he gives you the book which has to inventory slots and a combine button in it. He then laughs and tells you that a thief stole pages 30 and 34 and the book is worthless without them. He also says that the thief sold the pages to someone named Ran. If you then ask him who Ran is he tells you that Ran Flamespinner is a librarian in Highpass. If you go to Highpass and ask about The Testament of Vanear he tells you that he did indeed buy page 30 from a man named Moodoro some time ago but that it was recently stolen by a man wearing the uniform of a Qeynos guard and that this guard was still in Highpass, because he could "feel it". He also says that Moodoro also had page 34 but refused to sell it to him. If you go into highhold pass and go toward the orc side, you will see a lake or pond or whatever. Jump in and swim around until you come to a hidden camp of smugglers. Only one npc there has a name, which is Dyllin Starsine. Kill him and you get page 30 (and you also get a sealed letter which is for the Muffin for Panos Quest in Freeport). Beware that any smugglers around him will come to his aid. Faction hits when you kill him are: (-Corrupt Qeynos Guard, +Guards of Quenos, -Kane Bayle, -Circle of Unseen Hands, -BloodSabers) The faction hits for him are pretty lagre, so be aware after that of potentially slipping to KOS in Qeynos. After going back to Qeynos, I went back to Moodoro and said I want page 34. He said ahh so you figured out I have page 34 pretty smart of me to rip out the most valuable page and that it doesn't matter if I get it because the work is fiction anyway. He then offers to play a game with you for the page. For 4 gold he will deal you one card and if that one card is a jester he will give you the page. Along with the jester, you might also draw the King, Queen, Beggar, or Knight. [Note - keep the Knights and give them to the dwarf on Eriolissi Island for a reward]. Once the whole thing is combined you get the Testament of Vanear, which has stats of +10 wis and +10 mana, and is a no drop lore item. You can keep the testament and use it, or you can return the testament to Rarnan, who started the quest, for some platinum. (NOTE: I am told that you can avoid Erudin completely by giving Moodoro Finharn 2gp (the same guy that you get page 34 from) and he gives you the 2-slot book of the testament of Vanear. Once you get the book, you can combine the page you get from Moodoro (by playing his card game) and the other page from Dylin in HighHold Pass and you've got the book! No need to go to Erudia. And because Moodoro sometimes hangs out at one of the sewer entrances, evil races could sneak in and get the book, too.

Nicked Coins

There is a paladin in the deepwater paladin quild (1st floor) whom asks for proof of thieves swimming in the harbor in Erudin. If you kill one of these npcs (faction hit with circleof unseenhands is worsen) you might find a Nicked coin (Lore Item). Take the nicked coin back to him and he will reward you with silvers and factions.

Odus Pearls

Weligon Steelherder, the guildmaster of the deepwater knights in Erudin, wants you to bring him 4 odus pearls. He gives you a bag to combine them in. Giving hin the bag raises faction with The High Counsel of Erudin and the Deepwater Knights (one of the few ways I know to raise the Deepwater faction needed for several Paladin quests) and lowers it with the Heritics. You also get some coin and an item (often rawhide armor).

Peackeeper Staff

This quest will not be given after level 9 or so. Speak to Lumi Stergnon in the clerics guild and offer to retrieve the peackeeper staff. He will hand you a letter which you should take to Emil Parsini, a merchant in the first hut outside of Erudin in the Tox Forest. When you give him the note, you will gain faction with the Merchants of Erudin, which is a useful faction adjustment. He will give you a staff to fill with treant resin and an odus pearl. In the harbor there is a place where you can at times find 2 Odus Pearls under 2 Sand Sharks fairly close together (on a side note sometimes there will be NPC's seen swimming here who I believe also collect the pearls, if you kill one of these NPC's they say the Circle of Unseen Hands will get revenge your faction with the Qeynos Guards goes up faction with corrupt guards and the circle of the unseen hands go down I also think faction with Qeynos merchants went down)... You want to get both of these because in order to get the treant chips you need to talk to a man in the upstairs of the cobbler shop in North Qeynos... he says he will give you some treant chips if you do a favor for him... the favor is to get him 4 Odus pearls. Give him the pearls and he will give you the treant chips you need to get the treant resin made. Once you combine an odus pearl (from the harbor) with the resin, you get a peacekeeper staff (Damage 4/Speed 30--weapon for clerics and paladins). If you return the staff to the cleric, he will give you faction, xp, a random item and an additional quest, or you can keep the staff. This quest will not be given after level 12.

Poacher heads

Those nasty poachers wandering around Toxxulia haven't just annoyed you. They've apparantly pissed off one of the townspeople who stands just outside the Temple of Quellios. His request is a little gruesome, but hey it's a barbaric world out there. Bring him head of a poacher and he will reward you with a little money, experience and faction. The faction adjustments are as follows: KaranaBandits get worse; KaranaResidents, GuardsofQeynos, HighpassGuards and CircleofUnseenHands get better. Also, after bring back several heads and bone boxes, the npc ask you to find out who rules the poaches. You are told that you must find a clue on the body of a poacher, and the one who summons the skeletons and bring back their head. I don't know the outcome of this part of the quest.

The Power of the Gatecallers

Hail one of the mages in the guildhouse and say "The power of the gatecallers is Summoning." If you have good faction with the Gatecallers (at least amiable) he will tell you to bring him 2 summoned food and 2 summoned Daggers. Give them to him. Make sure that the food is not stacked when you give it to him, and you will get a pair of Gloves of the Gatecaller. The Gloves look like Black Iron Gloves.

Rungupp

The same mage who gives you the gatecaller quest will tell you about an ogre who roams Tox Forest. He is talking about Rungupp, and will reward you for bringing his his head.

Skeleton killing

This quest will not be given after level 9 or so. There is no greater service to your priesthood than battling the scourge that is the undead. Talk to Lumi Stergnon in the Temple of Quellios and you will be given a box to use in your battle. Fill it with bone chips and combine it to get a box of bones. When you return it to the cleric you will get a random item, some money and your faction will increase with the peacekeepers. You may also get an additional quest.

Vasty Deep Water

If you go talk to one of the Paladins on the top floor of the temple of divine light, he will tell you to fetch a book from the library. This starts either the library book or Vasty Deep Quest. The librarian tells you she doesn't have it but that a person on the top floor knows the person who last checked it out. When you ask him, he tells you that the person who checked out the book is names Modorro and that he was executed in Qeynos for crimes against the state. He tells you to go talk to his sister who is in the Erudin Palace. Once there, she says to meet her just outside the hotel in town to talk to her. When you meet her, she explains that her brother is still alive. She'll then tell you how Moodoro got corrupted by a halfling named Flynn Merrington. They used to sell the valuable Vasty Deep Water, till Moodoro got caught. Here's where there is a catch, part of getting to the quest requires asking a question that is not in brackets. Some people may miss it that way. Nolusia says that she created a doppleganger to die in Moodoro's place. You must ask Nolusia 'What is a doppleganger' or 'What about the doppleganger?' Even though it is not in brackets. She'll tell you it died in Moodoro's place and he relocated to Qeynos, and that she is afraid that he's started up again because of Flynn. Lanken Rjarn knows, and they're both afraid that the High Council will learn of it. So she wants to murder Flynn Merrington. She wants you to return a bottle of Vasty deep water to her, so that she can poision it to give it to Flynn. If you say yes, she tells you to find out how they get their Vasty Deep Waters and to bring a bottle to her and she will poison the lip you are then to take it to Flynn not Moodoro and that will end everything. You can get Vasty Deep Waters by talking to the Bank manager and asking him for it (it may be that only an enchanter can use it for this quest though). He says your guild must have sent you to get it. Bring me a guild coin and I will give you the water. Go back to the temple and ask for a guild coin which will be given with the message remember this is not currency. The coin is a lore item that gives +10 mana. Give this to the banker and he will give you the water and some experience and some faction. Then go back and gave the water to the sister.

Zombie Flesh

A guild trainer upstairs from the Deepwater Knights guild in Erudin will ask you to fill a bag with zombie flesh. She gives you a large bag in which you can put the zombie flesh and tells you that the zombie flesh can be found near erud's crossing. Kill zombie sailors on the island to harvest their flesh. This takes a pretty high level to survive. If you want, you can buy 4 zombie skins from the cat merchant, but you won't get your money back that way. Combine the bag and return it to the trainer for experience, faction and a minor item.
