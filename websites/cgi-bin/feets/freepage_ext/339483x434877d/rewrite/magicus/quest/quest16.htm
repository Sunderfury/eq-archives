Scalp Collection

Captain Ashlan collects orc scalps and Captain Orben collects gnoll scalps. They give you 1-6 gold and you will raise faction with these groups: Corrupt Qeynos Guards, Freeport Militia, Highpass Guards, Highpass Merchants and Carson McCabe. You will not lose any faction. This is a great way to improve faction with the shadier groups without losing any with the good groups.
