Maps of Norrath

    These maps are not of my creation, I take no credit for their work or accuracy.  I have searched high and low for the best maps I could get.   As I play I verify there accuracy.  If I find errors I will do what I can to correct them or get an updated map.  There are still a few areas that I don't have maps for.  If you come across them please let me know.   My aim is to complete this list with highly accurate maps.  Some of these are very descriptive while others are generic.  If you don't want the game spoiled you may not want to see them, but if your lost or want to see whats in the zone that will kill you next check em out!
