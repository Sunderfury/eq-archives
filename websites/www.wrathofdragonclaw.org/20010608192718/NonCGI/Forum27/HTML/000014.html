Divine guidiance... This skill allows the paladin to seek divine guidiance on one single attack per day. This increases the chance for a critical hit by 5%, even if the player has not bought critical in that weapon. Ie. If the player has critical in that weapon and if the attack is a 19 or a 20 it is considered a critical hit and rerolled. If the player doesn't have critical in that weapon then only a 20 would cause a critical.

For every 10 points put into the skill allows one more attack per day to be affected. For every 50 points put into the skill increases the chance of a critical hit on that attack by another 5%.

Notes: This is a god given skill to paladins only. The paladin must achieve 100 hps, have a verified +10 to attack in at least one weapon, and 20 mana before being able to use this skill. And finally it must fit within the realm of the god the paladin worships. Some gods require a quest to be fulfilled before allowing this skill.
