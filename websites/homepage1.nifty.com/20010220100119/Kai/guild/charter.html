Guild "Eternal Holiday" is an association of people who would like to enjoy the life together in the world of Norrath. Members are any race, class, level, etc.; however, only our friend can be a new member.
    We go on a journey all over the world to seek adventure. We will help each other and cooperate to confront danger and difficulties.
    We hope we can make a good part of the history of Norrath. We also hope we can establish a firm friendship through our activities.
