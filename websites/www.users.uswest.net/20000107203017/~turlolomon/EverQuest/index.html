ROGUE CHARACTER SHEET (v1.0)

This is an Excel spreadsheet I created to assist me in tracking my character's skill advancement during play. There are quite a few formulas involved, including a check box that will fill in when you have maxed a skill for your level. I keep a printed out copy by my computer, and use tic marks in the empty boxes when I get better. About once a week, I retype it into the sheet and print out a fresh copy.
