      Ok Reformatted to make more sense then it did when written at 1:30, and a few point's clarified my apologies for any confusion.

      General AAs I would like to see:
      *Spell Shield Type Line - This is something that I feel would be useful not only to us but everyone in general.

      General AAs I would like to see extended:
      *None at this time.

      Archtype I would like to see:
      *General Robustness - Does the equalavent for the Endurance pool that General sturdiness does for health.

      Vastly increased Regen for Endurance - Even high end rogues are struggling for endurance, This is our Mana pool and should regen nearly as quick as caster regen (Put end regen on gear for gods sake). Currently I doubt you will find a single rogue who uses the SoF upgrade to Razorarc for this exact reason.

      Archtype I would like to see extended:
      *More Ranks to Ferocity

      *Finishing blow needs more Ranks

      *Increased Triple attack - To be blunt I find it infuriating that I have to invest AA into a skill that my twink monk got at 46, and before level 50 already has a higher cap then what I can get at level 80 with 24 AA invested. However since that seems to be how we are going to be treated, At least let us get it over 150 with AA. I propose the first 3 ranks be changed to 10 skill per, putting us at 105, then 6 more ranks at 10 per for a total of 165.

      Class AAs I would like to see:
      *Give us a line to raise the level cap of Assassinate. This should have been obvious after Velious when the skill became useless. Raising this skill to mobs up to level 78 would hardly be overpowering and would make us stand out again. It never made any significant difference in Kunark, but it certainly Felt Good to see it happen. There is no viable reason I can think of for this not to happen. Rogues had the ability to 1 shot mobs that No other class could. That ability of course was tempered by it being a very low proc chance. There is no reason for rogues not to be given this ability back. I feel 78 is more then reasonable for level 80, because the vast majority of the time an exp grind group is going to be grinding 80+ mobs. This limits the skill to only firing in groups farming lower level stuff (alternate group composition, farming for drops etc), and not your Main key sof Areas.

      *It would also be nice if they made our Ranged AA apply to Bows and daggers instead of just daggers. I mean seriously, throwing daggers have been 100% pure crap the entire history of EQ. They either need to completely retool throwing weapons to make them actually viable against Bow's, or give us a reason outside role playing to use them. Allow me to use D&D Online as an example here, they have throwing weapons of "Returning". And they are fully equal to bows and crossbows. Basically the returning throwing weapons have stats, and you only carry 1 which never disappears from your inv. They do slightly less damage then a bow, but make up for it by means of you do not have to carry 5,000 of the ammo around. Returning Daggers with stats on par with some of the Bows in game would actually make throwing viable. Then that AA that was given to us to Boost throwing, might not seem like such a complete and total waste of time.

      *I have always been a Poison rogue, I am still waiting our poison revamp. EB is nice, but it is not a substitute for a good poison system. Also It needs to appear on the Aura's bar not on the main buff bar.

      Class AAs I would like to see extended:
      *Lines to shorten Escape timers even further. I honestly was fine with its time until it started getting seen through half the time in SoF. I figure if it is going to fail me, then I might as well be able to use it quickly.

      *Would like to see Ranks 2 and 3 of Rogues Fury and Envenomed blades, show a Marked difference that actually is clearly parsable for any rogue no matter what their gear level. Currently I fail as an Anguish level rogue to see any Notable difference on Parses from ranks 1 to 3, even though it Currently states the increase is from 25%-75% and 110%-130% respectively. ( I Apologize for earlier confusion leading to believe I did not know what they did, as I said I wrote that at 1:30 am )

      Outside of that, I honestly cannot think of any real major new AA lines we need. I see us just needing some lines extended and or improved. Most of our DPS lines got mostly ignored in SOF. Do not get me wrong the new ones were extremely nice, I just wish obvious things like Precision and ferocity had been given more ranks. I mean comon, siezed opportunity? thanks but..no thanks.

                            Re: AA Feedback Request.

                            * General AAs you'd like see more of
                            Can't think of any right now.

                            * General AAs you'd like to see
                            None atm

                            * Archtype AAs you'd like to see more of
                            (MEDIUM) More triple attack rankings.

                            * Archtype AAs you's like to see
                            None atm

                            * Class AAs you'd like to see more of
                            (LOW) Higher ranks on assassination so it becomes of real use again. It's still marginalised to trivially light blue mobs. Granted it DOES now work on some old raid bosses (yay for assassinating Chardok Queen!), but that's old history that's trivial for all anyway.

                            (HIGH) Improved SoS ranks. I'm not necessarily talking about the hard-coded see SoS mobs like eyes in crystallos as that's a design decision and one we'll just have to argue about if at all, but I'd like additional ranks to reduce the chance of random trash spotting us in places like zeka would be a huge benefit. Sort of like the tradeskill AAs that give you a chance to gain an item back after a failure. For it to work we'd need multiple see SoS ranks and mobs that see SoS would randomly spawn with one of those ranks. The AA dictates which rank the check is made against.

                            (LOW) Trap detection: the AA we have seems to have minimal impact. Maybe make it 5 ranks with 20% chance per rank so we're guaranteed to spot traps by the end of it. (Or perhaps have trap levels so we always spot low level ones - sort of like spell resists.)

                            * Class AAs you'd like to see
                            (HIGH) Distract: the ability to make a see SoS mob spin 180 degrees about due to throwing a stone to the corner of the room would help a lot as we can then sneak past. Make if it was a roamer it should stop briefly too. Yes ripped off from WoW, but it's decent roguish behaviour.

                            (MEDIUM) Capitalise on weakness: grants a small boost to backstabs on stunned mobs, allowing us to more precisely position the lethal blows on a steady non-moving target.

                            (HIGH) AE corpse dragging. Maybe at a reduced movement speed, but AE based to simplify the job. Either that or get them to fix targetting so that it actually works. I suspect this is an easier task!

                            (LOW) Trap avoidance: having spotted a trap it's very hard to disarm it still. Many times it seems we fail repeatedly unless close to the trap, eg in ssra (which sort of makes roleplaying sense), but unfortunately that sometimes mean we have to be within trigger range. This leads to a jousting action where you just run and click disarm hoping to beat the server tick. With this AA the trap trigger range is dramatically reduced allowing us to get higher chance of disarm by moving in closer.
