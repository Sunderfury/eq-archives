Barbara A. Culver

CFP, CLU, ChFC
A principal of Resonate, Inc., Barbara A. Culver is a nationally known financial and estate planner, speaker, consultant, and author of numerous articles and the book, "Getting to the Heart of the Matter." Unlike most professional advisors who bring a transactional approach to planning, Barb distinguishes her company with transformational planning. This process integrates all aspects of the clients' lives - financial, emotional, social and spiritual. She then aligns who people are with what they have through state-of-the-art plan design. Because Barb pioneered this "whole person" approach, she is a much sought after industry speaker, serves as Editor-in-Chief of the Commerce Clearing House Journal of Practical Estate Planning, has appeared on CBS "this Morning", CNBC "Money Talk" and National Public Radio. She has also given interviews to Money Magazine, the Wall Street Journal and New York Times.

Her extensive knowledge of philanthropic planning has established her as one of this country's top professionals.  Culver's dynamic personality and entertaining presentation style have led to repeated national speaking engagements with professional organizations such as the International Association of Financial Planners, National Association of Philanthropic Planners, and the Million Dollar Round Table.  Barb also offers continuing education seminars for attorneys, accountants, financial planners and non-profit professionals, as well as donor workshops. 

 
A prolific writer, she was recently selected as the Editor-In-Chief of CCH's Journal of Estate Planning. She has published dozens of articles in nationally distributed professional journals, the Cincinnati Enquirer, and has developed a comprehensive manual on charitable giving to serve as a resource tool for the financial planning professional. 

She enjoys working with high school-level girls’ athletic programs, teaching team building and goal-setting skills.  Barb is also an avid skier and loves to travel.
