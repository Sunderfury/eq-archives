Aligning financial resources with clients' goals and values. 
 
Resonate: To show or produce resonance. Resonance: the intensification of sound by sympathetic vibration. (Webster's New Dictionary, 1984). Our name is designed to reflect the image that the word Resonate portrays - the image of ever expanding ripples of effect on our clients' well being... "sympathetic vibrations," if you will. As ideas, thoughts, words and actions are put into place as a part of the planning we do for our clients, the effect is never-ending. As ideas travel from one family member to another, from one friend to another or from one organization to another, energy builds and something new is created - something that's bigger than, and better than what once stood alone. 

The "sympathetic vibrations" we experience by partnering with our clients move our planning services beyond the current paradigm. We have created a new model which takes our clients from transaction  to transformation; away from product to process; from compartmentalized planning to comprehensive planning; and shifts the focus from our client's economic value - to their human value. Our transformational team provides expert and caring "life planning," as we help link the valuation of our client's financial assets with the values of their lives.
