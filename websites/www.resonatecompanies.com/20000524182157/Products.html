Philanthropic Tools

We use a number of tools to explain the concepts of philanthropic planning and to help people move their lives from a state of success to a state of significance. We organize national seminars for non-profit organizations and their donors educating them on cutting edge philanthropic strategies. The presentations we have developed use Microsoft PowerPoint(TM) and are now available to you. These philanthropic presentations blend traditional charitable concepts with values based planning philosophy. 

At the conclusion of a philanthropic presentation, each attendee receives two handouts. One is the questionnaire called "My Wealth Plan and Philanthropic Legacy." This helps people further crystallize their values.
