Kris Arnzen

FIC

Kris Arnzen joined Culver-Freeman Group, Inc., a RESONATE Company in January, 1996. She brings with her a valuable blend of experience in the corporate business world and the life insurance industry. In the mid 1970s, Kris was employed by USM Parker-Kalon in Campbellsville, Kentucky where she held positions in the Human Resources Department, Operations Department and was appointed Assistant Controller just prior to returning to Cincinnati in 1978. 

Kris returned to Cincinnati to join Kenner Products, then a division of General Mills. Subscribing to the belief that flexibility and eagerness were essential attributes for learning and achieving in the business world, Kris worked in a number of areas- Purchasing, International Operations, and  Company Planning and Advertising- prior to becoming a member of the Legal Department as Supervisor of Legal and Insurance Services. The scope of this position helped Kris realize that she had a strong inclination and interest in the areas of contracts and finance. This led her to explore new career opportunities. 

In 1990, Kris left Kenner after 13 years to become a District Representative for Lutheran Brotherhood, a fraternal benefit society that provides insurance, investment products and financial planning for Lutherans. At LB, Kris qualified in her first year for Fast Track Leaders Club honors. 

 

She also has annually merited the NAFIC “Persistency Award” for maintaining a minimum of 90% persistent business and meeting production levels set by the NAFIC. Through her work as a LB representative, Kris came to know and work with Barbara Culver on mutual client planning. She subsequently joined Culver-Freeman where she is now a managing partner. Kris has extensive experience developing personal financial plans for individuals. She is now broadening her practice to include the Estate Planning and Charitable Giving areas of financial planning. 

Kris is a native of Greater Cincinnati where she attended Notre Dame Academy and Our Lady of Cincinnati College. She took additional business courses at Xavier University, the University of Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky University. Kris serves as a member of her church council and is on the board of the Cincinnati Conference for the Southern Ohio Synod of the Evangelical Lutheran Church of America. 

Kris currently resides in Hyde Park. She has two adult sons, Will and Aaron. She enjoys sports and exercise, especially golf, tennis and aerobics.
