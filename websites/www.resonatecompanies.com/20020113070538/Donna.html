Donna L. Freeman

CLU, CFP, CEBS

Donna L. Freeman is co-owner of the Culver-Freeman Group, Inc., a RESONATE Company, in Cincinnati, Ohio.  She began her career in the financial services industry in 1979, and since then has directed her energies to counseling individuals, corporations, municipalities and school districts in the areas of insurance and investments. 

Freeman's expertise is in the corporate marketplace where today's business owner has a full slate of financial issues to address: employee health and welfare programs, retirement planning, key employee needs, business continuation strategies, and individual financial challenges. 

Freeman helps the business owner coordinate and develop sound financial solutions.  She also works with employees of the firm, assisting them in understanding both the company's benefit programs and steps they can take to build their own preferred futures.  She accomplishes this through corporation-sponsored employee educational seminars that greatly benefit employees and entrepreneurs.  Freeman believes that, in today's complicated marketplace, learning the simple basics of financial planning is imperative if people are to establish their future security.  Her approach is based on commitment to a long-term, mutually beneficial relationship.

 

In the ever-changing financial industry, Freeman has been a keen advocate of education for her peers.  To this end she is extremely active both locally with the Cincinnati Life Underwriters Association and nationally with the Women Life Underwriters Confederation, and is a Past President of both organizations.  She has been a speaker for educational seminars, has written articles for professional publications, and has been interviewed on both radio and TV news programs. 

Leisure time is spent with her son Duane and daughter Kristina and her loves include aerobics, traveling, and reading.
