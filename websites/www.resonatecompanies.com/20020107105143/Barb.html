Barbara A. Culver

CFPÔ, CLU, ChFC

Editor-in-Chief

 

A principal of Resonate, Inc., Barbara A. Culver is a nationally known financial and estate planner, speaker, consultant, and author of numerous articles and the books, “Getting to the Heart of the Matter” and “For Women By Women – Financial Passages”. Unlike most professional advisors who bring a transactional approach to planning, Barb distinguishes her company with transformational planning. This process integrates all aspects of the clients’ lives – financial, emotional, social and spiritual. She then aligns who people are with what they have through state-of-the-art plan design. Because Barb pioneered this “whole person” approach, she is a much sought after industry speaker, serves as Editor-in-Chief of the Commerce Clearing House Journal of Practical Estate Planning, has appeared on CBS “This Morning”, CNBC “Money Talk” and National Public Radio. She has also given interviews to Money Magazine, the Wall Street Journal and New York Times.

 

 

A prolific writer, she was recently selected as the Editor-In-Chief of CCH's Journal of Estate Planning. She has published dozens of articles in nationally distributed professional journals, the Cincinnati Enquirer, and has developed a comprehensive manual on charitable giving to serve as a resource tool for the financial planning professional. 

Barb enjoys travel, is an avid skier and devotes herself to working with girls’ athletics teaching goal setting and team building skills in Cincinnati, Ohio where she resides.
