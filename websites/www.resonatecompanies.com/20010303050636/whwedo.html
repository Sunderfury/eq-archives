Philanthropic Estate Planning 

 

We have three key divisions: philanthropic estate planning, individual financial planning, and corporate benefit consulting. 

Part of the unique offerings of Resonate, Inc. are the creative financial planning concepts used with our individual clients & the unique and comprehensive benefit packages created for our corporate clients.

We are dedicated to helping non-profit organizations design and implement their planned giving strategies and generate charitable gifts from major donors. This is accomplished by educating and consulting with the non-profit board, development staff, and potential and existing donors. 

Planned Giving is a carefully considered gift process which emphasizes the donor(s)' philanthropic wishes. This results in gifts which integrate a donor(s)' values while maximizing income and estate tax incentives.
