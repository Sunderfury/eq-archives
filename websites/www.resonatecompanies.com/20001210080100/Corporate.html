What makes us unique? 
 
There is a “groundswell of growing awareness” of the need for workplace financial planning programs.  We at Resonate, Inc. not only know this, but also have built our benefit consulting system on this awareness.  This approach reinforces to employees the value of their overall benefits program.  Due to a better understanding of the tools available to them, employees increase their involvement in voluntary plan participation and enrollment.  The process also addresses the employer concern about future liability issues.  We are also “independent”, with hundreds of insurance and investment products available to us, so our choices are driven by the needs of our clients – not a “higher master”. 
 
What is our approach?
 
Benefit decisions are made to enhance the overall goals and strategies of the company.  When reviewing benefits programs, decisions are made from a “holistic” approach. Once employer goals, strategies and programs are fully understood, we survey the employees as to their wishes and desires.  Their concerns and needs are integrated into the final direction chosen. Employee and employer contributions are scrutinized.  When possible, “tax advantages” are tapped. Everything is studied and changes are made, if necessary.  A presentation is made back to the employees recapping their issues and noting strategies to address.  A systematized annual review is outlined.   Each quarter, on-going education programs are offered. 
 
What are the results of this approach?
 
More often than not, benefits are greatly improved.  Costs are stabilized for several years, and employees take more “ownership” of their “future”.  A powerful side benefit is that morale is substantially improved.  Employees appreciate and become more aware of the “value” of their benefits, often times improving employee retention.
