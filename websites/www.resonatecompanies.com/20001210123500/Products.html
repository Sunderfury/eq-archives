Life Planning Resources

We use a number of tools to explain the concepts of philanthropic planning and to help people move their lives from a state of success to a state of significance. We organize national seminars for non-profit organizations and their donors educating them on cutting edge philanthropic strategies. The presentations we have developed use Microsoft PowerPoint(TM) and are now available to you. These presentations blend traditional charitable concepts with values based planning philosophy. 

At the conclusion of a philanthropic presentation, each attendee receives two handouts. One is the questionnaire called "My Wealth Plan and Philanthropic Legacy." This helps people further crystallize their values. The other is the "Philanthropic Estate Planning" brochure which integrates philosophical and practical terminology.  While it may be true that the only certainties are death and taxes, there are some steps that you can take to leave behind the legacy that matters most to you. What really matters to you? Is it family? Is it your church? Is it a social cause? It really is your choice as to who should benefit from your efforts.

You don’t have to be ultra wealthy to benefit from philanthropic estate planning. Even people of modest means can leverage their gifts through proper planning. We are here to help. .
