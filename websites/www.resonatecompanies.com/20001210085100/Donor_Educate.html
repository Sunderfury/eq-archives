Development Resources, Inc. focuses on educating and assisting individuals through a unique Values-Based and Client-Centered planning process.  This process involves: 
 
 Understanding the concepts of Community CapitalTM
Analysis of gift suitability
Case design
Comprehensive illustrations
Implementation and coordination of the gifting process
Access to trust administration services and legal/accounting consultation
 
 

Planned Giving Benefits for Donors:

 Planned gifts shelter current income through substantial tax deductions
They offer the ability to avoid capital gains tax when appreciated assets are sold. 
They strengthen donors' financial security.
They offer professional management of donors' assests.
They reduce the possibility of a contested estate, and significantly decrease probate expenses.
Charitable gifts promote privacy, if desired.
They allow the donor to retain complete control over the disposition of his estate,even after death.
They can be used to provide income for the donor or loved ones.
They may be used to honor or memorialize a donor through projects which bear his or her name.
They provide satisfaction through contributions to a valuable community project.
