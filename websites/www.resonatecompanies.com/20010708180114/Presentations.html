The Estate Planners Seven Survival Skills for the New Millennium

 

This presentation is designed for professional advisors and appeals to both audience types-whether left or right dominant. It is an amazing blend of technical financial planning strategies, insights into the psychology of planning and changing demographic trends that are impacting our practices today. This information is accompanied by the Six Emotional Reasons Why All People Plan and The Four Dangerous Belief Systems currently present in most financial planners. The program then offers sound reasons why these dangerous belief systems need to be released. Replacement techniques are offered which are more congruent with the sociological trend in this nation, which involves a shift from the Age of Materialism to the Age of Social Transformation. It includes innovative marketing techniques and creative practice management ideas to increase both production and personal satisfaction.  Designed for a 90-minute seminar.

 

 
Making Peace with Money

 

This dynamic presentation helps donors or clients identify a new perspective around money by creating a new understanding of money and redefining wealth.  It provides principles and practical exercises for changing emotions, psychological attitudes and opinions/beliefs about money.  Targeted for anyone who wants to create a relationship with money that is free from stress, worry and pain.  Great introduction to the all day  Making Peace with Money interactive workshop.  Designed for a 90-minute seminar.

 

 

Values-Based Life Planning

 

This presentation can be used either with professional advisors or clients/donors.  It explores the Five Aspects of All People and helps identify what is really important in ones life. This information is accompanied by the Six Emotional Reasons Why All People Plan.  Inspirational quotes from Eleanor Roosevelt, Helen Keller, Stephen Covey and others increases values-based planning awareness, which leads to professional and personal action.  Designed for a 30-minute seminar.

Planning with Purpose

Teaches the importance of Community CapitalTM by contrasting traditional planning techniques with dramatically different charitable estate planning options.  Provides strategies for reducing the estate tax burden no matter what the estate size by starting with a simple will case study and progressing through a wealth replacement estate plan.  Introduces the concept of Personal Philanthropic Philosophy that adds significance to ones success.  Designed for an extensive two-hour seminar.

 

The Caterpillar Effect


Offers the opportunity to sell ones business in a tax-deductible way, which also escapes capital gains taxes.  Demonstrates how to creatively transfer business interests to family members without triggering traditional gifting limits.  Designed for a 90- minute seminar.
