Development Resources, Inc. specializes in building strong infrastructure necessary for a successful  planned giving program or campaign. Creative consulting with the development staff and board members includes developing state-of-the-art strategies and designs for issues such as: 
 
 Selecting the initial gifting instruments
Understanding of the gifting process
Building successful relationships with donors and prospective donors
Analysis of donor situations
Efficient implementation of the gifting process
Effective ongoing administration
 

Planned Giving Benefits for Charitable Organizations:

Planned gifts stabilize future planning and fund raising because many gifts are irrevocable.
They are considerably larger than other gifts because of the significant estate planning benefits to the donor and donor's family.
They often lead to other gifts.
They enhance public relations value, status and credibility; and donors recognize the charity as a full service organization.
They build opportunity for relationships with local professionals and organizations.
 
 They expand a charity's options for investment.
They enable charities to obtain gifts from outside the natural constituency.
They provide the opportunity to discuss future plans and address individual donor concerns.
They provide satisfaction of having helped donors effectively plan their estates.
They make the charity competitive with other non-profit organizations.
