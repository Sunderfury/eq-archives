RESONATE, Inc. is a financial planning and consulting firm dedicated to excellence in providing financial vision for our clients.  Our goal is to help our clients not only define their financial goals, but also develop sound and creative strategies for meeting those goals. We are also dedicated to helping non-profit organizations design and implement planned giving strategies and generate charitable gifts.  This is accomplished by education and consulting with the  Non-profit Board, development staff, existing and potential clients. 

 

Barbara A. Culver CLU, CFP, ChFC Donna L. Freeman CLU, CFP, CEBS and Kris Arnzen FIC are principals of RESONATE, Inc. We are seasoned financial planning professionals and registered representatives with VESTAX Securities Corporation, and have extensive investment and insurance backgrounds. We believe in the team concept of financial planning; therefore, we have surrounded ourselves with other professionals highly regarded in the areas of investment, law and accounting.  This comprehensive, value-added approach ensures clients that their financial plan will be done with the most accurate and up-to-date information and methodology.
