INSTRUMENT SKILLS

Available at Level: Singing - 1st; Percussion - 5th; String - 8th; Brass - 11th; Wind - 14th

Effect: Directly effects ability to not miss notes when starting a song using that skill, even if you are singing the song.

Maximum skill: 235

Quantified: ( lvl + 1) * 5

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: These skills are important to keep maxed, as your skill in the instrument for a song (ie Brass skill for Denon's Disruptive Discord) will effect your ability to avoid missed notes even if you are singing the song.  Check the Instrument page for the list of known instruments, their skill, and any special effects.

Strategies:

The best way to practice up your skill in an instrument is to leave yourself logged in for several hours playing that instrument.  With the Brass skill this can be dangerous as the only songs that use that skill are AE damage songs till much higher level.  The solution to this is to visit the Arena zone off of Lake Rathe.  This is a large open zone with no NPC's so you can safely play Denon's Discord for 8 hours without worrying about NPC's coming by to criticize your playing.
[Svearike] Another good spot to practice Brass skill is at the bottom of the hill in South Ro near the Oasis zone line.
[Kollins] On raising Brass skill:
Brass skill can also be raised easily without interruption in the following areas:

East Commons/North Ro tunnel close to the zone border on either side 
East Karana up top of the ramp (south hill works best) 
East Karana at the dead end valley that the ramp makes 
Any boat or shuttle as long as you do not step off with NPCs near and the song up

You can also get about knee deep in the water near the zone edge and be safe in these zones: The Karanas (any of them just not near NPCs like the barbarians or mobs like Broon/Droon) Northern Ro, ButcherBlock Mountains, and South Qeynos.

While the arena zone (outside of the PvP section) IS the best place to practice there are other places that work well. Be warned that these places, while still safe from wandering NPCs, CAN be trained to either intentionally or unintentionally by players resulting in your death if you are AFK. While there is a risk that someone may drag orcs past the players at the bazaar and to the north ro zone or all the way up the ramp to highpass this risk may outweigh the risk of getting to the arena for you. 

Also the boat is a recommended method to get those few extra points,  especially if you need to take it to get to a bard trainer or to obtain a new song. Make sure you stay away from NPCs on docks since being too close you are considered on the dock AND on the boat but if you stay back far enough you are considered on the boat and your songs do not affect anything on the docks even if they are within song range. 

This also brings up a point that a lot of people do not know, being in the water or out of the water or on a boat (not the small maneuverable canoes but the named boats that move automatically) is like being in a different world in certain situations. A good example of this is that if you jump in a lake with piranha they will attack you if you're in the water but if you leave the water they stop attacking; just remember that if you attack a skeleton or whatever on dry land then jump in the mob will usually follow you in anyhow. Crocs in oasis behave this way as well, you can only attack each other if you are both in or out of the water but they will follow you if you go in or out of the water, this is why you can't blast one that is in the water if your on land but if you go knee deep you can.
