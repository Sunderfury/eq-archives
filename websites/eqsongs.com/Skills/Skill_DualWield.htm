MELEE COMBAT SKILL - Dual Wield

Available at Level: 17

Effect: Allows you to wield a weapon in your second melee slot. Chance of off-hand attacking: (SKILL + LEVEL) / 400

Maximum skill: -50: 200 / 51+: 240?

Quantified: -50: (lvl + 1) * 5 / 51+: 200+(lvl-50)*4?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: Your dual wield skill will be checked whenever the delay on your off-hand weapon 'goes off'.

Strategies:

You do not need to train in this skill once reaching 17th level.  Just zone and you will have the skill in the mid 30's and be able to dual wield.
When training this skill up it is best to have a fast weapon in your off-hand.  After the skill is maxed you want use your best ratio weapon in your off-hand and your fastest in your primary (due to the level based damage bonus for the primary weapon).
Use weapons of mis-matched speeds, this will give you the best chance of interrupting spell casters.
Unless your DW skill is high try to keep your proc'ing weapon in your primary hand.  As the proc will have no chance of going off if you have it in your secondary hand and fail your DW check.  Proc'ing weapons do not need to hit and score damage to effect the target.

Cautions:

If you choose to DW heavy weapons keep a close eye on your yellow fatigue bar.  If your fatigue bar goes below 1/3rd you will start seeing stat decreases.  I find it good to keep Jaxan's Jig o' Vigor memorized in these cases, as one pulse will restore nearly half the bar in three ticks.
