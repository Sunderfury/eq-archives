TRADE SKILLS

Available at Level: 1 (Baking, Brewing, Fletching, Jewel Craft, Pottery, Smithing, & Tailoring)

Effect: Increase chance of success when performing various trades.

Maximum skill: 200 (One skill can be raised over 200)

Quantified: Given enough coin and time you can max these skills at any level.

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: I'm not going to write a long page out on trade skills (there are whole websites out there devoted to them). If you need more info on trade skills check any of the popular EQ sites (EQSS, Allakhazam, Eq'lizer, EQValut, etc..).  The best of these sites is run by the Denmother: EQ Traders Corner.

Strategies:

To raise trade skills, or any skill faster, pile on the +intelligence items.  The higher your intelligence the quicker you will learn skill.
If you want to minimize failures, pump up your wisdom.  When any skill, not just trade skills, fails a skill check you get a second check against wisdom.  The chance of making this check is not great, but is better then nothing.

Cautions:

Trade skills are a huge money and time sink. But it can be fun to hand out home brew to your buddies, or give a stack of rat-ear pies to the guy in your party that is always begging for food.
