DENON's DISSENSION

Level: 35

Effect: Direct Mana Drain

Skill: Brass

Target: Area Effect

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: ?

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: The mana drain rate is very slow, making this song hard to use.

Strategies:

Since the Mana drain is treated as a debuff you can use this on mesmerized targets without waking them up.
In my testing this song will drain about 22 mana a pulse at 37th lvl.  Using a horn does not increase this amount.

Cautions:

Since this song is a direct mana drain, weaving it will decrease its effectiveness.
