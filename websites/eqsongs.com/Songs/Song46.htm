TUYEN's CHANT OF FROST

Level: 46

Effect: Decreases cold resistance, damage over time (cold based)

Skill: Percussion

Target: Single

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: High

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song lowers the cold resistance of the target and has a small DoT effect (greater with a drum). Chant of Frost checks against the target's Cold Resistance for success.

Strategies:

Used with a drum this song does significant damage and a sizable cold resist debuff.  Use this to help the wizards in your group to hit harder with Ice Comet, also shaman have many cold based spells.
This song will stack with both Fulfil's Curtailing Chant and Tuyen's Chant of Flame.  Allowing you (with a drum) to do damage reviling melee classes while helping the casters hit harder.

Cautions:

Do to the dual effect (damage and debuff) it is hard to get it to stick.
