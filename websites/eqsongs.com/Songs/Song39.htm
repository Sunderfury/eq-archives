SOLON'S BEWITCHING BRAVURA

Level: 39

Effect: Charm, -MR (Limited to mobs your level or 51st whichever is lower)

Skill: Wind

Target: Single

Location: Kelethin

Does not stack with: Enchanter's mesmerize line of spells

Quantified: Charms the target turning it into your pet for anywhere from 0 to about 15 seconds. Using a wind instrument increases the magic resistance reduction (unconfirmed).  This song is highly dependent on your charisma, and will use 20 mana whenever pulsed.

Taunt: Very High

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana Server

Comments: See Solon's Song of the Sirens for a complete analysis of the pros/cons of Bardic charm.

Strategies:

Initial reports state that this song differs from Solon's Song of the Sirens in a couple of ways. It lowers magic resistance, using a wind instrument increases the the MR reduction.
On the PvP server (RZ) you can refresh this song before it breaks.  On the blue and PvP-Team servers you can not.
