NIV's MELODY of PRESERVATION

Level: 47

Effect: +STR, Damage shield (applies to magic damage only), Hit point regeneration

Skill: String

Target: Party

Location: Kelethin, Qeynos

Does not stack with: Psalm of Mystic Shielding, does stack with all the other Psalms and the Guardian line.

Quantified: ?

Taunt: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments:  This is an interesting song and not quite like any other in the game.  Niv's provides the party with extra strength, increased Hit Point regeneration (which stacks with Hymn of Restoration as well as the druid/shaman regeneration spells), and also provides a small 'magic skin' effect vs magic damage.  

Strategies:

[Examer Leafblight] This song is must in a group with a shaman, twist it during fight and the shaman can cannabalize without losing any hp. The song sung gives one free cannabalize each pulse, 2 with a string instrument. Excellent to play with cantanna on downtime if you have a shaman.
