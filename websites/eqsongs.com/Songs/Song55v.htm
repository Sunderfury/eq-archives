OCCLUSION OF SOUND

Level: 55

Effect: decrease magic, cold, and fire resistance

Skill: Percussion (required)

Target: Single

Location: Mob drop in Velious

Does not stack with: Fulfil's Curtailing Chant

Taunt: High?

Quantified:

Analysis by Cuthburt Dirgewielder, Ayonae Ro server

Comments:  Occlusion of Sound Cannot be resisted, it is - 30 CR/FR/MR. This song is unbelievable on raids. It is common practice that ALL bards on a raid are making sure they are sticking Occlusion. It seems a bit over kill but, if a bard dies the show must go on. Every This song has made mobs that seemed  unslowable, slowed in the first or second cast. Allows for other debuffs to land as well. Not to mention the wizards are very greatful. When my guild found out how much of a difference this song makes, bards are now worth granting a heal on a raid. This song is even more useful than your resist songs on a raid IMHO. Using different types does not seem to have an effect on it. I get the same utility using this song whether equiped with epic or selos drum of march. The important thing is that you have this song and you are using it.
There is no damage component to it. But it does seem to have a small interrupt to it. I cannot 100% confirm this but it has seemed that if you land occlusion just at the point when the mob is going to cast you interrupt casting. This may be just coincidence.

Strategies:

This song has no damage component so it is a great way to help Enchanters (or yourself) with mezzing tough targets.
This song is awesome for god lvl encounters. Whenever we go on a raid it is imperative that this song be on the boss mob at all times. It is completely unresistable and allows other casters to land their debuffs.
