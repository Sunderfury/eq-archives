SONG of TWILIGHT

Level: 53

Effect: Mesmerize, lowers magic resistance, Pull effect

Skill: Wind

Target: Single

Location: Firiona Vie

Does not stack with: Overrides enchanter's mesmerize line of spells

Taunt: Medium

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: Mesmerizes the target for anywhere from 0 to 15 seconds and lowers the targets MR slightly. Using a wind instrument increases the magic resist debuff. High charisma helps with success rate and duration.  It also has the effect of pulling the target toward the bard.

For a the full lowdown on the the uses of bard mez see Crission's Pixie Strike.  The only difference between the two songs is Pixie Strike will push the target away from the direction the singer is facing and Twilight will pull the target in the direction the singer is facing.
