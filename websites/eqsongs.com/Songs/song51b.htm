SELO's SONG of TRAVEL

Level: 51

Effect: Increase movement speed, levitate, invisibility & see invisible.

Skill: Percussion

Target: Party

Location: Firiona Vie

Does not stack with: Selo's Accelerando

Taunt: Low

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song is a pure travel song.  As any attack on or by you will break the effect.

Strategies:

[Crier] I have taken to keeping level 5 Selo's Accelerando memmed while using this song; when I come under attack, I fire up good old trustworthy Selo's to make my escape and then restart Song of Travel once out of range of the mob. Therefore I normally have Selo's Accelerando memorized and only mem Song of Travel when moving great distances.
If attacked while traveling with SSoT I usually chain the critter then fire up SSoT again. This usually gives me plenty of time to escape.  If more then one mob comes to play a quick charm will keep them occupied while you escape.

Cautions:

[Crier] Any attack on or by you will break the song's effect instantly, including missed swings, resisted spells and dodges. What that means is that this song does not give you several seconds to restart it while moving at a high rate of speed as Selo's Accelerando does; if a creature attacks you in any way you will instantly be moving at standard run speed.
[Crier] There are many places, especially in Kunark where you can get ridiculously high into the sky with this song coupling the speed with levitate. While this is an excellent means of staying out of agro range of the mobs far below, be cautious doing this when you have others in your party as a bit of lag or a wrong turn could cause them to plummet to their deaths.
If you are SoW'd (Spirit of the Wolf) this song will not take hold.  You will need to cancel SoW before zooming off.
The levitate portion of this song will override the lev components of spells like DMF, caster levitate, etc..  but will not remove the icon .  This can cause party members to think they are lev'd and lead to very bad things (like a 10k trip to their bind point).
