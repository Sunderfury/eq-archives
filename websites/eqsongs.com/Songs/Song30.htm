FULFIL's CURTAILING CHANT

Level: 30

Effect: -Magic Resistance, Damage over Time (magic based)

Skill: Percussion

Target: Single

Location: Freeport, Qeynos

Does not stack with: N/A

Taunt: High

Quantified: Played: Integer ((Lvl-29)/2) + 19 [Stormtower]

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song has a small (greater with a drum) dot effect and lowers magic resistance. Curtailing Chant checks against the target's Magic Resistance for success.

Strategies:

Used with a drum this song does significant damage and a sizable magic resist debuff.  All the casting classes have spells that go against magic resistance.
This song will stack with both Tuyen's Chant of Flame and Tuyen's Chant of Frost.  Allowing you (with a drum) to do damage rivaling melee classes while helping the casters hit harder.
