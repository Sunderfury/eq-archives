CANTATA OF SOOTHING

Level: 34

Effect: restores HP, Mana, and fatigue

Skill: String (required)

Target: Party

Location: Velious, can be purchased and also drops from mobs.

Does not stack with: Hymn of Restoration, Cantata of Replenishment

Quantified: N/A

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song is the light version of the level 55 song Cantata of Replenishment.  The mana regeneration is less then Cassindra's Chorus of Clarity and the HP regeneration is less then Hymn of Restoration. 

Strategies:

Cautions:

This song will block enchanter mana restoration spells (breeze, clarity, etc..) from taking hold, but will stack with them after they are taken effect on target.
