SELO's CONSONANT CHAIN

Level: 23

Effect: Slows down target's movement rate and attack speed(-31%)

Skill: Singing

Target: Single

Location: High Hold

Does not stack with: Attack speed debuff does not stack with Melodic Binding's attack speed debuff

Taunt: High

Quantified:
Attack speed: -31%
Delay change: +45%

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Ayonae Ro server.

Comments: This song will allow you to snare runners, a very useful thing, and also slows attack speed of target. One big advantage of this song is that it can be 'cast' while moving, unlike casters that must stand still to cast their snare/root spell. This can be a life saver in dungeons. 

Strategies:

This song can also be used to break root on yourself, you will move slowly which is better then not at all.

Cautions:

WARNING: This song was recently nerfed.  In the past when sung by level 38+ bards it would stop fleeing mobs at 50% health.  This is no longer true. 
[Harmonic] Constant Chains no longer breaks the Shaman Drowsy line or the Enchanter slowdowns.
