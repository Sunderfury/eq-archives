SONG of DAWN

Level: 53

Effect: Evade effect

Skill: Wind

Target: Single

Location: Firiona Vie

Does not stack with: n/a

Taunt: Very low

Quantified: ?

Analysis by Rokenn Swiftsong, Karana server

Comments: This song has an effect similar to the weapon proc "symphonic harmony". This effect makes the monster hate you less and someone else more similar to evade skill of our rogue brethren. Not as powerful as mem blur line of spells that enchanters use, but a nice tool for controlling aggro none the less.

Strategies:

Useful on plane raids when that Scorn Banshees wants to give you some sweet-lovin.  A pulse or two of this will have them looking for a new mate ;)
Could also be very useful on large target mobs that enjoy Death Touching bards.  Try weaving this in every other weave round to stay alive and buff your party. 

Cautions:

Using this song on a non-aggro mob will cause it to attack.
[Mackentil] For some reason my tanks in my group didn't immediately start attacking my former pet. I used this song and the mob immediately jumped on the cleric that was healing me. The cleric took quite a beating before we could get the mob off.
