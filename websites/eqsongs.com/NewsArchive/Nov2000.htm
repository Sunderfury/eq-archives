Archived News November 2000

11/30 7:30am - Updated Bard Song Reference Sheet, Bard Song Reference Sheet, and Shield of Songs with information on our new level 49 song.

 

11/27 7:00am - Kaearia recently became the first bard on the Karana server to complete the Singing Short Sword and sent me a nearly complete list of it's effect compared to store bought and planes/dragon level instruments.

 

11/16 10:20am - Updated Jonthan's Provocation, Jonthan's Inspiration, and Bard Song Reference Sheet.

 

11/07 11:30am - I would like to apologize for the recent lack of updates.  Between  work and some recent personal life upheavals I have not had the time or inclination to update the site much.  Things are settling down a bit now and updates should start appearing on a more regular basis.  

Anyway on to the news!  
As many of you now Absor posted on the WhinePlay forum that the single target mez songs are being fixed (Crission's Pixie Strike and Song of Twilight).  Specifically Pixie Strike is supposed to have the secondary resist lowered and Twilight will have it removed entirely with an extend duration.  I have read some posts and emails stating that none of the mez songs are giving any secondary resist messages, I have not had a chance to check this myself.  Not sure if this is a bug or they stealth patched it as the changes are not supposed to go in till the next patch (the DX 7 patch).

The other big change announced in Absor's post was the changes for the 2 upper level Jonthan's songs (Jonthan's Provocation and Jonthan's Inspiration).  I had grave misgivings about these changes personally as I have found them useful in certain cases.  But at the Las Vegas Event I was able to chat with a couple of the Live Team members about it.  The changes to these songs are going to be a nice power up to the songs, the goal they told me was that they would allow a bard using them to effectively tank while doing so.  It will be interesting to see how they are going to work.

At the Houston EQFest 2000 GZ had the following to say:
GZ: "Look for some VERY interesting things to be happening to Lullaby soon"
GZ: "We have been thinking about bard mez, we didn't want them to infringe on the Enchanter role, as that is what Enchanters do best, BUT we don't think that giving bards some mez abilities will hurt"
If anyone has more details on this please let me know.

Lastly Singing Short Sword is going to get a nice upgrade it seems.  Currently on the Test Server I am told the proc now has a much larger range and when the SSS is in the primary hand it will boost songs like it was a store bought instrument of the appropriate type.  This certainly makes the Sword worth getting.

All in all, I am very happy with the upcoming changes to the Bard class.  These are the best things to happen since they fixed the Chant series to work correctly with drums.

Great work Verant!
