Zoneに入り、右壁沿いを行くとClan Runnyeyeへの
入り口がありますが、さらに右壁沿いに行くと、
1人のDwarfが立っています。

You say, 'hail Diggs Duggun'
Diggs Duggun says
''ail, dere! Ain'tcha a sight fer me sore eyes! I knows dat we're strangers an' all, but might I ask a [boon] of ye?

You say, 'What is boon?'
Diggs Duggun says
'Well, me pickaxe is blunted awfully bad and me ale supplies are dangerously low. If'n ye would be kind enough t' get me a new pick and a couple o' bottles o' dwarven ale, I would be f'rever in yer debt.

どうやら
a new pickとa couple of bottles of dwarven ale
が欲しいようです。

骨をもらいました。
どうやら聖騎士の遺体のようです。
Valeronが興味を示すかも知れないとのことですが、
もちろん彼はFreeportのPALギルドマスターです。
Freeportまで行きましょう。
You say, 'Who is Diggs Duggun?'
Valeron Dushire says
'According to this crest on the tunic, you have recovered one of the bones of a lost Paladin [Hero].

You say, 'What is Hero?'
するとValeronが伝説の騎士について語り出します。
Valeron Dushire says
'His name has been long lost but his legend lives on in every young knight who aspires to continue his fight. He attempted to quell an evil that resided within Kithicor, perverting nature itself. Much to his relief, it was an earthly foe he fought against and not a denizen summoned from the planes of evil. Then, suddenly, he was surrounded by dozens of dark elves. He was captured and taken to the Dead to be experimented upon and had his soul twisted by vile necromantic magics. Then the Tier'Dal cast his remains to the winds. Where they landed, no one knew, until now. You have found one of his bones. I hereby charge you with a Holy Quest to bring this Heros bones back for a burial with honors. Do you [accept], knight?

Heros bonesを取り戻して欲しいとのことなので、
これを受けましょう。
You say, 'I accept'
Valeron Dushire says
'Good! I knew you would. It is one of the highest honors one can receive. Your quest will not be easy for I know not where his other bones lay. A spy of ours within Neriak was able to surmise that six of his bones along with his sword and shield were scattered throughout Norrath. You must search far and wide to find the remaining pieces, as you already have one.. Do not give up. It is now your sacred duty. Once you have retrieved all eight pieces, come back to me and inform me that you [have all the bones].

探し出すものは
six of his bones along with his sword and shield
