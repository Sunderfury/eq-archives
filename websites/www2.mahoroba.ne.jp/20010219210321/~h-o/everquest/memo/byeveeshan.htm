We would like to announce the impending launch of the two new split-servers Saryrn and The Seventh Hammer! From 6:00pm to 10:00pm PST on Saturday April 22nd, players on Bristlebane, Veeshan, Bertoxxulous, and Xegony who execute the /movelog yes command will be moved to the new servers. We will accept the first 2000 players from Bristlebane and Veeshan who will be moved to Saryrn, and the first 2000 players from Bertoxxulous and Xegony who will be moved to The Seventh Hammer. Both new servers will be available shortly!!! Please be aware that your characters will not be moved off of your current server until the new servers are ready.
