Kael Drakkal PAL専用鎧 Shining Armor
KaelのBankを南にずっと行くとKingの部屋がありますが、そこの裏手にQuest Roomがあります。
■注意!!■
KaelのFactionは複数あるので、Zone近くがNonKoSでも奥に行くと襲われることもあります。必ずConsiderしながら進んでください。
巨人がいっぱい並んでいますが、その一角にBarlek Stonefistがいます。
Barlek Stonefist regards you as an ally -- You could probably win this fight.
You say, 'Hail, Barlek Stonefist'
Barlek Stonefist says
'Greetings to you.
I seek the service of devout knights who have distinguished themselves.
Are you a paladin?
If not, then be gone.

You say, 'I am Paladin!'
Barlek Stonefist says
'A paladin of your order has not made it this far for quite some time.
Well, I wonder what it is you seek here.
Do you seek heroic and chivalrous deeds to distinguish yourself from your brethren?
Do you need accoutrements to aid you in your battles perhaps?
You are in luck, knight for I have both for you.
Do you wish to partake of my quests?

You say, 'I wish to partake of your quest'
Barlek Stonefist says
'Excellent.
By serving my cause, you in turn will further your own.
I require certain components to aid me and if you bring them to me, I shall reward you for your honorable service.
You may choose from among these items, a helm, a breastplate, armplates, bracers, gauntlets, greaves, and boots.

You say, 'What is helm?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'All that I require is an ancient tarnished plate helmet and three pieces of crushed coral.
This should be a small task for one such as you.
Go now and I shall await your return.

You say, 'What is breastplate?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'As the resolve of your faith shall protect you, so shall this breastplate.
All that is required is an ancient tarnished breastplate, and three flawless diamonds.
Do this quickly so that you may return to your place in the field of battle.

You say, 'What is armplates?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'So, a pair of armplates, is what you require?
Well, I require an ancient tarnished plate vambraces as well as three flawed emeralds before you will receive your prize.

You say, 'What is bracers?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'Bracers for the mighty?
Retrieve these components and I shall forge the item for you.
Bring me an ancient tarnished plate bracelet as well as three crushed flame emeralds.

You say, 'What is gauntlets?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'Mighty gauntlets to smite your foes for the glory of the greater good.
It is no easy task but I shall require a pair of an ancient tarnished plate gauntlets and three crushed topaz.

You say, 'What is greaves?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'Sturdy are the pillars that support the temple.
I shall make you as strong with these leggings.
Gather three flawed sapphires of the sea and a set of ancient tarnished plate greaves.

You say, 'What is boots?'
Barlek Stonefist says
'A pair of boots you shall have once you have gotten a pair of an ancient tarnished plate boots and a set of three crushed pieces of black marble.

それぞれAncient Tarnished Plateというのと3つの宝石を渡すことで鎧をForgeしてくれるようです。
どこにあるんだろう。
Ancient Tarnished PlateはTemple of VeeshanのMobを倒すと稀に持っています。
とは言っても1匹当りがかなり強いので、ハイレベルの複数Partyが必要です。
WurmやDrakeが持っているようです。
Plate（長いのでAncient Tarnished〜を略します）はLoreなので
Bracerについては、1個Bracerを完成させてから次のPlateを取ると、2個揃います。
GemはSoV全域（特にダンジョン系）で普通のMobが落とすようです。
必要なのは
Helm -------- Crushed Coral
Breastplate - Flawless Diamond
Armplates --- Flawed Emerald
Bracer ------ Crushed Flame Emerald
Gauntlets --- Crushed Topaz
Greaves ----- Flawed Sea Sapphire
Boots ------- Crushed Black Marble
それぞれ3つずつ集めましょう。
Bracerは2個必要だと思うので、合計6つのGemが必要になりますね。
材料が揃ったら鎧を作ってもらいに行きましょう。
