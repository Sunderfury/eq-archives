Hunt the reef sharks
Knight of Truth、Priest of MarrのFaction上げQuestです。
Freeport MilitiaのFactionは下がります。
修正が入り、現在ではFaction上がらなくなりました。（4/1修正）
North FreeportにあるHall of Truthの2階にTheron Roliusがいます。
You say, 'hail Theron Rolius'
Theron Rolius says,
'Greetings Loherangrin.
To enter these grounds is to proclaim your faith in the Truthbringer.
In this city you shall find no greater ally than the Knights of Truth.
We urge all knights and clerics who oppose the hand of the Freeport Militia to [ join the crusade ].

You say, 'I join the crusade'
Theron Rolius says,
'Then take arms against the Freeport Militia.
They serve no one save Sir Lucan, the fallen knight.
From this day forth I put a bounty upon all Militia members.
For every Bashed Milita Helm, a reward!!
If you are not prepared to battle the Militia just yet, then you may [ assist in other areas ].

You say, 'I assist in other areas'
Theron Rolius says,
'I have need of one such as you.
I have been awaiting a message.
I will need a young acolyte to [ retrieve the message ] for me or there is also some [ fishing duties ] I wish to relegate.

You say, 'What is fishing duties?'
Theron Rolius says,
'I have been creating shields for the crusade.
Right now I work on the Sharkskin Shield for the Knights.
I require two shark skins. Unfortunately most sharks are too dangerous for the squires.
I have begun using [ Reef Shark ] skins.
Will you [ hunt the reef sharks ]?

You say, 'I hunt the reef sharks'
Theron Rolius says,
'I thought I spied the shoulders of a swimmer upon you.
Take this large sack.
Travel to the Ocean of Tears.
There are numerous Reef Sharks there.
You shall require no less than two shark skins.
When the full sack is combined and returned to me, I shall reward you.
Sack of Sharkskinsを貰いました。
サメを退治して、Shark Skinを入れれば良いようです。
サメくんが怖い人はEast Freeportの港のお店で売ってるので買っても良いです。

'Fantastic work my young knight.
Here is a small token of the my appreciation.
I would offer you a Sharkskin Shield, but I have made only a few and the paladins are testing them.
