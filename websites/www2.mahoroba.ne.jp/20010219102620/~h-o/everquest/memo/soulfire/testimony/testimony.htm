Testimony(証明書)
Hall of Truthへようこそ。Truthのメンバーになることをお望みか？
もしあなたがそれを望むならテストを受けてください。
よろしい。では幾つか簡単な仕事を依頼するので
それを達成してほしい。
もしあなたが勇敢で寛大な行いをすれば
「証し」を得られるでしょう。
達成の暁には騎士の証しとして「Testimony」を授けます。
- Sir Merko Quetalis -
Hall　of Truthの外堀のほとりに彼はいます。

You say, 'hail Merko Quetalis. '
Merko Quetalis says
'Hail good citizen. You have entered the Hall of Truth. What is it that you seek? Many citizens come to request aid in dealing with the local rogues or the oppresion of our sworn enemy, the Freeport Militia. A few valiant ones have been [summoned to the Hall of Truth]. '

You say, 'I summoned to the Hall of Truth. '
Merko Quetalis says
'You have been summoned? You do not have the look of nobility. You must be a [squire]. There are many squires which have been summoned to our Hall. Not all pass the [Test of Truth]. '
テストをパスせよとな？
望むところだ。
勇気と寛大さについて
証言を得ないとだめらしい。		 You say, 'What is Test of Truth. '
Merko Quetalis says
'Only when a squire is ready may he tempt his fate, all he need do is hand the tokens of Bravery and Generousity to me. If the squire were to survive his ordeal, then he or she shall be entered into the order of the Knights of Truth. The squire will be given the Testimony of Truth and become a respected knight. '

You say, 'I am a squire. '
Merko Quetalis says
'Then I welcome you Squire Akakage. Being a squire is the first step to becoming a true knight of the Hall of Truth. Remember to always protect and serve the meek. I have a [small task] which suits a squire perfectly. '
You say, 'What is small task. '
Merko Quetalis says
'Venture to the Commonlands and seek out our noble friend Altunic Jartin. He lives and works out of his home. Hand him this note. '

CommonにいるAltunic Jartinに
この手紙を持っていけばいいらしい。
では出発。
