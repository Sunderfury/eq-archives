Ronium

Mountain Ratheにて、各部位のRo ArmorのQuestをこなすと、Lord Searfireに会うように言われます。
彼がRo Armorの材料の1つ、Roniumの作り方を知っているようです。
You say, 'who is Lord Searfire?'
Thomas says
'Lord Searfire is a great Forge Master.
He was called forth to the land of fire lakes.
It is said that he now toils in the name of Solusek Ro within a hidden temple within that land.
It is he and he alone who knows how to obtain [ Ronium ], a needed component for forging the Sacred Armor of Ro.
まずはTemple of Solusek Ro（通称Sol C）に行きます。
そこでLord Searfireを探します。(LOC 500, 80)
You say, 'hail Lord Searfire'
Lord Searfire says
'Greetings.
May the glory of Solusek Ro run within your veins.
I am the forge master of this temple.
Called to service by the Burning Prince.
Should you ever need my service and find me gone, have no fear, I shall return.
I must venture to other realms in service of the temple.
I shall always return, this is my eternal home.

You say, 'What is armor of ro?'
Lord Searfire says
'So you wish to know more of the Armor of Ro?
We have kept it's secret for a long time.
It can only be worn by paladins of high esteem.
They must be crafted by the one who wears it.
Ro wishes his mighty paladins to also be adept in blacksmithing.
They may take to the forge, a mold of the Ro Armor and a bar of the alloy [ Ronium ].
Then you may forge the armor of Ro.

You say, 'What is Ronium?'
Lord Searfire says
'You seek Ronium.
It is a fine blend of metals.
I alone hold the secret of it's formula.
I and I alone can offer it to those who serve this Temple.
Do you wish a [ bar of the alloy ]?

You say, 'I wish a bar of the alloy'
Lord Searfire says
'Take this Sol Cauldron.
Within it shall be combined elements of this world.
You will add to it, Melatite, Two Bars of Enchanted Platinum, Mistmoore Granite, the Sands of Ro and Soil of Underfoot.
If you be a master blacksmith then the elements shall combine to make the enchanted metal I have named, Ronium.
Roniumを作るための炉、
[ Sol Cauldron ]を得ます。
炉をもらう為にはTemple of Solusek RoのFactionがKindlyAmiably以上必要です。
(必要Factionの引き下げが行われているようです。Redaさん投稿ありがとうございました。)
各地にいるShadowmanを倒すとFactionが上がります。

コンテナになっており、その中にアイテムを入れてCOMBINEするようだ。
集めるアイテムは以下の通り。
  1. Melatite
  2. Two Bars of Enchanted Platinum
  3. Mistmoore Granite
  4. the Sands of Ro
  5. Soil of Underfoot
では、言われた通り材料を集めるとしましょうか。
Melatite:
Sol AのGnomeが作ったClockWorkGnome（CWG Model MB、CWG Model MC）を倒すと取れます。
倒すとGnome(Ak`anon)のFactionが落ちるので、KoSになる覚悟が必要です。
ですから、通常はAuctionで密売業者から買うのが良いでしょう（＾＾；
Two Bars of Enchanted Platinum:
ENCに頼みましょう。
知り合いのENCに頼めばすぐ作ってくれるはずです。
材料費は払ってね（＾＾
Twoということなので、2個必要です。
Mistmoore Granite:
Castle Mistmooreの城エリアにいるGargoyleから手に入ります。
(Zoneして来て最初の庭にいるGargoyleはDropしません。)
Sands of Ro:
OasisやSouth RoにいるSand Giantから手に入ります。
Soil of Underfoot:
North KaladimのCLRギルドのQuestで手に入ります。
Clerics of UnderfootのFactionがHigh Amiably以上必要です。
(奥のPALギルドでBone Chipを渡すQuestをするとFactionが上がります。)

CLRギルドのPriestess Ghaleaに話し掛けます。
You say, 'hail Priestess Ghalea'
Priestess Ghalea says
'Welcome to the Church of Underfoot.
Please open your soul to the greatness of Brell Serilis.
May he guide you in all your future endeavors.
And may your soles long for the [ Soil of Underfoot ].

You say, 'What is Soil of Underfoot?'
Priestess Ghalea says
'I can trust you with the Soil of Underfoot, but first you must obtain four portions of Fairy Dust.
Return them to me and I shall mix it and pray over it, then I shall give you a pouch of Soil of Underfoot.
Fairy Dust 4つを渡すと貰えるようです。。
