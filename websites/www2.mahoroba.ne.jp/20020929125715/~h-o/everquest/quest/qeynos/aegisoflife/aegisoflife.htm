2Fにいる場合もあります。

Capt Linarius looks upon you warmly -- he appears to be quite formidable.
You say, 'hail Capt Linarius'
Capt Linarius says
'Hail traveler.
You are free to rest within these keep or within any of my towers along the roads to Qeynos.
My guards shall keep watch for you.
While you trod upon the roads be warned that there are [ local dangers ] as well as rumors of [ corrupt guards ].

You say, 'What is local dangers?'
Capt Linarius says
'My guards report daily to me of dangers within the plains.
As of late they tell me of rumors of [ Lord Grimrot ].

You say, 'Who is Lord Grimrot?'
Capt Linarius says
'My guards have heard rumors of this Lord Grimrot.
It is said that he is a powerful shadowknight who is raising an army of undead.
Such things should be reported to the Temple of Life.
Journey to Qeynos and speak with Camlend Serbold.
Tell him of Lord Grimrot.

You say, 'hail Camlend Serbold'
Camlend Serbold says
'I say, good to meet you Loherangrin.
I am Camlend Serbold.
Some call me Serbold the Great.
I am the leader of the righteous order of paladins which serves the Temple of Life. Together with the clerics of this temple, we defend the wills of the great [ Rodcet Nife ] and smite all who dare to oppose us.

You say, 'Who is Rodcet Nife?'
Camlend Serbold says
'Do you feel your heart beating within your chest!!?
It is Rodcet Nife, The Prime Healer, whose breath keeps it in perfect rhythm.
If you do not pray to his glory then I assure you that someday when your heart begins to slow you shall be crying out loud the name of Rodcet Nife.

You say, 'Who is Lord Grimrot?'
Camlend Serbold says
'You have heard of Lord Grimrot?
He is a shadowknight working with the Bloodsabers.
We have word he is trying to build an army of undead.
Only a powerful knight can rid the lands of Grimrot.
So, did you come here to [ defend life ]?

You say, 'I came here to defend life'
Priests of LifeのFactionがWarmly以上で無ければ、Royal Memberでは無いのでダメだと言われます。
もしFactionがWarmly以上あれば
Camlend Serbold says
'Then go to the Plains of Karana.
Somewhere there, Lord Grimrot is hatching his little plan for the Bloodsabers.
Bring me his evil scythe and his heart.
It is said that upon his death, his soul will be absorbed into this weapon of darkness.
Do this and be rewarded.
Lord Grimrotのevil scytheとheart
この2つを渡せばいいようです。
さて、Lord Grimrotは何処に？
彼はSouth Karanaに入ってすぐ近くにあるSkeleton Campに稀にPopします。

Repop時間は200分。
LiveタイプかUndeadタイプもしくはPH（Place Holder、外れPopのこと）の立ったままいるPutrid SkeletonがPOPします。
PHの場合、放っておくとPM7:00に一旦消え、PM9:00ごろにRepopします。
こちらはUndeadかPutrid Skeletonのどちらかです。
これ以降、PHを倒さない限り毎日（90分）でRepopすることになります。
2001/04/15
現在、仕様なのかバグなのか、Depop時間が不安定で消えないとの情報あり。
LiveタイプをPopさせるには殺して200分待たねばなりません。
しかしこちらはよくPopしますので大丈夫です。

彼には魔法が殆ど効きません。もちろんRootも。
LV50でもかなり手ごわい相手でしたが、PALならLHを使えば倒せました。

死体からはScytheが2本手に入りますが
必要なのはNODROPの方[ Pestilence Scythe ]です。

残りはHeartなんですが・・・・・・
Undeadタイプは本当にRare Popです。
以下2000/09/25更新
苦節半年、やっと会えました。
Liveタイプより弱いので楽勝でした。
こいつを倒して
[ Decaying Heart ]を得ます。
