Addition:
It is the same means though indication varies inside the parenthesis.
Ex.
Tsubaki chan = Tubaki tyan

It becomes a prolonged sound when "-" or "h" or repeat are put behind the Vowel.
Ex.
A- = Ah = Aa
Ya- = Yah = Yaa

It becomes a jumped sound and different means when repeat are put behind the Consonant.
Ex.
Itta means Went out.
Ita means was(were) there or a board.
Which is different.
