Oracle of K'Arnon(Ocean of Tearｓ)
Ocean of TearsのSister Islandより
南に泳いだところにある島にいるOracle of K'Arnon。
Oracle Robeの所持者ということで有名ですが、QuestNPCでもあります。
You say, 'Hail Oracle of K'Arnon.'
Oracle of K'Arnon says,
'Greetings Loherangrin, I am the Oracle of K'Arnon. Do you quest for [knowledge] yourself , or do you offer that which I [seek]?'

You say, 'What is knowledge?'
Oracle of K'Arnon says,
'I have lived long and have insight into many matters , yet I despise charity. To learn from me , you must first bring me that which I [seek].'

You say, 'What do you seek?'
Oracle of K'Arnon says,
'I seek knowledge , which is superior to all things. And no knowledge is greater than that which is contained within the [Tome of Ages].'

You say, 'What is Tome of Ages?'
Oracle of K'Arnon says,
'The Tome of Ages has been compiled over the millennia by the Scribes of [Dal]. It was divided into seven distinct books by the scribes to keep the knowledge from one such as me. That said , with aid , I shall unite these books and the Tome of Ages shall be mine.'

You say, 'What is Dal?'
Oracle of K'Arnon says,
'They were elves once , but now are Tier Dal , and evil as they are wise. Many tomes have they written , but none greater than the Tome of Ages.'

Tome of Agesが欲しいらしい。Scribes of Dalが編纂しているようだ。
Dalとは他のクエストでもよく出てくるようにDarkelf のことです。
ただし現在Innoruukの支配下にあるDarkelfはTeir'Dalと呼ばれます。
Dalはそれ以前の古エルフということになります。
