PAL専用鎧Armor of Ro
Mountain RatheのPALキャンプに彼がいます。
FeerottからRatheに入って最初の十字路を北に行き、その後北西に行った辺りです。
You say, 'hail Thomas'
Thomas says
'Welcome to our camp. We are paladins gathered from many lands. We have been called to this land by our deities. Ours is a crusade of righteousness. Have you happened upon us by chance or do you [seek the sacred molds]?

You say, 'I seek the sacred molds'
Thomas says
'Many of us carry the sacred molds of the holy paladin armor. We will release it to only those who have proven themselves to each of our deities. If you seek it then speak up!! Tell us which part you seek. I carry the secret of the [Helm of Ro]. The others carry vambraces, breastplates, bracers, gauntlets, greaves and boots of Ro.
Paladin用の鎧のMold（型）を持っているらしい。
この人はHelmの部分で、他の人がそれぞれの部分を保持しているようだ。
周りにいるHumanやDwarfにも声をかけてみよう。
