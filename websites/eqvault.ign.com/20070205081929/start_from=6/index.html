  SOE Pumpkin Carving Contest  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
Halloween will be here soon, and SOE is holding a real life pumpkin carving contest to start the festivities!

"Community Pumpkin Carving Challenge!

Get out your knives and spoons! It's time for a pumpkin massacre!

That's right! We're challenging you to carve up a pumpkin to make a jack-O-lantern!

Set yourself on high adventure to find a pumpkin. Then capture it and bring it home. When you have returned with your squashy treasure, get that pumpkin up on the chopping block and carve yourself a jack-o-lantern!

Then take a picture of your jack-O-lantern and send it into us at cdelzer@soe.sony.com.

Please remember to put "Halloween Pumpkin Challenge" in the subject header of your email. Make sure you indicate which category you wish your pumpkin to be entered into the challenge under. Categories are listed below.

And don't forget to include your forum handle!

We'll post the pictures between now and Halloween. There will be three categories of pumpkins from which challenge winners will be selected: Halloween Theme, EQ Theme, and General Theme. One person from each category will receive a special forum title and an in-game item for one character of their choice.

You can send us pictures of your jack-o-lantern's up until 12:00 PM CST on October 31st.

Trick or treat!"

Friday, 20 Oct, 2006


  'Red Threads' This Week  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
What's buzzing on the message boards? A "red thread" has a red folder icon because lots of people are responding to it.

Here are some "red thread" topics for this past week from the Veteran's Lounge:

"One Ranger, One Month, All 15 Epics".
Tnexus Fyrewolf, Hand of Ka, Prexus Server explains, "I started out on my quest at the very end of October and finished today with the final Berserker fight. The only one I couldn't do is Beastlord because it's flagged for Beastlords only. (Berserkers is too, but I could shroud.) So my bank now has all 15 doable epics rotting in the bank for no other purpose besides me doing them. Overall it cost me about 300k in buying select items and MQs (Slime Blood of Cazic Thule, Soulleech, and Ball of Everliving Golem accounted for 225k of that)." An amazing accomplishment!

"DemiPlane of Blood Instance Crashing" Players aren't too happy about getting to various boss mobs and having the whole thing boot them back out to Dreadspire. The player theory is it's the charming ability of the NPCs that is causing the problem, but that's just a theory. The devs are looking into it.

"RANT: Enchanter Class - RIP?"
Pros and cons about the usefulness of the enchanter in a group in today's game. Some say they aren't needed anymore because other classes are superior for the job the enchanter does and the content doesn't require enchanter skills; others defend their utility and say it's the group's fault if the enchanter isn't effective. You can decide for yourself!

"Drakkin and Armor Dye"
Kytherea posted that it's intentional that the Drakkin can't dye their armor, and nine pages later, people are still discussing armor dye in the game.

"Weapons in TSS [the Serpent's Spine] So Far"
Lots of pages of EQ Player discussions about their favorite topic, itemization and the worth of the loot in the new expansion. Plenty of comparison of weapons from various expansions and the merits of each.

You can also check out EverQuest Trivia,a long running game with over 263 questions so far! You can only ask yes or no questions to get the answer.

Wednesday, 18 Oct, 2006


  No Rollback Planned for Exploiters  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News
Rhagnell posted that there are no plans to rollback any characters due to the recent experience exploit. Kytherea added:

"Yes we are working on a solution to deal with the people who participated in the exploit only, not to punish everyone regardless of whether they were part of it or not.

We will NOT be making it public knowledge who participated though, this is just something we do not do. Please accept that we are working on a solution and will deal with those who participated."

Apparently, the exploit involved the turning in of bonechips to get AA experience. There was a huge surge in the markeplace in the sale of bonechips, which were selling upwards of 25pp. The word on "the street" is that people were gaining up to a thousand AA points using this exploit.

The community is fairly unanimous in asking for the exploiters to be punished in some way, since gaining AA through a cheat seems unfair to those who grind them out in regular play. For those who do take advantage of exploits to progress, it's a shame that you don't see it ruins the game for everyone, giving an unfair advantage to the exploiting players.

It remains to be seen what actions the SOE staff will take against the offenders.

Friday, 6 Oct, 2006


  Chris Lena on The Serpent's Spine  
Print News | Mail News | Category: Columns
Chris Lena has given RPG Vault a nice an insider evaluation of the Serpent's Spine. And who better to do an inside evaluation than the game producer?

Lena says:
"The number one thing we wanted to with The Serpent's Spine was to show everyone what EverQuest has become today. Our high-level players have been experiencing what I like to call modern EverQuest for quite a while. This is complex scripted events, destructible objects, inter-player tactics and modern day graphics that rival all other MMOs on the market today. So, we decided to create a brand new player race - and starting city - and made this modern EverQuest content for every level in the game. Not only did we provide new cutting-edge high-end content, but we now have a great content path for any new or returning players.

Also, when we set out to do the content, we focused a lot of strong story-driven quest arcs. This is not kill, loot and repeat. We have brought a lot of our raid encounter knowledge down to group, mid-level "mini raids". We have also limited the amount instancing of content to just those encounters that are necessary for some sort of progression to encourage the old school social-based adventuring.

The bottom line is that we wanted to make an expansion that would have our current players saying to their friends "If you have ever thought about trying EQ, you should pick up The Serpent's Spine." Only the players can tell us if we succeeded in that or not."

There's lots more so be sure to follow the link above. You can read many of these same themes in my interview with dev Vahlar as well.

-- Kwill @ 2:16PM Fri 06 Oct 2006


Server Guild Listings  [ Comment ]
Print News | Mail News | Category: Columns
Thank you to all my readers who have written in asking to have their guilds listed on the server guild lists!

I love to get emails from readers and of course I answer them. Please feel free to submit your guild to me via email. If you can send the URL and some info about your guild, that's really helpful.

The work continues on updating these lists -- I hope people find them useful. They take a while because I go to each guild's website and look around for recruiting information. Since the lists haven't really been updated for about a year, it's interesting to see who is still around and who isn't. But I am glad to see new guilds popping up and better yet, that some recruiting is closed -- that should mean the server populations are healthy.

Even if you aren't looking for a guild, these lists can give you a nice idea of what everyone is doing around your server. If you are new to the game, these lists can help you find a guild that is recruiting, especially the family guilds that welcome new players. Even if you aren't at the uber level, the requirements for the upper level guilds are a great resource for learning what AAs, spells and gear you need for the end game.

Looking for a change? Lots of guilds accept cross-server applications. You can always pack up and move to another "town" (server) so to speak!

More generally, there's lots coming up on EQVault as far as content this fall -- there will be another EQ developer interview coming soon, and I have two new Kwill's Quill type articles in the works right now.

If there's something you would like to see, please write and let me know. Thanks for reading!

Kwill
