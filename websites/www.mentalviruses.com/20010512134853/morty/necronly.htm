Necromancer Only
Instruments of darkness

There are more items in the world reserved specifically for the Necromancer than there used to be, and they're well worth stacking up a pile of dead bodies to pick up. A determined Necromancer could eventually acquire all of these things and equip them simultaneously, for a total effect of +11 intelligence, +4 agility, -2 charisma, +20 health, +25 mana, +4 fire resistance, +4 cold resistance, -10 disease resistance, and -10 poison resistance. I've excluded otherworldly items from the list here - if you're picking up things like that, you don't need me telling you how good it is to be a Necromancer.

Necromantic weapons are slow-hitting things, but offer useful magics.

Dagger of Marnek
3 damage, 30 delay, Lore

The dark power invested in this dagger grants the wielder a boon of +3 intelligence and +15 mana. This dagger is intended for ritual, not combat - it's slow, hits for little damage, and does not hit for magical damage. However, it can be usefully kept on hand by putting it where you'd place a throwing dagger. Just be careful not to throw it, of course.

Staff of Undead Legions
20 damage, 47 delay, Magic

This evil artifact allows the wielder to Beguile Undead, or just whack them upside the head for a great deal of damage. Only the greatest of Necromancers will be able to acquire one.

Armor that is reserved for the Necromancer tends to be... special.

Dark Circlet
AC 4, Magic, Lore, No Trade

This evil piece of work sits nicely on a gnome's head (although other Necromancers can also make it fit). The circlet grants the wearer a boon of +4 intelligence and +10 health, but imposes a penalty of -2 charisma.

Festering Cloak
AC 5, Magic

Only a Necromancer would actually wear this - convenient, that. The wearer is granted a boon of +10 health and +10 mana, but suffers a penalty of -10 disease resistance and -10 poison resistance. Embrace the gifts of Bertoxxulous.

Robe of Enshroudment
AC 9, Magic

It's just so black, it's perfect. Beyond the blackness, it grants the wearer a boon of +4 intelligence, +4 agility, +4 fire resistance, and +4 cold resistance. The important thing, though, is that it's black.
