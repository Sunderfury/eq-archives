The Eleventh Circle of the Dark Arts
for Necromancers of 44 Levels

Arch Shielding
being an Abjuration of 200 mana

Provides the Necromancer with increased maximum health, an armor class bonus, and increased Magic Resistance.

Asystole
being an Alteration of 210 mana

Lowers the target's strength, reduces their armor class, and drains their health over time.

Corpal Empathy
being an Alteration of 315 mana

Drains a great amount of health from the Necromancer and grants it to the target.

Covetous Subversion
being an Alteration of 300 mana

Grants the target a good amount of mana.

Crackling Bones
being a Conjuration of 450 mana requiring 2? bone chips

Summons an undead skeleton to do the Necromancer's bidding.

Dead Man Floating
being an Abjuration of 200 mana

Allows the target to levitate, breath underwater, see things that are invisible, and greatly resist poison.

Diamondskin
being an Abjuration of 336 mana

Absorbs damage before it can affect the Necromancer until its limit is reached.

Ignite Bones
being an Evocation of 200 mana

Hits the target for a great amount of damage and turns them into a skeleton.

Pact of Shadow
being an Alteration of 10 mana

Drains a good amount of health from the Necromancer over a short time and grants it to the target.
