How to Devour Your Enemies
A recipe book for the traveling Necromancer

How many times have you been wandering through the Plains of Karana, starving to death? More than you can count? Put the flourishing touch on your necromantic arts by feasting on the fallen! Find the dead bits that you have on hand in the list below, choose your favorite recipe, put the ingredients in a nearby oven or on your handy spit, and get ready for your gourmet meal. Welcome to the Norrath Buffet.
