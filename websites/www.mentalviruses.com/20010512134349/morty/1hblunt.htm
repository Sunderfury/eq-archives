One-Handed Blunt
Giving things a good whack

For the Necromancer, the only usable one-handed blunt weapon is the staff. In theory, that's not so bad since there are a number of interesting staves that the Necromancer can use, and there are also several items that can be held in the other hand to make a one-handed weapon worth considering. A good staff deals out about the same damage as a good dagger in any given combat, and the greater delay may mean fewer spell interruptions due to your own attacks. On the down side, finding a magical staff can be a chore.

The mundane staff is, of course, far more common than the more interesting variations.

Cracked Staff
5 damage, 32 delay

Probably your first one-handed blunt weapon, found on monsters all over Norrath. Get one early and use it regularly if you plan on using one of the magical ones later.

Staff
5 damage, 28 delay

The basic staff. I've never seen one used, to be honest.

Magical staves are better, harder to find, and more expensive, but it's usually worth the trouble.

Staff of Fire
5 damage, 25 delay, Magic

It burns. Specifically, it will sometimes hit a melee target with a Burst of Fire. It also grants the wielder a boon of +5 fire resistance.

Orb of Tishan
7 damage, 25 delay, Magic

Someone powerful put a lot of energy into this thing. The entrapped forces grant the wielder a boon of +9 strength, +9 stamina, and +35 mana. As if that weren't enough, the poor fool you're attacking can also be hit with Tashania, lowering their magic resistance. It also looks much better than a plain stick.

Staff of Writhing
9 damage, 33 delay, Magic

Evil on a stick. In addition to dealing out a fair amount of melee damage, this will also occasionally fire off Chaotic Feedback for extra brain-frying power. The skull on the end of the staff scares people, though, so the wielder suffers a penalty of -9 charisma. Ha! Let them cower in fear. This is the staff of choice for the discriminating Necromancer.

Crystal staves are similar to the basic staff and throw in a magical punch for good measure. They also look more interesting than your garden-variety staff, which adds something to their desirability.

Fire Crystal Staff
5 damage, 28 delay, Magic, Lore

During melee, this staff will sometimes hit the target with Shock of Fire. Never turn down the chance to burn your enemies.

Ice Crystal Staff
5 damage, 28 delay, Magic, Lore

Every now and then, this staff will cast Shock of Frost on the melee target. Since most folks don't expect a Necromancer to be handing out damage from cold, the surprise value alone makes it worthwhile.

Slime Crystal Staff
5 damage, 28 delay, Magic, Lore

This staff will occasionally cast Sicken on the melee target. Bertoxxulous would be proud. Just because of that, I rank this as the second-best Necromancer weapon in this category.

Water Crystal Staff
5 damage, 28 delay, Magic, Lore

During melee, this staff will sometimes hit the target with Shallow Breath, lowering their strength and agility and doing damage over time. Nice, but... really, it sounds like the kind of weapon some weak-willed bright-eyed high elf flower child would use. Definitely not the first choice for any self-respecting minion of death, but it will do in a pinch.
