The Eighth Circle of the Dark Arts
for Necromancers of 29 Levels

Blood Boil
being an Alteration of 150 mana

Damages the target and drains their health over time.

A welcome upgrade indeed, this does much more damage than Heat Blood.

Dismiss Undead
being an Evocation of 90 mana

Hits the target undead for a good amount of damage, usually doing some damage even if resisted.

It's about as effective as Shock of Poison, for only a little less mana. You'll be tempted to ignore it since it only works on the undead, but remember to keep that evocation skill up.

Dooming Darkness
being a Conjuration of 120 mana

Slows the target's movement and drains their health over time.

Another very welcome and long overdue upgrade, doing significantly more damage than Engulfing Darkness. More importantly, it slows the target down a lot more - it's about equal to Snare.

Panic the Dead
being an Alteration of 125 mana

Causes the target undead to flee.

Renew Bones
being an Alteration of 125 mana

Heals the pet of the Necromancer.

Spirit Tap
being an Alteration of 144 mana

Drains a good amount of health from the target and grants it to the Necromancer.

About twice as effective as Siphon Life, for about twice the mana. When you need it, nothing else will do.

Summon Dead
being a Conjuration of 290 mana requiring 2 bone chips

Summons an undead skeleton to do the Necromancer's bidding.

This is the first pet that will wield two weapons. It needs weapons to do it though, so be sure to have some handy. I've seen the scroll for sale at an out-of-the-way vendor that had a number of hard-to-find spells. A troll or ogre should also be able to pick it up for you.

Vampiric Curse
being an Alteration of 148 mana

Drains a good amount of health from the target over time and grants it to the Necromancer.

Ahhh, the upgrade to Leach. This will eventually drain significantly more life from your foe than Spirit Tap, and easily offsets Allure of Death. It also lets the Necromancer step into battle for a bit and not worry too much. An absolute must.

Word of Spirits
being an Evocation of 150 mana

Hits enemies in the vicinity of the Necromancer for a fair amount of damage.

"Death is only the beginning."
