Lost Toys
A collection of misfits

Tinkering being the mysterious thing that it is, legends have naturally grown concerning what could be built, if only we knew how. I've listed a few of those things here for general amusement.

By all the gods, if you run across one of these things anywhere in Norrath, or stumble upon the secret to creating one, let me know! Gnomes everywhere will sing your praises.

Infravision Goggles
Legendary

This is probably the most popular of the legendary items, as it would have charges granting the wearer infravision. Quite a few people - well, quite a few humans anyway - would dearly love to have one of these little contraptions and would pay a handsome price for it.

Gnomish Clockworks I, IV, and XI
Legendary

Rumors about clockwork pets have been around for a long, long time. No one has ever seen a tinkered pet, and Those Who Should Know have said that it will never happen. Too bad. The idea of a clockwork rat pet amuses me.

Jet Pack
Legendary

This unlikely contraption would, according to legend, allow the wearer to float freely in the air. Pretty unlikely, if you ask me, since some of our tinkered items can get pretty heavy. It would presumably be black.

Gnomish Tinkered Pistol
Legendary

I've seen a picture, but I don't believe it. Still... one can dream. Rumor makes it a range weapon with 2 shots for some pretty hefty damage.
