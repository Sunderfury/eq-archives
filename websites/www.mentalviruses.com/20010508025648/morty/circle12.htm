The Twelfth Circle of the Dark Arts
for Necromancers of 49 Levels

Banish Undead
being an Evocation of 200 mana

Hits the target undead for a great amount of damage, usually doing some damage even if resisted.

Bond of Death
being an Alteration of 360 mana

Drains a great amount of health from the target over time and grants it to the Necromancer.

Cajole Undead
being a Conjuration of 245 mana

Charms the target undead to do the Necromancer's bidding.

Cascading Darkness
being a Conjuration of 300 mana

Greatly slows the target's movement and drains their health over time.

Drain Soul
being an Alteration of 225 mana

Drains a great amount of health from the target and grants it to the Necromancer.

Ignite Blood
being an Alteration of 250 mana

Damages the target and drains their health over time.

Invoke Death
being a Conjuration of 490 mana requiring 2 bone chips

Summons an undead skeleton to do the Necromancer's bidding.

Lich
being an Alteration of 5 mana

Turns the Necromancer into a skeleton, draining health over time and increasing mana regeneration.
