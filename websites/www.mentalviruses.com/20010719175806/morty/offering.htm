Inappropriate Offerings
Meals with a message

So you've got a backpack full of tasty delights that you've whipped up after the battle, and you're not hungry. Not to worry! Take a look at your companions and hand out some treats. They may or may not thank you, but at least they'll remember you.

Ogres

I've never met an ogre that didn't love a Rat Sandwich, or anything else with rat in it - Beer Braised Rat, Rat Kabobs, and Rat Ear Pie are all big hits. If you really want to impress them, though, serve up some Hot n' Spicy Toelings as appetizers.

Trolls

Trolls tend to eat a lot like ogres, and also have a certain fondness for prepared rat dishes. Most trolls grew up with Pickled Froglok, too, so being able to share some with them is like a trip down memory lane.

Paladins

If you really must offer food to a Paladin rather than letting them starve, let them feast on their own fallen. What better way to say "bite me" than by handing over some Dwarf Chops or Elven Veal as appropriate? Your best option, though, is to hit up a vendor and get yourself a nice supply of Paladin Pickles for those special occasions. Snack on them yourself, and share them when you can.

Druids

Any druids you run into aren't going to be asking for food, since they'll eat any weird shrub they trip over. As a result, you'll need to take it upon yourself to offer them things. I usually start with a Wolf Sandwich, and then move on to Wolf Steaks. They usually refuse. Naturally, I keep going, moving on to Bear Steaks and Beer Braised Bear. It's important to be persistent in these things.

Necromancers

Necromancers eat Druids. Set yourselves down to a picnic of Smoked Wood Elf, pull out the Hot n' Spicy Toelings that you hid from the ogres, and chow down. Grab some Bixie Crunchies for desert. Enjoy living at the top of the food chain.
