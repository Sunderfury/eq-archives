Picturesque Evil
Images of Norrath

After I returned from my preliminary expedition to Kunark, I was invited by "the Dead" of Neriak to report on my findings. Aside from some logistical problems during the presentation, my information was well-received, and several high-placed Teir'Dal began making their travel plans. I look forward to meeting my brothers and sisters of darkness in the new lands.
