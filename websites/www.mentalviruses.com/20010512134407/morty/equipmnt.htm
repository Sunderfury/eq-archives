Equipment
Useful things in general

If you've been using a dagger or a single-handed staff, you may have noticed that you've got a spare hand. Why not carry around something more interesting than a Coldstone? There are a lot of options, but a few of them will be particularly attractive to the fashion-conscious minion of the Plaguebringer.

Orb of Slime
Magic

If you're going with a slime theme for your accouterments, then you'll naturally want this orb. Besides nicely setting off other items of slime, the Orb of Slime grants the wielder a boon of +5 health and +15 mana.

Skull of Jhen'Tra
Magic, Lore

Every Necromancer should have a few spare skulls sitting around, and this one is a good addition. It grants the wielder a boon of +3 resistance to magic and +20 mana. Sometimes I wonder where all the headless Jhen'Tra bodies are.

Black Tome with Silver Runes
Magic

There's nothing like carrying a book around to impress people with your dedication to study. In addition to accentuating that scholarly look, this book will grant the wielder a boon of +4 intelligence and +30 mana.

Jeweled Skull
Magic, Lore

If you're willing to invest some time and effort - well, a lot of time and effort - you can acquire one of these ever-so-necromantic skulls. It grants the wielder a boon of +5 intelligence, +5 resistance to magic, +20 health, and +20 mana. Unfortunately, it also radiates the cold of the grave, so it gives a penalty of -5 resistance to cold.

Harvester
AC 4, Magic, Lore

There's nothing quite like a large scythe to say, "I've come about the reaping." This particularly grim tool grants the wielder a boon of +5 strength and +5 intelligence. The lucky necromancer is also blessed with Deadeye. Like so many purely necromantic things, there is also a penalty - in this case, -5 charisma. Naturally, that makes it all the more attractive.

Words of Darkness
Magic, Lore, No Drop

More than just a book, this dark tome grants the wielder the Allure of Death and a boon of +45 mana. Acquiring this may make you some new enemies, but you'll soon get over the loss.
