Petty Worms
Unworthy of your time, but fun to squish

There are times when I have doubts about the path I have chosen to follow. Why have I dedicated myself to bringing into the world more terror and bloodshed when I could be singing cheerful little gnomish ditties and making toys for children? At times like those, I regain my faith by remembering those people in the world who are completely and utterly worthless - not Good, not Evil, just an inconvenient blob of flesh blocking the view of the scenery. Such people remind me that death and decay have their place in the grand scheme of making the world a more beautiful place to live.

So, when one of these pointless wonders comes to my attention, I rejoice in the gifts of Bertoxxulous. Revenge is a dish best served with relish.

Currently, only one person has the supreme misfortune to be on my bad side.

Lukor
Dwarf Cleric

This miserable wretch managed to gain the trust of a good friend of mine, and then proceeded to steal everything from her corpse. While I have chided my friend for trusting a dwarf in the first place, I might have expected more from a so-called cleric. Messing with dead bodies is an invasion of necromantic turf in any case, and looting corpses is a professional insult. I look forward to watching this pathetic waste of dwarfmeat die in miserable agony. Over, and over, and over.

"This won't hurt. Much. Well, actually, it will. For a very long time."
