The Ninth Circle of the Dark Arts
for Necromancers of 34 Levels

Beguile Undead
being an Alteration of 170 mana

Charms the target undead to do the Necromancer's bidding.

This upgrade to Dominate Undead will allow you to control stronger minions for a short time. Always entertaining.

Call of Bones
being an Alteration of 5 mana

Turns the Necromancer into a skeleton, provides ultravision, and drains health over time while increasing mana regeneration.

This is the dramatic upgrade to Allure of Death, and it will change your life. With a mana regeration boost better than Clarity and a distinctive cackle, you'll be slinging around your incredible necromantic power like there's no tomorrow. Get this.

Greater Shielding
being an Abjuration of 120 mana

Provides the Necromancer with increased maximum health, an armor class bonus, and increased Magic Resistance.

As always, the upgrade is well worth it. But you don't need me to tell you that at this point.

Invoke Fear
being an Alteration of 120 mana

Causes the target to flee.

Despite being rather more mana-intensive than the Fear that we've come to know and love, this upgrade seems to be less resisted and lasts far longer.

Invoke Shadow
being a Conjuration of 340 mana requiring 2 bone chips

Summons an undead skeleton to do the Necromancer's bidding.

Tougher, meaner, and generally nastier. You want it.

Resist Disease
being an Abjuration of 50 mana

Increases the target's Disease Resistance.

Root
being an Alteration of 30 mana

Holds the target immobile.

Finally.

Steelskin
being an Abjuration of 149 mana requiring 1 jasper

Absorbs damage before it can affect the Necromancer until its limit is reached.

It's a bit more expensive than Leatherskin, and a bit more effective.

Surge of Enfeeblement
being an Alteration of 100 mana

Drains a fair amount of strength from enemies in the vicinity of the Necromancer.

It does. Really. Can't say I've found a good use for it, though.

Torbas Acid Blast
being a Conjuration of 130 mana

Hits the target for a good amount of poison damage.

Put away Shock of Poison, this is better by far. Direct and satisfying.

Venom of the Snake
being a Conjuration of 160 mana

Hits the target for a good amount of poison damage and drains their health over time.

This fast-acting poison packs quite the hefty kick.
