Watchman Armor
Tinkered protection for the gnomish elite

The recipes for Watchman armor were recently revealed by the gnomish pirates based on Velious. Only the most skilled tinkers will likely have a chance at constructing this armor, and many of the components are difficult to acquire. It will also be very, very expensive.

While I expect that this armor will not be available to Necromancers, it will prove useful for our trusted living companions who join us in our conquest of the known world.

I have yet to see any of this armor, so the details are rather fuzzy. According to the construction manual, any pieces that have charges can be recharged by replacing the mana battery.
