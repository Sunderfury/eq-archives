Grim Tools

When wading into the bload-soaked field of battle, you may wish to deliver a little death personally rather than simply rely on your Incredible Necromantic Power. While the Necromancer is certainly limited in the selection of weapons, there are a number of items of exceptional interest that are well worth the trouble to acquire.

These lists are not complete, for the wise Necromancer is always finding new ways to deal death.

In complete honesty, these lists are also fairly out of date.

As with anyone who has dedicated their life to spellcasting, the armor selections of the Necromancer are more limited than most. Cloth armor and apprentice robes will be your comfort for a long time... but if you want to strike terror into the hearts of any who behold you, you'll need to pick up some better threads.

These lists are not complete, nor are they intended to be; they serve only to offer information on particular items that may inspire the Necromancer to further effort.
