Evil Cults

On the whole, Necromancers are not terribly popular. Part of this lack of popularity is attributable to jealousy, as mere mortals stand in awe of our incredible power. Another factor to consider is rebellion, since most of the people we meet will eventually become our playthings. Whatever the motivations of the foolish, however, it is important for the Necromancer to have a clear idea of who may be considered a friend, and who may be struck down without a second thought.

Occasionally, someone will get on my bad side. This is fairly difficult to do. I'm a patient sort, and my wrath is usually tempered by the knowledge that I will eventually be torturing the remains of those petty mortals throughout an eternity of undying. When the line is crossed, though, vengeance becomes something of a personal hobby and I revel in my communion with the Plaguebringer.

Beyond the Graveyard

  • The Stormseekers - The guild in which I am proud to claim membership. Why does a dedicated minion of death spend time with this bunch of honor-loving do-gooders? Good question.
  • EQNecro - A fine collection of necromantic lore, and an excellent message base. Currently down and sorely missed, but it should be back soon.
  • Fulcrum's Tinkering Compendium - For some slightly out-of-date information on gnomish goods.
  • Casters Realm - The prime information site for any spellcasters, and many others. They have excellent information, active forums, and updated news that's relevant. One of my favorites.
  • AllaKhazam's Magical Realm - A central clearing house for every kind of information, with links to everything you might want to know.
  • EverQuest - The obligatory home page.
