Picturesque Evil
Images of Norrath

Whilst peacefully meditating in the plains of South Karana, I was approached by a rather large elephant with a gigantic cracked tusk. Since this injured incisor seemed to be causing the beast a great deal of pain - and since he kept goring me with it - I decided to take up some amateur dentistry and remove the offending ivory. I was successful, but my first dental patient didn't survive the procedure. I was crushed - but I finally managed to crawl out from under the corpse.

I've kept the tusk as a momento. If anyone knows of any use for it, just let me know.

"Elephant tipping time!"
