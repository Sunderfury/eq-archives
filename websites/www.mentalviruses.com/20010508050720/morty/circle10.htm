The Tenth Circle of the Dark Arts
for Necromancers of 39 Levels

Augment Death
being an Abjuration of 100 mana

Increases the combat abilities of the Necromancer's pet.

Chilling Embrace
being an Alteration of 200 mana

Hits the target for a good amount of poison damage and drains their health over time.

You'd think it would be cold, but it's not.

Counteract Disease
being an Alteration of 50 mana

Removes a lingering disease from the target.

Drain Spirit
being an Alteration of 189 mana

Drains a good amount of health from the target and grants it to the Necromancer.

Expel Undead
being an Evocation of 130 mana

Hits the target undead for a good amount of damage, usually doing some damage even if resisted.

Malignant Dead
being a Conjuration of 390 mana requiring 2 bone chips

Summons an undead skeleton to do the Necromancer's bidding.

Nullify Magic
being an Abjuration of 110 mana

Removes magical effects from the target.

Scent of Darkness
being an Alteration of 150 mana

Reduces the target's Disease Resistance, Poison Resistance, and Fire Resistance by a good amount.

Scourge
being a Conjuration of 170 mana

Afflicts the target with disease, preventing them from healing, and draining a good amount of health over time.

Summon Corpse
being a Conjuration of 700 mana requiring 1 jade inlaid coffin

Transports the target's corpse to the Necromancer's feet.

Word of Souls
being an Evocation of 171 mana

Hits enemies in the vicinity of the Necromancer for a good amount of damage.

"Death is the greatest gift, and 'tis better to give than receive."
