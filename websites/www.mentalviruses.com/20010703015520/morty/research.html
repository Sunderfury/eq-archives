It came to my attention that the "trivial" levels for researching various spells doesn't seem to be generally known. To assist in determining these levels for the researched spells of necromancy, this cheat sheet has been put together.

If you have any updates, please send them to Morty Undercrypt on E'ci, and I'll put the new information in the chart. Please send information only on combinations that you have performed yourself. Information should include:

  • Spell attempted.
  • Research skill level.
  • Trivial or not on a successful attempt. (If you received the "You cannot increase your skill" message, it's trivial. Failed attempts don't generate the message so they can't be counted.)

Until verified by experiment, I'm not assuming that spells of the same level trivial at the same point. I'm also not assuming that a lower level spell will necessarily have a trivial level below all other higher level spells. That would make sense, and I wouldn't be surprised to see that, but there's always the chance for something odd.

Based on multiple reports, though, spells of the same level do have the same trivial point, and all of those points have been determined. These numbers haven't been independently verified for each spell, but they do match the data so far. They're probably accurate, but until each spell is verified, those points will appear in the "estimated" column.

The complete information will be of benefit to the entire community of necromancers – thank you for your support!
