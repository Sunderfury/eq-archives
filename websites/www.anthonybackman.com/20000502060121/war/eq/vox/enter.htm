Ok, here we are at the end of the hall. This may be a crossroad for concern for a few reasons.

First off, you should know that when you leave the main hall you break an invisible wall. If you look back to where you came you may get disoriented and think you are further from the hall out than you really are. Be sure to get your bearings when you clear the hall.

Secondly, as you see in this picture looking to the right immediately after the hall opens up, there are a few big blue meanies waiting for lost adventurers to wander into the path. If I remember correctly there are 2 giants here so walk lightly unless your ready to mix it up right off the bat.

And last, but not least, behind me in this photo is the zone to Perma. I mention this to send out a warning signal to all evil races and those with bad Halas faction. "Why?", you may ask. Well, seeing as this is the closest zone from a hard hitting NPC by the name of Tundra Jack and his pet bear they can both be found here from time to time. That means you have the possibility to be double-teamed by high lvl mobs before even getting into the dungeon.
