Above is a very crude map of the zone, don't laugh I am by no stretch of the imagination, a cartographer. I did not list cardinal directions because I'm not 100% of them myself. I think that the top is north, left west, right east, down south. The only imperial data I can give is that the clouds moved from right to left, as did the sun, and the moon went from bottom to top.

Here is the key for the numbers above:

  1. The gate seen from Feerrott, I assume that this is where you port into but not having been there myself I can really tell you.
  2. The temple (surrounded by a fire wall)
  3. There are 2 sets of structures that are extremely tall and have a large flame on top of them. Each structure has a ramp on either side that leads you up to the top where the flame is.
  4. There are 2 sets of huge bell towers. One is functional (the one on the left side), and one that is broken (right side)
  5. An enormous hourglass that is almost smack dab in the center of the zone.
  6. A huge bird statue carving. This same statue can be found in Lesser Faydark on the South East corner.
  7. Each building labeled #7 is a cathedral like structure
  8. This building is identical to the towers found all over Norath. It is tall with a triangular top with and entrance at the bottom. This same structure can be found in the desert of Ro, Rathe mountains, and most memorable as the specter tower in the Oasis.
  9. This is a tall rectangular structure with an opening at the bottom. This same structure can be found in the Rathe mts.
  10. The purple structures labeled #10 are open alter type structures. They are 4 poles with a slab of rock in the center of each
  11. This is an area littered with runes and stones. There were so many that I couldn't possible show them all. Bellow this field is what appears to be a teleportation station. The type most commonly used by druids.

 

The orange blocks are stone spires (the same variety that are found outside of Cazic Thule)

First of all, lets try to describe the make of fear

plane and how you must think of it as you are entering

or walking about. There are numberous mobs in there

and each pretty much has its own section of the zone.

Each section is made up of roamers and stationary

targets. For this writing im going break the zone down

into 7 sections. Fear itself is basically one big

square pied out into those 7 sections. Unless you

spend hours apon hours in the zone you will probably

only see parts of 3 secs while your puller might

acutally spent a great deal of time in 5 section.

There are 2 sections of the zone that are just

extremely hard and more then like youll never get to

(sorry druids, warrior, and Sks because the bulk of

you drops are in these two hardest sections to get

too).

 

*okay time for everyone to switch their minds into a

gemometric kind of view*

 

As you break in, the safest spot to start out at (and

we will call this the SW corner or Camp 1) is Camp 1.

This is absolutely the hardest single one thing for a

group to do, that is getting the mass numbers you need

to this spot so you can make your presence know. You

see the gate in or portal is the apex of 3 of the

section. Roamers from the first 3 sections like to

meet up at the portal. So when you zone if your not

careful, fast and have a head on your shoulders it is

very possible that you attract mobs that roam from all

three sections. Which isnt fun because once you do

this you have effectively stired up the zone and make

it twice as hard to get in. So people say you need to

go in with invis, invis undead, and invis animals, but

in my opinion this doesnt work at all if you make it

to camp one that way its because you happen to have

caught the mobs in there open spot as they roam to

other locations in there repective section. You see

when a mob roams it moves for about 30 secs then stops

and looks around then decides to move again to there

next nav point in their section (standing still time

is about 30 secs also).

 

The three sections that you deal with when you first

zone is a long strech the goes from portal past the

yellow scareling town through all the boggiemen to the

back gorgon nest, then there is the portal section or

about 5-6 mobs that acutally spend their whole life

circling the portal (the meanist being the singlar

scareling in that section), and last of the first

three sections that you encounter is the section that

goes from the portal past the Fited house back along

the fire wall till you reach the section that houses

Cazic himself. The mobs you will encounter that roam

to toward the portal are Scarelings (huge argo radius

1 in portal section 1-2 in boggie section, and 2-3 in

section that leads to cazic), spine chiller spiders

found in portal area and section to cazic, Shiverback

apes found in portal area and section leading to

cazic, Fited fiends found in all three areas,

Amygladan warriors found in portal area and boggie

section.

 

Okay now that you know the make up. Easiest way to

make it to SW corner or camp 1 is to first try to take

in a group of 12-18 that acutally know where this spot

is so they can get there quickly. Then dont bum rush

the spot with all 18 becuase you know people just

arent made to walk in tight groups. Go one at a time

where at all possible with other trying to let people

behind them know how the gap in the 3 sections just

are. Then zone in. One you have zoned in do a turn to

you left head to the spot just next to portal and look

down if you see nothing on hill to your right or left

then run straight down til you hit zone wall. Once at

zone wall you need to STOP LOOK AND LISTEN (this is

very important to everyone in your group). If you see

nothing coming at you (make sure you relize scarelings

have a huge argo area and you might not see them on

your tail at first) head slowly to camp 1 hugging the

zone wall all the way there. Along the way there alway

take a time out to make sure you are not draging

anything with you. If you happen to pick up something

along the way for god shakes dont run it to the Camp 1

spot. Dont run off into the zone just gracefully sit

there and try to gate out. If you cant just die right

there and when you try to come back in again to get to

camp 1 spot just corpse your body with you. If people

do die it is very important that you tell them to

FORGET about their corpse they will get it later as

zone clears a bit. Last thing you need is people

wandering around the zone once they are in and already

at the camp 1 spot.

 

Okay you have assembled your army at camp 1 (what ever

the numbers may be. the more people the better but i

would suggest at least 18 people if you want to mark

that spot as your permantly but you can pull it off

Rule #3 Dont bicker in OOC about who isnt or is in
