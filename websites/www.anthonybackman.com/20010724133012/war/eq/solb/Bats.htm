Enemy:

Sonic Bat

 

Usually come in pairs. Decent amount of HP. Have a sonic scream attack that will stun you for several rounds. Can hit in the 70's. Can be charmed by Shaman and Druids as well as enchanters however enchanters would be better served with the bugs found later. They drop plat, wings, pages, and runes.

 

Experiance:

Give exp through 50

 

Bat cave #1

Above you see the first bat cave. This is a popular location for raid parties to meet before they go in too deep.  To the left is the 2nd cave and to the right is a hall filled with bats every step of the way.

 

 

Bat cave #2

If you do not plan on moving forward, this is as far back as you can safely stand to see the condition of the room. If you wait here you can see weather or not the Noxious Spider (nox) or its placeholder the lava duct crawler (ldc) is on patrol. Once you know the timing on the nox/ldc you can strafe in and check /pull the bats. Any further than this and you could get both the bats and the nox/ldc. Once you clear this area you can head right to the Efreeti area or left for more bugs on the way to the Fire Giant area.

 

 

 

 

Enemy:

Lava Beetle (beets)

 

Relatively low HP but hit very hard. Can hit for 110+ an attack. Double attack. Can "encase" you in fire.

 

Experiance:

Give exp through 50

 

 

Lvl 50 Gnome war Vailk fights a beetle.

 

Platform Area

Once you clear the second bat cave you will see that just up the ramp is a platform. That platform is a good camp if you want to try your hand at Lava beets. A much better camp is just up the ramp you can see just past the wolf back there to zone 4, the back way to sol A. Approach cautiously. There are 2 beetles up on the platform as well as one by the back ramp, with a possible Nox spider patrol. If that patrol sees you while in the LDC room he can pull the 2 that are waiting in there to join the frey.

 

 

 

Enemy:

Lava Duct Crawler (LDC)

 

A ton of HP. Hit very heard (110+). Have a DOT attack of "Blood Boil" that will drain around 1000 hp. You should not attempt an LDC without a healer in the party. Many times party members will make it out of the zone, only to die from untreated blood boil. If you wish to treat blood boil (recommended) cast either remove poison or regeneration / chloroplast to counter it's effects. An LDC is one of the more vicious pets an enchanter could claim up to this point.

 

Experiance:

Give exp through 50

 

LDC Room

On the left you see the ramp that goes up to the platform area. This room can spawn up to 3 LDC as well as have a patrol. Out the other side is Stone spider room and past that, the ramp to the fire giants. Outside the perimeter of this room is a hall way that circumvents this room and leads directly to the stone spider room. That hall has 1 stationary LDC and a possible patrol.

 

 

Sol A ZONE 4

 

I love this spot. If you know how to negotiate Sol A you may want to consider coming to this spot to avoid the chaos of below.  Zone 4 has 5 local bats with easy access to lava beets and LDC. IF the fight turns bad you are only a few strides from a zone. You do have to keep an eye on how close you are to falling off into lava. If you do fall in, don't panic. Swim down the river and make your first available RIGHT, climb on the ledge there and once on dry land head to your left. This platform is where the solo LDC pops, if it is camped you may be fine. If the LDC is there, your odds aren't very good, but at least you will know where your corpse is.

 

 

 

 

 

If you look toward the direction this picture was taken you would see a bridge at the distance spanning the lava below. That is the Fire giant area. IF you have levitation you can walk directly to the giants and pass over the LDC and Stone Spider Rooms.
