After this shot was taken a few more events happened that may provide good info for you.

All the tanks were dead so I threw caution to the wind an introduced myself to Vox's claws. As I got down to her I noticed that earth elementals were still doing remarkably well. This could be used as a possible buffer for retreating tanks to get healed in future attempts?

Once I got a line of sigh on Vox I let loose with a store bought arrow and got the message that she is invulnerable to non magic (DOE!). So then I got in her face. Well, maybe not face but I did give her toe nails a good clipping! That's when I noticed that she hit for 210 twice in a round... My AC was 664 so take that as you will.

With one bubble of health left I ran to the back of the hall and hoped that I could just sit and wait it out. Well, you see, since I tagged her at least once she felt she had the right to summon me after about 2 min. of resting *gulp*. That was just about enough for my little heroics for the evening.

Add on top of all of this damage the fact that Vox likes to totally heal herself. In the course of the last fight I hear she did so 4 times.

On the bright side, apparently SHE CAN BE SLAMMED OUT OF A SPELL! With a good core of large warriors bashing it is theoretically possible to keep her off her heals, frost and fears. I didn't witness this myself so take it with a grain of salt.
