My way is more effective.


While you are out mining I am smithing.


People dont like visiting a shop and seeing closed cause Im mining.


So sure you can sell plate for 200 gold and sell alot of it. Eventually you will have to go mine - I sell at a very competitive price - I get paid a % based on the standard ingot value of 2gp per.


I know what the cost for me is.


Your cost is lower because your time is higher.


Simple math - I go through apx 1k ingots in an hour when taking orders. It takes a good hour or so to get 1000 ingots. So in two hours you can sell 1000 ingots cheap. In two hours I can sell 2000 ingots a little higher. In that two hours you take 1000 ingots valued at 1.x gold (perhaps even 1) so Ill give you a proffit over the two hours of 1100- 1900. Over that two hours I sell all my ingots at around 3.5 or 7k - I pay out 4k for the ingots leaving 3.5k proffit. I sold more, made more and charged the fair price.


The customer wins when they get fast service and dependable hours - they do not win when they get low prices some of the time.


Here endeth the lesson
