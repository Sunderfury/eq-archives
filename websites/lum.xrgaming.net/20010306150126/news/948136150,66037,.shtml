OR JUST BE LIKE ME AND MAKE A NEW CHARACTER EVERY OTHER DAY
Monday, January 17, 2000 - 12:09 PM [Filed by lum]

John Smedley, Lord High Poobah at Verant, posted to the official Everquest forums that in conjunction with the opening of 1 to 2 announced new servers (um, guys, that would be either 1 or 2) characters from Fennin Ro (the oldest EQ server) would be allowed to transfer to the newer server(s). Here's some of what he has to say on the subject:

1) We do recognize the problem on Fennin Ro (it hit 2600+ people tonight) and we are actively putting into place a plan to fix it.

2) The plan involves allowing people on Fennin Ro AT THEIR OPTION to move to a new server that would go up very soon (no ETA yet). Please re-read that last part.

3) Yes, they would get to keep their items, but beyond that we don't have enough details to give out yet. We do think it would be neccessary to limit it to X number of people (still working out how many) so people that would move would have to be 100% sure they were willing to go. It would also involve completely moving EVERY character a person has on that server, not selectively moving 1-2 characters, otherwise there wouldn't be much of a point.

Give us a few days and we will announce all the details, but this isn't something that we plan on waiting very long on.

Also - AND THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT - we do see other servers getting more people on them as well. We're going to be putting up 1-2 servers very soon here and we hope this will really help. Please keep in mind we want to see how things go on Fennin Ro before we make any other plans.

Note that the EQ message boards are on a very overloaded server and you may have to [Reload] 3 or 4 times to view the thread.
