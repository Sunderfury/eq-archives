Once Upon a Pizza Place
Wednesday, June 14, 2000 - 5:28 AM [Filed by arcadian del sol]

*phone rings*
"Hello and thank you for calling Origin Pizza. How can I help you?"
"uh hi. What is your special?"
"Double land half PKing half no PKing factions housing party system lover's feast. All for ten dollars. Would you like to try one?"
"That sounds great! I'll take one please."
"Okay your order comes to ten dollars a month and it will be there in 30 minutes. Thank you for calling Origin Pizza."
*click*
*to the cook* "Did that pizza oven arrive yet?"
"We're unloading it from the truck now. Should be installed in the kitchen in about an hour."
*45 minutes later: phone rings again*
"Hello. Thank you for calling Origin Pizza."
"Hi. I ordered a pizza almost an hour ago and it's still not here."
"Oh I'm very sorry about that. It's right here waiting to go out, and I'll include a free side order of one month. It should be there in about 30 minutes."
*hangs up*
*to the cook* "What's going on with that oven? You said it would only take an hour."
"Well once we got it installed, I realized we don't have an electrical outlet to plug it into. I've called an electriction and he should be here soon."
*30 minutes later: phone rings*
"Hi. Thank you for calli..."
"yeah yeah, I know who you are by now. Listen, my order got here and..."
"It did?! uh. I mean, yes, it did. great."
"Well, I ate up all the free month side order but when I opened the pizza box, there was nothing inside it. You delivered an empty pizza box."
"I'm very sorry about that. Our 3 best drivers recently quit to go work for EverCrust Pizza so we are a little understaffed. You wouldn't want to deliver some pizza's would you? I can't pay you money but you can have a free ten dollar pizza every month, and..."
*laughing* "Look, I don't know what kind of pizza shop you have there, but I ordered a pizza hours ago and I still don't have it. All my buddies already left because they got tired of waiting and decided it was better to eat a microwave pizza than to wait forever for a good pizza to finally show up here. I'm beginning to think that...."
*phone goes dead. customer calls back and gets the following recording...*
"We're sorry. The PizzaMasters are in a meeting right now. Please call back after 5pm. Thank you for choosing Origin Pizza. If you wish to leave a message, do so at the beep."
*phone beeps once, then hangs up*

*5pm: phone rings*
"If you are calling because you are waiting for a pizza delivery, we are working hard to ensure that the new pizza oven is fully operational before we are willing to deliver a sub-standard pizza."
"WHAT THE F*(#@!!? I PLACED AN ORDER FOUR HOURS AGO, AND NOW YOU TELL ME YOU ARE INSTALLING THE OVEN?!"
"We goofed. We screwed up. We are over worked. We are doing doubleshifts. We are going to correct these problems, and when you finally do get your pizza, all our hard work will be worth it."
"Did you ever consider maybe NOT TAKING PIZZA ORDERS until, y'know - YOU HAVE THE FUCKING OVEN INSTALLED?!"
"That's a great idea. You should write a letter to our home office about that. In the meantime, we are going to keep working on installing this oven, and I will be personally contacting all our customers at least 24 hours before delivering their pizza."
"Look, I'm so hungry I'm about to eat this phone. Cancel my order please."
*hangs up phone*
"Hey boss! We got the oven installed! It still isn't hot enough to melt cheese, but this baby is online and cooking!"
*phone rings*
"Hello?"
"Hi this is Origin Pizza. I just wanted to inform you that our current plan is to deliver your pizza one week from friday.
"Dude. I just ordered an EverCrust Pizza, and it's already here, hot fresh, and not too bad. Granted, it kinda tastes like chalk, but at least it took care of those hunger pangs."

*3 years later, Origin Pizza closes. In it's place, Ultima Shoes Online: Origins opens and sells fashionable footwear that look very pretty, but for some reason, all smell like pepperoni and garlic powder.*

The End.
