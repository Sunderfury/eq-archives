WEIMAR BRITANNIA
Monday, June 19, 2000 - 2:08 PM [Filed by lum]

Markee Dragon sends in this:

I have been thinking about this problem for the past few months. Currently we have inflation in the UO markets that is out of control. Let me give you some examples. Rare items have been fetching record high prices on the market. I have a verified auction on Pacific where a newbie deed sold for 50 million gold. How about Towers that are selling for 15 to 20 million gold. Heck it was only about 4 months ago that I verified a sale of a castle on Baja for 5 million gold. Now 5 million gold for a castle sounds about right but where the heck did the economy go wrong and we end up with 15 million gold towers? Not to mention a Newbie Deed that sold for an incredible 50 million gold. I think that a responsible question that needs to be asked on everyone's behalf is where the hell did all the gold come from? I'll tell you where it's all from, but first let me give you a little recap of our UO history over the last few months and you'll have the answer figured out before I even say it.

About 4 months ago we started hearing rumors that it was possible to dupe items in the game again. This was a problem about 18 months ago but OSI seemed to get it resolved. Well, for the most part. During the life of the game we have heard of it from time to time but it was never something that was a mainstream problem. So getting back to the last few months... We have seen the duping instructions on all of the major bug sites. Heck back when we used to have UO Hospital here as one of our sub sites we had the instructions posted there on how to do it. I will give OSI some credit as they banned the webmaster of the site and all the news posters there. They also banned me because I owned the domain that the site was housed under. I 100% agree with their actions. But did that solve the problem? No. We still to this day see duping as one of the hottest topics on the bug sites.

So what do I think about all of this? Well the primary thing that I think OSI should be addressing is this problem of duping. (I'm sure that you now know the answer is duping) I think that this is much more important then the fact that we are still waiting to place houses. The problem is even apparent on other sites. The following is a quote from one of the more main stream bug sites. The only credit I'll give as we are not a bug site here is to say that the authors name is Merlin:

"Well friends my work is almost done. Through the spread of bugs and other ways to make money and dupe, the Ultima economy has been ruined. Thus, the game has been ruined. When a player can offer me 14 million gold for my tower i know that something is wrong. Also, the daily ICQs of people buying every statue, every rare, and everything for any price tells me that the economy is ruined. When the economy is ruined the game is ruined. People play to get gold to buy the things they want. If you are not rich or exposed to the evils and corruption of the game it is just a matter of time. Trust me, people like me work day and night to spread the germ as fast as we can. I could right a book on the injustices OSI has done to me and my close friends, but i wont waste your time. Those that have been through it know what i speak of. When an upstart warrior can became famed or illustrious in a day from killing liches with only 2 hits i know something is wrong. The whole system has been lowered from gold to fame. Not to mention now a whole new world for everyone to place a house that does not have one. To bad OSI does not have a FED to raise interest rates in Ultima. They might have been able to slow this process of rampant spending. I'm sure someone from OSI is reading this and if you are, mark my words friend it is just a matter of time before your looking for a new job instead of cheating the paying customers of Ultima Online. If you don't agree with the views of this site then please don't view the site."

So do we blame the bug sites for putting the information out there so that players can exploit it? No not at all. I'm not taking a pro bug site stance but when you really think about it who's the responsibility to fix this supposed to be put on? I think that it the primary responsibility of OSI to fix the problem so that it can not be exploited. Can you really expect the players to not take advantage of something that will give them an advantage? If you do expect it then you may need a reality check.

So what needs to happen to have this problem resolved? First off we need to have the duping bug fixed. Once we have the duping bug fixed we can begin the repair work on our economy. I think that repairing the economy will be more painful for the players of UO then it will be for OSI to fix the duping problem but both will need to be done.

The economy in UO is very similar to our economy in real life. Since we have so much money in UO we need to treat this just like we would our own real life economy. Currently we do have a dynamic economy in UO but I doubt that it was ever designed to deal with such a huge influx of gold. Another idea would be to delete everyone's gold or even turn everyone's gold into tickets like we did in the past to reduce the item count. I'm not 100% sure of what the answer is but something needs to be done. I'm open to suggestions and I would hope that the Dev team is also.

I invite anyone and everyone to respond to this with your ideas and then I will compile them together and present them to the Dev team. So... The first order of business is to fix the damn dupe bug. Then fix the economy!

http://www.markeedragon.com/economy.asp

I've heard the rumors of duping as well. However, whether through duping or simply the gold spigot that is hunting in Trammel, it's obvious that inflation in UO is out of control. What needs to happen? I've written a few ideas already last month (you know, back when I still played UO). Whatever is implemented, it's gonna hurt.

Medicine is good for you. So swallow.
