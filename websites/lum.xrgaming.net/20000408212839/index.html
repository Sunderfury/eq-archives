PAGING ST. JUDE...WE HAVE ANOTHER ONE HERE FOR YOU
Saturday, April 8, 2000 - 1:06 AM [Filed by great bob]

I'm a sucker for lost causes. It's one of the reasons I vote Libertarian. It's why I root for Boston to win the World Series. It's why blackjack dealers smile whenever I sit down at the table. Well, here's another one.

The Wing Commander Engine Source Code Release Project (now that's a mouthful :) )... the name is fairly self-explanatory. In essence, we're writing a petition to Origin, asking that they release the source code for at least some WC games. To be taken seriously, however, we need lots and lots of "signatures" (names + e-mail addresses) to add at the end of the petition.

The basic idea is if the Wing Commander source code, for any game in the series, was released, Doom like add-ons could be made by fans. It's not asking for the comercial rights but just the ability to make add-on files. You into lost causes like I am? Join the list and mail this guy. jumper@wcescrp.cjb.net.

Will it happen? Naw. Like Masters of Magic or M.U.L.E. the copyrights will be guarded by blood thirsty lawyers, and the games only remembered by those of us who spent waaaaaayyyy too much time playing them. For one reason or another, OSI decided to drive a stake through the heart of the Wing Commander series....although after seeing the movie, I was pretty tempted to impale someone myself. Sometimes, however, it's nice to dream. Consider it charity work for old geeks like me.

What impossible dream do you folks have about old games?
