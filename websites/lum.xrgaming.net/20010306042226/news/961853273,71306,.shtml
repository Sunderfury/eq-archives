MY WIFE, THE EXPLOITER
Saturday, June 24, 2000 - 6:27 AM [Filed by lum]

You know, for some reason I thought I'd be the first in my family to run afoul of EQ's Customer Service staff. Um, guess not. Here's a dispatch from Norrath from my better half.

Well, well - ya know I've heard a lot of horror stories - even witnessed things that didn't look quite right - but tonight is the first time I've ever been on the receiving end of a vindictive GM with a God complex.

I play a druid on Seventh Hammer server in EQ. Tonight - I was hanging out in Unrest watching some folks kill this 40th lvl critter that you spawn by giving a firebeetle eye to a little gnome in a closet. They are both pretty high level mages - and their pets make his life expectancy - oh 20 seconds.

Anyway - there is another person there that decides they want to be powerleveled with this thing - and gets pissy that the mages seem to be getting more kills - so he makes some remark about telling the programmers - and petitioning a GM.

About 15 or 20 minutes go by - and all of a sudden - Khrix spawns - the pets just vanish - and the mages are frozen in place to be killed by this thing. I made the mistake of trying to heal them - and go down too.

Well, apparently they weren't killing this thing according to GM Hakosako's approved 'vision' (tm) - so he decided to have a little fun at their (and my) expense. When I sent him a tell demanding a full experience ressurection - he sent me this....

"Please let the rest of your group and and your guild that exploiting on my server is death."

First of all - they weren't my group - secondly - I'm not IN A FREAKING GUILD - theirs or anyone elses.

I'd also like to know how spawning this thing and killing it - WHICH IS WHAT YOU ARE SUPPOSED TO DO - is exploiting - but that's a whole other matter.

That message lets me know two things - first off - he has an exceedingly poor grasp of the English language - and secondly - he thinks it's perfectly allright to purposefully cause player deaths with no warning - no communication whatsoever. I would hope SOMEWHERE in their freaking guidelines - direct interferrance in gameplay is not to be tolerated.

Now - after I told him to read this page tommorrow - I DID get a full xp res - but guess what, it gets better - he got the folks killed that could deal with this thing - resses me in it's path - I got killed by it AGAIN. He killed the players - but not the creature, who is tearing the whole zone a new one.

I paged immediately again - and guess what - he's on, but anon - and messages show he is not online.

OOPS!

In the FWIW department - this creature is hunted a lot - and not always as safely as this group was doing it. Usually a druid will spawn it - then kite it all over the zone getting lots and lots of folks killed in the process. No other players were in danger the way these guys were hunting it. I've petitioned myself on careless druids that would hunt this thing
for hours on end and get dozens of players killed - only to be told by guides and gms there was nothing they could do about it.

So GM Hakosako - I really hope Brad and John read this page today - and I just wish I could be a fly on the wall when they ask you what went on. I hear Burger King may be hiring.

I don't know when these companies are gonna learn that you cannot turn McDonald's burgerflippin refugees loose with god powers over people that are customers and have a LOT of time invested in what pays their salaries. Such a thing as happened tonight should NEVER EVER EVER happen. If you don't like people hunting efficiently - change the code - do NOT EVER punish people for doing exactly what they are expected to do.

A GM may be a policeman - but he cannot be allowed to be judge, jury and executioner at his whim.

I eagerly await everyone telling me how killing my wife was somehow correct customer service.
