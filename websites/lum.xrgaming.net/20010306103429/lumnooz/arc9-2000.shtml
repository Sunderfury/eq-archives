SNOWBALL DOES RUMOR CONTROL
Thursday, October 5, 2000 - 5:43 PM [Filed by lum]
Snowball talked to gamers.com today about the stories we've filed on their axing the Vault Network.

If you caught the rumors tearing around today, you'd think that Snowball, host to over 100 gaming sites, has dropped its affiliate network. But don't believe everything you hear.

The official word from Leslie Weber, Snowball's public relations manager, is, "Snowball has not nor do we plan on shutting down the Vault Network." She added, "There is no change in the [affiliate] lineup that I am aware of." That lineup includes popular sites like Diabloii.net, EQ Vault and AC Vault.

One affiliate's webmaster said, "I think everyone is in the dark as to what's going on right now."

I stand by the stories I have posted. I'm working on getting some of my sources to speak on the record and will post this when and if this occurs.
