Interview with Gordon "Tyrant" Walton
Vice President, Online Services and Operations, Origin Systems Inc.

From OSI's company profile:

Gordon is a veteran of the entertainment software industry. He signed on as the new vice president of online services at ORIGIN in March 1999. He came to ORIGIN from Kesmai Corporation where he served for 3 ½ years as Sr. VP for entertainment products and as studio general manager. He began his career as an independent game programmer in 1978. During the next 17 years he worked as an executive for several entertainment software companies including Three-Sixty Pacific, PRISM Interactive Products, Konami of America, GameTek and Alliance Interactive Software. At Kesmai Walton helped grow the company into one of the leading developers and publishers of online multiplayer games.
