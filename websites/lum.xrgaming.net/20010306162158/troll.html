Exploitation and Seige Perilious
Sep 3 1999 2:55PM CST
We would like to make you aware of a sequence of events that has unfolded on Seige Perilious. This morning it
was brought to the attention that exploitation was taking place on Seige Perilious that caused severe imbalances
within the shard. Those parties involved have been permanently banned from playing Ultima Online, and all possessions
relating to the exploitation have been deleted to the best of our ability.


We are now at a crossroads with Seige Perilious. Apparently the problem is more widespread than we had anticipated.
Those who initially logged on to Seige Perilious to play were interested in playing on an advanced shard with
extensive difficulties and many challenges. Due to the exploitation, what was once a fair playing field is such no
longer. With deep regret we will be wiping Siege Perilious effective the next maintenance period, tomorrow morning,
September 4th.


In an effort to compensate those who have played Seige Perilious diligently, we will be handing out special deeds
proportional to the amount of game time you have spent in Seige. These deeds, when double clicked, will allow you
to select a skill for which you will gain 5 skill points. This may not be the best solution but it is the one that
allows us to be most labor-effective. Unfortuneately, there are many other shards that need attention as well, and
our resources are not as vast as we would like.


We thank you for your patience on this matter, and will have a fresh Seige Perilious ready for those that dare
to take on the challenge tomorrow! =)
