Barbarian Cultural Smithing Recipes

The following recipes must be made by a Barbarian using the Northman Forge:

   Imbued Items:  Ivory is only usable by Followers of Tribunal    Jade is only usable by followers of Rallos Zek

Weapons

  • Northman Battle Axe = Flask of Water + Axe Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Folded Metal Sheet
  • Northman Imbued Ivory Battle Axe = Frost Temper + Flask of Water + Axe Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Folded Metal Sheet + Imbued Ivory
  • Northman Claymore = Medium quality folded sheet metal + flask of water + heavy-blade mold + hilt mold + pommel mold
  • Northman Imbued Ivory Claymore = Frost Temper + Hilt Mold + Duel Edged Blade Blade Mold + Medium Quality Folded Metal Sheet + Imbued Ivory
  • Northman Imbued Jade Claymore = Frost Temper + Hilt Mold + Duel Edged Blade Blade Mold + Medium Quality Folded Metal Sheet + Imbued Jade
  • Northman Imbued Ivory Long Dagger = Frost Temper + Hilt Mold + Dagger Blade Mold + Medium Quality Metal Sheet + Imbued Ivory
  • Northman Imbued Jade Long Dagger = Frost Temper + Hilt Mold + Dagger Blade Mold + Medium Quality Metal Sheet + Imbued Jade
  • Northman Imbued Ivory Mace = Frost Temper + Mace Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Metal Sheet + Imbued Ivory
  • Northman Imbued Jade Mace = Frost Temper + Mace Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Metal Sheet + Imbued Jade
  • Northman Long Dagger = Flask of Water + Blade Mold + Hilt mold + Pommel mold + Medium Quality Sheet of Metal
  • Northman Mace = Flask of Water + Mace Head Mold + Oak Shaft + Medium Quality Metal Sheet
  • Northman Throwing Axe = Flask of Water + Throwing Axe Mold + Medium Quality Metal Sheet
