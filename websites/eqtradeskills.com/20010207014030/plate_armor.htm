Fine Plate Armor

Boots = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Boot Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Bracers = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Bracers Mold + 1 medium quality folded sheet of metal. 
Breastplate = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Breastplate Mold + 3 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Chain Jointing = Metal Rings + file + smithy hammer + water (Chain Jointings are used in making Field Plate and Full Plate, no formula found yet) 
Cloak (splinted) = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Cloak Mold + 3 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Collars = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Collars Mold + 1 medium quality folded sheet of metal. 
Colored Plate = Normal Plate formula + dye + lacquer + acid
Gauntlets = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Gauntlet Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Girdles = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Girdles Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Greaves = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Greaves Mold + 3 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Helms = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Helms Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Metal Rings = Large Brick of Ore + File + Flask of Water
Pauldrons = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Pauldrons Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Vambraces = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Vambraces Mold + 2 medium quality folded sheets of metal. 
Visor = Smithy Hammer + leather backing + water + Plate Visor Mold + 1 medium quality folded sheet of metal. 

 

Shields

Buckler = Water + Smithy Hammer + Buckler Mold + 1 metal sheet 
Kite Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Kite Shield Mld + 2 medium quality sheets of metal. 
Round Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Round Shield Mold + 2 metal sheets. 
Targ Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Targ Shield Mold + 1 folded sheet of metal 
Tower Shield = Water + Smithy Hammer + Tower Shield Mold + 1 medium quality folded sheet of metal.

Arrows

Field Arrowheads = metal bits + water + file (trivial at 41)
Hooked Arrowhead = 3 small bricks of ore + File + Flask of Water (yields 5)
Silver Tipped Arrowheads = silver bar + file + block of medium quality ore + water. 
Steel Arrow Shafts = water + file + 2 large bricks of medium ore.

Folded Titanium Sheet = Block of Titanium + Flask of Blood Water + Forging Hammer
Medium Quality Block of Ore= 3 Large Bricks of Medium Quality Ore + Water
Titanium Scales = File + Large Brick of Titanium + Flask of Blood Water

How to make and dye Plate Armor

Alchemist's/Poisoner's Phase part 1
1) Take one of the above components with a Flask of Water and an Empty Vial to an Alchemist (Shaman), or Poison Maker (Rogue).
2) The Shaman or Rogue can combine these three items to make you an Extract.
Potter's Phase
3) Unfired Medium Clay Jar = Block of Clay + Water + Medium Jar Sketch, on a Potter's Wheel.
4) Fire the Jar with a Quality Firing Sheet, in a Kiln.
This creates a Medium Clay Jar which is a container with a Combine button.
***Trivial at Pottery Skill of 31
Alchemist's/Poisoner's Phase part 2
5) 1 resin = Dark color, 2 resins = medium color, 3 resins = light color.
6) Take the container from Step 4 back to your Shaman or Rogue, who can combine the Extract from step 2 and Resin(s) to make a Vile of Dye for dying Fine Plate. (You can actually do this part yourself as well because there is no skill check involved in combining these items in the medium clay jar.)
Blacksmith's Phase
7) Take a completed piece of Fine Plate, the Dye Packet, jar of acid and a Jar of Laquer and combine them in a regular Forge, voila, Colored Plate Piece.
