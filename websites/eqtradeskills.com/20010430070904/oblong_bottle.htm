The Oblong Bottle

This is part of the quest that people dread from what I have heard.  I have 3 shots of this part of the quest that will hopefully help you out a bit.  After the Sister Island, you will travel on the boat for a bit.  You will pass between to stone cliffs, aqua goblins and sirens usually inhabit these.  I jumped off the ship to the one on the left side of the boat and climbed up onto the rock.  Head to the front of the rock (toward the direction the ship was going).  There is a small ledge I got my bearings on before I took a leap of faith into the icy waters.

This is me looking to my right from the ledge.  I am going to throw up the next pic I took after I already got the bottle and swam straight up from the ocean bottom.

This is looking straight back from where I swam from.  Now when you are heading out from the rocks, you will see the rocky surface under the water.  The rocks taper off into sand and drops down a bit more.  This is when you know you are getting close.  The EXACT location of the bottle is -1611, 2924 and it appears as a bag on the ocean floor.

 

As you can see from the pic, my stamina didn't go down a whole lot, and this was my first attempt looking for it so you have a bit of time to hunt for it.  The water isn't REALLY deep but keep an eye on your meter if you don't have a fishbone earring or water-breathing equipment.  I have a swimming skill of 125 I think so the swim was not too bad, lower level swimmers will obvious have less underwater time and stamina so it may be close for some of you.  Anyway here is the final result of the quest for the oblong bottle:

Hope this helps and makes this a little less stressful for some of you.  It wasn't as bad as I thought it would be but If I had seen something like this before, it would have made it a lot easier so I have decided to add it to help you on your journey.
