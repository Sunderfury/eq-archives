What's us?
The charm of internet games is contact with person.
Indeed,if you play internet game alone,you might feel friends.
Specially,no way but internet game provides a contact point with people from abroad easily.
For this reason,we founded the FOX to play internet games with other people.
The main purpose of the guild is to have fun.That is first and foremost.
Of course we don't have ranks and free to join free to leave.
However,please keep to following regulations.
  • Don't blame people about country,religion and race.
  • If someone is in need,we have to help him at all cost.
  • Be polite than you thought as you ware.
  • Don't abuse game's bug to facilite our action.
  • Real life is most important. Don't let game interfere with your life.
We are waiting for people who can keep those.
