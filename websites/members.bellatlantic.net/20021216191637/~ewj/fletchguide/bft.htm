Blessed Faydark Thunderbolt Recipe

The wood elf cultural fletching looks quite nice, and I'll admit to wishing I could participate as well.  (Warning!  Digression Incoming!!......Why anyone would think that game play is somehow enhanced by making new and interesting tradeskill item recipes, and then restricting their creation and use to only a subset of players is beyond me....ok, hehe, now that I have gnashed my teeth on these familiar-by-now sour grapes for a bit, we can get on with things.  Digression over).

The fine folks over at EQ Traders are to be credited with this work, and I urge anyone interested in tradeskills to head over there.  In particular, Niami's coverage of the wood elf cultural fletching is much more complete than what I have here.
