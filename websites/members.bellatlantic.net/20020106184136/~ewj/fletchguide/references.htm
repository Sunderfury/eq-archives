References and Handy Links

The information for this page was taken from many sources, including my own experience to date as well as several other web sites.  Below is a list of excellent sites:

The Rangers Glade  Lots of excellent information, including articles, discussion forums, and more.  Definite bookmark material.
EQ Traders Corner  A truly amazing collection of information on every trade skill in Everquest.  This is the place to go when it's time to learn a new trade skill (after you've maxed out your fletching, of course...heh...)
EQ Atlas What ranger worth his or her salt is ever lost?  Anyone who hasn't been to this site, that's who...excellent maps.
Allakhazams  Excellent all around resource.  Impossible to spell.
Casters Realm  Great site not only for casters, but excellent news summaries as well of what's new and different in the world of Norrath.
