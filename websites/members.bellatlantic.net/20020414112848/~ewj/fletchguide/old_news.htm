What's New, 6-20-2001

Hehe, Verant snuck one in on us!  At some point in a recent patch Verant added the capability to restring your bow!.  This is actually documented in (of all places) the fletching book that discusses arrow recipes, rather than the one with bow recipes, as one might have guessed.

Changing strings is easy.  You simply place a completed bow into the fletch kit along with the new string and hit the combine key.  Initial testing indicates that restringing appears to become trivial at skill 15, but interestingly, seems to ALWAYS succeed.

What's New, 5-28-2001

Hooray!  Some excellent work by folks over at eqtraders.com has resulted in a possible breakthrough in the discovery of trivial values for darkwoods!  Gorthag Bashinfletcher has posted his results on the EqTraders message boards, and they look very interesting!  It appears that all darkwoods except compounds become trivial at 215, but compound darkwoods have a trivial value that is higher still.  If this turns out to be true, it will be a bit of a departure from the current prevailing theory that cams, which triv at 192, do not have an impact on the triv value of darkwood bows.  Apparently, the presence of the second cam has a higher triv value than one cam.

What's New, 9-22-2000

Wow!  The limit on fletching has been raised over 200, and data is beginning to come in!  At this point we know that (a) ceramic vanes and steel shafts are trivial at 202, (b) Trueshots are NOT trivial at 202, and (c) Darkwood is NOT trivial at 202.  Special thanks to Aurin, Arborealus Fletcher, and in particular Talisko Uisgebeatha, for going to the trouble of sending in the information as they discovered it.
Are steel shafts trivial at 201, or 202?  I need someone at skill 201 to check this (embarrassed look at still being skill 200).
The prospects for raising our collective fletching skills over 202 seem pretty grim - since all the arrows are trivial now, the only thing I'm aware of that would allow this would be Trueshots or Darkwood bows.  Ugh.  Remember how long it took to go from 199 to 200?  Can you imagine combining several hundred darkwood staves to get to 203?  Riiight....
One player on the E'ci server has been reporting to me that ceramic vanes and steel shafts are both trivial to him in the 180s, and to raise his skill he has been forced to use combinations of expensive components in order to create a non trivial arrow.  The obvious questions about possible reasons why this might only appear to be true have already been investigated, and it seems this fellow is experiencing something I've never heard of from anyone else.  So, has anyone else experience this type of behavior?  Please send me your specifics if you think you have related data.
Finally, Katorin has reported finding a Spectral Bow from a froglok hunter in Traks Teeth.  The data I have on this bow indicated that it dropped from Howling Stones, so either my initial data was wrong, or it drops in both, or it has been changed.

What's New, 9-15-2000

A major improvement in the determination of bow trivial values is presented here, courtesy of the sharp observations of several fletchers who postulated that just like arrows, perhaps bow complexity was driven by the complexity rating of the most complex component.  With this in mind, John Bashaw (Garulf Woolfetysh) has done some excellent research into this hypothesis and indeed confirmed exactly that.  Based on John's work, for the first time the trivial values for every bow combination are shown in the Bow Table.  Excellent work John, the fletching community owes you a debt of gratitude!
