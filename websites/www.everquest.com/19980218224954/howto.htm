How to Reach Us

For now, please email any questions or comments to everquest@sonyinteractive.com. We will also attempt to monitor The Adrenaline Vault's EverQuest Forum, comp.sys.ibm.pc.games.rpg, and any other applicable news groups, but if the volume of related threads becomes to high, we will have to return to responding to email or updating the FAQ as often as time permits.

Some people have reported problems accessing The Adrenaline Vault's EverQuest Forum with newer newsreaders such as Outlook Express (Microsoft Internet Explorer 4) and Netscape Communicator 4 -- the newsgroup appears blank. An alternate newsreader, such as Forte's Free Agent (Forte's Home Page), may be helpful for those users.
