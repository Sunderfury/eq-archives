The followers of Rallos Zek, The Warlord, believe in survival of the strong and death to the weak. Strength, courage, but above all, victory is what the heart of a true follower of Zek yearns for. In the heat of battle is the only place where enlightenment can be gained. The universe was formed by conflict and in conflict it will end. The victors feasting upon the remains of their fallen enemy. No respect or regard is given to the dead, for if they were worthy their hearts would still pump blood through their veins and not upon the soil of Norrath. Followers of Zek are almost exclusively warriors.
