About the Closed Beta

The Closed Beta will be the first EverQuest test to involve members of the general public. Select individuals chosen by the EverQuest team, as well as in-house testers, and the team itself, will participate in these trials. The EverQuest closed testing periods will be in several phases, each successive phase involving more and more people. In early March, we will begin accepting applications for all phases of closed testing.

*NOTE: we are NOT accepting applications for beta testing at this time. Please do NOT email us beta applications*
