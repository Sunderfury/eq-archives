Monsters

(incredible creatures, great in variety and appearance).

 Skulking Brute

Burrowing through the very crust of Norrath in search of prey, the Skulking Brute is a creature of unrelenting hunger. Many brave adventurers have fallen beneath a Brute's onslaught of hammering fists and gnashing teeth. From where the Skulking Brutes originate is unknown but rumors abound. Some say they are the creation of a mad Erudite wizard while others claim they were made by Brell Serilis himself. On one point all can agree: it is best to be where a Skulking Brute isn't.
Void Beast

If this creature even exists, most would argue it lives between the Planes, in the Great Void, leaving this place only to hunt. But since no mortal can survive that void, the location of the beast's lair must remain unproven. Legend says the magical creature teleports to its prey at night, stinging and poisoning with its tentacles. The victim, still able to slowly move, becomes then the toy of this feline monster, and eventually, of course, its meal.
Plague Dragon

The stench of rot surrounds the lair of a Plague Dragon and woe to any who stumble upon one uninvited. Pray that the dragon dissects you with talons and teeth, for if you are exposed to its breath your demise will be slow and excruciatingly painful. Your flesh will blacken and blister until it slides free from your bones. Yes, you'll find yourself wishing for a quick death by claw or fang.
Wyvern

Wyverns are distant cousins of dragons, somewhat similar in appearance, yet smaller and usually armless. While more intelligent than most creatures, they are far from possessing the intellect of their draconian kin. Wyverns are mute and merely fierce fighters, unlike dragons, which are capable of speech and even sorcery.
