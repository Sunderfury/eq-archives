Deities

(beings of great power, each ruling one of the Planes of Power and Influence).

 Innoruk, Prince of Hate

Innoruk is a tall and twisted being who rules the Plane of Hate. There is no safety in his world, no open spaces, only dark alleys and dwellings inhabited by the nastiest of creatures, including powerful undead. Legend says Innoruk is the father of the Dark Elves.
Fennin Ro, Tyrant of Fire

Fennin Ro rules the Plane of Fire, a forever blackened and burning mountainous landscape of heat. Its inhabitants are fire elementals and other like creatures. Fennin's son, Solusek Ro, rules the Plane of Flames.

  
Brell Serilis, Duke of Underfoot

The plane of Underfoot is ruled by this curious being. He rules over all sorts of creatures, good and evil, that normally dwell underground. Legend says he is also the creator of the Dwarves.
Rallos Zek, the Warlord

Rallos rules the Plane of War with an iron fist. His skin is jet black with visible pulsating lava-like veins. His world is a scene of constant strife and battle, corpses littering the ground, and the air dark and filled with smoke.
