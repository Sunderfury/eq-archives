Arcane Arts and Supreme Sorcerie

Many other sites focus on lists of spells and other spoilers regarding EQ's magic system, which has never been my intention.  You will learn plenty on your own; that is one of the greatest aspects of the game.

There are four schools of magic, which the evil Miragul studied incognito before becoming a lich. There are reagents for the more powerful spells, and the costs of spellcraft are proportional to the level achieved.

  • Necromancy the art of dealing with the dead. Speaking with spirits, stealing life from healthy opponents, and the animation and command of undead armies can be found within this school. It is studied by the Necromancer and the Shadow Knight.
  • Wizardry the art of raw magical power. It is the domain of those who wish instantaneous travel, the ability to scry over long distances, and can produce the most lethal and spectacular magical effects. This art has no rival in evoking directly damaging magic.
  • Sorcery. This is the art of summoning, bringing both servants and items to the caster to do his bidding. The strength of will necessary to command this form of magic ensures that its practitioners have the aptitude to even bring forth the living entities of the natural world known as the Elementals.
  • Enchantment. This is both the art of charming and enchanting other beings, as well as the art of magically enhancing items and weapons. With this art, one could charm a person into fighting for the caster, or one could add a dweomer to the aura of a broadsword, granting it a better chance of hitting.

There is arguably another school of magic, that of the magic granted through prayer and dutiful worship of a Deity. Clerics and Paladins eximplify this variety of magic.
