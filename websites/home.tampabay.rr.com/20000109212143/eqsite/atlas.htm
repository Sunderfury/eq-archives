The planet on which you live, grow, struggle, and possibly die is known as Norrath. There are rumors of possible lands beyond or below the sea, and even of extra dimensional space, but these notions are largely discounted by rational folk. (*Two words: Expansion Packs!*) These are the five known continents of Norrath which make up the known hemisphere.  Currently, on the continents of Odus, Antonica, and Faydwer are available for adventuring, but so are the Planes of Hate, Fear, and Air...

The mainland of Antonica was the original land in which Humans proved themselves. The harsh climate of the Northlands are home to the Barbarians, and some of the oldest settlements, such as Freeport and Qeynos, were created by Men. It is not exclusively the province of humanity; Ogres, Dark Elves, Trolls, and Halflings are also inhabitants of this continent.

The island of Odus was settled by the Erudites, searching for their own path, lands, and destinies. It is still the domain of the Erudites, or High Men.

Faydwer is home to the High Elves, Woodland Elves, Dwarves, and the Gnomish folk. It is a veritable province of demihumans.

Kunark is a land not to be trusted. It is unsettled, dangerous, and the source of many legends.

Velious is likewise uninhabited by mannish races, being a frigid, icy lair supposedly home to dragons and their ilk.
