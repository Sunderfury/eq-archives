RE: Beta Test

The Beta Test Applications was available Friday the 13th of March until October 28th, 1998.

Currently (October 28th):

The beta test application has been removed, because, as 989 Studios put it, they have received enough applications.  As of October 28th, they are about to email everyone who "correctly" filled out their beta application with information regarding Phase 4, which will begin in early December and will require a small fee.  It will NOT be subject to the Non-Disclosure Agreement.  They are also about to begin Phase 3, which will start in early November, with the NDA, for about 5,000 lucky participants.

Previously:

The official EQ Site states that in "early March" the application for beta testing will be available from their site. In a generous move, they will allow ANYONE who fills out an application form to participate in at least the final portion of the Closed Beta, before the open beta begins.

They will be conducting the testing process in stages, and plan to allow more "external testers" with each new stage. They are going to be very picky at first, and I for one don't expect to be involved until the later portions. They are looking for previous beta testing experience, MUD (multi- user dungeon) experience, and other online and traditional gaming experience.
