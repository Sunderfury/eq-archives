03 March 1998

The monthly chat with the EQ Team went over decently, apparently. Among the topics illuminated by Mr. McQuaid are that aerial combat will not be a part of the game, houses and boats owned by players witll be extremely rare, Ogres will be able to body slam foes and smash down doors, the worship of your deity will influence gameplay, and there is some seriously modern code being used to reduce lag. On the subject of lag, there was another mention of the number of players per server being limited to around 1000, thus reducing lag even further!

The monthly chat is IRC based, and takes place on Efnet, in channel #Everquest. I believe that there is also a newer #EQ channel, as well as an IRC based game inspired by EQ!
