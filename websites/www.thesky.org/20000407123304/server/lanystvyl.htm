lanys t'vyl news - March 23, 2000
THURSDAY


5:21pm CST Syruss Time for PoD to DIE!

Itzlegend Forever officer of the guild Soul Assassins was quite bored one Norrathian afternoon. He sent me a message through the lands that he wished to see our arch-enemy the Priest of Discord slain. Many trembled at the thought of messing with the Dark Priest and his 350+ damage staff whacked them on the head. Four brave adventurers were not afraid however. And knowing the stupidity of going face to face with the Leader of the Player Killers; Alyssa of Inner Circle; Kriall of Revelation; Itzlegend of Soul Assassins and myself your humble narrator quite unguilded decided to try anyway. Four people against the hardest hitter on the Norrath Plane. But verily we laid him low, once again showing the strength of the universe that is Lanys Tvyl.

Jait Swiftburner 50 Mage and scriber of deadly events

/ooc Congrats all, it was fun and the first priest slain on our server and possibly done with the least amount of people ever.


lanys t'vyl news - March 4, 2000
SATURDAY


2:56pm CST Syruss Silver Moon Event Recap

The Guild of the Silver Moon sends thanks to all who attended Friday's guild event. 100-150 adventurers took part in the 2 hour scouring of Greater Faydark. Guild Master Severas was much pleased by the return of guild items, which had been scattered about. For those who joined in and successfully found the wares, Severas was most generous with the bestowing of gifts. The Guild of the Silver Moon would also like to thank Everlore.com for spreading the word across the lands. Twas our first event and deemed a great success, which could not have been accomplished without your help.

Good Citizens of Norath, look for more fun, frivolous, and roleplaying events in the future from the Guild of the Silver Moon. Hunt Well till next we meet!

Regards,
Nickelle Daughter of the Silver Moon
