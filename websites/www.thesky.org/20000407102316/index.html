Cazic-Thule: Weekend News

Here's what's happening on the Cazic-Thule Server this weekend:


  • CTC Raffle: Forty great items have already been donated for the April raffle, but we're always looking for more! To view a list of currently donated items check here: Raffle Information The April raffle will be held this Saturday, April 8th in North Ro - tickets will go on sale at 12:00 pm PST with the drawing for all prizes and the GRAND PRIZE of all plat collected around 4:00 pm PST. You might remember that the March Raffle's grand prize was over 13,000 plat!

  • Norrathian Senate: The Norrathian Senate, a newly formed guild council on the Cazic-Thule server will be holding its first event this Saturday the 8th. We will be having a tournament in The Arena zone off of Lake Rathe. More information can be found at Cazic-Thule Corner.

    All are welcomed at the Corner!

    ~Aracnakat
