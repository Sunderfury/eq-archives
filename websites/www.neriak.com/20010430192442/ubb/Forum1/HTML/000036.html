Teir'Dal Brothers and Sisters,

Recently so, His Majesty King Naythox Thex was seen in the city of Neriak (Innoruuk Server). I regret that I wasn't there to greet his Majesty, or be humbled in his presence. Our King was involved with sacrificing a pale elf, in the 3rd Quarter. I can provide pictures, if contacted. My confusion goes thusly:


Our Majesty, as named in the pictures I have (unretouched) bears the name Naythox. Curiously, when dispatching Kizdean Gix in the west commonlands, our King is named Aythox Thex. To make matters more confusing, I have seen our Queen mentioned as Christanosthex (missing the "i"), and also as Christianos (no Thex-suffix). When in our home city, please take the time to inquire "Who is King?" or "Who is Queen?", and be amazed at the answers you will get.

Now, I have been told by others, that Aythox Thex is the King of the High Elves (not what I know, that Tearis Thex actually is). The person who claimed this seemed adamant upon this farcity.

I am desirous of compiling informations you may have obtained regarding this subject, and/or discussing here.

What I may theorize, is this: Since we know that our majesties were transformed by our Prince of Hate, from High Elf, to Dark Elf; could we assume that their names were different before the transformation? The family suffix "Thex" perhaps must be a mark of royalty, leaving the suffix out of the question, could there actually be a reason for this inconsistency? Since several Neriak guards report that King Naythox is on a mission in Felwithe, could our majesty be travelling incognito?

May Hate guide thy purpose,
