Bank - Not sure but I am 90% sure that you can bank in Neriak Commons.

Train - Nope. Not even if you worship Innoruuk. You will need to work up your faction with The Dead and even then the Ghouls at the entrance to the guild will be KOS to you.

Buy/Sell - Yes, in the Foreign Quarter anyway. Just don't go into the Arena in that area. At one end is an Ogre that hates trolls and at the other is a troll that hates Ogres. As a Dark Elf Necromancer, I ahve no trouble moving about freely in this area and have done a LOT of CRs for newbies that didn't know better.

Hope this helps.
