Well, I'm an Iksar Necro, and I've been working my DE faction quite a bit so I can have a "mainland" bank.

Here's what I did:

Step 1) Beg, borrow, or steal as many Lightstones and Greater Lightstones as you can. I went to Erud's crossing and killed wisps until my inventory was full.

Step 2) Go to S. Ro and give the GLSs and LSs to Smith T'Vysa. She'll reward you with one of three things . . . a Concordance of Research (for a GLS), or 1 of 2 books. Each one you give her will net you positive faction hits with Dread Guard Inner, Dread Guard Outer, and Dark Bargainers.

Step 3) Go buy 4 backpacks full of red wine. Get someone to invis you and go to the library in Neriak 3rd Gate. From there, find the NPC who's looking for red wine. Give him 4 stacks of 4 bottles each time. This will also raise your factions (DGO, DGI, and DB). After doing the above, I was at dubious to the guards and warmly to the dark bargainers.

Step 4) Run past the Clerics Guild . . . I'm still KOS to them.

Hope that helps . . .
