I have finally gotten into paineel. Here is how I did it. I killed guards till they stopped giving me faction. I became dubious with paineel and was able to get past the guards and use the bank but the vendors still wouldnt sell to me. I then did a low level cleric quest...at first I didnt get any experience for it...so I waited a day and killed more guards. Once I started to get experience and faction from the low level quests I moved onto the Summoning of Terror quest. After finishing this quest I was indifferent to the guards. A few more quests and I became amiable. Once I was amiable I was able to ask the guards near the elevator for a key. Say "I need a key". Now I can get in whenever I want.
