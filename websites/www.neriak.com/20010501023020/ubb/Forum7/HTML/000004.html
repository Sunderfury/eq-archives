In addition to the lack of SK quests (and Evil quests in general), does anybody besides me think the Reaper of the Dead quest is too tough for the weapon it yields?

In order to do the quest you need a good balanced party of people in their low twenties to take down Commander Windstream. The elf skellies aren't tough (I can solo them at level 21), but Windstream is a different story.

When you turn in the Thex Mallet, you get the Reaper of the Dead, a so-so 2H slash weapon (12dmg, 40dly, +4 STR, 1 charge complete heal). The charge of complete heal is nice, but seriously, what SK in their high teens or low 20s is going to really use this as his or her main weapon? By this point you should probably be using something like a Blackened Iron Bastard Sword or better.

It seems to be a trend that by the time you can acquire a weapon or item by yourself, you've already bought better stuff. Just for once I'd like to loot or quest for something that I can actually use, not just sell off to a lower-level player.
