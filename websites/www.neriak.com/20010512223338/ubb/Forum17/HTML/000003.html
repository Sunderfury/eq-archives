A Treatment of Deeds Teir'Dal
A State of Evil
on Being Spurned

Afore time mentioned by most, our Prince of Hate did create us in his way. All milk and honey turns sour left out in the cold. Feir'Dal rue the day they were born, everyday they look upon our face. Once and for all, no mistake can be made, we live to Hate our Birth, we exist to be the scourge, may it never end.

Once we were free to live, and come out of the dark womb of Neriak, we knew pain. After we saw the Fire, magic of the Dead left our eyes. For our efforts, we learned the art of destruction, the power of explosions, and the taste of raw energy. Her Majesty saw our desire, and cast us away. So, once again, Teir'Dal are shunned away from our purpose. We were Spurned from Her eyes, never to recover our bones, never to subsist from Death. Cast ye away thoughts of Necromancy, we are powered by Flame and Guise.

Wizards, Magicians, and Enchanters unite. Join us Mage, to know the conjurations that bring to hand the weapon of the moment, elemental power extended by your familiar's fist. Enchanter, join us, to know the evil of deceit, and twist the minds of our enemies, that they cannot think to act. Wizards, join us with your Flame asunder, throwing ashes of doubt upon the lands to conquer.

We are proof against the sword of good and right. Now it is time to make the rest of Norrath pay for forgetting our Prince. Once and for all, we can't stand to see "High Elves" strut across the soil. Teir'Dal should have been the first Elf, nothing else matters.

Evil is just a name for what we do with our distaste. Ego is a word misused for negative thinkers. EVERY advantage to commit deeds underfoot, break the workings of our enemies, is an Evil act fueled by our Hate. Our reasons are clear to us. Become enraged by our past, become who you really are, a Dark Elf owed alot of revenge.

Spurned is our place to fight from, the Fury everlast. Long fear the King.

Loristatt Cave'Lupum
