Well nobody replied, but I went to Faydwer yesterday for a guild hunt in Unrest. Here are my thoughts on traveling/hunting there.

I arrived in Butcher Block via a druid teleport. I was invisible when I arrived, and had SoW as well. We started running around, and I ran by a guard thinking I was safe with invis--WRONG! The guard saw through my invis and started pounding on me. I'm not sure if someone buffed the guard with See Invisible or not, but I was running around like mad with SoW trying to stay away from him. I finally made it to the Greater Faydark zone with a bubble of health left, training about 15 guards behind me.

I got a bind in Greater Faydark, just inside the zone. Not once did I ever see a guard inside there, since I stayed close to the zone.

For hunting in Unrest or Dagnor's Cauldron, this seems to be the best place to bind. You can zone back to Butcherblock and then head south with the wall on your left. I never had any problems with guards betweeen Greater Faydark and Dagnor's Cauldron.

In Dagnor's Cauldron, I was KOS to the NPCs at the small camp just south of the zone from Butcherblock. I avoided them with no problems. One tip--get Levitate cast on you if at all possible when traveling in DC. The ground is EXTREMELY broken and you will constantly be falling for small amounts of damage if you try to travel very fast at all.

There is a merchant in DC that you can sell too. He's a named dwarf on the top of the mountain in the middle of the lake. I think he sells stuff for making poison. I conned indifferent to him. There was another Dwarf NPC that was wandering around the island--stay clear of him. He killed my partner Aundrea when she came over to sell. The dwarf you want is stationary on the top of a hill, not roaming around.

Unrest was fun, although I died three times. I was getting horrible lag inside the house, and my deaths were the result of higher level people training tough stuff on top of us. I simply wasn't able to react in time to get out of the way. Also, the tentacle terrors in Unrest are MUCH tougher than the tt's in Najena. Also, don't mess with the named gnome necromancer (Khrix something)--he's level 35 and can smack you down VERY fast.

For the return trip back to Antonica, I traveled through Butcherblock very carefully while invisible. I tried to get another teleport, but was unsuccessful. I hung out near the docks, trying to stay away from any NPCs or guards in case my invis faded. Fortunately, the boat came fairly quickly, and I was able to get on the boat while invisible. From there on it was just a routine boatride back to Freeport.

Hopefully this will help anyone that is thinking of traveling to/from Faydwer. I'd still be interested in hearing suggestions from other people.
