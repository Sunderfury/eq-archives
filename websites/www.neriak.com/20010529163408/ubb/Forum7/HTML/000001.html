Crusaders spawn very rarely, but in my experience they most frequently spawn in areas in the following order:
sewers (best), upper tae, 4c top (worst)

I'm level 27 now, and have been in Cazic-Thule almost exclusively since level 22, except for last weekend when I took a break to kill shadowed men and a nice evening hunting in Solusek's Eye.

I've only gotten once piece of Darkforge so far (the breastplate) and I've got three decayed pieces in the bank. I'm just now starting to get to the point where I can join the groups where the crusaders spawn (instead of running like mad to loot crusaders when I'm the only SK in the zone).

I highly recommend heading there early, so you can learn the zone. It does get boring quick, but once you learn the good spots it's nice steady experience and great loot. I usually make about 50pp a night minimum from the plat dropped from the lizards, as well as the fine steel weapons. I also have a nice Lizard Scale Cloak now that I won the roll for in the lower throne room.

One thing I've learned that isn't mentioned much is that the Freeport Militia Helms needed for the Darkforge Helm are actually labeled "Damaged Militia Helm." All I need is a Decayed Helm (already have the visor) and I will have my Darkforge Helm.
