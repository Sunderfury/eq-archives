That my friend is easy.

The Freeport Militia are corrupt and we are not hated in East and West Freeport (where the docks are). We can simply stroll up to the dock with ease.

As far as getting off at the Sisters Isle or Butcherblock, it's also easy. Swim.

For Sisters, keep looking straight off the righthand side of the ship after you zone into OOT. When you see the dock coming up, jump off and swim for land long before you get to the dock.

For Butcherblock, do the same thing, except it will come up on the lefthand side of the ship.

To get back, either use Gate, or there is a trick where you swim AT the boat as it approaches you... You'll go under the bow sort of, and if you hit it right, you'll pop into the boat. Or if you have Gather Shadows, simply use that -- it's far easier.
