When it comes to the basics, once you get new spells (ie..8 lvl) you can summon a lvl higher (ie...9 lvl) pet, but you will mostly get lvl 7 and such. So when you get a new pet at 12...you can get a 13th lvl pet. The chances of getting a white conning pet just before making a new spell lvl are nil, they'll alway be blue then.
