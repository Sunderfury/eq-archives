Greetings. A year and a few months ago I founded an evil guild. My intention was to create a strict evil guild with an emphasis on roleplay. I decided before creating the guild that we would be open to all roleplayers who played characters that were undeniably evil.

i.e. we allowed all trolls, ogres and dark elves as well as humans, erudites and gnomes who played necromancers or shadowknights.

Eventually we became a little bit more lenient, allowing all clerics of Innoruuk, Cazic Thule and Bertoxxulous, but only after deity specific items were added to the game and such characters had an easy way to prove their allegiance to the dark gods. We also immediately accepted Iksar upon their arrival to the world.

I suppose I am posting here because I wonder how many guilds like ours exist out there. We have remained true to our original roleplay and we have grown fairly strong. Our main problems have been that due to our class/race limitations we have next to no wizards or enchanters. Both of which are necessities for major raids. (not to mention our obvious lack of bards/rangers/druids or any other tracking class! damn you verant!)

Our levels have become strong. We are a guild that has a player base of at least 50 members and most are 40+. We should soon be ready for major raids but we cannot seem to find dark elf enchanters or wizards that are willing to join a roleplay guild. To further my frustration, due to our inability to accomplish any major raids because of our lack of enchanters and wizards, over time we have lost many of our high level members to your run of the mill uber-guilds. =(

I suppose at this point I'm looking to hear there are other guilds out there who have struggled with this and eventually moved past it and accomplished those things they'd like to accomplish. Also I suppose I'd like to invite like minded players to join us on Terris-Thule in the Bloodhorde. Look us up at www.bloodhorde.com

Please. let me know that you're all out there fighting the same fight.

Hmmm, a few points...

Firstly, odd that you are having a problem finding Enchanters. Dark Elves do make the best Enchanters, and most Enchanters I run into are DEs.

As far as Wizards go, there's a lot of DE wizards floating around as well.

Secondly, there's a good number of guilds like yours around - probably 1-2 per server. It sounds as though yours is having the best luck, though. Both evil-only guilds on Xegony are perpetually low on members, and the members they do have seem to mostly hover around the level 30-40 range. Sure for roleplay that's fine, but as far as actually "doing" anything in EQ, that's not going to cut it of course. So, the high levels they DO have either join in on "pickup" raids, or end up leaving the RP guild for a gaming guild.

Sad, but true...

Though, one can roleplay and enjoy it without being in a roleplay guild. Join a guild for gaming, and roleplay when and with whom you find it =)
