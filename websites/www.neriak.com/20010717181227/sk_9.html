Level 9 Spells:
Disease Cloud
Mana: 10
Skill: Conjuration
Cast Time: 1.5 seconds
Recover Time: 6 seconds
Damage Over Time: 5
Components: None
Description: This spell causes a damage over time. It is a weak spell, but it is a nice addition to a shadowknight's arsenal at first.

Invisibility versus Undead
Mana: 40
Skill: Divination
Cast Time: 4 seconds
Recover Time: 4 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell renders the target invisible to undead.

Leering Corpse
Mana: 40
Skill: Conjuration
Cast Time: 6 seconds
Recover Time: 9.5 seconds
Components: Bone Chip (1)
Description: This spell summons a 5th-6th level skeleton as a pet for the shadowknight.

Lifetap
Mana: 9
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 1.5 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Healing/Damage: 3 to 5
Components: None
Description: This spell taps into the life force of the target and drains some of their hit points.

Locate Corpse
Mana: 5
Skill: Divination
Cast Time: 1.5 seconds
Recover Time: 3 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell locates the closest corpse within a certain radius.

Sense the Dead
Mana: 5
Skill: Divination
Cast Time: 2 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell locates the closest undead creature within a certain radius.

Siphon Strength
Mana: 5
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 1.5 seconds
Recover Time: 6 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell sucks 5 to 10 strength from the target and adds it to the shadowknight's strength temporally.
