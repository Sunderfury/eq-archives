This quest is very difficult, if not downright impossible, for any class other than Enchanters. It requires you to be able to travel freely and interact with NPCs in four "good" cities (Freeport, Rivervale, Qeynos, and Erudin), as well as one "evil" city (Oggok).

This quest is very easy for Enchanters because they have Illusion spells that allow them to appear as different races, plus the Alliance spell that raises faction.

I'm not saying it's impossible for another class to do this quest--with some very rare and valuable magic items it might be possible. By the time you're high enough level to acquire these items though the Stein would probably be a waste of your time, since you could just buy one.
