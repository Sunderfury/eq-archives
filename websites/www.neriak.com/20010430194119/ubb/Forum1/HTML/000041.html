If your any type of Iksar and REALLY need to sell or buy some food(you usually don't because your foraging skills should keep you going), you should go to the Kerra Isle. The cats will always sell to you unless you have killed a lot of them. This works for any other race that has a problem with Qeynos. Now, you may ask about exactly HOW you expect to get on the boat. Well, you can INVIS yourself if you are a Necromancer, OR, if you are a Shadow Knight, Shaman, or Warrior, you can do the following: Go to the zone border where you exit the Qeynos Catacombs and appear only a few couple of feet away from the dock and the ship dock. Then you need to wait for the boat to come. After the boat comes, swim around the tip(front) of it. After the boat begins to move, you will automatically be pushed onto the ship. This will take you to Kerra Isle. You MIGHT need to practice a few times before you get it right, but it shouldn't be too hard.
ALSO, Qeynos Catacomb Evil Guild is the EASIEST faction possible anywhere. All you have to do is go to Qeynos Hills, kill a couple of clerics, and you should in no time be indifferent to all the guild masters. You could also do the Prayer Bead(not broken) Quest and/or the Order of Thunder(necklace) quest to help raise your faction. I'm almost positive this is THE EASIEST way to find a guild anywhere on Antonica(or any other continent for that fact) quick. Now... if your a Monk that's another story... Good luck fellow Iksars!
