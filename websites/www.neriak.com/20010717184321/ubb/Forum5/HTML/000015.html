Just a note to all you DE Necros...

At level 16, you will never get a pet higher than 16. I am assuming that is how it will be from there on out. So for you to con your pet to realize what level he is, simply ask someone that is lower level to tell you what color he is to them. Because at level 18, my pet cons blue but I do not know if it's 15 or 16.

Also, Sevok Celith'ar, Teir'dal Necromancer of Darkmoon states in his guide on this site, that to get your premium pet you should "/pet get lost" and recast your pet spell. NEVER...I repeat NEVER do this. It is a huge waste of mana that does not need to be lost. When creating your pet, always have Reclaim Energy memmed as well. Use this spell to get some of your lost mana back if you did not get the pet you hoped for. It will save greatly on downtime in the long run.
