This is a topic that's sort of near and dear to me.

I played UO from Day 1 of its final release pretty much up thru now. Sure, there have been some breaks, and some of them quite long -- all veteran UO players have taken those I think -- but I've followed it even while I wasn't playing.

After a short stint as a good guy, I pretty much have always played evil characters in UO. I helped form the guild The Order of the Ebon Skull on the Atlantic shard, and we pretty much introduced the concept of roleplaying evil to the Shard.

Sure, there were "Evil" characters in the form of PKs, but they were never roleplayed. OES brought evil roleplaying to Atlantic.

That was... ages ago. Now, there is no open PKing due to stat loss for Murderers. Now, there are two lands, one of which has open PVP capability at all.

So - how are evil roleplayers handling all the changes? What avenues are people exploring to handling conflict when open PVP isn't available?

One great solution of course is the Catskills shard. There are several hundred excellent and strict roleplaying guilds there and most have declared War on each other so that open PVP is always a possibility if the roleplay leads to violence.
