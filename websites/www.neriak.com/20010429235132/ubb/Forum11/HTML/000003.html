A favorite hunting spot for all my Dark Elven characters has been the gate outside East Freeport. Being only 2 zones away from Neriak it is a pretty short walk for Level 1 characters. The biggest plus here is that the player to monster ratio is terrific (especially in comparison to Nektulos) and most of the loot is stackable, meaning you can spend a good deal of time here before making the walk back home. Strategy here is simple... keep your shielding spell up, blast a creature with a DD and melee it to death. Can do this one while semi-comatose

Around Level 4 or 5 you will start to receive little to no experience from creatures in most newbie areas. It's now time to head back to your homeland of Neriak / Nektulos and make your way to the Skeleton Ruin (near East Commons zone) or Undead Pyramid. Here you'll find spawns of mostly skeletons and the occasional zombie and lesser mummy. At level 4 - 5 I recommend you group until you can make level 6 of skeletons, unless your confident in your skills. After that you should be able to solo skeletons all the way to level 8, though I found it comes much quicker if you group up with 1 or two other people (plus you'll have company while waiting for the next spawn, always a plus ). If a zombie or lesser mummy spawns you should either make a cooperative effort with the other people there to kill it, or try to find a higher level in the zone to come knock it off for you.

After level 8 you can go into the East Commonlands and solo white, yellow, and even low red black bears, kodiaks, and orcs. If you do orcs I once again recommend a small group.
