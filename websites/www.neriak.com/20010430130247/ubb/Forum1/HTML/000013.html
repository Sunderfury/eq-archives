Wow, this city looks really good. Read the patch message though, they are not kidding about the low frame rate though, most people were reporting about 2 frames per sec. The dancing skeletons were popular, as were the ghosts in the Necromancer's guild.

Erudite Necromancers, Shadow Knights, and Clerics of Cazic-Thule can start here. There is a newbie area in the zone, but the monsters are super-buffed compared to normal. I did 76 pts to a decaying skeleton, and didn't even do 1 bubble of damage.

There are teleporters to move you around the city, but they are vertical mirror-like things, rather than the horizontal gems in Erudin. There is also an elevator to take you to the outside of the city and the rest of the world, it makes the Kelethin elevators look like little stick thingies.

The Hole. You'll know it when you see it. It's a big frickin' hole in the ground, follow the ledge around to find the newbie area, and Tox forest.
