I'm surprised nobody has mentioned the two SK/Necro quests given by Loveal S'Nez on the third floor of the SK/necro guild in Neriak Third Gate.

The first quest is to kill Sir Lindeal. He spawns in Lavastorm, and seems to be in the low-to-mid teens, level-wise. You loot Sir Lindeal's Testimony and return it to Loveal S'Nez.

Upon completing this quest, you are given a chance to do the Thex Mallet quest. You speak to Kizdean Gix in West Commonlands, saying the magic words "Utalk adarev nitco." Gix will then give you a note to take to the Skeleton L'rodd on the first floor of Befallen. Giving the note to L'rodd makes Commander Windstream (a level 20-something zombie) spawn on the third floor of Befallen. Killing Windstream gives you the Thex Mallet 2-item container, and you must kill the elf skeletons on the 3rd level of Befallen to get the other two pieces. Combining these pieces gives you a Thex Mallet, which you can then return to Loveal S'Nez for usually a Reaper of the Dead.

I've done the Sir Lindeal quest twice, and the Thex Mallet quest once. More details can be found on other sites, or if anyone's interested I can re-post ALL of the details here. However, this should be enough to get you started. :-)
