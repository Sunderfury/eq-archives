So you wish to gain fame and glory with your people? The road to fame is not always an easy one.


The Lodge of the Dead
Loveal S`Nez
A paladin in the Lavastorm mountains by the name of Sir Lindeal has compromised trade routes that are important to Neriak. You are commanded to find him and slay him. He is around the mid to high teens in season and wanders generally around the left hand side of the entrance, if he is there. (specific faction and reward information unknown)


Noxhil V`Sek
Part of the menial tasks that need to be done by all young ones is gathering various research components for their tutors. Noxhil V`Sek requires 10 fire beetle eyes for his experiments. Fire Beetles are found in Nektulos forest and are about level 2-3. You can also find the eyes on vendors for a few silver each. (specific faction and reward information unknown)


The Hall of the Ebon Mask
Pazin Punox
The king has commanded that Pazin find someone to teach a halfing, Lil Honeybugger, a lesson. Pazin instructions you to go to Misty Thicket beyond the Great Wall to find her, kill her and return her head to him. He also mentions that she's usually accompanied by her brother... Go to Misty Thicket, and find the two. Rumor has it that her brother is about level 12ish so bring friends. =) Kill her, return the head for your 'reward'. Unless this quest has changed you get about 3 copper and Pazin laughs at you and tells you that he just wanted her killed because he'd gotten her pregnant with his child. (specific faction and reward information unknown, but you take a huge halfling hit for killing her and her brother.)


Hekzin G`Zule
The Hall of the Ebon Mask are working on a new type of armor, and so they're calling for new recruits to gather items for it. You need to get three moss snake skins and three snake fangs and combine them in the bag he gives you. Your reward will be the Dark Shield of Ebon. This shield can be worn in the back slot, or the shield slot. The following faction hits result: Raised: Ebon Mask;  Lowered: Wolves of the North, Carson McCabe, Qeynos Guards, Guardians of the Vale.  (submitted by Szardos)


Tani N`Mar
He needs you to run to freeport and seek out Giz Dunree down near the docks. You are to tell her Tani sent you and await further instruction. (no further information known, yet.)


Eolorn J`Axx
The Hall has rogues in many cities, and Eolorn needs someone to retrieve the Foreign take. The last youth he sent to retreve the chest seems to have met with an untimely death, you will deliver a note to Giz and await further instruction. (no further information known, yet.)


The Spires of Innoruuk
Perrir Zexus
As a new member of the Spires of Innoruuk, you are required to obtain and wear the symbol of your station and faith. Find four holy symbols of the Halfling druids that infest our forest for your hematite symbol of Innoruuk. Go out there into the forest and kill the Druids (NOT the leatherfoot deputies, which are warriors). You *may* get jumped by the Leatherfoot Medic which can randomly spawn near them (he's killed a level 32 necro before, so be careful!). Gather four of their bracelets and you gain the Hematite Symbol of Innoruuk. The Symbol of Innoruuk has 3AC, 1 WIS, 3 MANA. (specific faction information unknown at this time)
Ithvol K`Jasn
If you are an initiate, Ithvol has a task for you. The halflings that invest our forest somehow seem to anticipate our next moves. Find Master Whoopal, slay him and bring the brain to Draxiz N'Ryt so that he may analyze the brain. (no further information known)


If you are a disciple, Ithvol has a task for you. King Naythox Thex wishes to expand our empire to Faydwer, and there is a band of Teir`dal there that depends on us to bring them supplies, since they are always under attack from the lesser Feir'Dal and Koada'Dal. You must go with haste and tell Dran Slug Rembor in the Foreign Quarter that Ithvol sent you. (submitted by Saver) (no further information known)
