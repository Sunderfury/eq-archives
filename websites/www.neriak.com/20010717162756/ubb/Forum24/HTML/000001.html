I've been in to massively multiplayer online gaming since UO's release (September 1997) - I never really got into online gaming before UO because, for some reason, I just can't get into a game where there's only like 4 players. *shrugs* I just love the possibility of random interactions between people that MMOG affords.

The way I see it, there's only three games out right now in this milieu: UO, EQ, and AC. Sure there's some others, but they're too small to notice. =)

Shadowbane will be out semi-soon, and that looks very promising.

I had VERY high hopes for Middle Earth, being a ME fan since my childhood, but... The project was essentially killed over disputes between the Dev Team and Management. =(

So... what are people playing? What games are people really looking forward to?

I'm planning to check out Shadowbane, hopefully I can manage to get into the Beta (I've been in the Beta for AC and EQ both). It looks like it should be a lot of fun.

I currently play all three games, with UO currently taking most of my gaming time (it shifts based on the amount of work my wife has to do for our business; she doesn't play UO but plays EQ and AC with me).
