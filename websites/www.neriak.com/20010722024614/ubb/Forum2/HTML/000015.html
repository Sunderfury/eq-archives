Actually, if you're careful in North Freeport and if you're an agnostic and maybe a few other religions, you can bank in North Freeport with no faction adjustments. Just don't get too comfy in the city, since there's KOS Npcs scattered pretty liberally through the city. I dunno about other religions, but my necromancer friend was not KOS in the majority of East and West Freeport. I tend to kill any deathfist orc I walk by, so that's solved somewhat.

Also (I don't know if this works for any other religions) if you take a load of bandages (About 100) with you to Misty Thicket, you can secure a banking and selling spot in Rivervale. Since I've been hunting King Xorbb's Gorge, East K, and Runnyeye, I'm dubious to warmly in MOST of Rivervale, except for the clerics who are KOS. Just be careful not to do any other quests in Rivervale other than the bandages otherwise you WILL end up KOS in Neriak. Also an added bonus of killing goblins is Butcherblock will open up a little bit to you.

Qeynos above ground, I don't know about, but Bandits, cheap quests, and Gnolls should fix that pretty quick. There's a bank in the Catacombs too in the temple of Bertoxx, I believe. Again, be careful.

For the realms of the Lesser 'Dal, I've seen a Teir'Dal warrior go from KOS to Warmly in Kelethin by slaughtering orcs. Just be prepared to switch guilds and run from the Fairy Folk.

*thinks* That's all I can think of for light races.

Ah the great subject of banking. I am making the assumption that you are a DE Necro here.
In queynos you will have no problems banking. There is a banker in the sewers for the followers of innoruuk.
FP is a big nono no matter what, even if you raise your faction enough to bank (which you can't do by default) 1/2 of the NPC walking around in NFP are KOS to you and you have virtually no way to raise this. Stay away from anyone with 'Sir' or 'Lady' in front of their names no matter what. Also you can bank in Oggok for sure and Grobb probably (although I have not tried never had to).

In short from the start you can bank in Neriak, Oggok, Queynos. with just a bit of work (not all that much) you can use the normal bank in Queynos as well.
