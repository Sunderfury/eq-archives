The time is coming! A new PvP server that is teams, but is based on deity. These 3 teams are Good, Evil, and Neutral.

Here are a few of the new rules (which may change) for this server:

One character per account

No holds barred pvp. A level 60 can slaughter a level 1.

You can't attack or kill someone from your own team.

You can't be healed or buffed by someone from another team.

Personally, I think this is the perfect scenario for pvp. Finally, all evil races and classes can come together in the fight against all.

I am a co-founder for a new guild that will be starting on the new pvp server. It is called Nefastus Deus. This will be an evil guild (obviously) and although we will slaughter as many Good and Neutrals as we can, we hope to do it with great roleplaying (not required hehe) finesse. Something to separate us from the other mindless pvp guilds on the zeks.

At the above link, the password to enroll is "discord". NOTE: This isn't to get into the guild, but to just get into the site and see what we are about. Once you have entered the password, you will be prompted to supply a username and password. Username can be anything at this point, but will change to character names when the guild starts. Then you have to wait for approval. Once approved, you will have access to the website and it's MANY features.

One of the features is our Forums where questions can be asked (or just reply to this message).

Is this a recruiting message? Yes it is! This is a chance for teams based on beliefs to actually stick together.

As of now, the only evil deities we are positive about are Bertox, Cazic Thule, and Innoruuk. This may change when verant lets us know the official word. But just from those 3 deities, you would be suprised at how many evil characters can be made!

Feel free to register at the site or reply to here. Thanks for your time! nefastusdeus.guildworld.com/
