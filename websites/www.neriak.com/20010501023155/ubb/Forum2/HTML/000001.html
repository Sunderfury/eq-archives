I posted this under another thread, but thought it important enough to mention here again.

I HIGHLY recommend any evil race character taking the time to raise their faction enough so that they are not KOS in Highpass.

For one thing, this zone is a very important when traveling between the east and west sides of Antonica. There are ways to avoid Highpass (by going through Runnyeye) but it doesn't really solve anything since you now have to travel through (ACK!) Rivervale, home of the Knee Biters. Some people might be content with spending their entire EverQuest life in only a few zones, but I love traveling and seeing new places. Having to sneak through Highpass with invisibility is a major pain in the neck, especially if you have to rely on others for the invis.

The next benefit to hunting in Highpass is the experience. WOW! I went up three levels (from 17 to 20) in a very short period of time hunting mostly at the Orc spawn. It's a free-for-all zone, which means you should try to get into a STRONG group in order to get the kills.

The final benefit applies mainly to you skilled craftspeople out there. Highpass has almost EVERYTHING it seems when it comes to trade skills! Whether you want to work on smithing, tailoring, pottery, or whatever...it seems like you can buy ALL the rare and difficult-to-find materials here.

So there you have it. Three reasons why I highly recommend hunting in Highpass in your high teens and low twenties. If you stay by the Orc spawn while you're still KOS, you can fight with a relative degree of safety since the nearest NPC is a "Volunteer" that conned VERY green to me at level 17. He stays put unless someone trains him to the zone, and even then he cannot really hurt you much. He's also handy for quick "faction checks". Ease around the corner, target him from a distance, and con him to see if you've gone from "glaring threateningly" to "glares at you dubiously".

Good hunting, and may your Hate keep you strong.
