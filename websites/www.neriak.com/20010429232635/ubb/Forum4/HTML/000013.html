I am of the Rodcet Nife Teir'Dals and I can say with certainty that there is but little Roleplaying done on Rodcet.. sadly..

The few people I know.. like myself.. that have been longtime tabletop roleplayers that have come to this realm to continue doing what they love have found that the mass of hack'n'slash/ubermonger players in the world do not want to do anything but get buffs/sows/ports/and stuff..

I know I.. and several of my friends.. have tailored our Roleplaying to the groups we find ourselves in.. we start to Roleplay it out.. and as long as we don't receive messages like "what the smgt are U talking like that 4?" etc.. we continue roleplaying and get the group to as well.. which is wonderful when it happens..

it happened to me just recently in the free-for-all zone of High Hold pass.. where but a few people go there for any other reason but "exp and loot".. I myself was there to aid the guards in their duties... secretly at first.. but then more and more gaining their trust ( I am now only apprehensive to them YAY ) and the scrounging group I had aligned myself with were there for nothing more than "the named spawn" who "drops the smgt loot".. a young Teir'Dal cleric sister and I were chatting back and forth between ambushes of the orcs that tried to use the pass... and after a few moments.. the rest of the members of the group were joining in.. and I was very proud of them all.. RPing with us..

So.. although my server doesn't normally Roleplay.. if exposed to it.. and if you don't give up.. maybe the non-believers can be swayed to the truth and the true way~! ^_^

So.. if you come to Rodcet... look me up.. I am the wee little Teir'Dal and her guild trying to change the realm of Rodcet..
