There is a very good guest for raising merchant faction in Freeport that is easily available to dark races (starts in tunnels below the city and you probably won't need to pass by any agressive NPCs). The following are the details from Allakhazam's Site (clickable).

Note for Rebby: This is a good quest to raise faction with the Freeport Militia and Corrupt Qeynos Guards and not lower any other factions. I would suggest you do it along with the note for Janam quest since both go to and from the same places. Nestral T'Gaza, who is in the tunnels, has need of a helper. She wants you to take a note to Rebby, who owes her a debt. Rebby is located in West Freeport next to the Theater of the Tranquil. Upon delivery of the note, you will get Rebby's rat wiskers, which you can deliver back to T'Gaza for experience, faction and some money. This quest modifies the following factions: coalitionoftradesfolk, coalitionoftradesfolkIII, CarsonMcCabe, corruptqeynosguards and freeportmilitia all go up. I have been told that after doing this a few times, you will no longer get faction adjustments. (Submitted by Rezo)

Hope this helps.

Keep doing that Nestral TGaza quest over and over again - So far, the Militia regards me as indifferent, and the merchants in Freeport regard me as 'warmly'. It's fun!

If you need help- that little description on Allakhazam is of NO help. I mean, at least it gives the general idea of where she is, but not enough information.

Enter East Freeport by going through Northern Ro. When you get into East Freeport Desert, where the rats, bats, orcs, and all the newbie players are, head to your left wall, and keep walking straight down that wall. (You'll see a bunch of campfires, wagons, and tents)

Walk into the wall and it's a secret passage. No sooner than later you'll run into the Nerfromancers' guild. Pass them up, and then go up until you find the Rogues' guild. When you see a rogue named Alisi, and see some Halfling with an odd name, turn to your left (whether you're going the right way or not- you may need to turn to the right) and you'll see a break in the wall.

Head up there, open the door, and Nestral TGaza is there, waiting for you to ask, "Where can I find Janam and Rebby's?"

Good luck
