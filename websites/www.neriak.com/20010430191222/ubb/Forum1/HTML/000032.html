Ah, Rivervale! A charming little hole in the wall (literally). The manual of which you speak I should abandon if I were you. It is rife with falsehoods, especially where race tolerance is involved. It likewise states that the gnomes and dwarves get along with everyone but the Iksar. Hah! As a gentleman and rogue (and a Bristlebane Mischievite by worship)let me tell you how truly false that information is.
Regarding the Halflings... know that every deathfist orc you kill raises the estimation of their guards (the Guardians of the Vale, with their odd little masks) to you, whilst dispatching Runneyeye Goblins does likewise (with the added benefit of having their druids liking you as well). However, their clerics yet look at me threateningly (and positioned as they are, just past the entrance to Rivervale from Kithicor, I always have to sneak past them to enter the city proper). I know of no beast or quest which will raise their estimation, unfortunately. Still, should you manage to get past them, I suggest you do the Honeybugger Brother bandage quest to raise faction with the rest of Rivervale. Luck and kind shadows to you all, my bretheren.

Yup. Clerics in Rivervale are on a unique faction. The ONLY thing which raises faction with them is the newbie quest they give out... only to halfling clerics of course.

Skip Rivervale.. it ain't worth the effort. Go right next door to Highpass Hold. There you can kill tons o orcs and gain sufficent faction not only to be as high as you want with the guards, but the vendors and other NPCs as well (except for the Wizard and Ranger trainers). There is a bank, a jeweler (for those that use gems as reagents), food vendors and more. Best part of this is that killing these orcs does NOT hurt your Neriak factions. Nice loot can be had there as well (Shralok Pack (a weight reducing bag) and a Shiny Brass Idol both drop there).
