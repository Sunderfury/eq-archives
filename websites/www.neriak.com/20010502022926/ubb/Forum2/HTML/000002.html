There seems to be a common misconception that we will be killed in Freeport. This is not exactly true. First of all, stay out of North Freeport the Paladins of Marr don't like us at all. Apart from that, the Freeport Militia are under the control of Innoruuk worshippers, so they will only be dubious at worse towards you. The mages at the Academy of Arcane Science will attack some dark elves but not others, based mostly on class/god so just stay away from there. Once you've done some of the quests for Sir Lucan D'Lere a couple of times you will most likely be KoS to the steel warriors, so stay away from the arena too.
