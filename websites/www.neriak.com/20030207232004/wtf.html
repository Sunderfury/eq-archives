So what happened to the old Neriak.COM? Well, in short, we no longer had any time to update it. The site was still receiving a lot of hits, and people were still coming, but we hadn't updated in over a year. No one was sending material in for us to post, and we can't write everything ourselves. Add to that the 10-20 pieces of complaining emails we got each week saying "waaa waaa when are you gonna add new stuff?". So we took it down

BUT, rather than let the domain expire and get gobbled up by some damn domain hoarder, we're keeping it. Nyah.

There is a chance, though, that we'll be putting the old essays and stories back online in an unsorted format at some point.
