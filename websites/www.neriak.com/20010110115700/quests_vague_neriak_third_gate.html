So you wish to gain fame and glory with your people? The road to fame is not always an easy one.
For more detailed instructions on how to perform each task, please read this other tome.


The Lodge of the Dead
Loveal S`Nez
A paladin in the Lavastorm mountains by the name of Sir Lindeal has compromised trade routes that are important to Neriak. You are commanded to find him and slay him.


Noxhil V`Sek
Part of the menial tasks that need to be done by all young ones is gathering various research components for their tutors. Noxhil V`Sek requires 10 fire beetle eyes for his experiments.


The Hall of the Ebon Mask
Pazin Punox
The king has commanded that Pazin find someone to teach a halfing, Lil Honeybugger, a lesson. Pazin instructions you to go to Misty Thicket beyond the Great Wall to find her, kill her and return her head to him. He also mentions that she's usually accompanied by her brother...


Hekzin G`Zule
The Hall of the Ebon Mask are working on a new type of armor, and so they're calling for new recruits to gather items for it. You need to get three moss snake skins and three snake fangs and combine them in the bag he gives you. Your reward will be the Dark Shield of Ebon. (submitted by Szardos)


Tani N`Mar
He needs you to run to freeport and seek out Giz Dunree down near the docks. You are to tell her Tani sent you and await further instruction.


Eolorn J`Axx
The Hall has rogues in many cities, and Eolorn needs someone to retrieve the Foreign take. The last youth he sent to retreve the chest seems to have met with an untimely death, you will deliver a note to Giz and await further instruction.


The Spires of Innoruuk
Perrir Zexus
As a new member of the Spires of Innoruuk, you are required to obtain and wear the symbol of your station and faith. Find four holy symbols of the Halfling druids that infest our forest for your hematite symbol of Innoruuk.
Ithvol K`Jasn
If you are an initiate, Ithvol has a task for you. The halflings that invest our forest somehow seem to anticipate our next moves. Find Master Whoopal, slay him and bring the brain to Draxiz N'Ryt so that he may analyze the brain.


If you are a disciple, Ithvol has a task for you. King Naythox Thex wishes to expand our empire to Faydwer, and there is a band of Teir`dal there that depends on us to bring them supplies, since they are always under attack from the lesser Feir'Dal and Koada'Dal. You must go with haste and tell Dran Slug Rembor in the Foreign Quarter that Ithvol sent you. (submitted by Saver)
