Level 15 Spells:
Bone Walk
Mana: 80
Skill: Conjuration
Cast Time: 7 seconds
Recover Time: 9.5 seconds
Components: Bone Chips
Description: This spell summons a (unknown level) level skeleton as a pet for the shadowknight.

Clinging Darkness
Mana: 20
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 1.75 seconds
Recover Time: 4 seconds
Components: None
Description: This is a movement reducing and Damage Over Time (DOT) spell. It makes monsters move much slower, allowing you to get away from them easily if needed, and also has a very small DOT portion.

Endure Cold
Mana: 20
Skill: Abjuration
Cast Time: 2.5 seconds
Recover Time: 4.5 seconds
Components: None Description: This spell add 10-20 points of resistance to cold based damage attacks.

Fear
Mana: 40
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 3.5 seconds
Recover Time: 7 seconds
Components: None
Description: If successful, this spell makes monsters run away in fear. Be warned, however, that they will come back when the spell wears off, possibly with friends.

Lifespike
Mana: 18
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 1.75 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell taps into the life force of your target and drains 7-11 hit points into the shadowknight.

Numb the Dead
Mana: 20
Skill: Abjuration
Cast Time: 2 seconds
Recover Time: 3 seconds
Components: Bone Chips
Description: This spell effectively numbs an undead creature's reaction radius and aggressiveness.

Shadow Step
Mana: 10
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 1 seconds
Recover Time: 5 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell teleports the shadowknight about 30 seconds away from his/her current position. This can give a great lead for running away to a "safe zone".
