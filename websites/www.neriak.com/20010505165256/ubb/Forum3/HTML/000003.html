OK here the deal with the grobb cleaver quest . first go to grobb and talk to carver cagnek (SP?) . tell him hehe meat and he will tell you to bring him 3 hehe meat and the recipe . hehe meat can be bought in oggok from chef dooga (SP?). hail chef dooga and do her preserved frog lok leg quest . take the frog lok leg to gobbler in neriak f.q. hail him and follow his brackets relating to nerblik or a grub locker . do the grub locker quest and take the grub locker to nerbilik in OOT . he is on the first cluster of rocks to your right when you zone out of bb back to freeport . give him the grub locker and he will tell you that the recipe is was in the box and you have to kill him to get it . take the 3 hehe meat and the recipe back to carver and you will get a nice new grobb cleaver .
