So you wish to gain fame and glory with your people? The road to fame is not always an easy one.


The Shamen of War
Zulort
Zulort, well, he will tell you the Warlord, needs 4 Froglok Tadpole Flesh. One gets the feeling that he may eat them himself, by the amount of salivation while he talks. These are easily gotten while hunting in the swamps or off of vendors. You will get raised faction with the Shamen of War, and the Burst of Flame spell.


Marda
Directs you to go and serve Grevak of the Greenblood Knights and indicates that she has a secret mission to give after you've advanced 5 ranks and performed the service for Grevak. (no other information on the secret mission available)
The Greenbloods Guild
Grevak
First, reassure him that you are a new peon and not an enemy. He would like you to give him Four Lizardmen Tails for a reward and two Lizardman Shaman Dolls for a greater reward. I've heard these can now be gotten from spawns in the swamp area, however it used to be that I only saw those drop in the Rathe Mountains zone off of lizards there.(specific reward and faction information not available)


Bonlarg
Gives a quest for a Shadow Black Tunic after you help Grevak.


Soonog
Is REALLY hungry! Would like 4 lizard meats (or more, but 4 at a time) to eat. These can be found as loot off of the lizards in the Feerrot or can be purchased off of vendors. You get better faction with GreenBloodKnights and Clurg, and worse faction with StormGuard and ShadowknightsofNightKeep.


The Craknek
Horgus
Such insecure ogres! First, reassure him that you are a Craknek, even if you are not. Horgus *also* wants 4 lizard tails, and he promises rewards and armor for the task. (specific reward and faction information not available)


Lork
Lork's Brother, Nork was bitten in two by an alligator in Innothule swamp. Find the alligator that bit Nork in back and bring Nork's body back to Lork (specific reward and faction information not available)


Guntrik
He would like your help in gathering the House Crests of the Noble Hunters of Humans and Elves in the forest. If you give him house crests, he will give you things. If you find meat, he'd like you to give them to Clurg's Cook, who will make stew from it and give coins to you in return. (specific reward and faction information not available)
Miscellaneous places
Chef Dooga
Would like High Elf, Human and Erudite Flesh and will reward for it so he can make his HEHE Meat. (specific reward and faction information not available)

Would also like you to deliver froglok legs to The Gobbler in Neriak. (specific reward and faction information not available)


Clurg
The inventor of the flaming Clurg would like you to go to Neriak and kill the person who is making an impostor drink. (specific reward and faction information not available)

Clurg has lost his Bartending Compendium in Freeport, and he would like you to fetch it back for him. (specific reward and faction information not available)
