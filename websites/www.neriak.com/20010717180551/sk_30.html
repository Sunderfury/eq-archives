Level 30 Spells:
Endure Disease
Mana: 20
Skill: Abjuration
Cast Time: 2.5 seconds
Recover Time: 4.5 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell adds a bonus of 10-20 points to the shadowknight's resist disease.

Feign Death
Mana: 60
Skill: Abjuration
Cast Time: 1.5 seconds
Recover Time: 15 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell creates the illusion that the shadowknight is in-fact dead. Good against monsters when you can't win a battle. (ie. playing possum)

Gather Shadows
Mana: 35
Skill: Divination
Cast Time: 5 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Components: None
Description: This spell makes the shadowknight completely invisible for a duration.

Heat Blood
Mana: 72
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 2.45 seconds
Recover Time: 4 seconds
Effects: Healing/Damage: (negative) 17 Components: None
Description: A fairly decent DOT spell, though not quite appropriate to the level a Shadowknight receives it. Heat based, so don't try it in Lavastorm. :)

Lifedraw
Mana: 63
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 2.45 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Effects: Healing/Damage: (negative) 27 to 45 Components: None
Description: This spell drains the life force of a target for approx 27-45 hit points, and gives it to the shadowknight.

Restless Bones
Mana: 160
Skill: Conjuration
Cast Time: 9 seconds
Recover Time: 0 seconds
Components: Bone Chips (2)
Description: This spell summons an (unknown level) skeleton as a pet for the shadowknight.

Wave of Enfeeblement
Mana: 40
Skill: Alteration
Cast Time: 2.5 seconds
Recover Time: 5.5 seconds
Components: None
Description: Area effect weaken spell. Don't use this if you have a pet up or your pet will attack you. "Wave of My Pet Kicking My Ass", it's been called.
