Nomaria Doseniar in Qeynos catacombs is a difficult quest to figure out. The quest was broken for over a year and now appears to be working. However, there are some typos in Nomarias response when you hail her. She says something and asks "are you willing to serve the Lord of Disese, or not?" Bertoxxulous is the Lord of Disease not Disese, this is clearly a typo. If you tell her "Yes I wish to serve the Lord of Disease" she should give you a quest to get a note from gharin. Get 6 or 7 of crows special ale (not sure if other liquor works) from crows bar near the main gate. Then look for Gharin near the big clock tower area. Give him one of crows ale. He will make a comment. Give him another. Give him another again. Do this until he gets drunk and gives you the letter (I think it took me 6 drinks, Gharin is quite the lush). Bring the letter back to Nomaria for following factions: +bloodsabers, +corrupt qeynos guards, +Opal Dark Briar, -Qeynos Guards (kill some gnolls to cancel this one out), -priests of life. She will then send you to Surefall glades with a forged letter from gharin. You will give the note to T'nare the druid guildmistress (I think she has that rank). For your nice little deception you with receive the following faction adjustments:+Jaggedpinetreefolk, +unkempt druids, -protectors of Pine (who ever they are), -QRG Protected Animals(again a mystery assuming these are the bears of the glade), -guards of Qeynos. If you do this quest, make sure to read the note that gharin gives you and the forged note that nomaria writes to fool T'nare...quite interesting how she reacts...what is the old quote...something like oh what a tangled web we weave as we practice to decieve.

Here is an interesting quest for worshippers of Bertoxxulous and is also good if you are trying to improve faction with his followers the Bloodsabers (although a simpler way is to kill brothers chintle and and estle in west karanas or brother nallin in north karanas if you are high enough lvl). It combines a freeport quest and a qeynos quest. Start in Freeport talk to Lagus Pladeson of the Steel Warriors (he can be found in the Steel Warriors guild near the arena, I think it is the next building over, but dont quote me). If you aren't killed on sight ask "Who is Milea". Tell him "Milea is in East Karana" He gives you a letter to bring to her. She can be found at the end of the gorge that runs next to the ramp to Highhold in East Karana. Giving her the letter gives the following faction: +Steelwarriors,+guards of qeynos, +knights of truth, -corrupt guards of Qeynos, -Freeport Militia. Now if you Murder Milia after she gets the note you get the following factions: +corrupt guards of qeynos, +freeport militia, -steel warriors, -qeynos guards, -knights of truth. Basically you have alot of factions canceling each other out and you get some minor experience for each time you complete the quest. Now, when you kill milea she drops a monogrammed veil...bring it to qeynos catacombs to rithur (I forget his last name...he is straight in from the entrance of plaguebringers guild). He gives a guest to kill milea...give him the monogrammed veil and get these adjustments...+bloodsabers,+opaldarbryer, +corrupt guards of qeynos, -priests of life,-guards of qeynos. I usually try to kill a few gnoll pups on my way into town to cancel out the bad faction with the guards im about to get. If I could raise priests of life faction this would be a nice little experience getter...but i guess bad faction with these do-gooders is part of playing an evil cleric. There is a fourth way of doing this if you are gonna do a round trip, you can talk to Nerissa Clothspinner in Qeynos, she will give you a message to give to milea. IF you give Milea the message, you will get some faction adjustments (Im not sure which but they are basically for the "goodie" side. Once you give her the message be sure to kill her quick or a corrupt guard will come and kill her for you and you wont get a chance to get faction for the kill. Killing the guard that comes to kill her will result in anti-bloodsaber faction adjustments...dont kill this guy if you worship bertoxxulous.

Here is a quest that is good for any character good or evil because several factions go up but no factions go down. I got this one off of Alakazams site and more info can be gotten there. Basically, get some tumpy tonics, these are made with the brewing skill although I don't know what the recipe is. Go to Groflah Steadirt in Freeport, at the seafearers roost next to the docks. He likes Tumpy Tonics. He spawns at 9pm game time and despawns at 9am game time. Give him the tonics and you get the following factions: +Coalition of Tradefolk, +Knights of Truth, +Merchants of Qeynos and +Coalition of Tradefolk III. I have seen people selling the toncis via auction at the docks so you may not have to learn the brewing skill to do the quest. This is apparently a good newbie form of experience too, if you have a secondary character who can supply you with alot of tonics.
