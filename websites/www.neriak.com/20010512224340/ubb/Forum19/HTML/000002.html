Iksar are considered the evilest race on all of Norrath. While I argue with the fact that Lizardmen should be evil ( IMO, they should be neutral. To quote the EQ ROK site-"Lizardmen are neutral/evil and can be Monks."), I have come to accept it.
*********************************************
I play an Iksar Shaman, named Dexta Ta'resh, or " The Child of Crimson Morning".
I believe that he lives up to the requiresments of his god and class..
"Followers of Cazic-Thule fear their Lord and believe that only by causing terror in others will they be spared his vengeful wrath. They strive to beat down and suppress all hope. Fear rules their lives and through fear they rule the lives of others. Pain, misery, violence, torture, living sacrifice; these are the tools of a Cazicite. Many Lizardman tribes are devout followers of Cazic-Thule, but his number of humanoid followers grows daily, a cold shadow slowly engulfing the bright spots of Norrath in a nightmare of horror and pain. "
Shaman are buffers and healers. So, what is to be expected of an Iksar shaman?
The Heirophant's of Cabilis' goals in life are to achieve eternal suffering. What better way to prolong one's suffering then to heal them before they are about to die?
Then there is chance to suffer again! As Dexta would say " Koi Riche Kala-li, Ko Sanke-Li." or, "Mystic healing brings suffering.". Now, Shaman are known for their healing/buffing reputation, so, as not to seem like a jerk to the non-roleplayers, I'll heal and buff them.. Blessing them with the words " May you come to find eternal sssuffering in your pathss...". Sometimes, people will just stand there, wondering why I would say such an awful thing. In other occasions, the "pety roleplayers" ( who IMO aren't roleplayers at all) say something idiotic like " Tunare will hunt you down!" or " Cazic Thule's evil will never overcome the holy powers of (enter your "good" god's name here)!". Then when I try to get into a roleplaying conversation with them, they say " Screw you! Leave me alone!". I've had many difficulties with such issues.
It's true, many will find me building factions with the "good" towns, but only to spread the fear, to spread the suffering....
*********************************************
On my server, throughout the two years I have been playing everquest, I have only met two serious roleplayers. 2! You think that you have problems on other servers? Come to Bristlebane.

Heirophant of Cazic-Thule.

mischievous vision of
the perfect Norrath.
