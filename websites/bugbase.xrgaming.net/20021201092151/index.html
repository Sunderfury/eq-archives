Two New Sites on Warcry
Posted by Warcry News Network at 11:08am on 11/05/2002

We're please to announce the unveiling of two new MMO sites at Warcry today. Both games are currently in beta and we promise to bring you the level of information you've grown used to in our Shadowbane, Asheron's Call 2 and Star Wars Galaxies sites.

Ascendath (who designed the AC2 and SWG's sites) has brough us WoWCry - the World or Warcraft. From the official site:

Four years have passed since the aftermath of Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos, and a great tension smolders throughout the ravaged world of Azeroth. As the battle-worn races begin to rebuild their shattered kingdoms, new threats, both ancient and ominous, arise to plague the world once again. Rich in legend and filled with adventure, the World of Warcraft awaits! For the first time, players experience the lands of Azeroth from a new, in-depth perspective. As heroes, they explore familiar battlefields, discover new lands and take on epic quests and challenges in Blizzard's massively multiplayer, on-line, role-playing game.
Modrick (also of AC2 site design) has brough us DeCry - Dragon Empires. From the official site:
In Dragon Empires you group with friends into clans and compete for the ownership of the 50 cities scattered throughout the world. As you grow your empire you tax the cities you own, foster trade and a strong economy and keep would-be rivals from challenging your position. With luck player-merchants will flock to you and make you rich. But be careful! Should you fail to win support for your city and to appease the Dragon in the empire you dwell you will find your city becoming unstable and ripe for conquest. Other clans will be lining up to challenge your ownership, wanting a piece of the wealth themselves.
As you can see, both of these sites will be a plus to the network, not to mention the games sound exciting! Please, visit them often and should you hear anything about the games in production, let the site staff know!
