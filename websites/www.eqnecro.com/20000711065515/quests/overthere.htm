Captain

Russell - If you will go out into The Overthere you will soon find Sarnak Bezerkers. They drop a no drop whip. Collect 4 of these whips and then turn them in to the Captain where he will give you a shield that is +10 Magic Resist. Pretty worthless shield but in the process he also increases your Venril Sathir faction.

Outcome: +10 MR Shield

 

 

Undead Foreman

Russell - The staff is really a Sledgemallet and it is real.  I have gotten my faction up to amiable and done the quest now. The gem for the Mallet is a Jade, it comes with 1 charge of overthere and the mallet disappears when you log. Still a nice item to have.

Faction requirement to complete the quest is amiable. Requires 2 jade to be given to the undead foreman when he asks for his favorite gem to fill his eye sockets. The jade costs me 7pp and some change in The Overthere from a clockwork merchant. You can do the quest over and over. So get one before you leave and always have a gate back.

Hail Undead Foreman - Foreman asks if you are a [new worker]  respond - 'I am a new worker'. With apprehensive and indifferent faction the foreman tells you he will not work with you until you prove your alliance with Venril Sathir. With amiable faction he asks for 2 jade to fill his eye sockets as payment for your sledgehammer.  Turn in the jade and it is done.
