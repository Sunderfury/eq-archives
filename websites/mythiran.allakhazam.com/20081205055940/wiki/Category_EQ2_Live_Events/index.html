Category:EQ2 Live Events

Live Events are quests, games and stories introduced to celebrate a season, or just to throw a little fun into the game.

 Real World EventNorrath EventDuration (see note)
St. Valentine's DayErollisi DayFeb. 7 to Feb. 20
St. Patrick's DayBrell's DayWeek of March 17
April Fool's DayBristlebane DayMarch 27 to April 2
Halloween/Samhain/All Hallow's EveNights of the DeadOct. 15-Nov. 10
Christmas/Winter SolsticeFrostfellDec. 10 to Jan. 10
NOTE: Real world dates are approximate, as they will turn it on with a Game Update and off again the same way.
