Quests (EQ2)

Quests are pretty much the best way to get XP in EverQuest II.

Most types of Quests are listable here at Allakhazam's by the traditional database lists but we would like to give you some new ways to look at quests...

Contents [hide]
  1. Quest Series
    1. Storyline and Epic Quest Series
  2. Traditional Database Lists

Quest Series

  • Quest Series - groups of quests that flow from one to the next
  • Progression Quests - a more refined look at the fastest routes from level 1 to 70!
  • Lore and Legend Quests - by level, with where to get them!
  • Heritage Quests - by level

Storyline and Epic Quest Series

A few Quest Series are worthy of special mention.

  • Prismatic Weapons Quest Series - The top storyline series of EverQuest II: The Shattered Lands (the original product launch)
  • Peacock Club Quest Series - from the Desert of Flames expansion
  • Claymore Quest Series - from the Kingdom of Sky expansion
  • Swords of Destiny Quest Series - from the Echoes of Faydwer expansion
