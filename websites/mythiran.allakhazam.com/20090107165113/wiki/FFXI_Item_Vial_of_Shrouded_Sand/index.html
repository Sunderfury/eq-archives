FFXI Item:Vial of Shrouded Sand

Contents [hide]
  1. Description:
  2. How to obtain:
  3. Needed for:

Description:

This vial contains sand that glows with a mysterious hazy aura. It is a mineral that comes from Dynamis, the Shrouded Land.

How to obtain:

  1. Obtain level 65 and Rank 6 in your home nation.
  2. Zone in to Xarcabard to receive a cutscene.
  3. Proceed to either Windurst Walls, Southern San d'Oria, Bastok Mines, or Ru'Lude Gardens and locate the Trail Markings, near the Mog House entrance.
  4. Check them to receive another cutscene.
  5. You will then receive the key item.

Needed for:

This item is required to enter Dynamis.
