Racial Traditions
For Arasai as a monster race, see Arasai

The Arasai are a twisted and malicious evil version of the Fae. They were corrupted by the Dark Elf Queen/Goddess of Neriak, Queen Cristanos, through torture and dark magic after the theft of spirit blossoms (which allow Fae to be reborn). This is very similar to Innoruuk's original corruption of Tunare's previous creations, the Elves, resulting in the Tier'Dal. The Arasai treat Queen Cristanos as a goddess (which she encourages). They take great pleasure in defiling and torturing the lesser races of Norrath.

An in-game book, written by a Tier'dal, details the story.

Like their uncorrupted brethren, the Arasai have more beginning resistance points than earlier races, and strong racial traditions such as Glide and Arasai's Flight.
