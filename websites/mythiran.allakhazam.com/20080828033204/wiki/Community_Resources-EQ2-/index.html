EQ2 and EQ2-Related Databases

  • EQ2i @Wikia
  • Legends of Norrath wiki @Wikia - A wiki for the Legends of Norrath Virtual Collectible Card Game
  • French-language EQ2 Wiki
  • EQ2-Daily's Wiki
  • EQ2 GamePressure - web site with decent offline maps. It has marked a lot of quests on its maps (some information is out of date).
  • EQ2 TenTonHammer - has guides written up for many zones, quests, classes, and so on. A good place if you want to read guides (some information is out of date).
  • RaidMobs and RaidWiki - these two sites cover much of the raid content in EQ2.
  • EQ2 Questlist - website developed from the old EQ2 Ultimate Quest Checklist. No quest details but mostly up to date on which quests exist and how you get them.
  • LootDB - A database of in-game items with a client you can run to parse your logs and make automatic submissions in real-time (Similar to Lucy for EQ1). Information includes the in-game link, item screenshot with stats and the names of all mobs that have dropped the item.
  • EQ2 Battle Mage - Spell listings, discussion forums, etc, for Mages only.
  • Myrelle's EQ2 Arcanum - the best resource for racial traditions, traits and talents.
  • EQ2 Census - the most accurate place to get profiles of the EQ2 population. It is gradually building up its ability to query the EQ2Players database in interesting ways. (out of date. Last census taken November 8th, 2006 - As of Dec 1,2007 this site appears to be gone and has been for a while.)
  • EQ2LLinks - Another great resource for item links and drop locations, with a client to parse your logs for submissions.
  • EQ2 MMODB - A player-driven database of zones, mobs, loot, and quests.
  • EQ2 Markets - A DB of in-game item prices parsed from player submitted store logs. Prices are searchable by server, market, price, and qty. Prices can also be viewed in stock market style "candlestick" charts.

Crafting

  • EQ2Traders - detailed web site for tradeskills. The best and most active tradeskill community.
  • EQ2Craft - Completely cross-linked database of items and recipes for tradeskills (Carpenter's recipes stops at tier 5)
  • Everseek - a terrific EQ2 Furniture and Housing Database with screenshots.
  • Tanzin's EQ2 Library - Harvesting information as well as turn-in locations for city task items.

EQ2 Add-ons (legal ones, approved by SOE)

  • EQ2MAP - in-game maps with points of interest. A must-have!
  • EQ2Interface - custom user interfaces.
  • Milquetowst Info Center - generalized in-game info window (HQs, spells, access quests, collections, harvestables, social commands, etc.) - updated monthly
    • There is also a web version of MIC at http://awsd.com/infocenter!

Norrathian Lore

  • The Athenaeum - A Library Dedicated to the Study of Norrathian Lore
  • EQ2Players History and Lore Forum - Official SOE/EQ2 forum for the discussion of the Lore and History of Norrath!
  • The Archive of Ik - a website dedicated to all things Iksar from all EverQuest versions.
