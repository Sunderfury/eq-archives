Category:EQ2 Epic Weapon Quests

GU42 re-introduced Epic Weapons to EverQuest II. Each class will have a different weapon, and each weapon has two versions. The Heroic version is rewarded by the initial Heroic quest(s). The Epic version is an upgrade, awarded when you complete a special raid quest.

The table, below, is a summary of the initial quests and rewards for each class.

See:

  • Epic Weapons Hunt to help with the search in a free-form brawl of information
  • Epic Weapon Quest Series for a listing, by class, of the quests in each class series
