Category:EQ2 Progression Quest Series

A Progression Quest Series is a special type of Quest Series where there is a definite direction, from quests for the recommended starting level of the zone, all the way to the point where you should be ready to move on to another zone. Progression Quests are Sony's answer to those who want to move as quickly as possible from a new toon to a raiding toon, skipping most of the other content along the way.

Almost all Progression Quests are soloable, with the occasional need to group for a few, usually optional, quests along the way.

Not every Quest Series is a Progression Quest Series. Progression Quests are specific paths or quest arcs designed to move the player through the stories and quests in a given zone or tier. Some series span more than a single tier, especially the newer series' for those starting in Kelethin, Neriak or Timorous Deep. Therefore all Progression Quests Series' will be listed only under the tier they begin in.

Tier 1

  • Isle of Refuge Quest Series (1-7) - leads to Qeynos Villages Quest Series or Freeport Villages Quest Series
  • Kelethin Quest Series (1-10) - Requires Echoes of Faydwer, leads to Greater Faydark Quest Series
  • Darklight Wood Quest Series (1-22)
  • Timorous Deep Quest Series (1-20) - Requires Rise of Kunark
  • Freeport Villages Quest Series (6-14) - leads to Commonlands Quest Series
  • Qeynos Villages Quest Series (6-14) - leads to Antonica Quest Series

Tier 2

  • Neriak Quest Series (10-20)
  • Antonica Quest Series (10-20) - leads to Thundering Steppes Quest Series
  • Commonlands Quest Series (10-20) - leads to Nektulos Forest Quest Series
  • Greater Faydark Quest Series (10-22) - Requires Echoes of Faydwer, leads to Butcherblock Mountains Quest Series
  • Fallen Gate Quest Series (14-24)

Tier 3

  • Nektulos Forest Quest Series (20-30)
  • Thundering Steppes Quest Series (20-30)
  • Butcherblock Mountains Quest Series (20-33) - Requires Echoes of Faydwer
  • Splitpaw Saga Quest Series (Scales 20-50) - Adventure Pack, Solo and Heroic content

Tier 4

  • The Enchanted Lands Quest Series (30-40)
  • Zek, The Orcish Wastes Quest Series (30-40)
  • The Feerrott Quest Series (35-45)
  • Steamfont Mountains Quest Series (35-48) - Requires Echoes of Faydwer

Tier 5

  • Everfrost Quest Series (40-50) - Limited Solo content
  • Lavastorm Quest Series (40-50) - Limited Solo content

Tier 6

Desert of Flames

  • The Sinking Sands Quest Series (45-58)
  • Maj'Dul Quest Series (45-55)
  • The Lesser Faydark Quest Series (50-60) - Requires Echoes of Faydwer
  • the Pillars of Flame Quest Series (53-60)
  • Fallen Dynasty Quest Series (55-70) - Adventure Pack, mostly Heroic

Tier 7

Kingdom of Sky and Echoes of Faydwer

  • Tenebrous Tangle Quest Series (55-65)
  • Loping Plains Quest Series (60-67)
  • The Barren Sky Quest Series (60-68)
  • The Bonemire Quest Series (64-70)

Tier 8

Rise of Kunark

  • Kylong Plains Quest Series (68-72)
  • Fens of Nathsar Quest Series (71-74)
  • Kunzar Jungle Quest Series (74-78)
  • Jarsath Wastes Quest Series (76-80)
  • Rise of Kunark Raid Quest Series (80 Epic)
