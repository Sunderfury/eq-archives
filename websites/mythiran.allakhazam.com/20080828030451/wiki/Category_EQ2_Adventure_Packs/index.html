An Adventure Pack is a content release, usually featuring a single storyline-driven quest series, and a new zone or related cluster of zones. Adventure Packs are free content for players with a Station Access account. All others pay a small fee to add access to this content.

Sony has not announced any new Adventure Packs since Fallen Dynasty and appears to have shifted focus to producing a single major Expansion per year instead.

Expansions are major content additions to EverQuest II and must be purchased.
