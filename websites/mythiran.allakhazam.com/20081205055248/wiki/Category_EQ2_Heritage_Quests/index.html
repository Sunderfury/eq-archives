Category:EQ2 Heritage Quests

This is a list of Heritage Quests, sorted by level. These quests are all heroic or epic in nature, and are more difficult to complete than normal quests. The rewards are familiar items from EverQuest. Signature Quests, which are similar in length and difficulty to Heritage Quests but reward items unique to EverQuest II, can be found on a separate page.

Heritage Quest Notes

[1] The quests An Axe from the Past and Stiletto's Orders Intercepted have steps in common and should, if possible, be done together.
[2] The quests Hadden's Earring, Restoring Ghoulbane and A Missing Mask have steps in common and should, if possible, be done together.
[3] The quests Training is a Shield and The Rescue of the Green Hoods have steps in common and should, if possible, be done together.
[4] If you have completed Berik's Revenge and possess Berik, Sword of Thunder you can combine the 2 swords into the Electrified Bone Bladed Claymore
[5] Ghoulbane Empoweredis a Sub-quest to The Symbol in the Flesh and is not counted as a Heritage Quest.
[6] This is the ONLY HQ (so far) that behaves like a Signature Quest series. There are 12 quests in the Cloak of Flames Quest Series starting with the Collection quest Tome: An Old Memoir. Also note that only the final quest in the series counts towards the Suffix Titles.
[7] The Reaching Blade of the Assassin and The Teachings of Yoru share mobs in the same zone and can be completed together at times.

Rewards

When a Heritage Quest reward is examined you are given the choice to mount it. This turns it into a House Item. If you examine the House Item you are given the choice to unmount it. You must unmount it if you wish to use it or to sell it.

Suffix Titles

There are suffix titles that may be won by completing Heritage Quests. To receive the title, speak to Indis Surion in the Concordium Tower in South Qeynos (yellow portal), Nevarius Orvalis in the Academy of Arcane Science in North Freeport, or Piritta Silvarri in Kelethin after completing the required number of Heritage Quests.
