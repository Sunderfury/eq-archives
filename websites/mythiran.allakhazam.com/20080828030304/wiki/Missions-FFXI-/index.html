Missions (FFXI)

Missions are quests that adventurers must complete in order to achieve certain in-game goals such as Nation Rank, special area access, and the discovery of extended storylines. Missions cannot be repeated, but certain cutscenes can be re-watched by speaking to a specific bard found in Vana'diel. Missions are linear, therefore each quest must be completed before access to the next one is granted.
