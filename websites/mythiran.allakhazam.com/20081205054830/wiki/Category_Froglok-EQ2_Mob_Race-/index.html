TypeSentient
CharacterFroglok

Frogloks in Kunark (Krupp) speak the Froak language.

The following titles are awarded for killing Frogloks:
  • Hunter of Frogloks - 500 kills
  • Slayer of Frogloks - 5000 kills
  • Destroyer of Frogloks - 10000 kills
Your current slayer status for this and other huntable races may be checked at any Slayer Status poster.
