November 28 - Greetings all! A lot of changes are about to take place!  I feel I have been doing a disservice to all of the Good Paladins in the Realm.  My presence in Norrath has truly ended and I fear it will do no Good for you all.  I have worked in the past with CastersRealm and have some great news for you.  CastersRealm has offered to carry the torch and continue this great site!  I am very proud of all the work the Moderators and Friends have given to me selflessly over the past two years!   I am sure the majority of you read CastersRealm ALL the time and someone adding content to Paladins of Norrath will be a nice change from the past two months. :-/  

Baelish is truly excited to take on yet another important segment of the EverQuest playerbase.  Please give him your support!  The time and effort that has gone into the CastersRealm database and news is unrivalled!  

As for the Paladins that have stuck with me since Day One, I salute you!  Can you all remember the beginnings?  Do we all remember those that have been a part of our lives?  How about an exciting look into our first Major Quest.  Dame Kathia Du Lac was truly a great Paladin, albeit somewhat controversial. :-)  Romidar was the first to break through some of Verant's "blind" policies and affected change that increased our class, its effectiveness and changed the entire view of combat!  Beldukil of course is our most recent "Father" and has affected change for the BETTER enjoyment of the game.  There are literally hundreds more I could mention and perhaps I shall as I find time.   

I never realized what impact this site would have on so many people.  True it is just a game, but the relationships and camaraderie we all  have experienced is very real!   I am a Systems Admin for a School Board that is understaffed. :-)  I am INCREDIBLY busy all of the time but I always found some time to update the site and assist all of you!  I did because I felt I had a duty to you all.  You all come to this site daily to post stories, read information and discuss strategies.  My only regret is not being able to put enough time into the site.  I STILL receive on average, 6 emails a day from Paladins.  I used to answer EVERY one but now I am hopelessly lost under about 300+ messages.   

The new admin is very excited and will certainly answer your emails and become a leader in the community.  The site will soon be redirected to a mirror over at CastersRealm.  I thank thee all for your years of support and will hope to see you in other online games!  As you can probably tell, I am ALWAYS a Paladin and if you look long enough, you will certainly find me!  I also read the message boards daily and will watch them closely over the next couple of weeks to help out in any way I can.  

For now, Gilfalas, Kleitus and Beldukil are still the moderators for the EZBoard Forums.  I am sure if they are interested, Baelish will transfer their moderator status when the Forums switch over.

For the Love of All that is Good,

Varrus Aurelius
Paladins of Norrath Administrator

PS, almost 1 million hits! It makes me very happy that you were able to make this site so popular!  Best of luck!
