Bracers of Ro

Quest given by David, Erudite Paladin

Quest

David asks you to travel to Rathe Lake and slay two Goblin Netmasters.  These Goblins are quite elusive and swim the waters of Lake Rathe.   Slay two Goblin Netmasters and return their Goblin Nets to David to receive the mold for Bracers of Ro.

Faction

Travel to Toxxulia Forest and search out the Evil Necromancer Elial Brooks.  Slay him many times and enter Erudin to check your faction.
