Smithing Ronium and Armour of Ro

The Armour of Ro requires the Paladin to combine Ronium and the correct mold to create the Armour of Ro.  There are various molds you must get from the Paladin Encampment in the Rathe Mountains.  Once you complete the Quests for the molds, you must gather the components to create the Ronium required. 

Lord Thomas Searfire, in the Temple of Solusek Ro, will tell you what you must do.  You must have good faction with Lord Thomas to get him to talk to you.  To increase faction with Lord Thomas, travel to the Southern Desert of Ro and you can do one of two things.  Give Ortallius, an Orc, Dervish Rings, taken from Cutthroats, or give Rathmana money by playing his game. 

Once you have high enough faction with Lord Thomas, ask him about the 'Bar of Alloy' and he will give you a Cauldron to forge Ronium.  Ronium requires 5 components that are spread throughout Norrath. 

Ronium requires :

2 Bars of Enchanted Platinum - you purchase Platinum bars from a jeweller NPC.  An Enchanter, level 34 and above can enchant the platinum for you.

Melatite - found on Gnomebots in Solusek A.  The models that drop Melatite most frequently are MC, XC and MB.

Mistmoore Granite - found on Gargoyles in Mistmoore Castle.   The Gargoyles near the Butler and Maid seem to have the highest rate of drop.

Sands of Ro - found on Sand Giants in Oasis or the Desert of Southern Ro. 

Soil of Underfoot - must be made by Priestess Ghalea from the Dwarven city of Kaladim.  You must have high enough faction.  Give her four portions of Fairy Dust and she will turn them into Soil of Underfoot. 

Once you have Ronium and the mold, go to a forge, put in one bar of Ronium and your mold.  It seems the Blacksmith component of the Quest has been removed and you no longer require a Blacksmithing skill to forge the Armour.  To be on the safe side, get your Blacksmithing up to 35 or 40.
