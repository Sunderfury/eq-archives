Breastplate of Ro

Quest given by Abigail, Elven Cleric

Quest

Abigail informs you that two cooks in Mistmoore are concocting Evil brews.  You must travel to Mistmoore, find the Ancille Cooks and slay them.  You must retrieve their Cauldrons and return them to Abigail.  Once you return, Abigail will reward you with the mold for the Breastplate of Ro.

Faction

Abigail is a Cleric from the High Elven city of Felwithe.   Ask Tynkale, Leader of the Cleric Guild if you are an honoured member.  To increase faction, find Pandos in the Hogcaller's Inn in Freeport.  Buy A LOT of muffins and keep giving them to him.
