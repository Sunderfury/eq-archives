Shining Star of Light

 Many thanks to everyone who sent in solutions to this Quest. A special mention should be given to Blackwing for the first submittal. I would like to perform this Quest and verify the information. If things do not seem exact, please send me an update to : paladin@lweb.net.

Chapter 1 - As a Paladin of some experience(level 8-12) you are capable of serving the Paladins of Norrath and are ready to begin your journeys. You have decided to leave the fold of the Temple and wander through Norrath, dispensing Justice and removing the stench of Evil from the Countryside. Astaed Wemor, a Paladin of some fame, is resting near the path that leads to the Temple of Life in Qeynos. You begin a conversation and he asks if you will be travelling afar or staying in the area. Of course, ready for adventure, you tell him you are travelling afar.

Chapter 2 - Astaed is very happy that you are going to make a journey and asks you to retrieve an item he requires in Rivervale. he asks you to find Kizzie Mintiopp of Rivervale as she would have what he is looking for. Travelling to Rivervale, you find Kizzie Mintopp tending to her duties. You introduce yourself and tell her you are from the Temple of Life. You tell her of your task and she will happily help you if you can bring her back 3 Honeycombs and 30gp for her time. Go into the lands surrounding Rivervale and search for Honeycomb.

Chapter 3 - Finding the Honeycomb, you return to Kizzie victorious. Hand her the 3 Honeycombs and 30gp. Kizzie smiles, throws the Honeycomb into a pot and shortly after, hands you a bottle to return to Amstaed.

Chapter 4 - After running through the Karanas, you arrive back at the Temple of Life and hand Amstaed the bottle. Amstaed smiles and throws a small herb into the bottle, whispering a short prayer. The juice in the bottle glows briefly and he hands you the Healing Potion. Amstaed tells you that Lampeck Hargrim, a farmer in West Karanas requires this potion. If you bring Lampeck the potion quick, Lampeck will give you an item that Amstaed requires, a keepsake(a rusty scythe).

Chapter 5 - Travelling into the Karanas once again, you wander South, knowing his farm is close to the water. After a short while, you find Lampeck in his house. You hand him the potion and he pulls an old rusty scythe off the wall. "Here", he tells you, "return this to Amstaed."

Chapter 6 - You take the old rusty scythe and return to Qeynos, wondering what Amstaed would want with such an old weapon. Upon arriving, Amstaed is smiling. You hand him the scythe. He reaches from a pack on the ground and hands you a weapon that is glowing slightly. "You have done well my friend.", Amstaed says, looking at the old scythe with reverence. "Take this Holy Weapon, dubbed the Shining Star of Light and go into the land, destroying any Evil that should happen upon thee and thy Companions. This weapon will keep you from harm and your soul Cleansed lest you should falter. Go in the Light." With that, you heft the mighty weapon and go into the countryside once again....

UPDATE : Renain sent in some valuable information on what to do in order to recharge the Shining Star of Light. Whysia Flock has undertaken the Task of keeping the Shining Star of Light full of Cleansing. She replies, "Are you interested in recharging your Shining Star of Life? I can do it for you, but I require the Shining Star of Life and 20 gold." Hand her your SSoL and 20 Gold and she will hand it back, charged and ready!
