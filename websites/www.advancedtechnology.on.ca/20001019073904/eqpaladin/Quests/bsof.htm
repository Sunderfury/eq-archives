Brilliant Sword of Faith

Many Thanks to Ceral Venomcross for getting me the Quest information.

Chapter 1 : Aye, you are a Paladin of great experience and are respected throughout the land for your Valour and Generosity. Valeron Dushire, your fearless Guild Leader, requests your presence in the Hall of Truth. You find Valeron atop a balcony, watching young Paladins train in the Art of War. He turns to see you approach, his face grim, his eyes unnerving. "I have the greatest of Tasks for you Friend."

Chapter 2 : You draw your blade from its sheath, place it before Valeron's feet and pledge your allegiance, and Life, to Mithaniel Marr and the Hall of Truth. "I am ready."  Valeron sits atop the battlements and tells you of the Great Crusade into the heart of Faydwer. When the Paladins of Freeport set upon their journey, Valeron left Sir Lucan D'Lere the task of maintaining the Hall and sending fresh troops to Faydwer. As the last ship floated out of sight, Sir Lucan and his men murdered all of the remaining Paladins while they slept. Sir Lucan quickly gathered up his forces and formed the Freeport Militia. The Militia quickly gained popularity and support as weak requirements and bribery quickly became commonplace.

Chapter 3 : Valeron looks at you, anguish is his eyes. He tells of their victorious return to Freeport, and the horror of seeing his kin, and the Hall of Truth in shambles from Sir Lucan's reign of terror. Sir Lucan's Militia was very powerful and is even now a force to be reckoned with. Valeron tells you of the only way to stop this madness. Go to West Freeport, find Sir Lucan and destroy him. Valeron tells you of a Testimony Sir Lucan possesses from his time as a Paladin. He pleads with you to return this testimony and restore the Honour and Integrity of the Paladins of Marr, the People of Freeport, and the Sanctity of a Paladins Testimony.

Chapter 4 : You quickly gather up your sword, collect your armour and leave the Hall. Gather your friends about you. Destroy Sir Lucan. After meeting Sir Lucan and his cohorts in the Freeport Militia House, a great battle ensues. With a final blow from your sword, Sir Lucan falls to the ground. As you reach down to retrieve the Testimony, Sir Lucan rises from the Dead! His gaping wounds no longer bleeding, he comes at you and your party. After an eternity, the soulless zombie falls to the floor. You reach down to grab the Testimony.

Chapter 5 : The Militia is broken! Sir Lucan is dead! Your party rushes back to North Freeport and the support of the Paladins who defend this part of the city. You enter the Hall of Truth and hand Valeron the Testimony. Valeron looks at you with pride and joy. He ushers you into his chambers and draws a blade from a scabbard on the wall. "Take this blade and defend all that require it. Thou art truly a great Paladin, and one who deserves the Respect of All." You remember seeing this blade on your induction to Paladinhood. It is the Famed Brilliant Sword of Faith! You are now truly on the road to destroying ALL evil in Norrath! Fare thee well Friend!
