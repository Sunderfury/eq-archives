Spells

Mana adjustment

40.14 x 50% =20.07%

The first figure is the sub class accessibility of spells from its parent class. The adjustment is the reduction of mana compared to the parent class.

A paladin is about 20% effective of its mother class – this number will would be less if we knew the formula explained in the special note above.

Paladin

55.48 + 17.78 = 73.26%

A paladin gets roughly 73% of the total combined power of its parent classes. This figure should be close to 100%. The paladin class is a combination of warrior and cleric unfortunately it pays a huge price for it. What compounds the problem is the hybrid classes are the hardest classes to level. Making a parent class (which is easier to level) over the sub class would be the best option. I would strongly advise against taking this class.

Ranger

77.10 + 18.09 = 95.19%

A combination of Druid and Warrior this class fairs the best of the hybrids. A Ranger is close to where it should be however a slight tweak wouldn’t hurt. It still takes a long to time level so you should keep that in mind. Sow anyone?

The Shadow Knight

A combination of Necromancer and Warrior the Shadow Knight doesn't come out on top. However, the Shadow Knight fairs a little better than the Paladin class - it's still a class you should avoid.

I was not able to evaluate the weapons unfortunately it doesn’t get better for the hybrids.

One major concern the new armor in the expansion is coming with spells. The paladin, ranger and shadow knight armor have low level spells. Take for example the Paladin Deep Water Helm – the spell Daring. No level 50 paladin in their right mind would ever want this low level buff on them - when they have much better – Valor or when they get close to 60 Resolution. Warrior Cobalt boots effect strength unlimited charges (+25 added to their str) Hybrids pay a huge penalty in offensive or defensive melee power for the use of spells. Now items are dropping for pure melee classes to cast spells – Class balance is about to be blown to hell if there ever was balance.
