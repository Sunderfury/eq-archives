Greaves of Ro

Quest given by Elisabeth, Human Paladin

Quest

Elisabeth asks you to slay a Nightfall Giant and return his heads.  They are causing terror in the West Commons and it is your duty to rid the land of this Evil.  Slay a Nightfall Giant and return his head to Elisabeth.   Elisabeth will reward you with the mold for the Greaves of Ro.

Faction

Elisabeth is a Paladin in the Hall of Truth in North Freeport.  Valeron Dushire, the Leader of the Knights of Truth, can tell you if you are an honoured member.  You can increase faction with the Knights of Truth by handing Theron shark skins.  He is right beside Valeron on the outlook at the rear of the Hall of Truth.  Shark skins can be purchased in the Port Authority.   Shark skins raise faction with both the Knights of Truth and the Clerics of Marr.
