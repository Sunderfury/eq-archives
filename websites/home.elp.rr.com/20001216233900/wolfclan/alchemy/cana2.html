Canibalize II Quest*

Canibalize II, also known as McMerin's Feast, is a 39th level shaman spell allowing the shaman to "canibalize" their hit points in exhange for mana.

The Canibalize II Quest can be started with either "Marlyn McMerin", the female barbarian that sells the alchemy herbs on the docks in Firiona Vie, or "Oompah The Great", the Ogre shaman at the ogre raider camp in Timorous Deep for evil shamans. 

Starting the Quest (ogres and trolls)

Once you hail Oompah the Great at the ogre camp in Timorous Deep, he talks of having spent time with a great barbarian shaman family (The McMerins) and he stole... errr came away with the spell McMerin's Feast when he left them. He wants the same four components as noted in the barbarian version of the quest below.

Starting the Quest (barbarians)

To start the quest in Firiona Vie, find Marlyn McMerin in the barbarian hut on the docks (location: - 3820 + 1660) and hail her.

You say, 'hail Marlyn McMerin'

Marlyn McMerin says 'Greetings hearty adventurer. Searching for the components necessary for fine shaman spells are we? I have come to Kunark in [search of rare alchemist components], but I have found the dangers of Kunark are far too great for me.

You say, 'I can help search for the rare alchemist components'

Marlyn McMerin says 'Grand!! I have heard word of four rare components. Clay of Ghiosk, Crushed Dread Diamonds and Powder of Yun. Most rare are chips from the bones of one who has touched the Bath of Obulus.

Getting the Items

In the simplest terms, the items you need are:

1) Crushed Dread Diamond, currently obtainable in one of two ways:

Method One (difficult):

Step One: Obtain a Dread Diamond by giving a Dufrenite to the Gemcutter Skeleton in the Combine City Ruins (city full of pyramids near the wizard spire and druid ring in the Autumn or Lost Valley - Dreadlands). The Gemcutter must be charmed by a high level enchanter/necro as he is normally KoS. The Gemcutter will in return give you a Dread Diamond, which is tradeable.

Step Two: Obtain a Spectral Pestle from any Spectral-type mob in Kaesora, a "one-way" dungeon in the Field of Bones. They won't give it to you, so you'll have to kill them for it. The spawn spot close to the zone-in is sometimes the Spectral Guardian, reportedly tough but solo-able in early 40's.

Player Tips:

"The ghost-type spectrals in Kaesora can also drop the Dusty Ransackers Pack a 20% weight reducing bag for medium-sized items."

"Spectral pestles can also be obtained in trakanon's teeth off of the Spectral Iksar ghosts. One has been reportedly obtained off of a Spectral Footman. Much easier to get there than in Kaesora since it is an outdoor zone"

Step Three: Combine the Dread Diamond and the Spectral Pestle in your Medicine bag which will either

1) give you the Crushed Dread Diamond if you succeed, or

2) will return the Dread Diamond to you if you fail,

requiring you to kill yet another Spectral Guardian to gain another Spectral Pestle and make another attempt at combining. Not sure of the trivial level to combine these items, but I seem to recall someone reporting it trivial at 85 alchemy skill or less, and someone else saying two attempts around 50 skill yielded the prize.

Method Two (cakewalk):

Proceed to Timorous Deep by whatever method you prefer.

Fire up sow and levitation.

Run to the ice-covered Gorla Island (location: -8840, -6080)

There, fairly close to the shoreline, you'll see a bag on the snow-covered ground. Pick it up. You'll have a pile of white powder called Crushed Diamonds, but which identifies at Crushed Dread Diamonds. Others have confirmed that this item works for the quest. Some have reported the re-spawn time on the bag to be 8 hours. Note, you might have to kill the Gorla (white ape) that stands near the bag, so cons him first.

2) Clay of Ghiosk (or strange ochre clay) drops from Army Behemoths in the City of Mist - Emerald Jungle. Reluctant to part with their clay, you'll likely need to get a group to "convince him" to give it up. Player Tip: "Go in, cross the bridge, stay to the right until you get to the right courtyard. Clear the area, then work your way to the top of the small pyramid there. He's in the upper deck, which you can't walk to, but can aggro pull him from there. Least that's how I've done it 3 times now."

3) Bones of Obulus (or greyish bones) drops from the Skeletal Warlord in Karnor's Castle - Dreadlands. Note, he also drops the Jarsath Bracer (ac 8, wis +4). Sometimes, one can simply shout for looting rights on the bones since they are of zero use to anyone but a shaman doing this quest. This is a very tough dungeon, however, considering Canibalize II is a level 39 spell, so use extreme caution or have a powerful, well-balanced, mid-forties or higher group. Some shamans have been known to simply wait near the relative safety of the zone for a "train-kill" and ask to loot the Warlord.

4) Powder of Yun (or shaman powder) can be obtained from Yun Shamans in Trakanon's Teeth. Note that the powder is black in color, not white! Accept no substitutes. The correct powder must be obtained from Yun Shamans in particular (not other shamans), in Trakanon's Teeth (not Firiona Vie) and must be black! He won't give it to you? Smack that froggie down!

Completing the Quest

Return the 4 items to either Marlyn McMerin on the docks in Firiona Vie, or Oompah the Great at the ogre camp in Timorous Deep, and you will be rewarded with the scroll McMerin's Feast (Canibalize II), a 39th level spell that is less efficient than Canibalize I in terms of hit point to mana tradeoff, but that can be a faster way to regain mana in groups, especially if there's a healer with you willing to pass along a few heals in the process.
