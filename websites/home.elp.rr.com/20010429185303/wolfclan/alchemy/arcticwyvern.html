Shaman Arctic Wyvern Armor*

Shaman may now quest and tailor a new type of lightweight, leather armor known as Studded Arctic Wyvern. This dark, new armor is shaman only, all races.

Combined ONLY in a Coldain Tanning Kit (see Rexx or Betti Frostweaver in the Thurgadin Bank),

EACH armor piece requires extremely high tailoring skill and:

1 Arctic Wyvern Hide - off wyverns in Cobalt Scar

1 Cod Oil - tailored with Cobalt Cod (fished in Cobalt Scar) and Flask of Water, trivial at 68

1 Leather Pattern (Rexx and Betti Frostweaver in the Thurgadin Bank)

PLUS VELIUM STUDS (see smithing recipes below) AS FOLLOWS:

Example: To make a Studded Arctic Wyvern Shoulderpad, combine 1 Arctic Wyvern Hide, 1 Cod Oil, 1 Leather Shoulderpad Pattern and 3 Velium Studs in a Coldain Tanner's Kit.

(b) Velium Ores: This ore is a loot drop in Velious only. Common sources for it are: orc miners in crystal caverns, various mobs in Tower of Frozen Shadow (5th floor, especially), sentries and such in Skyshrine, and various spider and golem-type creatures in other zones (Velketor's Labyrinth, underneath Icewell Keep, etc.)
