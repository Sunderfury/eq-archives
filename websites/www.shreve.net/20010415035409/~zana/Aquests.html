This part of the Crypt is set aside for information and suggestions about quests I have personally completed. At this point the only things I have done that I would consider true "quests" and not just camping an item are the four Necromancer quests that begin in the Temple of Solusek Ro. These are the quest for the Harvester, a magic scythe that is not a melee weapon and the Shadowbound Armor quests by which you obtain the Shadowbound Gloves, Shadowbound Boots and the Robe of Enshroudment.

Below are two lists. One gives the stats on the item, the NPC in the temple you need to speak to about it and the items you will need to bring to the NPC in order to complete the quest. The other compiles all the items you will need to complete all four quests and is sorted by zones in which the items are found or by the kind of creature that drops the item in the case of those that can be obtained in more than one zone.

Deeper within this part of the Crypt you will find a set of detailed instructions, suggestions and tips on how to go about all four quests simultaneously. That is how I went about them and the instructions found here are not exactly what I did on my own Dark Journeys, but what I would have done, along with some alternatives.
