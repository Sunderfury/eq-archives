Deuregar Soulseeker - Fearless Leader
of The Wayward Travelers.
Human cleric in his 33rd season he is
devoted to Mithanel Marr and to his Guild,
stating that he is here for the guild as long
as in his power to help.
Deur dabbles around some in tailoring and
recently trying his hand at jewelry making.
His quest for knowledge is unquenchable
and he strives to help others as well as
enjoy the hunt.


Carista Locsley - Woodelf Ranger in her
28th season, she is devoted to fletching
as her trade and her ultimate goal is to
make the Trueshot Longbow.
She is adventurous, playful and a very
social person. With her love of outdoors and
travel, you will see her most often running
amok between continents though she does
have a reputation for being lost all the time.
