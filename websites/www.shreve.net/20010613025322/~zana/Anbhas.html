As a member of the guild Keepers of the Balance, I consort freely with others of all races and classes, provided they maintain courtesy.

This place has been set aside by me to aid those who seek to master the disciplines of the Necromancer and those who fight alongside them. It may also be of some use to those who practice other forms of the magic arts as well.		 Herein may be found information on several quests for Necromancer only items which I have completed. Lists of research requirements for some (but by no means all) of the spells usable by those who have reached the level of 16 or higher. Some of my thoughts on solo and group fighting tactics. Tips on casting on yourself and some personal comments, grumbles and opinions.

Feel free to use and share anything useful you find here. Comments and constructive criticism are welcome. Ridicule from those who are self-appointed gods and know all there is to know about every class in Norrath will be deleted.
