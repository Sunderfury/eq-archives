{Part One}



She walks through the marketplace, happening upon the one called St.Nick, sitting on his throne of wood, receiving the children one by one. She wishes she were but a child, longing to tell him of her desire for Christmas Eve coming. Placing one child off his knee, he turns his head and looks into the face of Xanadu. She sees him staring and hopes that he is looking into her soul, finding what is her request to be hidden there. He smiles at her and nods his head, returning his attention to the next child already climbing to his lap.

Xanadu turns away and continues her walk, coming across a vendor selling apples. She picks through them, finding the reddest, purchases it and sticks it into her bag. "A fine gift this will make", is her thinking. She moves ahead, noticing a cluster of trees to be sold for adorning with balls and bells, ribbons and bows. She strolls through the maze, smelling of the pine scent.

As she turns, the one called Mardil stands before her,smiling. "Greetings, M'Lady Xanadu", he states. "Are you shopping for a tree or just imagining yourself in a Forest?" She greets him with a laugh, plucking a pine needle from his hair. "M'Lord, a pleasure it is to see you! Should I query as to your goings on in this place?" "Tis not to buy for adornment, for your presence alone decorates what is before me", he says most matter of fact. " Nor is it for the scent, for your fragrance has drawn me here over all else." Xanadu blushes, "Walk with me if you can." Bowing, Mardil obliges her and they walk and talk, laughing as they follow the path whence she came.

As they approach the place where the old bearded St. Nick sits, Xanadu looks and sees that he is smiling at her, nodding his head again. She grins and looks to Mardil......
