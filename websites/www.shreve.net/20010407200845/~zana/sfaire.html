Scarborough Faire is a Rennaisance Festival that we visit several times each year. It is located just off of I-35 on exit 399A about 15 miles south of Dallas, TX, and is a weekends only event that begins around the end of April and ends in mid June. We took some pictures this year ('98). If you'd like to see them just click on the links below.
