The Lockers were initiated as a means for members to contribute items to other members who might have use of them instead of selling them to vendors or the public. Members are invited to look over these items and are free to use them and also to contribute what they can to help fellow members. It is asked that if you take some of these items that once you have no use for them anymore you will return that item back to the lockers. The library is another situation though, for the items taken there will undoubtedly be consumed and not returnable. Our hope is to just be able to collect needed words, runes and pages for our caster members instead of selling them to the public.

Carista will be in charge of the lockers having a char that will hold these items and distribute or accept donations. Therefore, if you wish to make use of the Lockers you will need to contact her in the game. The Locker char is located in Freeport and will reside only there and nowhere else. This page will keep an update of items received so you will know what is available to you. We ask for no donations of coin for this is basically a non-profit idea. I also expect that periodically we will have to get rid of unwanted items that have sat in the inventory for a long time. We will have to determine how to dispose of these items via a majority vote of the guild whenever that time arrives.

One thing that should be mentioned...anyone found getting items from the Locker and intentionally selling them to the public will not be allowed access to the lockers there after and could possibly be removed from the guild. Our intent is not to help members pack their purses for those giving these items could just as well have done so.
