Bob Becks' Personal Interests

Computer Gaming

Lately I am very enamored with EverQuest. This is a fascinating massively multi-player online role playing game (RPG) that is quite addictive. Don't say I didn't warn you... I play as Legadwen (a Wood Elf Ranger), who has a few things to say about fletching and bow making, and finding your way around by understanding EverQuest Headings. The folks at EQVault liked my fletching guide enough that they used it as they basis of their guide to archery and fletching.

I often play action computer games. I've most recently been playing Heretic II, Half-Life, Unreal, Quake II, Hexen II, and Quake (but it's been a while for Hexen II and Quake). I limit myself to games that play on Windows NT, since I refuse to run Win95 and Win98 at home; odd (maybe), but enough good ones run on NT and I don't have enough time to play them all anyhow ;-)

For another fascinating experience in an online virtual world, you should consider Ultima Online... this is definitely not an action game, but has captured and held my interest longer than any other game I've ever played. It's also an interesting social experiment (to PK or not to PK; that is the question ;-) I play as Xang Tnrax on the Baja server, generally in and near the town of Vesper. Check out The Adventures of B0N3D00D and pLaTeDeWd and ImaNewbie does Britannia for a fun glimpse at the Ultima Online environment.

Games I've enjoyed in the past include Tomb Raider (gotta love Lara!), Duke Nuke'm 3D, Descent II (a nice improvement on Descent, including more 'bots, more weapons, more graphics modes, great cut scenes and background music), Crusader No Remorse, Hexen (a nice twist on the DOOM engine), Doom & DoomII (of course), Heretic, Descent, Terminal Velocity, Dark Forces.

Musical Interests

I've been learning to play the Didjeridou... an Australian Aboriginal instrument, basically a hollowed out log. It's amazing all the varied sounds that can be played on a 'didj by someone who knows what they're doing (no, that's not me (yet)). There are a number of folks around Portland who play, and a Didj Jam thursday nights at Australian Originals in downtown Portland (the Didj Jam has been on hold for a long time now (sigh)).

Otherwise, I mostly listen to rock music, with a preference for what's now called Heavy Metal (e.g., AC/DC, Metallica, White Zombie, Aerosmith, Motley Crue, lots of others), but Pink Floyd and a host of others are also interesting.

Kite Flying

I also enjoy stunt kite flying, mostly at the Oregon Coast. My current favorites are a Shadow Phantom for finesse flying, and a Kid Eager for fast high wind flying. Others include a 2-stack of 8' Cyborgs (by Mike Sterling of Portland), a 6-stack of 6' flexi's, a RevI, and a host of others.

Other Interests

My wife and I enjoy the local theatre in Portland. We regularly attend performances at The Portland Repertory Theatre (now defunct), The Artists Repertory Theatre, and Triangle Productions.

We're also avid Portland Trailblazer fans. Let's hope they start playing better!
