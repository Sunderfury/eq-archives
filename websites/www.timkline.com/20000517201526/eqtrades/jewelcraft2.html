More Jewelcraft Info
 

1 hematite & 1 silver bar = silver hematite ring
1 malachite & 2 silver bars = silver malachite ring
rose star sapphire & silver bar = engagement ring
cornelian & silver bar = wedding ring
onyx & silver bar = onyx bracelet
1 lazuli 1 silver bar = lazuli necklace

Enchanting the metal bars (enchant silver lvl8 enchanter spell), then combining them with a gem will release the magical property of the specific gem. Enchanting different metals recquires higer level enchanter spells. The type of metal determines how much of an attribute will be affected.

To create jewelry you place one gem and 1 or more metal bars into the jewelry kit. Each combination of gem and bar creates a specifice type of jewelry (ring, bracelet or necklace).
