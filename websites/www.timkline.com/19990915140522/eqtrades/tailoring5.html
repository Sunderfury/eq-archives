Backpacks
 


1 High Quality Bear Skin
1 Backpack Pattern

NOTE: Backpacks are tough to make. At skill level 26, I tried 5 times and only made one pack, and that could have been pure luck for all I know. However, making packs seems to be a great way to get your tailor skills up, instead of by making the ultra tedious studded leather. In those 5 attempts, my skill went up 4 points and is now at 30. Don't know if it'll be that good for others, but it was for me :)


Quivers
 


You need a High Quality Bear Pelt, a (30) Tailoring skill and a pattern.

They are tougher to make than backpacks and the patterns are VERY expensive.
