Patchwork Leather Armor
 


In many cases, Patchwork Leather is the first kind of armor you'll wear. It's cheap and easy to make, and all new tailors starting out will be making it.

Components needed:

    1 Large or Small Sewing Kit
    1 Pattern
    1 Ruined Bear, Cat or Wolf Pelt

To create your armor, first buy the Sewing Kit. Then get the pattern for the type of armor you would like to make (Boots, Sleeves, Gloves, etc). Right click the Sewing Kit and it will open. Then, click the pattern, and drag it over and place it in an empty slot in the Sewing Kit. Next, click the pelt, drag it over and put it in another empty slot in the kit and click the "combine" button. If you're successful, the items will disapear and you'll be holding a brand new piece of armor, if you fail, everything you put in the kit will be lost.

The patterns available for tailoring are: Cap, Gorget (neck armor), Shirt, Sleeves, Gloves, Pants and Boots. If there are more patterns available, to my knowledge no one has found them yet, and if they have they're being meanies and not telling anyone where :) To make the kind of armor you want, simply buy that pattern.

The types of pelts needed for patchwork armor are: Ruined Bear Pelts, Ruined Cat Pelts and Ruined Wolf Pelts. Ruined Bear Pelts are for large sizes only (Barbarians, Ogres and Trolls), Ruined Cat Pelts are for medium sizes (Erudite, Human, all Elves, and Barbarians) and Ruined Wolf Pelts are for small sizes (Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings, and all Elves)
