Tinkering Guide 1
 

So you would like to be a Tinker? Well it's a largely unknown Trade Skill and it took me quite a while just to find this much! I hope this helps and remember, most of this is UN-tested so you should take it all with a grain of salt.

First this is a race specific trade Skill, somewhat like Alchemy is a [Shaman] class specific Skill. You must be a gnome! First you will need a Toolbox which can be obtained at the Windmills near Ak'Anon or you can get a Smith to make you one. So far I haven't been successful but maybe you can figure it out? Below are the two books I've found and there are rumored to be 3 more in Ak'Anon itself although I haven't found them, these were found near the Windmills.

Journal of Gimblestan Vol XIV
Journal of Gimblestan XV

I've also included my adaptations of the book's recipes for you to experiment with. You can find these below with the name of the book I got it from.
