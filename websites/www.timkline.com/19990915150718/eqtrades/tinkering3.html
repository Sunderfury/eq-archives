Tinkering Recipes
 

Here are the recipes that should be working for sure (i.e. I or someone I know has tried them)

  • Firewater = Gnomish spirits+water flask
  • Compass = skewer+pie tin+water+cork+static orb (note tried once and failed, not the does not combine message)
  • Flameless Lantern = large lantern+metal twine+firewater (works have a few)
  • Cams = bolts+gears+grease (the cams themselves work not sure about the part of combining them with a bow though)

Untried recipies:

  • Mechanical Lockpicks = lockpicks+gears+sprickets and maybe + rat ear
  • Spyglass = metal rod+reflective shard+bottle+twine
  • Stalking Probes = gear+gear(yes 2)+metal rod+bottle+firewater (what this does I have no idea of.)
  • Thermal Cloak = tattered gnomish cloak(patchwork?)+metal rods+firewater and possibly a belt too

The cheapest item I have found to make so far is the lantern as the lantern can be purchased as can the twine, and the fire water is easy to make.

Maybe next cheapest to make is the compass, though you will have to hunt around for a pie tin and skewers ( these are player made items, find yourself a good potter for them, or an OK potter and a good smith) the firewater it self starts out trivial so don't even bother with it unless you need it for something =) (or if you are not a gnome.)

Also note that advancing your skill level seems to be incredibly difficult
