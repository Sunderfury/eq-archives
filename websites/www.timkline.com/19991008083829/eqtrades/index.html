Welcome!
 


Welcome to EverQuest Tricks of the Trades!

This site is devoted to the trade skills and professions one can learn and master in the world of EverQuest.

Simply follow one of the links to the left and you can learn how make your own armor, or weapons, or food, or almost anything you can think of!

This site is new, and we're expecting it to grow a great deal over the coming weeks and months!

If you are looking for something that isn't listed here, we apologize and hope to have it added soon.

However, this site is nothing without your help! If you know how to make something that isn't listed here, please tell us how! Please submit your knowledge, so all may benefit!

Thanks in advance for your help, and we hope you enjoy your stay, and keep coming back for more!
