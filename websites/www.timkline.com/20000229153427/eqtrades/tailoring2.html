Patchwork Leather Armor
 


Patchwork is probably the most common armor in Norrath. It's cheap and easy to make, or find someone selling it.

Components needed:

    1 Sewing Kit
    1 Pattern
    1 Ruined Bear, Cat or Wolf Pelt

To create your armor, first buy the Sewing Kit. Then buy the pattern for the type of armor you would like to make (Boots, Sleeves, Gloves, etc). Right click the Sewing Kit and it will open. Then, click the pattern, and drag it over and place it in an empty slot in the Sewing Kit. Next, click the pelt, drag it over and put it in another empty slot in the kit. Once both pieces are in there, click the "combine" button. If you're successful, the items will disapear and you'll be holding a brand new piece of armor, if you fail, both the pattern and pelt will be lost.

The patterns available for tailoring are: Cap, Gorget (neck armor), Shirt, Sleeves, Gloves, Pants and Boots. To make the kind of armor you want, simply buy that pattern.

The types of pelts needed for patchwork armor are: Ruined Bear Pelts, Ruined Cat Pelts and Ruined Wolf Pelts. Ruined Bear Pelts are for large sizes only (Barbarians, Ogres and Trolls), Ruined Cat Pelts are for medium sizes (Erudite, Half Elf, high Elf, Half Elf) and Ruined Wolf Pelts are for small sizes (Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings, Half Elves High Elves and Wood Elves)
