Guide to Pottery
 


Pottery is the art of making containers and other useful items from raw earth, water and fire.

Pottery is used in conjunction with other trade skills. A master potter will have demand for her products from many other tradesman.

Before you undertake your work as a potter, you will need a Pottery Wheel and a Kiln. Both can be found in most cities.

Place your raw materials on the pottery wheel. Once you have your product, you will need to fire your work in the Kiln.

Note: You will always need a Flask of Water to work with.

Once you have constructed your item, put it upon the appropriate firing sheet and place it in the Kiln.

Conversions:

Block of Clay = 4 Small Block of Clay or A Large Block of Clay
Small Block of Clay = A Block of Clay
Large Block of Clay = 3 Blocks of Clay
