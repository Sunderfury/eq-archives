Making Arrows, Quarrels and Shafts
To create arrows and quarrels you have to use the different types
of heads, by double clicking on them. This changes your cursor to a target
which you move over to the arrowshafts or quarrelshafts and click on them.
If successful you will have created arrows or quarrels.

One bundle of shafts and arrowheads will create 10 arrows or quarrels.

Even though they are called arrowheads they are used to create both
arrows and quarrels so do not worry about looking for quarrelheads.
