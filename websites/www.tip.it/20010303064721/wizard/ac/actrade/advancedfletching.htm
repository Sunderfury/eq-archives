Making Advanced Arrows and Quarrels
To create the elemental arrows or quarrels you have to use the
elemental arrowheads you created using the special oils.

You create them in the same way you do normal arrows and quarrels.
You use the arrowheads on arrowshafts or quarrelshafts.
