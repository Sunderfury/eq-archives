Making Cooking Spices
To create food spices you must use(double click) a mortar and pestle, which will
change your cursor to a target. Move the target over the raw good
and click on it. If successful you will create a cooking spice.

To date I know that this is what hot pepper and cinnamon bark is for
but have only used the hot peppers successfully so far.

Making Infusions
To create infusions you must use(double click) an alembic, which will
change your cursor to a target. Move the target over a potion, bought
at the magic stores, and click on it. If successful you will create an
infusion.

All infusions are worth 10 pyreals and weigh 15 in burden units.

Making Oils
To create oils you must use(double click) an infusion, which will
change your cursor to a target. Move the target over aqua incanta
and click on it. If successful you will create an oil.

All oils are worth 20 pyreals and weigh 15 in burden units.

Making Draughts & Potions
To create draughts you must use(double click) an infusion, which will
change your cursor to a target. Move the target over neutral balm
and click on it. If successful you will create a draught.

Be careful, though because you lose 10 or 20 mana or health on a successful
or unsuccessful try. I am guessing that if you have enough mana or health
to spare you will create a stronger draught(potion?, elixer?).

All draughts are worth 200 pyreals, weigh 135 in burden units
and restore 10 mana or 10 health
