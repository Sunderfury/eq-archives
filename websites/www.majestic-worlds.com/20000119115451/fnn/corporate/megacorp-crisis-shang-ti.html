Megacorp Crisis on Shang-Ti by Taliesin

Shang-Ti Moon
Shang-Ti Moon, Kidkit, Madderly's Star System - Tensions on the KidKit moon of Shang-Ti escalated dramatically today into open conflict. The crisis began three days ago over a production error in the Kraan-Works Industrial Complex between the Mega-Corps MercCo and Galactic Task Force (GTF). The government of KidKit has refused to become involved in the matter, and today proclaimed the moon to be a "freezone."

The Council of Worlds has demanded the government of KidKit force a peaceful solution, promising UPF assistance if necessary. In a press conference today, however, UPF officials warned the combatants that they would incur "Immediate and decisive intervention" if the fighting approaches the civilian residential sector of Shang-Ti. At the present time it appears that the two mega-corps are heeding that warning, as the conflict is contained within the moon's industrial sector, and there have been only a handful of non-corporate injuries reported.

Kidkit Planet
Insiders in the two military-based mega-corps claim that this dispute will most likely escalate to a full-fledged corporate war, at least from the lack of any armed conflict within the republic.
