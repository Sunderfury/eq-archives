Streel Diplomatic Team Ambushed by Christopher Smith

Pan-Galactic Headquarters by Dawid Michalczyk

Exodus Planet, Pan-Gal System - Negotiations on Exodus break down as members of a Streel diplomatic team are ambushed en route to talks on third day. Streel spokesman Juiloka Ki-Laoik [Hf] says Pan-Galactic Corporation is undoubtedly responsible and threatens resumed tactical strikes against Pan-Gal positions.

PGC Headquarters Director Ras Zi'klin [Vm] replied that Pan-Galactic Corporation has absolutely no reason to want to hurt the talks that are happening at their own headquarters. He suggested that this may be the result of an internal Streel power struggle.

Zi'klin stated that regardless of who ambushed the Streel diplomatic team, he plans on putting PGC's full resources to apprehend the ambrushers and bring them to justice. PGC's Security Forces have already sealed off the area and are now starting to scan where the shuttle when down. So far, only 2 of the 7 Streel diplomats have died, the rest are in critical condition at the Medical Facility.

Exodus Planet
Streel Corp and Pan-Galactic Corporation have been holding talks to advert more conflict between the two corporations. Some believe that this is just a show to prevent the Concil of Worlds and Star Law from becoming more involved in their corporate matters.
