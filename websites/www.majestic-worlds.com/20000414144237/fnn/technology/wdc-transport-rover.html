New Transport Rover by Timber Wolfe

Wolfbane Alpha I
Wolfbane Alpha I, Wolfbane Alpha System - The WolfBane Development Corp has just realeased to the various star systems their final release of the WDC's Transport Vehicle (or WDC TV1).

The WDC brought the transport into the public view this past week in an effort to allow various other private, governmental and personal agencies a means of transporting various workstations, weather outposts and the such.

The WDC TV1 was orignially designed by the WDC to prospect the new planet they have recently claimed as WolfBane Alpha of the star system, WolfBane. The WDC TV1 is sold without the optional workstation. No price has been set at the time of print, so stay tuned to future FNN releases as we hope to also gain more information on the workstations as well.
