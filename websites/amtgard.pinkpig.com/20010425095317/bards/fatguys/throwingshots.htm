The Story Behind this Song

I'd had the title and tune in my works-in-progress file for a while. At one fighter practice, John Perigrin (one of the better fighters in Drachenwald) was practicing a new series of off-hand shots - lots of force with little accuracy. He managed to bruise me in about every unarmored spot possible. I had bruises on the rump, under the armpit, under the tricept, inside the the thigh etc. He managed to do the same to the other fighters that night as well. All these shots were legal blows. I started the song with this in mind, but when writing the song, it just worked better with illegal blows as the subject.
