The Story Behind this Song

At the Knight’s Crossing Demo July 98, The prize was a period tent for the best Demo Team. The group from Meadowmarch did a really fine job and won. When the event was over they packed up the up the tent, stowed their gear and drove away, leaving their little dog behind. They came back and hour latter and got her, but the Baron proclaimed that in the future that all dogs and children left behind at Knight’s Crossing will be eaten by the clean up crew. That inspired this song.
