Blood Flows Where Lord Sigmundson Goes

(by Lord Gorlan of the Red Lands, 1998)
(Tune: "Love Grows Where my Rosemary Goes")

    Sigmundson's a large Lord
    And with a two handed sword
    He'll hack you down just like a tree
    Because blood flows where Lord Sigmundson goes
    And nobody blocks his blows

    There's something about that two hand sword
    You can't defend with sword and board
    It can ruin your day
    Sigmusnson can move it so well
    That you think you a pell
    And you want to run away.

    He hates the flat siders
    And when he sees their fighters
    He goes out on a killing spree
    Because blood flows where Lord Sigmundson goes
    And nobody blocks his blows

    He's both a Pirate and a Fat Guy
    On the field he's not shy
    He's just swinging away
    He hacks down princes and kings
    With his two handed swings
    It just makes his day.

    At the start his foes are thinking
    He tired from too much Drinking
    But they all die in misery
    Because blood flows where Lord Sigmundson goes
    And nobody blocks his blows

The Story Behind this Song

Lord Sigmundson is a Fat Guy in Sweden. On the field he fights with a great sword, calling all other weapons "toys for little boys." He's one of the funnest people I've ever encountered both on and off the field.
