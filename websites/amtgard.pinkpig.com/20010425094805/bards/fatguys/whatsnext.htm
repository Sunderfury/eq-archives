What's Next?

(by Lord Gorlan of the Red Lands, 1998)
(Tune: "Who's Next?" by Tom Lehrer)

    First we started the fire with our own wood
    And it got burning really good
    But we didn't bring enough, we soon learned
    So we looked around for what else would burn

    (Chorus:)
    What's next? What next can we put on the fire
    We want to make the flames go higher and higher
    We fought all the battles, We've taken the town
    But we wont feel compete, till it's burned to the ground

    What's Next?
    Well all the stray branches were the first to go

    We added in all the Coleman fuel
    And a shield someone left leaning on a stool

    So in they went and they burned real sound

The Story Behind this Song

As has been mentioned before, Fat Guys tend to like fires. I think the greatest example of self control I've ever seen was at Double Wars a few years ago. There was a huge stack of wood ten feet high and 50 yards long just off the fighting fields. When myself, Little Helmut and Mathew found it while looking for fire wood, we all concluded that it would make a fire that could be seen from the moon. It was hard but we didn't light it up. (But we thought about it).
