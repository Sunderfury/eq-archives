Get Me to Armor Inspection on Time

(by Lord Gorlan of the Red Lands, 1998)
(tune: "Get Me to the Church on Time" from My Fair Lady)

    It's the night before the mighty battle
    The tent is up and the beer is flowing fine
    Pull out the stopper
    Lets have a whopper
    But get me to armor inspection on time. If we're not singing
    Start up the fire
    And some songs so bawdy we'll perspire.

    Yes we'll all be fighting in the morning
    Ding dong the helms are going to chime
    But the nights not yet over
    Let's drink till were sober
    Just get me to armor inspection on time.

    If I'm not schmoozing
    Break out the beer
    And sing a song about a randy peer.

    Yes, there's a war on in the morning
    We'll kill and have a grand old time!
    Bash down some shield walls
    Watch all our foes fall
    So get me to armor inspection on Time.

    Yes, pull out the stopper
    Lets have a whopper
    But get me to armor inspection on time.

The Story Behind this Song

Just a song about a common problem of having a great time the night before a tourney or war, but also having to get up in the morning early enough to get armored and fight.
