The Story Behind this Song

There are some people that the gods have decreed should NOT be allowed to be near each other. Two good people, Lady Laura and Fat Guy Gerhart are two such people. When they get within 10 feat of each other, somebody gets hurt. One of the more recent cases happened when Laura had "borrowed" Gerhart's sandwich. Gerhart tried to salvaged much as much of his sandwich as he could by eating it from the other side and ended up chewing on Laura's lip instead of the bread.
