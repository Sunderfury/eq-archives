The Story Behind this Song

Little Helmut has started to bring an 8 man inflatable pool to events. It has yet to see any water in it. But for some unexplained reason, it is always full of people (At the time the song was written 15 was the record.). The song describes what has happened in and around the pool over the last four events. There may be more verses to come. And Who knows, one day we may actually put water into it.
