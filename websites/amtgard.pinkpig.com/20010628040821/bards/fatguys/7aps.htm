The Story Behind this Song

A while back I got a letter, telling me that some people were SHOCKED, because I had a Pink Flamingo (with Heraldic Tabard) in front of my tent. I put it there specifically to warn A.P. types that this is not a camp for like minds. For the last few years, the Fat Guys Camp (from a distance), has been one of the most period looking camps at most sites, mainly because of the tents. This confuses most A.P’s because we normally will set up in the non-period encampment. This song is about some of the various reactions I have observed.

(ed. note: A.P. stands for "Authenticity Police" and refers to those people who run around repressing anything remotely anachronistic - such as beer cans, charcoal grills and of course, pink flamingoes.)
