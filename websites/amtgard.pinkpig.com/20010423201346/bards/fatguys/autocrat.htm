The Story Behind this Song

This song started out as a teaching tool. The minstrel's guild was hosting a "Bard's Night" event in late 1997. In their anouncement, they had a statement that went something like "this will be your best chance to join and meet with the Minstrel's Guild and earn you ribbons." Much too serious a tone. I felt that the event needed some comic relief. So I offered to teach a class titled "Filks in the the SCA, or How to shamelessly take somebody else's work and make it your own." At the time I had "Autocrat, Autocrat" in my "works in progress file" with just the tune and first paragraph. I planned to have the class finish the song as a class project since everyone has been to the "Event from Hades." The event got postponed for 5 months and I ended up finishing the song myself. I did eventually teach the class as planned, but they wouldn't use my title and shortened it to "Filk Writing." No sense of Humor.
