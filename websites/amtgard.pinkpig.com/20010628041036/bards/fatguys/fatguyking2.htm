I'm Going To Be A Fat Guy King II

(by Gorlan of the Red Lands, from an idea by Eric McTavish 1999)
(Tune: "I Just Can't Wait to be King" by Tim Rice and Elton John)

    I'm going to be a Fat Guy King
    Let everyone beware
    Instead of a royal throne
    I'll use my Fat Guy chair
    Instead of a Chamberlain
    I'll have a Bartender
    I'll use red heads for my Royal Guard
    All armed with bunny fur

    Yes I'll be a Fat Guy King!

    Yes I'm true Lard
    I answer the beer's call
    When I go to battle
    I'll smash down every shield wall
    And then rest and drink a brew
    There's no better life I'm telling you

    Yes I'll be a Fat Guy King!

    The clue-less they don't understand why I have such fun
    They say I must be more sedate, an example to every one
    But I just cannot go along with such a boring dream
    So I'll reign as a Fat Guy King
    Yes I'll be a Fat Guy King!

    Everybody says OH NO!
    The King is obese
    Everybody look out
    The beer tax will increase
    Since we have a Fat King here
    The Court is overflowing with beer

    Yes I'll be a Fat Guy King!

    I'm going to be a Fat Guy King
    I still will heed the beer
    I won't change in anyway
    On that point never fear
    The others will have live with it
    For the next half-year
    And if they object too much
    I'll make all Fat Guys Peers

    Yes I'll be a Fat Guy King.

The Story Behind this Song

Eric McTavish gave me the title and wrote his own version of this song. Well when somebody gives me a tune and a title, many times a song just flows. That happened here. I had this one finished about an hour after he mailed me the idea. I have sat on it for several months, so his version got fixed in folks minds as the original. It was his idea after all. But I never like to let a work go to waste, so I include it here.
