The Story Behind this Song

The first King and Queen, and now Duke and Duchess of Drachenwald. Back at our first crown tourney, Elffin had just arrived in Europe from Australia. He was knight at the time and got a waiver to allow him to fight in crown. He did not have a Lady, and asked the person he was staying with if she knew a suitable Lady he could fight for. They called up Vanna and she agreed to let him fight for her. None of them had ever seen him fight, and did not expect him to win. Vanna was not able to make it the tourney, and got a surprising phone call telling her that she was now Queen. During his first reign, he was constantly starting his sentences "In the West we..."
