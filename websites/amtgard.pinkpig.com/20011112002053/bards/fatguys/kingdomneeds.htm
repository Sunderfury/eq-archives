The Story Behind this Song

In any organization, there are those who that feel everything has to be done their way only. As a rule they are full of envy for other people's accomplishments, and seem to feel that because another is succeeding, it somehow diminishes them. One clear case was a Knight who while a very good fighter, was a un-charismatic teacher. Gifts fall where they may. A very experience fighter, who was an authorized Marshal though not a Knight, was teaching glaive to some Lords who had asked him for instruction. Now this Lord was an excellent teacher. I would almost always stop what I am doing when he gives one of his lessons. They are a pure joy to watch. This Knight came up to him and told him that only Knights should be teaching fighting and told him to stop. I could see a new law coming if that Knight ever won the crown. The chorus to this song came to mind then.
