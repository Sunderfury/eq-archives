The Story Behind this Song

I know. Another Rhino Song. I've seen the cycles come and go. Unfortunately we seem to be in excessive phase right now. Most of the fighters are playing clean, but the last few events I marshaled, I saw an increase of ignored blows that I would have taken. Time will take care of it, but it spoils the fun for many.
