The Story Behind this Song

See the story behind the song "The No No Go Go song." The "Lady" involved was at it again. And I have to admit it really p*ssed me off for a few days this time. Sometimes it’s just to say "So?" Hypocrisy bothers me. At the same event that she was complaining about us, she passed out drunk in the feast hall and had be carried to her room. As far I can recall, all the Fat Guys made to our beds under our own power. Yet she goes around to other members of the Barony saying things like "We don’t want a bunch of drunken Fat Guys to come this Demo, that’s the last thing we need." The Fat Guys to my knowledge have never set up an encampment at a Demo. Most of us have been playing the game long enough to know where and when certain behaviors are allowed or not. In any case after a cooling off, this song was the result.
