The Story Behind this Song

This one started as a suggestion at Baronial Investiture, (Title and tune only). But at a recent meeting, our Baron Karl the Reislander ("Saint Karl"/"Get a Life Says the Baron" in earlier songs) made a plea to play nice again and said basically that "If you have enough time time on your hands to call me up and complain about how other people are leading their personnal lives, You have too much time on your hands. Get a hobby!" Looking at this work in progress, the words just flowed.
