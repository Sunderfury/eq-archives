    Then a story was spread, of a group in the Ilses
    An all woman barony, who would bring back their smiles
    They would be there at Hastings, for an event in the Fall
    But it was just wishful thinking, there were no girls at all

The Story Behind this Song

Back in the early days of Drachenwald, there were very few lady members. A rumor started about an all girl barony in England that was going to join the SCA at a Battle of Hastings re-enactment.
