The Story Behind this Song

At the last Feast of Fools, one member of my Barony created a "Wheel of Doom". This was a wheel of fortune-like device that would land on one of several Boffer weapons. He had created a Giant Drum Stick, a Q-Tip, A Giant Flower, A Giant Lollypop, Two Flattened Raccoons (Florentine Road Kill), and an Electric Guitar (Hammer of the Gods, given by a Page named Jimmy). He had a sick mind. But it was a fun Boffer tourney.
