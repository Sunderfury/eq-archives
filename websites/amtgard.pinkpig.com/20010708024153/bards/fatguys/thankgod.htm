(Tune: "In America" by ???)

      At times I thank God for the SCA

    Fighting in armor is so much fun
    Then feasting and dancing when that is done
    Singing at campfires or pounding drums
    There's a little of something for every one

    You will find others who like these things too.

    Where else can you be macho while in a dress?

    Chorus:

    We all work hard to be courteous
    Be at our best around folks we trust

    Modern day problems don't worry us

    Chorus:

    If you interested in older days

    It is a fine way to spend you days

The Story Behind this Song

The title is a phrase heard quite often around Fat Guy Encampments. In these days of Political Correctness, if you compliment a lady on her appearance, you can get slapped with a harassment suit. In the SCA, you can treat a lady like a Lady without that fear. Some have raised flirting to a high art, again in a safe environment. Most Lords in the SCA know how to behave around a Lady. As for the rest of the song, I just tried to capture some of the fun and spirit of the SCA.
