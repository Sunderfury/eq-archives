The Story Behind this Song

The current King (as of the time of the writing) is Sir Sven Gunnerson. He will be leading the Drachenwald forces at Pennsic this August (1998) which I am sure will contain several of his countrymen. The Swedish SCAdians have a reputation for being able to process Alcohol. The wise will advise "Do not try and keep up with the Swedes." The song "Ben" was on the radio and the title just came to mind.
