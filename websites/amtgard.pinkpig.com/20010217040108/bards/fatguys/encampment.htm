Fat Guy Encampment

(by Lord Gorlan of the Red Lands, 1998)
(Tune: "Garden Party")

    Went to the Fat Guy Encampment
    To socialize with all my friends
    To drink some beer,
    Scotch and everclear
    I wished it wouldn't end

    When I got to the Fat Guy Encampment
    I was greeted with a beer
    Some off key songs
    By a roasting fire
    It made me want to cheer

    Well let's live life large
    Hassles on the shelf
    If you must talk politics
    Please, do it somewhere else

    It was another large night
    And everyone was there
    Helmuts big and small
    Martin, Goetz and all
    In their comfy chairs

    We all drank and we feasted
    There were redheads, songs, and beer
    Helmuts special brew
    Hammocks for the crew
    And drumming in the air

    Well let's live life large
    Hassles on the shelf
    If you must talk politics
    Please, do it somewhere else

The Story Behind this Song

Fat Guy encampments are designated politics free zones. Just a comentary of the things that are usually found in our encampments.
