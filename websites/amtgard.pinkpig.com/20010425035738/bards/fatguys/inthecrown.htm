The Story Behind this Song

Most of the Crowns, and before them the coronet tourneys in Drachenwald have been pretty clean. But with any system based upon subjective judgement and honor to determine wins, there is a lot of controversy. This has been going on as long as the SCA has been around. We even have special names for this. A "Looker" is a blow that, to the crowd seemed a good kill, but both the fighters agree was not. A "Rhino Hide" is a person who consistently fails to acknowledge killing blows. This song is written from the perspective of an honorable fighter, who is tired of those who fail to fight fair.
