I'm a Viking and I'm OK

(By Gorlan of the Red Lands)
(to the tune of "I'm a Lumberjack and I'm OK")

    I'm a Viking and I'm OK
    I drinks all night and I fight all day

      I raid some farms
      I burn down towns
      I pillage most merrily
      And after a little rapine
      I head back out to sea

    Yes he's a Viking and he's OK
    He drinks all night and he fights all day

      I 'm a warrior born
      I raid the seas
      I want to die at war
      And then go to valhalla
      And have drinking bouts with Thor

    Yes he's a Viking and he's OK
    He drinks all night and he fights all day

      I drink my mead
      I eat roast boar
      I tell sagas by the fire
      If any say there's a better life
      I'd say they're a damn liar

    Yes he's a Viking and he's OK
    He drinks all night and he fights all day

    Yes he's a Viking and he's OK
    An that is all we have to say

The Story Behind this Song

At a campfire in Sweden, "I'm a Lumberjack and I'm Okay" by Monty Python was being sung, and this version just came to mind - must of been the location.
