The Story Behind this Song

At a Demo held at Munzenberg Castle, Lord Connor was continually referring to himself as the best looking dude on the field. And at one point he said the title line of the song. There were also jokes and references during the weekend about how you had to be studly and manly to hang out at the armoring tent and that the place was just dripping with testosterone. This song pokes fun at the Stick Jock mentality.
