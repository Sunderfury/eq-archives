The Story Behind this Song

In a conversation at the Celtic Sheep and Garter Raids, we were discussing some of the more humorous awards given out in the Kingdom of the West, and thinking it was a shame that Drachenwald (as a Kingdom) didn't have any. As usually happens after such talks, I heard "Cover of the Rolling Stones" on the radio and the title popped up. The Old Battered Helm is given out for a Glorious Death. The helm is an old freon tank helmet. Each winner is suppose to make his mark on the helm with the weapon of his or her choice in front of witnesses. I am not sure if they still give it out.
