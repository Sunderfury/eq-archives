The Story Behind this Song

On of the Original Fat Guys is Lord Therion. He is a member of House Lemings. The household badge is a Lemming with an inner tube around its waist. It's War Cry is "To The Sea". There was a discussion about war cries on the Drachenwald mailing list. This song came to mind.
