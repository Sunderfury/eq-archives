The Story Behind this Song

A response to some people who recently tried to give me some advice that I should use more weapons forms or I will "never get anywhere". They miss the point. Been there, Done That, Got the T-shirt, Burned it and Buried it. I first picked up a stick in 1974. At one point, Tennis Elbow forced me to fight right handed instead of left for a few years. It's not that I don't know how to use the other forms. I can do them either left or right handed. I just have the most fun fighting Ax and Mace. I'm not trying to "Get Anywhere". I'm quite happy right where I'm at. Thank you.
