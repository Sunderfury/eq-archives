The Story Behind this Song

The Double Wars is my favorite event held in Europe. This song is in no way intended to diminish that event. On the overnight ferry back home, a large group of us gathered in the nightclub and just had an absolutely grand time. Normally for me, some of the best times I've had at events are the night before the events start and you're greeting friends that you may not have seen for awhile. The close down of an event is usually a sad time filled with good-byes. This trip was an exception.
