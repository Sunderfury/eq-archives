The Story Behind this Song

The stories about Baron Michael of Aquitane continue as a Fat Guy urban legend. We have gone through several leaders of the Fat Guys since, all with their own legends, but Michael is a low standard that we try to follow. When I was suckered into leading the Fat Guys, this song came to mind. It sat around for awhile in my works in progress file, until I finally cranked it out.
