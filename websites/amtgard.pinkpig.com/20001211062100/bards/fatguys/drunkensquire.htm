The Story Behind this Song

After I wrote this, I was informed that someone else had done a similar song in the past. Same tune/Same theme. If so, It was a case of two minds on the same track. I do not recall hearing it anywhere, but it's possible.
All verses except the Duct Tape (By Karl Adder) are my own and were written this year.
