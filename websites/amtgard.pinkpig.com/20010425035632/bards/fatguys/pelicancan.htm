The Story Behind this Song

One of the Three Peerage Orders in the SCA is the Pelican. This is given for Service to SCA. While most people expect a lot from the Peerage, I think the Pelicans get the most abuse. It is amazing what some folks expect from this group. This song pokes fun at some of those expectations.
