The Story Behind this Song

Most scadians have at one time or another attended a "Court that would not end." At double wars last year , I had asked King Elffin, "Is this going to be a 2, 3, or 4 beer court?" He replied that it was going to be at least a Six. While it was a long court, I was only half way through my 4th beer by the time it ended. 6 Beer Court indeed! But it got me to thinking about a a Six Beer Court. Later, when I heard "Little Honda" on the Radio, the chorus and title fell into place. The awards and Honors listed are ones given out in Drachenwald.
