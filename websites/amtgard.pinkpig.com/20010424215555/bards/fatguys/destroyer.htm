The Story Behind this Song

In the Netherlands, they have many talented people who play in the SCA as archers, fencers, minstrels, artisans and officers. But there is only one heavy fighter, who also happens to be a Fat Guy (Floris) . We refer to him as the Dutch Army. The Knick Knack Tournament was held the same weekend as the German elections. So very few people from Germany Attended. Those who did were mostly Americans. To the North in Sweden there was another event, so none of their fighters came down. It was still a fine event, but only three heavy weapons fighters came with armor. Myself, Baroness Sylwien, and the Dutch Army. The Tournament was still held and we had fun. When It was announced that Baroness Sylwein won, Lord John Called out "All Hail Baroness Sylwein, Destroyer of Armies". I had two fighting tabards with me. My Fat Guy Tabard and My Baronial Tabard. Floris put on his Fat Guy Tabard and the Baroness put on her Baronial Tabard. Talk about a conflict!. I ended up fighting with my Fat Guy Tabard(it had a pocket for my cigarettes), thus the treachery. Fluffy is how the Baroness describes her persona.
