The Story Behind this Song

There was once a squire in the kingdom with an ego so large, it entered the room 5 minutes before he did. This is a true story. I added a few words, to make the song work, but she really did accuse him of lying and getting her to his tent under false pretenses. She turned to us and said "He's hung like a hamster, and can't use what little he's got."
