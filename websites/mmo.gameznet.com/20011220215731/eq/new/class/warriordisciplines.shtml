Warrior Disciplines


Introduction to Disciplines

Disciplines are not gained every level. They are spread out over different levels(almost every level). They can not be used over and over again. Each discipline has a base reuse time. These vary depending on the discipline. After using a discipline you will not be able to use any other discipline until the reuse time has expired. This means if the reuse time is 45 minutes you better be careful of when you pick to use this discipline. The reuse time decreases depending on your level. It will decrease one minute per level. The monks Stone Stance discipline for example has a 12 minute reuse time at level 51 but decreases to a mere 3 minutes at level 60.

Level 51

Warriors receive no disciplines at level 51.

Level 52

Evasive

This discipline allows a warrior to take less damage in melee combat. The disadvantage is that they will also do less damage themselves. The duration is a lengthy 3 minutes compared to a 15 minute base reuse delay.

In my opinion this is an extremely useful skill. Warriors are tanks first and are the best tanks in the game. This skill allows them to be more effective at what they do best.

Level 53

Charge

The charge discipline will cause all attacks to land for 14 seconds. This does not affect the damage output of these attacks but it does mean that you will land much more often than normal.

The base reuse time is 30 minutes.

Level 54

Mighty Strike

Mighty strike causes all hits during a 10 second period to be critical or crippling blows(depending on your level of health). This can be a great damage disher.

The downside is a 60 minute base reuse delay.

Level 55

Defensive

This discipline is somewhat of an upgrade to the evasive discipline from levle 52. In essence it does the same thing(decrease damage taken while also decreasing offensive output) bit appears to be more effective.

The base reuse is 15 minutes.

Level 56

Furious

This skill will cause you to riposte all attacks against you for 9 seconds.  It has a base reuse time of 60 minutes.

Level 57

Precision

This discipline will give you a greater chance of hitting your opponent but has the side effect of also allowing you to be hit more often. It is sort of like a reverse evasive. It lasts for 3 minutes.

It has a base reuse time of 30 minutes.

Level 58

Fell Strike

This skill will add four times the base damage of your weapon to your damage for 12 seconds. For example if you are wielding a Bladestorm(dmg 30 delay 45) you will get 120 points of damage added on to each succesful attack.

It has a base reuse delay of 30 minutes.

Level 59

Fortitude

This skill grants a warrior invulnerability to melee attacks for 8 seconds. This is greatfor tanking ultra mobs or for saving yourself in a tough situatino. It does not affect magical attacks.

It has a base reuse of 60 minutes.

Level 60

Aggressive

This discipline will cause you to do more damage on your attacks for 3 minutes. The downfall is that it also causes you to take more damage.
