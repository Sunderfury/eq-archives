EQ Cleric Changes On Test Server
 1:07 EST - jcsmith

Abashi dropped in to this thread on the official forum earlier today to inform the world of the cleric changes currently on the test server. Here's a summary...

1. The healing-hate cap is being reduced. (less hate max per spell)
2. The calculation of hate is moving a ratio of less than 1:1. In other words, currently if you heal 1000 (assuming the target is over 51), you gain 1000 hate. After the change you'll gain less than 1000 hate.
3. We're fixing a problem with the temporary hate from sitting. Sitting was causing an extraordinary amount of hate making it nigh on impossible to med when in combat.
4. Heal spells assign hate on what we call an "assisted" basis. In other words, any NPC that sees you cast a beneficial spell on their enemy (someone on their hate list) will add hate since they saw you doing that. They do this whether or not they are the primary target. This is why in multiple-attacker scenarios *everything* jumps the cleric as soon as he heals. This is also why everything that's mezed jumps the cleric as soon as mez breaks. We're fixing it so that critters that are mezed or stunned only add enough hate to prevent exploits of mez and stun, rather than the full load they do now when they see you cast a beneficial spell on their enemy.
5. Along the same lines, we're fixing that problem whereby hate lists would propagate across zones. Everyone has been sitting in the dread lands between combat when something ran from half way across the zone to slap your cleric, right? Basically the same thing.

More Cleric BP Nerfs
 2:22 EST - jcsmith

Here's some news that won't make EQ clerics very happy. Following up on the recent nerf of Donal's Breastplate of Mourning, Verant has decided to gimp the Velious healing breastplates as well. You can read the full thread by clicking here or get the summary below...

This is regarding the clerical Plane of Growth breastplates, the ones with the ability to cast Celestial Elixer (the Chestplate of Stability, Akkirus' Chestplate of the Risen, the Breastplate of Forbidden Rites and the Templar's Chestplate). For many of the same reasons that we made recent changes to the Donal's Breastplate, we are going to make some changes to these items with the next patch (which will be next week).

Basically, the existing breastplates are having their casting times increased to one minute and they won't be dropping anymore, new breastplates will take their place.

I'm sorry that these items are being changed so long after their introduction. But this is something that we feel needs to be done for the integrity of gameplay in EverQuest.

Massive Multiplayer Article

In an effort to reach a broader market, developers are moving from swords and sorcery to more familiar terrain. EQ Guild Magic
 13:24 EST - jcsmith

I just got a mail from some folks who have opened a new site by the name of EQ.GuildMagic. I'll let them explain it in their own words...

GuildMagic.com is a system for helping Everquest players and their guild masters to keep useful and accurate lists of their guild members, their levels and their skills.

Guild leaders will love GuildMagic because they no longer need to make endless changes to their guild roster to keep it up to date. Guild members are responsible for keeping their own character details up to date.

Guild members will love GuildMagic because managing your characters is easy and GuildMagic allows you to find other characters on your server with a similar level, race, class or timezone. You can even use GuildMagic to find players on your server with trade skills you need!
