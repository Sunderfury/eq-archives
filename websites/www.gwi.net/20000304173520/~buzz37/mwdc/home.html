Description

 

The EverQuest Mana and Weapon Damage Calculator (EQMWDC) version 3.6a has the following features:

  • Calculates your base mana
  • Calculates mana added by your equipment
  • Calculates your total mana
  • Shows total mana for previous and next 2 levels
  • Pick your class from a drop-down lost for mana calculation
  • Pick the weapons you want to compare from a drop down list (no writning down or trying to remember stats)
  • Calculates a weapon's maximum damage (not accounting for str bonuses)
  • Calculates a weapon's delay in seconds
  • Calculates a weapon's maximum damage per second
  • Compares the max damage/second for 3 weapons
  • Compares 2 handed weapons to 1 handed with the ability to turn duel weild and double attack on and off independently for 1 hand and 2 hand.
  • Displays hit percent of duel weild and double attack for your level.
  • Calculates and shows number of duel weilds and double attacks you will receive at a given level and uses that to calculate maximum damage for a given amout of time for both 1 hand a 2 hand weapons.
  • Ability to keep a "list" of all results calculated
  • Ability to choose to display normal weapon damage calculation, or calculation with history.
  • Ability to export a comma delimited file of weapon damage history for purposes of graphing.
  • The program now saves the following data, and defaults to those upon loading: Weapons chosen, Character Level, and Time To Calculate For; also the Mana Calculator saves all input values.
  • Weapon stats will automatically update upon choosing the weapon from the list

 

Known Bugs:

 
  • Calculations with mana are innacurate over 200 intelligence or wisdom.
