Brad on the Warrior Class
Moments ago I recieved this e-mail from Anthony C. Lee:

Recently I e-mailed Brad McQuaid about the Warrior class. I asked him if he was listening to everyone about the warrior class, whether or not they were going tomake changes to it, and what he thought were their strengths and weaknesses.
This is what he had to say:

"I personally feel warriors are in good shape, but we've been thinking about adding weapon specialization, probably a warrior-only skill. It will take us some time to get that implemented and there are some things we need to finish first that have higher priority

Do keep in mind these tidbits about the warrior that not everyone is aware of:

1. They get more hit points than anyone else
2. They have access to more weapons than anyone else
3. They have more freedom to wear items than anyone else
4. They have more special class-only weapons and items than anyone else
5. They have higher skill caps than anyone else."
