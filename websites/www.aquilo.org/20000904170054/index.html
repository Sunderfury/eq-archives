Aquilo Ferox ("the wild north wind") was born in December of 1999. Founded upon the principles of fairness, honesty, and compassion, we strive to be a positive force in the land of Norrath, helping others and building friendships wherever we can. Our goal is not to be the largest or most widely-known guild. Instead, we want to remain a tightly knit group made up of honorable, hard-working, and caring individuals who have united to improve the world in which we live. The focus of our guild is not on obtaining wealth or personal fame, but on growing and adventuring together as a family.

To learn more about what we stand for, please read our Rules of Conduct and Loot Rules. These will illuminate some of our beliefs and principles. To get to know our members, visit our message Forum and say hello.

We wish you safe travels, and hope that you have fun throughout your journey.

-Heather Stormwind, Leader of Aquilo Ferox
