9/17/99
EverBazaar Updates!
Several significant changes were made to EverBazaar today. First, there is now a "Trade Only" option when posting an item. If this box is checked, the price is ignored and the item is marked for Trade. When searching for items, Trade Only items will say "Trade" in the price column.

We've added USD$ as an option for price so you can now indicate that an item is for sale out-of-game.

A new status icon was added to the find page.
- Indicates the poster of the item included additional comments.
As always, indicates that readers have posted follow-up remarks about an item.

  Freeport Bazaar and Auction
Server: Tarew Marr
The Merchants of Norrath invite you to attend our Freeport Bazaar and Auction held in the tunnel between East Commons and North Ro. We welcome everyone to participate in the auctioning.

Our merchants will be on hand to craft and sell armor, bows and jewelry. We'll also be paying premium prices for all medium and high quality pelts.

In addition to the bazaar we'll be holding Tarew Marr's first lottery. The entry fee is 1pp and will allow you to choose a number between 000 and 999. At the end of the bazaar a random number is chosen and all winners will split the pot. If no winning number is chosen, the pot will roll over into the next bazaar's lottery. Lottery tickets may be purchased from any of our guild members prior to drawing the winning number Sunday night.

So come visit us near the shady swashbuckler at 7:00pm CST, Sunday August 8th.

Faellen Andreios

We've added support for Merchant Guilds
We can now register your guild on EverBazaar and add users to your guild. This will allow people to find all items sold by a single guild, very helpful for listing items to be sold during online bazaars.
To register your guild with EverBazaar, email us the name of your guild and the EverBazaar UserName of each of your guild members.
