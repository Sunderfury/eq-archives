Trade Skills

When bashing monsters for money starts to become boring you might want to start up a trade. But not all trade skills will make you money, some trade skills will cost hundreds of platinum just to master, and some trades are race specific (meaning some trades can't be done by some races). The following are guides for trade skills found in EverQuest. Also you can check out the forum for more tips on trade skills.

Baking
Baking is a trade skill which might not make you any money at all but it's one of the most fun in the game. You can find all the different foods you can make by going to the Baking Guide

Brewing
Brewing is another trade skill that you will probably not make any money doing. But getting drunk and giving people free beer might make you alot of friends. You can find out how to be a brewer at the Brewing Guide

Blacksmithing
Blacksmithing is a trade skills that you might be able to make money at. It's not an easy way to make money and you'll need some start up cash if you want to master this trade. To find out how to raise your skills and make banded armor check out our complete Blacksmithing Guide

Fletching
Fletching is a trade skill that will allow you to make bows and arrows. A good trade skill for rangers but anyone can take up this trade. You may not make too much money but it is a useful trade skill. Check out our Fletching Guide

Jewel Craft
Jewel craft is a great trade. It allows you to make jewelry which you can sell for high prices. Normal jewelry is worth nothing but if you can get an enchanter to enchant the metal you can make some really powerful stuff. Check out our Jewel Craft Guide

Pottery
Pottery is a trade skill that will probably not make you much money. And it's not a fun trade skill to master. But you can make vials for Rogues who need them for poisons. If you're looking to learn Pottery please check out the Pottery Guide

Tailoring
Tailoring is a trade skill that is useful to new players. You can make patchwork armor which is better than wearing cloth. You won't make too much money in Tailoring but I still make patchwork and give it to newbies whenever I can. To learn all about this trade you can check out or Tailoring Guide

Tinkering
This trade can only be done by Gnomes. The trade is pretty broken at the moment and there isn't much to make. When Verant gets off their lazy butts and fixes this trade we will have a guide available for you!
