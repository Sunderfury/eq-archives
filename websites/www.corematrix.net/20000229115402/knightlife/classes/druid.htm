Druid

A druid is a great class to play because they have some of the best spells. The spells aren't the strongest, but they make your life easier. Every group looks for a druid, mostly because of the "Spirit of Wolf" spell that makes you run faster. Other helpful spells include Gate, Teleport, SoW, Camo, and Bind.

A druid is a tree hugging, animal protecting, nature lover. But that's not a bad thing! Animals will not attack a druid unless you attack them first. You can also charm animals and make them your pet.

Most Druids will tell you to never attack animals.. it's just not right. But most of your spells will go unused if you never attack animals. If you are a pure roleplayer then don't kill animals if you don't want to, but there are alot of animals out there and if you don't kill them you will have a hard time leveling.

There are a couple rules you should follow when hunting animals:

  1. Never attack bears in Surefull glade!
  2. If you kill an animal you should loot the corpse so that it doesn't decay
  3. Use all the loot, don't just destroy or throw it on the ground. If you get pelts then make them into armor for newbies
  4. Take time for a moment of silence.

Druids are the master of the outdoors. Camouflage, SoW, Teleport all help you get from one place to the other. A druid is both a great roleplay class and also a fun character to play!

Half Elf, Human, Wood Elf
