Warrior

Warriors are a pure melee class. Warriors like to jump into the fight hands on and don't like the idea of standing back and shooting spells from afar. Warriors have the highest hit points in the game, this allows them to soak up all the blows the monster deals out which keeps the rest of the group safe.

Warriors always take the credit when the group wins a big fight.. And they have reason too... they did the hard part right?

The great thing about Warriors is that almost all races can become a warrior, all except Erudites and High Elves.

Warriors are needed in every group. A group with just spell casters are going to get killed unless some one is tanking for them... and the warrior is a pure tank. Warriors also get allot of skills which help them in combat situations.

The downside to the Warrior is since they have so much Hit Points it takes them awhile to regain all their health back after a fight. It's always a good idea to make friends with a Cleric.

You will never have a hard time finding a group as a Warrior, which is good because you'll have a hard time soloing at higher levels.

The Warrior is a great class if you're just starting out because it can be pretty easy. But the main reason there are so many Warriors in the world of Norrath is because they get to wear the greatest armor and the best weapons in the game (except for the Paladin's holy swords)

So what are you waiting for soldier? Get out there and kill something!
