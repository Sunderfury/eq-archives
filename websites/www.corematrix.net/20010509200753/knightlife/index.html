Monday, October 4th

New Article from The Knight Life - PvP fix idea
This week's article is written about the problems found on the PvP server.. Rallos Zek. The article deals with ideas on how to make life harder for people who PK. The Idea is to allow people to vote against someone if they do something wrong.. If your character receives allot of bad votes then you will certain penalties (found in the article). The best part about the article is you get to respond to it and tell us your ideas on PvP.
 
Here is a sample from the Article:
 
Most everyone will agree that their are problems with the way the PvP system works in EverQuest. I think I have come up with a solution that will, at the same time, allow characters to play versus each other in a more realistic fashion and let those who abuse the system get what they deserve.
 
In a nutshell; the solution is to have a character voting system to allow the large amount of "good" role-players to criminalize the few overly aggressive players.
 
A character could, at any time, vote for or against another character. This vote would be added to that of all the other characters that have voted concerning the same character. Each vote "for" a character would cancel out one vote "against" that character. If a character had a surplus of "against" votes, more and harsher penalties would be applied to that character (more details on these penalties later in this letter). Later on, a character could then change their vote to for, against, or neutral. Then a simple line could be added to the end of a "consider" message that shows each characters social tag. This would clearly identify which characters were liked or hated by the populous.

-Nate

Xegony Test of Tactics Rules
Xegony Test of Tactics
A six on six battle to the death in single elimination tournament style.
 
Date: Sunday October 3rd, 1999.
Starting Time: 3pm pacific.
Location: The Arena on Xegony.
Entry Fee: 3000 Platinum pieces per six-man team.
 
Prize: One Skull Fang Stiletto (not one for every member of the winning team) trophies being considered. The top three teams will receive a split of the total entry fees.
 
Eligible participants: All players, up to a maximum of 32 teams.
Level Limit: None.
Class Restrictions: None.
 
Sign Ups will begin at 1pm pacific on the day of the event, via /petition from the team leader only.
 
Test of Tactics Rules (Preliminary and subject to revision):
 
Attention: Due to the nature of the Arena, pets will not be useable during the competition.
 
The matches will be six vs. six players. Participants will begin the match in the areas designated by the Judges. The Judges will dispel all enchantments on the participants prior to the beginning of the match. After the Judge signals the start of the match, there are no rules restrictions except that the participants may not leave the designated combat area. Individual players who leave the designated area will be disqualified. This disqualification does not affect the remaining members of his or her team.
 
No spell may be employed to cause a participant to leave the combat area unwillingly. Thus no fear, charm, or heavy knockback spells are permitted. The Judges reserve the right to review any spell on these grounds.
 
Combat will continue until one team is eliminated or disqualified.
 
Event Security: The Arena will be closed to all non-participants. Event participants not in the current match will be permitted subject to the Judges discretion. Interference in a match in any way will result in disqualification of the offending player's entire team, with no refund of entry fees. Failure to obey the Judges will result in disqualification and expulsion from The Arena with a full refund of entry fees. Violation of Event Security rules by any non-participant of will be considered game disruption and handled according to GM's discretion.

Gambling Arena
When: Friday, October 8th, at 9pm EST
Where: West Freeport Arena
Who: All levels invited
Cost: 2pp
House Arazuel is running an event next Friday, starting at 9pm, at the Freeport Arena. The event will be normal gladiator arena matches with a few extras.
 
First off, instead of having all classes in a certain level fighting, we shall be splitting up classes as well, so casters fight only casters, and warrior types fight same.
 
The first part of event will involve teams of same lvl working together against over teams to win first round. Last 3 groups standing move on to second round. Second round will be each level fighting each other, and will be split by class as I said above. Once this is through, the winner wins a special prize, mostly money prize, but also perhaps something their class can use, like armor or a weapon. After winner is found, there will be a free for all match with all those who participated in event.
 
Another extra in the event is our betting system. People who come and watch can bet on who they want to win each round, and can bring back a hefty profit if they win their bet. Also, for the winner of the FFA and the rounds before that, we will also be having a special round where the winner could win even better prizes. This round would be run the gauntlet. Caster and warriors who participated will split up and block areas in arena and use any spells they can to stop the winner from reaching the exit. If he reaches it, he wins prize.
 
As a little extra for those watching, and those participating, we shall be serving free drinks and food, so forget your summon spells, and don't waste any money. Come and have fun. If you would like to be in the event, or learn more about it, email me at Wpsoft62@bellatlantic.net. Also, if you would like to come and watch, notify me as well. I hope to see many people there for this wonderful event. On a sidenote, we shall be having these events from now on every Friday at same time, if there is a good turn out. Those that came the week before will only have to pay 1pp to join, with the other 1pp coming out of our pockets, for you coming to first event.
