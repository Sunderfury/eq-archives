Baking Guide

Baking isn't a very profitable but it is one of the most fun to master. The food you make has no real purpose except it's light weight, but they can be fun to give to your friends.

Start out by making Edible goo or rat sandwiches, the ingredients you will need for edible goo is: rat ears, fire beetle eye. It's easy to make and will raise your skill fast. You can also make sandwiches with the meat you find. Meat like rat, bear, and wolf can be combined with store bought bread to make sandwiches.

One thing that will save you alot of time is buying a spit. A spit is like alittle oven that you can carry with you.

When making sandwiches becomes trivial you can start making fish fillets. Combine fish with a jug of sauces and if you're lucky you will have made 2 fish fillets. And when that becomes trivial you can make fishrolls by combining fish with a bat wing.

By this time you should have mastered the skill of baking. Now you should go out and try to make the rest of the foods. Below is a big list of things you can make..
