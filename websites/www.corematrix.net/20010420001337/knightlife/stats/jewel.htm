Jewelcrafting Guide

I have to admit that this is a trade I don't know TOO much about. But I have some enchanter friends who let me in on this trade. Although anyone can make Jewelry it takes an Enchanter to make them magical.

First you will need a Jewelers kit (which looks like a small bag), some kind of metal (gold, silver, coper, platinum), and a Gem. Also you might want to get the book on this subject since I'm not a master of the trade.

Before you start enchanting all your jewelry you will want to raise your skill. You can do this by making rings. This will help you build your skill quickly. Buy a Silver bar and a piece of machelote and combine them until it's trivial.

Different Gems do different things when combined with enchanted bars. Some will give strength and others may give added wisdom. Below is a guide that should help you decide what you want to make..

Malachite = Poison Resist
Lapis Lazuli = Disease resist
Turquoise = Cold resist
Hematite = Fire resist
Wolf's Eye Agate = magic resist
Cat's Eye Agate = Charisma
Bloodstone = Stamina
Onyx = Dexterity
Jasper = Wisdom
Carnelian = Agility
Star Rose Quartz = Intelligence
Amber = Strength
Jade = Mana and Hit Points and AC
Pearl = Poison Resist and AC
Topaz = Disease Resist and AC
Periodot = Cold Resist and AC
Emerald = Fire Resist and AC
Ruby = Wisdom and Strength
Sapphire = Intelligence and Strength
Black Pearl = Agility and Dexterity
Diamond = All Resistances

These are the Gems that I know about. Others can be found on high level mobs.
