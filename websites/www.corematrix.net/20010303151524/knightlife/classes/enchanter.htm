Enchanter

Enchanters get some of the funnest spells in the game. Spells that let you turn into any race, some monsters, and some other inanimate objects.

But besides spells being fun they can also be useful. When soloing you can turn into a different race and go into towns you normally couldn't because of faction.

When in groups your spells have many uses. Such as making a player attack faster, making an enemy attack slower, and even messing with monsters minds.

But one of the most useful spell is the enchant metal. Enchanting metals like gold and silver let you make enchanted jewelry. Enchanted jewelry sells for alot (if you can get your skill up) and could make you alot of money. Enchanters are the only class that can enchant metal.

You might be thinking that the Enchanter is a weak class and that is what many people think, but Enchanters can do things that other classes can only dream about.

Soloing isn't easy with the Enchanter but if you use tactics you can get by. Tactics is what makes you different from Warrior types who just hack and slash until the creature is dead. Enchanters can charm monsters and have them kill other monsters for them. By using tactics and your spells you will be one of the strongest classes in the game!

Races
Dark Elf, Gnome, Erudite, High Elf, Human
