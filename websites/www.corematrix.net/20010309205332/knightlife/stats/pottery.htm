Pottery Guide

Pottery won't make you rich. Let me get that out of the way. But Pottery is a MUST if you want to be a baker. With pottery you can make pots, jars, pie tin, etc. Also, pottery is used to make vials for Rogues.

At first pottery was very cheap to learn but times have changed and the gods thought it was a good idea to make pie tin and other sketchs cost over a plat. This was because people were making money by selling pie tins back to Vendors for a profit.. and I guess Verant doesn't think people should make any money with this trade skill.

There are two steps in the pottery process. The first is putting a block of clay, a sketch, and water onto the potter wheel. If you succeed you will make an unfired item. You then have to go to the next step which is firing the object in the kiln.
