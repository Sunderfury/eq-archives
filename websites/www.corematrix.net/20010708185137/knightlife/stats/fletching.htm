Fletching Guide

Fletching is a useful trade skill that will allow you to make both arrows and bows. Although this may sound like a ton of fun to do.. it's not. To raise your skill you will have to make millions of arrows before you will really master the trade. I even believe this trade will cost you more than Blacksmithing.

First thing you need to do is get a fletching kit. This will cost you over a plat. Next you need to buy 4 components.. An Arrow head, Shaft, Fletch, Nock. Buy 20 of each and put 1 of each in the fletching kit and hit combine. If you're lucky you'll make arrows. When this becomes trivial you need to upgrade one component to a higher priced one while keeping the other ones cheap.

So here is an example (incase I lost you already)..

level 1 tip
level 1 shaft
level 1 fletch
level 1 knock

Then when this becomes trivial you'll do this..

level 2 tip
level 1 shaft
level 1 fletch
level 1 knock

When this becomes trivial you do this..

level 1 tip
level 2 shaft
level 1 fletch
level 1 knock

Now you get it? So when you finally get to level 2 knock and that becomes trivial you will do this..

level 2 tip
level 2 shaft
level 2 fletch
level 2 knock

See now you have upgraded all the components. So when that becomes trivial you will start all over..

level 3 tip
level 2 shaft
level 2 fletch
level 2 knock

So by now you must totally understand what to do right? Good!

Well now that you have made all these arrows you'll probably want to go find a merchant so you can buy a bow... WELL DON'T! You're skill is probably pretty high and I think it would be alot smarter to just make a bow. You can do this with your fletching kit. Go find a merchant that sells bow components and buy a staff and a string. That's right... you will only need two components. When you really get good you can add a pully and maybe some other tool.

Well that's about all I have to teach you. Go out and make some bows and arrows!
