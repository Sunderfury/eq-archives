Shaman

Shaman can be compared to the Cleric, but Shaman are for the more primitive classes in Norrath. Most spells are used for healing and buffing, but they also have offensive spells as well.

Shaman aren't the best spell casters and they aren't tanks, but they are a little of both. Shamans have a whole collection of useful spells. They can also fight pretty well, but they aren't a substitute for a warrior or monk.

Shaman are restricted to the three more primitive classes.. Barbarians, Ogres and Trolls. Troll and Ogre shamans are a good choice because you'll want allot of strength so you can hold your own in a fight. But you might also want to choose a barbarian if you don't like the idea of other races hating you. Barbarians are welcome in most "good" towns.

Whatever class you choose is up to you. The truth is that all races have advantages and disadvantages.. so just choose the one you like the best!
