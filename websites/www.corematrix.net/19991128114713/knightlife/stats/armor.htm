Armor

When you first start a character you are almost completely naked to the elements so you will quickly need to find some kind of coverings. Below are the different types of clothes and armor you can find in Norrath.

Non-Magical

Banded Armor
Banded armor is in between chainmail and platemail in quality. It can be made by a player who possesses great skills in blacksmithing

Bear Hide Armor
Bear Hide armor can be worn by all races and classs. It is made by Chanda Miller in Qeynos Hills. Bear armor is of better quality than leather.

Bronze Platemail
Bronze armor has an identical armor class to regular platemail but it is quite a bit heavier. Bronze platemail can be found on higher level mobs in the game.

Chainmail Armor
Chainmail can be bought at vendors in town. Like banded armor but heavier.

Cloth Armor
Cloth armor can be worn by all classes and all races. It doesn't provide much protection but at lower levels it will be all you can afford.

Leather Armor
Leather armor is the second line of armor that you will find sold by vendors. Leather armor is between cloth and chainmail in quality

Lion Hide Armor
Lion Hide armor is made by Ged Twigborn. This armor can be worn by all races and classes. It makes great armor for casters.

Patchwork Armor
Patchwork can not be found in stores. It is sold by players who have a sewing kit and good skill in tailoring. Patchwork armor is made by ruined pelts. Wolf pelts make small, Bear pelts make Large, and Cat pelts make Medium.

Platemail Armor
Platemail is the best armor you will find in stores. Its weighs alot, is strong, and costs ALOT! Platemail can be used by Warriors, Shadowknights, Clerics, Bards, and Paladins

Ringmail Armor
Ringmail is as strong as chainmail but weighs more. It can be found on some of the higher level monsters (mid 20's)

Rawhide Armor
Rawhide armor can be found on lower level monsters. It is as strong as leather but weighs a bit more.

Magical


Barbed Armor
Barbed armplate can be found in Befallen at the second level. They are on a Ogre Shadowknight. Barbed legplates can be found at the 3rd level on a Dark Elf Female Shadowknight.

Blackened Iron Armor
Blackened Iron armor can be found in Runnyeye on elite guards, warlord and king. It is like chainmail but heavier. The only real advantage is it looks cool and it fits all sizes

Bloodstained Armor
Bloodstained is found in Unrest. It looks nice and can give added str and agi.

Bone Armor
Bone Armor can be found on Ogre Shamans in West Karanas. Gives added str, mana, hitpoints, dex, and wis.

Damask Armor
Damask Armor is for pure spellcasters. It is very light and features a better armor class than cloth. It can be found in several zones including Najena and Befallen.

Gossmer Armor
Gossmer armor is high level armor for pure spellcasters. This armor is not only strong but is also very light. It is rare but can be found in Najena, Befallen and Unrest.

Mesh Armor
Mesh is strong as leather but is magic and extremely lightweight. It can be found off many different monsters in Upper Guk. Great for monks because wearing mesh gloves allows them to punch monsters that need magic weapons to be damaged.

Netted Armor
Netted armor can be found on frogloks. This armor is like cloth except that it fits all sizes. Netted armor is also magical meaning that a players with netted gloves can strike monsters that require magic items to be hit.

Rubicite Armor
The Rubicite Breastplate gives the affect of Rubicite Auroa which doubles HP regen rates. Rubicite Armour is found in Cazic-Thule

Silvered Plate
Silvered platemail is a form of platemail that can be worn by several classes that can not normally wear platemail such as rangers. In strength it is similar to chainmail but it looks like platemail when worn. It can be found on high level frogs in Upper and Lower Guk.

Snakeskin Armor
Snakeskin armor can be found on nobles, the captain and the commander at Paw. This armor is generally a bit stronger than leather and also grants magical bonuses.

Woven Armor
Woven armor is found in Upper Guk. It is stronger than cloth but is a bit heavy. It can be worn by all races and all classes.

  

Sizes

All armor types come in three sizes: Small, Medium, and Large. Halflings, Gnomes, and Dwarves can only wear Small Armor. Dark Elves, High Elves, and Wood Elves can choose either Small or Medium Armor. Half Elves, Erudites, and Humans can only wear Medium Armor. Ogres and Trolls can only wear Large. And Barbians can wear Large and Medium.
