 Blacksmithing Guide

Smithing is one of the few trade skills that will make you money.. if you're good at it. It isn't a road to riches but it might make you good money if you work hard at it.

The only profitable item in smithing is Banded armor. But you wll first have to master the skill of smithing before you can make them.

To work up your trade you should first put 20 practice points into smithing. If you don't want to do this you can sharpen rusty weapons, which can get pretty boring.

Purchase as much ore as you can (small pieces of ore). Then, convert all of them into metal bits. Next, buy scaler molds and keep making scalers until level 40.

Now purchase lantern casing molds, as much water as you have metal bits, and empty bottles. Keep making lanterns until that becomes trivial. You can sell these items to players if you can (since humans can't see at night it might be a good idea).

Now you can make BANDED! Purchase as many metal sheets as you can. Buy gorget molds and keep making them until it is trivial.

Each piece of Banded can be sized to small, medium or large depending on the mold you use. Each piece of banded armor needs a sheet of metal, a flask of water, and a mold.

Gorgets, Bracers and Boots require 1 sheet of metal.

Cloaks, belts, helms, masks and sleeves require 2 sheets of metal

Leggings, mantals, and mail require 3 sheets of metal.
