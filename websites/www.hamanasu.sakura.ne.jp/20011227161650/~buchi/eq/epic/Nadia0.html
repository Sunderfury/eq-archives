〜 Nadia Starfeastの章 〜
Written Date: 2001/02/26

目的:3rd Piece of Staffを受け取る事

Firiona VieのNadia Starfeastに、以下のItemをEnchanter's Bagで
Combine(Sack for Nadiaになる)して渡す

Enchanted Diamond
Enchanted Sapphire
Enchanted Ruby
Enchanted Emerald




たぶんどこかで情報を聞くはずなんでしょうけど(^^;
とりあえず、GEMについて質問したのち、4種類のGEMについて聞くと渡されます。




これをEnchantさせて、Enchanter's BagでCombineです。

ちなみに、Enchantさせる相手は・・・
Dull Diamond ： Spectral Librarian (Kaesora)
Dull Sapphire ： Felia Goldenwing (Skyfire Mountains)
Dull Ruby ： Wraith of Jaxion (City of Mist)
Dull Emerald ： Impaler Tzilug (Overthere)

で、一通りEnchantしてもらいCombineするとコレができます。


これをNadiaに渡すと

これが貰えます。


Nadia Starfeast Log

You say, 'hail Nadia Starfeast'
Nadia Starfeast says 'Hello my dear. I am quite pleased to make your acquaintance,
perhaps even charmed.

You say, 'I need a Sack'
Nadia Starfeast says 'Take this sack and combine the items I requested in it.
Then return it to me.
[Enchanter's Bag]を受け取ります

You say, 'What Gems?'
Nadia Starfeast says 'I have prepared some gems that you will need to trap the essence.
You will need to force a diamond, sapphire, ruby and emerald onto special creatures
to obtain their essence.

You say, 'What Diamond?'
Nadia Starfeast says 'Here is the diamond that I have prepared for you.
Its only purpose is to trap the essence of a particular book worm.
If you should fail in your first attempt. Do not worry, I will provide you with another.
[Dull Diamond]を受け取ります

You say, 'What Sapphire?'
Nadia Starfeast says 'Here is the sapphire that I have prepared for you.
Take it and give it to the one referred to as the Golden Faerie.
[Dull Sapphire]を受け取ります

You say, 'What Ruby?'
Nadia Starfeast says 'Blood red is the ruby that I have given you.
The city that you must travel to has no sun, but still a day and night.
And at night, the mist could suffocate one. Tis the fate of poor Jaxion.
[Dull Ruby]を受け取ります

You say, 'What Emerald?'
Nadia Starfeast jumps. Eeekk... a scorpian. I thought they rid this area of these critters.
Those things give me the creepers. Take this emerald and be on your way.
[Dull Emerald]を受け取ります

[a Sack for Nadia]を渡します
Nadia Starfeast says 'Lovely! You are indeed quite the charmer here take the third part,
and seek out the last master. The time draws near.
Your faction standing with Turespirit got better
You gain party experience!!

[3rd Piece of Staff]を受け取ります
