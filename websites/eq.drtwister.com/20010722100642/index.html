DTN Site: Fallen Age
Posted on Wednesday, July 18, 2001 2:55 PM EST by Quantum

The Dr.Twister Network is looking for creative, talented, witty writers willing to report on Fallen Age -- and remain true to our principles.

Savant, of Fallen Age producing fame, has asked us to create a site that will be updating Daily, as there seems to be some lacking there within the Community. Though I am in beta, I cannot be the site Lead for this project.

I need a qualified Site Lead who can post at least once a day. This user must have good leadership skills, and be capable of motivating the whole team.

I need about three reporters who can do articles once every three days or so. Ability to not bastardize the English language is required.

Finally, someone willing to do HTML for the site is a plus.

Please send all emails to quantum@drtwister.com, from there we will work on seeing if you are qualified.

If you have not read our Manifesto, go to this url:
http://www.drtwister.com/dtn-manifesto.shtml

Thank you all.
