Everquest is fun for the whole family!
Posted on Wednesday, August 1, 2001 1:00 AM EST by Sebastian

If you haven't seen the new befallen, I'll give you a little run down on it.

Befallen: 100% more fun, 100% less rats!

I've been spending a lot of time there as of late. Verant decided that befallen needed a little changing, so they took out a lot of undead, threw in a good amount of dark elves, and seriously screwed the aggro range of everything in the dungeon! Sounds like fun right? Well that's only the beginning!

I'm not sure if the splintered wooden key drops in befallen anymore. I've spent about 12 hours there and haven't seen it drop once, but I have one from before they revamped the zone so I wasn't hurting.

Some good news for all of you Paw of Opolla seekers, Gynok spawns a little more commonly now. I've seen him three times tonight (Killing him myself twice), and it appears that with all the dark elves instead of rats his place holder will be a little easier to spot.

Anyone in the low to mid teens I must definitely suggest this zone to, I'm sure there is a huge exp bonus, and the amount of PP per hour is far greater than Solusek A. I haven't seen any rare drops other than no-drop necklaces which were something less than pointless in my opinion.

If you are planning on a vacation to befallen I would suggest you go in large groups, as a level 30 heavily twinked ranger I died twice to trains. Level 3 servants came out of the trap door on level 2, it must have been three rooms full.

I haven't seen the sleeper yet, but if Befallen is going to be sent out like it is now, I have no doubts in my mind that the sleepers tomb is not finished.

I'll see you guys on Fennin.

-Sebastian
