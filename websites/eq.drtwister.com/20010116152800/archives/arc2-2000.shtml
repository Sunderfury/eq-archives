Complied List of Bugs by AethDivine
Posted: Monday, March 13, 2000 by BeauChamps

The following list of exploits and bugs were posted on the Message Forum by AethDivine. Major props go out to him from me for taking the first step toward a better DT EQ site and posting without thought of reward. Enjoy!

High keep -
1) In the pickclaw area, in the 'lookout' room you can get mobs trapped against the back wall, just pull mobs forward then run back and to the left or right into the corner and the mobs will stick (I know this works i tried it today)
2) For lower levels you can pull prisioners in the jail area and then run into the princess's room and hug the wall behind her, the mobs wont follow you.
3) Comming down the stairs (the first set straight from the merchant) in the pickclaw area, you have to turn and go down another set of stairs, above those stairs theres a little ledge you can jump too, pull a mob and jump up there and it'll go around in circles, play with it.

Freeport/Qeynos:
You can get ontop the walls via levitate, 1 easy way is to try to find a high point then float over ontop of a roof, or try to get on the door frame and then use that to get on another roof, play with it, im yet to find a building i cant get on. Nuke any mobs from on the wall and they wont come, with the exception of PoD, im still working on a way to get him stuck, i'll post when i figure it out.

Cazic thule -
Down in the sewer pits, in the last pool on the way to the avatars bubble, comming right out of the pool with a mob following stop - the mob will stop dead in its tracks as long as you dont move too far to the right or go back across the safe line (still works, play with it)

Mistmoore -
Get up by the bridge into the castle, get lev, and float across to the ledge to you're left, you can get ontop the building and nuke mobs below without them attacking.

Beholder maze -
beholder has very bad pathing, run up the walls and nuke something, move side to side every now and then and it will keep running down and comming back up, giving you the time you need to cast.

Lower guk -
In the mino elder room, if you stand right where the room starts to widen comming from the evil eye / live side zone area you can nuke mobs and they'll get stuck in a loop and cant follow. Take this a step further, i've seen the assassin get stuck in this same loop, be creative.

A possible dupe -
A while back (maybe 2 months. im not sure if it will still work..) I duped a green silken drape with this method: I put the GSD into the trade window, the same time the other person hit trade i hit cancel, dumping 1 drape in my inv and one on my cursor.

A possible exploit -
You can cast on the boat.
Stand on the dock, start you're casting then run on the boat before the spell finishes, no matter how much you move it will always resume. Try binding, play with it, you might find something usefull.

Also useful -
Of course the tumpy tonics quest, work up brewing tonics go trivial at 133, put 1 kiola nut in 1 flask of water, combine to get a tonic, you can get lvls 1-20 in a week working with this.

Other idea -
Anyone tryed dragging a corpse with them off an island in PoS? what happens to the corpse?
might be fun to play with ;)

PoF has horrible pathing, maybe someone should get a slow mob when its broken and run it around, im sure theres somewhere for it to get stuck.

Once again, thanks AethDivine! I hope people take example from his openness and willing to share. Cheers!

Dont believe everything that you read
Posted: Monday, March 6, 2000 by Mox

Here is a little clarification on the Theater of Tranquil "Bug"

What was posted before, about just getting on the wall from the Theater
of the Tranquil is wrong. You need levitate (SOW doesn't hurt) and then
run to the monk guild roof. If you don't have levitate there is a really
easy way to get up there, too. Between the Theater of the Tranquil and
the zone to East Freeport there is an opening with an abandoned
building, a herb and medicine shop, and a tent. If you are a race of
human or elf height or better (I haven't tested it with short races) you
can jump onto one of the low spots of the tent, then walk onto a small
wall. After that you run on top of the herb and medicine shop's roof,
and jump to the safe city wall. If you miss the wall you'll fall for
around 60 damage. With practice you can get up 100% of the time! This is
a really easy way for someone to get around quickly, or maybe a KOS
character could get away.

Correction on BB Safe Zone...
Posted: Wednesday, March 1, 2000 by BeauChamps

This email pretty much clears it up.
That spot on top of the BB head thing really isnt safe. If you stand on the
snout of the thing, creatures can attack you through the bottom of it (they ignore most line of sight rules) and if you stand on the back part of it, they will eventually get to you. A good rule of thumb when dealing with 'safe' areas is: If you can easily get to the spot, NPCs can get there even easier.

However, if you can get somewhere that takes talent, or a bit of bending the rules (e.g. the top of a zone wall) NPCs are going to have a fairly hard time getting to you.
