Friday, September 15, 2000

Atomic Rhino Speaks
Posted: Friday, September 15, 2000 by Dr.Twister

Dear Readers,

Atomic Rhino Networks, the host of this and other Web sites, has experienced poor performance over the last few weeks as well as an outage for the past 80 hours. This has not been the fault of the webmaster(s) running this Web site. Rather, it was our fault.

It now appears we have discovered the issue behind our recent problems and have gone out of our way to get things resolved as quickly as possible.

Please direct comments or complaints to comments@atomicrhino.com, not to the webmaster(s) of this site. The webmaster(s) handle the content of their site, but we're the ones who handle the server.

We have taken this opportunity to assure that we won't experience these types of problems in the future. Although upgrades to our network does not justify the service over the past few days, we do sincerely apologize to each visitor to this site and we hope that you continue to support our growing list of entertainment sites.

Thank you all for your understanding.

Atomic Rhino Networks
