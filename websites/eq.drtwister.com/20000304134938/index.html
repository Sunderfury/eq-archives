Safe Zone in FP
Friday, March 03, 2000 - BeauChamps

A nifty safe point in Freeport...

If you are trying to get experience in Freeport by killing your favorite guard or NPC then here is a way that you can do this. If you go to the Theatre of the Tranquil with levitate you can either go upstairs on the stage if you are a regular height or if you are a gnome run across to the house and you can use that to get up onto the wall. Now, once you are up there, you can target any NPC that you want. DO NOT FALL DOWN ON EITHER SIDE!!! if you fall in then they can attack you, and that is bad. If you fall down on the outside then you fall 10,000 feet to your death and I am faily sure that the GMs will not res you for retrieve your corpse for standing on the wall. Be careful so you do not get caught, do not attract attention to yourself, and you may be able to get away with this 100%.

Wednesday, March 01, 2000

Correction on BB Safe Zone...
Wednesday, March 01, 2000 - BeauChamps

This email pretty much clears it up.

That spot on top of the BB head thing really isnt safe. If you stand on the
snout of the thing, creatures can attack you through the bottom of it (they ignore most line of sight rules) and if you stand on the back part of it, they will eventually get to you. A good rule of thumb when dealing with 'safe' areas is: If you can easily get to the spot, NPCs can get there even easier.

However, if you can get somewhere that takes talent, or a bit of bending the rules (e.g. the top of a zone wall) NPCs are going to have a fairly hard time getting to you.

Thanks Hooha!
