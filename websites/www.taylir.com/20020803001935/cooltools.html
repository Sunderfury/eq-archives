Here are some neat tools and such for EQ and just everyday life

EQ Caster - The best spell parser I have found that is easy to use. I use this in conjunction with a hex editor to maintain my SDPATE Changes Page.

Everlogs - A log file parsing program that traks the information in your EQ Log file, very nice for keeping up with the minute details.

Pasketti's Everquest Log Parser & Splitter - Another log file parser, I have yet to use it but some people prefer it over Everlogs.

EQParser - Another log parser, though it always times out when trying to goto the site, this link might be getting the axe shortly.
