This is a designed to be a means to cut down on searching for items with a specific focus, or spell effect on them.

For the roman numeraled effects, there is a level restriction to them;

I - Spells lvl 20 and below
II - Spells lvl 44 and below
III - Spells lvl 60 and below
IV - Spells lvl 65 and below

For Flowing Thought items, the roman numeral represents how many extra mana per tick you get, rememebr that this maxes out at a combined total of 15.
